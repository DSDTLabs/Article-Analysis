Vadodara: Food safety officers of the Vadodara Municipal Corporation (VMC) conducted extensive checking of packaged drinking water bottles across the city. The officers checked outlets selling these in Manjalpur, Karelibaug, Gorwa, railway station and Chhani areas of the city.

 Officials said that six samples were collected during the drive. These were of four different brands and would be analysed at the public health laboratory in the civic body. 
 
 If any adulteration comes to light, further action will be taken.
