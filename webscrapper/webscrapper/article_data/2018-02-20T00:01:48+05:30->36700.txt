NAVI MUMBAI: During the international football matches of   U-17 World Cup held in the city in October last year, the   ( ) had sent out formal notices to several textile and chemical industrial units in Nerul and   to shut down their operations while the international matches were on at Dr. D Y Patil Sports stadium in Nerul. 

 This was essentially done to ensure that the air quality index inside the football ground remains good and does not affect the players. 
 
 “It is rather ironic that such a big sports stadium in Nerul is located just opposite the   TTC industrial zone, which worsens the air quality,” said environmentalist D Stalin.
