The New Year is here, which means Tollywood cinema lovers can look forward to an exciting line-up of films set to hit theatres nationwide. It is especially a great time for fans of superstar Mahesh Babu, who announced three new films set to roll out this year.
 
Taking to Twitter, the ‘Brahmotsavam’ actor presented fans with his New Year's gift, which was announcing three upcoming film projects. The actor said that his next film will be with director Koratala Siva, who gave him the blockbuster ‘Srimanthudu’ in 2015. Post that, his next project will be with director Vamshi Paidipally that will be bankrolled by Dil Raju and Ashwini Dutt. Finally, Mahesh’s 26th film will be with director Trivikram Srinivas, who has already worked twice with the actor. 
 
 
 
The star also expressed his excitement to be a part of the AR Murugadoss project and said that his dream of working with the ‘supercool’ director has finally come true. Needless to say, Mahesh fans will be ecstatic to get their regular dose of action all year round.
