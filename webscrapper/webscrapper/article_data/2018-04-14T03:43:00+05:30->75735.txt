Chandrapur: Nagbhid police have arrested a 55-year-old man for raping two minor sisters in Taldohi Balapur village of Yavatmal.  The accused, Tilackchand Agnikar (55), lured the girls, who are aged seven and eight years, into a under construction building on Tuesday promising them to give Rs10 each for buying ice-cream, and raped them. The sisters narrated the incident to their parents on Thursday, who filed complaint with Nagbhid police station. Cops have booked the accused under rape and POCSO charges and arrested him on Thursday. He was sent into magisterial custody remand after being produced before the court.
