GHAZIABAD: In the wake of the upcoming   celebrations, security has been beefed up at Metro stations in Ghaziabad. 

 A double security check system has been put into place, with individual frisking being carried out by a CISF constable in the men’s queue and two female security representatives for the women.
 
 Speaking to TOI, the public relations officer and assistant commander of CISF, Manjeet Singh said, “We have introduced double checking at all Metro stations as a surprise element. We conduct these checks with separate teams at different times of the day. The number of personnel deployed and where is decided at the last moment.”
 
 There has also been a significant increase in the number of personnel deployed for surveillance at the Metro stations.
