GURUGRAM: Millennium City Expressway Private Limited (MCEPL) will be conducting a survey at the new location of the Kherki-Daula  , near   crossing in Bilaspur, around 11km further down NH-8 towards Jaipur from September 25 to October 1.

 The survey will assess the the profitability and feasibility of the new toll collection booth. MCEPL chief executive officer   said, “We are yet to receive a written notification from NHAI but we have been asked to assess the traffic movement at the new toll plaza location. In the survey we will find out whethere the new location is feasible for toll collection or not.”
