JAIPUR: Forest department at the   ( ) heaved a sigh of relief after T-83 also known as Lightning reunited with its two cubs on Saturday night - seven days after she had abandoned them.

 The tigress returned to the   forest area and fed her cubs. A forest official informed TOI, “the tigress stayed with the cubs throughout the night and fed them. She left the area around 4.30 am.”
 
 The forest department was concerned about the security and survival of them after Tigress T-83 had abandoned her two-and-half-month-old cubs. An official claimed, “The tigress possibly left again to protect her cubs as T-95 tiger, with whom she mated also reached the Amaghati area. We are hoping she would return again tonight. The movement of the tigress and cubs is being monitored constantly.”
