BELAGAVI: A constable who tried to rescue a girl from the clutches of troublemakers fell into a 20-ft well on Tuesday night. He was rescued only on Wednesday morning.

 Constable Raju Kerimani, attached to  , was returning home around 9 pm when he heard the screams of a woman coming from inside a car near Pearl Hotel off   .
 
 Constable Raju rushed to the car from where the screams were coming. When he neared the vehicle, four inmates from the car attacked Raju. Outnumbered, the constable ran. While escaping from his attackers, he fell into an open well in a field nearby .He spent the night in the well which had water.
 
 On Wednesday morning, the owner of the field heard Raju's cries for help and alerted police. The constable has fractured one of his legs.
