Shimla: Forest officials arrested two Haryana residents with a leopard hide from Solan on Sunday. Officials said the leopard hide was to be sold for Rs 70 lakh but the trio was arrested from the hotel they were staying in before the deal could be struck.  Officials said they had got a tip off that deal for leopard hide would be struck in the hotel the accused were arrested from and five teams were formed to arrest the accused involved in the illegal trade. The hide seized from the accused is around 8 feet length. A senior forest official said the teams formed to nab the accused had 30 officials from the department. The accused tried to flee from the spot but were arrested after a brief chase, he said.  Police officials said that those arrested have been identified as Manish (49) of Ambala and Sanjay (36) of Yamunanagar.
