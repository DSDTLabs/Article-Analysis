GURUGRAM: Forty-year-old Omprakash of Jatola village in Farrukhnagar tried to kill himself by consuming pesticide and then immolating himself using cooking gas on February 1 for allegedly being harassed over an old car loan for seven years. 

 He sustained 30%-40% burns and is undergoing treatment at   in Haripura, Gurgaon. 
 
 The victim said he has been depressed for seven years facing harassment at the hands of Sanjay Rewari, an   employee, and two other persons.
 
 An FIR was registered against them under sections 309 (attempt to suicide), 385 (putting person in fear of injury in order to commit extortion) and 506 (criminal intimidation) .
