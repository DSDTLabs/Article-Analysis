HYDERABAD: On the occasion of  ,   will be organising various programmes for children to promote awareness on wildlife, on Saturday. 

 The theme for this year is 'Big Cats- Predators Under threat.' Competitions like talk shows on big cats, drawing, and essay writing will be held between 10 am and 1 pm at the Children's Park. "We request the children to participate in large numbers in the above-said competitions and make this programme a grand success," read the release from the zoo.
 
 It further said, "Children below 15 age (up to class 10) are allowed to participate in the competitions. They can register at the spot and prizes, to the winners, will be distributed immediately after the completion of the event."
 
 Last year too, Zoo authorities organized various programmes for children. About 200 students participated.
