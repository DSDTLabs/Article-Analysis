NEW DELHI: A day after he was administered oath, lieutenant governor   made his debut on social media with   setting up an official   handle @LtGovDelhi. 

 In his first tweet, Baijal wished Delhhites a Happy New Year. "The LG has opened his new official twitter account for more productive engagement with media and fellow citizens," said an official at the LG's office. 
 
 The LG seen following the Twitter handles of the President and PM  . 
 
 The previous LG   was not active on social media but Baijal is clearly eager to engage with the online audience.
