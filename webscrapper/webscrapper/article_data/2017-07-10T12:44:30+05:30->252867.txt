 With Shimla police failing to nab those involved in the gruesome incident, public anger is increasing day by day. On Sunday, candle light march was organized by Shimla press club which was also attended by local residents, students besides journalists.
 
 People are demanding that Special Investigation Team ( ) should be formed under a senior police officer to solve this case at the earliest.
 
 AICC member and former general secretary of HPCC, Kuldeep Rathore said that barbaric act has shocked every resident of Himachal Pradesh.  CPM leader and former Shimla mayor, Sanjay Chauhan said that incident has exposed the poor law and order situation of state. “Incident shows that criminals have no fear of police,” he added. He too demanded formation of SIT to probe the case.
