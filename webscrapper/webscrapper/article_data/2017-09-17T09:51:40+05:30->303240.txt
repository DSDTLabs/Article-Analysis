KOLKATA: The elderly parents of an   resident, who works in Kathmandu, Nepal, are in a fix after their only son went missing after leaving his office on Thursday.

 Arindam   (39), who was working with a multinational company , has been living with his wife Olive in Kathmandu since 2013. Olive, who teaches in a local school there, informed her in-laws about her missing husband and the latter approached Asansol police.
 
 Asansol police station OC   said, “We have informed the higher authorities who will take up the issue with the Centre, that can take up the matter as it involves another country.“
