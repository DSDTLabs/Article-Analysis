VIJAYAWADA: Hyderabad boy   topped the Andhra Pradesh Eamcet-2017 ( ), the results of which were released on Friday. Most of the engineering entrance test toppers were from  , while AP candidates dominated the agriculture and medical stream.V Venkata Anirudh of Guntur topped the agriculture and medical stream test. 

 Girls outdid boys in agriculture and medical stream, while boys fared better in the engineering test. The entrance test results were declared by state HRD minister Ganta Srinivasa Rao in Vijayawada.
 
 Ganta Srinivasa Rao pointed out that conducting Eamcet online has helped in curbing malpractices and the results were announced nearly one week earlier than last year. "We have seen an increase of about 30,000 applicants for the engineering test and this shows that students prefer to do engineering courses in AP instead of going to other states," Srinivasa Rao said.
