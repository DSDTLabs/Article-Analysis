Vijayawada: Professors of Harvard University will conduct a field study at Uddanam in Srikakulam on Saturday.

 The team of professors will investigate the reason behind high number of kidney-ailment victims in the district. The team of experts will also visit Boruvanka of Kaviti mandal in Srikakulam, where huge number of kidney-disease victims have been reported. As per plans, the root cause will be uncovered. On Sunday, the professors will attend a convention being organised with other experts at Visakhapatnam. Janasena chief Pawan Kalyan, who has been vocal for the kidney patients, is expected to participate in the convention.
