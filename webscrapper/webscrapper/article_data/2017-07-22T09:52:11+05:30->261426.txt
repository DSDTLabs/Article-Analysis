AHMEDABAD: Seven inmates of   central jail made 101 idols of Lord Ganesha at the jail workshop over a period of two months, ahead of Ganesh Chaturthi that falls on August 25. On Friday, Navajivan Trust purchased all the idols from the jail authorities. These will now be available at the Navajivan office near Gujarat Vidyapeeth.
