This calendar launch was nothing short of a starry affair at this plush hotel in the city. The impressive guest list included actresses Regina Cassandra, Sanjana Singh and Taapsee Pannu. But the headturner of the evening was Bigg Boss Tamil fame Oviya. She looked flawless in a black asymmetrical dress and sported bare minimum accessories. Whereas, Regina, pulled off the power-dressing-look quite well, sporting a chic suit. Actress Kanika was also spotted among others. She turned up for the do in an emerald lace dress. Karthik Srinivasan and his wife Hema, played the perfect hosts, ensuring that the guests enjoyed the evening thoroughly.
