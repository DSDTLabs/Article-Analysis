THENI: Deputy chief minister   inaugurated a jallikatu event in Ayyampatti in Theni district on Sunday. The   is held every year in connection with the Ezhaikatha Mandikarasamy temple festival in Ayyampatti in Theni district. 

 Bulls from many parts of   including Madurai, Dindigul, Sivaganga and Natham participated in the event. Over 650 bulls and 600 qualified tamers participated in the game. Prizes ranging from utensils, gold coins, beds, chairs and cycles were given away to the bulls and bull tamers. 
 
 The deputyCM also participated in a community bangle ceremony for pregnant women organised by   services (ICDS). As many as 1,400 women were given gifts at the ceremony.
