PALANPUR: The local crime branch of   police on Thursday traced a 17-year-old boy who had allegedly created fake profiles of a class XI girl on social media and threatened her.

 The girl’s father approached police in September last year with a complaint that somebody had created fake Instagram and   profiles of his daughter with her photos as display picture. Later, a Whatsapp profile in her name was also created with a new mobile number. Someone harassed and blackmailed her through Facebook messenger. He also threatened to make the photos viral. 
 
 “We traced the boy who was familiar to the girl’s family. He used to come to the house of the girl whose father is a school teacher in Deesa,” said   police inspector, LCB. “We are verifying his age for further action,” he said. tnn
