 The body was identified as that of  , native of Hindoli town of Bundi district. He was living away from his family with another woman in Bundi, said  , SHO, Sadar police station. 
 
 Motorbike of the deceased with its disconnected petrol pipe was also found parked near the plot suggesting that petrol from the bike was used to set the man on fire, said Raghuveer Singh, ASI, Sadar police station. 
 
 The deceased reportedly ran a fruit juice stall. After the death of his wife three years ago, he had been living with another woman in Bundi, he added.
 
 On a report by son of the deceased, police lodged a case of murder under Section 302 of IPC against unidentified persons. Further investigation into the matter is underway,   said. The body was today noon handed over to the family members after post-mortem by medical board, he added.
