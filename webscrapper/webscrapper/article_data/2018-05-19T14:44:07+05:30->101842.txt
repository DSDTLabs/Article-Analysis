HYDERABAD: The Telangana engineering, agriculture and medical common entrance test (Eamcet) results were declared at 1pm on Saturday by education minister  . This is the first time that Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University-Hyderabad (JNTU-H) had conducted   online.

 
 
 Perigela Namratha of Kurnool secures top rank in agriculture and pharmacy with 93.38%. Ayyapu Venkata Phani Vamsinath of Hyderabad secures top rank in engineering with 95.72 per cent.
 
 The first phase of counselling will be held from May 25 to June 2 where qualified candidates can fill basic information online and pay the processing fee. Verification of certificates will be done from May 28 to June 3. Subsequently, candidates can exercise their options from May 28 to June 5, according to a press release.
 
 The TS EAMCET exams were conducted by the  , JNTU-Hyderabad from May 2 to 7, 2018.
 
 Provisional allotment of seats for the first phase will be made on June 8 after which candidates can pay the tuition fee and report at the colleges by June 12. “This year, the certificate verification will also be done online. If students have their mobile number linked to Aadhaar card, they do not have to go to the MeeSeva centre for verification of certificates,” said  , commissioner, collegiate and technical education.
 
 Candidates need to score a minimum of 25% of the maximum marks to qualify in Eamcet.
