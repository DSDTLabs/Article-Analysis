GHAZIABAD: A construction worker died after a balcony in a four-storied house partially collapsed in   on Tuesday. His identity is yet to the established.

 The incident took place in M Block of Pratap Vihar around 11:30 am. A railway employee, SN Banerjee, who lives on the ground floor of the building, said. “These two men had begun repair work of the second floor balcony when suddenly the third floor balcony collapsed upon them. The other worker sustained minor injuries,” Banerjee told TOI.
 
 The building had been constructed in 2004 and certain portions were in dilapidated state. The body has been taken into custody and sent it for a post-mortem. Efforts are on to trace his family members.
