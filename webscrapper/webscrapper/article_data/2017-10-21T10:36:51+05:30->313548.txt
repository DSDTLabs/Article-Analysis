 The action-comedy which released on the final day of Navratri, ahead of the long Dussehra and Gandhi Jayanti weekend, is expected to see good growth at the box-office.
 
 
 
 The plot of the film revolves around Raja (Varun) and Prem (Varun, again) who are conjoined twins and have similar reflexes. They are separated at birth by a smuggler, Charles (Zakir Hussain), who kidnaps Raja. The geek Prem grows up in the lap of luxury in London with his parents, the Malhotras; loud Raja finds shelter in a Mumbai's fisherman's colony. Of course, when the twins' paths cross, there's too much fun. The film also has the recreated versions of the original hit numbers ‘Chalti Hai Kya 9 Se 12’ and ‘Oonchi Hai Building 2.0’ which have been pepped up for this one. To top it all, Salman Khan also returns for the climax scene in his double role from the original.
 
 Besides Varun in a double role, the film also stars Anupam Kher, Upasana Singh, Rajpal Yadav, Pavan Malhotra, Ali Asgar, Vivan Bhatena and Vikas Verma in important roles.
