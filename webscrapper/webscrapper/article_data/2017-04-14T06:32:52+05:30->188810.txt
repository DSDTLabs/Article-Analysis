 Dhinakaran's senior counsel's plea to adjourn the case, on the grounds that a related case is pending in the Madras high court, did not find favour with the economic offence court-II judge S Malarmathi on Thursday. 
 
 When the matter came up for hearing on Thursday, Dhinakaran's counsel sought adjournment. However, judge directed counsel to commence arguments. When he said B Kumar, senior counsel for Dhinakaran, would appear before the court shortly, the case was passed over for 30 minutes. Kumar rushed to the court and the judge asked him to commence arguments.
