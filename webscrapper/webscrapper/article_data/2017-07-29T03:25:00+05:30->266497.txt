Margao: Margao police on Friday arrested four persons in connection with the death of Madar Sayed, 43, resident of Mariabandh, Navelim. The accused are Vipul Patari, Ayub Sayed, Shehjad Sayed and Akbar Melagudi, all residents of Navelim. The four were engaged in a scuffle at Mariabandh on July 24. The victim was on a motorcycle when he came between the warring parties. One of them pushed him, and he fell on the concrete pathway. The severely injured Sayed was rushed to GMC but he died on Thursday.
