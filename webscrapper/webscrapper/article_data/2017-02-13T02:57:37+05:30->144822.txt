 Birju and Surender, salesmen at the discovery wine shop located in Sector 37, informed police that four unidentified men riding two bikes reached the wine shop at 11.30 pm. Three out of the four had their faces covered. Three of the accused threatened the salesmen at gunpoint while the fourth man took out all the cash kept in drawer and emptied it in a cloth bag.
 
 According to the complaint filed by Birju, the accused managed flee with Rs 40,000 cash and two mobile phones in less than five minutes. He also pointed out that the accused would be between 20-25 years of age.
 
 An FIR was registered on Sunday at Sector 10 police station under sections 25, 54 and 59 of the Arms Act and Section 379 (punishment for theft) of the IPC.
 
 ASI Joginder Singh, the investigating officer said, “We have recovered the CCTV footage and are trying to track them down at the earliest.”
