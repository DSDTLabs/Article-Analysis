Rajkot: Pakistan Marine Security Agency (PMSA) apprehended two boats with 12 Gujarat fishermen off Jakhau coast on Wednesday.

 “Till now, we have confirmation that PMSA has apprehended two boats with 12 fishermen from the International Maritime Boundary Line (IMBL) on Wednesday. The number of apprehended fishermen might rise as we are in a process of getting exact number of fishermen and the boats apprehended by Pakistan,” said Manish Lodhari, secretary, National Fishworkers’ Forum (NFF). Lodhari said that two boats were from Porbandar. Earlier, on March 25-26, PMSA had apprehended 19 boats with 105 fishermen. These boats were from Porbandar, Okha and Mangrol. tnn
