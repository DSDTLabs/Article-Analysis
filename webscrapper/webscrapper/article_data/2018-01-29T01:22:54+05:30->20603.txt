Rajasthan will go to bypolls for two parliamentary seats in Ajmer, Alwar and one state assembly seat in Mandalgarh (Bhilwara) on Monday. In all these three seats, incumbent BJP is in direct contest with the Congress. The counting of votes will take on February 1.  The local administration has made all the arrangements for the free and fair polls. The electronic voting machines in the bypolls will have photos of candidates contesting elections. tnn
