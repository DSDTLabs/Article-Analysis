Visakhapatnam: The body of an 18-year-old engineering student was found on the beach near Vuda Park here on Saturday morning. Ashwin Kumar, a second year B Tech student of Raghu Engineering College, is believed to have committed suicide in the late hours of Thursday. Police said Kumar resorted to the extreme step over failed love.

 On Thursday night, he had gone to RK Beach and ventured into the sea leaving behind all his belongings, including bags and books.
 
 “Family members said the boy had attempted suicide earlier too. A case of suspicious death under section 174 of CrPC has been registered and we are investigating the case,” said B Venkata Rao, station house officer of III Town police station.
 
 A post-mortem was conducted at King George Hospital and the body was later handed over to the family.
