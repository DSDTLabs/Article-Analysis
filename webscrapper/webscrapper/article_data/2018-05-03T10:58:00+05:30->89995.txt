THIRUVANANTHAPURAM: The Kerala Board of Public Examinations (KBPE) announced the result of the Secondary School Leaving Certificate (SSLC) / Class 10th examination 2018 today. The Kerala Board KBPE Class 10th examination were held in March this 2018.

 
 As per reports, 97.84 per cent students have qualified for higher studies. The result was announced by education minister C Raveendranath here on Thursday. Of the 4,41,103 students appeared for the exam, about 4,31,162 have cleared the examination. Last year, the pass percentage was 95.98 per cent.

 Kerala education minister C Raeendranath announced the KBPE SSLC result at a news conference held at PRD chamber in Thiruvananthapuram at 10.30 am on May 3, 2018. 
 
 
 After the formal announcement, the Kerala Board Class 10th / SSLC results would be made available (around 11 am) on the mobile App PRD live and the following websites.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The students can visit the following websites to check their result. Just open any of the websites, click on the results link and then enter your details such as name, roll number, date of birth etc. After entering all the details, click on submit. Your result will be displayed online. Download or take a printout of your result for further reference.
 
 
 
 
 
 The results app - PRD Live - is available on google playstore for free download.
 
 The Kerala SSLC Examination 2018 (Class 10) was conducted by the KBPE Board from March 7 to 26, 2018. As per reports, the Kerala Pareeksha Bhavan had conducted the Class 10th examination for more than four lakh students.
