BARWANI: Police booked a forest guard for allegedly kidnapping his landlord’s minor daughter at Pansemal in Barwani district on Wednesday.

 SDOP   said forest guard Rakesh Thakur posted at Pansemal forest range office was booked under Section 363 and 366 of IPC and a manhunt has been launched to arrest him.
 
 Former-councillor of nagar panchayat lodged a complaint that his tenant Thakur had abducted his 15-year-old daughter on Tuesday night. He said his daughter was there in the room till 1 pm but was found missing in the morning. Girl’s mother is councillor at Nagar Panchayat Pansemal.
