VIZIANAGARAM/VISAKHAPATNAM: District education officer of Vizianagaram D   said guardians and parents of children studying in various government schools in the six drought-hit mandals of Gantyada, Vizianagaram, Kothavalasa, Vepada, Datti Rajeru and   can avail the mid-day meal scheme by acquiring per unit allotment of dal, rice and oil from the local ration shops even during the summer holidays. In an announcement made here on Wednesday, the DEO said the allotment would be made till the end of May 2017.
