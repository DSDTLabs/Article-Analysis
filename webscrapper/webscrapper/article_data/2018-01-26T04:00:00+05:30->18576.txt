Surat: The Fourth Dr Nanubhai Desai Sudoku Competition will be organized by Family Physicians Association, Indian Medical Association and Sarvajanik Education Society here on Friday. Sudoku enthusiasts in large numbers are likely to participate in the competition at K P Commerce College in Athwalines. There are two categories for participants in the competition where no entry fee will be charged.  Those below 60 will be in open category and older participants placed in senior citizen category. “We expect to get a good response from the participants since Sudoku is a popular game. We will be registering participants on the spot as well,” one of the organizing team members Dr Chandresh Jardosh said.
