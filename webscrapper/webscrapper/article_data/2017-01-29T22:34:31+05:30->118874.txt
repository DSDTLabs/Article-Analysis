Oscar-winning actress   gushed about her husband and musician  , saying he is the 'most beautiful man she has ever met'. 

  
  
 The 49-year-old actress heaped praise on her husband as she collected a trophy for the Best Supporting Actress at the 6th Australian Academy of Cinema and Television Arts International Awards in  .
 
 In her acceptance speech, the actress said, "To the most beautiful man I've ever met, Keith Lionel Urban. He's got a gypsy heart, and travels the world where I go," she said. Kidman and Urban, who shares two daughters together, tied the knot in 2006.
