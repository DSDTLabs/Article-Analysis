PANCHKULA: The 19-year-old youth, who allegedly murdered a 15-year-old girl on Wednesday night, died on Thursday. Mukesh Kumar had slit the throat of his 15-year-old neighbour girl Shaabi, with a sharp-edged weapon. He later consumed a poisonous substance. Family members had rushed them to General Hospital, Sector 6,  , where the girl was declared brought dead. "The accused died during treatment. Bodies of the victims were handed over to their family members after their post-mortem," said assistant commissioner of police Noopur Bishnoi.
