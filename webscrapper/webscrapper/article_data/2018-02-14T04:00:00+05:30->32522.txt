Vadodara: The general board of the Vadodara Municipal Corporation (VMC) will take up the budget for the year 2018-19 for discussion from Thursday. The budget meet is expected to last at least three days. The opposition may try to corner the ruling BJP over various issues including the hike in cleanliness charges and other services provided by the civic body. As many as 771 proposals and suggestions have been made by the opposition Congress and Rashtriya Samaj Paksha to the budget while the ruling BJP corporators have made none. TNN
