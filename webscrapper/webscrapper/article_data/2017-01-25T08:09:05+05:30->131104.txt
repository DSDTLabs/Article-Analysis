LUDHIANA: The court of Additional Sessions Judge   on Tuesday awarded life imprisonment to a tuition teacher for raping a minor student at his home and blackmailing her by making lewd videos of her.

 The court also slapped a fine of Rs 3.5 lakh on convict  , a 40-year-old resident of Bhai   Nagar.
 
 In September 2014, Pankaj had been booked by   police under Sections 376 (rape) and 506 (Punishment for criminal intimidation) of IPC and Section 2 of Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act. Later, the court had added some more sections in the case. The rape survivor had been taking tuition from the accused at his home for almost four years.
 
 In her complaint, the girl had alleged that the accused had called her at his residence before the class and allegedly raped her. He also threatened her not to reveal anything about the incident. The accused also made lewd videos of the rape survivor and blackmailed her. Later, the girl narrated the whole incident to her parents following which a complaint was lodged in Sarabha Nagar police station.
 
 CRIME AND PUNISHMENT
 
 The court awarded life imprisonment to Pankaj Arora under Section 6 of Protection of Children From Sexual Offences Act, 7-year imprisonment under Section 506 and 5-year imprisonment under Section 67 B of Information Technology Act, said complainant's counsels Parupkar Singh Ghumman, Gurbakhshish Singh Bedi and assistant public prosecutor
