NEW DELHI: The Punjab School Education Board (PSEB) is likely to release the PSEB Class 10th Result 2018 this week on its official website - pseb.ac.in. 

 However, the date and time of result announcement can not be confirmed with the officials.
 
 The PSEB had conducted the Class 10th examination in the month of March this year. The Punjab matriculation exams were conducted by the state education board from March 12-31, 2018.
 
 The students who have appeared for the   examination 2018, can visit the official website - pseb.ac.in - to check their result. 
 
 The Punjab Board Class 10 results have been postponed many times over the last few days. 
 
 As per information available on various websites, over 4.5 lakh students have appeared for the PSEB Class 10 examination in 2018. 
 
 The Punjab Board Class 12 results 2018 were announced on 23 April.
