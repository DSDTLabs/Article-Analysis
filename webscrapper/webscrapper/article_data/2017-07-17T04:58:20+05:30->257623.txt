NEW DELHI: Former Lok Sabha Speaker and the Opposition candidate for the Presidential elections,  , called on chief minister   to seek his support for Monday’s polls.

 Aam Aadmi Party had officially pledged its support for Kumar last week. The former speaker had first spoken to Kejriwal over phone and later met him and his family at the CM’s official residence in Delhi. “At the residence of Hon Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwalji. Thank you for your support and warm hospitality,” Kumar tweeted.
 
 Senior AAP member Sanjay Singh had said that the election for President and Vice-President should be above politics and that various parties should come together to decide on a consensus candidate.
