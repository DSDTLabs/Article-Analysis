VELLORE: Four people, including an 11-year-old girl, sustained burn injuries after they fell on to a pyre during a temple festival at Alangkuppam in   district of   on Thursday.

 The injured have been identified as M Saravanan of Alangkuppam, his daughter Sharmila, 11, and Santhosh and Sathiyavani. 
 
 They sustained 30% to 40% burn injuries after falling on to the pyre while performing the rituals during Poniyamman Theemithi Thiruvizha in the village around 7.30pm.
 
 They were rescued by locals and rushed to Government Hospital in Ambur.
