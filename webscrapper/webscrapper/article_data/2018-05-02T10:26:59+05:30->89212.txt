NEW DELHI: The State Board of Technical Education & Training, Telangana has released the TS POLYCET Results 2018 on the official website of the board on Tuesday, May 1, 2018.

 The students who have appeared for the examination can check   at the official website: polycetts.nic.in. 
 
 Last year the result was declared on May 6, 2017.
 
 Telangana State Board of Technical Education and Training has released the result four days after the scheduled date. The examination was conducted on April 21, 2018. 
 
 This year out of 1,21,393 students 74,168 boys and 47,225 girls have appeared for the TS POLYCET examination.
 
 After the announcement of the result, the counselling process is to begin within two to three weeks.
