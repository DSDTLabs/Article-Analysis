 Pandey, while interacting with the media at PWD guest house in Farrukhabad, also said "Everything is fine in the party and there are no differing voices." 
 
 "Once the committee will submit its report, we will introspect into the reasons for our loss in the Lok Sabha bypolls of Phulpur and Gorakhpur and will once again dedicate ourselves to the service of the people with greater vigour," Pandey said. 
 
 Pandey said that he has asked the party workers to visit houses of voters in order to increase contact and tell them about the achievements of NDA government at the Centre and also the state government. 
 
 "A Sabka Saath, Sabka Vikas programme will also be launched where beneficiaries of government schemes will take part," Pandey added.
