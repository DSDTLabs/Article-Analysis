 Addressing the media, president of Tamil Nadu Toddy Movement (TNTM) S Nallasamy said that about three lakh acres of agricultural land would remain barren if Kerala continues building the check dam.
 
 "We depend entirely on the Bhavani River for irrigation," he said. He also said that Kerala's decision would not only impact Erode district, but it will impact neighbouring districts as well, including Coimbatore, Tirupur and Karur.
 
 The farmers later raised slogans against the Kerala government. They also urged the Tamil Nadu government to take steps to stop the check dam construction works at the earliest.
