BHUBANESWAR: More than one crore children have been vaccinated with the   (MR) vaccine in the last 19 days across the state. The drive that started on January 29 has covered 70,367 by February 26,   sources said. 

 At present, the community-level campaign is going on and will continue till March 16. Till date, 37,160 outreach vaccination sessions have been conducted in villages. Children between nine months to five years, non-school going children and school left outs were vaccinated at  ,  , official sources said. 
 
 All districts have re-planned and conducting mop up drive to vaccinate schoolchildren who are not vaccinated during the school campaign. The mop up drive will start from March 17, 2018, to ensure every child is vaccinated.
