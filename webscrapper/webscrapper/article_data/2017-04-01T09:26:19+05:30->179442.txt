Pawan Kalyan’s ‘Katamarayudu’ and Allu Arjun’s ‘Duvvada Jagannadham’s’ teasers were released more than a month ago and ever since then, fans of both the superstars are at war with the number of views and likes the teaser got on You Tube.

 While 'Katamarayudu’s' teaser clocked 11,418,796 views, 'Duvvada Jagannadham’s' teaser on the other hand has reached 11,433,246 views on its official YouTube channel so far. Allu Arjun’s fans are elated and excited for taking over ‘Katamarayudu’.
 
 Nevertheless, ‘Duvvada Jagannadham’s’ teaser is still lagging behind with 190k likes as compared to ‘Katamarayudu’s’ teaser which gained 264k likes.
