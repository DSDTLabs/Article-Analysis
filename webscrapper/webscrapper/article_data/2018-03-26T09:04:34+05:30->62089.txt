BHOPAL: The state cyber cell nabbed a 22-year-old youth from Delhi for allegedly duping a 50-year-old woman, who works as a bank manager in the city. 

  The swindler promised to give bonus on her insurance policy on its maturity. The arrested youth and his elder brother called her up in 2016 and asked her to deposit money in different bank accounts which she did. 
 
 The manager was duped of Rs 27 lakh over a period of Almost two years. The victim later lodged a police complaint a few days ago after the accused dilly-dallied in giving her promised bonus. 
 
 The elder brother of the accused is absconding.
