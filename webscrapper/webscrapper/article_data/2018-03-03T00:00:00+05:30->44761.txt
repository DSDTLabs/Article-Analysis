Actor Vasishta N Simha had earlier confessed to us in an interview that he came to Bengaluru to be a   and became an actor by chance. He was thrilled when he made his singing debut with Kirik Party with the angst-filled track Neecha Sullu Sutho Naalige. Now, Vasishta has recorded the same track for the   remake of Kirik Party, which has been titled Kirrak Party. The Telugu film once again has B Ajaneesh Loknath as the composer and the filmmakers of the remake have decided to retain the same interval track. Vasishta recorded the song this week in Bengaluru, which will mark his debut as a singer in Telugu  .
