CHENNAI: The Chennai city   on Tuesday created a   to transport a heart and   from Chennai airport to Global Hospitals in Perumbakkam.

 The heart and lungs were harvested from a road accident victim in Trichy who was declared brain dead. The 30-year-old man's organs were airlifted from Trichy airport and brought to Chennai.
 
 An ambulance carrying the organs covered 17km in 14 minutes after it started from the airport at 12.56pm. All signals en route remained green. The organs reached the hospital at Perumbakkam via Pallavaram, Echankadu, Kovilambakkam junction and Medavakkam.
