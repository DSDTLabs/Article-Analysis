BATHINDA: Out of turn use of canal water for irrigating fields led to the killing of a farmer by his neighbour and son at   village of Mansa district on Saturday. 

 The victim, Bogha Singh (55), was working in his field and was waiting for his turn for using canal water when he had an altercation with his neighbour   and his son  , who wanted to use it out of turn. Bogha’s son   stated in his police complaint that his father was waiting for his turn when Ghuman and Baru attacked him with a sharp-edged weapon following the argument. Both escaped after the crime and were still at large.
 
 Bareta SHO   said, “A case under murder charges has been registered against Ghuman Singh and his son Baru Singh and efforts are being made to arrest them. Both are absconding.”
