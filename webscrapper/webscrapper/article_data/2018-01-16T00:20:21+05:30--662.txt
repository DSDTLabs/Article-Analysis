Pune: The crime branch of the Pune police rescued a one-and-a-half-year-old boy from the clutches of his kidnappers after the arrest of a beggar couple from a village in Karjat on Monday.

 Nasib Bagadi was kidnapped on January 9 from Maldhakka Chowk. A team headed by assistant commissioner of police (crime) Sameer Shaikh and inspector Raghunath Jadhav rescued the child, following which he was handed over to his parents.
 
 The suspects have been identified as Chanda (25) and Manoj Chavan (30).
