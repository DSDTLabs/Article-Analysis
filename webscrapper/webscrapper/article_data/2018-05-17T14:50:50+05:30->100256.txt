KOHIMA: Nagaland Board of School Education (NBSE), Kohima, in an official notification announced that the provisional results of the HSLC (Class 10) and HSSLC (Class 12) Examinations 2018 will be declared on May 18, 2018. The Nagaland Board results are expected to be announced by 12 pm. 

 The Nagaland Board HSLC and HSSLC examination 2018 were conducted by the NBSE in the month of March-April 2018. 
 
 
 
 
  
  
 
 The Results of the Nagaland Board Class 10 and Class 12 will be released in the afternoon of May 18, 2018. The results will be live on official website of the Board - www.nbsenagaland.com
