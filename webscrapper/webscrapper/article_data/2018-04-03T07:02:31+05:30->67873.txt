LUCKNOW: Additional sessions judge   on Monday granted bail to former   minister Gayatri   in a case of kidnapping and eveteasing. 

  The court also granted bail to co-accused  . 
 
 It directed them to file two sureties of Rs 50,000 each and personal bonds of the same amount.
 
 Filing the bail application, the accused had pleaded that they were innocent and had been implicated in the case. They also stated that even the complainant had denied having lodged any FIR against them.
 
 On October 26, 2016, a woman from Chitrakoot had allegedly lodged an FIR with Gomtinagar police against Prajapati and Shukla. Prajapati was taken into judicial custody in the case on April 29, 2017. 
 
 The police had filed a chargesheet in the case on July 27, 2017.
