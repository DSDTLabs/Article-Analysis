Margao: The Holy Spirit Church, Margao will celebrate it’s feast on May 20. The Church will also complete 453 years of its existence. “Maddi (blessing of the banner) was held on May 10. Also, 15 new eucharistic ministers, who have successfully completed their liturgical animators course, were inducted by the parish priest. The theme this year is Povitr Atmeachem foll (Gifts of the Holy Spirit). On the feast day, masses will be held at 5am, 6am, 7am, 8.15am, 9.30am (feast mass) and 11.45am (English),” said parish priest Fr Avinash Rebelo.
