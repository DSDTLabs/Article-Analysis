AHMEDABAD: A city-based organization   organized a discourse on contemporary issues of   in the country.   award winner Sandip Pandey, Rajiv Yadav of Rihai Manch and many other from dalit and Muslim community participated in the event. 

 City-based Muslim rights activist   said that the aim of the seminar was to discuss the condition and socio-political future of Muslims in India. A resolution was also passed to promote and strengthen the dalit-Muslim unity, to hold conversation between cast and community, to strengthen secular values and to stop encroachments on Waqf property. The participants also agreed to make bids to give compensation to the victims who were framed under terror charges and later acquitted by the court.
