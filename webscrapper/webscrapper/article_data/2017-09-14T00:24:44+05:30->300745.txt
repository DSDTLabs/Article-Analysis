KALYAN: Police have   a 45-year-old police   for allegedly molesting his   daughter. The accused, after drinking alcohol at home, allegedly molested her and abused her when she protested. 

 The victim alleged in her complaint that on Sunday afternoon, when he was on night duty, he came home in the afternoon and after drinking alcohol molested her. The victim on Tuesday informed her mother who approached the police station where he works and got an FIR filed under sections of the Indian Penal Code and Prevention of Children from Sexual Assault Act (Pocso). Acting on the complaint, a team arrested the constable and on Wednesday produced him before the Kalyan session court, which remanded him to police custody.
