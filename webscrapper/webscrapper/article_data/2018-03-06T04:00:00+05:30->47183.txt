Surat: National minorities commission member Sunil Sindhi held a meeting on Monday with leaders of minority community in city and officials responsible for implementation of minority schemes in Surat. On the occasion another member of National Minorities commission Vada Dasturji Khurshid K Dastur was also present. Sindhi said that it is mandatory for all state and central government departments to use 15% of its funds for the development of minority community. tnn
