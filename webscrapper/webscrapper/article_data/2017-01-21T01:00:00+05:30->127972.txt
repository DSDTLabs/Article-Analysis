Joining the masses, who are protesting across the state for a favourable move against jallikattu ban, are Kollywood celebrities. A large turnout of stars, including, big names from the film industry like Rajinikanth, Kamal Haasan, Suriya and Ajith, among others, came together at the Nadigar Sangam grounds at T Nagar to show their solidarity with the movement. Most of them chose to wear black as a mark of protest. It was a silent protest which lasted through the day. Also, spotted at the event were Trisha, Lissy, Rohini and Sukanya.
