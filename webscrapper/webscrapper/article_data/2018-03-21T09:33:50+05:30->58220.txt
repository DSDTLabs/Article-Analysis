SURAT: Surat District Bar Association (SDBA) has announced that its members would stay away from work on Wednesday to register their protest against the incident at Vadodara court campus. SDBA has asked its members to stay away from work on Wednesday and maintain peace.

 Currently, a number of lawyers sit under parking sheds on the court campus. The lawyers used to sit in the old court building which was recently demolished. After the demolition, lawyers were provided place to sit in the new court building in different floors, but majority of them are yet to evacuate the car parking.
