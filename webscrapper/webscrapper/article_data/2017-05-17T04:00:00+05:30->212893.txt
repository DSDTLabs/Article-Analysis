Vadodara: The special operations group (SOG) of city police arrested two persons wanted for bootlegging from different areas of the city on Tuesday. Police arrested Nilesh alias Nilu Nathani, a resident of Ajwa Road, near Khodiyarnagar during night patrolling. Cops said that Nathani was declared absconding by rural police in connection with a prohibition case registered at Waghodia police station. According to the police, Nathani was booked in 17 cases at Bapod, Karelibaug, City, Panigate, Waghodia and Dabhoi police stations. Nathani has been detained under Prevention of Anti-Social Activities Act (PASA) six times in the past.TNN
