NEW DELHI: The National Commission for Women ( ) on Saturday ordered a probe on Friday’s incident where JNU students and media personnel were allegedly manhandled while a woman journalist molested. 

 A day after the police were rapped for their behaviour, NCW took a serious note and sent a team to meet the police officers. The commission’s acting chairperson Rekha Sharma said they “will further pursue the matter on Monday.”
 
 Meanwhile, police on Saturday claimed that they are probing the allegations of a journalist being allegedly molested by a cop and will come out with a decision in the next two days. An FIR in the case is yet to be registered, police said.tnn
