Despite being a superstar on the screen, everyone admires   for the humble family man he is. After undergoing his routine medical check-up in the US, Rajinikanth returned to Chennai on Saturday. Interestingly, the ‘Thalaivar’ was clicked celebrating his grandson Ved’s birthday at his residence in Chennai.

  An excited Soundarya Rajinikanth took to Twitter to share the pictures. She wrote, “That’s celebration with family !!! #HappyBirthdayVed.” Apart from Rajinikanth, the occasion also saw the presence of Dhanush, wife Aishwaryaa and music composer Anirudh Ravichander.
 
  On the other hand, Rajinikanth is gearing up for the audio launch of his much-awaited film ‘Kaala’, which will take place at YMCA ground in Chennai on May 9. The launch will have the presence of the entire ‘Kaala’ team along with composer Santhosh Narayanan, who is expected to perform live at the venue.
 
  Produced by Dhanush’s Wunderbar Films, ‘Kaala’ is directed by Pa Ranjith and is scheduled to release on June 7.
