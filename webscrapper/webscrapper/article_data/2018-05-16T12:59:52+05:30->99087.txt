MADURAI: Four pilgrims, including a 12-year-old boy were killed and 14 others injured in a road accident that took place in   district in the early hours of Tuesday. 

 The victims were identified as Pushparajan, 36, his son Ribbon, 12, Punitha, 32, and van driver, 24, all from Thengaipattinam in Kanyakumari district. Police said three families from the village set on a pilgrimage to   in Nagapattinam district. In total, 17 people travelled in the tourist van. 
 
 The incident took place at Naganendal near Tiruvadanai when the van collided with the sand-laden truck coming from Kattumannarkovil in Cuddalore district and proceeding to Ramanathapuram.
 
 On being alerted, a police team from   station rushed to the spot and began rescue works.
 
  The 14 injured, including five children were shifted to the Ramanathapuram government hospital. Based on a complaint a case was also registered. 
 
 The incident brought traffic to a halt for more than an hour. An earthmover was brought to the spot to remove the mangled remains of the vehicle off the road to resume the traffic. 
 
 Ramanathapuram district collector N   visited the injured in the hospital and consoled them.
