LUDHIANA: In view of Ludhiana municipal corporation elections, a complaint cell has been established by municipal corporation commissioner,   on the directions of district electoral officer-cum-deputy commissioner   to deal with election-related complaints.

 The cell which started its operations from February has already received five complaints regarding illegal posters, said Kaur adding that action is being taken accordingly on these complaints.
 
 Nodal officer of the cell-cum-MC joint commissioner   said the cell will function till February 24 and it has been set up in MCs Zone-B office near Samrala Chowk. Residents can contact the cell at 0161-2224312 or lodge complaint on WhatsApp number 6280805734. They can also can send e-mails to mclcomplaintcell@gmail.com.
