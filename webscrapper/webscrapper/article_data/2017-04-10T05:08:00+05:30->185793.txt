Guwahati: In a joint operation in   district on Sunday, Assam Police and the Army’s Red Horns Division of Gajraj Corps along with other security forces busted a National Democratic Front of Boroland (Songbijit) hideout in the dense Manas reserve forest. 

 A huge cache of arms including one self-loading rifle, one carbine, three single barrel rifles, two hand grenades and large quantity of ammunition were seized from the camp. Besides ration and other items, solar panels were also found at the spot. 
 
 Sources said, “These items may have been used by NDFB (S) militants who are suspected to be part of G Bidai group and have been on the run since the December 2014 massacre.”
