ULHASNAGAR: A 13-year-old minor boy died in a firing held during   function in   on Wednesday wee hour.

 The boy identified as Pratik Gaikwad. He was resident of Gaikwadpada area in Ulhasnagar. Pratik on Tuesday night had gone to his neighbour Satyajeet Gaikwad's residence to enjoy DJ music organised on occasion of Naming Ceremony.
 
 During the program an unidentified person made firing in which bullet hit to Pratik's chest. 
 
 The locals later taken Pratik to Central hospital for treatment where on Wednesday at 2 am, he died during the treatment. The Shivaji Nagar police probing case said they have registered case and further probe is on.
