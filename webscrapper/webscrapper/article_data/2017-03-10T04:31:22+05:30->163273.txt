NOIDA:   has appealed to consumers across Noida to clear their pending bills with immediate effect failing which, they could face power disconnection. 

 Meanwhile, consumers with errors in their bills have been urged to get them rectified at the nearest discom offices.
 
 According to Mukul Singhal, superintending engineer PVVNL, out of the total 1.85 lakh consumers of Noida city, nearly 18,000 have not yet paid their bills for the month of February and earlier dates.
 
 While most of the defaulters are from rural areas such as Harola, Barola, Nithari and Sarfabad, consumers in urban sectors have also not paid their bills, Singhal said. “Hence immediate disconnections are already under way l for the month of February,” Singhal told TOI on Thursday.
