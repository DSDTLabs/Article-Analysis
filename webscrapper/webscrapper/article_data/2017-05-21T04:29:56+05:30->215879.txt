SRINAGAR: Two soldiers were martyred and two terrorists were killed in crossfire on Saturday evening in Jammu and Kashmir’s   district during an infiltration bid.

 Sources told TOI that the encounter was triggered after the   spotted suspicious movement along the   sector of the Line of Control (LoC).
 
 Four to five terrorists were trying to cross over to the Indian side. “The militants fired at soldiers. In the exchange of fire two soldiers and two militants and two soldiers also lost their lives,” a source said.
