VASCO: Seeking to enhance passengers’ travelling experience, the Vasco railway station on Sunday started the facility of free Wi-Fi service. 

 Though passengers will not be able to download content, they will be able to access the internet.
 
 Vasco railway station is only the second in the state, after Margao, to offer this service. It is, however, the first station under the South Western Railway in Goa be offer this service. The Margao railway station comes under the  . 
 
 Speaking about the facility, Vasco railway station manager P C   said that, “The service is available on all platforms. Each customer will be able to avail of free Wi-Fi for half an hour.”
 
 The public, in the vicinity of the station, will benefit from this facility.
