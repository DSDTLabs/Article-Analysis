A two-day affair — including a fashion show and expo — brought the fashionistas in the city under one roof recently. From flaunting the best designer costumes to showcasing their taste in fashion, this one was all about lifestyle choices women and men make to stand out from others.
 
 Saris draped in unconventional ways, outfits merging the traditional and the modern, symmetrical shimmery dresses and flowy lehengas rocked the ramp as models walked in. The evening couldn’t get any better as city-based violinist Shravan Sridhar added to the ambience and chic mood with his melodies.
