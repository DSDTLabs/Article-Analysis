
 
  Meanwhile, Naveen’s earlier film, Actor, has made it to the competition section of the 9th Bengaluru International Film Festival starting early next month. The film had premiered in last year’s BIFFes and this year made it to the Kannada competition section, bagging the distinction of being screened in consecutive festivals.
  
  In a parallel, Actor was shot in a single location, inside a home and, for the most part, Naveen was the only character. However, in Ee Kalarava, there is a substantial star cast including Rohini, Bharadwaj, Lakshmi, Niharika and others.
