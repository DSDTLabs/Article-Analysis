 DFO Gopal Ojha said 567 turtles will be released into Ganga at a function at Rajghat. An awareness programme would also be organised on the occasion to educate the public about the importance of conservation of nature. About 350 students for various schools will participate in the programme.
 
 Ojha said, "So far, over 48,000 turtles have been released into the river since 1987. Among them 42,087 turtles were reared at the breeding centre in Sarnath, while 5,985 turtles were rescued from poachers."
