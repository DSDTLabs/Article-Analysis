The puja and switch on function of directors Sajir Majeed and Vibin Velayudhan's film 'Karl Marx Bhaktanayirunn' was held at   of Sanskrit, Kalady, in Kochi.

 The event, which was attended by many from Mollywood film industry, also had the film's hero Dheeraj and heroine Gopika in attendance. The movie is scripted by Viju Ramachandran and has music by 'Oppam' fame composers 4 Musics, and Sreejith.
  
  
 
 Most of the crew members of the project are newbies and the film is expected to go on floors soon.
