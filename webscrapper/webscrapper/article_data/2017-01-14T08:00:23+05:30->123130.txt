Security personnel have been pressed into action to save the abducted government officials, while family members of the officials appealed to the naxals to release their kin.
 
 The five persons have been identified as poll officials Hrushikesh Naik and Purshottam Behra, junior engineers Praveen Soren and Kamini Kanth as well as Jodamba village head  's son, sources said. Another officer Lingaraj Majhi managed to escape.
 
 Maoists had faced a major setback due to the Ramaguda encounter in the cut-off area in October last year where 31 naxals and their sympathisers were killed, sources said.
