Guntur: City-based super specialty health care institute   has received yet another prestigious award. This time it was awarded for being a ‘patient friendly hospital’ by Association of HealthCare Providers India (AHPI).

 AHPI every year measures and evaluates hospitals nationally on quality, excellence of care, patient friendliness and cleanliness parameters, before picking the best among the industry. Ramesh Hospitals chief operating officers’ P Gopi Krishna and Dr Rayapati Mamatha received the award from KK Shailaja Teacher, Kerala health minister at a glittering function held in Kochi on Friday.
 
 MD of Ramesh Hospitals Dr P Ramesh Babu said, “Such awards are evidence of the quality of treatment that the hsopital provides. These awards increase our responsibility.”
