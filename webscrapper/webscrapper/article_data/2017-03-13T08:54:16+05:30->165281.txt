PANAJI: Defence minister Manohar   on Sunday said that “tasks performed in the defence ministry is done in the interest of the country, and I am proud that there was not a single allegation of corruption in the last two and a half years on Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s government”.

 Speaking to reporters, Parrikar said that the defence ministry was previously stained with allegations. “I am proud for clearing lot of contracts for armed forces, which will benefit the nation with crore of rupees,” he added.
