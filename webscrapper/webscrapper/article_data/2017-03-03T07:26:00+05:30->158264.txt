BATHINDA: Daughter of an   ( ) personnel on Thursday committed suicide by hanging from the ceiling fan at the residential quarters at Air Force station, Bhisiana, around 25km from  .

 Pooja Sharma, 19, daughter of airman Nand Kishore Sharma, a native of Champara in Bihar, was reportedly under stress for not concentrating on studies, said police. She had got 68% marks in Class XII (non-medical) last year and had failed to get admission in BSc (non-medical) at the Government Rajindra College, Bathinda. 
 
 Inquiry officer Gurmeet Singh of Balluana police post said, “Legal formalities under section 174 (unnatural death) of CrPC were completed and the body was handed over to her family after autopsy.”
