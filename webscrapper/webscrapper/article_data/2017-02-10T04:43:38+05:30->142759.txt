Ghaziabad: Rashtriya Lok Dal candidate from  , Sudesh Sharma, has alleged that two bike-borne men fired at his party office on Thursday evening. No one was injured in the firing. “Around 6 pm, when fifty to sixty party workers were present in the office, two men on a black apache bike stopped near the office,” said Sudesh Sharma. “ Suddenly they took out a gun and fired a shot at the office. They fled from the spot,” said Sharma. The police, which reached the spot shortly after the incident, have started an investigation.
