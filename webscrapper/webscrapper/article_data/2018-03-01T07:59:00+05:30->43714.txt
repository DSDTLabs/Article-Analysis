CHANDIGARH: The special investigation team (SIT) of Panchkula on Tuesday night arrested  , alias  , a close confidant of Dera Sacha Sauda head   Ram Rahim and a member of the core committee of the dera, for his involvement in the violence on August 25, 2017. 
 
 He was produced before a Panchkula court, which sent him to four-day police remand. While producing Rakesh before the court, SIT officials said that his custodial interrogation was required as he was in touch with fugitive dera spokesperson   before and after the violence that broke out in Panckukla and other places after the conviction of Gurmeet in a rape case. According to information, Rakesh had not only accompanied Gurmeet to Panchkula on the day of judgement in the case against him but had also actively attended the meetings at the dera headquarters in Sirsa, which were chaired by  , alleged adopted daughter of the dera head, on August 17. 
  
 “Since he was a member of the internal committee of 11 members of the dera, we are hopeful of getting good details from him. We are hopeful of leads about Dr Aditya and other accused wanted in the case. He is among those accused on whose head police had announced a reward of Rs 50,000,” said SIT chief Mukesh Malhotra.
