Sridevi, a versatile actress, died at the age of 54 on February 24, 2018, in Dubai where she was attending wedding ceremonies of her nephew Mohit Marwah.  Sridevi was one of the most successful Bollywood actress, in fact, she was also regarded as the first female "superstar" of her era.  The charm of Sridevi was not limited to Bollywood, the actress had shown her metal in regional cinema too. She starred in Tamil, Telugu, Hindi, Malayalam, and Kannada films as well.
