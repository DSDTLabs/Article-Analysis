BENGALURU: JD(S) president and former chief minister   on Monday released his party's manifesto in which he promises farm loan waiver if voted to power. 

 The prominent among the promises made for people of Karnataka include farm loan waiver within 24 hours of taking oath, if elected to power, and a special pension scheme for senior citizens.
 
 After releasing the manifesto, Kumaraswamy said he is confident that he will become the King and not the kingmaker.
 
 He also ruled out secret understanding or an alliance with either BJP or Congress.
