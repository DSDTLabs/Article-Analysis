BHOPAL: The Barkatullah   will crackdown on outsiders allegedly living in the hostels on the campus. Sources said that the university will organise regular inspection in this regard. This step has been taken after the recent clashes between two groups of the hostels.

 A senior official said that several outsiders have been living in the hostels, reportedly. “This is a major problem. Due to this, several times clashes have taken place. University will take regular inspection now,” said the official without quoting his name.
 
 Incidentally,   is likely to expel nine hostellers from the university’s two hostels after police registered an FIR against them last week, following a clash between the two groups. The BU administration has also summoned their parents. “BU is studying the FIR and action will be taken soon,” said university official.
 
 A senior officer of the university, on the condition of anonymity, said that the hostellers did not inform about their fight and went on filing complaints against each other. “Students should have informed us before taking police’s help,” said the official.
 
 About the objection regarding police entry in the hostel without informing the higher authorities of the university, the official said, “The students can’t raise this issue now. They went on to filing the complaint against each other without informing us. In such a situation, the police had no option but to control the situation.”
