 On this occasion, Major General Vinod Sharma, General Officer Commanding (GOC), Madhya Uttar Pradesh Sub Area (MUPSA) along with serving officers, veterans and jawans laid the wreath in memory of all those brave martyrs who laid down their lives in the defence of the motherland. The last post was sounded and a two-minute silence was observed for peace of the departed souls.
 
 Army Day is celebrated on January 15 every year in recognition of Field Marshal KM Cariappa’s taking over as the first Commander-in-Chief of the Army from Francis Butcher, the last British Commander, in 1949. Army Day marks a day to salute the valiant soldiers who sacrificed their lives to protect our country and our people.
