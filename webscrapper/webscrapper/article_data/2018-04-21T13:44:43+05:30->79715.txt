NEW DELHI: The   (HPBOSE) is likely to announce its Class 12th results on or before April 25, 2018 while the Class 10 results will be released later.

 
 Last year, the Class 10 and Class 12 results were declared in the first week of May. However, an official said, the board is planning to declare the results bit early this year and the efforts are being made to declare the results at least one week prior to the last year. "We have set an internal deadline to declare the Class 12 results on April 25," he said. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
  
 
 HPBOSE had conducted the Class 10 Examination from March 7 to 20, 2018 and Class 12 exams were held from March 6 to 29, 2018.
 
 Last year, the HPBOSE Class 10 Examination was started from March 4 and concluded on March 17 and the HPBOSE Class 12 Examination was started from March 3 and concluded on March 28.
