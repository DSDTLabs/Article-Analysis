MARGAO: A   erected in a private property located at Calconda, Margao, was found to be desecrated on Friday morning. Senior police officials visited the spot of the incident. Fingerprint experts also lifted some impressions from the site, it is learnt. Sources said that the cross was erected in memory of a policeman murdered years ago.

 The site of the incident is metres away from the Krishna temple, the ‘nandi’ idol inside which was desecrated ten days ago. Margao town police have booked an offence against unidentified accused and investigations are underway.
