GADCHIROLI: The Gadchiroli   arrested two Naxalites from the Etapalli forest area of the district, on Tuesday.

 The Naxalites, who are members of Platoon 3, have been identified as Nilesh Potawi and Ajit Pude. The Gadchiroli police arrested Potawi and Pude during a search operation to track down anti-Naxal activities. The arrested Naxalites were allegedly involved in various encounters, arsons and murders, police said.
