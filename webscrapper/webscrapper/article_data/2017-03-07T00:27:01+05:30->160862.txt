MUMBAI: The city crime branch late on Sunday raided a five-star   in   (east) and arrested 18 businessmen   inside the hotel room. 

 All 18 were booked under various sections of the IPC and were later released on cash bail and surety. Acting on a tip-off, the crime branch unit 8, led by assistant commissioner Vinay Kulkarni, raided room no. 1120 and arrested those playing a card game there. “We have also seized Rs 6.65 lakh in cash, a diary and plastic coins worth Rs 3 lakh from the room,” said an officer on condition of anonymity.
