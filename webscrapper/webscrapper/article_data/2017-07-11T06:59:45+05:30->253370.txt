JAMMU: Internet services were suspended in the Jammu region today after the terror attack on 
  pilgrims in Jammu and Kashmir's Anantnag district, official said.

 Internet and broadband services in the region have been suspended as a precautionary measure following the killing of 
  pilgrims in a terrorist attack in the Kashmir Valley, the SSP control room said.
 
 Terrorists tonight killed seven pilgrims from Gujarat, including six women, and injured 32 others as they struck at a bus in the district, in the worst attack on the annual pilgrimage since the year 2000.
