The court also directed to keep in mind the Council of Scientific & Industrial Research (CISR) and The National Environmental Engineering Research Institute (NEERI) report of 1985 to decide the width ad conservation of the river.
 
 The court also directed to authenticate the plan of the river by settlement commissioner and asked him to present in the court on March 22. It may be recalled that a large number of tenant shave claimed to have their land at the proposed river front and had field case sin the court.
 
 S D Khaspuria counsel for the petitioner said the court was trying to verify the position in 1955 when the Rajasthan Tenancy Act came into the force and the present situation. There were three settlements after the Tenancy Act came into force in 1955.
