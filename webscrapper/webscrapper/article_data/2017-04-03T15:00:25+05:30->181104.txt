CHENNAI: The   (Puratchi Thalaivi Amma) has replied to the notice issued by the Election Commission asking the party to explain the charges that it is misusing the electric pole symbol.

 The commission had asked party's R K Nagar candidate E Madhusudhanan to explain by April 3 why action should not be taken against the party for misusing the symbol.
 
 "We have replied that we are only explaining to the voters that our symbol consists of an electric pole with two lamps on the sides. There is no violation of the EC rules and we are not misusing the symbol to make it resemble as AIADMK's two leaves symbol," said a senior leader of the party.
 
 AIADMK (Amma) candidate and party's deputy general secretary TTV Dhinakaran had lodged a complaint with the EC alleging that the AIADMK (Puratchi Thalaivi Amma) was misusing the allotted symbol.
