Mumbai: The former alliance partners in the BMC—Shiv   and BJP—came together for the ward   elections held on Tuesday. 

 Akhil Bharatiya Sena corporator Geeta Gawli was elected chairman for wards A, B and E.   corporator Jyotsna Mehta was declared the winner for chairmanship of wards C and D. In F-south, Sena’s Prahlad Tombare was declared the chairman. TNN
