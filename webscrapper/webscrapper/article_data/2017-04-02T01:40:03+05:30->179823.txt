Mumbai:   Railway (CR) and   Railway (WR) will operate a block on its suburban corridors to carry maintenance of railway infrastructure on Sunday. 

 On CR, a block will be taken on Kalyan-bound fast line between Kalyan-Thane from 11.30 am to 4 pm. 
 
 On   line, a block will be taken on up and down lines between Kurla-Vashi from 11.10 am to 4.10 pm. 
 
 However special local trains will run on CST - Kurla and Vashi - Panvel sections during the block period. On WR, block will be taken from 10.30 am to 4 pm on Up and down fast lines between Andheri and Bandra.
