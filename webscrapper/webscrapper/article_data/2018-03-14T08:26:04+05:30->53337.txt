CHENNAI: Schools in the southern districts of Tirunelveli and Tuticorin in Tamil Nadu will be closed on Wednesday due to heavy rains.

 However, board exams will be conducted as usual. Tirunelveli and Tuticorin district collectors declared the holiday in the wake of deluge.
 
 M S university too declared holiday for colleges in Tirunelveli and Thookudi districts.
 
 On Tuesday, rainfall occurred at isolated places over Tamil Nadu, Telangana and South Kerala while dry weather prevailed over North Kerala, Andhra Pradesh, Lakshadweep and Karnataka.
 
 Heavy rain is likely to occur at isolated places over south Tamil Nadu and south Kerala on Wednesday.
 
 The sky is likely to be generally cloudy with maximum and minimum temperature likely to hover around 33 and 25 deg Celsius respectively.
