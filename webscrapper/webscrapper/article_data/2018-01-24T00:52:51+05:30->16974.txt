Jaipur: High   unfolded at secretariat premises on Tuesday afternoon when some youths of NREGA Karamachari Sangh climbed the PHED water tank to press their demand for appointment of LDCs under NREGA in the Panchayati Raj department. The drama began around 3pm and ended only at 6pm.

 Some members of NREGA Karamachari Sangh went to secretariat on Tuesday to meet the officials over their pending appointment. Not receiving any satisfactory reply, seven members of Sangh climbed up the tank. The security guard spotted them around 3pm. They refused to come down. After talks between union leaders and principal secretary, department of personnel, they were brought down. tnn
