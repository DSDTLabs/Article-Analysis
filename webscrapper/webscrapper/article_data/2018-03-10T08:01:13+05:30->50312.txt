LUCKNOW: The state government on Friday postponed the   ( ) for recruitment of teachers in government primary schools. The examination was scheduled on March 12. 

 In a government order issued on Friday, Examination   (ERA)   said the ATRE has been postponed on the directives of the Allahabad high court. The court on March 16 had asked the ERA to make a fresh evaluation of all answersheets of the Teacher Eligibility Test (TET) conducted by the state government on October 15, 2017. The order came on a bunch of writ petitions filed against 14 questions asked in TET, which, petitioners claimed were wrong. The high court had directed the ERA to scrap these 14 questions and prepare fresh results, within a month of the issuance of the order. 
 
 About 68,500 teachers were to be recruited through ATRE.
