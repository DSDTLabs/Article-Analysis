NOIDA: Two students were injured, when a speeding car rammed into a signboard in Sector 48 area on Wednesday night. 

 The victims have been identified as Pushkar (20) and Sahil (21), were allegedly driving under the influence of alcohol.
 
 Police also found liquor bottles in the car. Both victims were students of a private engineering college in Noida.
 
 According to police the two students were returning from a party in Sector 50 in a Hyundai i10 car. 
 
 “When the car reached near Sector 48, they rammed against a signboard. A police team reached the spot and rushed the victims to district hospital. The students were discharged from the hospital after primary treatment ,” said a police officer..
 
 No complaint has been registered in the case yet.
