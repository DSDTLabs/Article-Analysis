NEW DELHI: The Central Board of Secondary Education ( ) is likely to release the admit cards for National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) in the second week of April 2018. 

 
 The candidate please note that the admit cards will not be sent by post or any other means. 
 
 Those who have applied for the CBSE UG NEET 2018 can download their admit card from the official website - https://cbseneet.nic.in/cbseneet/Welcome.aspx - by entering their registration number and date of birth in DD/MM/YYYY format. When you will download the admit card, a copy of your admit will also be mailed to your e-mail ID, provided at the time of filing the registration form, in the PDF format. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The NEET UG 2018 examination for admission to undergraduate medical courses across the country is scheduled to be conducted on May 6, 2016. The result will be declared in 1st week of June 2018.
 
 The NEET is the only national level entrance examination for MBBS and BDS courses at the government, government-aided and private medical and dental colleges.
