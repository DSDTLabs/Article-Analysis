Rajkot: A class XII student allegedly committed suicide by jumping off the 10th floor of a boarding school near Mavdi Crossroads on 150 feet Ring Road in the early hours of Tuesday. Though police found a whale drawn on his hand, they denied that the suicide was a result of Blue Whale game. “We have not found any such game in his mobile phone,” said U B Puwar, investigating officer.

 Sagar Bhanderi (17) was a native of Kalavad village of Jamnagar district and was staying at the Leuva Patel Boarding School for the last two years. Police said he was studying till 2am and after everyone went to sleep, he went to the 10th floor and took the plunge. Other boys noticed his body lying in a pool of blood at around 7am.
