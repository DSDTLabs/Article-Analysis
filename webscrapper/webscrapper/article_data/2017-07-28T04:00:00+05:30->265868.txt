Surat: A woman died due to electrocution when the water she threw out of building fell on a high tension electricity line in Pandesara on Thursday. The woman suffered burn injuries and was flung from the second floor gallery to the ground. 

 Kali Damor (26), a resident of Varachha, was declared dead on being brought to hospital. She suffered multiple injuries on body and also suffered serious burn injuries. 
 
 Damor, a domestic help, was cleaning a new textile weaving unit in Maruti Industrial Estate. 
 
 After cleaning the second floor she flung the water outside the unit from the gallery. The water fell on high-tension wire and got electrocuted.
