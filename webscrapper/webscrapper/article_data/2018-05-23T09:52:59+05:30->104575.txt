NEW DELHI: The Tamil Nadu Secondary School Leaving Certificate (SSLC) or Class 10th exam results declared on Wednesday, May 23, 2018. The pass percentage of this year is 94.5 %. 

 The Tamil Nadu SSLC result is available on the official results website of the National Informatics Centre (NIC) - http://tnresults.nic.in 
 
 In a notification released online, The Directorate of Government Examinations (DGE) announced that the Tamil Nadu SSLC/Class 10 results will be made live on the official website (http://tnresults.nic.in/) at 9:30 am today.
