NEW DELHI: Delhi Metro’s smart   can now be used to   in Metro’s   buses and also to pay parking fee at some   parking lots.

 “This is in line with the objective of ensuring last-mile connectivity to commuters,” a DMRC spokesman said. 
 
 The common mobility card can be used at parking lots of 13 stations to pay charges. They are Vishwavidyalaya, Saket, Karkardooma, Dilshad Garden, Akshardham, Patel Chowk, Kashmere Gate, Rohini Sector (18, 19), Shastri Park, NHPC Chowk, Mewala Maharajpur, Old Faridabad, and Sector 28 stations.
