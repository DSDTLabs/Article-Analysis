‘Naam Shabana’ which is the prequel of the 2015 release ‘Baby’, stars Taapsee Pannu,  , Manoj Bajpai and  . The makers released the first look of the film recently, and it got a thumbs up from social media users. 
It looks like the actors of the film got along like a house on fire while shooting the movie and bonded really well with each other. Cast of the movie celebrated veteran actor Anupam Kher’s birthday on the sets. He turns 61 on March 7 and the film’s crew got together to cut a cake and wish him well. 
 The team including Akshay Kumar, Taapsee Pannu, Manoj Bajpai and others sang for the birthday boy as he cut, what looks like a delicious chocolate cake.
 Akshay Kumar took to Instagram to post a video of the birthday celebration with the caption, “A team that eats together stays together 🙌🏻 From Team Baby to now Team #NaamShabana, happiest birthday to our Shukla ji 😁Love & Prayers 🙏🏻 @anupampkher”
 
 
  
 
  ‘Naam Shabana’ is slated to release on March 31.
