 Harish Parmar had come to Ahmedabad about eight months ago from Saudi Arabia. Sources said that the police have recorded his ‘dying declaration’ in the presence of a magistrate.
 
 “He has alleged that the three women had set him on fire. We are investigating the details mentioned in the FIR,” said a Sardarnagar police official.
 
 Police said that Parmar, a resident of Vadaj, and his elder son Manish, had gone to meet his younger son Arun who lives with his wife Komal and 2-year-old son Harsh in Sardarnagar. He had gone there to sort out a family dispute.Sources in the police said that while Manish stood outside the house, Parmar went inside. He was about to take his 2-year-old grandson into his arm when Komal’s mother, Manju, rushed in and started abusing Parmar.
 
 “Manju pushed Parmar on the floor while somebody from behind poured inflammable liquid on him and set him ablaze. Manish rushed into the house on hearing his father’s cries and doused the fire. Harish was then rushed to civil hospital for treatment,” said a police official.
