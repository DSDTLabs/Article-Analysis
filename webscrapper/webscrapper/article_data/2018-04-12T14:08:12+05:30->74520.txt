LUDHIANA: The district administration would hold a number of functions under Gram   which is set to start on April 14 and go on till May 5. The administration has identified 110 villages of the district under the special outreach programme of the Central government to reach out the rural people and spread the awareness of the social schemes.

 Chairing an important meeting to finalise the arrangements for starting the Gram Swaraj Abhiyan, additional deputy commissioner   said awareness camps, seminars and meetings would be held in 110 villages of the district under   for 22 days continiously and people would be made aware about seven schemes including Ujala Yojna, Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana, Sahaj Bijli Har Ghar Yojana-Saubhagya, Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojna, Pradhan Mantri Jiwan Jyoti Bima Yojna, Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojna and Mission Indradhanush. She said eligible villagers would be enrolled by the teams at the spot.
 
 Aggarwal also said, events would start on the birth anniversary of Bharat Ratna Dr B R Ambedkar on April 14, which would be celebrated as Social Justice Day. Different programmes, Swachh Bharat Diwas on April 18, Ujjawala Panchayat Diwas on April 20, and   on April 24, would be celebrated in the district. Gram Shakti Abhiyan, Ayushman Bharat Diwas, Farmers Welfare Day and Aajeevika Diwas would be celebrated on April 28, 30 and May 2 and 5. Objective of the Gram Swaraj Abhiyan is to promote social harmony among the villagers, connect to poor rural households, to gain feedback on government’s programmes, focus on doubling farmers’ income, improving livelihood opportunities, and strengthening   institutions.
 
 ADC exhorted all the heads of various departments to appoint nodal officers to ensure better coordination and cooperation to make events successful. ADC said that the progress of the functions would be reviewed on a routine basis.
