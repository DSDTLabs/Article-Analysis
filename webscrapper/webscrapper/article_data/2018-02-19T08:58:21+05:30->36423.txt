NAGPUR: Somalwar Nikalas ‘A’, Centre Point School (CPS), Katol Road and School of Scholars (SOS) reigned supreme in their respective categories in the Inter-School Table Tennis tournament, organized by the   (NDTTA), at the   here on Sunday.

 
 Somalwar Nikalas ‘A’ trounced CPS, Katol Road, 3-1 to win the U-14 boys title. However, in the girls section, CPS KR emerged champions edging out BVM Civil Lines 3-2 in a thriller.
 
 The U-17 boys category final was also turned out to be an exciting one. School of Scholars performed well to pip BVM Civil Lines 3-2 to clinch the title.
 
 In the singles events, Aadi Chitnis pocketed the U-14 boys crown ousting Aditya Herulkar 11-8, 11-2, 11-3. Jui Pendharkar triumphed in the girls section defeating Jennifer Verghese 11-6, 6-11, 11-5, 11-8. In the U-17 girls singles, Disha Girdhar turned the tables on Harshali Deodhare 11-5, 11-7, 11-5.
 
 RESULTS
 
 U-14 boys team final: Somalwar Nikalas ‘A’ bt Centre Point School, Katol Road 3-1 (Aadi Chitnis bt Dhruv Gattani 11-0, 11-5, 11-2; Ajinkya Sahastrabuddhe lost to Kritay Daga 5-11, 3-11, 11-3, 4-11; Varenya Pannikar bt Ishawar Sarda 11-7, 11-9, 11-7; Aadi Chitnis bt Kritay Daga 11-3, 11-2, 11-0).
 
 U-14 girls team final: Centre Point School, Katol Road bt BVM, Civil Lines 3-2 (Jennifer Verghese bt Tarini Deshpande 11-6, 11-4, 11-6; Ashley Verghese lost to Jui Pendharkhar 5-11, 8-11, 9-11; Ishita Rathi bt Arya Kurup 11-2, 11-2, 11-4; Jennifer Verghese lost to Jui Pendharkhar 6-11, 11-7, 6-11, 13-11, 7-11; Ashley Verghese bt Tarini Deshpande 11-2, 8-11, 11-6, 11-2).
 
 U-17 boys team final: School of Scholars bt BVM, Civil Lines 3-2 (Jayesh Kulkarni bt Harsh Gosavi 11-6, 11-4, 11-1; Parth Kandekar lost to Saket Gandhi 2-11, 1-11, 7-11; Ritik Parmar lost to Atharva Gade 2-11, 4-11, 5-11; Jayesh Kulkarni bt Saket Gandhi 11-7, 11-7, 11-2; Parth Kandekar bt Harsh Gosavi 11-8, 11-8, 7-11, 11-5).
