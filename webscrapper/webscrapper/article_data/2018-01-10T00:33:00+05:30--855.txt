Trichy: The exhibition organised by the state government was opened for the pubic at Thennur, Anna Nagar, near here, on Sunday. It was the first time the district administration has chosen Trichy as the venue for exhibition. 

 Minister for information and publicity Kadambur Raju inaugurated the exhibition in the evening yesterday. 
 
 The   in Trichy remained the first one in the state for this year and the 190th exhibition in the state so far. It will be open for the public from 04 pm to 10 pm everyday till February 20, 2018. “The state government is conducting the exhibition in ten districts. Out of ten districts, six districts have the exhibition regularly every year. The event is being organized in rotation basis in four other districts,” Kadambur Raju said.
