JAIPUR: Muslim organisation—Rashtriya Muslim Mahasabha, condemned attacks on   Muslims at a public gathering organised at Rawalji Ka Bandha on Saturday evening.

 Organisation’s president   said, “We condemn killing of Rohingya Muslims in Myanmar.”

 “Muslims are lagging behind in education. It is need of the hour to provide Muslim youths better education. We also demand that the government provide Muslim youths reservation in educational institutes,” he said.
