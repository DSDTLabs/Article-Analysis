A four-day annual sports and cultural fest named   organized by Avadh Girls’ P.G College was a well-attended event with students participating from 29 colleges. The inter-college fest began with the sports meet with events like basketball, shot-put, table tennis, among others. 
The next day started off with various cultural events like dumb charades, dance, singing, Best out of Waste competition, street play, documentary screening, etc. Quite elated to attend the event, chief guest,  , mayor of Lucknow, said, "This is the first time when I am here for such an event. It gives me so much pleasure to attend such a fest and see students with all kinds of talents getting together under a single roof."
 The street play performed on the topic of the role of Indian Media earned several rounds of applause from the audience. However, the highlight of the fest was the dance competition where several Sufi and contemporary dance performances by the students won the hearts of all.
