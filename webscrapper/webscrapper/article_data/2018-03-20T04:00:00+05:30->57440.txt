Surat: Police booked an unidentified person in Chowk Bazaar police station on Monday for allegedly murdering a 28-year-old man in Pandol locality on Ved Road. The deceased was murdered when he was returning after watching a movie.

 Body of Pravin Mer, a resident of Ved Road, was found near Munjani Complex in Pandol Industrial Society at around 12 midnight on Sunday. Mer was stabbed on left side of his chest. 
 
 The complaint was lodged by Paresh Ghaydiya, a relative of Mer.
 
 “We are yet not clear that for what reason the deceased was murdered,” police informed. tnn
