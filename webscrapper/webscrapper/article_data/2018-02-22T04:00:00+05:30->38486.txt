The De Beers Group of Companies supplies over a third of the total global supply of rough diamonds by value. It sells rough diamonds contractually 10 times a year to ‘De Beers Sightholders’, a selected group of diamantaires, who have demonstrated both outstanding business performance and ethical accountability. Rough diamonds are supplied in Botswana, Namibia and South Africa at these regular ‘sights’.De Beers Sightholders are selected after strict procedure of assessing abilities of firms in the core areas of business and strategic planning, manufacturing excellence, business reputation and strict adherence to a code of ethical accountability. tnn
