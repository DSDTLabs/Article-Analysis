NAGPUR: The Nagpur Municipal Corporation Contractor’s Welfare Association ( ), under the leadership of president  , did Gandhigiri and presented a bouquet to executive engineer (EE) DD Jamburkar to stop harassing contractors. 
 
 According to Naidu, Jambhulkar is in-charge of slum department, traffic department and also executive engineer of Laxmi Nagar Zone. All contractors carrying out works in these departments were fed up with his laid-back attitude. “In the past too, we had lodged similar complaints against executive engineer   and junior engineer   to the civic administration. We will continue our Gandhigiri protests to stop the corrupt practices in the civic body,” said the members in a statement.
