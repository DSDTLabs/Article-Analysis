UDUPI: Chaitanya and Greeve class 12 students of Little Rock Indian School, Brahmavar have won the Grand   from   (Initiative for Research and Innovation in Science) jointly sponsored by Intel South India, The Indo-US Science and Technology Forum, and the Union science and technology department. 

 From among 3 lakh participants who had submitted projects online, 100 were shortlisted. The project SAMIS (Smart Phone Aided Multispectral Imaging System) prepared by Geeve and Chaitanya is one among 20 projects selected for the award. These awardees will represent India at international competition. The duo is eligible to attend the ‘Intel Science and Engineering Fair’ to be held in Los Angeles in May. “It is an incredible achievement,” said Mathew C Ninan, principal of the school.
