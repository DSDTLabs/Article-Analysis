
 Golden Globes 2017 was much about fashion as it was about awards. Here's what your favourite stars of Hollywood wore to the big night.
 
 
 
  'La La Land' star Ryan Gosling looked dapper in his white blazer and black bow-tie.
  
 
 
  
  
 
 
 
 The favourite of the night Emma Stone, who has been nominated for the Best Actress in a Motion Picture (Comedy or Musical) for 'La La Land', turned heads in a soft pink   gown. 
  
 
 
 
 India was waiting with bated breath to see what Priyanka Chopra has in store for them and she didn't disappoint. The actress looked ravishing in a golden sequined   gown which she paired with bold red lips.
  
  
 
 
 
 
 Michelle Williams, who is in contention for the Best Actress in a Supporting Role for her role in 'Manchester By The Sea', chose a grey Louis Vuitton gown for the evening.
  
  
 
 
 
 
 Natalie Portman shone in her chartreuse Prada outfit while winner of the Best Supporting Actress   chose a cheery yellow   gown. Reese Witherspoon opted for a shade of lemon chiffon Atelier Versace gown. 
  
  
  
  
  
 
 
 
 The lady who walked with all the applause, Meryl Streep, chose a black Givenchy Couture for the evening. 
  
 
 
 
 Nicole Kidman went the dramatic route in a shimmery, ruffled Alexander McQueen number. Ruth Negga went the metallic way in a zippered Louis Vuitton number.
  
  
 
 
 
 
 
  
 
 
 
 
 Amy Adams glittered in a Tom Ford column gown. 
  
 
 
 
 Sofie Vergara was all about sheer ' the voluptuous beauty looked smashing in a cold-shoulder sheer gown. Another actress channeling the cold shoulder trend was Drew Barrymore in a white gown. Fashion's favourite girl Sarah Jessica Parker too worked the trend in a white Vera Wang.
