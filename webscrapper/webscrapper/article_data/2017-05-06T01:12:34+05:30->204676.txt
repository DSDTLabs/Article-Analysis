NEW DELHI: The election of  , deputy mayors, chairmen of standing committees and members of standing committees will be held from May 18 to 22 in north, south and east Delhi municipal corporations.

  The oath taking ceremony of the newly elected councillors will take place before the elections. North corporation will conduct election of the political executive on May 18 while SDMC will do it on May 19 and EDMC on 22, a notification from the corporations said on Friday. The positions of mayors in all three corporations are reserved for women in the first year. The nomination for all these political positions can be filed from Monday in the office of municipal secretaries. It will continue till May 12 in SDMC and EDMC while north body will close its nominations on May 11.
