RAJKOT: In order to facilitate the passengers of Jamnagar and to fulfil their long pending demand,   will run Hapa-Tirunelveli   from Jamnagar instead of Hapa on permanent basis with effect from March 31.

 According to Railway official,   will start from Jamnagar and will reach Hapa and then got to Tirunelveli.
 
 Similarly, in the return,   starting from Tirunelveli it will reach Hapa on third-day and it later go to Jamnagar.
