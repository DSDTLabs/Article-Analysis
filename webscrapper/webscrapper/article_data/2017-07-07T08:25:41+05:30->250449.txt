NEW DELHI: A 10-year-old girl was raped by a self-styled godman at a village near  . The girl has told police that the man had been sexually assaulting her the past 4-5 months on the pretext of performing rituals. She narrated her ordeal to her mother after which a case was registered against the man and he was arrested. 

 The accused, who called himself Dongi Baba, stayed in a hut near the girl's village where he performed rituals for the neighbours. He used to call the child's mother to perform some rituals. During this, he used to bring the child alone in his room and sexually assault her.
 
  The girl said that she had not reported the matter since the man had threatened to place a curse on her family. But she later told her mother when the pain grew unbearable.
