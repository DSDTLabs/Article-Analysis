RAJKOT: A 50-year-old barber was allegedly stabbed to death by a drunk man over a petty issue of delay in shaving his beard in   village of Kutch’s Rapar taluka on Monday evening. 

 Naranbhai Valand, was allegedly stabbed by Laxman Rajput. On Monday evening,   came to Valand’s shop, allegedly in inebriate state and asked him to shave his beard.   politely asked him to wait his turn. An angry Rajput took out a knife and stabbed Valand several times, killing him on the spot. A case has been lodged against Rajput.
