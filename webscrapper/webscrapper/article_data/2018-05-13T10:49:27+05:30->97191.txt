KURUKSHETRA: Crime branch of   police has arrested three persons for allegedly betting on   matches and recovered Rs 4,500, one TV set, five mobile phones, one letter pad and other items used for the illegal activity. A police spokesperson said that the crime branch had got information about the accused running a betting racket on IPL matches in a house.

 The team raided a house in Krishna Nagar and arrested the accused identified as Sandeep alias Kaka and Vipin, both residents of Krishna Nagar, and Sushil Kumar of Subhash Gali in Thanesar. They were placing bets on the ongoing match between Chennai and Rajasthan when they were nabbed. Police have registered a case under the the Gambling Act against the accused.
