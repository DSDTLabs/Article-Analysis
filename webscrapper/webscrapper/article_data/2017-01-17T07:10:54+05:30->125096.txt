PANAJI: A musical journey of ‘east meets west’ will unfold in the city as sarod maestro   and UK-based Orchestra  , conducted by internationally acclaimed  , will take audiences through a mesmerizing night of melody, called the Roots of Fire.

 With the help of Deccan  , the concert seeks to celebrate young and aspiring instrumentalists with a musical dialogue that has existed between the orient and the occident for centuries. The concert will be held on January 19, at 7pm, at Kala Academy, Panaji.
