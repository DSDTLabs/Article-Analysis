NEW DELHI: The Meghalaya Board of School Education (MBOSE) announced the results of the Secondary School Leaving Certificate (SSLC) or Class 10 and Higher Secondary School Leaving Certificate (HSSLC) or Class 12 Arts today (May 25, 2018) at around 10 am. 

 The Meghalaya MBOSE SSLC 2018 results and MBOSE HSSLC Arts results 2018 was announced by the Board on its official website - megresults.nic.in. 
 
 The students who appeared for the Meghalaya SSLC, HSSLC Arts Examination 2018 can check their result here.
 
 
  
  
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The Meghalaya Board of School Education had announced the results of the HSSLC Science and Commerce on May 10, 2018. The results of Higher Secondary School Leaving Certificate Examination (Vocational) 2018 were also released on the same day.
 
 The Meghalaya Board has conducted the MBOSE SSLC 2018 exams from March 7 to March 19, 2018. In 2017, around 52,034 students had appeared for the SSLC examination and 79.90% of students have passed (Boys 80.84% and 79.12%)
 
 The MBOSE HSSLC examinations 2018 were conducted between March 6 to 29, 2018. In 2017, out of 48,486 students who appeared HSSLC 2017 exams, the overall pass percentage was around 74.57%.
 
 
 
 
 
 For SSLC (Class 10):Type MBOSE10 add space ROLLNUMBER and send it to 56263
 
 For HSSLC (Arts): Type MBOSE12A add space ROLLNUMBER and send it 56263 
 
 
 
 
 1) Keep handy your MBOSE Board SSLC and HSSLC Arts Roll Number
 
 2) Open the official website of the MBOSE Board - mbose.in
 
 3) On the homepage of the Board, you will see links - "MBOSE Board Class SSLC results" and "MBOSE Board Class HSSLC Arts results" , click on it
 
 4) It will redirect you to a new page where you need to enter your MBOSE Board Class 10 roll no. and other required details
 
 5) Once you click submit, your MBOSE Board SSLC and HSSLC Arts result will be visible on the screen
 
 6) You can download your MBOSE Board SSLC and HSSLC Arts result and print it for further reference
