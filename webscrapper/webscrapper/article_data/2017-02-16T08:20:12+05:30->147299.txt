SURAT: State monitoring cell formerly known as vigilance team raided Dumbal   under Puna police station and caught 30 people gambling there. All of them were booked under Section 12(A) of Gambling Act. 

 Police inspector, L B Dabhi, who raided the place said, “The accused were gambling behind the vehicles parked in the the godown compound.” 
 
 Officials of state monitoring cell also found Rs 3,27,592 in cash, 17 cellphones, two bikes valued at Rs 40,000 and an auto-rickshaw worth Rs 60,000.
 
 Police said that Jitu who ran the gambling den was not the boss. Jitu only got cut from the collections.
