MUMBAI: A pilot employed with a major Indian airline lost Rs1.5 lakh after funds were transferred using his credit card details. The victim, Romit Sharma (46), a   resident registered a case on March 17 after hotlisting his credit and debit cards. 

 According to Sharma, 15 fraudulent transactions were carried out between March 15 and March 17 using his credit card details. Sharma says when he woke up on March 16, he was shocked to see a series of text alerts from the bank about the transactions. "We have sought transactions details from the bank. We have also sought CCTV footage of ATMs from where the money was withdrawn," said a police officer.
