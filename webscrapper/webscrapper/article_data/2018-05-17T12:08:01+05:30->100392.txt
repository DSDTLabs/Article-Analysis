PUDUCHERRY: The centralised admission committee (Centac) has rescheduled the mop-up   for management quota, including the minority quota, to fill up vacant   medical seats in three private   colleges and hospitals in the Union territory of   to 1pm on May 17 pending the disposal of a petition before the Madras high court, said Centac coordinator (admission) P T Rudra Goud. 

 The Puducherry government has moved the Madras high court pleading to vacate its interim stay on the government order restricting the colleges enjoying minority status to fill up management seats with the minority students from the Union territory of Puducherry. 
 
 The colleges can later fill up vacant seats, if any, with candidates on all India basis.
