Rajkot: High drama ensued over rumours of resignation of Khodaldham Trust chairman Naresh Patel on Wednesday. On Tuesday, trust officials had strongly denied of any such development. However, on Wednesday, Khodaldham president Paresh Gajera said that after lot of ‘persuasion’ by senior trust members Patel withdrew his resignation. 

 “Two days ago Patel submitted his resignation to the trust secretary. He felt that because of heavy workload he was not able to give time for developmental work. But we all convinced him to withdraw his resignation,” Gajera told TOI.
