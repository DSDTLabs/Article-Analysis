Tirunelveli: A five-year-old boy was crushed to death when an incomplete concrete pillar fell on him at Sankarankovil in Tirunelveli district of Tamil Nadu on Friday.

 The victim has been identified as S Sabari, son of Shankar of Vadakku Ratha Veedhi in Sankarankovil. The boy had completed UKG and had been enrolled in Class 1 in a school in the locality. His father is a flower seller at Bannari in Erode district. The boy was staying with his mother, Karthika.
 
 The Sankarankovil police said the incident took place around 11am when Sabari and a few other children were playing in the compound close to his house. There were two incomplete concrete pillars in the house that had not been plastered for years, police said. 
 
 Police sources said the occupants of the house had tied two metal cords between the pillars and used it for hanging clothes. On Friday morning, the children were playing along the pillars and also with the cord when one of the weak pillars crashed on Sabari, who was sitting below.
 
 The body was taken to the Sankarankovil Government Hospital for postmortem.
 
 Police registered a case Section 174 (unnatural death due to accidental fall) of the CrPC.
