MUMBAI:   Devendra   appointed 1991 batch   officer Deepak Kapoor as the new   of the Slum Rehabilitation Authority ( ) on Monday. Kapoor was principal secretary of the newly created skill development department and was instrumental in signing several MoUs with industrial and business houses. He drafted the right to services rules when he was principal secretary of the food and civil supplies department.

 Kapoor takes over at a time of controversy in the SRA over the alleged clearing of an unusually large number of files by his predecessor, Vishwas Patil, just before he retired on June 30.
