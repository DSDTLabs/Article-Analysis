VISAKHAPATNAM: Two engineering students from East Godavari district drowned near   in Payakaraopeta mandal in the district on Friday. 

 The incident took place when the duo along with another friend went for a holy dip  . 
 
 The deceased were identified as 19-year-old students D Nanaji of L Kotturu and Ch Venkata Prudhvi of S Annavaram in East Godavari. Both were B.Tech first year students at a private college near Tuni. Sources said Nanaji, Prudhvi and their friend T Ashok went for a swim and got dragged by a strong current. Ashok manage to swim ashore while the other two drowned. Cops fished out the bodies and handed them over to the family members after post-mortem.
