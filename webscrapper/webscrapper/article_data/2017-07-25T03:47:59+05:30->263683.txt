NEW DELHI: The government introduced the Banking Regulation (Amendment)   in the   on Monday, authorising the RBI to issue directions to any banking company to effectively use the provisions of the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code for timely resolution of non-performing assets. The Bill seeks to replace the existing ordinance on the issue.

 “Stressed assets in the banking system or non-performing assets have reached unacceptably high levels and hence, urgent measures are required for their speedy resolution to improve the financial health of banking companies for proper economic growth of the country,” said the statement of “objects and reasons” of the bill, introduced by finance minister  .
