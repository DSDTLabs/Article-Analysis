KOCHI: The Muvattupuzha vigilance court on Thursday ordered a 'quick verification' against the Kerala Water Authority managing director and 17 others, following a complaint of corruption in the construction of the Chempanal check dam at Karoor in Kottayam, as part of the Jalanidhi scheme. 

 The complaint was filed by   engineer and   project manager  
 
 The QV will cover KWA MD Ashok Kumar, project commissioner   and contractors T D Chandrashekaran and Biju Augustin.
 
 As per the complaint, the contractor who quoted the least amount was not selected for the project. The respondents are also accused of erroneously mixing up the construction raw materials, thus   to the dam.
 
 The probe report is to be submitted within a month.
