SURAT: Income tax (I-T) officials, including gazette officers, went on a strike to press for acceptance of their various long-pending demands and protest against the retrograde recommendations of the Seventh Central Pay Commission (CPC) on Thursday.

 I-T officials in the city joined the nation-wide call given by the Confederation of   Employees and Workers, New Delhi against the retrograde recommendations of the 7th CPC and other pending demands. 
 
 The officials shouted slogans at the I-T headquarters at Majura Gate and demanded immediate redressal of their demands.
