ALLAHABAD: Days after chief minister   called for a greener and cleaner Uttar Pardesh, Allahabad's policemen have launched cleanliness drives at all 39 police stations in the city and trans-Ganga and   areas. 

 In tandem with PM  's Swachh Bharat campaign, police officials have also pledged to conserve the environment and keep their surroundings clean and green. 
 
 On Friday, policemen were seen wielding brooms and cleaning surroundings of their respective police stations. "From offices to barracks to lockups and canteens, all areas of police stations were cleaned by policemen on duty" said an inspector on condition of anonymity.
