Salem: As part of the MGR centenary celebrations in the district, nine vans fitted with LED TV have been flagged off by the district collector to telecast MGR films, his schemes and significant events in which he participated during his life time. 

 “The van will be parked at the important places across the district until September 30. Through the campaign, people, especially the younger generations would know more about MGR,” said the collector Rohini R Bhajibhakare.
 
 The state government has been carring out MGR centenary celebration events across the state. As part of the celebrations, a function will be held at Karuppur government engineering college premises in Salem district on September 30. Chief minister Edappadi K Palaniswami and many ministers will be participating in the event. 
 
 As part of the function, the CM has ordered to broadcast MGR related programmes. Based on his order, nine districts including Salem, Namakkal, Tirchy, Tiruppur, Pudukottai, Erode, Perambalur, Ariyalur and Coimbatore have been organising mobile van campaigns.
 
 Salem MP V Panneerselvam, Salem (South) MLA A B Sakthivel and others were present in the event.
