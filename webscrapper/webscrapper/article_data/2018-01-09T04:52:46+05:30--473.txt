LUCKNOW: Governor   on Monday gave his assent to eight bills, including   (Composition of Offences and Abatement of Trials) (Amendment) Bill, 2017, that seeks to drop 20,000 “politically-motivated” cases filed across the state over protest, demonstrations against leaders and workers of various parties, including one against CM Yogi Aditya Nath.

 Yogi and dozen other have been facing a 22-year-old case for holding a meeting in alleged violation of prohibitory orders. Apart from Aditya Nath, present union minister Shiv Pratap Shukla,   MLA Sheetal Pandey and 10 others were respondents in the case filed at Gorakhpur’s Pipiganj police station on May 27, 1995.
