NAVI MUMBAI: Police have launched a search for an autorickshaw driver who allegedly raped a minor passenger on Friday. The 16-year-old has told Rabale MIDC police she boarded his auto at   in Mahape to go to her house in Digha. 

 Assistant police inspector Manisha Kashid said, "The victim had travelled by the same auto a day before the incident. On Friday, he offered her a lift."
 
 Complainant said he took her on the Mahape MIDC road and raped her on an isolated stretch. Around 8 pm, he made her board the rickshaw, but fearing she would be raped again, she jumped from the running auto. As passersby gathered at the spot, the accused sped away.
