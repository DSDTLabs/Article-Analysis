The actress who is a mother of two grown up girls Jhanvi and Khushi shared a touching message ahead of Mother’s Day this weekend. Featuring in a video, the ‘English Vinglish’ actress shared how she used to get irritated on suggestions given by her mom but when she became a mother herself, she can’t help nagging her kids for their betterment. 

 As a loving gesture to the mothers all over the world, Sridevi asked her fans to give their moms a hug and say “I love you, mom”. She wrote on her Twitter handle, “This mom's day... give your #mom a hug ... and #SayILoveYouMom.”
