The Directorate of Government Examinations, Telangana, has released the admit card or hall tickets for the   Certificate / Class 10 
 March 2018 examinations. 
 
 
 The admit card/hall ticket is available on the official website of the board bse.telangana.gov.in.
 
 
 The students who are going to appear for the Class 10 or SSC examination 2018 can go to the official website to download their admit card. 
 
 
 The admit card / hall ticket has been released for all students including - regular, private, OSSC and vocational candidates. The Telangana Class 10 board examination 2018 is scheduled to be held from March 15 to April 2.
 
 
 The students can follow the steps given below to download their Telangana Class 10 2018 admit card / hall tickets:
 
 
 1) Open the official website of Directorate of Government Examinations, Telangana: bse.telangana.gov.in.
 
 
 2) You will find link for 'SSC March 2018 hall tickets', click on it
 
 
 3) Select your candidate type such as - regular, private, OSSC and vocational
 
 
 4) Enter your details as asked, for example, district, school, name and date of birth. 
 
 
 5) Next, click on 'download hall ticket'
 
 
 6) You admit card will appear on the screen
 
 
 7) You can save the soft copy of your hall ticket or take a print out for future reference
