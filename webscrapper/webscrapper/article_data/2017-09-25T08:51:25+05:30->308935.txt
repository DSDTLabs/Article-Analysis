NAGPUR: Kaustubh Udar and   captured double crown each in the Shakti Club Open District   Championship, organized by the  , at the Shakti Club Hall here on Sunday.

 Kaustubh reigned supreme in the youth and junior boys, while Ananya was unstoppable in the sub-junior and junior girls singles.
 
 In the youth boys summit clash, Kaustubh edged out Anshuman Naik 11-7, 11-6, 10-12, 11-6, 11-5. The talented paddler overcome the challenge of Jayesh Kulkarni 12-14, 11-6, 11-4, 7-11, 13-11, 11-8 in an exciting junior boys final.
 
 Ananya, who already won the sub-junior girls crown, added another title to her kitty when she downed Vaishnavi Devgade in the junior girls final, which lasted for seven games.
 
 Making a comeback after a long break, Saveri Rathod clinched the women’s singles title. The experienced paddler rallied back to beat Ruchita Panbude 7-11, 11-3, 11-9, 13-11, 11-7.
 
 The all-important men’s singles final was won by Ujwal Wani, who made a light work of Ajay Kamble in straight games.
 
 Promising Aadi Chitnis annexed the sub-junior title dismissing Jayesh Kulkarni 11-7, 11-3, 7-11, 2-11, 11-9. In the yough girls final, Kadambari Bhandarkar got the better of Ketki Bhrusundi 11-5,11-8, 13-11, 11-7.
 
 NDTTA president Deepak Dhote, secretary Ashutosh Potnis, Deepak Kanetkar, Rajesh Mopkar, CS Kulkarni, Ishwar Rao and Mangesh Mopkar distributed the prizes.
