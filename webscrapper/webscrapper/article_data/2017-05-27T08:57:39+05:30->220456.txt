KOCHI: Green protocol will be followed during   observance in the district. A decision regarding this was taken at a meeting convened by district collector   Y Safirulla with Muslim religious heads and organizations on Friday.

 Disposable products made of plastic and paper will be replaced with steel, glass and cera mic vessels while ending the fast and during Iftar meetings.
 
 The meeting has also resolved to spread awareness regarding environment protection through sermons and suggest that people should bring vessels from home for carrying food.
 
 A meeting will be held after Ramzan to review implementation of the green protocol, officials said.
