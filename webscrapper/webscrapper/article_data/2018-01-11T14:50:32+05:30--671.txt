PORVORIM: Blaming mediapersons and others for the   controversy, chief minister   on Wednesday said “When I said I’m ready to talk (with  ), everyone formed the opinion that I’m giving water.”

 “As the media has reported, the Mhadei matter is in the court, it’s not an issue for me,” Parrikar said at a press briefing on Wednesday, when questioned about whether he took up the issue with Prime Minister Narendra Modi during his visit to Delhi on Monday. The Mhadei controversy surfaced after Parrikar wrote to senior   leader  , stating his government will not oppose release of water for drinking purpose.
