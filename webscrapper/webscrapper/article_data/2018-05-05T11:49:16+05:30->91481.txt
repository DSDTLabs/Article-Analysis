AIZAWL:   home minister   assured on Friday the police will investigate the attack on Manipuri volleyball player Sakil Chesam and bring the miscreant to book. Last Saturday, after   beat Mizoram in the Mini-National Volleyball Championship final by a 3-2 scoreline at the R Dengthuama Multipurpose Stadium in Aizawl, an unidentified man jumped into the ground and attacked Chesam, breaking his jaw. "I was ashamed by the cowardly act on Mizoram's soil,"   said.
