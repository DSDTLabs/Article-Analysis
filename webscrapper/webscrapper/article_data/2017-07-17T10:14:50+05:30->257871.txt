KOTA: A 70-year-old man and his wife (65) were found murdered at their home in Kailashpuri Colony under railway police station area of Kota on Sunday morning. 

 The couple was killed with sharp weapons late on Saturday night. 
 
 The victim, Bajrang Singh Naruka, was a retired railway employee.
 
 The bodies were handed over to family members after post-mortem and a case of murder has been lodged against unidentified persons.
 
 “Bajrang Singh Naruka and his wife Sadhana Singh were found dead with fatal wounds on their bodies at their house in Kailashpuri Colony on Sunday morning,” the SHO said. 
 
 Police said, the couple was brutally murdered with sharp weapons by some unidentified persons, however, they prima-facie ruled out robbery as nothing was found missing from the house.
 
 Naruka lived with his wife on the first floor of his house while the ground floor is occupied by tenants, the SHO said.
 
 Weapons used in the crime were also recovered from the house, he further said.
 
 The investigation is underway and efforts are on to nab the accused, the SHO said.
 
 Senior police officers including ASP Anant Kumar and DSP Bhagwan Singh Godara rushed to the spot early on Sunday. The police team along with the dog squad inspected the spot and collected evidence.
 
 The neighbours told police that the couple’s married daughter lives in Jaipur.
