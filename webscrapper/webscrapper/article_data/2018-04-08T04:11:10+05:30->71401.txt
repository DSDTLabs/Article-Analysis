MUMBAI: Despite the police crackdown on extortionists over the past week, a 28-year-old   builder was threatened for money on Friday. The police have registered a case against four   activists. 

 The accused men sought information of an alleged illegal construction of a four-storey building by the   in Virar ( east). They threatened to complain to the civic body and demolish the structure if he did not pay them Rs 15 lakh.
 
 The builder in his complaint stated that he has been getting threats since 2017. On Friday, the four activists called and threatened him for money. Ten FIRs have been filed against politicians and RTI activists so far in various police stations. Most of the accused are absconding.
