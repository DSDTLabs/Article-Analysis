NAVI MUMBAI: Acting on a complaint lodged by a 28-year-old woman, resident of Dockyard area, alleging that two of her brother’s friends gangraped her inside their car near Ghansoli railway station, the Rabale MIDC police, on Tuesday, registered an FIR under Sections 376(G), 354, 323 and 504 of the IPC. 

 Assistant inspector   said, “The complainant claims that the accused had borrowed Rs 15 lakh loan from her brother. Under the pretext of repaying some amount, they called her outside Vashi railway station on May 22 evening. They told her that the money will be brought by another person near Ghansoli railway station. They parked their car at a secluded spot and took turns to sexually assault her and threatened her of dire consequences if she revealed the incident to anyone.”
