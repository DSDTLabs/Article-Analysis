LUCKNOW:   in the   following   and   on Wednesday night   throughout Thursday. Power supply to six sub-stations fed by Sarojininagar sub-station will remain affected partially till May 8. 

 As per the roster released by Lucknow Electricity Supply Administration, supply to Chandernagar sub-station that gives power to Chandernagar, Alambagh, Nutkheda road, Ramnagar, VIP road, Azadnagar and Patelnagar will be affected. Similarly, the Indralok and New Alambagh sub-station which supplies electricity to Vijaynagar, Vinaynagar, Manasnagar, Ashutoshnagar, Hindnagar, Hydel colony and Indralok colony will also be affected. The power cuts in the said areas will be from 9.30am to 10.30am, 2.30pm to 3.30pm, 7.30 to 8.30pm and 12.30am to 1.30am. 
 
 Utrethia sub-station that lights up Telibagh, Neelmatha, Rae Bareli road, Saraswatipuram and localities near SGPGI will face power rostering from 10.30am to 11.30am, 3.30pm to 4.30pm, 8.30pm to 9.30pm and 1.30am to 2.30am. 
 
 Supply to Nadarganj sub-station (I) will be restricted from 11.30am to 12.30pm, 4.30pm to 5.30pm, 9.30pm to 10.30pm and 2.30am to 3.30am. The feeder supplies power to industrial area, Sarojininagar, Amausi, Shaheed Path and Mansarovar in Transportnagar.
 
 Gehru sub-station that lights up Sarojininagar, industrial area and outskirts will not get supply from 12.30pm to 1.30pm, 5.30pm till 6.30pm and 10.30pm to 11.30pm. 
 
 Supply to Nadarganj sub-station (II) will be affected from 1.30pm to 2.30pm, 6.30pm to 7.30pm and 11.30pm to 12.30am.
 
 Strong winds and dust storm on Wednesday night damaged power distribution system in the city to the extent that supply could be restored completely in many localities only by Thursday evening. In Hydel colony in Balu Adda, the transformer got damaged after a tree fell on it. Restoration and repair started at around 11am after the tree which had blocked the way was removed. Some parts of Jopling road and Prag Narain road had no power from 9am till 5pm. Hydel colony had no electricity supply from Wednesday night till Thursday evening.
 
 "There was no electric supply and we could not sleep all night," said resident of sector-8, Vrindavan colony, Vijay Gupta. Residents of LDA colony on Kanpur road also complained of no water supply due to the overnight power cut. "We got water supply for only 15 minutes and it was dirty," said Vrinda Mehta of sector-D, LDA colony.
 
 Indiranagar residents suffered power cut all night and all morning because of fault in transformer. "Power supply was restored around 7.30am but was cut off again following a transformer blast," said sector-9, Indiranagar resident Manish Arora.
 
 In Gomtinagar, fault in transformer at Mithaiwalla crossing led to a power cut from 2pm to 5.30pm.
