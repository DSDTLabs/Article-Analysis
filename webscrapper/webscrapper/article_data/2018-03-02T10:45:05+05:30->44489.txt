 The teaser opens with   saying, "Kaala, what sort of name is that?". Dressed in an all-black outfit, Rajinikanth, is seen walking among a crowd celebrating Holi with black powder as Samuthirakani's voice explains, "Kaala means black. Kaalan - Karikaalan. The man who protects by fighting."
 
 Directed by Pa Ranjith, ‘Kaala’ is set in the backdrop of Mumbai city and will reportedly see Rajinikanth playing a gangster. The film has an ensemble of artists like Huma Qureshi, Sampath, Nana Patekar and Samuthirakani in important roles. 
 
 Touted to be a film on politics, Nana Patekar plays a politician and Rajinikanth seems to be playing the saviour of the masses, just like he is trying to becoming a politican to save the people by venturing into politics. 
 
 The teaser ends with a punch, where Rajinikanth says, "You haven't seen the full rowdyism of this   right? You will see it!". 
 
 Music composer Santhosh Narayanan is once again working with Rajinikanth after their gangster flick ‘Kabali’. Produced by Wunderbar Films, the film is all set for a massive release on April 27, 2018.
