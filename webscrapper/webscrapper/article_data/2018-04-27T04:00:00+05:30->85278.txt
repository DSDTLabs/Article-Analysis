A minor girl has been raped in Thorala village of Jetpur taluka in Rajkot district. The survivor’s father lodged a complaint against Praful Devipujak alias Ramalo, a resident of Thorala village. “Devipujak had allegedly lured the 14-year- old girl with the promise of marriage and had taken her to Gondal on April 17 where he had raped her repeatedly after confining her to a room for three days,” said an official from Virpur police station. Meanwhile, police have launched a manhunt for Devipujak.
