To get the glamourous look, apply darker shades all over the upper lids and along the rims of the lower lash line. Then apply a lighter, shimmery shade in the centre and inner corner of the eye. The colours most chosen for this look are warm shades like peach and mauve. But be creative and try out whatever colours you like. Finish the look with a couple of mascara coats.
