KOCHI: The Ernakulam district and principal sessions court on Monday decided to conduct an in camera trial in the rape and   at Perumbavoor, rejecting the oppositions raised by the advocate of the defendant.

 The decision of the court to conduct an in-camera trial is to protect the privacy of the 195 witnesses in the case and the victim. Judge N   asked whether the trial should be conducted in camera since the police investigation confirmed the law student was raped. B A Aloor, appearing for the accused  , objected to this and special prosecutor   agreed for an in camera hearing. 
 
 Discarding the objections of Aloor, judge decided to go with an in camera hearing.
