NEW DELHI: The Kerala Secondary Education Examination Board (DHSE Board) declared the Kerala Class 12th result 2018 on Thursday, May 10, 2018.  The Kerala HSE results 2018 was declared today on the official website of the DHSE Board (dhsekerala.gov.in) around 11 am. The Kerala education minister C Raveendranath announced the results at a news conference held today at Thiruvananthapuram.  After the official declaration, results would be available on the several government websites and Mobile Apps, including PRD Live, Saphalam 2018 and iExaMS. The mobile Apps can be downloaded from the google play store. The websites on which the results would be available include, www.Keralaresults.nic.in, www.dhsekerala.gov.in, www.results.itschool.gov.in, www.cdit.org, www.examresults.kerala.gov.in, www.prd.kerala.in, www.results.nic.in and www.educatinkerala.gov.in The Directorate of Higher Secondary Education, Government of Kerala had conducted the Kerala Plus Two examinations for science and commerce streams from March 7-27, 2018 and for arts stream the exam was held from March 7-26, 2018.  Over 4.42 lakh students had appeared for the exam at 2076 centres across Kerala and in Lakshadweep and Mahe. Out of the total, around 3.72 lakh students had appeared through the regular stream and 69,971 students appeared through the open stream for the DHSE examination.
