RAJKOT: A woman’s decomposed body was found from a well behind an industrial unit in   on the outskirts of Rajkot city on Tuesday morning. Police suspect that the woman was murdered and her body dumped about 10-12 days back in the well.

 According to police officials who reached the spot, the body of the woman, who appeared to be in her 30s, was found wrapped in a plastic bag and tied with a rope. Then, it was placed in another woven polypropylene bag, used to pack fertilizer and dumped in the well.
 
 The incident came to light when workers of   in   complained of foul smell in the morning. On searching the premises, the plastic bag with the body was recovered. Owner of the unit Nitin Mankadia informed the police.
 
 Police said that the woman had a tatoo of ‘Shri Ram’ and a ‘peacock’ on her hands, which will help identify the deceased. The woman was wearing a violet salwar and a sky blue top and had a ‘taveez’ around her neck.
 
 Cops have informed the nearby police stations to inform about any complaint of a missing woman lodged with them recently.
