NEW DELHI: Congress and NCP may renew their alliance for   next month, with the two parties eager to be ready for the crucial Lok Sabha bypolls in the state as well as for early general elections in case   springs a surprise.

 
 Congress president   and NCP chief Sharad Pawar held an hourlong meeting on Wednesday night. The meeting came in the backdrop of BJP’s defeat in the UP bypolls. There was no official word on the meeting, which is learnt to have been scheduled at Tuesday’s dinner hosted by Sonia Gandhi.
 
 Congress and NCP are learnt to be on the verge of a tie-up in the state. After   chief   in an interview with TOI invited NCP for an alliance, Pawar led an opposition protest in Mumbai in the form of ‘save Constitution march’ on Republic Day.
