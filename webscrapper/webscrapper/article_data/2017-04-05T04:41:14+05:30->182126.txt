NEW DELHI: Airports Authority of India ( ) has asked its security team to inspect airports at Chennai, Jaipur and three more cities so that hand bag tag stamping of outbound passengers from these places can be done away with. 

  From April 1, hand bag stamping has stopped at seven airports - Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Ahmedabad and Cochin. Chennai was the only metro airport left out from this list. 
 
  "I have now asked our security team to inspect Chennai, Jaipur and three more busy airports and send their reports to BCAS so that hand bag stamping can be done away with at these places too," AAI chief   told TOI . 
 
  Pune, Goa, Guwahati, Jaipur, Lucknow, Bhubaneswar and Patna are among the busiest airports after the metro airports. With Pune and Goa being defence airfields, three airports may be selected from the civil ones along with Chennai and Jaipur for this.
