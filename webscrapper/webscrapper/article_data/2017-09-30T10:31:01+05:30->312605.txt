MARGAO: The All   and Confectioners Association urged the government to address the issue of declining number of traditional Goan   (poders) operating in Goa, by providing them with a subsidy on toddy, giving first preference in firewood auctions, and tax rebate on the Goods and   (GST) levied on allied bakery products.

 “The association will submit a memorandum to the government and all 40 legislators regarding the problems we face in sustaining this centuries-old traditional profession,”  , association president, said at a press briefing on Friday. 
 
  “Bakers must also get first preference during the forest department’s auction of firewood,” he added.
