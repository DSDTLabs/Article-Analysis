NAVI MUMBAI: As part of preparations for the upcoming Ganesh festival, the civic body has asked mandal organisers to have eco-friendly  . 

 In a meeting between the municipal commissioner and other officials, it has been decided to ask organisers to restrict the height of the advertising decorative poles to 22m. They also want the height of the idols to be kept minimum. 
 
 The administration will also be starting with the registration process in advance. “This year, organisers can do the registration from July 25 to August 8 and at every ward one window will be set up wherein all permissions can be availed with ease,” a civic official said.
