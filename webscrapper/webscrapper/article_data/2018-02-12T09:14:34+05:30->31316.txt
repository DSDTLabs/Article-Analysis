ERODE: Two patients were injured after an   exploded in a government hospital in   here on Saturday. 

 According to a senior doctor, there were five oxygen cylinders in the hospital to be used in emergency cases. As a patient developed breathing difficulties on Saturday morning, a ward boy took an oxygen cylinder and attempted to open it. It suddenly exploded.
 
 The patients in the ward panicked and they started to run out of it. Two sustained minor injuries in the incident. Meanwhile, hospital authorities alerted   fire and rescue services personnel, who examined the exploded cylinder. “It might have exploded due to heavy pressure,” the fire and rescue services personnel said.
 
 Subsequently, the doctors replaced the damaged cylinder with a new one. After two hours, the patients returned to their wards.
