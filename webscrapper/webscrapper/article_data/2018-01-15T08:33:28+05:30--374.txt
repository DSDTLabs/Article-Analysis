HYDERABAD: Forest officials have recovered a   skin, which had been 'missing' for long, from the wife of a forest beat officer in   district. Asifabad district forest officer   informed the state forest officials about the seizure of the tiger skin from the wife of a forest beat officer, who has been under suspension for some time. 

 The tiger skin was seized by forest officials last year and was kept in the quarters of the beat officer. According to sources, he was subsequently suspended for negligence and his wife took the skin without knowing the implication. The forest department has ordered an inquiry, according to principal chief conservator of forests PK Jha.
