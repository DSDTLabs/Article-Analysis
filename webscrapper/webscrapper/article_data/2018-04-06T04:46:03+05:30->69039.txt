MUMBAI: The city crime branch arrested a 25-year-old   from Bengaluru for cheating a teacher from Gujarat of Rs 41 lakh. 

 The engineer impersonated a TV   on   and promised the teacher a film role. The teacher was asked to partially fund the film and gave the accused, Prajwal Gopalkrishnan, Rs 41 lakh. 
 
 The teacher began suspecting something was wrong when Gopalkrishnan, who met the teacher at Andheri to collect the money and said he was an assistant of the actress, stopped taking calls. 
 
 The police said Gopalkrishnan wanted to use the money for the treatment of an ailing member of his family.
