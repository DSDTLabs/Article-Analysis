CHENNAI: The city police have withdrawn the security cover to   neighbourhood where former chief minister  's residence is situated, after AIADMK party general secretary   surrendered at a Bengaluru prison on Wednesday. Private security guards engaged by Sasikala's family took over at Veda Nilayam, Jayalalithaa's residence. 

 The police have now trimmed down their force to a handful of constables. A police officer in the rank of deputy commissioner and a four additional deputy commissioners (ADCs) used to be on the ground supervising the 100 TN special police personnel deployed to provide security to the house and the neighbourhood.
 
 The move came after DMK leader M K Stalin criticised it.
