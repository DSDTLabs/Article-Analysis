CHENNAI: A five-day-old new born girl was found dead inside a leather bag at one of the compartments in Kolkata-Chennai Coromandel Express that reached   railway station on Tuesday night. 

 The issue came to light when the   (RPF) and Government Railway Police (GRP) personnel found an bandoned bag containing the corpse of a new born girl. The GRP, Chennai Centrail railway station has registered a case and are probing the issue. The police suspect that the child may have even died due to suffocation, as the bag was found locked. The police suspect that the child may have born to an illegitimate couple. Further investigations are on.
