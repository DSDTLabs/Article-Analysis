POINGUINIM: An adult male  , estimated to be 4-5 years old, was rescued from a partially full well at Tudal, Gaondongorim, by range forest staff on Saturday.

 Range forest officer  , said the office was alerted of the incident at around 11am, and that forest staff took four hours to rescue the wild cat. It was subsequently shifted to the Bondla   Sanctuary. This is the fourth leopard to be rescued within a span of six months.
