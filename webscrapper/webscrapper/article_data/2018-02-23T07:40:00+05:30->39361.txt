LUDHIANA: Ludhiana streets are all to shine as Radio Mirchi’s most electrifying event ‘Silver Arc Mall Mirchi Neon Run’, powered by  , will make its debut in Ludhiana. The 1st edition of the run will take place on March 3 at Silver Arc Mall, Ferozepur Road, Ludhiana.

 In keeping with Mirchi’s vision to create memorable, annual events that integrate with people’s lives, this party run is inspired by international neon theme-based runs which bring together partying and fitness to create an unforgettable experience.
 
 With a Zumba warm-up session, a finale post-run   and loads of exciting neon glow-in-the-dark merchandise and accessories, this run promises to set the city aglow with its vibrancy. There is a single category for the run this year of 5 km passing through prominent places in Ludhiana like   and Gurdev Nagar.
 
 When Mirchi Neon Run started in 2015, it was India’s first neon theme-based event. This experience caters to people across different ages and fitness levels and ensures a fun-filled party run for the entire family.
 
 Due to its spectacular success, this year Mirchi is taking this run to 13 cities including Bangalore, Chandigarh, Ludhiana, Pune, Delhi, Mumbai, Coimbatore and Baroda in addition to Chennai, Indore, Ahmedabad, Surat and Hyderabad where it has already taken place this year.
 
 More than 3,000 people are expected to participate at the event in Ludhiana. With the tagline ‘Run. Dance. Party’, Silver Arc Mall Mirchi Neon Run promises to be a scintillating experience where one can participate with friends and family. Registrations for the event are open at Silver Arc Mall.
