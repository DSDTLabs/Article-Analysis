The complaints shall be submitted before July 29. The receipt of submission of application for pension shall be submitted along with the complaint. 
 
 Until April the state government sanctioned disbursal of social welfare pension to the tune of Rs 431.78 crores. Of this amount six corporations in the state received Rs 25.73 crores. The government distributes five types of welfare pensions; old age pension, widow pension, unmarried women above 50 years, agriculture labour pension and disability pension.
