MUMBAI: The state has set up a four-member committee, headed by chief secretary D K Jain, to change rules for   of   on blended wines. At present, no excise duty is charged on wine produced in the state without addition of alcohol.

 The excise duty on wines from grapes imported from other countries or states or blended with wine produced within the state with or without addition of alcohol is 150% of the manufacturing cost. In case of wines manufactured as a foreign brand from bulk wine or wine concentrate or grape juice or imported grapes, it is 200% of the manufacturing cost.
