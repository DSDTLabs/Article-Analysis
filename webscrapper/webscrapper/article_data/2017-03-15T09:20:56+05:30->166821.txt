DAVANAGERE: A 38-year-old man and his son drowned in an   near   village in   taluk on Sunday afternoon.

 Yogaraj and his son  , 7, drowned in the pond which was dug recently in their   garden. The pond was being used to water the field. 
 
 Chethan accidentally slipped and fell into the pond. On hearing his son’s cries for help, Yogaraj rushed to the spot and dived into the pond. Both did not know swimming, said police. 
 
 When the duo did not return home till late night, the family members launched a search for them. On Monday morning, they found Yogaraj and Chethan’s slippers near the pond. Their bodies were fished out later in the day.
