NEW DELHI: The Directorate of Technical Education in Maharashtra (DTE Maharashtra) is likely to release the admit cards for the Maharashtra Common Entrance Test (CET) for Master in Business Administration (MBA) 2018 on the official website of DTE Maharashtra - dtemaharashtra.gov.in

 Candidates who have applied for the examination can check and download their admit card from the official website - dtemaharashtra.gov.in. 
 
 Candidates are advised to download their Maharashtra Common Entrance Test (CET) for Master in Business Administration (MBA) 2018 Admit Card from the official website - dtemaharashtra.gov.in - only.
 
 The Maharashtra Common Entrance Test (CET) for Master in Business Administration (MBA) 2018 will be held on March 10 and March 11 in different cities of Maharashtra. 
 
 In case you have also applied for the Maharashtra Common Entrance Test (CET) for Master in Business Administration (MBA) 2018 examination, you can follow the steps given below to check and download your admit card. 
 
 Steps to check Maharashtra Common Entrance Test (CET) for Master in Business Administration (MBA) 2018 Admit Card from - dtemaharashtra.gov.in
 
 1) Open the official website of Maharashtra Common Entrance Test (CET) for Master in Business Administration (MBA) - dtemaharashtra.gov.in
 
 2) On the homepage of the website, there will be a link "Maharashtra Common Entrance Test (CET) for Master in Business Administration (MBA) 2018 Admit Card/Hall Ticket". Click on the link
 
 3) A pop-up page will appear, enter your Maharashtra Common Entrance Test (CET) for Master in Business Administration (MBA) 2018 application number, date of birth, security pin and click on login. 
 
 4) Your Maharashtra Common Entrance Test (CET) for Master in Business Administration (MBA) 2018 admit card will be displayed on the screen. Carefully check all your details 
 
 5) You can download it and take a printout for future reference.
