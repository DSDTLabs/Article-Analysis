Kota: Body of a 25-year-old youth, who had been swept away by strong current on Sunday evening while crossing the   river near underconstruction Makheda overbridge in Bundi, was recovered on Tuesday about 3km away from the spot.

 The youth was identified as Suresh Rathore, a resident of Karwad village under Itawa police station of Kota district.
 
 His body was recovered from Chambal river, about 3km away from the spot where he had been swept away, said Hariram Choudhary, SHO, Deikheda police station. tnn
