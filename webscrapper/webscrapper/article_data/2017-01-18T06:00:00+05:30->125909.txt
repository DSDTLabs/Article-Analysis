Visakhapatnam: Members of the Yadava Sangham of Visakhapatnam led by Telugu Desam Party member VSN Murthy filed a complaint against Chaganti Koteswara Rao, a well-known Hindu Dharmic Pravachak and scholar, for allegedly abusing members of the community in a recent discourse. The complaint was filed at the Gopalapatnam police station here on Tuesday. The police have notified it a non-cognisant case. TNN
