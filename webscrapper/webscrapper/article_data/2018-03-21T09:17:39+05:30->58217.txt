 Forest officials, who reached the spot on Tuesday, said that while some animals were killed by the dogs, others died out of shock following the attack.
 
 According to forest officials, Mashru Lamka had left his herd comprising of 11 goats and 33 sheep at his farm outside the village to attend a wedding in the neighbouring village. However, during the night a pack of wild dogs entered the farm and attacked the animals. When Lamka returned on Tuesday morning, he found all the animals dead in the farm. He then informed the forest department and police.
