KOHIMA: A total of 225 candidates filed their nominations before their respective returning officers across the state for the upcoming civic polls till Wednesday. According to the state election commission, out of the 225 candidates, 139 are from   (NPF), 20 from BJP and 66 Independent candidates filed their nomination papers. Saturday is the last day for filing nominations. 

 Altogether, three municipal councils and 29 town councils across the 11 districts of Nagaland will go to the polls on February 1. There are 353 wards, out of which 120 are reserved for women. Several   have protested against the civic body election that will see 33% reservation of seats for women.
