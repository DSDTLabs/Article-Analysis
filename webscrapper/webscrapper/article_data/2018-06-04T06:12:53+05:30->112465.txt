HYDERABAD: The Additional Director   has issued a fresh directive, clarifying that the Cantonment Board as per the Cantonments Act will play a significant role in closure and reopening of roads. 

 
 A May 31 letter was issued to principal directors of all commands with a copy to chief executive officers of all 62 Cantonment boards, including  .
 
 It has been clarified that no road shall be closed for reasons other than security concerns and the proposal has to be examined by the Cantonment Board.
 
 The letter states that the   order on roads will be noted in the next board meeting.
