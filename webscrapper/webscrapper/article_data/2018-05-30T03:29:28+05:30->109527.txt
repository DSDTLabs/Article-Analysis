MUMBAI: Key political leaders and government officials were among passengers stranded as   cancelled its Aurangabad-Mumbai flight after it developed a snag early on Tuesday. 

  Flight 9W355 was to depart at 6.50am and reach Mumbai at 7.50am. Take-off had to be suspended soon after the plane started taxiing towards the runway. 
 
  The political leaders missed an important meeting in Mumbai while several passengers reportedly missed connecting flights. Jalna MP Raosaheb Danve, Aurangabad mayor  , former mayor  , MLA Narayan   and divisional commissioner   had to attend a meeting with chief minister Devendra Fadnavis.
