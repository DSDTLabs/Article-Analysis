THIRUVANANTHAPURAM: The fifth edition of   was flagged off from the state capital by tourism minister Kadakampally Surendran on Sunday.

 The annual programme of   brings together travel bloggers from across the globe. In the latest edition, 30 bloggers from countries such as France, US, UK, Canada, Germany, Italy, Spain, Bulgaria, Romania, Venezuela and   will embark on a two-week journey across the state.
 
 
 
 
 The bloggers will also document their journey covering the hills, beaches, backwaters, villages and various city life experiences in the state. Their journey will conclude in Kochi on April 1.
 
 Places such as Alappuzha, Kumarakom, Thrissur, Munnar, Kozhikode, Wayanad, Kannur and   will be covered during the two-week programme. Photos, videos and articles about the places will be uploaded by the travellers and will also be shared among their followers all over the world.
 
 Flagging off the event, Surendran said Kerala is the only state in the world that has combined social media campaign, familiarisation trip and bloggers meet in one grand event. “Around 400 blogs were generated from last year’s edition alone. We can see the results in terms of the increase in tourist arrival to the state in the past one year,” he said.
 
 Kerala Tourism has been on the tourism world map for quite some time now and have received numerous awards and recognition for its innovative campaigns, said tourism secretary Rani George in her welcome speech.
 
 Tourism director P   said Kerala Tourism’s efforts to explore new marketing avenues have given birth to the idea of the blog express. He urged the bloggers to give importance to the interactions with local people, which would add more colour to their experience in the state.
