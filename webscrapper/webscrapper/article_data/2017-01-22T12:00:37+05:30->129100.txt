JAIPUR: The Met office forecasts chances of rain or thundershower at isolated places in Rajasthan even as the cold wave conditions have eased in the state. It said shallow to moderate fog is likely to occur at isolated places. However, there is not much change in   predicted for Sunday.

 Churu and Bikaner reported mild rain on Saturday triggered by a western disturbance originated from Pakistan. It has also resulted in cloudy conditions. The cloudy sky condition has also pushed the temperature up as the cloud acted as a blanket trapping the hot air going upwards.
 
 Churu has been the coldest place with a minimum temperature of 4.4 degrees Celsius followed by   at 4.8 degrees Celsius.
 
 In Jaipur, the minimum temperature rose to over nine degrees Celsius partly due to cloudy conditions. Met office forecasts partly cloudy sky in the state capital with maximum and minimum temperature would be around 23 and 10 degrees Celsius, respectively. 
 
 Jodhpur and Udaipur have been the warmest places in the state with the maximum temperature over 25 degrees Celsius. Udaipur division has reported shallow to moderate fog condition at isolated places on Saturday morning.
