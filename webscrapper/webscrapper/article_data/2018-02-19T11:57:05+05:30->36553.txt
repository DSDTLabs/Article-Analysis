 In a letter given to  , Chennithala wanted the government to take urgent steps to ensure the availability of stamp papers worth Rs 50 and Rs 100.
 
 "Stamp papers of only Rs 500 and Rs 1,000 values are now available. Since stamp papers of smaller denominations are not available, people are forced to buy stamp papers of higher denomination in place of Rs 50 or Rs 100 worth stamp papers," he said.
 
 "Since stamp papers worth Rs 50 and Rs 100 are widely used for purposes like registration of births, deaths and marriages and for rental agreements, the government should take immediate steps to ensure their availability," he said. tnn
