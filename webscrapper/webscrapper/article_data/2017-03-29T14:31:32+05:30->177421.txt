GREATER NOIDA: A female African student was assaulted by some unknown miscreants early morning on Wednesday when the girl was heading to a society in   to meet her brother.

 According to the members of the  , the 24-year-old girl was getting down from an auto when the incident happened around 5 am in the morning.
 
 While no serious injuries were reported, she was admitted to the Kailash Hospital Greater Noida where she was given first aid and remained admitted for about four hours.
 
 The girl, who had recently taken admission in the Noida International University, was in shock and could not speak for some time, said Umar Najeeb, coordinator for the Association of the African Students in India.
 
 She was later discharged. A complaint would be lodged in Knowledge Park police station, Najeeb said.
