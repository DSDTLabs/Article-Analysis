BATHINDA: Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) MLA from   in  ,   Kaur, is in trouble for getting herself registered as a voter twice.

 The Talwandi Sabo electoral registration officer-cum-sub-divisional magistrate has stated that Baljinder registered twice at different times in 2005 and 2011. 
 
 Baljinder was facing allegations of preparing dual votes since 2014 when she filed nomination papers for Talwandi Sabo by-election and later when she contested the assembly polls in 2017.
