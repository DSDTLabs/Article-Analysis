It was a literary evening marked with a perfect blend of sher-o-shayari. The event received a great response from shayari lovers in the city. Several keen listeners reached the venue well before time. The event started off with chief guest Justice Shabihul Hasnain lighting the inaugural lamp along with Jaideep Narain Mathur, Kanak Rekha Chauhan and Farhat Ehsaas. 
Prominent shayars from all across the globe, including Ashfaak Hasnain, Farhat Ehsaas, Shareef Kaifi, Manish Shukla, Ranjeet Chauhan, Khushbir Singh Shaad and  , were among those who recited their shayari at the event.
