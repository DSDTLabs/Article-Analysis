Actress   is known for her love for animals. An active   activist, she is also the first person many people contact on social media when they see an animal in distress. This was the case on Monday morning too, when she received a message asking to help a cow and her calf in Koramangala. Samyukta immediately called for an ambulance to rescue them from there, while also ensuring that she contacted veterinary doctors to help administer some quick treatment. 
While the ambulance took a couple of hours to reach the spot, the cow, which was bleeding profusely, was treated by the doctor and the timely action ensured that both the cow and the calf were out of any danger.
