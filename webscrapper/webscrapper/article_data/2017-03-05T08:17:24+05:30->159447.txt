NEW DELHI: A 19-year-old youth died and his colleague was critically affected while cleaning the sewers of a residential house in south   on Saturday evening. Police said that a case has been registered against the house owner.

 The incident was reported around 8pm when the family members of the house owner admitted the two youths to a private hospital. One of them, Sanjay, was declared dead on arrival due to asphyxiation while the other, who is yet to be identified, was declared critical. They were later referred to   for treatment.
 
 Probe revealed that Sanjay and his colleague were hired by the house owner to clean the sewer pipes at the house without permission from the civic bodies. They were asked to climb into the sewer hole during which they had fallen unconscious due to the poisonous gases.
