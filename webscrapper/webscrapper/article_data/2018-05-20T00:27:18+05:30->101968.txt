Trichy: An army man shot his wife with a pistol before shooting himself dead at their house in Kallukuzhi in Trichy city on Saturday night. The injured woman has been admitted to Mahatma Gandhi memorial government hospital. The deceased was identified as B Ranjithkumar, 33, from Bihar while the injured wife, N Rajani Kumari, 29, a railway employee, also hailed from that state.

 The incident happened at their house in Kallukuzhi around 9 pm. Preliminary investigations revealed that Ranjithkumar was a constable attached to 113 battalion of border security force (BSF). He had come to Trichy to meet his wife in the evening. However, the details about the place of his service were not clear. The incident happened a few hours after his arrival.
