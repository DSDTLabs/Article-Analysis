Pune: A 28-year-old married man having four children was arrested by the Kondhwa police on Saturday night for repeatedly raping a 16-year-old schoolgirl at Hadapsar between June and August 2017.

 The minor is a std X student of a local school. Her parents and the suspect know each other and live in the same neighbourhood.
 
 Police said the suspect used to stalk her to school. He proposed to the girl and threatened her to not disclose his ill deeds.
 
 Kondhwa police senior inspector Milind Gaikwad said, “The girl started keeping her distance from the man after her parents got her engaged. The suspect got furious over this. He phoned her fiance and told him about their relation, following which the fiance called off the engagement.” Police arrested him under the sections of IPC and POCSO Act.
 
 Schoolgirl molested
 
 The Hadapsar police on Sunday arrested a 30-year-old man for molesting a 15-year-old schoolgirl at Bhekrainagar on Thursday under relevant sections of the Indian Penal Code and Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act.
