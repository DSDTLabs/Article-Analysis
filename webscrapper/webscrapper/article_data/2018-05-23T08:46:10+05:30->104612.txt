BHOPAL:   party president Rahul Gandhi on Tuesday approved constitution of committees of the state PCC for in view of the upcoming assembly elections. Former chief minister and Rajya Sabha MP Digvijaya Singh has been made the chairman of coordination committee.

 
 A letter from AICC general secretary Ashok Gehlot reached the state Congress office on Tuesday giving a list of the chairmen of different committees along with the names of the members. Singh in a recent meeting with state Congress president   had expressed his wish to work for mobilization of party workers before the elections. There are 12 other members who will work with Digvijaya Singh in committee, including former Bhopal mayor Vibha Patel, former minister Rameshwar Neekhra and former Rajya Sabha MP Satyavrat Chaturvedi.
 
 Jyotiraditya Scindia was appointed chairman of the campaign committee on the day Rahul Gandhi appointed senior party leader Kamal Nath as PCC chief.
