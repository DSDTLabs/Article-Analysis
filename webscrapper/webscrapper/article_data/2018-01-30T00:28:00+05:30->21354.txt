Coimbatore: The rural police arrested an 18-year-old boy on Monday for allegedly sodomising an 11-year-old-boy at Veerakeralam near Vadavalli here. The arrested boy, who has been working as a construction worker, is lodged at the observation home for juveniles. 

 On January 27, the boy was taken to the Coimbatore Medical College Hospital (CMCH) where doctors told the boy’s mother that he was sodomised by someone and gave treatment. 
 
 When the mother questioned him, the boy told that he was sodomised more than five times by a construction worker form the same area. The boy’s mother lodged a complaint with the Vadavalli police who registered a case under sections of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act. tnn
