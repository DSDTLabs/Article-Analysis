CHANDIGARH: The Punjab and   high court on Tuesday restrained the Haryana government from taking any coercive action against Ved Pal, Cheeka sub-divisional officer ( ), public health department.

 The   passed these orders after hearing a petition filed by Ved, challenging the recommendations of health minister Anil Vij who had ordered to suspend him during the grievances meeting on Friday.
 
 In his petition, Ved Pal has sought directions to restrain the Haryana government from taking any coercive action against him as per recommendations of Vij and had also sought directions an independent probe in relation to the allegations against him.
 
 Taking cognizance of the issue, the HC has also issued notice to the Haryana government seeking its reply on the issue. The matter would now come up for hearing on July 13.
