INDORE: After tracing the GS location of a vehicle,   police seized a stolen container truck filled with medicines worth crores of rupees at Bhopal square in Dewas.

 The incident came to the fore after   police received information from one in   that the truck carrying medicines had been taken away by the driver.
 
 The complainant, Vijay Jaiswal, told Dewas police about the location of the truck using the GPS that was enabled in the vehicle.
 
 Based on this information, police seized the truck at Bhopal square on Thursday morning. The accused, however, managed to flee the spot.
 
 “We have registered a case in the matter and investigations are on,” said Umrao Singh,   police station in-charge.
