GREATER NOIDA: A 30-year old man was was allegedly shot at by his younger brother over a property dispute in Chithera village on Wednesday.

 The police said the two brothers lived separately and had differences over a piece of ancestral land. “The two brothers divided the land after evaluation some months ago. However,the younger brother was not satisfied with the allotment and wanted to calculate the dimensions again,” the police said.
 
 On Wednesday morning the brothers had an argument over the issue. The younger brother
 
 took out a country-made revolver and opened fire. The elder brother was injured in his leg and taken to hospital by his family members.
 
  Sanjay Kumar Tyagi, SHO Dadri police station, said a complaint was notlodged yet.
