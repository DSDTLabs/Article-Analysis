Gopichand starrer 'Oxygen' has finished shooting and the movie is currently in post-production. It has been quite long that Gopichand has appeared on big screen. He is last seen in 'Soukhyam' in the year 2015. Apparently, while the other projects that the actor worked on were delayed, 'Oxygen' is going to hit the screens very soon. The recently released first look and teaser of   have already gained good reviews. Actress Rashi Khanna and 'Majnu' fame   are the leading ladies of the movie directed by A M Jyothi Krishna. Touted to be a high-octane action entertainer, the movie also has Jagapathi Babu, Shaam, Chandra Mohan among others playing prominent roles. Yuvan Shankar Raja scored music for it.
