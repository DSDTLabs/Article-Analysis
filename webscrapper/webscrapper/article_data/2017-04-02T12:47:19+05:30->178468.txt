RAJKOT: An office bearer of a village   was looted of Rs 13lakh cash and   to Rs 3.5 lakh while he was on his way to a bank near Waghnagar on   road in Bhavnagar. 

 Police said that the incident took place when Himmatbhai of Naip village was going on his bike. Two persons on a motorcycle intercepted him near Waghnagar and snatched the cash bag from him, before fleeing from the spot. 
 
 Himmatbhai later told police that he was carrying the cash that belonged to farmers and was going to deposit them in the bank. He further said that he was not able to see the faces of robbers.
