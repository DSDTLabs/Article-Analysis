Rajkot: A six-year-old girl suffered severe injuries after she was attacked by a leopard in Karsar village of Mahuva taluka in Bhavnagar on Wednesday morning. Khushi was taken to Bhavnagar Civil Hospital after primary treatment at Mahuva hospital. 
 
 The incident took place when the girl was sleeping outside her house in the village with other members of the family. The leopard attacked the girl at around 6 am. According to forest officials, the girl sustained injuries on her nose and forehead. The leopard ran away after girl’s grandmother shouted for help. 
  
 Range forest officer (RFO) H J Vanda said, “We have set up a cage to nab the leopard. After medical report we will approve the compensation as prescribed by the state government.”
