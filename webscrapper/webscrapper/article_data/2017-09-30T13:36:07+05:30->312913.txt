Julia Louis-Dreyfus' son   thanked fans for support after the actor's cancer diagnosis. 

  
  
 From former vice president   to 'Seinfeld' co- star Jason Alexander tweeted messages of encouragement for the Emmy-winning actress. 
  
 The 'Veep' star's son tweeted a charming image of the two from when he was a child alongside the message, "I couldn't be more thankful for the outpouring of support for my mom yesterday. Here's a picture of us taken last year. Love to you all." 
 
  
  
 The actress second these thoughts and responded to her son's message with a retweet and a note of her own. "I'm thankful, too. And also thankful for my dear boy with good manners," she wrote.
