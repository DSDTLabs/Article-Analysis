Salem: Two unknown miscreants on Tuesday pelted a medical shop at Chinnatirupathi in Salem with stones.  According to the Kannankuruchi police, two men who came in a two-wheeler, covering their face with cloths, pelted the MedPlus medical shop with stones and fled. “The shop was open since morning. About 90% of the medical shops in the city closed their shutters responding to the bandh called by the ruling AIADMK,” an officer said.  In the attack, the front door glass panel was broken. The employees including two women in the shop were shocked. They informed the police. Based on the complaint, the police have registered a case. They also obtained the CCTV footages from the shop and examining them. Further investigations are on.
