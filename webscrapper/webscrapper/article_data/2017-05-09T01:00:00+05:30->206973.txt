The Embassy of Israel in India recently celebrated Israel’s 69th Independence Day. Celebrations were also held for marking 25 years of   diplomatic relations.

  The Israeli ambassador to India, Daniel Carmon, raised a toast with the chief guest,  , MoS for Youth Affairs and Sports at the do. Carmon said, "This year we are celebrating 25 years of the diplomatic relations, friendship and ever-growing partnership between India and Israel. We are proud to be India’s technological partner. The best is yet to come."
