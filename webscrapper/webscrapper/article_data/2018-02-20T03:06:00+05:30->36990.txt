Vasco: The Vasco excise department on Monday nabbed two Odisha natives for attempting to smuggle 84 bottles of Indian-made foreign liquor (IMFL) to Odisha.  The estimated value of the bottles was Rs 9,960. Officials said Balaram Das and Rakesh Bank, residents of Bhatulem, Panaji, were noticed to be moving about suspiciously on platform 1 of the Vasco railway station, following which they were nabbed and their luggage seized.  Their bags were found to contain 84 alcohol bottles of varied brands.  A case has been registered against the duo by the excise department and the railway police.
