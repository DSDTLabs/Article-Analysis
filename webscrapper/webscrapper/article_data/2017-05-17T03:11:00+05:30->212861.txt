Mapusa: BJP leaders and workers are eagerly waiting for the bypolls in Panaji and Valpoi to elect BJP MLAs, party leader and former MLA from Tivim constituency and former chairman of the Goa horticulture corporation Kiran Kandolkar said, adding that Congress “don't even have the candidates” to contest the bypolls. Addressing a press conference in Mapusa on Tuesday, Kandolkar said that BJP will also gain supremacy in the upcoming panchayat elections. “We are waiting for the election to be held and we will make our candidates win,” Kandolkar said.
