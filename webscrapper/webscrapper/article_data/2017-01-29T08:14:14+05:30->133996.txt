HYDERABAD: TDP MLA   has filed a   in the   urging it to declare as illegal the decision of the   in according cabinet minister status to scores of retired bureaucrats after appointing them as advisers and chairmen of various corporations. 

 The cabinet size should not cross 15 percent of the total 119 MLAs. But with the addition of so many people as persons with cabinet minister status, the percentage prescribed has crossed the limits, he said. The state has no power to do this and hence it should be struck down, he said.
 
 Revanth Reddy in his plea challenged the conferring of cabinet minister status on the advisors to the state government.
