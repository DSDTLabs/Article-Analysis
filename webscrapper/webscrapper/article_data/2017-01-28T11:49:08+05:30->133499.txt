HYDERABAD: Justice   of the Hyderabad High Court on Friday referred all the petitions related to the election of Hyderabad Cricket Association, to the Acting Chief Justice.

 Justice Sanjay directed the registry to request the Acting Chief Justice to place all of them before a single bench. The judge also drove home the urgency conveyed by some   to have the tussle resolved early in view of the   scheduled to be held from February 9.
