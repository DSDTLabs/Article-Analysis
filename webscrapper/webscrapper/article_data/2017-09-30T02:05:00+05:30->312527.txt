Yavatmal: The inflow of the pesticide-hit farmers to the Yavatmal GMCh has shown a decline on Friday as various agencies have started creating mass awareness among the farmers.

 According to the Dean of the GMCH Dr Ashok Rathod, there are 45 patients admitted in three wards of the hospital of whom 10 are in the ICU. Eight patients are on ventilators of whom the condition of two patients is most critical.
 
 “We are taking utmost care of the patients and our efforts are for their speedy recovery,” Rathod said.
 
 However, two patients have been admitted in a private hospital in the city on Friday due to infection from spraying pesticide. But their condition is stable, it is learnt.
