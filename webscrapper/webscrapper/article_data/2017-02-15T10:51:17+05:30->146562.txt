 The   cabinet has cleared the latest draft of its transit oriented development (TOD) policy. According to the amendment, in case any individual applies for a licence in an institutional area in the TOD zone, he would have to transfer to the government an equal area of land in a residential zone.
 
 The government would utilize that land to set up institutions like colleges, hospitals, fire stations, power stations and police stations. The amendment will help in effective implementation policy for innovative financing of mass rapid transit system (MRTS) projects. The amended policy will become applicable with immediate effect.
