NEW DELHI: External affairs minister   and   defence minister   discussed on Friday the recent initiatives of the Afghanistan government for internal reconciliation and also efforts to combat terrorism, which they said received support from “safe havens and sanctuaries beyond Afghanistan”.

 
 According to a statement issued by MEA, Swaraj conveyed India’s support for the efforts of the government and the people of Afghanistan for building a peaceful, secure, stable, united, inclusive and prosperous nation.
