GURUGRAM: A 20-year-old trainee astrologer has accused a   friend of blackmail, sexual harassment and extortion. The complainant, a resident of Panipat, lives as a tenant in DLF-II while she is training to be an astrologer.

  She claims to have befriended a Satyender Verma, a resident of  , some months ago on Facebook.
 
 The complainant and the accused became friends through social media first and later they shared their numbers and started chatting on Whatsapp as well, eventually meeting once in the city.
 
  “On January 1, the accused called me and demanded some money, and when I refused to give him money, he started threatening me. He claimed that if I don’t pay, he will post some of my pictures online,” the complainant told the police. 
 
 An FIR was registered against the accused under sections 354 A (sexual harassment), 354 C (voyeurism), 384 (extortion), 506 (criminal intimidation) of IPC at DLF-II police station. 
 
 “The victim is a trainee astrologer and lived here as a tenant. We have register the case and dispatched a team to Madhya Pradesh to nab the accused. The accused will be arrested soon,” said inspector  , station house officer, DLF-II police station.
