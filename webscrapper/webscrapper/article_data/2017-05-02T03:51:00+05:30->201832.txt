Margao: Jaffer Imam Kagnelli, a 17-year old resident of Arlem, was feared drowned in an abandoned stone quarry at  , Monday afternoon. 

 Police sources said Kagnelli had travelled to Sarzora with three of his friends from Davorlim to take a swim in a quarry filled with water.
 
 Sources said Kagnelli was sitting on a stone beside the 20-feet-deep quarry, when he slipped and fell into it. He was feared drowned when immediate efforts to locate him proved futile.
 
 A search continued until late evening. Sources said naval divers may be requisitioned for the search exercise.
