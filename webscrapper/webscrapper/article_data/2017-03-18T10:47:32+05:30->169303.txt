VARANASI: The principal secretary (health) suspended chief medical officer of Ballia, PK Singh, on Friday. 

 According to reports, the CMO had issued an advertisement for recruitment of drivers in 2015-16 and had allegedly hired drivers after formation of a selection committee even when he had no such authority of providing them jobs.
 
 The state health department stopped the wages of the recruited drivers and initiated an inquiry.
