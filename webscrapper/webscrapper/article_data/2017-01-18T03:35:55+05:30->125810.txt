Gurgaon: Nearly   after a   girl went   from her home near Tau Devi Lal Biodiversity Park in Sector 52, police said they were yet to get any significant information in the alleged abduction case.

 A 10-year-old girl went missing in suspicious circumstances on Monday afternoon from her slum and her father, a labourer, filed an FIR in the Susant Lok police station after the family failed to trace her for several hours. The FIR was registered against unknown person under sections 363 (abduction), 366A (procreation of minor girl) of IPC. “We have formed a team to find the missing girl. So far, we have got no clue so far,” said  , additional SHO, Sushant Lok police station.
