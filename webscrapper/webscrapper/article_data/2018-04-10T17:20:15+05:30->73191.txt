NEW DELHI: The   ( ), Kerala, released the   Engineering, Agriculture and Medical ( ) Admit Card 2018 through online mode on April 10, 2018. 

 
 Students can access their KEAM 2018 Admit Card through the official website: cee-kerala.org
 
 KEAM is a state level entrance exam which is also a known by the name of Combined Entrance Examination (CEE). Through this exam students can get admission in various specializations of engineering, pharmacy, medical, forestry, agriculture and many other courses. 
 
 For medical course, a candidate should have appeared in NEET exam and for architecture courses, candidates have to be qualified in NATA entrance exam. 
 
 KEAM entrance exam will be conducted on April 23 and 24, 2018 in various exam centres. The KEAM 2018 result will be declared on or before May 25, 2018.
