PANAJI: Following a complaint lodged before the chief electoral officer (CEO) over her association with defence minister  , popular singer   on Tuesday tendered her resignation from the post of special icon for the 2017 state assembly election. 

 Sardesai has been accused of actively campaigning for the Bhartiya Janata Party ( ) in the past. Her resignation as the icon of the assembly poll, however, has yet to be accepted by the CEO.
 
 “The (Sardesai’s) resignation has been received. It is still under examination,” chief electoral officer, Goa, Kunal told reporters on Tuesday.
