The city recently organised an event   to felicitate veteran actor  . The actor was honoured at a function held at the University Senate Hall. Actors Sharada, Mukesh, Indrans, Kerala Legislative Assembly speaker P Sreeramakrishnan, KM Mani, D Vijayakumar, O Rajagopal, music director and filmmaker   were among the guests at the do. The occasion also witnessed a variety of entertainment programmes including a music show and a Madhu Gana Manjari where evergreen songs of the Chemmeen actor were presented.
