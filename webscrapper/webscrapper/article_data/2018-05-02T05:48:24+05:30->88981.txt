NEW DELHI: Former IAS officer Subhash Chandra Khuntia was appointed as the new chairman of Insurance Regulatory Development Authority of India (IRDAI) for three years on Tuesday. His appointment was approved by Appointments Committee of the   ( ),   said in a statement.

 
 Khuntia was the chief secretary of Karnataka. He will succeed T S Vijayan, who completed his five-year term in February. The post has been lying vacant since February 21. Khuntia was a 1981-batch   cadre officer.
 
 “The ACC has approved the appointment of Subhash Chandra Khuntia...to the post of chairperson, IRDAI for a period of three years with effect from the date of assumption of charge of the post, or till the age of 65 years, or until further orders, whichever is the earliest,” said the statement.
