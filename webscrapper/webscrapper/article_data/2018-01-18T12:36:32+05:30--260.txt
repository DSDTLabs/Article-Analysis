BHOPAL:   MP   has been fined by traffic police for not wearing a helmet while driving a bike in the city.

 Bhopal MLA   was riding pillion on Sanjar's bike on Monday as they took part in   - a statewide mission to collect metal to install a 108-feet tall statue of Adi Shankaracharya at Omkareswar. A complaint against the MP was filed on Bhopal traffic police's WhatsApp number on Tuesday and a challan was immediately issued in the name of Sanjar, said ASP-traffic  . On Wednesday, Sanjar went to the traffic police station and paid a fine of Rs 250. 
 
 According to ASP Jain, Sanjar told police that being a responsible citizen it is his duty to follow traffic rules.
