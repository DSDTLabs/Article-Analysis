BATHINDA: Bathinda administration has started making arrangements for counting votes in the six assembly constituencies in the district on March 11.

 District election officer-cum-deputy commissioner   visited the three centres that have been set up for counting votes on March 11, along with returning officers (ROs) of all six constituencies.
 
 He said work for installing barricades at the centres was in the advanced stages of completion. “Arrangements have been made for carrying EVMs from strongrooms to counting halls safely,” he said.
