MUMBAI: A three-month-old   girl was found   near the railway   in   (west) on Monday. The baby, dressed in a frock, was wrapped in a cotton towel and kept near Narangi phata. 

 The baby’s cry alerted passersby who informed the police. Her identity has not yet been ascertained. She is being treated at the Virar rural hospital. Her health is fine.
 
 Police has registered a case of abandonment against the baby’s parents. Police are examining closed circuit television footage to get leads of who left behind the child.
