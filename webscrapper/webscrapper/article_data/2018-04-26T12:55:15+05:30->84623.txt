AHMEDABAD: The   and Higher Secondary Education Board ( ) on Wednesday uploaded the provisional answer key to the   Common Entrance Test ( ).

 Officials said that to compensate for the errors in questions, Gujarati-medium students have been given four marks and English-medium students have been given three.
 
 In maths, Gujarati-, English-, and Hindi-medium students have been granted one mark. In physics and chemistry, students of Gujarati-medium schools have been granted three marks. 
 
 In physics and chemistry, English- and Hindi-medium students received one mark as one question had two correct answers. In biology, English-medium students have been given a mark as one question was incorrect. Officials said that stakeholders can report errors till final answer key is declared on April 29. tnn
