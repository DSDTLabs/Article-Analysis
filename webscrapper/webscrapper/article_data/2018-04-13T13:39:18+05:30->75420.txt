VISAKHAPATNAM: Human Resource Development Minister   on Friday released the intermediate first year results here in YVS Murthy Auditorium in Andhra University in Visakhapatnam. As many as 2,95,891 students (62 percent) out of 4,78,621 students, who appeared for the first year examinations, had cleared it.

 
 
 As expected the girl students have outperformed their counterparts (boys) in the Intermediate first year exams that declared today around 12:30 pm. In the first year, 67 percent girls passed the exam against 57 percent of boys. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
  
  
 
 It may be noted that the Andhra Government had introduced the grading system for the first time this year. 
 
 Krishna, West Godavari and Guntur districts stood in top three places in terms of pass percentage.
