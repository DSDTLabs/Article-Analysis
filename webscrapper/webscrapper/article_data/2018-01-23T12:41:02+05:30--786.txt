  On January 19 2018, Symbiosis Institute of Business Management hosted their Management, Cultural and Sports festival known as ‘Revelation- 2018’ with the theme ‘Aether Infinium — A chance to prove that your zenith is limitless’. The event had around, 1000 students from around 160 colleges participating in a number of events.  Some of them were paper presentation, dance competition, fashion show, battle of the bands, the informal event (which had the students’ race around the city to the final destination in order to find the treasure), basketball and badminton and a game-a-thon which had students participating in around 70 games. They winded up their fest by dancing to the tunes of DJ Johnson Patrick.
