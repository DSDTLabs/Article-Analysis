 Gulmarg, the famous ski-resort in north Kashmir, recorded the minimum temperature of minus 4.5 degrees Celsius, the official said.
 
 The MeT office has forecast light rain or snowfall at isolated places in the state tomorrow.
 
 Kashmir is under the grip of 'Chillai-Kalan', a 40-day harshest period of winter when chances of snowfall are most frequent and maximum, and the temperature drops considerably.
 
 However, a month into the period, the weather has largely remained dry, especially in the plains.
 
 This period is followed by 20-day long 'Chillai-Khurd' (small cold) and 10-day long 'Chillai-Bachha' (baby cold).
