ROHTAK: Haryana chief minister Manohar Lal Khattar, Rajasthan chief minister   Scindia, Uttar Pradesh chief minister   Aditya Nath, other BJP leaders and prominent social and religious figures attended the ‘samadhi’ ceremony of Alwar MP and   (61) here on Sunday.

 Yogi, who was not well for quite some time, breathed his last at a hospital in Delhi on late Saturday night. His body was laid to rest in a “samadhi” with state honours on the matth premises on Sunday evening. 
 
 Talking to media persons here, CM Khattar said Yogi's death had caused immense loss to the religious fraternity. He said his death had caused a void in the matth and religious institutions that had his followers across many states including Uttar Pradesh and Rajasthan besides Haryana. 
 
 Khattar said Yogi had started many educational institutes in Rohtak and other cities where thousands of students received education.
