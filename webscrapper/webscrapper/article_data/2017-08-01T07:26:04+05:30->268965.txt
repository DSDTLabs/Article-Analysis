NEW DELHI: Low cost carrier   on Monday reported a net profit of Rs 811 crore in April-June 2017, the highest ever quarterly profit by an Indian airline -whether listed or unlisted.

 This is 37% higher than the profit of Rs 592 crore recorded in same quarter last fiscal. IndiGo's record Q1 profit is much higher than its own previous best quarterly profit of Rs 640 crore in April June 2015-16. The airline's scrip closed 0.3% up at Rs 1,291 on the   on Monday.
 
  Aditya Ghosh said, "This strong financial and operational performance allows us to stay focused on growing our network."
