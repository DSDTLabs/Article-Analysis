MUMBAI: A month after sharing the dais with   politician Ajit Pawar,   MLA   shall now be seen on the same platform as state   chief Ashok Chavan on Thursday in Jalgaon.

 Khadse was with Ajit Pawar on the 61st   of NCP legislator Satish Pawar. Last week, he showered praise on NCP president Sharad Pawar for the agricultural progress made by Baramati.
 
 On Wednesday, Khadse confirmed he would attend Congress politician Rajiv Patil’s birthday celebrations in Ashok Chavan’s presence.
 
 Khadse had to leave the cabinet on June 4, 2016, over a controversial MIDC land deal.
