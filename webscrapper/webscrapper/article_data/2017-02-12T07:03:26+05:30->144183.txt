 Police suspect the woman might have been murdered at some other location and the body dumped there sometime on Friday night. 
 
 The   police said the body of the woman, aged between 40 and 45 years, was wrapped in a blanket and stuffed inside the bag. The incident came to light when a municipal sweeper found the body near the drain. He alerted the local village heads, who in turn informed the police.
