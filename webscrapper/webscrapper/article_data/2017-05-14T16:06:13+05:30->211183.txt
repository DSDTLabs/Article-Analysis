LUCKNOW: Prime Minister Narendra Modi would be performing Yoga on June 21, the third  , along with 55,000 people at Ramabai Ambedkar Maidan in Lucknow. 

 The venue was finalized at a meeting of Union home minister Rajnath Singh with chief minister   and Union minister of Ayush (Independent charge) Shripad Naik at CM's official residence 5 Kalidas Marg on Sunday morning.
 
 Along with a major show at Ramabai Ambedkar Rally ground, yoga programmes would also be held at district headquarters, tehsil, blocks, at public places, schools, colleges and public parks etc, the CM said. 
 
 For participating at Rama Bai Rally ground, registration process would be held and those who would get registered would be provided with a yoga mat, a t-shirt and refreshment, the CM said. 
 
 The PM's Yoga programme at Ramabai Rally ground would be shown on LED screens at parks and other public places. In a bid to make the programme smooth for the participants at Ramabai Rally ground, the CM also directed to organize a 28-day training programme. 
 
 Union Home Minister Rajnath Singh on the occasion applauded the preparation for the Yoga Day by the UP government and said that it was clear that the programme to be held in Lucknow on the day would be huge and successful. He urged the state government to ensure preparation for the programme well within the time. 
 
 On the occasion, Deputy CM Dr Dinesh Sharma, Cabinet ministers Rita Bahuguna Joshi and Ashutosh Tandon 'Gopalji', Union Minister Dr Mahendra Nath Pandey and Minister of State in Yogi Cabinet Swati Singh were also present.
