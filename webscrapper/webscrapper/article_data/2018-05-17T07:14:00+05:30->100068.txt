GURUGRAM: A 52-year-old police constable was arrested on Wednesday for allegedly molesting a sweeper and slapping her grandson after passing casteist comments. An FIR has been registered at Civil Lines police station. The accused was produced in a city court, which sent him to judicial custody.

 
 The accused has been identified as Dilbag Singh, a resident of Kaloi village in Rohtak. He was posted as an escort guard. According to police, the complainant, a 45-year-old widow, works as a sweeper at police lines.
 
 On Tuesday, the Class IV employee was going to a Mother Dairy booth nearby police lines around 5-5.20pm. The constable was sitting on a chair outside the booth.
 
 “My 3-year-old grandson, who had come along with me, started asking me to buy an ice-cream for him when I reached the milk booth. Singh suddenly caught my grandson and slapped him repeatedly. When I resisted, Singh grabbed my hand and tried to pull me towards him. He also passed casteist comments and threatened to kill me,” the woman said in her complaint. 
 
 “Another cop heard the commotion and reached the milk booth. He pulled Singh away and I rushed home with my grandson,” she said. The woman filed a police complaint later on Tuesday.
 
 On the basis of her complaint, an FIR was registered against the accused under sections 354A (sexual harassment), 323 (causing hurt) and 506 (criminal intimidation) of IPC at Civil Lines police station the same day. Singh was arrested on Wednesday morning. A senior police officer has confirmed that Singh was suspended and a departmental inquiry is going on.
 
 “The accused has claimed that he was drunk when he committed the act. He has been sent to judicial custody,” said inspector  , additional SHO of Civil Lines police station.
