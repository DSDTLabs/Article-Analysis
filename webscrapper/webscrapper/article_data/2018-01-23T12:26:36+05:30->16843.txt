A popular magazine featured the bookshelf of a blogger where she had flipped the books around so the spines were hidden and only the pages were visible. Book lovers all over the world were enraged and commented on how the idea was stupid, how she was an idiot and some even privately messaged her threats. Everyone's relation with their books and their decorating priorities are different. For some displaying what they read or their love for books is their priority when arranging their home while some find maintaining colour palettes more aesthetically pleasing.
