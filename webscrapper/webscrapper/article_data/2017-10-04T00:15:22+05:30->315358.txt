  

 
 Actress
 
 
  Bhabi Ji Ghar Par Hain, Chidiyaghar, Maayka
 
 
 
 
 Shilpa Shinde rose to popularity with her last show Bhabi Ji Ghar Par Hain where she played the role of Angoori Bhabhi. The actress started her career with a negative role in Bhabhi in the year 1999. Shilpa has been part of many successful shows like Miss India, Hatim,   to name a few. However, she was most appreciated for her characters of Angoori, Koyal from   and Soni Khurana in Maayka. 
 
 Until last year, she was in the news for her fallout with the producers of Bhabhi Ji. She was also at loggerheads with Cine and TV Artistes' Association. Shilpa had even filed a defamation case against the producer, CINTAA and Indian Films and TV producers' council (IFTPC). 
 
 The actress is seen in the item song 'Maro Line' from upcoming Hindi film Patel Ki Punjabi Shaadi and her weight became the talking point for a lot of people who trolled her on social media.
