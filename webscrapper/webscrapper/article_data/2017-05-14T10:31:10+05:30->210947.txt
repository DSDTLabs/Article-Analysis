AGRA: Secondary education department in the state has decided to include topics like Goods and   (GST), Value Added Tax (VAT) and service tax in the syllabus of high school students. Also, the project work for the new session will include obtaining   card and Permanent Account Number (PAN).
 
  Dr  , joint director of secondary education (Agra region), said, “The move is aimed at developing digital and financial temperament among students. Basic information regarding ITR, GST, VAT, Aadhaar and PAN are included in the course. Students will learn about these from the 2017-18 session. Understanding these topics will improve the personality of students.”
