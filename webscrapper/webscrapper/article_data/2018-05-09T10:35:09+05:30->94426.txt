SAKHARAYAPATNA (CHIKKAMAGALURU): Election flying squad officers stopped two “pilgrimage” buses near Sakharayapatna town on Monday and got 120 “sponsored” devotees to return home.

 Official sources said the passengers in the buses, one of them KSRTC, said their trip was paid for and they were being taken to temples in Dakshina Kannada. Most of them were from Kethamaranahalli and Gollarahatti villages of Kadur taluk. The squad directed   staff to refund the ticket amount and allowed the buses to go after offloading the ‘devotees’.
