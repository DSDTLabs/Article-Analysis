ERODE: A 45-year-old farmer today committed suicide by hanging himself at his house near here allegedly after being pressured by banks to repay his dues.

 Police, however, said the real cause of Palanisamy taking his life was under investigation.
 
 The farmer's wife and relatives alleged he took the extreme step as he was frustrated with his sugarcane and turmeric crops withering for want of water and bankers demanding that he repay his dues.
 
 A case has been registered, police said.
 
 Tight vigil is being maintained in the area, they added.
