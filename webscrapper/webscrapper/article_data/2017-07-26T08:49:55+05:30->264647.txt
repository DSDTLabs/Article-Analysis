AHMEDABAD/PALANPUR: Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Tuesday announced Rs 500 crore intermediate relief package for flood-affected districts of Gujarat. He also declared ex-gratia of Rs 2 lakh to the next of kin of the deceased, and Rs 50,000 to those seriously injured in the floods due to incessant rains since Friday last. 

 Modi rushed to his home state on Tuesday and conducted an aerial survey of flood-ravaged areas of north Gujarat where the authorities have shifted more than 36,000 people to safety and rescued more than 1,600 with the help of Indian Army, Indian Air Force and the NDRF personnel since Monday. The toll due to rain related incident touched 83 with at least nine deaths reported in last 36 hours ending 8pm Tuesday.
