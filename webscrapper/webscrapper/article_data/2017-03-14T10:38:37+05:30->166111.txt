 The Bharati Vidyapeeth police have identified the deceased as Choundappa Kumbhar (26) and Vijaykumar Atani (25), both residents of Vagaon Maval. The condition of one of the three injured is critical. He is undergoing treatment at a private hospital, while two are being treated at the Sassooon General Hospital, police said.
 
 The police said that the accident occurred around 4.30 am on Sunday. All of them were on their way to Satara from Pune. After crossing the viaduct, the driver lost control over his vehicle and crashed into the median. “The speed of the car was such that it tipped over, killing two on the spot and injuring three,” said an officer from the Bharati Vidyapeeth Police Station.
 
 The police and the local people rushed the injured to the hospital. “We have registered a case of accidental death,” the officer said.
