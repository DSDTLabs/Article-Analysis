Bathinda: The state vigilance bureau on Monday nabbed assistant sub-inspector (ASI) Tarlok Chand while accepting bribe of Rs 5,000. A vigilance official said that the ASI, posted at police station Kabarwala in Mukatsar, was nabbed on the complaint of Jaspal Singh of village Pakki Tibbi in Mukatsar.  The complainant has approached the VB and alleged that the accused ASI was demanding Rs.5,000 to help him in a complaint case. After verifying information, the vigilance team laid a trap and accused ASI was arrested on the spot taking bribe of Rs 5,000 in the presence of two official witnesses. tnn
