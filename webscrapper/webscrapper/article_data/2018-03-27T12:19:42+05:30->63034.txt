TV actor  , who plays Kanhaiya on "Kya Hal, Mister Panchal", wants more people to adopt stray animals.

 "I think more people need to adopt strays and have them sterilised. It will curb an increase in the population of strays.
 
 "Just adopt a pet, it will remain your biggest fan for the rest of its life. I've found my stress buster on the set and will also give a name to him to call it mine," Maninder said in a statement.
