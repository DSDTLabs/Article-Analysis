COIMBATORE: In a suspected case of revenge killing, a 24-year-old man was bludgeoned to death by a ragpicker near Flower Market here on Thursday morning.

 The deceased, N   alias Vignesh, of   at  , was also a ragpicker. About 10 days ago,   had assaulted the accused, Mani, 54, of  , in a public place.
 
 On Wednesday night, the duo consumed liquor together and picked up a fight. Mani, who held a grudge against Vignesh, took a stone and thrashed the latter, said the RS Puram police.
 
 Passersby noticed the incident and informed the police. Vignesh, who sustained head injuries in the fight, was taken to the Coimbatore Medical College and Hospital. Without responding to treatment, Vignesh died on Thursday morning.
 
 The preliminary probe revealed that Vignesh was a history-sheeter. After coming out in bail a few months ago, he started picking rags.
 
 The police have registered a case against Mani under Section 302 (murder) of the Indian Penal Code and arrested him. Further investigation is on.
