Jaisalmer: The security of Thar Express plying between India and Pakistan via Munabao Khokhrapar has been tightened in the wake of threats to Jodhpur rail division. Additional security personnel with weapons have been deployed at Bhagat Ki Kothi and Munabao railway station. 22 CCTV cameras have been installed at Munabao station. Besides, vigil has been stepped up at Jodhpur, Barmer, Jaisalmer and other stations of Jodhpur rail division. RPF senior commandant Rajnish Tripathi said after threats to Jodhpur rail division, security has been tightened at important railway stations and crowded areas. 

 Recently, ATS and RPF conducted a joint security drill.
