PUNE: Senior Bharatiya Janata Party leader and former chairman of the state legislative council N S   passed away at a private hospital here on Tuesday morning. He was 78. 

 “Pharande suffered intracranial haemorrhage, a bleeding in the skull, which caused paralysis on January 4. He was admitted to our hospital,” said Shreerang  , medical superintendent,   Memorial Hospital, Ghole Road.
 
 Pharande was a patient of hypertension. Later, he suffered a cardiac arrest at the hospital and died around 8.20am on Tuesday, Patwardhan said. tnn
