PANCHKULA: Roads in   are in dilapidated condition and people are facing harassment due to the bad condition of the roads.

 As per the local residents, driving on such roads has become a nightmare for them. They said these roads need repair on a priority basis.
 
 Neeraj Goyal, a resident of Zirakpur, said, “It is really difficult for me to drive on such road. It would damage the suspension of my car. MC officials are not at all taking the matter seriously and are not providing the required facilities to the residents.”
 
 Residents also alleged these potholes become invisible during rain and are dangerous for the lives of the people.
 
 Another resident  , said, “Now the rainy season has gone. And we see no reason to delay the repair work more. The administration should at least start the recarpetting work now.”
