MYSURU:   Mysore Midtown president Harish said that the club is conducting ‘Easy Biz’ a summit for ESI, EPF and labour law on February 14.

 Speaking to reporters here on Wednesday, Harish said that in today’s dynamic business scenario, compliance is a key factor for any organization’s success. 
 
 Though compliances are mandatory, it is also a booster for the well-being of every organization. It is important to know and update with the latest. This event is designed for knowledge sharing by eminent, experienced speakers.
 
 The chief guests will be CII chairman Muthukumaran, EPF commissioner  , ESI commissioner   and assistant labour commissioner  , who will update about the latest change in policies and will answer the queries of the participants from different business establishments, he said.
