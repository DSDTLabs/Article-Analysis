BATHINDA: Taking a serious note of protesting farmers disrupting vehicular traffic on Wednesday afternoon,   police have registered two cases against 325 persons. Bathinda senior superintendent of police   said a case has been registered at   police station against 200 farmers under Prevention of Damage to Public Property Act, National Highway Act and under sections 283 (damage or obstruction in public way), 147 (rioting), 431 (mischief by injury to public road) of the IPC. Another case has been registered at   police station against 125 farmers, he said.
