NEW DELHI: Admission for foreign students in  's undergraduate, postgraduate, M.Phil and Ph. D. programmes will start from February 15, the varsity announced on Thursday.

 "The   is committed to providing world class education to all Indian as well as foreign students. In this pursuit, the University of Delhi will invite the applications from foreign nationals from February 15 for Ph.D., M.Phil. postgraduate and undergraduate programmes for academic session 2018-19," DU said in a notice. 
 
 The students can apply for the programmes through the website of Foreign Students' Registrar office.
 
 The university offers more than 250 programmes at various levels. There is no entrance test for admission to these programmes for foreign students.
