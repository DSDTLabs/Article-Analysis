The orphans of   were in for a surprise, when the authorities decided to organise a car rally for them, followed by many other exciting activities. It was a rare sight for the organisers since the children had a great time being driven around in the the luxurious   cars, thus making it a very memorable experience for them. Besides the joy rides that were on offer, the foundation had also organised a painting competition followed by some music and dance. The food boxes and the gifts towards the end of the event served as the icing on the cake.
