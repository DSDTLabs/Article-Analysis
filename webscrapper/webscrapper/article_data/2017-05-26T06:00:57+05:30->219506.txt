AHMEDABAD: A cotton processing unit in   reported a fire on Thursday evening. The firefighting   by Ahmedabad   (AFES) continued till late in the night.

 AFES officials said GD Powder, a unit near Gujarat Estate in Vatva GIDC reported a fire at 6.44pm. “Four vehicles rushed to the spot. The cause and scope of damage could not be ascertained as the operation is on,” said an official. AFES officials said that on Thursday they received five calls for fires. A cloth warehouse in Amraiwadi reported a fire early in the morning, and a company in Kathwada GIDC reported a recurrence of fire.
