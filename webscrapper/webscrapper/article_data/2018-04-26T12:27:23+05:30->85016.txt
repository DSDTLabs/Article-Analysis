 There's an artist in every child and children should be encouraged to express their creativity. If you are interested in enjoying artworks by children then you must visit ‘Ojas — 13th Child Art   2018’. Around 24 kids have prepared 96 paintings on a range of topics ranging from landscapes, trees, people, sunrise, birds and more. Kids of age 9-16 years have prepared the artworks in past 12 months on the theme given to them. The exhibition will be inaugurated by   director Pradyumna Vyas.
