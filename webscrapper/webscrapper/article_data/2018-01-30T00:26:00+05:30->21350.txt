Coimbatore: The fourth additional district judge   here on Monday sentenced a 55-year-old man to life imprisonment for murdering his maternal uncle over a petty dispute four years ago.

 The  , Sivakumar, was a construction worker. “He was working with his uncle Natarajan, a construction worker from Kallipalayam near Kovilpalayam on the outskirts of the city. On November 16, 2014, when Sivakumar, a drunkard, demanded money from Natarajan to buy liquor, he refused to give the money. In a fit of rage, Sivakumar hit his maternal uncle with a boulder. Natarajan suffered severe head injuries and died on the spot,” a police officer said.
 
 Kovilpalayam police registered a case and arrested Sivakumar, who was later remanded in judicial custody and lodged at the Coimbatore Central Prison. TNN
