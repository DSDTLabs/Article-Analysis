PATNA: Unable to cope with huge rush of passengers during festival period,   has decided to run one more biweekly special   between   and Anand Vihar Delhi from September 23 to October 30. Railways is already running 17 pairs of puja special trains from different stations under   (ECR).

 According to ECR CPRO Rajesh Kumar, the special train (04003/04004) will leave Anand Vihar every Tuesday and Saturday at 6.35pm and reach Patna Junction at 10.55am next day. On its return journey, the train will leave Patna Junction every Wednesday and Sunday at 1.05pm and reach Anand Vihar at 5.10am next day. It has been given stoppages at Danapur, Ara, Buxar, Mughalsarai, Allahabad and Kanpur and will run with the load combination of 20 coaches, which included five of 3AC, nine sleeper class, four general and two brake vans, he said.
 
 Similarly, railways has started a weekly special train between Bhagalpur and Anand Vihar via Patna Junction from Friday. The train (04001/04002) runs every Friday from Bhagalpur at 3pm and reach Anand Vihar at 9.30am next day. On its return journey, it leaves Anand Vihar every Thursday at 4.55pm and reach Bhagalpur at 10.45am next day, the CPRO said, adding that it has stoppages at Kiul, Mokama, Rajendra Nagar Terminal, Patna Junction, Danapur, Ara, Buxar, Mughalsarai, Allahabad and Kanpur.
 
 Besides, railways has attached one more 2AC coach to   (12393/12394) from Friday on permanent basis to provide more accommodation to passengers. Now, it is running with 22 coaches, including one 1AC, two 2AC, three 3AC, nine sleeper, four general, one pantry car and two brake vans. Railways has also decided to run a weekly special train (04009/04010) between Jogbani and Anand Vihar from September 24 to October 29.
 
 While the special train will leave Jogbani every Tuesday at 9am and reach Anand Vihar at 3.40pm next day, it will leave Anand Vihar every Sunday at 11.45pm and reach Jogbani via Gorakhpur, Siwan, Hajipur, Khagaria and Purnia, sources said. Railways will also run another special train between Howrah and Anand Vihar via Dhanbad-Gaya-Mughalsarai from September 23 to clear passenger rush during festive period.
