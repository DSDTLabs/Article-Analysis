BATHINDA: In a different kind of protest, contractual employees here on Wednesday used an anniversary   to remind   chief minister   of his promise on contractual recruitment. The contractual employees cut the cake with a tweet of   printed on it, which he had made on January 24, 2017, claiming that no contractual recruitment would be made in Punjab and services of contractual employees would be regularised. 

 Later, the employees got together and held a protest.They reminded Amarinder that a year had passed but he had miserably failed in keeping his word. Contractual employees union activist   said, “We had high hopes from Amarinder Singh, but he miserably failed in keeping his word. We are pained by the fact that he forgot the promises made with the people and used this model to make him remember his promise.”
