The Metro route from Moshi to Hinjewadi will bisect the Pimpri-Swargate Metro rail project. 

 “People from Moshi, Bhosari, Nashik Phata, Kasarwadi, Pimple Gurav, Pimple Saudagar and Wakad will reach Hinjewadi IT Park and vice versa,” Jagtap said. MahaMetro and Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation (PCMC) could prepare the DPR, he added. 
 
 Last week, the state government approved the Metro rail project from Hinjewadi to Shivajinagar. 
 
 The Pune Metropolitan Regional Development Authority had earlier submitted the proposal to the state government. There will be a network of Metro rail routes from Pimpri to Swargate (16.5km), Vanaz to Ramwadi (14.6km), Moshi to Hinjewadi (16km), and Hinjewadi to Shivajinagar (23.3km), Jagtap said. 
 
 Landge earlier urged chief minister Devendra Fadnavis for extending the Metro route from Pimpri to Nigdi. The parliamentarian from Maval, Shrirang Barne, met Union urban affairs minister Hardeep Singh Puri and submitted a memorandum for the extension of the route from Pimpri to Nigdi. 
 
 There has been an increasing demand from citizens, social welfare organizations and political leaders for extension of the route from Pimpri to Nigdi. The length of the route is 4.5km. 
 
 “Not all commuters will benefit from the route from Pimpri to Swargate,” the Bhosari legislator added.
 
 MahaMetro officials claimed that the route will be extended to Nigdi in the second phase and discussions will be soon held with the Union and state governments.
