 Governor PB Acharya administered the oath of office to Kikon at Raj Bhavan here. Kikon will administer the oath to newly-elected legislators on the opening day of the assembly session on Tuesday.
 
 As pro tem speaker, Kikon will be chairing the first day of the new assembly on March 13, with the members of the NDPP-led People's Democratic Alliance on the treasury bench and Naga People's Front legislators on the opposition bench.
