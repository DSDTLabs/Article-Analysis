LUCKNOW: The row over eviction of former UP chief ministers from their government bungalows as per the   order took a curious turn on Friday when BSP chief   sent a representation to chief minister   claiming the 13-A Mall Avenue, for which she was served notice, was not her residence but a memorial named after party founder   Ram.

 
 Mayawati said the house allotted to her in the capacity of ex-UP CM was at 6, Lal  , allotted when she was still CM in 2011 and that she was ready to vacate the residence.
