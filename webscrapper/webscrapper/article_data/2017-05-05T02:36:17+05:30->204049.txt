GURUGRAM: A 28-year-old advocate was allegedly beaten up by a two persons near Park Hospital in Sector 47 on Wednesday afternoon.

 Accoring to police, Amit Chahal,   of sub-divisional magistrate ( ) deployed in Palwal district, was heading towards   road in his Ford Fiesta when two persons in a speeding Scorpio tried to   him. The duo then halted their car in front of him and started hurling abuses at him. When he opposed, they beat him up and threatened to kill him. He suffered minor injuries.
 
 Once they fled from the spot, Chahal, who lives in South city 2, informed the police. 
 
 An FIR has been lodged against the two unknown persons under sections 279 (rash driving), 336 (endangering lives of others), 341 (wrongful restraint), 323 (causing hurt), 506 (criminal intimidation) and 34 (common intention) of IPC at Sadar police station.
 
 “The victim has noted down the registration number of the Scorpio. We have been able to identify the culprits, both are residents of village Jharsa,” said inspector Vijay Kumar, SHO, Sadar police station.
