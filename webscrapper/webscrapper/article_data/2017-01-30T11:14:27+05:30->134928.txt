DWARAKA TIRUMALA: Alleging that Andhra Pradesh secured the number one spot in corruption in the country, YSRC chief YS Jagan charged chief minister   with dishonesty and corruption. 

 Former minister  's son Sridhar joined YSRC in the presence of Jagan here on Sunday. Addressing the public meeting, Jagan said no chief minister in the country is as corrupt as Naidu. He alleged that Naidu was the first chief minister to be caught on camera, offering bribes. Instead of quitting, Naidu shamelessly hung on to the post, said Jagan. He alleged the CM and his aides were encroaching lands including those belonging to temples.
