For the year 2018, the centre announced a total of 90 Bank holidays, including all Sundays, government holidays, second and Fourth Saturday that are counted among the usual bank holidays. Out of these 90 Bank Holidays, 89 are the regular public holidays, in addition to this year, the state government of Delhi declared January 5 as a government holiday on the occasion of the 350th birth anniversary of Guru Gobind Singh i.e Gurupurab.  As per the Gregorian and Julian calendars, March is the third month of the year. Moreover, it is the month of the Financial Year ending. Every financial year starts on 01 April and ends on 31 March every year. Banks work on tight schedules to close the books of accounts during March.
