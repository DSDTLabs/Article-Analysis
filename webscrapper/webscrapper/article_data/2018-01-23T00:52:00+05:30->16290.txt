GURUGRAM: A delegation of   met chief minister   on Monday and asked him to restore the ground of the club, situated in Sector 29, to its original position.

 The delegation informed Khattar that a private tent house is charging Rs 9 lakh for per booking from non-members of club, which is again against Huda's policy. Two   officers were found to be indulging in irregularities, illegalities and embezzlements of funds in process of leasing out club ground.
 
 The club members informed that on September 29, 2017, these two officials signed a lease agreement for nine years without floating a tender, e-tender or adopting any other transparent policy. They did not even issue advertisement in any newspaper for the purpose.
 
 Chief minister assured to look into the matter of leasing out the ground.
