The Indian National Congress party will contest Uttar Pradesh Assembly elections in alliance with Samajwadi Party. As per the sear agreement between both parties, Congress will contest on 105 seats out of 403 seats.  The assembly elections in UP will be held in seven phase from February 11, 2017 to March 08, 2017. The results will be announced on March 11, 2017.
