THIRUVANANTHAPURAM: During the review meeting of preparations of   temple  , devaswom minister Kadakampally Surendran said green protocol would be strictly followed during the festival and the   ritual. The festival begins on April 1 and will conclude with the pongala on April 7.

 The city corporation will deploy special squad to enforce the green protocol at festival venue. Maintenance work at Vivekandan Nagar Road and spots in the city where the pongala is offered will be completed soon and installation works of streetlights in the area has been progressing, observed the meeting.
 
 Six KSRTC buses will operate special services on festival days. Medical assistance will be provided by a team from health department . Also, trains will have temporary stoppages at Kochuveli on April 6 and 7.
