VIJAYAWADA: Vigilance and enforcement sleuths conducted simultaneous raids on social welfare hostels in Krishna district on Thursday. As many as 10 hostels were raided by the sleuths after they received complaints about misappropriation of funds. 

 Five teams were formed with 15 officials from vigilance wing and as many from the revenue department. Social welfare girls' hostels at Gannavaram, Nunna, Agiripalle and Nuzvid as well as boys' hostels at Nandivada, Moturu, Avanigadda, Movva,   and   were raided.
 
 The vigilance officials found serious discrepancies in   at all social welfare hostels. Vigilance DSP of Krishna district, R  , said, "We have found that physical strength of the students is less than that shown in the registers. At Kanchikacherla, almost 50% students shown in the register were not present."
 
 The situation was the same in other hostels. The number of students were less by 20% to 40%. The hostel wardens claimed that some students went home due to the the bandh and that a holiday was declared for schools.
 
 Vijay Paul said the police had seized all attendance registers.
