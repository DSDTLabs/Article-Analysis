Kannada actor   lodged a complaint with cyber police at the city police commissioner's office, requesting action against people uploading morphed photos of hers and other actors on the internet and social media platforms.

 Sruthi met city police commissioner   on Friday and filed the complaint. She said that morphed images of her had been doing the rounds for a while. DCP (crime)  , while confirming the allegations, said some people had created fake   accounts in Sruthi's name and published inappropriate images. 
 
 Police registered a case under the Information Technology Act, 2000, and began an investigation.
