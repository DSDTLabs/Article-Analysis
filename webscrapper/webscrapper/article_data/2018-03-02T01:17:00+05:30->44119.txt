Madurai: Railways has announced 52 summer specials to clear the extra rush. The special trains will be operated from Tirunelveli and Sengottai to Coimbatore.

 According to a statement, train no. 06019 Tirunelveli – Coimbatore special fare special will leave Tirunelveli at 6.20pm on all Sundays from April 8 to July 1 and reach Coimbatore at 4.30am the next day. Train no. 06020 Coimbatore – Tirunelveli special fare special will leave Coimbatore at 11.50pm on all Wednesdays from April 11 to July 4 and reach Tirunelveli at 8.35am the next day.
 
 They will stop at Kovilpatti, Sattur, Virudhunagar, Madurai, Dindigul, Oddanchatram, Palani, Udumalaipettai, Pollachi, Kinattukkadavu and Podhanur.
 
 Train no. 06021 Coimbatore – Sengottai special fare special will leave Coimbatore at 11.50pm from April 9 to July 2 on all Mondays and reach Sengottai at 9.20am the next day. Train Number 06022 Sengottai – Coimbatore special fare special train will leave Sengottai at 5 pm from April 10 to July 3 on Tuesdays and reach Coimbatore at 4.30 am the next day. 
 
 They will stop at Podhanur, Kinattukkadavu, Pollachi, Udumalaipettai, Palani, Oddanchatram, Dindigul, Madurai, Virudhunagar, Tiruttangal, Sivakasi, Srivilliputtur, Rajapalayam, Sankarankovil, Pambakovil Shandy, Kadayanallur and Tenkasi. 
 
 All these trains will have a composition of 1 AC 3-tier, 6 sleeper class, 6 general second class and 2 luggage-cum-brake van coaches.
 
 Advance reservation for these trains will begin from 8am on Friday
 
 As on March 1, Madurai division has announced 207 summer special trains, the statement said.
