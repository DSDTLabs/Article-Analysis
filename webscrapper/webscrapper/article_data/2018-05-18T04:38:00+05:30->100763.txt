Ahmedabad: At least 10 students of the Ahmedabad chapter of the Institute of Cost Accountants of India (ICAI) have been selected by companies at campus placements for ICAI institutes held in Mumbai. Ahmedabad boy Sagar Somjiyani has received a job offer with a package of Rs 18 lakh per annum.

 Somjiyani was selected by ONGC at the campus   in Mumbai. According to an ICAI release, the institute organizes campus placement for its fresh graduates every six months at four metros across India. The Ahmedabad chapter also organizes campus placements every six months. At the recent campus recruitment at Mumbai, the average salary offered freshers by companies was around Rs 7 lakhs per annum. tnn
