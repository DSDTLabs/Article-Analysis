Panaji: Former chief minister Laxmikant Parsekar on Monday filed his written reply before the Goa Lokayuka over the allegedly illegal renewal of 88 mining leases. Parsekar, who filed it through his counsel, said that he would object to the allegations and that his predecessor, chief minister Manohar Parrikar, had framed the policy.  Meanwhile, director of mines Prasanna Acharya filed his response in person.  Goa Lokayukta justice P K Mishra had issued notices to Parsekar, former mines secretary P K Sain, and director of mines Acharya, for the renewal of the 88 mining leases.
