Tirupur: A 34-year-old police constable committed suicide due to a family dispute at Samundipuram in the city on Sunday. M Sathish Kumar of Samundipuram was associated with the city police’s prohibition enforcement wing. A native of Mettur in Salem district, Sathish Kumar was married to Vishnudevi seven years ago, said the police. The couple had quarrels often. On Saturday, an argument broke out between the couple on visiting Sathish’s parents’ house in Mettur for Pongal. Disappointed Sathish went inside a room in his house and committed suicide by hanging, said the police. The 2008-batch policeman was survived by his wife, six-year-old daughter and two-and-a-half-year-old son. The 15 Velampalayam police have registered a case.
