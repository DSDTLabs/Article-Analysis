Surat: A 40-year-old man was booked for allegedly sodomizing a 15-year-old boy in Varachha on Tuesday. 

 Rana Sankhat, a resident of Kamal Park in Varachha, was booked for allegedly sodomizing the minor. The accused was booked under various Sections of the Indian Penal Code including 377 (unnatural offences), Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act (Pocso) and Prevention of Atrocities Act. The minor survivor belongs to scheduled caste. The accused enticed the minor with money and took him to a secluded place on Saturday and sodomized him. Sankhat threatened to kill the minor if he complained to anyone. Under shock, the minor could not discuss the incident with anyone but due to injury on his private parts he told his family members after which he was taken to police.
 
 The boy was later shifted to hospital for treatment. A medical examination confirmed that he was sodomized, police said.
