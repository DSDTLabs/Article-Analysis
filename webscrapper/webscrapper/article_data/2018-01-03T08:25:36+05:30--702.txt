 Pathela is former senior vice-president of the Muktsar MC and father of BJP district unit chief Rajesh Pathela. Satpal and Sukhdev had an argument during a meeting of councillors on Tuesday when Sukhdev accused Satpal of making casteist remarks against him and threatening him with dire consequences. 
 
 Sukhdev, who won elections from ward number 23 as an independent candidate and later joined the SAD, said, “A meeting was convened on Tuesday to clear encroachments in town. Satpal got angry at something and started abusing me, using casteist words and threatened me. Therefore, I lodged a complaint with the police.”
 
 Pathela denied the charges and said he never used casteist words or threatened Sukhdev. Muktsar City station house officer (SHO) Tejinderpal Singh said as per the complaint filed by councillor Sukhdev, a case under SC/ST (Prevention of Atrocities) Act and criminal intimidation had been registered against Satpal and matter was being investigated thoroughly.
