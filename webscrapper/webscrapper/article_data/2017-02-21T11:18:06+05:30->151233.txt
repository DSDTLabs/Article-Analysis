VARANASI: Bringing much relief to leaders trying to deal with discontent among the party cadre, sitting MLA from Varanasi South,  , on Monday agreed to campaign for BJP candidates in the upcoming assembly elections. 

 Roy Chaudhury, a seven-time MLA since 1989, was denied ticket this time. He was replaced by a new face Neelkanth Tiwari. Soon after the announcement of Tiwari's candidature, Chaudhary, fondly referred to as 'Dada', expressed his resentment over the party's decision and said he would not campaign for the party. 
 
 Chaudhary inaugurated Tiwari's election office on Monday evening. Later, they along with party workers went to Chowk area for campaigning.
