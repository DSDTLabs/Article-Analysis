HYDERABAD:   (AIU) detectives of the customs department arrested a smuggler at the   (RGIA) on Monday and seized 350 grams of gold from his possession. 

 The smuggler landed at the RGI Airport from Dubai in a Fly Dubai flight (FZ 259). During screening of checked-in baggage, suspicious objects were found concealed. On opening the baggage, the gold with a market value of about 10.22 lakh was discovered. A case was registered under Customs Act. Detectives were questioning the man to find out about organisers of Dubai-based smuggling racket. "The offender was to hand over gold to someone at the airport," an official said.
