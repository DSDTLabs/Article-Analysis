In the recently released film 'C/O Sairabanu' starring   and  , yet another renowned behind-the-camera personality made his acting debut.
 
  Scriptwriter   Puthusery, who has penned films like 'Chamayam,' 'Chaamaram,' 'Malootty' and 'Kathodu Kathoram' played the role of a cartoonist named Kerala Varma in the film. His character is the grandfather of Arundhati, played by actress Niranjana.
  
  Veteran actor Raghavan also makes a comeback as a district judge, in this film.
