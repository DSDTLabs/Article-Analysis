AHMEDABAD: The   has extended the time limit for installing the high security registration plates (HSRP) on the vehicles by one month. Now, the vehicle owners will have to install the HSRP on or before February 15, 2018.

 The decision was taken by a high-level committee after reports of difficulties faced by drivers besides bid to take undue advantage by some unscrupulous elements. The transport commissioner said that a  , consisting of RTOs and dealers, has been set up to install the HSRP. 
 
 The state government has fixed the rates of the installing new HSRP which are Rs 89 for two & three-wheelers and Rs 150 for four-wheelers. Those who will not fix the new HSRPs will face legal action.
