CHANDIGARH: Over a month after bodies of 27 Punjabi youths killed in   town of   were brought back, Punjab government has decided to give jobs to their kin on compassionate grounds apart from the Rs 5-lakh ex gratia. 

 
 At a cabinet meeting chaired by chief minister   on Tuesday, it was decided to provide employment to one dependent family member of each victim as per to the person's educational qualification and government policy. 
 
 Elaborating on the decision after the meeting, finance minister   said the ex gratia had already been disbursed in most cases. He also said that providing such appointment to dependent family members of the victims did not fall within the purview of state policy and it had to be amended.
