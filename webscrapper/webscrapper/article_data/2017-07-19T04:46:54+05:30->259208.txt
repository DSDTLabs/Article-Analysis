 “A list of all the alleged scams with their complete details will then be put before Uttar Pradesh chief minister Yogi Aditya Nath for further action,” he said.
 
 Kumar has launched several investigations including that of land acquisition by the Greater Noida Industrial Development Authority (GNIDA). In June, Kumar had ordered an investigation into land acquisition by the GNIDA in Badalpur and Sadopur villages of GB Nagar after farmers complained that their lands were forcibly acquired by the Authority for benefiting former UP chief minister Mayawati and her relatives including her brother Anand Kumar.
 
 Kumar at the time also directed officials to probe alleged grave irregularities by the GNIDA in declaring abadi land for famers whose land was acquired by the government.
