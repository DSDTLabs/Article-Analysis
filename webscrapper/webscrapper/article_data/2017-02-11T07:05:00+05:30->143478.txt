LUDHIANA: The   police have booked a resident of   for selling off sand seized by the police from illegal miners on Friday.

 The accused has been identified as Sukhdev Singh.
 
 Inspector  ,   at Meharban police station said the after seizing sand from illegal miners the police had dumped sand in a ground. On Thursday, when the police officials went to check they were shocked that there was no sand. In the investigation the police found that the accused has sold it to some people.
 
 A case under sections 379 (theft), 406 (criminal breach of trust) and 201 (Causing disappearance of evidence of offence) of IPC has been registered against Sukhdev. The SHO said the police has launched a manhunt for the arrest of the accused.
