MUMBAI: A collegian (17) has been arrested for allegedly raping his classmate in Vasai. The crime came to light when the minor girl fell sick and her mother took her to a doctor, only to realize that her daughter was more than seven months pregnant. 

 The Class XII student was arrested based on a complaint filed by the girl’s mother. The police said that the boy befriended the girl and the duo would often visit Bhuigaon beach in Vasai.
 
 In another case, a 19-year-old Virar boy was arrested for allegedly raping his six-year-old neighbour. The assault took place on February 9 at the teen’s Arnala home in Virar. The accused in both the cases have been sent to the Bhiwandi Children Home.
