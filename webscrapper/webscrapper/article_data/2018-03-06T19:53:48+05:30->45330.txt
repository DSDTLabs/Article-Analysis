Trade experts are looking to see just how much more this film makes over the weekend and the days ahead.
 
 
 
 The film's story revolves around Titu (Sunny Singh) who is getting married to a girl named Sweety (Nushrat Bharucha) who comes across as a perfect woman, but Titu's friend Sonu (Kartik Aaryan) finds her too good to be true.
