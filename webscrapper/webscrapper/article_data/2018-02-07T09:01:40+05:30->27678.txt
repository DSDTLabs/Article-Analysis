RAJKOT: A 20-year-old   accusing her maternal uncle of repeatedly raping her for two years. She also named her mother, who had allegedly threatened her to keep mum, despite repeated sexual assaults. 

 The woman reached Mahila police station on Monday and lodged a complaint against the two. Both the accused were arrested. The woman alleged that her uncle used to often come at their house drunk and then rape her several times between 2014 and 2016 when she was a minor. Whenever she tried to tell her mother about the trauma, she threatened her.
