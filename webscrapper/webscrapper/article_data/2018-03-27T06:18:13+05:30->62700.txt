A youth in his mid-20s was arrested on Monday for allegedly following chief minister Mamata Banerjee’s convoy. 

 The CM was on her way to the airport when Najmul Alam broke the signal at Chinar Park on New Town Expressway, through which the convoy was passing around 4.35pm. 
 
 He then allegedly followed the convoy till the airport gate on VIP Road. Police chased Alam and arrested him near airport Gate No. 3 on Jessore Road. Cops are trying to find out whether he was following Mamata’s convoy intentionally or trying to escape police for jumping signal.
