 As ‘PadMan’ tackles the issue of female menstrual hygiene, the film has received extra support from the government and also the film industry. A number of top stars including Deepika Padukone, Varun Dhawan,  , Anushka Sharma, Arjun Kapoor, Aditi Rao Hyradi and others, have lent their support to the cause by taking on the 'PadMan Challenge' and posing for a picture with sanitary pads. 
 
 'PadMan' which marks Twinkle Khanna's maiden production venture. tells the story of a real-life superhero Arunachalam Muruganathan, who invented a low-cost sanitary pad manufacturing machine to bring a menstrual hygiene revolution for women. The film also stars Radhika Apte and Sonam Kapoor in pivotal roles.
