TIRUNELVELI: Nanguneri sub division police engaged in special patrolling to check illegal sand   in the district arrested 14 persons on Wednesday and seized two tippers and the sand they had smuggled. The police said that nine persons were arrested after they were caught red handed while mining sand from   at Keezha Devanallur near Kalakkad.

 Two sand tippers and sand were seized from them. In another incident the same police team arrested five persons who were illegally mining sand in the same river bed near Padmaneri. The sand was seized and the gang arrested. They said 14 persons were produced before the court and remanded to judicial custody.
 
 Superintendent of police P Ve   said that such raids will continue regularly.
