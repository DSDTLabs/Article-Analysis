Fear is a negative state of emotions that is caused by the presence, or a mere anticipation of a stimulus which may have the potential to cause harm. This anticipation often creates a certain phobia towards the stimulus. Mahua Chakraborty’s new film is about the unpleasant feelings of fear that we encounter in our day-to-day lives. Amar Bhoy, Mahua’s third film after Glamour and  , is about how the protagonist is able to overcome her fears.
