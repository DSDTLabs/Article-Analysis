MUMBAI: Around 40 people were rescued in  , Andheri, after a fire broke out on the second floor of a seven-storied hotel around 11pm on Friday. The fire was confined to the second floor of Hotel Samraj, said a fireman. 

 Eight fire engines and seven tankers were dispatched to the spot. At the time of going to press, the fire brigade was checking if any more people were trapped on the upper floors. The hotel director,  , said the fire started from the smoking room. “ All the guests were taken to a safe location before the flames spread,” she said.
