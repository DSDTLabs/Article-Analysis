Blissfully untouched by the fast-gathering-steam social and political scene in the city is the party scene. Whether political parties are launched or not, whether people apologise for gaffes made on the stage or not, the party continues.   As we entered this venue, the goings-on of the outside world seemed unreal as we lost ourselves to loud music and happy chatter. All the guests were in a peppy mood, and were laughing and dancing, and some even dared to strike up serious conversations over all this din!
