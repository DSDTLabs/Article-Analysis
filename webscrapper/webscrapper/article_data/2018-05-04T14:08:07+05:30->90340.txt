 As many as 82 water vending machines have been approved for the Samastipur division. Of them, 73 have been installed and the rest is being installed.
 
 Darbhanga, Madhubani, Samastipur, Motihari, Bettiah, Bagaha, Raxaul, Narkatiganj, Sugauli, Chakia, Sitamarhi, Jayanagar, Saharsa and Sakri are the stations of the Samastipur division where the water vending machines are being installed.
 
 At least 35 water vending machines have been allocated to the Sonepur division, of which 18 have been installed. The water vending machines are being installed at Muzaffarpur, Hajipur, Sonepur, Barauni, Begusarai, Khagaria, Mansi, Dalsingsarai and Naugachhia stations in the Sonepur division.
 
 The Mughalsarai division has been allocated 34 water vending machines, of which 31 have been installed at Mughalsarai, Gaya, Sasaram, Dehri, Bhabhua Road and Anugrah Road stations, the CPRO said adding 36 water vending machines were being installed at Dhanbad, Koderma, Daltonganj, Parasnath, Gomoh, Singrauli , Barkakana, Garhwa, Renukut, Chandrapura and Chopan stations under the Dhanbad division.
