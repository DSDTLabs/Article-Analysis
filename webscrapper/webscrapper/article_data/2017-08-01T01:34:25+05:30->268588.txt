NAVI MUMBAI: A   from Dombivli was arrested on Monday for allegedly molesting two children at an academy in Nerul.

 The mother of the children lodged a complaint with the police on Saturday, saying that her 7-year-old son and 10-year-old daughter told her about the chess teacher touching them inappropriately on Friday. 
 
 The Nerul police has registered an FIR against the teacher. The accused has been booked under section 354 (outraging modesty) of the IPC along with the POCSO Act. 
 
 Senior inspector Ashok Rajput said, “The woman said her kids felt offended by the way the chess teacher had touched them. A police team went to Dombivli on Monday to arrest the teacher.”
