CHENNAI: The   government in TN will fall in a day after the HC gives its verdict on the disqualification of (18) MLAs case, DMK working president M K   said on Friday. Elections are round the corner and none can prevent DMK from forming a government, he said.

 At the wedding of a party worker in Chennai, Stalin said given the present political situation, several aspirants are coming to the fore, with an aim to destroy the Dravidian movement in the state. "No force can destroy the Dravidian movement, which is well-etched in the state and has worked for the welfare of the people," Stalin said. "Those, who were out to destroy the Dravidian forces, got destroyed eventually in the state," he added.
