Visakhapatnam: A two-day workshop on basic life support (Cardio Pulmonary Resuscitation) was held at the Divisional Railway Hospital here on Thursday. A group of doctors from various hospitals of Visakhapatnam organised the programme. Dr C Rakesh from KGH, Dr A Mohan from Seven Hills Hospital, Dr K Kiran Kumar from Indus Hospital and Dr M Mahesh Kumar from Railway Hospital participated in this programme which was inaugurated by Chandralekha Mukherjee, Waltair divisional railway manager.
