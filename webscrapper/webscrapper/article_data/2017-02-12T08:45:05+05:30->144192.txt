BATHINDA: Due to an old rivalry pertaining to petty matters, a resident of Bathinda’s   village was done to death on Friday night. Victim, 32-year-old Baljit  , was killed on the   road.

 Victim’s brother Shivraj Singh told the police that Baljit was working at a religious place in the village on the night of the incident when another villager Birbal Singh, along with two unknown persons, called him out and took him along. He alleged that Baljit’s body was found on Saturday morning from   road with injury marks made with sharp-edged weapons.
 
 Investigating officer   said, “Baljit was killed on account of an old rivalry and a murder case has been registered against Birbal and two unknown persons. Efforts are being made to arrest the accused.”
