Panaji: Opposition members in the Goa legislative assembly on Wednesday raised the issue of high   being issued to consumers in South Goa. In response, PWD minister Ramkrishna ‘Sudin’ Dhavalikar assured to constitute a committee to look into the matter.

 Dhavalikar said the committee will have to submit a report on the alleged high water bills within a month. The committee will also look into defective water metres, he said. 
 
 Margao MLA Digambar Kamat pointed out that high water bills are being issued to the residents of Margao. He said that residents of Moti Dongor in Margao have received water bill as high as Rs 20,000 to Rs 40,000. “I want assurance from the minister that he will look into the issue,” he said, demanding that the issue be resolved before Ganesh Chathurti.
 
 Curtorim MLA Aleixo Reginaldo Lourenco voiced concerns that many citizens are unable to pay high water bills and added that many of these people have been reaching out to politicians to pay their bills. “Don’t make the economically backward people beggers,” he added.
