TIRUNELVELI: The district police have arrested 20 people for allegedly torching a government bus at   on Tuesday afternoon. Soon after the bus (route number 45B) reached Vadakku Thalaiyuthu from Tirunelveli Junction, two people ordered the crew and passengers to get down at knifepoint. They then set the bus on fire. 

 The miscreants raised slogans demanding release of historysheeter and Nadar Makkal Sakthi Iyakkam founder Rocket  . The Thalaiyuthu police registered a case against the accused under sections 294(b) (using abusive language) 353 (assault or criminal force to deter public servant from discharge of his duty) and 506 (criminal intimidation) of the Indian Penal Code and 4 of TNPPDL Act.
 
 "After inquiries we arrested two people - J Joseph, 23, and S Anand, 22, of of Pandarakulam for torching a government bus," superintendent of police of Tirunelveli District P Ve Arunsakthikumar said. The police said that they also detained 18 more people as preventive measure under section 7(i)(a) of Criminal Law Amendment Act to prevent further unrest. 
 
 They were produced before the court and 13 of them were remanded to judicial custody. The remaining five men were granted bail. The police said they were on the lookout for more miscreants.
