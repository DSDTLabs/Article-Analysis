GREATER NOIDA: A 24-year-old man was arrested on Wednesday for killing a one-year-old after raping her in Deri Skenar village at   in  , on Wednesday. The accused,   alias Bijju, allegedly dumped the body in a pit near a railway track and fled.

 Police said that according to preliminary investigation, the accused seemed to be mentally unstable.
 
 Anand Dev Mishra, Badalpur SHO, said the accused lived in the neighbourhood and frequently visited the victim’s house. “He had visited the victim’s house in the afternoon and found the girl playing outside the house. He took her away to offer her some goodies. The accused took the child to a deserted location and raped her. He then killed the girl and threw her body into a small pit,” Mishra said. The accused then returned home.
 
 When the girl’s family members could not find her, they launched a search. Some children informed them that they saw Vijay going with the girl towards the field. The family members then informed the police and registered a complaint.
 
 A police team reached the spot and arrested the accused from his house. “The accused first feigned ignorance about the matter but soon confessed to having committed the crime. Based on his interrogation details, we conducted a search and recovered the body from the pit,” a police officer said.
 
 The police said the wife of the accused had allegedly deserted him because of his mental condition last year. “We will get his mental health checked at a government hospital,” the police said.
 
 The body has been sent for a postmortem. A case has been registered under sections 201 (disappearance of evidence), 302 (murder) and 376 (rape) of the IPC and under the POCSO Act against the accused.
