Ambernath: Acting on a complaint from an NGO, Hirali Foundation, the Shivaji Nagar police have filed case against Shiv Sena leader Sahebrao Ahire for violating sound pollution norms during   Art Festival recently held in Ambernath.

 While the three-day festival was organised by Shiv   MP Shreekant Shinde and all banners and invitations cards had his names but case was registered against Ahire who is ward president of party and had taken police permission on his name.
 
 Sarita Khatchandani of Hirali Foundation said, “The event violated decimal limits of sound pollution and on May 5. The music went on till 10.30 pm violating the court norms of 10 pm timing.”
