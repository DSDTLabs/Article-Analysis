GURUGRAM: Sanitation workers continued their protest for the second consecutive day on Friday, demanding  , fixed minimum wages, safety gear and compensation in case of any accident on duty. Around 300 workers, who gathered in front of the old   office, also demanded better working conditions, regular medical check-ups,   and   benefits for all sanitation workers.

 Mayor Madhu Azad said, “We have forwarded their demands to the state government.”
