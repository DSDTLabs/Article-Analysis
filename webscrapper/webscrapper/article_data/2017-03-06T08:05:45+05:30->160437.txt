NEW DELHI: A 17-year-old boy was crushed to death after a speeding Mercedes car rammed into his scooter near at   in west Delhi.

 Police say that the incident happened in the wee hours of Monday when the speeding Mercedes car lost control near the gates of an apartment at the G block and hit the scooter from behind, the youth riding the scooter was flung into the air due to the impact and landed on his head.
 
 The car driver then left the vehicle at the spot and fled. Police say efforts are being made to trace him down. The scooterist was taken to a local hospital by the security guards of the apartment where he was declared dead. 
 
 Police have registered a case of causing death due to negligence.
