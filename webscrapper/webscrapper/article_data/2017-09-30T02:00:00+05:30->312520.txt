To take the seats of choice, most guests reached the venue before the given time. Making most of the extra time at hand, there was a chai pe charcha over politics and other issues.
 Soon, chief guest  , minister of Tourism UP, lit the inaugural lamp after which two short films by   were screened. While the first film took the audience into the bylanes of Varanasi showcasing and highlighting the zardozi and   textiles, the second film was about Lucknow’s famous chikankari. 
 The screening was preceded by a panel discussion over the plight of artisans involved in the two crafts by speakers which included Zohra Chatterjee, Awanish Awasthi, Bhuwanesh Kumar and Muzaffar Ali. Also present on the occasion was Prabhas, a master weaver, who came all the way from Varanasi to explain the challenges faced by all craftsmen.
