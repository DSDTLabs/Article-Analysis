 The film's jukebox is here, and it has five songs composed by Prashant Pillai. The songs are penned by Shabareesh Varma, Anwar Ali, Sooraj Kurup and Sidhartha Siva. 
 
 The film is hitting screens on April 14. Gayathri Suresh, Aparna Gopinath and Althaf also play important roles in the movie.
