 "The children were rushed to the hospital in ambulances and other vehicles. However, they had sustained severe head injuries as the trolley had turned turtle and come down on top of them. They died while on their way to the hospital," said Hari Lal, head of Lahsoda police check post, to TOI over the phone.
 
 Kastur Verma, station house officer (SHO) of Rawanjana Dungar police station, said that about 25 people were injured in the accident, including a dozen women.
 
 "The trolley was fixed to the tractor. Near a steep turn in Lahsoda, the tractor overturned as it was being driven at a high speed. The victims were all residents of nearby villages in Sawai Madhopur district and were on their way to Khanpur to attend a mass wedding which was organised by the Bairwa community," he said.
 
 Police said that parents of Priyanka, Bhavna and Manish had sustained serious injuries and were currently undergoing treatment at a district hospital. The injured also include a groom. 
 
 "We will register a case against the driver under Section 304 A (causing death due to negligence) of the IPC. The statements of the injured have also been recorded," Verma told TOI.
 
 Meanwhile,chief minister Vasundhara Raje said on Twitter that she has instructed the district administration to provide   and all possible aid to victims and their families.
 
 Later, district collector K C Verma announced a compensation of Rs 50,000 to the family members of victims. 
 
 Former chief minister and senior Congress leader Ashok Gehlot also expressed his condolences on the social media.
