MADURAI: Alleging misappropriation of funds running into several lakhs by panchayat officials, a group of youths from a village panchayat petitioned the Madurai district collector on Monday. 

 They wanted him to   the accounts for the last two years and take appropriate action against the panchayat officials, if found guilty.
 
 District collector  , who received the petition assured the youths to conduct an audit. About 70 youths from   panchayat in   panchayat union came together to fight against corruption and to create awareness among villagers about their rights.
 
 They claimed that lakhs of money from the village panchayat were spent in the last couple of years without proper bills. This came to light when the youths took part in the gram sabha meeting held last week.
