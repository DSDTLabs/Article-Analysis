CNEW DELHI: The Goa Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education (GBSHSE) declared the SSC/Class 10 result today, May 25, 2018, at around 12pm. 

 The students who appeared for the Goa Board SSC / Class 10 exam 2018 in the month of April can access their results on the official website of the board i.e. gbshse.gov.in.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
  
  
 This year, the GBSHSE Board had conducted the SSC (Class 10) examinations from April 2 to April 21, 2018.

 The results of the GBSHSE Class 12 were announced on April 28 at 10 am in which 85.53 per cent students cleared the examinations. 
 
 If you are one of the students eagerly waiting to check your   2018, you can check it on the official website of the GBSHSE Board - gbshse.gov.in. 
 
 
 
 
 1) Keep handy your GBSHSE Class 10 admit card or your Roll No.
 
 2) Open the official website of the GBSHSE Board - gbshse.gov.in
 
 3) On the homepage of the Board, you will see a link - "GBSHSE Board Class 10 results", click on it
 
 4) It will redirect you to a new page where you need to enter your GBSHSE Board Class 10 roll no. and other required details
 
 5) Once you click submit, your GBSHSE Board Class 10 result will be visible on the screen
 
 6) You can download your GBSHSE Board Class 10 result and print it for further reference
