POINGUINIM: Goa police have arrested three security guards for molesting and raping a six-year-old girl over a period of four months at three different locations, police said. 

 The child, who lost her mother three months ago, was living with her father at a construction site at Nagorcem,  , where the accused — Ravi Ree, 25, Jaydeep Ree, 24, both from Assam, and Manoj Kumar, 20, from Madya Pradesh — were also working. The child’s family hails from Uttar Paradesh, but has been living in Goa for many years.
 
 Police said the guards, in turn, would lure the school-going child with sweets when her father was busy with masonry work, and take her by motorcycle to various locations where they would   her.
 
 On Friday night, the minor complained of pain to her brother who informed the father who in turn registered a complaint with police. 
 
 Police have registered the case under Goa Children’s Act after recording the survivor’s statement in the presence of a voluntary organisation. Based on the statement, police have registered an offence under sections 365 (kidnapping), 354 ( ) and 376 (rape) of the Indian Penal Code and Section 4 of Protection of Children Against Sexual Offences Act (POSCO). The minor was sent for a medical examination and the report is awaited, police said.
 
 The accused were produced before judicial magistrate first class Canacona, who remanded them to police custody.
 
 PSI Prashal Desai is investigating the case under PI Rajendra Prabhudesai.
