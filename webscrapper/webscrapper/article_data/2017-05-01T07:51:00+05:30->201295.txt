GHAZIABAD: Cash worth Rs 3,50,000 and jewellery items worth Rs 2,50,000 were allegedly looted from a   in Sanjay Nagar on Sunday. The house belongs to one Tejpal Sharma, who was away with his family to attend a wedding in Raj Nagar when the   was carried out.

 When the family returned to their home in Sector 3’s F-block around 3am on Sunday, they discovered the locks on the door were broken and the jewellery kept in a bag in a wardrobe was missing.
 
 The cash kept in a metal cupboard was also missing. “They returned home and found that the house was ransacked,” said Hemant Kumar Rai, SHO, Kavinagar police station.
