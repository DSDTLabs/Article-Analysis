CHENNAI: The ministry of human resource development will set up a committee to review the progress of research parks being established in IITs across the country, said   minister   on Thursday.

 In his address at the inauguration of conference halls at IIT-M Research Park, Javadekar said the ministry had provided funds to set up research parks in IITs including in Mumbai, Delhi, Hyderabad and Kanpur. "We will make a concerted effort to see that   research park model is replicated across the country," he said.
