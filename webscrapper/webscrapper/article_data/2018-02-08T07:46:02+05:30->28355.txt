CHANDIGARH: A   court, on Wednesday, sentenced a man to 10 years of rigorous imprisonment after holding him guilty of kidnapping and raping a Class VII student. 

 The court also imposed a fine of Rs 20,000 on the convict,  .
 
 The decision has come within 11 months of the case trial. Vikas had kidnapped the girl while she was on way home in Panipat in March 2017. He took her to a secluded place and raped her. After taking her back to her village, he threatened her against speaking about the incident. After the girl apprised her family of it, they lodged a complaint, stated the FIR.
