Dhanush took everyone by a sweet surprise when he donned the hat of a director for his upcoming movie, ‘Power Pandi’. The highlight is that he is also playing a prominent role in the film along with Rajkiran, who is the lead. The actor has revealed details of his maiden directorial venture on social networking site.
 
 
 
Taking to Twitter, Dhanush said that he has wrapped up shooting for ‘Power Pandi’ and thanked the people for encouraging him,” And that's a wrap for #powerpaandi .. so thankful and happy to be able to live my dream. Thank u all for all d encouragement. Spread love.” 
 
Fans are now eagerly waiting for the teaser and trailer of the film. Other than directing and acting, he has also contributed to singing and lyric writing of the film’s album.
