AGRA Four people including two children were killed and 20 others injured in road accident on GT road near village   on Saturday. According to police the victims were travelling in a  , which collided head on with Tata canter. 

 Talking to TOI, SSP, Rajesh Pandey said that the incident occurred around 2 pm when the jeep, loaded with about two dozen people was travelling from Sikandararao side, and the Tata vehicle was coming from the opposite direction. He said the victims were on their way to attend a marriage function in Aligarh. 
 
 He said that four people died on the spot. They were identified as Yogesh (23), Shahjahan begum (55),   (4) and Alfisa (4). The injured were admitted at JN medical College and are under observation.
