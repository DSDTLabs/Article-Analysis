The Indian National Congress will contest in 37 out of 40 assembly constituencies in Goa.  The assembly elections in Goa will be held in a single phase on February 4, 2017 to elect the 40 members of the Goa Legislative Assembly. Results will be declared on March 11, 2017.  In its election manifesto, the party promised free petrol for youth and wheat at Rs 2, rice at rs 3 and sugar at rs 7.
