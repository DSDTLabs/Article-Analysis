Ludhiana: A two-day international conference will be held by the Parvasi Sahit Adhyan Kender of postgraduate department of Punjabi, Gujranwala Guru Nanak Khalsa College, in collaboration with Punajb Bhawan Surrey (Canada) from January 16 onwards. Writers from different countries will take part in the conference. The meet will encourage Punjabi language, literature, and culture.

 Punjab Local Bodies Minister Navjot Singh Sidhu will chair the conference on the opening day. Leading Punjabi writer Dalip Kaur Tiwana and MLA Sukhjinder Singh Randhawa will be the guest of honour at the meet.
