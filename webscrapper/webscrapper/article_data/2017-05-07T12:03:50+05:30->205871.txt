MADURAI: Thousands of devotes thronged the Sri Meenakshi Sundareswarar Temple in   on Sunday morning to witness the celestial wedding of Lord Sundareswarar and Goddess Meenakshi.

 Two temple priests enacted the marriage -- C Santhoshbattar as Lord Sundareswarar and Halsyabattar as Goddess Meenakshi. There were ceremonies like tying the kappu and exchanging garlands.
 
 Married women changed their mangalsutra on the occasion.
 
 Temple's odhuvars (priests) chanted Thevaram while Sikkil Gurucharan gave a Carnatic music concert.
