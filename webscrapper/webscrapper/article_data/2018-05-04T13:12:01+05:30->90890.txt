LUCKNOW:  , principal secretary to   Speaker Hridaya Narain Dixit, has been given one year extension. Dubey retired from the service on April 30 but was given the extension by chief minister Yogi Adityanath on the Speaker's recommendation. 

 The officer has had a distinguished career. He served as the legal rememberencer to six UP governors before being appointed as principal secretary of the Assembly.Dubey is also considered an expert of the Constitution. As CM in 2003, Mulayam Singh Yadav had suspended Dubey on the suspicion that he had advised then UP governor T Rajeshwar against the state government on certain issues. However, Rajeshwar hit back and challenged Dubey's suspension in the Supreme Court which revoked Dubey's suspension
