MADURAI: The Madurai bench of the Madras high court on Friday ordered notice to the vice-chancellor of   (Deemed university) S   on a petition challenging his appointment. 

 President of the Gandhigram Rural Institute Employees and Pensioners Association, M   filed the petition. It came up before the division bench headed by chief justices  .
 
 His counsel said that the present vice-chancellor has been appointed by violating the UGC norms, regarding which the petitioner had filed a case in 2014 before the bench. When it came up for hearing on September 28, 2015, the petitioner due to ill health was unable to give instructions to the counsel due to which the case was dismissed. Hence, it was needed to restore the petition dismissed and to conduct hearing. Following it, the bench ordered notice to the vice-chancellor.
