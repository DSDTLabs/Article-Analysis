NEW DELHI: The Tamil Nadu Directorate of Government Examinations (TN DGE) declared the Tamil Nadu Plus One (Class 11) board results 2018 today, May 30, 2018. The results were declared on the official website of the Board - tnresults.nic.in - at around 9 am.

 The candidates who have appeared for the Tamil Nadu Board Class 11 examination 2018 can go to the official website to check their results online. 
 
 The Tamil Nadu Board had successfully conducted the Examination for Class 11 students in the month of March-April 2018.
 
 If you are one of the students eagerly waiting for the announcement of Tamil Nadu HSC first year results 2018, you can check it on the official website of the TN DGE Board - tnresults.nic.in.
