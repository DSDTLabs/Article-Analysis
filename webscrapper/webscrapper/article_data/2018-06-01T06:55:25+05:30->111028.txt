MAPUSA: Crime branch busted an inter-state   at   on Thursday, and arrested two pimps. 

 Sources said that a team of Goa crime branch conducted a raid at Shiva Chillout Bar and Restaurant, near Agar, Madhalawada, and rescued one 24-year-old girl, a native of West Bengal.
 
 Police said that the accused are Mohammed Fazal from Muzzaffarfur, Bihar, and Hirija Mansola, a native of Punjab. The accused used to run the prostitution racket by calling the customers to the said restaurant which has rooms attached to it, police said.
 
 After receiving a specific tip-off, crime branch police sent a decoy customer to the restaurant.
