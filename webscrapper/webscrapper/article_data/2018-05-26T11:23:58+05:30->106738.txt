This year, the board exams were marred by controversy with incidents of leak of Class 12 Economics paper and Class 10 Mathematics paper. The board then decided to conduct a retest for Class 12 Economics paper on April 25 and put in place a special strategy to ensure there was no possibility of a paper leak.

 Recently, the tech giant Google has collaborated with Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) to allow students check their results on its platform.
 
 This will help students quickly, securely, and seamlessly locate their exam score on their smartphone or desktop, it added.
