Surat: Three accused in 2016 hooch deaths were denied bail by a local court on Saturday. The accused had applied for bail after the charge-sheet was filed in the court. Bail pleas of Jitendra Chaudhary, resident of Reshma Row House in Parvat Patia, his wife Padma and mother Shanta, were rejected by the court. They are accused under Indian Penal Code sections of 302 (murder) and 307 (attempt to murder).The accused were arrested by detection of crime branch of city police following death of four lives in the city after consuming liquor. They had brought the liquor from Vareli of Surat district and sold it in Parvat Patia and Kapodra area through other bootleggers. Total 23 people had died due to spurious liquor in city and district last year.
