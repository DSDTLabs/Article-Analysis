Lucknow: President of   outfit Hussaini Tigers and city-based businessman Shameel Shamsi announced to form a ‘Shia Gau Rakshak Dal’ to check   slaughter. Shamsi told TOI the main objective of the group would be to alert police if it came to know about cow slaughter, besides stopping violent gau rakshaks who attack and kill human beings in the name of saving cows, he said. 

 Denying reports that he was joining BJP, Shamsi said the group will start working in Lucknow and gradually they will cover other parts of the state and country. “I am in touch with Shia community members in Sambhal, Kanpur, Moradabad and Maharashtra who want to form gaurakshak dals too,” he said.
