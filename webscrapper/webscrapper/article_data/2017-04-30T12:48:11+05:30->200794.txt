 Joining him at the ceremony will be senior police officials and Bollywood actor Akshay Kumar. Along with the ceremonious inauguration of the 24 tonne flag pole, a garden will also be inaugurated and opened for the public. 
 
 Special inspector general of Kolhapur Range, Vishwas Nangre Patil said, “Guardian minister Chandrakant Patil, minister of state for home  , state DGP   and other members of the Parliament and Members of Legislative Assembly representing the Kolhapur district will be present at the occasion along with the chief minister. Actor Akshay Kumar is the major attraction for the event, who will also be present for the inauguration.”
 
 The garden which will be inaugurated on Monday, is a replica of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel National Police Training Academy. Moreover, it will house an adventure park which will provide equipment like that of the police training academy. Plus, it will have a selfie spot, a mural expressing the importance of tricolour and an exhibition depicting the Indian Independence history between 1857 and 1947.
