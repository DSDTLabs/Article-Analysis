NELLORE: A day after Kadapa police cracked down on an international red sanders racket, its   counterpart arrested three people and seized 22 logs near   road in the district. 

 Police were conducting vehicle checks at   checkpost on Monday evening when a   Corola car (TN 22 AK 2059) did not stop and attempted to run over the cops. The police chased them and caught them at Naidupet road. They found 13 red sanders logs weighing 240 kg in the car dickey. 
 
 Upon interrogation, the accused revealed the kingpin to be Anamalai Paravasivam, who was later nabbed from Chennai. Nine logs (100 kg) were seized from him.
