The Kapoor khandaan has always been known for their grandeur. So when veteran actor Randhir Kapoor recently turned 70, daughters Karisma Kapoor, Kareena Kapoor Khan and son-in-law   decided to ring in his big day in true Kapoor style. The two daughters threw a lavish birthday party at their Chembur house, and invited close friends and family for the do. 

 The uber stylish daughters, dressed in ethnic wear, looked nothing less than gorgeous. While Karisma was seen donning a stunning embroidered beige outfit, Kareena dazzled in a Manish Malhotra number. Looks like the new mommy is already getting back in shape post pregnancy.
