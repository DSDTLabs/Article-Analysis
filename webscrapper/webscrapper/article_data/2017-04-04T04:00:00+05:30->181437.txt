Surat: Nemichand Shen, Navsari railway station master was arrested with three bottles of Indian Made Foreign Liquor (IMFL) on Monday.  According to case details, local crime branch officials of Navsari police received information that a person on a scooter with liquor bottles was to pass from opposite Sardar Township gate of station road. When a scooter was stopped and checked, police recovered three liquor bottles of 750 ml.  The person driving the scooter was station master of Navsari railway station who lived in Vijalpore of Jalalpore taluka of Navsari. Shen confessed to have bought these bottles from one Raju from Navsari railway station.  LCB officials have booked Shen under Prohibition Act. tnn
