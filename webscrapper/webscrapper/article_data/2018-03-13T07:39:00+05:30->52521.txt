Chandigarh: Lt Gen D P Vats (retd) on Monday filed his nomination papers as the candidate for the single seat of Rajya Sabha in Haryana which will be vacant after the term of Shadi Lal Batra comes to an end.

 Lt Gen Vats was accompanied by chief minister Manohar Lal Khattar, Haryana BJP president Subhash Barala, ministers Nayab Singh Saini, Krishan Bedi and Ram Bilash Sharma when he went to file his papers before the secretary of the state assembly. He filed his papers at 11am and was the only one to do so till 3pm.
