KOCHI: Over 300 passengers of a Jeddah-bound   flight from Kochi staged a protest after they were asked to deboard for the second day on Thursday. 

  "The flight scheduled at 5.55pm Wednesday was rescheduled to 11am next day by AI. But they were asked to deboard again," a   official said. 
 
 The flight finally took off at 9.30 pm on Thursday.
