NEW DELHI:  , deputy proctor,  , who was present when her colleague and deputy proctor   was threatening a TOI reporter with an FIR and snatched her mobile phone on Friday, rendered an apology on Saturday. She condemned the behaviour of her colleague and apologised for not holding her back when she had cornered the TOI reporter at the Conference Centre, North Campus when she was covering the violence by ABVP members.

 “I always stand for freedom of expression. I deeply apologise for not helping out the girl and not condemning the criminal act of Swasti Alpana. However, I never abused or shouted at the journalist. I condemn the criminal act of Swasti Alpana that she snatched the girl’s phone and bullied her,” she said.
