Vadodara: A livestock inspector in Dahod was allegedly fired upon by three men on bike at Godi locality in Dahod town late on Tuesday evening.

 The complainant Kiran Nisarta had gone to supervise the construction work of his new house. 
 
 When he was returning, three men came on a bike and allegedly fired at him. The police reached the spot, but did not find any marks of gunshots neither the shells of the bullet.
 
 “Even some people who were present there told us that they did not hear any sound of gunshots as claimed by Nisarta,” said Prem Veer Singh, superintendent of police, Dahod.
 
 Nisarta was allegedly fired upon a year ago too by unidentified persons.
