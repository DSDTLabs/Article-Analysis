Rajkot: Three truck robbers were nabbed from Jamnagar-Rajkot highway while they were plotting a robbery on Wednesday morning. 
 
 Jamnagar local crime branch (LCB) officials, who arrested the accused, identified them as Abdul Jokhia (25) alias Abudo, Abid Vagher (25) and Ismail Vagher (25), all residents of Dhararnagar area of Jamnagar. 
  
 “During interrogation, the trio confessed to having committed 18 truck robberies on highways in the past seven months. We recovered Rs 41,000 cash, seven cellphones, two motorcycles and two knives, all worth Rs 1.45 lakh from them,” said an official from Jamnagar LCB. tnn
