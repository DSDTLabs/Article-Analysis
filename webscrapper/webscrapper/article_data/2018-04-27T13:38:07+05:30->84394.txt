VELLORE: The   fast track mahila court has sentenced a 23-year-old man to 10 years rigorous imprisonment and slapped him with a fine of Rs 6,000 for sexually assaulting a   girl. It was the third conviction in the last one week.

 Judge P   pronounced  , who was then 20, guilty of sexually assaulting his neighbour’s five-year-old girl.
 
 The incident took place on April 19, 2015, when the girl was playing alone. The youth lured her to a defunct public toilet saying that he would buy her chocolate and then raped her, said public prosecutor S Priya Lakshmi.
 
 Following a complaint from the victim’s family member, the all-woman police in Vellore registered a case against him under various sections of the IPC and also invoked provisions of Prevention of Children from Sexual Offences Act, 2012.
 
 The judge awarded him one-year RI and a fine of Rs 1,000 under Section 363 (punishment for kidnap), two years RI and a fine of Rs 2,000 under Section 366 (kidnapping or inducing woman to compel her marriage) of the IPC. The judge awarded him 10 years RI and imposed a fine of Rs 3,000 under Section 6 (punishment for aggravated sexual assault) read with 5 (m) (punishment for penetrative sexual assault on a child below 12 years) of Pocso Act. The judge, in the verdict, stated that the imprisonment would run concurrently.
 
 On April 20, the judge awarded 10 years RI to a 27-year-old man for sexually assaulting an 11-year-old girl. Similarly, he awarded 10 years RI to another person in connection with the Pocso Act on Tuesday.
