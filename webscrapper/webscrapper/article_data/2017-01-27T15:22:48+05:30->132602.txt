The Bharatiya Janata Party has decide to fight the Punjab assembly elections in alliance with its long term partner Shiromani Akali Dal. The party will contest on 23 seats whereas its ally SAD will fight on 94 out of total 117 assembly seats of Punjab. Among the 23 candidates announced by BJP, nine are sitting MLAs.  The Punjab assembly elections will be held in a single phase on Februrary 04, 2017 and the results will be dclred on March 11, 2017.
