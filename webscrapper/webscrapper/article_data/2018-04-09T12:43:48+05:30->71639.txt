Bollywood actor Kartik Aaryan was at an   event in Mumbai for the launch of a virtual reality game combined with a roller coaster. The actor while interacting with the thousands of visitors mentioned how he is scared of heavy duty rides in parks. He said, "Amusement parks have many big rides and I can't go in them. I am scared of them. I enjoy virutal reality games. My friends love to go on these big rides and they enjoy them."

 The actor who has won good appreciation for his film ' ', received praises for the film at the event too. He said, "I am happy that people liked my film. I enjoy coming to amusement parks because apart from rides, there is a lot of fun and chilling out you do with friends. Lots of masti and therefore it is always great option."
