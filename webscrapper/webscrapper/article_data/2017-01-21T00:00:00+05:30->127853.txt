Students from across the State gathered at the   , Kochi, recently to showcase their talents during Tandav, an inter-collegiate literary-cum-cultural fest. It was a joyous affair for the audience as well, as they cheered for the participants. Shutterbugs got a chance to capture the fun moments, and it looked like the competitive and celebratory mood of one and all present were infectious. 
From dance, song to mime, there was everything to entertain the crowd, who had gathered. Some of the dance performances were so gripping that the on-lookers sat totally glued to their seats.
