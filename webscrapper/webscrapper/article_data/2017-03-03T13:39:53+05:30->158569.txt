LUCKNOW: A year back when Lucknow based   employee-turned-photographer Darvesh Singh Yadavendra wanted to photograph the  , he got little support from the network in Lucknow. Now, Yadavendra is organising a first of its kind photo-exhibition in the city on transgenders with an aim to break stereotypes around the community . The exhibit is also a prelude to the first Gay Pride planned to be organised in Lucknow on April 9.

 Beginning Friday, the exhibition called `TRANScending Beautiful' will hit floors with 29 portraits on the lives of transgenders and kotis (effeminate men who sometimes dress up like men). While the exhibit will be a first for the city, the 25 models pictured are residents of Delhi.
