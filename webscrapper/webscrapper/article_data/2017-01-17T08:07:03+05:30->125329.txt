GUWAHATI: Assam governor   has extended the term of the   ( ) for six months. 

 The term of the present KAAC expired on January 13 upon completion of five years of the council.
 
 A government statement said the state election commission, which is going to conduct the polls, has said it will not be "practicable" to hold the election before January 13 due to "unavoidable circumstances arising out of publication of the electoral roll on December 31, 2016".
 
 The KAAC was constituted under the provision of the Sixth Schedule of the Constitution. It is headquartered at Diphu. The 30-member council is currently ruled by BJP.
