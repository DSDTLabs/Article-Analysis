Visakhapatnam: Greater Visakhapatnam Municipal Corporation (GVMC) commissioner M Hari Narayanan issued an order of ‘loss of pay’ for one week to 22nd ward sanitation inspector Appala Raju.

 During his ward visits on Tuesday, the civic chief found waste dumped on the road and immediately took the decision to cut the salary of the ward’s sanitation inspector. He also instructed zonal commissioner V Chakradhara Rao to weekly pay a visit to the wards and inspect the works of the sanitation inspectors and workers. The civic chief inspected the conditions of various civic services including drainage canals, streetlights in ward number 24. TNN
