Aditi Rathore, who played the role of grown-up Avni in  ' ' ', is going through a tough phase of her life.
 
  The actress, who was dating a fitness model  , recently broke up and announced the news on social media.
  
  
 
  
  Aditi and Shreedhan were dating for quite some time before things stopped working well between them.
  
  
 
  
  According to reports, things started falling apart when Aditi got busy with 'Naamkarann' and was not able to give much time to boyfriend Shreedhan. This created a rift between the two, leading them to part ways.
  
  Aditi announced the news through her Insta story.
