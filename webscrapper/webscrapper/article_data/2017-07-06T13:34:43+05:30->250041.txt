 Both the pilots flying the aircraft ejected in time and landed safely. They have been evacuated and taken to the base for medical attention, IAF sources said.
 
 The incident occurred in Balesar area, SP (Jodhpur Rural) Dr Ravi said.
 
 It was a MiG-23 UB trainer plane and both the pilot and co-pilot are safe, defence source said.
 
 The   crash landed at Jodhpur's Gopalsar village at around 11 AM .
 
  
  
 
 A Court of Inquiry has been ordered by the Air Force to ascertain the cause of the crash.
