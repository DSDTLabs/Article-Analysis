BENGALURU: App-based cab aggregator Ola has put Rs 100 crore in its car leasing business, according to regulatory filings with the Registrar of Companies. Ola Fleet Technologies, which runs the company's car leasing business, was set up in 2015 to help drivers own their cars in partnership with banks and auto manufacturing companies.

 Ola had announced an initial plan of pumping in Rs 5,000 crore for this business over the years, without specifying the exact period of time.
 
 Prior to this fresh infusion, Ola had put Rs 50 crore at the beginning of the year into this vertical. Earlier, former   India executive   joined the car leasing business as its CEO.
