NEW DELHI: The National Capital Region (NCR) witnessed a cloudy and windy morning accompanied with mild rainfall in parts of the capital and Noida on Monday morning. 

 
 Rains lashed Delhi Cantonment area and parts of Moti Bagh in the capital on Monday. Parts of Noida and Greater Noida also witnessed light to moderate rainfall.
 
 The Indian Meteorological Department (IMD) forecast says Delhi-NCR is likely to witness thunderstorm and rains during the day. Thunderstorm accompanied with gusty winds are likely at isolated places over Haryana, Chandigarh and Delhi on Tuesday.
  
  
 Thunderstorm accompanied with squall very likely at isolated places over Haryana, Chandigarh and Delhi, Gangetic West Bengal, Jharkhand and Nagaland, Manipur, Mizoram and Tripura, said an IMD release.
 
  
  
 
  
  
 A feeble Western disturbance lies over north Pakistan and adjoining Jammu and Kashmir and another Western Disturbance likely to affect Western Himalayan region till April 10, the IMD press release said. 
 
 
  
  
 There have been no report of traffic jam due to the rain so far from any part of the NCR.
