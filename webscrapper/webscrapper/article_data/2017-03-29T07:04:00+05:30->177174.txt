Jalandhar: Three members of a family were burnt to death when their hut caught fire in the wee hours of Tuesday. The incident happened at Ahman Kazi village near Kartarpur, where the victims worked in the fields of a farmer. 

 It is suspected that the hut caught fire from a mosquito repellent coil. The victims, who were from Bihar, have been identified as Bhola Chaudhary (56), his daughter Savita (15) and son Ashok (7). 
 
 Kartarpur station house officer Sukhwant Singh said the fire was noticed around 2am by another family members living in another hut nearby. While children died on the spot, Bhola succumbed to his burns at Kartarpur civil hospital.
