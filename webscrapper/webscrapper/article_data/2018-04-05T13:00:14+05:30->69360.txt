FEROZPUR: A team of the   vigilance unit conducted a raid at the commercial branch in the office of the divisional railway manager (DRM) here on Wednesday. The team seized documents related to several parking contracts.

 According to officials, during preliminary investigation it was found that a former vigilance officer of Northern Railway while using his influence had allegedly got several parking contracts in the name of his son in Delhi, Ferozepur and possibly in other divisions. The officials also detected forged bank guarantees, solvency certificates and fixed deposit receipts (FDRs) issued by banks in Delhi and the character certificate issued by an   in Delhi. According to information, there was an outstanding amount of over Rs 60 lakh against the contractor but no efforts were being made by the department to recover these, causing a huge revenue loss to the Railways.
