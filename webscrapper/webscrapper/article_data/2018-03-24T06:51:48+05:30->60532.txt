NEW DELHI: India’s   subscriber base dipped to 117.5 crore at the end of January with smaller operators losing bulk of their customers, as per monthly   report released on Friday.

 “The number of telephone subscribers in India declined from 119.1 crore at the end of December 2017 to 117.5 crore at the end of January 2018, thereby showing a monthly decline rate of 1.3%,”   said.
 
 The mobile subscriber base declined to 115.2 crore at the end of January 2018 from 116.7 crore at the end of December 2017, down 1.3%.
