NAVI MUMBAI: A top official from world football governing body   on Friday inspected the  , one of the six venues to host the   matches.

 The World Cup is being hosted by India for the first time and it commences from October 6.
 
 FIFA general coordinator   inspected the stadium and its preparedness, sources told PTI.
 
 "The official is expected to go to Kolkata, where he will submit a report," an official said.
 
 According to the official, a final decision on the handover of the stadium to FIFA is expected to be taken shortly.
 
 The other venues for the World Cup are Guwahati, Kolkata, Delhi, Kochi and Goa.
