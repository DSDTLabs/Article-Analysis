 Accused Dipak Chhotalal Shah, a resident of Bhestan, was booked under various Sections of Indian Penal Code, including 304 (culpable homicide not amounting to murder) and 338 (causing grievous hurt by act endangering life or personal safety of others). He is likely to be produced in court on Friday.
 
 Ganesh Deshmukh (40) and Manoj Deshmukh (34), who were construction labourers, died after they got buried under the debris. Parvat Nagle (35) was admitted to a hospital with serious injuries. 
 
 Mahesh Patel, owner of the plot in Adarsh Pachhatvarg Society in Athwalines, had given contract to Shah to construct the house. The incident took place on Wednesday at around 7.30 pm when the work to lay the slab of the first floor was going on. 
 
 “We have arrested the accused contractor for negligence for not ensuring the required safety of the labourers. We are yet to investigate the case in detail and will seek technical opinion on the matter,” the police said.
