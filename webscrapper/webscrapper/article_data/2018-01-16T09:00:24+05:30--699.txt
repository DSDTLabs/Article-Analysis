BENGALURU: The Enforcement Directorate (ED) issued a notice to minister R Roshan Baig’s son, R Ruman  , for allegedly violating   norms.

 ED sources said his firm Ruman Enterprises, which is into software publishing and consultancy, was issued the notice under the Foreign Exchange Maintaiance Act ( ). The ED alleged the firm had received unaccounted-for funds from an Arab-based firm. Minister Baig and his daughters are directors of the company. “We have not yet received the notice. Whenever we get it, we will consult our auditor and reply to it,” said minister Baig.
 
 ED sources said there are records to prove that the firm had received crores of rupees in various currencies as investment, but the company hadn’t allotted any shares to investors.TNN
