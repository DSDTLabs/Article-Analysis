GURUGRAM: A 42-year-old businessman was shot at by four unidentified men near Khera Jhanjhrola village late on Monday.

 The victim, Ishwar Singh, was on his was to his aunt’s house from Sunderpur Jarau when he was attacked. The accused, allegedly, blocked his was near the village, snatched his licenced pistol and fired a shot at him. The bullet hit Ishwar on his shoulder and he fell unconscious. The accused left the pistol by his side and fled. Passersby took Ishwar to the general hospital where he is being treated. 
 
 Following Ishwar’s statement, an FIR under sections 307 (attempt to murder), 34 (common intention) of the IPC and Arms Act was registered at   police station.
