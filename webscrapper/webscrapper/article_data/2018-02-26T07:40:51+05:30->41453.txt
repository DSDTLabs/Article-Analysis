BENGALURU:   services between RV Road and Yelachenahalli stations on the  , suspended since 9pm Saturday, resumed on Sunday evening after completion of track-maintenance work. 

  Vasanth Rao, chief public relations officer, Bangalore   Rail Corporation Limited, said operations resumed from 7pm unlike the earlier announcement that they would be available only from Monday, 5am. The maintenance work had to be taken up as the crossover at Yelachenahalli station developed cracks. A crossover connects two parallel tracks, allowing trains to switch from one to another. 
 
  The Green Line connecting Nagasandra in the north to Yelachenahalli in the south opened in June 2017.
