 Nigel Philips, CBE, governor of the Falkland Islands, welcomed Tarini into Port Stanley on January 21, 2018.
 
 The six-member crew of the INSV Tarini started their maiden voyage on September 10 from Goa and is expected to return after rounding off the southern tip of Africa.
