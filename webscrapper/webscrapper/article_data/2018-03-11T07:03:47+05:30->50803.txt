According to government officials, of the 265 talukas in the state, 99 have the CSR less than the national average of 848. The officials said that of these 99 talukas, 48 are in Saurashtra and Kutch region.
 
 According to government data, of the 52 talukas in Gandhinagar region covering Arvalli, Gandhinagar, Patan, Sabarkantha, Patan, Mehsana and Banaskantha, 14 talukas had the CSR less than 848.
