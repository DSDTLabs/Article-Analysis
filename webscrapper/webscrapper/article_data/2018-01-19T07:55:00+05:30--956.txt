AMRITSAR: Mortal remains of veteran Akali leader Manjit Singh Calcutta were consigned to flames with state honours here on Thursday. Calcutta breathed his last on Wednesday after a brief illness.

 Akal Takht jathedar Giani Gurbachan Singh performed ardas (prayer) before the cremation at Chatiwind cremation ground. Police personnel presented the guard of honour to the former minister of higher education.
 
 The funeral ceremony was attended by various religious as well as political leaders including cabinet minister  , former cabinet minister Sewa Singh Sekhwan, SGPC president Gobind Singh Longowal and SGPC member Kiranjot Kaur
