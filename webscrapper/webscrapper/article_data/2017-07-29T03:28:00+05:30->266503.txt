Colva: A 34-year-old fisherman from Nepal was rescued on Friday morning by lifeguards off the Majorda coast after he got caught in the winch of a boat and suffered grievous injuries to his abdomen and groin areas. He was administered first aid and rushed to Hospicio hospital, Margao, and later shifted to GMC, where he is undergoing treatment.  The incident occurred at 9.45am when the fisherman accidentally fell into the water and was caught in the winch of the boat 1.5km off the coast.
