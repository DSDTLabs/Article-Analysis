Abrid Shine's third movie ' ,' which is released after being delayed for over a year, has a lot of songs, soaked in nostalgia, lined up. The video of one of its songs, 'Ini Oru Kaalathekku' shows how students and teachers get ready for the youth festival.

 
  
 It is composed by Leela L Girikuttan and written by Ajeesh Dasan. It is featured on the campus of Maharajas College, that gears up for the yearly youth festival and students who engage in nostalgic conversations with the teachers about kalolsavam memories.
 
 Kalidas' character Gauthaman, who is also a poet and the chairman of the college union is the main character in the song, through whom the song moves ahead.
