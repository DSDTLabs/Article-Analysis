 An FIR was registered under section 66A (sending offensive messages) of the Information Technology Act, sections 295 (A) (deliberate and malicious acts, intended to outrage religious feelings of any class by insulting its religion) and 505 (2) (whoever makes, publishes or circulates any statement, rumour or report with intent to cause, or which is likely to cause, fear or alarm to the public) of the IPC.
