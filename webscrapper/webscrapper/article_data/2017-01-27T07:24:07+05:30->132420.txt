BENGALURU: Defence PSU   (HAL) has rolled out the first indigenously  , which will also be displayed at the  ,  , beginning February 14. 

 Named Hawk-i, this was the 100th Hawk aircraft produced at HAL, said HAL chairman and managing director T Suvarna Raju. 
 
  "HAL had conceived a programme for indigenous upgradation of the Hawk Mk132 for achieving self-reliance and has accomplished it. The upgrade was taken up at HAL to so as to be independent in matters such as integration of new sub-systems or modifications, obsolescence management of avionics systems and to enhance the aircraft operational and training capabilities," he said. The imported mission computer and data transfer units have been substituted with HAL-designed and developed systems.
