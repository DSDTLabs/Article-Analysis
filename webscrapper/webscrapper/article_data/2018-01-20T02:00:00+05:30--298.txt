PATNA: Chennai-headquartered Southern Railway’s additional GM P K Mishra has been appointed as the new GM of East Central Railway ( ) which has its headquarters at Hajipur in Bihar.

 The appointment of Mishra has been approved by the President on India, sources said and added Railway Board’s joint secretary R K Meena issued a notification to the effect on Thursday evening.
 
 The post of the ECR GM has been lying vacant ever since the elevation of the D K Gayen as the Railway Board member three months ago.
