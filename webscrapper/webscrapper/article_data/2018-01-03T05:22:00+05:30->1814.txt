KOLKATA:   government has accepted senior police officer Bharati Ghosh’s resignation, a week after Ghosh put in her papers, following her transfer from   to   police lines as commanding officer of the state armed police’s 3rd battalion on December 25.

 The government accepted her resignation through a notification issued on Tuesday under the   (DCRB) Rules. The notification states that IPS Ghosh (SPS-2006) has attained 50 years and is eligible for seeking voluntary retirement.
