HYDERABAD: A meeting of all judges of the   (called a full court meet) on Wednesday decided to form three sub-committees with judges of the HC to study various aspects of setting up a sepearte   High Court.

 This meet was preceded by awritten request from AP Chief Minister N   who conveyed to Acting Chief Justice   his intention to set up a separate HC for the State of AP as early as possible. While one of the committees will be visiting proposed buildings in AP capital region area to check suitability for housing the AP HC, the other committees will look at aspects like facilities for staff in buildings and in the vicinity, residences of judges and also digitisation of HC records. The forming of subcommittees (with five judges each) was left to the discretion of the Acting CJ.
