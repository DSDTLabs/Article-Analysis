Tuticorin: Indian Coast Guard (ICG) station at Tuticorin along with VOC Port Trust took out the annual clean-up at VOC beach on the occasion of international coastal clean-up day on Saturday. September 16 is observed as international coastal clean-up day to clean beaches and make seashores environment-friendly.

 More than 700 volunteers from ICG, local schools, national service scheme, national cadet corps and scouts undertook the cleaning operation on Saturday morning. At the end of the drive, volunteers collected about 10 tonnes of marine debris such as fishing lines, waste nets, food wrappers, ropes, plastic bags and sheets.The event was flagged off by deputy chairman of VOC Port Trust, S Natarajan in the presence of ICG station commander DIG SB Venkatesh.
