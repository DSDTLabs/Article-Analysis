NEW DELHI: A 27-year-old man and his nine-year-old son were injured when an under-construction wall of a school fell on them in outer Delhi’s   area on Monday evening.

 The two were admitted to a hospital and were discharged after treatment. An FIR of negligence has been registered. The incident was reported around 4pm when Ravinder and his son, Yashraj, were walking near the boundary wall of the school. 
 
 Ravinder managed to save Yashraj from getting buried under the debris. However, he got injured in the process.
 
 Police officers said that a hole was being dug up near the school’s wall because of which it became weak and collapsed. Along with police officers, four fire tenders also rushed to the spot
