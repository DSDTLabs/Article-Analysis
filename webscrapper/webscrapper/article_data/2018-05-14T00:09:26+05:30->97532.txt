NEW DELHI: The   (MPBSE) has now set the date for releasing the results for Class 10 and 12 board examinations. 

 
 According to the officials, the results of High School Certificate Examination (Class 10) and Higher Secondary School Certificate Examination (Class 12) will be declared by the state chief minister   at his residence on Monday, May 14, 2018 at 10:30 am. Also, the chief minister will felicitate the topper of the state. 
 
 The students who appeared for the MPBSE Board exams 2018 can register below to get their result directly through SMS or email.
 
  
  
 
 The results will be made available for the students at 10:30 am on the board's official website: mpbse.nic.in
 
 The Class 12 board exam started from March 1 and ended on April 3 and Class 10 exams were held from March 5 to March 31.
 
 Nearly 19 lakh students - 7 lakhs for Class 12 and 11 lakhs for Class 10 - wrote their exams during March-April 2018. 
 
 Last year, the board had announced both classes' results on May 12. 
 
 The pass percentage of Class 10 was 49.9 (51.46 for girls and 48.5 for boys) and for Class 12 it was 67.8 - 72.3% girls and 64.1% for boys had cleared the exams in 2017.
 
 The pass percentage for the private students was only 27.81%. A total of 4,07,950 students were successful out of 6,90,323 students who appeared for the Class 12 exams
