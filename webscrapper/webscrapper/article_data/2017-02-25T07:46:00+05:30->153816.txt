 Prachi (23) had lodged a complaint at the women police station against husband Vikas Gaur, father-in-law  , mother-in-law Saroj Gaur, brother-in-law Praveen Gaur and sister-in-law Meenakshi, also residents of Sector 15, on Tuesday.
 
 In her complaint, Prachi alleged that her in-laws had been harassing her for dowry since her marriage with Vikas in 2013. The situation turned worse after she gave birth to a girl.
 
 Finally, they kicked her out of the house, along with her daughter, in May 2016. 
 
 Kailash Devi, SHO of the women police station, said an FIR had been registered under sections 323 (causing hurt), 34 (common intention), 377 (unnatural sex), 406 (criminal breach of trust), 498 A (dowry harassment) and 506 (criminal intimidation) of the IPC. “We are investigating the matter. They will be arrested soon,” she said.
