RAJKOT: Two teenager boys drowned while swimming in Bhadar-2   near Dhoraji of Rajkot district on Wednesday afternoon. Their bodies were fished out in the evening. 

 Police identified the deceased as Ajay Chauhan (18) and Shailesh Chauhan (18), both residents of Junagadh Road in Dhoraji. Both   had jumped into the dam for swimming along with their three friends.
