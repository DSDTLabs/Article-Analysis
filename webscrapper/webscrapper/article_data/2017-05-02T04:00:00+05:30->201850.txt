Surat: A competitive exam preparation centre was inaugurated at Veer Narmad South Gujarat University (VNSGU) on Monday. The centre’s building has been developed with donation from city’s businessmen.

 In his address, VNSGU VC Dakshesh Thakar spoke about importance of the training centre. “Maximum students should take advantage of the centre and university is working to develop the centre further. The civil service exams and other exams conducted for direct recruitment are very important and students should work hard for it,” said Thakar.
