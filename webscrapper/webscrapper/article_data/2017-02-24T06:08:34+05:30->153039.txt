 The decision was announced after a meeting of the Akal Takht ‘acting jathedar’ Dhian Singh Mand, Takht Damdama Sahib jathedar Baljit Singh Daduwal, Takht Kesgarh Sahib jathedar Amrik Singh Ajnala and priests Suba Singh and Major Singh.
 
 They also constituted a three-member committee to investigate the role of Sikh political leaders who had sought help from Sirsa-based Dera Sacha Sauda chief Gurmeet Ram Rahim during the recently concluded Punjab elections. The committee includes SGPC’s executive committee member Surjit Singh Kalabula, Paramjit Singh and Bahadur Singh Bhasaur.
 
 Daduwal later told TOI that the Sikh high priests had decided to excommunicate Maluka, SGPC member Major Singh and former SGPC member Satnam Singh Bhairupa after declaring them ‘tankhaiya’ (guilty of religious misconduct). He said that the priests had also excommunicated Baba Satnam Singh Pipliwale for his alleged objectionable religious utterings.
