Surbhi ( ) asks Soumya ( ) to tell her the truth behind her leaving the house. Even though Soumya is hesitant, she is forced to reveal the condition that Preeto had proposed to her. 
 
 She tells her that she was asked to get her married to Harman ( ), only then she'd be allowed to remain in the house. On hearing this, Surbhi is shocked.
 
 
  
 Adamant to get Soumya back in his life, Harman gets up to leave the house. But he finds Surbhi and Soumya at his doorstep. Like everyone else, he is surprised to see them together. 
  
 While Preeto ( ) pretends to be nice to Surbhi, she wonders what Soumya has in her mind.
