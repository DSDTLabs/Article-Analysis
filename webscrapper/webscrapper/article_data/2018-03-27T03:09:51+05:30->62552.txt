NEW DELHI: South Delhi Municipal Corporation on Monday sealed 18 properties in its central, south and west zone on directions of the monitoring committee appointed by the Supreme Court.

 “The building department sealed 18 properties in three zones — central, south and west. The department sealed six properties in Defence Colony in central zone against misuse of stilt parking. Three properties were sealed in Kailash Colony in south zone and nine properties were sealed against misuse of stilt parking in Ganga Ram Vatika in west zone by the department,” said a senior official of south corporation.
