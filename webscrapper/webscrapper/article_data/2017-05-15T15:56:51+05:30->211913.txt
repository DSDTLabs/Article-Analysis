NEW DELHI:   major   India today reported a marginal decline in net profit at Rs 142.58 crore for the fourth quarter ended March 31, 2017. The company had posted a net profit of Rs 143.27 crore during the same period last year, Colgate-Palmolive said in a   filing. Net sales of the company rose 2.57 per cent to Rs 1,171.77 crore during the quarter under review as against Rs 1,142.36 crore in the January-March quarter of last fiscal.

 Commenting on the result, Colgate Palmolive (India), MD   said: "In the fourth quarter, we saw encouraging signs of recovery from the impact of the liquidity crunch in the prior quarter". For the entire fiscal, the company reported a net profit of Rs 577.43 crore as compared to Rs 581.17 crore in the 2015 -16 fiscal.
