Palanpur: After eight juveniles ran away from observation home in Mehsana last month, three more have escaped from the Radhanpur Road observation homes on Sunday. 

 According to police sub-inspector A V Pranami of B division police station Mehsana, three juveniles aged 17-18 years from different places of the state escaped from the observation home after breaking the grill. 
 
 According to police the three were accused of murder, attempt to murder and rape and were brought in from Ahmedabad, Rajkot and Surat. 
 
 Last month eight juveniles had escaped from the same observation of which four were brought back. “On Sunday three more juveniles have escaped. It is not known as as to what is the reason for their escape repeatedly,” said police sources.
