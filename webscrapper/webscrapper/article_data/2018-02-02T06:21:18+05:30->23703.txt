MARGAO: Agriculture minister   on Thursday hinted at plans to increase the production of hybrid coconuts.

 Sardesai, accompanied by   (MMC) chairperson Babita Angle Prabhudesai, MMC vice- chairperson Tito Cardozo and agriculture official  , supervised the sale of small, medium and big coconuts priced at Rs 15, 18 and 20, respectively. Approximately 10,000-15,000 coconuts were sold at the Fatorda horticulture outlets.
