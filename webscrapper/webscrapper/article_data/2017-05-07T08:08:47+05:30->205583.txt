Lucknow:   currency   amounting to Rs 3.82 lakh were recovered from a   near Smriti Upvan in Aashiana on Saturday noon. Passersby witnessed chaos as people skimmed the muddy drain waters to extract the banned Rs 1,000 and Rs 500 notes. During a drive to clean drains led by   zonal officer Suraj Singh, scrapped currency worth Rs 3.82 lakh was found and he informed police officials, said Aashiana police station officer S K Rai.
