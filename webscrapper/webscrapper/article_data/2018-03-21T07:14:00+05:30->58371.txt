GURUGRAM: A sex racket operating out of a spa in Omaxe mall on Sohna road was busted late on Monday evening. Nine accused, including six women, were arrested and an FIR under the Immoral Traffic (Prevention) Act was registered against the accused. They were produced before a duty magistarate on Monday night and were granted bail. 

 A raid was conducted by the police team headed by inspector Kanta Devi of Sector 50 police station at around 7.30pm, following a tip-off about the Oyster spa in Omaxe mall on Sohna road.
 
  "We sent a decoy customer to the spa. He met a woman at reception and gave her Rs 500. After a little wait, she finalised a deal with one of the women staffers. He gave her a signed note of Rs 500. After receiving a signal from him, a missed call, we raided the spa and arrested all nine accused including six women. We also recovered the signed notes from their possession,” said inspector Kanta Devi, who conducts the raid. 
 
 "All the women are aged between 20 and 35 years,” police said.
 
  An FIR under sections 3 (keeping a brothel or allowing premises to be used as a brothel), 4 (living on the earnings of  ), 5 (procuring, inducing or taking a person for the sake of prostitution), 7 (prostitution in or in the vicinity of public places), 8 (seducing or soliciting for purpose of prostitution) and 9 (seduction of a person in custody) of the Immoral Traffic (Prevention) Act was registered at Sector 50 police station. 
 
 “After their arrest, all nine accused were produced before a duty magistrate court on Monday night and they were granted bail,” said inspector Deepak Kumar, SHO, Sector 50 police station.
