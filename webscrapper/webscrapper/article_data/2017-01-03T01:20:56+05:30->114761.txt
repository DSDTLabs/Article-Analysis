GURGAON: In yet another hit-and-run accident, a 30-year-old pedestrian was crushed to death by an   vehicle near Sector 9A on Sunday. The deceased could not be identified till late on Monday. 

 According to police, the accident happened around 2.40am on Sunday near   colony. The victim, who was crossing the road, was hit from behind by a vehicle coming from Sector 4 side. The victim suffered grievous injuries and the accused fled the spot immediately. 
 
 A passerby, identified as Ankit Sharma, spotted the victim and informed police. However, the victim died on the spot. Following a complaint by Ankit, an FIR was registered at Sector 10 police station under sections 279 (rash driving), 304A (death due to negligence) of the IPC against the unidentified accused. 
 
 “The deceased’s body has been kept in the  . We will soon nab the accused,” said ASI Mahabir Singh, who is investigating the case.
