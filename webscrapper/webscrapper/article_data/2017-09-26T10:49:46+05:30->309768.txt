Music composer   has forced karaoke app   to remove his songs from its database. The maestro has sent letters to Smule and 15 other music app developers and websites that charge users for his songs. In March, Ilaiyaraaja had sent a legal notice to S P  , restricting him from singing his songs without permission.

 Ilaiyaraaja's copyrights consultant said, “Ilaiyaraaja is only against the unauthorised use of his songs by private companies for monetary gains. His songs are available for free on Youtube but he is not opposing it.“
