BENGALURU: As many as 1,98,639 students in the state will be writing   Common Entrance Test today. The exam is a gateway for admissions to professional courses like  , Ayurveda,  ,  ,  , Pharma D courses, after second PUC. 

 
 On Wednesday, Biology and Mathematics exams will be conducted in first and second half respectively. On Thursday, Physics and Chemistry exams will be held. 
 
 There are 1,00,071 male and 98,568 female candidates who will be writing the exam. It is conducted in 430 centres across the state and 86 centres are in Bengaluru. 
 
 The candidates are not allowed wear or carry any type of wrist watch to the exam hall. Carrying mobile, tablets, calculations are also banned.
