Madurai: Southern Railways announced special trains during Pongal and Republic Day with the view of clearing extra rush of traffic during these festivals. Altogether 13 specials trains are to be operated. According to a statement from railways, Train Number 06001 Chennai Egmore – Tirunelveli special fare special train will leave Chennai Egmore at 9.50 pm on January 5 and 19 and reach Tirunelveli at 10.50 am the next day. Train Number 06002 Tirunelveli – Chennai Egmore special fare special train will leave Tirunelveli at 3 pm on January 7 and reach Chennai Egmore at 3.30 am the next day..
