THANE: A sessions court has rejected the bail application of a 54-year-old man from Kopri who was arrested for allegedly showed obscene photographs to a 15-year-old girl on his phone. Special judge Mridula V K Bhatia, in her order, observed, “If he is released on bail, he may continue to indulge in similar obscene activities with other children.”

 Bharat Shah, who was arrested and booked under various sections of the IPC and the Pocso Act, had sought bail on several grounds, including that he wou-ld abide by the court directions. But assistant public prosecutor Rekha Hivrale claimed that his family members were pressuring the victim and her parents to withdraw the complaint.
