NEW DELHI: A 17-year-old girl on a bike was killed on the spot in Najafgarh while the man riding the bike escaped narrowly after a bus belonging to Haryana Roadways rammed the vehicle they were travelling in. Police has arrested the accused driver.

 Police said that the   took place on Thursday evening around 5.45pm. The deceased girl who was riding pillion has been identified as Reema, a student of class XII. 
 
 Police officers say that while the biker who is yet to be identified sustained minor injuries, the girl was crushed to death after she came under the wheels and died instantly.
