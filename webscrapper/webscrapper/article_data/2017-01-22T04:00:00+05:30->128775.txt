Ahmedabad: The monthly MP-MLA coordination meeting was held at the Usmanpura office of Ahmedabad Municipal Corporation (AMC) threw up a long list of civic issues that were yet to be addressed by the AMC. MLAs of the northern and southern areas of the city were concerned about the slow pace of repair works of drainage and water supply. Councillors of the western areas complained of poor roads. While in the central zone, an MLA even picked up the issue of frequent drain blockages and the mosquito menace. tnn
