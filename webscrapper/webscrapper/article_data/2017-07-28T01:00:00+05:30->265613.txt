The Big Zee   Awards will host a star-studded gala night and honour exceptional talent. The night promises power packed performances by   actors. Karan Johar will turn host at this prestigious award ceremony, and Salman Khancontinuing his tradition of performing at the BIG awards, will set the stage on fire with his high voltage performance. From bringing back romance with his character of Prem (Maine Pyaar Kiya) to his other notorious characters in films, the actor will perform to three power-packed entertaining dance acts. Kajol Devgn, who completes 25 years in the film industry, will also mark her presence at the event. 
Amidst the general nominations, Big Zee Entertainment Awards offers categories that are diverse and emerge from exclusively designed genres.  , Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, Shilpa Shetty Kundra, Sonam Kapoor and Disha Patani have been nominated for their exceptional performances this year. And Amitabh Bachchan, Ranbir Kapoor, Akshay Kumar, Shahid Kapoor,   Rajput, Ajay Devgn and Tiger Shroff also topped the list of nominations for the many award categories.
