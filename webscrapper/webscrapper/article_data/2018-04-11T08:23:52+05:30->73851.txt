LUCKNOW: A constable was nabbed while accepting bribe from a man for a favour in a case. He was arrested by a team of anti-corruption organisation. SP,  ,   said complainant   of   had a dispute with his neighbour over construction of a wall. 

 He had lodged a complaint with Mohanlalganj police. Constable Balwant Chandra, who was posted at Mohanlalganj police station, allegedly demanded Rs 35,000 from Rajesh to settle the case in his favour. A team led by circle officer   laid the trap. Rajesh asked Chandra to meet him near a bank on Tuesday afternoon. Cops in civvies nabbed the constable when he was accepting the bribe.
