RAJKOT: Six persons were arrested for illegally excavating bentonite from government land in   village of   taluka in   on Thursday night. Three trucks — two loaded with bentonite, while one being loaded when the seizure was made — were impounded. “The cost and quantity of the excavated bentonite will be ascertained once the mines and mineral department of the state government investigates the scam,” assistant sub-inspector   of rapid response cell of Kutch police said. 

 Police have zeroed in on key accused of the scam identified as Mahadev Ahir, a resident of Mandvi, whose arrest is yet to be made. 
 
 Apart from the three trucks worth Rs 25 lakh, police have seized an earthmover worth Rs 30 lakh, two diesel cans and two motors.
