NEW DELHI: The National Institute of Technology Tiruchirappalli has released the official notification for the   Tiruchirappalli MBA Admissions 2018 for the candidates who wish to apply for the course. 

 As per the available information on the official website nitt.edu, the online application for   2018 would start in the month of March. 
 
 The candidates who are willing to apply for the admission to the MBA course are required to apply online for the examination from the official website - nitt.edu. 
 
 The eligibility criteria for MBA admission 2018 is similar to that of previous sessions. 
 
 The candidates are advised to check the official website of the examination to check their eligibility to apply for the admission. 
 
 Candidates who wish to apply for MBA admission 2018 are requested to go through the detailed notification available on the official website before filling the examination application.
