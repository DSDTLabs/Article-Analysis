GHAZIABAD: A teenager and his middle-aged father died on the spot after their   scooty had a head-on collision with an approaching truck from the other side in the Teela Kothi area of Loni, close to Jabli cut here on Sunday evening.

 The deceased have been identified as Rahul Kumar (16) and Brijpal Kumar (55), who were both residents of Mandoli, New Delhi and were visiting Loni for some personal work. They were both labourers by profession. 
 
 However, the truck driver has been absconding from the spot but the vehicle has been seized by the Loni Police.
 
 The duo was about to enter the main road, while driving from the Teela Kothi link road, when an approaching truck from the other side hit them with a huge impact. Both of them succumbed to the impact at the spot, according to police officials. The victims toppled from the scooty and died of grievous head injuries.
