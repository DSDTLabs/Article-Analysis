AHMEDABAD: Bollywood actor   on Wednesday moved Gujarat HC against the proceedings initiated by Vadodara railway police in connection with death of a man at the railway station while he was promoting his film “Raees” in January. Khan moved the court a day after police summoned him to record his statement. Justice   will likely hear the plea on Thursday. On January 23, Fareed Khan Pathan died of heart attack after he was trapped in the crowd at the station.
