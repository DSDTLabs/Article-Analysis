 This was the first meeting of Meena after been nominated for Rajya Sabha. Kirori also discussed on the scenario after the Supreme Court’s order on SC/ST Act.
 
 “I discussed about it and told how some political parties, including Congress, were misleading public by its wrong interpretation,” Kirori said in an official statement on Saturday.
 
 Meena added that Modi assured him that rights and interests of SC/STs would be safeguarded by the government. He also urged Modi to expedite the process for the Rajasthan Eastern Canal Project which is going to benefit Sawai Madhopur, Dausa, Karauli, Dholpur, Bharatpur, Alwar and a few more districts.
