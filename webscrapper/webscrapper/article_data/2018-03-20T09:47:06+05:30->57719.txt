NEW DELHI: Bihar PolytechnicDiploma Certificate Entrance Competitive Examination (DCECE) 2018 application form has been released by the Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB). 

 
 The interested candidates can apply online for securing an admission into Polytechnic (engineering)/four years of Part Time Polytechnic engineering/Paramedical (12th level)/Para Medical-Dental (10th level). The online application can be filled up to March 28, 2018.
