HYDERABAD: A five-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a 15-year-old boy at Kakamamidi village, Moinabad, on Saturday. 

 The   said the boy, who is a neighbour of the child, took her to his home in the absence of his parents and abused her. The girl later told her father about the incident. Upon coaxing, she revealed that the boy had abused her in the same way a month ago as well.
 
 The Moinabad police registered a case under the   (POCSO) Act and 376 (Rape) of IPC. Police said the boy is a class 8 student. The victim was sent for medical examination. “The boy is yet to be taken into custody,” police said.
