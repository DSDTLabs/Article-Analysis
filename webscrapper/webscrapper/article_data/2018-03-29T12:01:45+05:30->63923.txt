KOTA: Annoyed by the scolding of his elder sister over poor performance in studies, a 20-year-old medical aspirant from   district in  , committed suicide by jumping off the bridge.

 The student, Nikhil Dhillan, was preparing for the pre-medical test and was a coaching student in   for the past two years. Nikhil, who was reportedly trying to jump into the Chambal river, landed on the hard ground and died on the spot. 
 
 “The elder sister, who is also preparing for NEET, yesterday morning scolded Nikhil for his poor performance in studies, following which he left their room and did not return last night. His sister had lodged a missing complaint at the Jawahar Nagar police station on Tuesday night,’’ said Satyanarayan Gujar, ASI, Nayapura police station.
 
 Police received a call about a body being found under the bridge on Wednesday morning. On inspecting, police recovered the suicide note from his shirt’s pocket. The body was handed over to the family members after post-mortem and police initiated an investigation into the matter after lodging a case under Section 174 of CrPC.
 
 This is the seventh suicide by a coaching student in Kota this year.
