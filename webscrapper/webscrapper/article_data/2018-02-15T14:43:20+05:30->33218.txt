VASCO: Several persons who withdrew money from a bank   at   were left bewildered after they were alerted that someone was withdrawing money from their bank accounts from different ATMs in north Goa.

 The culprits installed a skimmer machine and a spy camera on the ATM operated by the bank at Dabolim, said police. 
 
 After gaining private information of the bank account numbers and their passwords, the culprits used the information to withdraw money from their victims’ accounts at various ATMs across north Goa, such as Bicholim, Porvorim and other places.
 
 On Wednesday,   police station received several complaints from bank account holders. 
 
 Vasco police has asked the the bank’s Vasco branch manager to lodge an official complaint and surrender   footage of the ATM.
