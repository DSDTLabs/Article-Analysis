Lucknow: After TOI highlighted the security issues to passengers at   railway station, government railway police (GRP) swung into action and checked each and every area of station as well as passengers. Also the constables have been instructed to be on duty. 

 On March 15, TOI raised the issue of no security at the entrance and exit points or other places, and passengers expressed concern in the light of alert following the recent terror attack in train in Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh, and the encounter in Lucknow. 
 
 On March 16, SP, GRP, Vinay Kumar Yadav inspected Charbagh railway station and deployed personnel at entry and exit as well as on all platforms. Yadav has also ordered for 24-hour duty at the station.
