PANAJI: AAP chief ministerial candidate and former inspector general of prisons Elvis   lost in the constituency of Cuncolim in South Goa. Congress candidate and ZP member Clafasio Dias won after a recount against independent candidate Joaquim Alemao. Gomes was declared AAP's CM candidate on December 19 at a public rally in Cuncolim by the party's national convener  . 

 A bureaucrat of the   cadre who was in government service for almost two decades. Gomes was in line to be promoted to IAS cadre when the list was revised and he lost his chance. He was holding the additional charge of director of municipal administration when he chose to quit government service last year. He was relieved in September and joined the party within a week.
