GHAZIABAD: A two-day   aimed at inculcating best   of waste management among citizens kicked off at Ram Leela grounds in Kavi Nagar on the occasion of Earth Day. Around 5,000 students, residents and various other stakeholders including officials from various government departments are participating in the event named as ‘Kachra Samadhan Mahotsav’.

 According to officials, ‘best out of waste’ is the underlying theme of this event. The event also aims to explore solutions in handling challenges pertaining to effective disposal of e-waste by agencies including   Development Authority (GDA) and Ghaziabad Municipal Corporation (GMC).
 
 Stalls have been set up where ways recycling municipal waste within household are being demonstrated.
