 Amending the Municipal Corporation Acts, the UP cabinet on Wednesday took away the power of running and opening of slaughterhouses from civic bodies. The municipal corporations, however, will regulate them, UP government spokesmen Siddharth Nath Singh and Shrikant Sharma said.
 
 In another amendment, the cabinet approved banning of slaughtering of animals in any other area other than slaughterhouses which would be opened. Therefore, retail meat sellers will not be allowed to slaughter animals in their shops as they had been doing so far. The only exception in this case is slaughter for religious purpose.
