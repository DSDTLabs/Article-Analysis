Dharamshala: The police here have registered a rape case against a 50-year-old person for allegedly raping a six-year-old at her home. The case was registered late on Saturday. Investigations were on in this case, said officials, but no arrest was made till Sunday evening.

 Superintendent of police Ramesh Chajta said that a case had been registered by the police on the complaint of the victim’s family. “Allegations were made against the uncle of the accused in this case. No arrest has been made and medical report is awaited in this case,” he said. It was learnt that the incident took place in a village near this hill town when the girl was alone at her home with her uncle. Family members got suspicious after which they registered a complaint with the police.
