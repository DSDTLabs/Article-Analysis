KALYAN: A week after a fire broke out at   dumping ground in  , those residing in the vicinity demanded that dumping of trash there be stopped at once. Locals protested outside the civic headquarters on Monday under the banner of an association, 'Dumping Ground Hatao Sangharsh Samiti'. 

 Rajesh Joshi, a member, said, "We are facing health issues due to dumping of garbage." New civic chief   assured them that he would soon resolve the issue. -Pradeep Gupta
