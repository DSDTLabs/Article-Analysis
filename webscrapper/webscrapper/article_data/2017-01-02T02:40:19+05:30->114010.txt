With a view to prepare athletes for Olympics and other international competitions,   government is establishing   in each of the 22 districts to impart training to school children in their chosen sport. 

  These children would also be provided   of up to Rs 2,000. Schools or institutes operating the nurseries would be provided Rs 1.25 lakh for sports equipment and other expenses, this was stated by sports and youth affairs minister   on Sunday. PTI
