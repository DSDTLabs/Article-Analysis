They sought help and local people rushed to the spot and pulled the trio out from the car and took them to Krishnagiri government hospital in an ambulance. Vincy died at the hospital without responding to the treatment.
 
 When alerted, the Burgur police rushed to the spot and sent the bodies of Muralidharan and his child to the Krishnagiri government hospital. The bodies will be handed over to the relatives of the deceased after the postmortem.
 
 Following the accident, traffic was affected on the stretch for half-an-hour.
 
 The Burgur police have registered a case and further investigation is on.
