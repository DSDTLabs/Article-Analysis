NEW DELHI: Capital's busy   witnessed heavy traffic on Tuesday morning during the rush hour owing to the repair work on the flyover towards Ashram.

 
 Traffic movement will remain closed on   till May 30 in the carriageway from   towards Ashram   due to ongoing repairing work on the flyover by the Public Works Department (PWD). 
 
 Delhi traffic police has advised the commuters to avoid the stretch.
 
  
  
 The PWD is also assessing the condition of all old flyovers (constructed in the 90s and early 2000) in the city. There are 86 flyovers on PWD roads, of which 11 were constructed in the 80s and 90s, while 13 were constructed in 2001 and '02.
