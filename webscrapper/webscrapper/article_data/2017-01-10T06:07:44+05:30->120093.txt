AHMEDABAD:   nabbed  , son of  , accused in Rs 270 crore drug seizure case of May 2016. He was arrested from Chambal valley on the border of Madhya Pradesh and Rajasthan late Sunday evening. 

 Rathod was on the run for about last eight months. He was wanted in connection with seizure of large quantity of ephedrine manufactured in factory on the outskirts of Ahmedabad in May 2016.
 
  Senior ATS officials said following rumours of Rathod fleeing abroad they had tracked his movement in Madhya Pradesh and were keeping a watch on him for three days.
 
 “On Sunday evening, our team that was already stationed in MP, tracked his movement near Bara, a village on Rajasthan-MP border, and nabbed him,” said a senior ATS official. In May 2016, teams of Gujarat ATS and city crime branch in joint operation had seized large quantity of ephedrine and had arrested a man named Narendra Kacha, who was hired by Kishoresinh to make methamphetamine drug from ephedrine.
 
 A few days later, Thane police raided a factory in Solapur and seized ephedrine worth Rs 2,000 crore. Rathod is also an accused in this case. “Rathod was running the drug racket with the help of Jay Mukhi, Punit Singeri and Manoj Jain who were later arrested by Thane Police and brought to Gujarat on transfer warrant for questioning in ephedrine seizure in Ahmedabad,” added the senior ATS official.
 
 ATS sources said, “During the probe it had come to light that Kishoresinh along with international drug mafia leader Vicky Goswami, hailing from Ahmedabad and Doctor Abdullah, presently in Kenya alongwith underworld gangster Ravi Pujari, were running an international drug cartel. The drug made in the Ahmedabad factory was to be smuggled out of India to European countries,” said a senior ATS official.
