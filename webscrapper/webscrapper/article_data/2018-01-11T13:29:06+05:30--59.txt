NEW DELHI: The   Class 12 results for Kashmir division have been released on the official website of the Jammu and Kashmir State Board of School Education (JKBOSE) - jkbose.co.in.

 The regular students of the   Board Class 12 Kashmir Division can download their annual examination 2017 results from the official website of the Board. 
 
 
 
 
 As per the latest information, the Board has announced the JKBOSE higher secondary part two annual 2017 regular examination results son its official website - jkbose.co.in. The students who wish to check their JKBOSE Board Class 12 results can go to the official portal of the board at jkbose.co.in. 
 
 If you are one of the students eagerly waiting to check your   2017, you can check it on the official website of the JKBOSE Board - jkbose.co.in. 
 
 
 
 
 1) Keep handy your   admit card or your Roll No.
 
 2) Open the official website of the JKBOSE Board - jkbose.co.in
 
 3) On the homepage of the Board, you will see a link - "JKBOSE Board Class 12 results for Kashmir Division", click on it
 
 4) It will redirect you to a new page where you need to enter your JKBOSE Board Class 12 roll no. and other required details
 
 5) Once you click submit, your JKBOSE Board Class 12 result will be visible on the screen
 
 6) You can download your JKBOSE Board Class 12 result and print it for further reference.
