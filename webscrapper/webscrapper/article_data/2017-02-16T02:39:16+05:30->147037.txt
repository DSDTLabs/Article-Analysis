GURUGRAM: Four armed youths held two salesmen and the accountant of a   on   at   and stole Rs 1.5 lakh from the premises on Tuesday night. They also snatched the mobile phone of a customer present in the shop before fleeing in two motorcycles. 

 An FIR has been registered against them under Section 392 ( ) of the IPC and 25-54-59 of Arms Act at City police station and cops are trying to identify the culprits from the CCTV footage. In a similar incident, four youths in two bikes robbed a wine shop in Sector 37 of Rs 40,000 after holding the salesman on gunpoint on Saturday. 
 
 The accused were captured in the CCTV footage of cameras installed in the building right in front of the liquor shop.
