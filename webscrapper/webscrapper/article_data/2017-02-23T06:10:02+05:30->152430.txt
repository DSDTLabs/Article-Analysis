KOCHI: Citing previous incidents of fire,   merchants say there is an urgent need to implement the project to renovate the shopping streer without delay . There are many old buildings in Broadway with wooden structures.The electric wiring of these buildings are also old. “If there is a short circuit there is every possibility of fire outbreak,“ said  , president, Kerala Merchants' Union .

 The renovation programme is aimed at making Broadway, one of the city's most crowded shopping spots, a pedestrian-friendly zone.Currently vehicle parking makes movement of fire-engines and ambulance difficult during an emergency.
