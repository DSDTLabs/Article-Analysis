DEHRADUN: A Canadian woman has filed a complaint with   alleging that she was sexually assaulted by a person whom she befriended during her trip to Rishikesh in December last year. The complaint was filed online on January 2 after the woman had returned to her country.

 In the complaint, which is addressed to the state director general of police (DGP), the woman has alleged that a man named Rahul whom she had befriended took her to a bridge near Muni Ki Reti on December 14 last year and sexually assaulted her.
 
 According to B L Bharti, incharge of   police station where the case has been registered, the woman has also written that “the accused tried to make unnatural sexual advances towards her.” “The case has been registered under section 354 (assault or criminal force to woman with intent to outrage her modesty), 354A (sexual harassment), 354 B (assault or use of criminal force to woman with intent to disrobe) and 377 (unnatural offences) of the Indian Penal Code (IPC),” the official added.
 
 The Rishikesh police have also sent a mail to the complainant asking her to contact them for providing further details. “We have asked her to provide more information and reveal the identity of Rahul, the person whom she has named in her complaint,” said Bharti.
