MADURAI: The annual   was held at the   in   on Monday. Two wooden cars carrying the idols of Lord Sundareswarar and goddesses Piriyavidai and Meenakshi were pulled by devotees through the four Masi streets covering about 5km. 

 Devotees chanted slogans ‘Meenakshi Sundara – Mahadeva’, ‘Hara Hara Sankara – Mahadeva’ and ‘Kadambavana Sundara – Mahadeva’ as the cars moved. 
 
 The festival began at 6am, and the cars returned to their came to their base in the East Masi Street by 12.30pm.
 
 The car festival was held a day after the celestial wedding of the main deities.
