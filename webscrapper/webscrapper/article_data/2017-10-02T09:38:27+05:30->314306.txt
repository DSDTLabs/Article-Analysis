TRICHY: Five people including two kids were killed and 10 others injured in two separate road accidents that occurred in Trichy on Sunday . Both the accidents occurred due to speeding, police said.

On Sunday morning, as they were driving towards Mandaiyur over Trichy-Pudukkottai highway , a couple of speeding Multi-Utility Vehicles (MUV) coming from the opposite direction reportedly collided with each other and one of the cars rammed into the two-wheeler. Saravanan and the two kids Ajay and Andrea died on the spot while   was admitted to Trichy GH with severe injuries. Eight passengers commuting in the two MUVs sustained minor injuries and are being treated at the GH. Bodies of the three victims were handed over to their relatives at Trichy GH after medical examination. The Mandaiyur police are investigating further.
 
 In a separate accident , two youths were killed on the spot after their vehicle collided with a bus near Kattur on Sunday morning.
