TIRUPUR: A 60-year-old woman, who was among a group of people who had allegedly forced the members of a   family to fall at their feet, committed suicide near Kangeyam in   district of Tamil Nadu on Sunday.

 The deceased has been identified as P Saraswathy of Theerthampalayam.
