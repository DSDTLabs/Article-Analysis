HYDERABAD:   on Wednesday announced admissions into various courses and said that six new courses will be introduced in the academic year 2018-19. A total of 1,945 seats will be available this year for admission to 117 courses. This include 15 integrated courses, 42 PG, 13 M  , six M Tech and 41 PhD programmes. 

  Applyications begin from April 5. Last date to submit online forms is May 5. Entrance exams will be held in 38 centres across the country between June 1 and 5. Students can download hall-tickets from May 22.
