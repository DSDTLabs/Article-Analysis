 According to a recent report on Boxofficeindia.com, the film has raked in as much as Rs 31.75 crore within three days of its release. The film opened to a good response with Rs 8.25 crore on Friday, followed by Rs 11 crore on Saturday and Rs 12.50 on Sunday. The upward graph has paved way for the film's possible entry into the 100 crore club. 
 
 ' ' is a musical adventure written and directed by Anurag Basu, and produced by Basu and Ranbir Kapoor. The film tells the story of a teenage detective in search of his missing father. 
 
 When asked about the challenges he faced along the way, Ranbir said in an interview, "Well, life happens, you know... You become greedier, skilful and self-conscious. You set a desire and work towards that... Fear of failure bothers you the most." 
 
 Meanwhile, megastar Amitabh Bachchan on Sunday lauded Ranbir Kapoor and Katrina Kaif starrer 'Jagga Jasoos' calling it innovative, delightful and a well executed film. "Just saw 'Jagga Jasoos' could not resist telling Anurag what a delightful, innovative, well executed film it was; a joy to watch," Amitabh tweeted.
 
 Besides Amitabh, filmmaker Karan Johar also hailed the film on the micro-blogging site. "I hear 'Jagga Jasoos' is the most unusually treated narrative with heartening performances! Basu is undoubtedly a beautifully chaotic mind," Karan tweeted.
