NEW DELHI: Around 15   had a close shave when an electric pole fell on their school bus in  's   on Friday morning. They were all rescued safely by the locals who witnessed the incident and then informed the police. 

  Police said that the private school bus was heading towards the school and was moving slowly due to heavy traffic when there was a loud thud. The driver noticed in the rear view mirror that an electric pole had fallen on the bus. 
 
  It was raining heavily at the time of incident. The driver rushed out of the vehicle and with the help of the locals, rescued the children out of the bus. The local authorities were also informed and an appropriate action was taken.
