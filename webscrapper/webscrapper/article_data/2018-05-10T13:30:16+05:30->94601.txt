NEW DELHI: The Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board (GSEB) declared the Gujarat Class 12 Results 2018 on its official website on May 10, 2018.

 
 The GSEB Class 12 for science stream was declared on the official website (gseb.org) on Thursday around 9:00 am. Around 1.35 lakh students took the class XII science exam in March.
 
 The candidates who have appeared for the examination can register below to get their result through SMS or via email.
 
 
  
  
 
 Board officials said 57,734 students had taken the exam in the 'A' group, with physics, chemistry and mathematics. Some 76,888 students took the examination in the 'B' group, with physics, chemistry and biology. Just 19 students chose the 'AB' group, with physics, chemistry, biology and mathematics.
 
 The admission committee for professional courses stated that the last date for admission for engineering and pharmacy was May 14. Board officials said that there was no possibility of extending the date for filing online application as the results are being announced on time and students have four days to file their online admission forms.
 
 Gujarat SSC exams were conducted from March 12 to 23, 2018 and HSC exams for general stream were held from March 12 to 28 while for science stream the exams were conducted from March 12 to 22, 2018. This year, around 6.9 lakh students have appeared for the HSC Science and General Stream examination.
 
 In 2017, 7,75,013 students had appeared for the HSC examination and the result for which was released on May 17. The pass percentage for Class 12 was 68.24%. Last year the girls had outperformed boys with 73.33% against 64.69% for boys
 
 The pass percentage for science stream students was 81.89 percent in which 1,38,727 students had participated in 2017.
