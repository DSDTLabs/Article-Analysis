Hyderabadis came out in large numbers to take part in a run titled ‘Run for A Cause’ that was organised to raise funds for betterment of village   in Adilabad on Sunday. Organised by the student-run non-profit, Street Cause, the run began with a warm up at People’s Plaza at 6 am. The walkers and the runners then took to the streets to sweat it out in the 4k run that went on till 9 am.
