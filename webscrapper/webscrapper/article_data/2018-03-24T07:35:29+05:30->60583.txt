 When the proposal was opposed, the faculty board meeting scheduled on Saturday was postponed and will now be held on April 3.
 
 As per the proposal, the first and third semesters will have MCQ-based examination while second and fourth semesters will have theory and practical examination. The fifth semester will be MCQ based and the final semester will be theory-based.
