CHANDIGARH:  , state BJP media in-charge, has been appointed the media adviser to   chief minsiter Manohar Lal Khattar. Jain, who is the husband of urban local bodies minister Kavita Jain, will have his second stint as the media adviser to the CM.

 Earlier, he had served as the media adviser to Bansi Lal between 1996 and 1999 when Haryana Vikas Party (HVP) and BJP had formed the government in the state. He had stepped down after the BJP broke alliance with HVP and joined hands with INLD.
