GURUGRAM: A four-year-old was killed in   village near Kherki Dhaula after he was   between a   and a wall on Saturday morning.

 The victim, Aditya, had gone out to relieve himself in an open plot next to his house when a pick-up truck crushed him to death.
 
 The boy’s father Gabbar Mandal, who was at a distance, started shouting in an attempt to stop the truck, but the driver couldn’t hear him. The driver kept on driving, thereby crushing the boy who was sitting next to the wall, killing him on spot.
 
 Mandal, who works in a private company in Narsingpur, told the police that Aditya was killed owing to the negligence of the driver who failed to spot the child. The driver identified as Roshan, victim’s neighbour, fled the scene immediately.
 
 A case has been registered against Roshan at the Kherki Dhaula police station. An FIR was registered under sections 279 (rash driving) and 304A (death by negligence).
 
 “The boy died on the spot after the accident. The driver was probably drowsy as it was early morning. He did not notice the child in front of him and kept on driving,” said sub inspector Naresh of Kherki Dhaula police station. The police is yet arrest the accused, Roshan.
