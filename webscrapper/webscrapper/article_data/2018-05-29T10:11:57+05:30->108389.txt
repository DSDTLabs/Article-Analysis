NEW DELHI: An injured Shane Watson was the hero for   champions Chennai Super Kings, who beat Sunrisers Hyderabad by eight wickets with nine ball remaining in Sunday’s final at the Wankhede Stadium in Mumbai. 

 
 
 
 Watson, who turns 37 next month, hit the highest score in an   final (117 not out) to hand   a record-equalling third title. For winning the title, three-time champions CSK were given a cheque worth Rs 20 crore. Runners-up SRH got Rs 12.5 crore, and there were other prizes handed out after the final.
