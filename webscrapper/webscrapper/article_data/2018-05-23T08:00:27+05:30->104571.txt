KOCHI: Motor vehicles department officials said that private vehicles will not be allowed to transport children to school by collecting charges. If a private vehicle is found to be transporting children after collecting money,   officials will suspend the licence of the owner for the next six months. Private vehicle owners can transport only their children or can take other students for free. 

 If a vehicle is found transporting children to school without safety sticker, it should be reported to officials. Public can alert MVD by sending registration number of the vehicle and name of the school to WhatsApp number 7025950100.
 
 MVD's safety sticker is must on vehicles transporting children from June 1.
