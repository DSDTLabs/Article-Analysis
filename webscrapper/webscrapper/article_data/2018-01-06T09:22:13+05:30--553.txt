CHANDIGARH: Panipat police on Friday arrested a student of Class IX for allegedly sexually abusing an eight-year-old girl in a village.

 The incident came to light when the girl who had become unwell and was rushed to the civil hospital where she narrated the incident to the nursing and paramedical staff on January 3.
 
 According to information, the girl was playing near her house on January 2 when the 17-year-old accused called her and took her to a secluded place where he allegedly sexually harassed her. He also threatened her of killing her parents if she narrated the incident to anyone.
 
 Cops said the hospital staff informed the parents and the police. On Thursday, the girl was counselled and her statement was recorded, cops said, adding that on Friday, the boy was produced before the   that remanded him to the Ambala   home.
