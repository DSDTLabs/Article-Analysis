Popular   singer   has recorded a song for a   film  , directed by Gopi Kerur. The singer, who was in the city recently, told us, “This is my second Kannada song, and is a very well-written sports anthem. A team came to Mumbai earlier this month to record the song. We are making a promotional video of the song, starring me. The video will have an international look and feel.” The film, which deals with the subject of skating, will see newcomer Mana Advik making his debut.
