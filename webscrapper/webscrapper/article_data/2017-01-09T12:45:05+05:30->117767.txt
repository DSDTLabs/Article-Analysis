SURAT: Police are yet to nab the abductors of a   boy who was taken away from the referral hospital of Kathor village of Surat district on Thursday. 

 The cops are groping in dark in the absence of any  . The small medical set up where the incident happened has no CCTV coverage. One of the accused was allegedly wearing an apron and a mask and took away the baby under the pretext of giving polio vaccine. 
 
 PSI M B Tomar of Kamrej police station said, “We have formed five teams to probe different angles. There seems to be only one kidnapper. When the incident occurred at around 1.30am, there was darkness at the place and no one else was present there except the newborn’s mother Sufiya Mansuri.”
