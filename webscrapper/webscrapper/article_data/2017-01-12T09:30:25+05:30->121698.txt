PANAJI: Surmounted with requests to exchange the demonetized Rs 500 and Rs 1000 currency with the new Rs 500 and Rs 2000, the Panaji office of the   on Tuesday said that it won’t be exchanging the currency anymore. 

 “The exchange of notes is available only at Reserve Bank of India offices in Mumbai, New Delhi, Chennai, Kolkata and Nagpur,” the statement by the office said, adding that the service at these centers is only available for Indian citizens and non-resident Indian ( ) citizens who were abroad between November 09, 2016 and December 30, 2016. 
 
 The office also clarified that the limited service is available for Indian citizens till March 31 and NRIs till June 30.
