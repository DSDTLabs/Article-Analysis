Actor Ram's upcoming film with his 'Nenu Sailaja' director Kishore Tirumala is titled 'Vunnadi Okate Zindagi'.

 
 The movie, which is touted to be a romantic entertainer features Anupama Parameswaran and Megha Akash as its female leads. Actor Sree Vishnu will be seen in an important role in the film. 
 
 About 50 per cent of the film's shooting has been completed and the unit is now gearing up for a fresh schedule that begins at Ooty from August 6. 
 
 
 Meanwhile, news about the movie's title has surfaced and sources also suggest that the poster and first look will be released very soon.
