JAIPUR: The hunger strike by jail employees across Rajasthan entered its third day with many protesters being hospitalised after their condition deteriorated. 

 “70 jail employees were hospitalised on Saturday, three have been rushed to critical care units in Jaipur and Ajmer. We will not budge till pay discrepancies are removed by the state government,” said  , deputy jailor, posted in Jaipur. 
 
 The prison guards have been demanding that their pay scale should be similar to that of   constables.
