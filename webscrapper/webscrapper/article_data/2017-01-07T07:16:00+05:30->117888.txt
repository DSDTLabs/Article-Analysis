Chandigarh: The wushu team of Chandigarh Police won one gold and one bronze   in All India Police Judo Cluster games at Kolkata on Friday. 

 The equestrian team of Chandigarh Police won one gold medal in single event. 
 
 Now, the total number of gold medals reached to three with one silver for Chandigarh Police in equestrian events.
