Pimpri Chinchwad: Union minister of state for finance Shivpratap Shukla distributed 325 free   to divyangjans (persons with special abilities) under the Deendayal Upadhyaya Welfare Scheme at Sai Garden, Sambhajinagar, in Akurdi on Friday.

 The programme was organised by the social justice ministry and Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation. Shukla said the finance ministry was providing financial help to divyangjans.
 
 He said 13 crore people had benefitted from mudra loan. Chinchwad MLA Laxman Jagtap said 3% of total funds for civic budget and Rs 2,000 pension per month were given to divyangjans.
