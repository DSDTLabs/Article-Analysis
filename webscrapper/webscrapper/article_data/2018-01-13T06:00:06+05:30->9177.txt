KOLKATA:   on Friday reserved judgment on the PIL filed by  , ex-VC of  , challenging Calcutta University’s decision to confer honorary DLitt on CM  . 

 Petitioner’s counsel Bikash Ranjan Bhattacharya argued: “It is a case of nepotism and the person is unfit to receive such an honour.” 
 
 Advocate general Kishor Dutta said the petition was not maintainable. Shaktinath Mukherjee, appearing for CU, said: “It is a discretion of CU. So, no question of illegality or wrongdoing arises in awarding the honour to the CM.”
