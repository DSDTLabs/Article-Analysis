Sreenu Vaitla’s movie ‘Mister’ starring Varun Tej,   and   is all set to release on 14th April and the director is busy shuffling to various media channels for promoting his film aggressively.

  In one of the movie promotional interviews, director   said that he is extremely sure that Mister’s box-office collections will be a success. He further added that his portrayal of the film is such that it will impress both the youth and family audience.
 
 ‘Mister’ will have Sreenu Vaitla’s trademark kind of comedy which is laced with an interesting triangular love story. The film has Lavanya Tripathi and Hebah Patel playing the lead roles.
