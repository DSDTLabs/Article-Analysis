VISAKHAPATNAM: The   (Vuda) has received 6,345 applications under the   (LRS) of which 2,311 applications have been put on hold due to improper plans, documents and road facilities, while 332 applications have been rejected due to various reasons, said   vice-chairman T  .

 Addressing the media here on Friday, the Vuda VC said so far permissions have been given to 135 applications. Regarding the pending applications, Vuda has issued notices and permissions will be given once all the necessary information and documents are produced before February 20, he added.
 
 The VC said six teams of officials from 12 zones have been verifying the applications and Vuda has been directed by the government to take a decision regarding all the applications by the end of February.
