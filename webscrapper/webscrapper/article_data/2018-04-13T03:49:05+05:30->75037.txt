NEW DELHI: Keeping in mind recent incidents of vandalism of statues and violence witnessed during ‘Bharat Bandh’ organised on April 2 and 10, the Union   has issued a fresh advisory ahead of   on April 14, asking states to take all precautionary measures to prevent any untoward incident.

 
 “The   has asked that patrolling should be intensified in all sensitive locations to ensure that no loss of life and property takes place and prohibitory orders may be issued wherever necessary,” the ministry said in the communication sent to all state governments. The states were also advised to activate community policing.
