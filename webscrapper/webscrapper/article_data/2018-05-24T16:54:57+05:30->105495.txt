NEW DELHI: The   ( ) declared the results of annual examinations of BA/BCom/BSc I, II & III year on its official website on May 24, 2018.

 The results was announced by Vice-Chancellor, OU Prof S Ramachandram at OU Guest House at 12 noon.
 
 
 
 
 The students who have appeared for the examination can check their results on the official website of the university - osmania.ac.in and also on the third party website - manabadi.com
 
 The Osmania University BA/B.Com/B.Sc exams were held from March 6 to April 9, 2018. 
 
 The students can follow the step-by-step process given below to check their Osmania University BA/B.Com/B.Sc exam result:
 
 1) Open the official website of the Osmania University (OU) - osmania.ac.in or Manabadi website - manabadi.com
 
 2) On the homepage, you will find link for the "Osmania University BA/B.Com/B.Sc exam results"
 
 3) Click on the results link 
 
 4) It will redirect you to the results page
 
 5) Enter your details such as name, roll number, select course and click on sumbit
 
 6) Your result will be displayed online
