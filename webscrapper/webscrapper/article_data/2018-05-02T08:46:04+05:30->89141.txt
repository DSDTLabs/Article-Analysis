CHANDIGARH:   police have arrested the youth who had allegedly forced a girl student of B Sc (I) to commit suicide in   of the city on Sunday night. The accused, identified as Sidharth alias Manni, was arrested on Monday night. 

 Sub-inspector  , the investigating officer, said Sidharth was produced before a local court on Tuesday that remanded him to judicial custody. During interrogation, he confessed to have been troubling the girl. Also, technical investigations have confirmed that the accused had called the girl on Sunday.
 
 The 19-year-old had committed suicide by hanging from the ceiling fan in her house. The girl's mother had accused Sidharth of harassing Neeru over phone.
