GANDHINAGAR: The   (ATS) is facing an acute shortage of staff. In his reply to a question asked by Congress leader   in the assembly, Gujarat minister of state (home)   said the sanctioned strength of the ATS (according to a April 1993 order) is 74. Of this, 42 posts are lying vacant.

 According to the minister, two posts of superintendent of police, three of police inspectors, 13 of police sub-inspectors, 14 of head constables and 10 posts of driver-cum-armed constables were vacant in the ATS.
 
 Senior police officials said the sanctioned strength of the ATS (74) is of 1993. “The situation is quite different now and the ATS needs more personnel to counter underworld and terrorist activities in the state,” a police officer said.
