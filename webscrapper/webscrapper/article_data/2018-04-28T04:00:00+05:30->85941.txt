Rajkot: The Rajkot Municipal Corporation (RMC) will issue birth and death certificate in each ward to provide more facility to citizens. In the initial phase, this facility has been started in wards 1, 2,4, 9 and 14. In the coming days these facilities will be provided in all 18 wards of RMC.
