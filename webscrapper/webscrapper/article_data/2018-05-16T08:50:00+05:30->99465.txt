MOHALI: Two men on a motorcycle   a gold chain from a school teacher in Anand Vihar, Baltana, on Tuesday afternoon.

 Alku Luthra, 35, was returning home after buying some items from nearby shops when the snatchers struck around 3pm. As she reached near her house, the accused came from the rear and pulled her chain and sped away. Police sources said the incident was caught on CCTV cameras installed nearby.
 
 Baltana police post in-charge Satinder Singh said as soon as they received information about the incident, they reached the spot and started investigation. “We have scanned footage from CCTV cameras but the images of the accused were not clear. They will, however, be arrested soon.”
