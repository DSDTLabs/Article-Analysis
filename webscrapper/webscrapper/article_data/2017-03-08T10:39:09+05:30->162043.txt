 A statement issued by   secretary AMP Jamaludeen said Speaker P Dhanapal has summoned the assembly.
 
 "The speaker has summoned the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly to meet at 10.30am on March 16 in the assembly chamber, in exercise of the powers vested in him under Rule 26(1) of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules," the statement said.
 
 Under Rule 181(1) of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules, Governor C Vidyasagar Rao has appointed March 16 as the date for presentation of budget for the year 2017-2018 and March 23 as the date for presentation of vote on account for the year 2017-2018. 
  
 Under Rule 189 (1) of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Rules, the governor has also appointed March 23 as the date of presentation of the final supplementary estimates for the year 2016-2017, it said.
