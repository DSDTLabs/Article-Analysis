New Delhi: The East Delhi Municipal Corporation on Friday approved the hike of minimum wage for it workers from Rs 9,000 to Rs 13,445 with effect from April this year. 

 The move came after the Delhi government hiked the wages. Now all employees engaged with   — sanitation workers on contract, security guards and domestic breeding checkers and other employees — will get the hike in their wages.
 
 The EDMC house also approved the rollback of hike proposed in the property tax, which was recommended by the third municipal valuation committee (MVC) last year and was adopted by the corporation to improve its financial health.
