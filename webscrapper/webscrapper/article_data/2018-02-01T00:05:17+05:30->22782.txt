Nagpur: The Nagpur Municipal Corporation ( ) in association with All India Institute of Local Self-Government organized a   to guide   about various policies related to regularization of slums under Equi City Project. Total 98 corporators were present at the workshop.

 The NMC has started the process to regularize slums and distribute ownership titles to slum-dwellers. Therefore, the workshop was organized to ensure no confusions among corporators and slum-dwellers.
 
 Mayor Nanda Jichkar said the corporators can easily solve citizens’ problems if they have detail knowledge about the policies. “Workshop will benefit corporators in resolving slum-dwellers’ confusions,” she said.
 
 Jayant Pathak from All India Institute of Local Self-Government, Leena Buddhe from NGO Centre for Sustainable Development and Shubhangi Padole guided the corporators and also replied to their queries on the occasion.
 
 Prominent among 98 corporators were standing committee chairman Sandip Jadhav, Sunil Agrawal, Praful Gudadhe, Dayashankar Tiwari, Avinash Thakre, Manoj Sable, Divya Ghurde and many others.
