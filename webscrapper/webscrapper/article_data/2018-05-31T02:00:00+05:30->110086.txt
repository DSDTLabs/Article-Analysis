PATNA: At least 50 Class XII students along with their parents staged a protest at the Central Board of Secondary Education’s (CBSE) regional office in Patna on Wednesday.

 The protesters claimed they had performed well in all the subjects, but were failed in one or two subjects when the Class XII results were declared on Saturday last. 
 
 One of the students said, “I secured good scores in the JEE-Main, but now getting admitted to National Institute of Technology is not possible because I failed in mathematics.”
