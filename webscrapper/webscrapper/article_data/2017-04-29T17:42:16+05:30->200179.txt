The frenzy over ‘Baahubali 2: The Conclusion’ has made even popular research engine   completely off-guard. The most searched topic on Google on Friday was, 'why did Kattappa kill Baahubali?', says Google trends data. It even reached 200 per cent of the search limit, added experts.

 Bhallala Deva (Rana Daggubati) marrying   and review of the   flick were some of the other popular searches in Google. Queries on free download of ‘Baahubali-2’ also topped the search. 
 
 The Google Trends page showed that most number of searches came from Vijayawada, Vishakhapatnam, Hyderabad and Ahmedabad in last three months. The movie starring  , Rana, Anushka and Sathyaraj hit headlines even before the release on Friday.
