Surat: Pellets of air gun were removed from the stomach of 12-year-old Suraj alias Abhijit R Pandey, who was shot at by an unidentified person when he was standing under a mango tree on April 25 in Vapi. The boy was operated upon at Vinoba Bhave Civil Hospital in the Union territory of Silvassa. Number of pellets had penetrated the boy’s stomach and he was taken to Silvassa where he was successfully operated upon.

 It is believed that the owner of the mango orchard had fired at the boy to scare him away. Dungra police said they have registered a complaint under IPC section 307 against an unidentified person and begun investigation. The police complaint was registered by the boy’s father on Thursday.
