 Katupalli Agnes Anu, a daily wage worker, has two daughters and lives opposite Gangisetti Lokesh's house at Prashant Nagar in Payakapuram area. Lokesh claims to have fallen in love with Anu's second daughter, 15, and lured her from her house on March 24. He forced himself on her and sexually abused her. Lokesh, who is already married, has a son. Anu approached Nunna police and filed a complaint against Lokesh, who went absconding with her daughter. Lokesh was caught at Pipulla junction. North ACP Ramesh Babu is investigating the case.
