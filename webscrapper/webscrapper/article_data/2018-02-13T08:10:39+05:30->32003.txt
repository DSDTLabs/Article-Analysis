CHANDIGARH: A 22-year-old girl whose two-wheeler was hit by a speeding autorickshaw on the Sector-35/36 dividing road on Sunday, succumbed to her injuries at Postgraduate Institute of Medical Education and Research (PGIMER).

  Her pillion rider and classmate, 21 is undergoing treatment at PGIMER and is reported to be out of danger. 
 
 As per information,  , a resident of Bhiwani, Haryana, was riding the scooter. She was living in a PG accommodation in Sector 31. 
 
 Her pillion rider and classmate   is a resident of Sector 35.   was an MA first year student of MCM DAV College, Sector-36 and so is Mahima. 
 
 In her complaint,   infomed cops they both went to the Elante Mall on Sunday evening and were returning home when the mishap happened. When Himani took a turn towards Sector 35 from   to drop Mahima at her place, an autorickshaw coming from behind hit them and they both fell on the road.
 
 Both the girls were rushed to PGIMER where Himani succumbed to her head injury. Mahima noted down the registration number of the autorickshaw as PB-65-AK-0364.
 
 A case under section 279 (rash driving or riding on a public way), 337 (causing hurt by act endangering life or personal safety of others) and 304-A (causing death by negligence) of the Indian Penal Code (IPC)was registered against an unknown person at the Sector 36 police station.
 
 The police officials called up the family members of the deceased and the body would be handed over to them on Tuesday after post-mortem. 
 
 Investigation officer, assistant sub-inspector,  , said, “The other girl is fine and will be discharged soon. We have registered a case and further investigation is on.”
