AHMEDABAD: A truck carrying passengers’ boarding stairs got stuck at the domestic terminal in   (SVPI) airport in the city. The incident occurred on Saturday when the truck was passing through a passage at terminal-2 in the airport.

 Despite a warning at the terminal building stating that maximum allowable height of vehicles entering the passage was 4.70 metre, the truck tried to use it. 
 
 Manoj Gangal, director of SVPI airport, said that he has ordered an investigation in the issue. However, he said that incident was not big as the truck carrying the passengers’ boarding stairs just touched the terminal building.
 
 No damage to property or life was reported in the incident.
