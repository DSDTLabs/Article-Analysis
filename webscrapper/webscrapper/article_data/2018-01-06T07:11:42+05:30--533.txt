SHIMLA: Signalling zero tolerance for crimes against women,   chief minister   on Friday announced to set up a round-the-clock  . He also announced to set up a special crime cell in all three police range offices to report any crime against women. 

 The chief minister made the announcements while interacting with media persons here on Friday. He said the Atal Helpline for zero tolerance against corruption would be also be launched soon.
