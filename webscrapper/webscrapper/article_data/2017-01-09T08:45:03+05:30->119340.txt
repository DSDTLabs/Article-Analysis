KOLKATA: A 58-year-old resident of Haltu was roughed up by an auto driver after he raised voice against the rash driving by another auto driver of the same route. Victim Santo Saha was returning home from zoo with his grandson (9) when an auto crossed them dangerously near   and almost hit the kid around 5pm on Sunday. After this, Saha yelled at the driver.

 When they were about to board another auto of the same route, its driver hit Saha on his face and eyes and also kicked him. Even though a police kiosk is located nearby, the driver managed to flee. Saha was later treated at  . Police said a search was on for the driver.
