 The IPO, which would open on February 12, would close on February 15. The IPO consists of a fresh issue aggregating up to Rs 725 crore and an offer for sale of up to 13,428,251 shares of the company by the promoter, Union Investments Private Limited.
 
 The company proposes to utilise the net proceeds of the fresh issue for repayment or pre-payment of debt, purchase of medical equipment and general corporate purposes. The anchor investor allocation will be a day prior to the bid/offer opening date (February 9).
