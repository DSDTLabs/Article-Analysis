PUNE: A truck driver was booked on Sunday for escaping from the revenue officials’ custody after being intercepted for transporting sand illegally, in Bhairobanala on  , between 1pm and 4pm. 

 The truck was detained after the driver failed to show documents of the sand being legally purchased. The officials conducted a panchanama and asked the driver to accompany them to the Haveli Pranth office for impounding the vehicle.
 
 Sub-inspector   of the Wanowrie police said, “The driver first refused to drive the truck, saying there was a technical snag. When he saw the officials’ attention had diverted, he started the vehicle and fled.”
 
 The officials led by Sachin Kalel lodged a complaint against the driver in the evening. 
 
 “When we contacted the vehicle’s owner, he claimed that the driver did not get in touch with him and fled with the sand worth Rs2 lakh,”   said. 
 
 The driver has been booked under Section 353 (assault or criminal force to deter public servant from discharging duty) of the IPC.
