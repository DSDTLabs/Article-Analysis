DELHI: A massive fire broke out at a shoe factory near Delhi's Peeragarhi area early on Friday.

 The   rushed 20 fire tenders to the spot to contain the blaze. 
 
 According to reports, a fireman has suffered burn injuries in the incident. 
 
 Officials said that the blaze started at about 2.30am but fortunately no one was in the factory premises at the time. The blaze englufed three floors of the factory. 
 
 The cause of fire is yet to be ascertained.
