 The accused, identified as Arvind Kumar and Rajkumar, are Gurgaon-based businessmen. 
 
 They were on their way to Lajpat Nagar when they were stopped by the police at a checkpoint. 
 
 According to police, they had installed a checkpoint at the Lajpat Nagar area. “During a patrol check, we seized Rs 10 lakh from the car. During interrogation, the accused revealed that they had brought the money to be exchanged from someone they know,” said Anup Singh, ASP, Sahibabad.
 
 Meanwhile, the income tax department has been notified about the incident. The accused have been questioned further.
