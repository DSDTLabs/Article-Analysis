Calangute: A West Bengal native was arrested at Calangute in the early hours of Sunday with narcotic substances worth over Rs 1 lakh.  Calangute PI Jivba Dalvi said they received a tip-off that a man be delivering narcotics to a customer near the St Anthony Chapel junction, following which they conducted a raid and nabbed the accused, 32-year-old Gokul Maity. Maity was found to be in possession of heroin worth Rs 80,000 and ganja worth Rs 25,000.  He has been placed under arrest. Police have registered an offence under sections 20 (b) (ii) (A) and 21(a) of the NDPS Act. tnn
