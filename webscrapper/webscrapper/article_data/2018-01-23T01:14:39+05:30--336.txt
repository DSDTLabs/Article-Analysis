NOIDA: The protesters on   had damaged a cab apart from destroying the toll booths on Sunday afternoon, the police said. Asim, an original resident of Vijay Nagar in Ghaziabad, was on his way from Safdarjung to Greater Noida via DND Flyway in his white Wagon-R when he was caught in the protest.

 The protesters forced him to stop the cab. “The accused surrounded me and smashed the window panes of the cab. They also snatched my purse and mobile phone and tried to flee with the vehicle,” he said.
 
 However, a police team reached the spot soon and the accused fled the spot. The police took the driver to district hospital. He was discharged after first aid. The police then brought the cab to police station for completing legal formalities.
