MARGAO: Konkan Railway will undertake the upkeep and upgradation work of its bridges over River Zuari and River Mandovi between May 4 and 28. 

 As a result, several trains along this route have been cancelled, some partially cancelled while few will be regulated at various stations during the period, says a press note issued by the   ( ).
 
 The 50102 Margao-Ratnagiri passenger train will remain cancelled on the following dates - May 5, 7, 8, 10, 11, 13, 14, 16, 17, 19, 20, 21, 22, 23 and 25 to 29. The 50101 Ratnagiri-Margao passenger train will remain non-operational on May 5, 6, 8, 9, 11, 12, 14, 15, 17, 18, 20, 21, 23, 24, 26, 27, 29 and 30.
 
 The 70104/70101 Karwar-Pernem-Karwar ‘DEMU’ passenger will remain partially cancelled between Verna-Pernem-Verna on May 5, 7, 8, 11, 14, 21, 22, 25, 26, 28 and 29.
 
 The 12051/12052 Dadar-Margao-Dadar ‘Janshatabdi Express’ will remain partially cancelled between Karmali-Margao-Karmali on May 7, 14, 20 and 21. It will depart from Karmali at 2.55 pm. The train will also remain partially cancelled between Thivim-Margao-Thivim on May 27 and 28 and will depart from Thivim at 3.10pm. 
 
 The 22119/22120 CSMT-Karmali ‘Tejas’ Express will remain partially cancelled between Thivim-Karmali-Thivim on May 27. It will depart from Thivim at 2.45 pm.
 
 The 11085 LTT-Margao ‘Double Decker’ Express will be regulated by 20 minutes at Karmali on May 4 and 11; by 45 minutes at Karmali and Thivim on May 20 and 26 respectively; by 60 minutes at Thivim on May 25 and by one hour and 35 minutes at Thivim on May 27.
 
 The 10103 CSTM-Margao ‘Mandovi Express’ will be regulated by 20 minutes at the Karmali,   and Thivim railway stations on May 20, 25 and 29 respectively; by 30 minutes at Pernem station on May 26 and by 55 minutes at Pernem station on May 27, states the press note.
