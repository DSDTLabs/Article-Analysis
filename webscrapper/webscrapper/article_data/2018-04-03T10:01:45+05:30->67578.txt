MADURAI: The Madurai bench of the Madras high court has declined to take suo moto cognisance of the   protests in the state and ban them, saying that it could not interfere in the issue.

 One K K Ramesh, who appeared before the first bench comprising justice C T   and justice A M Basheer Ahamed sought the court to take suo moto and ban the protests over the  , stating that it was affecting normal life.
 
 The petitioner stated that protests were continuing for over a week now throughout the state, causing much inconvenience to the public. Bandhs were being announced and shops closed. Apart from the general public, students who were sitting for various examinations were also being affected.
 
 The bench declined the petition saying that it could not interfere in the matter.
