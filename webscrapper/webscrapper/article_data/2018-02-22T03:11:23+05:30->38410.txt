NAVI MUMBAI: The   police has registered a kidnapping case after three minor school boys and a minor school girl together went missing on Tuesday. They had gone to school at 10.30 am but did not return home. 

 The four schoolchildren are aged 12-14 years and are Class 6 students. They reside in   village. 
 
 Their parents work as daily wage labourers and goods vehicle drivers, and hail from UP. The police suspect that the minor girl has eloped with one of the boys with whom she was in a relationship, while the other boys have helped them to elope. 
 
 It was learnt that the minors had gone to   railway station from where they have booked tickers for Varanasi.
