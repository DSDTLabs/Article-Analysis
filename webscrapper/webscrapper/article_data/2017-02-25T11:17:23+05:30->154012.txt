On February 24, actor-director Lal revealed that the Malayalam actress, who was abducted by a group of goons on February 17, was on her way to meet her friend and artiste   when the incident happened.

 Remya, who has been friends with the actress for long, told the media that the actress is happy about the police arresting the accused in the case. She added that the artiste, who has begun with the shoot of her next film today, is hoping that justice will be ensured by our law and order system at the earliest. 
 
 As per reports, the actress has been staying with Remya post the incident and she is happy that all major film industries have come out in support of her.
