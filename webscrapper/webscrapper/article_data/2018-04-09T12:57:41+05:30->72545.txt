Police in Bhubaneswar on Sunday arrested six Congress workers, one of them the president of the state   wing, for allegedly pulling down a poster of Prime Minister   during a protest over the hike in fuel prices at a filling station near Delta Square. 

 Youth Congress chief  , along with five of his supporters, were taken to   police station on charges of damaging public property (the petrol filling station), obstructing police personnel while they were discharging their duty and gesturing indecently at the direction of the police.
 
 Deputy commissioner of police   said the workers passed through some stretches, breaching prohibitory orders of 144 of the Code of Criminal Procedure.
