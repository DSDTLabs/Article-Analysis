After entertaining Tollywood audience with blockbuster hits like Chuutalabbayi and multi-starrer film Samanthakamani, renowned actor Aadi will soon join hands with television star Prabhakar. The magic which the duo would unveil on screen is eagerly awaited by Aadi's fans. The movie would be bankrolled by Geetha Arts 2 under the prestigious UV Creations banner

 As per sources, Prabhakar had his eyes on Allu Sirish for his directorial debut since almost a year but Allu Sirish was unable to allot time for the television star due to his packed schedule. Apparently, when Prabhakar narrated the comedy entertainer script to Aadi, he instantly agreed to work with him.
