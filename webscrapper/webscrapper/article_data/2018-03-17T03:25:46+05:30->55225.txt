GURUGRAM: A 21-year-old   of Northcap University, Sector 23, has alleged that he has been getting threat   from   numbers for the last one week. An FIR has been registered at Farrukhnagar police station.

 According to cops, Yogesh Kumar, a resident of Joniyawas village, said the callers used abusive language and threatened to kill him. At first, he thought these were prank calls, but when they didn’t stop, he approached police.
 
 An FIR was registered against unidentified accused under Section 506 (criminal intimidation) of the IPC at Farrukhnagar police station. Cops are trying to trace all three mobile numbers that were used.
 
 “We have put the mobile numbers on surveillance and are investigating the case. The accused will be arrested soon,” said inspector Karan Singh, SHO, Farrukhnagar police station.
