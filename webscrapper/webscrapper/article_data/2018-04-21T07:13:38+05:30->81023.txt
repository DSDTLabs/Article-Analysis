AGRA: Three days after a 7-year-old girl was raped and murdered at a wedding ceremony in   district, a 9-year-old was found allegedly raped and killed in district early on Friday morning. 

 The crime was committed in a village under   police jurisdiction, while the victim and her family were attending the engagement ceremony of their neighbour’s son. 
 
 The girl’s body was found at around 3:15 am in a field nearly 200 metres from her home. The victim’s father said, “She went missing around 9pm. We found her body six hours later. She was raped and murdered by Pintu, a local youth.”
