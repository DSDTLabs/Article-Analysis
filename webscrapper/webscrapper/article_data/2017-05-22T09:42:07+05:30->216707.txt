LUDHIANA: A 23-year-old homemaker ended her life after she was allegedly sexually harassed by her neighbour in  . The accused is a 22-year-old employee of a factory.

 In his police complaint, the deceased’s husband of six years said on Saturday around 8.30am, he and his father went to buy medicines, and his mother had gone to attend a funeral in the neighbourhood.
 
 “After sometime, my wife called on my mobile phone and said that the accused had forcibly entered our house and was trying to sexually assault her,” he added. 
 
 He said that when they reached home, they found his wife in an unconscious condition and noticed that she had consumed poison. They rushed her to a private hospital in Doraha.
 
 She breathed her last on Saturday afternoon, after which they informed the police. Sahnewal police reached the spot and started probe. They have booked the accused under Section 306 (abetment to suicide) of the IPC. 
 
 Investigating officer assistant sub-inspector   said the accused was on the run and raids were being conducted to arrest him. “Preliminary investigation suggested that the woman consumed celphos, but the exact cause of death would be known after the post-mortem examination,” he added. 
 
 A cop said, “The husband of the deceased said his wife had told him that the accused had harassed her on several occasions in the past, too. The deceased didn’t have a child and was married at the age of 17 years.”
