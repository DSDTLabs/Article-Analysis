MYSURU: Karnataka Rajya Raitha Sangha and   secretary Badagalapura Nagendra said that a bronze statue of farmers’ activist professor MD   will be installed at Maddur Town on February 13.

 Speaking to reporters here on Wednesday, Nagendra said that the statue will be unveiled by revenue minister   and the programme will be inaugurated by Melkote MLA KS Puttanaiah. Association president Chukki Nanjundaswamy will preside over the function.
 
 Progressive writer  , anti-corruption activist SR Hiremath and   coordinator Yaduveer Singh is expected to attend the programme. “The statue which is being installed at TAPCMS premises in Maddur town will be unveiled and later, a state-level meeting has been organized with farmers,” he said.
