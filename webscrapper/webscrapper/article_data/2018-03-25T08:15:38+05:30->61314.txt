LUCKNOW: Samajwadi Party chief   on Saturday raised doubts on whether Uttar Pradesh chief minister   was serious about observing fast during Navratra.

 Speaking at a meeting of party MLAs and leaders here on Saturday, Akhilesh said: “I saw him having boondi laddoos with other BJP leaders at   after the election results were declared on Friday.” Akhilesh added that he had been observing the nineday fast strictly along with his wife and Kannauj MP Dimple Yadav.
 
 
 After the RS elections on Friday evening, the chief minister along with his ministerial colleagues, party president   and Union minister   broke into celebrations, offering boondi laddoos to each other.
