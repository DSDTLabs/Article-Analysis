THANE: Six people from Mumbra and Kalyan allegedly involved in stealing and sedating cows meant to be butchered for beef consumption were arrested in Thane. 

 A complaint at Wagle Estate police station about theft of a cow led the police to Mumbra and nailed the six ---Mohammad Qureshi (24), Monis Qureshi (20), Irfan Mulla (35) and Fazal Qureshi (22) Laik Qureshi (32) and   (32). 
 
 The police kept vigil after a tip-off that some “cow thieves” were to reach Mumbra and nabbed them. Their interrogation revealed that they had stolen two cows, tranquilized them and sold them to butchers in Kalyan.
