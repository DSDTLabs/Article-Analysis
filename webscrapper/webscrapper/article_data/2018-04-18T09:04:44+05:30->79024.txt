VARANASI: Noted   poet and shayer   passed away on Tuesday after a prolonged illness. He was 67. People assembled at his residence in Ajmeri muhalla in   to pay homage to the departed soul.

 He had been suffering from ailment for a couple of months due to infection in his leg. According to family members, his health condition started deteriorating on Monday evening, and he breathed his last on Tuesday.
 
 Mufti   popularly known as Anjum Jaunpuri began his poetic career by reciting his ghazal at a mushayara in Bana in 1972. Since then he took part in a number of programmes, and also attended an international mushayara held in   (Pakistan) in 1981. TNN
