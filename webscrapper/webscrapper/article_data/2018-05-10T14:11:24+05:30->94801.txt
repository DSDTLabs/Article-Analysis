PANAJI: Panjim Hotel And Traders Association (PHATA) president  , on Wednesday said revenue minister   has assured that a no-objection certificate ( ) from the fire and emergency services would not be mandatory for renewal of licences for small traders. 

 The order mandating that small traders and professionals obtain an NOC from the fire department was issued by the collector. PHATA, which had demanded that it be withdrawn, called on the revenue minister to intervene.
