ROHTAK: Two days before the `Balidan Diwas' planned by   with sitins across the state,   state president Subhash Barala on Friday reached Charkhi Dadri sit-in and extended an open invitation to Jat community leaders for the discussion on their demands.

 Addressing the gathering, Barala said that dialogue was the way out to solve the ongoing protesters' demand and assured that the state government would consider them sympathetically.
 
 Responding to the urge of the AIJASS members about extending support to their demands, Barala said that not only him but the state government was ready to accept all the demands of the Jat community leaders within the ambit of law.
