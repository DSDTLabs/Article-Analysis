INDORE: Audyogik Kendra Vikas Nigam ( ) has been granted an interim relief by   (ITAT) after it agreed to pay Rs 35 crore of the Rs 158 crore fine imposed for non-payment of dues. 

 The Income Tax department levied the tax on the industry department for its earnings through premiums and lease rents earned on land. After AKVN failed to clear tax dues, the department seized their bank accounts of Thursday, paralyzing their financial transactions.
 
 On Friday, AKVN requested for a review and a breathing period so that the seized bank accounts can be released to carry out standing payments to dealers and contractors for ongoing industrial development works.
