Raju Murugan’s Joker, which has bagged the award for the Best Film in Tamil, is now all set to be remade in other languages. The team of   which included writer-director  , the film's lead actor  , music director   and the film’s produce   met the media after the awards were announced. 

 The film has won the award for Best Tamil Film and Sundar Ayyar, who sang the song 'Jasmine-u' has bagged the Best Male Playback singer for song. The film’s producer said that he expected the film’s hero to bag a National Award as well. He added that he has plans to remake in the film in other languages.
