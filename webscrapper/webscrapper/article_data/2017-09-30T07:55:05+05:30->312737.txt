AMBALA: Ambala police on Tuesday reportedly arrested two more members of Dera Sacha Sauda chief Gurmeet Ram Rahim Singh’s ‘suicide squad’. 

 A team of crime investigation agency-1 arrested Joginder Singh, a resident of   village in Kurukshetra district, and   alias Santra of Barara in Ambala district on the basis of information given by others arrested in a sedition case.
 
 So far police have arrested eight persons, who were part of the dera’s ‘suicide squad’. 
 
 The first to be arrested in the case was one Ashok  , who was held on August 25 and booked under sections 124-A (sedition) and 188 (disobedience to order duly promulgated by public servant) of the IPC.
 
 On the basis of the information given by Ashok, police had arrested another accused Puneet Kumar and also recovered Rs 38 lakh from the hosue of another dera follower   on August 26. 
 
 Parao station house officer (SHO) Bhushan Das said, “Joginder was produced before the court that sent him to three-day police remand. The other accused is yet to be produced before the court.”
