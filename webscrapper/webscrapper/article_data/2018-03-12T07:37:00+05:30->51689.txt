Various teacher organizations, including those of Sarv Shiksha Abhiyan ( )   (RMSA) and computer teachers, held protests across the state on Sunday and burnt state government effigies. Coming out in their support, AAP has decided to raise their demands in the upcoming budget session of the assembly.

 The teachers were protesting against not regularization of the services of various categories of teachers and asking computer teachers to work at Rs 10,300 per month for three years if they want services to be regularized.
