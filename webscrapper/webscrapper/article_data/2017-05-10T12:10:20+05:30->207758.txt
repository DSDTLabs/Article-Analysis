 Villagers grilled panchayat secretary Hanumant Borkar as to why the resolutions had not been followed up, as assured by the panchayat and local MLA, Antonio  . “We had withdrawn our agitation after the   had assured us of prompt action on May 2, but after a wait of seven days we had no other option,” Arturo D’Souza, an activist said.
