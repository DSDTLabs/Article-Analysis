Actress  , famous for the ‘Lux Papa’ song in ‘Narasimha Naidu’ is keen on returning to Tollywood. The actress was last seen in Bollywood films like ‘Lakshmi’ and ‘Dhanak’.

 In one of her recent interviews, Flora Saini said that she appreciated the Telugu film industry for its punctuality and would love to get back to Tollywood. She also mentioned that she had learnt to speak Telugu fluently and will take up Telugu films if she gets good roles. 
 
 Flora Saini will soon be seen playing a key role in the Bollywood film ‘Begum Jaan’ starring   in the lead role and she also has a couple of web-series to her credit.
