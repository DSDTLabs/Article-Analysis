PANAJI: Goa Tourism Development Corporation (GTDC) on Thursday organised a soft skill training programme for tourists taxi operators in  . 

 The training session was conducted based on the feedback received after the recently convened meeting between the tourism minister Manohar ‘Babu’ Azgaonkar, chairman GTDC Nilesh Cabral, MPT tourist taxi association and tourism stakeholders. 
 
 Presented by Sanjeev Sardessai, the session was aimed at giving tourist taxi operators an insight into Goa’s history. The session also dealt with understanding and addressing customer needs. 
 
 A written test was conducted and certificates were given to those who cleared the it.
