NEW DELHI: A 22-year-old   working as a household help in Noida allegedly committed suicide by jumping into the   river from DND bridge on Sunday. Police said that her body was recovered after searching for nearly one hour. No suicide note was recovered.

 The incident was reported around 4pm when the woman, later identified as Aarti, reached the bridge on a cycle rickshaw. Eyewitnesses said that she was seen speaking to the conductor of a bus. The conductor told police that he informed the woman that the   had broken down. She then left her wallet, mobile phone and slippers on the pedestal of the bus and jumped into the river.
 
 The conductor said that he tried to stop her but she pushed him away before jumping. The police were informed about the incident after which a team reached the spot and conducted a search in the river.
