CHENNAI:   chief minister Edappadi K Palaniswami on Monday inaugurated   and   built by the highways department at a cost of Rs 87.24 crore.

 The projects opened are 17 bridges, one rail over bridge and office buildings, an official statement said. They included high-level bridges on the Tiruverkadu Sridevi Karumariamman Temple Road in Tiruvallur, across the Uthiracauvery river in Kollakottai village in Vellore, and across the Noyyal river connecting Thukkachi of Karur district and Vadivullamangalam of Erode district. 
 
 Buildings were inaugurated in Coimbatore, Kancheepuram, Namakkal, Ramanthapuram, Thanjavur, Dharmapuri and Virudhunagar districts. 
 
 A rail over bridge has come near Ennore Athipet railway stations.
 
 The chief minister laid foundation stones for the construction of four-lane 927m long railway bridge near Kancheepuram railway station at Rs 49.42 crore and a high level bridge across the Nandi river in Vellore district.
