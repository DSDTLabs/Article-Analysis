BELAGAVI: The trial of gangster  , accused of murdering a businessman, will be conducted through the   module by the   district principal and sessions court on Friday. This will be the first instance of an e-trial in the state. 

 Introduced for the first time in the country in Hyderabad in July last year, the e-court module has been adopted in Madurai and other centres.
 
  A video camera will be placed at the  , along with a large screen. The hearing will be presided over by Justice RJ Satish Singh.
