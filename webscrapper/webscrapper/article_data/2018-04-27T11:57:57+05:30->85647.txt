After receiving mammoth love and registering huge success at the box-office with his recent release ‘Bharat Ane Nenu’,   has added yet another feather to his cap. He joined the list of film stars who have their wax statues at the   in London. The 42-year-old actor announced the news through his Twitter handle. Mahesh thanked the artists who are working together in order to get the details of him. 

  He revealed: “Super happy to be a part of the prestigious Madame Tussauds :) :) Thanks to the team of artists for their attention to detail. Incredible!”
 
  
  
  Mahesh’s figure would be unveiled at Madame Tussauds, London where he is the second Tollywood star after the talented Prabhas to get a wax statue. His charming look would be immortalised there soon. The Madame Tussauds sculpting team specially travelled to Hyderabad to meet Mahesh Babu and required measurements were taken.
 
 
 
  
  
  Apart from Prabhas and Mahesh, the wax figure of Sathyaraj will also be on the display.
