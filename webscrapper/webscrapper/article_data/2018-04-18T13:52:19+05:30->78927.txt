AMRITSAR: State special operation cell (SSOC) of the   arrested two   and recovered 7kg of heroin from their possession on Tuesday. SSOC assistant inspector general (AIG) Amarjit Singh said that Ranjit Singh and Gursewak Singh were arrested from the Airport Road near Hayer village while they were waiting for a party to deliver the consignment of heroin. 

 The preliminary investigations revealed that both of them were part of gang of Jaggu Bhagwanpuria, who is at present lodged in the Hoshiarpur jail. Ranjit was earlier arrested in 2015 with 500g heroin and was out on bail, said AIG Singh. He said the heroin was to be sold locally. The origin of the narcotics was yet to be ascertained, he added.
