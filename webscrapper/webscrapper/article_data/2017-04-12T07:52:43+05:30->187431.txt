New Delhi:All Delhi Auto Taxi Transport Congress Union plans to boycott the upcoming municipal polls by not supporting any of the three prime parties ­ BJP , Congress and AAP .Union members have directed all the drivers to extend their support to independent candidates in their respective areas.
Kishan Verma, an auto union member, said that every election, auto and taxi drivers play an important role for the political parties but their demands are never fulfilled.
 
 Verma said, “BJP has ruled the corporations for ten years and AAP is ruling Delhi government from the past two years but none of them have supported us.“
 
 He said that development work across the city has been stalled and the condition of many areas where they reside is pathetic. He added that a meeting has been called on Wednesday with the other driver unions and there is a possibility that maximum drivers will extend their support.
