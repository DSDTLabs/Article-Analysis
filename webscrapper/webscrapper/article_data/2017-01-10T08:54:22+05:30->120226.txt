NEW DELHI: One terrorist was killed on Tuesday morning in an encounter with security forces in the   district of northern Kashmir.

 "One terrorist has been killed and one soldier has been injured during the gunfight in Bandipora," an army official said.
 
 The official said that while acting on specific intelligence inputs about the presence of militants in   village of Bandipora, security forces cordoned off the area and launched a search operation.
 
 While the forces were conducting the search operation, militants who were in hiding fired upon them, triggering the encounter.
 
 "The operation is in progress and more details are awaited," the army official said, adding that the identity and group affiliation of the slain terrorist is being ascertained.
