Vadodara: A police constable of Panchmahal police was found in an inebriated condition at SSG Hospital on Sunday.

 The policeman identified as Anandsinh Hirabhai had brought a prisoner of Godhra sub-jail to the prisoners’ ward of the hospital for treatment. Hirabhai was found having liquor outside the ward. When some passers-by started to click Hirabhai’s pictures, he started to walk away.
 
 Even as he was being clicked, Hirabhai continued to walk in an inebriated state and blabber something. A bottle of liquor was found in his possession. His photos and videos went viral on social media but no police complaint was registered against Hirabhai till late in the evening.
