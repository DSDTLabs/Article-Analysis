 Two passengers lodged a complaint against the train's loco pilot at the station. However, senior officials of the Pune railway division claimed that nobody fell from the train. Senior divisional commercial manager   said, "We spoke to the passengers and the drivers and found that nobody fell off the train."
 
 Sagar Sharma, who was on the train and one of the complainants, said, "The train was supposed to stop at Kedgaon, but entered the platform at high speed. All of a sudden brakes were applied. Many people lost balance and around 8-10 people fell on the platform and on the tracks on the other side." 
 
 Some passengers allegedly manhandled the driver after the train stopped. The station officials reportedly pacified them.
