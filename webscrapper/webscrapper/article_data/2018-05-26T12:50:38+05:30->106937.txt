CHANDIGARH: Four lifts were inaugrated at the Advanced Cardiac Centre,   on Friday by professor  , director, PGI. The work was sanctioned under phase-II plan and the expenditure is around Rs. 85 lakhs. With the addition of these lifts, total 9 lifts would be available for comfort of patients and attendants. Prof.  , head of the psychiatry department briefed that these lifts comply with people with disability norms.

 
 These lifts are with provision of glass slits to avoid claustrophobic feeling to users during breakdown/lift jam and also have facility of communication to security control desk in case of emergency. P S  , superintendent hospital engineer said that the department is working to replicate these facilities in other centres too. TNN
