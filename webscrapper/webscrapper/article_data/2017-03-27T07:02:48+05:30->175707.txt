A video clip of Congress vice-president   has surfaced and is making the rounds on  , in which Congress workers are seen complaining to him about how relatives of party members always corner tickets for Delhi civic elections. Gandhi is then seen promising them that “no ticket for the polls will be distributed to any relatives“.

 Confirming the authenticity of the video, Congress sources said the video surfaced on Sunday but the same thing had been announced at a motivation rally held by the party on March 7. However, the ticket distribution is still underway and nothing has been finalised yet.
