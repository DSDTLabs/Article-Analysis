CHENNAI : A medical shop owner and his two staff members have been arrested for selling painkiller tablets to juveniles without prescription at MGR Nagar. The juveniles, in turn, had been found to be selling the tablets at an exhorbitant price to those approaching them to get a high from taking the drugs. 

 Based on a specific tip, the police nabbed the juveniles for selling the drugs to youngsters. They told the police that they bought the tablets from Sri Om Medicals at MGR Nagar. The police inquired and arrested the shop owner,  , 32, of  , and his staff M  , 28, and C Saravanan, 37. 
 
 The three were arrested and remanded in prison.
