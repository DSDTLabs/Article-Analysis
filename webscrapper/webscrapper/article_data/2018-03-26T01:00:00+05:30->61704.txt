After the trailer of Iranian filmmaker Majid Majidi’s first Hindi film, ' ', released and garnered a positive response, the director is all set to give the audience another glimpse into the world of Amir ( ) and Tara ( ) with a new trailer. Produced by  , the movie marks Ishaan’s film debut and Malavika’s first Hindi film appearance. The lead actors will be seen playing siblings. 

 
  
 
 
 
 
 The film gives a sneak-peek into the life and experiences of the protagonists, their dreams and the daily hustle to achieve them. While the first trailer was an introduction to the world Majidi has created for Hindi audiences, the new trailer will take you closer to the lead characters. Talking about the second trailer, Sujay Kutty, Business Head, Zee Studios, says, “Human relationships, emotions and family form the base of every Majidi film. With 'Beyond The Clouds', he has taken this belief up a notch by showcasing that relationships are not bonded by blood, but by love. Zee Studios is proud to present two new gifted actors, Ishaan and Malavika, to the Hindi film industry, under the direction of  .”
 Producer Shareen Mantri Kedia of Namah Pictures shares, “After the positive response we received to our first trailer, we are excited to give everyone a closer look into the world of 'Beyond The Clouds'. Following Majidi’s vision, Ishaan and Malavika truly shine.” Co-producer   adds, “Ishaan’s performance is uninhibited yet nuanced. Malavika exudes grace and restrain that was needed in her performance. They have done complete justice to Majidi’s vision by breathing life into his story in an incredible manner.”
 The new trailer will release today at 11 am. The film, which releases on April 20, has music by AR Rahman, cinematography by Anil Mehta and Hindi dialogues by Vishal Bhardwaj.
