The state assembly elections of Punjab will be held on February 04, 2017. The ruling party of Punjab, Shiromani Akali Dal, has decided to continue its alliance with its decade long ally BJP.  In the 117-member house, the SAD is fighting on 94 seats while 23 seats have been given to the alliance partner, the BJP. The election results will be announced on March 11, 2017.
