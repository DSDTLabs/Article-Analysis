CHENNAI: Professor P Duraisamy, the new Vice Chancellor of University of Madras is an accomplished academic and has published extensively, say academics who have worked with him. Duraisamy is a former HOD of econometrics department in University of Madras and has a PhD from Paris university and post-doctorate from Yale University. 

 Apart from research in econometrics and labour market, he was recently a member of the pay commission of University Grants Commission (UGC) for university and college teachers salary revision. 
 
 He has also submitted a paper on 'reforms in higher education' to the UGC. 
 
 Duraisamy led the NAAC accreditation team in Madras University during the previous VC R Thandavan's tenure.
