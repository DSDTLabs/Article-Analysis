 After months of speculation, Sonam Kapoor and Anand Ahuja's families recently released a joint statement announcing the wedding. The statement read "The Kapoor & Ahuja families, take great joy and pride, in announcing the marriage of Sonam & Anand. The wedding will take place on the 8th of May in Mumbai. Since it is an intimate affair, we request you to respect the family's need for privacy. Thank you for all your blessings and love, as we celebrate this special moment in our lives."  The couple will get married in a traditional Sikh ceremony on May 8. A reception will be held on the same day in the evening. The functions will be held at Bandra Kurla Complex, Bandra Bandstand, and The Leela hotel.
