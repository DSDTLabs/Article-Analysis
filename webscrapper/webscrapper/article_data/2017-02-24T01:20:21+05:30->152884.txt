MUMBAI: Two men were arrested with over Rs 15   in   Rs 500 and Rs 1,000   in Vasai on Wednesday night. Acting on a tip-off, the Manickpur police laid a trap near the gurudwara on Ambadi Road in Vasai (west).

 The two suspects were caught with a gunny bag containing Rs 15 lakh in   Rs 500 notes and another Rs 30,000 in Rs 1,000 notes.
 
 While police did not reveal the names of the arrested men, it is believed that the cash belonged to a Bhayander-based jeweller and was brought to Vasai to be exchanged with new Rs 500  . The two men have been remanded in police custody. The notes have been handed over to the income tax department.
