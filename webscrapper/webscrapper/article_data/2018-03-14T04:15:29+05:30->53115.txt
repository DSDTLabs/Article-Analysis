GHAZIABAD: A 17-year-old girl was found dead with much of her clothing removed and   in Sihani Chungi of Ghaziabad on Monday.

 The girl, from Barpeta district in Assam, lived with her family near Morta village and worked as a rag-picker. The girl had been missing since 5pm on Monday, her family said.
 
 The girl’s father told TOI, “She returned home around 4pm from a relative’s place in Dasna and went out around 5pm to pick up waste materials. When she did not return home till 7.30pm, I went out with other relatives to search for her. Around 9.30pm, we found her body in the bushes on a vacant plot in  , which is located at some distance from our residence. We informed the police about the matter.”
 
 Vinod Kumar Pandey, SHO of   police station, said the police were informed around 10pm that a body was found in a vacant plot. “The girl’s head was smashed with a brick. The body was found by the girl’s father,” he added. 
 
 An FIR was lodged against unknown persons under sections 201 (causing disappearance of evidence of offence) and 302 (murder) of the IPC.
 
 “The body was sent for postmortem and the reports confirmed that it was not a case of sexual assualt. Investigations are on,” the SHO said.
