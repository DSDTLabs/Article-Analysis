Panaji:   Sanstha will mark the 75th birth anniversary of its founder, Jayant Athavale on May 18 with an awareness programme about the ‘Hindu nation’. “To make the society knowledgeable about Athavale’s mission, programmes have been organised under the ‘Hindu Rashtra-Jagruti Abhiyan’ throughout India,” state convener of Hindu Janjagruti Samiti, Manoj Solanki, said. tnn
