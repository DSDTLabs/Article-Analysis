NEW DELHI:   on Monday constituted an eight-member committee, including two experts from outside the institute, to look into complaints of  , which allegedly caused the death of a nurse. The AIIMS administration had on Sunday suspended five doctors — three senior residents and two junior residents — in this regard. 

 According to Dr Amit Gupta, AIIMS spokesman, the administration held discussions with the nursing union and resident doctor’s association on Monday, following which a decision was taken to constitute the committee under the chairmanship of professor SC Sharma, and medical superintendent Dr DK Sharma. “No decision has been taken regarding the suspension of the resident doctors,” he said.
 
 Rajbir Kaur (28), a nursing officer with AIIMS’ medicine department, was declared dead on Saturday after being on ventilator support for three weeks.
