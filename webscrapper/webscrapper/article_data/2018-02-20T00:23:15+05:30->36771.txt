Pune: Automobile wheel manufacturer Maxion Wheels is set to build a new manufacturing facility in Khed City, Pune district, at a cost of Rs 350 crore.

 Spread over a 20 acre, the plant will have an annual capacity of two million wheels in the first phase, which the company intends to boost by another four million eventually.
 
 Maxion Wheels’ spokesperson said the facility will account for over 500 new jobs in the region. “Maxion Wheels is committed to serving its customers’ growing demand for light vehicle aluminium wheels. We are excited about having our new plant in Khed City as it will enable us to strengthen our global footprint and strategically expand our presence in the region,” Pieter Klinkers, chief executive officer, Maxion Wheels, said on Monday.
