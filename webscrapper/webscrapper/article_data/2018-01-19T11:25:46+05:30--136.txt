KANPUR: Sixteen school children were injured, three of them critically, on Thursday when a truck rammed a school van in dense fog in  , police said. The three critically injured students were rushed to Trauma Centre in Lucknow while thirteen children were taken to Purwa community health centre. 

 "While we were crossing the highway, we heard a lound bang. We rushed to the spot and rescued some children. Most of them were rushed to the nearest CHC for treatment," said Manoj Singh, a local. 
 
 The truck was trying to overtake the school van when it hit the vehicle. Dense fog on highway led to the accident, said police.
