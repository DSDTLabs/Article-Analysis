DEHRADUN:   excise minister   paid a surprise visit to the excise department headquarters on   on Tuesday and was shocked to see several employees missing from duty. Pant has directed excise department secretary to initiate action against all such errant staff members.
 
  He also examined files in offices and record room and was unhappy that the files were not kept in an organized manner.
  
  “The minister was furious over wastage of electricity in the offices as he found air-conditioners switched on in vacant rooms,” said a senior excise official, who was present during the visit.
  
  Pant also asked officials to ensure cleanliness. He also checked the applications for liquor licences and said that they should be disposed of on priority.
  
  Earlier this month, chief minister   had conducted a surprise inspection of state secretariat.
