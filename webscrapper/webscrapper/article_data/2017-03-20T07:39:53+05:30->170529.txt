FEROZEPUR: A series of events are being organised by Shaheed  , Rajguru and   ahead of the martyrdom day of the three freedom fighters on March 23. All roads leading to historical   gurudwara were crowded with hundreds of people present at the venue to pay tributes to the martyrs and to take part in the opening of 5-day long patriotic plays and rural games events organized by Shaheed Bhagat Singh, Rajguru and   memorial society. A march started from the Saragarhi gurudwara while passing through different localities and bazaars in the city and cantonment areas, concluded at Hussainwala memorial where floral tributes were paid on the busts of Shaheed Bhagat Singh, Rajguru and Sukhdev.
