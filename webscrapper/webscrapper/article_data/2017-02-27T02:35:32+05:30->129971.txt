The second trailer of  's 'Jolly LLB 2' sees the beloved character turn a shade darker as he gears up to fight against the corrupt and powerful. After giving fans a peek into Jolly's happy-go-lucky side, the new trailer sees the angry young lawyer taking up the lone fight for justice. 

 The film that is allegedly based on true events that have taken place in the country, sees Akshay's character raising questions about the judiciary system and the innumerable lagging cases. Montages of violent clashes, powerful monologues, along with interesting court battles topped with humour and great visuals, promises that the film will be a great watch when it hits screens on February 10, 2017. 
 
  
  
 The second instalment of the hit franchise has been directed by Subhash Kapoor and stars  , Annu Kapoor, Manav Kaul and Saurabh Shukla in pivotal roles.
