Rajkot: The forest department launched a wildlife awareness campaign in Sasan as part of the World Wildlife Day celebration. The World Wildlife Day is celebrated on March 3 but due to holidays in schools on account of Holi the department launched the campaign on Wednesday. 
 
 Around 5 lakh students of 2,000 schools from surrounding five districts took part in the painting and essay contests. The theme for this year is ‘Big cats: Predators under threat’. tnn
