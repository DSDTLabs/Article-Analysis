PANAJI: The Goa police’s crime branch on Saturday busted an   betting racket operating from a flat in   and arrested four persons in the case. The crime branch also seized cash and seven mobile phones.

 
 The police were acting on information that illegal betting had been taking place at Taleigao over the past three weeks. Accordingly, the crime branch raided the place and caught the four red-handed during a match.
 
 Apart from cash of Rs 12,690,thecrimebranch also seized seven mobile phones and a laptop, which were used as instruments of gambling. Police sub-inspector Marlon D’ Souza is investigating the case.
