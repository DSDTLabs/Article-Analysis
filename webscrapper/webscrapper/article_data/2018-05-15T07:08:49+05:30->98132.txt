NEW DELHI: The results for the Madhya Pradesh Class 10 and Class 12 students was declared on Monday, May 14, 2018. The results was announced on the official website of the Madhya Pradesh Board of Secondary Education (MPBSE Board).

 The Madhya Pradesh Board had successfully conducted the HSC Class 10 and HSSC Class 12 for academic session 2017-18 in the month of March-April 2018.
