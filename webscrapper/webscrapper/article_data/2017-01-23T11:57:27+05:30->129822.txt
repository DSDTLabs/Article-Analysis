KANPUR: A car caught fire in Chakeri area on   on Sunday morning. The incident occurred near   in Chakeri when the car was heading towards Kanpur from Lucknow. 

 Mukesh Kumar, a native of Lucknow, was going to visit his relatives in Machariya area along with his family. Mukesh lost control and the car carshed into a divider following which the car caught fire. Mukesh and his family got off the car and alerted policemen at the nearby picket. The policemen diverted the traffic towards other lanes to avoid any further mishap.
 
 Fire department was informed and a fire tender from the nearby fire station rushed to the spot. The fire was controlled within 20 minutes.
 
 The traffic movement on Kanpur-Lucknow was affected for a few minutes.
