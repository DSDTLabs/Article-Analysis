Power supply is likely to be interrupted in several colonies from 6am to 6pm on April 9 due to replacement of 132 kv power conductor from Chandrayangutta EHT sub-station to Jubilee Hills EHT sub-station. According to a press release, Kondapur, Kothaguda, Hi-Tec City, Banjara Hills Roads No. 2 and No.22, BNR Hills, Film Nagar, Apollo Hospital, Gagan Pahad, Indira Gandhi society, Katedan, Madhapur, Ayyappa Society, Golconda and NIMS will be affected . Measures have been taken to provide alternative power supply measures, the TSSPDCL said. tnn
