Nagpur: An Air India flight going from Delhi to Bangalore made an emergency landing at Nagpur airport due to medical emergency on Monday morning. One of the passengers NK Saxena got a paralytic attack on board and Nagpur being the nearest airport, the pilot decided to land here. Along with Saxena, another passenger Mahendra Kumar too was offloaded along with their baggage. The airport staffers called for an ambulance and Saxena was taken to Care Hospital immediately on arrival. The flight made an emergency landing at 7.52am at Nagpur and departed at 9.17am. It reached Bangalore at 9.35am.
