HYDERABAD: A 76-year-old man was arrested by  , Rachakonda, on Saturday for allegedly eve-teasing a female MBA girl student.

 Rachakonda commissioner Mahesh Bhagwat said that Vinod Dewan, a resident of   in Sainikpuri, was arrested and he was produced in a local court too.
 
 It was on Thursday, a 24-year-old girl and her grand father went to Sai Baba temple in Kushaiguda. Suddenly, the victim’s grand father fell sick and accidentally the accused, who was unknown to the victim, was also at temple. Vinod came to their rescue and offered to drop the victim’s grandfather at their residence and dropped him at their residence by making him sit on his two-wheeler.
 
 "After gaining the confidence of the victim, Vinod took her mobile phone number. After some time, Vinod asked her to come for a long drive with him but the shocked victim rejected the offer. Then the accused started to force her to come with him. When she told him that the same will be informed to her father, Vinod threatened the victim not to disclose it to anyone," Rachakonda police in a press note said.
 
 The MBA student started avoiding taking calls of the senior citizen but he kept calling her continuously. The girl contacted SHE Team seeking help and the police nabbed the the accused. The accused previously used to do Real Estate business and he resides in his residence along with his wife while his children are residing abroad. A petty case was registered against him before he was produced in court, which granted him bail.
