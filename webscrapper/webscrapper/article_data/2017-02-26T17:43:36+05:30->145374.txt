Actress   will be the face of Horticorp (Kerala State Horticultural Products and Development Corporation) said recently in a press release issued by Horticorp's board of directors. 

 The news has it that Manju has exchanged a word of agreement with corporation chairman Vinayan. A campaign led by Manju is expected to attract the potential buyers including super markets. It seems she will have to promote safe-to-eat vegetables, in terms of cultivation, distribution and consumption on a wider canvas.
 
 The Ennum Eppozhum actress was the brand ambassador of Kudumbashree's organic farming project launched two-and-a-half years ago.
