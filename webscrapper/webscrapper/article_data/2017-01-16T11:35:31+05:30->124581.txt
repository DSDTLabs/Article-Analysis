Chennai: A 24-year-old man was hacked to death near Uthiramerur in Kancheepuram on Sunday afternoon. 

 Police identified the deceased as S Jay, who earned his livelihood by operating harvesting machine. He came home in   on Friday to celebrate Pongal with his family. 
 
 On Sunday afternoon he he got a call on his mobile from an unknown number. Soon after the call, he informed his family members that he would be back in an hour. 
 
 His father went searching for him after he failed to return after midnight. Later, the family received an information about a body found on the outskirts of the village.
 
 Police suspected the caller invited him to come outside the village and hacked him to death with help of a gang.
 
 "We are trying to identify the caller. Only then will we be able to establish the motive for the crime," said an investigating officer.
