JAISALMER: Barmer city police arrested six people who were gambling in a house at Kalyanpura here. Police recovered Rs 2.79 lakh and other items. SHO   said that based on the information from an informer, a house was raided at Kalyanpura and six rich gamblers were arrested. Earlier too such gamblers have been caught. These people gamble on certain festivals.
