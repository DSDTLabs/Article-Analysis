HYDERABAD: A 70-year-old watchman killed his wife by slitting her throat and later hanged himself from a cell phone tower atop an apartment at Anandbagh on Wednesday . The couple used to often quarrel, locals said.

 Malkajgiri cops said Krish naiah from Yadadri was a watchman at   apartment where he lived with his wife Lakshmi (60). Before committing suicide, he even telephoned his younger son Santosh and informed that Lakshmi had got electrocuted.Santosh alerted his cousin Bhaskar who found Lakshmi in a pool of blood and later found   hanging from the tower.
