 The official representative of Guinness World Records confirmed that the number of people who sang the national anthem at Kagvad had crossed the current record held by Dhaka, Bangladesh, where 2.54 lakh people sang national anthem in March 2014.
 
 According to officials of Shree Khodaldham Trust, an estimated 3.5 lakh people sang the national anthem on Saturday. However, the exact number will be known on Sunday as participants were being enumerated manually.
 
 The record was created during the idol installation ceremony of deity of Leuva Patels 'Maa Khodal' in presence of community leaders from all walks of life. Eminent leaders present included former chief ministers Keshubhai Patel and Anandiben Patel, Congress leader Shidharth Patel, NCP leader  , BJP state president Jitu Vaghani, VHP leader Pravin Togadia, industrialists Pankaj Patel, Karsan Patel and Govind Dholakia, among others.
