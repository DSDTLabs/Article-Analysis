MUMBAI: A   who was harassed by a man at   beach last week tweeted to the Mumbai police after which a few others also expressed concern about the issue.

 The photographer from   visits the beach every evening. On September 13 around 6 pm, a man kept leaning over on a rock that she was seated on. “Initially, I thought he wanted to steal my bag,“ she said. He started smoking, blowing smoke on her face. She asked him to move away , but he kept staring at her and followed her till she drove away.
 
 The woman tweeted to the Mumbai police handle about lack of security at beaches. The police tweeted back: “Beach vigil is maintained routinely . In any such situation dial 100 & the nearest mobile van will be sent.“
 
 She said the police assured her that a van would be posted at the beach.
