MUMBAI: For smooth traffic movement on four days that maintenance work will be done on   flyover (Prabhodhankar Thackeray flyover) on Western Express Highway, traffic police will shut north-bound traffic movement on the flyover from January 27 till 6am on January 30.

 From 3pm, the entire southbound carriageway of the flyo ver will be open for north-bound motorists. DCP  , said traffic restrictions will start early on January 27.
 
 Those travelling to the north in the mornings should use the road near   bridge to or use one lane of the south -bound carriageway on the flyover.After 3pm, the entire south-bound carriageway will be open for motorists going north.
