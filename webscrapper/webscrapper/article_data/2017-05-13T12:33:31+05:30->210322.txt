VASCO: ‘Goa Against Coal’ (GAC), an activists’ group, has demanded an inquiry into the dredging work undertaken by   ( ), allegeldy without any permission from the ministry for environment and forest. 

 President of Holy Cross Fishing Society, Custodio D’souza, said, “To tackle all these illegalities and injustice done to both humans and the nature, GAC has formed various cells.” 
 
 Reacting to Siddhant Kuncalienker’s remark that Goans protest for the sake of protesting, D’souza said, “Let him come with his family and stay in Vasco for two years. Then he will know why we are protesting MPT’s expansion plan.”
