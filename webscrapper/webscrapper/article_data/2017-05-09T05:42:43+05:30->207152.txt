AGRA: A track to test driving skills of   will be developed close to   at Kuberpur, said transport officials.
 According to sources, district administration has allocated land for the same. Facilities to test fitness of vehicles wiould also be established near the track. Officials said that ground work for the same would be started soon.
  
  In the absence of a proper driving track, regional transport officials were conducting driving tests at police lines or other open spaces so far. To sort out this problem, transport officials were working on various plans for several months.
  Regional transport officer (administration)   said, “A proposal regarding requirement and development of driving test track was forwarded to district magistrate. The proposal has got approval of district administration and required piece of land is allocated at Kuberpur.” According to   officials, the proposal would be forwarded to the state government for final approval after which the development works would begin.
