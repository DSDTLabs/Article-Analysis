With his plea seeking time to vacate his government accommodation pending before the  , former CM and SP president   started shifting from his 4, Vikramaditya Marg bungalow in Lucknow on Wednesday night. 

 
 There were reports that Akhilesh's father Mulayam was also contemplating vacating the bungalow that he had been allotted as ex-CM. The deadline to vacate two properties is to expire on June 1. Sources said the call to vacate the bungalow was taken in wake of the 15-day notice that the state had served on Akhilesh and Mulayam on May 17 as per a Supreme Court order.
