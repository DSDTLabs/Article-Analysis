PATNA: A 27-year-old man posing as an IPS officer and regulating traffic in Patna was arrested just a few minutes before PM  's convoy was to pass through the city's   on Thursday . Police said the impostor was identified as Mayank Mrinal, a Patna native.

 Police sources said Mrinal was guiding several cops on Bailey Road when a station house officer from a nearby police station reached the spot.Mrinal identified himself as a 2015 batch IPS officer, which upon hearing the SHO saluted him. However, it wasn't long before he took Mrinal into custody after he failed to produce any identity card. He was also in civilian clothes.
