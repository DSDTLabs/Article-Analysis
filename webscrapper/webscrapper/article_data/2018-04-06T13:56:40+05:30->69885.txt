RAJKOT: The managing trustee of Sukhnath Mahadev temple in   village of Botad was threatened by three persons by firing four rounds in the air from their country-made pistol on Thursday morning near Turkha village bus depot. 

 The accused wanted control over temple administration, the police said.
 
 Accused Rajbha of   in Gadhada, Deepbhai Khachar and an unknown person were booked for threatening trustee of Sukhnath Mahadev temple   (34) when he was talking to other trustees near bus depot of Turkha village.
