MADURAI:   city police are searching for unidentified people, who damaged an   machine with the intention to steal cash. The cash remained safe, as the culprit could not break open the machine.

 The incident came to light when the customers noticed the damaged part and reported to the bank. A case was registered with Tallakulam police station on Tuesday and a team has entered into an investigation.
 
 Police said the ATM belongs to  , located at Thirumukkulam South Street in Tallakulam. The incident took place on Saturday night and came to the knowledge of the bank officials only the next morning.
