'Madhuranaranga' fame  , who has been shooting for the upccoming horror thriller Lechmi, gets injured while shooting in Trivandrum.

 As per reports, her head was hit by a hammer that was thrown as part of a sequence. Soon after the incident, she was hospitalised and the actress is left with an injury on her forehead.
 
 Lechmi, directed by Shajeer Shah, also has Molly Kannammally in a prominent role. The film's script is also by the director himself.
