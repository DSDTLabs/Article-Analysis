Salman Khan's 'Race 3' is one of the awaited films of the year. However, after the release of the trailer of the film, it started trending for all the wrong reasons. 

 With the high octane action sequences and almost everything perfect, the trailer had its own glitches which became the subject of many trolls and memes on social media.
 
 First it was Salman Khan’s hand missile shot that raged all over the internet. And trolls have now moved on from that to Daisy Shah’s dialogue in the film - “Our Business Is Our Business, None Of Your Business”.
