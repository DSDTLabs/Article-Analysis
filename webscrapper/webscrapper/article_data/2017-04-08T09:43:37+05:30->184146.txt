PUNE: Four unidentified men threatened a   at   in Ravet and robbed him of his cash,   collectively worth Rs 50,300 on Sunday when he was on his way home.

 The Wakad police have booked the four suspects under section 392 (robbery) of the Indian Penal Code. Police said the complainant, who is a resident of Ravet, works as a consultant with an information and technology company. 
 
 “On Sunday, while he was on his way home, four men riding a motorcycle stopped him near Mhaske Vasti,” a police officer said.
 
 The suspects abused and threatened him and snatched his belongings. The officer said the suspect lodged a complaint. “We will transfer the case to Dehu Road police for investigations,” the officer said.
