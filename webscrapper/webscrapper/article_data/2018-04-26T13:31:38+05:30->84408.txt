MADURAI: The   bench of the   on Wednesday dismissed a plea that sought to engage government teachers alone in paper valuation for X, Plus One and Plus Two examinations. Tamil Nadu Graduate Teachers Association Theni district president N Sivakumar filed the plea against the move to engage private school teachers in paper valuation work. When it came up for admission before the division bench of justices C T Selvam and A M Basheer Ahamed, the bench dismissed the plea.
