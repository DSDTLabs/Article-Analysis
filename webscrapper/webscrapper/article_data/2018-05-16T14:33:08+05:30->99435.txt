NEW DELHI: Tamil Nadu school education minister KA Sengottaiyan today announced the class 12 result. The overall pass percentage this year is 91.1 %. 

 "Question papers were designed in such a way that student will be able to handle entrance tests with confidence. Though, some might have found the question papers tough compared to the previous year, it will help the students in the longer run," said education minister KA Sengottaiyan. 
 
 
 
 
 "Action will be taken against schools which try to promote admissions by publishing rank lists," he added. The state had abolished ranking system earlier. 
 
 The Tamil Nadu Directorate of Government Examinations (TNDGE) upload the Tamil Nadu HSC +2 (Class 12th) result on its official website at around 9.30 am. 
 
 The 12th class examinations of the Tamil Nadu board have been conducted between 1st March 2018 and 16th April 2018.
 
 We have seen the best pass percentage in 2017 with an outstanding 92.2% among 8,93,262 students. The girls have achieved a pass percentage of 94.5% and boys secured 89.3%.
 
 In 2016, we had seen an increase in pass percentage of 91.4% with girls securing 94.4% pass percentage and boys getting 87.9% among the 8,33,682 students.
 
 
 In 2015, the overall pass percentage was 90.6% in which girls achieved a pass percentage of 93% and boys secured 87%
 
 We are expecting a similar numbers this year and hope girls will outperform boys this year too.
