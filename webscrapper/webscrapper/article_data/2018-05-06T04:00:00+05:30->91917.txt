Rajkot: Eight two-wheelers, oil cans, valuable spare parts and a closed circuit television camera were charred when a major fire broke out in Patel Suzuki two-wheeler showroom near Indira Circle on Friday night. Fire brigade officials said the blaze was triggered by a short circuit. “The fire broke out at about 2.30-3 am. We carried out operation to douse the flames till 4 am and finally put out the blaze,” a fire brigade official said. Showroom manager Narendrasinh Dhillo has pegged the loss incurred in the incident at Rs 70 to 80 lakh. However, the real estimate is yet to be ascertained.tnn
