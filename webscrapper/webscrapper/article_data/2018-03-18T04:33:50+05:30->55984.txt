RANCHI:   Lalu Prasad was admitted to   (RIMS) on Saturday following complaints of recurring fever and rectal bleeding. He decided to go for treatment soon after the special CBI court of Shiv Pal Singh confirmed the verdict in the fourth fodder scam case was deferred to Monday 

  RJD supporters in huge numbers arrived at RIMS upon hearing the news. However, doctors remained silent on Lalu's condition. "He is diabetic and has heart problems. He also complained of piles," Birsa Munda Central Jail Superintendent, Ashok Kumar Choudhary said. 
 
 Sources said because of rectal bleeding, Lalu's haemoglobin levels had dipped.
