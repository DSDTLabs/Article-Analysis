AHMEDABAD: NDA presidential candidate   arrived in the city on Saturday on his campaign ahead of the election for president scheduled on July 20. Speaking at SVPI airport in the city, Kovind termed Gujarat as his second home.

 Kovind, who has been projected as a dalit candidate, was felicitated by three groups of Koli community.
 
 He was accompanied by  , Union roads & transport minister and national general secretary of  , Kailash Vijayvargiya.
 
 Gujarat CM,  , deputy chief minister Nitin Patel and BJP Gujarat chief, Jitu Vaghani felicitated Kovind.
 
 Interestingly, Congress leader and leader of opposition (LoP) in Ahmedabad Municipal Corporation (AMC), Dinesh Sharma, also felicitated the NDA presidential candidate.
