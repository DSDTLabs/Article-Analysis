Gopichand’s upcoming film which has been under shoot from the past three years, due to various reasons, will soon wrap up shoot. 

 As per the latest reports, the movie is titled as ‘Aaradugula Bullet’ and it will be released during the summer of 2017.
 
 Nayathara will be seen playing the role of the leading lady and director   will soon announce the release date of the movie.
