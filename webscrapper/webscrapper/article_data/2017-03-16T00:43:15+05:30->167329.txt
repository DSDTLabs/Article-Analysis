NOIDA: Ten shanties were gutted in a fire in Sector 78 of Noida city on Wednesday night. The incident took place at around 9:30 pm.

 Fire fighters with two vans rushed to the spot. “The fire broke out in one of the shanties and it quickly spread to others. The fire officials immediately swung into action and controlled the blaze,” Sushil Kumar, Fire Safety Officer, Noida, said.
 
 The officials said it appears that the fire broke out due to short circuit in a hut. The residents quickly fled from the spot with their belongings and valuables. The residents themselves tried to douse the fire with buckets of water.
 
 They later informed the fire department. Soon fire tenders reached the spot and controlled the situation. Police said that no one was injured in the incident.
