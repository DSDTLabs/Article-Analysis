AHMEDABAD: The city police have challenged over 500   passed by the metropolitan court to end cases brought against citizens by police for breaching notifications issued by the city  . 

 City police have filed appeals at the   against each order of dismissal.
 
 Last month, the court had tried to end the numerous   filed by city police while booking citizens under Section 188 of the IPC. The section is invoked when people are found to have breached any notification issued by the police commissioner.
 
 The court dismissed the proceedings because it held the police commissioner should have registered the complaints. The court also questioned the evidence produced and did away with all the complaints.
 
 Public prosecutor   said that the city police have filed more than 500 appeals against the dismissal orders, some of which have been admitted by the sessions court, while the hearing for the rest may take place next week.
