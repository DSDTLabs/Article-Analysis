Visakhapatnam: Poonam Malakondaiah, principal secretary, medical health and family welfare, Andhra Pradesh and Dr N Subbarao, Director of Medical Education, visited the virology lab at King George Hospital (KGH) on Friday for new facility (PCR workstation) for detection of swine flu. Previously, the specimens had to be send to Hyderabad and later to SVIMS, Tirupati, for confirmation of swine flu. Within three hours of the commencement of the test, the result can be communicated to the centres from where suspected cases were admitted. TNN
