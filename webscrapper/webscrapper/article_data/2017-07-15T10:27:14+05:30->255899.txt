The Judicial First Class Magistrate Court in Angamaly on Friday has extended the custody period of actor Dileep, who was arrested in connection with the actress' abduction and rape happened in February. 

 The actor was produced before the court on Friday morning and the court extended his custody period till Saturday 5 pm. The court will consider the bail plea on Saturday afternoon. 
 
 The 48-year-old actor, charged with conspiracy, was remanded to 14-day judicial custody by a court in Angamaly on Tuesday. Dileep was then shifted to a jail in Aluva, which is also his hometown. The actor has pleaded not guilty, maintaining that he was ‘trapped’.
