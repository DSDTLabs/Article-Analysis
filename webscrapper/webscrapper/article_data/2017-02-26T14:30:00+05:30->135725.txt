Actor Vishnu and his wife   have been blessed with a baby boy. The   actor posted a picture on his micro-blogging page on Tuesday, 'Thnk u god for dis precious moment of our lives..boy it is for me n rajini :) need all d positivity n luv.' Vishnu and Rajini tied the knot in December 2011. This is the couple's first child. 

 On the film front, the actor has wrapped up the shoot for the comedy entertainer  , which also stars Catherine Tresa and Soori. He also has in the pipeline an action thriller with Mundasuppati director Ram and Ezhil's former associate Chella.
