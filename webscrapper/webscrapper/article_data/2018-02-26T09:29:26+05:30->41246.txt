MARGAO: Issues regarding stray cattle and open defecation dominated the proceedings of the Dramapur-Sirlim gram sabha on Sunday. 

 Gram sabha members expressed concern over a large number of stray cattle in the village that pose a danger to motorists and voiced their demand for a cattle pound.
 
 It was also pointed out that labourers engaged for some telecommunication work who have been put up in tents, were defecating in the open thus posing health hazards to locals.   told the gram sabha that the contractor has already been told to provide mobile toilets to the labourers, and if he fails to do so, he will be told to vacate the land.
