NEW DELHI: Over 125 aspirants of Delhi University’s law faculty have been shuttling between the faculty and university office for over a week to make payment of registration fees. With the payment link down, the faculty and the university administration have been busy passing the buck even as the last day of submission of fees — July 25 — passed.

 Speaking to TOI, an admission official at   said, “All portal related actions are conducted by the university administration. We have no say in that. We send them all the details of the admission list.” Chairman of DU’s admission committee   said, “We have assured the students that they will not suffer. We will sort out the issue soon.”
