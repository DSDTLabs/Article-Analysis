CHENNAI: A fortnight after releasing a video clip of late   chief minister   in a hospital, disqualified   legislator P Vetrivel has managed to secure anticipatory bail in the cases filed against him for the video release. 

 Justice A D   of the Madras high court granted conditional bail to Vetrivel on Thursday. As per the conditions imposed, the TTV Dhinakaran loyalist has to appear in the Anna Square police station every day at 10.30 am for two weeks. On December 20, a day before the RK Nagar bypoll polling, Vetrivel released the video.
