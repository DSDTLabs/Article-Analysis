PUNE: The   police registered an offence against two men on Tuesday for demanding sexual favours from a 19-year-old woman and also threatening to kill her husband.

 A case was filed under sections 354 (A) (sexual harassment) and 336 (endangering life or personal safety of others) of the IPC. An officer from Yerawada police said the victim and the suspects knew each other. 
 
 He added the two men entered the woman’s house on Tuesday afternoon and demanded sexual favours from her. “The woman told them to leave, following which the duo used lewd language and left,” the officer said. He added the two men returned to the house after some time. 
 
 Upon finding her husband, they threatened him with sharp weapons.
