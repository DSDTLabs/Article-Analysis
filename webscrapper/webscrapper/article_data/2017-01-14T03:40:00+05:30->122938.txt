Panaji: Goa Su-Raj Party (GSRP), on Friday, released its first list of candidates who will contest the ensuing assembly elections on its symbol ‘candles’. Floriano Lobo, party spokesperson, announced the candidatures of Francisco Pacheco alias Mickky (Nuvem), Rameshwari Morajkar (Mapusa), Domnic Noronha (Velim) and Charles D’Silva (Quepem).

 Former minister Pacheco recently quit the Goa Vikash Party (GVP) to join Goa Su-Raj Party. Lobo said the candidates for other constituencies would be announced shortly.
