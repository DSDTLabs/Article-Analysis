Vadodara: A prisoner lodged at Vadodara central jail was booked at Raopura police station on Tuesday after a mobile phone was found from him on Tuesday .

 Police said that during the routine search by jail staff in barrack of yard 2 on they found a mobile phone and a SIM card in possession of Soma Dabhi, a convict. Dabhi had hidden the phone in a canister in which he had stored garlic paste. Cops are trying to find out how Dabhi managed to get the phone inside the jail and whether any jail staff was involved. It was only recently that jail staff had found a phone from two undertrials. The accused had hid their phone after creating aperture in the toilet of the barrack. tnn
