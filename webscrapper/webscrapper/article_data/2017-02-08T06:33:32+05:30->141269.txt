KOCHI: The high court has imposed a fine of Rs1 lakh on a person who filed a public interest litigation (PIL) seeking a CBI probe into alleged anomalies in conducting MBBS exams at   at Thrissur, but failed to provide any evidence. 

 A division bench of the court dismissed the PIL filed by   of  . The court said a bare reading of the PIL revealed that vague allegations had been raised but not even a single specific incident was brought to the notice of the court in seeking a probe by the central agency.
 
 While declining to entertain the PIL, the court said it was very easy to make false and vague allegations to get popularity through PILs and that the court could not allow persons like the petitioner to misuse the process of law.
