KRISHNAGIRI: Two people, including a 13-year-old boy, were killed and three others critically injured after a speeding container truck rammed the omni van they were travelling in near Kaveripattinam in Krishnagiri district on Sunday evening.

 The container truck hit the omni van from behind and the mangled vehicle was trapped under the truck wheels. An earth mover was used to rescue three of its passengers and also to retrieve the bodies.
