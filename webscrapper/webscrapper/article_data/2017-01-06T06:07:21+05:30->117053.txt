JAIPUR: Rajasthan High Court has issued notice to the state government over renaming of Bharat Nirman   to Atal Sewa Kendras in the name of former Prime Minister  . 

 The single bench of Justice M N Bhandari issued notice on a petition by former Congress MLA Sayam Lodha who challenged the decision of the state government as illegal and arbitrary.
 
  counsel for the petitioner said the Rajasthan government rechristened the Rajiv Gandhi Sewa Kendra to Atal Sewa Kendra through an administrative order which is illegal. He contended that a central notification could not be superseded by an administrative order of the state government.
 
 He said the central government through an amendment to the National Rural Employment Act 2005 made provision for the construction of the   as village knowledge resource centre and gram panchayat Bhawan at gram panchayat level and was promptly notifed through an extraordinary Gazette in 2009. 
 
 He said a legislative notification issued by the Centre could not be superseded by an administrative order by the state government.
 
 The petition said both Rajiv Gandhi and Atal Bihari Vajpayee are former prime ministers and Bharat Ratna awardees. There should not disrespect of any of these respected personalities. The government decision has shown the former Prime Minister Rajiv Gandhi in a poor light. Besides there should be sufficient reason to make such a change which had significant cost the state government exchequer.
