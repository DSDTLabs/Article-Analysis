NEW DELHI: The Himachal Pradesh Board of School Education (HPBOSE) Class 10 Results 2018 which was expected to be released on Wednesday, May 2, 2018, has now been postponed to May 3. As per information available from sources, the HPBOSE Class 10 result will now be declared around noon on May 3 on the official website of the board. 

 The board had successfully conducted the Secondary Examination (Class 10) and Higher Secondary Examination (Class 12) for the session year 2017-18 in the Month of Feb-March. Over one lakh students appeared for the HP Class 10 exam 2018. 
 
 
 
 
 
  
  
 
 Last year, 67.15 per cent students cleared the HP Board Class 10th Exam 2017. 
 
 If you are one of the students eagerly waiting for the announcement of HPBOSE Class 10 and Class 12 Result 2018, you can check it on the official website of the   - hpbose.org. 
 
 
 
 
 OFFICIAL WEBSITES of HPBOSE: You can log on to the official websites of HPBOSE - www.hpbose.org to check your results.
 
 IVR: Since there will be huge traffic on HPBOSE website on result day, you can try IVR system that provides you with the results via a call.
 
 SMS: Students can also check their HPBOSE Class 10 result 2018 via SMS too.
 
 SCHOOL WEBSITE: Apart from above three ways, you can also check your HPBOSE Board result on the local portal of your school.
