NEW DELHI: President   and Prime Minister   on Saturday greeted the nation on the occasion of  .

 "Greetings to fellow citizens on Vijaya Dashami, a celebration of victory of good over evil. Shubho Bijoya!," President Kovind tweeted.
 
  
  
 
 "Greetings to everyone on the auspicious occasion of Vijaya Dashmi," PM Modi tweeted.
 
  
  
 
 Congress Vice President   also extended   greetings, saying: "Best wishes to all of you on the occasion of Vijaya Dashmi. Happy Dussehra."
 
  
  
 
 Vijaya Dashmi, also called Dussehra, is believed to be the day when Lord Ram defeated Ravana, and is celebrated as a festival of victory of good on evil.
