Singer-turned-actor   will appear on Surya TV’s popular  , ‘Deal or No Deal’.

 The actor will share the updates and interesting experiences of his latest film, ‘Aby’ on the show. The programme hosted by actor Suraj Venjaramood revolves around the opening of a set of numbered briefcases, which contain certain sums of money. The player chooses a case or a box to knock an amount of money off the board.
 
 As per the channel sources, the episode will also have some fun moments. The programme will be aired on the channel at 9 pm on February 26.
