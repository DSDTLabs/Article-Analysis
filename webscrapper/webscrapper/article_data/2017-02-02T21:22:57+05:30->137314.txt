HYDERABAD:   Capital Region Development Authority ( ) on Thursday announced that it has inked   with various organisations to mobilise about   at the recently concluded Sunrise Andhra Pradesh Investment Meet that was held during January 27-28 at Visakhapatnam.

 The proposed investments have the potential to create 2.01 lakh jobs in the Andhra Pradesh capital city of Amaravati, APCRDA said. Around 64% of the proposed investments are meant for developing infrastructure, it added.
 
 Commenting on the development, APCRDA commissioner Cherukuri Sreedhar pointed out that these MoUs were signed across 11 sectors.
