AHMEDABAD: Air Force Station   hosted the fourth Joint Indo-Oman Air Exercise--   on January 16-17. 

 The Royal Air Force of Oman (RAFO) participated with five F-16s belonging to No. 18 Squadron based at Thumrait.   participated with Su-30 MKIs, MiG-29s, Jaguars and MiG-27 aircrafts. 
 
 On Wednesday Chief of Air Staff Air Chief Marshal BS Dhanoa and Commander of RAFO Air vice Marshal Matar Ali Matar Al-Obaidani reviewed the progress of the exercise at Air Force Station Jamnagar. This was the first time RAFO F-16s participated in an Air exercise outside the Gulf nations.
