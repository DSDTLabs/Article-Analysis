Visakhapatnam: Andhra University has released the Andhra University Common Entrance Test-2018 (AUCET-2018) notification. 

 Qualifying the test makes the students eligible for admissions into various arts, science, engineering and law courses offered by the varsity. The submission of online applications will commence on Thursday, March 22. The applications will be received online from March 22 to April 21 without any late fee and from April 22 to April 30 with a late fee. The varsity has also come out with a notification of Andhra University Engineering Entrance Test (AUEET) for admissions into six-year integrated dual courses in several engineering branches.
 
 AU’s Directorate of Admissions director K Rajendra Prasad said the notifications are available at www.andhrauniversity.edu.in/doa and www.audoa.in. “The courses, fee structure and admission information brochure are available on these websites. Schedule of examinations and seat distribution will be posted on the websites in the next few days.,” he said.
