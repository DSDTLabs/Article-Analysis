To celebrate their love and concern for stray animals, the  , in association with an NGO, felicitated around 60 people who have been working tirelessly for the welfare of stray animals, at an event held at the Moti Mahal Lawns, recently. 
Among those who were felicitated were teachers, lawyers, homemakers and even cops. The event was inaugurated by Brijesh Pathak, law minister, which also had a drawing competition for underprivigiled children who drew paintings on the theme of ‘Love Indian animals’. Rishabh Shukla, the founder of the foundation, and  , ensured the event went off smoothly, which also saw the presence of bureaucrat Reema Singh.
