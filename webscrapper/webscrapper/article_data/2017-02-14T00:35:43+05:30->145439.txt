MUMBAI: The   has banned use of mobile phones, cameras or other audio/video recorders inside court rooms. A notice issued by HC registrar general Mangesh Patil on Monday said owners have to make arrangments for keeping their devices. 

 Defaulters are liable for confiscation of device and fine, said the notice. No audio or video recording on phones or any other device is permitted at present but lawyers and litigants were allowed to carry their mobile phones inside courtrooms provided they are on silent modes or switched off. No calls were permitted inside court rooms.
