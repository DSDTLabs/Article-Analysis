The damaged section of the Sri Meenakshi Amman Temple would be renovated with the help of experts to look as exactly as it did before the fire accident, said deputy chief minister O Panneerselvam.He said at Madurai airport on Sunday that fire had damaged a section of the mandapam and that the fire service personnel were in the process of removing the debris. All steps would be taken to renovate the structure with the help of the department of archaeology, he said. Investigation is on into the fire accident and action will be initiated based on the reports.
