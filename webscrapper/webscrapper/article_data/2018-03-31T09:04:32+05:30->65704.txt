GORAKHPUR: BJP spokesman   has filed a case in Deoria district fast track court under IPC 499 and 500 against   president   for making derogatory remarks against PM   during his party's national conclave in New Delhi on Friday. 

 April 5 has been fixed as the date of hearing under section 200 of CrPC.
 
 "During Congress party's national conclave in Delhi, Rahul Gandhi tried to draw similarities between PM Modi, Nirav and Lalit Modi and also said that the name Modi had become synonymous to corruption. 
 
 This remark has hurt the sentiments of BJP party workers and countrymen and for this reason I have filed a defamation case against him in Deoria court today," Shalabh Mani Tripathi said.
