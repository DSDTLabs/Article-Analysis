The official teaser of the B Unnikrishnan movie 'Villain' was released last month, and   fans had loved his salt n pepper look in the action-filled video.

 The team has just released the official poster of the movie as well and yet again, it's featured on the megastar's heavy-duty rough n tough look. His fans have already been lauding the actor for giving a 'Hollywood touch' for his character, wearing a long jacket and nerd glasses, on social media. 
 
 'Villain' is a thriller, which has Manju Warrier as the female lead and also has a stellar cast including Vishal, Hansika and Rashi Khanna. The film is expected to hit theaters in a few months.
