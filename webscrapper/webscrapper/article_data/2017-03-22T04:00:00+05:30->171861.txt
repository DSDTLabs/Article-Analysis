Parmar said that not only were atrocities on the rise, but dalits were also being denied fodder for their cattle. Citing an incident from Mehsana, Parmar said that a dalit family was socially boycotted, with villagers holding back fodder. He said that in 2015, the number of dalits murdered in the state was the highest in the country. He said that 13 temples in the state — one in Ahmedabad — deny entry to dalits.
