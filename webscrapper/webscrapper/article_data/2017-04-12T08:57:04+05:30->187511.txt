CHENNAI: Police detained five juveniles on Monday for stealing cellphones from houses with their doors left open.

 A police team nabbed the suspects as they were moving in a furtive manner at the   near   railway station at night.
 
 Of the five, four are aged 17 and the fifth is 15. They told the investigation officers that they entered into houses which were left open in the middle of the night.
 
 Police recovered two stolen mobiles from them. The Guindy police have registered a case and arrested the juveniles. They were produced before the   (JJB) magistrate and admitted at the   for boys and girls in Kellys.
