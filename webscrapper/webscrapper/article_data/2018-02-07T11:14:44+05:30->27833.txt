Pune: Students appearing for the secondary school certificate ( ,  ) and higher secondary certificate ( , std XII) have been asked to refer the time table sent to their respective schools and colleges, a statement issued by the   of secondary and higher secondary education said.

 The statement added that students should not rely on any printed material regarding time table on social media or handouts. The written exam for HSC begins from February 21 and ends on March 20 while the written exam for SSC starts from March 1 and ends on March 24.
 
 The oral and practical examination for HSC will be conducted up to February 17 and for SSC, the oral, written and aptitude test will be conducted between February 10 and 28.
