PANAJI: A delegation of   owners accompanied by BJP’s   met urban development minister Francis D’Souza on Wednesday evening.

 The barge owners requested D’Souza to intervene as the order issued by the directorate of mines and geology mandates stoppage of ore transport two days before the mining ban coming into effect from March 15 that will cause them to lose revenue. D’Souza said he has conveyed their grievances to the chief secretary.
