SANGRUR: Sangrur district project director of   (SSA) has issued a circular directing private schools to adhere to the norms and guidelines laid down by the   pertaining to capitation fee and other charges.

 As the next admission session is already underway, the district project director has asked the schools to stick to “no capitation or voluntary donations collected in the name of school” clause of the CBSE. In a relief to parents, the school management committee comprising parents or a nominee from the parents’ association would decide the hike in the monthly fee and annual charges.
 
 The schools have been directed to form a parent-teacher association by April 30. The schools have already abstained from having shops for uniforms and books on the school premises. Following recent accidents of school buses, it has also been made mandatory to follow the ‘safe vahan’ policy norms.
