Margao: Margao police on Tuesday arrested Christopher Diniz Rodrigues of Benaulim, after he was found in possession of ganja in the vicinity of the Margao municipal garden. Police sources said 114g of ganja estimated to be worth Rs 34,000 were recovered from him.  Rodrigues was caught red-handed by police when he arrived in the area to deliver the consignment to a customer. He was subsequently booked under various sections of the Narcotics Drugs and Psychotropic Substances (NDPS) Act.
