Malayalam film 'Kathakali,' directed by  , had created many headlines last year when the Central Board of Certification (CBFC) denied it a rating stating the reason of vulgarity. 
 
  The movie, which later won an A rating with the support of the court, has just released its first song. Composed and sung by renowned musician Bijibal, it is a touching poetic track about the river Nila. The song goes 'Oru Nool Njarambu Pol Nee Nile Neele,' and features the movie's lead actors Irina Jacob and Binoy Nambala.
  
  The song is penned by Nasin, and 'Kathakali' has a screen time of two hours.
