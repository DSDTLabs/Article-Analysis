JAIPUR: Active western disturbances in the state on Friday brought fresh troubles for the farmers. Mild showers were witnessed in the districts including Jaipur, Tonk, Ajmer, Chittorgarh and some parts of Sikar. In its forecast   office said that dust storm, hailstorm and rainfall are likely to occur at many places in north-eastern Rajasthan.

 Cloudy conditions prevailed across the state on Friday evening when some places in Jaipur, Tonk, Ajmer, Chittorgarh and a few places in Sikar witnessed mild showers. It guided mercury to take a dip, however, it has again made farmers in the state jittery. A week ago similar spell of rainfall followed by hailstorm had badly affected the crop of wheat and barley in Sikar, Jhunjhunu, Bharatpur, Dausa and Alwar districts of the state.
 
 Attributing the climatic change to the passing of western disturbances and vanishing of northerly winds the Jaipur Met office said that mild to moderate showers will occur at many places in the north-eastern parts of the state in the next 24 hours.
