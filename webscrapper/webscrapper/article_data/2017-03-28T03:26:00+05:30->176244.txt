MARGAO: A budget of Rs 1.40 crore was approved by the Camurlim gram sabha on Sunday. Dinesh Amonkar, who presently holds additional charge as Camurlim panchayat secretary, told TOI that, “Both, the budget and audit reports were approved by the gram sabha on Sunday. The Camurlim village panchayat receives royalties from petrol pumps. Hence, the revenue figures are higher.” tnn
