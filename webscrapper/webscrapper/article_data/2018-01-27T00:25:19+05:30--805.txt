Tirupur: The rural police will   45 vehicles, including 43 two-wheelers and two four-wheelers seized under the Tamil Nadu Prohibition Act, 1937, on January 29.

 The vehicles were seized in the last two years from the rural areas of the district. “We are doing out best to check the menace of illegal   sales. We have ensured that Indian-made foreign liquor from neighbouring states do not entering the district,” said district superintendent of police ES Uma.
 
 Despite their efforts, several people are involved in illegal liquor sales at houses and other places, another officer said. “We seized 45 vehicles, which were used for the illegal liquor sales in the last two years. The owners of the vehicle were not able to produce the required documents, while others were proved to be used in such crimes,” he added. TNN
