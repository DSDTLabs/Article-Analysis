 film’s latest song Changalla Changidipaane was out recently, and it was a proof of the sheer love that Malayalis have for the actor, The song, which is a fan anthem, is a tribute to Mohanlal, and features a bunch of artists expressing their love for the same. 

 Wearing mundu and shirt, these young lads are seen trying their hand at Mohanlal’s signature step, which is the slanting pose. 
 The song is composed by Sajid Yahiya and Prakash Alex, the lines of which are penned by Manu Manjith. 
 
 Changalla Changidipaane is crooned by Sajid Yahiya, Anil Kumar and Nihal Sadiq.
