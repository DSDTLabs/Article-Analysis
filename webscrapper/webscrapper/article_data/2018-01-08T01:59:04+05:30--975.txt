Mumbai: A 62-year-old   Shiv   corporator Ashok Sawant was   to death outside his house in   late on Sunday. Sawant, a two-time corporator, had been getting extortion calls and had filed a police complaint. 

 Police said Sawant, who is related to ACP Subhash Sawant attached to the anti-terrorism squad, was attacked when he was returning after meeting a friend. Two men, who were waiting in a car outside his Sur building house in Samta Nagar, attacked him with choppers before fleeing. Cops rushed him to a hospital where he was declared dead on arrival.
 
 A source in the crime branch said that closed-circuit television cameras in the vicinity captured the images of the killers and they have been identified. One of the accused, identified as Jagga, is a well-known criminal, the source said. 
 
 The police said Sawant had got into cable TV business a few years ago. He had reportedly been getting extortion calls over the past couple of days and he had complained to the police. The source added that the murder could be a fallout of the extortion threats.
 
 Sawant is survived by his wife, two daughters and a son. Two of his children are doctors and one of his daughters was to get married on February 5.
