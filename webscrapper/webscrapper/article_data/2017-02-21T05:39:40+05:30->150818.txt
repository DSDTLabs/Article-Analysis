MUMBAI: A 28-year-old woman died after an unidentified man stabbed her at least thrice in   (west) around 9.15pm on Monday . The deceased, a homemaker from Goregaon (east), had gone to buy vegetables and was returning home in an autorickshaw when she was stabbed.

 People who were around the station premises and a few auto drivers who witnessed the stabbing incident alerted the police control room while some of the onlookers took the severely injured woman to   where she was declared dead on admission. Some passersby tried to chase the man but he managed to escape.
 
 The police said they are checking for any closed-circuit television cameras around the spot of the crime to identify the attacker. A murder case has been registered against the man, said DCP Vikram Deshmane.
