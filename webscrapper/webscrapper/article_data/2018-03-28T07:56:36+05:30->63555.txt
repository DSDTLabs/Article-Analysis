LUCKNOW: UP government on Tuesday announced its transfer policy for 2018-19 to 2021-22. A provision has been being made to transfer officers of Groups A and B, who have spent three years in a district or seven years in a division. The appointment to a divisional office or that of a departmental head will not be counted towards the seven years posting allocated for a division. 

 In the decision taken on Tuesday, the government said that all transfers for the duration of this policy should be completed before May 31 of every year. The cut-off for determining duration of postings will be March 31 of each year. 
 
 It has also been decided that for Group C officers, who have two years or less left for retirement, they can be posted in their home districts. For Group A and B officers who have two years or less left of service, they can be posted anywhere except for their home district. "Routine transfers will not be applicable on such employees who are either handicapped or have handicapped dependants. The CM has been entrusted with the right to propose any amendment to this policy," said sources.
