Times News Network

 Chandigarh: UT Chandigarh Skill Development Mission in collaboration with National Skill Development Corporation celebrated World Youth   Day at SD College, Sector 32, Chandigarh.
 
 Rakesh Kumar Popli, PCS, mission director, Chandigarh Skill Development Mission, talked about various skill initiatives taken by the mission in Chandigarh.
 
 Anurag Agarwal, home secretary-cum-secretary, technical education, highlighted various initiatives being taken by the ministry with a special focus on upgrade of ITIs and skill development projects in the region.
 
 He said that there was a need to implement the counselling system in all schools of Chandigarh to help students get proper guidance about their future career. During the event, several students and members of industries were honoured for their contribution in skill development through various schemes. Various industries also gave away job letters to students who completed skill courses.
