MUMBAI: A 20-year-old man, Sambhaji More, allegedly killed a 17-year-old girl in his neighbourhood after she did not let him check messages on her cellphone on Sunday. 

 The incident occured near   slums on   at Vakola. The victim identified as Ayesha Shaikh (17) was known to More and lived close to his home. The teen was found with her throat slit open and lying in a pool of blood. A bystander informed the   police and the teen was taken to V N Desai hospital where she was declared dead before admission. Her body has been send for   for a post-mortem. 
 
 More was arrested in the evening. "We are questioning More to ascertain the exact cause of their tiff. We have learnt that More would make unwanted advances. On Sunday, he forcibly tried to check her cellphone, which she resisted. More went into a rage and stabbed her several times in her neck and fled," said a cop. 
 
 At the time of going to press, the police were in the process of lodging a murder case.
