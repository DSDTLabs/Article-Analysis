TIRUPUR: A group of enumerators had a lucky escape after a face-to-face encounter with a tigress during the recently concluded wildlife census in   in the district.

 “When a three-member team, including a forest guard and volunteers, was conducting census in   forest, they spotted the tigress with its cub. When it readied to attack them, they managed to hide from it,” said a forest official.
 
 The wildlife census was conducted from 6am to 10am between May 22 and May 28. Forest officials and volunteers took survey using mobile app and GPRS equipment. Soon, a report will be prepared on the census, said the official.
