NEW DELHI: The government expects   proceeds at Rs 52,495 crore from central public sector enterprises and other   during 2018-19. 

 This is going to be nearly Rs 2,315 crore less than Rs 54,810 crore estimated for the current fiscal, as per the   document. 
 
 However, according to the Budget estimate for 2017-18, the government expected Rs 67,529.24 crore from central public sector enterprises and other investments.
