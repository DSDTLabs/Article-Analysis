SURAT: Around 37-year-old farm labourer was arrested in   police station area of Surat district on Wednesday for allegedly murdering his wife at home over some petty issue. The deceased was punched and kicked by the accused after which the woman died.

 Police arrested Guriya alias Girish Rathod, a resident of   in Amaliya Faliya of Palsana, following a complaint by his relative  , a resident of Karad Talav. Police investigation revealed that the accused had argument with his wife Nayna over some issue. Rathod thrashed Nayna on Tuesday night and the deceased suffered injuries all over her body. She went to sleep at night and did not wake up in the morning.
