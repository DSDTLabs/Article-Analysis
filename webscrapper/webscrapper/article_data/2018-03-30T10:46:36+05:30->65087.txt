PUNE: The   police on Wednesday arrested a 28-year-old man from Market Yard for sexually harassing a woman at her house in   on March 10.

 The woman approached lodged a complaint against the suspect on Wednesday. “The woman and the suspect knew each other. On March 10, when the woman’s husband was not at home, the suspect arrived there and started touching her inappropriately,” an officer from the Bharati Vidyapeeth police said.
 
 When the woman raised an alarm, the suspect beat her up. “He also inflicted burn injuries on the woman’s body with a spatula. When she told him that she would lodge a complaint against him, he even threatened to kill her children,” the officer said.
 
 The terrified woman did not reveal anything to her husband. “However, when the suspect started harassing her again, she mustered some courage and narrated her ordeal to her husband, following which she lodged a complaint against him,” the officer said.
 
 Police have arrested the suspect under sections 354 ( ), 452 (house-trespass after preparation for hurt, assault or wrongful restraint), 323 (voluntarily causing hurt) and 506 (criminal intimidation) of the Indian Penal Code.
