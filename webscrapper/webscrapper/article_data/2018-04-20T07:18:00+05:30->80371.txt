Ludhiana: A 28-year-old labourer of Jeevan Nagar, Sat Prakash, was found hanging on Thursday morning from a tree near his house. It is alleged that he committed suicide as he had been depressed for the past some time. Prakash’s wife Poonam told police that they got married two years ago, but as they did not have any child, he was depressed. She added that Prakash went missing on Wednesday night. The family kept searching for him, but to no avail. On Thursday morning, his body was found hanging from a tree near the locality. Focal Point police have proceeded with 174 of CrPC in this matter, following Poonam’s statement.
