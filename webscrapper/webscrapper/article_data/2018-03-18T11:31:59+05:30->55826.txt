MADURAI: A farmer was killed when a tractor knocked him down near Rajapalayam in Virudhunagar district on Friday. 

 Police said Antoparimal Roshan, 18, son of Antonysamy of Devadanam, was returning on his two-wheeler after irrigating his crops when he was hit by a tractor driven by Chittiraikani of Pommakka Koil, at Pommakka Koil. He died on the spot. Sethoor police have registered a case.
