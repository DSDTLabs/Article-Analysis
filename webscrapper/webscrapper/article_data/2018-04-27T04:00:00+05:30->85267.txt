A 19-year-old woman on Thursday lodged a rape complaint against a resident of Dalwa village at Umrala taluka in Bhavnagar district. In her complaint, the woman has alleged that Jagdish Mahida (25), a resident of Dalwa village, raped her thrice in a flat behind Crystal Mall on Kalavad Road in Rajkot on March 20. “The survivor alleged that Mahida had lured her with the promise of marriage and had taken her to the flat in Rajkot where he committed the crime,” Rajkot mahila police station sub-inspector H D Solanki said.
