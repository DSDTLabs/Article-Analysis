AMRITSAR: An American national was today arrested for allegedly carrying five live cartridges of 38 bore caliber when he was about to board a Delhi-bound flight from the airport here.

 During preliminary investigations, George Danid Gilbert, resident of New York city, told the police that he was a shooter and while coming to India he inadvertently carried the cartridges along with him, SHO Parmdeep Kaur said.
 
 She said the bullets were found during the screening of Gilbert's luggage at the airport.
 
 The   (CISF), which looks after the airport security, handed over the American national to the   police which registered a case under Section 25/54/59 of the Arms Act against him, she added.
