PUNE: Three employees of   on the   were arrested on Friday following a complaint lodged by the parents of a special child alleging that the institution's authorities had "impaired their child for life". 

 "The employees were arrested under the Indian Penal Code, Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act and Juvenile Justice Act," said sub-inspector   of the Yerawada police. Based on the parents’ complaint, the police had registered a case against the school for alleged violation of norms related to education and care of children with learning disabilities. The child was admitted to the school last year in Grade I.
 
 The arrested employees — Kumar Madhukar Mahamunkar (30) from Yerawada, Jasinta Kumar Wilson (42) from Kharadi and Shalin Shahnawaz Adtani (32) from Kalyaninagar — will be produced before a magisterial court on Saturday.
 
 School principal Rashmi Gupta said, “We will take the legal route and ensure the three persons are released immediately. One of them is a teaching staff, while two others belong to the school administration,” adding, “Some people are trying to malign the school’s image.”
