Ahmedabad: A metropolitan court on Thursday sent chartered accountant Tehmul Sethna to police custody till March 31 for interrogation in connection with an FIR lodged against him by his wife, Kamalrukh Sethna, for cheating and forgery. 

 Tehmul Sethna had hit the headlines after a client of his, Mahesh Shah, made a Rs 13,860 crore disclosure under the Income Disclosure Scheme 2016 and then developed cold feet.
 
 Crime branch sought custodial interrogation of Sethna for five days, on the grounds that he was not cooperating with the investigation. 
 
 The cops said that they wanted to investigate the matter with the accused in custody to find where he had invested the money.
