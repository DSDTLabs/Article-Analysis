Madurai: Madurai city corporation on Sunday flagged off the super sucker machine, which it has procured for the cost of Rs 54.40 lakhs from the MLA development fund of Madurai West MLA, Sellur K Raju. The minsiter also flagged off the truck in the presence of Madurai district collector K Veera Raghava Rao and corporation commissioner Aneesh Sekhar. Corporation officials said that the super sucker machinery mounted on the truck will be useful to clear the clogging at manholes, ensuring that the underground drainage is not blocked. tnn
