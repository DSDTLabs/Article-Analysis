INDORE: Railways on Monday suspended three employees, including the locomotive driver, guard and locomotive inspector in connection of derailment of Indore-Ratlam   train. 

 "The incident has been taken seriously and an inquiry has been ordered into it, said a spokesperson of Ratlam division of the Western Railways. 
 
 All the eight wheels of the train engine got derailed when the train was coming to yard section at Indore railway station on Sunday night. However, no casualty was reported in the incident. 
 
 Railway has formed a committee to inquire all aspects of the incident and submit the report at the earliest. The committee comprises of senior operation manager, senior technical engineer, senior communication manager, signal manager and senior electrical engineer of the division.
