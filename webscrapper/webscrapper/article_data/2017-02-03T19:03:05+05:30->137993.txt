NEW DELHI: Police on Friday claimed to have arrested a local militant in a search operation in Lassipora area of   in south Kashmir's   district.

 Reports said that whole village was put on high alert since last evening after the information of presence of few militants in the village.
 
  
  During search operation jointly launched by 55 RR, Jammu and Kashmir Police, a local militant identified as Manzoor Ahmad was apprehended, a senior police officer said.
 
 One pistol with ammunition and 2 hand grenades were recovered from his possession, he said.
