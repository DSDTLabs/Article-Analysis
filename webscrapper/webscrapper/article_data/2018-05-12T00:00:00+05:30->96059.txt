A cricket match was recently held between the male and female members of a city club. The members were divided into four teams – 11 Musketeers. Gabbar 11,   and Rising Stars. The final match was played between Lucknow Tigers and 11 Musketeers, where the latter won the match. While the Best Batsman was awarded to Abhinav Khandelwal, Aman Agarwal was the Best Bowler.
