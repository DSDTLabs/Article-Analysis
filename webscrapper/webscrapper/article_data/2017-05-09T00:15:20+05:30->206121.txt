Hyderabad: A woman who had been cheating people by giving fake matrimonial advertisements in newspapers was arrested by Central Crime Station (CCS) officials on Monday. 

 CCS police said that K Sridevi of Sriram Nagar colony in ECIL was arrested after they got a complaint from Mohammed Nazar of Sheikpet. After seeing the advertisement, Nazar had contacted Sridevi who claimed to run a marriage bureau and promised to show suitable matches for him. The accused collected Rs 5,500 from the victim, but did not show any matches. Later, she switched off her phone . 
 
 Police nabbed Sridevi based on her mobile number details. The accused had cheated several victims and collecting money in a similar manner in the past. tnn
