VARANASI: Bhartiya Janata Party   Surendra Narain Singh along with his supporters blocked the   near Rameshwar under Jansa police station area for hours, late Tuesday night, alleging that police officials were turning a blind eye to movement of trucks in an area where the vehicles' entry was banned. 

 The MLA from Rohania claimed the movement of trucks on Panchkroshi Road was banned and alleged that policemen posted there were helping truck drivers use the road in exchange for monetary benefits. He ended the stir after the   assured him that necessary action would be taken in the case.
