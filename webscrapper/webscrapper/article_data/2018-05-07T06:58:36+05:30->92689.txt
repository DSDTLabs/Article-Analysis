AHMEDABAD: The body of a woman in late thirties was found from   early on Sunday morning. Naroda police have registered a case of murder and started probe.

 Inspector H B Vaghela has registered a complaint after the body of Posuben Marwadi, 37, a resident of G Ward, Sardarnagar, was found from behind Shivam Bungalows near Nandigram Society. A local resident informed city police control room about the body.
 
 “The victim’s right hand had been cut off and placed near her left hand. The body bore deep cuts on her left ear, jaw, neck and shoulders. Her son mentioned that Marwadi was working with a man identified as Viju, running a catering agency. She had a tiff with him. Further probe is on,” said an official. tnn
