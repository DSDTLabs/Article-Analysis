NEW DELHI: A 40-year-old financer died after being stabbed in his thigh by two men outside his house in   on Thursday night. The man, Sanjay Chawla, had returned from London on Thursday morning and had gone to his office to clear some dues. While he was driving his scooter, he was waylaid near a shop and stabbed. Family members said the miscreants also fled with his wallet and mobile phone. 

 Chawla's family members said that they heard him calling out for his mother outside his house. "We stepped out and found him bleeding from his thigh. He narrated the incident to us and then fell unconscious. He was taken to a private hospital in Saket where he died during treatment on Friday," said Hemraj Kalra, Chawla's uncle. tnn
