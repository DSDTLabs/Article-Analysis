AMARAVATI: The Andhra Pradesh government on Monday directed commissioner of the   (CRDA) to form a tender committee for fresh international bids for the development of seed capital area (SCA) in Amaravati. 

 The committee will prepare the tender documents. After the tender document is approved by the commissioner, Singapore companies will give the revised proposals, including Business Plan, Concession Agreement and Development Agreement (CADA), Shareholders Agreement (SHA) etc. This time, the bidder selection parameter will be based on percentage of gross revenue share.
 
 The process will be governed by Andhra Pradesh Infrastructure Development Authority Enabling Act amendment ordinance 2016, amending the earlier Act. Following the directives of the High Court, the government decided to go for fresh bidding.
