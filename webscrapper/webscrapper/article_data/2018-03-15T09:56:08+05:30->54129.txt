HYDERABAD: After being in the intelligence wing for eight years, 1996-batch IPS officer and inspector general   on Wednesday has taken charge as the   police commissioner replacing IPC officer   who was promoted as additional director-general (railway).

 Sajjanar said his focus will be woman and child safety, community and citizen friendly policing, with special focus on cyber crime, human trafficking. He said he will also focus on awareness and prevention of economic and white collar offences, capacity building to prevent criminal cases and L&O issues, IT and other industries security, road safety and others.
 
 “We will ensure that more technology enabled services will be provided to citizens,’’ the new CP said.
