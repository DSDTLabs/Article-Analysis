GANDHINAGAR: After the announcement of Patidar Anamat Andolan Samiti ( ) leader Hardik   that he will respond to the government’s invitation for talks on Wednesday, Nitin Patel, deputy chief minister and spokesperson of the state government said, “The government has not invited Hardik or anyone from the Patidar community for talks recently.” He went on to say: “However, we always welcome anybody who wants to meet the government for talks. Earlier, too, we invited all caste groups to discuss their demands.”
