NEW DELHI: The result of the Common Admission Test ( ) 2017 is out on the official website - iimcat.ac.in

 The Indian Institute of Management (IIM) conducts the Common Admission Test 2017 ( ) every year to select students for Management Studies. The CAT scores are used not only for IIM admissions but it is used by various other management institutes across the country. 
 
 
 
 
 The institute has announced the results of the CAT 2017 entrance exam on Monday, January 8, 2018, on its official website. 
 
 The candidates who have appeared for the CAT 2017 entrance examination can now check their score card on available Results link on the official website - https://iimcat.ac.in 
 
 The CAT 2017 admission test was conducted by IIM in November 2017 in over 140 test cities across the country. The CAT entrance examination was held on November 26, 2017.
 
 
 
 
 1) Open the official website - https://iimcat.ac.in 
 
 2) On the homepage, you will see a link "Scorecard for CAT 2017", click on it
 
 3) Enter your details such as roll no./enrollment no. to get the result
 
 4) Download and print your scorecard for further reference
