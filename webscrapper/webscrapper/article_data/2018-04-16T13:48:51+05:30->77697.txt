If you are somebody who loves to dance, then Nisha Bano’s latest song ‘Ohi Boldi’ can be your number.

 The Punjabi singer-actress took the internet by storm by releasing her latest song ‘Ohi Boldi’. Written by Vicky Dhaliwal and composed by KV Singh, the song has what it takes to make you groove.
 
 Earlier this year, on the occasion of Lohri, the singer released her song ‘Repeat’ under the album ‘Lohri Yaaran Di 2018’. Nisha is also known for her amazing performances in a number of Punjabi movies like ‘Nikka Zaildar’, ‘Fer Mamla Gadbad Gadbad’ and ‘Jatt Airways’.
