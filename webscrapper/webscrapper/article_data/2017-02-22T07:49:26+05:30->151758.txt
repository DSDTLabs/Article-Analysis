BENGALURU: Within 24 hours of the   issue flaring up and chief minister   expressing anguish over the forest department's inefficiency, additional chief secretary T M  , who held concurrent charge of the forest department, has been relieved of his duties. 

 Bhaskar, who was forest department ACS and development commissioner, is now only ACS development commissioner. The government posted energy department ACS P Ravi Kumar in his place.
 
 In another major reshuffle of bureaucrats, the government transferred out the ACS for IT-BT and science and technology V Manjula to the medical education department.
 
  The ACS post for IT-BT is vacant. The government also transferred ACS   to the energy department. IAS officer S R Umashankar is the new KSRTC MD.
