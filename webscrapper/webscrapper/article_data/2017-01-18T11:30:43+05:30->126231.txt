While we already know that actor Aadhi Pinisetty is playing an important role in Anushka starrer-'Bhagmati', now we hear that noted actor of both Malayalam and Tamil industries, Jayaram would also star in another prominent role in the 'Pilla Zamindar' fame, director G Ashok's next. The movie marks his straight Telugu debut. 'Bhagmati' is bankrolled by UV Creations and is touted to be a thriller. According to sources, we hear that Jayaram will play a baddie in the film. S S Thaman is scoring tunes for it.
