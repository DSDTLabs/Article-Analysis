NEW DELHI: Two days after being abducted, a three-year-old boy was rescued from northwest Delhi’s   village. The accused woman has been arrested.

 Police officers said the boy’s parents, who work as labourers, filed a missing complaint on Friday. Based on their statements, police teams were formed and the photographs of the child,  , were circulated. In a footage from the area, a woman was seen carrying the child. On investigation further, the police came to know that the accused was hiding in a house in Badli.
 
 Plain-clothed policemen laid a trap around the house and apprehended the woman. The woman told the police that she committed the crime because she was being taunted by her in-laws for not having a child. DCP (northwest)   said that the woman had been charged under Section 363 (kidnapping).
