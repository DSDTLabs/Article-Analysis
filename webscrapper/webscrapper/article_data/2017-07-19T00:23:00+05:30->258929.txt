Pune: The Hadapsar police on Monday booked a man for allegedly stalking a married   and also attacking her father-in-law with a knife. The suspect’s parents and brother have also been booked for beating up the woman and her family members.

 The woman runs a self-help group. Police said the woman was taking her daughter to school when the man started following her about eight days ago. “He accosted and proposed to her. Fed up with the harassment, the woman narrated her ordeal to her family members, following which her father-in-law visited the residence of the suspect,” police said. This is when the suspect and his family members picked up a fight with the woman’s father-in-law.
