AMARAVATI:   minister for human resources development   on Saturday released the schedules for Andhra Pradesh Teacher Eligibility Test ( ) and   ( ).

 
 While the TET notification will be issued on May 4, the DSC notification will be issued on July 6. 
 
 All 10,351 teachers' posts in the state will be filled up. From May 5 to 22, TET applications are being received.
 
 The hall tickets can be downloaded from June 3. TET tests will be held from June 10 to 21 and the results will be issued on June 30, the minister said.
