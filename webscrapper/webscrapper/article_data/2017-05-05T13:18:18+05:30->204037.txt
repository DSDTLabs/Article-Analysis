GURUGRAM: A 45-year-old   was arrested on Wednesday night for allegedly molesting a five-year-old girl. The van   was produced in a city court on Thursday, and was sent to judicial custody.

 According to police, the girl is a student of Rishi Public School in Sector 31 area. Her parents had hired the   for ferrying her to and back from the school. 
 
 On Tuesday around 2pm, on her way back from school the girl was alone in the van as she was always the last one to be dropped. The accused allegedly touched her private parts. The driver also allegedly threatened her. 
 
 Later in the night, the girl complained to her mother of pain in her private parts. Upon questioning her further, the girl told them about what happened to her in the van.
 
 On Wednesday morning, the parents immediately approached police and lodged a complaint against the van driver. “The victim was sitting on the front seat with the driver when the accused driver sexually assaulted her and threatened to kill her if she complained to her parents,” said the parents in their complaint.
 
 An FIR was registered against the accused under Section 506 (criminal intimidation) of the IPC and section 8 of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act at Sector 40 police station.
 
 The accused, identified as Lalit Kumar, reportedly confessed to the crime. “We have taken the school van in custody. We are verifying whether the van belonged to the school or it was hired. If it is found that the school owned the van, we will call the school officials for questioning,” said inspector Sudhir Kumar, SHO, Sector 40 police station.
 
 “No formal complaint has been made till now. The driver may be of a private van that ferries students of multiple schools. We are not responsible for any private vans,” Vibhu Pal, administrator of the school that the child goes to said.
