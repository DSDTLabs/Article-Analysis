Pune: A woman engineering student of a private college located at Wagholi succumbed to her injuries on Sunday at a private hospital located on Bund Garden road. 

 The student had jumped off the fifth floor of the college building on Saturday afternoon.
 
 According to Lonikand police, the 22-year-old student breathed her last on Sunday under medical observation. According to police, she was found bleeding on the ground floor of the college at around 11.30am on Saturday.
 
 The student, a native of Nashik, was in the last year of her engineering course and was considered a brilliant student. How and why she had jumped off the college building is not yet known. Lonikand police have registered the incident as an accidental death.
 
 She had sustained serious leg and head injuries in the fall, police said.
