CHANDIGARH: Justice AK Mittal has been appointed as the acting Chief Justice of the Punjab and Haryana high court Chandigarh. An order regarding his appointment was issued on Thursday late evening by the central government. 

 Appointment of Justice Mittal, who is first puisne judge of the HC, has been notified by the centre because of retirement of Chief Justice S J Vazifdar on Thursday.
 
 However, the Supreme Court (SC) collegium had recently cleared the name of Justice   of the Allahabad high court as the new Chief Justice of the Punjab and Haryana high court Chandigarh but the central government is yet to notify his appointment. 
 
 Born on September 30, 1958 at Chandigarh, Justice Mittal had passed B.Com. (Hons.) from Sri Ram College of Commerce, Delhi University in the year 1977 and did LL.B. from Faculty of Law, Delhi University in 1980. 
 
 He was elevated as judge of the Punjab and Haryana high court on January 9, 2004.
