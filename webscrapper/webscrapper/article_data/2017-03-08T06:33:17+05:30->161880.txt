 The director further added that after operating flights to international destinations, Jaipur airport is now geared up to provide ‘Visa on arrival’ facility to the applicants filling e-Visa. The facility would be started from April 1 onwards.
 
 Jaipur airport is already operating flights to the destinations, including Bankok, Singapore, Abu Dhabi and now the VISA on arrival would be added feather in its cap.
 
 “We will have four extra counters to facilitate the passengers of almost 161 countries who can avail the facility of VISA on arrival. Two entries would be allowed per passenger,” he added.
