 Dilip alias Lakhiyo Sojitra beat up Dilip Patel at a Holi milan programme organized at Sahyog Society common plot. Sojitra, a resident of Varachha, threatened to kill Patel if he dared to challenge him. The accused was arrested after the victim registered a police complaint against him under IPC Sections 323, 294 (B) and 506 (2).
