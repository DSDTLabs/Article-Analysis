 An SUK official said the assistance amount this year rose to Rs 11.25 lakh for the selected colleges. “The assistance may go up to Rs 75,000 for arts and commence faculties, and Rs 1 lakh will be given to the science faculty.  In 2012-13, Rs 1.45 lakh was allotted under the scheme, followed by Rs 3 lakh in 2013-14, Rs 1.95 lakh in 2014-15 and Rs 5.25 lakh in 2015-16 to the selected colleges.
 
 The official said that considering the increased use of digitisation in routine work and the need for equipment related to this, the university offers an upgrade to those colleges low on finances.
 
 The SUK provdes financial help to the colleges to fulfil basic needs like computer, laptop, fax machine, LCD projector, digital camera, generator and education material, the official said.
