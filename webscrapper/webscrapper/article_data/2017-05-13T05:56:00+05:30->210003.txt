 As many as 2,300 applications have been rejected by various banks and 622 cases are yet to be finalized by the respective banks. This was revealed during a review meeting chaired by district collector   on Thursday.
 
 Expressing concern over the deliberate apathy of the banks towards the scheme, the collector has asked them to submit clarification over the rejection of applications and the status of pending cases.
 
 He has asked bank authorities to submit their monthly distribution report to him and cautioned them of stringent action if they fail to follow the instructions. The collector has urged the banks to expedite the process and distribute loan to the needy unemployed instead of trying to find excuses.
