No list of Hindi cinema’s evergreen songs will be complete without   Jab Kisiko Kisi Se Pyaar Ho Jaaye, from the 70s’ hit, Julie. In fact, composer Rajesh Roshan had won a Filmfare Award for the album. 

 Cut to the present. The romantic track is back in a new avatar in Rakesh Roshan’s upcoming film,   that is directed by Sanjay Gupta. Rendered by Jubin Nautiyal, the reprised version has been picturised on the lead pair,   and  . It showcases the love between a visually-challenged couple, who who set out to make a perfect world for themselves, till destiny wills otherwise.
