Title: Luckunnodu
Language: Telugu
 Cast:  ,  , Tanikella Bharani, Posani Krishna Murali, Prabhas Srinu, Satyam Rajesh.
 Release Date: January 26
 Director: Raajakiran
 Producer: M V V Satyanarayana
 Genre: Action/Entertainer
 Synopsis: The story of a guy named Lucky (Vishnu) who turns out to be unlucky for people he meet, falls in love with Positive Padma (Hansika). While he attempts to convince her with his moves, Lucky gets involved in a money scam and has to bail himself out of it and win the love of his life.
