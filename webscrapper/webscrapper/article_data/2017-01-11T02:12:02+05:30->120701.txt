 Toll collector  , in his complaint filed at the DLF-I police station, said that four men in a white Maruti Swift car reached the toll booth around 6pm on Monday from Gurgaon side and were heading towards Faridabad. When Poswal asked them for the toll fee, they started arguing with him. They forcefully opened the boom barrier and tried to cross it. As Poswal alerted his colleagues and security, the car stopped, one of the accused alighted from the car came near Poswal, pulled out a gun and fired at him and fled the spot in their car. Presence of mind saved Poswal as he immediately ducked and the bullet hit the pillar behind him. 
 
 An FIR has been registered against the four accused under sections 307 (attempt to murder), 34 (common intention) of the IPC and 25-54-59 of the Arms Act. 
 
 “There is no CCTV camera footage of the incident, but we have the registration number of the car ( HR 51-BC-8640) in which the accused were travelling,” said ASI  .
