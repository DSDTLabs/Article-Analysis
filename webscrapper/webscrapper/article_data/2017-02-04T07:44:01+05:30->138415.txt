HYDERABAD: Chief minister   will meet Prime Minister   to request an announcement by the Centre on AIMS, special funds for Mission Bhagiratha and educational institutions, promised in the AP Reorganization Act. He will also lead an all-party delegation to Delhi on February 5 to meet the PM on February 6 to request him to give his assent to the categorisation of the SCs bill.

 On Friday, the chief minister wrote a letter inviting all the political parties to be part of the delegation and to be present in New Delhi on February 5 for a meeting with the prime minister Modi. PCC chief  , AIMIM leader  , BJP state president K Laxman, TDP state president Ramana, CPM secretary Tammineni Veerabhadram, CPI secretary   are among the invitees.
