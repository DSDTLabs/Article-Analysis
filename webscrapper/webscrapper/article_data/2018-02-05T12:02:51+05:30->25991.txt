NEW DELHI: The Central Selection Board of Constable (CSBC) Bihar released the Police Constable Written Exam Result on its official website on Saturday, February 3, 2018. 

 Check your result here: 
 
 
 Now, the candidates who have cleared the written exam will have to appear for the physical examination. The date and admit card for the Bihar Police Constable Physical Examination will be released by the CSBC soon on its official Website - csbc.bih.nic.in
 
 As per the information available on various media reports, the CSBC is likely to release the Bihar Police Constable Physical Examination admit card on or after February 9, 2018. 
 
 The students are advised to keep checking the official website for all the latest information related to the admit card and date of the Physical Evaluation Test . 
 
 Once the admit card is released, you can follow the below steps to download CSBC Bihar Constable Exam Admit Cards:
 
 1) Open the website csbc.bih.nic.in
 
 2) On the homepage, find link for "Download eAdmit Card for Constable Physical Exam" 
 
 3) Click on the link
 
 4) Enter the details as asked
 
 5) Click on submit to get your admit card for Bihar Police Constable Physical Examination.
