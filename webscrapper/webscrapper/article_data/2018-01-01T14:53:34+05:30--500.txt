Late   Amy Winehouse's father   said his daughter's ghost visits him regularly. Winehouse passed away in July 2011 due to alcohol intoxication.

  
  
 "After three years I was thinking maybe that one day she will come back in some shape or form, and she does come back - not physically, but spiritually - all the time," Mitch told to source.
 
 "Her spirit comes and sits on the end of my bed. She just sits there and it looks just like her with her beautiful face and she looks at me. I say to her, 'Are you alright?' because I get nervous with her being there. But it is comforting in a way to know she is here and around me," he added.
