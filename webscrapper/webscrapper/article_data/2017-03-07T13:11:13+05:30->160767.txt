Akshay Kumar’s courtroom drama ‘Jolly LLB 2’ collected approximately Rs 1.35 crore in its fourth weekend, taking its total to around Rs 106.13 crore, according to a report on Boxofficeindia.com. According to the website, the film had collected around Rs 72.98 crore in its first week, Rs 23.77 crore in its second week and Rs 8.03 crore in its third week.

 ‘Jolly LLB 2’ had some funny songs like ‘Jolly good fellow’ and ‘Go pagal’ besides a nice romantic song ‘Bawara mann’. 
 
 The team of ‘Jolly LLB 2’ shared their happiness on entering the Rs 100-crore club by organising a press conference. It was at this event that the makers confirmed that they will be taking the franchise forward with ‘Jolly LLB 3’. The first installment in the movie series, ‘Jolly LLB’ released in 2013 and had Arshad Warsi in the title role.
