BENGALURU: Mayor   on Thursday directed civic officials to expedite the eight-lane signal-free corridor project at Okalipuram near Majestic which promises smooth and faster connectivity between core city areas and west Bengaluru.

 “The project is only half done now. Four lanes of the corridor should be made open by February-end and the remaining should be completed as early as possible,“ she said.
 
 With multiple loops,   between   and  , the corridor promises   in all directions. Though the project was conceived more than a decade ago, it was only in June 2015 that groundwork began.
