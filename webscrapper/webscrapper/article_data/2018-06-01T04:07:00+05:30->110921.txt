Ahmedabad: Ahmedabad police commissioner A K Singh, has expressed his desire to go on central deputation. Sources said Singh recently wrote to the Centre expressing this desire. The state government has given him a NOC and conveyed this to the ministry of home affairs, the sources added. Singh was recently empanelled as DGP at the Centre. Another IPS officer, Gyanendrasinh Malik, presently Range IG Surat, has already been given clearance for central deputation in the Border Security Force (BSF). Sources said he may be posted in the Punjab or Gujarat Frontiers.
