CHENNAI:   on Saturday launched a project for the   called   - Vision for the differently abled. 

 The project aims at providing a privilege card which will enable comprehensive eye screening, out-patient procedures, eye surgeries, spectacles for refractive errors, lasers for diabetic retinopathy and glaucoma and medicines.
 
  All treatment modalities will be done free of cost for differently abled members in this project, said the hospital. VISDA is designed to benefit about 2.5 lakh members of   differently abled federation charitable trust. Free eye surgeries will be done at the Base Hospital.
