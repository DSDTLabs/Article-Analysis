A Holi celebration was organised by   and   at the residence of Kavita, soon after the festival. Attended by several eminent ladies of the city and Holi revellers, the event marked fun and frolic for everyone who attended the do. Sprinkling colourful water, dancing to hit numbers of Bollywood, especially those composed for the festival of colours, eating several mouth-watering dishes and wishing each other Holi by applying gulaal, marked the party.
