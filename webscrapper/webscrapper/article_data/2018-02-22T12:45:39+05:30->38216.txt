MHOW: A 22-year-old youth was arrested for sending obscene messages and making calls on mobile phone of a girl at Bhatkheri village under Kishangnaj police station limits.

 The girl had approached ‘We Care For You’ with a complaint that a youth used to   and talk to her in obscene language and also sent lewd messages on her mobile phone. 
 
 During investigation, the cops found that one Rohit Chowdhary (22), a resident of Dhannad village near Pithampur was making the calls and sending the messages. The case was transferred to Kishanganj police station as the incident was of that area. Kishanganj police arrested Rohit and registered a case against him under section 354 A of the IPC and 67 of IT Act.
