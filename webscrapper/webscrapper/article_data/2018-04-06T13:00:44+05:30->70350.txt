KOCHI: Taking into account the festival season and heavy rush during vacations, the Kochi   Rail Limited ( ) has decided to waive parking fees at all Metro stations except   and Edappally. The waiver will remain in effect for two months.

 During the holiday season, the Metro agency expects heavy passenger movement, and commuters will get the benefit of this waiver. The offer will come into effect from Friday.
 
 KMRL managing director A P M   said, “We hope that the waiver of parking fee would attract more passengers during this holiday season and would also help in controlling unruly parking across the Metro corridor.”
 
 Meanwhile, KMRL said that other motorists could also use the Metro’s parking facility. For parking cars, commuters have to pay a fee of Rs 20 per hour and Rs 10 per hour for bikes.
