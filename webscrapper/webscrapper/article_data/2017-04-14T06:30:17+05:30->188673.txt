 According to officials, the incident took place due to the leakage in the valve of a chlorine gas cylinder used for chlorination in a water tank in the   locality of the village. The gas spread in the locality affecting those staying there.
 
 Officials said that the family of the operator of the waterworks was the worst affected. Four members of the family were rushed to the SSGH along with 15 others from the village. Those affected by the gas were complaining of eye and throat irritation as well as coughing along with chest pain. Doctors said that the condition of one of them was serious while four of them were shifted to the general ward from the emergency medicine department of the hospital.
