Surat: A 34-year-old woman, married to a diamond polisher, who had set herself ablaze on April 30 at Katargam, died during treatment at Surat Municipal Institute of Medical Education and Research (SMIMER) here on Saturday.

 Tina Modi had set herself on fire at her residence in Ravi Apartment and she succumbed to burn injuries today. Her husband Kamlesh and 10-year-old son, who were sleeping in the bedroom, woke up due to smoke coming from the drawing room of the house. The couple, married for 11 years, had a son and a daughter.
 
 Preliminary investigation revealed that the couple had developed differences of late. Parents of Tina were to be called to discuss some issues. Tina thought this would be humiliating for her family members and committed suicide, police said.
