Ludhiana: The CIA-I of Ludhiana police has busted a gang of burglars with the arrest of three accused who used to target mobile stores.

 Police have recovered 4 LCDs, 40 mobile phones, tools used in breaking locks and a pick-up auto from their possession. A case under sections 457 (lurking house-trespass or house-breaking by night in order to commit offence punishable with imprisonment), 380 (theft in dwelling house), 511 (attempt to commit offences) of the Indian Penal Code (IPC) has been registered against the accused at police station division number 6.
 
 In-charge at CIA-I inspector Prem Singh said the accused, Mangal Singh and Harpreet Singh of Jassiyan and Pawan Kumar of Gill Chowk, have been arrested from the T-point of Transport Nagar during special checking. He added that the accused used to target mobile stores and electronic showrooms and had burgled shops and showrooms in Khanna and Phillaur recently.
 
 More members of the gang could also be arrested soon, he added.
