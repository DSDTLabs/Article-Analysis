Panaji: Days after delivering an offshore patrol vessel to the Indian Coast Guard, Goa Shipyard Limited ( ) on Saturday handed over one to the Sri Lankan navy.

 The vessel, with pennant number P623, is the first of the two offshore patrol vessels that Sri Lanka ordered in an April 2013 contract.
