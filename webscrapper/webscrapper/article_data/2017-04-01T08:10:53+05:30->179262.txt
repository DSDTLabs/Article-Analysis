THIRUVANANTHAPURAM:   vice-chancellor   has filed a complaint before state police chief Loknath Behera alleging that   leader and   member A A Rahim raised death threats against him. The complaint was filed on Friday and the DGP’s office confirmed receiving the complaint.

 According to the complaint, a group of senate members barged into KU VC’s office room on Thursday, led by A A Rahim, and created a commotion. During the melee, Rahim threatened him. They were protesting against the non-cooperation of   in the conduct of theKerala University youth festival.
 
 The VC also filed another complaint before chief minister   and education minister C Raveendranath demanding to expel Rahim from KU Syndicate.
