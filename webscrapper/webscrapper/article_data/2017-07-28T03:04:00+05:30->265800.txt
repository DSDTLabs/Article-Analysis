Panaji: The   (DoE) has shifted the Ganesh Chaturthi break for schools, which will now be from August 24 to August 30, as per a circular issued on Wednesday.

 Before this change, the break was to be from August 23 to August 29 as per the 2017-18 academic schedule, declared in January by the DoE. This schedule has two fewer working days, bringing Goa on par with the rest of India as is required by the Right To Education Act.
