Chandrapur: A full grown female bear was found dead in a farm on the outskirts of village Palasgarh under Kurkheda forest range in Gadchiroli on Tuesday. Foresters have overruled poaching and said that the beast died a natural death.  Livestock development officer Kurkheda Dr SS Sonar did the post-mortem and concluded that it had died of some ailment. Later, the dead body was burnt in presence of witnesses at the same spot.  ACF Deepak Choudikar confirmed the death of sloth bear and maintained the it had died of ailment ruling out the suspicion of electrocution.
