Ludhiana: Payal police have booked three persons for allegedly attacking a   of the Congress at his native village, Dau Majra, on Tuesday evening. 

 The accused were identified as Amarjeet Singh, Parameet Singh and Baljeet Singh of Dau Majra village.
 
 In his statement to police, Jasvir Singh Jassi said he along with his wife was on an evening walk outside his house when the trio, under the influence of liquor, surrounded them and started abusing and manhandling them. Jasvir told police that as he tried to save himself by running inside his house, the trio followed him and caught hold of him and beat him up. He added that his neighbours freed him from the clutches of the trio with great difficulty. 
 
 An injured Jasvir was later taken to civil hospital. After getting information about the incident, police reached the complainant and arrested the accused later. 
 
 Police have booked the accused under sections 323 (voluntarily causing hurt), 324 (voluntarily causing hurt by dangerous weapons or means), 506 (criminal intimidation), 341 (wrongful restraint), 452 (house trespass after preparation for hurt, assault or wrongful restraint) of IPC. Police said that the accused have been detained. They added that the two sides already had strained relation because of some issue related to sarpanch elections.
