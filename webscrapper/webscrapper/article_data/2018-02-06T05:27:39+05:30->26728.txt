THIRUVANANTHAPURAM: A   has imposed a travel ban on Binoy Vinodini Kodiyeri, son of   CPM secretary Kodiyeri Balakrishnan, after a cheating case was filed against him by Dubai-based Jaas Tourism LLC.

 The ban order, issued on February 1, also wants Binoy to pay AED 10,00,000 (approx Rs 1.73 crore) to the company. He has also been directed to submit his passport to the court.
 
 Interestingly, the   state secretariat and Balakrishnan himself had claimedearlier Binoy had no cases againsthim in India or abroad. People close to Binoy had also produced a certificate purportedly from Dubai police “confirming” this.
 
 Binoy’s brother Bineesh confirmed the travel ban reports. “Binoy is in Dubai to face thecivilcase and hehas not run away. Itis a factthat there is a case pertaining to the settlement of Rs 1.72 crore; he is staying back in Dubai for a settlement,” he said. However, he refuted reportsthat authoritieshad detainedBinoy at the airport.
