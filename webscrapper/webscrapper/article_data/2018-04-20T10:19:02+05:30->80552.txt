The rally in tech scrips was being led by the country's largest IT outsourcing company, Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) which was up 5 per cent at 10.07 am. TCS while declaring its fourth quarter earnings on Thursday, announced a 1:1 bonus for its shareholders. 

 
 
 
 This is the third bonus share offering by the company since its listing in 2004. TCS had allotted 1:1 bonus shares in 2006 and 2009. Last year, which happened to be the company's 50th anniversary saw it crossing the brand value of $10 billion. In the January-March quarter, the company reported a net profit of Rs 6,925 crore, up 4.57 per cent against Rs 6,622 crore posted in the same quarter last year.
