Jaipur: Unidentified   snatched bag from a shopkeeper containing Rs 2 lakh near   Friday morning. 

 The incident took place when the shopkeeper was sitting in his car with the bag. Two youths on the pretext of asking directions talked to him while a third person came from behind and decamped with bag. 
 
 The shopkeeper gave a complaint at Vidhyadhar Nagar police following which a team inspected the spot and cob through the   footage of the area. Official informed TOI that the victim was headed to a nearby bank to deposit money. However, even before he could step-out of the car, the miscreant made away with the bag.
