Rajkot: A man was shot in his leg when his friend fired off his gun in a marriage procession in Porbandar on Tuesday night. The complaint in this connection was lodged on Wednesday night.

 Police said Vipul Satyadev (34), a resident of Porbandar, was injured when Prakash Rajdev shot from his pistol accidentally during the marriage procession in Porbandar. 
 
 “The bullet ricochetted off the ground and hit Satyadev in his left leg,” said an official from Kamlabaug police station of Porbandar adding that Rajdev, a resident of Rajkot, was booked under Section 308 of Indian Penal Code and would be arrested soon.
