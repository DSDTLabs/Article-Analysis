Pune: A two-day literary meet for teachers at Balgandharva Rangmandir will begin from Saturday. The event has been organised by Shaniwarwada Kala Mahotsav and the education department of the zilla parishad.

 Lectures, poetry slam, storytelling, symposiums, reading theatre will be held during the  . It will be inaugurated by founder of Symbiosis S B Mujumdar. 
 
 BJP MLA Vijay Kale said, “Unlike the status and respect that teachers commanded in the earlier times, these days they are seen as labourers who need to finish their work on time. The additional work that teachers are burdened with apart from teaching affects their creativity and sensitivity. We are conducting this convention to boost their morale.”
 
 At least 1,200 teachers will attend the meet.
