LUCKNOW: About 40 persons were injured when a bus carrying them overturned in Nigohan on early Monday. The victims were coming from Mughalsarai to Lucknow to attend   Narendra Modi's rally at   

 A police team reached the spot on getting information. ASP rural Pratap Gopendra Yadav said the injured were admitted to a community health centre in Mohanlalganj. Later, 20 injured persons were taken to the trauma centre. They were discharged in the evening. Police said the accident occurred due to fog and reckless driving by bus driver.
