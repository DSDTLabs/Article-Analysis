MANALI: A 13-year-old girl from   was allegedly raped by two persons, including a juvenile, on Tuesday, the police said. Medical examination has confirmed rape and the police have launched a manhunt to nab both accused. According to the police one of the assaulters was known to the survivor. 

 The accused on Tuesday lured the girl to a room where they raped her. When the parents of the girl got to know about the incident, they reached women police station at Kullu to lodge the complaint at around midnight. A case under Section 376D of IPC and Section 4 of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act was registered by the police.
 
 Kullu women police station station house officer   said a case of rape has been registered and a manhunt launched to put the accused behind the bars. "One of the accused is a juvenile. The case is sensitive and we are dealing with it on priority. Both of them will be in our custody very soon," she said.
