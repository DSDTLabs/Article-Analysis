MADURAI: The Madras high court Madurai bench on Tuesday has closed a petition filed by   member  's son Nazar Ali seeking anticipatory bail in a cheating case.

 Based on a complaint lodged by one Rofina, the   police had registered a case against Nazar Ali for alleged offences punishable under Sections 294 (b) (obscene acts and songs), 417 (punishment for cheating), 420 (cheating and dishonestly inducing deliver of property) and 506 (i) (criminal intimidation) of IPC and Section 4 of Tamil Nadu Prohibition of the Women Harassment Act.
 
 Apprehending arrest at the hands of the Karaikudi police, Ali filed the petition.
 
 Justice   who heard the plea on Tuesday passed the order closing the petition after accepting the submissions of the additional advocate general (AAG). The AAG said that the entire alleged offence took place in Chennai and therefore the FIR registered needs to be transferred to Chennai on the basis of jurisdictional point of view. Hence, it would be proper for the petitioner to approach appropriate forum in Chennai, the AAG said.
 
 According to prosecution, Nazar Ali and Rofina had got introduced and developed cordial relationship. On account of the same, he made a false promise to marry her. Further, he received Rs 50 lakh from her with an intention to cheat her. Subsequently, he tried to marry another woman. When she came to know about it, Rofina questioned him, for which he abused her using filthy language and gave her life threat.
