Staged at an amphitheatre in Film Nagar recently, Taher Ali Baig’s theatre production,  , was a fun filled adaptation of the classic tale from  . Unlike in the original in which Ali Baba is the hero who steals the valuables from the dacoits, the play offered a modern twist to the tale by telling the story from the perspective of the dacoit, Sultan Sheikh. The story focused on how good guys come and rob the thieves of their hard earned loot, and how Sultan Sheikh pledges to hunt down Ali, a nice man, and chop his head off by all means; even if it means putting his best men in jars or seducing Ali’s eccentric mother, Mum Baba.
