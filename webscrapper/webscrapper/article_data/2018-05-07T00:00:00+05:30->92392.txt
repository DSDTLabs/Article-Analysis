Jisshu U Sengupta, who recently slipped into the shoes of Gangadhar Rao in the Kangana Ranaut-starrer Manikarnika: The Queen of Jhansi, has been signed on for one more Hindi film. Sources say the actor will be seen playing a pivotal role in Mahesh Manjrekar’s Devidas Thakur. The film also stars Naseeruddin Shah, Shruti Hassan, Vidyut Jammwal and others. A source close to the film’s unit informed, “The shooting of the film has begun and Jisshu has shot for a few scenes. He will soon be joining the team for the second schedule in Mumbai.
