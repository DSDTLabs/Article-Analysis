The   (EC) has ordered repolling in six booths of Sonamura, Dhanpur, Teliamura, Sabroom, Ampinagar and Kadamtala-Kurti constituencies on Monday. 

 The EC direction said it has come to the notice of the poll panel that presiding officers inadvertently did not clear the control unit of EVMs after mock poll on the day of polling in seven polling stations where matching of VVPAT slips with the EVM count was advised. 
 
 Taking exception to the decision, CPM state secretary Bijan Dhar lashed out at state election officials and accused them of 'criminal negligence' in performing their duties. "No political party had raised this demand and the EC ordered repolling on the basis of reports furnished by state election officials. TNN
