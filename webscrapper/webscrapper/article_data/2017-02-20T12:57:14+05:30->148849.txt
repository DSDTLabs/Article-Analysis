A popular   actress, who works in films across  , was kidnapped by her former driver and his friends on the night of February 17, Friday. The men, who reportedly set her free towards February 18th morning near the house of a filmmaker in Kakkanadu, was arrested later by the police.

 
 
 As per reports, police has booked her driver as of now, and the main culprit of the case is her former driver. They abducted the actress, who was returning from a shoot in Thrissur, late night near Angamaly. Apparently, they rammed their vehicle into the actress' car, later forced themselves into the vehicle and drove off. 
 
 The actress reportedly filed a case with the help of the director post which prominent police officers visited her and started with the investigation.
