Ahmedabad: As part of its golden jubilee year activities, Vikram A   ( ) has organized a three-day innovation fair to mark National Science Day on February 28. The fair will continue till March 2.

 The organizers said that it will be amalgamation of science fair, technology exhibition, interactive science workshop and community meet-up. It will have demonstrations, displays, and performances in science and mathematics including Interactive Science Corners, Science Talks, Model Rocketry and 3-D printing among others.
 
 “The free for all initiative aims at providing hands-on activities including model rocketry, model making, science games and so on to popularize scientific ideas and nurture inquisitive temperament,” said  , director of VASCSC.
 
 Organizers said that there are over 200 schools participating with their projects.
