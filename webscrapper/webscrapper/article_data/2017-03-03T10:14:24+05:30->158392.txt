AGRA: Students will no longer have to run to Dr   to get their certificates verified. The university has started an online facility for verification of documents. After paying a nominal fees of Rs 300 online, the students can upload their certificates for verification and can also check the status. 

 The applicants will have to create an account and fill in their details, following which the document can be uploaded. Applicants will have to pay Rs 300 as verification fees, for which e-banking and credit card options are available. User can check the status of their   on the dashboard. "The facility will ensure that verification is done in time and students no longer have to run to the university. Companies and government departments can easily get verification done on atdbrauverification.org," said university PRO Girijashankar Sharma.
