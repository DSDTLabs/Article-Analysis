 According to police, Biswas has allegedly confessed to have bought a knife and killed Mandal since he used to taunt him repeatedly and call him a "pagol" (lunatic) and had assaulted him on a number of occasions. "The youth has confessed his crime. Police are however yet to recover the murder weapon - the knife.
 
 Mandal's relatives have said before police that he himself is under medication and is not a person of sound mental health. "My son himself was a bit eccentric and was undergoing treatment.
