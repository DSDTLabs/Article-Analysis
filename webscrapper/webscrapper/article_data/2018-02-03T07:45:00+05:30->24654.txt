GURUGRAM: Four people were killed in less than 24 hours — late on Wednesday to early on Thursday morning — in the city in separate hit-and-run  . 

 In the first one, Karan Singh (45) and his friend Hava Singh (42), travelling from a village in Rewari to Sohna on Karan’s bike on Thursday morning, were killed on the spot after a dumper truck hit their bike from the rear end near Hailey Mandi. A few commuters approached police, who then informed Karan’s son. An FIR was filed against an unidentified driver. Both the deceased were from Rewari. 
 
 In another case, a 26-year-old resident of Bhondsi was killed near Badshahpur on Wednesday night. According to his brother Aditya Singh, the deceased, Jaiswal, a salesman, was hit by a vehicle which is yeat to be traced. “When I went to the spot after I got a call from a passerby, my brother was lying on the ground with his bike wrecked beside him,” Singh told police. Jaiswal was declared brought dead on arrival by doctors. An FIR was filed at the Badshahpur police station.
 
 In the third case, a resident of Gadoli, Prakash Kumar (32), had gone to Manesar late on Wednesday night. He was hit by an unidentified vehicle in Garhi Harsuru, his brother Shailender Singh told the police in his complaint. Singh said the vehicle had mowed him down. Police said they have registered a case under Section 279 (rash driving) and 304A(death by negligence) of the IPC against an unidentified person and were investigating the case.
