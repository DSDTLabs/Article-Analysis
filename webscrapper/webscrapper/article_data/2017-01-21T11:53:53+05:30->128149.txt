VISAKHAPATNAM: A 50-year old auto-rickshaw driver, identified as K Kanakaraju, committed suicide by hanging himself from a tree near   bus stop in Kailasapuram on Friday. 

 According to  , Kanakaraju was suffering from severe health ailments and was also addicted to alcohol. On Thursday night, Kanakaraju came home inebriated and left a few minutes later. However, he did not return. Local residents found the body hanging from a tree and informed the cops. The body has been sent for  .
