NEW DELHI: Commuters have been advised to avoid 14 stretches in central Delhi on Thursday as these would be blocked intermittently for the Asean leaders throughout the day. 

 These include Bhairon Marg, Mathura Road, Subramanium Bharti Marg, Prithviraj Road, Shahjahan Road, Motilal Nehru Marg, Akbar Road, Ashoka Road, Rajinder Prasad Road, KG Marg, Copernicus Marg, Tilak Marg, Purana Qila Road and Sher Shah Suri Marg. 
 
 The   police has suggested use of alternate routes through Ring Road, Ridge Road, Aurbindo Marg, Madarsa T-Point, Safdarjung Road, Kemal Ataturk Marg, Rani Jhansi Road and Minto Road.
 
 Traffic officers have asked commuters to completely avoid C-Hexagon, if possible. No parking space will be available in and around   due to the   celebrations. A large gathering is also expected in and around India Gate from January 26 to 29. To ensure smooth traffic, restrictions and   may be imposed on certain  . The metro services at Pragati Maidan, Central Secretariat, Khan Market, Mandi House, Patel Chowk and Udyog Bhawan may also be suspended between 4-7pm in case of an excessively huge turnout at India Gate. 
 
 DTC has been told to divert its buses bound for Central Secretariat and CP through the Panchsheel Marg-Simon Boliver Marg-Vande Matram Marg-Shankar Road-Park Street route. The buses for Central Secretariat will terminate at Udyan Marg and return by Kali Bari Marg-Mandir Marg-Shankar Road.
