HYDERABAD: A government school principal’s husband was booked for allegedly misbehaving with a girl student. Though the case was registered a week ago, police have not arrested the culprit till date.

 On May 1, the victim’s mother approached police alleging Government Gurukul School, Gowlidoddi, principal Pramodini’s husband Damodar had on Diwali day last year misbehaved with the girl, Gachibowli police said . A case was registered against Damodar under the POCSO Act and Section 354 (outraging modesty of a woman) of the IPC.
 
 A 13-year-old girl approached Sanatnagar police alleging that her step-father made a bid to rape her. He was booked under the POCSO Act.
