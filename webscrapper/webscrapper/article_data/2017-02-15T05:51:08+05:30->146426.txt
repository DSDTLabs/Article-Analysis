PANAJI: Three years after a chargesheet was filed, the hearing of arguments before charge against Tehelka founder editor  , is likely to begin on February 27 before a local court at Mapusa. Tejpal has been accused of raping a junior colleague in 2013.
 
  Additional sessions judge Vijaya Pol, is likely to hear the arguments.
  
  Tejpal’s counsel  , said, “We will advance the arguments before charge on February 27. All the material we have demanded has been supplied to us. This includes a clone copy of   footage and the prosecutrix’s mobile phone.
  
  The crime branch, which filed a chargesheet against Tejpal on February 17, 2014, seeks to prosecute the Tehelka founder editor under IPC Sections 354 (assaulting or using criminal force on a woman with an intent to outrage her modesty), 354-A (outraging modesty), 341 (wrongful restraint), 342 (wrong confinement), 376 (rape), 376(2) (f) (person in position of trust for authority over women, committing rape of such women) and 376(2) (k) (rape of a woman by a person being in position of control or dominance over the woman).
  
  Running into 12 volumes, the chargesheet was filed after a 79-day-long investigation that began from November 30, 2013, the day Tejpal was arrested. In all, 152 witnessesincluding investigation officer Sunita Sawantrecorded their statements against the accused. The main chargesheet consists of 132 pages with an additional 30 pages of witnesses’ statements. The annexures run into 2,684 pages.
  
  “There is sufficient evidence in the form of documents, statements and electronic records... to prove that on November 7, 2013, the victim was raped, sexually harassed, and her modesty outraged inside an elevator of a starred hotel at Bambolim, Goa. There is also sufficient evidence ... to prove that on November 8, 2013, she was again sexually harassed, and her modesty outraged inside an elevator,” the chargesheet read.
  
  On November 7 and 8, 2013, Tejpal allegedly sexually assaulted a young colleague at an event organized by Tehelka at the starred hotel. On November 22, Goa police lodged a suo motu FIR against him. Tejpal returned to Goa on November 28, 2013 and was arrested on November 30, 2013 after his anticipatory bail plea was rejected. He was granted interim bail on May 19, 2014 to attend the cremation of his mother and since then, has been out of jail. He was granted bail in July 2014.
