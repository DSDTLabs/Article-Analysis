Thanjavur: Sleuths from the directorate of vigilance and anti-corruption (DVAC) raided the regional office of the assistant director of town panchayats in Thanjavur and recovered Rs 3.80 lakh unaccounted money, sources said.

 Based on a tip-off that some money was set to be distributed among the officials at a meeting scheduled to be held at the office headed by the assistant director Ilangovan, the vigilance officials led by DSP Rajendran carried out raids in the late hours of Thursday.
 
 In the process, they recovered the money which was not accounted, officials said. Following the raids, vigilance officials commenced their interrogation with the town panchayat office staff and officials, sources said.
