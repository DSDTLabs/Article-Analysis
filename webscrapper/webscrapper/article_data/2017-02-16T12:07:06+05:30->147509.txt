NAVI MUMBAI:The Nerul cops have booked three men on Tuesday for duping 17 men of Rs 9.41 lakh by assuring them of lucrative jobs offers in Oman and Singapore.

 The complaint was lodged by Thane resident   and 16 others, alleging that the three men, who had set up an overseas recruitment agency in a mall in sector 19A, Nerul, claimed that there were vacancies in Oman and Singapore for the technical posts.
 
 The cops said that accused had also advertised in newspapers about job offers. The accused accepted money from applicants for visa processing fees, agreement with overseas companies, flight tickets and recruitment agency commission. They even took their passports for visa stamping between September and December 2016.
