 The deceased were identified as R Mohanasundaram, 59, a farmer from No 4 Veerapandi, and his wife M Kalavathi, 55, a lab assistant with   at GN Mills.
 
 According to police, the accident took place when the couple was travelling on a motorcycle on Coimbatore-Mettupalayam road. “When they neared Pioneer rolling mill at Narasimmanaickenpalayam, the truck hit their two-wheeler from behind. In the impact, the couple fell on the road and the truck run over them, killing the duo on the spot,” an investigation officer said.
 
 Local people alerted the Periyanaickenpalayam police, who rushed to the spot and sent the bodies to the Coimbatore Medical College and Hospital for postmortem. Police have registered a case against the truck driver, M Selvam, 35, of Dharmapuri.
