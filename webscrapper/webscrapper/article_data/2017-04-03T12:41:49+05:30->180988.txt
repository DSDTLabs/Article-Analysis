NASHIK: After nine continuous holidays,   auction will restart at   Agriculture Produce Market Committee ( ) on Monday.

 The Lasalgaon APMC remained shut from March 25 to April 2 as the traders were busy in completing their accounts for financial year 2016-17. 
 
 On March 24, the average wholesale price was at Rs 530 per quintal and the minimum and maximum prices were at Rs 300 and Rs 600 per quintal, when 20,000 quintals of onions were auctioned. 
 
 Around 60% of arrivals was of   crop and the rest   crop. The arrival of summer onions is expected to increase from this week, an APMC official said.
