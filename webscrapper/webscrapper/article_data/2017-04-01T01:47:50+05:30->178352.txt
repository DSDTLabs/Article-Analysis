Lucknow: A nursing student at King George's Medical University ( ) was admitted to the trauma centre after she allegedly consumed a   substance on Friday afternoon. Her condition is stated to be stable. 

 The incident took place at 12.30pm when the BSc first year student consumed a poisonous substance after a scuffle with her classmates. Proctor Prof RAS Kushwaha visited her at the trauma centre and said the issue will be looked into and action would be taken.
