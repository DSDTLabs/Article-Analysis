POINGUINIM: The body of a 23-year-old Odisha boy, who went missing from a vessel while fishing in the deep seas since May 15, was retrieved on Thursday at   where it was spotted floating at Cabo de Rama. 

 The coastal police at Talpona informed   police about the body, following which the police swung into action. After the inquest panchnama was conducted, the body was sent for post mortem.
