NEW DELHI: The   (MBOSE) announced the Class 12 (HSSLC) board exam results for Science, Commerce and Vocational courses today, May 10, 2018. The results can be checked on the official portal. 

 
 The results were declared on the board's official website (mbose.in) on Thursday around 10:00 am.
 
 The candidates who have appeared for the examination can register below to get their result through SMS or via email.
 
 
  
  
 
 
  
 Last year the HSSLC results for Science, Commerce, and Vocational were released on May 8 and the result for Arts stream was released later with SSLC result. 
 
 The result booklets will also be available at the Board office in Shillong/Tura and Jowai MBOSE Cell after its declaration. The full result booklet can also be downloaded from the MBoSE official website mbose.in. 
 
 The students can also access their results on the following websites: megresults.nic.in, meghalayaonline.in, results.net/meghalaya and examresults.net/meghalaya. 
 
 Around 29,840 students have appeared for the HSSLC exam which was conducted from March 6 29, 2018. 
 
 The number of female (12,801) students is more than male (10,451) students.
