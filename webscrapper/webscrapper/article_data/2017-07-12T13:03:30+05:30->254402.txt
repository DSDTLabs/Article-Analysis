RAJKOT: Twenty-six persons were booked of which at least 14 were arrested for illegal mining of sand from the riverbed in Vastadi village of Wadhwan taluka of Surendranagar. Police also seized equipment worth Rs 4.95 crore from Vastadi village during the raid on Tuesday.

 The main accused in the case —   and   — are yet to be arrested. The FIR was lodged at   police station by assistant sub-inspector   of Rajkot RR Cell.
 
 According to police, the accused used to illegally mine sand from the river and sell it in the market. Based on specific information, police raided the riverbed and seized 34 trucks, five earth movers, and six small-sized boats.
