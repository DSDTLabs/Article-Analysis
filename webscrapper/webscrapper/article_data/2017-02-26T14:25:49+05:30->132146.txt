The first teaser of ace director Mani Ratnam's Kaatru Veliyidai was released on the occasion of 68th Republic Day. The less-than 50 seconds teaser is a visual ecstasy and gives a peak into Rahman's music as well. The first single will be out on February 2. Sony Music has bagged the audio rights of the film.
