American   Pitbull has dropped out of his concert scheduled for the ongoing   Film Festival, due to certain issues with his voice.

  According to reports, the event 'Cannes Party and Concert' was to be held in the honour of actor   receiving the first Cinema Icon Award.
 
  
  
  A spokesperson from the organisers confirmed the news to a lookout for another musical talent who will be available for the much-awaited gala night, scheduled for Tuesday.
 
  The rep said, "Pitbull is having issues with his voice. We are working on a replacement/this situation at the moment."
