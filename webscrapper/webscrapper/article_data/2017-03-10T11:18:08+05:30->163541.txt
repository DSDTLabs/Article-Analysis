DIBRUGARH: A 12-hour dawn-to-dusk   called by the   in protest against the   led to a total shutdown in the town on Thursday. 

 On March 6, an office of the All   Students' Union (Aasu) was vandalised by members of Nikhil Bharat Bangali Udbastu Samannay Samiti (NBBUSS) at Silapathar in Dhemaji district. They were allegedly instigated by their president Subodh Biswas who is currently on the run.

Though government offices were open, attendance was thin. In several areas of the town, bandh supporters burnt tyres on the roads to voice their protest.
