MUMBAI: A 60-year-old man from Nalasopara has been arrested for allegedly raping his teenage granddaughter last week. 

 The police said that the accused, who is the 14-year- old girl's maternal grandfather, sexually assaulted her soon after her mother left home for work around 9 am on Wednesday . "He tied up her hands and legs and abused her sexually," said a police officer, adding that the girl, her mother and grandfather had been living in the same house. 
 
 In her police complaint, the girl has stated that her grandfather threatened her after sexually assaulting her. The girl confided in her mother two days later.
 
 The police said that a medical examination has confirmed sexual abuse. The accused, who has been booked for rape, has been sent to police custody.
