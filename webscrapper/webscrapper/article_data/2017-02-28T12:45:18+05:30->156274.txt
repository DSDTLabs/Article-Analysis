 Since in the Tejpal case there were such directions the case will continue to be heard in the Mapusa court, a decision that was conveyed to the Tejpal counsel on Monday itself, resulting in them seeking adjournment.
 
 Special public prosecutor Francisco Tavora was also present in the court.
 
 The crime branch, which filed a charge sheet against Tejpal on February 17, 2014, seeks to prosecute Tejpal under IPC Sections 354 (assaulting or using criminal force on a woman with an intent to outrage her modesty), 354-A (outraging modesty), 341 (wrongful restraint), 342 (wrong confinement), 376 (rape), 376(2) (f) (person in position of trust or authority over women, committing rape of such women) and 376(2) (k) (rape of a woman by a person being in position of control or dominance over the woman).
