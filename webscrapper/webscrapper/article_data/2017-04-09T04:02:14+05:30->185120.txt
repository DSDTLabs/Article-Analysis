NOIDA: Tension prevailed in Sector 62 on Saturday morning after a group of people gheraoed a small place with tents for   celebration and refused to vacate it after the celebrations.

 The residents covered the spot with clothes from three sides and placed idols of Hanuman and other deities. They were planning to occupy the spot permanently for a temple when the police officers swung into action. A police officer said the locals of Khoda Colony and its adjoining areas had occupied the land, that belongs to  , for prayers.
