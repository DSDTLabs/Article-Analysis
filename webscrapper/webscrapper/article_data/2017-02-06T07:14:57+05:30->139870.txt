VARANASI: The wife of a joint magistrate drowned in the Ganga on Sunday evening.

 According to reports, the joint magistrate   and his wife Sunita (35) had gone to   in   area to offer `deep daan' around 6.30pm. While performing the ritual in Ganga, Sunita slipped into the deep waters.
 
 Local boatmen were engaged in a rescue operation launched soon after police and senior officials arrived on the spot. The body was fished out from the river by late evening.Mirzapur SP   said the body has been sent for post-mortem.
