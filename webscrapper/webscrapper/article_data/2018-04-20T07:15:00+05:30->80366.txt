Ludhiana: The police have arrested a constable for attempting to rape a 10-year-old girl when she was sleeping in her house. The mother of the girl spotted the accused trying to sexually abuse the girl and even nabbed him for a while, but he managed to flee the spot.

 Later, police arrested him when he was trying to flee the city.
 
 The police have registered a case under Sections 376 (rape) and 511 (punishment for attempting to commit offences punishable with imprisonment for life or other imprisonment) of IPC and Sections 8 and 10 of Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act.
 
 The constable was deputed at Meharban police station and lived in the same locality where the girl lived with her mother in the labour quarters. Mother of the girl said on Wednesday late evening she had gone to see her neighbour, while her daughter was sleeping in their rented room. Soon she noticed that the light of her room was switched off. She thought her daughter might have switched off the light.
