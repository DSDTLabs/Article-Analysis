Actor Mukesh’s son Shravan’s feature film debut ‘Kalyanam’s’ first song is here and it has already been winning fans.

 The song, that goes ‘Pande Nee Enne,’ is sung by Thaikoodam Bridge fame Siddharth Menon and it is composed by Prakash Alex. The song is written by Rajeev Nair.
 
 
  
 
 The film’s heroine Varsha Bollamma had grabbed fame through her dubsmash, in which she looked just like Nazriya Nazim. It’s the first Malayalam film of the Coorg-based actress.
