GUWAHATI: The   (NIA), Guwahati, arrested senior   (Independent) leader  , alias Joydeep Cheleng, here on Friday. He was arrested in connection with a case the NIA had registered against Ulfa-I in 2013. 

 Sources in NIA said, "He has been taken into custody for six days. His arrest is likely to throw light on the activities of   leaders like chairman   and commander-in-chief Paresh Baruah."
