
 
  Kannada activist   has called for Sandalwood bandh on March 11 and a huge rally will be held in Bengaluru to protest against dubbing. Sandalwood actors Shivarajkumar, Jaggesh, Shivaram, Srinivas Murthy and several other actors have expressed their views against dubbing as the life of more than 6,000 people who works in film industry will be in jeopardy.
