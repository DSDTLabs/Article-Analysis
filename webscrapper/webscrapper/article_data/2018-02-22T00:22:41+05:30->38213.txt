Pune: A man (24) was arrested on Tuesday for outraging the   of a 19-year-old woman while she was feeding her child in her home.

 The suspect had abused the woman and threatened to kill her, too. An officer of the Bhosari MIDC police said the incident occurred on Monday evening, when the woman was alone at home.
 
 The suspect, who knew her, barged into her house when she was feeding her child. “The suspect outraged her modesty and talked to her in lewd language,” the officer said.
 
 When the woman resisted the suspect, he became furious and abused her. “He also beat up the woman and threatened to kill her before leaving her house,” the officer said.
 
 After a discussion with her husband, the woman lodged a complaint.
