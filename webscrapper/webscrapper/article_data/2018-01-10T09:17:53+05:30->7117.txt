RAJKOT: A four-month old lion cub died after being crushed under a passenger train near  , 10 km from Savarkundla town of   district on Tuesday night.

 Sources said that two cubs were crossing the railway tracks when the train was passing. One of the cubs managed to cross but another was crushed. Forest department team rushed to the spot and took custody of the cub's body. It was sent for post-mortem.
 
 Sources said that a lioness was also seen camping in a field adjacent to the tracks.
