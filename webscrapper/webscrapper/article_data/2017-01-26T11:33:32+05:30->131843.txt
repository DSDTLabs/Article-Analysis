JAIPUR: The Jaipur police on Wednesday issued directions to all   to ensure that   and   are given due respect in the city as per the directions of the  .

 The direction stated that doors of the cinema halls should be closed when the national anthem is being played. “The entry and exit doors to the theatre shall remain shut when the anthem is being played so that no person creates public nuisance inside the theatre,” the direction added. 
 
 The directions also said that national flag should be displayed on the screen when the anthem is being played. The Jaipur police also directed their officials to ensure that commercial use of the national flag in not done in the city.
 
 The police said that these directions were issued with regard to Supreme Court’s directions in Shyam Nayan Chouksey case and are important to be followed.
