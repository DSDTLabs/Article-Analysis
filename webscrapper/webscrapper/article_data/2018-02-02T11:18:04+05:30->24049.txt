NEW DELHI: PC Jeweller   on Friday dropped more than 60 per cent on the   exchanges today, extending fall to 4 days in a row.

 However, the stock recovered quickly from the early fall. At 11am,   share was trading at Rs 341.55 on the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) - 142.50 or 30.30 per cent down. On the National Stock Exchange (NSE), the share was trading at Rs 339.70, down 144.60 or 30 per cent. 
 
 PC Jeweller has 84 retail jewellery stores across the country. The company said in a statement that the number of retail stores is expected to touch 100 by end of this fiscal. Of the current 84 stores, about 10 are on franchisee model.
