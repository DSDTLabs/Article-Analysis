AHMEDABAD: City-based Centre for   (CEE) is hosting a three-day pan-India meeting of grassroots innovators on March 15. Over 350 participants from 100-odd NGOs will be showcasing their innovations at the CEE campus.

 “The central theme is main streaming grassroots innovations under which the participants would discuss and define strategy for replicating and scaling up innovations piloted and supported under the Small Grants India Programme (SGP),” said a CEE official. “The meeting will bring together partner NGOs, government representatives, corporate partners as well as research and development and certification agencies,” said the official.
 
 Kartikeya Sarabhai, director, CEE, said that many times they encounter good innovations in need of incubation and scaling up. “All the participants have practical knowledge of the issues they have tried to address through their intervention and innovations. It would be a learning platform for the participants and visitors to understand the process,” said Sarabhai, adding, “We have got on board renowned incubators to provide them guidance on various aspects such as registration for patent and seeking finance.”
 
 The events at the meeting will include Samvaad, a forum to share community-led innovative projects,   showcase and sales section for the participants, and Technology Fair displaying technology solutions for grassroots.
