DEHRADUN:   police on Saturday morning impounded four dumpers/trucks allegedly overloaded with illegal mining materials.
 
  Patel Nagar police station in charge   said, “The overloaded dumpers were impounded at   police post while transporting illegal mining materials. It was a regular exercise to check overloading of trucks and dumpers.”
  
  According to police, the dumpers were carrying materials more than the prescribed limit, and could not produce valid permission document to transport mining materials. Subsequently, the vehicles were impounded, along with the loaded materials.
