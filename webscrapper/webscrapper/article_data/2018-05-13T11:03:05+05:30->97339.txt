VARANASI: Taking action on the basis of a video footage on social media, the cantonment police on Saturday lodged an FIR against three known and five unidentified persons for stripping and beating a teenage boy. They were booked under sections 147, 323, 504 and 342 of IPC. 

 According to reports, a video footage went viral on social media on Friday showing some people beating a stripped boy, who was tied to an electric pole for allegedly stealing a mobile phone. The incident occurred in   locality in   area. 
 
 Cantt inspector   said that three persons including Abdul Maji Qureshi, Sachin Kumar Singh and Prakash and five unknown persons were booked in this connection. The investigation is on, and accused would be nabbed soon, he said.
