While we all know that anchor Anasuya danced for a special number composed after her name 'Suya Suya' in Sai Dharam tej starrer 'Winner', we also got to understand that the song is going to be one of the turning points in the movie, that is script-demanded. The song featuring the anchor in a never seen before avatar is entirely shot in a private night club, it is learnt. Ace musician, Anirudh Ravichander is releasing the song that is composed by S S Thaman. Gopichand Malineni helmed the project which has Rakul Preet in the female lead.The movie is set to hit the screens on February 24.
