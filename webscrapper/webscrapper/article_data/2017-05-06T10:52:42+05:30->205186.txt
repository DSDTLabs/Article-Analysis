ERODE:  , located in Sathyamangalam  , on Friday scared away a leopard that tried to kill a goat. 

 "The wild animal entered a cattle pound in the village and tried to maul a goat, but villagers who were alerted by the noise, raised an alarm. Hearing it, the leopard fled," a forest department official said.
