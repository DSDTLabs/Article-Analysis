Panaji: Lightning struck three houses in  , Aldona, on Tuesday evening, damaging several electrical appliances worth Rs 9 lakh. 

 The impact of the lightning was such that tubelights within the households burst, residents said. TV sets, refrigerators, washing machines and fans were also damaged. 
 
 Nicholas Sequeira suffered a loss of around Rs 5 lakh, while Jose Dias and Maria Dias claimed they incurred a loss of Rs 2 lakh each.
 
 Aldona MLA Glenn Ticlo Souza, later visited the area and assured the affected families financial assistance. tnn
