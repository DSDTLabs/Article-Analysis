HYDERABAD: The cyber crime police on Tuesday apprehended  , a resident of Chennai, for   people by creating a   msmerigister.com for registering micro, small and medium enterprises ( ).

 A top official of a plastics manufacturing firm claimed she accessed the website to register her company. After filling the form, she was asked to pay Rs 1,481. She paid up through her SBI credit card, but grew suspicious when she did not get any reply in 24 hours. “When she accessed the Udhyog Aadhaar website, she learnt that the MSME doesn’t charges any fee for issuing registration certificate,” said an officer. The victim lodged a complaint on Dec 30. The police learnt that the fake website was the first option to pop up during an internet search.
