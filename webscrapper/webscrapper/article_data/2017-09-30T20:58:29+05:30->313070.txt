PATIALA: The district police on Saturday registered a case against the son of an Assistant Sub-Inspector (ASI) on charges of raping a 14-year-old girl who was admitted to the Government Rajindra Hospital today after she was found to be four months pregnant. 
 
 The case has been registered under Section 376 of the IPC and Sections 4, 8 and 12 of the   act. 
  
 As per the complaint filed at the Tripuri police station, it has been alleged that  , son of ASI Jarnail Singh allured the minor girl and started taking her to different places over a period of time where he repeatedly raped her. 
  
 The matter came to light when the minor girl developed some medical complications and reported pain in her stomach. When her parents took her to doctor she was found pregnant. Following this, the girl's parents coaxed her to reveal name of the accused.
 
 The accused was yet to be arrested. 
  
 SP City Kesar Singh said the matter would now be put before a court which will take a final call in regard with termination of the girl's pregnancy.
  
 In yet another incident the Civil Lines police have also registered a case against a local resident, who has been accused of trying to rape his own daughter. 
  
 According to the police, the accused spiked a cold drink and give it to her daughter and tried to sexually assault her. However, some of the neighbours entered the house and caught him before he could harm the young girl. The police had already recorded the statement of the girl on the basis of which a case under Section 506 of the IPC and Sections 10, 12 of the Posco Act has been registered. No rest had yet been confirmed by the police.
