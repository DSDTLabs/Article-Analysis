LUDHIANA: The Jalandhar-based zonal office of the   (ED) raided RK Exchange and General Store, Ghumar Mandi, here on Thursday. The operation was carried out under instructions from ED joint director Girish Bali, after receiving specific inputs about sale of unauthorized foreign currency by the owners, and violation of the Foreign Exchange Managment Act (Fema). According to the department, assorted foreign currency worth Rs 28,50,000, and Indian currency worth Rs 10,45,000, was seized by the directorate in the action which continued until evening.

 During the search, certain documents and electronic devices too were seized by the team led by ED assistant director Sanjiv Prabhakar. As soon as the news of ED action spread in the city, several other shops dealing in foreign currency shut down their offices, fearing similar action against them.
