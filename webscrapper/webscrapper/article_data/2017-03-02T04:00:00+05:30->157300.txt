Surat: Surat Municipal Corporation (SMC) cattle catching party was attacked in Pandesara on Wednesday evening. One person was injured in stone pelting by the cattle owners. The cattle vehicle was also damaged after it hit the pole on the road.

 According to Praful Mehta, in-charge of the department in SMC, when a 15-member team went to catch stray cattle at Jay Ambe Nagar in Pandesara, a group of cattle owners surrounded them and started pelting stones. 
 
 “We had caught about 30 cattle but 15 of them were set free by the owners after heckling SMC staff. They fled with cattle and cattle van driver lost balance and rammed into a pole in the area,” said Mehta. 
 
 SMC has registered an FIR against the cattle owners.
