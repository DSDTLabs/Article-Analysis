STF inspector general of police Kunwar Vijay Partap Singh said the team had arrested Harcharan Singh, Daljit Singh, both residents of Batala and Gurpreet Singh of Amritsar who is a police constable.

 He said all the alleged accused had duped over a hundred persons from Punjab, Haryana and Delhi on the pretext of giving them jobs. The gang members duped people by taking a huge amount of money from those aspiring jobs in police and other government departments.
 
 “It has been found during our investigation that this gang is working in close association with notorious inter-state gangsters,” he said adding that further investigations were in progress.
