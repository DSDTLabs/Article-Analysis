VASCO: Vasco police station in-charge   on Friday said police would not hesitate to shoot those caught desecrating religious structures. 

 Raposo spoke to reporters after a meeting with his staff to check preparedness to prevent desecrations in Vasco. 
 
 Raposo also met with parish priests of churches located within the jurisdiction of the police station on Friday. Parishioners will form citizen patrol groups and one constable will be allotted to each group, he said, adding that some policemen have been even equipped with AK-47 rifles.
