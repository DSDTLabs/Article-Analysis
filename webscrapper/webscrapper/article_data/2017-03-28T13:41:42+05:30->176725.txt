LUDHIANA: The father of an 18-year-old girl, who was allegedly raped by same two youths who were allegedly involved in her gangrape along with two other youths in 2014, has urged the police commissioner to arrest the duo and provide security to the family.

 On Monday afternoon, father of the   survivor met police commissioner   and urged him to provide security to his daughter and family. Further he also sought arrest of both accused, who had been booked on Friday night under rape charges.
 
 Notably, the girl was allegedly raped by two youths on Wednesday near   palace. However, the father had alleged that the duo was given a clean chit during police investigation as cops took bribe for the same. Two more accused are facing trial in the same case.
