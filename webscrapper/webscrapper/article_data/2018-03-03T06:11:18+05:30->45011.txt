CHENNAI: A suspect who escaped from the   custody was caught by the   police on Friday. 

 The police said   was caught red-handed while breaking into a house on Thursday night. The residents who caught him handed him over to the police.
 
 The man, a past offender, was kept inside the police station. He requested policemen to allow him to relieve himself at 3am. When he did not return for long, police started looking for him. On the pretext of going to the bathroom,   scaled a wall and escaped. Though the police chased him he managed to give police a slip as it was dark. Later, a police team searched for him in the locality but in vain.
 
 Police who launched a hunt for him nabbed him near Washermenpet.
