KOHIMA: The ruling   (NPF) and   (NDPP) led by   and former chief minister   on Monday released their list of candidates. However, no candidate has filed nominations yet. 

 While NPF has announced the names of 58 candidates, the NDPP named 37 nominees for the February 27 assembly polls. NDPP, which has struck a pre-poll alliance with BJP, is contesting in 40 seats and BJP will fight in the remaining 20. NDPP is yet to name candidates for three seats - Dimapur-II, Mongoya and Mokokchung Town. Interestingly, not a single woman has been included in the NPF list. NDPP, on the other hand, gave ticket to just one woman candidate, Awan Konyak. She will contest from Aboi constituency.
