GURUGRAM: A   resident (25) has been arrested for allegedly raping his sister-in-law (12) during her visit to his home. 

 
 The man who is a chowmein stall vendor had also threatened her with consequences if she disclosed the same. However, the Class V student, who is a resident of Gurugram, called and confided in her mother and they lodged a complaint with police on Thursday. The girl and her brother were visiting their sister (21) and her husband in Pachgaon village near Manesar. They had reached the house on Saturday and were supposed to stay there till Thursday. On Wednesday night, the girl was sleeping in a room with the other three when she was allegedly raped by the brother-in-law.
