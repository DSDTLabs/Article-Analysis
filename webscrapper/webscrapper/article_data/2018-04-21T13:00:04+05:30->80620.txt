 Bharat Ane Nenu

 
   ,  ,  , Devaraj, R. Sarathkumar, Sithara
 
 
   
 
 
 20 Apr, 2018
 
 
  2 hrs 53 mins
 
 
  Bharat Ane Nenu is a 
  written and directed by Koratala Siva, featuring Mahesh Babu and Kiara Advani in the lead roles. Kiara Advani was roped in to play the role of the Chief Minister's girlfriend. Stunt duo Ram-Laxman have performed the stunt choreography for this film. Dance choreography was performed by Raju Sundaram, Dinesh Kumar and Raghu. The soundtrack of the film is composed by Devi Sri Prasad. Three songs "Bharat Ane Nenu", "Vachaadayyo Swami" & "I Don't Know" were released as singles. Farhan Akhtar has lent his voice for the song "I Don't Know", which is first time for a South Indian movie. All five songs are penned by Ramajogayya Sastry.
 
  To make good on a promise he made to his mother, a university graduate returns to India and becomes disillusioned by the government corruption he encounters.
 
 
 
 
 
 A political drama with a young and good looking CM at the helm, what's not to like? But you can't help but wonder if the director did the right thing by veering away from an intense political drama into 'song-dance-fights' routine, commercial potboiler.
