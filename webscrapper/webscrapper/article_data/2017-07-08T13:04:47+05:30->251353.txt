Pop star   has paid tribute to the youngest Manchester terror attack victim  , who would have turned nine on July 4. 

 The little girl from Lancashire, England lost her life in the horrific terror attack which followed the pop star's concert in Manchester on May 22 that killed 22 people including seven children.
 
  
  
 Remembering the adorable youngster, Ariana wrote on Twitter, "Saffie, we're (thinking) of you baby." 
 
  
  
 Ariana visited her injured fans in hospital after the explosion ripped through the foyer of the MEN Arena and hosted the   charity concert in the city to raise money for the victims.
