RAJKOT: A court in   of Kutch granted 14-day   of two men arrested in connection with the gang rape on 22-year-old woman from   in Naliya taluka of Kutch.

 The special investigation team (SIT) probing the case produced Shantilal Solanki (48), who runs Bharat Gas agency in Naliya town, and Bharat Chauhan (37), a resident of Naliya, before the court on Thursday and sought their 14-day police custody, which the court granted.
 
 With the arrest of Solanki and Chauhan, the total number of persons arrested in the case has reached five. Four other accused are still on the run.
 
 Earlier, police had arrested Vinod alias Baba Sheth Thakkar (68), his son Chetan Thakkar (35), both residents of Naliya town, and Ashwin Ravilal Thakkar (44), a resident of Maninagar in Nakhatrana town of Kutch. All three are in 14-day police custody.
 
 The woman was allegedly raped in 2015 upon her return from her in-law's house in Mumbai. She was in search of a job and talked to Babu Lohana, a stock broker. He introduced her to his friend Shantilal Solanki, who owned a gas agency in Naliya. Solanki later employed the woman.
 
 According to the woman's complaint, when she demanded advance salary ahead of Diwali, Solanki asked her to collect the money from his house in Naliya. When she went to his house, Solanki, Bharat Darji and Vinod Thakkar allegedly raped her. Then, Solanki and Thakkar took her near the coastal area and raped her again, she alleged. The accused raped her several times and at different places in the last one-and-a half-years, the survivor has alleged.
