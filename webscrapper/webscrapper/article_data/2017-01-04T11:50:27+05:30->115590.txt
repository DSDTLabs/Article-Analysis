PANAJI: Vithal Chavan, 54, resident of Pandarpur,  , on Tuesday was arrested at Panaji for illegal possession of narcotic substances worth Rs 13,000. 

 Police received a tip-off that a person staying in a hut at Campal was involved in the narcotics trade. A police team then laid a trap to arrest the suspect. 
 
 Police said when Chavan arrived near   at Campal to deliver narcotics to a prospective customer, he was arrested red-handed with a substance that appeared to be ganja. 
 
 Police have registered the case under various sections of the NDPS Act and police sub-inspector Pritesh Govekar is investigating the case.
