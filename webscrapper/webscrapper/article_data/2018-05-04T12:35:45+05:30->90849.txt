HYDERABAD: With several state government employees' unions threatening to go on strike seeking the acceptance of 18 demands including scrapping of the new pension scheme, chief minister   has constituted a committee of ministers lead by finance minister Eatala Rajender to discuss the employees' grievances. 

 The other members of the committee will include municipal minister   and energy minster  . The committee will hold discussions with representatives of the Telangana Gazetted Officers' Associations and Telangana   on Friday afternoon in the finance minister's chambers. It may be recalled that all government employees associations formed a joint action committee and gave a representation to the chief secretary on March 28 and gave a month to the state to resolve their problems.
