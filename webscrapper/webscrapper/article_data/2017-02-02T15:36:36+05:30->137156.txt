After 'Bichagadu' and 'Bethaludu', actor Vijay Antony, who has been on a career's high as an actor, is all set to enthral his fans as 'Yeman'. On the occasion of Maha Sivarathri,on February 24, Yeman is hitting the screens all over. Touted to be a political thriller, the movie depicts the actor in a new avatar. The makers of the film have announced that they would launch the Telugu audio on February 11. Having Mia George as the female lead, the movie is directed by Jeeva Shanand produced by Miryala Ravinder Reddy.
