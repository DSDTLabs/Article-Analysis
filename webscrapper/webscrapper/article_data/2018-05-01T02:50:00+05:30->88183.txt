Visakhapatnam: A former district education officer (DEO) of Srikakulam was convicted in ACB court on Monday.

 The accused Sripathi Dharma Raju, the then DEO of Srikakulam, was sentenced to three years of rigorous imprisonment for accumulating assets disproportionate to his known sources of income. A fine of Rs one lakh was also imposed. 
 
 The ACB officials said Dharma Raju was arrested for amassing illegal assets in December 2005. Subsequently, charge sheet was filed by ACB against the accused officer in 2006. tnn
