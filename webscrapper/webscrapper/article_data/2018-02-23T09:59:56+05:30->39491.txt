BHOPAL: The   ( ) on Thursday took cognizance of the stalking and molestation of two sisters by a group iof drunken bikers in MP Nagar last Friday midnight.

 Thankfully for the girls — and the city — a stranger courageously took on the gang and helped save them. None of the accused have been arrested though the younger sister had bravely snatched the key of one of the bike and police have its registration details.
 
 The commission has asked police to provide details of the case registered and if the accused have been taken into custody. A report has been summoned from Bhopal DIG in this regard. The commission has asked if names of those who were present on the spot as “mere spectators” can be published or put on record.
