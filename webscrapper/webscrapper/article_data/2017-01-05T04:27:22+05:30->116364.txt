GURUGRAM: Claiming to have taken action against illegal construction being carried out in the   near Manav Rachna University in  , the forest department said it demolished the boundary wall in the area on Tuesday. Three days ago an environmentalist, Jitender Bhadana, notified it about the alleged illegal activity. 

 TOI found out that a plot measuring over an acre has been robbed of its green cover and a boundary wall has also been constructed in the area protected under sections 4&5 of the Punjab and Haryana Land Protection Act (PLPA) 1990. No non-forestry activity is allowed in the area. M D Sinha, conservator of forests (Gurgaon circle), said, “We immediately sent a team to the site and part of the boundary wall was razed.”
