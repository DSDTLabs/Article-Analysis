 Pradeep’s main rival and Congress-RJD candidate Sarfaraz Alam had filed his nomination on February 16. Alam, a former JD (U) MLA from Jokihat, got the RJD ticket after he resigned from JD(U) on February 10. The seat had fallen vacant after the death of Alam’s father and senior RJD neta Mohd Taslimuddin.
 
 For Jehanabad assembly constituency, JD (U)’s Abhiram Sharma filed his nomination papers on Tuesday. His main rival is former MLA Mundrika Singh Yadav’s son Suday Yadav, an RJD candidate. The seat fell vacant after the death of Mundrika.
 
 NDA’s Bhabhua candidate Rinki Rani Pandey too filed her papers on Tuesday. The seat had fallen vacant after the death of Rinki’s husband and BJP MLA Anand Bhushan Pandey. Rinki has been pitched against Shambhu Singh Patel of Congress.
