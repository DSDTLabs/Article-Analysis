DEVIPATNAM ( , AP): Around 40 people are feared missing after a boat capsized due to strong winds in the   on Tuesday evening. 

 
 Twelve others, including two women and a child, swam to safety. The boat was carrying them from   in West Godavari district to Polavaram. 
 
 Twenty boats, expert swimmers and   teams have been pressed into service to trace the missing people. The rescue operation is on in the river in pitch-dark condition. Poor light is hampering the rescue work. Chief minister N   has directed the district authorities to take all necessary steps for the safety of the missing people.
 
 The mishap took place near Manturu, 18 km from the mandal headquarters of Devipatnam, around 6pm. The organiser of the boat has surrendered before the police. East Godavari district collector Karthikeya Misra has rushed to the spot.
 
 According to those who swam to safety, there were about 55 passengers, mostly tribals, on the boat. They complained that the operator did not heed their pleas to stop the boat even as strong winds were blowing.
