PANAJI: Following a successful Carnival parade, the   Shigmotsav float parade will be held on the new route from the Science Centre, Miramar, to the Harley Davidson showroom to ensure smooth traffic flow in the heart of the city.

 The government will spend Rs 1.88 crore on parades at 15 locations across the state between March 3 to March 17.
 
 
 
  
 As much as 60% of the money will be spent on infrastructure, while the rest will be used as prize money. 
 
 While the prize money from the government has remained the same, some committees have put in their own money to increase amount, said tourism minister   ‘Babu’ Azgaonkar. 
 
 The government has allocated Rs 6 lakh each as prize money for the Panaji, Margao, Vasco, Mapusa and Ponda float parades and Rs 4 lakh each for the floats in Quepem, Bicholim, Valpoi, Canacona, Curchorem, Cuncolim, Sanguem, Pernem, Sakhali, while for the Dharbandona parade, Rs 3.50 lakh has been allocated.
