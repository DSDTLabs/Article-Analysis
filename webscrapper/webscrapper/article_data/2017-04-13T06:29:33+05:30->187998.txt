 On Wednesday, AMC conducted surprise raids at meat shops across the city and ordered sealing of 10, claiming they were operating illegally. These meat shops are located in Sarkhej, Lambha, Indrapuri, Khokhra, SP Stadium, Chandlodia and Gomtipur. Civic authorities had also sealed seven meat shops in the east zone after a crackdown on Tuesday.
 
 “The decision has been taken after consultation with municipal commissioner Mukesh Kumar,” said Bhavin Solanki, in-charge medical officer – health, AMC. “The issue of illegally operating meat shops was raised in the recent health committee meeting, and based on recommendations, necessary action has been taken,” Solanki added.
 
 The recent report by Comptroller and Auditor General (CAG) also claims that around nine illegal slaughterhouses operate in Ahmedabad.
