AHMEDABAD: The district education officer (DEO) on Monday will hold a meeting of principals of schools across   and  . The admission process for class 11   will be declared during the meeting, which will be held in Maninagar around 3pm.
 Class 10 board exam results will also be announced on Monday. This year, admissions to class 11 science stream across the city will be conducted through a centralized system.
 
 The dates for distribution of forms and the beginning of the admission process will then be decided. Sources said that the admission process — i.e. the distribution of forms — is likely to begin by May 30, Tuesday, and conclude by the end of June. According to estimates, there are more than 200 schools in Ahmedabad City and Rural, that offer HSC science stream education.
