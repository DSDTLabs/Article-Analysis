GREATER NOIDA: A 24-year old man allegedly raped a one-year-old girl and murdered her after committing the crime in Deri Skenar village of   area in Greater Noida on Wednesday.

 The accused Vijay alias Bijju then dumped the body in a small pit near railway track and fled from the spot.
 
 The police have arrested the accused and charged him under sections of rape, murder and POCSO Act.
 
 Anand Dev Mishra, SHO Badalpur, said the accused lived in the neighbourhood and he frequently visited victim’s house. “He visited the victim’s house in the afternoon and found the girl playing outside the house. He took away the child to offer some goodies. The accused went to a deserted location and raped the child. He then killed the girl and threw the body in a small pit,” he said.
 
 A police team reached at the spot and arrested the accused from his house. The police officials said that the accused seems to be mentally unstable. “We will get a medical examination conducted to know his mental status,” said police.
 
 The body has been sent for a post-mortem examination and report is awaited. “The accused will be produced in court and sent to judicial custody on Thursday,” said police.
