 A rickshawpuller made a PCR call informing the cops about a woman, lying in a pool of blood. She was taken to a nearby hospital, where doctors declared her dead on arrival.
 
 Cops said that the woman’s face was crushed beyond recognition and a blood-stained stone was found lying beside the body. The victim’s clothes were also found to be torn, said a police officer. 
 
 Locals said they had seen the woman entering the park with three men late on Sunday. Cops said the possibility of a sexual assault has not been ruled out and teams have been formed to nab the accused.
