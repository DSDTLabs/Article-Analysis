IDUKKI: A migrant labourer woman was found stabbed to death and her body hidden in a bush at   on Monday. The deceased was identified as Sabitha Maji (30), wife of Kunathan Maji, from Devikund, Baleshwasr district, Odisha. 

  For the last one year the couple was working in Kalimali estate at Kuttikkanam. Kunthan Haji used to go for other jobs on holidays. When he came home after work on Sunday, he found his wife missing. Later, her nude body was recovered in a search by him and relatives.
 
  The body had 10 wounds and it was hidden in a bush more than 100 meters away.
