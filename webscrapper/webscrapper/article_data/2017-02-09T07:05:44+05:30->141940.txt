VADODARA: Fire broke out in a factory manufacturing thermocol in Por near the city on Wednesday morning.

 The fire broke out on the first floor of   and soon spread to other areas of the unit that was badly gutted. Short circuit is believed to be the reason for the blaze. Fire tenders from   as well as the Vadodara fire brigade were rushed to the unit.
 
 No workers were present as the fire happened before the factory could begin operations for the day. Firemen took around an hour to control the blaze. 
 
 The extent of damage is yet to be ascertained.
