 Alliance Air's test flight, ATR-42, had landed at Pasighat Airport on April 23. The aircraft reached Pasighat from Guwahati in 2 hours and 20 minutes.
 
  "A landmark step by Modi Govt towards enhancing air connectivity. After decades, Arunachal Pradesh, our beloved land of the rising sun, all set for 1st Guwahati-Pasighat flight on 21st May. It will give wings to dreams and boost tourism. A very heart-warming development indeed!" tweeted civil aviation minister Suresh Prabhu.
