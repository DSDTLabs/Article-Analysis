VIJAYAWADA:   chief minister   on Saturday shot off letters to the Union government regarding remunerative prices for farmers and increasing import of agriculture products.

 
 In his two page letter to Union minister of commerce Suresh Prabhu, Naidu brought to light the nation-wide cashew crisis. He asked the minister to plug the loopholes in the policy and put a ban on import of cashew.
 
 In his second letter to Union minister for agriculture Radha Mohan Singh, Naidu expressed concern over the country importing cereals and pulses. He said that such a trend will adversely impact not just Andhra Pradesh but also the rest of the country.
