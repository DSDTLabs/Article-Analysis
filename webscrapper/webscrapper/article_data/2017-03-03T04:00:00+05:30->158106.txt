Surat: A city girl secured ninth rank at nation-level in Cost and Management Accountant (CMA) exam, rankings of which was announced on Thursday. In all, three girls secured place among top 50 in the country. The exam was held in December by the Institute of Cost Accountants of India (ICAI).

 Priti Agarwal, secured ninth position nationally with 524 marks out of 800 in the exam. She is also first in Surat. Monica Agarwal stood 17th with 505 marks while Sima Pandav is 37th with 470 marks. Another girl student Prerna Agarwal scored 450 marks but missed a position among top 50 in the country by just one mark. tnn
