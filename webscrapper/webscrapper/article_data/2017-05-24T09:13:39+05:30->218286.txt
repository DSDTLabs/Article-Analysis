NASHIK: The stage is set for   residents to elect their representatives for the next five years in the local civic body. 

 Voting for the 84-member   will take place between 7.30am to 5.30pm on Wednesday   and  . 
 
 Over 600 electronic voting machines (EVMs) were despatched on Tuesday by the election authorities for the civic polls. While one EVM would be used for each of the 516 booths, additional 100 EVMs have been kept on standby in case there are technical snags during polling. 
 
 Election authorities said that if the number of candidates in a ward is more, there could be more than one ballot unit in each booth of that ward. Each voter from the 21 wards has to cast four votes as four corporators would be elected from one ward.
