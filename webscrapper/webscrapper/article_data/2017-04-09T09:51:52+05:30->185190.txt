NEW DELHI: As many as 221 candidates have withdrawn their nominations from the upcoming civic elections to be held on April 23.

 Saturday was the last day for the candidates to withdraw nominations. Surprisingly , the smallest of the three corporations -east corporation that has 64 seats compared to 104 each in north and south -has witnessed the maximum withdrawals at 84. South saw 79 withdrawals while the north corporation saw 58 pulling out from the race to become a councillor.
 
 Officials from the state election commission explained that many of these are covering candidates that the parties deploy in case something goes wrong with the nomination of the main candidates. Across all three corporations, the highest number of withdrawals -127 --have been from Independent candidates, followed by 44 candidates from AAP, 19 from BJP and 17 from Congress.
