CHENNAI: Two school students who left home allegedly to teach their fathers a lesson for assaulting their family members everyday, were traced on Thursday. 

 After a case was registered, a police team found the girls,   and  , sitting at an abandoned building near   railway station at 2am.
 
  The rescued girls are studying of Class IX and Class X at a government high school in Sankar Nagar. They were later allowed to go home with their parents at 4.30am on Thursday.
