Coimbatore: More than 2,300 members of all political parties except the AIADMK and the BJP were arrested on Monday for protesting against the Coimbatore Corporation for planning to increase taxes from the next financial year.

 The protest was staged at all the 100 wards of the Coimbatore Corporation. The municipal body had a few days ago decided to increase various taxes levied by it. The increase in the taxes is expected to be announced in the financial year that will be presented around the end of the current financial year.
 
 The political parties demanded that the Coimbatore Corporation to withdraw the decision to increase the taxes.
