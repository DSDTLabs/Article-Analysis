Tirunelveli: A total  ,379 people including 687 women were arrested for resorting to road blockades at 57 places in Tirunelveli, Tuticorin and Kanyakumari districts on Monday. The road rokos were part of a state-wide prison filling protest announced by the DMK to urge the state government to fully rollback the recent bus fare hike.

 In Tirunelveli city, hundreds of cadre blocked the Sulochana Mudaliar Bridge in front of the MGR statue near the collectorate. The protest, led by DMK MLA A L S Lakshmanan brought traffic to a standstill between Tirunelveli and Palayamkottai for nearly 30 minutes. Normalcy returned after police cleared and detained them in a marriage hall.
 
 Palayamkottai MLA T P M Moideen Khan led the blockade at the Palayamkottai bus stand and was arrested. In rural Tirunelveli, police arrested 2,569 cadres including 198 women for blocking roads at 25 places. Alangulam MLA Poongothai Aladi Aruna was arrested for blocking the road at Alwarkurichi. Former Assembly Speaker R Avudaiappan was arrested for leading a protest at Ambasamudram.
 
 In Kanyakumari district, 1,332 people including 165 women were arrested. The arrested included Nagercoil MLA N Suresh Rajan, Killiyoor MLA S Rajesh Kumar, Padmanabhapuram MLA T Mano Thangaraj, Kanyakumari MLA S Austin and Colachel MLA J G Prince.
