MADURAI: Adhering to a   order, the management of Sri   Sundareswarar temple has decided to ban cell phones inside the temple from March 3. The management has also arranged a limited facility outside the temple to safe-keep 1,000 cell phones, at a nominal charge of Rs 10 per phone. 

 “Considering the safety of devotees, welfare of the temple and suggestions by the central security force, bringing cell phone inside the temple will be completely banned from March 3. People coming to the temple are requested not to bring their mobile phones as far as possible,” said N  , temple joint commissioner. 
 
 He further said that the management has arranged facility to safe-keep 1,000 cell phones at the temple’s north and west entrances, for which Rs 10 will be collected per phone. TNN
