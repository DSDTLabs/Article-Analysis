NEW DELHI:   ( ) has released the official notification regarding the recruitment to the post of Sub-Inspector of Excise department including Lady Sub-Inspector of Excise. 

 The registration process of WBPRB Excise SI recruitment will start from February 2, 2018. 
 
 According to the official notification available on the website, the candidates who are willing to apply for WBPRG Excise SI posts, are advised to visit the official websites of West Bengal Police - www.policewb.gov.in and Excise Directorate www.excise.wb.gov.in to check the details.
 
 
 
 
 Age: The candidate must not be less than 20-years-old and must not be more than 27-years-old as on January 1, 2018. The upper age limit shall be relaxable for candidates belonging to the Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, and Other Backward Classes category as per the extant rules of the State.
 
 Educational Qualifications: Bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognised university or its equivalent
 
 Language: The candidate must be able to speak, read and write in Bengali/Nepali. 
 
 
 
 
 1) Open the official websites of West Bengal Police (www.policewb.gov.in) and Excise Directorate (www.excise.wb.gov.in)
 
 2) Cross check the eligibility criteria in detail, read about the method of selection, the scheme of examination, procedure for submission of application form and other terms & conditions.
 
 3) Submit the online/offline application form between from February 2, 2018, to March 4, 2018 (5PM).
