CHANDIGARH: Telemedicine facility would be launched in the state on pilot basis shortly to strengthen primary healthcare services,   Health Minister   said here today. 

 He said the scheme aims at providing telemedicine facility to the people at three levels.
 
 In the first level, health and wellness centres would be connected with Primary Health Centres (PHCs) and   (CHCs) through e-Sanjeevani software, a web-based comprehensive telemedicine solution. PTI
