Nandamuri Balakrishna's prestigious 100th outing, a historic marvel directed by Krish- 'Gautamiputra Satakarni' is all set for a television premier worldwide.

 The movie, which released for Sankranthi this year has went on to win critical acclaim for the efforts of the director and the crew for being able to wrap shooting in a crisp time of 79 days. The story of a Satavahana prince, Gautamiputra Satakarni and his prowess was depicted in a brilliant way with rich visuals and war sequences that are astounding.
 
 Shriya Saran and Hema Malini looked impressive in the movie which has a guest appearance of Shiv Raj Kumar of Kannada industry.
 
 Catch the entire movie only on Star Maa on March 29 at 6 pm and on Maa Movies at 9 pm.
