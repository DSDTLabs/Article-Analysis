Udhagamandalam: As many as 254 recruits successfully completed their 46-week training at the Madras Regimental Centre (MRC) in Wellington, here on Thursday.

 The centre also witnessed the attestation parade wherein all the 254 recruits swore their oath.
 
 Lt Gen Amrik Singh, SM, Commandant, Defence Service Staff College, Wellington, reviewed the parade.
 
 Speaking on the occasion, Amrik Singh said, “The continuous improvement in training methodology and the mechanism implemented with innovative initiatives have revolutionised the way the centre trains its recruits and achieve the highest standards desired.”
 
 Officers at Madras Regimental Centre and parents of the recruits, besides civilian and guests, attended the attestation parade.
 
 The 254 recruits will soon join various battalions of the regiment, an official told TOI.
