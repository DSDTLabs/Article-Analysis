NEW DELHI:   ( ) has activated the link to download the Review Officer, Assistant Review Officer admit card. 

 
 Candidates can now download their Admit Cards from the UPPSC official website: uppsc.up.nic.in
 
 
 
 
 As per the official notification the examination for UPPSC RO/ARO   is going to be held on April 8, 2018. There are a total of 465 vacancies for the RO/ARO position all over Uttar Pradesh.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Candidates who have applied for the examination can check and download their admit card from the UPPSC website - http://uppsc.up.nic.in/. 
 
 Candidates are advised to download their UPPSC RO/ARO (Samiksha Adhikari) 2018 Admit Card from the official website - http://uppsc.up.nic.in/AdmitCard.aspx.
 
 1) Open the official website of UPPSC - http://uppsc.up.nic.in/
 
 2) On the homepage of the website, there will be a link "UPPSC RO/ARO (Samiksha Adhikari) 2018 Admit Card/Hall Ticket". Click on the link
 
 3) A new page will appear, enter your UPPSC RO/ARO 2018 application number, date of birth, security pin and click on login. 
 
 4) Your UPPSC RO/ARO 2018 admit card will be displayed on the screen. Carefully check all your details such as name, paper, date of birth, gender, test centre name, city, state, code of eligibility and category on your UPPSC RO/ARO 2018 admit card. 
 
 5) You can download it and take a printout for future reference.
