Madurai: Southern Railway has announced special fare special trains in order to clear the extra rush of traffic during summer. According to a statement from railways, train no. 06046 Thiruvananthapuram – Karaikal special fare special will leave Thiruvananthapuram at 3.25pm on Wednesdays from April 4 to June 27 and reach Karaikal at 3.45am the next day. 

 Train no. 06045 Karaikal – Thiruvananthapuram special fare special will leave Karaikal at 10.45pm on Thursdays from April 5 to June 28 and reach Thiruvananthapuram at 12.15pm the next day. These trains will have 2 AC 2-tier, 3 AC 3-tier, 11 sleeper class, 2 general second class and two luggage-cum-brake van coaches.
