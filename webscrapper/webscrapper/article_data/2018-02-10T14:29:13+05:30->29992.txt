A special screening for the show '21 Sarfrosh: Saragrahi 1897', starring the handsome   and the talented actor   in the lead roles, was hosted on Friday night. 

  
  
 
 A horde of TV stars attended the event to show their support to the cast and crew of the show. Actress Barkha Bhisth was seen mingling with the guests, which included Rajshri Thakur, Manav Gohil, Romit Raj, Ankur Nayyar, Shravan Reddy, Rahul Kumar, Pankaj Berry, Mohit Raina, Mukel Dev,   Singh and Abhimanyu Singh who has produced the show. 
 
  
  
 However, the cynosure of all eyes was clearly the very beautiful Mouni Roy, who is rumoured to be dating the handsome hunk Mohit.
 
  
  
 
 The show is a work of fiction inspired by the real story of 21 brave soldiers of 36th Sikh regiment of the British Indian Army who defended an army outpost at Saragarhi in the North West Frontier Province against an onslaught by over 10,000 Pashtun and Orakzai tribals in September 1897.
