Bollywood's first true female superstar, Sridevi passed away at the age of 54 on Sunday night, leaving her fans across the world heartbroken and shattered. Born in 1963, the beautiful actress made her Bollywood debut in 1979 and it only took her a few years to become a fashion icon, thanks to her will to experiment and love for everything classy. Bollywood's most timeless diva, the actress was someone who just got better with age. From making saris fashionable to wearing enviable ensembles by world class designers, Sridevi never shied away from making style statements. Though her recent appearances were far more glamorous than the earlier ones, the beauty will always remain our 'Chandni'.
