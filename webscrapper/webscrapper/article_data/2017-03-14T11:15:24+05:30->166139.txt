

 Alamara talks about a young couple who experience many comic events after their marriage. The film has tried to explore the custom of giving cupboard to the bride along with the dowry. 
 
 Apart from Sunny and Aditi, Alamara has Renji Panicker, Saiju Kurup, Aju Vargheese, Indrans and Sudhi Koppa doing pivotal roles.
