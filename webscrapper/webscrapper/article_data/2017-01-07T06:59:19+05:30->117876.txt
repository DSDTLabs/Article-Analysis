MUMBAI: A session court this week acquitted two persons, one of them former homeguard personnel, of the charges of gangraping and murdering a woman hawker at her slum in  .

 On August 28, 2013, the woman's body was found inside the house. The prosecution had alleged that the accused, who were known to the victim, had gone to her house in a drunken state and raped her.The prosecution had said that when the victim threatened to inform the police, the accused killed her by hitting her head with an iron rod.
 
 Defence advocate   said that the evidence did not support the prosecution's claims and both the accused were innocent.
