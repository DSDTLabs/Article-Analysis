NAGPUR: The crime branch and excise department officials carried raids in a bar and 4   parlours bars on Saturday night. The officials raided SK bar, which allegedly belongs to a   general secretary according to the police, for violation of Supreme Court’s norms of liquor ban in hotels and bars within 500 meters from the highway.

 The hookah parlours were allegedly providing tobacco and other banned intoxications to its patron which included youngsters. A few have been rounded up in the action. 
 
 DCP (crime)   told TOI, “SK bar staffers were openly selling liquor. Excise officials have seized the liquor bottles.”
