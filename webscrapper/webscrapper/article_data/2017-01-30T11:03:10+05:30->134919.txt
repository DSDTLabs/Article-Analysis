DEHRADUN: A two-day long ‘Know Your   Mela’ at   ended on Sunday after offering a glimpse into the world of the armed forces. Many youngsters and school kids made a beeline to see military equipment on display and to understand the process of joining the defence forces.
 
  The fair was inaugurated by Major General SC Mohanty, General Officer Commanding, Golden Key Division on January 28. It aims to motivate the youth to join the Indian Army. The festival also had a stall for veterans and their dependants.
  
  “I liked the pipe band display and the cultural programme. My younger cousins were mesmerised by the military weapons on display and were curious to find out how to join the Army,” said Ankita Sharma, a visitor.
