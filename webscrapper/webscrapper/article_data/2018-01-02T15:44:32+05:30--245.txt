Actor  , the son of late   Chief Minister  , said he is extremely proud of the Mumbai police.

  
  
 Riteish on Tuesday shared a photograph of a police constable who saved several people from the   Fire. "Constable Sudarshan Shinde - saved 8 people. Kamla Mills Fire - Extremely proud of our Mumbai police. Sudarshan Shinde tumhe mera salaam (I salate you)," he captioned the image. 
 
  
  
  A blaze in the Kamla Mills compound happened on December 29, 2017, killed 14 and injured another 55.
