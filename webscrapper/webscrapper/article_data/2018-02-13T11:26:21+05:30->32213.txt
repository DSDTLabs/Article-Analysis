GUWAHATI:   chief minister Sarbananda   on Monday encouraged women working in the public sector to remain undaunted and showcase their ability and commitment towards their profession. 

 "The role of women is undergoing a dramatic change worldwide. Women today share the podium with men in almost all fields. Working women are no longer a rarity and are now accepted as an integral part of the work force," Sonowal said while taking part in the 28th national convention of forum of women in public sector.
