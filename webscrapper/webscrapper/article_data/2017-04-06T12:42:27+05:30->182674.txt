 The molestation happened between December 2016 and February 2017 till the suspect was sacked from the company following the woman’s complaint. The woman approached the Hinjewadi police when the suspect started making threatening calls and sending texts in obscene language to the complainant and her husband.
 
 The suspect has been arrested under Sections 354 (A) (sexual harassment), 507 (criminal intimidation) and 507 (criminal intimidation by an anonymous communication) of the Indian Penal Code, an officer from the Hinjewadi police station said.
 
 The officer told TOI that the 38-year-old complainant is from outside Maharashtra. She is married and shifted to Pune about eight months ago. She works at a senior post in the IT company at Hinjewadi. 
 
 He said the suspect, after getting acquainted with the complainant, began teasing her whenever they used to meet in the office. “The complainant initially ignored his advances. He used to force the woman to talk with him,” the officer said.
 
 He said that last December, the suspect allegedly molested the complainant inside the office. “The woman immediately informed the company management about the harassment by the suspect,” he said.
 
 The officer said that after the probe, the company sacked the suspect in February 2017. Angry after losing his job, the suspect started making threatening calls to the woman and also started sending obscene SMSs. “The suspect also made threatening calls to the woman’s husband,” the officer said.
 
 The frustrated woman again approached the company management and told them about the harassment by the suspect. “The company management advised her to approach police and lodge a complaint against the suspect. We verified the woman’s complaint and arrested the suspect from his house on Tuesday,” the officer said.
