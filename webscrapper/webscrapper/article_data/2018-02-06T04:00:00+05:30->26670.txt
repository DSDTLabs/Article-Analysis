Rajkot: Two youths were killed when a speeding train rammed them near Jetpur in Rajkot district on Monday afternoon. 

 “We are yet to ascertain identities of the deceased who appear to be in their mid-20s. According to eye witnesses, the accident occurred when the victims were seated on a railway bridge over Bhadar river near Jetpur. The Veraval-Jabalpur Express suddenly arrived on the bridge and crushed both the youths,” said Jetpur police station head constable Hitesh Savaliya. 
 
 Navagadh station master M C Gupta informed the police. 
 
 However, Savaliya added, “We are yet to know whether it was an accident or the youths had committed suicide.”
