 The accused have been identified as  , his wife  , father and brother of Gurpreet, Surjit Singh and Aman. 
 
 On Monday,  , 65, owner of a building construction material store and his wife Avinash Kaur, 62, attempted suicide by consuming a poisonous substance and hanging by the ceiling at their house in Sector 32,   on Monday. Neighbours rescued the couple and rushed them to a private hospital on Brown road, where their condition is stated to be critical. Police also recovered two suicide notes the couple had posted on the window of the house, in which they accused their younger son and his wife of harassing them and forcing them to take the extreme step. 
 
 Kulwinder Singh, elder son of Gurmeet Singh said both sons, who are married, initially lived in a joint family with their parents in  , but he shifted to MIG flats on Chandigarh Road following domestic issues. 
 
 He said his younger brother and wife used to harass his parents, due to which they shifted to Sector 32, Chandigarh road. He said his sister-in-law did not allow them to take furniture and other articles while they were shifting. Therefore, his parents approached the police and shifted from there. Still, he said, his brother and sister-in-law kept harassing the parents.
 
 "My parents used to go for morning walk every day and would spend time with neighbours. On Monday, when they did not come out of the house, the neighbours came to my house as I also live nearby. We went there and knocked. When there was no response, we broke the door open and found them hanging,” he said.
 
 SHO, Division no 7 police station, inspector Parveen Randev said an FIR has been lodged on the compliant of Kulwinder Singh’s elder son.
