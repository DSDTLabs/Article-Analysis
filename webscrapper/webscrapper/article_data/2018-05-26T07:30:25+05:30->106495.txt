MARGAO: Municipality workers throughout the state will stage a token one-day strike on June 29 in protest against the non-fulfilment of their pending demands.

 They are opposing the outsourcing of tasks performed by municipality workers. Their other demands includ regularisation of daily wage workers, appointment of dependents in the decade-long pending harness cases, lifting of the ban on filling vacant posts, revival of lapsed posts by the department promotional committee ( ) and promotional benefits such as the masters assured career progression ( ), grade pay arrears and child care leave.
