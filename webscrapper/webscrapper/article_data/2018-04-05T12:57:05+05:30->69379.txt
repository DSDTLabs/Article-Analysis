LUDHIANA: A 30-year-old man was arrested for trespassing into a women’s hostel and allegedly harassing a student and warden on Wednesday. According to police, the accused worked in a workshop. In the wee hours of Wednesday, he climbed a tree to enter the college premises, went upstairs, and harassed a student.

 On raising an alarm, the other women — including the warden — rushed to the spot, but the accused misbehaved with the warden too, after which the police was sounded. In her complaint to the police, the college principal stated that the accused had barged into the college hostel and misbehaved with the staff.
 
 Investigating officer   said the accused worked in a workshop adjoining the college. “He trespassed into the campus, went upstairs to the girls hostel, and sexually harassed a student who raised an alarm. When the warden rushed there, he misbehaved with her too,” he said.
 
 The   added that the police arrested the accused who would be presented before the court. An FIR was lodged by the police under sections 354 (assault or criminal force to woman with intent to outrage her modesty), 457 (lurking house-trespass or house-breaking by night in order to commit offence punishable with imprisonment) of the IPC.
