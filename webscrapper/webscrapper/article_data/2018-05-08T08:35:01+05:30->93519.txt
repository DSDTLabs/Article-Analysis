LUCKNOW: A fire broke out in the thickets in front of the Butler   on Monday afternoon. Two fire tenders were sent to the spot which doused the flames. No loss of life or property was reported.

 
 According to   residents, the fire broke out around 2.30pm. Soon it engulfed the Butler Palace court area. “I was washing my car when I saw the blaze at the boundary of the Palace. I tried to douse the fire but was unsuccessful. Other residents too tried to douse the flames, but in vain,” said  , a resident.
 
 Chief fire officer   said the wood kept in court area was dry. Probably someone threw a burning cigarette in the thickets which led to the fire.
