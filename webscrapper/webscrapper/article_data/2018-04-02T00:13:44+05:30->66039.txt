Pune: A zilla parishad teacher from Pulkoti in Satara district has been invited to attend a global conference organised by the International Association of Teachers of English as a Foreign Language (IATEFL), in the UK from April 10 to 13.

 Jadhav said he is the only teacher from India invited for the three-day event scheduled at Brighton. He teaches English and is known for his innovative methods and use of IT in teaching. 
 
 “I will be able to showcase my experiments at the international platform where teachers and experts from more than 100 countries are likely to participate,” Jadhav said.
 
 “My presentation titled ‘Connecting to Global World’ at the UK conference will comprise of methods and techniques used in rural Maharashtra for teaching children,” Jadhav added.
