KANPUR: Additional district and sessions judge on Wednesday framed charges against  , husband of the judicial officer   in the judge murder case. The accused has been charged under sections 302, 201 and 498A of IPC. The court rejected the defence counsel's plea to charge the accused under Anti Dowry Act. 

 Judicial officer Pratibha Gautam, who was posted at   district court, was found dead at her government flat in   on October 9, 2016. The police arrested her husband for committing murder of his wife. 
 
 Initially, the police had registered the case for murder and disappearing the evidence, but later reversed the charges after hearing the arguments of both the parties and framed murder charges against the accused.
