PIMPRI CHINCHWAD: Miscreants damaged many vehicles in   and Tapkirnagar on Tuesday night.

 The vehicles included around ten two-wheelers, a car and two autorickshaws. The miscreants used wooden sticks to damage the vehicles. The Wakad police have detained three people in the case.
 
 Police said the miscreants also tore up flex boards of some aspirants of the 2017 municipal elections around 3am. 
 
 Recently, there have been several incidents of damage to cars, two-wheelers and even PMPML buses, particularly during the night.There have also been incidents of damage to private vehicles in   and Bhosari .
