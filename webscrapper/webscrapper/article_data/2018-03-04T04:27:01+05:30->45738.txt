MUMBAI: More than 10,000 traffic violations were recorded by the police on Holi, including 743 cases of drunk driving. Officials said instances of drunk driving peak on Rangpanchami. 

 Separate nakabandis were put up by the traffic police and the city police. Traffic cops booked 375 for drunk driving, 1,342 for riding without helmets, 186 for riding triple seat, 19 for rash driving and 2,403 for speeding. 
 
 The city police booked 368 motorists for drunk driving, 3,075 for helmetless riding, 348 motorists for illegal parking and six for rash driving. tnn
