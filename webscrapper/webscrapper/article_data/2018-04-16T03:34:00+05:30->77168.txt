Ponda: A truck headed towards Kerala with 21 tonnes of TMT steel rods was found abandoned at Ramnagar, near Belgavi on Sunday. The drivers of the truck and around 10 tonnes of rods were missing. The Karnataka police are on the lookout for the drivers. The truck was loaded with rods worth Rs10 lakh at a steel factory in Marcaim Industrial Estate and was proceeding to Kerala on April 7. It was scheduled to reach Kerala on April 10. When the consignment did not arrive at its destination, a complaint was filed against the truck owner Punit Bhatia, said Ponda PI Harish Madkaikar.  When the Ponda police contacted its counterparts over wireless messages, the Belgavi police informed them about the truck abandoned in its jurisdiction. tnn
