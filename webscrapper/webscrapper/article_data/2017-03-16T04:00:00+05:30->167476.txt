CHENNAI: India Cements has approved the granting of 18.35 lakh options under ESOPs (employees stock options) to certain selection grade employees of the company. The scheme is in accordance with SEBI (Share based employee benefits) Regulations, 2014 and will come into effect from April 1, 2017, the company said.

 The options granted under the scheme will vest with the employees on April 1, 2018. The vested options should be exercised within one year from the date of vesting. On exercise of each option, one equity share of Rs. 10 each fully paid-up will be allotted at Rs. 50 per share including a premium of Rs. 40 per share, the company said.
