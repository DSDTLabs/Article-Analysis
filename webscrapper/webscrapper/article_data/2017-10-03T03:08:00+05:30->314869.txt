Panaji: Well-known Konkani writer, Yusuf Sheikh, passed away on September 30. His funeral was held on October 1.  Born on August 1, 1948, Sheikh served as the former station director of Doordarshan and as a programme executive at Akashvani, Mangaluru.  He is famed for his contribution to Konkani literature, mainly for his poems. His first collection of poems ‘Gantthi’ was published in 1982. His poems have featured in anthologies published by the National Sahitya Akademy. He also wrote ‘Divya Quran’, a translation of the 30th chapter of the Holy Quran in Konkani.
