Margao: Town and country planning minister Vijai Sardesai on Saturday inaugurated construction works of two drains in   constituency. One nullah will be located opposite Osia complex at Old Market, Margao, with the other in Borda’s Marlem ward, near the local balwadi. 

 “The nullah works along the national highway in Margao municipality’s ward 1 is being taken up at a cost of Rs 1.64 crore. Since this area is prone to flooding, this nullah will ensure the water is released into the nearby River Sal,” Sardesai said. The second drain will be built from Nagesh Garden till the Borda road at Marlem ward, at a cost of Rs 1.14 crore. tnn
