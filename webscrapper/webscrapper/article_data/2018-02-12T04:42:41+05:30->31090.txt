MUMBAI: Three persons from   film studio have been booked for causing death by negligence following a fire on the studio premises last month, a senior police official said on Sunday. 

 Based on a report submitted by the fire brigade, a first information report was registered against two co-owners and a manager of the studio at the Parksite police station on Saturday, he said. 
 
 The accused have been booked under section 304A (causing death by negligence) and other relevant sections of the Indian Penal Code, the official said, adding that no arrest had been made so far. 
 
 A 20-year-old old television production unit employee, Gopi Verma, had died in the fire that broke out at the studio in suburban Kanjurmarg around 8pm on January 6. 
 
 His body was found in the burnt debris a day after the fire. agencies
