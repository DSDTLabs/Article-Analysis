SURAT: A 28-year-old man, Mahommad Azhar Taufiq Shah, of Rander drowned in   river near weir-cum-causeway on Monday evening while trying to take a selfie. He had jumped into the river to recover his cellphone that had slipped from his hand. 

 Officials of fire brigade said that Shah had come to causeway on Singanpore side and was trying take a selfie with water in the background on Monday evening. His phone fell in the water so he removed his shirt and jumped into the water to recover it. However, he got caught in the under current and drowned.
 
 Fire official SA  , who led the rescue team said, “We reached after he fell in the river. The body was recovered after two hours of search.”
