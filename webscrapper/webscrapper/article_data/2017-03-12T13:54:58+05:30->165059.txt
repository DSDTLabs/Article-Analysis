Tollywood actress Anjali’s affair with Tamil actor Jai has been the talk of the town. While the ace heroine was in Hyderabad to promote her latest film ‘Chitrangada’, she spoke to the press about various issues regarding her career and personal life.

 When the actress was asked about her marriage plans with Tamil actor Jai she said that she has about a dozen films in her kitty and that she has no time for marriage. She further clarified that all the rumours about her marriage were baseless and false.
 
 Anjali’s ‘Chitrangada’ got mixed reviews but she is being applauded for her noteworthy acting skills in the movie. ‘Chitrangada’ is a horror film directed by Ashok.
