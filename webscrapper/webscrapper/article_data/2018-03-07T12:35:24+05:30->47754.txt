SALEM: An interaction session between representatives of micro, small and medium enterprises (MSMEs) from the city and senior officials of the ministry of defence and Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO), technical representatives from Hindustan Aeronautics Limited, Bharat Electronics Limited, Bharat Earth Movers Limited and   will be held in the city on March 8. 

 Speaking to TOI, president of the   District Small Scale and Tiny Industries Association (SADISSTIA) K   said the session to promote the defence corridor in Salem will create opportunities for the MSMEs to manufacture various components and machineries for the defence sector. 
 
 On January 18, chief minister   made a request to the central government and the defence ministry to set up a production facility of Hindustan Aeronautic Limited (HAL) in Salem. “The request is under the consideration of ministry of defence as they are in a process to set up a helicopter manufacturing plant in Salem,” Mariappan said.
 
 He said a team of DRDO Officials, including M Yagaiah, CCE (Works), Lt Col. (Retd.,) S K Dubey, Wing Cdr R S Sangwan and K K J Ravichandran had visited Salem and inspected three areas with an extent of 1,000 acres each on February 6, 2018. They also had discussions with the revenue department officials.
 
 The defence ministry had already conducted such interaction meeting to promote a defence hub at Hosur on February 26. Subsequently, a defence indigenization expo was conducted in Coimbatore on March 5. Collector Rohini R Bhajibhakare will inaugurate the interaction meeting in Salem.
