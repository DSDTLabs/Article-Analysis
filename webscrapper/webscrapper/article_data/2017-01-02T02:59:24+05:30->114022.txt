NOIDA: The police on Sunday marked the new year by organising an event to sensitise autorickshaw and e-rickshaw drivers about the need to obey traffic rules in Noida and Greater Noida. All the station house officers (SHOs) and other officers held a meeting with drivers in their respective areas and exchanged sweets and greetings on the occasion. 

 Dinesh Yadav, SP (city) also reached the   station at 4pm and interacted with drivers.
 
 Anil Pratap Singh, SHO, Sector 20 police station, said a meeting was also held at Noida City Centre. City magistrate   also joined the meeting at City Centre.
 
 Similar meetings were also held at different places in Greater Noida. Anuj Kumar, SHO, Surajpur police station, said the police officers had a healthy talk with autorickshaw drivers.
 
 Suresh Kumar, an auto driver, said they had a good talk with the cops and were also offered tea and snacks. "Such meetings should be held frequently to understand issues. It was a beautiful moment spent with the police," he said.
