Madurai: Major engineering works have been taken up for modification and improvement of Arakkonam yard. To facilitate these engineering works, changes will be made in the pattern of train services on May 4 and 5, according to a press release from the Southern Railway. Train no. 20601 Chennai Central – Madurai Express, scheduled to leave Chennai Central on May 4 has been fully cancelled. Train no. 20602 Madurai – Chennai Central Express, scheduled to leave Madurai on May 6 has been fully cancelled. tnn
