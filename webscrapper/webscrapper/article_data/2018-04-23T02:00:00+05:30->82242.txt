PATNA: Two children drowned in the Ganga near Gaighat pontoon bridge under the Alamganj police station area in Patna City on Sunday. 

 Alamganj SHO Om Prakash said the first incident took place in the morning when one Rakesh Kumar (12) of Jaganpura under the Ramkrishna Nagar police station area reached Gaighat with his family members to take a bath. He said the child failed to gauge the depth of the river water and drowned. 
 
 The SHO said the second incident took place on Sunday evening when one Shivam Kumar reached the riverbank with his family members and went deep into the water while taking bath. He also drowned in the river. 
 
 The SHO said both the bodies were recovered by the divers within a couple of hours after the accidents. TNN
