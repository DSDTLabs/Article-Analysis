AHMEDABAD: A convocation ceremony for   cadets, a first of its kind in the state, who have successfully completed three years of rigorous training and have qualified for the ‘C’ certificate, was held at   in Gandhinagar on Friday. A total of 775 cadets, who have earned the ‘C’ certificate were felicitated by additional director general NCC (Gujarat, Dadra Nagar Haveli and Diu), Major General  .

 Outstanding associate NCC officers and NCC instructors were also given prizes on the occasion. The award winning cadets in various categories were selected on the basis of their performance during their training. The convocation was attended by vice-chancellors of universities to whom the cadets belonged, NCC officers, and the proud parents of the cadets.
