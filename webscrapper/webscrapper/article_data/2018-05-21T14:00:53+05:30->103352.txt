SRINAGAR: Restrictions continued in the interior parts of Srinagar for the second day on Sunday as a precautionary measure to maintain law and order. Restrictions under Section 144 CrPc were in force in five police station areas of the city, a police official said. 

 He said restrictions were imposed in the old city police station areas of  , Khanyar, Rainawari, Safakadal and Maharajgunj. The curbs were imposed as a precautionary measure to maintain law and order in view of "Hafta-e-Shahdat" or the martyrs' week programme of the moderate Hurriyat Conference led by  . It was for the second consecutive day that parts of the city were under restrictions.
