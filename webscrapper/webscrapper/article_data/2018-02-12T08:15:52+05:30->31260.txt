BHOPAL: An 11-year-old girl was raped by a neighbour in   village of the city. The incident was reported to Sukhi Sewania police who later arrested the minor accussed on Saturday night.

 Police said that the incident took place when the survivor, a student of class IV, was asked by her sister to get newspaper from the accused’s house.
 
 The accused, who lives nearby, was alone at home when the girl went there. He then called her in and raped her after locking her inside. The incident came to light when the survivor’s sister went searching for her. The accused’s house was locked from the front side; and seeing the survivor’s sister there, he escaped from a backdoor.
 
 Police then registered a case of rape and arrested the accused.
