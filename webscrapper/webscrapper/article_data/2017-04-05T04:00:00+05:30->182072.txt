Surat: Hydraulic department of Surat Municipal Corporation (SMC) will start installing water meters in 10,000 commercial properties of the city from April 10. In this one month campaign, department officials will be issuing notices to these commercial properties. The water meters are being installed to measure water being drawn. 

 An official from SMC said that there are number of establishments that have meters but are not operational. He also said that as per rules, water connections higher than 0.5 inch must be metered, besides hotels, canteens, juice centers.
