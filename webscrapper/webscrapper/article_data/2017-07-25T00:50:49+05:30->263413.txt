THANE: A 19-year-old girl who had run away from her home in   tried to jump off the Kharegaon bridge into the Thane   on Monday afternoon, but she was stopped in the nick of time by a senior civic  . 

 Shankar Patole, additional commissioner of Mumbra   office, was passing Kalwa bridge around 1pm when he spotted the girl. “By the time I got out of the car and ran towards her, the girl had already thrown her belongings into the creek and climbed on the railing. I literally grabbed her and made her sit in my car and locked the door. My hands were shaking as I kept thinking about my two daughters,” he said. 
 
 The girl has been handed over to the Women’s Rehabilitation Centre in Manpada. A probe is on.
