GURGAON: The body of a 25-year-old   resident was found in Sector 114 on Saturday. An FIR has been lodged for  .

 The man has been identified as Lokesh, a resident of Mitraon village near Najafgarh in Delhi. Lokesh had left home around 9.30pm on Friday, but did not return. His body was discovered by a passerby in a field near the sector road adjoining Bajghera village in Sector 114. 
 
 Post-mortem report has revealed that he had been hit on his head with a blunt weapon, which led to his death.
 
 Lokesh’s father, Ranbir Singh, filed a police complaint at Palam Vihar police station on Saturday. Based on the complaint, an FIR was registered under Section 302 (murder) of IPC against unknown persons. Police handed over the body to the family after the autopsy.
 
 “We are investigating the matter. Lokesh might have been murdered due to personal enmity,” said inspector Sandeep Kumar, SHO, Palam Vihar police station.
