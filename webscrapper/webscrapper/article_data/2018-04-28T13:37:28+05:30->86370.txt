NEW DELHI: Two girls were injured in a fire at an LED manufacturing unit in northwest Delhi's   area this morning, a fire department official said.

 Muskaan, 14, and Payal, 12, have sustained 70 and 80 per cent burn injuries respectively, the official said.
 
 They are undergoing treatment at a hospital, he said.
 
 A call to the Delhi Fire Service was made around 9 am and three fire-tenders were rushed.
 
 The fire was doused within an hour.
