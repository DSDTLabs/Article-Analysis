MANALI: The police here have arrested a person for attempting to deposit fake currency notes in a bank at Bagsiad in Gohar region of the state on Thursday. It also seized 50 notes of Rs 2,000 denomination.

 According to police, the person identified as Lal Singh of Bhad village visited Himachal Gramin Bank to deposit the currency. The bank officials on finding that the currency was fake contacted the police. The accused told the cops that he received the money from a person at Malana village of Kullu district for working as a labourer and he had no knowledge about real or forged notes.
 
 Mandi DSP (headquarters)   said that, “We are searching for the source of the currency. The police have left for Malana to investigate the matter. The person has been arrested.”
