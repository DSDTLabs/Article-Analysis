It looks like directors   and  's multi-starrer superhero film 'Avengers: Infinity War' is all set to become the highest grossing film in the domestic circuit as the film shot up its collections once again in its second weekend at the box-office. The film raked in a total of Rs 30.50 in its second weekend which has taken its total collection to an astounding amount of Rs 187.02 crore, according to a report in BoxofficeIndia.com.

 
  
 
 The film is just a few lakhs short of becoming India's highest-earning   film as it looks all set to surpass 'The Jungle Book' which has a total collection of Rs 187.65 crore. According to experts, the film would even cross the Rs 200 crore mark this week given the love it's receiving from the audience. The film, which brings together the largest gathering of superheroes to battle it out against Thanos, also had the biggest day one opening ever for a Hollywood movie in the country.
 
 Helmed by Joe and Anthony Russo 'Avengers: Infinity War' stars Robert Downey Jr,  , Mark Ruffalo, Scarlett Johansson,  , Anthony Mackie, Paul Bettany, Elizabeth Olsen, Chadwick Boseman, Sebastian Stan, Don Cheadle, Chris Pratt, Dave Bautista, Zoe Saldana, Pom Klementieff, Benedict Cumberbatch, Benedict Wong, Tom Holland, among others.
