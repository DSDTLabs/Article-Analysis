Panaji: The Union ministry of electronics and information technology (cyber laws and e-security) has formed a high-level organizing committee, headed by Union minister for Electronics and IT, Ravi Shankar Prasad, to oversee preparations for the Global Conference on Cyber Space (GCCS). Goan entrepreneur and president of the Manufacturers Association for Information Technology, Nitin Kunkolienker, is part of the committee. The fifth edition of GCCS will be held in November in New Delhi. It will focus on inclusive development of a safe internet.
