KARUR: Three people sustained injuries after two bulls tied to a cart carrying DMK workers ran amok during in Karur on Saturday.

 The DMK had announced a state-wide agitation against the fare hike on January 27. As part of the protest, a group of party workers had taken out a rally in which some workers were riding on a bullock cart in Thanthonimalai.
 
 While the rally was approaching on the main roads of the town, the two bulls suddenly ran amok on the road.
 
 Two men, who were on a motor-bike, were hit by the bulls. Another person, who was in the cart, also got injured. 
 
 The trio was admitted to the government hospital for treatment, police sources said.
