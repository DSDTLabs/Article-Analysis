A 30-year-old man allegedly involved in robbery and murder of a security guard of a garment factory was arrested after an exchange of fire with police in Gadhi Chaukhandi on Tuesday night. Police received information about movement of Satyendra Kumar, a resident of Bulandshahr, and his accomplice in the neighbourhood. “We deployed a team at the designated spot. Two accused came on a TVS Apache bike. The criminal opened fire in order to escape when the police signaled him to stop for checking. Police returned fire in which he suffered injuries on his leg and fell down. He was arrested while his accomplice escaped,” said Arun Kumar Singh, SP (city) Noida. tnn
