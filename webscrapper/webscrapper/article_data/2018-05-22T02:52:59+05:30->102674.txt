NEW DELHI: A woman was targeted by a snatcher who tried to choke her and when she retaliated, threatened to shoot her till she handed over her jewellery to him. When she still resisted, he pushed her aside and snatched her gold jewellery.

 The incident took place in   on Sunday evening. The victim, Fakhre  , was with her 10-year-old son when the man attacked her near Batla House. Her son, who tried to help her mother, was also kicked by the attacker.
 
 After the robber fled, Tasreen made a PCR call and informed the cops about the incident. A   team soon rushed to the spot and took the woman to a hospital, where she was given treatment and discharged later.
 
 “I was crossing the road, holding my son’s hand, when suddenly a man came and grabbed my neck and tried to choke me. I struggled to free myself from his clutches, but he tried to further choke me. Seeing this, my son tried to attack him but the man did not budge,” she said, adding that the attacker then pulled out a gun and threatened to shoot her.
 
 Cops said the attacker snatched her gold chain and scratched her neck by digging his nails deep. Tasreen started bleeding profusely as the attacker fled with her chain.
 
 Police said that they have registered a case under sections 356 (Assault or criminal force in attempt to commit theft of property carried by a person) and 379 (Punishment for theft) of the   at   police station. Teams have also been formed to nab the accused. 
 
 Cops said that CCTV footage from the area is being scanned to ascertain the sequence of events and the identity of the accused.
