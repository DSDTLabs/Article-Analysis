GREATER NOIDA: In an alleged case of  , a 30-year-old woman was found hanging in her house in Dadri’s Gadhi village on Sunday.

 The family of the woman, Sapna, has alleged that her parents-in-law used to thrash her demanding Rs 15 lakh and a Toyota Fortuner. Sapna married Rahul, a resident of Gadhi, in 2015.
 
 “The in-laws called the control room and said the woman had committed suicide by hanging herself from the fan,” Nishank Sharma, circle officer,   told TOI. 
 
 Sapna’s family came from Baghpat when her father-in-law Joginder informed them about the incident. “The family has complained that Sapna’s in-laws used to beat her demanding dowry. She was supposed to get married to Rahul’s elder brother but instead got married to Rahul. The age factor was also an issue as Rahul seemed younger than Sapna,” Sharma said.
 
 Police have lodged an FIR under sections 304B (dowry death) and 498A (husband or relative of husband of a woman subjecting her to cruelty) against the woman's husband, father-in-law, mother-in-law and brother-in-law.
