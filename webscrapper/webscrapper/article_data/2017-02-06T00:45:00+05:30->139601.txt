Hyderabad: The Telangana government on Saturday announced that its annual flagship event – BioAsia – will be held during February 6-8.

 The three-day event will be inaugurated by the governor of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana, ESL Narasimhan, in the presence of industry leaders.
 
 During the inaugural ceremony, the governor would also present the Genome Valley Excellence Award to Nobel Laureate Prof Kurt Wuthrichand and Dr Paul Stoffels, worldwide chairman (pharmaceuticals) and chief scientific officer of Johnson & Johnson for their contribution in the field of life sciences and healthcare.
 
 The event is expected to see participation of around 1,500 delegates from over 50 countries.
