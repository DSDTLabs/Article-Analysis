So   reality show   has wrapped up. And for those who will experience withdrawal symptoms after it ends, here’s some news that will cheer you up. Popular game show   is set to come back for the third time and we’ve got all the details for you. Unlike the first season where film folk came as players and the common folks participated in the second, in its third season, the game show will see 16 teams from across various television shows such as Agnisakshi, Radha Ramana, Gandhari, Akka, Hubli Huduga Mangaloor Hudugi and Kinnari participating in the show. Two teams will be pitted against each other to compete in each episode and the show will air for eight consecutive weeks. Golden Star Ganesh will once again return as the host of the show.
