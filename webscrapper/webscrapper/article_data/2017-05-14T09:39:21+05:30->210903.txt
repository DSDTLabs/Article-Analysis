VIZIANAGARAM: Joint collector   ordered   project officials to increase the minimum wages and coordinate with other departments to identify other works that can be taken up under the scheme. 

 At a review meeting with all the major district and forest department officials here on Saturday, Nageswara   observed that proposals must be made to accommodate more workers under the scheme for larger projects to be taken up. He added that at present the proposals must focus on increasing the daily employment from 2.92 lakh to 3.5 lakh.
 
 Regarding payment of wages, the   pointed out that the budget is Rs 299 crore for this year. He also enquired about the targets given under the scheme. He noted that about 30,000 farm ponds must be built under the scheme for the year.
