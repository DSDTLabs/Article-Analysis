CHANDIGARH: Chandigarh district court on Friday granted   to an accused in a case where a person was attacked with beer bottles while trying to save his female friend from sexual harassment. 

 The accused  , a Sector 22 resident, had been booked by police under sections 308 (attempt to commit culpable homicide), 324 (voluntarily causing hurt by dangerous weapons or means) and 354 (assault or criminal force to woman with intent to outrage her modesty) of the Indian Penal Code (IPC). The court granted him bail after furnishing bail bond of Rs 50,000.
