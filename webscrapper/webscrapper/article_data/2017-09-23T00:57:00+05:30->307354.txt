Coimbatore: Indians should live without fear, Hindu Munnani founder Rama Gopalan said here on Friday. “Only if you learn how to live without fear can the country will have a bright future,” he said while addressing a public meeting at Rathinapuri.

 “Violent incidents are happening across the globe because of people from a community. People should fight against terrorism and  ,” he said.
 
 Rama Gopalan, along with Hindu Munnani president Kadeswara C Subramaniam and other leaders, also paid homage to Hindu Munnani functionary C Sasikumar, who was hacked to death by a four-member gang at Subramaniyampalayam near Thudiyalur on September 22, 2016. The organisation observed the first death anniversary of Sasikumar on Friday.
