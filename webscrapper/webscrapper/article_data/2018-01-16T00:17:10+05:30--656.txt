Udhagamandalam: Army Day was celebrated at Madras Regimental Centre (MRC) in Wellington near Coonoor in the Nilgiris, on Monday. Wreath laying ceremony marked the occasion. The day is celebrated on January 15 every year in recognition of Lt Gen (later Field Marshal) KM Kariappa taking over as the first commander-in chief of Indian Army from Sir Francis Butcher, the last British Commander in 1949. Brig GS Rathore, BGS, Defence Service Staff College (DSSC), Wellington and senior officers laid wreaths at the War Memorial on behalf of service personnel and veterans.TNN
