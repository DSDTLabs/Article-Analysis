Pune: A four-year-old boy died after falling through the window of a third-floor flat at Ambethan village near Chakan, about 35km from here, on Monday morning.

 According to the police, the incident occurred when Durversh Dnyaneshwar Karwar of Saibaba Paradise in Ambethan was playing in the bedroom. 
 
 “While playing, Durvesh came to the window and tried to look down. He lost his balance and fell,” an officer from the Chakan police said.
 
 Durvesh’s sister, aunt and grandmother were in the flat at the time. His parents had gone out for work. “Durvesh was rushed to a private hospital at Nanekarwadi, where he was declared dead,” the officer said.
 
 The officer added that Durvesh had sustained severe head injuries, which resulted in his death. 
 
 His aunt Jayashree Karwar reported the matter to the police. 
 
 “We have registered a case of accidental death,” the officer said.
