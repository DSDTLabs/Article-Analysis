 The woman, Sahin, and her son Asad died on the spot while her husband Yusuf, who was driving the scooter, sustained injuries and has been admitted to a private hospital.
 
 “My sister Sahin had come to   from Bulandshahr to take her son for treatment in a hospital. On Thursday, while they were returning on their scooter, a truck came from behind and hit it. Sahin, who was holding her son fell on the road and got crushed by the truck while Yusuf sustained serious injuries,” said Irshad, Sahin’s brother.
 
 “According to doctors, Yusuf has suffered injuries in his ribs and limbs and his condition is serious,” said Irshad.
 
 According to Irshad, a passerby chased the driver but he sped away. However, he managed to note the truck’s registration number. 
 
 An FIR has been lodged at Masuri police station under sections 279 (rash driving), 337 (causing hurt by act endangering life), 338 (causing grievous hurt) and 304 A (causing death by negligence) of IPC and the case is being investigated.
 
 “The accident took place around 9.30am. The eyewitness told us that the truck was being driven at a high speed,” said Satyender Singh, SHO, Masuri police station. “We’ve the truck’s registration number and it’s only a matter of time before the driver is arrested,” added Singh.
