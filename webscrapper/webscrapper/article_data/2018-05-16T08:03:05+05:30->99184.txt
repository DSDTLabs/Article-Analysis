PATNA: A  , Sushil Kumar Singh (45), was shot dead in his shop located near   under the Industrial police station area at   in Vaishali district at around 8.30pm on Tuesday. Singh was a resident of Hajipur town area. Vaishali SP Manavjeet Singh Dhillon said two assailants on a bike reached Singh’s shop and posed as customers. They inquired about marble samples. The SP said after brief discussion, the duo left and Singh also went up to the shop’s gate to see them off. Dhillon said no sooner Singh turned towards his shop than the two opened fire and shot him twice in his back. He died on the spot. The entire incident was captured in the   camera installed inside the shop. The SP said the assailants are visible in the CCTV footage.
