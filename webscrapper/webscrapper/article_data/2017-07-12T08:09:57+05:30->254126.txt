KOZHIKODE: Poultry traders in the state have called off their strike after talks with finance minister TM   on Tuesday. 

 Isaac said that traders have agreed to sell live chicken at Rs 87/ kg and dressed chicken at Rs 158/kg. Customers will have to pay dressing charges and waste disposal charges in addition to rates fixed for live chicken,   added.
 
 "We had directed poultry traders to sell live chicken at Rs 87/kg after deducting the 14.5% VAT from the prices which prevailed on June 30 as chicken had been moved to the zero-tax category with the advent of GST," he said.
