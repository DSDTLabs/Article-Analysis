Rajkot: A 21-year-old woman, a resident of Kamarkotada village of Gondal taluka in Rajkot district, accused a resident of Ghoghavadar village in Gondal takuka of raping her on Monday night. 
 
 The complaint was lodged on Tuesday night and the accused, Ashwin Dobaria, was arrested the same night. 
  
 “In her complaint, the survivor has alleged that Dobaria lured her with promise of getting her a contract of a singer worth Rs 5,000 daily wage and took her to Chotila for an interview in his SUV. While returning to Kamarkotada village, the accused raped her in the vehicle,” said a Bamanbor police official. tnn
