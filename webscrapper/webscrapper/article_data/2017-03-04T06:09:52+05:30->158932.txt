GURUGRAM: A vehicle theft gang, involved in robberies in Sohna, Badshapur and Gurugram reported over the past year, was busted on Thursday night and its two gang members were held by the crime unit, Sohna. 

 A Maruti 800 and seven stolen motorcycles were recovered, police said. Acting on a tip-off, police set up barricades near Indri road, intercepted the duo on a bike.
 
  They had fake documents of the bike. The accused confessed they had stolen six other bikes and a Maruti 800 car.
