Cuddalore: A 35-year-old man murdered his wife, daughter and son and later committed suicide at his residence near Kullanchavadi in Cuddalore district on Wednesday.

 Police said S Aadhimoolam, a pushcart icecream vendour, who would take up odd jobs, hacked his wife A kumara, 30, daughter A Abitha, 6 and Abishek, 4 to death using a knife. He later committed suicide by hanging at his house.
 
 A police team from Kullanchavadi reached the spot after the neighbours alerted them. 
 
 The team recovered the bodies and sent them for postmortem. Police have not ascertained the motive behind the murders and the suicide.
