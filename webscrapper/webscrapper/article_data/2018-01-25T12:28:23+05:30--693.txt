 Elections for the 15 elected representatives in the new syndicate body will be held in the last week of February. While election for the one representative from amongst the head of departments will be held on February 24, elections for the rest 14 representatives will be held on February 27. 
 
 Elections for the constituencies of two deans of faculties, one principal of colleges, six persons who are neither teachers nor employees nor students of the university and five teachers (including one professor and four teachers other than deans, heads of department and principals of colleges) will be held on February 27. 
 
 While in the case of the one representative of head of department, one head amongst all the heads gets elected to the syndicate body, for the rest of the seats senate members form the electoral base.
