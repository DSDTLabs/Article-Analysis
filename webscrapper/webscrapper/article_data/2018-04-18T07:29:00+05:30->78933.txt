Sangrur:   (retd) Commission, formed by the Punjab government to probe all incidents of sacrilege of Guru Granth Sahib, Bhagavad Gita and Quran Sharif, will be visiting Sangrur on Wednesday and Malerkotla on April 25 to record statements of witnesses wanting to give statements.

 A state government spokesperson said the commission had so far visited places of incidents of sacrilege in Mohali, Fatehgarh Sahib, Patiala, Ropar, Nawanshahr, Hoshiarpur, Barnala, Ludhiana, Jalandhar and Moga districts. The spokesperson said the commission would be recording statements of all witnesses of the sacrilege incidents in Sangrur and Malerkotla during the visit.
 
 The announcement came a day after the Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) MLA from Dirba, Harpal Singh Cheema, sought the incident of sacrilege of Quran Sharif in Malerkotla should also be probed by the panel.
