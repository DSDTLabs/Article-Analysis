AHMEDABAD: Two groups clashed over water connections in   near   in Ahmedabad district on Friday morning. Three persons were injured in the incident and were taken to a hospital in Sanand.   GIDC police officials said no complaint had been filed as of Friday night. 

 Police investigators said the issue started with heated arguments between two members of the same community over a water line. One of the groups was not ready to pay for it, they added. The matter soon escalated as the groups came to blows with clubs and iron rods.
 
 Sanand GIDC police officials said police are probing the incident and a team was sent to hospital. “The situation in the village is under control and additional forces have been deployed to prevent an untoward incident,” said an official.
