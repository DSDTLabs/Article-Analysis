Ahmedabad: Data from the Union ministry of earth science’s System of Air Quality and Weather Forecasting and Research (Safar) showed on Wednesday that the air quality index (AQI) of Ahmedabad was worst among the metro cities monitored by Safar. Officials said the city AQI was worse than the Delhi AQI again on Wednesday and was highest among the four Safar monitored metro cities — Ahmedabad, Delhi, Mumbai and Pune. Ahmedabad’s overall AQI was 269 while Delhi’s was 267. Officials said that in Ahmedabad the Gift city area had the highest AQI of 308. In Ahmedabad city, Rakhial in the Walled City area had the lowest AQI of 112. Satellite which had the most polluted air with AQI of 330 on Saturday showed better AQI of 304 on Wednesday.
