LUCKNOW: As many as 11 career counsellors from across the country had their Graduation Day on Tuesday at the Institute for Careers Studies (ICS). May 9 is celebrated as ‘Career Counsellors Day’ by ICS every year in memory of  , the inspiration and driving force behind ICS.

 The career counsellors belong to the first batch to be awarded with the institute’s post graduate   in career guidance and counselling for students. Educationist Mohini Manglik was the chief guest and Prof Sabra Habib the guest of honour. Manglik felicitated Monika M. Dass, psychologist and counsellor, with a plaque for her exemplary contribution in design and content development of the diploma course. Later, she awarded the diploma certificates to the graduates.
