NAGPUR: Nagpur Municipal Corporation ( ) has continued bicycle bank scheme for its students for fourth consecutive year. Union minister Nitin Gadkari launched this year’s scheme on the occasion of his birthday on Sunday.

 A brainchild of NMC’s Pannalal Devadiya School, the scheme aims to contain school dropout cases, encourage girl students and help poor students. After   introduced the scheme in 2015, the civic body took up the project and started distributing bicycles worth Rs10 lakh to the students every year.
 
 Gadkari presented bicycles to 12 students at the inauguration. Deputy mayor  , municipal commissioner  , chairman of education consultative committee   and other officials were present.
