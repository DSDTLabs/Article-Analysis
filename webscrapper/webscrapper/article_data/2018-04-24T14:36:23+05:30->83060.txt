PANAJI:   panchayat member Lourenco Marcelline  , for whom police have been on the lookout since a court rejected his anticipatory bail plea, surrendered before Panaji police on Monday.

 He had been booked March for allegedly forging a bonafide certificate to obtain an Other Backward Class ( ) certificate during the panchayat election.  , who contested against Rodrigues in the election, had lodged the complaint. 
 
 Panaji police registered an offence under Sections 465, 468 and 471 of the Indian Penal Code against Rodrigues.
