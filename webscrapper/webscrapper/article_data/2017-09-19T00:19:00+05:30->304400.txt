Chennai: An assistant manager from air traffic control at Chennai collapsed and died while on a   at Manali in Himachal Pradesh on Sunday.

 Airports Authority of India (AAI) sources said that Vasanth Narayanan S, 27, from Guduvancherry was part of a team of AAI employees who had gone on a 10-day trek. He developed breathing trouble and fell unconscious. He was given first aid and was taken to a hospital in Manali by rescue team but was declared dead.
 
 “He had stayed back for a day but continued the next day to an altitude of 12,000 feet but returned on a mule after he felt uneasy,” said an AAI official. tnn
