GURUGRAM: Two persons, including a 10-year-old boy, on Friday succumbed to their injuries sustained in separate hit-and-run accidents on Thursday. 

 In the first accident, a speeding car rammed into a motorcycle from behind near Sirhaul plaza on Thursday afternoon. The two persons on the bike, identified as Delhi residents Mohammad Salim and Shafik Ahemad, were badly injured and rushed to a nearby private hospital. Ahemad died during treatment in Delhiâ€™s Safdarjung Hospital on Friday evening, police said. An FIR was registered against the driver, who had fled, under sections 279 (rash driving) and 304A (death due to negligence) of IPC at Udyog Vihar police station.
 
 In the second accident, a motorcycle hit 10-year-old Mohit, who was playing on the road in front of his house in Babra Bankipur village in Farrukhnagar area around 3pm on Thursday. Mohit was critically injured and succumbed to his injuries on Friday morning. An FIR has been registered at Farrukhnagar police station.
