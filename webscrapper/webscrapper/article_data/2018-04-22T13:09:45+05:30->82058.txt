MUMBAI: There is a megablock on the Mulund-Matunga Up slow line till 4.10pm and this could cause inconvenience to the commuters. 

 The Down fast/semi fast services leaving   till 2.54pm will halt at Ghatkopar, Vikhroli, Bhandup, Mulund and Diva stations in addition to their respective scheduled halts and arrive destination 20 minutes behind schedule. 
 
 Similarly, the Up fast/semi fast services leaving Kalyan till 3.06pm will halt at Diva, Mulund, Bhandup, Vikhroli, Ghatkopar and   stations in addition to their respective scheduled halts and arrive destination 15 minutes behind schedule.
 
 All slow line services leaving/arriving CSMT till 6pm will arrive destination 10 minutes behind schedule. 
 
 "Up slow line services will not be available at Nahur, Kanjur Marg and Vdyavihar stations. Passengers of these stations are permitted to travel via Bhandup, Vikhroli, and Kurla stations," an official added.
