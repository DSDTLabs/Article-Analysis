 In compliance of the order of ADJ court (No2), Agra, for attaching the official vehicle of district collector, officials seized the car of collector Narendra Gupta on Friday. The court order followed a petition seeking compensation for crop loss due to leakage of water from a canal in Bharatpur. Ram Swaroop and  , a resident of   block of Agra in UP had filed a petition seeking compensation for their loss of Rabi crops in 32 bighas of land due to a breach in a canal of Bharatpur in 1997.
