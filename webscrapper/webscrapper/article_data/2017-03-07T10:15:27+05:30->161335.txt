ALLAHABAD: The district court on Monday acquitted BJP leader   of all the charges of damaging the office of   (ADM) of Allahabad in 1998.

 At present Maurya, is member of Parliament from Phulpur Lok Sabha constituency and is also state president of the BJP.
 
 Maurya has been absolved of all the charges of damaging the public property and others related charges.He was present in the court when the decision was pronounced.
 
 Earlier, an FIR was registered against Maurya and some others at the Colonelganj police station, Allahabad in the year 1998, alleging that he along with others damaged the public property in the office of ADM (city) Allahabad. After investigation, police submitted chargesheet only against Maurya.
