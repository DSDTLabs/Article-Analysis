THIRUVANANTHAPURAM: The Kerala University has announced the M.Phil Result October 2017 today on the official website of the Board - keralauniversity.ac.in.

 All the students who have appeared for M.Phil Examination in October 2017 can check their result online on the official website - keralauniversity.ac.in.
 
 The M.Phil exam was conducted in the month of October 2017. Students who have appeared for the examination and wish to check their M.Phil Result 2017 can follow the steps given below:
 
 
 
 
 1) Open the official website - keralauniversity.ac.in
 
 2) On the homepage of the website, you will find the Result link. Click on it
 
 3) Once you click on the link, it will redirect you to a new webpage where you will have to enter your details such as your roll number etc. Next, click on submit
 
 4) Your M.Phil 2017 result will be displayed onscreen. Cross check all the details given in your M.Phil 2017 Result.
 
 5) You can download and print your M.Phil 2017 Result for further reference.
