MARGAO: The Railway Protection Force,  , rescued a 13-year-old   from Jamkhed, Maharashtra, who is believed to have escaped from the clutches of his kidnappers and boarded a Kerala-bound train.

 RPF sources said that the boy was found at Margao railway station at around 6 am on Tuesday. “He told us that on February 3, he had gone to the market in his native place when an unidentified man approached him from behind and suddenly put a white towel smeared with some powder, on his nose which made him unconscious, sources said. The next day when he regained consciousness, the boy found himself in a car with four persons. When the occupants went for tea, the boy broke open the car’s window glass with a stick found inside and escaped
