Panchkula: Thieves targeted a canteen stores department (CSD) canteen in the Chandimandir Cantonment area on Tuesday night, taking away only costly cosmetic goods and leaving the others untouched.

 CSD canteen in-charge Subedar Baldev Dutt, a resident of Hamirpur in Himachal Pradesh, in his complaint to the police, said when he opened the canteen on Wednesday morning, he found goods lying scattered on the floor.
 
 When he took a round of the canteen, he found that a glass window was broken.
 
 Cops said the thieves stole costly cosmetic items like soaps, L’oreal shampoo bottles, Oley cream packs, Vicco fairness cream packs and other items.
 
 Subedar Dutt, said, “There is no CCTV camera installed in the canteen. A list of stolen items has been given to the cops.”
 
 Police registered a case under Sections 380 (theft in dwelling house) and 457 (lurking house-trespass or house-breaking by night in order to commit offence punishable with imprisonment) of the Indian Penal Code at the Chandimandir police station.
 
 Assistant sub-inspector Pratap Singh, the investigating officer, said, “On the basis of the complaint, we have registered a case. Investigation is under progress.”
