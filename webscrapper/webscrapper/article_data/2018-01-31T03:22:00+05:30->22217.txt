Vasco: Vasco police arrested Vikram Bhandari, 23, resident of Darjeeling, West Bengal, for attempting to steal from a foreign tourist. He was caught red-handed on Tuesday, police said. At 5pm, Bhandari was seen committing theft of a shoulder bag containing a mobile phone, ATM card and cash of Rs 2,400, all worth Rs 35,000, belonging to Juuso Pentti Ahvenainen, a Finnish national, from an open place at Bogmalo beach. Bhandari was arrested and lodged in police custody. Police sub-inspector Prashant Bhagat is conducting further investigation into this case.
