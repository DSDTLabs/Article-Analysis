RAIPUR: State capital's civic body filed an FIR and imposed a fine of Rs 1 lakh for defacement of public property against organisers of a music concert of a Bollywood singer.

 The civic body also instructed the organisers to remove the posters pasted for promotion of the event and clean the walls of the city.   spokesperson said the private organisers invited a Bollywood singer to perform in the state capital on the eve of   and   festival on Saturday evening. The organisers and sponsors of the event illegally pasted posters on walls and under bridges without seeking the permission from the civic body.
