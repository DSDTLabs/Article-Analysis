As Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Li’l Champs came to an end last weekend, new dance reality show Dance Karnataka Dance little master will be aired in place on Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Li’l Champs from next weekend at 7:30pm.
 
  Several small screen actors performed at the grand premiere of DKD little master on last Saturday.   DKD Little Masters will see thirteen pairs comprising of past contestants from reality shows – Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Li’l Champs, Drama Juniors season 1 and 2, and child artistes from fiction shows like Jodi Hakki and Gangaa.
