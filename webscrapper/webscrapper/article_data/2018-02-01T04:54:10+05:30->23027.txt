MUMBAI: Mumbai's annual tryst with the winged beauties will happen at the   on Sunday, February 4, as avid bird watchers will scan various places in the city and suburbs to spot and record various species.

 “We have closed registrations for at 260 participants with over 50 teams. But to accommodate others, we have more than 50 people joining for a special BirdWalk,” said naturalist  , one of the founders of Mumbai BirdRace.
 
 Throughout the day, various bird-watcher teams will visit wetlands, grasslands, jungles and other places to check out birds and later in the evening meet others to discuss their feathered findings.
