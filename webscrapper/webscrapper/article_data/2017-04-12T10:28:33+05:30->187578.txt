KARAIKAL:   government on Tuesday assured that it would seek the Centre's intervention to secure the release of six Puducherry fishermen, arrested recently by the Sri   on drug trafficking charges. 

 The assurance was given by Puducherry agriculture minister   when he met fishermen from 11 hamlets of the region, who are on an indefinite strike since April 3, condemning the arrest of their colleagues.
 
 Chief minister   had already urged the Indian high commission in   to take steps to secure the release of the innocent fishermen, he said and appealed to the protesters to suspend their agitation and resume fishing.
