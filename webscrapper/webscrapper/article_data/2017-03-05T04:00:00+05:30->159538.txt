Ahmedabad: A group of Sikh youths gathered at the crime branch office on Saturday to lodge an application of   against defamatory and vulgar posts on religious leaders including Sikh Gurus. Community members mentioned in their application that there have been very disgusting posts on several social media platforms about Sikh Gurus along with religious leaders and deities of different religion.

 The Sikh youths said that similar complaints have been lodged in different cities including Mumbai. “We have given the dates and names of the social media platforms on which such vulgar material was posted,” said a Sikh.
 
 Crime branch officials said they have forwarded the application of complaint to the cyber cell. “We will write to the service providers to locate the IP address from which such posts have been made,” said a crime branch official. tnn
