Madurai: A teenager succumbed to a suspected case of   at the Government Rajaji Hospital on Tuesday.

 Sources said, Rajapandi, 17, son of Rajendran of Maravamangalam in Thirumangalam in Madurai was a Class XII student studying in a school in Thirumangalam. He was down with fever for the past ten days and his parents had treated him at a private hospital, where they had told them it was a viral fever.
 
 As his condition became serious they had taken him to a hospital in Arupukottai and were referred to the Arupukottai government hospital. As his condition remained critical he was brought to the Government Rajaji Hospital on Monday. He was kept in the ICU and initial tests revealed that he was suffering from dengue. The boy succumbed on Tuesday evening.
