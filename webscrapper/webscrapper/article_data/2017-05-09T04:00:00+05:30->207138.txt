Rajkot: A driver and his cleaner died after their truck overturned and got engulfed in fire near Malvan Chowkdi on Viramgam-Kutch highway late night on Sunday.

 According to Patdi police, the accident occurred when the truck loaded with fertilizer overturned by the roadside and suddenly caught fire.
 
 Driver Lokesh and cleaner Nandram, both residents of Rajasthan, could not escape from the truck and were charred to death. There was heavy traffic jam on the highway after the accident and police took several hours to clear it.
