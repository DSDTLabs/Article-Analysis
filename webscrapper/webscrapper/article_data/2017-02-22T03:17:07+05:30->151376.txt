GURUGRAM: A   was found dumped in a bin in an upscale residential locality in Sector 41 on Monday evening. While the postmortem was conducted on Tuesday, police said the gender of the foetus could not be determined.

 Rajender Singh Rana, the security-in-charge of A2 tower of the World Spa-East society, reported the incident to the police. He said a sanitation worker informed him when he found the foetus while clearing a garbage bin. The   footage are being scanned by police to find out who dumped it.
 
 Sudhir Kumar, the SHO of Sector 40 police station, said the sex of the foetus could not be determined from the postmortem report as the foetus was only three to four months’ old. 
 
 The police have also preserved the DNA of the foetus for further investigation, for confirmatory tests in future.
 
 An FIR was registered under Section 318 (concealment of birth by secret disposal of dead body) of IPC in Sector 40 police station on Monday.
 
 “We are investigating the matter,” Kumar said.
