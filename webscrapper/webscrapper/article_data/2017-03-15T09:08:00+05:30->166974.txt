BELAGAVI: Mirasab Yargatti, the husband of a gram panchayat member from Modaga, has been arrested for sharing on social media a picture of a man abusing the Indian tricolor. Mirasab was arrested after villagers staged a protest and locked up the   GP office on Tuesday.
