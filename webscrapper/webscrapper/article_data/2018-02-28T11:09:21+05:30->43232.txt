The   (SBI) released the admit cards for their Pre-exam training admit cards for Clerk Prelims 2018 exam on their official website. All the candidates from the reserved categories, who had applied for the pre-exam training, can access their call letters from the official website sbi.co.in/careers. 

 Please note, you can download the pre-exam training call letters only till March 11, 2018. Candidates from the SC/ST/Ex-servicemen/ Religious Minority who opted for Pre-exam training have to download the admit cards. The timing, venue and other details about the training will be available on the admit cards.
