NEW DELHI: Rajasthan Subordinate & Ministerial Services Selection Board (RSMSSB) has invited applications for the post of Live Stock Assistant(LSA). As per the notification, the last date to apply online is May 11, 2018.
 
 
 A 12th pass candidate, who has done one year/two years training of Livestock Assistant from any recognised institution is eligible and can apply for these posts.
 
 
 
 
 Interested candidates can apply online through official website http://rsmssb.rajasthan.gov.in on or before May 11, 2018.
