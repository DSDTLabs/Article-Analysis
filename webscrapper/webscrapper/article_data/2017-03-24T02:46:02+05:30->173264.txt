GURUGRAM: One youth was killed and three others injured on Wednesday night in a hit-and-run crash near Shankar Chowk on  . 

 The vehicle, that ran over the four friends, are yet to be identified, said police. Also, police don’t have any clue about the accused driver.
 
 The accident was reported around 1am on Wednesday. Two men — Jatin and Manoj — reached Shankar Road on bike to meet their friends, Sunil and Vicky. The four were planning to go to Sheetla Mandir and they were waiting for Sunil and Vicky to board a bus when a vehicle hit them from behind and fled, police said.The four youths, who were seriously injured, called police with the help of a passer-by. They were rushed to Fortis Hospital where 26-year-old Manoj was declared brought dead by doctors. 
 
 The other three, who are between 25 to 30 years of age, are still being treated.
 
 An FIR has been filed against unidentified vehicle driver under sections 279 (rash driving), 337 (causing hurt), 338 (causing grievous hurt by endangering life), 304A (causing death by negligence) of the IPC at DLF-II police station. 
 
 “We are trying to identify the vehicle and its driver,” said sub inspector Balraj, the officer investigating the case.
