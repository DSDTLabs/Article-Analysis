NAGPUR: Formidable Reserve Bank of India (RBI) expectedly bagged team championship title, while RBI   Alhad Gunjal and Shaheen Khan reigned supreme in the men’s and women’s singles respectively in the Inter-Bank   tournament, organized by the Bankers’ Sports Council (BSC), at the Ambedkar College Sports Academy Hall here on Sunday.

 
 In a one-sided team championship final, RBI thrashed Bank of India 3-0. Gunjal provided a flying start to RBI by ousting veteran Abhay Pradhan in straight games. Sachin Gaur beat Mangesh Mopkar 11-6, 13-11, 11-6 to make it 2-0. Prafulla Nandedkar then overcame DV Joshi also in straight games to clinch the title. Earlier in the semifinals,   trounced Punjab National Bank 3-1, while RBI got the better of Union Bank 3-0.
 
 In the men’s singles final, Gunjal quelled the spirited challenge of Sachin Gaur in tough four games to annex the crown. RBI completed the clean sweep when Shaheen Khan pocketed the women’s singles crown. In the final, Shaheen carved out a facile 11-5, 11-3, 11-4 win over Arti Saraf.
 
 BSC officials Sharad Padhye, CD Manke, Ravi Joshi, Rajesh Joshi, Pradeep Khanorkar, CP Karkar, Mangesh Pandit and chief referee of the tournament Deepak Kanetkar were present during the prize distribution function.
