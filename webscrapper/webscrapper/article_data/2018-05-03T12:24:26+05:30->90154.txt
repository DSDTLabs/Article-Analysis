NEW DELHI: The The   of School Education ( ) has released the Mizoram HSLC Class 10 results on Thursday, May 3, 2018, on the official website - mbse.edu.in.

 The MBSE Board had successfully conducted the Secondary Examination (HSLC) and Higher Secondary Examination (HSE) for the academic session year 2017-18 in the month of Feb-March 2018. 
 
 As per the latest information, the Board has announced the MBSE Board HSLC Results 2018 on its official website - mbse.edu.in. 
 
 The students who wish to check their MBSE Board HSLC results can go to the official portal of the board at mbse.edu.in. 
 
 If you are one of the students eagerly waiting to check your MBSE Board HSLC Result 2018, you can check it on the official website of the MBSE Board - mbse.edu.in. 
 
 
 
 
 1) Keep handy your MBSE Board HSLC admit card or your Roll No.
 
 2) Open the official website of the MBSE Board - mbse.edu.in
 
 3) On the homepage of the Board, you will see a link - "MBSE Board Class 10 results", click on it
 
 4) It will redirect you to a new page where you need to enter your MBSE Board Class 10 roll no. and other required details
 
 5) Once you click submit, your MBSE Board HSLC result will be visible on the screen
 
 6) You can download your MBSE Board HSLC result and print it for further reference.
