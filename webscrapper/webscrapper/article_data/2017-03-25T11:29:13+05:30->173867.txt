 At around 03.45pm, officials sent out an alert that one of the coaches of train no.52973, Mhow-Sanawad passenger, derailed between Balwad and Choral section and two passengers were injured in the incident. The staff was asked to reach the spot immediately with emergency relief material.
 
 Upon hearing siren, railways staff and officials swung into action and rushed to the site in an accident relief train that left from Mhow railway station.
 
 A senior official said that the drill was conducted as a routine measure to check the alertness of the staff and officials. He said that the mock drill went well. “No train delay was recorded, as only one train was used for the drill,” he added.
