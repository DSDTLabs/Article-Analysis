SHIMLA: Six persons died and several others were injured when a state   skidded off the road and fell into a deep gorge along the   road in Shimla district on Friday morning.

 
 Sources said that   bus (HP-03B-6205) was heading towards Tikker when during the journey the bus driver lost control over the vehicle near Theog-Chhaila road.
 
  
  
 
 On seeing the bus falling into the gorge, locals immediately informed the police and district administration after which a rescue operation was launched. 
 
 Those injured in the accident have been shifted to a hospital, while the police is busy in retrieving the bodies from gorge with the help of locals.
