AMRITSAR: After winning PR Award in the US, ‘We are Sikhs’, the $1.3-million ad launched by the National Sikh Campaign ( ), has been named finalist for the 2018 ‘Multicultural Community Engagement’ and ‘Social Activism’ Shorty Awards.

 Co-founder and senior adviser of NSC Rajwant Singh told TOI on Monday, “We are excited to be in the final for these two prestigious Shorty Awards that showcase importance and achievement of the ‘We Are Sikhs’ campaign online and in communities across the US.”
 
 The Shorty Awards honour best content creators and producers on the social media.
