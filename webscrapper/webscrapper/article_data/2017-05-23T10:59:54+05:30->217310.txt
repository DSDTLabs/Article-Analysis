VISAKHAPATNAM: Rashtriya   (RINL) has completed the modernisation of converter-B 18 days ahead of schedule. With this, the modernisation of Steel Melt Shop-1 consisting of three converters is complete at a cost of around Rs 400 crore. 

 Chairman-cum-managing director P Madhusudan commissioned the modernised Converter-B and commended the Steel Melt Shop-1 collective as well as the agencies involved in the successful completion of the modernisation project. 
 
 The modernisation project included Baumco Scrubbers for primary waste gas cleaning, improved features of gas cooling systems, hydraulic skirt lift mechanisms, high levels of modernisation apart from dog house and secondary emission control systems for complying with the latest environmental norms.
