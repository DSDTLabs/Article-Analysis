Vadodara: Three road accidents were reported in the city on Tuesday in which one person died and another was injured.

 Manhar Patel (63) died after he was mowed down by a car on National Highway-8 near GSFC on Monday evening. The incident took place when Patel, a resident of Chhani, was crossing the road.
 
 Another road accident took place on Jetalpur road as a car rammed into a motorcycle late on Monday evening. The motorcyclist, who was injured, was rushed to SSG Hospital.
 
 In another incident, a car rammed into a light pole on Gotri Road late on Monday night.
