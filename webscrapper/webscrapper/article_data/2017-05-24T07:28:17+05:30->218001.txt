VADODARA: Driver of   students’ bus died due to heart attack while he was returning to the campus from the city on Tuesday.

 Dalpatsinh Rathod (50) was returning after dropping the students at their homes. When the bus reached near Khatamba on Vadodara-Waghodia Road, Rathod suffered severe pain in his chest and lost control over the bus that veered off the road. The bus hit a hoarding of a near-by construction site.
 
 Sources said that Rathod’s son  , who also drives the university’s bus, driving just behind him. 
 
 Pintu took Rathod out of the bus with the help of passers-by and rushed him to the hospital. But, Rathod died on the way.
