GURUGRAM: A Maruti   employee was arrested on Thursday for allegedly raping a 16-year-old Class IX student for past two months. 

 According to police, the incident came to light on Wednesday when the girl complained of severe abdominal pain. The girl’s mother rushed her to a nearby clinic where the doctor, after examination, told her that the girl was one-month pregnant. When her mother enquired how had it happened, the girl told her that the accused, Mohit (20), had been raping her for the past two months on the pretext of marriage.
 
 The mother and daughter then approached police and filed a complaint against Mohit in Sector 40 police station. “Mohit and I know each other as we are neighbours. Two months ago, he promised to marry me and and “then raped me”. After that, he often raped me when I was alone at home. He had threatened to kill me if I had divulge it to anybody,”the girl told police.
 
 The girl was then rushed to   for medical examination, which confirmed  . Following her complaint, an FIR under Section 6 of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act and Section 164 of the CrPC has been lodged against Mohit at Sector 40 police station. “We have arrested the accused who has already confessed to the crime. He will be produced in a city court on Friday,” said Deepak Saharan, DCP (east),  .
