Actress  , who shot to fame playing Mohanlal's daughter in the super hit film 'Drishyam,' will soon debut as a heroine.

 However, it's not in Malayalam that the little girl would start her lead innings. Esther will be the protagonist in the upcoming movie 'Kuzhali,' directed by Chela Kalaiarasan. As per reports, she plays the character of a 10th grader in the movie.
 
 Esther's latest movie in Malayalam is Gemini, in which she shared screen with Renji Panicker, Sijoy Varghese, Kishore Sathya and Tanusree Ghosh.
