HYDERABAD: Traffic will come to a standstill across the city for two minutes at 11 am on Monday to mark Martyrs’ Day, a day to remember those who laid down their lives for India’s freedom. 

 The  , in a release issued here on Sunday, urged every citizen to participate in the Martyrs’ Day. “There will be observance of silence on the roads and traffic will be in a standstill position throughout Hyderabad city for two minutes at 11am on January 30, (Monday),” it added.
