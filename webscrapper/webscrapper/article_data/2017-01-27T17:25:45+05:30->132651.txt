Ruling BJP in Goa will field candidates in 37 out of total 40 constituencies in upcoming Goa assembly elections and will support contestants in Vellim, Benaulim and Nuvem area.  The assembly elections in Goa will be held in a single phase on February 4, 2017 to elect the 40 members of the Goa Legislative Assembly. Results will be declared on March 11, 2017.
