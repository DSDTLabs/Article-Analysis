NEW DELHI: The Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board ( ) released the Karnataka SSLC or Class 10th Results 2018 on Monday, May 7, 2018, at around 11 am. The result is now live on the official website of Karnataka Board.

 
 The students who have appeared for the Karnataka SSLC / Class 10th examination can check their results at kseeb.kar.nic.in and karresults.nic.in. The students can also follow the steps given below to check their Karnataka Class 10th or SSLC results 2018:
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 1) Open the   page
 
 2) On the homepage, you will find results link, click on it
 
 3) It will redirect you to a new webpage
 
 4) Enter your details such as name, roll number and click on submit
 
 5) Your Result will be displayed on screen
 
 6) Download or take a print out for future reference
