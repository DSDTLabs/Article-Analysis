NAGPUR: The Bombay high court has named four judges to hear urgent judicial matters at its Nagpur bench during the summer vacations. The vacations would begin from Monday and would continue till June 3. 

 From May 5 to 11, justice Murlidhar Giratkar would hear cases while justice Rohit Deo would take over from May 12 and continue till 18. Justice   would be making a come back to his hometown and preside over vacation bench from May 19 to 26. Justice Arun Upadhye would take over from May 27 till last week of vacations, as per HC registry.
