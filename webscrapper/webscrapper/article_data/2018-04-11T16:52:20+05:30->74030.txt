A city based foundation Heartspring Social Cultural and Educational Foundation in   celebrated   with their event Umang. Women from all walks and all age groups were invited for the special day celebration held in Kolhapur. The main highlight of the event was the performance put forth by students of a city based blind school. They were felicitated by the foundation for their stunning performance. Along with performances, exhibition was also a part of the event where women put up stalls for food, jewellery display, and handicraft items. Games for women of all age groups were organised and prizes for winners were distributed post event.
