The Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board ( ) on Thursday announced results for Class 12 science stream examinations. 

 The students can check their   12th Result 2017 online through the official website or from the "KNOW YOUR RESULT" given below.
 
 The examinations were held in March 2017. In Class 12  , out of a total of 1,41,370 registered candidates, 1,38,252 appeared.
 
 The Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board is a government of Gujarat school education board. The Gujarat Board was set up in 1960 and its HQ is located at Gandhi Nagar. The Board also performs the duties of recognizing new schools, performance evaluation of schools and inspections of various schools associated with the Board.
