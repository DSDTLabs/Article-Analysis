Panchkula: Two capsule filling machines were stolen from a capsule making factory in Ramgarh on Wednesday. A case of theft has been registered against an unknown person at Chandimandir police station in this regard.

Karambir is a doctor by profession. The other complainant works at his factory that is located at village   in Barwala.
 
 On Wednesday at 8.30 am, when Karambir returned to his factory to start his work, he found that the locks on a shutter behind the factory were broken. It was then, he learned that two of his capsule filling machines were stolen.
 
 Following the incident, a complaint was made to the cops by him and a team of cops rushed to the spot to examine the crime scene.
 
 After observation the cops lodged an FIR against an unknown person under section 380 (theft in dwelling house, etc.) and 457 (lurking house-trespass or house-breaking by night in order to commit offence punishable with imprisonment) of the Indian Penal Code (IPC).
