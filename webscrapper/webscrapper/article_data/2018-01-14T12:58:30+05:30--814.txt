ALLAHABAD: Lakhs of devotees including seers, saints and kalpwasis on sunday took holy dip on the occasion of Makar Sankranti at Sangam.

 
 The 'snan' began on 15 ghats at the Sangam the confluence of Ganga, Yamuna and Saraswati at 3: 45 am and devotees from different parts of the country flocked the ghats to earn punya. 
 
  
  
 
 IG of Allahabad range   said that laks of devotees took holy dip at Sangam with the beginning of 'snan'. He said elaborate security arrangements were in place and over 3000 policemen were assigned duties at static locations. Personnel of jal police have been assigned duties at ghats.
