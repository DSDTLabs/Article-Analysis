BARNALA:   has been ranked first in   and Swasthh Campaign under the Swachh Bharat Mission-Gramin (SBM-G) that was started to make all rural areas of the country open defecation free (ODF). Barnala and some other districts from across the country were successful in securing 90% marks and bagging the rank.

 “All the rural areas of Barnala district are already 100% ODF and the entire district would become ODF by December 31”, said Barnala deputy commissioner  .
 
 The rankings were recently released by the Union ministry of drinking water and sanitation. Last month, Barnala district was ranked 51st in the country. A total of 11,460 toilets were constructed in the rural areas of Barnala district under this mission.
