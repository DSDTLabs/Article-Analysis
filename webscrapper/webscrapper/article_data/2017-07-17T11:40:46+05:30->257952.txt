THIRUVANTHATHAPURAM: The city corporation has directed   to take strict action against agencies which install unauthorised advertisement hoardings in the city. As the first step, the city corporation has removed all   which were erected on the stretch between  . 

 The revenue wing has laid down specific norms for erecting ad hoardings in city limits. The legal permit must be obtained from city corporation. The boards shall display name of the person or agency installing the board. Phone number and address should also be shown on advertisement hoardings. 
 
 As a new step, the company which undertook printing of flex and number of copies printed shall also be displayed. The new rules come in the wake of reports that   are being erected in a dangerous manner and could fall over, posing threat to public during heavy rain. Besides complaints have also emerged regarding ad hoardings which have been installed blocking the path of pedestrians.
