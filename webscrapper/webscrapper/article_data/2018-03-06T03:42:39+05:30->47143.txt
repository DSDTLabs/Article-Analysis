NEW DELHI: A man in his mid-50s allegedly committed suicide by jumping in front of a moving   at Kashmere Gate metro station on Sunday night.

 The deceased is yet to be identified. Senior metro officials said that the incident took place around 11.00pm. 
 
 “The man was seen walking around the metro station for 20 minutes, trying to avoid attention. As soon the   approached the station, he slipped towards the end of the platform and jumped in front of the train,” said a police officer.
 
 The victim was immediately rushed to a nearby hospital, where he was declared dead on arrival. Metro services were disturbed for a few minutes on the Yellow Line as a result of the incident.
 
 No suicide note was found from the deceased’s possession.
