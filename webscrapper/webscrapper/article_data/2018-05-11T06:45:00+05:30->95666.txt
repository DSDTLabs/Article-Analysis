Kolkata: Storage battery major   Industries has witnessed a 15% jump in net profit in the fourth quarter of 2017-18 to Rs 189 crore from Rs 164 crore in the corresponding quarter of the last financial year. 

 The net turnover for the quarter ended March 31 , 2018, was Rs 2,459 crore compared to net turnover of Rs 1,954 crore during the quarter ended March 31, 2017. For the financial year 2017-2018, the net turnover was Rs 9,186 crore against Rs 7,583 crore in the previous year, a growth of 21%. 
 
 Exide MD & CEO G Chatterjee said volumes in automotive, motorcycle, inverter, UPS, telecom and solar batteries as well as in other infrastructure segments were strong during the fourth quarter. In order to improve the bottomline, the company is now focussing on cost control and technology upgradation, he added.
