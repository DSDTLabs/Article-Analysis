Voicing support for their co-star who had to face an alleged attempt of assault on February 17, Mollywood actors are all standing united, both online and offline.

 Most recently, actor   wrote on his online page that it is great that despite the shocking experience, the actress did not stay quiet and bravely spoke up. He also adds that her warm smile always used to spread joy and it would have, even in the lives of her tormentors. "No court would forgive those who wiped off your beautiful smile," he wrote.
 
 
 
 Anoop also says that every girl would be thankful to her for being the brave-heart that she is. As per the latest reports, the police has issued a lookout notice for the convicts who are yet to be nabbed.
