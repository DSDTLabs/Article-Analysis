LUCKNOW:  , the Islamic month of fasting will commence from either Saturday or Sunday this year, depending on the sighting of the moon on Friday. This was stated by Maulana Saif Abbas, head of Shia   on Monday. 

 With Ramzan around the corner, the annual   number for the month run by   under Maulana   was also launched on Monday. To be functional from May 25, between 2pm and 4 pm, questions related to Ramzan by people across the globe will be received and answered through phone and social media platforms by clerics. 
 
 It is because of the difference in the lunar calendar and the Georgian solar calendar, the former being list of Islamic months, that Ramzan comes 10 to 12 days earlier each year. The first day of the month last year fell on June 6.
