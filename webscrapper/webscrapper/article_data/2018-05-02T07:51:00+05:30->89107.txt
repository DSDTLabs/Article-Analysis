 After walking a few steps, she met an acquaintance — her brother’s friend — who offered her a lift in his car. A PCR call was made and after reaching the bus stand, the duo was apprehended by police. 
 
 On the basis of woman’s complaint an FIR was registered under sections 354-D (stalking), 120-B (criminal conspiracy), 34 (common intention) of the IPC at Sector 14 police station and the accused were arrested. 
 
 During interrogation, the duo — Harishankar and Rashish Shrivastav — claimed that they were employees of a private detective agency and were hired by the woman’s husband to spy on her.
 
 “I got married to   on February 28, but within a few weeks trouble started cropping up between us. I left his house after two months and have not been in contact with him,” the complainant told cops. 
 
 “The duo was produced in a city court on Tuesday and sent to judicial custody. We are verifying their claim that they are employees of a detective agency. The woman’s husband will soon be called for questioning,” said ASI Devender Kumar, the investigating officer.
