Cinematographer – turned filmmaker Shamdat Sainudeen’s directorial debut ‘Street Lights’s’ first look poster had recently gone viral, and the film is one of the much-awaited projects of megastar  , this year.

 The teaser of the film, which is a bilingual and scripted by debutant Fawaz Muhammed, will be out today. The film also has ‘Maheshinte Prathikaram’ fame Lijomol Jose, Dharmajan Bolgatty, Joy Mathew, Neena Kurup, Hareesh Kanaran, Sohan Seenulal and Sudhi Koppa in major roles.
 
 ‘Street Lights’ has music by yet another debutant, Adarsh Abraham.
