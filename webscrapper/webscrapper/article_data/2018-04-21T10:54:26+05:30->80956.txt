NAGPUR: BJP corporator and former mayor   alleged that KSL Industries and Limited continued construction in Empress City despite status quo imposed by the high court. He alleged the   officials were aware of it but did not taken any action. Datke demanded an inquiry followed by action against NMC officials for nexus with KSL. “Unauthorized construction of over 2.5 lakh sqft was done in Empress City including thr mall. NMC issued notices to KSL between 2013 and 2015. HC granted stay on NMC’s action on March 17, 2017, but KSL continued construction,” he said. Mayor Nanda Jichkar directed the administration to conduct an inquiry followed by action.
