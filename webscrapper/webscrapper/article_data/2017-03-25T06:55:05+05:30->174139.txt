AHMEDABAD: ADG NCC, Major General  , met governor   on Friday and apprised him about the successful conduct of the Dwarkadheesh   sea sailing expedition.

 The expedition by Maj Gen Sharan, created a record by sailing 720km.
 
  The sea sailing expedition was flagged off by education minister   from Ghogha Beach near Bhavnagar on February 24, 2017. About 40 naval and NCC cadets, including 10 girl cadets, participated in the expedition.
 
 Wing Commander Abhishek Matiman said governor O P Kohli was highly appreciative of the efforts and congratulated the entire Gujarat NCC for successfully carrying out such a challenging expedition.
