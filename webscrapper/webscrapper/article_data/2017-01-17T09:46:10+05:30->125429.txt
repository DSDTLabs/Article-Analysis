CHENNAI: Police on Monday arrested 10, people including the district secretary of   for conducting jallikattu at a forest department land near Sholavaram in Tiruvallur.

 Police said hundreds of young men gathered at a place near Red Hills in which 15 bulls were brought in and were let loose in the morning.Workers belonging to the party and youth from nearby villages tried to tame the bulls. A team of police led by an inspector from Sholavaram station rushed to the spot after receiving information.However, people fled the venue. Police nabbed the party's office-bearer Elumalai and nine others who had organised the event despite the   ban. The party had organised jallikattu in Cuddalore district also defying the restriction.
