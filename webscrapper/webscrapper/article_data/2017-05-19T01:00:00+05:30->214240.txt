At the inauguration ceremony of the Wall of Heroes – a wall located in JNU's Convention Centre where 21 portraits of   awardees are exhibited – JNU VC M Jagadesh Kumar announced that they are looking forward to more such patriotic events in the future. Talking about the preparations for Kargil Vijay Diwas, the VC said, "We will celebrate the day with war veterans, ministers, officials and the families of martyrs. We have invited Akshay Kumar to attend the function. Besides,   will also join us for the celebrations."

  He added, "It goes without saying that Akshay will be given a tour of the Wall of Heroes. Kargil Vijay Diwas is one of the most important days for us and we will celebrate its 18th year to remember our heroes and will honour the families of the soldiers who lost their lives in the war."
