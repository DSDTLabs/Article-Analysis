KANPUR:   ( ) has extended date of   rebate till January 31 to enable house owners to avail 10% rebate. 

 Municipal commissioner   said house owners should deposit tax in time for KMC to provide them civic amenities and added that house owners will get 10 % rebate if they deposit taxes before January 31. He said tax payers should go to their respective zonal offices to deposit the amount. "If they fail to deposit house tax before March 31, it will be recovered with interest," he said. TNN
