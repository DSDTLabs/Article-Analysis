RAJKOT: The Rajkot railway division has set up an automatic sanitary napkin   for women travellers at the station. 

 The vending machine has been installed on platform number 1 near the entrance. The user has insert Rs 5 coin to get one napkin.
 
 “Considering the needs of women commuters,   has got this machine, which is perhaps the best gift for women travellers,” the railways said in a statement on Tuesday. The machine has can hold 100 sanitary pads. The machine has been sponsored by  . The facility was inaugurated by Albina Sagathiya, waiting room bearer and Prasannaba Jadeja, senior booking clerk in presence of Bharti Ninawe, president of the association.
