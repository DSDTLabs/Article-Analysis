There were two male passengers —Garib and Kismat Ali alias Poppu— already in the three-wheeler. Later, Mohammad Irfan drove her to a jungle opposite the fuel station where all the three committed the heinous act. An hour later, two men on a motorcycle spotted the woman crying on the roadside. She told them about the crime, following which they informed cops. The gang-rape survivor identified the accused auto rickshaw driver and the two others during the identification parade.
