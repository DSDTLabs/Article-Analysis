NOIDA: The residents of   2, Sector 78, have complained that broken roads in the area are leading to dust rising from all around and entering the flats. 

 The   had promised to build a park, but the project is still pending, and thus many animals like Nilgai or blue bull, and snakes have occupied the park area, they claimed. It has also become a breeding ground for mosquitoes and many slum dwellers are using the area for defecating and dumping garbage. 
 
  Apart from this, residents are also facing problem due to lack of public transport and connectivity to the sector. 
 
 Residents have submitted a petition to Omveer Singh, director, horticulture, for the development of the park. Singh assured the residents that the civic work which was stopped due to some issue, will resume soon.
