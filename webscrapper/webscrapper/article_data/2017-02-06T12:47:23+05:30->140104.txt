CHENNAI:   governor   on Monday accepted the resignation of chief minister   and his council of ministers.

 Hours after AIADMK MLAs unanimously elected party general secretary   as legislature party leader on Sunday, Panneerselvam tendered his resignation citing personal reasons.
 
 In a letter to Panneerselvam, the Governor said: "I hereby accept your resignation and the resignation of your council of ministers tendered vide your letter dated February 5. I request you and the present council of ministers to function until alternate arrangements are made."
 
 Governor Rao who was in Ooty on Sunday cut short his visit and left for New Delhi in the evening in the backdrop of political developments. He was to stay further to attend convocation at   in Coimbatore on Tuesday.
