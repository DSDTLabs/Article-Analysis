Panaji: Goa Carbon Ltd reported a net profit of Rs 22.5 crore for the third quarter which ended on December 31, 2017, against a loss of Rs 93.4 lakh reported for the corresponding period in the previous year. Comprehensive income for the Dempo Group company also grew to Rs 22.7 crore in the third quarter of 2017-18 against the Rs 1 cr loss posted at the quarter ending December 2016. “We are delighted to achieve 100% capacity utilisation during the quarterly period ended December 31, 2017. We attribute this growth to a growing demand for aluminum and steel in the country, which has also favoured our growth during this quarter,” chairman Goa Carbon Ltd Shrinivas Dempo said. The board of directors of Goa Carbon approved the unaudited results for the third quarter on January 6. Goa Carbon is the second largest manufacturer of calcined petroleum coke (CPC) in the country.
