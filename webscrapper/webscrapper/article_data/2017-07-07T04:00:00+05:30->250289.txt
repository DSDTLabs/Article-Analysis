Ahmedabad: The National Cadet Corps (NCC) organized an awarness programme on Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s ‘Digital India’ drive in Mehsana. 

 Colonel Sandeep Singh of the seventh Gujarat NCC battalion said that they had organized the programme to generate awareness among citizens, schools, college students and NCC cadets about variosu payment systems. 
 
 NCC, the largest voluntary organization, will also extend door-to-door campaign to make people aware about Digital India initiative. 
 
 The participants were taught downloading, installing and using various digital payment transactions, said Col Singh. 
 
 Cadets also gave a presentation on Digital India at the event held at Municipal Arts and Urban Bank Science College.
