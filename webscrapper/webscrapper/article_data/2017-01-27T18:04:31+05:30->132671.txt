NEW DELHI: Examination dates for three class X subjects and five class XII subjects to be conducted by the   has been revised. 

 The dates have been rescheduled either due to overlapping of exams with the language subjects or to give adequate gap for preparation between two exams.
 
 The class X exam for the subjects Tamil and Gurung have been rescheduled from March 10 and 23 to March 18 and March 10 respectively, while the NCC exam has been shifted to March 23 from the earlier date of March 15.
 
 The new dates for class XII theatre studies and Tangkhul is April 10, while physical education has been shifted from April 10 to April 12. Sociology will be conducted on April 20 now and foor service-II exams will be held on April 26.
