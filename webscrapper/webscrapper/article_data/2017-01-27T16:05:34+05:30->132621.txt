The Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) will fight Punjab assembly polls for first time. The party has fielded its candidates in 112 seats out of 117 assembly seats in Punjab. Its alliance partner Lok Insaf Party, led by Bains Brothers, is contesting on five of the 117 seats.  The Punjab assembly elections will be held in a single phase on February 04, 2017 and the results will be declared on March 11, 2017.  Prominent faces of AAP in Punjab includes - Bhagwant Mann, H.S.
