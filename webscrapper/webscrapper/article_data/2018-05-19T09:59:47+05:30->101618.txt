HYDERABAD:   Rail officials on Friday requested commuters using the extended train services for the IPL match on Saturday to avail trains from the NGRI station too apart from   and Stadium metro stations to ‘balance the crowd’.

 
 Services will be extended till 12:30am on Saturday from Uppal, Stadium and NGRI stations. It will be extended further if required, based on the demand, authorities tweeted on Friday.
 
 “We request @IPL commuters to avail our services from NGRI station too, since this will balance the crowd. When you commute for @IPL match return tickets will be available tomorrow only at Miyapur, Ameerpet, NGRI and stadium stations,” tweeted L&T Metro Rail Hyderabad Limited authorities on Friday.
