LUCKNOW: An FIR was lodged against UP cabinet minister   for model code of conduct violation after a truckload of saris were seized on   on Wednesday evening. The saris, purchased in the minister's name, were allegedly to be distributed as freebies to voters. Along with Gayatri, truck driver and cleaner have also been booked,   confirmed.
SP   said the truck was seized during routine checking. Police recovered more than 4,500 saris from it. The driver furnished a receipt in the name of Prajapati, police said. An FIR was registered under Sections 171B, 171F & 188 of the IPC.
