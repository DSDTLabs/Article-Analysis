NEW DELHI: A 22-year-old woman was allegedly attacked on the face with a   by an   man in west Delhi’s Uttam Nagar on Thursday.

 The woman told the police that the incident happened at 8am when she was going to a metro station to leave for Karol Bagh, also in west Delhi. 
 
 A man on a bike came, slashed her face with a sharp object that looked like a blade and fled. Police officers said that the 22-year-old woman did not sustain serious injuries because her face was covered with a muffler.
 
 Based on the woman’s statement, a case under Section 324 (causing grievous hurt) of the Indian Penal Code has been registered and investigation has been started into the incident.
 
 Police said they have taken the CCTV footage from the area to establish the exact sequence of the incident. They said that they are questioning the woman to know if she suspects anyone who could have attacked her. The family members of the woman are also being questioned to find out if they had any personal enmity.
 
 The woman’s parents had recently moved to Uttam Nagar from Karol Bagh and she had gone there to meet them. The accused man on the bike was reportedly wearing a helmet.
