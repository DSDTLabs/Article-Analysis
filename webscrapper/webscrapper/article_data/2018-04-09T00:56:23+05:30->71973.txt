Tuticorin: V O Chidambaranar Port Trust on Tuesday handled 14,800 metric tonnes of cattle feed from the vessel M V Meray Glyfada, bettering its former record of 10,748 metric tonnes.

 The port officials said that there was an increase in the quantity of cattle feed handled in the port. They said that they handled 1, 38, 969 metric tonnes of cattle feed in the last fiscal while the number is 23,953 metric tonnes from April 1 to 6. 
 
 The cattle feed is mostly imported from Ukraine and is used in poultry and cattle farms as feed or in the oil extraction Industry.
