 According to officials, they had received information about an   carrying liquor consignment, following which they kept a watch at Jetalsar crossroads on Saturday night.
 
 “Post midnight we spotted the SUV and tried to intercept it. However, seeing us the driver sped off. After giving it a chase, we intercepted the SUV near Jetalsar junction bus stop. Upon checking the vehicle, we found 585 bottles of Indian-made foreign liquor (IMFL) worth Rs 2,28,000. We also seized the SUV which is worth Rs 4 lakh and arrested the driver whose identity is yet to be ascertained,” said an official from Jetpur taluka police station.
