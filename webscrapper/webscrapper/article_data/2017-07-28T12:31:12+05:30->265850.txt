AHMEDABAD: In a notification issued by Ahmedabad city DEO,  , on Thursday, academic work in all primary, secondary and higher secondary schools in city limits will remain suspended for two more days on   due to warnings about heavy rains. For Ahmedabad district, holiday was declared for primary school students for Friday. School principals have been asked to remain in contact with the state administration.

 Mahesh Mehta, district DPEO, has announced primary schools will remain closed on July 28. 
 
 “Orders have been issued to keep the schools in ready condition if there is any need to shift people to school buildings during emergency evacuation,” he said.
 
 Gujarat University (GU) will also stay closed on Friday, said GU authorities.
