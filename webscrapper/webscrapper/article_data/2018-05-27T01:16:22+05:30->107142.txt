GURUGRAM: An executive of an IT company filed a complaint with police alleging that the HR head and a senior manager of the firm thrashed and abused her when she enquired about the change of her designation. 

 An FIR was filed at   police station.
 
 The 29-year-old woman, a resident of Delhi, stated she had joined the IT company, located in Udyog Vihar area five years ago. Until last year, she was employed as personal assistant to a senior official who left the company in the middle of 2017. Soon after that, her designation was changed to the receptionist. When she expressed her disagreement about this move to the senior authorities of the company the accused HR country head and the senior manager started to harass her, she claimed.
 
 As per the complaint an FIR has been registered against duo under sections 323 (causing hurt), 509 (word, gesture act intended to insult the modesty of a woman) of IPC at Udyog Vihar police station. “The complainant is still working in the company. 
 
 An FIR has been registered and we are investigating the matter. The accused will be soon called to join the investigation. As per the findings action will be taken as per the law,” said inspector  , SHO of Udyog Vihar police station.
