Ludhiana: In a setback to businessmen who were seeking deferment of   system the government has refused to extend the date of the bill application and it will be rolled out from February 1in Punjab. A notification in this regard is likely to be issued in a day or two by the government and the draft has already been finalised. 

 Confirming the development joint director of investigation, HPS Ghotra said, “E-way bill system will be rolled out from February 1in Punjab like elsewhere in the country. On the same day, E-way bill will be applicable on inter and as well as intra state billing of above Rs 50,000. “E-way is a very easy to follow system and it will not only facilitate faster movement of goods but will also save the businessmen from a lot of paperwork and hassles. As per the rules it is only applicable over the single invoice of more than Rs. 50,000.”
 
 Ghotra also presided over a meeting of the city’s transporters and businessmen to apprise them of the rules and regulations of the E-Way billing system. The session which was conducted at Transport Nagar turned out to be successful as more than 50 participants took part in it and took expert advice on their queries regarding E-Way bill.
