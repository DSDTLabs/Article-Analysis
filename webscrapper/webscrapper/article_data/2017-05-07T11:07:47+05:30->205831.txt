 The other home occupants had reported to the authorities about ragging at the complex by the three boys. Following this, the home authorities formed an inquiry committee to probe the matter. 
 
 Machindra Sutar, one of the probe officers, filed a complaint with the police on Friday.
 
 Based on the complaints raised by the inmates, the inquiry committee conducted a confidential investigation of the matter. It was revealed that the boys in question had sexually abused other inmates between January and May.
 
 The committee report stated that the three boys are suspected to have sexually abused four inmates.
 
 The three suspected minors, who have been staying at the facility for over a year now, were detained on Friday.
