Surat: Surat Municipal Corporation (SMC) has started severing water connections of residential societies that have failed to set up on site organic waste converters (OWC) in their premises. 

 It severed the water connection of Water Hills Residency, a housing society in 20,000 square metre plot at Athwa zone, on Tuesday and issued notice to Vastugram Residency, Vesu, asking it to install on site OWC within seven days.
 
  There are 30 housing societies in Vesu and 28 of them have already installed OWCs at their sites.
