It's in the 2015 film 'Urumbukal Urangarilla' that we saw actress Ananya last in M-Town.

 The actress has been taking up projects in other industries as well and her latest release is the Telugu movie 'A.Aa', which also had Anupama Parameswaran in a leading role. Recently, the team of the upcoming Malayalam film ' ' released a poster revealing the name of Ananya's character in the movie.
