‘Tu Cheez Badi Hai Mast Mast’ the cult song of the 90’s has been remade in the movie ‘Machine’ with ‘Mustafa Burmawalla and   reprising the roles of   and   respectively. The foot-tapping number that gets the crowd grooving instantly is till date the favourite of many from that generation. You surely don’t want to miss out on few facts about the original song. Check them out here:

  
  
 
 1. Raveena Tandon was in the middle of two film shoots while working on ‘Mohra’. She was so tired and exhausted during the shoot of ‘Tu Cheez Badi Hai Mast Mast’ that she collapsed on the sets. 
 
 2. The shooting of ‘Tu Cheez Badi Hai Mast Mast’ had to be stopped for two hours because of Raveena’s ill-health. 
 
 3. The song was picturised keeping actress Divya Bharti in mind. But post her tragic death, Raveena Tandon came on board. 
 
 4. Akshay and Raveena were reportedly dating while shooting ‘Tu Cheez Badi Hai Mast Mast’. They worked together on ‘Mohra’(1994) and in several other films during their three year long relationship. 
 
 5. Raveena Tandon became widely known as the ‘Mast Mast’ girl post the crazy success of the song. However, Akshay Kumar maintains till date that his wife Twinkle Khanna is his ‘Mast Mast’ girl.
