Mohali: A Punjab Khadi Board employee died after his head was crushed under the wheels of a tractor in Naya Gaon in Mohali on Thursday morning. The deceased has been identified as Mohan Singh, a resident of Nayan Gaon.  According to a police officer, Mohan was hit by the tractor when he was about to start his scooter outside a shop in Naya Gaon. The officer added Mohan was wearing a helmet at the time of the accident. Police have arrested the tractor driver and a case has been registered under Section 304-A of the IPC in Nayan Gaon police station. Investigation officer Baljinder Singh said, “Mohan was rushed to the government hospital but was declared brought dead. We have arrested the tractor driver and registered a case.”
