Tuticorin: Lighting strike left a woman dead and two others including a woman badly injured at Cholavaram village near Eppothum Vendran in Tuticorin district on Thursday afternoon. 

 Eppothum Vendran police, who are investigating the case, said that the trio was grazing cattle in the open area about a kilometre away from the village when the accident took place around 1.30 pm.
 
 The victim, M Arunachalam, 65, wife of Mariappan of Vadakutheru in Cholapuram, succumbed to injuries suffered in her stomach and leg on the spot.
 
  Two others from the same village, V Saraswathy, 55, and K Mookaiah, 53, who suffered injuries, fainted and were rushed to the Thoothukudi Government Medical College and Hospital. 
 
 The two were unconscious and undergoing intensive treatment. 
 
 The body of Arunachalam was brought to the same hospital for post-mortem.
