Panaji: Highlighting the recent Varanasi flyover tragedy which claimed the lives of 18 people, Congress leader Shantaram Naik has demanded a structural audit of the new bridges being constructed. In a letter to Union minister for shipping, highways and roads Nitin Gadkari, he highlighted the need for appropriate audits of the two bridges over the Mandovi and Zuari rivers, as well as other bridges being constructed.tnn
