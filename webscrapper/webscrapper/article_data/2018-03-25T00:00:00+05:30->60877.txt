A Holi party, titled Rangarez, by Manya club, turned out to be a fun-filled affair. The ladies arrived wearing mainly white, which was the colour code of the evening. Adding colour to the do were the flowers that were amply used in the décor.
The games played on the occasion, highlighted the spirit of ‘Bura na mano Holi hai!’. Stickers with interesting titles, denoting various film songs, were distributed to all the members, who in turn had to give to the person who they thought most-suited the title. Those earning the maximum number of titles were declared winners. Neelu Mishra and Pooja   were the winners of the game. 
 The event concluded with a phoolon wali Holi.
