RAJKOT: The   of   ( ) continued its drive against   defaulters and sealed 34 properties on Saturday. 

 According to RMC officials, they sealed 34 properties in three zones of Rajkot after as its owners had failed to pay tax dues to the civic body. It also recovered Rs 36 lakh as property tax on Saturday. 
 
 RMC sealed properties in Chunarvada chowk, Bhavnagar Road, Lakhajiraj Udhyognagar, Shakti Industrial zone, Raghuvir Industrial area and Sainath Industrial area among others in city. RMC has so far collected over Rs 215 crore as property tax for the current financial year.
