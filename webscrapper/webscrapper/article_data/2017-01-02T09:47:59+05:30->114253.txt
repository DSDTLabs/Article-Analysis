MUMBAI: Pune-based two-wheeler maker   has registered a 22 per cent decline in sales in the month of December, 2016. The company said in a statement that it sold 2,25,529 vehicles as against 2,89,003 in the corresponding period a year ago.

 Motorcycle sales too registered a dip with Bajaj Auto selling 2,03,312 bikes in comparison to 2,47,782 in December, 2015.
 
 The company also saw a decline in the number of commercial vehicle sales by nearly half as against the numbers in 2015.
