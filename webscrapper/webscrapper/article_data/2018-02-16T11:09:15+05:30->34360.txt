NEW DELHI: The   (VSU), Nellore, has announced the Under Graduate (UG) BA, BSc, BBA, BBM, BCom, BCA first year, second year, and third year degree Result 2018 today on the official website of the Board - simhapuriuniv.ac.in.

 The university has also released revaluation result for UG (CBCS) II and IV Semester Examination conducted in April 2017. 
 
 All the students who have appeared for Under Graduate (UG) BA, BSc, BBA, BBM, BCom, BCA first year, second year, and third year degree Examination in the year 2017 can check their result online on the official website - simhapuriuniv.ac.in.
 
 The Under Graduate (UG) BA, BSc, BBA, BBM, BCom, BCA first year, second year, and third year degree exam was conducted in the month of December 2017. 
 
 Students who have appeared for the examination and wish to check their Under Graduate (UG) BA, BSc, BBA, BBM, BCom, BCA first year, second year, and third year degree Result 2018 can follow the steps given below:
 
 Step-by-step guide to check Under Graduate (UG) BA, BSc, BBA, BBM, BCom, BCA first year, second year, and third year degree Result 2018 online through official portal
 
 1) Open the official website - simhapuriuniv.ac.in
 
 2) On the homepage of the website, you will find the Result link. Click on it
 
 3) Once you click on the link, it will redirect you to a new webpage where you will have to enter your roll number
 
 4) Your result will be displayed onscreen. 
 
 5) You can download and print your result for further reference.
