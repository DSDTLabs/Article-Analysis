NEW DELHI: A man is missing since Saturday night after a speeding car hit his Harley Davidson bike on DND Flyway and he fell in   river. 

 Police said that search operation is still underway for the missing bike rider whose bike was hit by a Toyota Etios car. 
 
 Eyewitnesses told police that the speed of the car was so fast that the errant driver could not stop his vehicle even after hitting the motorcyclist and continued to drive while the biker stuck to its bonnet.
 
 As the man braked, the biker fell off the bridge into the river.
