NEW DELHI: Two newly elected members of the   today took oath.

 Vemireddy Prabhakar Reddy, who was elected on a   ticket from Andhra Pradesh, took oath in English in the name of god.
 
 Congress leader Syed Nasir Hussain, who was elected to the Upper House from Karnataka, took oath in the name of 'Allah'.
 
 Chairman M Venkaiah Naidu welcomed the newly elected members to the House.
 
 Of the 58 newly elected or re-elected members, 53 had taken oath over the last two days. Three members including Finance Minister   are yet to take oath.
