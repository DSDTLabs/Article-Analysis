NEW DELHI: Crude oil prices climbed Rs 61 to Rs 4,033 per barrel in futures trade today as participants raised bets on positive global cues.
 At the Multi Commodity Exchange, crude for delivery in March was trading higher by Rs 61, or 1.54 per cent, at Rs 4,033 per barrel in a business turnover of 3,968 lots. Likewise, the oil for delivery in April shot up by Rs 60, or 1.51 per cent, to Rs 4,029 per barrel in 128 lots.
  Analysts said the rise in crude oil futures was largely in sync with a firm trend overseas where oil prices extended gains, but the increases were limited as the dollar regained some ground after touching its lowest level in more than three years on Friday.
