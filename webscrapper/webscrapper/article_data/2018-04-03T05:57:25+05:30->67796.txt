 Air-conditioning in operating theatres is not just for comfort. It helps to filter air and maintain temperature as well as a certain number of air exchanges per hour, besides humidity. The controlled environment removes odour and anaesthetic gases, and reduces the risk of infection to patientsby controlling airborne microorganisms in the room, say senior microbiologists.
