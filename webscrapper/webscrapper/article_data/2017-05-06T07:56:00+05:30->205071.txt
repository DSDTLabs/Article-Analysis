GURUGRAM: Two   robberies were reported on Thursday night, within four hours of each other, from Dwarka   and  . Sources said police have recovered both cars, including an   cab, and launched raids to arrest the accused soon.

 In the first incident, Ola driver Mohammad Aadil of Loni, Ghaziabad, picked up three people who had booked a ride from Bajghera village in his Hyundai Eon. As they reached   expressway around 10.30pm, the ‘commuters’ held him at gunpoint and fled with his car and cellphone. 
 
 An FIR was lodged against unidentified accused under sections 379B (robbery with attempt to cause death or grievous hurt) of IPC, and 25-54-59 of Arms Act.
 
 In the second incident, a cab driver reached Rajiv Nagar around 2am to pick up staff of a BPO, when he was overpowered and thrashed by eight unidentified men, who fled with the Innova he was driving, which belonged cab service owner Karambeer of Sector 3 in Rewari, who had lost another Innova cab in an identical robbery that took place at the same time and place on April 25. 
 
 Based on the complaint Karmbir lodged at Civil Lines PS, an FIR was registered against unidentified accused under Section 395 (dacioty) of IPC.
