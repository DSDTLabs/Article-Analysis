FEROZEPUR:   arrested one   after a   in   district on Tuesday. According to information, one of the accomplices of the arrested accused managed to flee from the spot.

 After receiving a tip-off about some suspected gangsters hiding in Kohala, police surrounded the village. When they started searching every house, the suspected gangsters opened fire at the police party led by Ferozepur superintendent of police (detective) Ajmer Singh Baath. Police succeeded in arresting one of the accused and seized a weapon from his possession. 
 
 Ferozepur senior superintend of police (SSP) Gaurav Garg said the arrested person had been identified as Gurdip Singh alias Gopi, a resident of Kohala, while his accomplice identified as Ashok Kumar alias Aman Seth had managed to escape.
