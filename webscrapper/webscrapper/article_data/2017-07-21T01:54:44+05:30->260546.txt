NEW DELHI: Fresh from the tussle between teachers of the morning and evening section,   on Thursday had another set of worry when several students came out to protest against the  . 

 The students, in support of the morning college teachers raised slogans and tried to disrupt the General Body (GB) meeting of the college. They claimed that with the bifurcation, there would be no space left for classes in the college. The protesting students also clashed with the students of the evening college. However, despite the chaos, the GB meeting took place. 
 
 Speaking to TOI, morning college teacher   said that the GB has decided to postpone the morning classes for the evening college after the protests. “We cannot have morning classes for the evening college until the University’s Executive Council decides on the infrastructure and the possible path of this bifurcation mess,” he said.
