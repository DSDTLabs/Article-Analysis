GAYA: Altogether 126 persons were arrested and 71 motorised vehicles seized in   district during ULB election on Sunday.

 Heavy polling was recorded in Bodh Gaya   where 72% of the electors exercised their franchise. Several robed monks joined the laity in queues formed for the purpose of casting votes.
 
 Voting was held up for some time in ward number 46 following the mysterious disappearance of a relative of Rinki Kumari, one of the contestants from the   area. Supporters of the candidate boycotted poll for sometime in the morning. The police staged flag march and the polling resumed after the intervention of the district officials.
