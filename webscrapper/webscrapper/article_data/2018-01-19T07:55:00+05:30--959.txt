BATHINDA: Badhni Kalan police of   district has booked Buttar Kalan village   Jaswinder Singh following allegations of harbouring gangsters at his place. According to police, the case was been registered following a tip off that   used to shelter gangsters, provide them logistical support and keep their illegal weapons with him.

 Police are still to arrest Jaswinder. 
 
 Badhni Kalan station house officer (SHO)   said, “We got secret information on Wednesday evening that Buttar Kalan sarpanch   was harbouring gangsters and keeping their illegal weapons. Acting on the information, a case under sections 212 (harbouring an offender), 216 (harbouring an offender who has escaped from custody or whose apprehension has been ordered), 216A (harbouring robbers or dacoits) of the IPC and the Arms Act has been registered against him.”
