SURAT: Police arrested a gang of three thieves on Tuesday allegedly involved in series of thefts in shops in  . The accused were nabbed from Kamrej when they came to receive payment for sale of stolen mobile phones.

 Kamlesh alais Kamo Rathwa, 26, Narvin alais Naresh Rathwa, 30, and Sagar Vasava, 19, were arrested for allegedly carrying out thefts at shops in Olpad, Sayan, Kim, Kamrej, Umarpada, Mangrol and Karanj village of Surat. Police recovered 14 mobile phones and accessories worth Rs 16,200 from the accused.
