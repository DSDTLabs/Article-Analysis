 The film had opened at an estimated Rs 12 crore and collected Rs 14.25 crore on its second day. The romantic comedy went on to earn Rs. 15.75 crore on its third day. The Shashank Khaitan-directed movie enjoyed the benefit of a holiday on Monday on account of Holi. 
  
 Made under Dharma Productions, ‘Badrinath Ki Dulhania’ is a spin-off of the 2014 film ‘Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania’. This is the third time that Alia and   are working together after ‘Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania’ and ‘Student Of The Year’. Their effortless chemistry has often been appreciated by the audience and this outing was no exception.
 
 
  
 ‘Badrinath Ki Duhania’ sees Varun playing a small-town boy Badri who falls in love with Alia’s character Vaidehi. While he hopes to tie the knot with Vaidehi, the feisty girl has dreams and aspirations of her own – marriage does not figure in her list. Based in Kota and Jhansi, the movie deals with the evils of a regressive society in the north Indian hinterlands, and touches upon the subjects of dowry and patriarchy.
