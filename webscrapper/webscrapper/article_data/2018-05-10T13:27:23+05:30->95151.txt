TRIVANDRUM: Kerala Education Minister C Raveendranath announced the Kerala State Board results for Plus Two (Class XII) today, May 10, 2018. The overall pass percentage for the Kerala Plus Two 2018 examination is 83.75%.

 The result is now available at the official website of the Directorate of Higher Secondary Education Kerala (DHSE) - https://dhsekerala.gov.in
 
 Raveendranath announced the results in a press meeting held at the Pareeksha Bhavan in the PRD Office (Public relations department office), Trivandrum.
 
 The exams have been conducted between March 7 to 27, 2018. 
 
 
 
  
  
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 In 2014, a total of 3,92,456 students have appeared for the result and 74.78% of the students have passed among them.
 
 In 2015, 83.85% of the 4,65,567 students have cleared the examination compared to a pass percentage of 80.69% among 4,70,884 students in 2017.
