JAMSHEDPUR: At least eight persons died and 20 others were injured after a   broke out at a   operating from a building in   in   area on Sunday evening. Police said the fire erupted after an explosion in the cracker factory that was running illegally. 

 "The injured have been shifted to the community health centre for medical attention," SP (rural) Prabhat Kumar said. Two fire tenders were rushed to the spot.
 
 Police officials said the toll might rise. "The owner of the house, Durgapado Santra has fled after the mishap," said a police official.
 
 Local residents said minor incidents of explosions had taken place at the building earlier, but they did not draw the attention of police.
 
  
  
 
 "The area is close to Bengal border and several cracker factories run illegally in the region. The authorities do not take note of these units even as these pose a danger to the workers as well as the people living nearby. Most of the workers at these illegal firecracker factories hail from Bengal," said a local resident.
