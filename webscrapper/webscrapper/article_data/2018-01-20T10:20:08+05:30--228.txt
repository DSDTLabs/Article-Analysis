PUNE: A 26-year-old man was shot dead on Friday morning by two motorcycle-borne persons at   near Shikrapur. 

 The victim,  , from Nanded worked as a delivery boy with catering contractor   from Sanaswadi. “Dasarwad was shot dead around 11am when he was on his way back after delivering ‘chapatis’ to a canteen of a private company in Sanaswadi,” said inspector   of the Shikrapur police. He said the reason behind Dasarwad’s murder was yet to be ascertained. 
 
 For the past few years, Dasarwad was working with Darekar. He used to deliver food items, mainly ‘chapatis’, to canteens of various companies in the area.
