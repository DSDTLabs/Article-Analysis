Jaipur: The Indian Naval Symphonic Orchestra Band will perform live at Central Park at 6 pm on Wednesday and Thursday. The band will enthral the audience with a wide range of repertoire of stirring martial music, arrangements of Western classical, Indian classical and other forms of contemporary popular and folk music. It will play varied choices like fanfare, overture, fusion, xylophone, pop, swing and patriotic music. The entry for the event is on first come and first serve basis. The band had recently performed at military band festivals like Royal Edinburgh Military Tattoo, Scotland and Spasskaya Tower International Military   Festival at Moscow. The musical evenings are being organised by the Department of Tourism in association with the Indian Naval Army.
