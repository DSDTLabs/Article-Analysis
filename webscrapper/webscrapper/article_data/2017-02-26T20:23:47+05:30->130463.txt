The much anticipated next film in the 'Star Wars' saga finally has a name, and it is going to be called 'Star Wars: The Last Jedi'. The film will start off from where 'The Force Awakens' ended. Unlike Episode VII, which had its first trailer debut over a year before release, no trailer for "The Last Jedi" has surfaced yet.
