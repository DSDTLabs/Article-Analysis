ALLAHABAD: A man allegedly killed his wife and two minor daughters in village   under Karvi police station in wee hours of Friday. He later went to the police outpost and confessed his crime.

 The police have arrested the accused identified as Ajeet Raidas of Chakla Guru Baba village. The deceased have been identified as   (32) and her two daughters   (8) and Pinki (6). SP   Pratap Gopendra told TOI, “Ajeet killed his wife on suspicion of her illicit relationship with a local grocery shop owner”. Ajeet runs a small vendor shop in the village, he added.
