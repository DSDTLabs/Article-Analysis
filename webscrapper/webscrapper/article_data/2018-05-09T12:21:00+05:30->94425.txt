NEW DELHI: The PSEB Board has released the Punjab Class 10 results on the official website of the PSEB Board - pseb.ac.in.

 The PSEB Board had successfully conducted the Secondary Examination (Class 10) and Higher Secondary Examination (HSE) for the academic session year 2017-18 in the month of Feb-March 2018. 
 
 As per the latest information, the Board has announced the PSEB Board Class 10 Results 2018 on its official website - pseb.ac.in. The results is also available at - indiaresults.com - website.
 
 The students who wish to check their PSEB Board Class 10 results can go to the official portal of the board at pseb.ac.in. 
 
 If you are one of the students eagerly waiting to check your PSEB Board Class 10 Result 2018, you can check it on the official website of the PSEB Board - pseb.ac.in. 
 
 Here's how to get your PSEB Board Class 10 Result 2018:
 
 1) Keep handy your PSEB Board Class 10 admit card or your Roll No.
 
 2) Open the official website of the PSEB Board - pseb.ac.in
 
 3) On the homepage of the Board, you will see a link - "PSEB Board Class 10 results", click on it
 
 4) It will redirect you to a new page where you need to enter your PSEB Board Class 10 roll no. and other required details
 
 5) Once you click submit, your PSEB Board Class 10 result will be visible on the screen
 
 6) You can download your PSEB Board Class 10 result and print it for further reference.
