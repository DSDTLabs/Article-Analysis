HYDERABAD: After spiritual leader and founder of Art of Living Ravi Shankar made a controversial remark regarding Babri Masjid-Ram Janmabhoomi dispute, a case was registered by   police on Thursday based on a complaint given by Darsagah-e-jehad-eshahadat (DJS).

 Moghapura police said DJS secretary  , alleged that Ravi Shankar made a ‘provocative statement’ against Muslim community with an intention to hurt feelings. “A case was registered under 295A (Deliberate and malicious acts, intended to outrage religious feelings of any class by insulting its religion or religious beliefs) of IPC,’’ R Devender, Moghalpura Inspector told TOI.
 
 At   police station, another complaint was lodged against Ravi Shankar on the same issue by one Syed  , but police clarified that no case was registered as they are seeking legal opinion.
