RAJKOT: Rajkot Municipal Corporation (RMC) destroyed 445 kg mangoes ripened by using carbide and chemical substances on Friday. The   teams also seized 53 kg carbide besides destroyed 242 kg chikoos kept in unhygienic condition and unfit for human consumption.
