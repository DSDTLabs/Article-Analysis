 one-act play titled 'Wagh Yenaar Aahe' received a huge round of applause from each and everyone present at   Mulinchi Shala ,where it was staged. Delivering superlative  , young actors Ashutosh Belekar and   Kale took audience on an entertaining   ride. A wonderful script and brilliant execution were appreciated too. We saw audiences congratulating the artists personally post the show. Apart from young aspiring actors, the play had many senior artists in attendance.
