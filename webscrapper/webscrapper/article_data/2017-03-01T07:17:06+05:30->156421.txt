New Delhi: The National Human Rights Commission has issued a notice to Delhi Police commissioner Amulya Patnaik over allegations of police high-handedness while dealing with protestors outside   College in Delhi University last week.

 The commission said it had acted on a complaint that police personnel attacked a female student and snatched cameras from media persons who were recording the incident on February 22.
 
 In a statement, NHRC said, “Allegedly, some journalists covering clashes were slapped and kicked by the policemen.” The commission has taken on record complaints as well as media reports making similar allegations including threats to some students on social media. It has given the commissioner four weeks to submit a detailed report in the matter.
