Pune: A website and a book on the political journey of former President   will be launched at Balgandharva auditorium on Saturday.

 Former President Pranab Mukherjee will launch the website and the book at a function, which will have NCP president Sharad Pawar as the chief guest. Chief minister Devendra Fadnavis, former home minister Sushilkumar Shinde and ex-CM Prithviraj Chavan will also be present.
 
 At the age of 27, Patil was elected to the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly from Jalgaon constituency in 1962. Thereafter, she won in the Muktainagar constituency for four consecutive times from 1967 to 1987. 
 
 She was also elected as a Member of Parliament from Amravati constituency in 1991. On July 25, 2007, Patil was appointed as the 12th President of India. She served as the President till July 25, 2012.
