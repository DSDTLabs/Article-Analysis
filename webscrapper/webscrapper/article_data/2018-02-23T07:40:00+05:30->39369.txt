Ludhiana: A Class X student of a private school in Machhiwara attempted suicide by jumping off the first floor of the school building on Thursday afternoon. The girl was rushed to hospital, where her condition is stated to be stable. According to information, the girl was upset following some family dispute. On Thursday, her father accompanied her to school for the exam. While the father sat on the ground floor, the girl appeared for the exam on the first floor. After the exam, the girl allegedly jumped off the building from the first floor. The Machhiwara police are awaiting the statement of the girl, who is undergoing treatment in a private hospital of Ludhiana.
