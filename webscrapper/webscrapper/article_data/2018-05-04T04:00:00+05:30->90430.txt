Rajkot: A trader in the city on Wednesday night lodged a complaint against another trader of cheating him of Rs 1 crore. 

 According to details of the case, Vijay Vaishnav (40), a resident of Vrindavan Society on Pedak Road, had given silver jewellery weighing 237 kg worth Rs 1 crore to Kishor Ramani, who had promised to give him silver plates in return, in June 2017. However, Ramani reneged on his promise. 
 
 “Whenever Vaishnav demanded his jewellery back, Ramani would threaten him that he would commit suicide and frame him in abetment to suicide case,” B division police station sub-inspector K K Parmar of Rajkot police said.
