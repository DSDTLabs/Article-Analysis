NEW DELHI: The   on Monday postponed its English B Third Year exam after the question paper was leaked. 

 
 The English paper of the third year Bachelor of Arts (BA) and Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) is to be held on April 2, 2018. 
 
 The Jammu University has expressed regret for the inconvenience caused to the students and have assured of thorough investigation and strict action against the culprits involved in the case. A probe has been ordered in the English B paper leaked case. 
 
 As per the reports, the new date for the exam will be notified after conducting the last paper of the third year students.
