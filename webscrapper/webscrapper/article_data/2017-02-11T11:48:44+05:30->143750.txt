 A division bench comprising justices Bhushan Gavai and Indira Jain asked the government why deans were required to seek approval from Mantralaya every time for purchase of vital equipment needed for these hospitals.
 
 The court’s orders came after amicus curiae submitted an affidavit stating that GMC and IGGMC failed to utilize Rs 3 crore grant while SSH had Rs 5 crore in its kitty which were not spent. 
 
 The judges pointed out that on October 29, 2015, HC passed an order that deans wouldn’t be required to wait for administrative approval for spending funds and if they failed to do so, they would be held liable for contempt. However, the government recently came out with a GR prohibiting all deans from spending funds of over Rs 50,000 without its approval.
