NEW DELHI: Over 50 families were rendered homeless after a   at a slum in  's   Sector 9 on Sunday. The occupants of the shanties were all labourers and said that they have lost all their belongings and cash in the fire. 

  Fire officials said that 10 fire tenders were rushed to the spot and the fire was doused within an hour. No casualties were reported. A short circuit is said to have started the fire. Before the fire tenders could reach the spot, locals from the area started filling buckets of water and poured them on the houses to douse it. 
 
  According to cops, all the residents of the shanties were evacuated in time and no casualties were reported. The fire was brought under control within an hour.
