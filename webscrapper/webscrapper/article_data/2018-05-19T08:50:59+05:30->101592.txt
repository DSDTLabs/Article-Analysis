KOCHI:   police arrested two people and seized one-and-a-half kg ganja from them on Friday. 

 The arrested have been identified as Razak (33) of   and Salam (45) of Erumeli in Kunnathunadu.
 
 The duo were arrested following a tip-off that they were bringing ganja from  . They were arrested by a team headed by   G Venu. The team was constituted under the direction of   rural superintendent   to curb ganja peddling.
 
 The accused abandoned their vehicle and tried to run away when they saw the police who were inspecting vehicles. Police chased them down and managed to seize the ganja. The police officers said the accused used to bring ganja from Tamil Nadu to sell it in small packets of Rs 500.
