Writer-director Saurabh Varma’s ('Mickey Virus', '7 Days To Go') short,   is a psychological thriller about a beautiful girl   ( ) who wakes up in her outhouse with a faded memory and realises she is trapped. Adding to her woes is a mysterious woman Gauri (Kyra Dutt), trying to convince her that she’s her close friend and her husband Arjun is no good. As Radhika begins to regain her memory, we notice a dead body in the house. Whose body is that? Where’s Arjun? What’s Gauri’s motive?
