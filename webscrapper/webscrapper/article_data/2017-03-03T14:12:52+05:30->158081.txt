PANAJI: A   opposite   in the city was gutted by fire early Wednesday morning. The cause of the fire is not known yet. The shop also sold spare parts and electrical items. 

 Fire service officials said that they received a call at about 4.54 early morning about the fire at Mohan Cycle shop and within a few minutes, a fire tanker truck was dispatched.
 
 The fire service personnel said that it took the fire fighters about an hour to bring the fire under control. They managed to save property worth Rs3 lakh and also stopped the fire from spreading to nearby commercial establishments.
