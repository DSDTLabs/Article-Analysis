New Delhi: Even as the Delhi assembly debated the tampering of EVMs on Tuesday, Delhi   leader and Union sports minister   Goel was conducting “snap” polls on Twitter with the hashtag #Kejriwalmustresign. 

 Goel, who claimed that a majority of respondents to his   agreed with his hashtag, conducted another poll even as he spent the day tweeting about the current political crisis in Delhi.
 
 Minutes after the first poll, which asked respondents to vote on whether Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal should resign till he got a “clean chit”,   went on to post another poll, this time asking if Delhi had suffered due to the internal bickering within the AAP.
