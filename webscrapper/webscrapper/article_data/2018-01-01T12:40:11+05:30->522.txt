NEW DELHI: Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) has declared the 2017 IBPS Clerk Prelims examination   on their official website - ibps.in. 

 The IBPS Clerk Prelims 2017 results is now available on the official website ibps.in. The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection had conducted the preliminary examination for clerical post between December 2-10, 2017. 
 
 The result status for the examination can be check from the link available on the official website of the IBPS. 
 
 Through this common recruitment process of 2017, a total of 7,833 clerks will be appointed. The candidates who clear the preliminary test will become eligible for the main examination which is scheduled to take place on January 21, 2018. 
 
 In case you have appeared for this examination, make sure you have your registration number handy to access your result from the official website of the IBPS. 
  
 
 
 
 1) Open the official portal - http://www.ibps.in/
  
 2) On the homepage, there will be ticker running on top of the page for the Prelims results
 
 3) Click on it or you can directly click here - http://ibps.sifyitest.com/crpclk7sep17/resstdck7_dec17/login.php?appid=d1f39c6ebefbbb879ecb2cc6c53f47cc
 
 4) Now enter your log in credentials such as registration or roll No. and password
 
 5) Now click on view result to check your result
 
 6) You can download and save the result or take a print out of the same
