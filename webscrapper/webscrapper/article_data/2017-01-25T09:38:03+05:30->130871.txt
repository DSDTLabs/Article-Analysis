MUMBAI: A   was assaulted with a knife in his clinic by an unemployed man who walked in posing as a patient to rob him last week.

 Police said when Dharmendra Thakkar (56) was at his clinic on Cabin Road, Bhayander (east), the accused, Lalbahadur Yadav (42), entered around 9 pm, and narrated his health issues. 
 
 When Thakkar asked him to lie on the examination table, Yadav pulled out a knife and threatened him. As Thakkar raised an alarm, Yadav attacked him, causing injuries on left hand, and tried to flee. He was caught by locals and handed over to police.
