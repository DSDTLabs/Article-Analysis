AHMEDABAD: As westerly winds brought moisture from   over the state, mercury across the state dipped below 40°C mark on Thursday. Kandla airport was the hottest place on Thursday where the maximum temperature was at 39.6°C. 

 Ahmedabad city too got relief due to partly cloudy weather. The maximum temperature in the city remained 38.6°C, 0.6 degree above normal and the minimum temperature was recorded at 23.7°C, 1.7 degrees above normal on Thursday. 
 
 Humidity level ranged between 52% and 23% in the city on Thursday.
 
 According to   (IMD), city may record 39°C temperature on Friday and the sky is likely to be partly cloudy.
