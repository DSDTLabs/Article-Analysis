RAIPUR: A government school teacher allegedly tonsured the heads of eight students of Class 7 and 8 as a punishment for "indiscipline", in  , about 250km from here. The students refused to go to school and missed their exam the next day, following which parents demanded action against the teacher. 

 The teacher said that the students had scribbled on the school wall and deserved a punishment. He allegedly said the students were mischievous and had done similar things before. The education department is yet to act on the case.
 
 Parents alleged that the police refused to register a case and counselled them to compromise with the teacher.
