SRINAGAR: The J&K Police on Wednesday arrested five   (OGW) from   district who reportedly provided logistical support to Lashkar terrorists who attacked a bus carrying Amarnath pilgrims in Anantnag on July 11, killing seven and injuring 19 others.

 Sources said the arrested OGWs include two students who had provided motorbikes to the terrorists who carried out the attack on the pilgrims.The police had earlier arrested a policeman, serving as PDP MLA  's driver, in connection with the terror attack.
