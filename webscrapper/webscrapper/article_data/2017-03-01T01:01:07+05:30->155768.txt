Hyderabad: Among those who thronged the Kuchibhotla residence and the crematorium on Tuesday was a large gathering of politicians who raised slogans against the US government and demanded justice for the family. Spotted with the family was Union minister Bandaru Dattatreya who said that the Centre will take up the issue with the US government. “The external affairs minister Sushma Swaraj will take up the issue during bilateral talks with the US government. The US and the Indian embassy will collaboratively work to regain the trust of people about their safety in the US,” he said. tnn
