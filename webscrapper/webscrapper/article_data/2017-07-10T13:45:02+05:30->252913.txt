NEW DELHI: The National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) will declare the result of the Common Entrance Examination (CEE-2017) on July 10 on their official website. 

 Students can follow these steps to check their results:
 
 * Visit the official website - 
 
 * Click on the CEE 2017 result link
 * Enter your details like registration number, etc
 * Your result will appear on the screen
 * Take a print out for future use
 
 The NCERT CEE 2017 exam is conducted for admissions in various courses like B.Sc, B.Ed, BA B.Ed, M.Sc Ed, B.Ed, M.Ed and B Ed-M Ed (Integrated). 
 
 
 
 The National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) was set up by the Government of India in 1961 as an autonomous organization registered under Societies Registration Act (Act XXI of 1860). It was formed to advise and assist the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India and Departments of Education in States/ UTs in formulation and implementation of their policies and major programmes in the field of education.
