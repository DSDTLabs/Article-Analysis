Vadodara: Three more burglaries on New Year night have come to light in Fatehgunj.

 Thieves targeted army officials’ houses near EME School when they were attending a function in the campus on Sunday night. Raj Singh Duhet, a lieutenant colonel at EME, told the police in his complaint that when he returned from the function on Sunday midnight, the door of his house was open. When Duhet checked inside, gold and silver ornaments worth Rs 2.58 lakh were missing.
 
 Later it was learnt that the burglars had also broken into the houses of Major Ravi Shankar Vyas and Colonel Harendra Vyas nearby but nothing was stolen from their houses.
 
 Earlier on Monday, four incidents of burglaries on New Year’s eve were reported from different parts of the city.
