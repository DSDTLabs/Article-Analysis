BHANDARA: Anand  , a junior engineer in   municipal council, was caught red handed while accepting a   of Rs1 lakh from an electrical contractor, by sleuths of the anti corruption bureau on Friday. 

 A registered electrical contractor was awarded a tender of Rs1.34 crore for fixing LED lights in ward number 1 and 2 of Paoni. After completing the work he submitted a bill of Rs1.34 crore. He was paid Rs1.20 crore and for the remaining amount unpaid since 2015 he asked Bandebuche to release the amount. Bandebuche sought Rs2.5 lakh to release the amount. The contractor approached   and a trap was laid.
