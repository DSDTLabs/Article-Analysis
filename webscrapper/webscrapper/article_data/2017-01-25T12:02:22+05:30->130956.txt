PANAJI: In view of the February 4 assembly elections, the government has ordered that all licenced premises “for the sale of liquor” be closed from 5pm on February 2, till 12am on February 5. All   premises have also been ordered to remain closed on the day of counting, March 11. 

 Premises with a bar and restaurant licence may be allowed to keep the restaurant open to serve food only, while the bar counter will remain shut. No liquor will be allowed to be served on the above mentioned days. 
 
 Owners of licenced premises with a bar and restaurant have been asked to display a board stating that no liquor will be served and that only the restaurant is open for food.
