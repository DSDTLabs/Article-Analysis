KOCHI: Police arrested a 50-year-old man for allegedly sexually assaulting a 13-year-old boy in Aluva on Thursday. 

 The accused,   (50), a native of   attempted to sodomise the boy who stays in the same locality, after luring him to his house. He threatened the boy with dire consequences if he reveals the incident to anyone.
