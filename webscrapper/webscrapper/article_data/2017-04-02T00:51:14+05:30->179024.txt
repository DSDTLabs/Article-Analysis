Hyderabad: The officials of the Enforcement Directorate ( ) attached assets worth Rs 3.04 crore under the provisions of Prevention of Money Laundering Act in a multi-level marketing case against Vishwajyothi Realtors Pvt Ltd. Promising to pay huge sums as dividends for deposits of Rs 10,000 and above, the promoters, T Pramod Kumar, and others cheated as many as 34,480 individuals and collected Rs 34 crore. Though police tried to nab the culprits, they delayed the process through several legal methods. tnn
