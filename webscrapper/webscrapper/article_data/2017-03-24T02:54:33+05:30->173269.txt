GURUGRAM: A 27-year-old contractual employee of power discom DHBVN has been arrested for allegedly raping a teenage girl three months ago.

 The crime came to light when the 17-year-old girl’s father took her to Civil Hospital on Tuesday night after she complained of abdominal pain. On examining the girl, doctors discovered she was three months’  , but had a   because of lack of medical care.
 
 After the girl broke down and narrated her ordeal, her father lodged a complaint with the police, following which the accused, Manoj Kumar, was arrested on Wednesday. He was produced in a city court on Thursday and sent to judicial custody for 14 days, police said.
 
 Cops said Manoj, who was posted in DHBVN’s sub-divisional office in New Palam Vihar, had befriended the girl in her father’s tea stall that he used to frequent.
 
 “In the last week of December, Manoj lured the girl to a secluded place near Dwarka expressway and raped her. He even threatened to kill her and her father if she reported the matter to anyone. He had sexually assaulted her a number of times in the past three months,” according to the police complaint. The girl kept quiet as she was afraid of Manoj, cops said.
 
 An FIR has been filed against the accused under sections 6 and 12 of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act and sections 354-D (molestation) and 506 (criminal intimidation) of IPC at Palam Vihar police station. “We lodged an FIR and arrested the accused from his home in Tau Devi Lal Colony on Wednesday evening. He has confessed to the crime. The   survivor is a student of class VIII and is stable,” said inspector Sandeep Kumar, SHO of Palam Vihar police station.
 
 Manoj is married with three children.
