Title: Kanupapa
Language: Telugu
 Cast: Mohanlal, Vimala Raman,Anushree, Samuthirakani
 Director: priyadarshan
 Release Date: February 3, 2017
 Genre: Crime/Thriller
 Synopsis: Jayaraman (Mohanlal) is a visually challenged lift operator, whose senses of touch, smell and sound are quite sharp. He happens to be the sole witness of a murder that takes place in the apartment he works in, and police register a case against Jayaraman based on circumstantial evidences. To prove his innocence, he sets out to find evidences and find the real culprit.
