After becoming President, Kovind had visited Sabarmati Ashram in Ahmedabad and attended a Jain community event in Mehsana on September 3, 2017. On September 4, 2017, the President had laid the foundation stone of ‘Saurashtra Narmada Avataran Irrigation Yojana’ (SAUNI) Phase-II Link-4’ at Rajkot. 
 
 The President had visited Gujarat in October 2017 also . He had visited Porbandar to pay his tributes to Mahatma Gandhi at his birth place, Kirti Mandir (in Porbandar). Later, he attended a function organized by the Gujarat government to declare ODF status for Rural Gujarat at Kirti Mandir. He had also gone Gir-Somanth district during his October visit. This will be his 3rd official visit to Gujarat.
