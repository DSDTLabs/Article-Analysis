SURAT: A 13-year-old girl from   in   town of Surat district was raped by a 20-year-old boy on Saturday.

 The   survivor’s mother registered a police complaint at Kosamba police station in this connection. 
 
 The girl had got up to answer the nature’s call at 5 am when the boy belonging to another community dragged her to a lonely place and raped her.
 
 The girl’s mother lives with her second husband. The rape survivor and her mother are labourers. The accused is also a labourer and is on the run. They all belong to Uttar Pradesh. 
 
 Police said they will nab the accused soon.
