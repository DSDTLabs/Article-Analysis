GURUGRAM: Four members of a family have been booked for allegedly molesting a 12-year-old girl during an argument with her family in   near Manesar.

 According to police, the two families came to blows following an argument, and one party thrashed the other. 
 
 The family which thrashed the other has claimed that the other party had molested their daughter.
 
 According to police, they received information around 8.30pm on Monday that a brawl had broken out between the two families in Uton village near Bilaspur. 
 
 When a police team reached the spot, members of both the families managed to escape, leaving those who were injured behind. 
 
 Two persons from each family were injured in the brawl, and are now being treated in a private hospital.
 
 On Tuesday morning, the father of the girl lodged a complaint at the Women’s police station, Manesar.
 
 According to his compliant, members of the other family had barged into his house on Monday night and molested his daughter. When he objected to the same, an argument broke out between the two families, he has claimed. 
 
 An FIR was registered against four persons of the other family, including the two injured, under sections 323 (causing hurt), 452 (house trespassing) and 34 (common intention) of the IPC and Section 12 (punishment for sexual harassment) of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act (POCSO) Act.
 
 “We are verifying the facts first, and action will then be taken as per the law. We’ll record the statement of the accused soon,” said sub-inspector Meena Devi, the investigating officer.
