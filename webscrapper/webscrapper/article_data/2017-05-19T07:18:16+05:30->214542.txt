PUNE: The state government on Thursday issued a notification to all   in Maharashtra to form ‘Good Morning Squads’ to greet citizens who defecate in the open.

 “All local governing bodies must form these squads to ensure cities are   and the cities having achieved the open defection free tag maintained the status,” states the notification.
 
 The squads will comprise elected members of local governing bodies, members of self-help groups, students and activists and representatives of non-government organizations. The squads will operate till October 2 and facilitate construction of toilets as well as initiate action against those citizens not using toilets. CM   has announced to make the urban parts of Maharashtra Open Defecation Free by October 2.
 
 As many as 8.32 lakh families in the urban Maharashtra defecate in the open due to lack of toilet facility.
