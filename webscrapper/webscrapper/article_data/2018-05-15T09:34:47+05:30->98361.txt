AJMER: The carcass of a   was found in the debris of granite mine in   village of   district on Monday afternoon. Forest department and police were informed and the body was sent for post-mortem. According to   police, labourers working in the mines informed that when they were removing the debris from the site of mining, they found the carcass. It was not torn and had no signs of attack.

 Forest ranger   said that it is possible that because of heat the animal came to this place and hid in the blocks of stones but died because of suffocation when debris came on those blocks. He added that the reason of death will be clear after the post-mortem.
