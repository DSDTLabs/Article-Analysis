It was an evening to celebrate friendship when recently Sahitya Adda organised its seventh literary evening, Yariyan, at a cafe in Gomti Nagar. The event was a perfect amalgamation of music and poetry. 
Ravi Mishra, the key organiser, said, "This is a small initiative where we promote young talent of Lucknow. Lucknowites are so talented but unfortunately they are not getting the right kind of platform and opportunity to showcase it. This is one such way where we can highlight their talent."
