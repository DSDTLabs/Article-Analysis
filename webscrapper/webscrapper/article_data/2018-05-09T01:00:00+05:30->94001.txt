 TV actor Karam Rajpal, who was last seen on 'Naamkarann', will soon join the cast of 'Chandrashekhar'. The show revolves around the struggles of freedom fighters Chandrashekhar Azad and Shaheed Bhagat Singh. Karam has been finalised to play the role of Bhagat Singh on the show.
