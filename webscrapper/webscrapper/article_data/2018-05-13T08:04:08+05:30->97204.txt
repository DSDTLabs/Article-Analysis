PUNE: An unidentified man stole Rs90,000 from a lawyer’s bag kept on a table on the parking premises of the Pune Cantonment Cour, at M G Road between 12.30pm and 1pm on Friday.

 Sub inspector of Cantonment police, Prashant Khedkar said, “Shashikant   (39) from Undri, a lawyer at the court, had kept his with cash on a table and had gone for lunch. When he returned, he noticed the cash was missing from the bag. He first searched for the cash at his residence and then filed a complaint around 5 pm.”
 
 In his complaint, Koli said the thief stole Rs90,000 kept on top of the bag while an additional Rs30,000 underneath was left untouched. “There are no CCTV cameras in the parking premises. We hope to detect the case at the earliest,” Khedkar added. 
 
 The case has been registered under section 379 (theft) of the Indian Penal Code.
