ALLAHABAD: Avigilant railway patrolman,  , on Wednesday averted a major tragedy by stopping the   before it could cross a fractured track in the Manikpur-Naini section of the busy   railway route.

 Shyam was on a routine round around 11:45am when he spotted a fracture in the main line between Bewera and   stations under Manikpur-Naini section of Allahabad division. 
 
 “Shyam contacted the Lohgara station master who in turn alerted the control room. The information was immediately passed on to the loco pilot and assistant loco pilot of Tulsi Express coming to Allahabad from   station. They applied emergency brake and stopped the train before it could cross the fractured track,” a railway official said.
