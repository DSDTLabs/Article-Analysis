The incident took place in Kumbhapani forest range of the reserve on Friday, and the injured was brought to government hospital at Chaurai and later referred to district hospital Chhindawara and later to Nagpur where he died during treatment. 

  Sources said that when Kewal Navreti, 45, resident of Konapidrai, had gone to see his sugarcane crop in the farm situated near the jungle, he was attacked by a tiger. He sustained severe injuries and after villagers informed the local forest officials he was rushed to the nearby community health centre in Chaurai town. Later he was taken to Chhindwara district hospital, but the doctors there referred him to Nagpur due to his serious condition.
