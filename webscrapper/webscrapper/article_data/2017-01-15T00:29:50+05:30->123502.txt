BHIWANDI: A 42-year-old man, Dolile Das, was arrested in Bhiwandi for allegedly killing a 30-year-old man, Vasudev Das. The accused suspected that his wife had an   affair with the victim. 

 Both the accused and the victim worked as power loom workers and stayed in Bhadwad in Bhiwandi. Police said, on Friday night when victim was going for night duty, the accused stopped him and attacked him with sharp weapon and fled from the spot. The accused has been remanded in police custody for five days.
