Rajkot: As many as 27 foreign athletes will participate in the Rajkot Marathon organized by Rajkot Municipal Corporation (RMC) on February 18. 

 According to a statement released by the municipal corporation, the visa granting process for these athletes is in progress. Seven participants are from Ethiopia and rest of the foreign participants are natives of Kenya. 
 
 RMC received 22,000 registration from across the country. This year, the theme of the marathon is to spread awareness on cleanliness.
