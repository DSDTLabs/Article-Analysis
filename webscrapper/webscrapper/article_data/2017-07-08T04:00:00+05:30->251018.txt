Vadodara: A jeweller was looted by two unidentified persons when he was returning home on Friday night. The accused looted gold and silver jewellery worth about Rs 2.5 lakh and fled from the spot. A complaint was registered with the Gorwa police.

 The incident occurred when Girish Agrawal, owner of Rajasthan Jewellers, was passing from Gorwa-Laxmipura area. “When he was passing from a poorly lit road on his two-wheeler, the accused came from behind on a motorcycle. One of them kicked the two-wheeler following which my father fell down. The accused snatched the bag full of jewellery and fled from the spot,” said Agrawal’s son Hardik.
