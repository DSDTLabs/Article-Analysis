Students from ten architecture colleges sweated it out in a three-day   held at  , Secunderabad. Hosted by JNIAS School of Planning & Architecture, the fest brought together students from various city colleges, such as  ,  , Jawaharlal Nehru Architecture and Fine Arts University,  ,  , Ashoka Institute of Engineering & Technology, JBR College of Architecture. Competitions were closely fought in both outdoor and indoor games such as cricket, football, throwball, kho kho, badminton, kabaddi, chess and carom. The fest also organised a photography competition, which got students with an artistic bent of mind pick up the camera and capture some amazing moments.
