Veteran actor   has sought justcice for   girl  , who was raped and murdered brutally and dumped into a well in Ariyalur. Her decomposed body was discovered two weeks later by the Ariyalur police.

 Kamal Haasan posted on his micro-blogging page, 'Justice for Nandhini a must. Kaavi(saffron) khadi green white red or black doesn't matter. God is no reason for crime .. I am human 1st Indian 2nd. Apologize for voicing my concern this late', tweeted Kamal Haasan. The actor also clarified that he wanted justice for the girl. 'Pls. understand my plea is 4 justice not 4 revenge. Revenge is mine it seems said a lord. I wont even grant him that. Dont repeat history.'
