NEW DELHI: Andhra Pradesh Intermediate second year (Class 12) and first year (Class 11) results 2018 will be declared April 12 and 11 respectively. The results will be declared on the official website of the Board of Intermediate Education Andhra Pradesh (BIE AP) - bieap.gov.in

 The AP Inter second year exams were held from March 1 to 19, 2018 while the first year examinations took place from February 28 to March 17, 2018. Around 457,292 students registered for the AP Board Inter-II exams in 2018.
 
 If you are one of the students eagerly waiting for the announcement of   2018, you can check it on the official website of the   - bieap.gov.in.
 
 
 
 1) Open the official website of the Andhra Pradesh Education Board - bieap.gov.in
 
 2) Click on the results link "AP Intermediate Results 2018"
 
 3) Once you click on the link, it will redirect you to a new web-page
 
 4) Enter your Roll No. and other details
 
 5) Your result will be displayed on the screen
 
 6) Download your result or take a printout for future reference
