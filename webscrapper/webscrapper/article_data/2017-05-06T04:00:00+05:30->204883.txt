Rajkot: A Congress councillor from Jetpur municipality died after his SUV veered off the road and fell from a bridge near Pithadiya toll booth on Rajkot-Porbandar highway on Thursday. 

 The councillor Prakash Gida (26) was returning from Virpur along with Bhaya Bhutiya. 
 
 The accident came to light on Friday morning when some passersby saw the mangled SUV on the river bed beneath the bridge. Police rushed to the spot and found that Gida had died on the spot. Bhutiya was rushed to the hospital with severe injuries.
