AHMEDABAD:  , 79, a resident of Ghosha Society,  , has filed a complaint with   police for theft of valuables worth Rs 2.64 lakh. According to her complaint, she works as a receptionist and had left home for her office at 10.30am on March 23. She had returned to her home at 7.30pm. 

 "At that time, she found the latch of the main door open. When she ventured inside, she found three cupboards ransacked in the bedroom," said a Vastrapur police official.
 
 "She has reported theft of gold and diamond ornaments along with silverware from the premises," he added.
