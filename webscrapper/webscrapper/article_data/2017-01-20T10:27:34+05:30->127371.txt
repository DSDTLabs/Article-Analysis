RAJAHMUNDRY: AP DGP   said the police department would take measure to address ganja and other sedative drugs smuggling problems in the state. Cops have identified two main railway stations for the ganja smuggling in North Andhra.

 Anakapalli in Visakha and Tuni in East Godavari have become hubs for ganja smuggling, said the DGP. He said the police department would train sniffer dogs to bust smuggling rackets. If necessary, dogs would be brought from foreign countries and trained.
