MUMBAI: The prosecution on Wednesday submitted   charges against software engineer, Anees Ansari (26), who is accused of conspiring to kill people at an American school in Bandra Kurla Complex by plotting to use a thermite bomb. 

 The prosecution has told the court that he has tried to incite another person in joining him and and spreading terror activities propagated by ISIS. He has been booked under sections of the IT Act and IPC for criminal conspiracy. The matter will be heard on August 1.
