 Last year, the Uttarakhand Board had announced the results on May 25, 2016.
 
 The Uttarakhand Board of School Education (UBSE) is an agency of Government of Uttarakhand entrusted with the responsibilities of prescribing courses of instructions and text books and conducting examinations for secondary school students in Uttarakhand. 
 
 It was set up in 2001 and has its headquarters in Ramnagar. At present more than 10,000 schools are affiliated with the Board. The Board sets up over 1,300 examination centres for over 300,000 examinees every year. The Board is committed to provide quality education and related facilities to students and educators.
