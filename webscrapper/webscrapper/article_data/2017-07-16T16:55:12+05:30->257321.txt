KOLKATA: The   ( ) has installed the second   in Eco Park after the successful trial run of the first water ATM and plans are on to install such water ATMs in Sector V and other places of New Town also.

 NKDA had installed the first water ATM in Eco Park that was set up by the state public health engineering department and the second water ATM has been sponsored by a private company as part of their CSR activity. The first water ATM was installed adjoining the entry plaza of gate number II of Eco Park.
 
 NKDA officials said that the water ATM is primarily a German technology that has been modified and manufactured in India. One can get a litre of chilled pure drinking water by inserting a 2 rupee coin at the ATM counter. Bottles or glasses have to be brought in.
