KORAPUT: A 35-year-old farmer sustained serious injuries in an impoverished explosive device ( )   near Kandikigurha village under Boipariguda police station on Wednesday, five days after the Red rebels damaged a road in the area in a landmine blast. 

 The injured person, Gupta Pradhan of Ramagiri, was admitted to Boipariguda hospital and shifted to a private hospital in Jeypore when his condition deteriorated.
 
 The incident took place around 1 pm when Pradhan was on his way home from Dandabadi on his motorcycle. "The explosives wrapped in a bag were lying unattended on the roadside. The explosion took place while Pradhan was trying to open the bag to check it. He received serious injuries," said  , a Ramagiri resident.
