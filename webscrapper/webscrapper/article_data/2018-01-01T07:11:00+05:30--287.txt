Ferozepur: A debt-ridden farmer of village Gajniwala in Ferozepur district reportedly committed suicide by jumping into a canal here on Saturday evening. The deceased, identified as Suba Singh left a note before taking the extreme step. Search was on to recover his body. 

 Suba Singh earlier owned 10 acres of land, which he had to sell. Besides a loan was also taken by him to sustain his livelihood. He jumped into Rajasthan canal near Deep Singh Wala village. tnn
