PANCHKULA: A 56-year-old woman was killed and her 20-year old son injured after a truck hit their scooter in Sector 25 on Sunday. 

 Kiran  , a resident of Sector 25, was going for some personal work when the vehicle hit and dragged her   Activa scooter for 100 metres around 1pm. Eyewitnesses said the truck driver was driving rashly and hit the scooter from the front. The driver escaped. 
 
 Both the persons were rushed to General Hospital, Sector 6, where doctors declared Kiran brought dead. Her son was under treatment but out of danger, police said. He was wearing a helmet at the time of the accident, while Kiran was not.
 
 Police registered a case under Sections 279 (rash driving at public place), 337 (causing grievous hurt) and 304 A (death caused due to negligence) of the Indian Penal Code at the   police station.
