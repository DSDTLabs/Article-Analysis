 "The Etihad flight from  , with about 190 passengers on board, hadn't reported an emergency and everything was fine till it touched down on the runway," said an air traffic control official. After it touched down, the aircraft exited the runway and had a tyre burst. Flight operations had to be moved to the other runway and arrivals and departures during the evening peak hour were moved to the secondary runway, which led to delay in flight operations. 
 
 An Etihad spokesperson said, "The aircraft was towed from the runway. All passengers and crew are fine and engineers ready to replace the deflated tyres."
