Surat: A 22-year-old man was stabbed by an unidentified person on Friday who wanted to loot his mobile phone. 

 Sonu Singh, a zari machine operator, was going to Udhna railway station at 5.15 am when a person stopped him by showing him a knife near Krishna temple. 
 
 The person asked him to hand over the mobile phone with him and when Singh refused, he was stabbed in the back. tnn
