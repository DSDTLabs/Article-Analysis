MARGAO: In the backdrop of the   gangrape case, the local panchayat has decided to prevent visitors from entering the beaches after 7.30pm. A special gram sabha to be held on Sunday will deliberate on the issue, it is learnt. The   police have also written to the state tourism department to put in place adequate security measures at the beaches. South Goa SP   said that the tourist department has been particularly asked to install high mast lamps at isolated places around the beaches, CCTV cameras, and advisory boards. tnn
