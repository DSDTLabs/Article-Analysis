LUDHIANA: Bains brothers-led   (LIP) has declared that their party would field candidates in the next   (SGPC) elections. They made this announcement in Amritsar where they had organized a political rally on Saturday. During the rally, both the brothers attacked   members, and termed the committee’s move to expel 523 employees as ‘illegal’.
