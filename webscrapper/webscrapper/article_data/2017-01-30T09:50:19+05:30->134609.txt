VISAKHAPATNAM: The Pendurthi police on Sunday registered a case against an auto-rickshaw driver for trying to rape a 26-year-old woman on Saturday night. 

 A manhunt has been launched to nab the accused. Sources said the woman boarded the auto-rickshaw while returning home. 
 
 Taking advantage of the situation, driver K Suresh drove to an isolated place and tried to rape her. However, the woman managed to escape and return home. On Sunday, the victim lodged a complaint with the police.
