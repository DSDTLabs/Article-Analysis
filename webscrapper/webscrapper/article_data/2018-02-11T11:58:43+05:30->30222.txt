KANYAKUMARI: Tension prevailed after police baton-charged to disperse an irate mob who were staging a road blockade after a motorcyclist fell off the bike during a vehicle check at   on Saturday.

 A two-wheeler rider in a bit to escape from being fined for helmetless driving fell off his bike and injured himself. Angry at the incident, local residents staged a road block at Arumanai
 
 Police said the motorcyclist did not stop despite being signaled to stop during the check. They said he was looking to evade the inspection when he tried to drive away. This resulted in the accident. The youth is said to have fallen down and suffered injuries. More than 200 people, who gathered at the spot, alleged that the police beat up the bike rider, leading to his fall.
