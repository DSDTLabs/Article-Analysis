 According to Kamal Nayan, SHO, accident police station (South Jaipur) the incident took place when Vishwas Garg (18) was taking his father to their shop near Patel Nagar when a speeding school bus ran hit then from behind and over the duo. “While Vishwas died on the spot, his father, Vishnu, was rushed to the hospital where he is undergoing treatment,” Nayan added.
 
 The police said Vishwas was driving his bike when all of a sudden a speeding school bus, first knocked them from behind and then ran over them. 
 
 The cops said that a case has been registered and the body has been handed over to the family members after post-mortem. Nayan added that CCTV footage would be examined. He added that the police have begun their investigation in the case and are expected to record the statement of the witnesses soon.
