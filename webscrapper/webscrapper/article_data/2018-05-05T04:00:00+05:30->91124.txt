Surat: One person was killed and 15 others were injured when a luxury bus carrying a marriage party overturned near Rani Kui village of Mandvi taluka of Surat district on Friday. The injured people were taken to nearby hospitals.  Police said 65 people were returning after a marriage at Vera Kui and their bus was on its way to Vyara in Tapi district when the accident happened in the evening. The bus overturned at around 7.30 pm near Rani Kui turning on the state highway road. An officer from Mandvi police said the person who died was conductor of the bus. He was identified as Ankit Gamit. His body was taken for postmortem. Police have registered a case of accidental death and begun investigations.
