MUMBAI: Office goers and school children were caught off guard on Thursday morning when auto rickshaws in   went on a flash strike to protest against the assault of a driver last night.

 Public including senior citizens were forced to board crowded buses or walk due to the strike. School students were also affected due to the sudden strike.
 
 An auto rickshaw driver Mohammed Khan was allegedly assaulted by three to four unidentified men at Kashimira. Khan who sustained serious injuries has been admitted to the hospital. His condition is said to be serious. The cause of the fight has not been ascertained.
 
 
 Auto drivers demanded the arrest of the suspects and accused the Kashimira police of going slow.
