NAVI MUMBAI:   Rajiv Mishra has sought disqualification of corporator   for allegedly submitting a false affidavit of having no tax dues. Shirke is a director of a cable TV company and the company is said to be owing Rs 1.83 cr as entertainment tax. 

 “This is a clear case of tax evasion and providing of false information to the government. The company headed by the corporator had pending tax dues but while filing affidavit for 2015 civic election this information was not disclosed,” alleged Mishra. 
 
 Mishra termed this to be a gross violation and has sought for disqualification of the corporator on Friday. 
 
 Shirke denied of owing any dues and said that the sum in question is being challenged in court. — Sameera Kapoor Munshi
