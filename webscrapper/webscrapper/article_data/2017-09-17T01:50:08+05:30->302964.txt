GURUGRAM: A 10-year-old girl was crushed to death by an autorickshaw in   on Friday.

 The victim has been identified as Elama, a class IV student, who was playing outside her house at the time of the  . 
 
 The victim’s father, Isub, a resident of ward 11 in Pataudi, lodged a complaint with the police. 
 
 “I was inside the home when I heard my daughter shouting. I went out immediately with my wife and found her lying in a pool of blood. Eyewitnesss say that she was first hit by the autorickshaw and then runover by the vehicle. I rushed her to a nearby hospital where doctors declared her brought dead. The driver managed to flee but I know him as he lives near our house. His name is Moni,” said the victim’s father in his complaint.
 
 After receiving information about the accident, a team of police reached the spot and took custody of the body. It was later handed over to the family after an autopsy. 
 
 An FIR has been registered under sections 279 (negligence of driving), 304 A (death due to negligence) of IPC at Pataudi police station. The accused is yet to be arrested.
 
 “We conducted raids at Moni’s house but he is absconding. We will arrest the accused soon,” said ASI  , the investigating officer.
