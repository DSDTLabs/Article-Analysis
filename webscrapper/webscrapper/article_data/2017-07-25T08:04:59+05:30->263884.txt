NEW DELHI: One terrorist was killed by security forces during an encounter in Assam's Kokrajhar district on Tuesday. 

 The operation was conducted by the Army and the Assam Police.
 
 "Based on intelligence inputs, a joint operation was launched by the Army and Assam Police on Monday night in the area. Around 6 am today morning, the patrol party was fired upon. Our troops retaliated with controlled fire and in the gunbatle, a terrorist was killed,", a defence spokesperson based in Guwahati said.
 
 He also said one AK 47 rifle along with ammunition, one hand grenade and other war-like stores were recovered from the site.
