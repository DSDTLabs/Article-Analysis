GUWAHATI: A man hacked his wife to death with an axe on Tuesday morning in the city's Panjabari area, before being nabbed by Satgaon Police while he is believed to have been on his way to surrender at a police station. The accused has been identified as Biswa Das of Bogorital area of Panjabari, a daily wager. 

 According to neighbours, Das was going through a period of depression recently, even having mentioned the idea of killing himself.
 
  "He was unable to find any work for the last couple of days and was engaged in heated arguments with his wife. His wife, Surushi Das, was hit on her head and neck with the axe," a Satgaon Police source said. TNN
