NAVI MUMBAI: Volunteers collected around 24,100 kg of   on Sunday during an awareness   organized by   about the harmful effects of plastic. The waste collected was taken to Turbhe landfill to be used for making   pellets.

 The drive was undertaken as a part of NMMC’s plan to reduce the plastic waste to 10 tonnes. Civic officials said that maximum amount of waste was found in Nerul and Koparkhairane. “In zone 1, maximum waste was recorded in Nerul at 3,410 kg whereas 9,180 kg was collected in Koparkhairane in zone 2,” said an official.
 
 The civic chief requested residents to take all measures to reduce use of plastics in daily life. —Sameera Kapoor Munshi
