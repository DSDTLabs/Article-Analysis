HYDERABAD: Aspirants for teaching jobs on Sunday urged the government to extend the deadline to apply for the recruitment test for at least another 20 days. 

 The aspirants said that the   ( ) has not yet made changes in the requirements and that aspirants with less percentage are still unable to apply for the test.
 
 "Even after the court has directed the TSPSC to allow all the aspirants, who have cleared the qualification exam, the commission is yet to make changes in the website as it is not accepting applications of aspirants with less than 45 percent marks. The commission should immediately extend the date so that all the candidates can apply," said V Beemrao Nayak, chairman,   JAC.
 
 The TSPSC officials said that they are not going to entertain any requests for extensions.
