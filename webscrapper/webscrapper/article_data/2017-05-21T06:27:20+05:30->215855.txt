PANAJI: The   core committee on Saturday discussed seven names as potential candidates for the Rajya Sabha elections. The party is expected to finalise the name for the Rajya Sabha seat next week.

 The nomination for Rajya Sabha elections would begin on Monday and the last day of filing nominations is May 29. The election would be held on June 8.
 
 Chief minister Manohar Parrikar chaired a core committee meeting which discussed names such as BJP state president  , BJP general secretary Sadanand Tanavade, BJP treasurer Sanjiv Desai, former environment and forest minister Rajendra Arlekar, former Fatorda MLA Damodar ‘Damu’ Naik, former Panaji MLA Sidharth Kuncalienker and BJP leader Keshav Prabhu.
 
  In the next step, all the names will be put before the central leaders who will be in Goa to select the candidate. Union minister   and BJP national organizing secretary B L Santosh are likely to come to Goa to select a candidate. 
 
 Parrikar will also hold talks with his alliance partners - Maharashtrawadi Gomantak Party (MGP),   and independents, before taking the final decision on BJP candidate for Rajya Sabha elections, a source said.
