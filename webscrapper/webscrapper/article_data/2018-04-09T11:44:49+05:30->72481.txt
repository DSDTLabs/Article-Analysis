PANCHKULA: Two persons attempted to rob an ATM machine of   located at   on Saturday night. Both the accused have been arrested. 

 A police patrolling party found something unusual happening at the ATM. When the cops went inside, they found that the accused were trying to break open the machine. The cops said that the machine was half broken, however they failed steal cash from it.
 
 Cops immediately called up forensic experts and after investigation they also called up the bank officials, who filed a complaint.
 
 The police have registered a case under section 457 (lurking house-trespass or house-breaking by night in order to commit offence punishable with imprisonment) and 380 (theft in dwelling house, etc.) of the IPC (Indian Penal Code) at Mansa Devi Complex ( ) police station.
