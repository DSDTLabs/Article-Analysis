NEW DELHI: Aam Aadmi Party spokespersons Sanjay Singh and Ashutosh have withdrawn allegations they had levelled   a private   in connection with the Delhi District Cricket Association (DDCA).

 In documents filed in the Delhi high court, where the company Twenty First Century Media (TCM), had filed a defamation suit against them, the two AAP   expressed deep regret and withdrew their allegations of favoritism and wrongful benefit against TCM and its owner, Lokesh Sharma.
 
 In their letter to Sharma which was also filed in HC, Ashutosh and Singh said the allegations were were unfounded and expressed deep regret for the damage or loss caused by these wrong allegations.
 
 HC then allowed Sharma and the two   leaders to end the litigation, which had been pending for more than two years.
