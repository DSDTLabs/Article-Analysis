VADODARA/ANAND: A delegation led by commander-in-chief of   senior general Min Aung   visited Amul Dairy premises in milk city Anand on Monday. The 11-member delegation headed by the commander-in-chief met officials of Amul Dairy and the Gujarat Co-operative Milk Marketing Federation – the Anand-headquartered body that markets brand Amul. 

 The commander-in-chief who along with his wife   who are on a two day visit to Gujarat were shown a power point presentation showcasing the journey of Amul from dusty town Anand. The delegation while visiting dairy’s plant was explained the different dairy products produced by the dairy major while it also visited Dr   Museum – a memorial which the dairy union has set up in the memory of father of India’s White Revolution late Dr   Kurien.
