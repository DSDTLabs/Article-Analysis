The wait is finally over for the most anticipated trailer of the year. The trailer for ‘Baahubali 2’ will finally be out on 16th of March at 9 AM. The trailer is all set to be aired across the theatres of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.

 Ever since ‘Baahubali: The beginning’ was released, T’town fans have been eagerly waiting for ‘Baahubali: The Conclusion’ to hit the screens.   fans are eager to find out why did Kattapa kill Amarendra   and the suspense will be revealed in the movie ‘Baahubali: The Conclusion’.
 
 The trailer launch event of ‘Baahubali’ 2 will be attended by the likes of Rana, Anushka, Prabhas, Rajamouli and Tamannah. The film is slated to release on the 28th of April 2017. The movie’s music director is M.M. Keeravani and it is bankrolled by Arka Media.
