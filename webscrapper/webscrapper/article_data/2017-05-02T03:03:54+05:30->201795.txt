NOIDA: In tune with the Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act (RERA), which came into force in the country from Monday, the Noida Authority is all set to put up on its website the details of existing and ongoing projects in the city.

 In a meeting with the   Home Buyers’ Association, CEO, Noida Authority, Amit Mohan Prasad told them that all the details of ongoing builder projects will be put up on the website of Noida Authority for transparency. In the meeting on Monday, the NCR Home Buyers group represented 21  . 
 
 “We have been told that in the next 30 days, things like the number of towers, area of apartments and all construction linked specifications of projects will be put up on the site,” Rashesh Purohit, co-founder, NCR Home Buyers’ Association told TOI. “The homebuyers on Monday submitted a memorandum to the Noida Authority stating that they want tripartite meetings at the Noida Authority with a representative from the buyers’ end, and the builder’s end to track progress with construction as well as issues related to post-construction details like fittings, safety measures etc. We have also made a request that the setting up of the homebuyers’ grievance cell should be expedited,” Purohit added.
 
 While Authority officers are hosting a series of tripartite meetings with buyers and builders, Noida Authority ACEO Shishir Singh said that a fresh list of buyer, builder and Authority meetings would be released soon.
