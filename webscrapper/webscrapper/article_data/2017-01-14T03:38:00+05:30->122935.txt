Panaji: The Maharastrawadi Gomantak Party ( ), on Friday, released its first list of 18 candidates for the February 4 assembly elections. It will contest 27 seats. 

 The party, which had announced Ramkrishna Dhavalikar, alias Sudin as its CM candidate, said its stance would be maintained in the post-poll scenario as well. Apart from Sudin, MGP president and Priol MLA Pandurang Dhavalikar, alias Deepak, and Ponda MLA Lavoo Mamledar figured in the first list.
