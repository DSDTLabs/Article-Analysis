Ludhiana: Two unidentified masked miscreants attempted to loot an ATM on Rahon road by holding security guard hostage on Tuesday. The accused barged into the ATM, tied up the security guard with a piece of cloth and attempted to break open the ATM, but all in vain.

 On the complaint of ATM guard Ashok Kumar of New Sant Nagar, Haibowal the Daresi police have registered a case under sections 379-B (snatching), 457 (Lurking house-trespass or house-breaking by night in order to commit offence punishable with imprisonment), 380 (Theft in dwelling houseand others), 511 (Punishment for attempting to commit offences punishable with imprisonment for life or other imprisonment), 342 (Punishment for wrongful confinement), 427 (Mischief causing damage to the amount of fifty rupees), 323 (Punishment for voluntarily causing hurt) of IPC. 
 
 In his complaint Kumar said that he works as a security guard in a Canara Bank on Rahon Road. At around 2.30am, two masked miscreants opened the shutter and tied him up with a piece of cloth. Further they attempted to break open the ATM however failed and fled form there. 
 
 The Investigating officer from police station Daresi, ASI Gurjeet Singh said that cops are searching for the CCTV footages to trace the accused.
