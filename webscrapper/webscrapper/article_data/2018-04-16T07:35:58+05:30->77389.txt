MALDA: Hundreds of tribals gheraoed the police station at   in   for two days to protest against the disappearance of some body parts from the local burial ground on Friday. 

 The tribal people, who have a separate burial ground in the Koil area, found on Friday that miscreants had dug some graves. They alleged that the skull of one Nimai  's mother was missing. 
 
 On Saturday morning, hundreds of tribal people reached the local police station with their bows and arrows and gheraoed it demanding immediate arrest of the culprits. Police arrested six persons on Saturday and produced them in court on Sunday, but the miffed tribals gheraoed the police station again on Sunday. 
 
 SDPO   said, "We have already arrested six persons and produced them in court. Two of them were remanded in police custody."
