Chandigarh: State Transport Authority ( ) on Friday inspected the buses of Saupin’s School in Sector 32 Chandigarh. A total of 22 buses were inspected and no anomalies were found.

 “The system was well maintained by the school authorities and we would urge all schools to be equally vigilant. The director principal, transport supervisor and the teachers have done a remarkable job,” said additional secretary STA Rajiv Tewari.
 
 The inspection drive was initiated on Wednesday after Punjab and Haryana high court order. The order stated, “We are of the view that the school buses are liable to be impounded till the speed governors are fitted. The Chandigarh administration shall forthwith take necessary action in this direction and file a fresh status report.”
