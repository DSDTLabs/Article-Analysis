Ahmedabad: Swine flu claimed three more lives on Tuesday taking the death toll to 403 in the state this year. The state reported 76 new cases of swine flu on Tuesday. With this, the total number of swine flu cases reached 6,626 this year.

 A statement from the state government claimed that the number of swine flu cases have decreased over last four weeks. In last 15 days, 2,522 patients got discharged from various hospitals. As of now, 5,701 swine flu patients have been cured across the state, claimed the statement.
 
 It further claimed that not a single case of swine flu was reported in 15 districts and five municipalities in the state. tnn
