 The rise in onion arrivals has led to decline in average wholesale onion price. The average wholesale onion price has declined by 17% in just two days from Rs 480 per quintal on Wednesday to Rs 400 per quintal on Friday.

 “The average wholesale onion price has already declined a lot and farmers are incurring huge losses as they are unable to recover even their production cost. It costs Rs 1,000 per quintal to farmers for growing onions. Farmers incur losses if they get price below that. Now, the farmers are incurring losses of around Rs 500 per quintal due to crash in onion prices,” the APMC sources said.
 
 The average wholesale onion price at Lasalgaon was recorded at Rs 400 per quintal on Friday. The minimum and maximum prices were recorded at Rs 250 and Rs 545 per quintal, respectively.
