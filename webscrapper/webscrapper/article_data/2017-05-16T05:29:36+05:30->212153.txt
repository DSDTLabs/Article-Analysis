AHMEDABAD: As many as 22 primary, secondary and higher secondary schools of Ahmedabad have voluntarily reduced their annual fees. These include 14 secondary and higher secondary schools and eight primary schools in Ahmedabad district. With this, the number of schools voluntarily reducing their fees after introduction of   Self-Financed Schools (Regulation of Fees) act- 2017 has gone up.

 The fee reductions ranged between Rs 500 and Rs 5,000 in the 14 secondary and higher secondary schools that fall within the limit of Ahmedabad district. In these 14 schools, seven each from Gujarati and English medium, the fee structure was between Rs 24,000 and Rs 31,000 per annum.
 
 K R Raval Secondary and Higher Secondary School in Ranip area of the city has reduced its annual fees from Rs 31,500 to Rs 26,500.
 
 Eight primary schools within Ahmedabad district also reduced their fees by sums ranging from Rs 2,000 to Rs 5,100. Galaxy Global Primary school in Ahmedabad district has introduced the highest reduction in school fees among these Rs 5,100.
