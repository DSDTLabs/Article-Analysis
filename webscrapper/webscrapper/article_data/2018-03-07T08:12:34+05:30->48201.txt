HYDERABAD: For the fourth time since its inception in 2008, the Hyderabad International Airport, on Monday, was recognised as the world’s best   in the Airport Service Quality (ASQ) passengers survey — in the 5-15 million passenger per annum category — for 2017. The award was conferred by the Montreal-based Airports Council International ( ), based on passenger feedback.

 Commenting on the announcement, Srinivas Bommidala, chairman (airports), GMR Group said: “This is a recognition of the collaborative work of all stakeholders at Hyderabad Airport. I applaud the efforts made by employees of   (GHIAL) and that of entire Airport community.”
 
 SG K Kishore, CEO, GHIAL termed the award as a matter of “great pride” and thanked the   for its support.
