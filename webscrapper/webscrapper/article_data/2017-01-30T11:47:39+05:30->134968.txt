RAIPUR:  , celebrated its   'Indradhanush' at Kalibadi, Rabindra Manch in Raipur on Saturday evening. 

 The programme began with a traditional prayer in the presence of the chief guest MM Tripathi, chairman,   group of schools, along with academy director Sharmila Sur. The annual school report for the academic year 2016-17 was read out, highlighting the activities that took place and festivals celebrated.
 
 Playgroup, nursery, KG-I and KG-II students stole the show during the cultural programme based on the theme 'Colours of Life'.
 
 Sharmila Sur said, "It is our duty as parents and teachers to develop a conducive atmosphere for children. The outstanding performance of the students drew applause from one and all."
