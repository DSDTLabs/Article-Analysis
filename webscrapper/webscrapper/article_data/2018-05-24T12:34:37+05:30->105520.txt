A fire broke out on the premises of   on Wednesday.

 On noticing flames engulfing the open area, a worker called up the fire department around 1.30pm. The department sent three tenders to the spot and brought the situation under control till 5.30pm. 
 
 MC operation and maintenance cell superintending engineer Ravinder Garg said, “There was no damage to the plant and no one was injured in the incident.” 
 
 It is yet to be ascertained what caused the blaze. “It seems someone tossed a burning cigarette on the dry grass, which caught fire,” said a fireman. 
 
 Fire was reported at the site on Tuesday too.
