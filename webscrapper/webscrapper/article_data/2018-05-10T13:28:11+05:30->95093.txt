NEW DELHI: The Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board (GSHSEB) released the results of Gujarat 12th Class Science and GUJCET today morning.

  
 
  The honorary education minister of the state Bhupendrasinh Chudasama announced the result at a press conference.
 
 The students can register below to check their Gujarat Board results:
 
  
  
 
  
 
 
 
  
 
 
 
  This time the exams have been conducted very strictly so as to reduce the amount of malpractices and cheating with the help of security squads and surveillance.
 
  A total of 1.5 lakh students have appeared for the HSC examination and the exam has been conducted in 140 centres. Along with HSC, GUJCET results are also scheduled for 9 am. The results are getting released an hour before compared to last year. 
 
  According to information from A J Shah, the chairman of the GSHSEB, around 1.35 lakh students appeared for the GUJCET examination which has been conducted in 34 centres in Gujarat.
 
 
  
  
 
 The people who are qualified in GUJCET can apply for admissions to different engineering and diploma /degree pharmacy programmes in the Gujarat.
 
 The GUJCET also releases a merit list every year which has the criteria of taking 60% weightage to marks obtained in Maths, Physics and Chemistry and 40% weightage will be given to percentile obtained in GUJCET 2018.
 
 Considering previous year numbers, a similar number of 1.32 Lakh students have appeared for the examination.
