NEW DELHI: The   has asked its officials and others in-charge of   to ensure that in the next three months each fuel station along the NHs have adequate toilet facilities for men and women. 

 In a circular to the officials concerned, the ministry has said that the toilets provided at fuel stations are “small in size” and cannot cater to road users. 
 
 Moreover, there are often no separate toilets for men and women. The circular says, it’s also observed that the toilets are in unhygienic condition. Moreover, these are not accessible for all road users or are kept under lock and key.
