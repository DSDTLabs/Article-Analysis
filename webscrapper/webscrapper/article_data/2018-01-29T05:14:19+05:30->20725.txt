MUMBAI: The city recorded a further drop in minimum   on Sunday at 15 degrees Celsius from 15.6 degree Celsius a day earlier.

 Weathermen said they were not expecting any big change in temperature in the next two or three days. But day temperature could rise, they said.
 
 On Sunday, the minimum temperature recorded by the   Colaba observatory was 18.5 degrees Celsius while at Santacruz, it was 15 degrees Celsius. The drop in   was observed at both observatories. The minimum temperature at IMD   and   observatory just a day earlier on Saturday was 19.5 degrees Celsius and 15.6 degree Celsius respectively.
 
 Residents said the weather throughout the weekend has been very pleasant.
