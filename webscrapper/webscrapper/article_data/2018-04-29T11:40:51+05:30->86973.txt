 * Swachh Bharat Summer Internship: PM Modi invited college-going students, NCC cadets, NSS jawans and other youngsters to participate in the central government's newly-launched Swachh Bharat Summer internship programme. 
 
 "This is an opportunity for those young people who wish to contribute to the country, bring about a change in society and learn something in the process," he said.
 
  
  
 
 
 * After speaking about sanitation and cleanliness, PM Modi went on to stress the need for conserving water. 
 
 "Conservation of water is in the heart of Indians. For centuries, our ancestors have shown this in their way of life. They gave importance to even a single drop of water," he said.
 
  
  
 "Conservation of water should be a social responsibility, every person should be contribute in it. Let us work towards water conservation," he urged.
 
 
 
 * In a few days the holy month of Ramzan will begin. The month of Ramzan is celebrated with full reverence and respect throughout the world, the Prime Minister said. Ahead of this holy month, PM Modi spoke of Prophet Muhammad.
 
 "Prophet Mohammad sahib believed in knowledge and compassion. He did not have an ego. He used to say that ego only defeats knowledge. He believed that if you have anything more than your requirement, you should give it to a needy person, therefore the spirit of giving is important in Ramzan," Modi said.
 
 
 
 * PM Modi also paid his tributes to Lord Buddha ahead of Buddha Purnima.
 
 "Lord Buddha inspires equality, peace, empathy and brotherhood, and inculcating these values is increasingly essential in the world today, " he said.
