Gurgaon: Two bike-borne youths were killed after a speeding vehicle rammed into their motorcycle near   gate in Farrukhnagar on Tuesday night.

 The duo, who was returning after attending a marriage function in Farrukhnagar, died on spot.
 
 Police are yet to identify the vehicle and the accused, who fled the spot immediately.
 
 The victims have been identified as Narender Kumar (25) and Pradeep Kumar (24), who wetre from Uttar Pradesh and were living in Devi Lal colony in Gurgaon and working with private companies.
 
 The victims were rushed to the nearby hospital, once police reached the spot. Both of them were declared dead on arrival.
 
 Vijay Singh, brother of Narender, identified his brother and filed a complaint against the unknown vehicle driver. Police have lodged an FIR under sections 279 (rash driving), 304A (negligence of driving) of the IPC at Farrukhnagar police station. 
 
 “We are still investigating the matter. Bodies have been handed over to the families after autopsy. The vehicle is yet to be identified,” said ASI  , the investigating officer.
