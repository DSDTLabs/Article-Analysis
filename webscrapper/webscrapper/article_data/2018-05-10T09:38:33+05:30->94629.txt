PUNE: The   police arrested a man on Tuesday night for passing lewd comments at a woman as well as molesting her on May 6. 

 A 27-year-old business woman came in contact with the suspect who is employed as an agent at a revenue office involved in registering property documents.
 
 An officer from the Vishrantwadi police station said, “The suspect caught the woman’s hand on finding her alone. He passed lewd comments, molested her and threatened to kill her after she resisted him.”
 
 “The 29-year-old suspect was arrested after the woman lodged an FIR against him under the relevant sections of the Indian Penal Code. The suspect was produced before a magisterial court on Wednesday but he was later released on bail,” the officer added.
