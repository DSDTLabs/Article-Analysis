After 'Janatha Garage', young tiger NTR took a long break and had his brother, Nandamuri Kalyanram announce about his next project, which is produced by the latter under their home banner NTR Arts. However,for the movie, it is reliably learnt that Kalyanram had registered 'Jai Lava Kusa' as a title under his production company.
