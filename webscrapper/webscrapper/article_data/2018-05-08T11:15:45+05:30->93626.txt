Ahead of the audio launch of  's ' ' directed by Pa Ranjith on May 9th, the official tracklist of the songs has just been released by the film's team. The much awaited audio of 'Kaala', will be released on May 9th at the YMCA grounds in Chennai. Music composer   is reported to be performing live at the event. 

 Directed by Pa Ranjith, ‘Kaala’ is set in the backdrop of Mumbai city and will reportedly see Rajinikanth playing a gangster. Santhosh Narayanan is once again working with Rajinikanth after their gangster flick ‘Kabali’. Produced by Wunderbar Films, the film is all set for a massive release soon. 
 
 It should be noted that there is one acapella version and 8 songs in the movie. 
 
 
 
 
 
 The film has an ensemble of artists like Huma Qureshi, Sampath, Nana Patekar and Samuthirakani in important roles. Touted to be a film on politics, Nana Patekar plays a politician and Rajinikanth seems to be playing the saviour of the masses, just like he is trying to becoming a politician to save the people by venturing into politics.
