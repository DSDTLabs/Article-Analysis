LUCKNOW: A class XI girl ended her life by hanging herself in her home in   after she was allegedly scolded for not studying. 

 Puja Mishra (16), of Basant Vihar Colony, went to her room on Saturday night after she was asked to concentrate on studies.
 
 On Sunday morning, her father  , employed with skill development department, went to inquire. When she did not open the door, he peeped through the window and saw her hanging. 
 
 SHO Madiaon Amarnath   said no suicide note was recovered.
