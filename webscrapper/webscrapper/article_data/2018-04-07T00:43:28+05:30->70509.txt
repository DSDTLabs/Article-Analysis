Salem: Sona College of Technology has topped the list of engineering colleges in the district, thanks to its good portfolio in research, academics and placement. The ranking, instituted by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF), was released by the Union human resource minister Prakash Javdekar on April 3. Titled India Ranking Report 2018, engineering, medicine, pharmacy, architecture and law and management colleges were ranked by a core committee based on five criteria comprising teaching, learning and resources, research and professional practices, graduation outcomes, outreach and inclusivity, and perception. Sona College of Technology has topped the list of engineering colleges in the district since the introduction of the ranking system in 2016.
