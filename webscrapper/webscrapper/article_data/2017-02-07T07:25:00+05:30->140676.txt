PANCHKULA: Lawyers in   district court held a silent protest on Monday showing solidarity with a colleague whose minor daughter was allegedly kidnapped on Saturday and many lawyers did not appear for their hearings too.

 The alleged kidnapped girl returned home in the evening on Monday and it was found that she had gone to Chandigarh at her own will.
 
 A girl, resident of Zirakpur, had gone to her school in Sector 14, Panchkula, on Saturday and went missing since then. Father of the girl reported the matter to the police station in Sector 14 that his girl did not return home from tuition and has been kidnapped. A zero FIR was lodged and the case was transferred to   police station following the jurisdiction matter.
