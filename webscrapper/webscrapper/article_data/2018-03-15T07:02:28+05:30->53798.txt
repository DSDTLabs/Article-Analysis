AHMEDABAD: Students who took class X (science and technology) and class XII (chemistry) exams found the papers tricky.

 In the science and technology exam, questions worth around 10 to 12 marks came with a twist. Some students said that part B section, the descriptive type, was simple.
 
  
  
 
 Some students found the class XII chemistry paper tough. Questions worth around 15 marks posed a challenge, they said. Section B was relatively simple. Overall, students found the general stream statistics paper easy.
