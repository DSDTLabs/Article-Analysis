Bathinda: Reeling under debt, a farmer of Chugge Kalan village in Bathinda ended life by consuming poison on Monday late night . Victim, identified as Nirmal Singh (45), had been suffering from depression for some time for being unable to repay a loan of nearly Rs 15 lakh.

  Farm organisation BKU (Ekta Ugrahan) activist Harjinder Singh Baggi said Nirmal owed Rs 15 lakh to a cooperative bank, a nationalised bank, village agriculture cooperative society and a commission agent. Nirmal left behind his wife and a 22-year-old son who helped him in farming. He owned 4.5 acres of land.
