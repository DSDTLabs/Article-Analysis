 The key benefit of demonetisation for Kotak Mahindra was the surge in retail deposits where the bank was lagging. Savings deposits grew by 45% to nearly Rs 38,566 crore as on December 31, 2016 compared to Rs 26,579 crore as on December 31, 2015. Current account deposits grew by 24% to Rs 24,217 crore as on December 31, 2016 compared to Rs 19,609 crore as on December 31, 2015. CASA as on December 31, 2016, stood at 42% compared to around 35% as on December 31, 2015. TD Sweep deposits as on December 31, 2016 were 7.3% of the total deposits.
