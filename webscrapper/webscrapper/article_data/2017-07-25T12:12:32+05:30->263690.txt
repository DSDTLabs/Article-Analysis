AHMEDABAD: EMRI 108 officials said that rains have spurred an overall rise in emergencies related to falls and vehicular accidents. However, the most significant deviation has been reported in cases related to collapse of structures and electrocution. According to paramedics, on July 23 and 24, EMRI received 12 cases of collapse and 16 cases of electrocution from across the state. In Ahmedabad, EMRI reported 55 cases of falls and 59 of vehicular accidents. For  , the figures were 247 and 343 respectively. EMRI officials said that while all ambulances have been placed on alert to deal with any emergency, six calls on Monday had to be cancelled because ambulances could not reach the destination.
