Vadodara: The crackdown by the Vadodara Municipal Corporation (VMC) on tax defaulters seems to be bearing results. The civic body has recovered over Rs 18crore during the drive that began on January 1.

 VMC officials said that till January 18 this month, the civic body had sealed 4,595 properties. The civic body also severed water connections of 150 properties that had not paid large amounts towards property tax. The civic body recovered pending taxes amounting to Rs 18.23crore during the drive. Source said that 345 properties still remained sealed as their taxes had not been paid.
 
 On Thursday itself the civic body had recovered taxes amounting to Rs 3.56crore as a part of the drive. It had sealed 275 properties on the day and severed water connections of 18 properties. tnn
