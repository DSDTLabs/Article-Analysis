Panaji: A 55-year-old male attempted suicide and suffered critical injuries despite rescue attempts by Goa Police and the fire and emergency services personnel.  The victim, identified as Krishna Durgadas Tamang, was rushed by the police to Goa Medical college. Panaji police said that Tamang was working at a lodge for the last six days. “We found an Aadhar card on his person showing him as a resident of Pune. We are trying to trace his family,” a police official said.  Doctors said that Tamang had suffered multiple fractures and was bleeding from the nose and ears and is presently unconscious. “We don’t know the reason for his suicide attempt. The ambulance was delayed so we took him to hospital in our vehicle,” a police official said.
