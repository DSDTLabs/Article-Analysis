GURUGRAM: A  , which is running a paying guest (PG) home in Kanhai village allegedly thrashed a head constable and tore his uniform after he enquired about a complaint lodged against them by a guest, who was staying in their accommodation.

 On Monday, head constable Anil Kumar of Sector 40 police station reached the PG house owned by Vinod Kumar following a complaint lodged by one of the guests staying there against Vinod. The guest told police that Vinod beat him up and threw his luggage out of the room. “We have lodged an FIR and are trying to nab the accused who are absconding,” said ASI Harichand, the investigating officer.
 
 The duo is absconding.
