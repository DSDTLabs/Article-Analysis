ALAPPUZHA: In a major blow to BJP, the   (BDJS) has made it clear that it would not support the party in the upcoming   bypoll until its demands were met. The decision was made at the   council meeting in Chengannur on Sunday. 

 BDJS president   said, "We will continue to be an ally of NDA but will not support the BJP candidate in the bypoll. BDJS has informed about its demands to the BJP leadership and is waiting for a reply."
 
 Last week, while speaking to TOI Thushar had said that the meeting of the other allies of the NDA in the state has been called off. BJP national leaders have promised to meet the demands of the BDJS after   elections, he had said.
