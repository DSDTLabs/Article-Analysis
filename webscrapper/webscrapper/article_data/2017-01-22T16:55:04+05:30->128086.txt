
  The festival kicked off by vocal performance by Pt. Venkatesh Kumar an exponent of Gwalior and  , who is known for rendition of Haridasi bhajans. Renowned for his husky, vibrant classical singing style, he mesmerized the audience with Raaga Shri, Raaga Durga and Raaga Basant Bahar. He concluded his performance with a melodious Meera Bhajan - Payoji Meine Raam Ratan Dhan Payo.    Later in the evening, Sangeet Manapman, a famous musical drama featuring Rahul Deshpande and Priyanka Barve made the fest a real feast of Natya Sangeet for music lovers.
