TENALI: Depressed over wife's death and mounting financial problems, a man committed suicide by hanging himself to a tree after killing his eight-year-old daughter. The incident occurred at Yadlapalli village in   district on Monday. 

 According to Tenali inspector Kalyan Rao, Angirekula Raju (30) took his daughter   to a nearby field and hung her to a tree. Later, he also committed suicide by hanging himself from the same tree. Raju married Bhulakshmi (27) nine years ago. Ever since his wife died a year ago by accidently falling in a crusher, Raju went into severe depression and stopped going to work.
