GURUGRAM: As the city gears up for the MCG   scheduled on Sunday, police are leaving no stone unturned to ensure a hassle-free election. Of the 546 polling booths in the city, 153 have been categorised as   and 99 as sensitive booths. The government has already deployed 3,600 cops in the city. 

 On Friday, a flag march was conducted in the city. The march began from the police commissioner’s office and culminated at the Mini Secretariat after covering sensitive areas like   Chowk, Palam Vihar, Rezang la Chowk and Old Railway road. “We will ensure peaceful polling. A total of 3,600 cops have been deployed so far,” said police commissioner Sandeep Khirwar.
