Yellow was the new gold at the 2017 Golden Globes award, and keeping up with the trend was Bollywood star  . The actress, who will soon make her Hollywood debut in 'xXx: Return of Xander Cage', made a glamorous appearance at the Warner Bros and InStyle after-party. 

 The news of Deepika attending the Golden Globes party came as a rather big surprise to her fans, who soon began sharing a close-up shot of the actress, that was posted by her stylist Shaleena Nathani. She shared the picture with the caption, 'So excited to have been a part of this Feel so so so proud @deepikapadukone at the Instyle party #goldenglobes2017 styled by the amazing @elizabethsaltzman @teamsaltzman wearing @ralphlauren makeup @hungvanngo hair @cwoodhair #dreamdreamdreamteam.'
 
 Dressed in a canary yellow Ralph Lauren one-shoulder ensemble with side-swept wavy locks, Deepika shone at the party. A fan club shared a glimpse of the star interacting with a few guests and having an enjoyable time at the bash.
 
  
  
 
  
  
  
  
 
  Also present at the same bash was Deepika's 'Bajirao Mastani' co-star Priyanka Chopra and her 'xXx: Return of Xander Cage' co-star Nina Dobrev.
