  ‘Vunnadhi Okate Zindagi’ features the character of Abhi as a member of the rock band ‘Against Evil’ that actually exists in Visakhapatnam. The story charts the beautiful friendship he shares with Vasu (Sree Vishnu). It also stars Anupama Parameswaran as Maha, the girl both Abhi and Vasu fall in love with, and Lavanya Tripathi plays the role of Maggie, a happy-go-lucky wedding planner. 
 
  Talking to Hyderabad Times before the release of his film, Ram had revealed, “Anupama (Parameswaran) had to work hard to become Maha, because she's nothing like the character. For Lavanya though, it was a cakewalk to play Maggie because she's extremely chilled out and entertaining in real life too. She's the entertainment factor in the film.”
 
  He also revealed that he loved playing Abhi so much, that he didn’t feel like he was working hard at all. He had said, “"I genuinely do not remember. Maybe I did work hard, I don't know.” 
 
  The film that released on October 27, 2017 had received good reviews from critics and audience alike.
