VADODARA: The man, who killed his father in   of   on March 28, was arrested on Saturday.

 Ghanshyam alias   was arrested by police from a farm in Padra. “We had activated our local sources after the incident, and on Saturday we received an information that Tadpada was hiding in a farm near his village. We raided the farm and nabbed him,” a police official said. After the arrest, the police did not seek his remand and Tadpada was sent to judicial custody.
 
 Tadpada had killed his father Bhagwan with an axe in the front yard of their house after the latter asked him to find a job. He first hit his father on head and followed it up with multiple times on other parts of the body with the axe. His father died on the spot and he fled from the spot.
