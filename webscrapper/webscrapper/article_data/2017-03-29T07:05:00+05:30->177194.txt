Panchkula: As part of the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan, a walkathon was organized in Terminal Ballistics Research Laboratory (TBRL), Ramgarh Test Range, Panchkula, on Tuesday. The walkathon was held within the   campus. On this occasion, TBRL director Dr Manjit Singh planted a tree sapling and addressed the officers and staff to keep their residential, environment and working place clean. He led the TBRL workforce for walkathon of about 4km in which more than 400 scientists, officers and staff members participated.
