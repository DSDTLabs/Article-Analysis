JIND: Seven people, including three women, suffered injuries in celebratory firing during a pre-wedding function at a house here, police said on Monday. 

 The incident took place during the 'ladies sangeet' ceremony at the house in   on Sunday night, when a person allegedly fired shot from his double barrel gun in the air. 
 
 After hitting the roof of the house, the bullet splinters injured seven peeople who were dancing, police said. They were rushed to the PGI, Rohtak for treatment, police said.
