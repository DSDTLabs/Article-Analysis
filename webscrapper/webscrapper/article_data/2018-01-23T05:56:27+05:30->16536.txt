CHENNAI: Junaid got the shock of his life on Sunday night when a man dropped out of the sky and crashed on the bonnet of his car. 

 Junaid, 29, was driving from   to Kilpauk when Vasanth, 31, a professional dancer, leaped off the Royapettah High Road flyover and fell on the car below, smashing the windscreen, police said.
 
 "Vasanth, who was depressed, rode to the flyover on his motorcycle around 9pm," an investigating officer said. "He climbed on the parapet, shouted that he was going to commit suicide and flung himself off the bridge.
 
 Motorists saw Vasanth rush to the parapet, but did not have the time to rescue him. By the time they got to the spot, Vasanth had fallen on the car below. Junaid stopped the vehicle and ran for cover in panic. 
 
 Police said Vasanth, from Thiruvalluvar Nagar in  , fractured his shoulder in the fall. Bystanders took him to   Government  .
 
 "We spoke to Vasanth's family members and found he was upset that he had not got married as yet," the officer said. "Vasanth's father Sundaram said none of the marriage proposals for his son worked out." 
 
 Sundaram informed police that his son had been undergoing treatment for depression and been under medication. He had expressed suicidal tendencies.
 
 "He threatened to end his life several times but his family did not take him seriously. He left home on Sunday night asking his parents to donate his organs if he were to die," the officer said.
