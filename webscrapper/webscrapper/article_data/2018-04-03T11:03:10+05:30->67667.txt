PANAJI: The Confederation of Real Estate Developers’ Association - Goa ( ) welcomed the state government’s decision to revive the Regional Plan 2021 and to implement the provisions of RP 2021, which have been on hold for almost six years, since June 2012.

 “We appreciate the first bold step taken by the government,” said Credai-Goa president  . “We fully support the same and further request for a complete operational RP 2021 in order to expel the long-standing issues related to the Regional Plans.” 
 
 Town & country planning minister   reportedly asked the department’s secretary to issue an order allowing construction activities in settlement areas, which were hitherto restricted under the Regional Plan 2021.
