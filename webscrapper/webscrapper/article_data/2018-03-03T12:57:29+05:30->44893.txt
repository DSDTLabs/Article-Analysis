GUNTUR: A third year BTech student died after a lorry rammed his bike and ran over him at Narasaraopet, in   district, on Friday. The deceased was identified as Odugu Ravi Teja (20), a native of Narasaraopet town.

 The incident took place at Kakumanu Bazar, while Teja was going to the sports stadium for a workout. He was heading to pick his friend up, and took a turn from   to the main road. A speeding lorry coming from the right rammed his bike, killing him on the spot.   police rushed to the spot, and shifted the body to the government hospital for post-mortem. A case was registered on the driver. TNN
