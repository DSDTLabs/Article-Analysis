SRINAGAR: An intelligence officer was found dead under mysterious circumstances in Shivpora here on Thursday. Sources said DSP of Intelligence Bureau (IB)   died mysteriously in his residential quarters. 

 A police officer, confirming the incident, told news agencies that Joshi was posted in Srinagar and found in unconscious state. “Joshi, a resident of Lucknow, was rushed to military hospital where doctors declared him brought dead,” the cop said, adding that “further probe is on”. 
 
 “The body has been sent to police control room (PCR) Srinagar for post mortem,” the officer said, adding that the cause of death will be ascertained after the post mortem report. A case under 174 CrPc has been registered in this regard, the officer added.
