JALANDHAR: A Youth Akali Dal ( ) leader and his brother, who is alleged to be a bookie, have been arrested by anti-terror squad (ATS) for allegedly funding gangster Vicky Gonder. Balwinder Singh Monti and his brother   were arrested along with two others.

 It is learnt that the arrests were made a tip off that Gonder was getting funding through a network associated with his aide Harry. The arrested accused were taken to a special cell of ATS Amritsar on two-day remand.
