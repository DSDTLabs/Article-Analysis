Uttarakhand legislative assembly election will be held on 15 February 2017 in a single phase for the 70 seats. The results of the elections will be annnounced on March 11, 2017.  In the previous election in 2012, none of the parties won majority but the Indian National Congress formed government with the help of PDF in the leadership of Vijay Bahuguna. * Indian National Congress (INC) * Bahujan Samaj Party (BSP) * Uttarakhand Kranti Dal (P) (UKD (P) * Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP)
