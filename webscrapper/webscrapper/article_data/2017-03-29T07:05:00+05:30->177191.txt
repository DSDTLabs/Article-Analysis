 Chandigarh: A 32-year-old woman was raped by a tenant living in the same building in Mohali. The accused works in private company.
 
 The incident happened when the woman went to the second floor to meet her friend living in the same house around 11pm last night. Accused person who also lives in the same house on the first floor followed the woman and forcibly entered the room while locking the door from inside and raped her.
 
 The accused person also assaulted her while threatening her of death in case she narrated the incident before anyone.
 
 However, the woman told about the ordeal to her friend after which they reported the matter to police. After her medical examination, the accused person, who is at large, was booked under Section 376 of IPC.
