 The deceased people were identified as Sakthivel, 32, an employee of a private firm from Oragadam, Chennai and his father-in-law  , 55, from Sirkazhi, Nagapattinam district. His relative Kabilan sustained severe injuries and was admitted at   government general hospital and research institute.
 
 Sakthivel was at the wheel. When they crossed Reddichavadi on the East Coast Road, a speeding TNSTC bus bound for Velankanni from Chennai coming from the opposite direction collided head on with the car killing Sakthivel and Krishnamoorthy instantly. Kabilan sustained injuries. The car was completely damaged in the accident. The bus driver and passengers escaped unhurt in the accident.
 
 Reddichavadi police booked the bus driver under Sections 279 (rash driving or riding on a public way), 337 (causing hurt by act endangering life or personal safety of others) and 304-A (causing death by negligence) of the Indian penal code and begun investigations.
