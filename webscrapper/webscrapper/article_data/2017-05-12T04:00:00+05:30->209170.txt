Ahmedabad: Gujarat Forensic Sciences University (GFSU) signed MoUs with  ,  ,  , Bialystok University of Technology and Warsaw University of Technology, all from Poland, on May 10 in Gandhinagar. State education minister Bhupendrasinh Chudasama was present at the ceremony.

 GFSU officials said the MoUs are for academic collaboration in various areas of forensic sciences. The team from Poland was visiting GFSU from May 8 to 10. “Issues such as student and faculty exchange, collaborative research, credit transfer, joint degree and other areas of mutual cooperation were discussed. Thrust areas of the collaboration will be forensic science education, new-age and cyber crimes,” said an official.
