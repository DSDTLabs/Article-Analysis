 The trio—Gulab Shaikh (45), Azad Khan (25) and Taufiqullah Khan (27)—had attempted robbery at a jewellery shop in Andheri last week but aborted the plan when they spotted a police patrol team. Their luck ran short as their images were captured on a closed-circuit television camera installed nearby.
