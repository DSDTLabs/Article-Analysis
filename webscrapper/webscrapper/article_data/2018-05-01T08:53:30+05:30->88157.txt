CHENNAI: State Human Rights Commission (SHRC) on Monday directed the additional director general of police ( ), prisons, to submit a detailed status report regarding steps taken by the department on issuance of sanitary napkins to women inmates in prisons across the state within two weeks.

 SHRC judge D   initiated suo motu proceedings based on his visit to   in   recently, when many of the women inmates alleged that they have not been provided with sanitary napkins since November 2016.
 
 Prison officials also corroborated the version and shared the letters sent to the director of public health and preventive medicine from August 2016 to March 2018, which showed no proper steps were taken by the health department. The judge directed deputy director of health services, Trichy and the director of public health, Chennai, to appear on May 15 for inquiry. TNN
