PANCHKULA: A 24-year-old Ludhiana resident was killed while his uncle was injured when a speeding   bus hit their car near old Panchkula light point on Wednesday night.

 Harvinder Singh, the deceased, and  , resident of Galoti village, Ludhiana, were going towards Shimla when the accident took place.
 
 In his complaint to the police, Harpreet Singh claimed that accident happened as the bus (HR-55-U-5045) took a turn towards Panchkula at a high speed and hit their car in the process.
 
 The bus driver was later identified as  , a resident of Panipat in Haryana .
 
 A police vehicle, stationed at the old Panchkula light point, took the injured to the civil hospital in Sector-6 but Harvinder had succumbed to injuries by then. Harpreet Singh under admitted to the hospital.
 
 Police have registered a case under Section 279 (rash driving on public way), 337 (causing hurt by act endangering life or   of others) and 304-A (death caused due to negligence) of the Indian Penal Code (IPC) in Sector 5 police station, Panchkula, against the bus driver.
 
 The body was later handed over to the family members after the autopsy at the civil hospital.
 
 Assistant sub-inspector Desh Raj said, “We have registered a case and the accused driver would be arrested soon. Investigation is under progress.”
