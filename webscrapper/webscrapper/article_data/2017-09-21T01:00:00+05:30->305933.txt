After impressing critics and cinegoers in India, the Arjun Rampal-starrer ' ' seems to have wowed the audience in other countries, too. Hundreds of Asian and Scandinavian fans gave a standing ovation to the actor-producer, and the film’s joint producers Raju Chadha, Chairman, Wave Group and Rahul Mittra, CEO, Wave Cinemas. The trio were felicitated at the   Bollywood Film Festival which concluded in Oslo recently. “It was heartening to see the response. It’s amazing how Indian cinema has become a global force and that drives us to continue to make strong content-based films,” says Rahul.
