GURUGRAM: Two men were arrested on Thursday for allegedly thrashing a 27-year-old  , Jeetu, who put up his kiosk in Khandsa  . On Tuesday, four men allegedly kidnapped him from the market when he denied to pay them the extortion money they had demanded. 

 An FIR was registered against the accused under sections 365 ( ), 341 (wrongful confinement), 323 (causing hurt), 506 (criminal intimidation), and 34 (common intention) of the IPC at Shivaji Nagar police station.
 
 On Wednesday night, two of the four accused, Vishal and Monu were arrested.
