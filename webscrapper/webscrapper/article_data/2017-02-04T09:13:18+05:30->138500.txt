MEERUT: A 27-year-old girl hanged herself to death at her house in Meerut’s Saket area on Friday.

  Shilpi (who goes by her first name) was set to get married on February 24.
 
   , station officer (SO) of Civil Lines police station, said: “Shilpi’s father   informed the police about his daughter’s death.
  
 The matter will have to be investigated now and the reason behind the suicide will be looked at.”
 
  “The body has been sent for post mortem examination and a probe is on,” he said.
 
  Interestingly, while the media got a copy of the purported suicide note, police denied to have found any such letter from the spot.
