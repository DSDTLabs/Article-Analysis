While Harman takes care of Soumya ( ) as usual, she is adamant to get him married to Surbhi ( ). He also finds it odd when his family members begin to treat Soumya properly. 
 
 Preeto ( ) asks Soumya to cook food in the kitchen and wash utensils, something which she wasn't allowed to do earlier. This sudden turn of events makes Harman suspicious. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 When Soumya tries convincing him again, Harman gets angry. And while he is holding her neck, Soumya asks him to take her life as that would help him find happiness in someone else.
  
 He feels that it is all a part of Preeto's plan. After finding out that it actually is, Hartman refuses to marry Soumya as she his sister-in-law.
