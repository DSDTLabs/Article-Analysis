INDORE: The   ( ) has issued notices to 30 officials who have failed to act on complaints received during a  . More than 500 such complaints are pending with the  . 

 Officials said that all those officials with whom more than 10 complaints are pending have been issued notices. Department sources said that they have been asked to look into the complaints within two days.
 
 Public hearing is conducted by the civic body every Tuesday. Heads and in-charges of all departments directly interact with people and look into their complaints. On an average, 30-50 complaints are registered with the civic body.
 
 Sources said that more than 60 per cent of complaints were encroachment or building-related. 
 
 The move comes three days after a woman tried to immolate herself during a public hearing .
