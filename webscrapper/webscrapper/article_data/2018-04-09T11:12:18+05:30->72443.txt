NEW DELHI: One person received serious injuries after an explosion at   cantonment railway tracks in Kolkata on Monday. 

 A woman scavenger accidentally picked up a country-made bomb and lost one of her hands in the explosion. Her other hand is already paralyzed.
 
 Ten crude bombs were recovered from the site of the explosion, reported news agency ANI. Local district police and railway police personnel have rushed to the spot and probing into the case.
