The much-awaited multi-starrer film ' ', directed by   and scripted by Murali Gopy, will hit screens in June.

 The trailer of the movie, which has Prithviraj, Padmapriya, Indrajith and many other actors from various film industries, will be out on May 22, Tuesday. The team announced it through their official social media page, recently. 
 
 The movie was shot entirely outside Kerala with main locations being Hyderabad, Pune, Mumbai and Nashik. The film apparently has two timelines, the early 90s in Mumbai and the present in Badrinath.
