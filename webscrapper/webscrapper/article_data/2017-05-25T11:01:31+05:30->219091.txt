HYDERABAD:   (UoH) has been ranked among the best universities in the country in a new nationwide survey. 

 The HANSA-led research survey for 2017 ranked UoH at fourth position. The central university and Osmania University, which stood fifth, are the only two universities from Hyderabad to find a place in the top 75 multi-disciplinary universities in the country that includes state, central, private and deemed universities.
 
 University of Delhi, Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU) and Banaras Hindu University were ranked first, second and third respectively. UoH is ranked at the first position among the top multi-disciplinary universities in the south.
 
 The survey was conducted by collecting data from more than 500 leading universities in India.
