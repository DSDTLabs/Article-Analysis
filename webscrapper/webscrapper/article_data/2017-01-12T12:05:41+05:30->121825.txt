GANDHINAGAR: At the Vibrant   Global Summit-2017, Gujarat Chief Minister   while addressing on the occasion of “Inspiring   Business” seminar said that Poland is the country partner in Vibrant Gujarat. Poland is the heart of Europe and has always showed eagerness to invest in India. As a result various investment will take place in Gujarat and both the nations will be benefited along with increasing employment opportunities.

 He said that extensive business opportunities with Poland will take place through the medium of Vibrant Gujarat Global Summit and relationship between Poland and India will be strengthen through exchanges of investment, training and knowledge.
