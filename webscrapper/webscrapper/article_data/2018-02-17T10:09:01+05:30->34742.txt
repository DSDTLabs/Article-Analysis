AHMEDABAD: A 27-year-old youth has registered a complaint for abetment of suicide against his mother. His father had hanged himself in October 2017. The complaint was filed after handwriting experts gave their opinion to police, that the natural handwriting of the deceased matched that in the notes found on him.

 Naranpura police said Chiragkumar Dutt, a resident of  , stated that his father Satish, 57, was found dead in a room of his residence. Two notes — one from his trousers and the other on a table — were found. In them he had accused his wife Bhagwati Dutt, now living in Shahpur, of mental harassment.
 
 Dutt alleged in his complaint that his mother had threatened to file a police case against his father and other family members.
