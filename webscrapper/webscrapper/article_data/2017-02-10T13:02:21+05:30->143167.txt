VISAKHAPATNAM:   Prof G Nageswara Rao noted that the Union budget 2017 was special in the context of demonetisation. He said the budget is comprehensive with an alternative focus on sustained economic growth.

 He was participating in a discussion on Union budget 2017-18 organised by the department of economics at Andhra University. Speaking on the occasion, other economic experts including Prof M   observed that it was a reform budget and unique in several ways as it had merged the railway budget into the general one and abolished the distinction between plan and non-plan expenditure for the first time. He also opined that the current budget is a performance based and outcome based one.
