AJMER: For the first time, provisional admission to next class will be provided to government school students, who have appeared in classes V, VIII and X board examinations. The admission will be provided to these students on the next day of completing their annual exams. Till now, students appearing in these board examinations had to wait till they got their results to be admitted to the successive classes.

 Following the footsteps of   board, the government schools will now provide provisional admission to students to successive classes on completing their board examination.“The decision has been taken so that there is no time wasted just for the sake of result,” said  , state education and panchayat minister.
 
 He said that directions have been issued to all schools in this matter. This pattern is adopted by CBSE affiliated schools where Class XI academic session starts in April while the result of board exams comes in May.
