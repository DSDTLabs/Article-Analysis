AMRITSAR: A Panthic leader,  , has filed a complaint against 23 persons, including producer of movie 'Nanak Shah Fakir' Harinder Singh Sikka, with respect to their respective role in first allowing to make the film and then imposing a ban on it which, he claimed, had severely hurt the Sikh 

  The complaint also includes the names of Akal Takht jathedar Giani Gurbachan Singh, SGPC president Gobind Singh Longowal, and SAD president  . It has been filed with the Amritsar police commissioner, demanding action against them for hurting religious sentiments.
 
 The police are yet to act on the complaint and turn it into an FIR.
