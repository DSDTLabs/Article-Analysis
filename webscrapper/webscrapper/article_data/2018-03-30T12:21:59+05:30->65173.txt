HUBBALLI: To mark the International Women's Day, the   Chamber   organised 'The Women Towards Leadership,' a symposium, at its auditorium here on Wednesday. 

 Successful women entrepreneurs were felicitated on the occasion. Women displayed homemade products. Rotary Inner Wheel district chairperson   called upon women to come forward to set up large industries.
 
  officials and members of Rotary were present on the occasion.
