Jalandhar: A doctor and a computer operator of the Jalandhar civil hospital were killed and a nurse was injured when the car they were travelling in rammed into a tractor-trailer parked at Kathar village bus stand on the Jalandhar-Hoshiarpur highway.

 The deceased have been identified as Dr Surinder Pal and computer operator Pritpal Singh and the injured nurse has been identified as Jasbir Kaur. The accident took place at around 3.30 pm. Dr Pal died on the spot, Pritpal was declared brought dead when to the Adampur civil hospital. Jasbir also sustained serious injuries and was later shifted to the Jalandhar civil hospital.
 
 The driver of the tractor-trailer fled from the scene with his vehicle. Police have registered a case against the driver and have begun a search for him.
