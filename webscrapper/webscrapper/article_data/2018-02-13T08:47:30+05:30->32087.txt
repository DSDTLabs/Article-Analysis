LUCKNOW: Passengers injured while travelling in any of the   (UPSRTC) buses or buses plying on contract will get up to Rs 2.5 lakh for treatment.

  has come into effect from Friday last and applies to general,  ,  , Scania and private buses operating on contract. The medical compensation will be given only in case of a bus accident. Those who sustain minor injuries will be given Rs 25,000 and those with serious injuries Rs 1lakh. However, those who get critically injured, maimed or suffer over 50% burn injuries will get Rs 2.5lakh.
 
 The amount will be given post treatment, either against medical bills and vouchers verified by the concerned CMO or against fitness certificate issued by the concerned CMO. The scheme does not apply to chartered and city buses.
