PATNA: A group of around 70 heavily armed Maoists kidnapped eight workers of a Jharkhand-based private construction firm at   under Kharagpur police station area in Munger district on Wednesday night. The Maoists also torched seven vehicles at the construction site.

 
 Munger SP Gaurav Mangla said four of the   were released by the Maoists around midnight and four others were rescued on Thursday following a massive search operation launched by the police,   and SSB personnel.
 
 The four rescued have been identified as Ujjwal Kumar Singh, Deepak Kumar, Maksood Alam and Mohammad Hamid. Police sources said four heavy earthmovers, two trucks and a two-wheeler were torched by the Maoists.
