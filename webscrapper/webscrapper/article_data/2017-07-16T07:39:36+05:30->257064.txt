PUNE: There was a commotion in the general body meeting of the Pune Cantonment Board ( ) on Saturday over the installation a   of Chhatrapati   Maharaj in the office premises.

 Vice president   and board member   staged a walk out when president Brig Rajiv Sethi objected to Gaikwad’s higher pitch in the meeting.
 
 Gaikwad said, “The board has had enough discussion regarding the issue in the past. It is unfortunate that a decision has not been taken yet”.
 
 While replying to the vice president’s views, chief executive officer (CEO) D N Yadav said that the PCB had to take into consideration Supreme Court’s guidelines before finalizing the matter. 
 
 He further clarified that the board did not have any objection to the installation of the statue.
