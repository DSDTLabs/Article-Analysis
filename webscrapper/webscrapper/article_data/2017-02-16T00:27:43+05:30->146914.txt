MUMBAI: Power outage in parts of Borivli, Dahisar, Mira Road and Bhayander on Wednesday afternoon inconvenienced several citizens.

 Reliance Energy officials, who communicated with consumers over Twitter, said that the technical teams were “on the job and serving hard to restore supply at the earliest.” While the   in some areas lasted for nearly an hour, in other parts it was restored within two hours, sources said.
 
 Residents said that hot and humid weather added to the woes. Water pump operations were also hit. 
 
 Said a source from Reliance Energy: “The interruption in power supply was on the account of a HT (high tension) tripping. The field team worked on a war footing to restore supply.”
