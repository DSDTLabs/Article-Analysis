While   is gearing up for the release of his upcoming film, AR Murugadoss’ ‘Spyder’, his upcoming political drama ‘Bharat Ane Nenu’ is already making waves. 

 The shoot for the political drama is reportedly commencing at a brisk pace under the direction of Koratala Siva. But what’s making news is that the movie is being bought left and right with buyers reportedly betting on the Srimanthudu combo.
 
 Latest reports state that the movie was sold overseas to a leading distributor for a whopping sum of Rs 18.18 Cr. It’s almost the amount of what Pawan Kalyan and Trivikram Srinivas’ yet-to-be-named film ‘PSPK25’ was sold, at Rs 21 Cr. 
 
 The movie is said to be getting good offers from the Telugu states as well. Kiara Advani plays the female lead in the film, marking her Telugu debut. In ‘Bharat Ane Nenu’ Mahesh Babu will reportedly essay the role of a Chief Minister, while Sarat Kumar plays the role of his father.
