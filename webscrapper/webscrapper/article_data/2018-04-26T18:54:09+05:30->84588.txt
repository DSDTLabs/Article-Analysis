Panaji: The Goa Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education will declare the results of Class XII examination at 10am on April 28, board chairman Ramkrishna Samant said on Tuesday.  As many as 18,502 students registered for the examination in 2018, which is around 300 fewer than last year. The board has said that after the results are declared, certificates and mark/grade sheets of students will be distributed among schools on April 30 from 9.30am to 1pm. Students will start receiving their certificates only from May 1, following which the admission process in colleges will begin.  This year, the examination was held from March 5 to 26 across 16 centres.
