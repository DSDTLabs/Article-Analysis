Rajkot: Even in this hour of mourning and severe mental trauma, family members of five victims decided to donate their eyes. President of Bhavnagar district Koli community Narendra Gohil said, “It’s an extremely tragic incident for our community. But we have convinced the deceased’s family that this is a calamity for us but we can be helpful for others even in our most testing situation.” Those whose eyes were donated were Vasantben Makwana (40), Puja Makwana (16), Dinesh Parmar (40), his wife Jasi (37) and their daughter Varsha (16). tnn
