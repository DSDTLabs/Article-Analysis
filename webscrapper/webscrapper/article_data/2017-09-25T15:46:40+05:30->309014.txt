 The UP home department has already finalised the names of 100 jail inmates who would be released on Monday. These inmates have either completed their tenures and have not been able to pay the penalty or fine imposed by court or have been leading a peaceful life with good conduct.
 
 A home department official said that while 80 inmates are those who have been overstaying in jail, the remaining 20 were given life term and their plea for release was considered under provisions of the UP Prisons Release on Probation Act or following their mercy petitions.
