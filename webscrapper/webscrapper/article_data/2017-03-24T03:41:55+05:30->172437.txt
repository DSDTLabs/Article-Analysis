 The limits on expenditure by a candidate have been increased from Rs 5 lakh to Rs 5.75 lakh from 2017. 
 
 In the 2013 and the 2015 assembly polls, flying squads of the Election Commission and Income Tax department had nabbed people carrying cash and had seized huge amount of cash and liquor.
