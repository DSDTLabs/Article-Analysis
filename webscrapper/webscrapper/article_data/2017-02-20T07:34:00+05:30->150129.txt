PUNE: People travelling by public buses should brace themselves for a tough time on Monday and Tuesday as   Mahanagar Parivahan Mahamandal Limited (PMPML) will divert them for election duty. 

 Officials said Pune Municipal Corporation and   have sought 818 buses. “There are around 1,500 to 1,700 buses on the road. With most buses on election duty during the two days, there can be a shortage of buses for the people,”  , general manager (operations) of PMPML, said.
 
 On February 20, the buses will transfer EVMs to polling booths. The buses will remain busy for transportation of election personnel and carry back the EVMs after polling on February 21 to the counting centre.
