KOLHAPUR: Shivaji University, Kolhapur (SUK) will conduct entrance tests on August 16 for admission to its pre-guidance centre, which offers coaching in competitive exams.

 The university circular on July 26, stated that interested candidates will have to submit their applications by August 5 at the pre-guidance centre in the humanities building in the campus.
 
 An official from the centre said that the university’s pre-service centre offers coaching to students hailing from backward and minority communities. It was started from the year 2004. Students from reserved categories, and minority communities like Muslim, Christian, Sikh, Buddhist, and Jain are eligible for admission.
 
 According to the official, application forms for the same are available at the Humanities department in the campus.
 
 The centre receives financial aid from the   ( ) for its functioning. The centre provides coaching for civil service exams like the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) and   (MPSC) along with banking and other competitive examinations.
 
 Another university official said that the centre offers expert lectures or coaching on personality development and skill enhancement at the centre for the duration of one year. Classes are held daily for three hours. The focus remains mainly on group discussions on important happenings.
 
 Officials added that the centre is equipped with computers having internet facility, and there are as many as 1,200 books available at the library.
 
 “From the year 2005 to 2014, over 90 students have qualified for the exams and have been appointed in various positions,” said the official.
