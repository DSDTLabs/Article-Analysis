NEW DELHI: The much-awaited information on the Madhya Pradesh Board of Secondary Education (MPBSE Board) Class 10 examination is finally here. The examination for   students will begin in the month of March. 

 After much speculation on the internet regarding the date sheet of Board examination, the MPBSE Board has finally released the High School Leaving Certificate Examination 2018 dates for Board students on its official website - mpbse.nic.in. 
 
 
 
 
 The students appearing for the annual board examination can check the timetable of the MPBSE Board Examinations on the official portal of the MPBSE Board - mpbse.nic.in
 
 The official notification regarding the date sheet of   Board Exam is available on the official website of the MPBSE Board. 
 
 As the date sheet is now officially released by the MPBSE Board, the students can download it from the official website of the MPBSE Board - mpbse.nic.in. 
 
 The students are also advised to save and print their   Date sheet for further reference. The students appearing for MPBSE Board Examination should revise the important topics of their textbooks prescribed by the MPBSE Education Board. 
 
 The MPBSE Board Examinations are generally held in the month of February-March. The results of the MPBSE Board examination are announced in the month of May-June.
