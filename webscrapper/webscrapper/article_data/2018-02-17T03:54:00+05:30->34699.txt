Amaravati: Chairpersons of the Urban Development Authorities ( ) from the State met chief minister N Chandrababu Naidu at his office on Friday.

 The UDA chairpersons briefed the chief minister about their recent study tour to several UDAs and Industrial Development Authorities (IDAs) in the country. The UDA chiefs from the state visited the city and Industrial Development Authority, Navi Mumbai, Ahmedabad  , Haryana Urban Development Authority, Greater Noida Industrial Development Authority, and Jaipur Development Authority during their study tour.
 
 They explained to the chief minister about the issues that they have observed during their study tour and would ensure that those measures are adopted in the state. Minister for municipal administration and urban development P Narayana was also present when the UDA chairpersons met the chief minister.
