GUWAHATI:   (TU) has geared up to turn the summer vacation into a unique learning experience for school kids by teaching them robotics this July. 

 The premier varsity has come up with a host of activities to engage school students of Assamese-medium schools in extracurricular activities this summer. TU, jointly with the   sub-divisional administration, will organize a three-day summer camp for students belonging to rural schools of Kaliabor from July 14 to 16 at the university campus.
 
 The camp, which is expected to be attended by 150 students, will include a workshop on introduction to Robotics, a talk on 'Developing Scientific Temper' and 'Night Sky Viewing' by the Astronomy Club.
