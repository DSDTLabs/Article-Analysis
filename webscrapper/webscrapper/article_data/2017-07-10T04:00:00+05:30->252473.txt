Ahmedabad: Directorate General Resettlement (DGR) Seminar and   for the retired and retiring defence personnel was organised at   on Sunday in collaboration with the Confederation of Indian Industries (CII). 

 
 The event is a step to further the ‘Skill India’ initiative under the aegis of Directorate General of Resettlement, Ministry of Defence. Many firms from the corporate sector and around 2,500 ex-servicemen enthusiastically participated in the event. 
 
 AOC-in-Charge, SWAC Air Marshal R K Dhir, secretary of department of ex-servicemen welfare Prabhudayal Meena, Director General (Resettlement) Major General Jagatbir Singh and senior officials of the CII along with several military and civil dignitaries were present at the event.
