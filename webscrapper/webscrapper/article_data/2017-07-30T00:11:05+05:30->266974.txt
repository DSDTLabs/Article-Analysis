Panchkula: District Legal Services Authority (DLSA) on Saturday organized 14 special legal literacy camps in district Panchkula and 6 camps were organized in sub-divisional legal services committee, Kalka for creating awareness among women of their rights and entitlement under MNREGA.

 Chief judicial magistrate-cum-secretary DLSA Nidhi Bansal along with panel advocates attended the camp at Bareli village, Barwala in Panchkula. They apprised the gathering about legal-aid schemes and addressed groups of people such as women, poor, backward classes, weaker sections and senior citizens about the concept of rights of women and entitlements under MNREGA, child labour, fundamental duties among others.

 Sarpanch of the village was also present in the said camp in which women put their grievances and CJM Bansal directed the sarpanch and para legal volunteers to collect their applications and submit in the office of DLSA, Panchkula for further correspondence.
