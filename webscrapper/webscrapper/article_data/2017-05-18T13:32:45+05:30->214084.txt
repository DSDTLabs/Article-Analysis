NAGPUR: The   Administration (FDA) seized 3,871 kg of substandard and adulterated betel nut worth about Rs8.3 lakh.

 The betel nut was seized in a raid by FDA food inspectors from M/S SM Enterprises, Eknathsao Wadi, Tandapeth. The samples of the betel nut have been sent for laboratory analysis. 
 
 The betel unit, owned by Mahendra Baliram Hedau, was under watch for some time by FDA officials. Action will be taken against the trader as per the 2006 law of food safety depending on the analysis report, said FDA joint commissioner (food)  
 
 Deshpande said such planned raids will continue in future at all suspected places in the city. He has appealed to the people to inform FDA about any such illegal activity on phone number 0712-2562204.
