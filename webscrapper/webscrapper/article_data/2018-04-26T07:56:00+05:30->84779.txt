Chandigarh: A Chandigarh district court on Wednesday acquitted two persons in a   case. The court of district and session judge Balbir Singh acquitted Rocky (alias Bunty) and Neeraj, who were booked under sections 397 (robbery, with attempt to causing death or hurt) and 394 (voluntarily causing hurt in committing robbery) of the IPC.

 The accused were arrested by police on January 25, 2017. As per the complainant, Prakash, at 10.15pm, he along with two others -- Satdeep and Amandeep – was somewhere in Ramdarbar. A car owned by Satdeep was parked near his office. Parkash said that he had gone to take some documents from the car when it suddenly started raining. In a bid to protect himself, he took shelter under a nearby shed, which was already being used by the accused -- Rocky and Neeraj.
 
 It was then that a sudden argument broke out among them. “The accused then hurled abuses and started beating me,” Prakash said.
 
 He also said that the accused used iron rods in the brawl, which injured him in his head. The complainant stated that both the accused snatched a sum of Rs 32,000 from him.
