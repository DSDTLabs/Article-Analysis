Gandhinagar: Sanjay Joshi, former BJP national general secretary, visited Ahmedabad and Gandhinagar on Wednesday. Joshi, considered bete noir of PM Narendra Modi, visited former chief minister Keshubhai Patel’s home to express his condolences on the death of Patel’s son. Joshi took part in a few social events in Ahmedabad before leaving. Sources said state BJP cadre avoided meeting Joshi. Joshi said that the BJP government in Gujarat and Delhi is doing good work. “BJP will win 2017 assembly elections,” Joshi said. tnn
