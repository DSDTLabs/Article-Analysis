MADURAI: A 49-year-old man died while taking part in a pro-jallikattu   on Tamukkam ground in   on Sunday. 
 
 The deceased has been identified as Chandramohan, 49, of Nethaji Road in Jaihindpuram in Madurai. He was working as a receptionist at a private hotel here.
 
 The incident took place around 10am on Friday, while he was protesting along with his wife and daughters. 
 
 He was rushed to a nearby hospital where doctors declared him brought dead.
 
 A hardcore supporter of  , Chandramohan took part in the protest day and night. He had also taken a couple of days leave to lend his support to the ongoing statewide protest.
 
 Apart from actively taking part in the protest, he prepared food for protesters.
 
 Chandramohan is survived by his wife Hemalatha, a private school teacher, and two daughters.
