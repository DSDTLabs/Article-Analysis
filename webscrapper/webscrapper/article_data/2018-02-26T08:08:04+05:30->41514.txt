BHUBANESWAR:   Maheswar Mohanty courted a controversy on Sunday for his comments on Mahatma Gandhi. 

 Addressing a seminar on '  and  ' at a law college in Puri, Mohanty, also an MLA from Puri reportedly said, "Even if Mahatma Gandhi had to contest an election, he would have lost today, as he could not have offered money." 
 
 This was in response to leader of opposition Narasingha Mishra's allegations that voters were offered Rs 5,000 per vote in a recent election, with an apparent indication to the just-concluded Bijepur bypoll. On being contacted for further comments on the matter, Mohanty did not respond.
