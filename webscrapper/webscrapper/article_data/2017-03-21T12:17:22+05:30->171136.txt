SURAT: Around 300 students of   (GMC) took out a rally on   objecting to the demand of university preference in post-graduate   by   (GU) students. The   students joined in demand of other universities of state that counselling for PG medical courses should be carried out by common body without providing preference to any institute.

 The students carried out the rally with placard and banners and demanded that common counselling for PG should be conducted by state as per directives of centre. The students shouted slogans and made presentations to the officials of GMC. “Recently, Centre has ordered to hold common counselling. But GU students are demanding that university preference should be given so that the student who did his or her under-graduation from some institute get preference of the same institute for PG course,” said a protesting student.
