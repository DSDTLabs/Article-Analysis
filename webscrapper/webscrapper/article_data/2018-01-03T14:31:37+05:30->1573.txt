Coimbatore: Two persons were killed in as many road accidents in the city on Monday.

 In the first incident, Dhivakar, 23, of Gandhi Maa Nagar near  , was killed when the motorcycle he was riding hit the parapet wall of a flyover at Nanjundapuram. He was employed with a private firm in the city.
 
 According to Traffic Investigation Wing of the city police, Dhivakar was riding the motorcycle in a rash and negligent manner when he lost control over the vehicle and hit the parapet wall of the flyover. He was rushed to a private hospital at Ondipudur with severe head injuries. Later, he was shifted to the Coimbatore Medical College and Hospital, where he died without responding to the treatment. Based on his wife Madhumithra’s complaint, TIW police have registered a case.

He was thrown off from the motorcycle along with pillion riders P Socrates, 20, of Melur in Madurai and  , 21, of Virudhunagar district.
