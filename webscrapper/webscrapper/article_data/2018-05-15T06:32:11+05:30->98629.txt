 Rajiv Kumar, 45, a resident of Rattpur Colony, Pinjore, said he had known Balbir Singh, the accused, for a long time and discussed with him about purchasing a house. 
 
 In January 2013, Singh told Kumar that he owns a 5 marla house in Kharar (Mohali). Singh told Kumar that he was in dire need of money and interested to sell the house at a reasonable price. 
 
 Singh assured Kumar that as they were personally known to each other for several years, there would be no problem of any kind, Kumar alleged.
 
 The deal was finalised at Rs 83 lakh. Kumar paid Rs 26 lakh earnest money on May 28, 2013. 
 
 Singh later switched off his mobile phone and never came to make the deal. Later, Kumar came to know that Singh had invested the Rs 26 lakh in several properties in Pinjore.
 
 Police registered a case under Section 420 (cheating and dishonestly inducing delivery of property) of the Indian Penal Code at the Pinjore police station.
