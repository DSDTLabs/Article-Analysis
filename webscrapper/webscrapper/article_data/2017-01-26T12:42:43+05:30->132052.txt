Singer   says one of his biggest 'sorrows' is not being able to meet King of Pop  , but he praised his daughter Paris as a 'lovely girl'. 

  
  
 The 55-year-old singer opened up about his meeting with Paris on  's 'Watch What Happens Live'. "I told her that she was very beautiful and I told her that her dad was amazing, said that I never met him. It was one of my big sorrows that I never got to meet him. She was lovely, she's such a lovely girl," George said. 
 
 Paris recently opened up about her life with her legendary father. In the interview she said she believes her father was murdered.
