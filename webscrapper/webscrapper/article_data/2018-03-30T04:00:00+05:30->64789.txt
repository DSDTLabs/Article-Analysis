Ahmedabad: A team of Sabarkantha anti-corruption bureau (ACB) registered a complaint against four persons for allegedly using grazing land of a village for commercial activities and pocketing the rental income.

 The ACB started probing an application alleging corruption in the use of land at Harsol village as an asphalt plant was set up on grazing grounds. The probe revealed that Kalashiya Engineering, a private firm, had set up an asphalt plant on grazing land administered by the gram panchayat, for construction of a road nearby in 2005-06.
 
 The complainant named Rajendrakumar Raval, Jaya Raval, Somsinh Makwana and Ashwin Patel for the illegal actions.
