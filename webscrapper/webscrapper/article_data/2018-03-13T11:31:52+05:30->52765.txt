LUCKNOW: In a high-level meeting of   on Monday, infrastructure and industrial development commissioner   directed officials to immediately allocate land for a mega food park and mega skill development centre in Gorakhpur. The skill development centre will provide free training to enrolled candidates. “The meeting was convened to resolve pending cases related to industrial development in the state and to review development work for improvement of infrastructure. The commissioner directed that 65 cases related to various departments, including labour, environment, power, renewable energy, IT and electronics,   etc., be resolved at the earliest,” said an official.
