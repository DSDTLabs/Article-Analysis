NEW DELHI: The Champions Trophy was played between June 1-18. India's team is opened its campaign on June 4 at Edgbaston against Pakistan, followed by matches with Sri Lanka on June 8 and South Africa on June 11, both at Kennington Oval.  In the final match on June 18, Pakistan beat India with 180 runs. This is the first time Pakistan has won the ICC Champions Trophy. Four years ago, in 2013, an Indian team led by MS Dhoni lifted the Champions Trophy by beating hosts England.
