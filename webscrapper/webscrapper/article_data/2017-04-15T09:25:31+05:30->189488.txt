SURAT: A 22-year-old man was abducted by unidentified persons from Old Bombay   here on April 13. They demanded Rs1.80 lakh from the victim’s sister for his release. Varachha police suspect Aakash Naresh Marvadi may have been kidnapped over a financial dispute that had gone wrong.

 Sunita Marvadi, 30, the victim’s elder sister, who lives at  , received calls from the kidnappers who demanded Rs1.80 lakh from her for Marvadi’s release. 
 
 Police officers said, “It seems a financial dispute between two parties is the reason behind kidnapping of the victim. We would soon find the accused.”
 
 The victim lives in   slums in Varachha.
