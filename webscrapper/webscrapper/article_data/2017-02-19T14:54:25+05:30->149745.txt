 Speaking to newsmen here on Sunday, Chennithala said whenever any untoward incidents takes places, the party has the responsibility to protect and take care of its government. But at the same time, the party should not forget the agony of the people.
 
  When asked about the former chief minster Oommen Chandy who has been named as the fourth accused in the Pattoor land encroachment case by vigilance, Chennithala replied "I don’t think Chandy had any role in the Pattoor case and it will become clear once the investigation is over’’.
