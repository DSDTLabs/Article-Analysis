HYDERABAD: Armed dacoits barged into a factory at   in   district on Thursday and decamped with 1.5 lakh worth booty. 

 At 1 am, nine armed robbers barged into the  , when a handful of workers were present, at   industrial area. 
 
 "The dacoits bashed up three workers, threatened them with knives and stole 49 lead rods worth 1.5 lakh," Kothur sub-inspector A Srisailam told TOI. 
 
  The factory management told police that the stolen lead rods are used in batteries.
 
 Based on a complaint lodged by the factory management, Kothur police registered a case under section 395 (dacoity) of the Indian Penal Code (IPC).
 
  A manhunt has been launched to nab the offenders, who spoke in Telugu. Police suspect that the offenders have some local links.
