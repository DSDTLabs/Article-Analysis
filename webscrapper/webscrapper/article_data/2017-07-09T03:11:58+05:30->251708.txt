NEW DELHI:   has stepped up   measures at   Village, ahead of the Independence Day celebrations, in view of the recent terror attacks on foreign tourists in various European countries. 

 Police have asked restaurant and pub owners to ensure security of the foreign visitors and also act as eyes and ears of Delhi Police.
 
 “The Hauz Khas Village is visited by a large number of   every day. We want to ensure security for all. We have not stopped any restaurant from hosting parties or programmes,” said Ishwar Singh, DCP, south.
 
 Apart from employing additional security personnel and installing CCTV cameras, officers have requested the eatery owners to ensure that some of the employees keep an eye on suspicious persons around the area. 
 
 Police officers said they have also requested the excise department to conduct drives to check incidents of liquor being served to minors.
