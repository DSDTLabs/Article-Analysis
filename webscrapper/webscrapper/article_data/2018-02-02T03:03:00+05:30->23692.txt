Bicholim: A leopard was rescued from a well in Ardhawada, Mayem, at 5.45pm on Thursday and handed over to Bondla wildlife sanctuary.  The Rawle family from Ardhawada were shocked to discover the animal on Thursday morning when they went to fetch water. They immediately informed forest department officials. RFO Geeriesh Bailudkar along with a forest team rushed to the spot. Police and fire brigade personnel were also roped in. tnn
