Surat: Police booked a resident of Unn area under Sachin GIDC police station limits on Friday for not paying Rs 21,990 for a television set that he had purchased through an online shopping website. Arif Pathan alias Akram was booked for not paying the money to the website and also refusing to return the TV set. The complaint was lodged by Ambika Sharaf, an employee of the online shopping website. tnn
