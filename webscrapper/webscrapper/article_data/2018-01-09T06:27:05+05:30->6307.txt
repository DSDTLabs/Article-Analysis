CHENNAI: Tamil Nadu governor   on Monday expressed confidence in the Edappadi K Palaniswami-led   government. Delivering his first address as the state governor in the assembly on Monday, he said he hoped the state would be able to contain the fiscal deficit, in spite of the increasing revenue deficit.

 Purohit’s address was interrupted early, with DMK MLAs walking out after leader of the opposition M K   accused the governor of his failure to test whether the EPS government enjoyed majority support in the house. Amidst the din, rebel AIADMK leader and independent MLA,   sat through the session keenly following the governor’s address.
