JAIPUR: A 35-year-old person beggar living on the footpath near   area was stoned to death allegedly by another beggar late on Thursday night. Police have collected the   footage of the area to solve the   mystery. 

 According to the police, the body of a person was found near Amrapura area under   police station area. Blood was oozing out of the body and it appeared to be a murder. “We have sought the CCTV footage of the area to get any clues. It appears that it was a verbal spat that became violent in which the person was beaten to death. He was hit by a brick lying on the road,” said a senior officer. 
 
 Police have also recovered the blood stained brick that was used to hit the deceased. “We have asked the beggars and other people living on the footpath on the identification of the deceased. Only after identification the mystery is likely to be solved,” said an officer. 
 
 Police said that both the beggars have a violent spat.
