KANPUR: An elderly   was run over by a speeding   at   railway station on Sunday.

 GRP Etawah sub-inspector   said the couple along with their daughter had come to attend a marriage function on July 11. “On Sunday, they had come to board a Agra-bound train and reached Etawah railway station. However, the couple started crossing the track after they came to know that the train was coming on another platform.However, while crossing the track, the train crushed them to death, their daughter fortunately survived,“ the SI said.
