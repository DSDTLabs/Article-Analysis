 (Krishna district): Around 21 passengers escaped the jaws of death after a mini-van fell side-ways at a turning in Akkireddy Gudem village of Musunuru mandal in Krishna district on late Friday night.

 According to Musunuru police, the incident happened at around 10.50 pm, when the mini-van overloaded with 21 passengers lost balance on one side and fell sideways at a turning in Akkireddy Gudem. Cops said the vehicle was moving slowly and a major mishap was averted, however around five persons suffered minor injuries and were admitted at a local PHC for treatment and later referred to Vijayawada Government General Hospital. Cops said they seized a brand new Mahindra Supro. A case has been registered in this regard at Musunuru police station.
