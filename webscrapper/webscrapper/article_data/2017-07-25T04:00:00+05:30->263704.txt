Vadodara Municipal Corporation (VMC) opposition also targeted IAS officer Dr N K Meena at its general board meeting on Monday. Meena is deputy municipal commissioner at VMC. The opposition pointed fingers at Meena alleging that irregularities had taken place in the recruitment of engineers at the civic body. They claimed that Meena was responsible for the recruitment. They also alleged that the VMC was turning a blind eye to issue and not conducting a probe.
