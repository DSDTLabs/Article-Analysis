MUMBAI: A fire erupted underneath the   bridge near   station, affecting services on the   line on Sunday morning. No one was hurt. 

 The flames were noticed by the motorman of a Panvel-bound train which had arrived at Dockyard Road around 11 am. He used fire extinguishers to douse the blaze. 
 
 The fire brigade was also alerted which put out the fire.
 
 Railway officials said services were affected for 10 to 20 minutes. The cause of the fire is yet to be ascertained. T
