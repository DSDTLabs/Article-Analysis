Pune: The city police’s economic offences wing (EOW) on Wednesday seized five more cars belonging to arrested developer D S Kulkarni, also known as DSK.

 Assistant commissioner of police (EOW) Nilesh More told TOI that the five cars were seized from DSK’s Senapati Bapat Road bungalow.
 
 On February 26, the police had seized five imported cars and an imported motorcycle, totally worth Rs5 crore.
 
 DSK (68) and his wife, Hemanti (59), are in magisterial custody following their arrest on February 17 in a case of cheating. TNN
