LUCKNOW: A senior manager of a private   company was arrested on Wednesday for duping policyholders of Rs 1crore.

 Some policyholders had complained to the police about the case.
 
 Employeed with a company at Ratan Sqaure, Shariq Husain Kidwai had an account in a fake name and got cash belonging to others transferred into it, said ASP (west)  .
 
 Being a senior official, he knew the maturity period of policies of customers. To get cash earned against a policy, he gave a request with fake signatures of policy holders, demanding the company to transfer the cash in his account. As he himself used to deal such requests, he issued orders for the same and got the cash transferred.
