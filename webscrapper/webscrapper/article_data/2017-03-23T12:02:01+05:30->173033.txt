After tasting box office success with his period movie ‘Gauthamiputra Satakarni,’ Telugu star   has tied up with hit filmmaker   for his 101st movie.

 The actor will sport a new look in the movie and a Bollywood stylist has been roped in specially for Balakrishna. The flick will start its second schedule soon in Hyderabad and some of the integral scenes featuring lead actors will be shot in coming days. 
 
 The movie is expected for a world wide release in September.
