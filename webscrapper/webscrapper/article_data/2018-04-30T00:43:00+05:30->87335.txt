Coimbatore: Municipal administration minister SP Velumani on Sunday performed the Bhoomi Puja at Athupalam for the long-awaited Ukkadam Flyover.  The project would decongest traffic in about 2km-long stretch from Athupalam to Ukkadam. “There was a demand for the project for the past 30 years. It will be completed within 30 months,” Velumani said. Rs 216 crore was allocated for the project, which would be a four-lane way for 1.34km-long stretch from Athupalayam to UkkadamJunction and two-lane way for descending stretch from Ukkadam Junction to Oppanakara Street. Similarly, a two-lane way would rise from Town Hall and meet at Ukkadam Junction covering a distance of 284 metres.TNN
