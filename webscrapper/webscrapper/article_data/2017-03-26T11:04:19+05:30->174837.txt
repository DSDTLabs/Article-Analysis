CALANGUTE: A 58-year-old British tourist died while working out at a gymnasium in Calangute on Friday evening.

  Calangute police inspector   stated that  , 58, collapsed while exercising at a popular gym and was declared dead on the spot by a 108 ambulance staff who rushed to the gym. 
 
 The tourist, who was staying at a guesthouse in Maddawado, Calangute, had joined the gym last month and was a regular visitor.
 
 Calangute police have registered a case of unnatural death and are conducting further investigations. The body has been preserved at the Goa Medical College (GMC) and Hospital morgue, Bambolim, and the British embassy has been informed, PI Dalvi said.
