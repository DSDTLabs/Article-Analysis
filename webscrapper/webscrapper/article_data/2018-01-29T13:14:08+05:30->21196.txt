THIRUVANANTHAPURAM: The   is likely to conduct its syndicate meeting on February 6, 2018. 

 The meeting, which is being held after a series of arguments and counter arguments over the impact of the recent amendment of the Kerala university act, is expected to clear a number of academic and administrative proposals pending for a long period. 
 
 The last syndicate meeting too witnessed unceremonious scenes and protests over the alleged irregularities in the appointment if a candidate to teaching post in the university.
 
 The senate meeting, which is supposed to discuss the university budget, is scheduled to be held on March 16 and 17. 
 
 However, the term of the present Vice-Chancellor P K Radhakrishnan would expire on February 22. 
 
 State governor, who is also the Chancellor of the University, has recently set up a search panel for the selection of a candidate suitable for the post for the next four years.
