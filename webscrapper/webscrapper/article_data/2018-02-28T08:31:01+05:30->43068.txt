HUBBALLI: Irked by the number of political rallies and frequent visits of VVIPs like Prime Minister  , Congress president Rahul Gandhi, Union minister   and JD(S) state president  , some citizens of   have launched an online campaign demanding a ban on political events in city limits.

 In an online poll conducted on HubliCity-eGroup, a Facebook page, 320 members supported a ban, while 10 favoured such rallies. The forum has about 25,000 participants.
