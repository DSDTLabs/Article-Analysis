NEW DELHI: A   carrying 15 boys overturned near   in southwest Delhi’s Dwarka on Friday afternoon. Two of the students escaped with minor injuries while others remained unhurt. Police said that they have registered an FIR and the van’s driver has been arrested.

 Police said that the accused, identified as Rahul, was handed over to the police by a crowd that had gathered after the incident. The private school van overturned in the middle of the road while trying to overtake the van that was ahead of it. Eye witnesses told police that the car was speeding after which it suddenly overturned.
