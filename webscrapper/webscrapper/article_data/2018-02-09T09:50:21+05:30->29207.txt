The   (RBI) has declared the results for Office Assistant on the official website of the central bank. The   assistant results are available on the official website, rbi.org.in. RBI had invited applications for the recruitment of 526 posts of "Assistant" in various offices of the Bank.

 The selection for this post is being done through a country-wide competitive examination in two phases i.e. Preliminary (Prelims) and the Main examination followed by a Language Proficiency Test (LPT).
 
 RBI assistant exam was conducted online on January 5 and 6, 2018.
 
 
 
 1) Go directly to the results link of the RBI - https://opportunities.rbi.org.in/Scripts/resultsnew.aspx
 
 2) Click on "Recruitment of Office Attendant - Display of Roll Numbers of Provisonally Shortlisted Candidate" 
 
 3) On the new page that opens, click on the "Roll numbers of Provisionally Shortlisted Candidates" option. The link will directly take you to the pdf with the roll numbers of all the selected candidates
 
 4) Check for your Roll Number in the list
