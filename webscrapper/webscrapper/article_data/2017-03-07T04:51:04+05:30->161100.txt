GHAZIABAD: A 15-year-old boy was crushed to death by a truck near Sunder Deep Group of Institutions in Masuri, Ghaziabad around 3.30 pm on Monday afternoon.

 According to the police, the accident happened when the deceased, identified as Amar (15), a resident of Rajivpuram in Dasna, was headed to a fuel station in   his scooter. When Amar reached near  , he took a left turn and collided head on with the truck that was ferrying sand to a construction site. The impact of the collision was so strong that Amar came under the front wheels of the truck and was crushed to death. 
 
 Passersby immediately caught hold of the truck driver and later handed him to the police. The accused has been identified as 24-year-old Riyaz.
 
 An FIR under Section 304-A (Causing death by negligence) of the IPC was registered in Masuri police station and Riyaz was sent to judicial custody.
 
 Soon after the incident, the shocked and agitated relatives of Amar blocked traffic on the national highway and held demonstrations against the police and the administration demanding Rs 50 lakh as compensation for Amar's death. “The family members kept the traffic blocked for a long time. After a lot of persuation they called off their protest as we assured them of stringent action against the accused,” said Dinesh Yadav, SHO, Masuri Police Station.
