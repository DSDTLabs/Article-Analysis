Rajkot: Two persons including a woman were burnt to death after their house caught fire in Jamraval village in Jamkalyanpur taluka of Devbhoomi Dwarka district on Wednesday. The deceased were identified as Kajal Solanki (23) and her distant relative Dinesh Solanki.

 According to police, the accident took place when the duo were inside the house. Police said that the fire was caused by a short circuit in the electrical wire of the house.
 
 The fire spread so quickly that the duo could not escape and got engulfed in it. Both died on the spot, said police.
