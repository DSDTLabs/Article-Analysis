THIRUVANANTHAPURAM: The   state secretariat on Thursday termed the allegations against party state secretary  's son   baseless and said no cases of financial irregularities were pending against him in Dubai. The party had on Wednesday maintained that it had nothing to do with the allegation of cheating.

 "The media reports are about a financial dealing that happened in a foreign country, where the state government and the CPM have no role to play. There has been a calculated attempt to tarnish the image of Kodiyeri Balakrishnan and his son and defame the party," it said in a statement.
 
 Earlier in the day, Balakrishnan is learnt to have produced documents at a state secretariat meeting to claim that the allegation was baseless.
