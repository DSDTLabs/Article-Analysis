 The first bench comprising Chief Justice Sanjay Kishan Kaul and Justice M Sundar dismissed a PIL seeking a direction in this regard, on Friday, saying: "The award of Bharat Ratna to any person falls within the domain of the executive function and it is not for the court to issue directions as to who should be given the award or not."
 
 The PIL filed by K K Ramesh prayed for a direction to the Union home secretary and the relevant committee to confer Bharat Ratna on Jayalalilthaa, by considering his representation dated December 15, 2016.
