PUNE: Assuring that the situation was under control in Aurangabad, chief minister Devendra   on Saturday said action would be taken against the perpetrators of the violence on Friday night.

 “The perpetrators would be booked. We have also invoked Section 144,” Fadnavis said, adding that action would be taken against those spreading rumours.
 
 Speaking about the incident on the sidelines of an event in the city, the chief minister said there was enough police deployment and the overall situation was peaceful after the intervention of the district officials and police.
 
 “I would like to appeal to the people of Aurangabad to remain peaceful. Do not take law in your hands and do not believe in rumours,” Fadnavis said. TNN
