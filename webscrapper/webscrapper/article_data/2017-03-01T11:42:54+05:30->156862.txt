KOLKATA: Chief minister   will now turn her gaze to private hospitals and nursing homes in the districts. Sources in the state secretariat said   would meet the authorities of the private health facilities during her visit to the respective districts.

 The chief minister, who met the heads of private hospitals and health facilities in Kolkata last week and pulled them up for a host of allegations, plan to repeat the exercise in the districts.She decided on the move after innumerable complaints against inflated bills, lack of transparency in charging, unnecessary medical tests and bodies not being released till dues were cleared poured in,   sources said.
 
 The chief minister usually visits the districts frequently and holds administrative meetings on a quarterly basis.
