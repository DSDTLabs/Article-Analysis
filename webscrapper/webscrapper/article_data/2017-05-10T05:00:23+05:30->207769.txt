RAJKOT: Acting on a tip off,   police seized Indian Made Foreign Liquor ( ) and beer bottles worth Rs 36.85 lakh from a house in   village in   taluka of Jamnagar on Monday night.

 According to police, there were total six accused at the time of raids but they all managed to escape from the spot.
 
 Head constable Digvijaysinh Jadeja lodged a complaint against Jetha Mori (a member of district panchayat), his brother Kana Mori, Ramesh Mori, Vijay Rabari, Kishor Gadhvi and Paresh Patel under various sections of Gujarat Prohibition Act.
