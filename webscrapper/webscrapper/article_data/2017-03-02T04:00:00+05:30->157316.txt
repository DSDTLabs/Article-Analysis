Surat: A 75-year-old man died while his wife was hospitalized after the couple consumed acid to end their lives on the Tapi River bank at Modi Mohalla on Ashwini Kumar Road in Varaccha on Tuesday night. 

 The deceased was identified as Ratna Kalsariya while his 70-year-old wife Balu was admitted to Surat Municipal Institute of Medical Education and Research (SMIMER) in a critical condition.
 
 In his dying declaration in presence of a magistrate, Kalsariya said that they were fed up of prolonged illness resulting from diabetes and blood pressure. They were under medication for several ailments including chest pain. The couple lived with their only son. tnn
