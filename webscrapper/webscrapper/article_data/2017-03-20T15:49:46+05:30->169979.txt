 Besides being a love story, ‘Badrinath Ki Dulhania’ also deals with the evils of a regressive society in north India, and touches upon the subjects of dowry and patriarchy. The duo has earlier worked in hits like ‘Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania’ and ‘Student Of The Year’.
