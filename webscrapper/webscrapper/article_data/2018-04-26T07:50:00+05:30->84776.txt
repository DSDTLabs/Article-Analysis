Chandigarh: Outsiders barged into DAV College, Sector 10, on Wednesday and attacked a few students.

 The students alleged they were protesting peacefully outside the administrative block of the college over some demands when the accused armed with knives attacked them.
 
 College students alleged it was a fight among members of two different student parties.
 
 Police have not registered any case so far. Inspector Sher Singh of Sector 3 police station said the students were taken for a medical examination. “We have registered a DDR (daily diary report). The accused will be rounded up soon.”
