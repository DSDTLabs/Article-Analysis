KOLKATA: A blaze gutted a   on   premises as the iconic institution celebrated its 200 years. 

 According to eyewitnesses, fire broke out at around 6.10am and five fire tenders took around 1 hour to douse the flames. 
 
 The cafeteria which is around six years old is located on the rear of the historic administrative building of the university. 
 
 Some students spotted the flames and alerted the officials of the university. According to firemen, an electrical short circuit was the reason of the fire. The resgistrar of the university and other officials reached the university soon after receiving the news of the fire spread.
