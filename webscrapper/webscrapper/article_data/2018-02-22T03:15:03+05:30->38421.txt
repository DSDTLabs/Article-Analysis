NEW DELHI: Any   compliant vehicle manufactured before April 2020 will not be registered after June 30 that year, the road transport ministry has notified in its bid to avoid any crisis. 

 Mini buses, cargo vehicles and normal buses which are sold in the form of drive away chassis manufactured before April 2020 will not be registered after September that year. The   emission norms kick in from April 2020.
 
 The ministry has notified these norms after taking a cue from the past experience when   suddenly ordered ban on the registration of BS-III vehicles from April 1, 2017.
