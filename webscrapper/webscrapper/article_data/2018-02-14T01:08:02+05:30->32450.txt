Erode: A three-year-old male   was found ill at Eakathur in Sathyamangalam forest division on Tuesday. Officials from the Sathyamangalam forest division rescued the calf. The forest officials were informed about the sick elephant calf starving for food and water at Eakathur.

 The forest officials and veterinary doctors reached the spot. They administered vitamin injection and glucose to the calf. The doctors said the calf looked very weak due to dehydration. “The calf regained strength after we administered vitamins,” a veterinary doctor said. The forest officials said they will continue their treatment to the calf until it starts moving on its own. tnn
