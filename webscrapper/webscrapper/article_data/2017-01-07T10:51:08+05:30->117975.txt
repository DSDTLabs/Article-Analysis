JAIPUR: The sleuths of   ( ) on Friday arrested a   (JDA) clerk for accepting a bribe of Rs 3,000 for issuing a lease deed to a complainant. 

 According to ACB’s additional Bajrang Singh Shekhawt, the anti-corruption watchdog arrested RN Agarwal who was deputed at JDA’s zone 11. 
 
 “The complainant had received a plot from the JDA through lottery in a housing scheme for economically weaker sections (EWS) but his possession was getting delayed as he couldn’t obtain his lease deed,” Shekhawat added.
 
 After running from pillar to post to obtain the deed, somebody suggested the complainant to meet Agarwal. 
 
 “The complainant came of a economically weaker section and couldn’t shell out hefty amount demanded by Agarwal, therefore, the complainant agreed to pay Rs 3,000 out of which Rs 1,000 was already paid to Agarwal,” Shekhawat added.
 
 On Friday morning, the sleuths of ACB laid a trap and arrested Agarwal red handed while accepting a bribe of Rs 2,000. 
 
 The ACB suspect that Agarwal was operating in connivance with officials of the department and therefore his office and home were raided by a team of ACB official later in the day.
 
 “Some documents have been sized by us from his office and a team is also searching his house in order to find more evidence against him, meanwhile, a case under prevention of corruption act has been filed against the accused ,” a senior ACB official told TOI.
 
 The ACB suspect the role of other people within JDA who might have took bribes from other people in similar cases. “The complainant in this case couldn’t afford hefty amounts therefore Agarwal reduced the bribe, but in other cases he might have taken huge amount of money to clear important papers,” added another senior official .
