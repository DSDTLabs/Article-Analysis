 A number of employees, accompanied by other teachers, went to New Colony police station and staged a protest, demanding immediate arrest of the accused. In her complaint to the police, the manager alleged that the teacher not only thrashed and molesteD her but also threatened to kill her. Based on her complaint, the police first detained the teacher for questioning. Later, an FIR was lodged against the teacher under sections 323 (causing hurt), 354A&B (molestation), 506 (criminal intimidation), 509 (act intended to insult the modesty of a woman), 506 (criminal intimidation) of the IPC.
 
 School sources claimed a dispute had been brewing on the campus for some time with members of the old and new management fighting for supremacy. 
 
 “We are verifying the allegations. If a complaint is filed, we will also take action,” said inspector Babu Lal, SHO, New Colony police station.
