LUDHIANA: Three men, including two in police uniform, allegedly looted Rs 4 lakh from a manager of a private firm in Ludhiana. The incident happened on April 29. In his compliant to police,   alleged that on April 29, an employee of their   branch came to his house to hand over Rs 4 lakh of the company. A little later, three men entered his house and asked him to hand over the money. “When I refused to do so, they slapped me and took away the bag of cash,” he told the police.

 Vindo Kumar, SHO at Division no 5 police, said, “Te complainant did not raise an alarm or informed the police soon after the incident. However, we are probing the matter from all angles.”
