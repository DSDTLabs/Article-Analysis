GANDHINAGAR: The state’s   details will now be available on mobile applications. State revenue minister   on Wednesday launched the https://anyror.gujarat.gov.in/ portal, on which revenue records (village Form No.6) details of all 18,595 villages will be available online. People can view them from anywhere. Official certified prints will be available on E-Dhara and at E-Gram centres immediately, said Chudasama.

 Chudasama said that government authorities took a lot of time earlier, to provide old records, but everything will be available online now.
 
 The minister also launched a ‘Know Your Revenue Case’ ( ) mobile application, to know the latest status of any case pending with the revenue appellate authority, Ahmedabad.
