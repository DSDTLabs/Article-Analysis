The event, which took place in Kormanagala, saw the presence of PB Acharya, Governor of Nagaland as the chief guest. Some of the artistes who performed were Lama Tashi, Swarathma, Pandita Anuradha Pal, Alobo Naga and the band, Red Light Passsengers, DJ Karma, LC Sekhose and Colored Keys among others.
