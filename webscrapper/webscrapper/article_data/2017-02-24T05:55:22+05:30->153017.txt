NEW DELHI: Directorate of Education has directed all   in the capital to organise a parent-teacher meeting on Saturday for Class XII students, ahead of the Board exams.

 According to a circular sent to all schools, the directorate wants the parents to be aware about their ward's performance in pre-board exams and subsequent tests. The teachers are also going to give specific guidance and counselling to each student.
 
 “The teachers and head of schools will motivate the parents to give full moral support and an appropriate environment at home to their wards,“ read the circular. Members of school management committees have also been asked to attend the  .
 
 The meeting will take place in two shifts -8am to 9am and 5.30pm to 6.30pm. The parents will be informed about the PTM through messages and notices.
