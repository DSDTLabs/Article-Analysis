 An FIR has also been lodged against the accused under Section 8 (sexual harassment) of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences ( ) Act and Section 506 (criminal intimidation) of IPC at Bilaspur police station, following a complaint by four students of class VIII. Police teams have been formed to nab the accused, who is on the run, cops said.
 
 As the news spread, scores of parents staged a protest at the school in the morning, demanding safety of their children. They shouted slogans against the school authorities for failing to prevent the alleged sexual harassment of girl students that had been going on for some time and demanded immediate arrest and termination of the accused. 
 
 Senior police officers rushed to the spot and pacified the protesters. They finally dispersed after cops assured them of prompt action in the case.
