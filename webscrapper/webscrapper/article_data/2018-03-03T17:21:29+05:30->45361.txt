NEW DELHI: Employees State Insurance Corporation   has invited application to fill up the Teaching Faculty positions - Professor, Associate Professor and Assistant Professor for its colleges located in various parts of India. 

 
 The last date to apply is April 2, 2018. 
 
 The ESIC's Selection Board will conduct the interview to select the suitable candidate. The ESIC will publish information regarding the candidates called for interview and result of the interview on its official website www.esic.nic.in. Also, candidates will get their call letters through email, provided at the time of registration, for appearing before the Selection Board.
