PUNE: A 40-year-old woman was run over by a   at Indrayaninagar in   on Monday morning.

 The accident occurred when the woman, Ashabai Pole of Chakrapani Vasahat, was walking towards her workplace at the Bhosari MIDC. The police have arrested the crane operator, Anil Saravade (29), for negligent driving.
 
 An officer from the Bhosari MIDC police said Pole was working with a private company in the MIDC. Around 9.45am, she on her way to work when the speeding crane hit her. “The impact was such that Pole fell on the ground and was crushed under one of the wheels of the crane,” the officer said.
 
 Some local residents took her to a nearby hospital, where she was declared dead on arrival by the doctors. “We have arrested the driver. Our probe is on,” the officer said.
