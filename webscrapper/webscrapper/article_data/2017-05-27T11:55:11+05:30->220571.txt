GHAZIABAD:   on Thursday night arrested two miscreants who were involved in stealing gold and silver jewellery worth lakhs from locked houses in the last few months.

 The accused have been identified as Vaseem, who is a resident of Sundarnagri, New Delhi and Rihan who is a resident of  . Both the accused are over 22 years of age.
 
 Silver and gold jewellery worth several lakhs, one Apache motorcycle and two county made pistols (315 Bore) have been recovered from their possession by the police.
 
 "The accused used to recce the posh residential complexes in Ghaziabad to look for locked houses which would prove out to be easy targets for them and then attack them the next night," said Sudhir Tyagi, SHO, Sahibabad Police Station.
 
 The accused also used to loot people of their valuables on gunpoint around the Mohanagar and Rajender Nagar area of Sahibabad.
 
 "We were looking for them because lately various incidents of theft and loot were being reported from all over Sahibabad and their arrest was imported," police said. 
 
 An FIR under Section 356 (Assault or criminal force in attempt to commit theft or carried by a person) and 379 (Punishment for theft) of the IPC has been registered against the accused in   and the miscreants have been sent to judicial custody.
