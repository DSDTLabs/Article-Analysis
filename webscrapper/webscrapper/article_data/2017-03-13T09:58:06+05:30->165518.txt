LUCKNOW: Two youths riding a bike were killed when a speeding car knocked their bike from behind, in Madiaon on Sunday morning. 

 According to police, Jitendra Paswan (23) of Khanpur village of Itaunja along with Ashok Yadav (22) of the same village in Mutakkipur village when the car coming from the opposite direction hit them. 
 
 Villagers rushed to the accident site and called the police. Some of them chased the car and overpowered the driver and owner Ashok Rai of Madiaon. He has been arrested under charges of causing death by negligence and rash driving. He works in a chemical factory in Gajraula.
 
 The victims were declared brought dead at KGMU.
