IMPHAL: A bomb was recovered here on Tuesday ahead of the visit of Congress leader   for campaigning for the   assembly elections.

 Police said the bomb was found near the gate of a residential building around 6am in Khongman Bashikhong Turel Mapal area in   East district. The bomb was later defused.
 
 The bomb was recovered about 6 km from the venue where the Congress Vice President is scheduled to address a rally.
 
 Rahul Gandhi will campaign for Congress candidates at Hapta Kangjeibung ground.
 
 Elections in the state are slated for March 4 and 8.
