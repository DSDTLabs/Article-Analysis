KOCHI: Vadakkekkara police on Friday arrested two people for allegedly brewing   arrack.

 The arrested are   (32) and   (36), both residents of Pattanam. The duo was arrested after police got information about brewing arrack behind a house at a colony in Pattanam. Police seized utensils for brewing from the spot.
 
 Police raided the secluded area in the early hours of Friday and found five people engaged in making illicit liquor.
 
 Three of the suspects escaped by jumping into a nearby river. Police said that the accused used to supply arrack to wedding functions and other parties. They said the absconding accused would be arrested soon. TNN
