NEW DELHI: Equity markets buoyed by an impressive showing on Monday, kept pushing ahead in Tuesday's early trading even as   and   scaled down from their opening levels.

 
 The 30-share BSE index opened at 35,349.85, up more than 140 points and the broader 50-share Nifty gained almost 50 points to start trading above 10,750. However, both the indices erased a majority of the early gains and had fallen back nearly to previous closing levels.
 
 On Monday, the markets had surged on back of bank stocks which as a sector gained more than 2 per cent. 
 
 In Tuesday's morning trade, major sectoral sub-indices with the exception of pharma, were struggling.
