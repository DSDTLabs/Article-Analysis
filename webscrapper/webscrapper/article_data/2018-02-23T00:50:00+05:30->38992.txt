Coimbatore: The 76th annual conference of All India Ophthalmological Society (AIOC) 2018 commenced at Codissia trade fair complex on Thursday.

 The four-day conference, organised by the Tamil Nadu Ophthalmic Association and Coimbatore Society of Ophthalmic Surgeons, was inaugurated by Subroto Bagchi, chairman, Odisha Skill Development Authority along with B K Krishnaraj Vanavarayar, chairman, Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan, Coimbatore, and P Namperumalsumy, chairman emeritus, Aravind Eye Care System, Madurai.
 
  The event comprised technical skill transfer course, surgical skill transfer course, instruction courses, symposia, presentation of scientific papers, posters and videos.
