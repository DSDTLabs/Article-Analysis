Kolkata: A Class-XII student of Army Public School in Barrackpore, junior under officer Rittik Kumar Jana, was awarded ‘Best of the Best Cadet’ of the Air Force wing on NCC Republic Day Camp 2017 that took place in Delhi. Jana received his   from Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Saturday at the Prime Minister Rally 2017.
