GURUGRAM: Four children lost their lives after fire broke out in a slum on Friday late night in  .

 In the age group of 5 to 14 years, two girls and two boys got severe burns at night. They were rushed to Civil Hospital early morning, but were declared brought dead.
 
 An FIR has been filed against unidentified persons and a team of Gurugram police is currently at the spot to investigate the case. As of now, no official statement has been released on how fire broke out in the slum.
 
 Over 200 slums have been destroyed in the fire.
