LUCKNOW: How many   died due to fire at   on Saturday evening? While media reports and attendants’ claims put the number at eight, the hospital administration said that no patient died due to fire.

 Santosh Trivedi (40) from Rae Bareli had been operated upon in the neurosurgery unit on Friday . He was responding well to treatment. However, just after fire, when he was being shifted to Shatabdi Hospital, he breathed his last, say his relatives. Santosh’s brother-in-law Sri Krishna Tiwari said, “We were asked to shift him on our own. There was panic everywhere and doctors just took off his oxygen mask. When we reached the entrance of the building, he barely managed to take two deep breaths and died on the spot.”
