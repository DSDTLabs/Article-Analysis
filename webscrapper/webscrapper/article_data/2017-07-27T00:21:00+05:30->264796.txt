Margao: South Goa collector Swapnil Naik has been transferred and posted to the AYUSH ministry, government of India. Anjali Sehrawat, IAS, has been appointed as the South Goa collector, states an order issued by under secretary (personnel-I) Shashank Thakur, of the department of personnel, government of Goa.  While the order states that Naik’s order was being issued separately, sources said that Naik has been transferred to the AYUSH ministry and appointed as the personal secretary to AYUSH minister Shripad Naik.  Naik served as the South Goa collector for 14 months. Sources said that Naik was selected by the AYUSH minister some time ago, and following the former’s consent, the transfer order was issued.
