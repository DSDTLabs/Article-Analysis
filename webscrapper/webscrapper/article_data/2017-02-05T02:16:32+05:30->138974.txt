GURUGRAM: A trader was robbed of Rs 3,500 and mobile phone at gunpoint by two bike-borne youths on   on Friday night. 

 Traders of the area met the police commissioner on Saturday and demanded the immediate arrest of the accused.
 
 Cops said Gyan Chand Mangla was returning home from his grocery shop around 9.30 pm on Friday on his two-wheeler when he was waylaid by the miscreants on bikes, who robbed him of the money and his mobile phone at gunpoint.
 
 Based on his complaint, an FIR has been registered under Section 379A (snatching) of IPC and sections 25-54-59 of Arms Act at City police station. “We are on the job and trying to identify the accused with the help of CCTV cameras in nearby area,” said sub-inspector Samsuddin, additional SHO of City police station.
