New Delhi: A gang of criminals that was planning to open their own mobile   in Rohini was arrested from Kirari on Monday. Around a dozen expensive stolen smartphones were recovered from them.

 DCP (outer) MN Tiwari said that a team led by inspector Sukhbir Malik arrested the criminals. The accused have been identified as Satish, Vijay, Sachin and Sachin Kumar.
 
 The accused told cops that they were in the process of buying a shop in a mall in Rohini. They started committing thefts and snatching of high-end mobile phones to stock supply. Cops said that the gang would also use chloroform to knock people unconscious after targeting them in desolated places during the night. TNN
