 Divakaran made this comparison while attending the funeral of his sister-in-law Santhanalakshmi at Thanjavur.
 
 Santhanalakshmi, wife of Sasikala’s elder brother TV Sundaravadanam, passed away in Chennai on Thursday. She was the mother-in-law of party deputy general secretary TTV Dhinakaran. Her mortal remains were brought to Thanjvaur for last rites. 
 
 Divakaran said it is a testing time for the party. However, he expressing confidence in saving the party. Divakaran ruled out differences with his nephew, Dhinakaran.
 
 Asked about the absence of many party functionaries at the funeral, he said that there was no compulsion on party cadre to attend the funeral.
 
 When asked about the absence of many AIADMK party functionaries, Dhinakaran refused to comment saying he was not interested in speaking politics at a funeral.
 
 Appealing to not to politicize absence of state ministers at the funeral, Dhinakaran said, “Some of them condole the loss of our family member over phone, and some are coming in person.”
