PANAJI: A low pressure area at 0.9 km above mean sea level from north central   to   across   and north interior   is likely to yield light rain or thunder showers at isolated places in Goa on Sunday and Monday.

 The Indian meteorological department (IMD), Goa had forecast by April 2 that light rain or thunder showers were likely after April 5. A low-pressure area was forming over Comorin in south India, extending towards north-west.
 
 “Light rain or thunder showers are likely to occur at isolated places over both districts of north and south Goa on Sunday and Monday,” an official at the meteorological centre,  , said. Due to the trough of low pressure, there will be moisture incursion over the region and rainfall is likely.
