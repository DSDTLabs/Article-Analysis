NEW DELHI: Staff Selection Commission ( ) Combined Higher Secondary Level (CHSL) Tier I Examination 2017 is going to be held from March 4, 2018. 

 As per available information, the SSC CHSL 2017 Tier I Examination Admit Cards are expected to be uploaded today on the official website of SSC - ssc.nic.in. 
 
 The candidates are notified that the examination would be conducted from March 4, 2018, to March 26, 2018, as mentioned in the initial notification.
 
 All the candidates who have filled the application form for SSC CHSL 2017 Tier I exam are informed that there is no official confirmation on the release date of the admit card at present. 
 
 However, as per various sources, the SSC CHSL 2017 Tier I admit card will be available for download today or latest by tomorrow. 
 
 Candidates are requested to keep checking the official website for latest update. 
 
 The candidates can follow the steps given below to download their SSC CHSL 2017 Tier I admit card once it's available on the official website:
 
 1) Open the official website of SSC - ssc.nic.in
 
 2) On the homepage of the website, there will be a link "SSC CHSL 2017 Tier I Admit Card/Hall Ticket". Click on the link
 
 3) A pop-up page will appear, enter your SSC CHSL 2017 Tier I exam application number, date of birth, security pin and click on login. 
 
 4) Your SSC CHSL 2017 Tier I admit card will be displayed on the screen. Carefully check all your details such as name, paper, date of birth, gender, test centre name, city, state, code of eligibility and category etc. mentioned on the admit card. 
 
 5) You can download it and take a printout for future reference.
