 Chandigarh: The Chandigarh district court acquitted 10 persons of rioting in a matter related to fight inside Burial jail. Two FIRs had been registered against those involved in the fight.
 
 The court of civil Judge Harleen Pal Singh acquitted them under charges 147 (punishment for rioting), 149 (every member of unlawful assembly guilty of offence committed in prosecution of common object) of IPC and Section 3 (mischief causing damage to public property) of Prevention of Public Property Damage Act. The witness turned hostile in the matter and also both the parties compromised with each other and it lead to acquittal of all 10 persons.
 
 The matter dates back to November 2016 whereby a fight took place inside the jail between two groups consisting of 10 prisoners. The fight started over calling over mobile phone. The persons who stand acquitted are namely Sanjeev, Pardeep, Sukhdev, Varinder, Akash, Sonu, Manoj, Gurpreet, Pradeep and Kamal Thapa.
