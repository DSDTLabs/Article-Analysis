NOIDA: District magistrate B N Singh issued a directive on Tuesday to all hotels, guest houses and paying guest accomodation owners of the district to get their business registered under the  , 1867, of the state government to avoid penal action against them.

 “The owners of hotels, guest houses and PG accommodations have been instructed to get their property registered through a prescribed application at C block office of the district magistrate in Sector 19. If they fail to do so, action will be taken,” said Singh. 
 
 As per the Sarai Act 1867, all hoteliers and commercial PG accommodations are supposed to submit a copy of the trade licence to the district administration as proof of their work.
