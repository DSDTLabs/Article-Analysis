NEW DELHI: The  , Central University, has released the admit cards for its different Undergraduate, Postgraduate, Doctorate, Diploma and Certificate programmes for the academic session 2018-19.

 This year the university received 1,56,420 applications, a rise of around 15% as compared to last year
 
 Through the JMI University entrance exam, the candidates can get the admissions to the 72 Masters, 12 PG Diploma, 45 Undergraduate, 11 Advanced Diploma, 30 Diploma, 24 Certificate and MPhil/PhD courses.
 
 The candidates seeking admission in various courses of JMI are required to qualify the entrance test. 
 
 It may be noted that the admission into UG engineering and architecture courses will be done on the basis of JEE Main and admission into BDS course will be done through NEET. 
 
 Last year, the JMI University did not provide direct link for downloading the admit card, the students downloaded their admit cards through login.
 
 
 
 
 
 The candidates have to paste their recent passport size self-attested photograph in a specified place. 
 
 The students are required to print two copies of online generated admit card.
