GURUGRAM: Thieves broke into another Indicash ATM booth in the city on Saturday, though the machine targeted this time was empty. 

 The   was reported in  , where another ATM machine having Rs 8 lakh was stolen barely a week ago. An FIR has been registered against unidentified accused at Badshahpur police station. 
 
 Police said over a dozen thieves arrived at the closed ATM booth in a pickup van in the early hours of Saturday. They broke open its locks and tied a length of rope around the ATM machine. They tied the other end to the van and uprooted it, and fled with the machine.
