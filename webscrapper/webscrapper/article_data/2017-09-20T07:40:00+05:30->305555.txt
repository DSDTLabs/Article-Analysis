Chandigarh: Punjab local government department on Tuesday dismissed executive officer Bhupinder Singh Sran who was found guilty of corrupt practices by a lower court recently.

 Local government minister Navjot Singh Sidhu in a statement said that the vigilance bureau had in 2014 registered a case against Sran in which a court recently pronounced him guilty. Sran had remained suspended for the last many months.
 
 A corruption case was registered against Sran after the complainant of Rama Mandi. Sran was earlier posted as an executive officer in municipal council Rampura Phul in 2012. Thereafter, he remained suspended for a considerable period of time before being reinstated in 2014.
