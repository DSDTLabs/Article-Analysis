GREATER NOIDA: Nearly seven years after they were allotted land by the Yamuna Expressway Industrial Development   ( ), relief is in store for about 21,000 allottees. The first lot of 55 allottees have been issued   letters by the Authority under the Residential Plot Scheme, 2009. 

 Officials said another 1,600 allottees will be handed over possession by mid-2017, while around 5,000 a year later. “The plots allotted are located in A and B blocks of Sector 20 in YEIDA,” said  , additional CEO, YEIDA. Officials said another 4,833 plots will be delivered to owners by end of 2017 and mid-2018.
