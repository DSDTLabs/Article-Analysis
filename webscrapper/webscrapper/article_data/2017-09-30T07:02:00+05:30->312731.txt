NOIDA: The Noida-Delhi carriageway of the Mahamaya-Kalindi Kunj route will remain shut for commuters from Saturday afternoon till the completion of the idol immersion ceremony at Kalindi Kunj ghat and gaushala in Sector 94.

  police said they will divert the traffic approaching from Noida towards Chilla border and DND Flyway from around 2 pm till the time the celebrations end.
 
 “Processions are expected to line up near ghat towards afternoon and traffic will be diverted as it suits the demand of the situation,” traffic inspector Layak Singh told TOI.
