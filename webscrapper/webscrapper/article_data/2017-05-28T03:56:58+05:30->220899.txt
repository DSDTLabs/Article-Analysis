GHAZIABAD: The   ( ) is conducting a survey of all schools across six zones in the city for the safety measures taken by them following a recent complaint by the   (ASPA). The complaint had pointed out that in violation of Supreme Court orders, primary wing classes of some schools were allegedly being held in the basement some others had allegedly installed mobile towers atop school buildings.

 The GDA intends to crack down on schools found flouting safety guidelines and will issue notices to them after the survey.
 
 Dayanand Prasad, officer on special duty, GDA, said, “As of now we are in the process of carrying out a survey of schools flouting safety norms and by next week we will get a complete picture on the number of erring schools after which we will issue notices.”
