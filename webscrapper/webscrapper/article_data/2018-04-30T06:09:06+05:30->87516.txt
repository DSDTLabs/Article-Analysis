PONDA: The Ponda municipal council (PMC) election witnessed a voter turnout of 76.43% on Sunday. The election process was peaceful and there was no untoward situation in any area, said returning officer  . 

 
  
 
 The total percentage of male voters was 76.33% and female was 76.53%. Of 8,055 male voters, 6,148 cast their votes and out of 7,920 female voters, 6,061 exercised their franchise.
