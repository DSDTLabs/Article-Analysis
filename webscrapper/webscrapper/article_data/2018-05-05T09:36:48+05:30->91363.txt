NEW DELHI: The exam conducting authority has released the Admit Card 2018 for the Under Graduate Entrance Test,   has been released on the official website,  .

 
 COMEDK   can be downloaded from the official website, by using user ID and password, from May 4-12, 2018.
 
 It is important for candidates to check all the details mentioned on the admit card of COMEDK UGET 2018 like name, roll number, exam centre, date, time and venue etc. before downloading it. 
 
 The examination is set to be conducted in online mode on May 13, 2018 for admission to various engineering courses at member COMEDK institutions across Karnataka.
