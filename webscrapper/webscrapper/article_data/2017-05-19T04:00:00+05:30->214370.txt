GANDHINAGAR: The   BJP has organised a listening session of Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s radio programme ‘Mann Ki Baat’ on May 28 at 48,000 election booths across the state where the BJP workers will gather and listen to the PM’s speech on radio.

 The state BJP will organize Vistarak (extension) programme from May 18 to May 25 and May 28 to May 31.
