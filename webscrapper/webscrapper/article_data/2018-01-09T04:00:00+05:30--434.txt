Rajkot: Two persons were killed and three injured in a head-on collision between a truck and a car on the road between Vanthali and Manavadar on Sunday evening. 

 Police identified the deceased as Viram Khunti, a resident of Gadu village of Bhanvad taluka in Devbhoomi Dwarka district, who was driving the truck and Harikrushna Thanki, a resident of Junagadh who was driving the car. 
 
 “Such was the impact of the collision that the front portion of the car was mangled beyond recognition, while the truck skidded off the road and turned turtle by the road side. Both Khunti and Thanki were killed on the spot,” said a police official. 
 
 “Thanki was going to Porbandar from Junagadh along with his family whose three members were injured,” the official added.
