JAIPUR: A 45-year-old woman died after she was hit by a speeding car near   under the limits of   police station area on Wednesday morning.

 The police have identified the victim as Kailash Kanwar. “She was standing alongside the road and waiting to cross the road when a speeding car coming from   hit her,” an official told TOI.
 
 A profusely bleeding Kanwar was rushed to the hospital where she was brought dead. “The car was coming from the wrong and has been identified. The people managed to note down the number of the car which was a white Swift. We are checking with the authorities to arrest the accused,” the official added.
 
 The police said that the driver of the vehicle abandoned his vehicle on the road and fled form the scene in order to evade the arrest. “A complaint was given by the brother-in-law of the deceased. We are investigating the case and an FIR will be filed soon,” the official told TOI.
 
 The Jaipur police said that the incident was reported in the afternoon when the woman was crossing the road to buy something. “While she was waiting to cross the road, a speeding bus was headed towards her from Chomu side, she could not spot it as it was coming from the wrong direction,” the official said, adding that Kailash Kanwar suffered severe injuries and was rushed to the hospital but could not save.
 
 The city police said that they are probing the accident as a case of rash and reckless driving. “The driver fled from the scene as crowd had begun to swell around the accident site. We have retrieved the CCTV footage of the nearby areas to track his location,” the official said, adding that the vehicle will be sized.
