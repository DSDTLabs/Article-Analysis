The City of Nawabs, which is known for its biryani and kebabs, had some   flavour added to the traditional aromatic dishes at the recently concluded three-day mango festival. Organised by   at Paryatan Bhawan, Gomti Nagar, the main attraction of the festival were dishes with mango as the hero like aam malai tikka, aam shahi paneer, mango chutney, mango salsa, mango bhelpuri and aam kaleji, apart from a host of other activities. Visitors were kept entertained with events like a mango-themed fancy dress competition for kids, a mango eating contest and mango cooking contest, among others.
Inaugurated by Dr  , deputy chief minister, UP, the event was also graced by the presence of Prof Manoj Dixit, VC, Dr Ram Manohar Lohia Awadh University, Faizabad,  , principal secretary, Tourism,  , popularly known as Mango Man and chef  .

 On the first day of the festival, cyclists Farhan and Lakshya showcased their riding skills and performed some jaw-dropping cycle stunts like slides, wheelies, stoppies, bar ride and bunny hop. 
 Day two of the festival had the mango cooking contest where ladies showcased their culinary skills by preparing dishes from mangoes which were judged by Pankaj Bhadouria. On the concluding day, a mango-themed fancy dress competition for kids along with a mango eating contest was organized for the city folk.
