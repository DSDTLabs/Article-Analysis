Noida: With the Goods and Sales Tax ( ) set to be implemented across the country on July 1, traders, businessmen, real estate builders and entrepreneurs are looking for clarity on its implementation, when it comes to transactions.

 The trader community of Noida and Greater Noida will come together at a state-level meeting of Uttar Pradesh Vyapar Mandal in Lucknow on April 11 to make a list of suggestions which they would then submit to the committee overseeing the implementation of GST, under the Union ministry of finance.
 
 A similar wait for clarity also prevails among entrepreneurs in the twin cities.
