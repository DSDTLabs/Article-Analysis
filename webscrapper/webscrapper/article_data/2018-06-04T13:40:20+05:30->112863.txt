Samuel Robinson, who captured the hearts of Malayalis with the Nigerian footballer act in  , was so adored that he was soon fondly ascribed the name ‘Sudu’ by movie lovers. His love for Kerala was such that before leaving the place, he assured fans that he would soon come back to God’s Own Country. 

 He recently divulged that he would be soon coming back to Kerala for filming his next titled Purple. He said that he is super excited to be back. 
 
 This is what he posted, “This is to Confirm that I will be returning to India this June to join the shoot of my next Malayalam movie titled "Purple". The movie will be directed by Dr V.A. Bijin and Produced by Mr Rathish Nair Sir. I am super excited and looking forward to filming again in Kerala. (sic).”
