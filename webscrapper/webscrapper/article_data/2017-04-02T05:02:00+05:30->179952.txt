New Delhi: The   has extended the time for filing of nominations for civic polls to be held on April 23 by three hours.

 The SEC was supposed to accept nominations till 3pm on April 3, but now the timing has been revised to 6pm.
 
 In an order, SEC commissioner SK Srivastava stated that a petition was filed by BJP and AAP requesting for extension of date of nomination by a day or extending the time. He said that till date 800 nomination papers have been submitted. “After April 1 was declared a public holiday, there is a possibility that on April 3 there will be a rush,” he added.
