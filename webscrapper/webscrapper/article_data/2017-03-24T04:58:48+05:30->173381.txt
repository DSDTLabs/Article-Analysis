BURDWAN: A day after the accident on  , at least 22 people were injured after a bus they were travelling in collided with a trailer near   under  . Three among the injured are critical. 

 The incident took place around 10.30am on Thursday.
 
 According to locals, the bus was heading to Durgapur from Krishnagar when it tried to overtake the trailer. But, the driver lost control and hit the trailer. Locals rescued the injured and brought them to Pursha Health Center. Those who were severely injured were taken to Burdwan Medical College and Hospital. Cops seized the bus and trailer, but the bus driver managed to flee.
