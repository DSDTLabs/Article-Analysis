 A college lecturer in Swami Vivekananda Fine Arts College of Raibag, Baburao Nadoni has been teaching art since 1986. He has conducted more than 47 solo art exhibitions and 36 group art exhibitions across the country.
 
  "Though I'm identified with portrait painting, creating art with nature and music is also one of my unique styles," Baburao said. While painting portraits, Baburao never draws lines. His dream is to establish a cultural centre in Raibag. "Whatever the prize amount I have received has been used to pay for the education of talented students from poor families," he said.
