SURAT: One person was injured in an explosion of a LPG cylinder in   area of the city on Tuesday. He was rushed to SMIMER and is responding to treatment. 

 Fire brigade officials said a group of six men was making dinner when the explosion occurred at   at Kapodara crossroad at 7 pm. 
 
 Fire officer   said, “The cylinder caught fire when a man was busy cooking. He immediately removed the cylinder to prevent blast, but could not avert it. The blast damaged the kitchen walls and the debris fell on Supan Ram Singh. He suffered injury on the right side of his head. The blast took place due to vapour creation when the LPG cylinder was shifted sideways.” 
 
 The six men were tenants and worked in diamond and embroidery units in the city.
