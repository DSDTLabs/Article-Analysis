CHANDIGARH:The Northern Railway on Monday announced that as many as seven trains of the   division plying between 10.55am and 5.55pm will be diverted from May 1.

  
 
 According to a spokesperson of the Northern Railway, they are working on strengthening the infrastructure by constructing two low-height subways in lieu of level crossing 129 C and 132 C between Ambala Cantt and Shambhu. The traffic on Ambala-Ludhiana section will be diverted to carry out the works.
 
 Another project due to which the traffic will be affected is the shadow block for replacement of T-beam slabs of bridge number 304 between Ambala Cantt and Ambala city.
