UDAIPUR:   municipal council chairman Kamlesh   is missing since Thursday. Doshi had gone to Jaipur on Wednesday (May 16) with a former councillor and next afternoon, he flew to Mumbai alone where he had stayed at a hotel. He spoke to his wife last on Thursday night at around 8 pm when he told her that he was leaving for Burhanpur to meet some Jain saints. Unable to contact him thereafter, the worried family members enquired relatives and friends about him, but there has been no information. Sources at Burhanpur too said that Doshi didn't reach there. 

 Doshi's brother on Friday night lodged a 'missing' complaint at   police station in Pratapgarh. According to police, Doshi's mobile location was last traced to Kurla in Mumbai at around 11 pm.
