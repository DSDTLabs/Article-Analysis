KOCHI: The high court on Thursday asked the state government to respond to a petition seeking framing of rules to allow persons who convert to Islam to get the conversion recognized legally. 

 A petition filed by   of   is being considered by the court. He was born a Christian, got married to a Hindu woman, and then converted to Islam three years ago.
 
 The plea says the state is yet to frame rules for Section 3 and 4 of The Muslim Personal Law (Shariat) Application Act, 1937 that provides for a declaration that a person has converted to Islam.
 
 While the Muslim community has accepted him, he is unable to get his conversion legally recognized as the rules prescribing the authority before whom he can apply for the same has not be framed, he said.
