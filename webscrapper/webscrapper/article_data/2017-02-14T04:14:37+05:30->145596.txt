CHENNAI: Chief minister   on Monday announced a compensation of 3lakh for the family of a 7-year-old girl who was sexually assaulted and murdered by her neighbour. 

 Police recovered the child's body on February 7, two days after she went missing from her apartment in Moulivakkam.
 
 In a statement, the   said, "I extend my condolences and sympathies to the family of the child."
 
 Panneerselvam said the accused in the case had been arrested by police, booked for the crime and lodged at Puzhal Central Prison. He said he took a decision to pay the compensation from the chief minister's relief fund.
