GUWAHATI: A 'low intensity'   of 4.5 magnitude shook Lower Assam today, the met office said.

 The earthquake occurred at 9:35 AM and its epicentre was in East Garo Hills of Meghalaya.
 
 People in almost all the Lower Assam districts felt the jolt and came out in the open.
 
 Officials from Assam State Disaster Management Authority said there was no report of any damage from any part of the state.
