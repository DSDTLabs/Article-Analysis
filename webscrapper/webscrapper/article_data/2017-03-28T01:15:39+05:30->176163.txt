GURUGRAM: A 27-year-old   was killed on Sunday when he allegedly rammed his rickshaw into a   near the office of Munjal Showa Limited. 

 The police have lodged an FIR and further investigations are underway. 
 
 Raju, a native of UP’s Rampur, was headed towards Udyog Vihar from Sector 18 after dropping a passenger late on Sunday evening, according to police. He reached near the office of Munjal Showa Limited and could not see the tractor as it was pitch dark, and rammed the rickshaw into the vehicle, as per the FIR. He was rushed to a private hospital, however, he was declared brought dead. 
 
 An FIR has been registered under sections 283 (obstruction in public way), 304 A (death due to negligence) of IPC at sector 18 police station. The vehicle has been seized. “The owner of the vehicle has been identified and a notice has been sent to him. He will be arrested soon,” said ASI Jitender Kumar, the investigating officer.
