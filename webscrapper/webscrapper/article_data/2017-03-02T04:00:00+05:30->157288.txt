Surat: A four-year-old girl was abducted from a marriage party in Borigada village of Mandvi taluka of Surat on Tuesday night. 

 According to Mandvi police, the girl was seen in a video recorded in the dance programme during the marriage function. She was wearing a violet dress. However, she went missing late Tuesday night. 
 
 The girl was living with her uncle in the village as her mother was no more.
 
 Police sub-inspector H M Chauhan, of Mandvi, said, “We are searching for the girl.” Police are exploring all possibilities like leopard attack and old rivalry.
