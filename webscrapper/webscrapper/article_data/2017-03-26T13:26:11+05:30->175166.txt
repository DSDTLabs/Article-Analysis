After working with Mohanlal in 'Munthirivallilal Thalirkkumbol' and ' ,' Asha Sharath has just finished a movie with Mammootty.

 The actress, who has worked with him in the Ranjith Sankar movie 'Varsham,' says the project directed by Shyamdhar is yet to be titled. "I'm also waiting for the name..Varsham had been a great experience with him and I’m glad to have had another opportunity to share screen with him after a long time. My character has been portrayed as ‘Manjari’ the teacher."
 
  The movie is directed by Shyamdhar and is written by Ratheesh. Vinod Illampally is the film's cinematographer.
