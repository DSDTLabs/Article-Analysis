CHANDIGARH: Police arrested three men and two women for allegedly tearing the uniform of two policemen and assaulting them at vegetable market in Manimajra on Friday night. 

 Head constable   and constable Pankaj, posted at Manimajra police station, alleged that they were at the receiving end of the assault when they had gone to the market to ask vendors to close their shops, as it was beyond permissible time. In his complaint, Jaipal alleged that Manimajra residents Lalchand, his brothers Harish and Rajinder, and Suman and Geeta also pelted stones at a police team that had come to rescue the two.
 
 He alleged that the accused started arguing with them when asked to close their vends and then attacked Pankaj. When Jaipal intervened, they assaulted him too, it has been alleged. 
 
 Jaipal alleged that they called additional force from Manimajra police station, but the accused started pelting stones at them.
