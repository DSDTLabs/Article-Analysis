 The woman told police in her complaint that the accused, also tenants in the same apartment as her, came to her house around 10pm and started enquiring about her husband. “My husband, who works with a security agency, had a night shift on Monday. When they got to know that he wasn’t at home, they forcibly entered the house and started molesting me. They fled when neighbours gathered outside my house after hearing my scream,” the woman alleged. 
 
 One of the accused has been identified as Surender Kumar, a resident of Sarhaul village. The woman alleged that the duo threatened to kill her and her husband, if they told anyone about the incident. 
 
 Following the complaint, an FIR under sections 354A (molestation), 452 (house trespassing), 506 (criminal intimidation), 34 (common intention) of the IPC has been registered against Surender Kumar and his accomplice at the Sector 18 police station. 
 
 “The accused have been absconding since the incident. We are conducting raids and will arrest them soon,” said inspector Ram Kumar, SHO, Sector 18 police station.
