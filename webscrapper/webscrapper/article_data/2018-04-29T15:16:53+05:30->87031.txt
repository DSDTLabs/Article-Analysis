NEW DELHI: The Andhra Pradesh state SSC Class 10 results 2018 timing has been postponed to 4 pm today. Earlier, the result was scheduled to be announced at 11 am on April 29, 2018. 

 The AP SSC Class 10 results will be declared on the official website of the board: bseap.org.
 
 
 All the students who have appeared for the Andhra Pradesh Class 10th or AP SSC examination 2018, can register below to get their result via SMS or email. Enter your details such as name, roll number, mobile number, email ID and click on submit. 
 
 
  
  
 
 The Board of Secondary Education Andhra Pradesh has made the result page active at http://resn18.bseap.org/, but the result has not been declared yet. Students are advised to keep checking the page, as the result is going to be made live anytime from now onwards. 
 
 The homepage of the BSEAP is being redirected to the result page (as shown in the image given below). 
 
  
  
 The sources have confirmed that the AP Board has shifted the result release timings again, this time by five hours. Now the AP SSC Class 10 results will be declared at 4pm. And the notice, displaying on the official website, is also saying that the results will now be declared at 4 pm. Earlier, the result was to be announced at 9 am and then deferred to 11 am. 
 
 The notice reads as: The SSC Public Examinations, March 2018 Results will be released at 04.00 PM on 29-04-2018 (Sunday).
