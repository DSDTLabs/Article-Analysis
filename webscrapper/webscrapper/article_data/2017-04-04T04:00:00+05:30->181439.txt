Surat: City’s Udhna area MLA and former minister Narottam Patel raked up triple talaq issue while addressing BJP workers at BRC Ground here on Sunday evening. The meeting was organized to felicitate party workers who had worked for BJP in Uttar Pradesh assembly elections. Gujarat Pradesh BJP president Jitu Waghani was also present. 

 Patel said, “There will be fun in politics now. We had Modi in the past and now have Yogi with us. Muslims marry thrice and divorce their wives by just uttering talaq, talaq and talaq.”tnn
