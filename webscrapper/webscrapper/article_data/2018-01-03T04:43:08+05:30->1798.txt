PATNA: A special   court in Ranchi is set to pronounce the quantum of sentence against RJD chief Lalu Prasad on Wednesday in a fodder scam case. 

 Prasad was convicted by the court on December 23 for fraudulent withdrawal of money from the treasury in Deoghar. 
 
 Earlier, in 2013, he was convicted for a similar offence in   treasury. He was released on bail, granted by the SC, three months after the conviction.
