PUNE: Students who wish to get admission in First Year Junior College ( ) in the   2017-18 but have not been allotted any colleges or have not been allotted a college of their choice can change their preferences online on July 26 and 27.

 Students, who got their first choice in round 2 but did not take admission in the allotted college, cannot change their preference forms. They can now apply only when the re-examination results of standard X are out and the new round opens for successful candidates.
 
 “The students can go online on pune.11thadmission.net and check the vacancy in various colleges,” and then make their choice,”  , deputy   Pune division, said. Preferences can be changed between 11am on July 26 and 5pm on July 27.
