Rajkot: Renowned cardiologist and physician Dr. B M Hegde will deliver a lecture on ‘Health beyond Medicine’ at Pramukh Swami Auditorium on Raiya Road in Rajkot Sunday.

 According to Vasant Mangrolia, whose trust has organized the lecture, Hegde will give information about how to avoid unnecessary medications and harmful effects of medicines on the body. 
 
 “His understanding of disease and knowledge about medicine will help people understand the health issues and how we can deal with it,” said Mangrolia.
 
 “Dr. Hegde has been speaker and cardiologist of an international repute. In his lecture on Sunday, he will share his knowledge on many important health issues like diabetes, high blood pressure, cholesterol, cancer and other aspects of medical practice,” he added.
