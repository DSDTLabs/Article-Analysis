BELAGAVI: A first year student of a private polytechnic,  , committed suicide by running in front of a moving train near first railway gate under   limits around Friday noon. 

 Sanjana Chandrakanth Angolkar, 19, a resident of Tilakwadi suburban area, doused herself with fuel and just as the   passenger neared, set herself ablaze and jumped in front of it, said police.
 
 Police identified Sanjana with the help of the textbooks she was carrying in her bag, which was found beside the track. 
 
 After conducting a panchanama, police shifted the body to   hospital for postmortem and also informed her family members.
 
 Her suicide note spoke of health ailments that were troubling the teenager, added police. A case has been registered with railway police.
