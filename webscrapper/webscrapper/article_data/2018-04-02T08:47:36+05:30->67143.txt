HYDERABAD: A case was registered against an uncle who thrashed his 12-year-old nephew, under the influence of alcohol at   village, Rachakonda. The victim suffered injuries on various parts of the body, including his private parts.
 
 Ramannapeta police said that the victim, Ganesh, a class 4 student, is a resident of   in Maredpally. Ganesh, along with his mother went to   to meet his aunt. On Friday night, accused Venkanna, the aunt’s husband, in a drunken condition, called the boy on the pretext of taking him to attend nature’s call. The boy was taken to an isolated spot, where for no reason, he was beaten with a stick on his face and other parts of the body.
