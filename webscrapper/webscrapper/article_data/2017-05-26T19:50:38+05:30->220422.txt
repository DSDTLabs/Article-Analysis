PANAJI: A 24-year-old man was caught red-handed in Panaji with narcotic substances worth Rs 80,000 on Friday.

 Acting on a tip-off that Rahul Pawar, a resident of Mumbai, was involved in the sale of narcotic substances, Panaji police kept surveillance on him. He was caught red-handed at Bal Bhavan when he was going to deliver a consignment to prospective customer. On searching his bag, police found 814g of a substance suspected to be ganja.
 
 Police said that the accused has been living in Goa for the last four years. Pawar was arrested under various sections of the NDPS act and will be produced before the judicial magistrate first class to seek police remand.
 
 This is the second drug-related arrest at Bal Bhavan.
