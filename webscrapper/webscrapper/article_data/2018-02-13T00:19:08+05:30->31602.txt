Pune: The Pimpri police registered an offence against an employee of a gift shop on Sunday for outraging the modesty of a 36-year-old medical practitioner.

 The incident occured on Sunday night when the woman had gone to the gift shop to purchase mehendi. The suspect has been booked under section 354 (outraging modesty) of the Indian Penal Code.
 
 An officer from the Pimpri police said that on Sunday, around 8 pm, the woman was inside the shop. “The woman stated in her complaint that while showing her Mehendi, the 20-year-old suspect had touched her inappropriately,” the officer said.
 
 In another incident, the Wakad police have booked a suspect for sexually harassing his friend’s wife around 3pm when the woman was alone at home.
