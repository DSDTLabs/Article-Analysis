LUCKNOW: Telling officials to follow the   order on singing of the national anthem, especially in cinema halls, chief secretary   has reiterated that till a final order is issued in the matter, differently abled persons will remain exempt from standing when the anthem is being played. 

 In an order issued by the chief secretary to upper chief secretaries, principal secretaries, department heads, district magistrates, police etc, it has been said that as per the   order, playing of the national anthem in cinema halls before the start of the film is not compulsory. 
 
 "It is optional or dependent on directions. However, when the anthem is played, shown or displayed, it is compulsory to show respect to it as per the SC order," the direction from Kumar says. It adds that persons with disability will be exempt from this order.
