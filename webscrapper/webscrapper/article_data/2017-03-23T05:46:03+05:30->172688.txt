VISAKHAPATNAM: In an attempt to keep tribal youth away from Maoist influence,   cops have started a programme   to impart training in motor vehicle driving. 

 The rural cops with the help of the   (ITDA) have already provided a 15-day training to 51 youth from the interior packets of   and GK Veedhi mandals and given them temporary driving licenses. They can get loans for buying vehicles once the youth get their permanent licenses.
 
 Speaking on the last day of the training programme at Chintapalli on Wednesday, Visakha Rural SP   said the police department will extend its help to the trained tribal youth for buying vehicles. "The police department will also help the youth to operate their vehicles in the Agency areas and plains," he added.
