COIMBATORE: The   ( ) will host the 17th World Tamil Internet Conference from July 6 to 8. The theme of the conference is ‘Arivusaar Tamil Theduporigal’ (Intellectual Tamil search engines).

 Seminars, public arena and exhibition stalls will set up as part of the event, organised by the   (Infitt) and TNAU.
