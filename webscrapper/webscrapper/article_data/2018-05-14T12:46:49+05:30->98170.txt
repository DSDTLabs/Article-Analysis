NEW DELHI: The Andhra Pradesh Common Law Entrance Test Result 2018 was declared on Monday, May 14, 2018, by the Sri Krishnadevaraya University, Anantapur.

 The AP LAWCET Result 2018 is now available for the students at the official website: sche.ap.gov.in. 
 
 The candidates can check their AP LAWCET 2018 result by providing their registration number and date of birth in DD/MM/YYYY format. 
 
 
 
 
 
 The Sri Krishnadevaraya University, Anantapur had conducted the AP LAWCET exam on behalf of the Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education for admission on April 19, 2018. 
 
 
 
 
 1) Log on to the official website: sche.ap.gov.in
 2) Click on link "AP LAWCET 2018" available on the homepage
 3) A new page will appear on your screen, click on "View Result"
 4) Enter your registration number and date of birth in DD/MM/YYYY format
 5) Click on "Submit" button
 6) Your AP LAWCET 2018 Result 2018 will be displayed on screen
 7) Download your result and take printout for future use
