Panaji: The family of Bismarque Dias will lay him to rest once they receive all his reports, Dias’ brother, Mario Dias said on Tuesday.  The family, he said is still waiting for the viscera and diatom reports. The high court of Bombay at Goa on Tuesday, hearing the petition over the burial of Dias, didn’t permit Mario to represent his older brother in court and asked him to follow proper procedure. The court will hear the petition on May 2. On Monday, Dias’ family stated that they would lay his body to rest, almost two-and-a-half years after he passed away.
