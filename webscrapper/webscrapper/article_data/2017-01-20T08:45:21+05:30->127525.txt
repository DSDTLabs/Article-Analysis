PUNE: The Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited ( ) on Wednesday suspended two assistant   of its Parvati division for not following rules while granting new power connections.

 An MSEDCL official said  , assistant engineer of Vadgaon Dhayari office, and Anil Igwe, assistant engineer of Kirkitwadi office, were found guilt of not following instructions of the distribution firm and relevant circulars while granting new connections.
 
 The official said the duo were found to have given firm quotations on local level for new connections to complexes without taking permission from the competent authority. “Also, they did not create the basic infrastructure first before sanctioning the new connections,” he added.
