SURAT: Ten people were injured when a private luxury bus overturned at Dikshan village in Kaparada taluka in Valsad district at 4 am on Tuesday. 

 Kaparada police said the bus belonged to   and it was on its way to Shirdi from Ahmedabad with around 50 passengers. The driver lost control over the bus around a curve and it overturned. The 10 injured were rushed to Kaparada Civic Health Centre. Seven people were later taken to Valsad Civil Hospital. Three had suffered broken limbs. Most of the injured passengers were from Surat, Vadodara and Rajkot district.
