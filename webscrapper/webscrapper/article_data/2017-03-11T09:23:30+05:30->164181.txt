RAJKOT: The Rajkot-Delhi Air India flight was discontinued on March 2 despite occupancy of over 80%. 

 Following this Rajkot BJP member of parliament  , made several representations to the   minister and Prime Minister Narendra Modi.
 
 Kundariya said that earlier Air India used to operate an 18-seater flight from Rajkot to Delhi which proved to be uneconomical and was discontinued. 
 
 “However, after my representation the Air India has decided to resume Rajkot-Delhi flight, but with a bigger flight A320,” Kundariya added.
