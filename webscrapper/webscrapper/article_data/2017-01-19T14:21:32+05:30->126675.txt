VISAKHAPATNAM: In order to clear the extra rush of passengers, the railways will operate a tri-weekly   train between   and  .

 The 82853   Suvidha Express will depart from Visakhapatnam at 10 pm on Mondays, Wednesdays and Saturdays from January 16 to March 29 and arrive at Vijayawada at 3.50 am the next day. It will depart at 4 am and arrive at Villupuram at 3.30 pm.
 
 In the return direction, the 08354 Villupuram-Visakhapatnam tri-weekly Suvidha Express will depart from Villupuram at 8.30 pm on Tuesdays, Thursdays and Sundays from January 17 to March 30 and arrive at Vijayawada at 7.45 am the next day. It will depart at 8 am and arrive at Visakhapatnam at 3:15 pm.
