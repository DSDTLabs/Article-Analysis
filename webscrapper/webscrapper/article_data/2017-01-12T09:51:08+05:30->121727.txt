BHOGAPURAM(VIZIANAGARAM): A curfew-like situation prevailed at Bhogapuram and neighbouring areas as cops made house arrests before the public hearing for the proposed greenfield airport. 

 Most of those arrested at the public hearing venue were YSRCP and Left party leaders including B Prabhakara Reddy, U Suryanarayana, CPI district secretary P Kameswara Rao and Bugata Ashok. 
 
 The government held the public hearing at Maradapalem with staff of the Pollution Control Board, Airports Authority of India and concerned departments. 
 
 District collector   chaired the public hearing. Local MLA Patiwada Narayana Swami Naidu of TDP was also present.
