The 2012 Uttar Pradesh Legislative Assembly was held in seven phases from 8 February through 3 March 2012 and the results for the same was announced on March 6, 2012.  The incumbent chief minister Mayawati's Bahujan Samaj Party, which previously won an absolute majority of seats, was defeated by Mulayam Singh Yadav's Samajwadi Party, which gained an absolute majority in the election.
