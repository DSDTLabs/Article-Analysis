BOKARO:   (I-T) officials conducted a raid at  , a distributor of ITC and Havels products, in Chas area on Thursday.

 A 12-member team headed by  , joint commissioner, Bokaro range, raided the area at around 2pm after learning that proprietor   had deposited more than Rs 3 crores in a bank after demonetisation.
