JAIPUR: A 36-year-old advocate was detained by the city's   police for molesting a 14-year-old girl on Tuesday evening. Transport Nagar SHO Naresh Kumar Meena said the accused has been identified as one Manoj Kumar. The accused and the girl are residents of the same locality. "On Tuesday when the girl was returning from her tuition classes, Kumar stopped her and took her to a secluded place near Jamwa Ramgarh," Meena said. 

 The police said that he was caught by people. The parents of the minor filed a complaint against Kumar at Transport Nagar police station. "We have filed a case under section 354 (Assault or criminal force on woman with an intent to outrage her modesty) and POCSO Act. We have detained him," Meena added.
 
 The police said that the accused had intercepted the girl a few kilometres from her house. "He took her to a secluded place on his bike. We will record the statements of the girl," Meena said.
 
 The police said that an FIR has been registered against the accused who claimed to be a lawyer. "We are investigating the matter and verifying his credentials," Meena said. The incident happened between 5 pm and 6 pm.
