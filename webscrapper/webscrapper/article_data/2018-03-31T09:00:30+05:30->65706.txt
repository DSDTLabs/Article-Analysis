CHENNAI: The total number of   registered with the   Real Estate Regulatory Authority ( ) touched 132 in the last three months this year. Of these, 35 projects were registered in March, data available with the realty regulator's official portal said.

 According to rules, ongoing housing projects with a minimum of eight units should be registered with the   regulatory authority. Besides, new residential layouts also come under the purview of the TNRERA. 
 
 The Real Estate Act came into effect in Tamil Nadu after the state government notified the Tamil Nadu Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Rules, 2017 on June 22, 2017.
 
 While about 280 housing projects were registered with the TNRERA in 2017, a total of 132 projects were registered with the realty regulator till March 27.
