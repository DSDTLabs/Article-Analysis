Panaji: The directorate of education (DoE) has asked students of primary, secondary and higher secondary schools to register with the directorate’s office if they have failed to get admission to a school for any reason for the academic year 2018-19. DoE has said that parents can register with its zonal offices ín Panaji (near National Theatre), Mapusa (near post office), and Margao (near Holy Spirit church).  “Students are requested to register their names along with addresses and phone numbers/mobile numbers of their parents. The directorate of education will make arrangement for students in any school in their respective zones where the vacant seat is available,” the directorate stated. The 2017-18 academic year officially ended on Monday, with declaration of results of students of various classes by schools. State board schools are on summer vacations and the new academic year 2018-19 begins in June.
