 According to government order issued here, the schools located in snowbound areas of Machil, Jamgund, Keran, Gurez, Karnah, Kodara, and Hajibal Gogaldara of Kashmir Division shall observe extended winter vacations upto March 12, 2017.
 
 The vacation has been extended in view of apprehensions of avalanches and road connectivity issues in these areas. The schools shall reopen on March 13.
