BHOPAL: At least 24 bogies of a goods train derailed in Satna district in Madhya Pradesh late on Friday. The incident disrupted the railway traffic on the Mumbai-Howrah up line and the Satna-Rewa route. 

 However, there were no reports of any casualties. 
 
 The railway has started the reconstruction work of the track to restore services.
