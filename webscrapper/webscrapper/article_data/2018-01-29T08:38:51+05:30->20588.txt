PATNA: Leader of opposition in state assembly   on Sunday called upon the party’s rank and file to make house-to-house visits and enrol new members irrespective of their caste and creed.

 Tejashwi, who was addressing the   office-bearers from   division at his official residence, also asked the party’s functionaries to constitute 15-member booth committees by mentioning the details, including phone numbers, of the members concerned. He asked them to send the details of the booth committees to the party’s state headquarters for future reference and access.
 
 “People from all sections of society will be given representation in the party,” he said.
