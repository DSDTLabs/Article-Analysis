 Reshma Rohit jumped into Karjan river as a scooter mechanic has been harassing her for the past one month, police said. Her body was brought out of the river in the night. Her parents earn their livelihood by removing the skins of animals. 
 
 Her father lodged a police complaint against Shahbaug Nakum 24, for harassing his daughter. He was booked for harassment and on charges of abetment to suicide. 
 
 Shahbaug had threatened the girl that if she did not surrender to him, he will kill her, police said, adding that the accused has been arrested.
