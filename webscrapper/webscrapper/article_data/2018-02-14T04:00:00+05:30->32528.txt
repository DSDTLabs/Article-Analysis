Rajkot: Two persons were killed when a speeding truck coming from the opposite direction rammed the bike they were riding on near SRP camp on new 150-feet Ring Road in Rajkot on Monday night. Police identified the deceased as Rajesh Babaria (30), a resident of Amarnagar on Dudhsagar Road in Rajkot and Dilip Pithawa (50), a resident of Gandhi Smriti Society on Pedak Road. tnn
