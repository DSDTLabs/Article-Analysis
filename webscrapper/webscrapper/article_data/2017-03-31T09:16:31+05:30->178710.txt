NEW DELHI: A 22-year-old youth was arrested for stealing his father's phone in northwest   as he was short of cash and wanted to give a present to his girlfriend on her birthday. 

  Unknown to him, his father lodged a complaint of 'snatching' which led the cops to him. Police said that they received a complaint from the father, Ram Shankar, about the snatching of his phone near   while he was on his way to purchase vegetables. "A team was formed to track down the miscreant. It was found that the phone was stolen by the complainant's son, who was later arrested," said DCP (outer) M N Tewari. 
 
  During interrogation, Shankar told cops about the false snatching incident in order for them to recover his phone quickly.
