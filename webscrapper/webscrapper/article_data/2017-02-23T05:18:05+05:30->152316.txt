SURAT: Veer Narmad South Gujarat University (VNSGU) has been accredited with A grade by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). The university got higher grade from B grade achieved in last assessment. The varsity got score of 3.02 in cumulative grade point average calculated by  .

 Recently, a peer team of NAAC visited the university and examined various aspects of infrastructure and development at the university. As part of the assessment the team considers overall infrastructure and academic atmosphere in educational institute based on which the grades are awarded.
 
 “It is a matter of great pleasure that the university got A grade and high score in the assessment. This reflects the development done at the university. We look forward to grow further,” said  , vice-chancellor, VNSGU.
 
 It is compulsory for all the government recognized and grant in aid educational institutes to get NAAC accreditation. The NAAC team consists of senior academicians from across the country and from different educational institutions.
