Rajkot: A lioness attacked on 46-year-old farm labourer, Mohan Devipujak, in Mitiyala village of Savarkundla taluka in Amreli Monday morning.

 According to forest department officials, Mohan, a native of Ambardi village of Savarkundla taluka was plucking mangoes in the orchard when the lioness attacked him. He sustained injuries on his face and abdomen.
 
 He was first rushed to hospital in Savarkundla and then he was referred to civil hospital in Amreli for further treatment. Forest officials said that he is out of danger.
