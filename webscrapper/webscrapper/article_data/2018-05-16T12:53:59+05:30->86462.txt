French band Pj5 is influenced by English and Scandinavian rock, pop and electro bands, as well as the New York jazz scene. They have strongly stayed attached to their roots from the jazz scene. They portray a modern sound through their melody making the crowd want more. The band comprises of   - Guitar and Compositions,   - Tenor Saxophone, Lo Pellet Trombone,   - Double Bass, Ariel Tessier Drums.
