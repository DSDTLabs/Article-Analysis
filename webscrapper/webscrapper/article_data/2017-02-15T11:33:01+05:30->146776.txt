NAGPUR:   mayor of   and sitting BJP corporator Mayatai Iwnate and two other sitting BJP corporators Sadhna Barde and Jagdish Gwalbanshi on Tuesday have been pulled up by city police for violating model   guidelines laid down by the state election commission. Apart from them, the NMC have also filed complaints against six other persons or political parties.

 The latest action is part of the ongoing drive against such violations conducted by joint team of police and election officials whereby offences have been registered at different police stations under relevant provisions of the law.
