KANPUR: A man has been   for   in a prohibited zone in the residential block of Air Force premises in Chakeri   area on Saturday. The man, identified as Arun Kumar, a resident of Chakeri, was alleged to have been roaming in a prohibited zone of the residential block of Air Force premises in Chakeri in an inebriated state. 

 As he continued walking, a master warrant officer of Air Force was alerted and he rushed to the spot and nabbed him before apprising Chakeri police. The police reached the spot and detained the man and took him to the police station. 
 
 The accused has been booked under Section 454 (lurking house-trespass or house-breaking in order to commit offence punishable with imprisonment) of IPC on the basis of a complaint by KV Rathi, master warrant officer, Air Force, Chakeri.
