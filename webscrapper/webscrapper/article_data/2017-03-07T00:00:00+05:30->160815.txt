Ahmedabad: Common Entrance Examination for Indian Army for Category of Trades of Soldier (General Duties), Soldier (Technical), Soldier (Clerk and Store Keeper Technical) and Soldier Tradesman, which was held on February 26 stands cancelled and has now been rescheduled to April 30 because of leak of question papers in Maharashtra and Goa. The admit cards for re-examination in Ahmedabad will be issued on April 3 at Army Recruitment Office, Ahmedabad between 9 am to 6 pm. tnn
