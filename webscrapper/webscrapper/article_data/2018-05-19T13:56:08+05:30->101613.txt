NEW DELHI: The School and mass education minister Badri Narayan Patra has declared the Council of Higher Secondary Education (CHSE) Plus Two results for the science stream on Council's official website: orissaresults.nic.in around 11 am. 

 
 Despite disturbances like boycott of evaluation process by teachers, result published on time, the minister said. This year 76.98 per cent students have cleared the examination, he added.
