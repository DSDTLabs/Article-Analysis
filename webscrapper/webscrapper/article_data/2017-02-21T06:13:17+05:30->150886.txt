 Some passers’ by in the morning intimated the police control room at 7.00 am in the morning about spotting of a charred body. Soonafter the incident a team of senior police officers with the experts from the forensic science laboratory (FSL) and others rushed to the spot to take a stock of the situation. 
 
 “The body appears to be of a man aged around 26 to 30 years. The whole face and the lower abdominal area is charred. We are tallying the missing record of various police stations to get the body identified,” said a senior police officer of Shivdaspura police station. 
 
 Police said that it is a clear case of murder. “Nothing much can be said before the identification of the body, still it is clear that it is a case of murder. There were also some circumstantial evidences which prove that this man was probably brunt alive,” the officer added. 
 
 On the other hand, a body of a youth aged around 25 years was also found at Gujjar Ki Thadi locality on Monday morning. The exact cause of the death of yet to be identified youth was yet to be known. “Only after the post mortem the cause of death would be ascertained,” said an officer.
