Noida: A five-year-old girl was allegedly molested by a 12-year-old boy in Sorkha village on Tuesday. The accused juvenile, a Class VIII student in a government school, lives in the neighbourhood and was known to the child.

 The girl lives with her mother in a rented accommodation. Her father died a few years ago. The victim’s family members have registered a case at the Sector 49 police station. The police said the crime occurred in the afternoon. The boy visited the girl’s house and took her along with him to play. After half an hour when the girl returned home, she told her mother that the boy had touched her inappropriately.
 
 The girl told her mother that the boy had been doing this to her for the last three months.
