Ludhiana: Model Town police arrested a father-son duo for assaulting cops on duty when they were stopped at a checkpoint on Wednesday. The father-son duo not only argued with cops, but manhandled them as well, tearing uniform of one of the cops.

 The accused have been identified as Jaipal and his son Keshav Sood of Model Town Extension.
 
 The team of Atam Nagar police post had established a checkpoint near Kartan Nagar railway crossing in afternoon. During checking, the cops stopped Jaipal and Keshav who were crossing through the area on a motorcycle. The cops stopped them and asked to show them licence and RC of bike. The accused were also not wearing helmet. But instead of cooperating with police, the duo allegedly misbehaved with them.
 
 Complainant head constable Avinash Rai said the duo snatched the RC and licence from him when he was taking the same to a senior official for challan and misbehaved with him. When another cop started videographing them, they snatched his mobile phone and manhandled him.
 
 “Further when I intervened, the accused manhandled me as well. I got my phone and started capturing them. They tried to snatch my phone too. During this spat, they tore my uniform,” said Rai.
 
 The accused were arrested and were booked under Section 353 (Assault or criminal force to deter public servant from discharge of his duty), 186 (Obstructing public servant in discharge of public functions), 506 (Criminal intimidation) and 34 (Acts done by several persons in furtherance of common intention) of the IPC.
