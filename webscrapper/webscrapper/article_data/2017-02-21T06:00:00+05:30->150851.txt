The opening of artist Seema Kohli’s art show titled ‘Golden Womb — Dawn of Time’ drew city’s art lovers in large numbers on Sunday. Featuring art works in varied media, the exhibition was a celebration of all things feminine, in multidimensional forms at that. And Kohli was thronged by enthusiastic visitors who couldn’t stop raving about how much they loved the art works on display even as animated conversations about art and mythology abounded.
