While several MLAs and Congress functionaries praised GPCC president Girish Chodankar’s rise from the grassroots level of politics, Curtorim MLA Aleixo   Lourenco chose the moment to train his guns on the party’s senior leadership, a subject he often vents over. He said more MLAs within the Congress needed to emulate outgoing state party president Shantaram Naik. “Rahul Gandhi has given a direction that it is time for the youth to take over. I wish more Congress leaders take Shantaram Naik’s example,” Lourenco said. He avoided naming any Congress legislators. tnn
