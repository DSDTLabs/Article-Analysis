IMPHAL: The state government has transferred chief secretary   and appointed  , popularly known as RR Rashmi, to the post, stunning the administrative apparatus in the state. An order to this effect was made by the department of personnel and administrative reforms on Thursday appointing Rashmi 'with immediate effect in public interest'. 

 Rashmi, from the 1983 IAS batch, will take charge of being state chief secretary in place of Nabakishore, a 1984 IAS cadre who is under order of transfer. 
 
 Nabakishore has been transferred and posted as director general of State Academy of Training.
