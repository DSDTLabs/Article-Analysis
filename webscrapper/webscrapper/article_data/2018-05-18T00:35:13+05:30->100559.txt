Udhagamandalam: The 122nd edition of Ooty flower show will be inaugurated by chief minister Edappadi K Palaniswami at the Government Botanical Garden (GBG) on Friday. 

 The much-awaited show will be on till May 22. Governor Banwarilal Purohit will take part in the valedictory function. 
 
 Around 70 varieties of flowers in about 45,000 pots and about 1, 50,000 plants of about 150 varieties will be featured in the event. Additionally, the show will have a floral replica of Mettur Dam and a Barbie doll.
 
 According to Shiva Subramaniam Samraj, joint director (in charge) of horticulture department, a replica of Valluvar Kottam, made of spices will also be displayed. Another attraction at the GBG this year will be the newly-developed glass house arranged with varieties of flower pots.
 
 Tamil Nadu ministers, R Duraikannu, P Thangamani and S P Velumani will also take part in the inaugural function.
 
 Palaniswami will also inaugurate the 500 megawatt Kundha underground hydroelectricity project estimated at Rs1, 850 crore.
 
 After inaugurating the flower show and the hydroelectricity project on Friday, the CM will leave for Yercaud.
