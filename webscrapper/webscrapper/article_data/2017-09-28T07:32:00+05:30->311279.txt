Mohali: A woman committed suicide by hanging herself from the ceiling fan at her house in Mohali on Wednesday. 

 A native of Nepal, Maya aged around 27 years was residing with her husband Devesh and two children. The couple used to work as domestic helps in the same house.
 
 Station house officer of Phase 1 Sukhwinder Singh said they got a call at around 9am informing that a woman had committed suicide. On reaching the spot, Devesh told police he found her hanging from the ceiling fan when he returned to their room after cooking breakfast for their landlords.
