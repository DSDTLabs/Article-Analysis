Coming as a treat for Lucknowites was a musical night which saw Indian and French artists coming together to perform together. Organised at  , the event was attended by the who’s who of the city. Unfortunately famous flute exponent Pt.   could not perform at the event due to ill health. Instead his disciple Vivek Sonar along with French vocal ensemble Diabolus in Musica performed on Indo-French music. 
Led by choir conductor Antonie Guerber and Raphael Boulay, Oliver Germound, Ennanuel Vistorky, Phillipie Roche and Jean-Francois Delmas presented vocals of the medieval period and Latin period. The music sung by the choir had everyone glued to their seats.
