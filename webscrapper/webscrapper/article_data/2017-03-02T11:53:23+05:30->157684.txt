JAMMU: A temple and four   were gutted after a massive fire broke in   of   here, police said on Wednesday.

 Fire broke out from a temple around 8:15 PM yesterday, Kishtwar SSP Sandeep Wazir said.
 
 He said that four residential houses adjoining the temple were also burnt in the fire and no human casualties were reported in the incident.
 
 However, a dog and a calf were charred to death in the fire, he added.
 
 Wazir said a case has been registered in this regard and investigation in underway to ascertain the cause of fire.
