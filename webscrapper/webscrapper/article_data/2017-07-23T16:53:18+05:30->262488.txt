THIRUVANANTHAPURAM: The state government has decided to reauthorize the unspent balance of   aided Kerala   Service Delivery Project under Development Fund at the end of the financial year 2016-17. The decision has been made based on the data furnished by the   pending finalization of reconciliation process.

 The government had examined the matter in detail and has authorised Rs 350.79 crores as additional funds equal to that of the unspent balance for World Bank aided   under Development Fund at the end of the financial year 2016-17 to each Local Government.
 
 Government has authorized an amount of Rs 549.31 crores as World Bank aided   Delivery Project fund for 2016-17 to the Local Governments The Local Self Government had forwarded the request of the Project Director, Kerala Local Government Service Delivery Project (KLGSDP) and informed that the unutilized fund during 2016-17 had to be utilized before 30th June 2017 since the project is due to end on 30th June 2017. 
 
 The Project Director had also requested to make necessary steps to issue reauthorization of Rs 383.63 crore as unspent balance of World Bank aided KLGSDP Fund to Local Governments which have not utilized the fund during 2016-17.
