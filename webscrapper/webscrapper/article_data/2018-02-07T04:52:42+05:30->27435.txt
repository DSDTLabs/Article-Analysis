MUMBAI: Chief minister Devendra   on Tuesday directed the urban development department (UDD) to submit a detailed plan report on the national   of Shiv Sena leader, the late Bal Thackeray, to the government for further action. Fadnavis reviewed the progress of the   memorial at a meeting at Sahyadri guest house.

 Senior Sena minister Subhash Desai, who is also a trustee of the Balasaheb Thackeray Smarak Samiti Nyas, said the CM had told the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC), UDD and environment department to submit a draft report so that work will start soon. “Work on the memorial will start soon once the detailed proposal is submitted,” said Desai.
 
 A presentation on the Thackeray memorial, being planned at the mayor’s bungalow at Shivaji Park, was made at the meeting. The CM directed officials to take into consideration issues like the number of persons likely to visit the memorial, facilities for them and parking space, while preparing the report on the memorial. Once the detailed report is received, action can be speeded up to complete it, he said.
 
 The prime sea-facing property, measuring around 11,500 square metres, was handed over to the Balasaheb Thackeray Rashtriya Smarak Nyas last year, clearing the decks for the memorial.
 
 Tuesday’s meeting was attended by chief secretary  , principal secretary (urban development department) Nitin Karir, Manisha Mhaiskar and BMC officials.
