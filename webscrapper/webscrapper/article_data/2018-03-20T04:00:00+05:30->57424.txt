Rajkot: A woman ended her life by jumping from the seventh floor of a high-rise building of Chitrakut Apartment at Panchayatnagar Chowk on University Road in Rajkot on Monday. Police identified the deceased as Radha Makadia (65). 

 Makadia, who was a resident of Panjuri Apartment on Sadhu Vasvani Road in Rajkot, had ended her life after meeting her cousin sister at Chitrakut Apartment. 
 
 “Preliminary probe has revealed that Makadia was tired of a prolonged illness which prompted her to end her life,” said an official from the University police station.
 
 Police sources said they will record statements of her family members later. She was living with her husband, son and daughter-in-law.
