Title: Mixture Potlam

 Language: Telugu
 
 Cast: Jayanth,  , Geethanjali Tasya, Bhanu Chander, Suman, Posani Krishna Murali
 
 Release Date: May 19, 2017
 
 Director: M V Sathish Kumar
 
 Producers : K Veeranna Choudary, K Lakshmi Prasad, L Srinivasa Rao
