NEW DELHI: The Uttar Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Parishad (UPMSP) has confirmed the UP Board result 2018 date and time for Class 10 and Class 12. 

 
 As per the information available on the official website of UPMSP - upmsp.edu.in - the Board will declare the UP Board Class 12 result 2018 on April 29, 2018 at 12.30 pm. The UP Board result 2018 for Class 10 students will be declared on the same day at around 1.30 pm. 
 
 The students can register using the form provided below to get their UP Board Result 2018 directly on their mobile phone or email ID.

 The Uttar Pradesh Board Result 2018 for senior secondary (Class 12) and secondary (Class 10) annual examinations will be hosted by the National Informatics Centre on the official website - upresults.nic.in. 
 
 
 Last year, the Uttar Pradesh Board had declared the Class 10, 12 results on June 9, 2017.
 
 
 The Uttar Pradesh Board had conducted the Class 10 exams between February 6 and February 22 and the class 12 Board exams were conducted between February 6 and March 10. 
 
 
 A total of 66,37,018 students appeared for the annual board exams of the UPMSP board in the year 2018.
