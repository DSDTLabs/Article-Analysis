GURUGRAM: The income-tax (I-T) department carried out raids at G D   University on Sohna road and   in Sector 48 on Wednesday. 

 Around two dozen I-T officials, accompanied by police, arrived at the   campus around 8am and reportedly questioned registrar Hitesh Bansal, in the absence of the vice-chancellor. 
 
 When the team reviewed records, security guards were asked not to allow anyone to enter the premises. Around 11am, the team conducted a raid at the Goenka school and reviewed several records. 
 
 The I-T officials refused to share any information about the raids. No spokesperson for the school or the university could be contacted for comments despite repeated attempts. Sources said the team included officials from Delhi and Chandigarh I-T departments.
