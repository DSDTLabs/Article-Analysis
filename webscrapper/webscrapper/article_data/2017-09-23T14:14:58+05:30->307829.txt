Rajkummar Rao’s ‘Newton’ has hit the bull’s eye by being named India’s official entry at the Oscars. The black comedy that has opened at around mere 300 screens recorded an opening of approximately Rs 85 lakh, as reported by Boxofficeindia.com. The fate of the film is expected to change for the better amid the positive reviews.
