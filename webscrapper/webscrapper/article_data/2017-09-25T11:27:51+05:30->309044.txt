The shares of Capacit'e Infraprojects got listed today on market exchanges - BSE and NSE. In morning trade, the shares of the company went up 59.60 per cent at Rs 399. At 10.51 am, there was a decline in the price yet it was high 44.64 per cent at Rs 361.60. 
 

 
 
 
 
 The Capacit'e Infraprojects announced the opening of its initial public offering (IPO) subscription on September 13, 2017, at the price band of Rs 245-250. 
 
 
 The issue was subscribed 1.32 times on the first day of the offer. The quota for Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) was subscribed 1.77 times, NII segment got subscribed 0.11 times while Retail investors put in bids at 1.58 times. 
 
 
 On Day 2, the issue was subscribed 4.71 times, with QIB quota being subscribed 5.64 times and NII segment 0.28 times. The retail subscription stood at 6.08 times. 
 
 
  The issue saw a bumper demand on Day 3 as it got overall subscribed at 186.45 times on the last day IPO application. The QIBs quota was subscribed 134 times and non-institutional investors put in bids totalling 651 times. The retail investors section got 16 times subscription. 
 
 
  The shares were allotted to the bidders on September 22, 2017.
 
 
  The share got listed on both - Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) and National Stock Exchange (NSE) on Monday September 25, 2017.
