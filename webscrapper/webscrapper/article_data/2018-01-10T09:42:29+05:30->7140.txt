HYDERABAD: Tollywood celebrities with clean records like   of   fame, Bahubali director SS Rajamouli and Junior NTR have been roped in by Hyderabad police to be a part of short films to campaign against crimes. Central Board of Film Certification (CBFC) has certified the short films for screening in theatres. Till date, five films have been made.

 “CBFC has cleared these five films. They’ll be released soon by the Hyderabad police commissioner. The films deal with issues like OTP fraud, social media crime, job frauds, multi-level marketing and matrimonial frauds,” CCS cyber crime additional deputy commissioner of police KCS   told TOI.
 
 The short films were made based on the instructions of CCS deputy commissioner of police  .
 
 
 
  
  
 
 
 
 
 A police official said, “We selected Tollywood actors with clean records and avoided those who were questioned during the recent drug racket.” No cop acted in the films.
 
 Nikhil, who was caught in a drunk driving drive in 2011, is one of the actors who figures in the films. He was vocal in the campaign against drunk driving. Traffic police have used his videos to promote the campaign against drunk driving.
 
 In August 2017,   police headed by commissioner of police Mahesh Muralidhar Bhagwat had made short films on drunk driving and cyber crime. Rachakonda police bagged a prize for the film ‘Beautiful Life’, which revolves around two youth who knock down a girl while driving drunk. Telugu actors Sanan and Meghna Lokesh played key characters in the film.
