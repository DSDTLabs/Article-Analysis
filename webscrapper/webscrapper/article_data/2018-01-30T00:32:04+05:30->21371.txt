Two men died on Sunday after they fell from a motorcycle near the government higher secondary school at Pethikuttai. The deceased, M Arunkumar, 30 of Veerakeralam and S Ramachandran, 28 of Poosari Mariamman Street, were returning to their home from Sirumugai. Arunkumar, who was riding the motorcycle, lost control and fell down with Ramachandran, who was the pillon rider, police said. Both men suffered severe head injuries. “Arunkumar died on the spot. Ramachandran was taken to the Mettupalayam Government Hospital where he was declared as brought dead,” the police said. TNN
