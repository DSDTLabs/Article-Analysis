RAJKOT: Senior politician   who quit Congress and floated new outfit Jan Vikalp on Tuesday promised a legislative assembly for Saurashtra region if his outfit was elected to power. 

 Vaghela, who is campaigning for the Jan Vikalp, was in Rajkot to meet his supporters.
 
 The former Gujarat CM said his outfit will contest assembly elections in Gujarat from all 182 seats. He slammed the BJP government for breaking the backbone of people by increasing prices of essential commodities like petrol, diesel and power. 
 
 He alleged that the additional income from tax on petrol and diesel was being diverted towards the publicity of government.
 
 Vaghela also promised to reduce value-added tax on petrol and diesel to bring down prices.
