 “We are happy to present this year’s ‘Sir Jehangir Ghandy Medal for Social and Industrial Peace’ to Adi Godrej,” XLRI Director E Abraham said. He added, “This year is an important milestone for us at XLRI as we celebrate the 61st convocation of our institution.”
 
 The medal will be conferred to Godrej on the occasion of the 61st annual convocation of the institution on March 25. The medal recipient will also deliver the convocation address to students graduating at the event.
