PANAJI: The district magistrate (North Goa) cum adjudicating officer,   has imposed a penalty of Rs 30,000 on two food business operators for selling misbranded and substandard food under the Food Safety and Standards Act, 2006.

 The penalty was imposed on   of M/s A B Chips,   and the local food vendor Siva Gurunathan of M/s A B Chips of Zuarinagar, Mormugao for selling and distributing banana chips, tapioca sticks and tapioca wafers, all of which were declared and reported to be misbranded under the Act.
 
 Senior food safety officer, Rajiv Korde had detained the consignment on August 30 at Margao Railway station.
