Rajkot: An 82-year-old man lost his gold chain worth Rs 1.15 lakh to female thieves in an autorickshaw on Nana Mawa Road in Rajkot. Though the crime was committed on April 27, the complaint in this connection was lodged on Thursday. According to the case details, Premji Parmar had boarded the autorickshaw in which four unidentified women were seated.
