Sandalwood actor  , who is one of the judges of the weekend comedy reality show Comedy Khiladigalu, will not be part of the show for a few weeks, as he is contesting for the   assembly elections, which will be held on May 12. The other judges of the show are actor   and director  . The show is hosted by Master Anand.
A spokesperson from the channel says, “As Jaggesh is completely devoting his time for the election campaign, he won’t be part of the show Comedy Khiladigalu, but once the results are announced, he will be back on the show as a judge. For now, there is no replacement for Jaggesh.” On the filmi front, Jaggesh will be seen in a cop role in the film — 8mm.
