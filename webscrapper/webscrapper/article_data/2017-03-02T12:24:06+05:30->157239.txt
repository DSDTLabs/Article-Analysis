PATNA: Prime Minister   (NaMo) and several other politicians called up Bihar CM   on Wednesday to wish him on his 66th birthday.

 “Spoke to Shri @NitishKumar & wished him on his birthday. May he be blessed with a long life filled with good health,” Modi tweeted.
 
 West Bengal chief minister   also wished Nitish on his birthday. “Birthday greetings to @NitishKumar ji. Wish you a healthy life ahead,” Mamata said in a tweet.
 
 Besides Modi and Mamata, several top leaders of the country, including railway minister Suresh Prabhu, Union rural development minister Birender Singh, Rajasthan CM Vasundhara Raje, former Rajasthan CM Ashok Gehlot and BJP senior Shahnawaj Hussain also tweeted birthday wishes for Bihar CM. 
 
 In Patna, RJD president Lalu Prasad, former CM Rabri Devi and their daughter Misa Bharti (Rajya Sabha member) made a call to CM in his birthday.
 
 Deputy chief minister Tejashwi Prasad Yadav and his elder brother and health minister Tej Pratap visited the CM house at 1, Anne Marg and extended their birthday wishes to the CM by handing over bouquets.
 
 “Birthday greetings to the Chief Minister of Bihar, Sh. @NitishKumar ji. May God bless you with good health and a long life,” Rajasthan CM Vasundhara Raje tweeted.
 
 “@NitishKumar best wishes on your birthday. May god give you best of health and plenty of happiness,” Prabhu said in his tweet.
