 Sixteen cops had sustained serious injuries, two police vehicles were torched and around a dozen vehicles were damaged during the over one-hour clash.
 
 As per the FIR registered at Bhuna police station, the accused have been booked for attempt to murder, voluntarily causing hurt to deter public servant from his duty, wrongful confinement, mischief by fire or explosive substance with intent to destroy house, rioting and under the Prevention of Damage of Public Property Act, among others.
 
 Superintendent of police Fatehabad O P Narwal told TOI that names of some persons figure in the FIR on the basis of their identification for their involvement in the clash, but no arrest has been made so far.
