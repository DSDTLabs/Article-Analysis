AHMEDABAD: People had little respite from extreme weather conditions in Ahmedabad on Tuesday as the maximum temperature in the city remained below the 40°C mark settling at 39.5°C, 1.5 degrees above normal.

  The minimum temperature was at 23.4°C, 1.4 degrees above normal. Surendranagar was the hottest town in  , with the maximum temperature touching 41.1°C, followed by Amreli which reported 41°C temperature.
 
  There is no major change in the maximum temperature likely on Wednesday, when the maximum temperature will hover around 40°C. According to the   forecast, there is no major change likely in the maximum temperature in the next two days.
