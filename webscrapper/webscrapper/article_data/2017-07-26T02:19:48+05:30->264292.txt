GURUGRAM: A cash   was   of Rs 15 lakh by two men near   on Tuesday afternoon. 

 The victim, identified as Naresh Kumar, works as an agent for Radiant cash management services that collects cash from outlets across the city and deposits it to the bank on the outlets’ behalf.
 
 Naresh told cops that he was on his way to Iffco Chowk after collecting the money from Ambience Mall. He was also looking for transport to reach IndusInd Bank on MG Road as he had to deposit the cash there. Around 2pm, two men attacked him near National Media Centre and started thrashing him. 
 
 As Naresh fell down, one of the accused slit the strap of the bag, which had the cash, and snatched it. Before anyone could hear Naresh and come to his rescue, the accused ran away from the spot with the bag. 
 
 An FIR has been registered against the two under Section 392 (robbery) of the IPC at DLF-2 police station. 
 
 “We have registered the case but are verifying the facts. We are trying to identify the accused with the help of CCTV footage gathered from the area,” said inspector Sudip Kumar, SHO, DLF-2 police station.
