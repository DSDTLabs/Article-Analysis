Southern Railway will be operating Suvidha special trains between Chennai and Tirunelveli in the first week of February to clear extra rush of passengers. According to a statement from the railways, train no. 82601 Chennai Egmore – Tirunelveli Suvidha special will leave Chennai Egmore at 9.50pm on February 2 and reach Tirunelveli at 10.50am the next day.  Train no. 82602 Tirunelveli – Chennai Egmore Suvidha special will leave Tirunelveli at 3pm on February 4 and 11, and reach Chennai Egmore at 3.30am the following day. The trains will have one AC 2-tier, two AC 3-tier, 12 sleeper class and two luggage-cum-brake van coaches.  The trains will have stops at Tambaram, Chengalpattu, Villupuram, Vriddhachalam, Tiruchchirappalli, Dindigul, Madurai, Virudhunagar, Satur, Kovilpatti and Vanchi Maniyachchi.  Train no. 82602 will stop at Mambalam also. Advance reservations for the above trains will commence at 8am on January 21, the statement added.
