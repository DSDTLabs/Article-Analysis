NEW DELHI: The West Bengal Council of Higher Secondary Education ( ) is likely to announce the West Bengal Board Class 12 Results 2018 on its official website - wbchse.nic.in - in the first week of June, 2018. 

 
 The candidates who have appeared for the examination can register using the form given below to get the result directly on their mobile through SMS or on email. All you have to do is, enter your name, mobile number, roll number, email ID and click on submit. 
 
 
  
  
 
 
 The WBCHSE Board has successfully conducted the Higher Secondary Examination (Class 12) for the session year 2017-18 in the month of Feb-March. 
 
 The exact time and date of the result announcemnet for the West Bengal Class 12th Board Examination 2018 could not be confirmed by the officials. They just provided the tentative date of June forst week as of now. 
 
 If you are one of the students eagerly waiting for the announcement of WBCHSE Board Class 12 Result 2018, you can check it on the official website of the WBCHSE Board - wbchse.nic.in.

 IVR: Since there will be huge traffic on WBCHSE Board website on result day, you can try IVR system that provides you with the results via a call.
