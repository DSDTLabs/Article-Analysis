PIMPRI CHINCHWAD: Pimpri Chinchwad municipal commissioner   has appealed to the people to enthusiastically participate in the February 2017 municipal elections to contribute to the development of the twin cities. 
 
 Waghmare made the appeal after inaugurating a cycle rally organized on the occasion of cycle day by Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation ( ) and the Rotary Club of Chinchwad Morya. 
 
 He said students participating in the rally must create awareness among people to vote in the elections to be held on February 21, 2017. “If best among the lost are elected, it will help in the development of the area,” he added. 
 
 The 4-km long rally began at Bhakti Shakti Chowk in Nigdi and saw huge participation from primary, secondary and college students. Pamphlets about voter awareness were distributed through the students participating in the rally.
 
 Two-day festival 
 
 PCMC, Pune district collectorate, Savitribai Phule Pune University, the National Service Scheme and Pune Zilla Shikshan Mandal will organize a two-day youth festival on January 24 and 25 at Prof. Ramkrishna More Arts, Science and Commerce college in Akurdi on the occasion of National Voters’ Day. 
 
 Streetplays,cartoons, elocution and skit competitions will be held for college students. Prizes to the winners of various competitions will be distributed later.
