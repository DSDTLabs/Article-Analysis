GURUGRAM: A man was allegedly robbed of his   Vitara Brezza after being held hostage at gunpoint by four robbers near Daulatabad village on Saturday night. An FIR was registered but the accused are yet to be nabbed.

 According to Pawan Kumar, the accused overtook him in a   Amaze and forced him to stop after putting a gun to his head. Three of them held Kumar captive in their car, while the fourth one drove Kumar’s car to a petrol pump to get fuel. They dumped him near Farrukhnagar and fled. 
 
 An FIR was filed under sections 379-B (snatching & use of force) and 34 (common intention) of the IPC and the Arms Act at Rajendra Park police station. “We are trying to identify the accused with the help of   footage of the petrol pump,” said SI Abdullah Khan, the investigating officer.
