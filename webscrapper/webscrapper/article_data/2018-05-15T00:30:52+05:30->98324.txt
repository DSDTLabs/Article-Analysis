Police have slapped Goondas Act on four of the six people arrested and lodged in prison on charges of sexually abusing a Class 11 minor girl in Thanjavur. The youths were earlier arrested by the All Women Police.

 According to police, the four accused — Ilavarasan, 24, Karthik, 22, another Ilavarasan, 20, and Selvam, 20, — were arrested based on a complaint lodged at the police station on April 1. It said that when the 16-year-old girl was speaking to her friend near Vettikkadu bridge on March 31, the six accused kidnapped her and took her to a secluded place where she was allegedly sexually abused, police said.
