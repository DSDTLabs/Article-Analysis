Ludhiana: The students of Khalsa College for Women, Civil Lines, have achieved outstanding results in MA (fine arts) semester-II, which was declared by Panjab University on Wednesday. Five students of the college bagged positions in top 10 rankings of the university. Aishwarya Sehdev bagged first position by scoring 88.7% marks, Gagandeep and Jasjot Kaur bagged second position by 87.7% marks, Shifali Kapoor bagged eighth postion by scoring 83% marks, Sukhsagar Deep Kaur bagged 10th position with 82% marks. The students who had appeared in the examination have scored more than 73% marks. Principal Mukti Gill and the staff of fine arts department congratulated students for their sincere efforts in getting excellent results.
