AMRITSAR: A majority of candidates fighting assembly polls and Amritsar Lok Sabha byelection spent the day seeking blessings of the Almighty, holding meetings with their core group members, chalking out tour programme for the voting day, getting last-minute poll assurances and visiting some `influential' group of voters.

 Punjab deputy chief minister Sukhbir Singh Badal and his wife Union minister Harsimrat Kaur Badal paid obeisance at the Golden Temple.“Parmatma di mehar honi hai bahut (God will bless),“ was the first reaction of Sukhbir after paying obeisance. His wife Harsimrat Badal said, “Malik rehmat karangay (God will be benign).
 
 Former cricketer Navjot Singh Sidhu, who is Congress candidate from Amritsar (East) assembly constituency , stayed at his house holding meetings with party workers and interacted with guests and mediapersons.
