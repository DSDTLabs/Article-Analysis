PUNE: The Pune rural police on Monday arrested nine men in connection with the January 1 violence at Koregaon Bhima. Police also detained three minors. All those arrested or detained were involved in rioting and attempts to murder. 

 Three of the suspects are juveniles and involved in the attempt to murder cases. They were produced before the juvenile justice board and released on bail on Monday evening. All the boys are between 16 and 17 years of age.
 
 The rioting suspects were produced before a court which remanded them in police custody till January 10, police said.
 
 The number of arrests in the rioting and attempt to murder cases, registered with the   and   police stations, have now increased to 27.
 
 Earlier, the police had arrested 15 people from the villages of Vadhu Budruk, Koregaon Bhima and Sanaswadi. Police on Monday said life in the violence-hit villages is returning to normal and shops were open.
