 Electricity department officials said power supply was disrupted in different sectors for around two hours and field staff had to work hard to restore power.
 
 From the intervening night of Friday and Saturday till 5 in the evening, the department received around 500 complaints from different parts of the city at   (CFC). Normally, the department receives around 300 complaints per day in the summer. 
 
 Unhappy with frequent breakdowns, Ashwani Kumar, a resident of Sector 21 said power supply snaps even when there is slight rain. “Chandigarh administration needs to improve its ageing infrastructure,” said Kumar.
 
 UT superintending engineer M P Singh said power supply was interrupted as power lines from Mohali developed a technical snag. “We pressed all our resources into service, but it took us some time to restore power supply,” he said.
