NEW DELHI: The Himachal Pradesh Board Of Secondary Education (HP BOSE) announced the HP Board Class 12 results for the academic session 2017-18 on its official website on Tuesday, April 24, 2018. 

 
  Out of a total of 98,281 students who had appeared in the HP BOSE Class 12 Board examination this year, 68,469 candidates have passed. This year, the overall pass percentage for HP BOSE Class 12 Board exams is 69.67%.
