
 
  Speaking to Mysore Times, Vasu says, “Last time, when I was here for a shoot, I noticed that Mysuru is changing, but we don’t want the essence of the city to be lost in the name of development. As a Mysurean, I want the culture, heritage and greenery of the city to remain the same and even our roots should not be forgotten, which is the theme of the song. The song also mentions famous landmarks like Mysuru Palace, Chamundi Hills and KRS and how they have beautified this city.”
