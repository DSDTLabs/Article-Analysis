AHMEDABAD: Ahead of  , the health department of the   raided 28   in various part of the city, including several famous outlets.

  The health department took samples after it suspected that unhygienic preparations were about to be sold. The samples have been sent for analysis. The   will take action against the shops whose samples turn out to be substandard. The outlets raided included Oswali Ice creams (Ashram Road), Laxmi Gathiya Rath (Navrangpura), and Chandravilas (Gandhi Road).
 
 AMC officials said that in almost all these outlets conditions were unhygienic, the oil was being extensively reused, and ingredients for making fafda and jalebi were of inferior quality. The officials said that jalebis were being fried in ghee which was not pure as specified.
