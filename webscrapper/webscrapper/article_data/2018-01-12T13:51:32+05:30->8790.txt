NEW DELHI: The time table for Higher Secondary Certificate Examination (Class 12) has been released by the Telangana State Board of Secondary Education (TSBIE  ) on its official website - bie.telangana.gov.in

 The exams for Intermediate first year and second year students will start from February 28, 2018. 
 
 
 
 
 
 After much speculation on the internet regarding the date sheet of Board examination, the TSBIE Telangana Board has finally released the higher secondary annual examination dates for Board students on its official website - bie.telangana.gov.in. 
 
 The students appearing for the annual board examination can check the timetable of the TSBIE Telangana Board Examinations on the official portal of the TSBIE Telangana Board - bie.telangana.gov.in
 
  
 The official notification regarding the date sheet of TSBIE Telangana Board Intermediate Exam is available on the official website of the TSBIE Telangana Board. 
 
 As the date sheet is now officially released by the TSBIE Telangana Board, the students can download it from the official website of the TSBIE Telangana Board - bie.telangana.gov.in. 
 
 The students are also advised to save and print their TSBIE   sheet for further reference. The students appearing for TSBIE Telangana Board Examination should revise the important topics of their textbooks prescribed by the TSBIE Telangana Education Board. 
  
 The TSBIE Telangana Board Examinations are generally held in the month of February-March. The results of the TSBIE Telangana Board examination are announced in the month of May-June.
