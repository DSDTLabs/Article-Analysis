 According to police, Lalit Sharma, a resident of Kondli in Delhi, had gone to a relative’s house in Greater Noida. “He was returning to Delhi when a speeding   van rammed his motorcycle near Sector 168. Sharma fell off the bike and was seriously injured,” a police officer told TOI.
 
 Witnesses said Sharma was dragged several metres by the van, driven by Kumar. Passersby informed police who rushed him to a private hospital. The doctors declared him brought dead. 
 
 Meanwhile, locals gathered on the spot and nabbed Kumar as he was trying to flee. The accused was thrashed and later handed over to police.
 
 An FIR under sections 279 (rash driving) and 304A (death due to negligence) of IPC was registered against Kumar at Noida Expressway police station. “Sharma’s body was handed over to his family members after a post-mortem,” an officer said.
