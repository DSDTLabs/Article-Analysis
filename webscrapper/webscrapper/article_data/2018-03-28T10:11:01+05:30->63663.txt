NEW DELHI: The   released the admit cards for the   PET 2018 on the official website of   - https://dsssbonline.nic.in

 
 Candidates who have applied for the examination can check and download their admit card from the DSSB website - https://dsssbonline.nic.in
 
 
 
 Candidates are advised to download their Head Constable PET Admit Card 2018 from the official website - dsssbonline.nic.in
 
 In case you have also applied for the Head Constable PET 2018 examination, you can follow the steps given below to check and download your admit card. 
 
 
 
 
 1) Open the official website of Head Constable PET - dsssbonline.nic.in
 
 2) On the homepage of the website, there will be a link "Head Constable PET 2018 Admit Card/Hall Ticket". Click on the link
 
 3) A pop-up page will appear, enter your Head Constable PET 2018 application number, date of birth, security pin and click on login. 
 
 4) Your Head Constable PET 2018 admit card will be displayed on the screen. Carefully check all your details such as name, paper, date of birth, gender, test centre name, city, state, code of eligibility and category on your DSSB Head Constable PET 2018 admit card. 
 
 5) You can download it and take a printout for future reference.
