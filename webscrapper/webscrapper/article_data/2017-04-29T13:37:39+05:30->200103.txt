HYDERABAD: The Sukma police have placed a reward of Rs 40 lakh on the head of Ramanna. The police who want him dead or alive have announced that anyone providing information leading to his arrest could get the reward. The name of the informant will be kept a secret. On Friday, Sukma police released posters with Ramanna's photo graph in a uniform carrying weapons. As many as 12 telephone numbers have been given for information.
 
 Ravula Srinivas alias Ramanna is said to be secretary of Dandakaranya Special Zonal Committee (CPI Maoist) and a central committee member. He is now said to be around 55 years.
