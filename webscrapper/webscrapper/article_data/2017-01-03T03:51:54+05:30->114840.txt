Chennai: On a day when the Union agriculture minister   met   fisheries minister Mahinda Amaraweera and foreign minister   in Colombo to solve the disputes between the fishermen of both countries, chief minister   on Monday urged Prime Minister   to take steps to release 52 fishermen from Tamil Nadu, who are detained in Sri Lankan prisons. 

 He also urged Modi's intervention in releasing more than 100 Indian fishing boats that are in Sri Lankan custody.
