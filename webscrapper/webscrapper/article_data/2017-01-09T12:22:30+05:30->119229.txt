BHARUCH: A 25-year-old   gave birth to quadruplets without   procedure at Sewa Rural's   at Jhagadia taluka of Bharuch district on Friday night. 

 Sewa Rural's Kasturba Hospital trustee Bankim Sheth identified the mother as Ushaben Vinod Vasava from Amba village of Dediapada taluka in Narmada district. She gave birth to four baby boys through normal delivery. 
 
 As their weight was less than a kilogram, they are being kept in intensive care unit of the hospital, he said.
