MARGAO: The Konkan Railway police on Tuesday recovered the two bodies lying besides the tracks at Sarzora.

 The bodies were identified as those of  , resident of Bhatibhat, Quepem, and Faviton Rebello, a resident of Chinchinim. Both 17-year olds, who were missing since Monday evening. The police are ascertaining the cause of death, particularly whether they ended their lives for some reason. Investigations are under way.
