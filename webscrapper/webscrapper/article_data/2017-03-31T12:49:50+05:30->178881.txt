ULHASNAGAR: The BJP who for the first time with help of local SAI ( ) party going to make their mayor in  , has decided Meena Ailani for the mayor post. Today candidates will file their nomination form for Mayor, deputy mayor and standing committee chairman post. 

 The Mayor election will be held on April 5. The party however yet to announced their officials candidate but a senior BJP leader has confirmed TOI that Meena Ailani, wife of Ex-BJP Mla Kumar Ailani will Mayor candidate from party. 
 
 For Mayor post there were two strong candidates were in fray that includes Meena Ailani and Jaya Makhija. In UMC election which held last month, BJP has emerged single largest party by winning 34 seats while Shiv Sena who fight separately win only 25 seats.
