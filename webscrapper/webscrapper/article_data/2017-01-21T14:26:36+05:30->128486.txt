Harman ( ) continues his romantic banter with Soumya ( ) despite her resistance. 
 
 A group of priests come to Harak Singh's (Sudesh Berry) house to collect donation for Lohri celebrations. Harak Singh gives them a generous amount.
  
 The priest demands that the prasad should be taken by only the daughter-in-law of the house. Since Surbhi's( ) position in the family is still a secret the society, Soumya accepts the prasad on her behalf and assumes the role of Harman's wife. 
 
 
  
 Later, Soumya repents being a kinnar and begins to cry when Harman enters her room. He stops her from feeling sad but leaves the room angrily when Soumya mentions Surbhi as his lawful wife.
