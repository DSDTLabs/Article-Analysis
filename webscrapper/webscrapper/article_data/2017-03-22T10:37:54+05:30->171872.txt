SURAT: Two unidentified persons were booked at Khatodara   on Monday for picking Rs1 lakh cash from the pocket of a 54-year-old resident of Bhatar. 

 Kamlesh Desai was walking on   near Udhna crossroads when a man on scooter came towards him.
 
 While Desai was trying to give way to the scooter, a man walking alongside bumped into him. Taking advantage of the situation, the man picked cash from Desai’s pocket and escaped. 
 
 Desai realized about the theft only after a while. Police suspect that the person riding the scooter was involved in the theft with the one who actually picked the cash from Desai’s pocket.
