DHARAMSHALA:   Board of School Education has cancelled public administration examination of   open school that was conducted on March 23.

 The officials of the board said the examination has been cancelled as a lot of students complained that the question paper was out of syllabus. The examination would be conducted in the same examination centres on April 5, the board secretary said.
