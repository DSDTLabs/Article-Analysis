Since the last two days, reports kept stating that the  , Rakul Preet and   starrer ‘Jai Lava Kusa’ has almost touched the Rs 100 Cr milestone.

 
 
 But now, just six days after its release, the film has managed to rake in Rs 103 Cr worldwide, breaking Jr NTR’s previous records. The movie raked in Rs 71 Cr from the Telugu speaking states alone, Rs 12 Cr from Karnataka, Rs 2.50 Cr from Tamil Nadu, Rs 2.75 Cr from rest of India, Rs 9.50 Cr from US and Rs 5.25 Cr from rest of the world.
 
 ‘Jai Lava Kusa’ that sees Jr NTR portray the role of triplets – Jai, Lava and Kusa – released on September 21, 2017. The movie received positive response at the box office and has been doing brisk business. With ‘Spyder’ released today and ‘Mahanubhavudu’ up for release on September 29, 2017, it remains to be seen how well the movie fares in the coming days.
