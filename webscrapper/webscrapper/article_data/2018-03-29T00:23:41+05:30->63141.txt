New Delhi: A 21-year-old man has been arrested for allegedly raping his   cousin in Bharat Vihar area in Dwarka. The man had taken the girl out on the pretext of some work following which the family filed a missing complaint.

 Senior police officers said the accused was arrested on Tuesday after being absconding for a few days. 
 
 DCP (Dwarka) Shibesh Singh said he has been charged for   and under the POCSO Act.
 
 Police said that the man told the 15-year-old girl her that he is taking her for some work but later allegedly raped her. The girl’s family approached the police when she didn’t return home and a manhunt was launched for the accused.
 
 The girl was rescued on Sunday evening, where she said that she was raped by the accused. Cops tracked the phone number of the accused and nabbed him from his hideout near Dwarka.
 
 Cops said that the girl’s parents work as daily wage labourers and had gone out for work when the accused came and went away with their daughter.
