Visakhapatnam: Weathermen at the Indian Meteorological Department ( ) on Saturday forecast thunderstorms accompanied by squally winds over isolated places in north coastal AP, Nellore, Prakasam and Rayalaseema region besides Telangana in the next 24 hours. Following the announcement, the state government has alerted the administrations of north coastal and Rayalaseema regions to take up precautionary measures to reduce damage. Visakhapatnam district revenue officer (DRO) C Chandra Sekhar Reddy has instructed all the tehsildars, revenue division officer (RDOs) and other concerned officers to be on alert. He has also asked them to stay at their respective headquarters and monitor the situation. tnn
