SHIVAMOGGA: State   president B S Yeddyyurappa, MLC K S Eshwarappa, former minister   and JD(S) youth leader   filed their nomination papers on Thursday after performing puja at various temples in their area and holding a big show of strength before filing their papers.

 
 Star candidate B S   filed his papers before the Shikaripur returning officer at 1.30pm as suggested by his astrologer. After filing his nomination, Yeddyurappa said that voters will not just be electing an MLA this time but also the chief minister of the state. 
 
  
 He promised his supporters that he will take up irrigation projects worth Rs one lakh crore in his constituency.
