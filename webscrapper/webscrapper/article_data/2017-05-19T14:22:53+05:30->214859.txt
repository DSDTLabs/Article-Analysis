NEW DELHI: L&T Power has won export orders worth Rs 300 crore from Japan-based   Hitachi Power Systems.

 "The power business of Larsen & Toubro (L&T) through its joint venture company L&T-MHPS Boilers Pvt Ltd has secured export orders worth approximately Rs 300 crore from Mitsubishi Hitachi Power Systems Ltd, Japan," L&T said in a BSE filing.
 
 The contract covers supply of pressure parts to 2,000 mw and 650 mw power plant in   and Japan, respectively, for water wall panel, coils, piping and header.
