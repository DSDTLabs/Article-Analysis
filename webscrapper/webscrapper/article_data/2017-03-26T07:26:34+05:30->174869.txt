AHMEDABAD:   (AII) and  , USA on Saturday announced an international academic collaboration in education and research.

 “The collaboration aims to promote exchange of students, faculty and research scholars and facilitate joint research, technical assistance, exchange of materials, publications and academic information,” Dr Pankaj Singh, head of services of AII, said.
 
 Dr Robert F Cox, senior associate dean of Purdue University said, “The collaboration of Adani Institute of Infrastructure and Purdue University will facilitate and develop a genuine and mutually beneficial exchange process and research relationship.”
