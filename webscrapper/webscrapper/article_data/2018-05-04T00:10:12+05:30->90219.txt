Later, she told reporters that the   was not just a monument of tourist interest, but an anchor for Hinduism, its tradition and culture. So its restoration was very important. It is important that the damaged mandapam is restored to its past glory, she added.
 
 The minister also offered worship to Lord Kallazhagar, the deity of the Alagarkoil temple, as he departed from Madurai for his abode on Thursday morning. Madurai collector K Veera Raghava Rao accompanied her. Devotees and people who had adorned various disguises to redeem their vows to the deity posed for photographs with the minister, when she came there.
 
 BJP state president Tamilisai Soundararajan, who accompanied the minister condemned the DMK cadres for showing black flags to the minister and for pelting stones on vehicles that followed her convoy in Ramanathapuram.
