Bollywood's very own Greek God  , turns a year sexier today. On the occasion of his 43rd birthday, Hrithik revealed that he will celebrate the day with his family and close friends. However, the actor's birthday celebrations commenced late last week while he was in Dubai to promote his upcoming thriller 'Kaabil'. 

 We have learnt that over a thousand fans gathered to wish Hrithik the best for his birthday. A cake cutting session was also organised for the star, who was overwhelmed with all the love. 
 
  
  
 On the work front, Hrithik gearing up for the release of 'Kaabil' on January 25, where he will be seen opposite Yami Gautam.
