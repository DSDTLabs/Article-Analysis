TRICHY: A 35-year-old woman allegedly committed suicide by electrocution and also attempted to kill her daughter at their residence near   in   on Sunday morning. 

 The deceased woman’s nine-year-old daughter, who suffered injuries to her arm, received treatment at the Mahatma Gandhi Memorial Government Hospital. Thiruverumbur police have registered a case.
 
 The deceased was identified as Velammal, hailing from Kuvalakudi. Velammal, who was a mother of three, allegedly had problems with her husband, Arumugam, for a while and he did not return home following an argument a few days earlier. As a result, Velammal was said to be depressed. 
 
 On Sunday morning, Velammal, allegedly wrapped her and younger daughter's arm with live wire to commit suicide. On hearing the girl’s screams, neighbours came and turned off the main switch. While Velammal died on the spot, the girl suffered injuries on her arm. 
 
 Velammal’s other daughters, who are working professionals, were not at home during the incident.
 
 Thiruvembur police reached the spot and sent the girl to the hospital and Velammal’s body to the government hospital at Thuvakudi for postmortem.
