Magical Whispers recently celebrated World Children's   Day and World   Day at Keshavrao Bhosale Natyagruha. 

  The event was organised by well-known film and television actress   who has played an instrumental role in popularising puppetry in India. Her group   presented various forms of puppetry and familiarised children with one of the oldest forms of storytelling in India. 
 
  
  They presented their latest production  , using handcrafted and shadow puppets. This energetic presentation showcased a life size puppet, dance, drama and shadow puppets created especially for the young audience.
