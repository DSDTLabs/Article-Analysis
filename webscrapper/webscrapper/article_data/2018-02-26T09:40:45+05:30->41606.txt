VARANASI: The managing committee of Kashi Jivdaya Vistarini   evam Pashushala has landed in troubled waters as an FIR has been lodged against it with   police on the order of additional chief judicial magistrate-I court.

 
 This FIR has been lodged on the basis of allegations leveled by a social activist   that the cattle recovered by police from smugglers in Varanasi and Chandauli districts between 2001and 2014 and given under the custody of this Gaushala had been sold to butchers by its managing committee.
 
 The SO   on Sunday informed that an FIR has been lodged against the managing committee of this   under section 407 of IPC and sections of Prevention of Cow Slaughter Act.
 
 He said that the investigation in this case has been started and the Goshala managing committee has been asked to provide details of the cattle handed over to it in the period of 15 years.
 
 
 Dharmendra had given an application before ACJM-I court under section 156(3) of CrPC appealing lodging of an FIR against the authorities of this Goshala.
 
 
 In his application he alleged that the Goshala authorities had sold the animals rescued by police from cattle smugglers for butchering.
