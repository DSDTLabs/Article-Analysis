TRICHY: Burglars managed to flee with Rs 1 lakh in cash from a grocery shop near   in   on Sunday. A case has been registered and police have begun investigation. The incident took place in the early hours of Sunday at Kattur. The burglars reportedly damaged the CCTV cameras which were set up outside the shop and broke in. They also destroyed the CCTV cameras which were installed inside and took Rs 1 lakh in cash kept in the store. The grocery shop belonged to Thirumeni Azhwar.

 Police said, the gang also attempted to break into other shops in the neighbourhood.
