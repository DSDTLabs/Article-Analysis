PALAKKAD: SC/ST Welfare minister AK Balan said here on Monday that the government will provide an assistance of Rs 10 lakh to each student from the backward communities who wanted to study abroad.   He said the financial assistance will be given through the Backward Communities Welfare Department or the Kerala State Backward Classes Development Corporation.  At the valedictory function of the trade fair organized by the Corporation at Fort Maidan here on Monday, the minister said the monthly educational assistance and hostel fees of the SC and ST students have been increased from Rs 1,000 to Rs 2,000.
