Portraying the life and legend of the Telugu ruler, thus promoting Telugu culture   seems to have appealed to the Chief Minister,  . He announced that he would exempt entertainment tax on the film for bringing the grandeur of the   history to the screens on the request of Nandamuri   who is playing the protagonist. The film also stars Shriya Saran and Hema Malini in the key roles. Set to release on January 12, the film's trailer has already gotten a lot of attention online. Besides shooting at Kotilingala in Telangana, the film was shot at several historically important locations in the country. 

 
 The chief minister said that the Telangana government has decided to support and encourage films based on noted personalities and historical figures. And the same was applied to the film   earlier.
