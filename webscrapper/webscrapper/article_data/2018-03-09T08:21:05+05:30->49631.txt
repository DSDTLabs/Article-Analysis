HYDERABAD: After a swanky   on nine acres in the city, chief minister   has built another camp office-cum-residence at  , his constituency headquarters. 

 Irrigation minister T Harish Rao and Medak MP K Prabhakar Reddy on Thursday performed the house warming ceremony of the building, which has been constructed on 4,000 sq yards land and equipped with state-of-the-art facilities.
 
  (GADA) officials said the camp office was constructed as part of the plan to build camp offices at all constituency headquarters to hold meetings and to interact with local residents. Though the roads & buildings department had earmarked 1 crore for each camp office, the department has reportedly spent about 5 crore on this one due to security reasons.
