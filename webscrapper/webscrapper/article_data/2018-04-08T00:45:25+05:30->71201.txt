Coimbatore: Three city-based brands and a sportsperson were honoured at the fourth edition of Brand Coimbatore Ambassador awards organised at the Residency Towers here on Saturday.

 Instituted by the city chapter of Indian Chamber of Commerce and Industry and Advertising Club, Coimbatore, the awards recognise the role played by local brands to put Coimbatore on the global and national map.
 
 This year, the awards were given in four categories - iconic brand, legacy brand, emerging brand and brand persona. The iconic brand award was given to Sri Krishna Sweets, while the legacy brand award went to Roots Group of Companies, emerging brand award to Naana Naani Retirement Homes and the brand persona award to the city-based formula one racer Narain Karthikeyan. tnn
