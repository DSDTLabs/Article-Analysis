Hyderabad: City-based vaccine maker   on Wednesday announced that it has initiated a voluntary recall of combopack (Comvac 3 + Biohib vaccine) due to package printing error. It has recalled a single batch of 40,000 vials as a precautionary and safety measure due to wrong detailing on the carton printing, the company said. “The recall was made only because of printing error and not due to any quality issue. The vaccine is a Combopack of (Comvac 3 + BioHib) with two distinct component vaccine batches. After a swift investigation the company also informed the state drug authorities, central drug authorities and its marketing agents, vaccine administrators and doctors,” it said. tnn
