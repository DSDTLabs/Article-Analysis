AHMEDABAD: To promote para-sports and raise awareness and sensitivity towards para-athletes, the Indian para-cycling team along with 60 riders of a city club will go on a ride on Sunday morning, from a starting point at a township on SG Road.

 This road show is part of a series of awareness programs that the AMF ( ),   and Border Security Force (BSF) have planned as a prelude to the Infinity Ride (Shimla to Manali) that will begin on June 10. 
 
 The riders will start at 6am, cycle for an hour and a half hours and end their ride at the same point on SG Road. At the venue,  , who suffered from polio, will also put on display of his brute strength with a session of para-arm wrestling.
