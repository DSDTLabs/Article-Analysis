NASHIK: The Shirdi Police will make use of drones to get a bird's eye view of the town during the centenary celebration of   starting October 1. Police expect a huge turnout of devotees from across the state and the country during the yearlong celebration and have made their plans accordingly .

 Inspector General of Police (special) Vinoy Kumar Chaubey , Nashik Range, said, “We are taking all precautions to ensure that there is no law and order problem or difficulty in movement of vehicular traffic. Deploying   units will be a part of the measures that have been taken.“
 
 “Drone units have also been used by us during big events in the past in Nashik Range. The step will help police take necessary steps to streamline the rush of pilgrims, traffic etc," Chaubey added.
