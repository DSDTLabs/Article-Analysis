Narasaraopet (Guntur): A   Digital Store manager allegedly duped customers by collecting money promising them electronic goods at cheaper prices, at Narasaraopet in Guntur district . The accused was identified as Ram Prasad.

 According to the police, Ram Prasad had been working at the showroom situated on the Palnadu Road for the the past few years. Complaints were filed many people at I Town police station stating that Ram Prasad had collected money promising TVs, refrigerators and laptops at cheaper prices. 
 
 The police said Ram Prasad had spent Rs 10   of the amount collected from people. He failed to deliver the goods as promised. When the people did not find him at the store, they filed complaints against him. Sources said that Ram Prasad was taken into police custody and registered a case against him.
