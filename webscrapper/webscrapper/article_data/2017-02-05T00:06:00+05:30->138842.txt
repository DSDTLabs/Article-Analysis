Mumbai: The city police during an inquiry has found an assistant inspector guilty of allowing deported gangster Mustafa   travel with his wife from Mumbai to Gujarat while he was being taken there for a trial in another case. The cop, Ayaz Patel, has been suspeded.

 Dossa, one of the last few suspects who is still facing trial in the 1993 Mumbai serial blasts case, is lodged at Arthur Road jail. Dossa’s wife had accompanied him during the overnight journey and also discussed business issues with him. The escort police team allegedly did not take any action and allowed the couple to speak despite Dossa being in its custody. 
 
 Deputy commissioner of police Ashok Dudhe, Mumbai police’s spokesperson, said, “After due inquiry in the case, Patel has been found guilty. ”
