AGRA: Etah police arrested the kingpin of an inter-state   racket on Wednesday.

  Identified as Pradeep alias Bantu, the accused is the gram pradhan of Darikpur village and a   leader.
  Police said that they had announced Rs 5000 reward on him as more than half a dozen cases were pending against him.
 
  Superintendent of police (crime)   said, “Bantu is the kingpin of a racket involved in smuggling illicit liquor from Haryana and Chandigarh.”
  Even though the first case against him was lodged in 2012, he has remained absconding so far. “We have recovered a rifle, country-made pistol, cartridges and an SUV in which he was  ,” added the SP.
