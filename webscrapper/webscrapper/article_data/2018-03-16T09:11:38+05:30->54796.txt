KANPUR: If an alliance with the BSP been forged for the 2017 assembly elections, Akhilesh would have been the chief minister of UP, said senior   member and Akhilesh’s uncle Shivpal Yadav.

 
 He was speaking to the media in Saifai, the ancestral village of the Yadav family, on Thursday. Congratulating his nephew and Samajwadi Party
 
 
 president   for the bypoll victories, the   credited him and workers for the results.
 
 
 “It’s due to the hard work by Akhilesh and party workers that we have won. Had an alliance with the BSP been made earlier, Akhilesh would have been CM of UP,”  
 
 On an alliance with the Congress in the future, Shivpal said, “I will share my views if the party asks me.”
 
 
 The gesture by Shivpal is a sign of possible burying of once massive differences between him and his nephew.
