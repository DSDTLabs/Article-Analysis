Pune: An 18-year-old man was crushed in an accident near the Khandala tunnel on the Pune-Mumbai   on Sunday evening. 

 According to the police, Sayyed Sadaq Chandpasha Sayyed (18) of Karnataka worked as a cleaner on a truck headed for Mumbai from Pune. Shortly after crossing the tunnel, the truck’s fan-belt snapped, forcing the driver to pull over. 
 
 Sayed got out and was inspecting the truck from the front, when another truck, also headed for Mumbai, crashed into the stationary truck. Sayyed was found under the truck’s front tyre.
 
 This was the third fatal accident this month on the expressway. On April 19, three people including a nine-year-old girl died after their car hit a tree on the expressway. Six others suffered injuries and were admitted to a local hospital.
 
 On April 16, the cleaner of a tempo was killed instantly while its driver sustained serious injuries when the vehicle rammed into a stationary truck on the expressway.
