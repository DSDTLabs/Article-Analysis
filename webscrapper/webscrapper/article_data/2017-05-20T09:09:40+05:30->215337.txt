BENGALURU: Five persons were killed when a car collided with a truck in Raichur district on late Friday night. The victims included three children.

 The incident happened at Dadhesaguru of Sindhanuru taluk in Raichur district.
 
 All the victims --   (33), wife Malana (32), Sabeera (14), Gulabi Shah (5), Hussain Pasha (3) -- belonged to the same family and were residents of Gangavathi.
