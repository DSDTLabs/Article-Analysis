The upcoming Tamil drama flick ‘Padaiveeran,’ directed and written by Dhana, has Malayalam actor   in the lead role.

 The trailer of the movie is just out and one can’t help getting surprised by the ease makeover of the State Award winner singer into a rustic youth who goes on to become a police officer. Director – actor Bharathi Raja is also part of the film, which has Amritha as the female lead. Karthik Raja is the composer of the movie, which has songs penned by the director, along with Mohan Rajan and Priya.
 
  
  
 ‘Padaiveeran’ is expected to be in theaters on February 2 and is bankrolled by Madhivanan.
