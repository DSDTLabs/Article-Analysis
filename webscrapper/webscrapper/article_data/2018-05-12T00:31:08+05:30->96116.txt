Jaisalmer: A labourer died when a wall of an under construction house collapsed in Jaisalmer. Although other labourers managed to pull him out, doctors declared him dead on arrival.

 As per information from police, construction of a house was going on and five to six labourers were working when the wall of the underground portion collapsed in which Raees Khan (32) resident of Bhaniyana Pokharan got trapped and died. Police said the body was handed over after post-mortem to the family and investigation has started after lodging case.
 
 The house was being built just 100 meters from Sonar Fort, which is a UNESCO world heritage site. There is no information on whether due permission from ASI was sought or not and it is matter of investigation.
