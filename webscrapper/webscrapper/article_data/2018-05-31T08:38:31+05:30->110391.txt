KOLKATA: Doctors at the   are waiting for a test report from the National Institute of Virology (NIV), Pune before arriving at any conclusion on the cause of death of Seenu Prasad (27), a soldier of the Signals Regiment. Prasad, posted at Fort William, was admitted to hospital on May 20 with Nipah-like symptoms. He died on Friday. Till the reports arrive, doctors can’t arrive at any conclusion, a defence spokesman said. Prasad had returned from Kerala after nearly a month’s leave. His falling ill immediately after the home visit and the symptoms prompted doctors to suspect that he may have been infected by the virus.
