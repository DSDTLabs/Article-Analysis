NEW DELHI: Four shops, including two gun shops, and a guesthouse were gutted in a fire that broke out in  's Bara Bazar,  , on Saturday . Officials said 25 fire engines were sent to the spot as the cops feared that ammunition stored in the gun shops could cause a major mishap. No one was injured. Fire officials said that they received a call around 8.30pm about the fire breaking out at a shoe showroom in the market and immediately sent 10 engines to the spot. However, the fire was found to be spreading to the neighbouring structures, after which more fire tenders had to be sent. Officials said that the quick response had helped in averting a major mishap. The guest house was more than 50-year-old. Police said the fire was caused by a short circuit in one of the many loose wires that hang from the ceilings in the market.
