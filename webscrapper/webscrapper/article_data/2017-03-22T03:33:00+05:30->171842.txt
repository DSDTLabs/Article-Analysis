Margao: The   panchayat constituted its biodiversity management committee at a meeting held recently. 

 Jose Rodrigues is the committee chairperson, and Royla Fernandes, Glyda Fernandes, Nanda Vagurmekar, Remedios Fernandes, Roque Fernandes, and Dinesh Gaonkar its members. The panel is tasked with preparing a people’s biodiversity register in consultation with villagers. 
 
 In a press statement, Rodrigues said, “Besides documenting our natural resources, we have to protect and preserve them so that our future generations can have the life we’d like them to have.” tnn
