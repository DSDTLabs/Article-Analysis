Rajkot: Chief Minister Vijay Rupani will be in Rajkot on Saturday and Sunday to attend various programmes. 

 On Saturday, Rupani will attend BJP’s booth-level workers’ programme at 6:30 pm. He will be felicitated by various organizations at 9:15 pm. 
 
 On Sunday, CM is scheduled to inaugurate newly built police housing quarters at 9am. He will also lay foundation stones for new building at civil hospital at 10 am. 
 
 Later in the day, he will attend a mass marriage.
