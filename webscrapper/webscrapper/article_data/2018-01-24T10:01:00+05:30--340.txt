MUMBAI: The State Bank of India (SBI) will be hiring Junior Associates in the Customer Support and Sales department for the year 2018 across the SBI branches located in India. 

 The recruitment of the applicants would be at the clerical grade and the position is open for a total of 8,301 Junior Associates or JA's or clerks. 
 
 The vacancy also includes the 1,101 backlog from last year. The hiring would be done on in two stages - preliminary and main written examination followed by a test of local opted specified local language. 
 
 The SBI has specified that the candidates can apply for a vacancy in one state only. Also, candidates can apply appear only once for this test. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The candidates who wish to apply for the SBI clerk position are required to apply online before February 10, 2018, on the official website sbi.co.in/careers. 
 
 
 
 
 The preliminary exam for SBI Clerk 2018 is likely to be held in the month of March or April.
