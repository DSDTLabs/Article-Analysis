AHMEDABAD:   on Saturday found yet another mobile phone, taking the tally of the phones recovered on the premises over the past two months to eight.

 According to   police,  , a jailor with the jail, has filed a complaint under provisions of IPC and Jail Manual after the discovery of the mobile phone on Saturday evening outside barrack number 6 of Bhagat Singh Yard III. The phone was found during an inspection as a portion of the ground outside the barrack had been freshly dug. A shallow pit had the phone with battery and SIM card.
