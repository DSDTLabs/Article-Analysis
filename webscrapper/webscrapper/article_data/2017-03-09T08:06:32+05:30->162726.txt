NAVI MUMBAI The   has asked members of political parties to remove the dozens of newspaper stands and offices that have allegedly been constructed on footpaths and open spaces in  . 

 Members of BJP and PWP were allegedly involved in setting up these offices.
 
 PCMC commissioner Sudhakar Shinde during a visit had come across these newspaper kiosks built on footpaths by BJP and PWP activists in some sectors. Party workers have been warned that the PCMC would dismantle these kiosks if they were not removed in two days. 
 
 Activist Vijay Kale said, “These offices are a nuisance and they are illegally constructed. All these newspaper kiosks and political offices should be dismantled.”
