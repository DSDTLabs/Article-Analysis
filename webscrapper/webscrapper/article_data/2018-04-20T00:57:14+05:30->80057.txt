Udhagamandalam: A tourist from Kerala was killed and three suffered injuries when the car they were travelling crashed into an SUV near Sandynallah bus stop here on Thursday. The deceased, K Sameer Ali, 33, and three others, all from Kerala, were on their way to Pykara Dam, a tourist spot 30 km away from Ooty.  “When their car was near Sandynallah bus stop, it collided with the SUV. Sameer Ali died on the spot. The SUV was carrying nine from a family in Karnataka. They sustained minor injuries and are being treated at the Ooty Government Hospital,” said a police officer.
