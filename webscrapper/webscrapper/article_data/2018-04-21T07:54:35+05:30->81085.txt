FEROZPUR: The supporters of   MLA Rana Gurmit Singh Sodhi took to streets to celebrate his elevation to the Punjab cabinet on Friday.
 
 The   activists danced to the dhol beats, distributed sweets and burst fire crackers in   district.
 
 Within minutes of release of the official list of the ministers to be inducted, his supporters gathered at the Guruhar Sahai residence of Sodhi to celebrate.
 
 His son   thanked party chief   and CM   for giving this honour to Ferozepur district.
