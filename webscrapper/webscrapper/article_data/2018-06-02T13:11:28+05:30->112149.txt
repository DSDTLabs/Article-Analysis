The Audio Tech music recently organised a live   concert at Savitri Lawns, Aurangabad. A 15 member team captivated the audience with their music and vocals. The audience were singing along with the band and were seen dancing too.
Some of the favourite numbers performed by the band were Dulhe ka sehra, Nisarg Raja, Kalya Matit Matit a and many more. The band comprised of semi classical musicians, Bollywood, solo, pop and rock, including instrumentalist who played the harmonium,  , guitar, octopad, dholak,  , tabla, flute and many more.
 This was a houseful show, and audience were mesmerized by the performance.
