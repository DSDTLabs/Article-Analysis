Rajkot: Three persons, including two women, were arrested in connection with attacks on two people at two different places in the city on Sunday. On the basis of a case registered with Bhaktinagar police station, all three are nabbed with knife and a bike without a number plate. 

 According to police, three accused, Hushenmiya Bukhari (21), Radhika allies Rakhi Dhamecha (19), and Ravina allies Ravu (19) were arrested from Nanamava chowk of Rajkot. 
 
 During interrogation, involvement of a minor was also revealed. The trio is accused of attacking one Vishal Patadia with a knife near Sorathiyawadi circle and breaking glass of car on Kevdavadi road after altercation with car owner.
