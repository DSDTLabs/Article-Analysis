HYDERABAD: Burglars gained entry into   (IKP) office located at Shamsahabad and escaped with Rs 50,000 worth valuables, including some computers, on Wednesday night.

 According to police, burglars broke in through the main door of the IKP office and gained entry into the building. 
 
 “The offenders stole two computers and escaped from the office,”   inspector M Mahesh said. There were no CCTV cameras at the IKP office and a forensic team visited the spot to lift fingerprints.
 
 Based on a complaint lodged by the representatives of IKP, Shamshabad office, a   case was registered under sections 457 and 380 of the Indian Penal Code (IPC).
