Chennai: A 33-year-old   was fatally run over by a  , near Anna Salai on Wednesday night.

 Police identified the victim as Syed Kasim, 33, a resident of Seven Wells. Kasim was on his way home on his bike after meeting one of his friends, police said. When he reached Periyar statue, he rammed into a jeep and fell down, when a MTC bus ran over him.
 
 The bus with route number 32B (Korrukupet to Ice House), crushed him to death on the spot. The victim who was not wearing a helmet, died of severe head injuries. He was taken to Rajiv Gandhi government general hospital where he was declared brought dead. His body has been sent for a post mortem.
 
 Police have registered a case under IPC section 279 (rash driving) and 304 A (causing death due to negligence) and are questioning the bus driver Nagaselvam, 32, of Arupukottai.
