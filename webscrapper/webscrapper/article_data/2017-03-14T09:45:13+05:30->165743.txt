 It recommended the creation of an environment management and development authority to draft special building rules for Munnar in six months after stopping all construction. It also sought special funds for scientific disposal of waste in Munnar.
 
 Stating that land should not be assigned for dangerous non-housing constructions hereafter and that buildings erected beyond permissible height should be immediately demolished, the committee recommended cancelling title deeds if any violation of conditions was found in land use.
 
 Similarly, it wanted publicity initiatives for tourism in the region to comply with the green protocol while all programmes should be subject to a social audit by research students and environmental activists. The committee sought stringent penalties for vehicular pollution in Munnar besides recommending an immediate ban on planting of eucalyptus trees in the region. It emphasised that the style of farming and use of pesticides in the region should be environment-friendly.
