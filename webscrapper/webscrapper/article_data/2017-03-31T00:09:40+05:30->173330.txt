Vadodara: A Non Resident Indian (NRI) and his friends lost their luggage from their car on National Highway 8 near Karjan on Wednesday.

 Snehal Desai, a native of Navsari, told police that an unidentified person stole the luggage from his friend Sanjeev’s car when they had stopped at a restaurant. Police said that the thief might have stolen the luggage from the car’s boot as one of the doors was open.Desai and Sanjeev were coming to Vadodara from Surat along with their other friends Yamini and Bharat Joshi. The thief stole a camera, wrist watch, cash, Desai’s passport, driving license and PAN card, mobile phone, ornaments and apparels.TNN
