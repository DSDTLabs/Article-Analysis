The fear of burglars has sent the entire State into a tizzy, after black stickers were found on houses, attached to the window panes. 

 The satirical show ' ', which always takes up contemporary issues, will showcase the issue in its next episode, which will be aired today. The teaser of the episode shows the neighborhood panicking over the stickers and trying to patrol the area at night. As always, everything turns into a laugh riot.
 
  
  473 incidents of black stickers were found in a week, from Kasaragod to Thiruvananthapuram. The maximum number of cases were reported in Thiruvananthapuram rural, followed by Thiruvananthapuram city, Kottayam, Ernakulam city, Ernakulam rural and Kasaragod. The cases were registered under IPC sections 425 and 426.
