NEW DELHI:   ( ) is likely to announce VITEEE 2018 results today, April 25, 2018. 

 
 Last year, the VIT had announced the VITEEE results on April 23, 2017 and as per the schedule published in the VITEEE 2018 brochure the result will be announced on or before April 27, 2018. 
 
 The results will be announced on vit.ac.in. For checking the results, the students would need to submit their application number and date of birth in DD/MM/YYYY format. Also, the students, who have enrolled themselves for the exam, will be informed about their rank via SMS and email, provided at the time of registration. 
 
 The VITEEE 2018 results were prepared through an equipercentile equating method. 
 
 On the basis of VITEEE 2018 rank, the students will be granted admissions in the four campuses of the university. On the basis VITEEE rank, the students will be called for counseling and seat allocation.
