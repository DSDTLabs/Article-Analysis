BELAGAVI: Union minister Anantkumar Hegde’s statement that AICC president  , who has been visiting places of worship, should also go to  , the abode of Lord Bahubali, has sparked off protests from the Jain community.

 
 Members of the community from  , Degaon and Degulahalli in Kittur, 48km from Belagavi, on Monday staged protests condemning Hegde’s derogatory statement. They had brought an effigy of the Union minister to burn, but election authorities did not allow them to do so.
 
 They expressed their anger by beating the effigy with footwear and raising slogans against him. Later, they submitted a memorandum to tahsildar Praveen Hucchannavar.
 
 On Sunday, addressing a BJP rally at Tigadolli village,   insisted that Rahul should go to Shravanabelagola. “Rahul wears a skull cap when he visits mosques, saffron robes to temples, rudraksha mala to mutts and a cross to church. He should also go to Shravanabelagola,” the minister said, adding, “I think you all understand what I am saying."
