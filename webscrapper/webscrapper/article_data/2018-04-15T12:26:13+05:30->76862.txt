JAMSHEDPUR: A 14-year-old girl was allegedly gangraped by three people while she was on her way to school in   early on Saturday. Police said the three men intercepted the girl and took her to a railway quarters, where they took turns to rape her. 

 A passer-by saw the men forcing the girl into the railway quarters raised an alarm, following which the girl's parents reached the railway quarters under Manoharpur police jurisdiction. They found the door locked and broke it open, but the men managed to flee the spot. The parents immediately informed the police, which filed an FIR against three people.
