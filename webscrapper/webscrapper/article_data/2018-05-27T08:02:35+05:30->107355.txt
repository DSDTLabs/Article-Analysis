CHANDIGARH: Two   helicopters were pressed into action to lift water from Sukhna Lake and douse fire that broke in the forest area of Kasauli on Saturday evening.

 The helicopters, which were equipped with buckets to carry around 2,000 liters of water at a time, made repeated trips between 5:30pm and 6:30pm.
 
 The fire broke in a part of a forest which later spread in a large area, forcing the local authorities to seek help from the Indian Air Force.
 
 The Air Force authorities informed Chandigarh deputy commissioner before taking water, who according to sources, advised them to take water from   as   was facing problem of depleting water level. However, water was lifted from the lake.
