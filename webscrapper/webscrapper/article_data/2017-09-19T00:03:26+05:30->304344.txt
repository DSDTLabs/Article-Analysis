GHAZIABAD: Taking on powerful land mafia in the district,   administration has lodged 130 FIRs against them including under stringent Goonda act. The administration had identified 603 hectares of government land across the district which has been taken over by land mafia out of which officials claim ta 131 hectares have been freed in a series of drive that was undertaken by the administration.

  “During the course of survey we had identified 603 hectares of land belonging to government agencies and departments which was taken over by land mafia” said Gyanendra Singh ADM (administration). “About 468 hectares of land belonging to GMC and   and another 135 hectares of land of GDA, UPSIDC, electricity, Irrigation and other departments were encroached and due to our concerted we have freed 131 hectares of land till 31 August” added Singh.
 
 In a span of three months there have been 130 FIR’s lodged against land mafias under different IPC sections and the case is going on in tehisdar’s courts against 1369 people which will deliver its verdict in stipulated 90 days time. “We have also identified 17 hard core land mafia against whom   has been registered under   act ” said Singh.
 
 Even though drive against land mafia is underway, administration is in a fix over encroached government lands where full fledged colonies have come up. As per one record there are close to 250 such illegal 250 colonies in the district which includes Arthala, Ambedkar Nagar, Mata colony to name a few. “We have sent the list of such colonies to decide on this” added Singh. But an official in GDA said that the matter came up for discussion in a high level meeting in Lucknow in which it was discussed as to how such colonies could be regularized. 
 
 Yogi government soon after assuming charge had vowed to take on land mafia in the state and free government lands. An anti- land mafia task force was also constituted across districts to identify the encroached land and initiate action to free it.
