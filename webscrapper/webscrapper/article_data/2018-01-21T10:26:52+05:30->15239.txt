Ranchi: Six persons were injured in a clash between   (BAU) students and local residents under Kanke police station on Saturday. A team of police force was rushed to the spot to bring the situation under control. 

 A police officer said, "A group of locals entered the BAU campus to look for a student. When the students refused to share any information about him, they started beating them up. The injured were later admitted to   hospital."
 
 However, BAU students blocked the   located on the   demanding arrest of the culprits as soon as possible. The agitation was finally withdrawn later in the evening after assurance by a DSP on due consideration to the student's demands.
