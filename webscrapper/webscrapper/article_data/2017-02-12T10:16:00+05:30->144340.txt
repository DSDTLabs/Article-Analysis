VISAKHAPATNAM: Lok Adalat judgements are permanent and if the circumstances are favourable for a settlement, the complainants and victims can resolve their cases, said district judge P   while the inaugurating the Lok Adalat in the district courts premises on Saturday.

 District joint collector D   said 15 benches were set up for Lok Adalat and around 4,536 cases came for settlement of which 97 were related to banks, 157 land disputes, 297 related to cash transactions, 2,802 criminal cases, 692 cheque bounce cases and others. 
 
 Joint commissioner of police A   said they have identified 1,292 cases and issued notices to 1,011 for early justice to the complainants.
