Rajkot: A 52-year-old woman succumbed to the deadly H1N1 virus (swine flu), at a hospital in the city on Wednesday night. This was the first case of swine flu death this year. Dr Pankaj Rathod, health officer at RMC, said the woman Kanta Bodar was admitted to the private hospital after complaining of severe cough and fever. “ We have performed health check-ups of her immediate kin and nearby residents,” Rathod added. tnn
