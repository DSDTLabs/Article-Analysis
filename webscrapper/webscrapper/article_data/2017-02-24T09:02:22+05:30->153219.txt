MANGALURU: Despite calls from saffron fringe groups to hold a hartal on Saturday opposing the visit of Kerala chief minister  , the department of   (DPUE) is all set to go ahead with the ongoing annual examinations of I PUC on Saturday.

 K R Thimmaiah, DDPU, DK, in an official note said that all the examinations of Saturday will be held and there is no plan to postpone the same. He added that a decision has been taken after a round of talks with the regional commissioner, deputy commissioner, DK and C Shikha, director, DPUE. 
 
 “We have made sure that all transport facilities are in place for students travelling to exam centres to write their exams,” he said.
