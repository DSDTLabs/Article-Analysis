GREATER NOIDA: For ease of doing business and to promote setting up of industry,   chief   Yogi Aditya Nath will inaugurate a single window portal for entrepreneurs during the upcoming Investors’ Summit in Lucknow next month. The development authorities of Noida, Greater Noida and Yamuna Expressway (YEIDA) will be part of this portal named ‘Single Window’ under the banner of the already existing platform of Udyog Bandhu’s ‘Nivesh Mitra’. Udyog Bandhu, a government agency for investment promotion, will act as a nodal agency for implementation of this project.

 A pre-launch meeting was held by CM’s principal secretary S.P  , with officials from several districts of the state besides that of three industrial development authorities of Noida, Greater Noida and Yamuna Expressway, in Lucknow on Tuesday.
