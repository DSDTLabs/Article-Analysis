MUMBAI: An   twin-engine A321 aircraft bound for Ahmedabad was towed back to a parking bay at the   on Sunday evening after a fire was reported in its left engine. Flight AI 091 had begun the takeoff run when the pilots aborted the takeoff at 9.21 pm, said an airport official.

 An aircraft rescue and firefighting manager who was monitoring the situation reported visible fire in the left engine around 9.26 pm. Following this, the commander switched off both the engines and the aircraft was towed to a parking bay.
