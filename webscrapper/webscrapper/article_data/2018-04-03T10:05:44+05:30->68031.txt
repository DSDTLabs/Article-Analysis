 The deputy CM’s son is facing allegations that he was part of a conspiracy behind the murder of YSRCP leader Lakshminarayana Reddy in Kurnool district. Following a private complaint by Sridevi, the deceased leader’s wife, the trial court took up the case and issued non-bailable warrant against Syam Babu.
 
 Syam Babu approached the high court challenging the proceedings. The HC passed an interim order staying the process and called for counters from the authorities.
