KANNUR:   Kannur mandalam vice president   got injured when he was stabbed by an   that came on motorbikes near   on Wednesday evening.

 According to police, the BJP leader was attacked around 9 pm, near Olacherikkavu in Kannur Town by a gang of more than six members. Since they wore masks, they could not be identified by the victim, said police. Sushil Kumar has been rushed to Kozhikode for treatment. Though the injuries on his stomach and leg are serious, he is not critical, said police.
 
 Meanwhile the BJP alleged that the CPM is behind the attack, and registered a police complaint. However, nobody has been named personally in the complaint.
 
 Police said they have registered a case and a man hunt has been launched to nab the attackers.
