KANYAKUMARI: A 13-year-old girl died while having breakfast in her house at   on the   in   on Thursday. The victim was identified as Afreen, daughter of Jilani and Irfana. According to the police, preliminary inquiry revealed that the girl was eating idly with her relatives at her home on Thursday morning when she fainted.

 Relatives rushed her to a nearby private hospital, where she was declared dead on arrival. Her body was taken to the Asaripallam Government Medical College Hospital for post-mortem. Police suspect that an idly might have got stuck in her windpipe and died from suffocation. However, they said that the reason could be ascertained only after receiving the post-mortem report.
 
  police have registered the case as unnatural death under the code of criminal procedures and are investigating.
