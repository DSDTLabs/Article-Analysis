Ludhiana: Jamalpur police on Thursday busted a gang of snatchers with the arrest of five miscreants. Cops seized 12 mobile phones from the accused, identified as Rajinder Singh Khurki, Amit Sharma of Giaspura, Deepak of Civil Lines, Mani alias Rajesh of Manjit Nagar and Raj Kapoor of Sant Pura. 

 Avtar Singh, SHO, Jamalpur police station, said they arrested the five at a check post at Jamalpur Chowk on Chandigarh road. He said the accused were coming from Samrala side when they were stopped for checking. 
 
 Police said the accused were going to sell mobile phones that they had snatched in the area. The accused in their 20s are drug addicts.
 
 Cops said while Rajinder is already facing cases at Dugri police station for burglaries, Deepak was wanted in four cases of burglaries in Chandigarh. They added that the accused have been booked under relevant sections of IPC and are being questioned to get more information about their crimes.
