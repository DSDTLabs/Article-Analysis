HYDERABAD: Dry and warm weather conditions would prevail over Telangana over the next one week pushing day temperatures to 39 degrees Celsius in Hyderabad and up to 42 degree Celsius in districts by March 3.

 Officials attributed it to flow of easterly and southeasterly winds over the state. The  , in a press communique on Friday, said the maximum temperature is likely to rise by three to five degrees, with no or minimal change in the minimum temperature.While the maximum temperature would range between 34-42 degrees Celsius, the minimum temperature would hover between 16-22 degrees Celsius. The society monitors the weather condition on a daily basis.
