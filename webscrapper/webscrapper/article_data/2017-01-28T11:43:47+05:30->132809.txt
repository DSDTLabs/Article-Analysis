AGRA: City police on Thursday busted an arms factory that was illegally manufacturing a large number of country-made pistols keeping in view the upcoming elections.

  Based on a tip-off, police raided the factory and seized 20 pistols, equipment and machinery besides 30 semi-fabricated firearms. The factory was located in a closed railway godown in Freeganj. Police arrested  , a resident of Govardhan in Mathura, in connection with the incident.
