NAGPUR: Independent candidate from prabhag 4 Surekha   was asked to appear for investigations by the Yashodhara Nagar police station on Monday in connection with the case in which her campaign car crashed into a roadside hutment killing two persons and injuring five others near   last week.

 However, Surekha failed to turn up at the police station. 
 
 Senior PI GM Mujawar of the Yashodhara Nagar police station said that a notice under section 161 of the Criminal procedure code would now be issued to Surekha.
 
 Police also kept on quizzing Gaurav Borkar (19), who was behind the wheels of the campaign car. Borkar, who is now custody till February 15, did not had licence.
