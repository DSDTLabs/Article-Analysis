Actress   says film industry can be an extremely challenging place especially for newcomers.
 
  
  
 The actress, who made her big screen debut in  's ' ', says one goes through a lot of emotions during their initial days in the industry. "When you come to the industry, specially for a female, it's quite a whirlwind. Especially when things take off, you think 'I want to be a heroine, I am getting roles, my debut film is out, so I need to do a big banner project,'" Sapna said.
