GUWAHATI:   (TU) faculty member   has been selected for the Indian   (INSA) Young Scientist Medal. 

 Pratihar, from the department of chemical sciences of TU, has been awarded the prestigious INSA Medal for Young Scientist Award 2017 for his excellent contribution to multi-metal complexes, theoretical understanding, selective sensing of Hq2+ ions and important applications in agriculture. The award, which consists of a bronze medal, cash award of Rs 25,000 and other important incentives will be given to   in the anniversary meeting of INSA to be held in December.
 
 Instituted in 1974 by the Indian National Science Academy, INSA Medal for Young Scientists is awarded annually to honour talented young Indian scientists in recognition of their outstanding contributions in any branch of science or technology within the purview of the academy.
