PANAJI: Aam Aadmi Party volunteers have complained that they were being threatened by political opposition while campaigning in the state capital. 

 The party, which is conducting its anti-corruption movement for the past three days, has complained that workers of other political parties have confronted and threatened them to stop standing in public areas with placards and pamphlets. 
 
 “This has been going on for the past three days. Some people came, encircled and threatened us that they would complain to the   and the police. We showed them our permission papers and then they went away,” AAP volunteer Anjit Kumar said, adding that political rivals have been threatening them with obnoxious language. tnn
