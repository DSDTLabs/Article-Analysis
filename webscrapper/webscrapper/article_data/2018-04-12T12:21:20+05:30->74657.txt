NEW DELHI: Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani (BITS Pilani) has released   today on its official website: bitsadmission.com. The candidates can download their Admit Cards from April 12 to May 10, 2018. 

 
 The   Admit Card can be downloaded by submitting application number and date of birth in DD/MM/YYYY format.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The BITSAT 2018 Admit Card will only be available for those candidates who have opted for slot booking. 
 
 The BITSAT 2018 Admit Card will only be available online and it is important to carry the admit card on the day of the examination. 
 
 Candidates should note that the official website is displaying the message "BITSAT-2018 hall ticket will be released on 12th April 2018" but the link to download the BITSAT 2018 Admit Card has not been activated yet. Candidates are advised to keep checking the official website as the link can be made active anytime from now. Also keep a tab on this page for more information.
