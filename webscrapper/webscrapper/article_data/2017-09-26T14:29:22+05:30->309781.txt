VARANASI:   Vice-Chancellor GC Tripathi on Tuesday left for Delhi.

 Regarding his Delhi visit, the BHU spokesman Rajesh Singh claimed that he had gone to attend Executive Council's quarterly meet that was pre-scheduled. On the other hand, rumours were rife that Tripathi has been summoned by the HRD ministry. Singh held such claims as baseless. 
 
 
 
 Meanwhile, in his report on   submitted to state government, divisional commissioner Nitin Ramesh Gokarn held BHU administration responsible for violent incidents. Had varsity tackled girl students issue timely, the untoward incident on campus could be averted, he added.
