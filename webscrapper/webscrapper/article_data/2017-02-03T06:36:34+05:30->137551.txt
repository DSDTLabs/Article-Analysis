AHMEDABAD: In a major fire at  , a plywood unit was gutted after the flames engulfed the unit’s production and storage area early on Thursday morning. It took Ahmedabad   ( ) 16 hours to douse the flames.

 According AFES officials, they received a call at 4.40am on Thursday from Sarvoday Plywood in Naroda GIDC on  . A team started operations with one firefighter and seven water tankers. There were no casualties or injuries reported.
 
 “While the cause of fire is not known, we suspect it may be due to a spark from a boiler or a naked flame. As the unit was full of plywood, raw material, finished goods and packing material, the flames spread quickly and gutted the boiler, machinery and factory shed,” said an AFES official.
