 Shah Rukh, who has featured in some of the biggest hits helmed by Chopra like ‘Darr’, ‘Dil Toh Pagal Hai’, ‘Veer-Zaara’ and ‘Jab Tak Hai Jaan’ said that it was very special moment for him to receive the award that is in memory of the legendary Yash Chopra who has been a father-figure to him.
 
 At the event, in one of the media interactions SRK mentioned, “Yash ji was unique in his own way. A very earnest, honest and straightforward person. His simplicity makes him special.” On being asked if it’s an emotional moment, he said it was more of a personal moment than an emotional one.
 
 The ‘Raees’ actor was presented with the award by Rekha. Also present at the award function were the late director’s wife Pamela Chopra,  ,  , Padmini Kolhapure, Simi Grewal and many more.
