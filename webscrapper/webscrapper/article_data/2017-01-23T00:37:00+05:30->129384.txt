MUMBAI: A special  , on Saturday, allowed   Bhujbal and 15 other accused in a case of money laundering to furnish bonds of Rs 2 lakh each after they presented themselves before the judge P R Bhavake in the case. 

  The court also allowed an interim   to be released on a provisional cash bail to be furnished within 15 days, said advocate Ashwin Thool.
