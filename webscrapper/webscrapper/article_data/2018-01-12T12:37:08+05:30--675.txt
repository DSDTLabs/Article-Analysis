 After much speculation on the internet regarding the date sheet of Board examination, the NBSE Board has finally released the higher secondary annual examination dates for Board students on its official website - nbsenagaland.com. 
 
 
 
 
 
 The students appearing for the annual board examination can check the timetable of the NBSE Board Examinations on the official portal of the NBSE Board - nbsenagaland.com
 
 The official notification regarding the date sheet of NBSE Board HSSLC Board Exam is available on the official website of the NBSE Board. 
 
 As the date sheet is now officially released by the NBSE Board, the students can download it from the official website of the NBSE Board - nbsenagaland.com. 
 
 The students are also advised to save and print their   Date sheet for further reference. The students appearing for NBSE Board Examination should revise the important topics of their textbooks prescribed by the NBSE Education Board. 
 
 The NBSE Board Examinations are generally held in the month of February-March. The results of the NBSE Board examination are announced in the month of May-June.
