 Deepak Aggarwal, a resident of Model Town, Delhi, is a CA working with a Noida-based firm. On Saturday, he had gone to the Gurgaon branch of the firm, located in Sector 42, Gurgaon. According to the police, Aggarwal was travelling with a friend and had stopped his car opposite a liquor vend in Maharana Pratap Chowk around 11pm. He got out of the car and was walking towards the vend when he saw two men trying to get inside his car. When he raised an alarm, the duo fled. Aggarwal left the spot and was driving towards Old bus stand when the accused accosted him, and pointed a gun at him through the car window. Aggarwal and his friend were then forced to get out of the car and hand over the car keys to the duo. The accused fled in his car.
 
 An FIR has been registered against the accused under sections 379B (snatching) of IPC and 25-54-59 of Arms Act at Civil Line police station.
