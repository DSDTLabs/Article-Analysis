In an event conducted by the fine arts society of Raipur, youngsters painted the walls adjacent to the   railway station. As part of the event, they came armed with paint brush, colours, and other things to paint the walls. These walls were worn off earlier, thanks to the youngsters, they look brand new. As many as 200 youngsters were part of the event to beautify Raipur.
