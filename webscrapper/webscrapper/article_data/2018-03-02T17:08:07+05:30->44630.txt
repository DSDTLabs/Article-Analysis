It’s a well-known fact that ‘Chiyaan’ Vikram’s son Dhruv is making his Tamil debut with the forthcoming film ‘Varma’, a remake of the Telugu blockbuster ‘Arjun Reddy’. The makers have finally kickstarted the shoot at Kathmandu in Nepal earlier today. An elated   took to Instagram to share a picture from the sets. Titled ‘Varma’ in Tamil, the film is being directed by filmmaker  , who is basking in the success of his recent outing ‘Naachiyar’. The makers are yet to finalise the female lead for the film. Produced by E 4 Entertainment, ‘Varma’ will have dialogues by the National Award-winning director Raju Murugan.

 ‘Arjun Reddy’ was a sensational hit when it released last year. The film directed by Sandeep Reddy Vanga tells the story of a medical surgeon   (Vijay Deverakonda), who falls for his college junior Preethi (Shalini). Arjun becomes alcoholic post break-up and the rest of the story is about how he redeems himself. Upon release, the film won both critical and commercial success.
 
 ‘Varma’ is likely to release by the end of this year.
