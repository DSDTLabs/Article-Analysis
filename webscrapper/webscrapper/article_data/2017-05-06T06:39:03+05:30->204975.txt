 In a release issued here on Friday, SGPC president Kirpal Singh Badungar said that in wake of problems faced by Sikh students during last NEET examination, the committee had written a letter in advance to the CBSE chairman Rakesh Kumar Chaturvedi.
 
 He was been requested to issue required instructions to examination centres not to disallow baptized Sikh students supporting their religious symbols from appearing in NEET examination being held on May 7.
