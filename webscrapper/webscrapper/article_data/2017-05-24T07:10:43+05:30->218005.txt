SURAT: A   attempted suicide by cutting his wrists and throat with a blade near Tapi river embankment here on Tuesday. Ashwin  , owner of an embroidery unit, told his family members that he was going to his factory but reached causeway at around 8.30am. 

 He cut his wrists and throat with a blade and soon fell on the river embankment near the causeway. He was admitted to New Civil Hospital by the people.   police station sources said he was facing financial problems and owed money to a lot of people. Doctors said his condition was stable now.
