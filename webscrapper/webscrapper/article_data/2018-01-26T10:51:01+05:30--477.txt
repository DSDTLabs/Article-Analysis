AMRITSAR: A scuffle broke out between the   (SAD) and   activists at Sheron village in Tarn Taran district on Thursday. According to police, a group of Congress activists led by Kulwant Singh entered into a verbal duel with   activist Sona Singh, a former sarpanch, over some issue following which shots were fired in the air but no one was injured. Patti deputy superintendent of police (DSP) Sohan Singh said an incident of firing shots had taken place in Sheron. He said police were identifying the alleged accused and was in the process of recording statements.
