MARGAO: The Curchorem police, late Monday evening, detained 33 mining trucks at Tisk, Sanvordem, for operating beyond the permissible time limit. The trucks were proceeding towards Capxem jetty to unload the ore they were transporting from Sesa Goa’s Codli mines, police sources said. 

 PSI   Coutinho told   action was taken under Section 188 of the Indian Penal Code for disobeying the order promulgated by an authority. “A report will be submitted to the deputy collector for necessary action,” she said.
