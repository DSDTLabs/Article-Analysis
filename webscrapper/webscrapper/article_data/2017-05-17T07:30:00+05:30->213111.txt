GURUGRAM: Five people were booked on Monday for allegedly pressuring a 45-year-old building material supplier, Nihal, to commit suicide. Nihal consumed poison in his   office on Sunday evening. 

 Nihal passed away on Monday morning during treatment at a private hospital, after he was taken there by his colleagues who found him in an unconscious state in his office on Sunday evening. 
 
 Nihal’s family was informed at around 9pm on Sunday. Nihal’s brother   told police on Monday that during Nihal’s treatment at the hospital, he had told Chaturbhuj that he took the extreme step under pressure from five people. 
 
 Police have registered a case against Rajesh and Lala of Daultabad, Janak and   of Palda village, and Masti of  , all acquaintances of the deceased. 
 
 A post mortem was conducted on Monday, after which the body was handed over to the family for the last rites. Nihal is survived by his wife and two children.
 
 Based on Chaturbhuj’s complaint, an FIR was registered against the five accused at Sector 53 police station, under sections 306 (abetment of suicide) and 34 (act by several people in furtherance of common intention) of the IPC.
 
 Assistant sub inspector Krishan Kumar said they are investigating the matter, but no arrests have been made so far. “During preliminary investigation, we found the deceased may have been under financial duress, and could have borrowed money from the accused. 
 
 The brother told us he had borrowed money, but the amount is not known currently,” he said. He also said that the Gurgaon police were unable to interrogate Nihal following the suicide attempt, as doctors had declared him unfit to give a statement.
