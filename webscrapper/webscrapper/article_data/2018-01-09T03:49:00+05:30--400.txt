Panaji: Third year undergraduate students with grievances in their fifth semester examination results will be refunded the revaluation fees if the fault is found to be a technical error by the university. Goa University Students’ Council chairman Urvesh Redkar, along with other members, met with Goa University registrar Y V Reddy and the controller of examinations Anuradha Wagle on Monday. The duo assured them that if errors were caused due to clerical or computer errors, the revaluation fees would be refunded to the aggrieved student. Redkar raised the issue in a letter addressed to the university’s examination division on January 2 along with the marksheets of few candidates who have alleged underevaluation of their papers. The university has stated that it is independently looking into the issue.
