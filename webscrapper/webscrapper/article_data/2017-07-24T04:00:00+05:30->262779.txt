Surat: A 35-year-old man Mahesh D Rathod was swept away by the waters of Sayan-Sarsana creek near Eathan on Surat-Olpad road on Sunday evening. His body could not be located. 

 Fire brigade officer Bhavesh Ravat said, “Rathod lived at Takarma colony, Halpati Vas. He was immersing an idol in the creek when he lost balance and fell into the water. He was swept away and his body was not found.” Rathod originally belonged to Kosamba town in Surat district.
