Veteran   director   Ramprasad Sharma of the famous Laxmikant-Pyarelal duo appeared on a   music reality show, Sur Nava Dhyas Nava. All the three judges, Shalmali Kholgade,   Kale and   Gupte were thrilled to see the legendary musician amidst them. In fact, quite overwhelmed to receive the warmth by the audiences, Pyarelal won everyone's hearts by saying he will never forget this day. He also gave his expert comments on the performances and shared many interesting incidents from his career.
