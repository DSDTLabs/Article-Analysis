Vadodara: Four women died when their car crashed into an electric light pole in Padamla on national highway 8 on Wednesday. 

 The victims were on way to Vadodara from Ahmedabad. According to police, the driver lost control after one of the tyres burst on the bridge and the car was in full speed.
 
 “The car driver first rammed into the divider and then crashed into an electric light pole. The four women in the car died on the spot. Their bodies were sent to SSG Hospital,” the police said. 
 
 The deceased have been identified as Kailash Vasava, Harshita Vasava, Alka Vasava and Kavya Vasava, all residents of Vadodara. Driver of the car didn’t suffer much injuries.
