Nidahas Trophy triangular Twenty20 International tournament is being held in March 2018 to mark the 70th anniversary of Sri Lanka's independence. The matches will be played between - India, Sri Lanka and Bangladesh.  India will play against host Sri Lanka in the opening match on March`6, 2018. All the matches will be played at the R Premadasa Stadium in Colombo. Each of the three teams will play each other twice, with two teams progressing to the final.
