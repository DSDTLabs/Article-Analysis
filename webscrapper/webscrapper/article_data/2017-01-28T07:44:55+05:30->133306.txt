KANNUR: The war of words between CPM and BJP escalated on Friday over an alleged   with the former accusing the RSS of deliberately trying to create unrest in the state and the latter alleging that the bomb attack story was a concoction.

 A bomb was allegedly thrown near Nangarathu in Thalassery where CPM state secretary   was addressing a public meeting.
 
 “The RSS hurled bombs...but the leadership of the BJP or the RSS has not come forward to condemn the attack,“ said CPM district secretary P Jayarajan. Former chief minister   also blamed the BJP and RSS.
 
 BJP district president   said the `attack' was intended to help the CPM save face as it was on the defensive following the murder of RSS worker  .
