PATIALA: The court of special   ( ) judge Harjit Singh on Saturday sentenced an Army Colonel to three years in jail and imposed a fine of Rs 15,000 after he was found guilty of having accepted bribe for releasing payments to a contractor.

 It was in April 2009 that the CBI had arrested Colonel P K Mohanty from Bathinda cantonment for accepting bribe from a contractor after a trap was laid by the bureau. Mohanty was posted with the   (MES) and had collected Rs 60,000 as bribe for clearing the bills of a road contractor executing work at Bathinda cantonment.
 
 He was tried in the special CBI court,  , which found him guilty of the charges and awarded him the sentence.
