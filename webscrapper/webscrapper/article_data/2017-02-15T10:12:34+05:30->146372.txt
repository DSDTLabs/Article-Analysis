RAJKOT: The   (NCB) sleuths seized 6kg charas estimated to be valued at Rs 30 lakh near Shyamlaji and arrested three persons who were transporting the contraband in their pick-up van on Tuesday. 

 Following specific information, the NCB team from Ahmedabad kept a watch near   on   and stopped the pick-up van coming from Rajasthan. On checking, they found charas stocked on the dash board. The van had a registration number of Vadodara. The contraband was to be supplied in Bharuch, sources said.
 
 Those arrested were identified as Amalam Mohammed Shafi, Shakil Ahmed Ansar, both natives of Bijnaur in Uttar Pradesh and  , a resident of Vadodara.
