NOIDA: A 24-year-old man who used a photo of   and Bollywood actor Deepika Padukone and   an image of Uttar Pradesh chief minister   onto the photo was detained on Saturday.

 Members of Hindu Yuva Vahini informed Sector 24 police station that a resident of Sector 22 had morphed the photo of Deepika Padukone with Hollywood actor Vin Diesel. The complainant alleged that the accused had replaced Diesel with the chief minister.
 
 Vinay Prakash Singh, SHO of Sector 24 police station, said the police team picked up the suspect and went through his phone. 
 
 “We did not find any such photos in his mobile. The suspect was later released,” Singh said.
