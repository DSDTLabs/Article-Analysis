BHUBANESWAR: Police found the body of a man - identified as   - in a hotel in Nayapalli here on Monday evening. Gopal is the brother of   and Trinamool Congress leader  . He was a resident of Bara Bazar in Kolkata. 

 Police suspect that Gopal (42) - who had checked into the hotel alone on Sunday - died of a heart attack. The hotel authorities said staff had entered the room on seeing the door ajar and found him lying motionless on his bed in his third floor room.
 
 "We first tried to wake him and then we informed police. He was taken to a hospital where doctors declared him brought dead," a member of the hotel's staff said.
 
 DCP (Bhubaneswar) Satyabrata Bhoi said a preliminary investigation ruled out foul play. "The man had visited a private hospital near his hotel on Monday afternoon after complaining of chest pain. We found a record of his visit in the hospital and also checked his medical prescription. He purchased some medicines and returned to his room," Bhoi said.
 
 Gopal had also hired a taxi to return to Kolkata on Monday evening. "He seemed to have collapsed after his return from the hospital," Bhoi said. "A postmortem will be done. There were no external injuries on the body," he further said. 
 
 Police said Gopal had ordered an omelette after returning from the hospital. "We found a portion of the omelette on a plate near the bed. Some medicines were also found," the DCP said. 
 
 Hotel authorities claimed he had not met anyone since checking in on Sunday. "We will supply CCTV footage to police," another hotel worker said.
 
 Police said the hotel management had informed the deceased's family. "We received several calls from family members who told us that he was the brother of a minister," inspector in charge of Nayapalli police station Sangram Pattnaik said.
