MUMBAI: Former   fast bowler Chaminda   and ex-India batsman   will coach the boys in under-14, under-16 and U-16 to 21 category for two weeks under the  's new coaching programme in city known as "Centre Of Excellance For Cricket".

 This will be a full-day camp and will cover fielding skills, fitness and training, batting skills, match temperament, art of fast bowling , cricket psychology and mental toughness.
 
 The coaching programme, in association with Brain4Sports, will be held in the month of April. The JSF will hold trials to select the boys on February, 27 at plot No. 8, Oval Maidan, Churchgate.
