St. Joseph’s   recently celebrated its 136th College Day, where the best outgoing students were awarded and retired faculty members were honoured. While the scion of the Mysuru royal family, Yaduveer Krishnadatta Chamaraja Wadiyar, was the chief guest at the event, Reena Raju, Karnataka’s first woman heart recipient, was the guest of honour. 

 The event saw performances from the college’s Western Dance Group, among others. The college also honoured Reena for her   and Yaduveer for his contribution to society. At the event, the college presented its annual report highlighting the achievements for the past academic year. Towards the end, a surprise awaited Yaduveer, who was made to cut a cake, as his birthday was the next day.
