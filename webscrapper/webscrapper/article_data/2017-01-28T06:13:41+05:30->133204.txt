PUNE: City-based urosurgeon   has been selected for the prestigious Dr B C Roy award - considered the highest honour in the medical fraternity - for excellence in the field of medicine. The award will be presented to him at the hands of the President of India on July 1, observed as doctors’ day.

 Kulkarni is being awarded for ‘Best talents in encouraging the development of specialities in different branches of medicine’, states the letter he recieved from the   on January 25.
 
 His contribution to urology has been recognised on national and international forums. He invented surgical procedure known as ‘Kulkarni’s technique for urethral reconstruction’ which is practised all over the world.
 
 The   Award was instituted in 1976 in the memory of the physician and freedom fighter.
