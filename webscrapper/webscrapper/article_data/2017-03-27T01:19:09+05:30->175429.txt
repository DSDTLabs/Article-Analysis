GHAZIABAD: Five persons were booked for assaulting an RWA member and his wife at  , Rajnagar Extension on Sunday. A long-standing dispute pertaining to the RWA elections between the two sides is allegedly the reason behind the attack. 

 Sankalp Jain (37), an engineer and secretary of the society RWA and his wife Neha (35), a homemaker, in their complaint alleged that one Sunil Yadav and four other residents of the same society allegedly barged into their house and physically assaulted them. 
 
 The other accused are identified as Salabh Gupta, T D Sharma, Rohit Gupta and Rajat Gupta, all residents of the same society.
 
 Sunil Yadav, the accused, however, denied any such incident claiming it as a false accusation.
 
 Speaking to TOI, Daal Chand, SHO, Sihani Gate police station said, “The complaint has been registered by Neha Jain and we are looking into the matter. This is not the first time such an incident has happened. We have received similar complaints from the society in the past.”
 
 An FIR under sections 323 (voluntarily causing hurt), 504 (intentional insult with intent to provoke breach of peace) and 352 ( assault or criminal force otherwise than on grave provocation) of the IPC has been registered against the accused.
