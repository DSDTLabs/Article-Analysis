PATNA: A body was found floating in the drain beside Patna-Digha railway line near Punaichak railway crossing under the S K Puri police station area on Wednesday. 

 A plastic bottle of aerated drink filled with country-made liquor was also found in the underwear of the deceased. SHO Arvind Kumar said the deceased was identified as Shiv Raut (36), a mason by profession. He lived along with his family members near pump house in Shastri Nagar locality. 
 
 His family members suspected that he might have consumed liquor and got drowned after falling into the drain, the SHO said. “Raut had not returned home for almost two days. His family members said he was a liquor addict,” Kumar said. T
