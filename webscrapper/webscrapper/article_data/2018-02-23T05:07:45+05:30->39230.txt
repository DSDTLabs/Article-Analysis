MUMBAI: Nirav Modi’s farmhouse was served with a notice in 2009 by the   tehsildar over illegal construction on land under the coastal regulatory zone. 

 The property is located on Kihim beach, 10km from Alibaug. The matter is pending in the Bombay HC. A CBI team had sealed the farmhouse on Tuesday.
 
 Tehsildar   said, “The farmhouse is spread over 7000 sq m.   owns the farmhouse jointly with one Siraj Bholubhoy. Following the notice, our office moved the HC with 16 other farmhouse owners. Their petition is pending in the high court since 2009.”
