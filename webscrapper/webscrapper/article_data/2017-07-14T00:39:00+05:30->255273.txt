Ludhiana: The court of additional sessions judge JS Kang on Thursday sentenced Gurjinder Singh of Jodhan to life imprisonment for the   of Gurmit Singh in Jodhan on 25 June 2008. Jodhan police station had booked Harwinder Singh and Gurjinder Singh for the alleged murder of Gurmit Singh after Harwinder held him responsible for the rejection of his father’s papers to contest the panchayat member elections.

 Police officials said while Harwinder was arrested and already convicted by the court, Gurjinder — who was declared a proclaimed offender — was arrested later. The police later produced a chargesheet against Gurjinder, and he was pronounced guilty by the judge on the basis of evidence against him.
