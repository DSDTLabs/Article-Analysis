Visakhapatnam: District collector Pravin Kumar conducted a review meet with education department officials at his bungalow on Saturday where he urged them to ensure that LPG is used to prepare   in schools.

 He further said steps must be taken to ensure that all Anganwadi and residential schools as well as welfare hostels in the district are provided with cooking gas supply. 
 
 “Education department officials should coordinate with other officials and complete the task by July,” he added. 
 
 Regarding land acquisition for the permanent Kendriya Vidyalaya campus in Anakapalli, the district collector directed the revenue officials to speed up the process.
