Chennai: As rebel AIADMK leader and MLA T T V Dhinakaran has been giving trouble to the government with his presence within the assembly and outside, the ruling AIADMK has re-started its efforts to cleanse the party of TTV supporters after a gap of more than a week. The party on Thursday expelled more than 100 functionaries including party spokesman and former MLA Dheeran.

 The expulsions were carried out by AIADMK steering committee coordinator and deputy chief minister  , and party joint coordinator and chief minister   on Thursday to send out a strong signal to rebels within the party. Most of the expulsions happened in districts like  ,   urban,   rural. Among those expelled include a former Rajya Sabha MP, S Anbalagan and a former MLA from Salem, S C Venkatachalam.
