THIRUVANANTHAPURAM: The 10th session of the 14th   will begin on Monday and continue for 24 days. The first day will be dedicated for the discussions on the supplementary demands for grants in the budget for 2017-18 and voting on the matter. 

 Second day of the session has been reserved for government business which will have discussions on two bills   (amendment) bill and Kerala road safety authority (amendment) bill. On the same day, the assembly will consider finance appropriation bill relating to the supplementary demands for grants for the 2017-18 budget. The session has earmarked 13 days for detailed discussion and voting on demands for grants for the 2018-19 budget, and has also included three days for private member business. 
 
 In the other days earmarked for government businesses, the assembly will consider those bills on the basis of the recommendations by the business advisory committee. At present, there are 19 ordinances that are in force, that are awaiting to become acts. As per the  , the assembly will not meet from March 1 to 4 and from March 29 to April 1. The session will conclude on April 4.
