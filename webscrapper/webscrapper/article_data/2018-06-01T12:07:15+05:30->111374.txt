BICHOLIM: Islamuddin Sheikh, a senior citizen from Muslimwada, Bicholim, drowned in the  , where he had gone to wash clothes, on Wednesday evening. 

 The local police said water stored from the Vathadev Riverwhich is further upstreamwas released while Sheikh was washing clothes in the Bicholim river, causing water levels in the latter to rise. The police suspect that Sheikh, who must have not been able to adjudge the depth of the water, got washed away by the current and drowned.
 
 His body was later retrieved and sent to the Goa Medical College and Hospital, Bambolim, for a postmortem exam, following which it was handed over to his relatives.
