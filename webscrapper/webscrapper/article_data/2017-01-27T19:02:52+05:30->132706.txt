DEHRADUN: The Congress list of 40 star campaigners in an interesting packages as it includes the names of   and also a number of members who were denied a ticket for the 2017 assembly elections. However, unlike Uttar Pradesh, the list is sans the name of  .

 Party chief Sonia Gandhi, Rahul Gandhi, Ghulam Nabi Azad, Mukul Wasnik, Sushil Kumar Shinde, Jyotiraditya Scindia, Jitendra Singh, Manish Tiwari, Sachin Pilot, Randeep Singh Surjewala and Kumari Selja have been included in the list.
 
 Through a letter to the Election Commission, senior Congress leader and party treasurer Motilal Vohra informed the election body about the list of 40 start campaigners for Uttarakhand.
 
 Keeping in mind the sizeable chunk of Punjabi voters in Uttarakhand, especially Udham Singh Nagar and Dehradun district, the party has in the list has included the names of former PM Manmohan Singh, Navjot Singh Sidhu, Ambika Soni and Amarinder Singh.
 
 The list also mentions party members like Rajiv Jain, Mathura Dutt Joshi and Jot Singh Bisht, who were not even considered for the party ticket.
 
 When contacted, Congress state chief Kishore Upadhyay told TOI that the list has been prepared keeping in mind the requests by people and party members of different assembly constituencies. “We have invited a number of senior party members and their rallies, events and roadshows are likely to begin in the next few days,” said Upadhyay. On being asked about leaders denied ticket being made star campaigners, he said, “They are senior members who have the experience and good knowledge about the entire state. They have experience and can play a major role in party winning the elections. The Congress has always been about team spirit. These leaders will help other party members win elections.”
