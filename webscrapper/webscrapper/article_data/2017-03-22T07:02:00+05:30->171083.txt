LUCKNOW: The   ( ) on Tuesday directed the   to implement the ‘Trust Billing’ system wherein a consumer submits the meter reading details at the designated web portal or office of the power distribution company and pay the bill.

 The Commission in the Seventh Amendment of the Electricity Supply Code, 2005 provided that   in all towns where online billing facility is available can opt for submission of their meter reading to the licensee through e-mail and SMS. 
 
 The option of Trust Billing once given can be changed only after six months or more, the commission said. The consumers opting for this facility will get their e-mail address/mobile number registered with the licensee on the designated website or through SMS on the specified number. The urban consumers can submit their meter reading every month, whereas the rural consumers can submit their readings bimonthly.
