THANE: A   suffered 30% burn injuries after his second floor flat in the middle class enclave of Kausa  , a suburb of Thane caught fire early morning.

 The elderly citizen stayed alone and had stocked a pile of paper and plastic scrap in his house. Rescue officials suspect an unattended   left near the scrap pile may have triggered the blaze. The fire department has launched an investigation in the case.
