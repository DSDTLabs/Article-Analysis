 Sekhar Basu, chairman, Atomic Energy Commission and secretary, Department of Atomic Energy, distributed the prestigious Homi Bhabha Prizes comprising a medallion and books to the toppers and released the souvenir featuring the training school programme during the academic year.

He motivated the young graduates to put their best efforts towards achieving the goals of the department.
 
 Since the inception of the BARC Training at IGCAR, 447 trainee scientific officers have graduated and placed in various units of DAE such as BARC.
