BHIWANDI: A man allegedly killed his wife and then cut off her breasts as he suspected she was having an affair. 

 The accused Ejaz (22) married to Shamina (21) (both names changed) are residents of Narpoli in Bhiwandi. The   said the incident took place on Sunday night after Ejaz had a quarrel with Shamina over her alleged affair. After the incident, Ejaz informed the police that his wife had died after falling on floor. 
 
 However, during the post-mortem the police learned that victim had stab wounds on the body and that her breasts had been severed. 
 
 “After we got the post-mortem report, we grilled Ejaz who confessed to the crime,” said Manikrao Kature, sub-inspector of Narpoli police station.
