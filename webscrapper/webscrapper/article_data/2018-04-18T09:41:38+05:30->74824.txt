Sandalwood actress   was in   on Wednesday as the the chief guest of the valedictory function of the national short film festival 'Cinerama', organized by the Department of Visual Communication of   Vishwa Vidyapeetham. The students got more than what they bargained when the actress not only interacted with them at length, but also broke into an impromptu jig on stage with them.
 
  Before taking the stage,   joined the students to watch the short film Thanks to God, which impressed her. She advised the youngsters, especially those aspiring for a career in showbiz, to develop a keen sense of observation in day-to-day events. She later distributed awards to the three best short films. The fest was held to allow students to showcase their talent and skills in short film making and to give them an opportunity to interact with industry experts.
