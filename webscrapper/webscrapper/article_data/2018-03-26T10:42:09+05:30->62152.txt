NEW DELHI:  , New Delhi has invited applications for its B.Sc. (Hons), B.Sc. (Post Basic) and MSc (Nursing) courses at various centres across India. In total, there are 567 seats on offer for nursing courses. The application form for the same can be filled from March 23, 2018 to April 12, 2018 till 5 pm. 

 Interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the   official website: aiimsexams.org 
 
 Direct link to apply online for AIIMS BSc/MSc courses: nursing.aiimsexams.org
