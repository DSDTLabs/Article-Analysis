CHANDIGARH: Police have arrested a 40-year-old man for sexually harassing a woman in Sector 29.

 The accused,  , entered the house of the woman and molested her in an inebriated condition. The accused also hit the woman. She raised the alarm and people gathered at the spot and caught him. He was later handed over to the police. His medical examination confirmed he was drunk.
 
 In her complaint, the woman said the accused had earlier too tried to harass her but she ignored his activities. Z P Khan, station house officer, Industrial Area police station, Phase I, said the victim was a mother of two children. The wife and son of the accused had left him due to his habit of consuming liquor daily and not working, police said.
 
 Police registered a case under Sections 354 (assault or criminal force to woman with intent to outrage her modesty) and 354 B (assault or use of criminal force to woman with intent to disrobe) of the IPC.
 
 A court on Sunday sent the accused in judicial custody.
