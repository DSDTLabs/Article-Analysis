A bash organised by a private institute in Varanasi was a perfect way to say goodbye to the outgoing students. Since   and   was the theme of the bash, the students came dressed in their glamorous best.

  
  (Clockwise from left) Shreya Singh, Paro, Mahak, Simaran, Pardeep Singh, Pratik and Vaibhav (BCCL/ Arvind Kumar)
 
 The juniors gave a scintillating dance performance at the do. And Shreya and Paro's performances on a medley of songs stood out. They began on a remix of 'Tu Cheez Badi Hai Mast Mast' and then went on to dance on 'Nashe Se Chadh Gayi' and 'Udi Udi.'
 
  
  (L-R) Aparna, Taiba and Dimple (BCCL/ Arvind Kumar)
 
 Shashikant danced on 'Banno Tera Swagger', giving glimpses of his classical music background, taking the event to a different level. Showcasing his crooning abilities, Rohit sang 'Ae Dil Hai Mushkil.'
 The anchors of the show, Mehak and Simran further pepped up the event while they quizzed the youngsters on several Bollywood related questions that led to a lot of brainstorming and prompting. 
 Finally, the title of Miss   went to Khushboo while Ashu Singh was chosen as Mr Farewell. Miss Eve title was bagged by Taiba and the Best Dressed title was given to Pragya Singh.
