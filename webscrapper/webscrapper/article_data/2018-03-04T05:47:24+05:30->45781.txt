GURUGRAM: At least 73 patients were brought to the emergency ward of General Hospital, Civil Lines, with Holi-related complaints on Friday. The health department also received nearly 120 calls from those who suffered injuries during the festivities. 

 Hospital staff said most of the patients were brought in with minor injuries suffered while arguments whil playing   or due to accidents. Out of the 73 patients, 28 were accident victims who were either inebriated or were driving too fast.
 
  Another 30 patients were bruised in scuffles while playing Holi. Around 15 from the total were hospitalised. These included patients whose eyes and ears had come in contact with colours, and those suffering from skin infections and food poisoning.
