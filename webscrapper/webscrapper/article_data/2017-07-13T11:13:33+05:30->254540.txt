 Savale and other committee members were unhappy with the transport body for giving step motherly treatment to the residents of Pimpri Chinchwad even though the civic body had 40 % share in it. 
 
 The standing committee approved a resolution on Wednesday to give Rs 5.72 crore to PMPML to meet operational losses and pay Rs 16.52 lakh to PMPML for providing buses for the educational tours of municipal students in 2016-17.
