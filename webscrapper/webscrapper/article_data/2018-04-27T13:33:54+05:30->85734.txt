They question that his can a 75% disabled man who cannot even stand on his own steal chickens.
 
 Sources from the village said that there was a long standing dispute between two castes which have been rallying behind two main parties. The caste that was with the ruling Telugu Desam accused Balaiah of stealing chickens following which a case has been booked against him.
 
 The other caste rallying behind opposition   allege that the case was politically motivated. Even the local sub-inspector   acted in biased manner supporting ruling party leaders and harassed Balaiah. As the torture by police do not end even after 17 days, Balaiah committed suicide they alleged.
 
 Family members staged protest in the village with the dead body raising slogans against police and local Telugu Desam leaders. Cops deployed in large numbers as tensions prevailed in the village.
