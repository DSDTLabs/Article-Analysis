Veteran singer-music composer has given music for an upcoming   film titled Panipat. Known for his classic compositions in movies like 'Gharabaher', 'Hrudaysparshi', 'Laxmichi Paule' etc. Phadke is quite thrilled to have made a comeback with a period drama directed by Abhay Kambli. 
Speaking on the same, he shares, “I have composed three songs in the film. While   Singh has lent his voice for '  Marathi Rakt...Rakhnya Takht',   Sharma has sung 'Lahu Ke Rang Se'. Jasraj Joshi and Avadhoot Gandhi have given playback for 'Pail Teerala'. I had a wonderful experience working with all of them.”
