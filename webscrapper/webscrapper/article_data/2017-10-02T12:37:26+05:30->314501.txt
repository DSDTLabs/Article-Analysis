
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The term OTT refers to applications and services which are accessible over the internet and ride on operator networks offering internet access services, that is, social networks, search engines, video aggregation sites. Skype, Viber, WhatsApp,   and Hike are some examples of OTT services.   MKJ ANU
