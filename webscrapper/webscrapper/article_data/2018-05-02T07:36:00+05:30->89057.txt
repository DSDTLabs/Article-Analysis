Chandigarh: Jhajjar police arrested a youth for allegedly raping a Class XI girl student after kidnapping her from outside the school. The accused was arrested on Monday and produced before the court, which ordered two-day police remand for him.

 The youth kidnapped the girl last week, after she alighted from the school bus at her stop, where he was waiting for her on his motorcycle with his friends. He allegedly took her to a local hotel and sexually abused her. 
 
 The survivor’s family has also alleged involvement of two more people in the criminal act.
 
 DSP in-charge of Jhajjar women police station Bharti Dabas said the main accused has been arrested and the both survivor as well as accused examined medically. “We are verifying the role of the other two accused,” she added.
