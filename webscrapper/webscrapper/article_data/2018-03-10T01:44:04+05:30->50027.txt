NEW DELHI: Deputy chief minister   has sought a report from the women and child development department about the status of   being run in Delhi government hospitals for   survivors. He has also asked the department to explore the possibility of preparing a proposal for setting up these centres that can be submitted to the Centre to seek support under the Nirbhaya Fund.

 “Has Delhi government sent any proposal to the Centre for establishing One Stop Centres in Delhi. If so, details of the same have been sought. If not, then possibility be explored and a detailed plan be submitted to this office within 10 days,” Sisodia said.
