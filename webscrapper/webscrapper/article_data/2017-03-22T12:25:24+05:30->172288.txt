Theater artiste cum movie actor  , who has starred in films like 'Kaliyoonjal,' 'War and Love' and 'Thavalam' has been away from the silver screen for long.

 Recently, the veteran actress, who is 73, was in the news when she made a police complaint that her son is resorting to domestic violence. Meena said that her son wasn't giving her food or medicine on time. Apparently, he wasn't in terms with her as he felt the artiste's property was solely handed over to her daughter alone.
 
 Police said that after a discussion with her children, the issue now stands resolved and the actress will live with her daughter, now on.
