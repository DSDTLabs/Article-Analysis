SURAT: An eight-year-old boy died and four others were injured when sand piles on the earth caved in during construction of a protection wall around a lake in Vadadala village, some 8 km off  , on Tuesday. 

 Seven labourers were digging a 10 feet hole around lake and Rama Pir temple. Suddenly, piles of dug up sand on the side of the earth caved in. Five persons were buried under the mud of whom   (8) died of suffocation.
 
 The injured — Bhuri Bhuria, Geeta Bhuria, Premila Bhuria and Vikram Bhuria — were rushed to Bharuch Civil Hospital. 
 
 The construction work was given by Vadadala gram panchayat as the lake water was entering the temple.
