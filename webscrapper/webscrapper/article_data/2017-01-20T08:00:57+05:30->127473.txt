CHANDIGARH: From   and   to  ,   and Bollywood star Sonu Sood, the Congress has drawn up a list of 40 star campaigners for the Punjab assembly elections.

 From the state unit, Punjab Congress chief Captain Amarinder Singh will be electioneering for the party across the state in the next two weeks, while cricketer-turned-politician Navjot Singh Sidhu has announced his intention to campaign against CM Parkash Singh Badal on his home turf of Lambi, where Amarinder is pitted against the Akali leader.
 
 The list includes several Congress chief ministers and former chief ministers from various states, as well as a host of senior national-level party leaders, including Himachal Pradesh CM Virbhadra Singh, former Rajasthan CM Ashok Gehlot and former Punjab CM Rajinder Kaur Bhattal. AICC general secretary and MP Ambika Soni, former Lok Sabha speaker Meira Kumar and former Union ministers Sushil Kumar Shinde and Pawan Kumar Bansal, are also on the list.
 
 AICC in charge of Punjab affairs Asha Kumari is already camping in Punjab for the past several days, while AICC general secretaries Digvijay Singh and Mukul Wasnik would fly down to add their weight to the campaign soon. Rajasthan Congress president Sachin Pilot, Pradesh unit chief Raj Babbar and All India Mahila Congress president Shobha Oza have also been enrolled for the purpose.
 
 In addition, several senior Punjab PCC leaders, who are contesting the elections themselves, are on the list of campaigners whom the party high command has directed to lend their support to the candidates in other constituencies.
