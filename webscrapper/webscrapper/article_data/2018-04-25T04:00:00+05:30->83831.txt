Surat: A mock drill on ‘Emergency Preparedness’ was conducted on Wednesday at NTPC Ltd’s Kawas Gas Power Project at Hazira in association with the Director Industrial Safety & Health (DISH), Government of Gujarat. It was conducted under the supervision of Surat district collector Dr Dhavalkumar Patel and others. 

 The officials from DISH, GPCB, SMC, Civil Defense Surat, District Emergency Response Centre and other stakeholders witnessed and participated in the mock drill. 
 
 The drill was conducted on the scenario of leakage of naphtha from flame of naphtha tank pipeline, resulting in fire. As the fire could not be controlled internally, incidence was reported to collector’s control room. Offsite disaster plan was activated and with the help of all district authority’s fire was controlled.
