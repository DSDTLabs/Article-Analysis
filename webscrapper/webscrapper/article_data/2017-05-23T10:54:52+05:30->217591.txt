The 10th season of the IPL came to a dramatic finish as   clinched a one-run victory against Rising Pune Supergiant. The final was a fitting end to a fantastic season and fan engagement touched new heights on Twitter this season with the IPL Playoffs crossing the million Tweet mark for the first time in the history of the league.

 Aneesh Madani, Head of Sports Partnerships, India and South East Asia, Twitter said, “It was a fitting tenth season for the IPL, on the field and on Twitter. From the player Twitter emojis which were a global first for cricket to champions Mumbai Indians using the exclusive Twitter Cam app for the #MICam video series, to the million plus Tweets for the IPL Playoffs, the cricket conversation played out on Twitter throughout the season.”
 
 Among the teams, Mumbai Indians managed to win the battles both on and off the field.   (@ImRO45) and team certainly won the fans over, becoming the most mentioned team this season. 
 
 
  
  
 
 The top five teams also had a surprise entrant in Royal Challengers Bangalore (@RCBTweets), who snuck into the list of most mentions despite a woeful IPL season. 
 
  
  
 
 One thing that stood out this IPL was stunning individual performances. The duo made it to the list of the most mentioned Player Emojis, with Dhoni leading the charts.
