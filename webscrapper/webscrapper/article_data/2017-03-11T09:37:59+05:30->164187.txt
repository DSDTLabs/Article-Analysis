THANE: In a major crackdown, the anti-narcotics cell of the city police arrested four persons and seized gutkha worth Rs 80 lakh on Friday , the police said.

 The police had received a tip-off that two trucks carrying the banned products were heading towards Saki Naka. The cops intercepted the trucks at Kharegaon toll post and arrested four persons who were ferrying the goods, assistant inspector   said.
