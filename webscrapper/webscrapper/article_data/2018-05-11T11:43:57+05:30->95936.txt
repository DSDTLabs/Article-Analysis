 Hanamkonda inspector Sampath Rao said Yadagiri, 35, had been running an e-Seva centre for two years. He was depressed after a decrease in services, affecting his income. Locals told the police that Yadagiri had suffered heavy losses in the last two months.
 
 E-Seva centres' association representatives staged protest and demanded that the government to increase services offered through e-Seva centres. They also demanded that all the existing services should be continued. 
 
 Hanamkonda police registered a case. Police recovered a suicide note.
