Master blaster   gave his fans a lot to cheer for when he visited his sports center Smaash at Forum Sujana Mall on Thursday. There was a thunderous roar from the large crowd that had gathered in the city to catch a glimpse of the   superstar. 

  
  
  Speaking to the fans, Sachin said, “Hyderabadi biryani and Hyderabadi hospitality are very special to me. It’s very important for me to meet the people of Hyderabad- because of the love and support they showed towards me.” 
 
  
  
 He also stressed the importance of health and requested every Indian to be fit and healthy. Urging more youngsters to take up sport, Sachin wished for India to transform from a sports-loving nation to a sports playing nation.
 
  
  
  The legendary cricketer also presented trophies to the Pinstrike national corporate bowling tournament 2018 winners. Hyderabad’s Infosys team bagged the first prize in the tournament.
