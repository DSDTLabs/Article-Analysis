UDUPI: As many as 4,580 from 23 degree colleges created a world record by singing the national song 'Vande Mataram' with full orchestra on Malpe beach on Sunday.

 The feat has been registered by the  . 
 
 The event was organised by Udupi Samvedana foundation as part of the 155th birth anniversary celebrations of Swami Vivekananda.
 
 All the students donned white clothing while singing the national song along with noted singers including K Surekha, Ravindra Prabhu, Surekha Hegade, Suhana Saiyyad, Yashavanth, Jagadish Putturu, Vaishnavi Manipal, Rajesh Shyanubhag and Nikhil Tavro.
 
 
 In addition, a 1750-feet-long Tricolour was carried in procession from Gandhi Shatabhi ground to Malpe beach Andra Pradesh's Shara Redians Society.
 
 The students also sported a badge with the slogan ‘Save Nature for Future’ during the event.
