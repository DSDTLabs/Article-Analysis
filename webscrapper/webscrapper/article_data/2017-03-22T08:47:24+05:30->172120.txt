JAIPUR: A 24-year-old woman, who went missing from   where she was living along with her husband in a rented accommodation, committed suicide by jumping into Amber maotha. Her body was retrieved by the police divers on Tuesday. Police have also recovered a suicide note in which the woman has stated that three friends of her husband used to harass her by offering liquor to her husband. 

 According to the police, the husband of the woman identified as   approached   police station stating that his wife went missing since Monday evening. “On Tuesday morning, her body was recovered from the Amber maotha. We have also recovered a handbag out of which we could get a suicide note of the deceased woman. The woman has stated that three friends of her husband sexually harassed her many a times by getting her husband drunk. She said that she can’t live such life,” said a senior police officer. 
 
 Police said that the couple hails from Alwar and earlier they used to reside at Amber area and just 10 days ago they got a rented accommodation at Jawahar Circle area. 
 
 “We are investigating into various aspects of the case. Prima facie, it appears to be a clear case of suicide, still we will also probe under what circumstances she went missing and if there is some foul play involved in this case,” an officer added. 
 
 A large number of tourists and locals gathered after the body was found floating into the Amber Mawtha just under the Amber fort.
