Jaipur:   Justice SS Kothari takes suo motu cognizance of reports of thousands of business establishments functioning in Jaipur city without proper fire safety measures and NOC from the fire safety department. It has sought a detailed report from Additional Chief secretary Local bodies for its failure to enforce fire safety measures.

 Reports says thousands of business establishments like marriage gardens, educational institutions, go-downs, factories, hotels, restaurants are operating without proper fire safety measures and without obtaining fire NOC. According to reports out of 40,000 such business establishments only 625 have fire NOC.
 
 Such large scale violations of fire safety are carried on with official connivance, callousness resulting in serious safety hazard.
