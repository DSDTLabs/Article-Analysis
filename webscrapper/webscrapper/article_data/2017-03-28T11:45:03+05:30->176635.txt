

 Each house raises a Gudi to mark the first day of the first month, Chaitra, as per the Hindu calendar and the Kothare family is no different. Actors Adinath and  , despite having a busy schedule through the day, will be celebrating Gudi Padwa at home. 
 
 
  
 “Our celebrations are similar to those across Maharashtrian households. We will raise a Gudi and perform puja,” informs Urmila. “This is also one day when my diet goes for a toss and I indulge in the specially prepared dishes without guilt,” she adds. Adinath informs, “Sakhar bhaat and mutton cha goda are the specialties of our family for this occasion. I am looking forward to relishing these today.”
