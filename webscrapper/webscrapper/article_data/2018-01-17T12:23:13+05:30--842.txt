 : Digambar Chafadkar, 29, resident of Dattagad,  , was booked by Ponda police on Tuesday evening for abusing and intimidating a lady police sub inspector on Monday. 

 He allegedly abused her while she was issuing fines to erring motorists at Bethora junction and filmed her. “He uploaded the video to social media later in the evening,” police said. 
 
 The youth was booked, but no arrest was made till late Tuesday evening.
