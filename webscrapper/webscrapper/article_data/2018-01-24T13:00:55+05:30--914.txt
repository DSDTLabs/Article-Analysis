 Hence, it was necessary to issue a direction to the Theni police to take action against Raja by registering the complaint.
 
 Opposing it, police told the court that based on the complaint, they made inquiries and sought the assistant director of   minerals department and village administrative officer concerned to inspect the site. The officials in turn did it and filed a report stating that no wrong happened. The police then close the complaint. The copy of it was also given to the petitioner, police said.
