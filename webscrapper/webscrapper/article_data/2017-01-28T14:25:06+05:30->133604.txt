JAIPUR: A Delhi-bound   flight made an emergency landing on Saturday at   airport here after a woman passenger suffered cardiac arrest mid air.

 "The Rajkot-Delhi flight of Air India made a landing at Sanganer airport at around 11:30 am due to a medical emergency. The woman passenger,  , who was traveling with her husband, had suffered a cardiac arrest," Sanganer airport director RS   said.
 
 The woman was rushed to a private hospital, he said.
 
 The airport sources later claimed that the woman died at the hospital.
