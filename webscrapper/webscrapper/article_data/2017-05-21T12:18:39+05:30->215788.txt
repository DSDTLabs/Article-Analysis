PATNA: The Congress on Saturday enumerated the failures of the Narendra Modi-led NDA government, which is going to complete three years on May 26, 

 Addressing a press conference,   chief and state education minister   and state’s animal and fisheries resources minister   besides former Union minister   criticised the Modi government for not fulfilling the promises Modi made. “Every promise he made has turned out to be ‘jumlabazi’,” said Choudhary.
 
 The Congress leaders said PM Modi had promised to give employment to two crore youths every year, but the available data showed only 2.31 lakh people got jobs while around two lakh people lost jobs due to demonetisation.
 
 They said 1,400 farmers committed suicide during the three years. There were 172 terrorist attacks and 1000 soldiers were martyred during the period, they added.
