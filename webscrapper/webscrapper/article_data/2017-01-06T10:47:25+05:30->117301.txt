MUMBAI: A 30-year-old woman, who was disturbed by her relationship with boyfriend, stabbed herself at Mahalaxmi in south Mumbai on early Friday. 

 The victim hurt herself with a razor blade and has been admitted to the Nair Hospital. Tardeo Police said that she has received minor cuts on her left wrist and is stated to be safe. 
 
 During questioning, the girl told cops that she was tired of her boyfriend's promises of marriage and injured in a fit of rage. Tardeo Police has detained her friend for questioning.
 
 According to police, the woman, a divorcee, hails from a middle class family from Tardeo and is employed. Her two-year-old son stays with her father.
