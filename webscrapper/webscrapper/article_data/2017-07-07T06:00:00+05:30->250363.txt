More than half a century since Mughal-e-Azam enchanted cine lovers with grandiose sets, soulful song and dance routines and iconic costumes, members of an elite ladies’ club decided to relive its magic by organising a musical event titled ‘Darbaar-e-Suroor’. The ladies slipped into designer anarkali suits and completed their look with accessories reminiscent of the Mughal era. The strains of divine   music filled the air as the artistes,   and   held the audience in a thrall.
