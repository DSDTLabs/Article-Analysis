 According to the police, the   happened near the T-point of Shankar Chowk Road at around 3am. The deceased, identified as Vinit (22) and Dinesh (24), were returning from a friend’s house in Dundahera when they met with the accident. Apparently, Vinit and Dinesh had accompanied Sunil Kumar, their friend, to his house after work on Sunday night. Around 2.30am, the duo left Sunil’s house for their residence in Delhi. Around 15 minutes later, Sunil received a call informing him about the accident. On reaching, Sunil was informed that Vinit died on the spot while Dinesh was taken to a nearby hospital. He too succumbed to his injuries during treatment. 
 
 Sunil alleged it to be a case of hit-and-run. Following his statement, an FIR under Sections 279 (rash driving), and 304A (death by negligence) of the IPC was registered against the unidentified accused at Udyog Vihar police station. 
 
 “We are yet to find out what actually led to the accident as there was no CCTV cameras installed in the area and no witness has come forward. We are still investigating if the duo were wearing helmet when they met with the accident,” said ASI ahesh Kumar.
