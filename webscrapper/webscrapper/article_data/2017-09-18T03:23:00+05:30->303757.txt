Panaji: Former deputy speaker Vishnu Surya Wagh’s health is improving since his admission to Goa Medical College (GMC) and hospital on last Monday.  Dr S M Bandekar, medical superintendent told TOI that Wagh is doing better without a ventilator now. “Wagh’s condition has improved but he is still in the ICU,” said Bandekar. Wagh was shifted to GMC after he developed aspiration pneumonia and was in a critical condition.
