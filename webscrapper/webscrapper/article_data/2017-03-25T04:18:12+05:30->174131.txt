With this, five accidents were reported from NH-2 in 72 hours. 

 Around 11.50am, a Durgapur-bound Scorpio hit the divider while trying to overtake a truck and overturned. Locals and Shaktigarh PS officers took the injured to hospital. The car was shifted and traffic was restored within an hour. While one of the passengers, Sujata Sapui, received head injuries, others escaped with minor wounds.
