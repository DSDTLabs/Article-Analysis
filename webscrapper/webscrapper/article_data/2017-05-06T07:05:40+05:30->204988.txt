KOCHI: The fishery sector has the potential to bring nutritional security to the poor and malnourished farming community members and can double their income, said   agriculture minister  . The minister, who visited the two premier fisheries institutes, CMFRI and   in Kochi on Friday, called for a fruitful blending of traditional knowledge with modern technologies to make the technology more cost-effective and sustainable. 

 During the interaction that followed, fishermen groups sought declaration of a   famine package to compensate the losses following a sharp decline in availability of fishes for the last three years. They also demanded for a separate ministry at the Centre for fisheries. The minister said that he would seek the opinion of the state government for successfully resolving the issues.
