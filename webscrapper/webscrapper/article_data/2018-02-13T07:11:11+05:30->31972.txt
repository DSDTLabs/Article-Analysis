CHANDIGARH: Punjab chief minister   has directed the tourism and culture department to start preparations for the 550th birth anniversary celebrations of the founder of Sikhism, Guru  , on a war footing with just about 18 months left for the event. 

 Though the   president and the opposition leaders did not attend the first meeting of the organization committee on Monday, the chief minister said all political parties should be taken along.
 
 Prominent leaders who did not attend the first review meeting of the organizing committee on Monday despite special invite are   (SAD) president Sukhbir Singh Badal, Rajya Sabha members Sukhdev Singh Dhindsa and Naresh Gujral, Shiromani Gurdwara Parbandhak Committee (SGPC) president Gobind Singh Longowal, Punjab BJP president   and   (AAP) MP Bhagwant Mann. Tourism and culture minister Navjot Singh Sidhu said Guru Nanak's 550th birth anniversary was an occasion which required all political parties to rise above their narrow political considerations
