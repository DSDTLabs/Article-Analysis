NEW DELHI: The Andhra Pradesh Intermediate First Year results 2018 will be announced on Friday, April 13, 2018, at 12pm, by the Andhra Pradesh Educational State Minister Ganta Srinivasa Rao and Intermediate Board Secretary B Udayalakshmi in a press conference. 

 The students who had appeared for the AP Inter 1st year exam 2018 and are waiting for their result can now check their scores right here. Just enter your name, email ID, mobile number and roll number in the form provided below. 
 
 
 
 
 
  
  
 
 As per the official notification, the AP Inter 1st year results 2018 will be declared on the official website of BIEAP - bieap.gov.in. The AP Intermediate first year results will be declared by the Andhra Pradesh Educational State Minister Ganta Srinivas Garu. 
 
 This year, more than 10 lakh students appeared for the Intermediate examinations with 5.3 lakh students in Inter first year and 5 lakh in Inter second year. The AP Intermediate second year results were announced on Thursday, April 12, 2018, at around 3pm. The overall pass percentage for the AP 2nd year exam is 73.33%
