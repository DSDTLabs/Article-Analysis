NEW DELHI: The National University of Advanced Legal Studies, Kochi, will release the admit cards / hall tickets for the Common Law Admission Test 2018 on the official website - www.clat.ac.in

 Candidates who have applied for the examination can check and download their admit card from the Common Law Admission Test website - www.clat.ac.in. 
 
 Candidates are advised to download their Common Law Admission Test 2018 Admit Card from the official website - www.clat.ac.in - only.
 
 In case you have also applied for the Common Law Admission Test 2018 examination, you can follow the steps given below to check and download your admit card. 
 
 Steps to check Common Law Admission Test 2018 Admit Card from - www.clat.ac.in
 
 1) Open the official website of Common Law Admission Test - www.clat.ac.in
 
 2) On the homepage of the website, there will be a link "Common Law Admission Test 2018 Admit Card/Hall Ticket". Click on the link
 
 3) A pop-up page will appear, enter your Common Law Admission Test 2018 application number, date of birth, security pin and click on log in. 
 
 4) Your Common Law Admission Test 2018 admit card will be displayed on the screen. Carefully check all your details
 
 5) Download your admit card and take a print out for future reference
