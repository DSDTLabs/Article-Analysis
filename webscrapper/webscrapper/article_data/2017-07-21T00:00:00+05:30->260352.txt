Pune: The Khadak police on Thursday booked 13 people for allegedly duping a credit cooperative society of Rs 1.66 crore in last few months.

 The suspects include directors of the credit cooperative society, an auditor and a jewellery shopowner. They have been booked for cheating and forgery. “The suspects urged the credit cooperative society’s members and citizens to mortgage gold with the society and to take loan against the mortgaged ornaments,” police said. However, they distributed these ornaments among themselves even after taking the loan on them.
 
 Similarly, they prepared fake vehicle loans, made forged and fabricated entries in the tally register and misappropriated the funds, police said. TNN
