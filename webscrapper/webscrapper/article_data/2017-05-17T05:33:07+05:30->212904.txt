SURAT: A fire destroyed eight vehicles that were parked at   compound here on Tuesday. There were no casualties in the fire. A team from the forensic science laboratory ( ) reached the spot and is expected to submit its report soon. 

 The fire started around 2pm and destroyed eight vehicles that had been seized from offenders in the last one year. 
 
 A police officer said, “A short circuit in one of the vehicles or a mischief could have caused the fire. The fire spread quickly and turned the eight vehicles into ashes.” 
 
 A Fire Brigade officer said, “The FSL team is expected to come out with its report in a day or two. Then, we will know the exact reason for the sudden fire.” 
 
 It is being suspected that someone who didn’t want his vehicle to be examined thoroughly set off the fire in the police compound.
