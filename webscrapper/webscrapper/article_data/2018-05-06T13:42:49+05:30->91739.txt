MADURAI: A tense situation prevailed at   in   district on Saturday after vehicles were set on fire and houses were damaged following clashes between two communities.

 Trouble started on April 24 when one community objected to take out a funeral procession by another community through one of the streets where the former community members are living together. People from both communities sustained injuries in the clashes on that day.
 
 As tension continued, clashes erupted between the two communities again on Saturday. A two-wheeler and two cars were set on fire. Ten people from both the sides sustained injuries. Police pickets were put up in the village following the clashes and around 10 people from both sides were arrested.
