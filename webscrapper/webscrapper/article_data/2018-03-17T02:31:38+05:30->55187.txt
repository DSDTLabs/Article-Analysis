NEW DELHI: A 33-year-old woman was stabbed multiple times by her husband at their house in southwest Delhi’s Baba Haridas Nagar early on Friday morning. Police said that the accused had doubted the woman’s character due to which he often thrashed her in the presence of their three children.

 Police said that the accused, Nasir Ali (40), had shifted to Delhi with his wife and children eight years ago. He was suspicious about his wife’s activities for the past few years due to which they regularly had arguments.
 
 Police said they received a call from the landlord at 5am, who lives on the floor above the couple. Police said that the landlord informed the cops after he heard cries for help. When he went to check, he saw the man stabbing his wife,  . The woman was taken to a hospital where she was declared brought dead, police said.
 
 DCP (Dwarka) Shibesh Singh said that the man used a blunt object to stab his wife.
