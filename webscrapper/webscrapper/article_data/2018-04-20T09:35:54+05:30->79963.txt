MADURAI: The   bench of the Madras high court on Thursday granted bail to three people who protested against the hydrocarbon project at Kathiramangalam village in Thanjavur. The bail plea of the activists - law student Kurunjithen, Palsamy and advocate Manoharan, came up for hearing before justice  . Their counsel said that the hydrocarbon project will cause irreparable ecological and health hazards in Thanjavur district. Against the project, there has been continuous protests in the district. The petitioners also raised their voices against the project. Raising dissenting voice is a fundamental right of the citizen.
