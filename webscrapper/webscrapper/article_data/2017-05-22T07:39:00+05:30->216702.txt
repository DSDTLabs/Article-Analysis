AMRITSAR: Seven persons, including four women and three men were killed in a road accident near Umranangal Chowk in Beas, 35 km from Amritsar on Sunday. The accident took place when an over speeding SUV crashed into two vehicles. The deceased were identified as Sewa Singh, Kamaljit Kaur, Sarabjit Kaur and Avtaar Singh.

 All the 16 victims from   district were travelling to Amritsar to visit the Golden temple when the SUV rammed from behind. According to reports a car and an auto rickshaw were parked on the roadside while the commuters were having tea at a stall.
 
 An SUV coming from   1 towards Amritsar crashed into the car and the auto rickshaw killing 7 people and severely injuring 6. Says in-charge of Beas police station Amandeep Singh “The SUV driver was driving at high speed and lost control, dashing into vehicles parked on the roadside. The injured have been admitted to government medical facilities for treatment”
 
 The driver of the SUV who is undergoing treatment, is among the injured. He has been booked for causing death by negligence and causing hurt by act endangering life or personal safety of others.
