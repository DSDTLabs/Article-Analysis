NAVI MUMBAI: Acting on several verbal complaints about women prostitutes   men in public places near Panvel ST bus depot and Panvel railway station, 10 women were arrested on Tuesday for making obscene gestures with intention of offering sexual favours. 

 Senior inspector Sunil Bajare of Panvel city police station said, “Four women prostitutes were detained near Panvel ST bus terminus. Six others were detained along the road leading to Panvel railway station and near Patwardhan hospital.” 
 
 They were found soliciting men by offering sexual favours in exchange for money. Individual non-cognizable offences have been registered against the six accused women, who have been booked under sections 110 and 112 of the Maharashtra Police Act. —George Mendonca
