Ludhiana: Ludhiana civil surgeon Parvinder Pal Singh Sidhu flagged off an awareness van for achieving 100% target in the vaccination campaign against measles-rubella (MR) in Ludhiana in the coming weeks.

 Sidhu said the van will cover all towns, blocks, and villages of the district and will sensitize people about the importance of MR vaccination for their children.
 
 He revealed that till May 22, a total of 4.06 lakh children have been vaccinated in Ludhiana and appealed everyone to not believe certain rumours that have doing the rounds about the vaccine. 
 
 He solicited the support of all parents and asked them to take maximum benefit from this campaign for making Ludhiana a measles-rubella-free district.
