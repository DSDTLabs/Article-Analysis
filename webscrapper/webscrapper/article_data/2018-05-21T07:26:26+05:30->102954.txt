LUCKNOW: Four more cases of card cloning were reported from the city on Sunday, taking the count to 70 in just four days. All four cases were reported from   police station area.

 From January 1to May 10, only 60 cases were reported, but from May 17 to May 20, 70 fresh cases of card cloning have been reported. Of these, 90% are reported from   region, said cyber cell officials.
 
 In the first case reported at Vibhuti Khand police station, Sanjiv Gupta, a businessman, was conned of Rs 40,000. Gupta said he received a message of Rs 40,000 deduction for shopping on Saturday night while his card was with him and he had not done any transactions.
 
 In the second case, retired Army personnel   of Virat Khand was conned of Rs 50,000. Joshi told police that at 10.30am on Sunday, he received a message of deduction of Rs 25,000 first then another of Rs 25,000 deduction despite him not having made any transactions.
