LOS ANGLES: A speeding car driven by a man allegedly on drugs was hurled into the upper floor of an office building in California after it hit a road divider, media reports said on Monday.

 The crash, which left one half of the vehicle hanging out of the building, occurred early Sunday morning in  , a city 56km south of Los Angeles.
 
 Both people in the car survived the crash but suffered minor injuries, according to police.
 
 Police told US media outlets that the driver was on drugs and has been admitted to hospital for observation.
 
 One of the two people was able to get out of the car, but the other was stuck inside for more than an hour until rescuers arrived.
 
 The crash also set off a small blaze which was put out by fire officials.
