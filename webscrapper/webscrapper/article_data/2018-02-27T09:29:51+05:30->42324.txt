JORHAT: The carcass of two adult male elephants, who have been electrocuted to death, were found in a   in   district on Monday morning. The incident took place at around 3am on Monday at  , about 25 km from Bokajan town, under Borpathar police station. 

 Members of the forest staff said the elephants had visited the tea garden in search of food and came in contact with a high volt electric wire while they were returning to the forest. "Both the elephants were adult males. Preliminary investigations suggest the trunks of the elephants touched the live wire while passing through the tea garden. They were killed on the spot," said a forest officer in Karbi Anglong.
