 Speaking exclusively with TOI, MPBSE Chairman SR Mohanty said, "Result increased by 16 per cent than last year. Girls have outnumbered boys." 
 
 As per available updates, out of 180 state toppers of Class 10, 98 are girls and 83 are boys. For class 12, out of 103 toppers, 82 are girls while as 41 are boys.
 
 Blind student Shahi Shekhar Prakash tops class 10 in differently-abled category. Around 66.54 per cent students have cleared Class 10th exam and 68 per cent students have qualified class 12 exam. More than 39% students have secured first division. Neemuch district secured the highest pass percentage. Class 12 student Ritika Goyal tops in differently-abled category.
