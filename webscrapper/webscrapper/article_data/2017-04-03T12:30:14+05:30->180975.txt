HUBBALLI: Two people died after a private luxury bus collided with a tiles-laden truck at   in   on Sunday.

 While bus driver Lava, 35, died on the spot, Ravi Kumar Mehta, 39, succumbed to injuries at KIMS, Hubballi. Mehta hailed from Bihar and was employed in a factory in Bengaluru. Over 15 injured passengers were shifted to KIMS.
 
 The bus was moving from Bengaluru to Goa and the truck from Haveri to Hubballi.   Circle police inspector S K Pawar said that the accident could have taken while the bus was trying to overtake the truck. A case was registered at Tadas police station.
