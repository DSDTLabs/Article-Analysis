GURUGRAM: A 27-year-old man was arrested for allegedly molesting a nine-year-old girl in Surat Nagar on Saturday night. 

 The accused, Shakir, who is originally from Rampur in UP, used to live in the same neighbourhood. 
 
 He was produced in a city court on Monday and sent to judicial custody.
 
 According the girl’s mother, she was going to a grocery shop to buy some items around 8.30pm when Shakir, taking advantage of the darkness, grabbed her hand. 
 
 “As he tried to drag her to an isolated place and molest her, she raised an alarm. I heard her cry and rushed to the spot. Meanwhile, some neighbours also came out and caught him. Later, we called police,” the mother said.
 
 After getting information, a police team reached the spot and took the accused into custody.
 
 On the basis of the complaint filed by the girl’s mother, an FIR was registered under Section 8 of the   at   police station. “The accused has confessed to the crime. We have produced him in a city court, which sent him to judicial custody,” said sub-inspector  , additional SHO of Rajendra Park police station.
