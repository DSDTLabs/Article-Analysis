GURUGRAM: A 42-year-old man was nabbed from Palam Vihar area for allegedly attempting to   a five-year-old girl. The accused, Sant Ram, a resident of Chauma village, had lured the child under the pretext of buying her something to eat and took her to a secluded spot where he tried to rape. The child, however, raised the alarm which led to the accused being caught by people present nearby.
