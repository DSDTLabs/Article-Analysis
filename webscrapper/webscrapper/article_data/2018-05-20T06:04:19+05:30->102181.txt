PANAJI: The Goa state pollution control board ( ) has completed the inspection of 88 mining leases to check pre-monsoon preparedness. A report will be prepared based on the inspection and necessary directions issued to mining companies.

 “Mining companies have to take safety precautions based on the directions to prevent any untoward incident due to collection of water in open pits,” said a GSPCB senior official.
 
 The inspection of mines commenced on Monday and ended on Thursday. A senior GSPCB officer said that the board has requested mining companies to take safety measures as the leases do not belong to them.
 
 He said that the directorate of mines safety has already inspected the mines to see that companies have taken all measures to avoid any incident during the monsoon. He added that officials from the   are likely to conduct an inspection soon.
