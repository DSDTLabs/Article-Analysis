Rajkot: Five persons including a three-year-old child were killed on Monday afternoon when a truck that was driving in the wrong side on Anjar-Mundra highway in Kutch district rammed the SUV they were travelling. 

 Police identified the deceased as Sayam Patel (3), Meena Patel (28), Jashoda Patel (55), Kanti Patel (56) and Shanti Patel (45), all residents of Khidoi village of Anjar taluka in Kutch district. 
 
 “The victims belonged to the same family. They were returning from Anjar to their village when the accident occurred. The truck suddenly appeared from the wrong side and collided head on with their SUV,” said Anjar PSI Bharatsinh Parmar.
