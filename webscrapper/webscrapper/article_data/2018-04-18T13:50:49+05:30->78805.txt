VADODARA: An inmate at   sub-jail in   district died on Tuesday morning. The inmate was an accused in an attempt of murder case.

 The inmate   (43), a resident of   village of Santrampur taluka, was rushed to hospital he took ill. He was declared dead at the hospital by the doctors.
 
 The death led to ruckus at the jail with inmates and relatives of the deceased blaming jail authorities for the incident. 
 
 The body of the inmate was sent to Vadodara for an autopsy. Security was beefed up at the village of the deceased and the jail.
