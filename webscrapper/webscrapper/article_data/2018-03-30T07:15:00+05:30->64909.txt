 Ludhiana: A 55-year-old woman allegedly committed suicide by jumping into the Sidhwan canal near Ishwar Nagar bridge on Thursday afternoon. Sources said ill-health drove her to suicide. The deceased has been identified as Ranjeet Kaur of Janata Nagar. 
 
 Her son Harminder Singh told the police his mother was depressed due to a protracted illness. On Thursday, he said, she left the house saying she wanted to visit the doctor. However, she jumped into the canal. 
 
 Passersby raised an alarm and called the police, who took the help of divers to fish her body out. SHO, Daba police station, inspector Gurbinder Singh said the police have initiated inquest proceedings.
