LUCKNOW: Dense fog continued to affect rail traffic on Thursday. About 80 trains arrived late at   station while four trains were cancelled. 

 Trains running late included New Delhi-Jalpaiguri, Kota-Patna Express, Dibrugarh-Amritsar Express, Chandigarh-Dibrugarh, Marudhar Express, Kumbh Express, and Lucknow Mail . Four trains: Janata Express, Avadh-Assam Express, Sadhbhawna Express and   were cancelled. Passengers said they got no information from railways about cancellation of trains.
 
 DRM, Northern Railway, Lucknow division,   said, "Trains were late or cancelled because of the fog."
