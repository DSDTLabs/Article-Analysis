LUDHIANA: All India   Employees Federation (AISBOPEF) on Sunday held its first   and general body meeting in the city. C H Venkatachalam, general secretary,   (AIBEA) inaugurated the convention while Atul Anjan, general secretary, Bharatiya Kisan Sabha was the chief guest. S K Gautam, joint secretary, and Rajesh Bansal, joint treasurer, AIBEA were the guests of honour. Jagdish Sharma, president, AISBOPEF, Amrit Lal, chairman, Punjab Bank Employees Federation, M S Dhaliwal, general secretary,  , Naresh Gaur, general secretary, AISBOPEF and other prominent leaders also addressed the bank employees. More than 1200 employees from all over the country attended this convention and general body meeting.
