NAGPUR: BJP   has been booked under section 501 of Indian penal code (IPC), pertaining to defamation, by Sitabuldi police on Monday. The MLC allegedly made   remarks against Indian soldiers during a civic election rally at Bhose in   taluka of Solapur this week. 

 The offence was registered following a complaint from Ex-Air Warrior’s Welfare Association, Nagpur. The former air force personnel, apart from approaching cops, also staged a protest against the MLC, demanding his immediate arrest. They also said that the remark by the MLC was ‘antinational’. 
 
 Cops, following the written complaint from the association’s joint secretary  , have registered a non-cognizable offence. Other associations of ex-army men and women too have supported the demand for action against Paricharak.
