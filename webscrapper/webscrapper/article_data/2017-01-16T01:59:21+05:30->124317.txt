ISLAMABAD: Four men accused of raping two female health workers at a public health facility in   district of Pakistan's central   were killed in a police encounter, police officials said on Sunday. 

  The suspects had allegedly entered the basic health unit in   town of Gujrat on December 29 and raped two woman health workers. They were arrested last week. Police said the suspects had tried to escape police custody after which they were killed in an encounter. Every year, hundreds of people are killed in police encounters termed by human rights investigators as fake "encounter killings".
