Surat: A 17-year-old girl student of class XII died after she consumed a poisonous substance at her residence in Simada area of the city. She was taken to a hospital where she died during treatment a day after on Tuesday. 

 Shivangi Vaghasiya had consumed pesticide in the evening and her family members did not come to know about it immediately. They rushed her to a hospital when her health began to fail but she did not respond to the treatment, police said, adding that she died on Tuesday. 
 
 Police have registered a complaint of accidental death and started investigation in the matter. Shivangi was reportedly scolded by her mother who had asked her to focus on studies following which she consumed a poisonous substance, sources said.
