Chandigarh: The Registering and Licensing Authority ( ) here will start e-auction of registration numbers 0001 to 9999 of series CH01-BP from Tuesday. The registration for e-auction will start on October 3 and the process will continue till October 9. The e-auction will start on October 10 and will continue till October 12.

 The owner of a vehicle can register on the national transport website — https://parivahan.gov.in/fancy — and obtain the unique acknowledgement number (UAN). The owner of a vehicle who has purchased it by submitting a Chandigarh address only will be allowed to participate in e-auction. The sale letter (form number 21), Aadhaar card and address proof of Chandigarh are mandatory to participate in the e-auction.
 
 After getting registered, the owner of the vehicle will have to deposit the registration fee for participating in e-auction and the reserve amount of the special/choice registration numbers through a DD in favour of “Registering & Licensing Authority, UT” payable in Chandigarh. The details of registration fee for participating in e-auction, the list of reserve price for each special number, procedure of online auction and terms and conditions for e-auction are available on the Chandigarh Administration, Transport Department website — www.chdtransport.gov.in. 
 
 During the auction held on July 15, 0001 number of the series CH-01-BN went for Rs 5.01 lakh against reserve price of Rs 30,000 in the e-auction of registration numbers. The 0005 number emerged as the second most sought after number as it was bought by Raffles Educity (I) Private Limited for Rs 3.77 lakh against the reserve price of Rs 50,000.
