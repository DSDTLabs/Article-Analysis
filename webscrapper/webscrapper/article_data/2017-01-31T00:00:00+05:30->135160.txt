The flexible silicone sheet, with printed nails, can be used to stamp new designs, check colour combinations, fill colour and experiment and create personalized designs, without the mess. Once done, you can just lift the design, cut it to the existing nail size and stick the 
decal to your nail using transparent nail paint. “We have been using the nail mats in Bengaluru. It gives you a lot of choice and is super-customized, but you have to have patience because it can’t be pre-prepared and kept. To make designs for all the 10 nails can take anything from one-and-half hours to two hours,” said beautician and nail expert Beena Sondhi.
