CALANGUTE: Goa police on Friday arrested a youth from West Bengal for sexually assaulting a minor   girl while she was enjoying a dip at Candolim beach. 

 Riyajul Sayed, 24, was apprehended based on complaint lodged by the girl’s aunt. In her complaint, she said that one person had sexually assaulted the minor girl while they had gone to bath in the sea at Candolim beach along with other relatives, around 4 pm, on Thursday. An offence under Section 354 A, Section 12 of the POCSO Act and Section 8 of the Goa Children’s Act has been registered and further investigation is in progress, PI Jivba Dalvi said
