BATHINDA: Angered with the hike in the prices of petroleum products continuously for the last 13 days, farmer organisation   on Sunday burnt effigy of the Union government in  . The organization has decided to burn these effigies till June 2. 

 They have demanded that the prices of petroleum products be rolled back immediately to save farmers from shelling out more during sowing of paddy.
 
 
  The organization has also announced to start sowing of paddy from June 10 instead of June 20 as fixed by the state government.
