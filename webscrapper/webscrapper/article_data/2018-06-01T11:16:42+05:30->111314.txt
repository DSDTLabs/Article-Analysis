CHANDIGARH: The Chandigarh district court awarded 10 years of jail to a Sector 38 resident, who was held guilty in a case under Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act (NDPS) on Thursday. Soon after the judges pronounced the order, a high drama was witnessed in the court. The family members of the convict created a hue and cry inside the court. 

 The courtroom drama intensified when the family members swarmed around him, thus forcing the police to take away the convict soon.
