Coimbatore: A policeman from rural armed reserve police, who conducted vehicle check in an inebriated condition, has been placed under suspension by Pa Moorthy, superintendent of police, Coimbatore district on Friday. Police officials said that departmental action would also be taken against the cop.

 S Karthick, 37, conducted vehicle check at Thiyagaraya New Street at RS Puram in uniform and demanded money from motorists who were not wearing helmets. The cop was in an inebriated condition and two college students informed about this to RS Puram police who detained him. TNN
