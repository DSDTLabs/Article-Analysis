CHENNAI: Around 3240 pirated copies of recently released Tamil films were seized from a 32-year-old resident of Alandur by the   of the city police. 

  Mansoor Ali Khan, was picked up from the Chennai Beach suburban railway station. Cops found DVD copies of recent releases like Bairavaa and Vishal's Kaththi Sandai among others.
 
 After raiding Ali's house on Jinnah street in Alandur, police unearthed an illegal pirated DVDs writing setup. Seven high tech DVD writers and other related material worth nearly 20 lakh was seized from his residence.
