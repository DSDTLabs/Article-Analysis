VISAKHAPATNAM: Around 19 students including 13 third-year and 6 fourth-year students of Damodaram Sanjivayya National Law   were suspended for clashing with juniors on Sunday night. Three students were allegedly injured in the clash. 

 University registrar   said the 19 students were suspended for indiscipline. The decision would be officially announced after a meeting is convened with other faculty members by in-charge vice-chancellor prof   on Wednesday.
