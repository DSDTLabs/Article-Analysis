NEW DELHI: The Tamil Nadu Directorate of Government Examinations (TNDGE Board) is likely to release the annual examination result for the Class 12th students on Wednesday, May 16, 2018, post 9.30 am. 

 The Tamil Nadu Higher Secondary Examination (Class 12) or TN +2 exam was conducted by the Directorate of Government Examinations (DGE) from March 1- April 6 for the academic session 2017-18. 
 
 As per the latest information, the TNDGE Board is likely to announce the Class 12 Results 2018 on its official website - tnresults.nic.in and dge.tn.nic.in - tomorrow. 
 
 If you are one of the students eagerly waiting for the announcement of TNDGE Board Class 12 Result 2018, you can check it on the official website of the TNDGE Board - dge.tn.gov.in. 
 
 
 
 
 OFFICIAL WEBSITES of TNDGE Board: You can log on to the official website of TNDGE Board - www.dge.tn.gov.in to check your results.
 
 IVR: Since there will be huge traffic on TNDGE Board website on result day, you can try IVR system that provides you with the results via a call.
 
 SMS: Students can also check their TNDGE Board Class 12 result 2018 via SMS too.
 
 SCHOOL WEBSITE: Apart from above three ways, you can also check your TNDGE Board result on the local portal of your school.
