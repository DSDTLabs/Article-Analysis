NEW DELHI: Sikkim Public Service Commission has invited applications from the eligible local candidates for 261 Staff Nurse on temporary regular basis in Health Care, Human Service and Family Welfare Department, Government of Sikkim. Interested candidates can apply online up to April 30, 2018. 

 
 All applications are invited through online mode www.spscskm.gov.in
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 (as on March 31, 2018)
 Minimum: 18 years
 Maximum: 40 years 
 
 
 
 
 
 Candidates are directed to bring a Photo Identity proof like (Addhar Card, Voters Card, Driving Licence) along with valid e-Admit cards in the examination centre for proof of identity.
