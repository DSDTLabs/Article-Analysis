KOTA: A 24-year-old man drowned in a pond at  , a famous picnic and religious spot, about 18 km from   district headquarters under Dablana police station area. The body was recovered from the pond on Wednesday morning and handed over to the family members after post-mortem. “The deceased was identified as Deshraj Gujjar (24), a resident of nearby Siyana village,” said Mahendra Singh, ASI, Dablana police station.

 “The deceased man worked at a cycle parking stand at Rameshwar Mahadev Temple. He had gone to take bath in the pond but lost balance and fell into the water after hitting his head against the stones on the bank of the pond,” the ASI said.
