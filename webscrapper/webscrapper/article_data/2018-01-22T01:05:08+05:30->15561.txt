Tiruvannamalai: A 45-year-old man was found dead with his throat slit in a hillock near Devigapuram village in Vettavalam police station limits here on Sunday.

 The deceased was identified as G Sankar, a native of Devigapuram village. He was found dead in a pool of blood at the hillock near the village in the early hours of Sunday when the villagers took their cattle to graze near the hillock.
 
 The police recovered the body and shifted it to the government hospital in the vicinity for postmortem.

 The victim was running a printing press. “We are conducting an inquiry with the family members and relatives of the victim to ascertain the motive behind the murder. Someone known to him might have murdered him,” the police said.
