JAMMU: A man was arrested and seven grams of heroin were seized from his possession in   city, police said. 

 During surprise checking near the   cremation ground, the securitymen intercepted a pedestrian roaming around suspiciously, a police spokesman said. 
 
 During the frisking of the person, seven grams of heroin were found on his possession, he added. 
 
 The contraband was seized and a case under the NDPS Act registered against the accused, the police spokesman said, adding that a probe was launched in this regard.
