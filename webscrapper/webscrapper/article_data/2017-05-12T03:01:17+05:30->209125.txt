NOIDA: The   ( ) is taking several measures to keep tabs on line losses across the state. The measures have been taken on the directions of UPPCL.

 For the purpose, the commission has directed licensee discoms to carry out energy audit to determine distribution loss. Noida, which accounts for minimum line losses at 6%, is set to comply.
 
 “Keeping in view the high distribution losses (both technical and commercial) of the state-owned distribution licensees, the commission through its previous tariff orders has been consistently directing the licensee discoms to carry out the energy audit/loss estimation study with a voltage-wise break up of distribution losses into technical loss and commercial loss,” said  , UPERC secretary.
