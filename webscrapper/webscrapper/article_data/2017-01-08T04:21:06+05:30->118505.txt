NEW DELHI: The   ( ) Class X and Class XII board exams would not be delayed on account of the assembly polls in five states in February and March. 

 However, no exam would be held on March 4, 8 and 11. Board officials said the exam schedule would be issued next week.
 
 CBSE had written to the chief election commissioner to keep in mind the schedule for board exams, usually held in March and April, while scheduling the polls dates. According to CBSE sources, the exams will start from March 1.
