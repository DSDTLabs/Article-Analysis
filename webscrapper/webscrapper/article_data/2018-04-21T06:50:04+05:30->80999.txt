 5 outfits form front, may dent BJP votes
 Stating that the BJP has failed as a political party to protect Hindutva ideology, five rightwing groups on Friday announced an alliance to contest from 90 constituencies in the elections. The front — comprising Akhila Bharata Hindu Mahasabha, Shiv Sena, Sri Ram Sene, Sanatana Hindu Janajagruti Samiti and Sampoorna Bharat Kranti Party — has sought ‘hat’ as a symbol from the Election Commission. If they are not allotted a symbol, then candidates will enter the fray as independents.
