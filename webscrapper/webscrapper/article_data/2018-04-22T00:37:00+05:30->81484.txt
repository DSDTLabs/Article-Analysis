Jaipur: An FIR was filed by three junior resident doctors against their seniors alleging they were ragged and beaten up by them at multiple times. A counter FIR was also filed against complaint.

 Sahib Singh, sub-inspector at   police station said that three students alleged they were roughed up on april 17, 19, and 20. “Some students of different yeas clashed with each other, a second FIR was also filed by senior students. We are investigating the matter,” Singh said.
 
 After the complaint was received, several policemen were sent to the college to enquire about the incident. The police said that only minor injuries and some scratches have been found on the bodies of the complainant.
