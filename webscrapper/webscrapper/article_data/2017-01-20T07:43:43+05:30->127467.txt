AHMEDABAD:   for   Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board ( ) will be held on Friday . Candidates shall be elected to 18 posts of the total of 26 posts on the board.

 Only one candidate has applied for the eight remaining posts, and will therefore be elected uncontested. There are 61 candidates contesting   to the 18 posts.
 
 Voting will be held at 90 polling booths across the state on Friday . Schools across the state affiliated with the state education board will be closed on Friday as their teachers will go to vote. The elected candidates will serve three-year terms. Counting of votes will be done on Sunday . Around170 forms were filled for the posts, with applications have been given for various categories of posts in the GSHSEB. These include teachers, principals, management, administrative officers and secondary teachers, among others.
