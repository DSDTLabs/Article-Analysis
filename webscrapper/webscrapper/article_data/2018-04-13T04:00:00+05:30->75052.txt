Palanpur: Two cousins, one of whom was a college student, died after a speeding milk tanker rammed their bike on the highway near Dhanera on Thursday. 

 The deceased were identified as Sangeeta Parmar (19) and her cousin brother Bhura Parmar (30), both natives of Khimat village of Dhanera. Sangeeta was going to take her exam in the college, police said. 
 
 The tanker driver abandoned the vehicle and fled from the spot. tnn
