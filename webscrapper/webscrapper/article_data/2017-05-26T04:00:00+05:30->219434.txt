Chennai: National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) on Thursday said that its Unified Payments Interface (UPI) is currently offered by more than 50 banks in the country; with the most recent banks to go live being Thane Bharat Sahakari Bank, Apna Sahakari Bank, Janata Sahakari Bank, Mehsana Urban Co. Op. Bank and Vasai Vikas Sahakari Bank. 

 Small finance banks, including Equitas Small Finance Bank will also be shortly joining the platform. “The UPI ecosystem now covers all PSUs and major private banks. We are in the process of extending the service for co-operative bank customersand increase UPI’s growing merchant base,” said Dilip Asbe, COO, NPCI. "Among UPI-merchants are players like Paytm, PhonePe, Myntra, Flipkart, LIC etc. BillDesk, Razorpay, CCAvenue, PayUmoney and Citrus Pay. As on end of April 2017, volume of transactions increased 13% to 7 million from 6.2 million a month ago," he said.The BHIM App, which is, available on Google PlayStore and iOS, will have new regional language interfaces, split bills facility etc. It will also integrate Bharat QR code based payments.
