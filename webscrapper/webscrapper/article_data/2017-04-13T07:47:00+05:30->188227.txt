Gurgaon: Five people attending the Hanuman Jayanti function at the Leisure Valley ground on Tuesday were robbed off their valuables. The function, which was organised by Gurgaon MLA Umesh Aggarwal, had a huge deployment of security still the theives were successful in stealing mobile phones, jewelleries and cash. 

 The victims, three from Alwar in Rajasthan and two from Gurgaon, have lodged an FIR but police are yet to find any clue about the thieves.
 
 According to the police, Anju Gupta, native of Alwar, Rajasthan came to Gurgaon with two family members Sharda Gupta and Satyanarain Gupta to attend the Hanuman Jayanti function on Tuesday. While the family was busy praying, the thieves stole gold chains of Anju and Sharda and also stole the wallet of Satyanarain, which contained Rs 3,000. Only when the function ended, the Gupta family realised that there belongings were missing.
 
 The other two victims, Deepak Aggarwal and Gaurav Aggarwal, resident of sector 5, Gurgaon also lodged a complaint with police after their three mobiles were stolen from the same function. According to Deepak the thieves stole a one plus 3 mobile from him and iphone 6 and 7 mobile from Gaurav.
 
 A joint FIR was lodged against the unidentified thieves under section 379 (theft) at the sector 29 police station.
 
 “Victims were attending the Hanuman Jayanti function at Leisure Valley ground from where their valuables were stolen. We have lodged an FIR and probe is on, ” said ASI Sandeep Kumar, the investigating officer.
