CALANGUTE: Six women were arrested from   beach on Friday for allegedly obstructing   and creating a nuisance in public.

 Calangute PI   said police patrolling the beach found the women trying to pressurise foreign tourists into buying articles from them.
 
  All six are residents of Arradi, Candolim, and belong to the lamani community. 
 
 Two of them are 27 years old, while the others are between 35-37 years of age.
