 Assistant sub-inspector Rasila Solanki and head constable Rekha Chauhan were arrested by the anti-corruption bureau (ACB) police inspector J G Chauhan of Bhavnagar division.
 
 According to ACB officials, the two women cops had sought Rs 2,500 from the 34-year-old complainant to avoid harassment while preparing the chargesheet in the case. After the complainant approached the ACB, they set up a trap.
 
 ACB officials said Rehka was caught accepting money on behalf of Rasila at the mahila police station in Junagadh. Initially Rekha was arrested and later after interrogation Rasila was also arrested under the Prevention of Corruption Act.
