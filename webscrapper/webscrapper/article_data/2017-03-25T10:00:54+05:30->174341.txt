NEW DELHI: A fire broke out in a plastic factory in Narela here last night, leaving a man dead.

 A call was received around midnight about a fire breaking out at the plastic factory, said a senior officer from Delhi Fire Services.
 
 One person was killed in the fire while another was rescued safely, he said. The deceased is yet to be identified.
 
 Thirty fire tenders are working to douse the fire.
 
 Further details are awaited.
