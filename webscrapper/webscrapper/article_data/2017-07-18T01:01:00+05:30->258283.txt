 The new batch is set to arrive this month and all the 11 clubs of the Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology, Shibpur, are gearing up with a   to attract more students to the clubs. Even though this is an unofficial competition, students give their all to win it. Each of the clubs offers their own presentations and literature to impress the freshers. On the day of orientation, all the 11 groups get a specific time to speak to the students. Moreover, the new students are taught about the working of the group and are even trained sometimes. “For instance, if a student signs up for the photography club and he does not know anything about photography, the senior students teach him about it,” said  , a student.
