Meanwhile, Reserve Bank of India, under the Liquidity Adjustment Facility, purchased securities worth Rs 34.90 billion in 6-bids at the overnight repo operation at a fixed rate of 6.00 per cent as on today, while its sold securities worth Rs 511.24 billion in 56-bids at the 2-days reverse repo auction at a fixed rate of 5.75 per cent as on December 30.
