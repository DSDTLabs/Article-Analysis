Visakhapatnam: The department of science and technology (DST), Government of India, convened a review meeting regarding the National Networking Project on ‘Geo-spatial Public Health Data Management System’ at Gitam University on Monday. 

 DST principal scientific officer AK Singh chaired the meeting while DRDO scientist Raja Ramanna, distinguished fellow Prof IV Muralikrishna and other DST scientists attended the meeting. 
 
 Prof Muralikrishna explained the importance of networking projects in salvation of various issues in health care sector for the benefit of the society at large.
 
 Gitam president MVVS Murthi appreciated the DST scientists for their dedicated services to promote science and technology in general and the networking sector in particular. tnn
