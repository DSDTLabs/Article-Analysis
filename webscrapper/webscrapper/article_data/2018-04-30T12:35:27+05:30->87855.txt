‘Avengers: Infinity War’ has garnered huge success on domestic as well as International box office. With theatres packed ever since its release, the movie has reportedly already raked $630 million globally making it the highest worldwide opening weekend grosser of all time.

 ‘Deadpool’ anti-hero   took to his social media to congratulate his Marvel superheroes in a rather special way. Known for his comic sense and whacky attitude, Ryan congratulated them by sharing a letter, wherein he had applied to be a part of the team but was rejected by Tony Stark aka Iron Man.
 
 
 
  
 
 Directed by Joseph and Anthony Russo, the film features Robert Downey Jr., Chris Hemsworth, Mark Ruffalo, Chris Evans, Scarlett Johansson, Benedict Cumberbatch, Don Cheadle, Tom Holland, Chadwick Boseman, Paul Bettany, Elizabeth Olsen, Anthony Mackie, Sebastian Stan, Dave Bautista, Zoe Saldana, Josh Brolin and Chris Pratt in pivotal roles.
 
 ‘Deadpool 2’ will see Ryan Reynolds with his own super team to manage against Cable aka Thanos actor Josh Brolin in the highly anticipated sequel that is set to hit theaters everywhere on May 18.
