Amritsar: In a fresh search operation, two more mobile phones were recovered from Gurdaspur jail, even as nine mobile phones were earlier recovered during a search of the jail carried out on Wednesday.

 Senior superintendent of police of Gurdaspur, HS Bhullar, informed that the newly appointed superintendent of Gurdaspur jail had carried out a search operation in which two mobile phones were recovered. He said the jail administration had sent a complaint to police which would register an FIR. According to sources, the two mobile phones were found hidden in the shoes in barrack number 2.
