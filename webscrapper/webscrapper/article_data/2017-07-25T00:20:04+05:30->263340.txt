Ludhiana: Ludhiana   association is going to conduct an open district badminton championship from July 28 at Shastri Hall, Guru Nanak Stadium, Ludhiana where players across the district will show off their talents in the district matches. 

 A few among them will be selected for the state level badminton championship also. While providing information regarding this general secretary of the district badminton association Anupam Kumeria said, “Around 600 players across the district will participate in the championship in which matches will held from July 28 onwards. Some of the best players will be selected for the upcoming state level matches which will held in the mid of August.” In opening match of the championship, junior category player will present their performance and on the second day senior category matches will be held, he added.
