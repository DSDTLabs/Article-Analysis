CHANDIGARH: Following directions of the local court, on Thursday, cops registered an FIR against the school staff of  . A case under Section 323 (punishment for voluntarily causing hurt), 352 (punishment for assault or criminal force otherwise than on grave provocation), 506 (punishment for criminal intimidation) and 120-B (punishment of criminal conspiracy) of  , sections 75 and 82 of Justice   Act and sections 16 and 17 of Rights of Children to free compulsory education Act 2009 has been registered in Sector 39 police station against officials of Vivek High School.

 On December 23, 2016, mother of one of the student studying in Vivek High School got a call from the school that her son was carrying cigarettes and when she reached school, she found her son in a panic-stricken condition and the student later remained quiet and isolated and on December 27, 2016, the child broke down and revealed that he was beaten up by a teacher on December 23 and was also threatened not to tell anyone about the thrashing and when parents took him to a hospital, medical reports confirmed that he was beaten up and swelling was found on his face and ear. Parents also alleged that he was suspended and terminated by the school and was still under state of shock.
 
 According to the petition, it was not an isolated case of discrimination against the child as the child was allegedly continuously harassed and discriminated by the school. On Tuesday, Judicial magistrate first class Karanvir Singh Maju also directed police to investigate whether such complaints were also made by other guardians or parents of students against the school administration.
