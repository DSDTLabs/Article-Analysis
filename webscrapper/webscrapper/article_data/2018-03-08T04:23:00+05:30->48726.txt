Vadodara: All schools in the district are supposed to submit their affidavits or proposals regarding fee hike before the Fee Regulatory Committee (FRC) by March 14, in-charge district education officer (DEO) M N Patel has said.

 Patel said that schools which are charging fees as per the new exemption limit of Rs 15,000, Rs 25,000 and Rs 30,000 will have to submit affidavits by March 14. Similarly, schools charging above limit will also have to submit proposals by March 14. 
 
 Patel said that no school should create any kind of pressure on students. “The schools are supposed to directly get in touch with parents without involving students in the process,” he said. tnn
