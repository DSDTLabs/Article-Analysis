KAPURTHALA: SSP Sandeep Sharma, Kapurthala on Wednesday held a public meeting in  , on  , which is considered a drug prone village in the district. 

 Police officials said that the village had several drug addicts and they suspected that there could be drug peddlers too. 
 
 SSP Sharma gave an open call to the villagers said that drug addicts could approach them and police would get them admitted in de-addiction centres and would extend support in shunning drugs and adapting a new way of life. 
 
 He said that when a crackdown was set to be launched against peddlers the addicts should get away from the drugs and should rather help the police in catching the peddlers.
