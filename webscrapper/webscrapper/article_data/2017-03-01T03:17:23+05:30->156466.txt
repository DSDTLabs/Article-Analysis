Gandhinagar: Gujarat government spent less than what was allocated in the previous budget for Schedule Castes (SC), Other Backward Classes ( ) and minorities. 

 Atmaram Parmar, social justice & empowerment minister, said in a written reply to a starred question, that a total Rs 158041.64 lakh and Rs 126894.14 lakh in 2015-16 and 2016-17, respectively. 
 
 Of these amounts, a total Rs1,48,085 lakh and Rs1,15,316.42 lakh (till December 2016) were spent on OBCs, minorities and SCs, the minister said. 
 
 The starred question was asked by   whip and senior MLA Balwantsinh Rajput question regarding budget allocation for OBCs, minorities and SCs and the actual amount spent on them during 2015-16 and 2016-17.
