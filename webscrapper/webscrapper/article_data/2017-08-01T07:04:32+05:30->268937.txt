BENGALURU: The tussle between teachers and management of   doesn’t seem to end. Four days after teachers at Baldwin Girls’ High School successfully protested against alleged harassment by the principal and demanded her resignation, 300 staff members of Baldwin Boys’ High School went public with their grievances on Monday. 

 The teachers, supporting staff and other staff members of the school demanded the resignation of their school manager, Beena Unni Peter, alleging she was treating them inappropriately and insulting, harassing and abusing them. Late in the night, the school administration suspended the manager. The protesters, who had said their demonstration would continue overnight, called off the strike.
