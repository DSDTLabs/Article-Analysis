Visakhapatnam: A team of specialists and medical experts have reached Uddanam in Srikakulam to investigate the kidney ailments prevailing in the area. 

 Dr T Ravi Raju, vice-chancellor of NTR Health University, said that the teamcomprising doctors and experts from various places of India arrived in Uddanam on Tuesday and would study the chronic kidney ailments common to the area.
 
 Kidney ailments is affecting 107 villages in Uddanam area. For a permanent solution and to protect the people of this area, the government had decided to set up a committee and send an expert team to Uddanam.
