February 17th marked the anniversary of the incident which shocked the entire film industry in Malayalam - the assault of the actress. 

 The incident had led to major changes in the industry, together with the formation of the   ( ) an association made up of a group of women from the industry. The association has recieved a lot of flak since but is going strong still.
 
 On the anniversary of the incident, they posted: Today February 17th, WCC remembers with grief and horror the incident that rocked the   Industry. We respect our colleague who withstood tremendous mental, social and physical pressure to speak up. The struggle is now ours, every single professional in the film industry .. to make this space equitable & free of fear. Today WCC reiterates that we want justice for our colleague and remind everyone : Justice delayed is justice denied.
