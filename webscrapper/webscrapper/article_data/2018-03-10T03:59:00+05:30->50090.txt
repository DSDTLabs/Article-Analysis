Panaji: Goa police on Friday transferred five police inspectors and 65 police sub inspectors.  Old Goa PI Ashish Shirodkar has been transferred to Police Training School (PTS) Valpoi, while Special Branch PI Sudesh Velip has been transferred to Old Goa. Deepak Pednekar will take charge from Velip. Quepem PI Manoj Mardolkar has been transferred to Goa Human Rights Commission (GHRC), Panaji while Gurudas Kadam moves to the Economic Offences Cell. tnn
