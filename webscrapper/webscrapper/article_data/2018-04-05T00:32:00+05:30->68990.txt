Madurai: Southern Railway will be operating   between Tambaram and Kollam to clear the extra rush of traffic. According to a statement, train no. 06027 Tambaram – Kollam bi-weekly special fare special will leave Tambaram at 5.30pm on Mondays and Wednesdays from April 9 to June 27 and reach Kollam at 10.30am the next day.

 Train no. 06028 Kollam – Tambaram bi-weekly special fare special will leave Kollam at 1pm on Tuesdays and Thursdays from April 10 to June 28 and reach Tambaram at 5.05am the next day. These trains will have 1 AC 3-tier, 6 sleeper class, 2 second class chair car, 3 general second class and 2 luggage-cum-brake van coaches.
 
 They will stop at Chengalpattu, Villupuram, Vriddhachalam, Trichy, Dindigul, Madurai, Virudhunagar, Tiruttangal, Sivakasi, Srivilliputtur, Rajapalayam, Sankarankovil, Kadayanallur, Tenkasi, Sengottai, Bhagavathipuram, Tenmalai, Edaman, Punalur, Auvaneeswaram, Kottarakara and Kundara.
 
 Advance reservation for the above trains are open, the statement said.
