VELLORE: The Vellore fast track mahila court on Friday awarded ten years’   to a 27-year-old man for  , raping and impregnating a   girl a few years ago.

 Sathish alias Dhayalan, then 24, abducted a girl, then 11, from his village on September 13, 2014. He held the girl captive for several days and raped her repeatedly. Following a complaint from the girl’s mother, the All women police, Vellore, arrested him. Judge P Mathusuthanan awarded him 10 years’ RI, besides imposing a   of Rs 3,000 under Section 6 of Pocso Act. TNN
