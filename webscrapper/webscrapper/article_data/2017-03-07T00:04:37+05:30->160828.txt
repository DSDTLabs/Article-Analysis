NAVI MUMBAI: The  , in association with D Y  , has organized a first of its kind mini-marathon for women participants to celebrate  . It aims to create awareness about fitness among the women and help them enhance their confidence levels on all fronts.

 The event will be flag off from D Y Patil International Sports Complex in Nerul at 6.30am. The competitors will cover around 4km from starting point to east side of Nerul railway station and back. The organizers appealed to women and girls from the city to be part of the event. The top three runners will get trophies and prizes.
