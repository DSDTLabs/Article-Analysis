PATNA: State energy minister   on Thursday said altogether 16 state government officials and employees have been dismissed from service since 2011 for their involvement in corruption cases. 

 Replying to a call attention notice of JD (U) member Neeraj Kumar in the legislative council, the minister said the state government has initiated departmental proceedings against more than 70 officers and employees for their involvement in corruption and other unethical practices.
 
 The minister said altogether 12 government officials of different cadres have arrested and sent to jail by different agencies of vigilance department since 2011 on charges of corruption. Through his call attention notice, Neeraj had sought a statement from the government on the work being done by the vigilance investigation bureau, special vigilance unit and economic offices unit of the state.
