PUNE: About 227kg of plastic waste was collected by students of Hume Mchenry Memorial High School, Salisbury Park, in a drive launched by non-government organisation Vanraai on Friday .

 The   has a launched a “Plastic free, E-waste free“ project in Pune that saw the participation of several institutes.Students of the school collected dry plastic waste, and Ewaste from their school and their homes.
 
 Ravindra Dhariya, president, Vanaraai, said, “We are also planning to go in societies in the days to come .“ The collected plastic will be given to garbage management institute.
