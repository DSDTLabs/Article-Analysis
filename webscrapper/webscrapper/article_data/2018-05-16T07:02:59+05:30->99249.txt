AHMEDABAD: Ramesh Kediya, a resident of Setu Apartments in  , filed a complaint with Sarkhej police for a burglary. 

 Kediya stated that someone entered   on Ambli-ISKCON Road between 7pm on May 12 and 5.15pm on May 13 after breaking the lock on the main door and stole gold and diamond ornaments worth Rs 1.6 lakh and Rs 1.5 lakh cash from a safe. 
 
 He stated that the thieves got hold of the key of a motorcycle worth Rs 50,000, which was parked outside the bungalow, and drove it away. Investigators said the investigation has begun and   footage from nearby locations is being scanned for the motorcycle.
