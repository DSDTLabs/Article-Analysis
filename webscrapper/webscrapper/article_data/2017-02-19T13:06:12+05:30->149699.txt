Popular cinematographer  , who recently completed Rana Daggubati’s war flick ‘Ghazi’ revealed he might have rejected at least 50 Telugu movies so far. He added, after completing ‘Srimanthudu’, he was offered offers but couldn’t commit to them because of his busy schedule for ‘Ghazi.’

 Madhie added films are his passion and he has dedicated his life for film making. Madhie is considered as one of the best technicians and worked for movies like 
 ‘Run Raja Run’ earlier.
