Life is not easy, and fulfilling your dreams is definitely not. And this is what Manish Joshi’s Hindi play   shows. It’s a story about a daily laborer Bhagwaan who comes to the city wanting to fulfill all his dreams before he comes face to face with the harsh realities of life. Working as a shoe polisher, Bhagwaan realizes what an important status symbol it is for men from different walks of life. 
Being staged as part of the ongoing  , Patloon has been directed by award winning writer and director Manish Joshi who was awarded Ustad Bismillah Khan Yuva Puruskar of   in 2016 for his contribution to the field of theatre. 
 So, catch this hard hitting play at Tagore Hall at 9 pm today.
