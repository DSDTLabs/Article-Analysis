PATIALA: Patran police here have booked a 22-year-old resident of   for outraging modesty of a 13-year-old girl in a private bus. The accused has been sent to a 14-day judicial custody by a local court. 

 According to the police, the accused would often visit his relatives, who used to live in a village in  . The minor girl used to live in their neighbourhood. "The accused is a resident of a village in Kaithal district of Haryana and used to frequently visitor his relative's house here," the cops said. 
 
 "The accused in the past had also stalked the girl many times but the girl did not report the matter to her parents. On May 16, when the girl had gone outside, the accused followed her and took the same bus in which the girl was travelling," they added.
 
 It was then that the accused harassed the girl sexually. The girl objected and revealed the same to her relative who was travelling along with her. 
 
 The matter was taken up with the village panchayat on the same day where the accused refused to apologize and instead threatened the girl's family of dire consequences, said the police. 
 
 "The accused threatened the family of the girl and announced that he would marry the girl and will not refrain from stalking her. The village panchayat and other villagers tried to guide the accused but could not achieve anything substantial. Following the threats, the family of the girl told the police about the incident, following which a case was immediately registered against the accused under various Sections of IPC and Section 12 of POCSO Act," the cops said. 
 
 The accused was nabbed on Thursday and presented before a court, which sent him to a 14-day judicial custody.
