MAPUSA: Anjuna police on Thursday arrested two persons allegedly involved in the theft of empty   cylinders that were to be exchanged for refilled ones by consumers. Police have also recovered 11 cylinders from the accused.

 Police said that thefts were reported in Assagao and the vicinity during the past few days. 
 
 On Thursday, an alert villager nabbed the duo who were allegedly attempting to steal a cylinder. Police said that during interrogation, the accused -  , 32, and Ulka Kadam, 30 - both residents of  , confessed to the crime. 
 
 Police stated that every Thursday, the duo would flee with empty cylinders which were stationed by consumers outside their houses in exchange for refilled ones.
