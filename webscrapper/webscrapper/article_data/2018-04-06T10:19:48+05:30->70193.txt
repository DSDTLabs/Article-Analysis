NAGPUR: Gaikwad-Patil International School-SJAN Media Badminton tournament, organised by the Sports Journalists’ Association of Nagpur in association with the   will be held here at the NMC Indoor Hall at Vivekanand Nagar from April 25 to 28.

 
 Men’s singles, women’s singles, men’s doubles and mixed doubles events will be played during the annual four-day tournament. Attractive prizes will be given to the winners, runners-up and semi-finalists. The entry fee for singles is Rs 30 and Rs 50 for doubles. Matches will be played with Yonex feather shuttles. Yonex will sponsor the T-shirts for all the winners and runners-up.
 
 Last year Lokmat’s Ashish Jain triumphed in the men’s singles while Suhas Nayse of The Times of India (TOI) finished runner-up. In doubles, Jain-Raviraj Ambadwar emerged winners, while Suhas Nayse-Fakruddin Bora achieved the second place.
