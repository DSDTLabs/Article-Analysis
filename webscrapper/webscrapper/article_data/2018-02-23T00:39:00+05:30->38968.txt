Coimbatore: A major fire broke out in a textile unit godown situated on Kalapatti Road here on Thursday, reducing waste cotton worth Rs 1 crore to ashes. However, no casualty was reported in the incident. According to a source, the fire started at the godown of Chandra Textiles around 3.30pm. The flames spread quickly as it was used to store waste cotton.

 When alerted, fire and rescue services personnel rushed to the spot with three fire tenders. They were yet to bring the flames under control. Peelamedu fire station officer Senthil Kumar said they were yet to ascertain the reason for the blaze. TNN
