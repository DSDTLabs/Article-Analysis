 He said, “We have recovered blood-stained stone used in the crime. We have not found any identification papers from the possession of the victim, who appears to be around 50-year-old. We tried to ascertain his identity from nearby villages, but got no clue. A case of murder has been registered against unknown persons.”
 
 The SHO said prima facie it seems the work of two or more persons as police have found liquor bottles from the crime spot and traces of scuffle. The forensic team and a dog squad were pressed into action and samples of blood and other suspicious items picked up from the spot. Police are also scrutinising the footages of CCTV cameras installed in the area and nearby.
 
 The body has been kept at Derabassi civil hospital, the SHO added.
