DEHRADUN: Students of the DAV PG College along with volunteers of local NGO   held a clean-up drive at   on Saturday.
 
  The drive started at 9:30am and continued until around 12pm. In addition to garbage being collected, large amounts of construction waste were removed, posters were washed off and the gutters cleaned out. A total of 785 kg of waste was collected from the area.
  
   , education manager, Waste Warriors, said, “This area is a well-known part of the city and over the years we have put in a lot of effort to keep it clean. It’s wonderful to see how much better a place can look with a bit of effort. We have a number of events planned for the next few months and appeal to the people of Dehradun to come forward and join us. The more volunteers we have the more we can accomplish.”
