PATNA: Railways will run a weekly   to cope with rush of passengers during festive period. The train will run between October 8 and November 26 on the nominated days.
 
  East Central Railway (ECR) CPRO Rajesh Kumar said the train will leave Indore every Sunday at 10am and reach   the next day at 12.35pm. On its return journey, the train will leave Patna Junction every Monday at 5.25pm and reach Indore the next day at 10pm. The train will run with the load combination of 17 coaches, including 2AC (one), 3AC (two), Sleeper (six), general (six) and brake van (two), Kumar said, adding the special train will have stoppages at Dewas, Ujjain, Bina, Jhansi, Kanpur Central, Lucknow, Varanasi, Mughalsarai, Buxar, Ara and Danapur.
