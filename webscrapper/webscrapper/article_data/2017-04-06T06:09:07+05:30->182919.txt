DIBRUGARH: The ongoing   at  , which entered its second day on Wednesday, has thrown academic activities into a complete disarray. 

 On Tuesday, the Dibrugarh University Teachers' Association had joined hands with the Dibrugarh University Officers' Association and Dibrugarh University Employees' Union under the banner of Dibrugarh University Officers, Teachers and Employees Coordination Committee for a four-day strike in protest against the non-implementation of pension scheme for university employees. 
 
 General secretary of the Dibrugarh University Officers, Teachers and Employees Coordination Committee David Kardong on Wednesday said the the strike will continue till Friday.
 
 "It's a complete strike. All teachers, officers and employees of the university are participating. Our demand is early solution to the pension issue," Kardong said.
