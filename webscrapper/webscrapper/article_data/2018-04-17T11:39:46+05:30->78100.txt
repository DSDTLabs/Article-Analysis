Mohali: Unidentified miscreants broke into   (PNB) next to   police station and tried to break its locker. Failing in their attempt, they decamped with 6 LCD TVs from the bank. Miscreants also took away the digital video recorder (DVR) of the CCTV cameras installed at the bank. 

 The incident came to light when the employees opened the bank on Monday morning, following which they informed the cops. A case has been registered in the Derabassi police station,  .
 
 According to police, the burglars drilled a 1.5ft hole into the rear wall of bank’s building, which is located just next to the police station building. 
 
 Initially, they tried to break into the locker room of the bank, failing which, they stole 6 LCD TVs and the DVR of the CCTV cameras, said police sources. 
 
 Derabassi station house officer (SHO)   said employees of PNB informed them on Monday Morning. After receiving the information, they immediately rushed to the spot.
 
 “A case has been registered against unknown person under sections 457( lurking house-trespass) and 380 (theft in dwelling house) of the Indian Penal Code (IPC) at Derabassi police station,” he added.
