To honour armed forces   on January 14, an informal gathering of army veterans, wives of martyred soldiers and their dependants was organised recently.

  The event was organised under the guidance of   Aurangabad. It was held at Ex-Servicemen Contributory Health Scheme (ECHS), Polyclinic.
 
  This was the first time Veterans Day was celebrated in the city. Ex-servicemen from the army, navy and air force were present.
 
  Station staff officer   congratulated the veterans,   (widows) and their dependents who had come from different parts of Marathwada.
