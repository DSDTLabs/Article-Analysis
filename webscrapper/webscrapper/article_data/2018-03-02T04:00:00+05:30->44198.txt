Chennai: Online marriage portal, matrimony.com on Thursday said it has acquired secondshaadi.com for an undisclosed sum. In a filing to stock exchanges, Matrimony.com’s CEO Murugavel Janakiraman said, “Divorcematrimony.com, part of matrimony.com, is the largest player in that segment and has already been helping lakhs of divorcees find a life partner. Secondshaadi.com is one of the early movers in this space and has built a good brand name. Buying secondshaadi.com will help us consolidate our offerings and help divorcees and widowers get wider choices than before.” The shares of Matrimony.com was marginally up at the BSE on Thursday and closed at Rs 832. tnn
