GREATER NOIDA: A gang supplying   was busted on Friday. Four gang members were arrested in Kasna after a brief exchange of fire with a cache of illegal weapons. 

 The accused have been identified as Satish Yadav, from Ghaziabad, and Rajkumar, Shriram and Basant Singh, all residents of Delhi. The ringleader,   (35), was earlier involved in a kidnapping case in Greater Noida.
 
 Sujata Singh, SP (rural), Gautam Budh Nagar, said the police received a tip-off that the accused would be in Kasna on Friday. “We deployed a team of police personnel at the Honda Crossing. The accused came in a car. When they were signalled to stop for patrol checks, they opened fire at the police and tried to speed away,” she said. The cops then overpowered the accused and arrested them.
 
 The police recovered seven country-made revolvers, one licensed revolver (stolen), and 10 live cartridges from their possession. 
 
 The accused revealed that they used to procure weapons from western UP and sell them in Noida and Greater Noida.
 
 “The accused have several criminal cases registered against them in different police stations. We are investigating the crime records of the three other accomplices. They will be produced in court and sent to judicial custody,” the police said.
