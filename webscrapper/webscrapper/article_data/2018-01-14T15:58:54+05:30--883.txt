Superstar Mahesh Babu’s upcoming film ‘Bharath Ane Nenu’ is one of the most awaited film’s in Tollywood. As per latest updates the makers are planning to unveil the first look poster and title logo of the film on January 15, as a   festive treat. Expectations on this film are high as Mahesh and   Siva are reuniting after the success of blockbuster ‘Srimanthudu.’

  According to sources the film’s shoot is nearing completion. Mahesh will be seen essaying the role of a Chief Minister in this high budget entertainer. Bollywood actress   is the female lead in the movie. It also has Prakash Raj, Rao Ramesh, Sarath Kumar, Posani Krishna Murali, Devaraj in supporting role. Devi Sri Prasad is composing music for the film. The film is expected to hit screens on April 27.
