VISAKHAPATNAM: To clear rush of passengers,   has decided to extend the services of two existing  .

 Train 08573   Weekly Special Express will leave Visakhapatnam on Mondays at 10.55 pm from February 6 to 27 and reach Tirupati the next day at 1.25 pm.
 
 In the return direction, train 08574 Tirupati-Visakhapatnam Weekly Special Express will leave Tirupati on Tuesdays at 3.30 pm from February 7 to 28 and reach Visakhapatnam the next day morning at 6.50 am.
 
 This pair of trains will have stoppages at Duvvada, Anakapalle, Samalkot, Rajahmundry, Eluru, Vijayawada, New Guntur, Tenali, Ongole, Nellore, Gudur, Sri Kalahasti and Renigunta between Visakhapatnam and Tirupati.
 
 Similarly, train 08501 Visakhapatnam to Secunderabad Weekly Special Express will leave Visakhapatnam on Tuesdays at 11 pm from February 7 to 28 and reach Secunderabad the next day at 12 pm.
 
 In the return direction, train 08502 Secunderabad to Visakhapatnam Weekly Special Express will leave Secunderabad on Wednesdays at 4.30 pm from February 8 to March 1 and reach Visakhapatnam the next day morning at 4.50 am.
 
 These trains will have stoppages at Duvvada, Anakapalle, Tuni, Samalkot, Rajahmundry, Tadepalligudem, Eluru, Vijayawada, Khammam, Warangal and Kazipet between Visakhapatnam and Secunderabad in both the directions, according to G Suneel Kumar, divisional commercial manager (co-ordination) for Waltair division of East Coast Railway.
