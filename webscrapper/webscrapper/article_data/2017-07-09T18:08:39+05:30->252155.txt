Veteran Hindi and Bengali actress Sumita Sanyal has passed away at the age of 71. Sanyal was married to film editor  . She made her debut in Bengali film - ‘Khokhababur Pratyabartan’ in the year 1960. She acted in over 40 Bengali films including ‘Sagina Mahato’ opposite   and in the lead role in ‘Kuheli’ alongside Biswajit and Sandhya Roy.

 Sanyal also acted in a number of Hindi films, the most notable of which was ‘Anand’ in 1970 opposite  . She is also remembered for her performances in Hindi films like ‘Guddi’, ‘Aashirwad’ and ‘Mere Apne’. She was born on October 9, 1945. More details about her demise are awaited. 
 
 Meanwhile, Chief Minister of West Bengal   tweeted her condolences. She posted, “Saddened at the passing of veteran actress Sumita Sanyal. My condolences to her family, friends and fans”
 
  
  
 
 Bengali superstar Prosenjit Chatterjee also tweeted that he was saddened to hear about Sanyal’s death. He wrote, “Saddened to hear about the demise of the veteran actress Sumita Sanyal May her soul rest in peace”
