JAIPUR: Fed up with the domestic violence and inappropriate touching under inebriated condition, a 26-year-old woman has lodged a case against her husband at   police station on Monday. The woman (identity not disclosed) alleged that her husband is an alcoholic and every evening he brings his friend at the house.

 
 “After coming home he again starts drinking and touches her inappropriately in front of his friend. She has also alleged that whenever she resisted she was beaten black and blue,” said a senior police officer, adding investigating was on in the case.
