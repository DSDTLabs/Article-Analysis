PANAJI: Minister for housing and   MLA Jayesh   has filed a police complaint against a member of his constituency, Jayesh  , for circulation of defamatory material via social media and personal messages to the people of the constituency.

 Salgaonkar filed the complaint before the North Goa superintendent of police (SP) and the SP Crime Branch. “Samant deliberately, with malafide intentions and in connivance with some unknown persons has made defamatory allegations, falsely alleging that I am trying to protect a rape accused,” Salgaonkar said. The minister also said that Samant has also cast aspersions and falsely accused his brother-in-law  .“The fabricated allegations are aimed at tarnishing my image and have caused extensive damage to my reputation as the public representative of Saligao constituency,” he said
