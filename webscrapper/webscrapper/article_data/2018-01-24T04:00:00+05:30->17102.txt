Ahmedabad: Angry protests erupted in Ahmedabad after mobs resorted to stone pelting at PVR, Cinemax at Devarc Mall on Sarkhej-Gandhinagar Road demanding the multiplexes refrain from releasing `Padmaavat'. Earlier, the Karni Sena members held a protest at Income Tax circle in city. "PVR cinema is not screening the movie in Ahmedabad city," a PVR spokesperson said. Sources in Cinepolis also confirmed they won't screen the movie in Ahmedabad. At least eight cinema halls have declared they will not release the film apprehending trouble.
