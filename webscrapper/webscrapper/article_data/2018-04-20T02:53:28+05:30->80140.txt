GURUGRAM: A man in his early thirties was arrested on Wednesday for allegedly stalking and molesting a 25-year-old   at her paying guest accommodation in Sector 22-A. He also harassed her over the phone, police said. 

 According to police, the woman, who is from Punjab, who works in a private firm located near Sector 22, said in her complaint that the accused, Sunny Grover, had been stalking her for a few weeks from her office to her   accommodation. “On Wednesday evening, when I came back from work, he was at my PG. When the owner tried to force him out, he got in to an altercation and forcibly entered my room,” the woman told cops.
 
 The accused was thrown out after much resistance, with the help of others in the PG and some employees there. He then started calling and verbally abusing her over the phone. He also told her that he will wait for her outside her office the next day as well. An FIR was registered against the accused under sections 452 (house trespassing), 506 (criminal intimidation), 509 (insult the modesty of a woman), and 354-D (stalking) of the IPC at Palam Vihar police station.
