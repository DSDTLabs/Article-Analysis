PANAJI: ‘K Sera Sera-Ghodpachem Ghoddtelem’, directed by   and produced by the Goan duo   and Rajesh Pednekar, under the banner of de Goan Studio, will be commercially released at Ravindra Bhavan, Margao, on Friday. Showings will subsequently be held at Inox.
