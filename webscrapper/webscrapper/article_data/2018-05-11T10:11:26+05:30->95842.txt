KOCHI: Engine of the   passenger train derailed at Ernakulam   station on Thursday morning. The derailment occurred during the shunting of the engine coach at 7.25am. No casualties were reported. 

 
 The mechanical team of the South railway station checked the rail and the coach following the incident, and reportedly found no particular damage to either. 
 
 Traffic on the route was disrupted for a short time.
 
 Meanwhile, a Guruvayur-Ernakulam passenger train, which was held at Ernakulam North station following the delay, caused panic among the passengers when the train started moving before the announced time.
