PATNA: Central University of South Bihar (CUSB) on Tuesday invited applications for online   to different courses for the 2017-18 academic session. The last date for online submission of application forms is April 19.   controller of examinations Rashmi Tripathi said the last date to apply for Central Universities Common Entrance Test (CUCET) 2017 has been extended by five days to April 19. “The decision to extend the date was taken at a meeting of representatives of participating central universities from Bihar, Jharkhand, Haryana, Jammu, Punjab, Rajasthan, Kerala, Karnataka, Jammu & Kashmir and  ,” said Tripathi.
 
  Kumar Kaushal, assistant registrar (academic section), CUSB, said the university has invited applications for 23 courses, including three undergraduate and 20 postgraduate courses. “The university has so far received 5,544 applications,” he added.
  
  PRO Mudassir Alam said admit card for entrance exam can be downloaded from May 5 onwards from the website for CUCET. “The online test – CUCET 2017 – will be conducted on May 17 and 18 at several centres in Bihar, Jharkhand, Uttar Pradesh and other states. The results will be declared on June 9,” Alam added.
