PUNE: The crime branch on Tuesday arrested two Nigerian nationals for allegedly possessing 23g of cocaine — worth Rs1.84 lakh — on  , near Hotel Aurora Towers, in Camp.

 Uchechikua Chizhoke (20) and John Akene Medke (34), from Kondhwa and Mumbai respectively, were waiting for a client when a police team, led by   under   Suresh Bhosale, arrested them and recovered the drugs, passports and two cellphones. 
 
 The Nigerians had come to India on a business visa. Medke’s visa had expired around 13 months ago.
 
 A case under the relevant sections of the Narcotics Drugs Substance Act was registered against the suspects with the Bund Garden police station.
