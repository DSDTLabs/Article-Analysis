Navi Mumbai: The Panvel City Municipal Corporation ( ) has engaged consulting firm   to design,   and prepare tender for a proposed amusement park. The planned park, to be built on the lines of Wonders Park, will come up on a 20-acre area plot in Panvel.

 Sanjay Katekar, engineer, PCMC said that KPMG was appointed a few days ago. “Their scope of work includes design, plan and estimate the tender for the proposed park.”
 
 He added that the said plots were earlier earmarked for slum rehabilitation but massive opposition stalled the work. “The two plots of around 20 acre located along the Gadhi river bank will be developed,” he added. The area near Bunder Road has been land filled, however, many had started dumping construction debris there.
