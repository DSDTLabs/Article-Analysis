Murmu chaired a review meeting of the universities in Raj Bhawan and directed the vice-chancellors to increase security in the girls' and boys' hostels of colleges and universities. In the meeting, which was attended by officers of the state's higher and technical education department, Murmu asked the VCs to ensure that colleges are put through routine inspections by the national assessment and accreditation council ( ). She also directed that libraries should be Wi-Fi enabled in places which face power crisis. She urged vice-chancellors and university officials to ensure that their campus become cashless. 

 Murmu expressed her discontent over unregulated admission fees in B Ed and other courses and directed that a pay fixation committee be formed.
