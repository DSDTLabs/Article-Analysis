 A bench of Justices B D Ahmed and Ashutosh Kumar demanded a comprehensive report from both Centre and AAP government.
 
 It asked additional solicitor general Sanjay Jain and Delhi government’s standing counsel Rahul Mehra to compile a table after scrutinising all its directions passed till now.   said both government lawyers should inform it how many orders have been implemented and which ones are yet to be carried out so that it can get a view on extent of implementation.
 
 Amicus Curiae in the case advocate Meera Bhatia also highlighted to the court that despite repeated orders the governments have failed to install CCTVs and hasn’t made full use of the Nirbhaya Fund.
