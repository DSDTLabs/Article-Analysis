BHOPAL: A journalism student, who was caught by crime branch sleuths on Wednesday for allegedly blackmailing   Hemant  , was produced in the court of judicial magistrate first class (JMFC)   and sent to jail on judicial custody.

 The accused was caught while accepting Rs 5 lakh from the   MLA who she was allegedly blackmailing, claiming she had his ‘objectionable’ video which she would make viral, if he failed to concede her demands. She was demanding Rs 2 crore from Katare, who lodged a complaint with the police. 
 
 Crime branch of   police laid a trap and caught her while she was taking Rs five lakh from the Congress legislator on Wednesday. Her male accomplice is still absconding.
