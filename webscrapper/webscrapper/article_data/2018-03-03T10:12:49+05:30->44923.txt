PONDA: The   shigmo, with which shigmo festivities in the state kick off, this year gets an added impetus with the   pitching in an additional amount towards the prize money given by the state government to participants.

 The committee has raised an amount of Rs 1.9 lakh for the purpose. 
 
 “Artistes spend lakhs of rupees on making tableaus. In comparison, our prize money is small. Therefore, the committee has decided to add some amount to the prize money sanctioned by the state government,” committee chairman, PWD minister Ramkrishna ‘Sudin’ Dhavalikar told TOI.
 
  In the tableau and romtamell category, the first prize winner will get Rs 25,000 extra and the second and third winners will get Rs 15,000 and 10,000 extra respectively on the actual prize amount fixed by the government. 
 
 The prize money as fixed by the government for the first, second and third prize winner in the tableau category is Rs 60,000, Rs 50,000 and Rs 40,000, respectively. In the romtamell category the first, second and third prize winners will take home Rs 35,000, Rs 25,000 and Rs 15,000, respectively,   deputy chairperson   said. The Ponda shigmo parade will be held on Saturday.
