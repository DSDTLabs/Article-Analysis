Shah Rukh Khan’s ‘Raees’ is reigning supreme at the box office, since its big release on January 25. The film which stars  , Mahira Khan and Nawazuddin Siddiqui in leading roles has continued its fabulous run at the box office despite a working Friday. Rahul Dholakia-directed film enjoyed a fabulous opening collection of Rs 20.42 crore, followed by a phenomenal rise on day two with an estimated collection of Rs 26.30 crore. Continuing its winning streak, the film garnered in an estimated total of Rs 13.11 crore thus taking the total to Rs 59.83 cr in three days.
