 CCB police arrested  , 64, the chief preacher and the court remanded him in judicial custody on Tuesday. Baskar landed in the police net when he came to the city after absconding for few months.  Police said they received a complaint from VGP real estate against Norman Baskar in 2016. According to their complaint, the preacher had agreed to sell a land and a building of the church to the real estate firm in 2011. It was said that the property was estimated at 7 crore.
