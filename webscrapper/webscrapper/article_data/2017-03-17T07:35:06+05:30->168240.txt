GANDHINAGAR: In reply to the question of Mahudha  , the government has stated in the   that chief minister   and his ministers were paid a travelling allowance of Rs 81.20 lakh. 

 Thakore sought to know how much the government had paid to the ministers on account of travelling and daily allowance. Senior Congress leader Shaktisinh Gohil claimed that according to norms, ministers are supposed to travel in economy class, but several ministers used business class. 
 
 Replying to Gohil on behalf of   Vijay Rupani, Bhupendrasinh Chudasma,  , said that the only the CM is entitled to travel in business class, while all other ministers travel in economy class.
