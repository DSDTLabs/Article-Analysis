CUTTACK: Body of a Class XI student, who had gone missing since January 25, was found floating in   on Friday under   limits.

 The deceased, identified as Nisith Mohanty, was studying in Class XI at a private English Medium school, DAV Public School located at CDA. Nisith was staying at Palamandap and had gone for his tuition classes in Pithapur locality at around 7am on January 25. But till the noon hours he had not returned following which his family searched for him. Unable to trace him, the boy’s family had lodged a missing complaint at Badambadi police station. The police had also started its probe but they could not gather any information about the boy’s whereabouts.
 
 On Friday morning some passersby saw the boy’s body floating near Hadia patha and informed the police about it. The body was fished out by police team and even they found Nisith’s school bag on the riverbank. “The boy’s school identity card was in his bag which helped us to ascertain his identity,” said a senior police official.
 
 Police suspect it as a case of suicide. “Prima facie it seems the boy died of drowning. There are no injury marks on his body. However, we are waiting for the post mortem report as it will give us a clear picture of the incident,” said a senior cop.“We are trying to ascertain the real cause behind the death of the boy,” said inspector in charge of Badambadi police station, Biranchi Narayan Pati.
