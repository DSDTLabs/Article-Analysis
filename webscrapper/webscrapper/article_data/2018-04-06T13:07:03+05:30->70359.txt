JAISALMER: BSF guarding borders at the international border adjoining   has to face many challenges and dangers being on duty.

 During patrolling at   area in Jaisalmer district on Wednesday, a   got violent due to severe heat and attacked jawan Thakra Ram. The jawan fell down and the camel tried to kill him by sitting over him. The animal was shot dead to save the jawan. Ram suffered injuries and is out of danger. After the incident, BSF senior officers reached the spot and took the injured jawan to primary health centre.
 
 Following the incident, all the jawans were directed to remain alert during patrolling.
 
 Every year at the international border especially during the winters, camel during breeding season come in heat and attack on jawans and five such incidents have taken place at the border.
