 Similarly, power supply remained suspended in Sectors 38, 38 (West), 39, 40, 42, 55, 56 Palsora and Maloya from noon to 1pm on May 25.
 
 The electricity department caters to 2.25 lakh consumers divided into nine categories. Of these, 1.99 lakh are domestic, which accounts for more than 87% of the total consumers. The remaining 23% belongs to other categories i.e. commercial, small power, medium supply, large supply, bulk supply, public lighting, agriculture power and temporary supply.
