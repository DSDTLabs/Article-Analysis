The smog that has enveloped all of NCR throughout winter, has the   environment ministry worried about the well-being of its residents. According to reports, the Haryana government has decided to follow the Beijing model and will set up two giant air purifiers, in Gurgaon and  , respectively, to curb pollution. These 'smog free towers' will be designed by Dutch artist Daan Roosegaarde, who has also designed the Beijing  . 

  Reports say that Vipul Goel, Haryana's industry and environment minister, will be soon visiting Beijing for the same purpose. "Haryana surrounds the NCR from the south, west and north side, and it becomes our responsibility to share the burden of NCR’s fight against deadly air pollution," he has been quoted as saying.
