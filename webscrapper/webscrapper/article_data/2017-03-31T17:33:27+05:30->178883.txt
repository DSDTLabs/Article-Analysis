ABU DHABI:  , the first Ferrari-branded theme park in the world, has a new ride to boast of.

  The rollercoaster ' ' was officially launched at a high-profile inaugural event attended by   Chairman Mohamed Khalifa Al Mubarak, Miral CEO Mohamed Abdalla Al Zaabi and   Chief Brand Officer Luca Fuso, among other distinguished guests. 
 
  "Turbo Track isn’t just a rollercoaster; it’s an experience that combines the passion rooted in Ferrari’s traditions and values, with the excitement of a unique thrill ride. Ferrari World Abu Dhabi is going to be the location for mega rollercoaster enthusiasts all over the world," said Jesse Vargas, General Manager, Ferrari World Abu Dhabi. 
 
 
 
 
  
 
 
 The Turbo Track launches guests at 102 kmph through a glass funnel, propelling them vertically through the red roof to the highest point on Yas Island at 64m, before they experience zero gravity as they plummet vertically. Major structural changes were made to the building to accommodate the rollercoaster.
 
 Turbo Track becomes the third ride to be launched at Ferrari World Abu Dhabi in the last 12 months.
