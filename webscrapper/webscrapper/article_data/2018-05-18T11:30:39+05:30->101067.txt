HYDERABAD: Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture & Medical Common Entrance Test (  2018) Results are expected to be released on May 19 on the official website - eamcet.tsche.ac.in. 

 The TS EAMCET 2018 examination was conducted by the Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU) Hyderabad on behalf of Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE). 
 
 The TS EAMCET 2018 examination was held from May 2 to May 7. 
 
 The candidates who have appeared for the TS EAMCET 2018 exam can visit the official website - eamcet.tsche.ac.in - for latest update on the result date.
 
 The Preliminary Answer keys for the TS EAMCET 2018 exams were released and objections were invited till May 10, 2018. 
 
 
 
 
 1) Go to the official website of the TS EAMCET 2018 - eamcet.tsche.ac.in
 
 2) On the homepage, you will find link "View Result", click on it
 
 3) It will redirect you to a new webpage
 
 4) Enter your registeration number, name, date of birth and click on submit
 
 5) Your result will be displayed on screen of your mobile or cumputer
 
 6) Click save & download or take a printout for future reference
