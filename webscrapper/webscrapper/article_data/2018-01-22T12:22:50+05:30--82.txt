NEW DELHI: The Gulbarga University has announced the Commerce (BCom) 3rd-semester Result today on the official website of the Board - www.gug.ac.in.

 All the students who have appeared for Commerce (BCom) 3rd semester Examination in the year can check their result online on the official website - www.gug.ac.in.
 
 The Commerce (BCom) 3rd semester exam was conducted in the month of November-December 2017. Students who have appeared for the examination and wish to check their Commerce (BCom) 3rd semester Result can follow the steps given below:
 
 Step-by-step guide to check Commerce (BCom) 3rd semester Result online through official portal
 
 1) Open the official website - www.gug.ac.in
 
 2) On the homepage of the website, you will find the Result link. Click on it
 
 3) Once you click on the link, it will redirect you to a new webpage http://karnataka.indiaresults.com
 
 4) Select B.Com III Semester results and enter your details such roll number etc. Next, click on submit
 
 4) Your Commerce (BCom) 3rd semester result will be displayed onscreen. Cross check all the details given in your Commerce (BCom) 3rd semester Result.
 
 5) You can download and print your Commerce (BCom) 3rd semester Result for further reference.
