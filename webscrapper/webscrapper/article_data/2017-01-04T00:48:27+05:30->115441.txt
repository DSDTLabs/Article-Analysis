NEW DELHI: Two people died when the bike they were travelling on, rammed into a divider in south Delhi’s Fathehpur Beri on Sunday afternoon.

 The deceased were identified as Vijay (22) and Umesh (32). “Both the riders were not wearing helmets and had come from   in search of jobs in the city,” said a senior police officer.
 
 In another incident, a biker was crushed to death when a speeding vehicle hit him from behind in east Delhi’s Kalyanpuri on Monday night. The man was rushed to a private hospital where doctors declared him brought dead.
