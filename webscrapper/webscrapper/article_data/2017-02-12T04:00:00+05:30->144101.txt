Vadodara: Tragedy struck Ajwa Road-based Shirke family when they learnt about the death of their son Sunny Shirke (26) and son-in-law Sanjay Rajemahadik on Saturday morning.

 Shirke and Rajemahadik had gone to Navi Mumbai to attend the wedding of a friend on Friday. When they were returning to the city during the early hours on Saturday, their car met with an accident with a truck near Manor in Maharashtra and both died on the spot.
 
 The local police contacted Shirke’s family through the details mentioned on his company identity card and informed about the incident. The duo’s funeral was conducted later on Saturday.
