BHUBANESWAR: Noted Oriya musician, lyricist and bhajan singer   passed away in the wee hours on Thursday. He was 56. 

 Family sources said Muduli complained of chest pain and was rushed to a private hospital in Bhubaneswar at 2am where he breathed his last. 
 
 Born in 1961, Muduli won hearts of people by singing devotional songs and bhajans. 
 
 As the news of his death spread, people from all walks of life thronged to his residence to pay their last respects to Muduli.
 
 The bhajan maestro's body will be taken to his native village in   and then to Swargadwar Puri where his cremation will be done.
