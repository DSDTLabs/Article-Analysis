KOLKATA: Chief minister   on Saturday urged political parties to desist from taking out processions and holding meeting in the run-up to Durga Puja.

 “The Durga Puja inaugu rations start on October 18. will be inaugurating severa myself. I appeal to all not to disturb people by conducting processions and meetings Let us spend the Puja day peacefully,“ she said. The chief minister's appeal comes after Kolkata was choked last week with political rallies and meetings.With upcoming pandals encroaching upon roads, as well as the last-minute shopping rush, traffic has been slow paced for the entire week.
 
 Police on Friday released a series of road restrictions for the festival days, stipulating traffic curbs in Auckland Road from Government Place (east) and   from Plassey Gate Road.
