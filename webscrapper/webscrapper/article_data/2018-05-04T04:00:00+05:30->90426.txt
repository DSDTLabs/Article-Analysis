Surat: The Surat Municipal Corporation’s health officials of Athwa zone collected Rs 25,000 from Padmavati Association and Rs 10,000 from Rajubhai for throwing debris on public road and spilling sand and gravel too under ‘Operation Healthcare’. Moreover, recently five teams of Surat’s health department monitored trucks filled with coal moving out of Magdalla port and recovered Rs 30,000 from Magdalla Port Importers’ Association, Rs 25,000 from Sanarth Sai Export Services and Rs 5,000 from Amar Shah for spilling coal on road. They were asked to ensure that coal is not spilled on public road. tnn
