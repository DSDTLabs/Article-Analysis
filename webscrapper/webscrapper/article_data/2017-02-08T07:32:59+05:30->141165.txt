SURAT: Some posters came up in parts of the city on Tuesday against railway minister  . The posters under   asked Prabhu to come to Surat and solve the problem of leakage of dirt from passing trains that remains unresolved for the past many years. No group has claimed responsibility for the posters. The railway minister was gheraoed by   workers in the city on Sunday and given a lollipop for not taking up the long-pending demands of the city. He was also garlanded with a black scarf by the protesters.
