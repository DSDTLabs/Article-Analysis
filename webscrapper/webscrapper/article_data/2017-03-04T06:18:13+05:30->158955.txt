MEERUT: In a disturbing incident, three youths allegedly gang-raped a 16-year-old girl in a village in the district, shot the video of their act, blackmailed her for over a month and then sent the video clip to the girl's father before uploading it on social media. According to police, the girl was gangraped on January 28. 

 An FIR has been registered against three youths accused. Police are conducting raids at various places to nab the culprits.
 “The father of the girl approached police on Thursday after receiving the   video.An FIR has been filed, and we hope to nab the accused soon,“ said superintendent of police (rural) Shravan Kumar.
 
 Police said medical examination of the survivor was done on Friday .
