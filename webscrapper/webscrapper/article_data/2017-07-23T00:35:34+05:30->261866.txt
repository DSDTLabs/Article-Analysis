Kota: Two teams of   police busted sex rackets at two separate places on Friday night and arrested 18 persons, including eight women from Thailand. The kingpin of the sex racket, a couple from Bundi, were among those arrested.

 On a tip-off, sudden raids were conducted simultaneously at two spas in Central Square Mall under Gumanpura police station and in District Centre under Jahawaharnagar police station area of the city. The 18 accused were produced before the court on Saturday. The court sent 17 accused to judicial custody while the owner of the spas Rajesh Madhwani was sent to police remand till July 25.tnn
