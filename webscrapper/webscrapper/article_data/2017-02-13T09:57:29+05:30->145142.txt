ALLAHABAD: A local   has again ranked up a controversy on Sunday by displaying posters at  , where   and   have been described as braggers (laffaz) from Gujarat. 

 Irshad Ullah, general secretary, city Congress committee has put up the poster describing Modi and Shah as 'laffaz' and said that they would not be able to take the people of UP for a ride again.
 
 The poster reads 'UP ke do janbaz or Gujarat ke do laffaz - Faisala aapka (Choose between two heroes of UP or two braggers from Gujarat). 
 
 The poster carries the photographs of chief minister Akhilesh Yadav and Congress vice-president Rahul Gandhi along with that of Irshad Ullah. The Congress leader has not used the photographs of Modi and Shah in the poster. 
 
 Irshad Ullah told TOI that Prime Minister Narendra Modi and BJP national president Amit Shah are levelling baseless allegations against chief minister Akhilesh Yadav and Congress vice-president Rahul Gandhi during their election campaign despite the fact that the duo (Modi and Shah) had done nothing for the welfare of the people. Modi had befooled the masses through his fake promises but he stands exposed now as people have become aware of it. 
 
 He claimed that Akhilesh Yadav and Rahul Gandhi are working for the overall development of the state. SP-Congress alliance would get over 300 seats, he said.
