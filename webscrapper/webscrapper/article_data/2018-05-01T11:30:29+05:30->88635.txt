PANCHKULA:Some unknown person after breaking down the back window glass of an SUV   stole away laptop and laptop bag from the car on Sunday late at night in Sector 9,  .

 Some unknown person broke the window of an SUV car and stole a laptop, the bag containing a laptop and other valuable items on Sunday night in Sector 9, Panchkula.
 
 According to police, Manish Saini, a resident of Sector 9, told the cops that he drove down to Sector 9 market around 7.30pm in his Innova car (registration plate HR-75-0060). He parked the car in front of a brewery. When he returned, he found his car window broken, which had likely been caused by a brick.
 
 Later, Saini found out his laptop, its bag, hard disk and other important documents were missing from his car. Saini made a complaint to the police and a case under section 380 (theft in dwelling house, etc.) of the Indian Penal Code (IPC) was registered in Sector-5 police station.
 
 Surinder Singh, the investigation officer, Sector-10 police post, said, “Investigation is under progress. CCTV cameras are installed in the market and we will look into the CCTV footages.”
