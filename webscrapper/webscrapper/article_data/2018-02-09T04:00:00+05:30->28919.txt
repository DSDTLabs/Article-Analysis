Rajkot: A house was raided in Ganeshnagar area of Gandhidham in Kutch district and Indian-made foreign liquor (IMFL) worth Rs 4.6 lakh seized on Wednesday night. Police arrested one person. Police said, the house where the liquor was stored belonged to Govind Chande, who had rented it out to Ibrahim Hasam, who along with his two associates had fled before the raid. tnn
