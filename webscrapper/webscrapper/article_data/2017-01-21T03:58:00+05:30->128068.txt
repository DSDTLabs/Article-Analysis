Quepem: Deepak D’Costa, 37, of Cunneamoddi, Assolda, allegedly committed suicide as the woman he was in love with for the last two years refused to marry him. His body was found hanging near the Cotombi cemetery by passers-by.  Clifford D’Costa, Deepak’s brother, said, “For the last two years Deepak used to send money to a woman as they planned to marry. On his return from the ship in November, the woman refused. This cost my brother his life.” tnn
