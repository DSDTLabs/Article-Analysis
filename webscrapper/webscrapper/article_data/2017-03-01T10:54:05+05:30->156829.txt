An Indian columnist, humorist, writer and playwright,  , who was also known for the popular column 'Duniya Ne Undha Chashma' in Gujarati language, passed away at the age of 87.
 
  He died after prolonged illness, reported news agency ANI.
  
  
  
  His humorous column first appeared in Chitralekha in March 1971 and ever since there was no looking back for him.
  
  In 2008,   TV started the popular show 'Taarak Mehta Ka Ooltah Chashmah' that is based on his column and soon became the flagship show of the channel.
  
  Delhi's CM   also tweeted about the loss.
  
  
  He is survived by a daughter from his first marriage.
