CHANDIGARH: A woman   got killed while another sustained injuries after a speeding car hit their bicycles at   light point on Tuesday evening. 

 The deceased has been identified as Kaushalaya of Vikas Nagar,  .
 
 According to the police, the two women were riding on their bicycles when on Tuesday. When they reached Vikas Nagar light point, a speeding Alto K10 car came from behind, hit them and fled from the spot.
 
 Kaushalaya was crushed under the tire of the car and got severe injuries on her head. She died on the spot due to excessive bleeding from her head. The other woman sustained minor injuries in the  .
 
 The police rushed the two women to the GMC-32 hospital, where doctors declared Kaushalya dead on arrival.
 
 The police have registered a case against the errant driver under Sections 279 (rash driving on public way), 337 (causing hurt by act endangering life or personal safety of others) and 304-A (death caused due to negligence) of the Indian Penal Code in the Mauli Jagran police station.
 
 Mauli Jagran police station house officer inspector   said, "We recorded the injured woman's statement following which a case has been registered against the negligent driver. Our investigation is on and we will arrest him soon."
