Margao: The issue of illegal constructions was raised during the gram sabha on Sunday at   in Salcete.

 Sarpanch Sanjana Chari told TOI that “gram sabha members inquired about the illegal construction in ward 1 of the village whereupon they were informed that a show cause notice had been issued to the party concerned. The panchayat reassured villagers that the demolition process was in progress”.
 
 “Gram sabha members further expressed their resentment towards the director of panchayats’ notification issued regarding including the any other business (AOB) on the agenda of the gram sabha,” the Dramapur sarpanch said.
