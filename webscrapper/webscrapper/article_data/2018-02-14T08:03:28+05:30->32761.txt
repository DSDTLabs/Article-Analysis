The budget session of the state assembly will commence from March 9 and will conclude on March 16. 

 An assembly communique on Tuesday informed that governor Brig (Retd) BD Mishra has summoned the eight-day session (excluding Saturday and Sunday) and that the 2018-19 budget will be presented on the floor by deputy chief minister  , who also holds the finance portfolio.
 
 This will be the second budget of the   government in the state.
