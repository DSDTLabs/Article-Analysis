Panaji: The Panaji police on Friday arrested Narayan Goltekar of Merces and Anthony Nadar of St Cruz in connection with a case of extortion. The duo was arrested following a complaint by Gauresh Kalapurkar. In his complaint, Kalapurkar stated that the accused abused him and warned of dire consequences with the intention of extortion, on Friday. The accused were allegedly in possession of a chopper and a knife, Kalapurkar added.  An FIR was registered under sections 504 (intentional insult with intent to provoke breach of the peace), 504 (II) (criminal intimidation) and 387 (grievous hurt) read with section 34 of the IPC. tnn
