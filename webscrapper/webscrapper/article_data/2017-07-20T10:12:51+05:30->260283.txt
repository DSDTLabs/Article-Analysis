The   institutions cannot insist that its women employees cannot avail the 730-day   at a stretch, the Kerala high court has held. It is up to the employees to decide if the leave should be availed at a stretch or not, it said. 

 A single bench gave the ruling on a petition filed by Beena S, a constable with the Pallipuram Central Reserve Police Force unit, who moved the court after authorities refused to consider her application for leave extension.
