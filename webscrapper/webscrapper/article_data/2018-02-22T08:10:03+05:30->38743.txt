JAMSHEDPUR: Unidentified thieves allegedly stole Rs 25 lakh from an   under   area in the wee hours on Wednesday. According to the police, the thieves broke the teller machine and decamped with the cash. 

 "Investigation into the matter is underway," SDPO Ghatshila Rajendra Prasad, said.
 
 Private security guard on duty at the teller kiosk had locked the machine on late on Tuesday and went home. On Wednesday, when he arrived at the ATM, he found the machine chest open and cash missing. The ICICI bank had filled cash Rs 25.33 lakh in the ATM chest on Tuesday , a police officer said.
