VADODARA: A   fell in an open well in Pav village of   taluka in   district late on Thursday night. The animal was rescued only by Friday evening.

 The leopard is believed to have preyed on chicken being reared by one Mansinh   in the village. On its way back from the house of Sangod, the leopard may not have spotted the open well and fell in it.
 
 The leopard started roaring frantically and villagers rushed to the well. The villagers informed the forest department regarding the animal. Forest officials rushed to the village on Friday morning and began a rescue operation.
 
 A ladder was lowered in the well in an attempt to encourage the leopard to climb over it. The leopard eventually did so late on Friday evening and escaped into the forest nearby.
