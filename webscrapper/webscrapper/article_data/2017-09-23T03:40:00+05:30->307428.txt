Panaji: Economic Development Corporation (EDC Limited) has registered a record profit of Rs 61 crore for 2016-17, a rise of Rs 13 crore as compared to the Rs 48 crore profit recorded last year,   chairman Sidharth Kuncalienker told reporters after the EDC board meet held on Friday. “Out of Rs 61 cr profit, Rs 12 cr is interest. There is 12% increase in the loan segment and interest rate has been brought down by 1%, to 10.5 %,” Kuncalienker said, adding that EDC net worth was Rs 450 crore. “We have given Rs 880 cr as term loans, and have managed to bring non-performing asset (NPA) to 1%,” he said. tnn
