PANCHKULA: A Naib Subedar allegedly committed suicide by hanging himself from a ceiling fan with the help of a rope in his room in   on Wednesday.

 Dilip Singh, a resident of Unnao district in Uttar Pradesh, was posted in the Western Command Hospital. He had been serving in the Army for 27 years.
 
 When Singh did not report for duty, his colleagues went to his room and knocked the door. On receiving no response, they broke it down only to find him hanging from the fan. No suicide note was found.
 
 A police team from   took over the crime scene. The body was taken to  , Sector 6, where the post-mortem examination would be conducted on Thursday.
 
 The police registered a case under section 174 (non-attendance in obedience to an order from public servant) of the Indian Penal Code (IPC) at the Chandimandir police station.
 
 Chandimandir station house officer Harbhajan Singh said, "No suicide note was found from near the body. We have kept the body in the mortuary and the post-mortem would be conducted on Thursday."
