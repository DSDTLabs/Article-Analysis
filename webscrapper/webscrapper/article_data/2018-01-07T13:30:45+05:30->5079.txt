MUMBAI: There is a megablock on the Mulund-Matunga Up slow line on the   main line and this could lead to trains running behind schedule this afternoon. 

 Officials said that all Up slow line services leaving Thane till 4.02 pm will be diverted on Up fast line between   and   stations halting at Mulund, Bhandup, Vikhroli, Ghatkopar,   and   stations and further re-diverted to Up slow line proper at Matunga station.
 
 "The Down fast line services leaving Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus till around 3 pm will halt at Ghatkopar, Vikhroli, Bhandup, Mulund and Diva stations in addition to their respective scheduled halts and arrive destination 20 minutes behind schedule," an official said. The UP fast line services leaving Kalyan till 3.06 pm will halt at Diva, Mulund, Bhandup, Vikhroli, Ghatkopar and Kurla in addition to their respective scheduled halts and arrive destination 15 minutes behind schedule.
 
 UP slow line services will not be available at Nahur, Kanjurmarg and Vidyavihar stations. Passengers of these stations are permitted to travel via Mulund, Bhandup, Vikhroli, Ghatkopar and Kurla stations, the official added.
