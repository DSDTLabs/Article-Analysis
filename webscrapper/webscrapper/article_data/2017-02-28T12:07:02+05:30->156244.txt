VARANASI:   Rajnath Singh said the ISIS won't be allowed to become a threat for India. 

 "The government is keeping an eye on the ISIS. It won't be allowed to become a challenge for the country," said Singh while talking to reporters here on Monday.
 
 Replying a query on the arrest of two suspected ISIS men on Sunday in Gujarat, Rajnath said that the ISIS will not be able to influence Indian youth as people are alert and supportive to the government. "In fact, the parents of some misguided youth contacted me and informed about their concerns," he said adding that the government is keeping an eye on them. 
 
 Gujarat   arrested two ISIS suspects, who were allegedly planning to carry out serial blasts in Gujarat, from Rajkot and Bhavnagar, on Sunday.
