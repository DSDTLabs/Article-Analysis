CHENNAI: A 7-year-old girl playing near her house in   died in an accident on Tuesday night when a reversing car rammed into a stationary vehicle which in turn knocked her down. 

 The child Pavitra was standing behind the parked car during a game of hide-and-seek with her friends on Vanniyar Second Street when the accident took place at around 9.30pm. Police said the driver, identified as Preethi, 25, was learning to drive her husband's Santro when she lost control of the vehicle and collided with the parked   car belonging to a neighbour Babu. 
 
 
  
 
 
 Hearing screams, residents came to the child's rescue and rushed her to a private hospital where she was declared brought dead. Her body was sent to the Kilpauk Medical College Hospital.
 
 Anna Nagar traffic investigation wing inspector Chandramohan subsequently visited the accident spot. Police later arrested Preethi under IPC Sections 279 (rash driving or riding on a public way) and 304 (a) (causing death by negligence).
