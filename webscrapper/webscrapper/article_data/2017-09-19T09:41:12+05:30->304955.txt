The cast of the upcoming film   got their Sunday fun vibes on with a special curated event hosted for them at the popular hangout place, Verbana Sky Gardens in Southern Mumbai. 

 The entire cast of the film including  , Shahana Goswani,  , Nakul Bhalla and Jay Upadhyay got together along with their director Milind Dhaimade. 
 
 At the event, even fans of the star cast were invited selected via a contest and it was Barun and Shahana who turned chefs making several dishes for the entire cast and in turn Avinash became a bartender and stirred up some cocktails.
 
 Barun recently returned to Television. He is currently seen in a TV show   where he plays the role of Advay Singh Raizada.
