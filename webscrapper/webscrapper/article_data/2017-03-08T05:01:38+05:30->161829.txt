GURUGRAM: A 55-year-old man and his wife have been thrashed and robbed of Rs 45,000 in cash, a gold chain and a pendant by four assailants in Farrukhnagar. 

 Talib Khan and his wife Gulab Khan said they were attacked and robbed by Asgar Khan, Israr Khan, Ajhar Khan and Babbu Khan on Sunday. An FIR under sections 323 (causing hurt), 324 (use of weapons), 379B (snatching) and 34 of the IPC has been registered.
