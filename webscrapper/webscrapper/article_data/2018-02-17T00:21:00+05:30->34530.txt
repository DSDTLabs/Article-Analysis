Kota: A 21-year-old youth from Jaisalmer, who was taking NEET coaching from a premier institute in Kota, committed suicide late on Thursday evening by hanging himself from the ceiling fan at his PG in Talwandi area under Jawahar Nagar police station.

 Police suspect study stress to be behind the extreme step. The deceased was identified as Rahul Rathore (21), a resident of Jaisalmer. tnn
