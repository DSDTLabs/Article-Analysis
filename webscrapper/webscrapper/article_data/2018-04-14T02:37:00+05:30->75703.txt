NEW DELHI: A major mishap was averted at Delhi's   railway station on Friday evening after an alert   (RPF) officer quickly helped in dousing a fire at one of the platform stalls. 

 Railway officials said no injuries were reported. However, the swift thinking of SI   helped keep the damage to a minimum. "A major catastrophe was prevented due to his quick thinking. He showed exemplary courage by dousing the fire without waiting for the fire brigade. We will award him for his courage," said a railways official.
 
 Investigation of the spot has revealed that the fire may have occurred due to a short circuit at a stall located at platform number 3 of the station. 
 
 "It appears to be a case of short circuit. However, an investigation will be carried out. The fire was extinguished within minutes of it breaking out," said an official.
