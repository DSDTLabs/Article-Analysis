Vadodara: A 31-year-old man committed suicide by hanging at his house on Thursday morning. Harish Manani, a resident of Ajwa Road, is suspected to have taken the drastic step due to poor financial condition. Police said that Manani left a suicide note behind but he didn’t blame anyone for taking extreme step. According to the police, Manani was a tempo driver who also dealt in scrap items. His sister’s wedding was fixed some time ago but he wasn’t able to arrange finances for it. Manani was reportedly tense over last few days. On Thursday, he hanged himself with a piece of cloth when he was alone at home. Cops are investigating the case on the basis of the suicide note Manani left behind.TNN
