CHANDIGARH: A driver stole a mobile phone and gold jewellery items from the house of an elderly couple in  , police said on Saturday. 

 Virender Gill, 66, who lives with her husband   and maid   and her family, said they had hired the driver identified as Amarnath, a resident of Sector 2, Chandigarh, last year. On Friday, he came to their house when her husband was not present.
 
 The accused told the maid that Gill had told him to bring his phone. When the maid left, he took away the phone, one gold pendant, three gold buttons, three pair of ear rings and two gold rings. 
 
 Police registered a case under Section 380 (theft in dwelling house) at the Sector 11 police station. Inspector  , station house officer, Sector 11, said, "We have registered a case and investigation is under way."
