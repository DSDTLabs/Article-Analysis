VIJAYAWADA:   too will come up in   soon, said   E S L Narasimhan who visited   Devasthanam on Sunday. 

  Narasimhan, who will address the assembly session on Monday, reached Vijayawada around 5 pm and had a darshan of the presiding deity Kanaka Durga atop the Indrakeeadri. 
 
  Temple executive officer A Surya Kumari and priests accorded him a ceremonial reception and vedic scholars at the Aseervachana mandapam offered traditional blessings to him. 
 
  Addressing the media after the darshan, Narasimhan praised the efforts of CM Naidu for completing construction of the assembly building in a record time and said Amaravati will be a model capital.
