MANGALURU: With a view to having a single point of contact for all sorts of guidance, assistance and grievance redressal for on board passengers of trains,   has come out with the novel concept of ‘Train Captain’ service in trains. 

 In  , various trains are provided with the services of on board ‘Train Captain’. In line with that, under the directions of  , Divisional Railway Manager, Train Captains have been nominated with effect from Sunday on Train Nos. 12671/12672 – Mettupalayam-Chennai Central-Mettuapalayam  . For these trains Commercial Staff (Travelling Ticket Examiners-TTI) namely - R. Rathish, TTI, 7904557219; S. Dinesh Kumar, TTI, 9600425860 and A. Sandeep, TTI, 8681844886 - have been nominated as Train Captains.
 
 GV Venkatesan, PRO/Salem Division said their names will be displayed in all the reservation charts in the coaches. In the near future, it has been planned to send all passengers of the particular train with the details of the day’s on board  , via SMS. Passengers can also contact Railway Helpline No. 138 to know the details about the Train Captain.
 
 The train captain will be overall in charge of the train from end to end and will be responsible for ensuring passenger amenities throughout the train, including availability of water, cleanliness of coaches/toilets, working of electrical fittings such as fan, light, mobile charging points, etc. He will initiate necessary action to redress the problems faced by the passengers instantly and in case the problem could not be solved immediately, he will take up the matter with higher officials for necessary action.
