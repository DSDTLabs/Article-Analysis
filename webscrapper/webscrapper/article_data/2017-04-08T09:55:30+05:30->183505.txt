LUCKNOW :More than 270   were booked and over Rs 1.44 lakh collected through fine in a single day in a massive drive against illegal parking on Friday from   crossing to   crossing, in Lalbagh, Darul Shafa, on Sapru Marg and in front of Vidhan Bhawan. 

 Traffic sub-inspector PK Shahi said a total of 176 four-wheelers and 97 two-wheelers were booked under the ‘no-parking’ drive. A sum of Rs 1,000 was imposed as fine on four-wheelers while Rs 500 fine was imposed on two-wheelers. Fourteen cranes are used for lifting vehicles. The drive created a flutter in the area as such a massive exercise by the traffic police is rare.
