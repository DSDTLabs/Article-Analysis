NEW DELHI: Delhi BJP chief   announced an election committee on Tuesday night. The committee will oversee all the election preparations, including candidate selection process. 

 Headed by Tiwari, the committee will have all the seven BJP MPs, Delhi BJP in-charge Shyam Jaju, co-incharge Tarun Chugh, Union sports minister and former state chief Vijay Goyal, national general secretary Anil Jain, former Delhi BJP chief Satish Upadhyay, LOP and BJP MLA Vijender Gupta, all the three state unit general secretaries, Delhi unit organisational secretary Sidharthan, Pawan Sharma and president of women's wing Poonam Parashar Jha.
