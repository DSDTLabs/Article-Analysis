Ludhiana: An unidentified miscreant snatched the Royal Enfield of a 19-year-old on Wednesday during test drive. In his police complaint, Krishna colony resident Ravi Kumar said he wanted to sell his Royal Enfield motorcycle, for which he went to the scooter market on Gill Road. There, a man showed interest in buying his bike and both went on a test drive. The man was riding the bike and Ravi riding pillion. Suddenly, he said, the man stopped his bike, flashed a sharp-edged weapon and threatened to kill Ravi, who got off, after which he fled with the bike. 

 Investigating officer, Dugri police station said police have registered a case and CCTV cameras installed on Gill road to CRPF colony are being scanned to trace the accused.
