Telugu actor  , known for his macho looks is yet to get a major movie after his box office hit ‘Bhale Manchi Roju’ in 2015.

  Now, the buzz is that Sudheer has bagged an offer from ace director   to play the major antagonist in his upcoming movie, which has actor Balakrishna playing the lead role.
 
 However, the PR team of the actor has rejected these rumours and said that the actor hasn’t signed any contract with the filmmakers. Sudheer has recently forayed into Bollywood with ‘Baaghi’.
