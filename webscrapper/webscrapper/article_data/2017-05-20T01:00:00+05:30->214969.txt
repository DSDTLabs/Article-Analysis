Punjabi singer  , who is known for tracks like 'Dil Le Gayi Kudi Gujarat Di' and 'Koka', is on a high these days. He has recreated his ’90s hit, 'Dil Le Gayi Kudi', for the film ' '.  Jasbir says, “The team of 'Sweetiee Weds NRI' is amazing and high on energy. So, I wanted to be a part of the promotions.” He even cancelled his   trip to tour various cities with the movie’s team. 
 
 The film, written by   and directed by Hasnain Hyderabadwala, stars Himansh Kohli and Miss India 2013 Second Runner-Up   in the lead.
