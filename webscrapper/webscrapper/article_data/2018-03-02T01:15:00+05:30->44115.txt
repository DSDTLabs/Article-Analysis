Madurai: Two alleged rowdies were shot dead by the city police on the outskirts of the city on Thursday evening in what police claim to have been an act of self-defence. The alleged encounter took place at Sikkandarchavady in the rural police limit, when a six-member police team in civil dress attempted to arrest a gang hiding in a residential house.
