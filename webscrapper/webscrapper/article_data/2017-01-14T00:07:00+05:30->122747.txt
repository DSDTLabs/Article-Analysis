Mangaluru: The city police on Wednesday arrested a person for posting a derogatory comment against Dr B R Ambedkar on  . 

 Police said, Deepak Kamath,39, a Mangalurean but settled in Bengaluru, had posted a derogatory comment on Ambedkar two months ago. A case was registered against him by dalit activist Ramesh Kotian at Mangaluru South police station. 
 
 Police, who began the investigation, traced the internet protocol related to the posting and found that it was done by Deepak Kamath. Meanwhile, Deepak had shifted to Bengaluru from Mangaluru. On Wednesday, police arrested him from a hotel in Mangaluru.
