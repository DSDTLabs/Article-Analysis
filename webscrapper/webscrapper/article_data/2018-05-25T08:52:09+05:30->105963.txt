 Dr Khan, who was a lecturer of pediatrics at the Baba Raghav Das (BRD) Medical College in Gorakhpur, is out on bail. 
 
 On May 22, Dr Khan had expressed his willingness to work for   virus hit patients at the Government Medical College, Kozhikode, and had taken to his social media to share the same. In his post, Dr Khan mentioned that he was disturbed with the deaths of people in Kozhikode and of Sister Lini, and requested Kerala CM Pinarayi Vijayan to allow him to serve in his state.
 
 In a prompt response, Kerala CM's office asked Dr Khan to get in touch with the health department or the superintendent of Government Medical College, Kozhikode.
 
  "The health department of Kerala then took all my details and formalities were run through," he said.
