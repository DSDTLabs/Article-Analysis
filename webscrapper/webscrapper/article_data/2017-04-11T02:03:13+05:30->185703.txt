GHAZIABAD: The Ghaziabad district administration on Monday shut down 25 illegal factories in   area of  .

 The units, mostly chemical-manufacturing and clothes-dyeing factories, did not possess permits from any government authority, including  .
 
 Monday’s move came as part of the administration’s ongoing drive against illegal units. In the first phase of the drive last week, 40 illegal units in Loni were sealed by the administration. The drive on Monday was undertaken jointly by the district administration, UP Pollution Control Board, UP Power Corporation Limited and Loni Nagar Palika Parishad.
 
 “The units sealed on Monday were releasing untreated effluents into water bodies thereby causing pollution. We had issued prior show-cause notices to them, but there was no response. We have initiated criminal proceedings against these units,” sub-divisional magistrate   told TOI.
 
 Of the 25 factories sealed, eight did not have any name. An FIR has been registered against all 25 units in Loni Border police station and the factory owners have been booked under Section 277 (fouling water of public spring or reservoir) of IPC.
