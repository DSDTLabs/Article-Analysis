BICHOLIM: A 5-year-old boy from Gawandiwada Karapur, Bicholim, drowned after accidentally falling into a well near his house on Wednesday.

 Locals said,   had gone looking for his father who was working as a labourer at a place near the accident spot. However, it was only when the man returned home at night and started searching for his son, that the family members realised the child was missing. 
 
 They immediately lodged a police complaint and after a massive search, the body was fished out on Thursday morning. The body was sent to GMC Hospital, Bambolim, for post-mortem and later handed over to the family in the evening. Bicholim police are investigating the matter.
