From being a Delhi boy who had no Godfather in the industry to becoming one of the biggest superstars of B-town, King Khan’s career trajectory is awe-inspiring. The superstar completed 25 years in Mumbai this month and it coincided with the inauguration of a new VFX office under his banner of Red Chillies. 

 
  
  
  
 
 Overwhelmed by the joyous occasion, a nostalgic SRK took to Twitter to share his thoughts. He wrote, “25 yrs in Mumbai, which has given me my life. For the 1st time at the opening of RC vfx new office I felt, I must have done something rite.”
 
  
  
 
 Over the last 25 years, SRK has reigned supreme in Bollywood. After starting his acting career with the television series ‘Fauji’, he made his debut in movies with ‘Deewana’ in 1992. The actor was catapulted to superstardom with ‘Baazigar’ and the rest, as they say, is history.
