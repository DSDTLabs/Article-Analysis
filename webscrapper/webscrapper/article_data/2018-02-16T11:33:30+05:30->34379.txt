CHANDIGARH: Clouds made a comeback on Thursday. According to the officials of the  , the day temperature witnessed a slight drop in temperature. However, the mercury spiked a little at night. 

 After witnessing a warm Tuesday, the city witnessed a drop in temperature on Wednesday. The mercury fell by a few points. The officials said that the minimum temperature dropped till Wednesday.
 
 "The clouds would stay, however the temperature is expected to rise once again," the Met officials said.
 
 On Tuesday morning, the city recorded 15mm rainfall and the day became clear thereafter. This led to a rise in maximum temperature. On Monday, it had dropped to 18.3 degrees Celsius, 5 degrees Celsius below normal, and on Tuesday it spiked to 22.5 degrees Celsius. On Wednesday it was recorded at 21.5 degrees Celsius and on Thursday it further dropped to 21 degrees Celsius, which was 2 degrees Celsius below normal.
 
 The minimum temperature, on the other hand, rose to 9.2 degrees Celsius, just one degree Celsius above normal, while it dropped to 8.4 degrees Celsius on Wednesday. The temperature dropped to 10.8 degrees Celsius on Tuesday from 12 degrees Celsius.
 
  Weathermen have predicted a mainly clear sky turning partly cloudy towards evening on Friday. The day's temperature will be recorded at 23 degrees Celsius and the minimum temperature will be recorded at 10 degrees Celsius.
