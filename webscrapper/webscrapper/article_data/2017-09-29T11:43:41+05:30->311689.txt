CHANDIGARH: Chandigarh organised a tree   by planting more than 200 saplings on Thursday. Over 150 volunteers took part in the campaign that witnessed the participation of scientists, technical staff and students of Indo Swiss Training Centre. The volunteers planted saplings at designated spots along the parking of CSIR-CSIO.

 The drive was inaugurated by director  . The aim of the drive was to increase the awareness of the importance of biodiversity for our well-being and work to improve the tree canopy of the campus.
