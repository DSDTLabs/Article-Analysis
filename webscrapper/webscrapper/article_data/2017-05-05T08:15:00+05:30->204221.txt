 Manjeet Singh, SHO, Samrala police station, said police had set up a check post as part of the drive against peddlers on Tuesday. A Ford Icon car was signalled to stop for checking in the evening. During frisking, police found 15g of psychotropic powder from the constable. 
 
 Police said the samples of recovered drugs were sent to the forensic laboratory in Kharar to ascertain the exact composition of the narcotics powder. They added that the accused was produced before a local court which remanded him in three-day police custody.
 
 Deputy superintendent of police (DSP) Samrala G P Singh said preliminary investigation suggested that the accused used to get drugs from two Nigerian drug suppliers John and KC. They used to send him the consignment from Delhi. He added that both the drug suppliers had cases against them in police stations in Ludhiana and Patiala.
 
 The DSP said they had brought KC on production warrant from Delhi and are questioning him to track the supply chain. A departmental inquiry has already been initiated against the constable following his suspension by SSP Fatehgarh Sahib Alka Meena. 
 
 “The accused has been suspended and a departmental inquiry has been marked to DSP headquarters Palwinder Cheema into the matter,” he said.
