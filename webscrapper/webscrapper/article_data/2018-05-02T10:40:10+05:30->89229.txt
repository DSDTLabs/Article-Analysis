NEW DELHI: The Punjab School Education Board (PSEB) will release the PSEB Class 10 secondary examination results 2018 in the first week of May 2018. 

 
 The exact time of the announcement of Class 10 result is not mentioned on the official website of the PSEB board. However, as per report from sources, the result may be declared during the second half of the first week of May 2018.
 
 The Punjab Board Class 10 examination 2018 result will be released on the official website of the PSEB board - pseb.ac.in 
 
 The Punjab Board Class 10th Matriculation examination was held from March 12 to 31, 2018. 
 
 The PSEB had announced the Punjab Board Class 12 results on April 23, 2018, on its official website.
 
 The students who have appeared for the Punjab Board secondary examination (Class 10 Exam) can follow the steps given below to check their result from the official website.
 
 
 
 
 1) Open the official website of the Punjab Board - pseb.ac.in
 
 2) One the homepage, you will find the Punjab Board Class 10 result link
 
 3) Click on the result link
 
 4) You will be redirected to a new page
 
 5) Enter all your details such as name, roll number and click on submit
 
 6) Your result will be displayed online
 
 7) Download it or take a printout for future reference
