NEW DELHI: Equity markets opened positive on Tuesday with   scaling up more than 100 points. The 30-share BSE index opened 41 points above previous closing mark at 34,491, but   soared riding on bank stocks and was nearing 34,600-mark at 9.50 am. The broader 50-share NSE   too was testing a psychological mark, that of 10,600.

 Private bank stocks like  , Yes Bank and Axis Bank were leading the charts while the Nifty Pharma was topping the sectoral sub-indices' list gaining half a per cent. IT stocks which had an impressive run in the lat few sessions were undergoing correction as the Nifty IT sub-index was down 0.30 per cent.
