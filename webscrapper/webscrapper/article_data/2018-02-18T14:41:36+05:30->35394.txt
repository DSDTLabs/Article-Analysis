VADODARA: M S University’s syndicate members on Monday will give a nod to a proposal through which one-time registration   and examination fees for   getting registered at   will be fixed. 

 The university has proposed to collect Rs 3,000 as one-time registration fees, Rs 7,500 as examination fees at the time of submission of synopsis and Rs 12,500 as fees at the time of submission of thesis. The fees will be revised at rate of 10% every year. According to university officials, while fees of PhD research scholars at other faculties has already received nod from the university’s syndicate body, the proposal for Faculty of Medicine is being brought to bring it at par with other faculties.
