PANAJI: In its effort to regulate   transportation, owners of all   used for the purpose have been directed to register their vehicles with the directorate of mines and geology (DMG).

 The decision that has been taken is aimed at stopping illegal mining of minor minerals and ensuring proper collection of government revenue, besides preventing accidents and facilitating proper accounting of the minor minerals extracted.
 
 Vehicle owners, including those used to bring in minor minerals into the state, will have to register their vehicles with the DMG against a one-time registration fee of Rs 5,000 to qualify for carrying out   of minor minerals from April 1. 
 
 All vehicles will have to have install GPS systems, stated the DMG circular.
