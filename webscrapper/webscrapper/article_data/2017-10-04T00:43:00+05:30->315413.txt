Udhagamandalam: Nilgiris collector J Innocent Divya on Tuesday lanuched an online service, ‘At Your Service’, for people to submit their petitions. People can send their petitions on WhatsApp, Twitter and Facebook. By going digital, weekly petition submission at the collectorate has been replaced by the collector’s WhatsApp number.

 According to a press release, public can send their petitions, complaints and suggestions to the administration through collector’s WhatsApp number-99431-26000.
 
 Implementation and information about government’s welfare schemes will be made public through the newly launched Twitter handle and Facebook page, the release stated.
 
 People can connect with the collector on www.facebook.com/collector Nilgiris and www.twitter.com/collrnlg
