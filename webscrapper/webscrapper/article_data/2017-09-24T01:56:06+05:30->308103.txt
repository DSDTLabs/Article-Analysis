MUMBAI: A  , Javed Patel (43), was arrested after a BEST  , Vijay Kamble, lodged a case against him. Patel had conned Kamble of Rs 19.5 lakh by claiming to offer inexpensive   housing. Kamble had submitted a written complaint to the vigilance department of Mhada which established that he was conned. The Kherwadi police lodged a case of cheating against Patel and arrested him. 

 Patel allegedly took money from many home buyers by telling them that he has good contacts with top Mhada officials. He also gave them forged documents. Involvement of Mhada employees in this racketis also being probed.
