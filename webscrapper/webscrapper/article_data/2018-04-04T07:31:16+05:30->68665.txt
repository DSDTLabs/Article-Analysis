CHENNAI:   (RGGGH) has successfully tested a novel method of using zoledronic acid on giant cell tumor ( ), a cancer-like condition affecting the bones. 

 In this method , a zoledronic acid injection is given to the patients to reduce the destruction in the bone due to GCT. After three weeks, the patient is taken for surgery. The defect is filled with a bone called fibula taken from the patient's leg. Implemented under the chief minister's health insurance scheme, the procedure has benefited 10 patients.
