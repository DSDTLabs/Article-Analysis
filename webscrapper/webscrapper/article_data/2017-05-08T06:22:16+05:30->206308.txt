GUWAHATI: President of the   Jayshree Mehta inaugurated the first   fellowship course of eastern India being held at   here on Sunday. 

 The three-day training-cum-fellowship course for surgeons is being organized under the aegis of the Indian Association of Gastro Intestinal Endosurgeons, president-elect of the association and chairman of the organizing committee as informed by Prof Subhash Khanna said. More than 70 surgeons have been inducted in the programme.
