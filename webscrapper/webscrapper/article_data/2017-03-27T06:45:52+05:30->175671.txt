KOZHIKODE:   police in   on Sunday registered a case against an assistant vicar under the Mananthavady diocese on charges of outraging the modesty of a woman.

  Fr Jino, assistant Vicar of Chundakkara church, was charged under Section 509 (word, gesture or act intended to insult the modesty of a woman) of the Indian Penal Code. 
 
 The complainant lodged the petition on Sunday. An officer attached to Kambalakkad police station said that the priest insulted the woman and proposed to marry her, the police said.
