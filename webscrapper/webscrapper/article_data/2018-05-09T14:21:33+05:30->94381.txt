RAIPUR: The Chhattisgarh Board of School Education (CGBSE) Class 10th and 12th results were declared today, May 9, 2018, on the official website of the Board - cgbse.nic.in

 Chhattisgarh education minister Sri Kedar Kashyap released the Board results at a press meet to be held in the new building of Madhyamik Shiksha Mandal in Raipur.
 
 
 
 
 
  
  
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 This year, the Class 10th exams were conducted from March 5 to March 28, 2018, whereas Class 12th exams were held from March 7 to April 2, 2018.
 
 
 
 
 In 2015, around 2,45,453 students have appeared. Pass percentage of the students in around 79.62%
 
 In 2016, the performance was much better with a pass percentage of 83.73 for the strength of 3,56,953 who have appeared for the examination.
 
 Last year was highest in terms of number of students appeared. Out of 4,42,060 who have appeared for the examination 3,86,349 students have passed in the examination with a pass percentage of 62.06% among girls and 59.86% pass percentage among girls.
 
 
 
 
 In 2015, a total of 2,37,356 students have appeared for the examination with a pass percentage of around 71.12%
 
 In 2016, there was a pass percentage of around 73.43 among 2,77,114 students appeared.
 
 Last year 2,79,906 students have appeared for the examination and the overall pass percentage is around 76.36%. The boys had a pass percentage of around 73.07% and the girls had a pass percentage of 79.05%
