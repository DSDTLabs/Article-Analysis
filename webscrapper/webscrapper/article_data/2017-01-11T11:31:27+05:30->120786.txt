Vadodara: It was a chilly morning on Tuesday but colourful kites of all sizes and shapes swarmed the city   of the dawn. Hundreds of Barodians turned up to participate in and enjoy the International Kite Festival 2017 that was organized at   by   ( ). From birds to butterflies and cartoon characters to the sun, kite enthusiasts flew a variety of kites of different shapes and vibrant colours. 

 Apart from locals, nine foreigners participated in the kite festival that was supported by Gujarat tourism department and district administration. The kids and youngsters marvelled at the colourful kites that took off from the ground and reached the skies within moments. And many Barodians also turned up at the venue to buy kites, caps and toys for Uttarayan.
