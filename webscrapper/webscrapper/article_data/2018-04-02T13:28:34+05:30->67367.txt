 All public transport services, including Kerala State Road Transport Corporation buses, stopped service from Monday midnight. Commuters who arrived at railway stations were stranded due to non-availability of vehicles to reach destination.
  
  
 Barring a few private vehicles, roads look deserted on Monday. All institutions, including banks, were not functioning since morning.
 
 The united trade union forum, comprising 16 unions such as CITU, INTUC, AITUC, STU, HMS and UTUC conducted march in Thiruvananthapuram. The union activists also staged blockade of some major roads in capital city.
