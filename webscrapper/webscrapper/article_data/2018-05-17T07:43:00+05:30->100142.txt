 The woman was married to a Tigaon resident in 2011 and has a four-year-old son. Owing to differences they got divorced in November 2017 after which she started staying with her mother. The woman said, “On April 8, my mother with a   came inside my room, where my son and I was sleeping. The tantrik tried to rape me after my mother took my son out of the room.”
 
 Apparently the victim approached the cops, and then decided to stay somewhere else. “After a few days, I met my ex-husband in Sector 49. He threatened me to return to my mother’s house else he would kidnap my son. As I returned to the house, my mother informed him following which he came and thrashed me,” the woman said.
