VADODARA:   organized 34th- 35th all breed dog show at Lalbaug here on Sunday. Around 195 dogs from 33 different breeds participated in the event. 

 Doberman,  ,  , Dalmatian, Culture Pom, Chow Chow and pugs were some of the breeds at the show. 
 
 The dogs were judged on the criteria of pureness of breeds, their movements and how accurately they followed the instructions of their owners. 
 
 The show started at 10:15am and concluded at around 6:30pm. Asked about dual-numbered event,  , honorary secretary of the Gujarat Kennel Club, told TOI, “This is one of its kind event. There are two shows taking place in two different rings which are being judged by two judges simultaneously.”
