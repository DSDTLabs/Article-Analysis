NOIDA: An unidentified body of a woman has been found in the   area of Sector 121.

 According to the police, the women could have been poisoned since the body had turned blue. 
 
 Phase III SHO Umed Singh Yadav said that the woman has injury marks on her face and it seems that she was killed somewhere and her body dumped here. The post-mortem report is awaited.
