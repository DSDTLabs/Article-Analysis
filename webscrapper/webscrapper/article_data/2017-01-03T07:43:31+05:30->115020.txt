RANCHI: More than  ,500 people from Ranchi are expected to attend the celebration of   at   from January 3-8. At least a 1,000 of them left the city on Saturday and Sunday while the remaining are expected to leave by Wednesday. Special seats were reserved for the pilgrims by the railways to ensure their movement. The event, which is the biggest in Bihar, will be attended by Prime Minister Narendra Modi. 

 Nine 'langers' and three tents, each with a capacity to accommodate 30,000 people, have already been set up at Gandhi Maidan, Kangan Ghat Gurudwara and near Patna old bypass for the occasion.
