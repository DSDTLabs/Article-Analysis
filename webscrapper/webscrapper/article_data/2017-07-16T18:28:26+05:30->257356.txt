‘Ranchi Diaries’ starring  ,  ,   Sharma, Himansh Kohli and   tells the story of a young aspiring actress/model and her Godfather who can do whatever it takes to make it big in life. 

 The makers of the film recently dropped a teaser of the film, which is fast paced and intriguing. A voice in the video suggests that characters are making plans to rob banks. Meanwhile, they are also sure that the task will not be an easy one. In the 32 seconds trailer, we are introduced to characters who are fresh. 
 
 ‘Ranchi Diaries’ is a film set in a small town with Anupam Kher and Jimmy Shergill spearheading the cast.
