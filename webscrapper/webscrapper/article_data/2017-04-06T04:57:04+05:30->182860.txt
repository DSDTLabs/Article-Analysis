NEW DELHI: Two men were crushed after a wall bordering a   collapsed on them in outer Delhi's   area on Wednesday. Police said that the men were yet to be identified and a probe has been initiated to determine the cause of the accident. The owner of the plot, beside which the wall was constructed, has been detained for questioning. The incident occurred around 9pm when the men had gone to attend nature's call. Tuesday's rain had made the wall weak due to which it came crashing down. Police suspect the victims to be labourers from a camp nearby.

 Locals managed to pull them out and rushed them to a hospital where they were declared dead on arrival. A case of negligence has been registered against the owner of the plot.
