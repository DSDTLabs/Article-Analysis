The play ‘Varhad Nighalay Londonla’ a classic   is to be staged in the city tomorrow. This one of its kind play will feature   single-handedly portraying 52 characters. It tells the story of a village family accepting a British woman as their daughter-in-law. 

  This is a comedy that includes 52 characters all played by the actor alone and all of them inspired from the   culture, speech and lifestyle. 
 
  The production is a comedy about a marriage party from India going to London for the wedding of one of their sons.
 
  When: July 9, 1 pm
 
  Where: Balgandharv Rangmandir, JM Road
