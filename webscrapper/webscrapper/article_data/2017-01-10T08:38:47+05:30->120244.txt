KOCHI: A 21-year-old nursing graduate was found dead at her bedroom in  , Thrikkakara, on Monday. 

 Ashwathy Prathapan, who was planning to join a post-graduate course in Pune, was last seen by her family around 10.30pm on Sunday, said the police. 
 
 The woman, who completed general nursing from Kalamassery Government Medical College, was found hanging from the top of a window rail in her room. The police said that the woman was found dead when her sister's knocks on her door went unanswered. 
 
  The family has not raised any suspicion as of yet. An FIR of   has been filed at Thrikkakara police station.
