
After working his way to superstardom in the Kollywood film industry, music director Yuvan Shankar Raja is all set to stretch his skills and work his magic in other film industries as well. 
 
The ace music composer took to Twitter to announce that he will be foraying in the Kannada film industry by scoring the film ‘Gowdru Hotel’ which stars debut actor Rachan Chandra. The renowned musician made the happy announcement saying, "Hi guys Happy to announce my first Kannada movie with Rachan Chandra debut actor #gowdruhotel happy times." 
 
This is indeed a big achievement for Yuvan, as he is one of the few music composers from Tamil film industry, other than AR Rahman, who is spreading his influence across different film industries.
