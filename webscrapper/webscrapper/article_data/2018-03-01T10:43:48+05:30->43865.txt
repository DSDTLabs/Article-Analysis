RAIPUR: A blessing ceremony was held at the auditorium of the   for Class XII students. 

 Manju Shandilya, chairperson of the   Educational Academy, was the chief guest for the event. At the ceremony, teachers spoke motivational speeches and songs to bless the students for their bright future.

The video, compiled by principal Rajgor, tugged at the heart strings of all present. TNN
