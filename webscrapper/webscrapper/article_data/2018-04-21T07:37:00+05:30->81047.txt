Kurukshetra: Madan Mohan Chhabra took charge as the secretary of the Kurukshetra Development Board (KDB) on Friday. He paid floral tributes at the statue and memorial of former Prime Minister Gulzari Lal Nanda before taking over the post.

 Haryana chief minister Manohar Lal Khattar has appointed Chabbra on the specially-created post for two years. Chabbra is an entrepreneur and the state coordinator of Hindu Helpline, a service launched by Vishwa Hindu Parishad, providing a platform to Hindus who have been harassed or victimized. He is also an affiliate of Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS).
 
 KBD is a high-profile board with the governor and the CM as its ex-officio chair and vice-chair respectively. Several other cabinet ministers and bureaucrats are also members of the management board.
