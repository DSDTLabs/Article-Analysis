 Kulwant gave Rs 50 lakh as “token money”. However, on the day the sale deed had to be signed, neither Kulwant nor the other accused turned up to make the payment. After this, Ravi filed a complaint at the Sector 3 police station. The police then carried out a detailed investigation and arrested Kulwant on April 2.
