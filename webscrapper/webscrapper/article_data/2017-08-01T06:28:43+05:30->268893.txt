JAISALMER: An   ferrying schoolchildren on   under   area collided with another vehicle killing the SUV driver on the spot and seriously injuring 24 people including 12 children.

 “Around 18-20 students of Rajgarh village of Jaisalmer were being ferried to Jagdamba School by the owner of the school, Mahendra Singh in his SUV. The SUV collided with another vehicle coming from Sabalpura Fanta. Mahendra (35) died on the spot while twelve children and six villagers were injured,” SP Guarav Yadav said.
