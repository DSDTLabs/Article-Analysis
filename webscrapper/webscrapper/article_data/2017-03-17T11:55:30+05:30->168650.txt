HYDERABAD: Holding that a state is within its rights to promote trade, commerce and employment on its soil, the   upheld a notification issued by the erstwhile AP government levying two categories of entertainment tax, one for the films made in AP and another for films made outside. The AP's 2008 notification imposed 10% tax on films made in the state and 24% on those made outside.
