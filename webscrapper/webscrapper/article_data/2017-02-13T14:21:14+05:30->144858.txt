MARGAO: Suspended Majorda-Utorda-Calata village panchayat secretary   has been dismissed from service by the director of panchayats  , for being “guilty of misconduct”.

 In his order issued on January 24, 2017, Pilarnekar stated, “Within the powers vested under Rule 14 of the   ( ), Classification Control and Appeals (CCA) Rules, 1965, I hold Siddesh Phaldessai, village panchayat secretary, as guilty of misconduct and thereby award him the penalty of dismissal from service. The period of suspension from December 14, 2016, till the conclusion of this order shall be treated as leave without pay”.
