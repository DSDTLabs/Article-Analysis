GHAZIABAD: Three men robbed a petrol pump of nearly Rs 1.42 lakh cash on Wednesday. 

 Vikas Kumar, owner of Krishna Murari Filling station, said: “On Wednesday afternoon, our salesmen Sanjay, Babloo and Bunty were working at the petrol pump when three men came on a bike. One of them took out a gun and slapped my men even though there were customers there. They snatched a bag containing Rs1.42 lakh cash at gunpoint and fled.”
 
 Though the entire crime was captured on CCTV, the police have not been able to make any headway in the case. 
 
 “Their faces were covered so it is difficult to identify them, ” said Om Prakash Choudhary, SHO, Loni police station.
