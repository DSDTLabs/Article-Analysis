PATNA: State BJP president Nityanand Rai flagged off a two-day sports car rally from gate number 2 of the Sanjay Gandhi Biological Park on Friday. 

 “The event, christened ‘Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya Car Rally’ saw the participation of 30 teams that covered a distance of 800km between Patna (Bihar) and Ranchi (Jharkhand) and back to Patna in two days. It was organized by voluntary group Buddha Organisation of Women’s Awareness and Rural Development to spread the message of unity,” rally coordinator Amrita Bhushan said, adding several women took part in the rally.
