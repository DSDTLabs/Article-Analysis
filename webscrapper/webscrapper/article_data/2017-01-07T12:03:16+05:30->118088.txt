  Bledel joins star Elisabeth Moss in the 10-episode series, along with Joseph Fiennes, Samira Wiley and Yvonne Strahovski, reported Variety.
  
  
 
  
  The streaming series is set to debut on April 26, 2017.
  
  Based on the novel by Margaret Atwood, "The Handmaid's Tale" follows Offred, a handmaid played by Moss, who is forced into subjugation in an attempt to repopulate the fictional, male-dominated society of Gilead.
  
  Bledel will play Ofglen, Offred's (Elisabeth Moss) fellow handmaid and companion.
