Lucknow: Senior   leader   Chaudhary died after a major heart attack he suffered during a meeting at the party headquarters here on Saturday. He was rushed to the Civil Hospital immediately after he complained of breathlessness. He died at the hospital. Later,   national president Akhilesh Yadav visited the hospital and the body was brought to the party office where senior leaders paid floral tributes.

 Condoling the death of Umashankar, SP founder Mulayam Singh Yadav described him as a true socialist who dedicated his life to the party. Senior party leaders, including Rajya Sabha MPs Ram Gopal Yadav and Naresh Agarwal and Badaun MP Dharmendra Yadav attended the condolence meeting, in which everyone observed two-minute silence. Senior SP leader and party MLA Shivpal Yadav, who was not present at the meeting, later issued a press statement condoling the death of Umashankar.
 
 Chaudhary, who was with the SP since his student politics days and was appointed organisational in charge of the party for different parts of the state, played an important role in the recently concluded membership drive of the party which was being reviewed at the party headquarters when he took ill.
