 MUMBAI: A eight-member team of of IT department Mumbai arrived at the   headquarters on Tuesday morning to conduct a 24-hour-long inspection of the   deduction at source ( ) made by all the civic departments. 

 It was done to ensure that TDS has been correctly deducted and the amount deposited with the government account as well. NMMC commissioner,   confirmed the visit but said it was a routine survey. “All required documents were provided to the team. 
 
 There was no irregularities or discrepancies found,” said the civic chief. As per sources the reason for the sudden inspection has to do with low TDS collection reported last year compared to 2015-16. 
 
 “The expenditure in 2016-17 was just half of 2015-16 which impacts the TDS as well,” said an official on condition of anonymity.
