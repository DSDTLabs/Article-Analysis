Visakhapatnam: A two-member expert team from ISO toured Andhra University (AU) to assess its progress. Based on their audit report, the university will be granted the ISO-9001-2008 certificate. 

 The certification will boost the academic appeal and stature of the university and help it to acquire more international students and projects in collaboration with various international agencies and institutions.
