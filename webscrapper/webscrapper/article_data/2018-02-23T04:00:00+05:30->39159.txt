Rajkot: A private luxury bus crashed into a tractor trolley and then hit a group of devotees going on foot near Lakhabaval village of Jamnagar district early on Thursday. 

 One person identified as Kaku Bharwad, who was sitting in the tractor trolley, died on the spot after it skidded of the road and overturned. At least 15 others including women suffered head injuries as the bus lost control and hit them. All were shifted to GG Hospital in Jamnagar. They were from Bharwad community and natives of Koth village near Dholka and were on way to temple town Dwarka for Holi festival. tnn
