SURAT: A short film   will be organized by the  ,   and Film Society of Surat to highlight urbanization issues in the city and their solutions. 

 The contest began on March 6 and will continue until May 5. It is open to class XII, graduate and postgraduate students as well as others. Contestants can take up issues related to traffic, cleanliness, environment conservation, water conservation, renewable energy, social harmony and housing.
