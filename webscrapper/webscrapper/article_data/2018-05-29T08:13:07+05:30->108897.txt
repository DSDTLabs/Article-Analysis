CHANDIGARH: Chandigarh police claimed to have solved multiple   and burglary cases in the city with the arrest of three youths on Monday.

 Cops also seized stolen vehicles from the trio, identified as Sanju, 18, a resident of Old Panchkula; Vicky, 19, a resident of Manimajra; and Joginder alias Titu, 19, a resident of Vikas Nagar, Mouli Jagran.
 
 Cops said following a car theft case registered at Manimajra police station on May 18, a special team was formed to nab the criminals. They got a tip-off about the accused following which they nabbed them from near RIMT School, Manimajra.
 
 During the course of investigation, the accused disclosed about their other associates and confessed about 11 vehicle theft cases registered in various police stations in Chandigarh and  . The accused Sanju is booked in seven different vehicle theft cases in Sector 5 police station, Panchkula.
 
 The accused person also disclosed that they used to park stolen vehicles in the free parking lots in Chandigarh. Cops managed to recover stolen vehicles and other items worth Rs 5 lakh.
 
 Inspector Ranjit Singh, station house officer, Manimajra police station, said, “A total of 11 cases of Chandigarh and Panchkula have been solved with the arrest of these teenagers. Further investigation in the case is underway.”
