Visakhapatnam: To clear rush of passengers in the ensuing holidays and summer vacation, East Coast Railway has decided to extend special train services.

 Train 08573 Visakhapatnam- Tirupati Weekly Special Express will leave Visakhapatnam on Mondays at 10.55 pm from March 6 to June 26 and reach Tirupati the next day at 1.25 pm.
 
 In the return direction, train 08574 Tirupati-Visakhapatnam Weekly Special Express will leave Tirupati on Tuesdays at 3.30 pm from March 7 to June 27 and reach Visakhapatnam the next day morning at 6.50 am.
 
 The pair of train will have stoppages at Duvvada, Anakapalle, Samalkot, Rajahmundry, Eluru, Vijayawada, New Guntur, Tenali, Ongole, Nellore, Gudur, Sri Kalahasti and Renigunta between Visakhapatnam and Tirupati.
