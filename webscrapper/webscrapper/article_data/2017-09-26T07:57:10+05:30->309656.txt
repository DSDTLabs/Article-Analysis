BENGALURU: Justice  , a senior judge of the Karnataka high court, resigned on Monday, even as the court is on vacation. 

  Sources confirmed that Patel sent his resignation to   chief justice  , but did not specify the reason for it. Justice Patel is the puisne judge (senior among all judges barring the CJ) of the HC. 
 
  Justice Patel entered the Bar in August 1979 and started practice at the district court, Rajkot and was shifted to Gujarat HC in 1985. He was elevated as additional judge, Gujarat HC in December 2001 and confirmed as a permanent judge in August 2004. In August 2015, he was appointed as the acting   of Gujarat HC before being sworn in as a judge of the Karnataka HC in February 13, 2016.
