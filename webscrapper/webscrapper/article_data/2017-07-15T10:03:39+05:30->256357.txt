LUCKNOW: Experts at the Mahanagar-based   tested the explosive found in the Assembly building on Friday. 

 Director Shyam Bihari Upadhyay along with Arun Kumar Sharma, Narendra Kumar and Manoj Kumar tested the substance using a kit installed in the mobile explosive vans procured by UP Police. 
 
 They said the substance was PETN. It was insoluble in water and tested positive with nitrate regnant as well, said the report. National Investigation Agency is likely to send the substance for further examination once it takes over the case.
