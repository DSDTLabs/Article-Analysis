MUMBAI: The death   in the   commercial   blaze of May 27 touched five on Tuesday. 

 Fire brigade officials found the dead body of one of the labourers on the seventh floor of the 11-storey Techniplex Complex on S V Road, inside the closed electric panel, around 9am. 
 
 The fire is known to have erupted from the seventh floor and eventually spread across the high rise. “In an attempt to save him from the flames, the person tried to hide inside the electric panel,” said a fire official. 
 
 The search operation was called off on Tuesday.
