Soumya ( ) confronts Kareena and asks her about the posters. She tells her how she is unable to decipher the reason behind her actions. 
 
 It is then that Kareena tells her that her mother died because of her refusal to help her. She does not believe in Soumya's explanation and makes it clear to her that she won't stop until she brings her back to the kinnar community. 
  
 Soumya is disheartened when she learns about Varun's lie which led to the demise of Kareena's mother. When she walks out of her room, Harman ( ) sees Soumya teary-eyed and asks her what Kareena had to say on the matter.
 
  
  
 To not cause further trouble in the family, Soumya tells him that Kareena wants her to come back to the kinnar community. Harman tells her that he would like to speak to Kareena but Soumya stops him.
  
 Back home, Soumya confronts Varun about the same, but Preeto ( ) stops her from doing so.
