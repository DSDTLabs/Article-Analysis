RANCHI: Power conglomerate   on Tuesday conducted a public meeting with prospective land donors for the proposed 1,600 MW thermal power plant in  .

 Over 500 raiyyats of Patwa, Gangta and Motia village of Motia panchayat took part in the meeting held in the primary school campus in Gangta-Goindpur village. The public meeting was held under the supervision of Godda SDO Saurabh Sinha, representatives of Adani Group and heads of the villages. 
 
 “The raiyyats took part in the meeting and placed their concerns and questions before the present senior officials. The officials allayed their doubts and explained the benefits of the power plant to the local people,” the company said in a statement.
