HYDERABAD:   will be organizing centenary state-level competitions in   songs, dance, and elocution among other competitions in the month of February. Students from constituent colleges are eligible to participate.

 "This is an excellent opportunity. Colleges are requested to send the scanned copy of complete list of participants latest by February 7," read the circular.
 
 "For Telangana Song and dance, only two minutes will be given for time for stage or instrument setting, and film songs should not be presented in the competition. While a college can send three participants in the in song category, for Telangana dance only one team of three per college or institution will be allowed," said P  , dean, student affairs and cultural coordinator,  .
 
 The state-level competitions will also be organized in Quiz, painting, essay writing on the said dates.
