 Attributing the climatic change to the active upper air cyclonic circulation the Jaipur Met office said that similar conditions will prevail in the state for the next couple of days. Many places are likely to witness mild showers and dust storm in the next 48 hours. 
 
 Jaipurites woke up to a comparatively pleasant morning as a minimum temperature 24.4 degrees Celsius was recorded in the city. It was followed by a sultry afternoon as a maximum temperature of 37.2 degree Celsius accompanied with a humidity of 45 percent was recorded. In the afternoon heavy dust storm lashed various areas on the city outskirts.
