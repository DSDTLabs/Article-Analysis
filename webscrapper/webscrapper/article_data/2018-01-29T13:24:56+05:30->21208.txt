THIRUVANANTHAPURAM: The University of Kerala has postponed the BA Honours and BSC Hotel Management examinations. These exams were earlier scheduled to be held at the Government Women's College in Thiruvananthapuram on January 29 and 31.

 As per the revised schedule, the first semester BA Honours in English language and literature examinations (BAHE 111 British Literature 1-1350-1600), BAHE 122 (British Literature 2-1601-1649) would be held on February 9 and 12, respectively.
 
 Similarly, the semester based BSc Hotel Management and Catering Science   scheduled to be held on January 31 would now be conducted on February 2. 
 
 The University has made no changes in the schedule of other examinations.
