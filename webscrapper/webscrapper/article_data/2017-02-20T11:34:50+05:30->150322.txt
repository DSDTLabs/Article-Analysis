With a slew of people coming out in support of the prominent actress in Malayalam film industry, who was assaulted recently, and whose brave action has bolstered the spirits of one and all. 

 Actor-director Vineeth Sreenivasan has recently voiced his feelings about the same, and also discussed about what is of dire importance at this particular juncture. 
 He recently posted on his social networking page, that there is a need for a generation, whose mind is not clouded with the thought that ‘a woman is just a woman’, just by seeing a father talk to a mother in jest.
 
 He also said that we all have the social responsibility to mould such a generation
