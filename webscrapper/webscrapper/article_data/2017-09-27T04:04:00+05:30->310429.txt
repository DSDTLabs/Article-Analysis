Ahmedabad: At the 60th annual general meeting of Ahmedabad Management Association (AMA) held in Ahmedabad on Saturday, names of the elected president and vice-president were declared. Rajiv Mehta, the chief executive officer of CEL Packaging, was elected as the president of the institute while 

 
 
 Dr Malay Mahadevia, whole-time director, Adani Ports and SEZ Ltd, was elected as the vice-president of AMA. Names of five office-bearers, who were also elected on the same day, were also declared. The office-bearers are Sawan Godiawala, Rajiv Gandhi, S B Dangayach, Reena Bhagwati and D J Radia.
