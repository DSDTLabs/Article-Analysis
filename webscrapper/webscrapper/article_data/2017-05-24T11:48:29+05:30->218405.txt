SHIMLA: High hills of Kullu, Lahaul-Spiti, Kinnaur, Chamba and   have continued to receive fresh snow, lowering temperature in the lower areas. The peaks here are covered with fresh white snow. Both day and night temperatures across the state have decreased by 1 to 2 degrees Celsius in last 24 hours. Snowfall has affected the   clearance work on Manali-Leh highway. The highway was expected to open before May 25 but now it may take one more week. Night temperature of Manali, Kinnaur and Lahaul-Spiti is hovering between 7 and 8 degrees Celsius while maximum day temperature is between 19 and 21 degrees Celsius. Residents here are still wearing sweaters and jackets.
