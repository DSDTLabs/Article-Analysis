It was a special show for which special people made an appearance. 

 A prominent mall in Kochi laid out the red carpet for many big names of Mollywood recently, when a special screening of a recent   film was held there. 
 
 Actress Vineetha Koshy, who won a State Award recently for her performance in Ottamuri Velicham, Vinay Forrt, director  , child artiste Parvathi, Sudeep Karat and Pramod Mohan were among the guests at the occasion.
