 He was identified as M Senthil, a native of Arakkonam. “He was working as a cook and used to sleep on a pavement on Mettupalayam Road at  . The victim, who was about 35 years old, also used to sleep on the same place. On Saturday morning, Senthil demanded money to consume liquor from the ragpicker, who refused to do so. Following a heated argument, Senthil hit the ragpicker with a boulder on his head. The man, who suffered severe head injuries, was admitted at the Coimbatore Medical College and Hospital (CMCH), where he died on early Sunday morning,” a police officer said.
 
 RS Puram police arrested Senthil under Section 307 (attempt to murder) and 506 (ii) (criminal intimidation) of the Indian Penal code. He was lodged at the Coimbatore Central Prison.
 
 Police have formed a special team to identify the deceased.
