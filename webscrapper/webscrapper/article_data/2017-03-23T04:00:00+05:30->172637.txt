Ahmedabad: As the senate of Gujarat University is set to meet on March 30, NSUI activists have declared that they will gherao the varsity and protest against the cancellation of elections to the student senate and welfare board. Amrita Dhawan, national president of NSUI was in Ahmedabad on Wednesday and met in-charge vice chancellor Himanshu Pandya.

 Dhawan questioned the cancellation of elections for the student senate elections and welfare board and threatened that NSUI members will take to the streets to protest this decision.
