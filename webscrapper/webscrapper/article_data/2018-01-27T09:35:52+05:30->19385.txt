 The Lok Sabha MP was taking to about the Nationalist Congress Party (NCP) chief,  , announcing yesterday that a meeting of "like-minded parties" on January 29 would take place to make a plan to take on Bharatiya Janata Party.
 
 "We are already untouchables for Bharatiya Janata Party ( ). Why will these elite parties invite us? They do not need help from poor people like us," AIMIM chief Asaduddin Owaisi told ANI.
 
 NCP chief Sharad Pawar announced that the parties with like mind will hold a meeting in Delhi on January 29 to discuss ways to counter BJP.
