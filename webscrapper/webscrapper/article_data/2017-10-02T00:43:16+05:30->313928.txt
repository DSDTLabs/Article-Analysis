Trichy: Burglars decamped with 15 sovereigns of gold and cash worth Rs 5 lakh after they broke open three locked houses located within the premise of Heavy Alloy Penetrator Project (HAPP) staff quarters during the wee hours on Sunday. Police have registered a case and are investigating.

 According to police, staff of HAPP, Subramanian, Sridhar and Mehendran, residing in their quarters located near Thiruverumbur in Trichy had gone to their relativeâ€™s house for puja holidays.
 
 When they returned here on Sunday morning, they were shocked to see that the front doors of their houses were broken. When they checked for their belongings, they came to know that some gold ornaments and cash were stolen from the almirahs in their bedrooms.
 
 Subsequently, they informed Navalpattu police. On the basis of a complaint lodged by the three staff separately, police rushed to the spot and conducted an inquiry with the housemates and neighbours at the township.
