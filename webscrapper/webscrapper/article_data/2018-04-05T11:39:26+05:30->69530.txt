Few days after meeting Former CM  , Kichcha Sudeep has met Chief Minister   at his residence today morning, to invite him for the inauguration of Kannada Chalanachitra Cup( ) cricket tournament on Saturday.
 
  It has been reported that Siddaramaiah invited Sudeep to campaign for congress party in the upcoming state assembly elections. Even Former Chief Minister H D Kumaraswamy had requested Sudeep to campaign for JD(s) party candidates. It would be interesting to see now for which party Sudeep decides to campaign.
