Rajkot: Morbi collector RJ Makadia has issued an order to provide nearly 1.25 bigha land for Dalits to be used as a crematorium. On Thursday, Dalits from Khanpur village in the district came with body of man who died on Wednesday to the collector office complaining that they had no land for cremation. Collector R J Makadia said, “I have talked with the leaders of both communities and the issue is settled.” tnn
