CHANDIGARH:Chandigarh registering and licensing authority (RLA) has received moderate response for the e-auction of registration numbers 0001 to 9999 of series CH01-BR as only 160 residents have registered for the auction, so far.

 The three-day auction will start from Tuesday and conclude on Thursday. In last few years, there has been drop in the craze for the VIP registration number with a large number of numbers going unsold in the auctions.
 
 As per the laid down norms, one person can bid for a maximum of five   of choice per application, but will be allocated only one registration mark of choice. Those interested in obtaining more than one registration number of choice for more than one vehicle will have to   separately for each vehicle and obtain unique acknowledgement numbers (UAN).
 
 The successful bidders are required to get their vehicles registered as well as to deposit the balance bidding amount within one month from the date of auction, failing which penalty at the rate of 10% per annum as well as interest at normal rates of 10% per annum on the balance amount would be charged till the date of payment. The owner of the vehicle, who has purchased the vehicle at Chandigarh address, will only be allowed to participate in e-auction. Sale letter, that is, form number 21, Aadhaar card and address proof of Chandigarh are mandatory to participate in the e-auction.
