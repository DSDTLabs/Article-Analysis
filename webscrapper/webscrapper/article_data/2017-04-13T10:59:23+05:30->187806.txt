PUNE: The city crime branch arrested a 20-year-old youth for allegedly stealing gold jewellery and purses of women passengers in Pune Mahanagar Parivahan Mahamandal Ltd (PMPML) buses. 

 Police have recovered gold bangles and cash worth over Rs1.50 lakh from him.
 
 The suspect was identified as Avinash Siddharam Jadhav (20), a resident of Keshavnagar in Mundhwa.
 
 Acting on a tip-off, a team led by senior police inspector   arrested Jadhav from the   area where he had come to sell the stolen ornaments.
 
 Last month, a couple from Satara was travelling in a PMPML bus between Swargate and Shivajinagar when unidentified persons stole cash and jewellery totally worth Rs2.50 lakh from them. The couple had claimed that there were gold bangles in the stolen purse. 
 
 Bhosale-Patil said they recovered gold bangles and cash totally worth Rs1.52 lakh from Jadhav. The suspect has been executing similar crimes in crowded buses for the past six years. He is involved over 40 cases of thefts in running buses, he said.
