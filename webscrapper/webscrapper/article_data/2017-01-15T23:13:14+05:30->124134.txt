AGRA:  , a 73-year-old ascetic from Mathura, is well-known in the city of temples, mainly because he has been contesting elections for years. Following a prophecy by his guru,  , that he would win the 20th election he contested, the baba is now preparing to file his nomination papers for the 16th time.

 Talking to TOI, he said, “All politicians are liars. Only ascetics like me can ensure honesty in public life and development”. He said he has full faith on his guru and he will surely become MLA or MP.
 
 He said, he had contested his first election in 1977 and got 8000 votes and since then the journey for the prophecy to come true continues. Interestingly, in every election the Baba is the first to file his nomination. He had contested Lok sabha elections eight times and assembly elections seven times from Mathura.
 
 "Elections expenses are getting costlier", said Baba adding that he had collected Rs 12000 after selling his scooty and utensils. "Of these, Rs 2000 would be for my election expenses, while Rs 10,000 has gone in as a deposit", he said.
 
 He said he knows this for a fact that he would not win this election, as he would be successful only in his 20th attempt.
 
 As for his profession, Fakkad Baba has recited Ramayan in 13000 homes in Mathura. He hails from Kanpur and came to Mathura when he was 11 years old. Since then he has made Mathura his home and ' .
 
 "I'm committed to work for the people of this holy town and would one day serve them as their elected representative", he says adding that unlike politicians who forget voters after winning polls, he would ensure development of the city and its residents.
 
 He said he has no family of his own and the people of this town are his family. "I'll give all my time to this town and it's people", he says.
