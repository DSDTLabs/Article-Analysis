NAGPUR: The BJP is likely to release its manifesto for the civic elections on Friday. Chief minister   and Union minister   are likely to be present.

 Meanwhile, BJP state president   will kick-off election campaign for the party on Friday. Danve is arriving for the first time in the city after the election dates were announced.
 
 He will first campaign for zilla parishad and panchayat samiti elections at Chandrapur, Gadchiroli and Wardha. In the evening, he will campaign in wards in East Nagpur.
