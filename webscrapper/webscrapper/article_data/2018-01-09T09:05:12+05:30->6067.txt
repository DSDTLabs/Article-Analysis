MUMBAI: Chaos prevailed in Mumbai's Reay Road area when a fire broke out at a metal workshop in Abhilasha Nagar dockyard shortly after midnight on Tuesday. 

 The dockyard falls in a densly-populated slum area near Nariyalwadi Kabristan. 
 
 According to reports, five fire engines and water tankers have been pressed into service to douse the flames. The fire has been doused, said reports.
 
 The incident is the latest in a series of fire accidents in the city.
 
 On December 29, a blaze in Kamala Mills compound claimed 14 lives while on Sunday a fire gutted the Cinevista Studios leaving one person dead.
