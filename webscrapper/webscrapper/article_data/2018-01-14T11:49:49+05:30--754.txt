BUXAR/PATNA: Police arrested 17 people till Saturday evening in connection with the attack on chief minister (CM)  's convoy at Nandan village under the   police station area in   district during his Vikas Samiksha Yatra on Friday. 

  The CM's convoy was pelted with stones, but he was taken out of the village safely. While eight police personnel, including Dumraon police station SHO  , received minor injuries, at least eight vehicles in the CM convoy were damaged in the attack. 
 
 Patna zonal IG   said 12 FIRs were in process of getting lodged against people who were involved in the attack or hatching the conspiracy. TNN
