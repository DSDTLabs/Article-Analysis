BARWANI: Two six-year-old boys,   outside their house, were killed and their grandfather sustained severe injuries when a rashly driven mini truck hit them on Khadkifata road under Rajpur police station area on Wednesday.

 SDO (P) Rajpur PS Baghel told TOI that around 9.30 am a speeding mini truck hit the twins and their grandfather. One of the kids, Samar Kushwaha died on the spot while his brother Saurabh succumbed to injuries on the way to district hospital. Their grandfather Purushottam Kushwaha, 60, was undergoing treatment at district hospital.
 
 Baghel said that the truck has been seized and a case was registered against the absconding driver.
