PONDA: Aimed at hotels,   introduced a new thick toned   variant called Cold Gold on Monday. A litre of this variant makes about 17 cups of tea instead of the usual 12, the dairy claims.

 
 Dairy chairman   revealed that they have procured a homogenizer machine for this variant and the machine increases the thickness of the milk. “We have launched the product after receiving complaints that the toned milk of the dairy was too diluted,” he said.
