MALAPPURAM:   police on Wednesday arrested a teenager for threatening to kill Prime Minister  , through a WhatsApp post. 

 18-year-old Shahul Hameed from   in Kozhikode district was arrested by a police team from Pothukal station. Police said Hameed circulated a post which said the Prime Minister should be killed. The picture and text were posted in the WhatsApp group, 'Voice of Youth'. The group was also involved in spreading the message supporting the controversial hartal on April 16.
 
 After police started nabbing people in connection with the violence related to the hartal, Hameed left the group and deleted the post. However, the cyber cell initiated a detailed probe and the police seized his phone and arrested him.
