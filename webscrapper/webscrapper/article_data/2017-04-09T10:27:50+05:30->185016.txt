PATNA:  , over 500 people visited the Silk and Cotton Fab of   at   on Saturday to buy dress material,   in the range of Rs 500 to Rs 2 lakh. Most of the saris which attracted the buyers were in Rs 1,000-1,500 range. There were some who bought a sari as costly as Rs 50,000 to Rs 60,000. The exhibition, which started on Friday, will conclude on Wednesday. 

 Men preferred linen dress material while women concentrated on daily office wear items. College girls were seen inquiring about and buying cotton dress material to beat the summer heat and look fashionable on the campus as well. 
 
 Organisers said over 100 artists from 20 states were participating in the six-day fair. There are 70 stall displaying products from states like Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, West Bengal, Assam, Jammu and Kashmir, Delhi, Rajasthan, Gujarat, Maharashtra, Chhattisgarh, Jharkhand and Punjab. 
 
 As marriage season is approaching, many visitors were also buying dress material and saris to suit the ceremonies.
