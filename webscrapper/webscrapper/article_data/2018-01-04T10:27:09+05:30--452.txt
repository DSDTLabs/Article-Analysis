NEW DELHI: The Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board (GSHSEB) has released the schedule for board examinations of Class 10 and Class 12 to be held for the academic session 2017-18. 

 The Class 10 or SSC exams 2018 will begin from March 12 and will end on March 23. Similarly, Class 12 or HSC exams 2018 for General Stream will be held from March 17 to March 28 while for the Science stream, the exam will start on March 12 and will end on March 28, 2018.
 
 As per the date sheet, this year the Board will conduct all examinations in a single shift of three hours from 10am to 13:20pm for Class 10. Whereas for Class 12, the exams will be held in evening session from 3pm to 06:15pm. 
 
 Class 10 (SSC) and Class 12 (HSC) students are advised to start their revisions to take the Gujarat state board exam which will be held in next two months. The students can check the date sheet for examination on the official website of the board - www.gseb.org
