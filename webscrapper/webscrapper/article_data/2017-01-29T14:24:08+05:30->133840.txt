PANAJI: Two Goa-based officers of the   were awarded medals at the 68th Republic Day anniversary in New Delhi for their service. One of two officers has roots to Goa. 

 Naval officials said that president of India Pranab Mukherjee awarded the Ati Vishisht Seva Medal to commandant of the naval war college, rear admiral Monty Khanna, for distinguished service of an exceptional order.
 
 The Vishisht Seva Medal was awarded to INS Mandovi’s commanding officer and   station commander, captain Sanjay D’Cunha, for distinguished service and devotion to duty of a high order. Captain D'Cunha hails from Curtorim in Salcete. tnn
