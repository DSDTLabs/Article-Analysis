PANAJI: With the final dates for submitting nominations for the upcoming panchayat elections nearing, the state election commission has observed a multifold increase in the number of applications. Records state that 1,477 nominations were received on Monday, which took the total tally to 2,059. In all, 840 nominations were received in North Goa, while 637 were received in South Goa. 

 Maximum number of nominations on Monday came for panchayats in   taluka, with 334 nominations being registered. The lowest numbers were reported from   where 41 people submitted their applications. 
 
 Talking about the law and order situation in the state, state election commissioner   said the situation remains calm. tnn
