NEW DELHI: A 26-year-old man was allegedly beaten up and sodomised by four men inside a park in southeast Delhi's   on Monday. The man told the police that one of the assaulters was an acquaintance.

 The victim told the police that while he was walking past  , a man whom he knew for the past few months stopped him saying he wanted to discuss something important. There were three other men standing with him.
 
 The men then pulled him to a corner and hit his head with a brick. They thrashed him until he collapsed. The victim was then dragged to a corner and sodomised. The rapists then fled leaving him bleeding. He then walked up to the road and made a PCR call. Police rushed the man to AIIMS Trauma Center where he was treated for his injuries.
 
 A case under   sections 377 (unnatural offences), 323 (punishment for voluntarily causing hurt) and 34 (acts done by several persons in furtherance of common intention) has been registered. “The accused men have been identified and will be arrested soon,“ said DCP (southeast) Romil Baaniya.
