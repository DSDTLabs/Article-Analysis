NEW DELHI: Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU) Hyderabad has released the Telangana State Engineering Agriculture and Management Common Entrance Test 2018 examination answer keys on its website. 

 The students who have appeared for   can visit the official website at eamcet.tsche.ac.in to view the answer keys.
 
 The   2018 for agriculture related courses was conducted on May 2 and May 3. TS EAMCET for engineering admission was held from May 4 till May 7.
 
 The Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE) conducts the EAMCET examination every year for admission to undergraduate engineering, pharmacy and agriculture courses in the universities in Telangana. 
 
 
 
 
 
 1. Open the TS EAMCET official website- eamcet.tsche.ac.in and login
 
 2. On the homepage, you will find the 'Question Papers & Preliminary Keys' link, click on it
 
 3. It will redirect to a new page, select the answer key you want to check - Question and keys for Agriculture and Medical (AM) exam, Engineering (E) 
 
 4. You can check and tally your answers
