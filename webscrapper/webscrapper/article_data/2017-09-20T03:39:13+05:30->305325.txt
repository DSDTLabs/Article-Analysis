NEW DELHI: A speeding   mowed down three motorcycle riders, killing one at   on Sunday morning. The truck driver, along with his helper, fled from the spot leaving the truck behind.

 DCP (southeast) Romil Baaniya said that one victim, Sujeet Kumar, died on the spot while the other two, Upendra Pandit and Nagender, are undergoing treatment. A case has been registered.
 
 Police said that the incident was reported around 5am. “Near pillar number 259 of the Metro line on the Sarita Vihar flyover, a truck coming from the Badarpur side hit the motorcycle from behind. Nagender, who was driving the motorcycle, fell off and hit the divider, while Pandit and Kumar got crushed beneath the wheels of the truck. The truck dragged both of them for a few meters before stopping,” a police official said.
 
 A PCR van passing by the area saw the people lying injured and they were taken to Apollo Hospital where Kumar was declared brought dead.
 
 In another incident, a three-year-old girl is battling for her life after she was hit by a car in Rohini on Monday.
