GURUGRAM: A 22-year-old man was booked for sedating and raping an 18-year-old girl on Valentine’s Day. 

 According to the police, on Wednesday the survivor, a Class XII student, along with her parents approached the Sadar police station and filed a   case against one Vishal, who works with a courier company. “Four months ago I met the accused through a friend in a temple and exchanged our numbers. On February 14, Vishal called me to meet him for a movie, following which we went for lunch. I started feeling uneasy and lost my consciousness after drinking the cold-drink he offered me. Then he took me to a hotel and raped me. Later, he dropped me near my house and threatened me that if I revealed anything, he would kill me,” the survivor stated in her complaint. 
 
 According to the police, the girl revealed about the crime to her mother on Tuesday, following which they approached the cops.
