ROURKELA: Three persons - a woman, her grandson and her granddaughter - were killed after the stones being carried by a truck fell on them when the vehicle hit a tractor. 

 The three had been walking down a road in Kalodihi under Kansbahal police outpost area of   district on Saturday afternoon when the freak accident occurred. At around 3.30 pm, the stone-laden truck was moving from the quarry to a village road when it hit a tractor coming from the opposite direction, and overturned. The stone blocks killed them instantly.
 
 Hundreds of locals reached the spot and staged a blockade there, detaining the bodies and seeking compensation for the victims' family. The blockade was on till the time this report was filed.
