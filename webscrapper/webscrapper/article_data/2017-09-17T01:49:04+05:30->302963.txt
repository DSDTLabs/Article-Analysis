GURUGRAM: Police on Saturday nabbed one of the accused in the   of 32-year-old Savita Dagar, who was shot dead near a private school on Naharpur Rupa road in Rajiv Colony on Friday afternoon while she was on her way to pick up her son and daughter.

 The victim, Savita, was sister-in-law of  , a close aide of gangster Kaushal.
 
  According to police, the arrested accused has been identified as 55-year-old Jogender Hans, father of main accused Johnny Hans. 
 
 Four special teams, including Sadar police station and crime units of Bilaspur, sectors 31 and 39 are conducting raids at possible hideouts to nab the other accused. Meanwhile, police have got CCTV footage from the nearby area in which three men are seen on a motorcycle, including Johnny who is riding pillion.
 
 “Johnny’s father had asked his son to avenge the murder of his wife and Johnny’s mother. Amit Dagar, close aide of gangster Kaushal, had murdered Johnny’s mother earlier this year. We are questioning the accused and will be arresting the other accused soon,” said inspector Vijay Yadav, SHO, Sadar police station.
 
 Savita’s mother-in-law, Anju Dagar registered a complaint with the police and named jailed gangster Binder Gujjar’s brother, Manoj, as the prime suspect. 
 
 An FIR has been registered against Johnny Hans, Jogender Hans, Surdarshan, Manoj and one unidentified person under sections 148 (rioting armed with deadly weapon), 149 (unlawful assembly), 302 (murder) of IPC and sections 25, 54 and 59 of Arms Act at Sadar police station.
