Pune: The   police on Tuesday booked a security supervisor of a Pune Municipal Corporation-run hospital for allegedly   a female security guard for the past two years.

 She did not approach the police earlier since the suspect had threatened to remove her from the job, said an officer of the Bund Garden police. The officer said frustrated over the prolonged harassment, the woman on Tuesday approached the police and lodged the complaint. “We have booked the suspect under Section 354 (D) (stalking) of the Indian Penal Code,” the officer said.
 
 He said the suspect followed the security guard till her house and molested her on the road. “He used to demand sexual favours from her,” the officer said. “We are yet to arrest the suspect,” the officer said.
 
 Case against youth
 
 The Pune rural police booked a 19-year-old youth for sexually assaulting a student (17) inside a car. The suspect has been booked under sections 354 (A) and 354 (D) of IPC and the relevant sections of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act. The student and the suspect knew each other for the past two years.
