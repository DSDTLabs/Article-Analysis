MUMBAI: The state government has set up a committee to carry out an impact analysis if it decides to increase the size of free tenements under Slum Rehabilitation Scheme from 29 sq metres (269 sq ft) to 30 sq metres (323 sq ft). 

 The committee, comprising chief executive officer, SRA, additional municipal commissioner,  , additional metropolitan commissioner,   and deputy director (planning)   as member secretary will ascertain its impact on ongoing, sanctioned and future SRA schemes. 
 
 The panel will submit its report within three months.
