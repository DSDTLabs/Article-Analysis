AHMEDABAD: A group of   ( ) jawans deployed in the city, were hospitalized for   on Monday afternoon. While senior police officials could not be contacted, sources said the jawans had consumed food, especially khichdi, made at a common kitchen for police personnel at Ghodasar.

 Dr M M Prabhakar, medical superintendent at Civil Hospital, said that all 25 SRP personnel were rushed to the hospital on Monday afternoon. “They had nausea and other complications related to food poisoning after eating in their police mess. They will be kept under observation for time being,” said Prabhakar.
 
 Hospital sources said that their condition was stable as on Monday night and decision about discharge will be taken on Tuesday. Sources said that SRP units across the state primarily depend on tiffins or common kitchens to get their meals.
