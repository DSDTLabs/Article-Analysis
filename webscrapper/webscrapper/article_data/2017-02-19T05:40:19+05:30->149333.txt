The accused has been identified as   alias ‘Titli’, who was supposed to take the illegal drugs from his home to Mandsaur in Madhya Pradesh. The accused was arrested from his home after the cops from Hathunia police station laid a sudden raid there, Kaluram Ravat, Pratapgarh SP told TOI. 
 
 The case has been registered under section 8/21 of the NDPS Act. According to the police, the accused manufactured the drug in the secluded fields near Pratapgarh-MP border areas. 
 
 “They work within a network of pedallers who are engaged in the smuggling and selling of this illegal drug in Mandsaur, Ratlam and some districts of West Bengal. The accused confessed that he procured the poppy seeds from Malda district in West Bengal, a place which is an infamous drug center,” Rajendra Singh, SHO Hathunia police station said. 
 
 The accused was also found to be in regular contact with a large gang of drug pedallers. The police is in lookout for the other members of the gang. The accused has been given in police custody till February 21, an officer said.
