The Army has ordered a probe into 'leaking' of photographs and video footage of the flag meeting between the   and the Chinese PLA, held at the Line of Actual Control in   district in Arunachal Pradesh last Saturday, to the media.

  A defence source said, "The pictures and videos were taken on mobile phones. The meeting was conducted and the leaked photos and videos are not against the Army. But the question is why were these leaked to the media?" T he source added there were no civilians at the meeting between the personnel of the two armies on January 6, indicating that the pictures could have been leaked by Army personnel themselves.
