CHENNAI: Senior   Dal leader and member of parliament from Bihar's Araria district   died in a hospital in the city on Sunday.

 Taslimuddin, 74, was admitted to Apollo Hospitals here on August 26 after he developed breathing problem.
 
 He was in the city in connection with a   committee meeting.
 
 He breathed his last on Sunday at 1.10pm, said hospital sources. His body will be sent back home on Sunday night.
 
 Taslimuddin was a seventime   and five-time MP.He had served as Union Minister of State for home in the past.
