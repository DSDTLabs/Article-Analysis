CHENNAI: As protest by secondary grade teachers demanding 'equal pay for equal work' entered day three, leader of opposition MK   visited the protest venue on Wednesday expressing solidarity. More than 1,000 teachers along with their family members gathered outside the DPI building at   on Monday urging the state government to resolve disparities in salary hike.

However, they refused to disperse. A group of teachers were on a hunger strike since then and over 30 among them had reportedly fell sick.
