Chandigarh: Punjab minister for scheduled castes, backward classes and minority welfare Sadhu Singh   on Saturday met   for social justice and empowerment Thaawar Chand Gehlot to demand a release of Rs 1,048 crore pertaining to the post-matric scholarships for the SC students.

 After the meeting in Delhi, Dharamsot said for 2015-2016 under the post-matric scholarship (PMS) scheme, Rs 328.72 crore and for 2016-2017, Rs 719.52 crore is due from the Central government. 
 
 Dharamsot said the user certificates with regard to the amount earlier released by the Union government so far concerning the various welfare schemes of Punjab have been sent to the Centre. tnn
