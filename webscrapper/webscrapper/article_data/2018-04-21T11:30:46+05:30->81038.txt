PANCHKULA: A mini school bus of  ,  , on Friday rammed into a tree after losing control near industrial area. In the accident, four children sustained minor injuries. They were taken to Civil Hospital, Panchkula, and discharged after giving first aid.

 Following the incident, police arrested the driver of the school bus. He was later released on bail. “He was driving the bus without a valid driver’s licence,” the police said.
 
 The school bus was taking 10 children to the school when the driver lost control of the vehicle, while trying to avoid collision with a motorcycle. The bus, after losing control, jumped over a divider and rammed into a tree.
 
 The police said that the vehicle was impounded and further investigations were being carried out.
 
 They added that they would also take an action against the contractor and the school.
