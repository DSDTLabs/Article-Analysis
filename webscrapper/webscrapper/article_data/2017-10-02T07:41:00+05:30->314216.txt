Chandigarh: A 12-year-old boy was electrocuted at a birthday party celebration of a child who had turned one-year-old at a ground near his house in Ram Darbar, Phase I, on Saturday night. 

 The victim was identified as Vikas, the youngest among four siblings. A tent had covered the ground for the party. According to Indra, mother of Vikas, he was having his food when he accidentally touched an iron pole and was electrocuted. 
 
 According to witnesses, an electric wire was touching the pole. Vikas fell down on the ground and became unconscious, family members said. They took Vikas to Government Medical College and Hospital, Sector 32. He died during treatment. Police handed over the body to family members after conducting the post-mortem on Sunday.
 
 Indra said three to four children had complained about receiving a mild shock when they touched the iron pole. They even reported the matter to Amit, the owner of the tent house, asking him to repair it. He, however, did not pay heed to the requests.
