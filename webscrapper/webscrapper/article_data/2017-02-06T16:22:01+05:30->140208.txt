Malayali actress   has been sashaying in film industries across South India. However, the recent film that she has taken up in Telugu has the Action Hero Biju actress in a glamorous avatar. Her get-up is such that it can make the audience swoon. 

 The film titled 'Kittu Unnada Jagratha' is directed by Vamsi, and has actor Arbaaz Khan essaying the role of a villain in it. Anu will also be the female lead of the Vikram starrer Dhruva Natchathiram.
