AMRITSAR: A prominent Sikh religious leader was shot dead in Peshawar city of   province of Pakistan on Tuesday. The president of Peshawar-based Khalsa Peace and Justice Foundation of Pakistan Radesh Singh Tony told TOI that unidentified gunmen shot dead Charanjit Singh in   area while he was sitting in his grocery shop. Charanjit was vice-president of Peshawar Singh Sabha Society and was vocal in taking up issues of community. Radesh informed that the assailants managed to flee after shooting the veteran Sikh leader.
