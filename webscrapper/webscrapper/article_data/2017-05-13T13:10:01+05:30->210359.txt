PANAJI: Governor Mridula   recommended that the governments at the state and Centre make pre-marital counseling a prerequisite for marriages/marriage registration in all  .

 She made the recommendation in her report titled ‘The Practice and Effects of the Civil Code in Goa’. Sinha interacted with women of six different communities and a group of religious leaders in the state. 
 
 Her interactions revealed that the need for pre-marital counseling was supported by women of all communities. She has recommended governments take steps to make pre-marital counseling a prerequisite.
