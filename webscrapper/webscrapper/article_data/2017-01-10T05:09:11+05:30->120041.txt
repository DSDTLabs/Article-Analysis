MUMBAI: State   general secretary   on Monday called on State election commissioner J S   to demand immediate removal of posters, banners and hoardings with photographs of Prime Minister Narendra Modi and chief minister  . 

 "We have submitted a memorandum to Saharia as well as the chief electoral officer to demand removal of all such hoardings and banners in view of the code of conduct in force across the state," Sawant said.
 
 He said that in view of elections to the legislative councils for teachers and graduates' constituency as well as to municipal councils and nagar panchayts, a code of conduct is in force in most parts of the state. 
 
 "Hoardings have been put up at most of petrol pumps in the state with the PM's appeal, while advertisements on all state transport buses have the CM's pictures on them," he said.
