MOHALI: Police here have booked 12 persons of two families under various sections of Indian Penal Code for clashing over some old enmity. The cops said that on Wednesday, the two families of   attacked each other with sharp-edged weapons. The clash injured a few members on both the sides. 

 "The incident took place at Shampur village near Saneta at 10am on Wednesday. The accused have some old enmity. They had also attacked each other before," the cops said.
 
 "We have booked 12 persons under Sections 341 (punishment for wrongful restraint), 506 (punishment for criminal intimidation), 148 (rioting, armed with deadly weapon) and 149 (every member of unlawful assembly guilty of offence committed in prosecution of common object) of the IPC Act," said Saneta police post inchareg Parmjeet Singh.
