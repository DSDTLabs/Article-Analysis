BENGALURU: The high court on Monday said the   has no authority to grant recognition to institutions imparting courses such as auxiliary nursing and midwifery, general nursing and midwifery, bachelor of nursing (Bsc) and master of nursing (MSc).

 Disposing of a batch of pleas,   also restrained the council on its website from publishing materials indicating that institutions imparting nursing courses have to obtain recognition from it. The court noted that the action of the council in derecognizing institutions was against the law.
 
 The Karnataka State Association of the Management of Nursing and Allied Health Science Institutions and others have moved the court challenging various communications issued by the Indian Nursing Council.
