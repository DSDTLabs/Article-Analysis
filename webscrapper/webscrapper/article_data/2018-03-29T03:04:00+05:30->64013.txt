Panaji: The North Goa district magistrate has invoked CrPC Section 133(1) and passed a conditional order that all   lease holders shall continue to release water for drinking and irrigation requirement downstream as was regularly being done in the past. If they object to do so, they have to appear before the DM at 11am on Monday and show cause within seven days of the date of the order as to why the order should not be made absolute. The order also states that failure to comply with the aforesaid directions shall attract criminal action under IPC Section 188. tnn
