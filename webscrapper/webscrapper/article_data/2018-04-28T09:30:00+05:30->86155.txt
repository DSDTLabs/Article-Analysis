Sunny Wayne's next film is set in the 90s and if its new poster is any indication, those who loved cycling with his friends will have a few great riding moments to wait for. 

 The poster, featuring three more actors other than him, shows the foursome heading somewhere quickly, on their bicycles. Earlier, the director Maju had revealed Sunny plays a chef in the movie, who is though very lazy in life. Certain incidents turn his life upside down and that's what the story dwells on, the team had said.
