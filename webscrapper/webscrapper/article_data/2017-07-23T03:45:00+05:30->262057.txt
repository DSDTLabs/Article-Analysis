Panaji: Byelections to the Nuvem and Curtorim South Goa zilla panchayat constituencies, Calangute and Corlim North Goa zilla panchayat constituencies, ward IV of the Dramapur-Sirlim village panchayat, and ward III of the Morlem panchayat will be held on August 20. 

 Nominations will be accepted from August 1 to August 8, between 10 am and 1 pm except on August 6. The deputy collectors and SDMs of Tiswadi, Bardez, Salcete-I and the mamlatdars of Salcete, Tiswadi, Bardez and Sattari have been appointed as the returning officers and assistant returning officers. tnn
