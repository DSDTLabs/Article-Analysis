THIRUVANANTHAPURAM: In the wake of recent incidents of atrocities against children in many parts of the state, the Kerala State Commission for Protection of Child Rights (KeSCPCR) has registered a suo motu case. The commission has registered the case based on media reports and has sought for an inquiry report.

 In a recent incident reported at   in Wayanad, minor girl inmates were allegedly subjected to sexual abuse. The commission has directed   district police chief, district child protection officer ( ) to inquire into the incident and submit a report within 15 days.
 
 In Kozhikode at  , nine girl students of a private school were abused by their teacher.The commission has sought the district police chief, DCPO, Childline and education co-director to submit a report within 15 days.
 
 In yet another incident at   in Kozhikode, a girl student in Class IX was found stabbed to death in mysterious circumstances near her school. The panel has sought a report from the district police chief, DCPO and the medical college superintendent within 15 days in the case as well.
