NABHA: A 35-year-old man from Gajjukehra village near Banur, who was undergoing life imprisonment at Nabha maximum security jail after being convicted in a murder case,   by hanging himself from the ceiling of his barracks on Tuesday. 

 Body of Sarabjit Singh, who was sentenced to life imprisonment for murdering his sister and her husband, was found hanging by the jail staff around 2pm in barracks number two. He was taken to the Nabha civil hospital where he was declared brought dead.
 
 A case under Section 302 was registered against Sarabjit by the Banur police on June 14, 2008. Later, he was convicted and shifted to Nabha jail, where he was undergoing his life imprisonment.
