BHUBANESWAR: Chief minister   paid a courtesy call to Prime Minister   at the latter's residence in New Delhi on Wednesday. 

 "It was just a courtesy call. I met the PM for 20 minutes. I gave him the recently published book on   (The Tall Man Biju Patnaik) and we discussed about the life of Biju babu. That was all," Naveen told reporters after the meeting.
 
 To queries whether he discussed issues related to Odisha, the chief minister said there was no other discussion during the meeting.
 
 The meeting raised eyebrows back home and many leaders from the BJD and the BJP said they got to know about it from the media.
