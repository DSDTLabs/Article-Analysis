Last evening, on Sunday, along with thousands of Mumbaikars, Bollywood actors and other celebrities gathered for a peaceful protest. The celebrities protested the barbaric rape and murder of an eight-year-old girl in Kathua, Jammu and Kashmir and also the sexual assault of another young girl in   in   Pradesh.

 
  
 
 
  
 
  
 
  
 
  
 
 Rajkummar Rao, Patralekha, Sameera Reddy, Twinkle Khanna along with her son Arav, Akshay Kumar, Aditi Rao Hydari, Tara Sharma, music director  , singer and social activist  , filmmaker   and veteran actress Helen were among the many who took to   streets. 
 They were protesting the incidents of rape and were seeking justice.
 
 
  
 
 
 They were also holding placards and posters that read #justiceforasifa. There were some youngsters amongst the crowd that had posters reading , “This is not just rape, this is genocide with state support”.
 Another young burkha-clad girl was carrying a poster that read, “Before having a Swachh Bharat, we want a safe Bharat”.
 
 
  
 
  
 
  
 
 
 Bollywood music composer Vishal Dadlani said, “We want to stop this nonsense now... we want action and we want it now! Once the man is accused and guilty of raping and killing a baby girl, he should be hanged then and then.”
 
 Bollywood playback singer Sona Mohapatra, who is known for being vocal about injustice and taking a stand said, “Our gathering shows that we as a community stand for the cause; there is a helplessness that we all feel and therefore we are standing here. We want justice.”
 
 
 Several other Bollywood actresses like Kareena Kapoor Khan, Swara Bhaskar, Sonam Kapoor, Shruti Seth and   also held placards asking for justice for the eight-year-old girl, who was kidnapped, sedated, starved, gang-raped and murdered in Kathua district of Jammu and Kashmir.
