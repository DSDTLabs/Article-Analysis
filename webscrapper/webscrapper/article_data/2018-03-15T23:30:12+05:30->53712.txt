Vijayawada: Union minister for railways   Goyal on Thursday has denied allegations made by the Telugu Desam Party MPs that he did not speak to them. Speaking to media persons in Delhi, Goyal alleged that none of the TDP MPs have approached him. 

 Goyal r further blamed the Congress for creating problems in AP even after the bifurcation. He said, “It (Congress) only said (in AP Reorganisation Act) that the government shall study the feasibility of establishing a zone,” and reiterated that the Centre was studying the matter. tnn
