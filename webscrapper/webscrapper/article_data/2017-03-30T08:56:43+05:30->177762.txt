Vadodara: Four persons were arrested for pilfering chemical from tankers at a compound in Ranoli on Tuesday midnight.

 On receiving a tip-off, staff of Jawaharnagar police raided the compound and arrested Bharat  , Manish Patel, Tajaram Chaudhary and Pannaram Chaudhary. 
 
 Cops said that Chavda and Patel used to steal chemicals from tankers that used to ferry between Kandla and Dahej.
 
 According to the police, the duo used to steal chemicals in collusion with Tajaram. Police will interrogate the accused to know if they had stolen chemicals in the past too. Police seized 48,000 litres of chemicals worth Rs 13.20 lakh.
