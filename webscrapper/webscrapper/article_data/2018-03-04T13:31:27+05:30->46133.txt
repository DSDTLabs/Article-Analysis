Be it the poster, teaser or music or even the trailer. Whenever a new promotional campaign has been launched, ‘Aami Ashbo Phirey’ made it really huge. The brilliant cast ensemble, the energetic music and the recent music launch party are all making heads turn to this film.
The   movie features the director Anjan Dutt himself,  ,  ,  , Anjana Basu,   in pivotal roles. The music of ‘Aami Ashbo Phirey’ has been composed by Anjan’s son Neel Dutta. Now the makers have launched a brand new poster. Anjan Dutt has penned lyrics and sang for his own film after a long time and the soundtracks haven’t disappointed at all.
 The musical drama is slated to release this April. The story of the film revolves around the journey through pain and loneliness and how music becomes the sole hope inspiring the characters to forgive, forget and move on.
