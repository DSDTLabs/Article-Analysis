MUMBAI: Eighteen members were elected to the   on Friday. The elections were held after a seven-year gap. Nine principals were elected on the principals’ constituency unopposed as two principals’ organisations came together and nominated members for the selections. 

 Six members of the management constituency too were elected unopposed. The last senate election was held in 2010. After the new Maharashtra Public Universities Act 2016 was implemented, the election is held for the first time. With no candidate for scheduled tribe, only nine candidates are elected instead of 10 in the senate. 
 
 Three members from teachers constituency of the university departments were elected on the varsity's senate. Candidates in the graduates constituency and teachers constituency from affilated colleges are still pending.
