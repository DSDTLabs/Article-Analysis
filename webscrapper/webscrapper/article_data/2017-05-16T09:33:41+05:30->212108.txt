Surat: A 32-year old embroidery worker committed suicide in his house in Hirabaug locality of Kapodra area of the city on Sunday night. He was originally from Junagadh district of Saurashtra. 

 Kapodra police said Atul R. Langhani, who worked as an embroidery worker in Katargam GIDC unit, hanged himself with a rope from a hook in his room when his wife went out for an outing to Mota Varachha Chowpaty with her friends. 
 
 He left no suicide note behind. His neighbours described him as a good man who was facing financial problems. He was not able to earn enough and hence remained depressed, sources said. Police said they are investigating all aspects related to the case.tnn
