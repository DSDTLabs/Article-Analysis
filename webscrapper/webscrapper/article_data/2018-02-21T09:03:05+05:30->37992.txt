CHANDIGARH: The body of a baby boy was found wrapped in a poly bag near a nallah at Vikas Nagar,  , on Tuesday evening. 

 Kapil Dev, station house officer, Mauli Jagran, said a passer-by saw dogs eating parts of the body. He shooed them away and informed the police. Police took the body to Government Medical College and Hospital, Sector 32. Police said initial inquiry suggests the baby was born around two or three days ago and the body was dumped at the site. Police said post-mortem report would reveal the timing of the death. Police are checking registers of government and private hospitals of nearby areas to get clues about parents of the baby. Police have registered a case under Sections of Indian Penal Code (IPC).
