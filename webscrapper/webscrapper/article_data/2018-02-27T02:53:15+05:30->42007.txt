 The victim,   Pal, got crushed under a truck while it was reversing. The accused driver,  , fled from the spot after the incident.
 
 As per a worker, the incident took place around 7.30pm on Friday when a truck transit mixer, which is used to mix concrete, was being used at the construction site. “The driver was reversing the vehicle near the batching plant. He was bringing in the truck at a high speed while reversing it and failed to see Pal,” the worker said.
 
 The victim was hit by the truck and came under the wheels of the vehicle. Pal suffered extensive head injuries and was rushed to a nearby hospital where he was declared brought dead. 
 
 “A case under sections of dangerous driving and causing death due to negligence was registered against the errant driver at the   police station,” said a senior police officer.
