AMBALA: A Class II student was sexually abused by a man, police said on Friday.

 The seven-year-old boy was returning home after his school, when the accused lured him with some eatables and took him on his motorcycle to a secluded place. After abusing the boy, the accused dropped him near his house and fled.
 
 The accused threatened the boy to not disclose about the incident to anyone. The parents of the boy first visited the house of the accused to inquire about the incident, but he denied the allegation. The parents then filed a complaint with the police.
 
 Police arrested the accused after the medical examination of the boy at the   civil hospital confirmed he was sexually assaulted. A police officer said the accused was booked under Sections of POCSO Act. A local court has been sent to the judicial custody in   Jail.
