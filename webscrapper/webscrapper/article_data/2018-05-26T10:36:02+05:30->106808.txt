BHUBANESWAR: The council of higher secondary education (CHSE) will publish the results of the Plus II Arts and Commerce by June first week. Bijay Kumar Sahoo, CHSE’s controller of examinations, said this here on Friday.

 He said the evaluation process is already over. “Tabulation process of the marks is going on. After tabulation, we will announce the results,” said Sahoo. 
 
 The council has already published the results of Plus II science on May 19. School and mass education minister Badri Narayan Patra had unveiled the results book. 
 
 The minister had told media that Plus II Arts and Commerce results will be out by end of this month. 
 
 The CHSE had organised the examinations for Plus II examination 2018 between March 6 and 29.
