CHANDIGARH:   senate and syndicate member   Gill joined   (AAP) on Friday. A former student leader, he has been chairman of the Punjab University Students Union (PUSU). 

 He was formally inducted into the party by AAP incharge of Punjab affairs Sanjay Singh.
 
 Gill said he is a resident of Abohar and will be campaigning for the party in the area by supporting the AAP candidate. “There are several Panjab University graduates in the area and most of them have already aligned with AAP. Congress is facing anti-incumbency in the area and people are looking for change this time,” he said.
