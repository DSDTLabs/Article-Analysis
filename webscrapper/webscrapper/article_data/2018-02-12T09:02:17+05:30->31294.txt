GUWAHATI: Various labour organizations in   have extended their support to the 24-hour strike called by All India   Workers Federation on Monday. 

 All Assam Motor Workers' Joint Council and All Assam Cab Operators' Union among others have supported the strike. 
 
 The unions have been demanding pension, medical benefits for their families, financial aid for education of their children, and providing training to drivers and helpers of the vehicles.
