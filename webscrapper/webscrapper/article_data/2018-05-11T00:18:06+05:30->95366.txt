Kota: A 50-year-old man was allegedly killed in Shankarpura village under Dablana police station area of Bundi district Wednesday night. The body was recovered from an agriculture field in the village on Thursday morning and was handed over to the family after post-mortem. Police have booked three persons in this connection and initiated the investigation. However, no one has been arrested so far.

 “Body of a 50-year-old man identified as Joshi Kanjar, a resident of Shankarpura village was found in an agriculture field of the village Thursday morning,” SHO at Dablana police station Satyaveer Meena said. “Prima facie, the man was choked to death,” he added. The deceased man had left for his agriculture field on Wednesday night but was found killed Thursday morning.
 
 On the report by the deceased’s son, police have lodged a case under sections 302 and 34 of IPC against three persons of the village, the SHO said and further investigation into the matter is underway.
 
 “The deceased man was reportedly engaged in a land dispute with Lalshasha Kanjar and two others and a village panchayat was called on Wednesday evening to resolve the dispute,” the SHO said. According to locals of Shankarpura village, Lalshasha Kanjar and two other in the panchayat meeting threatened to kill Joshi Kanjar.
