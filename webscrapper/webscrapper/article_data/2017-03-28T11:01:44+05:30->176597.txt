Music composer   is learning to play the mirudhangam. And guess who his guru is. It’s none other than the   expert  . The composer is brushing up his skills for his upcoming romantic film   directed by cinematographer-turned-director Rajiv Menon. The film is said to be Menon’s comeback film, who directed Kandukondein Kandukondein in 2000. 

 Music for the film is by GV Prakash Kumar’s uncle, the Academy Award winner  . The album already comprises nine songs out of which one is a kuthu number. Reports are that the team has approached Sai Pallavi to play the female lead. There is no official confirmation on the same.
