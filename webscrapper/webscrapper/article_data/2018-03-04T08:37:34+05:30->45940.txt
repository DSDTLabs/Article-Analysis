HONG KONG: A fugitive wanted by Indian police for crimes ranging from a jailbreak to financing terrorism was denied bail in Hong Kong on Friday, where he is charged with a multi-million dollar robbery. 

 Ramanjit Singh  , an Indian who is on  's global watch list, was detained this month by authorities over the robbery. He is accused of stealing more than 450 million Japanese yen (USD 4 million) in cash from two people on February 9.
