 The official website (www.mu.ac.in) has been down since Monday.

 A university official attributed it to increased traffic. "Students were downloading hall tickets and filling exam forms. Forms of at least six to seven courses were made available online at the same time. The server not crash, but it was just slow. Our officials have added servers to reduce the problem," said the official.
