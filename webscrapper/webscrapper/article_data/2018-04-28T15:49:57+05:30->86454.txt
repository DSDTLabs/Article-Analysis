The Kalarang Festival 2018, organized by the directorate of Art & Culture and   Margao, saw spellbinding performances by several eminent  ,   and dance personalities. The five day event at Ravindra Bhavan,  , featured a tabla recital by Pandit Suresh Talwalkar, a kathak recital by Mahua Shankar, and a vocal recital by Ram Deshpande. Next slated are the plays Kashmaksh (Hindi) and Sola Eke Sola (Marathi).
