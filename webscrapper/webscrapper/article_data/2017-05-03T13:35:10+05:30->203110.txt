MANGALURU: With temperature soaring in  , a fuel station in Mangaluru has come up with a   to help its customers  . 

 Customers queuing up in front of the fuel station can quench their thirst by sipping unlimited cups of buttermilk. 
  
  
 Shivanand Prabhu of Prabhu Service Station on Balmatta-Kankanady road came up with the unique idea on grounds that buttermilk is healthier than aerated drinks. 
 
 Mangaluru recorded a maximum of 37 degree C on Wednesday.
