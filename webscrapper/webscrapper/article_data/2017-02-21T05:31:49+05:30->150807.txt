MUMBAI: The police will deploy additional staff at 726 polling booths which have been found to be sensitive. Combat vehicles, quick response commandos, riot control police and SRPF will be deployed in the city along side  's personnel.

 “We have listed 726 booths as sensitive. These areas are the ones that have seen scuffles between political groups in the past and where people with criminal history have created law and order order issues during elections,“ said a police officer.
 
 Security at vital installation and buildings, consulates and leading banks has been beefed up. “All cops on election duty have been asked to stay alert. Nakabandis too have been put in place,“ said a police officer.
