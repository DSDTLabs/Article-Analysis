Surat: Two thieves on a motorcycle broke a car’s window glass and escaped with a bag containing Rs 4 lakh at Jambusar crossroads near Bharuch on Monday. Bharuch city police said Imtiyaz Dawood Ali had taken Rs 4 lakh from an angadia firm and was on his way to Pakhej village in car. He stopped at Jambusar crossroads and went to a public urinal when two men on a motorcycle broke the car’s window and escaped with the bag. tnn
