JAIPUR: Rajasthan High Court on Monday asked the secretary, department of personnel to present in the court on May 15 over the backlog of posts reserved for the differently abled.

 The division bench of chief justice Pradeep Nandrajog and   issued the order on a PIL by Rattanlal Bairwa.
 
 The petitioner said the   in 2013 directed all the states to fill up the vacancies reserved for the differently-abled through a drive including the backlog of vacancies by the state governments. But so far, it has not been complied with the apex court directive in Rajasthan.
 
 According to sections 33 and 34 of the Rights of Persons with Disability ( ) Act 2016, if the vacancies of the differently-abled of a year could not be filled up it is being carried forward to the next year. In the third year the post could be filled up with inter exchange (candidates with other disabilities) after securing prior permission from the department of personnel.
 
 Section 34 of the PWD says, every appropriate government shall appoint in every government establishment, not less than four per cent of the total number of vacancies in the cadre strength in each group of posts meant to be filled with persons with benchmark disabilities of which, one per cent.
