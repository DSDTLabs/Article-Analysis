GANDHINAGAR:   government on Thursday announced that government employees and pensioners will get arrears from the   from March onwards in three monthly instalments.

 Gujarat deputy chief minister and finance minister   announced this in the assembly through a motion for a matter of public importance. The Gujarat government will pay the arrears in three instalments. A total of Rs 3,279 crore will be spent on this.
 
 The deputy CM said, “As the 7th CPC was implemented with retrospective effect, employees and pensioners are eligible for arrears. The arrears will be paid from March onwards and in three instalments every alternate months. Employees will get arrears for seven months and pensioners for nine. These benefits will go to 4.65 lakh employees and 4.12 lakh pensioners of the Gujarat government.”
