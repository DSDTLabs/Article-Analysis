Margao: The 21st edition of India International Seafood Show (IISS) will commence at Margao on January 27.  The three-day festival will see a congregation of industry leaders and experts who will deliberate on policies, market trends, technology and roadmaps to achieving an export target of US 10 billion dollars by 2022. In 2016-17, India exported seafood worth Rs 5.78 billion US dollars.  The biennial exposition is organized jointly by the Marine Products Export Development Authority (MPEDA), the nodal agency of the union ministry of commerce and industry, and the Seafood Export Association of India (SEAI).tnn
