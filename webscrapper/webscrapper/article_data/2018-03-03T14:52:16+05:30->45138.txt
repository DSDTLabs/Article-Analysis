CHENNAI:   chief minister   was admitted to Apollo Hospitals in Chennai on Friday night for routine annual medical check-up, the hospital said in a bulletin on Saturday. He would be discharged on Sunday, it said.

 Vijayan was admitted to Apollo Hospitals, Greams Road, at 11.55pm. He was monitored by a team of doctors. 
 
 On Saturday, he was taken to Apollo Speciality Hospital in Teynampet for diagnostic procedure, sources said.
 
 On Friday, the 72-year-old visited the home of  , a tribal youth in Kerala who was lynched by a mob for allegedly stealing provisions, and assured his family that stringent action would be taken against the accused.
