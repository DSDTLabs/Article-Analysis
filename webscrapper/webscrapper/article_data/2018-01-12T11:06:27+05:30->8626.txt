AGARTALA: A 14-member CBI team, headed by inspector Brotin Ghoshal, has begun an investigation into the   scam in the state. 

 The CBI team, which arrived here on Tuesday, has launched an investigation into the role of top ministers in the Left Front government in the promotion of Rose Valley Hotel and   Limited in the state. Social welfare minister   and   central committee member Goutam Das have already been questioned by the CBI. Three cases have been filed in the   police station and one case has been referred to CBI by the state government, a police officer said.
