A group of puppeteers recently organised a puppet show based on the Indian mythology at   at Naya  . As many as 150 audience witnessed the puppet show. What is interesting to note here is that the show was organised in its traditional format. With songs and narration the puppet show kicked started with a lot of enthusiasm. At a time when the focus of the audience is more towards digital visuals, audience really enjoyed the show. The organisers were welcomed with loud applause at the auditorium. One of the organisers   says, "We need to keep alive Chhattisgarhi tradition and puppet show is one step towards preserving our unique heritage."
