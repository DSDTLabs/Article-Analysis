HYDERABAD: A major fire broke out at an   bottling plant at   late on Thursday evening, creating panic among locals. The fire department pressed into service as many as six fire tenders to extinguish the flames. No casualties were reported. 

 According to fire officials, the incident was reported to the control room at 8.45 pm. "Soon, we rushed the fire tenders to the spot to douse the flames. It took the firemen over an hour to complete the operation," said V Papaiah, regional fire officer, central region. 
 
 The fire tenders were dispatched from Malkajgiri, Moula Ali, Musheerabad, Gowliguda, Kukatpally and Cherlapally.
