PIMPRI CHINCHWAD:   municipal commissioner   on Friday has directed civic officials to streamline water supply in three days by analysing technical problems in areas receiving less water and rectifying those faults.

 Staff who operate the valves should ensure that water is released on time. Junior and deputy engineers must monitor the situation. He said strict action will be taken against employees who delay action.
