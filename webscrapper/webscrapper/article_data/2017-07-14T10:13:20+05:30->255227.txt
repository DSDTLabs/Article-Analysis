PIMPRI CHINCHWAD: Residents affected by construction works related to the High Capacity Mass Transit Route ( ) project in Pimpri   held a silent march on Thursday. The protestors walked to the head office of the Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation.

 The project is being developed by the Pimpri Chinchwad New Township Development Authority (PCNTDA).
 
 The   organized the march from Chinchwad to Pimpri. Residents held placards stating “we own our houses” and demanded regularization of unauthorized constructions. A large number of women participated in the march, which included nearly 3,000 people.
 
 As the HCMTR passes through PCMC and PCNTDA limits, many houses will be demolished in Bijlinagar near Akurdi railway station, Ravet, Walhekarwadi, Pimple Gurav, Thergaon and Kasarwadi.
 
 In 1995, the administration had planned to construct a Ring Road or HCMTR. However, the government failed to acquire land. Due to urbanization, houses were constructed on the Ring Road.
 
 The residents now demand that the government scrap the HCMTR project. Nearly 1.5 lakh people will be affected by it. As the municipal commissioner and the additional municipal commissioner were absent, members submitted the memorandum to the assistant commissioner, Vijay Khorate, who assured the protesters that the document would be submitted to the authorities concerned as well as state government officials.
