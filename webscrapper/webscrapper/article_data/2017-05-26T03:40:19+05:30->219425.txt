GHAZIABAD: Four men were arrested on Wednesday evening for robbery and theft. The accused have been involved numerous cases in NCR for a long time.

 The accused have been identified as Sanbar, Saddam Hussain, Nawazish and Aamir, who are all residents of Bhowapur village in Indirapuram. They were involved in stealing silver and gold jewellery along with cash from the house of Ehsan, a rickshaw-puller, in Bhowapur on Wednesday morning. 
 
 An amount of Rs 13,790, one mobile phone, one wrist watch, one purse, one anklet and a pair of earrings have been recovered from their possession. 
 
  An FIR under the relevent sections of the IPC was registered and the accused have been sent to judicial custody.
