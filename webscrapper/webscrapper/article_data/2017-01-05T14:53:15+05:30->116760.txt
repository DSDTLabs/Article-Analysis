Watch the scintillating performance of dancers- Yamini and Mythili on Zee Telugu's dance reality show 'Anybody Can Dance'. The two girls have set the stage on fire with their swift moments to a beautifully composed medly including rock music and hit numbers from recent releases. Guests- Rambha, Sneha and choreographer Sundaram showered appreciations upon them. The entire episode can be watched on January 7 at 9.30 pm.
