VISAKHAPATNAM: In a humanitarian gesture, police personnel, including Chintapalli DSP  , donated blood on Monday to a tribal girl suffering from heart ailment.

 An SSC dropout, Korra Bhavani (17) has been ailing for the past couple of years.The district rural police admitted her to Care Hospital in Vizag after her condition deteriorated. Bhavani is the eldest of three children of a farmer, Korra Danayya, from   village in GK Veedhi mandal, a Naxal-affected Agency area.
 
 This is the second time that   police donated blood. Two anti-naxal wing cops donated blood to two injured Maoists at Pedapadu in GK Veedhi mandal in October last year.
