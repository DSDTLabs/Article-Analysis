VIJAYAWADA: Secretary of the Andhra Pradesh   Adityanath Das on Wednesday said that 6,28,081 students will appear for   scheduled to begin on March 17. 

 Das said the board has set up 2,931 examination centres across the state and 156 flying squads have been formed for conducting surprise inspections at the centres.
 
 He appealed to the students to be present at the exam centres by 8:45 am to avoid last-minute rush. The exams will start at 9:45 am and end at 12:15 pm. The students will be given 15 minutes to read the question paper, he added.
