VADODARA:   officers of   (VMC) on Thursday cracked down upon mango dealers in the city as the supply of the fruit has increased and it is being sold widely. 

 The civic body began the drive to check   and shops in the city in the wake of directives in this regard by the commissioner of food safety at Gandhinagar. The officers were asked to take up the drive as a precautionary measure to prevent sale of substandard quality mangoes.
 
 The drive on Thursday was conducted in   that also has VMC headquarters. Food safety officers checked 19 establishments in the market. The teams seized 250kg mangoes that were ripened with artificial ripeners.
 
 The stock was destroyed and the owners of the shops were warned against using the substance again. Officials said the drive would continue in the coming days across the city.
