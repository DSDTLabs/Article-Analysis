VELLORE: Around 300 employees and administrative staff of the   Industrial Explosives Limited at Christianpet near Katpadi withdrew the   protest on Friday after the management disbursed their two-month salary.

 According to the workers, the management held a talk with them and they settled for an interim decision.
 
 The management also assured them that they would disburse the pending salary within a week, added the workers.
 
 The officials and administrative staff had not received salary since June last year, while the workers did not receive the salary from July.
