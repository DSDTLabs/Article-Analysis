NEW DELHI: Vellore Institute of Technology today declared the VIT Engineering Entrance Examination ( ) 2018 results on its official website. The result link can be seen live on the official website of Vellore Institute of Technology - vit.ac.in

 
 The direct link to download   Entrance Exam Result is here, check this out - https://vtop11.vit.ac.in/viteeeresults/
 
 The candidates who have appeared for the VITEEE examination 2018 can check their result by entering their 10-digit application number, date of birth and verification provided on the page. After entering all the details, the candidates will have to click on submit button. Once the result is displayed online, they can download or take a printout of the same for further reference. 
 
 
 
 
  
  
 
 Earlier, the VITEEE results 2018 was expected to be launched on April 25, 2018, but it got delayed due to unknown reasons. However, now the candidates can check their result on the official website of VIT. 
 
 The admissions to various B.Tech branches is offered to the students who clear VITEEE entrance exam, at VIT campuses in Vellore, Chennai, Amravati and Bhopal.
 
 After the declaration of the results, a merit list will be prepared and based on this merit list the candidates will be called for counseling round.
