CHENNAI: A group of youngsters wearing masks and T-shirts having the Batman logo pelted stones on a   in Nungambakkam here on Wednesday morning.

 The boys, stood above the subway near Nungambakkam station and threw stones on a 47D bus at around 9.45am, breaking the rear windscreen. None was injured.
 
 A policeman, who was barely 200 meters away, reported to the control room. Traffic was briefly halted.
 
 Senior police officials said the incident might be isolated and might have nothing to do with the political drama unfolding in the state.
