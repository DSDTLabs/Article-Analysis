LUCKNOW: President Ram Nath Kovind, Prime Minister  , Reliance chairman  , Adani Group chairman  ,   CEO Chandrasekaran and Aditya Birla Group chairman Kumar Mangalam Birla would attend UP Investors’ Summit.

 
 PM Modi would deliver the inaugural speech during his two-hour stay and will also meet top CEOs separately. President Ram Nath Kovind would be present in closing ceremony. The key note address on closing day would be delivered by finance minister Arun Jaitley, said UP government spokesman Siddharthnath Singh. Essel Group chairman Subhash Chandra, GM Rao of GMR, L&T MD and CEO SN Subramaniyam, ITC CEO Sanjeev Puri, Torrent Pharmaceuticals Sudhir Mehta, Arvind Mills Ltd executive director Kulin Lalbhai and SREI Infrastructure’s Hemant Kanoria would also be present.
