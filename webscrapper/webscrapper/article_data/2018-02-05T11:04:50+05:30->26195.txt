ITANAGAR: Arunachal CM  , clearing the air on a recent video which falsely stated some side effects of the measles-rubella (MR) vaccine, got his children inoculated with the vaccine on Saturday. Taking cognizance of the target set by the Centre for eliminating measles by 2020 and controlling the rubella virus, he appealed to parents to bring all children between the ages of nine months and 15 years to vaccinate them.

 State immunization officer D   also informed that the vaccine is free from side effects. Emphasizing the role of the vaccine, Padung added that every child, irrespective of previous vaccination status, has to be covered. TNN
