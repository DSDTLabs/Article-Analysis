ALLAHABAD: Y-category security cover provided to mafia-turned-politician   has been withdrawn after the court questioned it following a PIL from a   resident. 

 Responding to the Allahabad high court on Friday, additional solicitor general of India Shashi  , representing the central government, said that security to the former MP had been withdrawn following a notification from the Union home ministry on May 24.
 
 Additional advocate general  , representing the state government, submitted that the state government had also issued directions on the same day to the Jaunpur SP to withdraw the security cover. 
 
 Besides, bail in cases in which the ex-MP is an accused has also been cancelled . A bench of chief justice   and Justice   disposed of the PIL filed by Prahlad Gupta of Jaunpur district.
