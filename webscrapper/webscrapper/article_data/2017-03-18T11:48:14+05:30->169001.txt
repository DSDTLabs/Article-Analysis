RAJKOT: With current financial year nearing end, the   of   ( ), started a property sealing drive in city on Friday.

 According to RMC officials, on Friday itself, they sealed over 30 properties as its owners had failed to pay tax dues.
 
 The properties in Parevadi Chowk, Kuvadava Main Road, Jay Gurudev Park, Akshardham Complex and other areas were sealed. In Parevadi chowk, RMC sealed 21 properties including 20 shops for the recovery of Rs 35 lakh tax. RMC also sealed ATM of   on New Ashram Road in Rajkot over undue Rs 1.10 lakh tax.
 
 RMC has so far collected Rs 215 crore as property tax for the current financial year.
