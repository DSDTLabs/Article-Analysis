COIMBATORE: Three of a family died and two others suffered injuries when a speeding truck rammed a sport utility vehicle (SUV) they were travelling in on the Annur Road in Mettupalayam on Wednesday.

 According to police, the accident took place when  , 57, the director of YFC church in Madurai, and his family were returning to their home town after attending a three-day annual meeting of pastors at Brookland in Coonoor.
 
 At the time of the accident, Stanley, his wife Daisy, 48, sons S Seemark, 16, and Simon Saith, 23, and their relative T Kingsley, 40, were in the SUV. Simon, a physiotherapy doctor, was driving the vehicle.
 
 When they reached Tank Medu, a speeding truck coming from the opposite direction rammed the SUV. Kingsley, Daisy and Simon died on the spot while Stanley and Seemark were critically injured.
 
 Stanley and Seemark were taken to Mettupalayam Government Hospital. Later they were referred to Coimbatore Medical College and Hospital (CMCH).
 
 Police said Kingsley was a resident of Palani and used to work for the Youth for Christ church in Madurai. Seemark was a Class 12 student.
