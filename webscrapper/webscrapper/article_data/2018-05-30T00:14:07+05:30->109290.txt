 Chinchwad: An architect has been appointed to prepare a detailed project report for the expansion and repairs of the day-night shelter at Pimpri vegetable market. 

 Under the central government sponsored National Urban Livelihood Mission for Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana, PCMC has decided for the expansion of the  . A survey was done in March 2017, in which 105 people were shelterless. The standing committee has already approved the proposal. The Centre is providing the required funds to the civic body. The architect will be paid a 2% fee of the tender cost of Rs 50 lakh, standing committee member Vilas Madigeri has said.
 
 Civic officials said surveys for the urban poor is an ongoing process and more shelters can be constructed in various parts of the city. TNN
