PANAJI: BITS Pilani K K Birla Goa campus is preparing to sign a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the Texas A&M University, USA, making a student as well faculty exchange possible between the two institutes.

 Recently, from December 28, 2017 to January 14, the BITS Pilani Goa campus hosted 17 students from the university. They were here for a course in engineering ethics. 
 
 This two-week programme was the Goa campus’ first study India programme. Associate dean, international programmes, professor  , one of the coordinators, said that there is now a proposal to conduct such programmes annually.
