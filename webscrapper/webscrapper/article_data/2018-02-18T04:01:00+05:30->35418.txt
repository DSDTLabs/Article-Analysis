Ahmedabad: Gujarat chief minister Vijay Rupani, flagged off a direct flight from Ahmedabad to Mundra, at an elaborate ceremony held here on Saturday. 

 In a major boost to regional connectivity, Ahmedabad-based GSEC Aviation launched the intra-state flight, under Ude Desh ka Aam Nagrik (UDAN) scheme, with a 19-seater aircraft, owned by Air Odisha through a joint venture. 
 
 Another flight from Ahmedabad to Jamnagar also began operations soon after. Top sources revealed that an Ahmedabad-Diu direct flight, which is set to be operational by the end of February, may be flagged of by Prime Minister Narendra Modi.
