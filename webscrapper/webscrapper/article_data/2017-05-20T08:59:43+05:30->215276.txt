AMARAVATI: Aiming to resolve water issues across the state,   chief minister N   launched 'Jalavani' call centre. People from all the 13 districts can call on toll-free number 1800-425-1899 for redressal of drinking water grievances. 

 The toll-free number was announced on Friday at the CM's residence in   in the presence of rural development and panchayat raj minister  , who conceived the project.
 
 Lokesh said that Jalavani call centre was launched as part of real-time governance. He said the project has been under implementation for the past six months on pilot basis. "People from any part of the state can call and we shall address their complaints immediately," he said.
 
 Lokesh said that the call centre has received 3,294 complaints so far.
