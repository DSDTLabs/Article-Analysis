Naga Shaurya has struck a chord with the Telugu audience with his boy next door looks and superb acting in romantic comedy ‘Oohalu Gusagusalaade.’ 

 Now, the actor is all set to repeat his success in his next outing ‘Ammammagari Illu,’ which is said to be a family entertainer. The flick will be helmed by debutante Sundar and is likely to set rolling soon.
 
 Eminent music composer   has been roped in to score music in the movie.
