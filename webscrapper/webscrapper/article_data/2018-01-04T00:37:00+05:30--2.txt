Ajmer: With the nomination process for Lok Sabha by-elections beginning on Wednesday, nine probables picked up forms from the district election office. Security measures have been strengthened with heavy deployment of forces at the district headquarters. No private vehicle was allowed inside the premises. Police are recording every activity and have started identifying regular criminals in the district.

 Both BJP and Congress have not announced their candidates for the parliamentary constituency. Sources said BJP and Congress were planning to field dummy candidates as independents as part of election strategy to benefit official candidates.
 
 The district election officer and district collector Gaurav Goyal said the application forms for filing nominations were available online.
