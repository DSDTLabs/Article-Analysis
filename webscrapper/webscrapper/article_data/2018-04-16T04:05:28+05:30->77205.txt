THANE: The homemaker who was reportedly set on fire by her husband and mother-in-law died of burn injuries on Sunday, the police said. Daksha (30) had suffered 80% burn injuries after her mother-in-law   (71) and husband   (40) had allegedly doused her in kerosene and set her on fire following an argument in   on Friday. 

 The Wagle Estate police have already arrested Jamnaben and Ashok for domestic violence and added IPC Section 302 (murder) to the case. The couple were living separately and their daughter was living with Daksha in Mumbai. "Daksha wanted to get some documents and hence she went to Ashok's house. They got into an argument after which the two set her on fire,"said a police officer. The victim ran out of the house and the neighbours tried to douse the flames.
