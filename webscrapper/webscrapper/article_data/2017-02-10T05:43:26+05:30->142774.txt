MUMBAI: A 25-year-old   from   was arrested on Thursday for stabbing and injuring a scriptwriter who allegedly tried to molest him.

 Caught in Surat, Sajid Mehandi Ansari (21) was booked for attempting to kill a person.
 
 On January 26, the scriptwriter, Sahil Sultanpuri (28), allegedly called Ansari home and tried to molest him. “Ansari, who didn't like these advances, allegedly removed his scissors from his bag, stabbed Sultanpuri in his throat and fled,“ assistant commissioner of police Arun Chavan said.
 
 Ansari, who was naked, ran downstairs and took a taxi home, said the police. Police sources said they could book Sultanpuri for sexual assault if Ansari filed a complaint.
