PUNE: The   police on Saturday arrested a 42-year-old man for raping his 15-year-old daughter over the past two months.

 The suspect has been charged under Section 376 (rape) of the Indian Penal Code and the relevant sections of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act.
 
 An officer from the Nigdi police told TOI that the suspect is a widower. He has been staying in Nigdi with his minor daughter. “The victim stated in her complaint that for the past two months her father was raping her in their house,” the officer said.
 
 The suspect used to beat her up and threaten the minor to not reveal his deeds to anyone. “On Friday, the girl informed a woman in the neighbourhood of her situation. The woman then consulted a social organization,” the officer said.
 
 The organisation workers took the girl to the police station and lodged a complaint. “We have registered the offence and arrested the suspect. Our probe is on,” the officer said.
