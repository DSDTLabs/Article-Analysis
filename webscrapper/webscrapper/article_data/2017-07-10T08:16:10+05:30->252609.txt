GUWAHATI: The first battalion of the   (NDRF) conducted a drill on school safety at  , Bongaigaon in lower Assam recently. 

 As children in schools are among the vulnerable groups during any disaster, about 340 students and 10 teachers were imparted training during the exercise, where the NDRF personnel made presentations on school safety, disaster in general, drop cover and hold method during earthquake, demo on basic life support, bleeding control, emergency moves, improvised rescue aids and school evacuation drill. 
 
 "Disasters often strike without warning and leave a trail of devastation. The loss of life and damage to property can be reduced by sensitizing local population, preparedness and preventive actions to be taken in case of a disaster. No task is as important as creating safe learning environment for our nation's children," said an NDRF statement.
