BHOPAL: A 16-year-old girl committed suicide by hanging herself at her house in Parwalia locality on Wednesday afternoon. The kin rushed her to a nearby house where doctors declared her brought dead. 

 To avoid any post-mortem examination, the kin concocted a story that the teenager fell on ground and died. However, after finding marks on her neck, police sent the body for post-mortem.
