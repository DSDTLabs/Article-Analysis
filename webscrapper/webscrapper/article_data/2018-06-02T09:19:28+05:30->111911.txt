NEW DELHI: The Board of Secondary Education   ( )   has released the Rajasthan Eligibility Exam for Teacher (REET) Cut Off marks.

 
 The REET district wise Level 2 cut off is released on the official website http://education.rajasthan.gov.in. 
 
 Candidates can check and download their REET 2017 from the official website. 
 
 The REET exam commonly conducted for third-grade teacher in the state of Rajasthan. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 This year the REET exam was held in two shifts, first Morning Shift was started from 10:00 AM to 12:30 PM and the Evening Shift exam was started from 2:30 PM to 5:00 PM. The REET exam was of two and a half hours.
