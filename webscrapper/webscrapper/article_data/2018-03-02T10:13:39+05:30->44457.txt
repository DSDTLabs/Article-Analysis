 The alleged Naxalites were killed in a joint operation by Telangana Police and Chhattisgarh Police in Pujari Kanker in the state's   district. A policeman was injured in the operation.
 
 Earlier this week, three security personnel were injured in an encounter with Naxals in Chhattisgarh’s Dantewada district. That gun-battle took place Monday afternoon on a hillock between Mungari and Dulargufa in Dantewada.
 
 Acting on a tip-off, a joint team of the Special Task Force and the District Reserve Guard raided a Naxal hideout on the hillock, about 18km from Barsoor, police said. 
 
 “During the raid, there was a heavy exchange of fire. The troops fought back bravely and destroyed the hideout,” the DIG said. “Three jawans sustained bullet injuries in the gunfight,” an IPS officer said.
