  While Lipstick... failed to get clearance from India’s censor board, and will not be releasing in India, it also managed to win the Audience Choice Award for Best Feature at the 10th edition of CinemAsia. The festival director, Lorna Tee while introducing Lipstick Under My Burkha to Amsterdam audiences said, “At CinemAsia… we try to bring together the different narratives that are coming out from Asia. Lipstick Under My Burkha is currently being banned by the Indian film censors. We find this unfortunate, and we strongly oppose this. We believe that films are voices. And voices should not be censored.” 
 
 
  
  
  Tee also added that the Audience Choice Award for Best Feature Film, is being given to Lipstick.. as it “is one our most beloved films with one of the highest rated films ever by our audience. It is a testament to the relevance and resonance of the film powerful subject matter and characters that break barriers beyond borders.”
