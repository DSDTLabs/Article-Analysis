RAJKOT: For the convenience of commuters, one separate Computerized Reservation Counter will be set up at Kanalus   from Thursday. According to railway officials, reservation tickets can be booked from this counter on all weekdays from 8 am to 4 pm.

 “At present there is one counter of PRS cum UTS (Passenger Reservation System cum Unreserved Ticketing System) already functional at   station for purchasing both reserved as well as unreserved tickets. This separate Reservation Counter will be of immense help to the local public as there will be different counters for booking of reserved and unreserved tickets,” said an official.
