Rajkot: A 23-year-old youth was hacked to death with a sword by his friend at Khar Gate area in Bhavnagar on Sunday night. The victim died during treatment early morning on Monday. Ajay Baraiya alias Nanku, a resident of Mafatnagar area near Prabhudas Talav and accused as Deepak Bambhania (25), a Khar Gate resident were friends. “Deepak was asking Ajay to give him money to buy liquor which he refused. Incensed, Deepak attacked him with a sword. Ajay was rushed to Sir T Hospital where he died during treatment,” said a Bhavnagar C-Division police official. tnn
