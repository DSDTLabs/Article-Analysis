BENGALURU: Armed with reports from Bhopal-based National Institute of High Security Animal Diseases (NIHSAD), the   government on Friday announced that it was “safe to eat country chicken”. However, it is yet to come clear on farm chickens.

 Awaiting results regarding farm chickens
 
 Allaying fears about outbreak of avian flu among chickens being sold in and around Bengaluru, animal husbandry minister   said: “We had sent the samples of country chickens collected from   district amd farm chickens from Yelahanka and   to NIHSAD for tests. The results pertaining to country chickens are negative. We are awaiting results about farm chickens.” tnn
