The floral bloom, which comes with the arrival of spring, was on full display in the recent   in Gurgaon organised by the Haryana Urban Development Authority (HUDA) at the  . Titled  , the flower show was held over the weekend and had hundreds of Gurgaon residents reach the venue despite a sudden increase in the temperature over the last few days. Amit, a Sector 14 resident, said, "It's much hotter as compared to last year, but good to see that people’s enthusiasm is high. I come here every year because a day spent here is always refreshing." The flower show had hundreds of colourful flowers of several varieties, showcased in various categories, apart from stalls selling various kinds of potted plants, seeds, and other gardening equipment. Innovative flower arrangements, such as those in the shape of a peacock and a snail, proved to be quite popular among the visitors.
