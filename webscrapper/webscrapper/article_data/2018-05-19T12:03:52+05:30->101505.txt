 Investigating officer assistant sub-inspector Simranjeet Kaur said on Thursday they received a call that a boy near Sahnewal bus stand was begging with four men and he looked very scared.
 
 The police have registered a case under sections 363-A (kidnapping a minor for purpose of begging), 365 (kidnapping or abducting with intent secretly and wrongfully to confine person), 34 (act done by several people in furtherance of common intension) of the IPC.
 
 On Thursday late evening, the police handed over the boy to his family. The ASI said so far the police have not been able to find the criminal background of the accused.
