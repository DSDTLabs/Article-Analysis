Megastar Chiranjeevi's comeback flick, which also happens to be his 150th, ' ' is having a grand pre-release event at Haiiland on Vijayawada-Guntur highway. News is that family members and the entire unit are leaving no stone unturned to make sure that the veteran is welcomed back in style amidst his fans. For that matter, the actor-producer, Ram Charan Tej and the family members have annnounced a special welcome dance for the actor where, a 150 minutes dance performance to 150 of his hit songs will be performed from all his movies. The mega comeback is planned elaborately as 150 dancers too, were roped in for the feat. The event is said to kick start with this special performance. The event is set to begin on January 7 from 5 pm onwards.
