 Police said the deceased, S Veera alias Veerabathiran of Merku Thottathu Veethi at Kadapadi near Sulur, was a drunkard. “Veera was working as an earthmoving machine operator. He had married Pandirani four years ago and the couple had two sons, V Ramu and V Lakshmanan, both aged about two-and-a-half-year old. Veera was an alcoholic. Upset over the habit, his wife used to quarrel with him and pleaded with him to quit the habit. Unable to bear the mental torture, she had left to her mother’s home along with her sons three months ago,” a police officer said.
 
 On Tuesday, Veera had gone to his mother in-law’s house and requested his wife to stay with him. “However, she refused. Subsequently, he took his sons with him to his house and poisoned them. He also killed himself by consuming poison,” the officer told TOI.
 
 Before committing suicide, the man called his wife and told her that he has administered poison to the children and would soon commit suicide. The woman alerted his relatives and rushed home only to find her husband and sons dead.
 
 Sulur police sent the bodies to the Coimbatore Medical College and Hospital (CMCH) for postmortem. Police have registered a case and further investigation is on.
