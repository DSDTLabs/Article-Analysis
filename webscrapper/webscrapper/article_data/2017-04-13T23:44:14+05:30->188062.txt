Gandhinagar: Top leaders of the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS), including the sarsanghchalak Mohan Bhagwat, sah-sarsanghchalak Suresh Soni, all the state in-charges of the RSS, and members of the Sangh thinktank, Bharatiya Vichar Manch (BVM), will take part in a two-day national seminar on April 15 and 16 at Ahmedabad. 

 The BVM has organised the seminar whose topic will be ‘Decolonising the Indian Mind’. Bhagwat and Soni will address around 1000 top RSS leaders who will come from across the country. 
 
 The BVM will discuss the influence of colonial thought in education, literature, art, social issues, administration and the global image of the country. The seminar is important as in the last national executive of the RSS, ‘How to Decolonise the Indian Mind’’ was considered a key topic. tnn
