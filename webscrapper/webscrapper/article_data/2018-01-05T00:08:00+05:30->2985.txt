Jaipur: A 2-year-old   slipped off from the lap of his father and fell down into a well near Parvat Wali Dhani under Bassi police station area. The villager sand his father managed to bail him out of the well, however the boy was drowned. Locals said that the villagers and others took almost 20 minutes to start the rescue for the drowning child and the delay cost them a life.
