The third day of the on-going cultural extravaganza   saw artistes from   performing folk dance forms   and   at   premises. The artistes donning colourful outfits used props like matki (pitchers) and chata (umbrellas) to give visually appealing performance. While the male folks wearing black dhotis performed holding sticks, the women artistes danced balancing sword in the mouth and matkas on the head which had the audience in awe.
