Vasco: The air intelligence unit of Goa Customs on Wednesday seized gold worth Rs 17 lakh from two different passengers flying from the United Arab Emirates. 

 According to officials, both are Bhatkal natives. This is the third case reported in a week's time. On Wednesday, 374 grams of gold, worth Rs 10 lakh was seized in the form of jewellery and in the raw state, which was concealed inside the handle of a non-stick frying pan and two door handles. The passenger was flying Qatar Airways from Doha via Dubai.
 
 In the second case, another Bhatkal native, flying Air India from Dubai, was found with 262 grams of gold worth Rs 7 lakh. The gold was concealed in the handles of non-stick cookware. tnn
