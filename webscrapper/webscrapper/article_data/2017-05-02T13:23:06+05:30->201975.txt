Director   Thomas’ ventures have been such that it has managed to amass a lot of attention, among the audience, and carved a niche for himself as well. The young filmmaker got hitched to Fibi Kochupurackal on May 1, anid the pristine aura of Wayanad. 

 The wedding was held at St Sebastian’s Church, Paralikkunnu, near Kumbalakkad, at Wayanad, and it was a simple, yet joyous affair. The guests list included only friends, family and well-wishers, and it was not starry in any way at all. 
 
 Midhun had scripted Ohm Shanti Oshaana, and donned the director’s cap for films such as Aadu, Ann Mariya Kalippilaanu and Alamara.
