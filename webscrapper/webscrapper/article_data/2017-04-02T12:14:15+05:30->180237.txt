FARIDABAD: Society for the prevention of cruelty to animals ( ), in coordination with  , Chandigarh, organised a adoption-cum-rehabilitation camp for animals. In the camp, five dogs were adopted and one horse and one goat were rehabilitated. 

 The horse and goat were found injured along the roadside by SPCA volunteers. The these animals have been rehabilitated by the shelter home named "All Creature Great and Small" situated in Faridabad.
