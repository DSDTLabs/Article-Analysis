VADODARA: The city got some respite from the extreme heat with temperatures going down marginally on Friday as compared to Thursday. Vadodara recorded a maximum temperature of 42.8 degrees Celsius. While this was lower than the 43.4 degrees C recorded on Thursday, the temperature remained four degrees higher than the normal. The minimum temperature recorded on Friday was equal to that recorded on Thursday at 24 degrees C. This was higher than the normal temperature by one degree. According to forecasts by the  , the temperature is expected to remain above normal for the next few days.
