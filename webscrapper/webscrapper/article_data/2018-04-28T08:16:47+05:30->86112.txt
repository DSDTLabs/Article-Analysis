 14 Indians in first list of qualifiers for Worlds
 Fourteen Indian shuttlers have made it to the first list of the qualifiers for the BWF World Championships in Nanjing from July 30 to August 5.
 
 Sindhu, Saina, Srikanth, Prannoy and Sai Praneeth made the cut in the singles category while two Indian pairs qualified in three doubles categories.
 
 List of qualifiers
 Men’s singles: Kidambi Srikanth, HS Prannoy and B Sai Praneeth. Doubles: Satwiksairaj Rankireddy, Chirag Shetty, Manu Atrri and Sumeeth Reddy.
 
 Women’s singles: PV Sindhu and Saina Nehwal. Doubles: Ashwini Ponnappa, Sikki Reddy, Jakkampudi Meghana and Poorvisha Ram. Mixed doubles: Pranaav Jerry Chopra, Sikki Reddy, Ashwini Ponnappa and Satwiksairaj.
