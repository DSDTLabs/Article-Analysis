 Hundreds of protesters across the district reached the Police Memorial, where deployed police personnel prevented them from staging a dharna for indefinite period. The agitated women protesters raised slogans against the administration and blocked the road. Due to this commuters travelling between Nangli crossroad and Mini Ka Baad faced inconvenience. Several schools buses were also stuck in the traffic jam.
 
 Later, the protesters reached Collector Gate, where they made voluntary arrests. The protesters also submitted their memorandum to additional district magistrate Mahendra Meena. Anganwadi workers president Latesh Sharma announced that if the state government will not fufill their demands they would intensify the protest.
