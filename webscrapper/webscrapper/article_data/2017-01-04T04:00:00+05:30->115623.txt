Ahmedabad: Indian Air Force (IAF) will conduct rehearsal of air show on Saturday as a part of pre-cursor activity of Vibrant Gujarat Global Summit-2017. This exercise will be a dress-rehearsal for the actual event which will take place on January 9. “The 40 minute rehearsal of the air show will start at 11am over the Swarnim Park in Gandhinagar,” siad a statement of Ministry of Defence (MoD). The show would comprise a para-drop from a MI-17 V5 helicopter by paratroopers of the Akash Ganga team, dropping of petals by a chetak helicopter, aerobatics display by a SU-30 MKI aircraft and a aerobatics formation show by Surya Kiran Aerobatics Team. tnn
