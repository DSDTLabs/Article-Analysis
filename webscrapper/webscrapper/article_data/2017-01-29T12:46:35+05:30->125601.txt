Trishya Screwvala, daughter of Ronnie and Zarine, is getting married this Thursday to her long-time sweetheart and leading sports commentator Suhail Chandok. Chandok belongs to a family of racers ' his father Vicky was one; his brother Karun races overseas at the moment ' while the elegant Trishya runs the Lighthouse Project that connects mentors with street children. The wedding will be held at the Turf Club, in Mahalaxmi, and big names from both the film and sports fraternity are T expected to attend. Congratulations are in order for the couple.
