Gurugram: A man was booked on Saturday for allegedly   his   in an inebriated state and threatening to kill them if the matter was disclosed. Rajesh Kumar (42), a resident of Bajghera, allegedly hit his 10- and 12-year-old sons with a belt until one of them fell unconscious on Thursday.

 According to police, Rajesh’s wife was away at her brother’s house in Delhi at the time. Her father-in-law called her up on Friday morning and told her about the thrashing.
 
 “My sons are studying in classes IV and VI in a reputed school in the city. When I came to know of what my husband had done, I hurried to meet my sons at their school on Friday. They told me that Rajesh came home drunk on Thursday night and start thrashing them with a belt over some petty issue. My father-in-law tried to intervene, but Rajesh kept thrashing them. My younger son fell unconscious. He said his father held him under a water tap and turned it on to make him regain consciousness. I want strict action against my husband,” the woman told police in her complaint.
 
 The accused also allegedly threatened to kill the boys if they divulged the matter to anyone. 
 
 Rajesh’s wife filed a police complaint on Saturday, on the basis of which an FIR was registered against him under sections 323 (causing hurt) and 506 (criminal intimidation) of IPC and Section 75 (punishment for cruelty to child) of Juvenile Justice Act at Bajghera police station the same day.
 
 “We registered a case against Rajesh Kumar on Saturday and are verifying the facts. The accused will be arrested soon,” said inspector  , SHO of Bajghera police station.
