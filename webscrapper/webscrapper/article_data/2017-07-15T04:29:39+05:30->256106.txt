Navi Mumbai: Youngsters who have not enrolled their names in the voters’ list can now do so on   during a drive to be held in the entire Thane district. 

 The election office has roped in colleges, skill development centres, civic ward offices, malls, ST depots and railway stations to conduct the special drive. The focus is on college students to get them enrolled in large numbers. 
 
 Eligible voters in the age group of 18-21 can also enroll till July 31 at designated centres. 
 
  “More than 1.70 lakh students are expected to take advantage of the month-long enrollment drive,” said  , deputy election officer, Thane district. Voter ID cards are to be given to the students in a month which will help them get other documents like Aadhar Card, Passport and driving licence.
