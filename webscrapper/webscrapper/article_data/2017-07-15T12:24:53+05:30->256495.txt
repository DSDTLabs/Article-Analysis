JAIPUR: After a hiatus, the monsoon has become active in the state again much to the relief of farmers and the common man. The regional Met office has warned of heavy to very heavy rainfall at a few places in the state in the next 24 hours. The state forecast said rain and thunder showers are likely to occur at most places in East Rajasthan and at a few places in West Rajasthan.
