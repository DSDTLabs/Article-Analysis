MANIPAL: Syndicate Bank conducted a national-level seminar in Hindi on government scheme at Syndicate Institute of Bank Management (SIBM) on Monday. Priyanka Mary Francis, CEO, Udupi zilla panchayat, was the chief guest. 

 The subject of the seminar was various important initiatives of the government in socio -economic development. The sub topics were ‘Make in India’, ‘Digital India’, ‘Stand up India’, ‘Swachh Bharat Abhiyan’, ‘Mudra Yojna’, ‘Beti Bachao-Beti Padhao Yojana’, ‘Pradhan Mantri Aawas Yojna’, ‘Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana’, ‘Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana’ and ‘Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana’. 
 
 Participants of various banks/financial institutions made power point presentations on all sub-topics in this seminar. 
 
 A book published in Hindi on ‘Alternate Delivery Channels - a Boon and Challenge for Bankers and Customers’ was released on this occasion. Tekchand, deputy director, deparment of official language, Union ministry of home affairs was the special invitee. Subramani R, GM, Syndicate Bank, head office presided over the seminar. Achyutananda Das, GM, Satish Kamath, field GM, Manipal were also present in the seminar. Delegates from various nationalized banks and financial institutions of all over India took part in the seminar.
