RAIPUR:   ( ) has issued notices to 23 doctors for violation of the government norms regarding the compulsory government service after completing post-graduation through the state quota. 

 Council president Dr   said that the state council has issued notices to 23 doctors for the violation of government norms. The doctors have completed their PG course after getting admission through the state quota. They have also furnished a bond for serving government in service for a period of two years or to pay Rs 25 lakh. 
 
 These doctors have neither joined their duties nor deposited the bond money so far and are doing private practice, which is violation of state quota rules, he said adding that they have been asked to reply within one week, failing which their registration will be cancelled for two years.
