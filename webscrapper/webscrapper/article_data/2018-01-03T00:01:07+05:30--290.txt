Kolhapur: Three persons were killed and three others injured after the driver rammed the car against a tree on the Pune-Bangalore National Highway at Nipani in Karnataka, in the wee hours of Tuesday.

 Police suspect the driver lost control of the car causing the accident.
 
 The deceased have been identified as Savitri Gupta (47), Shobha Gupta (26) and Arati Gupta (21).
 
 The injured have been identified as Nilesh Gupta (27), Ravi Gupta (23) and the driver Surya Sawu (31). The injured have been admitted to a private hospital in Kolhapur.
 
 A police officer attached with Nipani police station said, “Passerby informed us about the accident, following which we reached the spot. All the doors of the car were locked. We had to break the door to remove the people trapped inside.”
 
 The officer said, “Savitri was found dead on the spot while Shobha and Arati succumbed to their injuries on the way to the hospital. All the victims were taken to Chhatrapati Pramila Raje (CPR) civil hospital where the hospital authorities declared them brought dead.”
 
 He added, “The three injured were later on sent to a private hospital for treatment. They are under observation.”
 
 The officer said, “All the victims except the driver belong to the same family. They were travelling from Goa to Mumbai after celebrating the New Year. The family belong to Vikroli area. We are gathering more information about their exact address.”
