Panaji: A 23-year-old man was killed at   on Tuesday afternoon, when the two-wheeler he was travelling on was hit by a truck.

 Jeevan Colaco was riding pillion, while Mousinho Fernandes was riding the two-wheeler. At Porvorim, near the KTM showroom, the duo was hit by a truck from the rear. Colaco died on the spot, while Fernandes was taken to Goa Medical College and Hospital (GMC) for treatment. Fernandes, who had sustained minor injuries, was discharged after treatment.
