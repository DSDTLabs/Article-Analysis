A 14-year-old class IX student allegedly committed suicide by hanging herself from the ceiling fan using a dupatta at her   residence on late on Wednesday. 

 Senior inspector Dilip Kale said, “In her suicide note, the teenager wrote that she was ending her life as she was depressed after failing in three subjects in her final exams.The results were out on March 27.” 
 
 He added, “The post-mortem report stated cause of death due to asphyxiation. We have registered an accidental death case.” tnn
