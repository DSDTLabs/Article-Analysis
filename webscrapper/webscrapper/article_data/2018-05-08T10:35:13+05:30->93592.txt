NEW DELHI: Amrita   has declared the results for the Amrita Entrance Examination- Engineering (AEEE) 2018 on May 7, 2018, at 12:30 pm. 

 The Amrita Entrance Examination- Engineering (AEEE) 2018 results were released on the official website of the university. 
 
 
 
 The candidates who have appeared for the AEEE 2018 can visit the official website to check their result. The candidates should note that the counseling call letter can be downloaded on Wednesday, May 9, 2018.
 
 The Amrita Engineering Entrance Exam (AEEE) 2018 was conducted by the university from April 19 to April 22 and the Pen & Paper based (Offline) test was conducted on April 28, 2018. 
 
 
 
 
 1) Visit the official website of the university - www.amrita.edu
 
 2) On homepage, you will find the   link, click on it
 
 3) It will redirect you to a new webpage
 
 4) Enter your registration number, date of birth, code as seen on the page and click on submit
 
 5) Your result will be displayed online
 
 6) Download it or take a printout for futher reference
