Yavatmal: During the review meeting of the officers of the government run Ashram Shalas in the district on Monday,   district collector Sachindra Pratap Singh expressed is concern over their deteriorating condition. He has even given assent to putting on hold the annual increment of a number of offices and employees who allegedly abstained from their work places wilfully. 

 The death of class ten student Pavan Tekam of the village of Jatala in the Ashram Shala tank by drowning was brought to the notice of the collector. Singh has directed officers to set up various committees.
