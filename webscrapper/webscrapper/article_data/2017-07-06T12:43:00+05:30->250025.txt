Famous   reality show Bigg Boss is getting bigger and better every year and this year too it is no different. For the first time, the   has allowed common people to participate in   season 5, through online auditions.
 
  The interested people have to answer several questions like why do you want to be a part of BBK5 and how do you differentiate yourself from others to get qualified. The registration has opened from today. After the audition concludes, a contestant will be allowed to participate in BBK5, which will kick start from September. The show hosted by Kichcha Sudeep will be aired on Colors Kannada channel.
