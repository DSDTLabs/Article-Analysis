Gandhinagar: In a move aimed to woo animal husbandry and farming communities in the state, revenue minister Bhupendrasinh Chudasama, on Wednesday, announced in the assembly that around 1.41 lakh unauthorized animal shelters in villages will be regularized.

 The minister, while speaking on the budgetary demands of the revenue department, also said, “The government will soon start issuing property cards in city survey areas.” 
 
 In four cities, namely Ahmedabad, Surat, Rajkot and Vadodara, dedicated revenue service civic centres will be started soon. Chudasama also said that through Integrated Land Management System(ILMS) all old land records will be digitized in the coming days.
