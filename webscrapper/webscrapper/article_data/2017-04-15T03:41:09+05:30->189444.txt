THANE: A two-and-a-half-year-old lost his life after a truck rammed into a two-wheeler on which he was riding with his father near   on Thursday. 

 Police said that the accused, Parmeshwar Pandurang Olale, a 36-year-old truck driver, has been arrested for negligent driving that led to the death of the deceased, Sankalp Darpan Gund.
 
 It is learnt that the child was on the bike with his father, Darpan Ramesh Gund (31), and they were on their way to Digha. 
 
 Owing to a heavy traffic flow on the stretch, Gund stopped the vehicle on the side of the road and waited for some time for the traffic to ease. 
 
 It was at this moment that Olale, who was recklessly driving the truck, hit the motorcycle from behind. This sudden jerk grievously injured the child while the father sustained minor injuries. 
 
 Senior police inspector Arvind Mugle of Kalwa police station said, “The child was rushed to Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Hospital at Kalwa, but doctors could not revive him. Olale has been arrested and is now under judicial custody at Kalwa.”
 
 The driver has been booked under Sections 337 (causing hurt by act endangering life or personal safety of others), 279 (rash driving) and 426 (punishment for mischief) of the Indian Penal Code.
