SURAT: Probe by the   in a loot case registered in   police station on February 9 revealed it was a fabricated complaint. A goods tempo driver had lodged a complaint of loot after some boxes of electrical goods fell off the tempo during transportation. To avoid paying for the losses the driver lodged a loot complaint with police.

 Investigation revealed that  , 62, a resident of Sumul Dairy Road, was carrying tube lights and fans in his tempo and going from Saroli to   office on February 9. Since the boxes were not tied properly some of them fell and got damaged. Fearing that he will have to pay for damages, Modi fabricated a loot story and lodged loot complaint at Dindoli police station.
