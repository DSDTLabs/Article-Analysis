The recent brutal   rape and murder case has shaken the nation once again. While thousands of Mumbaikars came out seeking justice for the rape victims by staging peaceful protests, they were also joined by some of our   celebrities as well.

 B-town celebs joined the thousands Mumbaikars who had gathered on Sunday for a peaceful protest against the barbaric rape and murder of an eight-year-old in Kathua in Jammu and Kashmir. From amongst the stars who came in to vent their dissent over the fateful incidents were  ,  ,  ,   along with   and Aarav, Kiran Rao, Vishal Dadlani, Anushka Manchanda, Tara Sharma, Kalki Koechlin, Sona Mohapatra,   and others.
 
 The celebs were seen holding placards asking for justice for the eight-year-old and other rape victims. Earlier, Bollywood stars like Kareena Kapoor Khan, Sonam Kapoor, Mini Mathur and others were seen sharing picture and videos on themselves on their social media with placards.
