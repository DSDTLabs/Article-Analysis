Jaisalmer: Five members of a family died on Monday morning when their SUV overturned on the Jaisalmer-Barmer Highway near Devikot village under Sangarh police station area in Jaisalmer district.

 The deceased include two children and a woman. Seven others were injured in the accident. The family was returning to Jalore after offering prayers at a dargah near Ramgarh in Jaisalmer district.
 
 According to police, on seeing an overspeeding vehicle coming from the opposite direction, the SUV applied sudden brakes due to which it overturned.
 
 Sangarh police and ambulance reached the spot. 
 
 Three died on the spot, while two died while undergoing treatment at the district hospital.
 
 The deceased have been identified as Roshni Bai (28), Arban (4), Amina (12), Mahboob Khan (34) and Wasir Khan (42). 
 
 All injured have been admitted to district hospital. Two seriously injured patients have been referred to Jodhpur.
