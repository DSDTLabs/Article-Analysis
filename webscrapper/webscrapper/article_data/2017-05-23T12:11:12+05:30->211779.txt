The Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education (RBSE) declared senior secondary (Class XII) board results today. 

 According to the board, there were 2,34,523 students registered in class XII science and 48,113 for class XII commerce. 
 
 The board has done away with publication of merit list for the examinations it conducts, starting this year.
 
 The results are available on the website of the Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education and also on different websites related to results.
