DHARAMSHALA: Tibetans have now planned a major event for March 17 to mark the escape of their spiritual leader the Dalai Lama from   to India. They had earlier planned ‘Thank You India’ programme in Delhi this month end, but it was shifted from there days after a government note to senior officers to stay away from events being organized by Tibetans.

 A statement released by the department of information and international relation on its official website stated that the   Lama would also be attending the event.
