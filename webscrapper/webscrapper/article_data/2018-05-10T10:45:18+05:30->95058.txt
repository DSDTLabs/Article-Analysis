AMRITSAR: A woman has accused a sarpanch of a village near Amritsar of blackmailing her after making an objectionable video. “The sarpanch met me at a marriage function. He kept me in the dark and abused me,” alleged the woman while talking to media persons here on Wednesday. 

 She said sarpanch also threatened to make her video go viral on social media if she didn’t give in to his demands. She further alleged that the sarpanch allegedly took Rs 9 lakh from her on one or the other pretext. Amritsar deputy commissioner of police (DCP)   said police were investigating the allegations leveled by the woman and would take action accordingly.
