Vadodara: A teenage boy and a girl believed to be in love ended their lives by hanging themselves from a tree in a Dahod district village. The police has registered the case as an accidental death and is conducting further investigations.

 The incident came to light on Monday morning at the Nani Ranapur village of Dahod taluka. The bodies of Vijay Bhuria (18) from Ukardi village and Janki Hathila (17) from Nani Ranapur, were found hanging from a mango tree in the Mehndi Falia.
 
 The bodies of the victims were sent for an autopsy. While the reason for the suicide is not known, it is believed that the victims were in love. Dahod taluka police is conducting further investigations into the case.tnn
