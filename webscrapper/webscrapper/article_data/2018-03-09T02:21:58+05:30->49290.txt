NEW DELHI: The first section of Delhi Metro’s   Line between Majlis Park and Durgabai Deshmukh South Campus is going to open for the public next  .

 The 20.6km section has 12 stations and will connect north Delhi to south Delhi. The section was inspected by the Commissioner for   Rail Safety between February 26 and 28. 
 
 “DMRC has received clearance from the CMRS for opening the Majlis Park-Durgabai Deshmukh South Campus metro section,” said Anuj Dayal, executive director (corporate communications), DMRC. This section includes three interchange stations — Azadpur, Netaji Subhash Place and Rajouri Garden.
