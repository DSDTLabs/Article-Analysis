Around 5,000 Hyderabadis from all walks of life hit the streets in support of empowerment of girl children at the recently-held 10k and 5k runs. The ‘Run for Girl Child’ was organised in city by a non-profit  , Telangana to promote awareness about the ‘Kishori Vikas Yojna’ — the initiative 
of the   that seeks to empower adolescent girls. During the flagging off of the run,  , general secretary of Seva Bharathi, said, “This run is aimed at adding 400 Kishori Vikas centres to the existing 100 centres in various parts of Hyderabad. These centres work to empower girl children residing in slums and low income areas by helping them with their education, honing their skills and help them lead a healthy life.”
