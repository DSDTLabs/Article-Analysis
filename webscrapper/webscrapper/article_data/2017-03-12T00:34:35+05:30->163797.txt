Four persons, including a degree student, were arrested by Task Force police near Osmania University as they were transporting 420 kg   and Rs 11 lakh cash. Ch Pavan, a degree student, V Naresh, Y Narasaiah and K Srinivas were arrested, while kingpin G Nagaraju, a resident of Habsiguda, is absconding. The gang had modified lorries and used to hide the ganja in the base while loading the top with empty plastic vegetable crates to fool the cops. Police also seized three lorries. tnn
