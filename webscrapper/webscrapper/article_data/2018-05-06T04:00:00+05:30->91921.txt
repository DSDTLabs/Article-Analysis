A man was stabbed to death in camp area of Bhuj on Friday evening. Police identified the deceased as Mamad Suregi (40) and accused as Rohan Sheikh (28). Police said both the victim and the accused are relatives and the murder was result of a domestic dispute. Sheikh was assisted in the crime by his mother Abida Sheikh, following which she, too, was booked for the crime. Sheikh used to beat his wife, who complained about it to Suregi, who was her maternal uncle. When Suregi took up the issue with him, Sheikh attacked him with a knife.
