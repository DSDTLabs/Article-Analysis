Panaji: Reclamation of low-lying land in Khadapwada of Chorao island is being carried out by some persons in blatant violation of Section 17-A of the Town and Country Planning Act, sources said on Thursday. The unauthorized land filling is being done in property bearing survey no. 308/1. After receiving complaints regarding the violation, the flying squad visited the site and carried out an inspection on January 2 this year. Based on the report submitted by the flying squad, the deputy collector issued a notice to the property owner to explain why action should not be taken against him for restoration of the land.  The person reclaiming the land has been directed to halt work with immediate effect. The property owner has also been asked to produce the requisite documents to prove that the reclamation had been permitted by the town and country planning (TCP) department.
