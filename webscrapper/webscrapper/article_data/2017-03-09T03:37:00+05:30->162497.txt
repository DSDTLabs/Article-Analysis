Vasco: A fire broke out on the top floor of an automobile showroom at Verna on Wednesday evening. Verna PI Uday Parab said, “The fire started in the wiring of the building and spread through the plaster of paris, and reached the computers. It was soon brought under control.”

 At around 6.40pm, Verna police were alerted to a fire. Within 20 minutes of reaching the accident site, the Verna emergency and fire services personnel doused the fire. Property worth lakhs of rupees was saved and nobody was injured.
 
  The fire services personnel are still assessing the extent of damage and the loss caused by the fire. The fire station staff averted any further disaster with their timely action. Only a few electronic gadgets and some portion of the building was damaged.
