AHMEDABAD: In two separate incidents of robbery — which took place on Saturday in Odhav and   — robbers fled with cash and valuables worth Rs 12,000. However, in one of the incidents a man who was robbed at knife point was injured with a minor cut on his throat.

  According to police sources, an auto driver and his two accomplices threatened Jagdish Tiwari at knife point and robbed him of Rs 1,000 and his cell phone. In another case, two bike borne robbers, snatched a bag containing Rs 10,000 cash from 59-year-old Ajay Chaturvedi near Kotarpur   on Saturday night.
