NOIDA: A 22-year-old woman has filed a complaint against her ex-boyfriend for allegedly posting her   on social networking sites after their breakup. 

 Both work in a private company in Sector 8.
 
 According to the complainant, she was in a relationship with the accused,   (25), for one and a half years. 
 
 “We had a breakup in June over some differences and decided to part ways. But, he started pressuring me to continue the relationship. He posted my personal photos on Facebook when I ignored his request,” the woman said. 
 
 The complainant, a resident of Mayur Vihar Phase I, said the accused has been troubling her for the past two months. 
 
 The accused lives in Pandav Nagar in Delhi.
