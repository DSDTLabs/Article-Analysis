GHAZIABAD: A massive fire erupted in a chemical factory in   in Kavi Nagar around 10pm on Sunday. While no casualties were reported, the blaze destroyed goods worth lakhs of rupees, according to estimates. 

 As many as 15 fire tenders were rushed to the spot from Kotwali and   fire stations. However, the cause behind the fire could not be ascertained.

 When the last reports came in, the fire-fighting was still in progress.
