Trichy: Three men, including a pedestrian, were killed in a road accident near Trichy on Saturday evening.

 According to police, a car travelling from Thanjavur to Trichy, in which three people were travelling, lost control near Thuvakkudi and crashed against the central median. The car flew to the other side of the highway and hit a pedestrian. Two travellers in the car and the pedestrian died on the spot. The other person who was travelling in the car fell unconscious and was taken to a hospital where he is undergoing treatment.
