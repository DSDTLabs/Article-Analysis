HYDERABAD: Customs sleuths apprehended a   smuggler at   on Sunday night and seized 379 grams of gold from his possession.

 While checking the luggage of the offender, who arrived from Dubai on an Indigo flight (6E 025) on Sunday night, customs sleuths detected the presence of a suspicious object in a domestic gas cylinder regulator. The officials then pulled aside the luggage, opened the regulator in the presence of the offender and recovered six gold buttons which were concealed in it. They have filed a case against the smuggler under the Customs Act.
