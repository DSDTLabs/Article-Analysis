Chennai: A school student Shajahan, 14, a resident of Dasarathapuram in Saligramam, was injured after portion of a balcony fell on him on Friday. 

 Police said, Shajahan, studying in Class IX at a private school, was walking down the road towards his house on Friday when a portion of a concrete slab from the second floor of a house fell on him. 
 
 After the concrete slab collapsed, some of the bricks also fell on him. As the debris fell on Shajahan, he suffered severe injuries on his leg and shoulder. On seeing this accident, residents rescued the boy and rushed him to a private hospital nearby and alerted his parents. 
 
 On information, the Virugambakkam police registered a case and questioned Shajahan. Further investigations are on.
