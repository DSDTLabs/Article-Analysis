Hrithik Roshan-Yami Gautam starrer ' ' may have got less screens compared to Shah Rukh Khan's 'Raees' but that is not hampering the film’s box-office might. 

 
 
 'Kaabil' that opened with Rs 10.43 Cr on day one, has come out with flying colours on day two with as much as Rs 18.67 Cr in its kitty. The film is being screened in just 40 per cent of the theatres across the country, as compared to ‘Raees’ that released alongside this Sanjay Gupta directorial. 
 
 
 ‘Kaabil’ saw a huge jump on day two, it cashed in on positive reactions from the audience. Hrithik's portrayal of a visually-impaired man won appreciation. With weekend round the corner, the graph is expected to go up for this Hrithik-Yami starrer.
