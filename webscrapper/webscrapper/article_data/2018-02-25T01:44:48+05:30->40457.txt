NEW DELHI: A 17-year-old youth was shot in his arm by three youths when he protested against them taking drugs near his shop in   Delhi’s   on Friday afternoon. The accused fled later.

 Police officers said that the victim, Furkan (17), saw three youths in the morning, aged 18-19, consuming drugs in a park as he was going towards his shop.
 
 “I shouted at them and had an argument following which they left the place and I also went to my welding shop,” Furkan said.
 
 Around 11.30am, one of the accused reached the shop and found the boy alone there. He fired at Furkan. The bullet went through the youth’s arm as the accused fled. He was taken to a nearby hospital where the bullet was removed.
 
 Police officers said that a case has been registered against the accused youths.
