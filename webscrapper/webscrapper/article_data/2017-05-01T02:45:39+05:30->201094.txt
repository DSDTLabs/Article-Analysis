GURUGRAM: A 50-year-old man was killed in an   after being mowed down by a truck on Friday on  .

 Madan Singh was on his way to a factory in Narsingpur village, where he worked, along with his nephew Nitesh Singh, 21. The two had reached the fish market in Khandsa Road, when a truck came from behind and hit Madan. 
 
 Nitesh told police that after hitting Madan, the truck did not stop and one of its wheels ran over Madan.. The incident happened around 8.20am on Friday. Nitesh took Madan to General Hospital in Civil Lines, from where he was referred to Safdarjung Hospital. He died during treatment.
 
 An FIR was registered at the Kherki Dhaula police station on Saturday under section 279 (rash driving) and 304A (death by negligence) of the IPC.
