JAIPUR:  , court (No-2), has rejected the bail plea of 15   (RU) students who were booked for rioting among other Sections of IPC after their agitation got violent on Thursday. The court sent the students to 15-day  .

 The agitating students were protesting against the decision of the university to conduct re-examination of five out of the six B.com papers after the incidents of  . The police resorted to lathicharge after the agitation went violent.
 
 The Gandhi Nagar police had arrested 15 students in connection with violence and were booked under various Sections of IPC and were produced before the court on Saturday 
 
 The students were charged with rioting, obstructing public servant in discharge of public functions, knowingly joining or continuing in assembly of five or more persons after it has been commanded to disperse, assault or criminal force to deter public servant from discharge of his duty, voluntarily causing hurt to deter public servant from his duty, disobedience to order duly promulgated by public servant, act endangering life or personal safety of others among others under relevant sections of the IPC.
 
 Rajesh Maharishi, the counsel appeared for the students said, “Today, the court rejected the bail to the students and sent them to judicial custody for 15 days but we will move another petition on Monday again.”
