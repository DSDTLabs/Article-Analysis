JAMMU: Two persons were today arrested and around one and half kg cannabis was seized from them on   in   district of Jammu and Kashmir. 

 A car going to Jammu from Srinagar was intercepted near Jawahir tunnel area in Banihal belt of Ramban district and three packets of cannabis were recovered, police said.
