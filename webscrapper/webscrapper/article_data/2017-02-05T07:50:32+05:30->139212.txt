THIRUVANANTHAPURAM: Health minister K K Shylaja will hold talks with clinical oncologists of the   (RCC) here who have been protesting against the alleged autocratic ways of the RCC director. 

 They had alleged that the director has been introducing changes to the   treatment techniques in the institute without holding discussions. The striking doctors, under the umbrella of Kerala clinical Oncologists joint action council state committee, had decided to go for a state-wide strike and observe "black day" on Saturday, which also happens to be the World Cancer Day. 
 
 The doctors who were protesting said RCC director Dr Paul Sebastian has unilaterally decided to alter the existing methods of diagnosis prevailing in RCC without any support from studies. They have also been alleging that the changes have even sabotaged government policies on cancer treatments.
