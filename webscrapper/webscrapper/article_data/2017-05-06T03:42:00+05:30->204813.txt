Nagpur: The Nagpur Municipal Corporation (NMC) relaid the damaged road in Dhantoli on Friday after being blasted by Nagpur bench of Bombay High Court a day earlier. On Friday, it submitted a report that the road in narrow lane behind Gorakshan Dham, which was damaged during construction of an upcoming hospital in the area, had been repaired.

 A division bench comprising justice Bhushan Gavai and justice Atul Chandurkar then adjourned the hearing till after vacations. It was hearing a PIL filed by Dhantoli Nagrik Mandal through counsel Ashutosh Dharmadhikari regarding mess created due to haphazard growth of hospitals in the area, which was leading to severe traffic congestion and parking problems.
 
 On Thursday, the court had given NMC 24 hours to re-lay the road or face the music. Earlier, petitioners had pointed out that inferior quality of material had been used to re-lay the road.
