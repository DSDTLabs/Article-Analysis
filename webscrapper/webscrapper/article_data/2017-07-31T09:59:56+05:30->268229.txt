KEONJHAR: A 25-day-old male Juang child was seriously hurt and admitted in the   district headquarters hospital on Saturday night after being branded with a hot coconut by his family. The child, born to parents who hail from the remote village of Kharaba under Harichandanpur block, about 65km from here, is undergoing treatment in the paediatric ward of the hospital.

 According to his family, the child had a wound on his stomach which they branded when other traditional cures failed.
 
 This is the second such incident to be reported in the last two days. On Friday, a seven-year-old tribal boy was admitted in the district headquarters hospital after being branded with a hot iron rod in   of Banspal block.
 
 Chief district medical officer K P Sahu said the condition of the child was stable.
