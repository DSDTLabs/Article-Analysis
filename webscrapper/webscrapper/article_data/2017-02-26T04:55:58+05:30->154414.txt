Noida: The   ( ), a residents’ body, has sought rationalisation of hospital and diagnostic bills. 

 In a letter to Union health minister   on Saturday, the ICC called for all charges for treatments and tests being mentioned on the  . “A printed booklet with all the information regarding charges and rates, which include doctor’s fee and room charges, should be made available,” ICC president P S Jain said.
 
 Hospitals should allow the patient or their attendant to decide where to get the medicine from, said Prashant Tyagi, ICC general secretary.
 
 “Hospitals charge on MRP whereas in the market they are available at a discount of 30% to 50% ,” Tyagi said. The ICC also said that every hospital should have a designated space for attendants, especially of patients under critical care.
