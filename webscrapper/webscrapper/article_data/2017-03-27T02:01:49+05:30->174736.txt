Lucknow: Huge posse of police took charge after reports of a boy beating a   to death with a   in   locality on Sunday. “The boy began hitting the cow for eating vegetables kept outside his house and didn’t stop till it was dead,” said a DySP deployed in the area after tension mounted in the area. 

 The incident took place in the morning and by noon a crowd began to gather even as some mischief mongers spread rumours. By late night police were struggling to disperse the crowd. District magistrate and SSP monitored the situation and also briefed chief minister Aditya Nath Yogi about the situation. An FIR was registered against the youth for treating the animal so cruelly and police assured sending him to jail.
