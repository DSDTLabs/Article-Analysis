MYSURU: A tribal from   Girijana Rehabilitation Center in Hunsur escaped with minor injuries after a tusker attacked him on Tuesday morning. He has been hospitalised.

 Chandru, 50, from Nagapura, the border area of Nagarahole Tiger Reserve, was with his cattle in his field when a tusker which had strayed into the village attacked him.   managed to escape. However he suffered broken ribs and injuries on his head and legs. Other   in the village admitted him to Hunsur government hospital where he is recovering. Veeranahosahalli ranger forest officer   who visited the hospital said that department will bear the medical expenses of the injured tribal. TNN
