DEHRADUN: A 32-year-old man has been sentenced to five years imprisonment on Monday by the court for Protection of Children from Sexual Offences ( ) Act for sexually assaulting a minor girl in her house in December 2014.

 The court of additional district judge (ADJ)   which exclusively deals with cases of child sexual abuse also imposed a fine of Rs5000 on the convict, Sonu alias  , for committing the crime
 
 The incident dates back to December 18, 2014 when the 11-year-old girl was alone in her house. Knowing that the minor girl is alone, Sonu who lives in neighbourhood broke inside the house and sexually assaulted her.
 
 “The survivor raised an alarm after which people living in vicinity came to her rescue. However, Sonu escaped from there. The convict is an auto-rickshaw while the girl is a sixth standard student.
 
 After the victim narrated her ordeal to her parents, they immediately filed a complaint in police station against the culprit,” said prosecution lawyer Jaya Thakur.
 
 The police managed nab Sonu within couple of days of incident and he was booked under section 376/511 of Indian Penal Code (IPC) and section 7/8 of POCSO Act. During trail, the prosecution examined seven witnessed including the survivor girl to prove the offences.
