RANCHI: The   government set the tone for global investment summit on Wednesday night with Muruawa Khassi, Chilka Roti and Gur Pitha for foreign business delegates. 

 The private dinner for business delegates from several countries, hosted by chief minister Raghubar Das at a city hotel, featured continental and Indian dishes. Traditional tribal cuisines however stood out on the menu list as the government, determined to present an investor friendly image, used   to create “first impression”.
 
 Wednesday’s private dinner was hosted for 150 foreign delegates including representatives from Japan, Tunisia, Mongolia and   – the partner countries for the two day summit. Senior bureaucrats of the state and Union governments also attended the dinner. 
 
 Though media persons were kept away from the event, the hotel management detailed some of the tribal dishes that were served.
