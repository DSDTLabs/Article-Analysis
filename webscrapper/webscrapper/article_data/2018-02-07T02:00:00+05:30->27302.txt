PATNA: At least 85 LKG and UKG students took part in a baby show at Tender Hearts International School on Tuesday.

 Kids, dressed up as Lord Ganesha, Lord Krishna and yellow-coloured flowers, fruits and vegetables, were given certificates. General health and dental check-up camps were also organized on the occasion. 
 
 School director Rajiv Bhargava and teachers were also present.
