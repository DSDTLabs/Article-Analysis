CHANDIGARH: The police have booked three persons of two different companies for allegedly duping people by posing themselves as representatives of software giant  . 

 All three accused, Kunal Bansal, Mohit Bansal and Amanpreet Singh, have their offices in Sector 7. 
 
 Arvind Gopal, corporate external and legal affairs, Microsoft Corporation India Private limited, said the accused ran IT companies called Geek Technical Solution Private Limited and Mark Software Systems Private Limited from the first floor in Sector 7 and  . He alleged both deceived public of providing technical support services by falsely claiming to be certified with Microsoft. "The illegal activities of the accused have been damaging the goodwill and reputation of the company," Gopal alleged. 
 
 The three have been booked under Sections 420 (cheating), 120 B (criminal conspiracy) of the IPC .
