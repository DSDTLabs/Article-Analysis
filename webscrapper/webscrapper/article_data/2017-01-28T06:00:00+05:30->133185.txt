Visakhapatnam: In order to clear extra rush, the railways will run special trains between Kacheguda and Tata Nagar via Visakhapatnam from March 6 to June 27 for 17 trips in each direction.

 Train 07438 Kacheguda-Tata Nagar Weekly Special will leave Kacheguda on Mondays at 1 pm from March 6 to June 26 and reach Visakhapatnam on Tuesdays at 1.30 am. It will depart at 1.50 am (Tuesday) and reach Tata Nagar the same day at 7.45 pm.
 
 In the return direction, train 07439 Tata Nagar-Kacheguda Weekly Special will leave Tata Nagar on Tuesdays at 10.50 pm from March 7 to June 27 and reach Visakhapatnam on Wednesdays 2.50 pm. It will depart from Vizag at 3.30 pm (Wednesday) and reach Kacheguda the next day (Thursday) at 5 am. 
 
 The pair of trains will have stoppages at Duvvada, Visakhapatnam, Vizianagaram, Srikakulam Road, Palasa, Brahmapur, Khurda Road, Bhubaneswar, Cuttack, Sukinda Road and Kendujhargarh over East Coast Railway between Kacheguda and Tata Nagar.
 
 These pair of trains comprises of one 2nd AC, two 3rd AC, seven sleeper class, six general second class and two second class-sitting-cum luggage coaches in their composition.
