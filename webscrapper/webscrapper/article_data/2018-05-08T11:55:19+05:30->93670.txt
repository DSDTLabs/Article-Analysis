NEW DELHI: The Kerala Secondary Education Examination Board (DHSE Board) is likely to declare the Kerala Class 12th result 2018 on Thursday, May 10, 2018. The Kerala HSE results 2018 will be declared on the official website of the DHSE Board - dhsekerala.gov.in

 However, the exact timing of the result could not be confirmed by the officials of DHSE Board. Register below to check your result here:
 
 
  
  
 
 The Board has successfully conducted the Higher Secondary Examination (HSE) for the session year 2017-18 in the month of Feb-March. 
 
 If you are one of the students eagerly waiting for the announcement of DHSE Board HSE Result 2018, you can check it on the official website of the DHSE Board - dhsekerala.gov.in. 
 
 Here's how to get your DHSE Board HSE Result 2018:
 
 OFFICIAL WEBSITES of DHSE Board: You can log on to the official website of DHSE Board - www.dhsekerala.gov.in to check your results.
 
 IVR: Since there will be huge traffic on DHSE Board website on result day, you can try IVR system that provides you with the results via a call.
 
 SMS: Students can also check their DHSE Board HSE result 2018 via SMS too.
 
 SCHOOL WEBSITE: Apart from above three ways, you can also check your DHSE Board result on the local portal of your school.
