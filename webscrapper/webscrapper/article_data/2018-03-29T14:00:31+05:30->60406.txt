MUMBAI: Seven foreign nationals, including a woman, have been arrested at the international terminal of the airport for carrying 360kg of a herbal   they showed as green tea. 

 This is the biggest haul of the stimulant, known as khat, in India, the police said. The leaves, worth Rs 9 crore in the international market, were stuffed in small packets and packed into 18 large luggage bags.
