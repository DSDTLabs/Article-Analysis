NAGPUR: Aadi Chitnis in the boys and Jui Pendharkar in the girls claimed sub-junior titles in the   Ranking   tournament being played at the Shakti Club Hall, Wardha Road, here on Wednesday.

 
 Aadi steamrolled Anagh Bondawe 11-9, 11-7, 11-4, 11-5 in the boys summit clash. Earlier in the semifinals, Aadi thumped   in straight games, while Anagh prevailed over Aaditya Hirulkar in tough five games.
 The sub-junior girls final was a nail-biting affair. Jui had to overcome the strong challenge from   11-6, 7-11, 11-8, 11-6, 14-12 to clinch the crown.
 
 Earlier in the semis, Jui downed Arpita Sapre 11-6, 11-6, 11-7, 8-11, 11-7, while Ananya quelled the spirited challenge from Jennifer Varghese in a seven-game thriller. After both the players won three games each, the seventh and deciding game was a cliff-hanger. Ananya kept her cool in the crucial moments to emerge winner.
 More than hundred paddlers are participating in the annual tournament.
 
 On Thursday, junior boys and girls, youth boys and girls and men’s and women’s singles events will be played followed by the prize distribution function. Corporator Ujwala Sharma will be the chief guest for the concluding programme slated to be held at 8 pm.
