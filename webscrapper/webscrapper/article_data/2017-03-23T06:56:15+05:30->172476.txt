PUNE: The crime branch of the Pune police early on Wednesday arrested three men from a lodge in Rasta Peth and recovered 5 kg opium worth Rs 12.50 lakh from them. They had brought the contraband from Jodhpur in Rajasthan for sale in Pune.

 Acting on a tip-off, a crime branch team led by inspector   arrested   (23),   (25) and   (24) all from Jodhpur in Rajasthan from the lodge,  , assistant commissioner of police (crime), said.
 
 Three teams were constituted to trace the lodge and the suspects.
