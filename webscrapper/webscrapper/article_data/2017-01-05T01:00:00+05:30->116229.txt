Mitaali Nag, who was working till the eighth month of her pregnancy, is now mom to a baby boy. The actress delivered her bundle of joy at 1 am yesterday. 
She told us, “My husband Sankalp and I are ecstatic; we have jotted down a few names and will finalise one soon.” Mitaali, who is currently in her hometown Nagpur, is expected to return to Mumbai in a month. So, will she take a break from acting to enjoy motherhood? “As of now, I am in a blissful phase with my little one. Having said that, I will surely get back to work — God willing, soon,” she replies.
