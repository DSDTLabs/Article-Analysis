One of Bollywood’s favourite filmmakers,   turns 45 today. This year is all the more special for the ace filmmaker as it is his first birthday as a father. And a big birthday bash is on the cards. According to reports, Karan will be hosting a grand party for his Bollywood friends in his house tonight. The guest list includes his favourites, the three Khans – Shah Rukh, Salman and Aamir. The who’s who of the industry has reportedly been invited –   and Twinkle Khanna,   and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan,   and Saif Ali Khan,  , Deepika Padukone,  ,   and Parineeti Chopra among others. Of course, KJo’s ‘students’  , Sidharth Malhotra and Varun Dhawan are a part of the celebration too. 

  
  
  
 The ‘Ae Dil Hai Mushkil’ director apparently flew down from London on Wednesday and personally called up his B-town friends to invite them to his soiree. A dance floor has reportedly been set up in KJo’s pad, along with a DJ and a bar for the big night.
