 The three day survey of the city by Quality Council of India (QCI) officials as part of Swachh Sarvekshan-2017 concluded on Friday. 
 The team headed by QCI assistant manager Kotresh, which arrived on January 4, covered 44 locations of the city and assessed its cleanliness. 
 
 Speaking to STOI, Dr DG Nagaraj, health officer,  , said, “On the last day, the team assessed the documents regarding waste collection and disposal by the civic body. The team visited 16 individual and community toilets and uploaded data and photographs to the Swachh Sarvekshan portal. The MCC procedures are completed by feedback collection from citizens will continue. The whole survey will conclude on February 4 and the result will be announced in February second week.”
