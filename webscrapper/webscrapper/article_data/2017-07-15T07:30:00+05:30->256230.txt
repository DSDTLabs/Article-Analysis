GURUGRAM: A 25-year-old shopkeeper, identified as Hemant Kumar, was shot at in   on Friday afternoon by four armed assailants. The accused managed to flee in their car, while the   sustained five bullet  . 

 He was taken to a hospital, where he is in the ICU and his condition is reportedly stable.
 
 According to police, around 2.30pm, Kumar, a resident of Badha village, was sitting outside his grocery shop. In the meantime, four armed men came in a car and two of them fired at him. Villagers gathered at the spot and rushed him to the hospital. 
 
 Kumar claimed a brawl had broken out between him and the main accused, Umesh, around 2 years ago when he was studying in college with him, police said.
 
 An FIR was registered against the accused, Umesh and three unidentified persons under sections 307 (attempt to murder), 34 (common intention) of the IPC and 25-54-59 of Arms Act at Kherki Daula police station.
