LUDHIANA: The police on Saturday booked an 85-year-old man for raping his 29-year-old   at a village in Ludhiana district. 

 Police said the woman lodged a complaint at 181 helpline, saying the man raped her on March 15 when she went to his house to fill a bucket with water.
 
 Investigating officer sub-inspector   said the woman has four children and was pregnant. “We are yet to record the statement of the complainant, as she was not at home when a police party reached there,” the sub-inspector added.
 
 A case under Section 376 ( ) of Indian Penal Code has been registered.
