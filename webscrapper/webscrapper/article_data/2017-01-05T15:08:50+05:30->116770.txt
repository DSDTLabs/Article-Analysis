NEW DELHI: About 30   MPs were on Thursday detained by police while they were again trying to march to the Prime Minister's residence here to protest the arrest of their party colleagues Sudeep Bandopadhaya and   in the   chit fund case.

 Accusing the government of indulging in vendetta politics, TMC MPs raised slogans of "Modi hatao, desh bachao" (remove  , save country} before they were taken into custody and taken to Mandir Marg police station.
 
 TMC MPs had yesterday took out a protest march and were detained at Tughlaq Road police station for about three hours also.
 
 According to TMC, MPs were taken into custody by police when they were on their way to PM residence at Lok Kalyan Marg.
