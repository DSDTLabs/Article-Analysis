 Agra: ‘Agra Taj Car Rally’ covering around 400 km will be held from March 31 to April 2 this year. The rally is being organized by Motor Sports Club and Agra administration in association with UP tourism department as per the norms of the Federation of Motorsport Clubs of India (FMSCI). President of the Motor Sports Club, Rajeev Gupta, said that men and women from across the nation are expected to participate in the rally. “The participants will have to follow a certain speed limit and have to reach the check points within the mentioned time,” he added. He further added that Fatehpur Sikri would also be included in the route of the rally for the first time.
