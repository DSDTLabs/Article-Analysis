Shanthi Krishna, the enchanting beauty of Malayalam cinema in 80’s and 90’s after a hiatus was back to the silver screen through Althaf Salim’s debut directorial, Njandukalude Nattil Oridavela. The actress will be the next guest on singer Rimi Tomy’s celebrity chat show,  , aired on Mazhavil Manorama. 

 As per the sources from the channel, the actress will share about her acting experiences in the industry and a few updates about her personal life too. She will also open up about her acting experiences with new age actors and the change she witnessed in film field at present. 
 
 The episode will be aired on the channel at 9.30 pm on September 30.
