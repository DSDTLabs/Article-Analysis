MUMBAI: Three men aged 19-20, including a college student, have been arrested for gangraping a 16-year-old girl in Ghatkopar. The survivor and the arrested student studied in the same college and were friends, the police said. 

 The men have been booked for rape and unnatural sex under the IPC and under the Protection of Children from Sexual offences (POCSO) Act, reports Ahmed Ali. The police said the girl's parents had lodged a missing complaint on March 19.
