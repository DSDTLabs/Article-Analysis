AGRA: Two home guards of   police station in   were suspended on Thursday night for allegedly demanding a bribe of Rs 100 from a bike-borne youth.
 
  The action was taken after the video showing the home guards, Satyendra and Ramveer, allegedly demanding bribe from the youth went viral on social media on Thursday.
  
  During inquiry it was found that both constables had demanded the bribe from the youth. Following the report, SP Mainpuri, Ajay Shankar directed to register FIR against them under the prevention of corruption act.
  
  Talking to TOI, Ajay Shankar Rai, said, “We have zero tolerance towards corruption. An FIR has been registered against them. They have also been suspended by the department on the basis of the report sent by us.”
