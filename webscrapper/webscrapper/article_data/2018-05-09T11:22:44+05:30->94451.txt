Gear up all   fans! The singer-songwriter has announced a massive run of tour dates.

  Titled 'Shawn Mendes: The Tour', it will kick off in March next year and will end in New York City in August.
 
  
  
  The   hitmaker, who will visit North America, the UK, and Europe, took to social media to reveal the 2019 Global Tour dates.
 
 According to a report, ticket pre-sales begin May 12, and tickets for the general public will go on sale May 19.
