NAVI MUMBAI: The Rabale MIDC police has found a woman's decomposed body in a drainage near   company at   along the   road, on Monday. 

 As per the woman's postmortem report, a murder case has been registered. Police inspector (crime)   said, "Prima facie, we had registered an accidental death case. But, after the postmortem report stated that the cause of death was due to asphyxiation by strangulation and head injuries, we have registered a murder case ." 
 
 The murder victim is aged around 30-35 years. She was aroud 5 feet 3 inches and was wearing a green printed top , black dupatta and black pant. The only identification mark visible on her body is a tattoo of a flower on her right forearm, said cops.
