VASCO: DIG   on Saturday interrogated accused connected with the Seby Patra   case, which still remains a mystery, two weeks after the murder. Gupta travelled to   police station to follow up on the case. 

 So far, Vasco police have avoided revealing any details of the slow-paced investigations. 
 
 Two weeks ago, Patra's body was found dumped in the bushes opposite a bar at New Vaddem, Vasco, with his face smashed. Patra, a resident of Chicalim, was acquitted in a murder case, about 18 years ago.
