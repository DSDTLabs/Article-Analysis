Noida Authority had on January 1, 2018 announced an open-ended industrial scheme to allot a dozen plots measuring over 5 acres in four sectors across Noida. Some of these plots are located in sectors 155 and 156 along the Noida-Greater Noida expressway. The allotment rate fixed for these plots is Rs 13,090 per square meters, officials explained.
 
 Noida Authority will also launch three mega infrastructure projects UP Day. These include the long-awaited multi-level parking facility in sector 18 and two underpasses at sectors 94-95 and sectors 60-61 in the city.
