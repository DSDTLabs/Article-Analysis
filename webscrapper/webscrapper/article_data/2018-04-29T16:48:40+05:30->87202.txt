NEW DELHI: The Board Of Secondary Education, Andhra Pradesh released the AP Class 10 (SSC) result 2018 today at 4 pm. The result is now live on the official website of the board and the Android app - SSCAP. Here is the website link to check the AP SSC Class 10 results: resn18.bseap.org. 

 
 
 
 
 
  
  
 
 The students who have appeared for the Andhra Pradesh SSC Class 10th examination 2018, can check the result here. The students can submit their name and roll number in the form given above to check their result. 
 
 This year, 94.48 per cent students cleared the AP SSC Class 10th Exam. The pass percentage of girls is 94.56% while for boys it is 94.41%. Prakasam secured first place with 97.93 pass percentage. Nellore district has the highest pass percentage with 80.30 per cent. About 5,343 schools have got 100 pass percentage while 17 schools have nil or zero pass per cent.
 
 The AP Board has announced advanced supplementary exam date. The exam will start from June 11, 2018.

 Earlier, the result was scheduled to be declared at 11 am on April 29, 2018, on the official website of the board - bseap.org but the release of the result has been deferred to till 4 pm. The information, about the change in result declaration time, has now been updated by the board on its official website. 
 
 The notice reads as: The SSC Public Examinations, March 2018 Results will be released at 04.00 PM on 29-04-2018 (Sunday).
 
 
  
  
 The AP board SSC Class 10 results can also be accessed through the official app. Students can download the app and check their results. Here, we are providing the direct link for downloading the application for android phone users from google play -- the app store. 
 
 
 
 
 The notification regarding the result announcement was published on the official website of AP Board, "The SSC Public Examination, March 2018 Results will be announced at 11.00 am on Sunday, April 29, 2018."
 
 Previously, the results for the AP SSC Class 10 board students was scheduled to be announced at 9 am, on April 29. 
 
 The Andhra Pradesh 10th Class 2018 results will be declared by K Sandhya Rani, commissioner of School Education, Government of Andhra Pradesh and Andhra Pradesh Human and Resource Development minister Ganta Srinivasa Rao. 
 
 This year, more than 5 lakh students have appeared for the Andhra Pradesh SSC board examination in March 2018.
