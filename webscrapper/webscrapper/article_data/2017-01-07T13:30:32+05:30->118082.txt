Actress   known for her role, ‘Tahasildar Priyadarshini’ in the film, ‘Ayal Kadha Ezhuthukayanu’ will soon appear as guest on Flowers TV’s comedy show, ‘Comedy Super Nite 2’. The south Indian actress, who was loved by the Malayalai audience as a vengeful ‘Gazzetted Yakshi’ in the film will talk about her experiences working in the industry and more. The show hosted by actress   will be aired on January 9, at 8.30 pm.
