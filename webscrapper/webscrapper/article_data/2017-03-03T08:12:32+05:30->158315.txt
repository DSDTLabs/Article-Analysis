NEW DELHI: A 28-year-old nurse was killed in a gruesome manner at her home in outer Delhi’s   on Thursday morning. Police said that the suspect,  , had slit his wife’s throat and stabbed her multiple times before fleeing. 

 Preliminary investigations have revealed that the victim, Neha, and her husband often quarrelled over the latter’s drinking habit. Cops said she was the sole earning member of the family. Praveen was unemployed and he was not willing to take up any job, relatives said. 
 
 A senior police officer said the incident came to light around 9am when some neighbours found the front door of the house open and they saw Neha lying in a pool of blood. She was rushed to a hospital nearby where doctors declared her dead on arrival.
 
 “Praveen Rana is missing. He is the primary suspect in the case,” the police officer added.
 
 Neighbours and relatives told police that Neha worked as a nurse at a government dispensary. “The couple used to fight over monetary matters. They said that Praveen was an alcoholic and he often demanded money from Neha to buy liquor,” said the officer.
 
 On Thursday, Praveen woke up early and sent the children to school. He then attacked his wife with a sharp knife after which he fled the scene, police said.
 
 Investigations further revealed that the couple had a heated argument on Wednesday night as well. Neha and Praveen got married 10 years ago and the couple has two kids— aged five and six years. 
 
 “The man is absconding and raids are being conducted to nab him. A crime and forensic team was rushed to the spot. CCTV footage collected from the area is being checked as well. The body was handed over to the family members after the postmortem. We have registered a case of murder against the accused,” the officer said.
