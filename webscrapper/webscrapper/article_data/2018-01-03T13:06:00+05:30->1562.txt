MADURAI: The sixth annual conference of interventional cardiologists of Tamil Nadu is to be held in Madurai on January 6 and 7. Dr B.Kesavamoorthy, secretary of the   and senior cardiologist from  , said that it was being organised by the   India-Chennai Chapter. 

 More than 30 international and national faculties, 300 cardiologists from the state and forty cardiology superspeciality students were expected to attend the event. He said it was mandatory for practising doctors to attend such continuing medical education program in their respected fields for 12 hours every year to keep updated. The highlight of the conference would be percutaneous implantation of valves through catheters which obviates surgery.
