VADODARA: The prevention of crime branch ( ) raided five mobile shops in the city and seized duplicate accessories worth Rs 9.09 lakh on Friday. The cops acted after they received information about the duplicate accessories business. Officials of some mobile phone companies too accompanied the cops during the raids.

 According to the PCB, the cops raided five shops at   near   Market. These shops stocked and sold duplicate accessories like headsets, back cover, tempered glass, adaptors and back stands. The cops booked the owners under the Indian Copyright Act. tnn
