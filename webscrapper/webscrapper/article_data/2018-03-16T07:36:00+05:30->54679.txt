 Chandigarh: Shiromani Akali Dal (SAD), on Thursday, appointed senior leaders as party unit in-charge in various states to expand and strengthen its base. SAD president Sukhbir Singh Badal said Balwinder Singh Bhunder has been appointed in-charge of Delhi, Sikander Singh Maluka of Rajasthan and Bikram Singh Majithia of Jammu and Kashmir.
 
 SAD general secretary Manjinder Singh Sirsa will be co-incharge of Jammu and Kashmir. He has also been appointed in-charge for Jharkhand and Madhya Pradesh.
 
 Sukhbir also appointed SAD Delhi president Manjit Singh GK as in-charge of for Chhattisgarh, Bihar, West Bengal and Tamil Nadu. GK and Sirsa have been also been appointed as in-charges for Maharashtra. Kuldeep Singh Bhogal has been appointed in-charge of Uttar Pradesh and Kulwant Singh Baath of Himachal Pradesh.
