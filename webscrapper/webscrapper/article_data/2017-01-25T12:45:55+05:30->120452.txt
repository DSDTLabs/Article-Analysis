For the first time ever,   will be seen shaking a leg to a Gujarati track in his upcoming film ' '. The track called 'Udi Udi' has been sung by Sukhwinder Singh, composed by Ram Sampath and written by Javed Akhtar.

 Sukhwinder has lent his voice for several songs featuring Shah Rukh in the past, and they all have turned out to be massive hits, be it 'Chaiyya Chaiyya' or 'Chak De India'.
 
 
 
 'Udi Udi' celebrates the occasion of Makar Sankranti in the movie where Shah Rukh and his leading lady   try their hand at garba.
 The film which is set and shot in Gujarat, called for a peppy garba track. 
 
 Speaking about the number, director Rahul Dholakia let in that no film rooted in Gujarat could be complete without having a traditional dance number in it. Reportedly, 'Udi Udi' is special song takes the story forward.
