MUMBAI: A woman who planned to jump in front of a train at   escaped narrowly as the motorman halted the train in time and she moved to one side. 

 The woman, Sharda Fulvate (24), told RPF officials that she was harassed at her marital home. CCTVs have captured the incident. Around 11.30 am on January 21, Fulvate jumped onto the tracks and stood in the path of an oncoming train, but later moved aside. 
 
 “She had a minor bruise,” said an RPF official. “RPF officials and a woman commuter got her to safety.” She said she somehow changed her mind and moved away from the track.
