On the death anniversary of former Prime Minister of India Lal Bahadur  , actress  , who played the role of his mother in a film ' ' feels India needs more leader like him. 

 "When we were kids, our grandparents used to tell us about Shastri ji that how he protested when his wife smuggled two mangoes to him when he was in prison and how he used to travel like a commoner in trains. As I have grown up, I realized, he is one of the best PM India's had. I feel really sad, when politicians play dirty politics just to survive and spreading corruption. Shastri ji served altruistically, politicians should learn from him. Our Nation desperately needs more and more politicians like him," said the actress who is currently seen as Asha in Rajshri Productions 'Ek Shringaar-Swabhimaan'.
 
 Akhilesh Jain essayed the title role, where as Om Puri, Prem Chopra, Rati Agnihotri played the prominent roles in the film. Although 'Jai Jawan Jai Kisan' didn't do well in the box office, but won accolades in various film festivals. "I was always fascinated by his persona, so when the makers approached me, I immediately said yes," said the actress, who earlier did the shows like 'Pavitra Rishta', 'Bhagya Laksmi' and 'Balika Vadhu'.
