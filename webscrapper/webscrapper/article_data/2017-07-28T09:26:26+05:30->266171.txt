Yet another   cabinet minister got caught in a litigation, with the Madras high court issuing notice to higher education minister K P Anbalagan on a petition filed by a contractor in a bribery case on Thursday.

 Justice M   issued the notices after   of   district said the minister had personally demanded 20% commission (approximately 16 crore) for awarding multi-crore contract for building government polytechnics in Madurai, Theni, Pudukottai, Villupuram, Vellore, Krishnagiri, Trichy and Erode districts. The petition alleged the minister threatened to scrap the contracts, valued at 79 crore, if the bribe was not paid.
