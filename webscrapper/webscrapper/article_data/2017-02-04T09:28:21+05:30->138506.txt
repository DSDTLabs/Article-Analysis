JALALABAD: Following a complaint by Sangrur MP and Aam Aadmi Party nominee from Jalalabad assembly constituency, the   ordered transfer of the Jalalabad deputy superintendent of police (DSP) on Friday. 

 In his complaint, Bhagwant Mann alleged that DSP Harinder Singh was involved in alluring voters of the area in favour of the ruling Shiromani Akali Dal. 
 
 "We approached the Election Commission with relevant proof on Thursday. Taking strong note of the facts, the DSP has been shifted from Jalalabad," he informed.
 
 Ferozepur range deputy inspector general of police Sukhchain Singh Gill confirmed about the transfer of the DSP, he expressed ignorance about the details of the complaint against the officer.
