 Last week, the state cabinet had decided to pursue a resolution passed by the state   to urge Centre s intervention for resolving the ban on coal mining in the state. 
 
 In 2015, the Assembly had unanimously adopted a resolution to urge the Centre to invoke Para 12 A (b) of the Sixth Schedule through a Presidential notification to exempt Meghalaya from the central laws related to mining which include the Mines and Minerals (Development and Regulations) Act, and the Coal Mines (Nationalisation) Act.
