The audition for fbb Campus Princess Contest in association with   Institute of Fashion Technology was held recently in the city. The event saw participation of 40 girls from different parts of the country.
Having a tough time judging this talented lot were Aradhana Buragohain, Yamaha Fascino - Miss Diva 2016 2nd runner-up, and fashion designer Asma Hussain.
 Out of the 40 girls, a total of 15 contestants were shortlisted for the second round, which comprised a brief introduction and a ramp walk. The seven girls chosen for the final round confidently answered several questions asked by the judges and also showcased their talent in singing and dancing. "There is a lot of talent in the state. It was really exciting to watch so many girls coming from various part of the state and perform at this event," said Aradhana.
 Finally, after a lot of brainstorming,   and Rishika Bali were adjudged the fbb Campus Princess finalists from Lucknow. "It’s a great honour to be a part of the fbb Campus Princess family. Now my focus is to win the title of fbb Campus Princess in Mumbai for which I will have to work really hard," said an excited Pooja.
