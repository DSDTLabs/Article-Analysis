MUMBAI: The   are on the lookout for a   bookie, Anil Jaisinghania, who had forged the signature of a Lilavati hospital doctor while seeking anticipatory bail before the HC. 

 Cops said the case dates back to 2015, when ED raided two premises, co-owned by Jaisinghania, in connection with betting. He allegedly first got admitted to hospital saying ill and later sought anticipatory bail in HC saying is wife needed to undergo test for a surgery. "For this he submitted a letter signed by a Lilavati hospital doctor," police said.
