Mumbai: A total of 412   were vacant after the fourth round of seat allotment across the IITs, NITs, IIITs and other centrally funded government institutes. The Joint Seat   Authority declared the schedule for the other rounds of admission. 

 On Friday, July 14, and Saturday, July 15, candidates need to accept or withdraw their admission. On Sunday, July 16, seats filled and seats available would be displayed, and seats allocated for the fifth round. 
 
 Candidates wanting to accept or withdraw a seat need to do so on Monday, July 17, after which the sixth round would start. On July 21, seats would be allotted for the last round. Seats must be accepted on July 22.
