THIRUVANANTHAPURAM: Kerala self-financing engineering college management' association representatives met education minister   here on Friday and expressed willingness to face any inquiry on   in their colleges in the state. 

 The association representatives alleged conspiracy behind the ongoing attacks against self-financing colleges and requested the government to give protection to their properties and equipment. The association members claimed that the recent attacks on their colleges were unleashed by people from outside the campus. None of the students or the student unions in the colleges were involved in these attacks.
 
 The association members submitted representations to both chief minister   and education minister, seeking steps to restore law and order in colleges.
