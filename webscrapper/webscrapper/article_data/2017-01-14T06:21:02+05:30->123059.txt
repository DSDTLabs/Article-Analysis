LUDHIANA: With the region enveloped in thick fog, 10 trains were cancelled and 14 trains ran behind schedule. The trains were delayed by more than 10 hours. Worst affected trains are those coming to and from Delhi.
The   include  ,  , Chandigarh Varanasi Junction, New Delhi to Katpadi, Haridwar Amritsar Jan Shatabdi, Patna-Mumbai CST.
 
 District traffic manager,   division, S P Bhatia said while the fog is likely to disrupt train schedules in the days to come, trains cancelled in the past month will resume plying from January 16.
