VELLORE: The fast track mahila court, Vellore, on Tuesday sentenced a 55-year-old man to four years   for attempting to sexually assault his   four years ago.

 Hearing the case, Judge P Mathusuthanan pronounced L Panneerselvam, of Guruvarajapalayam village, guilty of trying to sexually assault his daughter-in-law in 2013.
 
 The judge awarded him four years RI and imposed a fine of Rs 2,000 under Section 354 (B) (assault or use of criminal force to woman with intent to disrobe) of the IPC, besides awarding him one year imprisonment and slapping him with a fine of Rs 10,000 under Section 4 of Tamil Nadu Prohibition Harassment of Women Act.
 
 The judge, in the verdict, stated that Panneerselvam would undergo the imprisonment concurrently.
 
 In 2013, Panneerselvam, in an inebriated condition, had tried to sexually assault his daughter-in-law when she was alone in the house. She was 22 then. Based on the statement of the victim and other evidence, Panneerselvam was found guilty, said special public prosecutor S Priya Lakshmi.
