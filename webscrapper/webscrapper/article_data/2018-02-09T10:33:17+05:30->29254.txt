GUWAHATI: A six-year-old girl, who had been missing since Wednesday afternoon, was found   at the verandah of her house in the   area of   district on Thursday morning. 

 Deceased  , a Class II student, had at least four injury marks on her chest. Swapnaneel Deka, SP (Morigaon), said, "The parents had lodged a missing case at Jagiroad police station on Wednesday evening when they could not find her. However, one of the family members who woke up early on Thursday found her body. There were multiple burn-like marks near her ribcage. The cause of the death will be ascertained after getting the post mortem report."
