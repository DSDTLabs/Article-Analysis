SHIMLA:   chief minister   said on Sunday that the state would be made part of a regional air-connectivity scheme, Udde Desh Ka Aam Nagrik (UDAN) , to improve tourism. 

 The state will be included in the second phase of the scheme, Thakur said, while addressing a public meeting in Jogindernagar, Mandi district.He said the state government was committed to providing air connectivity to farflung areas and he was grateful to Prime Minister   for including Himachal Pradesh in the scheme.
