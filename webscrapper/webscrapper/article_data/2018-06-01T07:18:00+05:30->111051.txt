Ghaziabad: Days after issuing show cause notices to enforcement officials across GDA’s eight zones, vice-chairperson Ritu Maheshwari on Thursday said she will withhold one month’s salary of nine enforcement officials. These officials are from zones 3, 6 and 7 who have not been able to generate revenues in their respective zones in May. 

 Maheshwari on May 28 issued show cause notices to the officials for failing to meet the revenue target for the financial year 2017-18, which was around Rs 500 crore. “In zone 3, the salaries of five officials, including one assistant engineer and four junior engineers, have been withheld while in zones 6 and 7 the salaries of two junior engineers in each zones have been withheld,” said a GDA official.
