LUDHIANA: The economic offence (EO) wing of the vigilance bureau, Ludhiana, on Tuesday evening arrested an assistant sub-inspector (ASI) of Phagwara police while accepting bribe of Rs 10,000 from a Ludhiana resident. It is alleged that the   had demanded Rs 25,000 to get the complainant acquitted form a road accident case. 

 The arrested ASI has been identified as Narinder Singh and a case under various sections of the Prevention of Corruption Act has been registered against him.
 
 Inspector Rajinder Kumar of the economic offence wing of the Ludhiana vigilance bureau said Bhupinder Singh of   had approached them with a complaint stating that the Phagwara police had registered a case under sections 279 (reckless driving) and 304-A (causing death due to negligence) of the IPC against him on December 29, 2017, and ASI Narinder Singh was demanding Rs 25,000 from him for acquitting him in the case. Later the deal was finalized at Rs 10,000. 
 
 The official added that the accused had called up the complainant outside a police station in Phagwara with money on Tuesday. As soon as the ASI accepted the bribe, sleuths from the vigilance bureau caught him red-handed.
