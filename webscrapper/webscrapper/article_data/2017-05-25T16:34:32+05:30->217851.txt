INDORE:   in many areas of the city will remain affected on Wednesday for up to three hours as Madhya Pradesh Paschim Kshetra Vidyut Vitaran Company Ltd has started conducting  . This will continue for around 13 days in   of the city. 

 Around 20 colonies will be affected due to the maintenance work like line maintenance, charging of distribution transformers, replacement of electronic equipment,   etc. To avoid inconvenience to residents, the Discom has announced to shut power between 5:00 am to 11.30 am when electronic devices are not in use. 
 
 Officials said feeder lines and their connecting cables and distribution transformers are in need of immediate work and tree branches that get entangled with overhead supply cables will also be trimmed during the maintenance drive.
 
 Most feeder lines in the city’s south division needed to be repaired before monsoon. Tree branches are also being pruned as they turn out to be the biggest problem ahead of the rainy season, so we have started the work, said discom’s superintendent engineer Subroto Roy.
