Rajkot: Three persons were arrested for a dozen two-wheeler thefts in Gondal, Rajkot, on Sunday night. 

 The accused have been identified as Dilipsinh Vala (40), Jungle Bharai (22) and Bharat Jeta (28), all residents of Vavadi village of Jamkandorana taluka in Rajkot district. 
 
 “The trio was arrested following a tip-off about them riding on stolen bikes on the outskirts of Gondal. Two stolen motorcycles were recovered from them. They confessed to 10 two-wheeler thefts from Dhoraji, Jamkandorana, Gondal and Rajkot,” an official from Gondal police station said.
