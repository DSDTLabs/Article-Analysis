NEW DELHI: Indian   ( ) is releasing Admit Cards 2018 for Groupo X, Y on its official website airmenselection.gov.in.

 
 
 
 
 Date of Examination: May 3,4,5 & 6, 2018
 Admit Card release: April 16, 2018 
 
 To download the admit cards, candidates need to enter their 11 digit Unique Registration Number along with their Password. 
 
 The Admit cards will be issued only to the candidates who have been verified by   (CASB). The admit cards for GROUP X trades (Except Education Instructor) and Group Y trades {Except Automobile Technician, GTI, IAF (P), IAF(S) and Musician are made available on the official website.
 
 The online exam is scheduled for May 3 to 5, 2018.
 
 
 
 
 
 1) Log on to the official website: airmenselection.gov.in
 2) Go on 'CANDIDATE' tab on the homepage and select 'Download Admit Card'.
 3) You'll be redirected to new page Central Airmen Selection Board.
 4) Login with your 11 digit Unique Registration Number, Password and enter Captcha Code.
 5) Click on "Submit" button
 6) Your Admit Card will be displayed on screen.
 7) Download the admit card and take printout for future use
