AHMEDABAD: A 20-member   team, led by Lieutenant  , embarked on the 943 km long cycling expedition starting from Jamnagar through 7 districts of Saurashtra region to promote and foster the spirit of adventure and camaraderie.

 The expedition was flagged off on Thursday. It will cover almost 110 kms across Jamnagar, Rajkot, Bhavnagar, Diu, Somnath, Dwarka and Porbandar districts. While speaking to the participants,   commander Headquarters 31 Infantry Brigade, wished them good luck.
