JALANDHAR: The   (DSGMC) has urged the Union ministry of external affairs ( ) to establish a separate cell to take up issues of attacks on Sikhs in the US. In separate letters to the Union external affairs minister   and American ambassador to India Richard Verma,   general secretary Manjinder Singh Sirsa urged them to take up steps urgently on the matter as the community was a worried lot keeping in view the changed circumstances in the US.

 He said though Swaraj had been taking up issues keeping in mind feelings of the Sikh community, the special cell for the purpose could have members of the Indian government and representatives of Sikhs from the elected bodies, namely DSGMC and SGPC. He also said that along with this a proper campaign should be launched to ensure that such incidents might not occur in future.
