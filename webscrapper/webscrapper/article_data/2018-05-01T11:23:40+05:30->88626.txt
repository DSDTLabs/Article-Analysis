PATIALA:The police here have booked a resident of Barnala on charges of abducting and raping a 22-year-old girl. The police claimed that the   survivor had recorded her statement. She told police that the accused was known to her. The accused had taken her to Barnala and then raped her.

 Police said the rape survivor told them that the accused had abducted her and took her to a park in Barnala. They were in the park for a while after which the accused took her to a room behind the park. The girl alleged the man raped her on the pretext of marriage. “We have registered a case of abduction and rape against the accused. The accused is at large. Police teams have been formed to find him,” SHO said.
