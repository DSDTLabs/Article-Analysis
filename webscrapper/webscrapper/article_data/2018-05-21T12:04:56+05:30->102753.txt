PATNA:   Abhishek Singh was arrested near   village under the   police station area in   district on Sunday. City SP (West) Ravindra Kumar said Abhishek was a contract killer and his gang was active in western Patna and Bhojpur. He said altogether 12 cases of murder and extortion were lodged against him with Bihta, Naubatpur and other police stations in Bhojpur. Kumar said a semi-automatic country-made pistol and two loaded magazines were also recovered from his possession. He said a team of Bhojpur police was also interrogating Abhishek after reaching Patna. He had threatened several traders at Bihta for extortion.
