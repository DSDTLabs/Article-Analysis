CHENNAI: The moon was at its closest point to the Earth in its elliptical orbit on Monday. Known as supermoon, it is a popular celestial event closely watched by amateurs and astronomers around the world.

 The moon is supposed to be within 90% of its closest approach to the Earth during its orbit and appears 14% larger and 30% brighter. According to  , the term was coined by an astrologer in 1979 to describe periods when the new moon or the full moon is near perigee.
