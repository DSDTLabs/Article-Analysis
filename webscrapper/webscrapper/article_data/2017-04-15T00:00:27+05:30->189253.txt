
 
  Speaking to  , Executive Director of the Zoo, Kamala Karikalan, says, “The inauguration date is yet to be fixed. The seating capacity of the amphitheatre, which will be used only for wildlife-related activities, is 500. The trees act as a natural canopy for people to sit and enjoy shows. There are two entries into amphitheatre. If time permits, even visitors to the zoo can attend events at the amphitheatre. The activities will be carried out in such a way that it won’t cause any disturbance to the animals and birds housed in the zoo.”
