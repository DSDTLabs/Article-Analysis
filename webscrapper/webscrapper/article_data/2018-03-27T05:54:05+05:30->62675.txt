KOLKATA: Cops probing the   suspect that men very close to the family were behind the armed heist that took place in one of the most densely populated areas in the northern fringes of the city around 7.30pm on Sunday.

 Victim  , 52, said the robbers were wearing helmets when they entered the house and held a knife to her throat. They decamped with cash and gold worth Rs 2.5 lakh.
 
 “We have recorded Kundu and her neighbours’ statements for leads. The incident happened barely five minutes after her husband left home and when their daughter was away. Such information can only be known to someone close to the family,” said a senior police officer.
