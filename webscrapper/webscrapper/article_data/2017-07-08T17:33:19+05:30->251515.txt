THIRUVANANTHAPURAM: The state level co-ordination committee has directed local bodies in the state to convert unused buildings for plastic shredder units or recycling units. In the recent meeting convened by Local Self Governments ( ) minister   it was pointed out that many local bodies had purchased land and constructed buildings under people planning scheme, however many of these properties remained unused. 

 Local bodies were directed to make use of these buildings on an emergency note and performance audit department has been directed to compile district wise list of assets owned by each local body. 
 
  director, urban affairs director and rural development commissioner were directed to monitor the compilation of data. It was directed that local bodies may convert unused buildings owned by them for the purpose of setting up plastic shredder units and recycling units.
