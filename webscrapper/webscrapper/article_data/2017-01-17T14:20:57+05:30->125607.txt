BENGALURU: A day after   mall in Malleswaram has been closed following a wall collapse incident, city's civic body, the Bruhat Bengaluru Mahanagara Palike ( ) has formed an expert committee to conduct an inquiry into the incident. 

 BBMP Commissioner N Manjnath Prasad said experts from civil engineering department of  , BMS College of Engineering, Civil Aid and town planning officials from BBMP have been made part of the committee. "The committee will do a thorough inspection into the incident and give a report on structural stability of the Mall as well. Till then the Mall will remain closed and the occupancy certificate will remain suspended," he added.
