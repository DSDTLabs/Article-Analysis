Vadodara: Four persons were killed and three others were injured in a road accident that took place near Jhalod in Dahod district on Monday night. The accident happened when a group of persons from Rajasthan was returning home from Chosala in Dahod district.

 The group from Rajasthan had come to Dahod for training at a NGO. On their way back, the multi-utility vehicle (MUV) that they were travelling in collided with a truck. The driver of the MUV and three women died on the spot in the collision.
 
 Seven persons injured in the accident were rushed to Dahod for treatment. The deceased were not identified till Tuesday.
