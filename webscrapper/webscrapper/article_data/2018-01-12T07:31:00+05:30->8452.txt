GURGAON: A joint team of the   ( ),   ( ), local administration and police, visited Gurgaon on Thursday to review arrangements for the inauguration of the   (SPC) programme in the city next month.

 The national initiative is aimed at sensitising school students about the basics of policing. 
 
 Union home minister Rajnath Singh will be the chief guest at the inauguration. A meeting was held with joint secretary (home) Vivek Bhardwaj as chairman at Tau Devi Lal stadium. BPRD director and inspector general of police Nirmal Kumar Azad, state additional director general of police O P Singh and additional deputy commissioner Pradeep Dahiya were part of the meeting.
 
 Bhardwaj said the logo for the programme, which is being designed by school students as part of a competition, will be finalised by end-January. Police commissioner Sandeep Khirwar has formed an organising committee, he added. 
 
 “The SPC programme provides for a comprehensive development of the child, something on which the Centre has lately been focusing. Around 5,000 students from Haryana and other states will be participating,” said Khirwar.
