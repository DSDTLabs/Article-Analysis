Jaipur: The Rajasthan   has taken cognizance of the shoddy construction of roads and sought a detailed report from JDA and urban development department.

 Justice SS Kothari took the suo moto action based on media reports indicating pathetic condition of city roads which were constructed by the government agencies.
 
 The reports had indicated that roads worth about Rs 100 core were shoddily constructed. The reports stated that samples of materials used in the construction were collected by three additional chief engineers and issued notices to 87 engineers for sub-standard construction, yet only 31 of such engineers received charge sheets.
