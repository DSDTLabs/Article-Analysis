GANDHINAGAR: Gujarat chief minister   on Wednesday expressed the state government’s resolve to promote the Gujarati film industry. Rupani handed over a cheque of Rs 1 crore, as part of the government’s incentive scheme, to the film unit of “Wrong Side Raju”, which was adjudged the best Gujarati film at the recent national film awards ceremony.
