MADURAI: Southern Railway has announced Suvidha specials during summer to clear the extra rush. According to a statement, train no. 82607   –   Suvidha special will leave Chennai   at 8pm on Saturdays on April 21 and 28 and May 5, 12, 19 and 26 and reach Nagercoil at 11.05am the next day. Train no. 82608 Nagercoil –   special will leave Nagercoil at 4pm on April 15, 22, May 6, 13, 20, 27 and June 3 & 17 and reach Chennai Central at 8.30 am the next day. These trains will have 1 AC 3-tier, 6 sleeper class, 2 second class chair car, 3 general second class and 2 luggage-cum-brake van coaches.

 They will stop at Arakkonam, Katpadi, Jolarpettai, Salem, Namakkal, Karur, Dindigul, Madurai, Virudhunagar, Satur, Kovilpatti, Tirunelveli and Valliyur. Train no. 82608 will stop at   also.
 
 Advance reservation for the above trains will commence at 8am on April 6.
 
 So far, 247 summer special trains have been announced.
