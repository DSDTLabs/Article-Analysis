NEW DELHI: Delhi Commission for Women’s   campaign will get its own   to create more awareness about the need for a robust criminal justice system for cases of sexual crimes, especially against children. The anthem has been composed and produced by playback singer Caralisa Monteiro, and will feature Farhan Akhtar, Shankar Mahadevan, Anushka Manchanda, Mahalaxmi Iyer and Luke Kenny. None of the celebrities took any fee for it.

 “I felt the need to support Swati as a movement cannot be just one voice. It needs more people,” Monteiro said. After deciding to compose the anthem, the singer approached her friends in the entertainment industry.
 
 The anthem will be unveiled on Thursday during aent protest in Connaught Place.
