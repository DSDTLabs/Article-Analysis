CHANDIGARH: Ahead of the 2019 Lok Sabha elections, the Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) will get a party office in Mohali that will be inaugurated by party in charge of Punjab affairs and Delhi deputy CM Manish   on May 5.

 Party’s state co-president   told TOI that the AAP also planned to have offices in other parts of the state. 
 
 Meanwhile, party sources said name of the AAP candidate for Shahkot assembly by election is also likely to be announced by Sisodia during his visit. He will meet the state leadership in Chandigarh.
 
 This is the first visit by a leader from the top brass of the party after its Punjab unit registered strong protest over the apology offered by party’s national convener Arvind Kejriwal to Akali leader Bikram Majithia. A section of AAP leaders had also said the issues pertaining to Punjab should be discussed in Punjab. Hence, Sisodia on his visit, is likely to discuss various issues regarding the state’s organisational structure with the local leaders.
 
 Recently, Balbir Singh has been given the responsibility of organisation building in Punjab.
