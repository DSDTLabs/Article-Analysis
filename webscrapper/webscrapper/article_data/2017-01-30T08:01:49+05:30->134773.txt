KOLHAPUR: Students of the Electronics department of the Shivaji University, Kolhapur (SUK) had organised a week long digital literacy campaign at the Varange village earlier last week.

 During the campaign, the villagers were given lessons on the cashless transactions, digital lockers and e-commerce. As many as 450 families were given lessons about digital locker to the cashless transactions. The campaign got a good response and the villagers appreciated the efforts taken by the students.
