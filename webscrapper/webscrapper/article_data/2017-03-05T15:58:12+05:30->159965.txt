32 years ago, Rajendra Prasad’s film by   Ladies Tailor released and made waves in the industry. With its quirky story and natural comedy, it won the hearts of Telugu cinema fans. And now the director is back with its sequel. 

 The film traces the idea that if that Ladies’ Tailor had a son what would he be doing today. 
 
 And thus essaying the role of a Fashion designer   plays the protagonist in this flick which recently completed shoot. Currently in post-production the film is racing towards release.
