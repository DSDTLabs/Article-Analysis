Trichy: Burglars continue to strike at the residences in Trichy. In a house break-in at Thiruverumbur, burglars decamped with 18 sovereigns of gold ornaments and Rs 12 lakhs cash on Wednesday.

 The residence of Raja, 51 from Thiruverumbur was the target for the burglars. The incident came to light when he and his family members returned home after visiting a temple in their native on Wednesday evening.
 
 They found the main door of the house broken and the ornaments and cash kept in the bureau were stolen.
 
 The Thuvakudi police have have registered a case based on a complaint from Raja. A sniffer dog was pressed into action. Investigations are on to nab the cuprits.
