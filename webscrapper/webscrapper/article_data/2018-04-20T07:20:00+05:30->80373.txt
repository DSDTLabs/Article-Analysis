Ludhiana: The Daresi police have arrested a man — Deepak of Samrala Chowk — for assaulting a traffic cop on Wednesday, while his two accomplices — Jatinder Kumar of Indra Puri, and Sahil Ali of Sanjay Gandhi Colony — managed to escape. Deepak was later bailed out.

 A case under sections 353 (assault or criminal force to deter public servant from discharge of his duty), 186 (obstructing public servant in discharge of public functions) and 506 (punishment for criminal intimidation) of the IPC has been registered against them.
 
 Complainant ASI Ravinder said he was checking vehicles near Basti Jodhewal, when all the three accused were passing by the area on a motorcycle. When he stopped to challan them for triple riding, the accused started abusing him. Meanwhile, they started their motorcycle and tried to escape. The police team gave them a chase and managed to nab Deepak, while the two others managed to flee.
 
 The SHO at Daresi police station, sub-inspector Surinder Chopra, said a hunt was on for the arrest of rest of the accused.
