Ahmedabad: A security siren at the Dena Bank branch in Maninagar went off suddenly on Saturday evening, causing a stir in the area as local residents rushed to the bank to check on what was happening. 

 Vans from the local police station and crime branch rushed to the spot after locals informed police control room of the siren blaring. 
 
 Police sources said they searched the bank only to find that the siren had gone of accidentally and nothing untoward had happened. Police had a tough time clearing the large crowd that had gathered around after hearing the siren. tnn
