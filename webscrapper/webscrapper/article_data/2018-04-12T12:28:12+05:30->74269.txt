PATNA: Bihar chief minister (CM)   will inaugurate a state-level   skill competition at   ground on April 20. Governor   will distribute prizes to the winners at the closing ceremony on April 22.

 The Institute of Hotel Management-Hajipur (IHM) is playing a major role in organizing the contest. 
 
 According to a press release issued on Wednesday, “At least 24 participants will display their skills in the culinary competition meant for four different trades — cookery, bakery, restaurant service and patisserie and confectionery. After being trained at IHM, six participants from each trade were selected for the state-level competition.”
 
 While the cookery and bakery competitions will be held on  , the remaining two will be organized at Bihar Veterinary College.
