BSP candidate  's claim to fame is not just being the son of don-turned-politician  . The 25-year-old management postgraduate is also a national-level shooting champion. He spoke to Pankaj Shah while campaigning in his constituency Ghosi. Excerpts...

 
 
 
 The opposition has painted him that way, otherwise he is a messiah of poor. Besides, I come from an illustrious family. My grandfather   was a Congress president and my `nana' (maternal grandfather) Mohammad Usman was the highest ranking army officer who got killed in Indo-Pak war of 1947.
 
 
 
 
 He lost it but I will win. He did his best for the entire area, including Ghosi. In a way, he prepared the seat for me five years ago.
 
 
 
 
 She did that just as a backup for my father. My father is in jail. Agar koi gadbad ho to woh contest kar sakti hain.... But once his nomina tion was accepted, she withdre w her candidature.
 
 
 
 
 I was very happy. A day after my candidature was announced I came to Mau and started campaigning. I hope I would not let her (BSP chief) down.
 
 
 
 
 Does being a sportsman stop anyone from contesting an election? I also want to serve this area as a legislator. Inshallah wo hoga (God willing that would happen)
 
 
 
 
 The first priority would be to ensure safety of people. The fascist forces suppress the poor. Communalism is raising its head and I want to put a check on it.
