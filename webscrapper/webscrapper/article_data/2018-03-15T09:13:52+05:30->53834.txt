 Once the QR code is scanned, the passengers will get information related to rail and the Vadodara division which it updates on twitter. 
 
 “We have pasted such QR code stickers at all major spots of the railway station like booking windows, waiting halls or inquiry counters,” said an official. The division is first in the Indian Railways to introduce such a system to provide regular updates to railway passengers, he added 
 
 “The QR code has been created to give better updates to rail commuters and take advantage of the steps that are shared on the official twitter handle @DRMBRCWR,” he said.
