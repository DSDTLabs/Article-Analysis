PANAJI: Goa will witness yet another enthralling night of music. Commemorating International  ,   is hosting an hour-long concert and after-party on April 30.

 Jazz Goa plays host to accomplished jazz artists from Goa, across India and around the world, making it a point to also showcase young budding jazz talent each year.
 
 For the international jazz day this year, an hour-long concert will be held at the   auditorium in Panjim from 6pm to 7pm, followed by an after party jam session at Bay15, Dona Paula from 7.30pm to 10.30pm. Musicians interested in performing at the after party may contact Jazz Goa on 9657574480. For other enquiries call 7447499069.
