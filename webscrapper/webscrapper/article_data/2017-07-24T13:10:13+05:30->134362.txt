Tulsi or holy basil is one of the most wondrous herbs and is known for its medicinal properties. Here we tell you ways to prepare healthy concoctions using these humble leaves. 

 
  
  
 
 
 
  Tulsi leaves and water
 Pour two cups of water in a pan. Add some tulsi leaves and bring to boil until the water becomes half. Strain and have it warm. This helps to soothe throat pain and soreness. 
  
 
 
  
  
 
 
 
 Tulsi leaves, jaggery, water, lemon juice
 Blend jaggery and tulsi leaves. Add one and half cup of water and lemon juice to this mixture. Bring to boil and strain. Have it warm.
  
 
 
 
  Ginger, black pepper, cloves, tulsi leaves, cinnamon, cardamom
 Mix all the above ingredients and add water to it. Boil this mixture. Strain and you can have it warm or at room temperature. This herbal tulsi water helps in recovering from dengue and malaria by increasing your number of platelets.
  
 
 
 
 
  Ajwain, tulsi leaves, cumin, amchoor powder, salt and mint leaves
 Take four cups of water and add all the above ingredients to it. Boil it for 10-15 minutes. Have it warm or at room temperature. This juice helps in recovery during dehydration.
