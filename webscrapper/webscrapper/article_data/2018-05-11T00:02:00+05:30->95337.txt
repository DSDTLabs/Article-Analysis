PUNE: The online filling of form for admissions to Class XI will start from May 10. Students will be able to log in to the official admission website https://pune.11thadmission.net from 11 am onwards to fill the first part of the form using the user ID and the password given in the information booklet that they have received from their respective  , said an official notification by  , assistant deputy director of education, Pune division.

 Students of Maharashtra state board who have appeared for Class X examination from Pune Municipal Corporation area or Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation area should approach their respective schools. The headmaster/principal of the school will get the online forms submitted and approved for these students from May 10 to the day SSC results are declared by the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education.
