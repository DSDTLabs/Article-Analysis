Ahmedabad: Shikha Sharma, the managing director (MD) and chief executive officer (CEO) of Axis Bank, will be the chief guest at the 52nd convocation of Indian Institute of Management-Ahmedabad (IIM-A) which will be held on March 25. Nearly 500 students will be conferred degrees at the convocation.
