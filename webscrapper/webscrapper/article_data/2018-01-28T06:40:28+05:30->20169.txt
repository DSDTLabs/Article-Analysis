LUCKNOW: Lucknow   hostellers will not have to rush to railway and bus stations to book tickets.

 The facility will be available from the new academic session. A Student Facility Centre is coming up on the campus which will offer facilities like bus and train tickets booking,  ,   and bookbinding to students.
 
 “The centre is being constructed near the CLL library which is near to LU hostels. The facility will of great help for hostellers,” said vice-chancellor SP Singh.
 
 “The service will be of great help as many a time we have to book  ,” said LU hosteller  .
 
 Students said the move would be of great help to women who stay on the campus.
