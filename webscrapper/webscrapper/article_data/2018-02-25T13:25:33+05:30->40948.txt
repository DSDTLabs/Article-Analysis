JAMSHEDPUR: The   district police on Saturday cracked the February 20   in   in which Rs 25 lakh was looted. After a thorough investigatio, police have arrested the ATM security guard, Debu Rai, for the crime. 

 Police said 30-year-old Rai had broken into the ATM and stole the cash on the night of February 20. To mislead police, he cooked up a false story of unidentified criminals executing the crime. Police have recovered the stolen money from his under-construction house at Dahigura in Ghatshila and from the Panch Pandav cremation site near Subernarekha river.
