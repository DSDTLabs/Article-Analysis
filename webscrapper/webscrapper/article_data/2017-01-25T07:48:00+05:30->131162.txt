GURUGRAM: Four unidentified men held a driver at gunpoint and robbed him of his tempo, mobile phone and cash in Sector 52 on Sunday.

 The incident took place around 8pm when Inderpal Singh, who belongs to Uttar Pradesh and lives in Wazirabad village, was heading home in his tempo. As he was about to reach Botanical garden in Sector 52,   intercepted him and held him at gunpoint. “They took Rs 3,000, mobile phone and tempo and fled,” said Inderpal in his complaint lodged at the Sushant Lok police station. 
 
 An FIR was registered against them under section 392 (robbery) of IPC and sections 25-54-59 of   on Monday, according to police.
