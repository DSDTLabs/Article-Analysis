Trichy: Air Intelligence unit of the customs seized gold bars weighing 430g from a passenger, who arrived at the Trichy International Airport from Kuala Lumpur in the early hours of Saturday.

 The passenger has been identified as Vasafa Maraikayar of Ramanathapuram district who has been detained and being interrogated.
 
 According to reports, the passenger was travelling from Kuala Lumpur in Malaysia to Trichy through Air Asia. 
 
 While the officials were carrying out regular security check, they found that gold was hidden in a pressure cooker handle, which Maraikayar had brought along.
 
 The gold, which was seized was said to be of 24 karat purity, weighed 430g and worth Rs 13 lakh.
