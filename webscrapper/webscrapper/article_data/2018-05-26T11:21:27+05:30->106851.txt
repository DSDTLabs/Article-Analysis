Salman Khan's much-awaited film of the year 'Race 3' is all set to hit the screens on Eid- June 15, 2018, but that's not all that the Sultan of Bollywood has to offer to his fans and followers. Salman is going all out now with officially announcing the dates of his Da-Bangg The Tour – Reloaded on his Twitter handle.

 His tweet read, "Itni saare filmein, aur kitni saare yaadein. Aa raha hoon big screen se seedhe big stage par!" with a video wherein you can see Salman introducing his team members in his style with them coming out of his film posters. 
 
 
  
 
 Katrina Kaif, Sonakshi Sinha,  , Daisy Shah, Maniesh Paul,   and Guru Randhawa, who are all a part of the tour can be seen introducing themselves.
 
 Salman, who is all set to entertain the audience in the third installment of the 'Race' franchise which is helmed by Remo D’Souza along with  , Jacqueline Fernandez, Bobby Deol, Saqib Salim and Daisy Shah, will begin his tour a week after the film releases.
