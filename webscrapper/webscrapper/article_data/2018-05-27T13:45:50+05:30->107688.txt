HARIDWAR: Police on Saturday busted a sex racket operating from a rented house in   colony under the jurisdiction of Kankhal police station. Two women were arrested from the spot while three others, including the alleged kingpin, were detained by the police.
 
  The two arrested accused have been identified as   kumar and Rahul Sharma.
  
  SHO Kankhal police station, Omkant Bhushan said, “Acting on tip off, anti-human trafficking unit and local police conducted raids with the help of decoy customer. Sex racket had been running from a rented flat in the area. We rescued two girls hailing from Dehradun and recovered mobile phones and other items from the room.”
