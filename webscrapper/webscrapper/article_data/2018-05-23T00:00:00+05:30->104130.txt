Rajkot: The officials of Morbi local crime branch (LCB) nabbed two robbers and detected three loot cases on Sunday night. Police identified the accused as Sagarsing Nanakia and Idu Kadaksing, both residents of Dhar district in Madhya Pradesh. “We arrested the accused duo, following a tip-off about their movement near Pipali village. The accused were driving a bike without a registration number, which they confessed to having stolen,” said Morbi LCB inspector R T Vyas. 

 During their interrogation, the accused confessed to three robberies including 7kg silver jewellery worth Rs 1.2 lakh from Morbi and 1.88 lakh cash and gold jewellery worth Rs 1 lakh from Vankaner.tnn
