PUDUCHERRY: The fee committee constituted under   judge justice S Rajeswaran has fixed the fees structure temporarily for the postgraduate medical and dental courses under government and management quotas for 2017-18 in three private medical colleges cum hospitals in Puducherry.

 The committee fixed Rs 5.5 lakh as fee for postgraduate medical courses (clinical) and Rs 4.5 lakh for postgraduate medical courses (nonclinical) under government quota and Rs 14 lakh for postgraduate medical courses (clinical) and Rs 6 lakh for postgraduate medical courses (non-clinical) under management quota in Pondicherry Institute of Medical Sciences, Sri Manakula Vinayagar   and Hospital and Sri   and Hospital.
