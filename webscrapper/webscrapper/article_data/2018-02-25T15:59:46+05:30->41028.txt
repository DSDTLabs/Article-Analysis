The students of National Institute of Technology, Raipur recently organised on-campus   which was participated by the collegians and faculty members. The initiative was part of college's green movement to keep the campus - plastic free and clean. 

  
  
 
 The faculty members happily joined hands with students and cleaned every nook and cranny of the campus. Students had put up pamphlets on notice board informing everyone to maintain cleanliness on campus. Interestingly, the collegians also organised a tree plantation drive on campus recently, which received a good response. The college also has a special social media account wherein they promote green lifestyle.
