 According to sources, the lion carcass was found on February 4. The postmortem conducted at   revealed that the lion died because of electric shock. 
 
 One of the forest officers said, “We searched nearby villages but found no evidence of movement of lion and electric fencing around farms. The lion carcass was found in jungle area where there is no electricity connection.” 
 
 Forest officials have started searching for the person who administered shock to the lion.
