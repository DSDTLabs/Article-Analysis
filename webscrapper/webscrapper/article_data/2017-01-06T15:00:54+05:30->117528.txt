CHENNAI:   working president and treasurer M K   on Friday gave up party’s   secretary post, after holding it for over 33 years since 1984. 

 The party has appointed former highway minister M P Saminathan as the secretary. S P Chandrasekar has been appointed as Saminathan’s deputy, according to a press release issued by DMK general   K Anbazhagan.
 
 The party release asked the present deputy secretaries of the youth wing to work with new office-bearers.
