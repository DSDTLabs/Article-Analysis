NEW DELHI: The Madhya Pradesh Board Of Secondary Education, Bhopal, will declare the result of High School (HSC - 10th Class) and Higher Secondary School (HSSC - 12th Class) Examination 2018 on Monday, May 14, 2018 at 10:30 am.

 The results will be announced by the Madhya Pradesh chief minister Shivraj Singh Chouhan. 
 
 The students who appeared for the MPBSE Board exams 2018 can register below to get their result directly through SMS or email.
 
 
 
  
  
 
 
 The MPBSE Board had successfully conducted the Secondary Examination (Class 10) and Higher Secondary Examination (HSE) for the session year 2017-18 in the month of Feb-March. 
 
 If you are one of the students eagerly waiting for the announcement of MPBSE Board Class 10 and HSE Result 2018, you can check it on the official website of the MPBSE Board - mpbse.nic.in. 
 
 How to get your MPBSE Board Class 10 Result 2018
 
 OFFICIAL WEBSITES of MPBSE Board: You can log on to the official websites of MPBSE Board - www.mpbse.nic.in to check your results.
 
 IVR: Since there will be huge traffic on MPBSE Board website on result day, you can try IVR system that provides you with the results via a call.
 
 SMS: Students can also check their MPBSE Board Class 10 result 2018 via SMS.
 
 SCHOOL WEBSITE: Apart from above three ways, you can also check your MPBSE Board result on the local portal of your school.
