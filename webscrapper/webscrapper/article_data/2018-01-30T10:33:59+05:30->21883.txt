NEW DELHI: The Institute of Chartered Accountant of India (ICAI) has released the timetable for the Common Proficiency Test (CPT) 2018 examination. 

 As per the notification available on the official website of ICAI, CPT 2018 examination will be held on June 17, 2018. 
 
 The   2018 exam will be conducted in two sessions - morning session from 10.30am to 12.30pm and afternoon session from 2pm to 4pm. 
 
 The application forms for ICAI CPT 2018 will be available from April 4, 2018. The candidates willing to appear for the examination can register their application form till April 26, 2018. 
 
 The last date to fill the ICAI CPT 2018 online application form with late fees is May 3, 2018.
 
 The ICAI CPT June 2018 examination will be conducted at 196 centres across the country along with 5 centres overseas.
 
 ICAI CPT June 2018 Exam Session Schedule
 
 Morning Session (10.30am to 12.30pm): Fundamentals of Accounting, Mercantile Laws
 
 Afternoon Session (2pm to 4pm): General Economics, Quantitative Aptitude
