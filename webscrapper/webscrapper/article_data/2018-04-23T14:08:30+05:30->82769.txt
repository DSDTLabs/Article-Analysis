NEW DELHI: The Himachal   of School Education ( ) is likely to announce its Class 12th results by Tuesday evening i.e., April 24, while the Class 10 results will be released later.

 
 The HP BOSE will announce the Class 12th Result 2018 on its official website: hpbose.org.
 
 The students can register using the link below to get their result directly on their mobile or through email. 
 
 
  
  
 
 "The evaluation has been done and OMR sheets have also been checked. Now the process of uploading the results online is going on, that will be done today or by tomorrow afternoon. After that the results will be declared on the website," an official, who did not want to be named, said. 
 
 "The board will probably declare the results by 7 pm on Tuesday," he said. 
 
 HPBOSE had conducted the Class 10 Examination from March 7 to 20, 2018 and Class 12 exams were held from March 6 to 29, 2018.
 
 Last year, the HPBOSE Class 10 Examination was started from March 4 and concluded on March 17 and the HPBOSE Class 12 Examination was started from March 3 and concluded on March 28.
