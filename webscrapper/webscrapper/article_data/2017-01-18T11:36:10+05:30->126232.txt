SALEM: Two people, including a security guard, were arrested on Tuesday for shooting at a 37-year-old man, who swindled temple money, using a country-made gun in  . 

 The incident took place late on Monday night. The victim, identified as M Shanmugam, of Salaiparai village, was admitted to the Salem government hospital where his condition is said to be stable. Shanmugam, was a security guard with a coffee estate. He was a history-sheeter. Shanmugam had swindled Rs 10,000 from Mariamman temple of the same locality where his father P Mani, 60, works as an accountant, by forging documents.
