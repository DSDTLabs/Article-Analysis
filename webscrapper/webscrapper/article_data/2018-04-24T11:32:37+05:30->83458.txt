Actor and judge of the comedy show Comedy Khiladigalu season 2   will be contesting in   state assembly elections on May 12, from   constituency on   ticket.
 
  The actor has sought the support of people to win elections on twitter. According to sources from the channel, though Jaggesh has to dedicate his time for election campaigning, he will continue to be part of the show. Actor Jaggesh started his career as a comedian before getting roles as a lead actor in Sandalwood. Later, he joined politics. His suggestions on Comedy Khiladigalu show have been invaluable for contestants. Meanwhile, on film front, he will be soon seen in the movie 8MM, in which he plays the role of a cop.
