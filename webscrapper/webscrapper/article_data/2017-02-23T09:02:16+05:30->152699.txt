KOZHIKODE: Janadhipathya Rashtriya Sabha chairperson   said that she would file a   against   coordinator M   for raising the allegation that she was getting funds to split tribal people. 

 Speaking at a press meet on Wednesday, she said Geethanandan should prove his allegation or be prepared to face legal action. "I am ready to face a probe by any agency. Is Geethanandan ready to prove his charge? The allegation is nothing new as I had faced charges of getting foreign funds before," she said.
 
 Geethanandan had demanded an inquiry into the income sources of Janu and JRS and alleged that she had spent Rs 15 lakh for the observance of Muthanga agitation in Wayanad last week.
