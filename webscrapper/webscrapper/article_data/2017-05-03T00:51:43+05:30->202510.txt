THANE: After receiving several complaints from a citizen activist about repeated violations by drivers of the   Municipal Transport ( ) buses, the undertaking has initiated disciplinary action against the erring officials.

 A citizen activist Satyajit Shah had written three letters to the TMC on April 23, 29 and 30, complaining that the TMT   drivers are violating the Motor Vehicle Act.
 
 ‘‘On April 23, a TMT bus driver was driving with an incomplete license plate. On April 29, another TMT driver was speaking over the phone while driving the bus on the highway. In the third incident, a TMT bus driver jumped the signal at Cadbury junction,’’ Shah stated. 
 
 ‘‘We have taken action against the second and third driver, but we are yet to trace the first incident.
