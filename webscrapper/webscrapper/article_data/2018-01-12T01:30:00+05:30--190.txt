Hyderabad: Beleaguered Hyderabad-based infra player IVRCL Ltd on Thursday informed the bourses that its lender State Bank of India had initiated corporate insolvency proceedings against the company under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code 2016 (IBC). The company said   had initiated Corporate Insolvency Resolution Process (CIRP) against the company and filed an application against the company under regulation 7 of the IBC 2016 with the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT).

 “Pursuant to regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations 2015, we inform you that State Bank of India (lender) have initiated Corporate Insolvency Resolution Process (CIRP) against the company under Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code 2016 and filed an application under regulation 7 of the IBC with the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT),” the company said in a regulatory filing to the Bombay Stock Exchange. “The hearing on the application was held on January 8, 2018, and the orders were reserved,” the company said.
