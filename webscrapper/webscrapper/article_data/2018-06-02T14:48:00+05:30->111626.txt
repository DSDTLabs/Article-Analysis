NAGPUR:   the first person from Nagpur to be convicted in the coal block allocation scam was granted interim bail on Friday, till the next hearing on July 23. He has been also granted three weeks time to deposit the amount of Rs1 crore fine, as spelt in the earlier verdict of the special   court.

 
 Daga was sentenced to four years of imprisonment along with a fine of Rs1 crore. He was convicted of misrepresenting facts and wrongfully selling his stake in the company   which was granted the coal block.
