Vadodara: The detection of crime branch (DCB) has seized a big stock of cigarettes and booked two persons. 

 The police said that the accused had stocked and were selling cigarette packets that didn’t carry the mandatory health hazard warning. According to the police, Mahesh Agrawal, who owns a shop near the railway station, was selling these cigarette packets. He was selling imported cigarette brands made in Indonesia, Switzerland, USA and Korea. They seized cigarette packets worth Rs 24,850. The accused didn’t have bill of the stock. 
 
 Another shop owned by Girish Bharwani on Rajmahal Road was also selling such branded cigarette packets without the mandatory warning sign. He too has been booked by the cops.
