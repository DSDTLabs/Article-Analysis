Mapusa: Filmmaker Ram Gopal Varma raised public hackles on Women’s Day with a sexist and controversial tweet that wished that “all the women in the world give men as much happiness as Sunny Leone gives”. Expressing their objection to the “obscene well wishes that have gravely insulted all women”, Ranaragini, a women’s group and wing of the Hindu Janjagruti Samiti, lodged a police complaint against the 54-year-old director on Wednesday.

 The complained was lodged by Vishakha Mhambre of Ranaragini at the Mapusa police station in the presence of women from other organizations. State organizer of the group, Pratiksha Korgaokar, demanded that Varma tender a public apology.
