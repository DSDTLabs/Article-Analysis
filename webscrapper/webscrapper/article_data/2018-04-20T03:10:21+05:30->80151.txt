MUMBAI: A 38-year-old pedestrian died after a tree crashed on him outside   in   on Thursday evening. A portion of the wall of the premises, where the tree was situated, also crashed in the impact. 

 The incident happened when Dinesh Balkrishna Sangale, a photocopy centre owner and resident of Naigaum, was walking on the footpath. A police van rushed him to KEM hospital where he was declared dead before arrival. Kishore Desai, assistant commissioner of F South ward, said, “The tree needed to be trimmed as it had become uneven. A notice was issued to the private premises on April 6.”
