LUDHIANA: Panic gripped   area on Friday when two rival groups of Gurdwara Hargobind Saheb Singh Sabha, Islamgunj, clashed over the issue of acquiring gurdwara keys. Both the groups attacked the rival members, and later filed a complaint against each other. The clash between the two groups and the attack on the car has been captured in the CCTVs installed in the area, and was going viral on Whatsapp groups on Saturday. 

 Police said following the tussle over having keys of the gurdwara, one group allegedly thrashed   Raja and chased him to his house. The miscreants barged into his house and also thrashed his paternal aunt,  , who had to be admitted to the Civil Hospital. Later in retaliation, the rival group attacked the car of their rivals with swords and sticks, and damaged it badly. Some women were also inside the car.
 
 Following the complaint of Gurdeep Singh, whose car was damaged, the police have booked nine persons under sections 323 (voluntarily causing hurt) 427 (causing damage) 324 (voluntarily causing hurt by dangerous weapons or means) 148 (rioting, armed with deadly weapon) and 149 (every member of unlawful assembly guilty of offence committed in prosecution of common object) of the IPC. 
 
 Later on Friday night, the group supporting Gurdeep Singh allegedly again attacked the house of Ravinder Singh by pelting stones. Another group also approached the police for filing an FIR.
 
 Additional deputy commissioner of police (  -I)   said complaints have been filed by both the groups against each other. 
 
 "Action would be taken against all those who have been identified in the video," the ADCP said.
