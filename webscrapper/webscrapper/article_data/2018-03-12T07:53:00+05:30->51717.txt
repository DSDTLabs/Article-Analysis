LUDHIANA:   police have booked the 25-yearold son of a money exchanger for allegedly raping a 24-yearold woman who was part of his staff. The accused had allegedly developed “physical relations” with the woman by promising to marry her, but he later tried to get engaged to another woman, it has been alleged. The accused used to assist his father in his business. The complainant, separated from her husband, alleged that she got married to a person in the village near   city about four years ago. She alleged that since her husband never trusted her, she parted ways with him and was living with his two-year-old daughter for the a year.

 As per the complainant, about a year ago she joined the money-exchange firm. She said about five months ago, the accused assuring to marry her, established physical relations with her.
