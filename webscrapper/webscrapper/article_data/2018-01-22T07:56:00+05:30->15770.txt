Ludhiana: As many as 200 shuttlers from across the state impressed the audience with their performances on the final day of the Punjab State Masters Badminton Tournament, which was concluded on Sunday at Guru Nanak stadium, Ludhiana.

 Speaking on the occasion, Anupam Kumeria, member of the district badminton association, said, “In this tournament, players in the 35+ to 65+ age groups have put up a great show.” He also lauded the efforts of the talents and senior shuttlers, who encouraged the young players.
