MUZAFFARNAGAR: The bodies of two persons, who were evidently murdered somewhere else, were on Thursday found from a canal in Muzaffarnagar’s   area. Police are yet to identify the deceased, whose bodies were found wrapped in clothes in   canal.
 
  Police rushed to the spot and sent the bodies for post mortem examination.
  
  “We are trying to identify them. A probe has been launched into the matter and we will soon crack the case,” said superintendent of police (City), Omveer Singh.
  
  According to eyewitness, the bodies were wrapped in cream colour clothes. After spotting them, a farmer informed the police.
  
  Their age would have been between 30 and 35 years, police said. Police suspect that the youth belong to neighbouring   district and their bodied were dumped in a canal after murder.
