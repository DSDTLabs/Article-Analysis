PATNA: The High Commissioner of Mauritius to India Jagdishwar Goburdhun on Tuesday called on Bihar CM   at the latter’s residence and invited him on behalf of the Mauritius government to the 183rd anniversary celebration of ‘Aapravasi Diwas’ in Mauritius on November 2.

 ‘Aapravasi Diwas’ is observed in Mauritius every year to commemorate the arrival of the first batch of indentured labourers from India. 
 
 It could not be known immediately whether the CM would attend the event or not. 
 
 Sources said Nitish honoured Goburdhun by offering him a shawl and memento. Goburdhun also offered a shawl to him.
 
 Bihar shares a special bonding with Mauritius. More than 60% population of Mauritius is of Indian origin, and majority of them are Bhojpuri-speaking people having roots in Bihar.
