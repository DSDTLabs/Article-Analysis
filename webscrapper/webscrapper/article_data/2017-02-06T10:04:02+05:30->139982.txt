GUWAHATI: Governor   has urged students to adopt a   and build a strong moral character to succeed in life. 

 Speaking at the prize distribution ceremony of Quizitive 2017, organized by PRERNA at the Royal Group of Institutions, Betkuchi, recently, Purohit called upon the students to lead a simple yet disciplined life.
 
  He advised them to develop the spirit of a sportsman as this would help them march forward in spite of challenges.
 
 With an attempt to provide exposure and encouragement to students belonging to all sections of society, PRERNA, the IAS Officers' Wives Association, conducted the   Competition-Quizitive 2017. Students from Classes VIII to XII took part in the quiz.
