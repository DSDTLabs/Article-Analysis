BICHOLIM: A theft worth around Rs 1,83,000 was reported from the   temple at Sakhali on Saturday night. Thieves broke open the side door to effect their entry. A complaint has been lodged by devasthan secretary   at   police station. The burglars decamped with one silver crown, two golden ghalsaries, one sliver idol of Lord Mahadev and two fund boxes. The thieves locked the house door of the devasthan priest, who resides near the temple, before committing the crime.

 Police tried to trace the thieves with the assistance of a dog squad, but failed. CCTV footage and finger prints have been collected from from the crime site.
 
 PSI Harish Gawas conducted the panchanama with the assistance of   under the guidance of PI  .
