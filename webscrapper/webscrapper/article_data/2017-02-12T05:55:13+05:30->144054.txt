 A total of 2.60 crore voters, including over 1.17 crore women and 1,508 belonging to the third gender, were eligible to cast their ballot to decide the fate of 839 candidates. The first phase of polling will decide the electoral fortunes of Pankaj Singh, son of home minister Rajnath Singh, from Noida; Congress Legislature Party member Pradeep Mathur in Mathura, where BJP spokesman Shrikant Sharma is also in the fray; Mariganka Singh, daughter of BJP MP Hukum Singh, in Kairana, and controversial BJP MLA Sangeet Som from Sardhana.
