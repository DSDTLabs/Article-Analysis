NAGPUR: Forest minister   has constituted a committee under principal secretary (forest)   to study a presentation made by US firm   to make   a world-class spot.

 The committee will submit its report in three weeks. Tenders to appoint a consultant for Tadoba were floated in which only Solimar applied. 
 
 Solimar has implemented projects in various countries in America, Africa, Mexico, Malaysia, Singapore.
