It was a proud moment for actor Vijayakumar and his family. Well, the actor was honoured with a doctorate by a Chennai university at their 25th convocation. There was also a felicitation program that was conducted for the actor at a star hotel.   The event saw Vijayakumar’s son and actor Arun Vijay, who looked dapper in a suit and was looking after the guests at the event. Also present was his wife Aarti who was seen having a great time with friends like actor Maheshwari. Vijayakumar’s daughter Preetha was delighted to welcome all the guests, so was her husband and director Hari.    Director Sundar C congratulated the actor and was seen interacting with the other guests. Also among the guests was lyricist Vairamuthu and actor Vishal.
