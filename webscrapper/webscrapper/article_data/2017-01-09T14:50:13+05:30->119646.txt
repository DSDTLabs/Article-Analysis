BHOPAL: Three days after a Congress leader and a gangster were shot dead in a shootout in Jabalpur district; police arrested three accused on Sunday. 

 The arrest comes a day after the video of the entire shootout became viral on social media.
 
 Earlier on the night of January 4, two persons, including a Congress leader were shot dead in Jabalpur town by unidentified assailants. The incident took place around 10.15 pm near Cherital area. The deceased were identified as Raju Mishra, 46, and Kukku Punjabi alias Ranjeet, 32.
