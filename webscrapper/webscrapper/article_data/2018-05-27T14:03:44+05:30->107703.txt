It looks like the protest against   smelting plant is not going down anytime soon. A section of Tamil film directors and actors recently staged a protest where they protested against the recent police firing in   and demanded the closure of the Sterlite industry. The directors who took part in it included  ,  , Sasi, Ram, Pandiraj, SP Jhananathan, Balaji Sakthivel, Seenu Ramasamy, Nalan Kumarasamy, Bramma, Karthik Subbaraj and Mari Selvaraj. Actors like Ashok Selvan, Aravind Akash, Kalaiyarasan, and music director Santhosh Narayanan also took part in this protest. 

 While speaking on the occasion, director Ranjith said that the Tamil Nadu and the central government have been instigating protests by the public. He added that they have been constantly creating problems for the public, including the latest violence in Tuticorin. 13 people were killed in police firing on May 22 when they staged an agitation against the expansion of the plant in the town.
