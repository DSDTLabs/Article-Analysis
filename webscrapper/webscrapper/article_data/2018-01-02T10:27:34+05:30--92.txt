LUCKNOW: The dawn of New Year failed to put rail traffic on the track. More than 70 trains arrived late at   railway station and Lucknow Junction due to fog on Monday morning. Among the late trains were prominent ones like Shatabdi Express, Rajdhani Express and Marudhar Express.

 Railway officials said key trains running in the night are likely to arrive late on Tuesday as well owing to the prevailing fog in north India.
  
  
 Railway officials claimed that equipment like anti-fog devices to loco pilots, detonators to gatemen, modern signalling and extra crew for patrolling have been provided to divisions for proper operation of trains in fog.
