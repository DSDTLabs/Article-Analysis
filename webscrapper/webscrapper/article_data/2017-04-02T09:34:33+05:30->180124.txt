HYDERABAD: A 19-year-old girl committed suicide by setting   at Meerpet on Friday night after she came to know that her boyfriend uploaded her photos on the  . 

 Meerpet police said that S Mamatha, daughter of a kirana store owner in  , had become friends with Nagaraju who works at a jewellery shop located close to the kirana store. They took a few photographs while they were together. 
 
 But when Nagaraju proposed to Mamatha, she rejected it. Nagaraju then threatened the girl that he would upload their photos on social media if she did not change her mind and accept his proposal. When she continued to refuse him, Nagaraju carried out his threat, causing extreme distress to the girl. 
 
 On Friday, she doused herself with kerosene and set herself ablaze. She succumbed to burns late in the night while undergoing treatment at a hospital. 
 
 Police registered a case under section 354 D (Any man who follows a woman and contacts, or attempts to contact such woman to foster personal interaction repeatedly) and 306 (abetment to suicide) of the Indian Penal Code.
