MUMBAI: There wlll be a megablock on the   main line on the   Up fast track from 11.15am to 4.15pm.

 The Up Fast line services leaving   from 10.54 am to 4.19 pm will be diverted on Up Slow line between Kalyan and Thane stations, halting at all stations and further rediverted on Up Fast line from  , halting at Mulund, Bhandup, Vikhroli, Ghatkopar, Kurla, Dadar and Byculla stations and arrive destination 15 minutes behind schedule.
 
 Similarly, all Down fast line services leaving   from 10.16am to 3.22pm will halt at Ghatkopar, Vikhroli, Bhandup, Mulund and Diva stations in addition to their respective scheduled halts and arrive destination 15 minutes behind schedule, an official said. 
 
 "Due to these blocks, suburban trains are likely to be more crowded than usual. Passengers are requested not to take any risks while travelling. They are also requested to avoid travelling on footboard, rooftop of locals and not to board extremely over crowded trains," the official stated. 
 
 "These maintenance mega blocks are essential for infrastructure upkeep and safety. Passengers are requested to bear with the Railway administration for the inconvenience caused," he added.
