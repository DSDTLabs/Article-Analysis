Asif Ali’s recent release   found a lot of support online after the actor’s heartfelt message on social media, pleading the audience to watch the film.

 The general consensus is that the movie had the actor putting on one of his best performances, of late. His latest film   could showcase one better, if the words of its director   is to be believed.
 
 During a video shared by the team on social media, the filmmaker said Kaatu will have Asif Ali’s best performance yet. The movie has him playing a ‘naadan’ youth and is set in a rural milieu. Murali Gopy and Varalaxmi Sarathkumar also star in the film, scripted by Ananthapadmanabhan of August Club fame.
