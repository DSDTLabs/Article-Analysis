NEW DELHI: The Uttarakhand Board of School Education (UBSE Board) has announced the Class 10 and Class 12 exam results on its official website today, May 26, 2018. 

 
 Divyanshi Raj, from Jaspur, tops Class 12 while Kajal Prajapati, of Khatima Uddhamsingh Nagar, tops Class 10 exams. Both the students scored the identical marks i.e., 98.40% in their respective examinations. 
 
 
  
  
 The   2018 were released bye the School Education Minister Arvind Pandey at School Education headquarters in Dehradunon. Students can now check their respective results from the official websites   and uaresults.nic.in.
 
 
 
  
  
  
 
 
  
  
 Divyanshi Raj, from Jaspur, tops Class 12 with 98.40% while Kajal Prajapati, of Khatima Uddhamsingh Nagar, tops Class 10 with 98.40%.
 
 In Class 12 around 78.97% students have passed the exams while in the Class 1074.57% students declared successful.
 
 
 
 
 
 The exams have been conducted between 6th March 2018 and 24th March 2018.
 
 
 
 
 
 A total of 1,32,381 students took the Uttarakhand Class 12th examination. Last year, a total of 1,45,914 students had taken the Class 12th examination. There are more than 17,000 government schools across all 13 districts in Uttarakhand.
 
 In 2017, 73.67% Class 10 students cleared the exam while 78.89% candidates were declared successful in Class 12.
 
 The Board had conducted the Secondary Examination (Class 10) and Higher Secondary Examination (Class 12) for the session year 2017-18 in the month of Feb-March. 
 
 Neeta Tiwari, Secretary Uttarakhand Board of School Education (UBSE), would declare the Class 10 and 12 results at the Board's headquarters at Ramnagar in Nainital district. 
 
 As per the latest information, the Board is likely to announce the UBSE Board Class 10 and Class 12 Results 2018 on its official website today at 11 am.
