PUNE :The   has recently arrested an executive director (works),   and a representative of Kolkata based private firm in a bribery of Rs 1 lakh.

 A case was registered against the executive director a GM and a representative both private persons working in Kolkata based private firm and unknown others under section 120 (b) of Indian Penal Code and sections 7 and 12 of Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988.
 
 It was alleged that the executive director allegedly entered into criminal conspiracy with said private persons as well as others in the award of supply orders of electrical, electronics, electro-mechanical machineries and parts to the steel plant in favour of manufacturers whom the private persons represent as well as for smooth execution of those contracts in violation of the laid down rules, and for ensuring the same, private parties were allegedly paying illegal gratifications both in cash and kind to said executive director.
 
 It was further alleged that executive director demanded gratification from the company through said two persons of a private firm as he had sanctioned work order in the “AMR Cell” matter in favour of their company while working as In-charge CEO of Rourkela Steel Plant.
 
 After registration of the case,   laid a trap and arrested the executive director and private person at Rourkela, when the private person handed over bribe amount of Rs 1,00,000 along with a watch to ED at his residence.
 
 Searches were conducted at nine places including at Rourkela, Hazaribagh, Ranchi, Bokaro and Kolkata in the premises of the accused persons which led to recovery of incriminating documents.
 
 During house search of executive director, cash amount of Rs 20 lakh (approx) in new currency was recovered. Further investigation is continuing.
