LUDHIANA: At least 10 people were injured as a speeding   bus collided with a truck at   on Monday mid-night. The collision was so strong that the truck turned turtle and the windowpanes of the bus were smashed. 

 
 On hearing the screams of passengers, passers-by rushed to the spot and called up the police and the 108 emergency ambulance. While the bus driver and passengers sustained minor injuries, the truck driver’s left knee was fractured. The injured were taken to a nearby hospital and discharged after the first-aid.
 
 The truck driver,  , said, “I was driving from Moga to Ludhiana. When I reached   Chowk, the speeding bus — coming from Fountain chowk — ran into my truck. The truck turned over. With much difficulty, I came out. The crash left my knee fractured.” 
 
 Bus stand police post in-charge assistant sub-inspector Chand Ahir said on the statement of the truck driver, the police have booked bus driver Tarlochan Singh, a resident of  , under sections 279 (rash driving), 337 (causing hurt by act endangering life or personal safety of others), 338 (causing grievous hurt by act endangering life or personal safety of others), 427 (mischief causing damage to the amount of fifty rupees) of the IPC.
