LUDHIANA: A seven-year-old girl was kidnapped from outside her house, raped at an isolated place and then abandoned at   bus stand in a serious condition on Wednesday evening. Daughter of an auto driver, the girl was spotted by a group of students. The police have launched a manhunt for the accused, whose image has been captured in a   camera installed in the area. 

 Father of the rape survivor told the police that his two daughters were playing outside their house on Wednesday around noon when a biker came and asked his elder daughter to accompany him. "He told her that I was calling her," the father said, adding that the accused gave Rs 10 to her younger sister before taking the girl away.
 
 Police said some nursing students of a local college saw the girl, a school drop-out, around 4pm and rushed her to civil hospital in Sudhar. When her condition worsened, she was referred to Ludhiana civil hospital.
 
 The girl's father told the police that his wife had gone to their native place in   and the two girls were alone when he left for work.
 
  police have registered a case under sections 366 A (procuration of a minor girl), 376 (rape) of the   and Section 4 of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act.
 
 SHO Dalbir Singh said cops have procured the footage of CCTV cameras installed in the area. "The cameras have captured the accused, who is around 40 years old and sports a beard. Whether the accused is an acquaintance of the girl's family is not yet clear. The condition of the girl is stable."
 
 Police personnel from women's cell, too, reached the hospital to probe into the incident.
