SALIGAO:   on Monday arrested 15 workers and a landowner on   in a field at Saligao.

 Police sources said a complaint from the town and country planning department was acted upon, resulting in the arrest of the workers and the landowner.
 
 Some residents had recently filed a complaint with the flying squad stating that a person allegedly dumped mud into a field at Sonarbhat. Accordingly, officials of the flying squad inspected the area. Deputy collector   had also issued a show cause notice ordering the landowner to stop the work. Locals informed government agencies again that the landowner was continuing with the work. 
 
 Police conducted a raid at the spot and nabbed the accused landowner red-handed after he was seen dumping mud in the field.
