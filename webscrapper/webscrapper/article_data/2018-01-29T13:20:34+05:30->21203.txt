The Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI), in its job notification, wishes to hire engineers at uidai.gov.in. Candidates who are willing to apply can now visit the official website of the organization to submit the application form. The openings are for people with 0-5 years of experience and have openings in Delhi, Bengaluru, Mumbai, Hyderabad and Chandigarh. Moreover, candidates with higher experience than this may also be considered based on the requirement.

 The candidates are expected to have experience in software development, technology architecture design, software testing, biometrics, cryptography, cyber security, project management etc. 
 
 The job will be initially offered to the candidates on a 2-years’ contract after which it may be further extended on the basis of a person’s performance and job availability.
