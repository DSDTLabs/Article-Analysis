ALLAHABAD: The Allahabad high court on Tuesday declined to interfere in the  's order through which   Atiq Ahmad was transferred from   here to Deoria District Jail. 

 Hearing an application moved by Atiq, a division bench comprising Chief Justice Dilip Babasaheb Bhosale and Justice Yashwant Varma, on a request made by Atiq's counsel, deferred the hearing of the application till April 7. Now this application will be heard along with the writ petition relating to SHUATS assault case, where the next date fixed is April 7. Former MP Atiq is an accused in the SHUATS assault case. Atiq, who was elected MLA several times from City West assembly constituency of Allahabad, was subsequently elected MP on   ticket from Phulpur parliamentary constituency of Allahabad and severed as parliamentarian from 2004 to 2009. The BJP government has transferred several mafia dons from the jails they were lodged to other jails. Following this order, Atiq was shifted from Naini Central Jail to Deoria District Jail.
