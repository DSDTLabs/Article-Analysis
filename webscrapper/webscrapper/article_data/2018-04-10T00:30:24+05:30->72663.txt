Pune: A youth was booked on Sunday for raping a minor girl (17) of his college for the past three months and threatening to post her obscene photographs on a social networking site if she revealed his heinous act to anybody.

 The Khadak police said the incident came to light on Sunday when the girl informed her mother about the harassment. Along with the mother, the girl approached the police and lodged a complaint.
 
 The police have booked the 17-year-old youth under sections 376 (rape), 354-D (stalking), 323 (voluntarily causing hurt) of the Indian Penal Code and the relevant sections of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act.
 
 An officer of the Khadak police said the girl and the youth were studying in the same college and knew each other. “According to the girl’s claim, the youth took her to a room in Kondhwa in January and raped her,” the officer said.
 
 The youth allegedly took the girl to another room in Bhavani Peth a few days later and raped her again. When the girl refused to go with him, the suspect abused her and beat her up.
 
 “He even threatened the girl that he had clicked her photographs in a compromising position and would post them on a social networking site,” the officer said.
 
 He said the youth was raping the girl till the last month by blackmailing her. “Frustrated over the harassment, the girl on Sunday narrated her woes to her mother and approached the police,” the officer said.
