SRINAGAR: A civilian and a soldier were killed when terrorists attacked the Army’s 50 Rashtriya Rifles camp at Kakapora in south Kashmir’s   district late on Sunday evening. 

 
 Police said  , a Sumo driver, was critically injured in the crossfire and later succumbed to injuries in hospital. When the terrorists fired at the camp, soldiers retaliated. 
 
 In the encounter, a jawan also died, SSP Pulwama Mohammad Aslam Choudary said. The terrorists later escaped from the encounter site, sources said.
