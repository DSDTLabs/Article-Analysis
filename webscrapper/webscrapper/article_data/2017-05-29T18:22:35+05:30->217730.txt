SS Rajamouli’s ambitious project ‘Baahubali 2: The Conclusion’ is roaring at the box-office since the day of its release. Even in its fourth week, the film is continuing its dominance at the ticket windows, giving its contemporaries a run for their money.
 
 The film’s Hindi version earned Rs 3.25 crore (nett) on its fourth Monday, thus taking its fourth week collections so far to Rs 21.50 crore (nett), according to a report on Boxofficeindia.com. With this, the total collection of the film’s Hindi version stands at Rs 477.74 crore.
 
 
  
 The first week collection of ‘Baahubali 2’ (Hindi) was an astounding Rs 246.47 crore, followed by Rs 142.52 crore in its second week and Rs 67.25 crore in its third week. With a phenomenal start to its fourth week, chances are that the   starrer will stay strong in its fifth week too.
  
 ‘Baahubali 2: The Conclusion’ enjoyed a grand release in about 9000 screens worldwide. The film was earlier scheduled to release in 2016, however, the makers pushed the release to 2017.
