MUMBAI:After almost a week of poor air quality, the city witnessed its cleanest air on Saturday. According to System of Air Quality Weather Forecasting and Research ( ), Mumbai, recorded an the air quality index of 123 in the morning which improved to 120 by evening. 

 On Tuesday and Wednesday, as haze engulfed the city skies, Mumbai witnessed its worst air quality levels since Diwali. However, bringing respite, the AQI finally dropped into the ‘moderate’ category (between 101 and 200) on Friday when SAFAR recorded an AQI of 189.
