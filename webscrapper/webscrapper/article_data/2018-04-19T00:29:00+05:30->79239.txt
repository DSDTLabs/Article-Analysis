Pimpri Chinchwad: The class-IV employees of Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation (PCMC) will now get an   of Rs 10 lakh.

 Sagar Charan, vice-president, grievance redressal committee of the health department, PCMC, told TOI, “I have received a letter from municipal commissioner Shravan Hardikar that these employees will get the benefit of the accident insurance from April 1, 2018.”
 
 He said the safai karmacharis (cleanliness workers) play an important role in keeping the city spick and span. “If an employee dies on duty, his family will receive Rs 10 lakh as compensation through this scheme. In case of injury, the employee will get re-imbursement for his medical bills,” Charan added.
