AMRITSAR:   (AAP) leader Gurpartap Singh   had a narrow escape after a couple of unidentified miscreants opened fire at him late on Sunday. Talking to TOI on Monday, Gurpartap, who was   candidate from Dera Baba Nanak assembly constituency in the   assembly elections, said that he was parking his vehicle in front of his residence at Khushalpur village in Gurdaspur district when he heard gunshots, following which he scampered towards his house for safety. "Even after that I heard few more rounds being fired," he said.
