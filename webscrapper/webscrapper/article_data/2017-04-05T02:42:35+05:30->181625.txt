NOIDA: As a confidence-building measure between the Indian and African communities, a friendly football match will be organised between students from both communities on Wednesday at Sharda University,  .

 The match, which is being organised by the university, will be played between teams comprising students and members from both the communities. While one team will represent the university, the other will represent ambassadors of the various African countries.
 
 The district magistrate had asked the educational institutes to organise such events to promote harmony and understanding between the two communities, in the wake of the attacks on African students in Greater Noida.
 
 The match which will be played at 4pm on Wednesday at the varsity campus, will also see attendees from the embassies of Nigeria, Kenya and other African countries, along with members of the   in India (AASI).
