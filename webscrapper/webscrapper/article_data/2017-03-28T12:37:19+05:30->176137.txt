  Salman Khan from Astitva performed on the last day of the fest (BCCL/ Lokesh Kashyap)
 
 
  
 The students of Bennett University organized a face painting competition on the second day of the fest. They were given 'Vice and Virtue' as the theme to paint the faces of their team members. While some students painted the face of The Joker from the movie 'The Dark Knight', others experimented with characters like Deadpool and Batman.
 
 
  
  
 
 
 
 
  Nandana Varshna, who participated in the competition with her teammate Cairo Gulati, told us, "Since the theme was 'Vice and Virtue', I painted half of my partner's face with flowers, which signified a positive aspect, while the other half was painted black.The first half was like heaven and the remaining half like hell. We didn't pick any particular character from any movie for the competition."
 
  Prateek Soni, who painted Shaurya Vir Singh's face like The Joker from the movie, 'The Dark Knight' added, "We thought that this was the right character to go with the theme."
