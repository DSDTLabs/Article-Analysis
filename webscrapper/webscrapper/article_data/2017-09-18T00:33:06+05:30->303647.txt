Trichy: Devotees converged in large numbers on the banks of the Cauvery in Trichy and Mayiladuthurai in Nagapattinam district on Sunday to take part in the Cauvery   festival.

 Being the first Sunday after the beginning of the festival, which is held once in 12 years, crowd had started gathering on the banks of the river at Amma Mandapam and also at Mayiladuthurai from the early hours of Sunday. Varuna Homam was carried by the priests at the yagasala created at Amma Mandapam Road around 8 am. Vaibhava Shahsti Homam was carried out later in the day. 
 
 Around 6 pm, Maha Deeparadhana was observed on the river bank at Amma Mandapam. This was followed by Shanthi Homam around 8.30 pm.
 
 A total of five temporary bus stands have been created for the huge crowd gathering at the Pushkaram venue in Trichy. However devotees had a tough time moving out of the venue due to traffic congestion. 
 
 Chief minister Edappadi K Palaniswami is expected to visit Mayiladuthurai on Wednesday when Mahalaya Amavasya is to be observed.
