NEW DELHI: National Council for Hotel Management & Catering Technology ( ) has extended its Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) date from April 11 to April 16, 2018. 

 
 Through this exam the NCHMCT offers admission in three years of B.Sc Hospitality & Hotel management courses. Candidates can get admission in various NCHMCT affiliated institutions across the country. 
 
 
 
 
 The NCHM JEE 2018 exam will be conducted on April 28, 2018 and the admit card for the same will likely be released on April 20, 2018 (5 pm onwards).
