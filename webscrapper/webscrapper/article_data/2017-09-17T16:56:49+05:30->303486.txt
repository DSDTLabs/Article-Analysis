PUDUCHERRY: Chief Minister   today paid tributes to the founder of   outfit, E V   on the occasion of his 138th birth anniversary.
 
 Welfare Minister M Kandasamy, legislators and delegates of different wings of the ruling   also garlanded the statue of the rationalist leader here.
  
 Leaders and MLAs of the opposition AIADMK ( ) led by the leader of the legislature wing of the party A Anbalagan, functionaries of the Dravidar Kazhagam, DMK and several other outfits also paid floral tributes at the statue on the occasion.
  
 EV Ramasamy Periyar, founder of the self-respect movement, fought for social equality and is a respected leader across party lines.
