LUDHIANA: Another major fire incident happened in a   factory on   in   on Sunday early morning. Material worth crores gutted in fire but exact reasons behind spread of fire are yet to be ascertained. The fire officials took almost four and half hours to douse the flames. 

 The fire took place at around 3.55am in Radha Plywood. Around 7 fire tenders were stationed at the spot and 20 rounds of fire tenders were made to control the situation. 
 
 The sub fire officer   said there was no loss of life or injury to anybody and situation came in control in few hours.
