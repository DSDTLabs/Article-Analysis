GUWAHATI: State BJP on Sunday has served a   notice to its MLA   on charges of defying the party's order in constituting the   members with help from the All India United Democratic Front ( ), Congress and other Independent members. 

 Dev represents Hojai in the assembly.
 
 BJP president Ranjeet Kumar Das has suspended five of the party's ward commissioners for violating party order. Das said that the dissident ward commissioners in presence of Dev took the help of AIUDF, Congress and independent members to form the municipal board on Saturday.
