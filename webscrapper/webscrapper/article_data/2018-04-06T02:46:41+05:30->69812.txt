NEW DELHI: Armed robbers attacked a jewellery   in   on Thursday and decamped with valuables worth Rs 30 lakh. 

  DCP (Rohini)   said that a case was registered and teams were formed to nab the accused. Initial probe revealed that the four men entered the showroom around 2.30pm and threatened to shoot the employees if they did not handover the cash and jewellery. After collecting the valuables, the men fled on their two-wheelers.
