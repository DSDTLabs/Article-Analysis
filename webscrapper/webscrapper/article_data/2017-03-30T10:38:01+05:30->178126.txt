 This is for the first time that INA has won the Bakshi Cup since its inception, said an official press release.
 
 Bakhshi Cup is an annual Inter Services sports meet for trainees of six premier Officers’ Training Academies – the National Defence Academy (NDA), Khadakwasla, the Indian Military Academy (IMA), Dehradun, Indian Naval Academy (INA), Ezhimala, Air Force Academy (AFA), Dundigul, Officers' Training Academy (OTA), Chennai and Officers Training Academy (OTA), Gaya.
 
 57 Cadets of the INA participated in six sporting events including basketball, football, volleyball, tennis, squash and cross country. The cadets of INA won both cross country and basketball and were the runners up in football and volley ball, said the release. NDA is the runners up in the Bakshi Cup. The INA team was given a reception upon its arrival at INA on Tuesday.
