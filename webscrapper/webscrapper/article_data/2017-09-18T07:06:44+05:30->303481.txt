CHENNAI: Former Union minister and senior   leader   died in a Chennai hospital on Sunday. He was 74.
 
 A member of parliament from Bihar's Araria constituency, Taslimuddin was admitted to   here on August 26 after he developed breathing problems. He was in the city in connection with a Lok Sabha committee meeting. 
  
 Taslimuddin breathed his last at 1.10pm on Sunday, hospital sources said.
  
 His body will be sent back to his native place on Sunday night. 
  
 Taslimuddin was a seven-time   and five-time MP. He had served as the Union minister of state for home in the UPA I government at the Centre.
