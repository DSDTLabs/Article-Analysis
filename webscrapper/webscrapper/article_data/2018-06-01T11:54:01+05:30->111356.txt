PANAJI: Inspector general of police ( )   has asked the police staff to be sensitive to the needs of women, children and senior citizens and to increase police visibility around the state, especially on the coastline.

 At a   on Thursday in  , he advised the staff to be professional in their work and be courteous in their dealings with citizens. However, he asked them to deal firmly with anti-social elements.
 
  He also told the staff to continue their drive against drug dealers. He particularly cited the case of foreigners who are openly flouting traffic rules in certain parts of the district. 
 
 The IGP also solicited suggestions from the staff and officers to further improve policing in the district. He then listened to the grievances of the police personnel.
