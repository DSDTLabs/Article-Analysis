Madurai: A woman worker sustained burn injuries in an accident at fireworks manufacturing unit at Panayadipatti village near Sattur in Virudhunagar district on Thursday. 

 Arumugasamy from Sivakasi owns a fireworks making unit which has around 50 working sheds, manufacturing mostly small type of fireworks. More than 100 workers were involved in fireworks manufacturing on Thursday.
 
 Around noon, there was a blast in one of the working sheds. Megala, 29 from Melapudur working in that shed sustained injuries. 
 
 Other workers doused the fire since it was a smaller fire in nature and rushed Megala to a private hospital at Sattur. Police sources said that the woman worker is stable and is out of danger.
