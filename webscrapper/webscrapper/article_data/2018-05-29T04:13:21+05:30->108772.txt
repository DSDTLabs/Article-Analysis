GANDHINAGAR: Chief Minister   on Monday went on an unscheduled visit to New Delhi which fueled speculations in the political circles. There were speculations that Rupani was called by the Prime Minister   for some political issues.

 When contacted CM told the Times of India that, “I went to Delhi to meet Union railway minister   to discuss pending issues of Gujarat. I checked with the PM office for his time but he was occupied so could not meet him.’’ 
 
 “I discussed key projects like bullet train, dedicated freight corridor, metro rail, development of   railway station hotel and other pending issues related to railway,” he added.
