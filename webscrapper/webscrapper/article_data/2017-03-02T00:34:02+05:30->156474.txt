Hyderabad: The state government has accorded an administrative sanction for release of Rs 20 lakh towards payment of   to the family members of Kotha Rakesh and Devarakonda Praveen under Telangana Amaraveerula Pathakam.

 The two youths had laid down their lives in the Telangana statehood struggle. The government will give Rs 10 lakh each to the members of the bereaved families.
 
 The Telangana revenue department issued orders on Wednesday stating that the Warangal collector had submitted a detailed enquiry report stating that the two youths - Rakesh and Praveen - of the district sacrificed their lives for Telangana statehood and submitted the proposal for relief to the eligible family members of the martyrs.
