GREATER NOIDA: A group of men barged into a house and shot at a couple in   in Dankaur on Tuesday night. Chatar Singh received a bullet injury to his shoulder, while his wife Rajwati was shot in the leg. 

 Police said Singh and his neighbouring family had differences due to personal enmity.
 
 Farmood Ali Pundir, Dankaur police station SHO, said in the evening, Singh’s nephew and the neighbour Sunil Kumar’s son clashed with each other while playing outside their house. Sunil’s son received minor injuries, and returned home. Kumar, along with 5-6 armed men, barged into Singh’s home and thrashed his family members, he said. 
 
 Other neighbours approached police and rushed the injured to a private hospital.
