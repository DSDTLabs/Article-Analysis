 The   fave, polks dots are the easiest to achieve. To get the look, first paint your nails with a single colour base shade. Anything will do, from pink to peach or even black. Use a toothpick to dab on dots and you’re done!
 
 
 
 Glitter confetti nail art is perfect for a day or evening out. To get the look, paint your nails with a nude shade. Now use glitter confetti  . Once it dries, top it with a transparent top coat. 
 
 
 
 
  
  
 
 
 
 
  
  
 
 It’s winter so having snow on your nails is a must-do. Start with a transparent or semi-transparent look at the base. For a snowy look, dip a sponge in white enamel and tap it on the edges of the nails. Once dry, top it with a transparent coat.
