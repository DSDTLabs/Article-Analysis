New Delhi: The Rajasthan State Open School results for class 12 board exam were declared on May 31, 2018, yesterday. Updated results are available on the official website - www.education.rajasthan.gov.in/rsos.

 Students can check their results using their respective roll numbers. The examination for RSOS students was held in the month of March 2018 and the results will be available on the official website of RSOS. 
 
 A report in Rajasthan Patrika stated that this year nearly 49, 279 students had appeared for the RSOS Class 12 board exam. Out of the total, 33.95 percent students passed the RSOS 12th exam, constituting 16,772 candidates. The pass percentage for girls in RSOS 12th is 37.85 percent and for boys is 29.77 percent. The topper among boys is Tarun Sharma and among girls is Yashika Audichya. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Board of Secondary Education, Rajasthan (BSER) had initially announced the result for the regular Class 12 Science and Commerce stream students on May 23, 2018. 
 The pass percentage for Science stream students was 87.78 percent and the pass percentage for Commerce stream students was 91.1 percent.
