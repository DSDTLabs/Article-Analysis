Panchkula: Three people were injured after a speeding jeep hit their car from the rear on the road dividing sectors 9 and 10 in a hit-and-run on Friday night.

 Narender, a resident of Sector 12, said he along with two others was returning from a dinner party. When he reached the sector 9-10 dividing road, the jeep hit them around 11.30 pm. The car hit the divider and overturned.
 
 Passers-by pulled out the three occupants. They were taken to General Hospital, Sector 6. All three were discharged after first aid.
 
 Police have registered a case under sections 279 (rash driving or riding on a public way) and 337 (causing hurt by act endangering life or personal safety of others) of the Indian Penal Code (IPC) at the Sector 5 police station.
 
 Inspector Satish Kumar of Sector 5 police station said, “The complainant could note down only four digits of the registration number of the jeep. We are looking for CCTV footage to trace the vehicle.”
