Amritsar: Three persons, including a married woman, committed suicide by consuming poisonous substance in Amritsar on Friday. According to reports, the victims were identified as Satnam Singh (22), Amritpal Singh (23) and Lovedeep Kaur (26).

 All three of them were spotted wriggling in pain near Fatehabad and were rushed to the hospital where they were declared brought dead. Fatehabad police post in-charge police Lakhwinder Singh informed that police had registered a case and has started investigation. Though the police didn’t find any suicide note, sources didn’t rule out a love triangle behind the three taking the extreme step. Satnam had got an Australian visa and was to leave in coming time.
