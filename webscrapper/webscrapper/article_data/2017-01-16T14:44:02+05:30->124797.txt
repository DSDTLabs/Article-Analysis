SAMBALPUR: The state forest department on Sunday begun the census of migratory birds arriving at the  . It has roped in wildlife enthusiasts,   for the counting. 

 Divisional forest officer (Hirakud) Sudeep Nayak said, "We have divided the entire area into 14 sectors. Seventy people have been divided into 14 teams."
 
 "We will send the data to the office of the Chief Wildlife Warden in Bhubaneswar latest by Monday morning. They will tabulate the data" he said. 
 
 Last winter, 95,912 birds were counted.
