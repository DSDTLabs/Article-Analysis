HYDERABAD: Travel agents in the city have their hands full. Compared to last year, many more citizens are packing their bags and   for vacations to both   destinations. Online travel booking agents and portals such as Yatra.com, Booking.com and Expedia all reported a significant surge in the number of domestic and   from and to Hyderabad this summer, as compared to the same period last year. 

 Between January and April this year, domestic flight bookings from Hyderabad have almost tripled compared to the same period in 2016, while international flight bookings have seen a 33% surge. Travel agents predict this growth will continue well into the end of 2017.
 
 "We have seen around a 25% increase in air traffic to and from Hyderabad over the last one year. According to Airport Service Quality scorecard, Hyderabad airport consistently grew from 4.4 in 2009 to 4.9 in 2016. The trend for 2017 follows the growth pattern of the last few years," said Manmeet Ahluwalia, marketing head, Expedia, India.
 
 While domestic destinations such as Bangalore, Vishakhapatnam and Mumbai were preferred by travellers in the city in 2016 and 2017, international destinations such as Amsterdam have now gained popularity.
 
 "While the top domestic destinations for travellers from Hyderabad have remained consistent through 2016 and 2017, number of international bookings and travellers is on the rise. The highest number of inbound international travellers to Hyderabad came from UAE and USA, both in 2016 and 2017," said  , head of Indian subcontinent, Booking.com.
