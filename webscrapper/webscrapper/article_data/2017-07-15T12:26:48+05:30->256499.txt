KOCHI: The Greenstorm Ecostar awards, 2017, were presented to professor  , actor   and architect G Shankar. Speaking at the occasion, speaker   said that the development of democracy increases by developing commitment towards the environment.

 Film director Sathyan Anthikkad gave away the awards to the winners of Greenstorm photography contest held in connection with World Environment Day. The winners were given trophies, citations, and cash prizes worth Rs 50,000.
 
 Anthikkad said that everything was getting polluted these days, including our eyes and mind. Sreeramakrishnan, Anthikkad, Srinivasan, Shankar, Prasad, B P S managing director   spoke at the event.
