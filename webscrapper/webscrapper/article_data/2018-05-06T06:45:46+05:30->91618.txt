NEW DELHI: The official notification for the Combined Graduate Level (CGL) 2018 examination has been released by the   (SSC) on the official website. 

 
 Interested and eligible candidates can now apply for SSC CGL 2018 examination through the official website: ssc.nic.in 
 
 
 
  
  
 Every year, the commission conducts the CGL examination to fill various posts across the country. The SSC is mandated to make recruitment to Group 'B' (Non-Gazetted) posts and Group 'C' of (non-technical) posts under Government of India and their attached and subordinate offices.
  
  
 
 
 : 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The students have to appear in all the four phases of the examination (Tier- 1 to 4) to get a job in the commission.
 
 The SSC CGL online applications will be accepted at www.ssconline.nic.in. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Note: The application fees can be paid online through debit/credit card or netbanking.
