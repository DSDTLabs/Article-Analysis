LUCKNOW: A traffic constable slapped a motorcyclist allegedly for violating a traffic rule on Tuesday morning. Taking suo motu cognizance of the incident, which went viral on social media, SP (traffic)   sent the constable to traffic police lines. 

 Police said a man was going the wrong way on the one-way route between   and   crossing. At Hazratganj crossing, constable Prem Shanker Mishra rushed to him and stopped his vehicle. 
 
 Then, he started slapping the youth for violating the rule. SP   said, "The man did not lodge any complaint. We are investigating the matter."
