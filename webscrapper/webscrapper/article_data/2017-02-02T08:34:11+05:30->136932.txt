MALAPPURAM: Kottassery Jayakumar, 48, the eighth accused in the murder of neo Islamic convert Faisal in Kodinhi in Malappuram, was arrested by the special investigation team led by   M P Mohanachandran on Tuesday evening. 

 Jayakumar, a native of Athanikkal near Vallikkunnu, is the Tirurangadi taluk secretary of the Vishva Hindu Parishad.
 
 The Thrissur range IG had reshuffled the investigation team in the murder case after a mass protest by thousands of people under the banner of an all-party committee two weeks ago. The protesters had demanded the immediate arrest of three remaining accused in the case, including Jayakumar.
