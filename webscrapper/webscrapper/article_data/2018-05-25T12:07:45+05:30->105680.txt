BHAGALPUR: At least 10   and a villager were injured in a violent clash near the NTPC’s   Super Thermal Power Station (KhSTPS) on Thursday. 

 The clash broke out when the locals started pelting stones at the   personnel while protesting the death of a man from Jamunia village. The villager had died due to snakebite on Wednesday. The mob blocked the gate number one of the KhSTPS, demanding compensation to the next of the kin of the deceased, who worked for the thermal power station.
 
 Bhagalpur DM   and SSP Ashish Bharti rushed to the spot soon after receiving information. “We have ordered a probe into the incident,” the   said.
 
 Meanwhile, additional police forces have been deployed in the area to avoid any untoward incident.
