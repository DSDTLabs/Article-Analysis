LUCKNOW: A day after hoardings carrying photos of Mayawati, Akhilesh, Kanshi Ram and Mulayam together came up outside   headquarters, SP national president   Yadav was presented portraits of Kanshi Ram and Lohia at a party event on Saturday. 

 Akhilesh said that the two developments (hoardings and portraits) carried the message that party workers want the two parties to come together. While former BSP leader   presented a portrait of Kanshi Ram, Nawal Kishore, son-in-law of cabinet minister Swami Prasad Maurya, presented a statue of Buddha. Taking a dig at CM  , he said, , "It is good that he is talking of development of state, that's what I have been reminding him till now."
