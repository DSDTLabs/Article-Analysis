KANPUR: Close on the heels of the ragging incident which shook the IIT-Kanpur campus last week, now an incident of alleged harassment of two of its girl students has surfaced. An FIR was registered against the accused by the IIT administration. 

 The incident happened on September 17 evening, but it came to light after it went viral on social media on Monday. The institute administration, in a clarification issued on Tuesday, said that as per reports of the institute's security office, a youth, posing as an IIT-K student, had passed lewd comments on two girl students who strongly objected and reported the incident to the institute authorities.
