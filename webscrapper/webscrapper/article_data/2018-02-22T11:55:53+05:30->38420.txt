MARGAO: The mortal remains of Bhiku Pai Angle, the noted theatre personality, educationist and writer, were consigned to flames at the   crematorium on Wednesday. Angle passed away in the wee hours of Tuesday. He was 93. 

 The funeral pyre was lit by his son Hemant. 
 
 People from all walks of life attended the funeral.   minister Vijai Sardesai, MP Narendra Sawaikar, former   MP  , former MLA Damodar Naik, were among those who attended the last rites.
 
 Born in November 12, 1924 in Goa, Angle began his career in education in 1946 in Maharashtra. In 1973, he was awarded the ‘Ideal teacher award’ by the Maharashtra government.
