PIMPRI CHINCHWAD: The Bharatiya Janata Party will conduct an inquiry into allegations of corruption and irregularities while the NCP was at the helm of affairs in the Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation. 

 “NCP implemented several development works but there was massive corruption,” said Pune district guardian minister Girish Bapat in Pimpri on Friday. 
 
 “The people rejected NCP rejected because of these allegations. We will not target any individual, but take action against those who misused tax payers’ money,” Bapat added.

 The party, Bapat said, will implement a time-bound programme to implement their manifesto. Meetings with experts and party leaders will be held every three months and the details will be made public. 
 
 “In both Pune and Pimpri Chinchwad, there are several issues concerning citizens, like health, water supply and public transport. The metro rail project will be completed in five years as Rs950 crore has been sanctioned in this year’s Union budget. Also, the metro line from Pimpri to Swargate will be extended to Nigdi,” he said.
 
 In the pipeline are the procurement of 1,500 PMPML buses, implementation of the development plan, Red Zone, Lohegaon airport expansion, the new airport, an international exhibition centre in Moshi in the Bhosari assembly constituency, Pimpri Chinchwad New Township Development Authority development, road widening, and construction of the third line between Pune and Lonavla, he said.
