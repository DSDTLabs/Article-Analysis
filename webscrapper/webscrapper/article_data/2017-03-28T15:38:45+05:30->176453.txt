NEW DELHI: Two people were killed and 17 others were wounded after an encounter broke out between terrorists and security forces in Chadoora area in Jammu & Kashmir's   district early on Tuesday morning.

 Security forces launched a cordon and search operation in Durbagh locality in Chadoora area following information about the presence of terrorists, a police official said.
 
 "The search operation turned into a gunbattle after militants opened fire on security forces," the official said, adding that the exchange of firing was going on when last reports came in.
 
 The fatality occurred when stone-pelting protesters gathered near the site of the encounter and tried to disrupt the operation, which led to the security forces taking action against them. A protestor was hit by a bullet in the neck and succumbed to his injuries on the way to a hospital, the police official said.
 
 17 people suffered injuries as security forces fired pellet guns and tear gas shells in a bid to disperse the protesters, the official said.
