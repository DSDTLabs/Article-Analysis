VADODARA: Investigations in the scandal of removing machinery from the now-sealed Apex Electricals Ltd, which has investments from  , have revealed that the machines were being transported on the instructions of two persons.

 On Saturday, Manjalpur police recorded statements of security guards of the company. “The security guards told us that the machines were being moved on the instructions of one Deepak   and a lady named Nivedita. However, we are not able to contact them on their numbers,” said N N Parmar, Manjalpur police inspector.
 
 It was on Thursday that cops had seized machinery at Makarpura GIDC belonging to Apex Electricals Ltd, which was closed and sealed long time ago. TNN
