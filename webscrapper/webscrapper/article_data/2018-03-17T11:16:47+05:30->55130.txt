  The 8th Theatre Olympics, established in 1993 in Delphi, Greece, is being held for the first time in India, with participation from 30 countries in a series of performances.
 
  
  
 
  The 51-day-long event travels to 17 Indian cities with 450 shows, 600 ambience performances and 250 powerpacked youth forum shows with participation of 25,000 artists from the globe. The festival will conclude on April 8.
 
 
  
  
 
  The festival is being hosted in India by the National School of Drama under the aegis of Ministry of Culture, Government of India. The theme is ‘Flag of Friendship’ and aims to bring people of different cultures, beliefs and ideologies together, through the medium of theatrical art.
