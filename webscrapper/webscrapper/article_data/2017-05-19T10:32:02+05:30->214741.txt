SRINAGAR: The police on Friday found a bullet riddled body of a 45-year-old man who was reportedly working for both militants and security forces in south Kashmir   district.

 The body of   Lone was found in   village of Pulwama this morning, a police official said.
 
 The body had multiple bullet injuries, he said, adding that the police was investigating the circumstances in which he was killed.
 
 Preliminary investigation has revealed that Lone was working as an overground worker for militants as well as a source for security forces, the official said.
 
 Further details are awaited, he added.
