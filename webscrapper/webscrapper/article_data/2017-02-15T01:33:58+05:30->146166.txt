NAVI MUMBAI: A mobile service provider distributor managed to catch hold of one of the four men who attempted to hijack his car and snatch his cash bag in sector 8,   on Monday. However, three other robbers fled with Rs 41,000.

 Senior inspector Ashok Rajput said, one robber was caught on the spot, as the victim managed to raise an alarm and several nearby shopkeepers rushed to his help. “The accused   on the spot, Preshit Narvekar, is a Mumbai resident. The other accused was arrested on Tuesday morning,” he added.
 
 The cops also recovered one country-made revolver and four live cartridges. The accused robbers have been booked under sections of the IPC along with the Indian Arms Act.”
