NEW DELHI: A coach attendant of   express was arrested on Friday by the Railway Protection Force and Rs 11.91 lakh in new currency was recovered from him.

 RPF men deployed at the exit gates of the New Delhi Railway Station noticed the 32-year-old man moving out with a suspicious-looking bag and intercepted him.
 
 “As we approached him, he started walking fast to exit the station area. An RPF team, led by Inspector RS Singh, chased and caught him,” said a senior RPF officer.
