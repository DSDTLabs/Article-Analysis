 Madurai: The essential track   work in the Mandapam – Rameswaram section has been completed and passenger trains cancelled for the past few days will be resumed as per their actual schedule from September 25 onwards. Madurai railway division had earlier announced that train no. 56723 Madurai – Rameswaram and train no. 56722 Rameswaram – Madurai passengers were partially cancelled between Mandapam and Rameswaram in view of track maintenance work from September 18 to 30.
 
 On Saturday, the division announced that the work has been completed. So, train numbers 56723 and 56722 will leave from Rameswaram itself as per scheduled timings from September 25 onwards. Further, train no. 56829 Trichy – Rameswaram passenger will run as per scheduled timings from that same date, a statement said.
