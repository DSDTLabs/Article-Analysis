Vadodara: The Western Railway Mazdoor Sangh (WRMS) collected nearly 2,100 units of blood during a blood donation camps that were organized on Tuesday to celebrate birthday of the union’s general secretary J G Mahurkar, who recently turned 83. 

 Like in the past, the railway union, organized blood donation camps across all the six railway divisions of Western Railways to celebrate birthday of Mahurkar, who is fondly referred as ‘Dada’ by railwaymen. The camps were organized at Vadodara, Rajkot, Surat, Bhavnagar and Church gate, Dadar, Bandra, Andheri, Nandurbar, Billimora, Valsad, Surat, Bardoli railway stations that fall under Mumbai railway division. 
 
 In Vadodara, 136 units of blood were collected on Tuesday. Apart from Mahurkar, DRM Amit Kumar Singh, WRMS president, Sharifkhan Pathan and other officials were present at the occasion.
