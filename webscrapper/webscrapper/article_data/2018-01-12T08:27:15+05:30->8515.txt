BHOPAL: Madhya   (MPHRC) on Thursday took cognisance of the incident wherein girls in a school at   district were stripped by their teachers on suspicion of stealing money.

 Few students in the school had complained that their money was stolen, after which the students were searched by the teachers in a room inside the school.
 
 The Class XI students lodged a complaint with the sub-divisional magistrate in the district on Wednesday, naming two women teachers. Accompanied by their parents, they filed a complaint at Jobat police station as well.
 
 Police officials said they have received the complaint. Alirajpur district collector has also taken cognisance of the matter and asked the sub-divisional magistrate to conduct a thorough investigation.
 
 Officials who recorded the statements of the girls said a student complained to the school principal that someone had stolen her money.
 
 Two girls who were suspected of the theft were taken to a room and asked to remove their clothes by two female teachers on January 8, the complaint stated.
 
 “The girls feel very hurt and humiliated. They said that other students were not subjected to a strip search,” said the officials.
