AGARTALA: The Election Commission has appointed  's Principal Secretary   as the   Electoral Officer (CEO) of the state, an official said.

 Taranikanti replaces S.K. Rakesh, who has moved to the central government on deputation.
 
 Taranikanti is an IAS officer of 1992 batch Tripura cadre and was looking after Transport, Food,   and Consumer Affairs departments.
 
 Under his supervision the next assembly elections would be held in the Left-ruled state, an official said.
 Elections to the 60-seat   are likely to be held in February next year along with Nagaland and Meghalaya.
