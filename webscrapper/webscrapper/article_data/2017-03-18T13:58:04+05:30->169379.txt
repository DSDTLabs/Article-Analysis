Actress   says she involves her two children in meal preparation because the kitchen tasks double as bonding time for the family. 

 The 'Buffy the Vampire Slayer' star and her husband Freddie Prinze, Jr. are both culinary enthusiasts, and they have made sure to pass on their love of food and cooking to their daughter  , seven, and four-year-old Rocky. 
 
  
  
 "It's quality time we wouldn't get if I prepared meals while they watched TV in another room. I love every minute in the kitchen with my kids, even the mess; I've learned to embrace it. 
 
 "I find that in the car on the way home from school, I often get one-word answers when I ask about their day. But when we're cooking, I get in-depth stories,"   says.
