Vadodara: The Panchmahal police seized Indian Made Foreign Liquor (IMFL) worth Rs 1.04lakh from the Jhojh village in the precincts of the Rajgadh police station early on Monday morning. 

 The accused had brought the stash on three different motorcycles and escaped after seeing the police.
 
 The police had kept a watch on a road near the village after receiving a tip off regarding bootlegging activities there. Six persons came to the spot on three motorcycles. They were carrying liquor kept in cartons inside gunnysacks.
 
 Seeing the police, the accused on two motorcycles threw their motorcycles and liquor on the spot and ran away into the fields nearby. Two others threw the gunnysacks on ground and sped away on the motorcycle that they were using.
