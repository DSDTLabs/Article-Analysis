Supermodel Naomi Campbell says she feels King of Pop  's daughter Paris will be an incredible actress.
 
  
 
  
  Paris, 18, recently scored her first television role in the Lee Daniels drama series "Star," and Naomi, who has also filmed for a guest appearance in the show, sees great potential in her, reported People magazine.
  
  "I just feel in my gut that she's going to be an incredible actress," Campbell says.
  
  And while the fashionista never got the chance to meet the budding actress during the shooting of the show, she says she did once "have tea" with a young Paris with her late father.
  
  "I met Paris with her father, and I was very lucky enough before Michael passed away to have tea with Michael and the kids (Paris, Prince Michael and Blanket).
