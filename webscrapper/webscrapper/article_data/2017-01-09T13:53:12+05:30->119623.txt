INDORE: Like every year the traffic police would organize traffic awareness week from Monday. Preparations are in full swing for road safety week that would continue till January 16. 

  Pankaj Srivastav said that ministry of road transport and highways will organize all-India road safety week in January to raise traffic awareness among commuters.
 
 Officials said that many awareness programmes would be organized during the course the week. Emphasis would be laid on creating awareness amongst small children. Events like painting competition, debates, poster designing and helmet rally would be conducted. "Our focus would be to teach them about safe driving and not penalizing. Our previous initiatives have helped us improve the condition of traffic in the city," said  , ASP traffic.
