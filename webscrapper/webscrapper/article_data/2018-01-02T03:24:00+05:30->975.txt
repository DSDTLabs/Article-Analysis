Panaji: Since late last week, the mercury has been hovering around 18C at night.  While a minimum of 18.5C was recorded in Panaji on Friday, the India Meteorological Department (IMD) centre at Altinho, Panaji, recorded 18C on Saturday morning. On Sunday, the mercury rose by 0.2 degrees to 18.2C, only to drop to the lowest of the winter season so far— 18C— for the second time in three days. The Met department has forecast that the minimum temperature is likely to be in the range of 19C and maximum in the range of 32C. tnn
