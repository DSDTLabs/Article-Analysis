IMPHAL: For the second time in less than 10 days, suspected rebels exploded an IED at   in Imphal West early on Monday. The spot is close to a nationalised bank.

 Though no one was injured in the blast, the compound wall of a house belonging to a non-Manipuri was damaged. Immediately after the explosion, police rushed to the spot and carried out a search operation in and around the area, However, no one has been arrested yet.
 
 Police believe that the explosion might be linked to a monetary demand slapped by rebels. Following the blast, security has been tightened at Mantripukhri.
 On September 16, there was another bomb explosion near a rented house at Mantripukhri, injuring at least four non locals.
