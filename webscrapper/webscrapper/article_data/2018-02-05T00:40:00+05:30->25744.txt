Tirupur: As many as 11   were   in a fire that broke out at Cheran Kandasamy Nagar in Vaikkalmedu in the city on Saturday. No causalities were reported in the accident, which was caused by a short-circuit.

 “Three water tenders were pressed into service and the fire was extinguished within half-an-hour. We suspect that a short-circuit could have caused the fire,” said a senior fire and rescue official.
 
 Several household articles and a few motorcycles were destroyed in the accident. 
 
 The residents of the huts, which were on a poramboke land, were told several times to vacate the place, police sources said, adding that they have launched a detailed investigation into the incident.
 
  north police have registered a case.
