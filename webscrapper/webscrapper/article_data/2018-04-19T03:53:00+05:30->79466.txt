Margao: The Margao Municipal Council (MMC) has embarked on a public participative process with regards to the long-pending proposal of shifting the   at Borda to eliminate traffic bottlenecks. 

 Acting MMC chairperson Tito Cardozo on Wednesday convened a meeting of all stakeholders, including the chapel committee, owner of the land where the chapel would be relocated, officials of the PWD, GSIDC, police, traffic police, etc.
 
  “The owner of the land where the chapel is proposed to be relocated has agreed to the proposal. The chapel committee is also in favour of the proposal. The meeting was aimed at arriving at a consensus, and in-principle all parties have given their go-ahead at the meeting,” Cardozo told reporters after the meeting,
