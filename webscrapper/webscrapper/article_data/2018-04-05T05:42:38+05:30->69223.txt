GURUGRAM: Three unidentified men were booked for snatching a   motorcycle from a salesman who works for a cellular service provider on Wednesday in Sector 39, near  . 

 The men also robbed him of Rs 4 lakh which he had collected on duty from mobile recharge shops. The bike was later recovered by cops from a vacant plot in Sector 38.
 
 The complainant,  , resident of Jacobpura colony, said, “Around 2.30pm, I was on duty near Jharsa village when three men stopped me, snatched the bike keys and a bag containing Rs 4,35,000, which I had picked up from recharge shops. They threatened to kill me when I resisted, and fled on my bike.”
 
 Based on Khurana’s complaint, an FIR was registered against the accused under Section 379 A (snatching) of the IPC at Sadar police station. “The motorcycle has been recovered. The accused have not been identified yet. We scanning the footage of CCTV cameras installed in nearby areas to trace them. They will be arrested soon,” said inspector  , additional SHO of Sadar police station.
