 The proposal for formulation of a scheme for providing physical aids and assisted-living devices for senior citizens belonging to BPL category was announced in the 2015-16 budget. Pursuant to this, according to an official statement, the Rashtriya Vayoshri Yojana has been formulated. The scheme aims at providing assistive devices to senior citizens belonging to the BPL category and suffering from any age related disability such as low vision, hearing impairment, loss of teeth and locomotor disability.
  
 Noting that it is a central sector scheme fully funded by the union government, the statement said that the expenditure for implementation of the scheme will be met from the "Senior Citizens' Welfare Fund". Under the Scheme, aids and assisted-living devices such as walking sticks, elbow crutches, walkers/crutches, tripods/quadpods, hearing aids, wheelchair, artificial dentures and spectacles will be provided to eligible elderly beneficiary senior citizens.
