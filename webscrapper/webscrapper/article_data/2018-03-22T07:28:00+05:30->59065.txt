NOIDA: A 40-year-old man died after a   in   on Wednesday afternoon. The deceased has been identified as Laxman, who worked in an export company’s office. 

 According to police, Laxman was going to work in Sector 62 from his home when the accident took place. “A private   threw him off the bicycle while trying to overtake him. The victim received critical injuries,” said a police officer.
 
 A police team reached the spot after receiving information about the accident. The cops rushed Laxman to the district hospital where he was declared brought dead. The police have seized the bus and arrested the driver. The bus was empty and was returning after dropping students home when the accident took place. 
 
 “We have registered a case of death due to negligence against the driver. He was produced in court later in the day which sent him to judicial custody,” said a police officer.
