NAVI MUMBAI: The traffic department’s   unit is cracking the whip on   plying on  , despite a ban being imposed on heavy vehicles. Three dumpers were caught in a drive on Friday and penalized Rs 1,200 each. The traffic wing will continue to monitor such heavy vehicles plying illegally, as entry is restricted on this 9-km arterial road.

 Traffic cops were deployed near NMMC headquarters from where the nodal road opens out to sector 50, Nerul. The dumpers were let off with a warning not to use Palm Beach Road.
 
 Speaking to TOI, Anil Pawar, traffic inspector of Seawoods unit said, “We have received complaints of heavy vehicles plying on this road and have stationed personnel. All three dumpers were penalized and released later.”
