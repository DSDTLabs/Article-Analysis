 The elections to the municipalities spread across five districts in the northern, central and southern areas of the state will provide a peek into the mind of voters in small towns.
 
 
 
  
 The polls are a test for the Trinamool to continue its electoral successes and make fresh inroads in the north Bengal hills by breaking the stranglehold of the Gorkha Janmukti Morcha.
  
 The BJP is striving to carry forward its bid to become the main opposition force in the state. For the Congress and the Left, the challenge is to revive their political fortunes against the backdrop of electoral battles wherein both lost ground to the BJP.
