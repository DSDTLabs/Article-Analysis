NEW DELHI: The Maharashtra Criminal Investigation (CID) Department has released the MAHACID Hall Ticket 2018 on its official website - maharecruitment.mahaonline.gov.in.

 Candidates who have applied for the examination can check and download their admit card from the MAHACID Examination website - maharecruitment.mahaonline.gov.in. 
 
 
 
 
 Candidates are advised to download their MAHACID Examination 2018 Admit Card from the official website - maharecruitment.mahaonline.gov.in - only.
 
 In case you have also applied for the MAHACID Examination 2018 examination, you can follow the steps given below to check and download your admit card. 
 
 
 
 
 1) Open the official website of MAHACID Examination - maharecruitment.mahaonline.gov.in
 
 2) On the homepage of the website, there will be a link "MAHACID Examination 2018 Admit Card/Hall Ticket". Click on the link
 
 3) A pop-up page will appear, enter your MAHACID Examination 2018 application number, date of birth, security pin and click on login. 
 
 4) Your MAHACID Examination 2018 admit card will be displayed on the screen. Carefully check all your details such as name, paper, date of birth, gender, test centre name, city, state, code of eligibility and category on your MAHACID Examination 2018 admit card. 
 
 5) You can download it and take a printout for future reference.
