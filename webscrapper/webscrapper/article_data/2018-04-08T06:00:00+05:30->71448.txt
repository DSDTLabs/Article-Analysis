Bhargavi Chirmule's comeback play ' ' is hilarious

 Well-known actress   has come back to theatre after a long while, with her latest offering 'Amchya Hicha Prakaran.'
 
 Doubts, misunderstanding, confusion lead to a comedy which is quite entertaining for the audience. The mature relationship's base slides down and it is a kind of a test for the characters which make the audience burst into laughter and yet bring the reality to the fore.
 
 Nikhil Ratnaparkhi,  ,  , Nandita Patkar, Prasiddhi Kishore are also an integral part of this play that is giving the audience a big reason to laugh out loud.
