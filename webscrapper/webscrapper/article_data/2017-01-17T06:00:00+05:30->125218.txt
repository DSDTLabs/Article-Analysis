Visakhapatnam: Two persons had a miraculous escape on Monday when their car hit a divider on the road connecting Beach Road with MVP Double Road and caught fire. 

 According to eyewitnesses, both the persons managed to get out of the car before it went up in flames. 
 
 Sources said the car hit the median while trying to avoid collusion with a two-wheeler coming from the other side. The petrol tank burst and caught fire. The car was gutted by the time fire tenders arrived. 
 
 The passengers were admitted to a nearby hospital by passers-by.
