RAJKOT:   workers on Thursday protested in Rajkot and Jamnagar against rise in petrol and diesel price. Congress workers held a rally in Rajkot from Trikon Baug to Hospital Chowk chanting slogans and blocked the roads. In Jamnagar, Congress workers held unique protest against petrol and diesel price rise. The took out a bike rally from near Lal Bangla circle chanting slogans against the union government.
