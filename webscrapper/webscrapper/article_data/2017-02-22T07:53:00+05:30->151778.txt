 Now, only oustees with general power of attorney (GPA) and   (SPA) need to be rehabilitated. A case related to rehabilitation of GPA and SPA oustees is pending before the Punjab and Haryana high court and hearing in the case is scheduled for March 16.

Remaining 64 oustees with GPA and SPA will be rehabilitated after the conclusion of the case pending before HC, officials said.
