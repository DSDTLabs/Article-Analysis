GURUGRAM: A building contractor’s one-and-a-half-year-old son, who was abducted on September 10, was rescued and brought to Gurgaon on Thursday night following the arrest of his kidnappers — a man and his wife — from Gadhwa railway station in Jharkhand on Tuesday. 

 The accused, Om Prakash and Sarita, were produced in court on Friday and sent to jail. As per the couple, they kidnapped Santosh Kumar’s son to take revenge after Kumar reportedly refused to pay them Rs 5,000.
