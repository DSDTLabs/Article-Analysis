Ghaziabad: There was a lukewarm beginning on Tuesday to the nomination filing process for the assembly election in   and   districts with not even a single candidate coming forward to submit papers.

 The eight assembly seats of GB Nagar and Ghaziabad go to the polls on February 11 along with 65 other constituencies of UP and the nomination filing process for contesting in these seats will close on January 24.
 
 District administration officials in Ghaziabad said about two dozen nomination forms in all were procured from the election office on Tuesday. These forms were procured from across the five assembly constituencies.
 
 According to election officials, the pace of filing of nomination papers might gather pace in the next few days.
