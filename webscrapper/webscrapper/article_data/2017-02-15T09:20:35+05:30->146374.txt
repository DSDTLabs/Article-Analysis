AHMEDABAD:   (GERMI) and the ONGC on Tuesday launched  , a first-of-its-kind indigenous software platform for pore pressure prediction, using seismic velocities. The launch function was held at PDPU.

 Anil Sood, chief guest of the event and executive director of GEOPIC, ONGC, said that the software will help the ONGC save foreign exchange on purchase and renewal of sofware license providing similar technology. ONGC will extend more software development projects to   (DIC) of GERMI in future, Sood said. 
 
 Dr Harinarayana, director-general, GERMI, said that India had remained an importer of software in oil and gas sector.
