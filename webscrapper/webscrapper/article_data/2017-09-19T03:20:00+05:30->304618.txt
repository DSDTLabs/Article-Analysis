Ponda: Ponda police arrested two migrant workers in two separate cases in Ponda town with 1kg ganja worth Rs 40,000.  Two police teams raided two separate locations—new KTC bus stand and old bus stand—simultaneously on Monday afternoon and arrested two persons with ganja. Mutappa Talwar, 27, native of Gadag, Karnataka, was nabbed with 490g of ganja and Ishappa Narsapur, 26, native of Bagalkot, Karnataka, with 552g ganja. Both the accused have been booked in separate cases.
