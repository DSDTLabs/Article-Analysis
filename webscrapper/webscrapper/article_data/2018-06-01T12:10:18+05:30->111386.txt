The state   party on Thursday questioned the motive behind the tourism department’s participation in a road show in the US and asked tourism minister Manohar Azgaonkar to openly state how the Goa government delegation, which went to promote Goa as a tourism destination, would benefit the state. Congress spokesperson   and   Dayanand Sopte also told reporters the tourism department must explain why four of the minister’s relatives travelled as part of the delegation.
