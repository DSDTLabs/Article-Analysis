MADURAI: Karaikudi police in   district booked Naser Ali, son of  , AIADMK MP from   district, for allegedly cheating a Chennai-based woman of Rs 50 lakh on Sunday. The accused, who was allegedly in relationship with the woman, had taken the money on the promise of marrying her.

 A police officer said that Ali was booked under IPC Sections 417 - punishment for cheating, 420 - cheating and 506 (i) – criminal intimidation and section four of Tamil Nadu Prohibition of Woman Harassment Act. The case was file based on a complaint from one Robina, 35, from   in Chennai. She is working as a radio jockey. 
 
 Robina approached the Chennai commissioner’s office to lodge a complaint against the MP’s son a couple of days ago, after she came to know about his marriage that was scheduled to take place on Sunday. She was directed to approach the Ramanathapuram district police. When she approached the district superintendent of police, he assured of action. 
 
 Meanwhile, she went to Karaikudi where Ali’s marriage was scheduled to take place place in a hotel. She tried to barge into the hotel. As she was not allowed, Robina left the place and lodged a complaint with the Karaikudi police. She has stated that Ali took the money to start a business, but did not return it. While he had promised to marry her, he went on to marry another woman. She had raised the money by pledging her jewellery and money borrowed from her relatives, she claimed.
