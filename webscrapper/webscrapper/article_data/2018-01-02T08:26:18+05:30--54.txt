HYDERABAD: About 6,000 party goers took late night   back home from New Year parties held around the city. Authorities had extended   services till 2:30am to provide ‘safe travel’ to people. A total of 1.07 lakh commuters travelled in Metro rail on the New Year’s eve on Sunday.

 Though the services were extended till 2:30am, but people kept turning up at the stations beyond the said time, which prompted authorities to continue services till 2:45am on Monday. “About 6,000 people travelled in late night/early morning Metro trains for reaching home safely. No untoward incident happened and the passengers behaved well,” said NVS Reddy, managing director,  .
