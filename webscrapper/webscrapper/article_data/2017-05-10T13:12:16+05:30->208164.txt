PANAJI: Ending all speculation about Goa chief minister  's choice of constituency,   MLA Sidharth Kuncalienker on Wednesday resigned from the state legislature in Goa.

 With Kuncalienker offering his resignation, the way is clear for Parrikar to contest the bypolls for the state assembly elections.
 
 The former defence minister is yet to resign from the Rajya Sabha. The BJP central leadership has asked Parrikar to wait till the elections of President in the July before resigning.
 
 According to BJP sources, Kuncalienker stepped down after being told by BJP state president   to do so.
 
 Parrikar had shortlisted Panaji and Curchorem as likely constituencies to contest from.
