DEHRADUN: Two supporters of the Bharatiya Janata Party ( ) were killed and 21 others injured after a bus carrying party workers fell into a ditch near Dandachali in Tehri district.
 
  The BJP supporters were on their way back from New Tehri after attending the nomination of BJP leader and party candidate from Narendra Nagar seat, Subodh Uniyal, on Wednesday. The mini bus carrying 23 people skidded off the road at Dandachali on Ranichori-Gaja road, some 24km from district headquarters.
  
  “One person identified as Jaychand, a resident of Odada in Tehri Garhwal, died on the spot while another person Ravi Rana, 30, died at the hospital during treatment,” said Brijesh Bhatt, district disaster management officer.
  
  The 21 injured people were admitted to the district hospital at Baurari in Tehri. One person, who was in a critical condition, was later referred to a higher centre in Dehradun for treatment.
  
  Chief minister   also expressed grief at the deaths in the road accident.
