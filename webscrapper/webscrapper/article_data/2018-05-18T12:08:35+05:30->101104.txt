SRINAGAR: Three rifles were reported missing on Thursday from a Jammu and Kahsmir   post in Srinagar.

 The police said three rifles were missing from a post in   area of the city, adding that the policemen posted at the guard post were being questioned to ascertain the facts.
 
 The incident came a day after unknown assailants snatched the service rifle of a policeman at the main gate of Kashmir University.
