PANAJI: Goa police recently informed the Bombay high court that its investigation into the murder of Swedish national Felix Dahl has been completed, and that a report would be submitted to the magistrate in four weeks, reports Murari Shetye. 

 Dahl's family had moved the high court in 2017, seeking a direction to transfer the case to the   ( ) and to establish a commitee to probe the deaths of 'a large number of foreign nationals' in Goa. Dahl, 22, was found dead on a gravel road under mysterious circumstances at Patnem, Canacona on January 28, 2015.
