LUDHIANA : A day after police booked a   resident for impersonating an IPS officer, cops found the cheat was a   passout who worked as a driver at a hosiery factory, here.

 It was   Singh Mann’s dream to become an influential man that made him pose as a police officer. The 35-year-old had told his acquaintances and family that he had cracked UPSC examination and was posted at Chandigarh.
 
 
 “He would leave the house every morning and return in the evening.He has links with some officials and politicians, too,” said his younger brother  , a tailor. Salem Tabri police, on Sunday, had booked Rupinder under sections 170 (personating a public servant), 171 (wearing grab or carrying token used by public servant with fraudulent intent), 420 (cheating) of the IPC.
