Bengaluru: City traffic police booked as many as 1,187 motorists for alleged   on Saturday, a record number for a single day, police say. The earlier record stood at 1,090 which was achieved on the night of December 30, 2016.

 Of the total motorists booked on Saturday, 667 were caught in east division, while 419 in west division and 101 were caught in north division, traffic police said.
 
 Police commissioner T Suneel Kumar had announced earlier that traffic police would scale up their drive to check for drunk driving on December 30 and 31 as part of their efforts to ensure peaceful   celebrations in the city.
