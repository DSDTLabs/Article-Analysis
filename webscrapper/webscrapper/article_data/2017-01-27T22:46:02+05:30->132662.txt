Arvind Kejriwal's Aam Admi Party will be contesting Goa assembly elections in 2017. The party has declared a total of 36 candidates for the Goa Assembly Elections out of 40 Assembly constituencies. The elections in Goa will be held in a single phase on February 4, 2017 and the results will be declared on March 11, 2017.  To attract the attention of voters, the AAP is distributing leaflets and debit card-sized plastic cards in Goa with a list and 'guarantee' of promises to boost to its door-to-door campaign called 'Zhadu Yatra'.
