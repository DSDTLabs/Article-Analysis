COIMBATORE: The Railway protection force ( ) held an awareness programme outside the   on Tuesday. Inspector of the force,   along with railway staff, women police constables and government railway police staged a street play and distributed pamphlets warning the passengers about consuming any food or soft drinks given by strangers.

 “A case was reported recently where a woman lost her consciousness after consuming soft drinks given by some men. They then took her jewellery and luggage and escaped. Also, when women passengers are seated in the window seat, robbers snatch away their chains and bags. Hence, we asked them to be careful,” an official said.
 
 They said if women passengers face any troubles by unidentified men and robbers, they can call 182 and police will reach and assist them in the next station.
