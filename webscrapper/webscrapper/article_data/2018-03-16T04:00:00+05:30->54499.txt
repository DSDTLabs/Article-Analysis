Rajkot: Bhuj-based government pleader, Kalpesh Goswami, was appointed special public prosecutor in the sensational Naliya gang rape case on Thursday. An SIT is investigating the case and some members of BJP have been named as accused. Goswami was also appointed public prosecutor in a graft case against former Kutch collector Pradip Sharma who is being investigated by CID crime, ACB and crime branch. tnn
