NOIDA: Two Nigerian students were beaten up by locals after they allegedly assaulted a woman and her brother in Greater Noida.

 According to police the Nigerian students were returning from   to Sector Alpha I on a bike, when a car sped past them, touching their bike in the process.
 
 “The Nigerian students caught hold of the driver and started beating him,” said police. When the victim’s sister intervened, the Nigerian students beat her and tore her clothes. Locals immediately surrounded the Nigerian students and thrashed them.
 
 A police team reached the spot and controlled the situation. Both the parties have lodged complaints against each other in Kasna police station.
