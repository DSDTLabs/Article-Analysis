Vasco: Vasco police, on Thursday, recorded the statement of the girl and her brother whose video went viral on Wednesday, wherein she was seen behaving abnormally and speaking in a hoarse voice.  The girl said she remembers going to Bogmalo with her brother and boyfriend. When she came to her senses, they were in front of a temple in Baina. Her brother said they were in Bogmalo when she started behaving strangely and he contacted a man who called them to the temple. As they were on a bike and his sister was uncontrollable, they took the help of two men, in a car, who asked them what was happening. tnn
