Ludhiana: An elderly woman allegedly committed suicide by jumping in the Sidhwan canal near Barewal bridge on Friday afternoon. The body was recovered by PAU police by evening. 

 The deceased identified as Sushma Gupta, 85, was a resdient of Sukhmani Enclave in the southern part of the city. She had left from her residence in the morning without informing her husband, who lived with her. According to police, the woman was in depression for the past three years and suffered from diabetes and high blood pressure.
