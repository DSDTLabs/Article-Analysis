On May 12, the   -   movie 'Ramante Edenthottam' will be released and the movie's director has just announced it's rating.

 Director Ranjith Sankar says the movie gets a clean U rating, and he announced it via his page on the social media. The story of Raman who runs a resort in a forest area and Malini who comes to visit the scenic location from Kochi, the film is a romance drama.
 
 'Ramante Edenthottam' has music by Bijibal and cinematography by Pravin Bhatt
