NEW DELHI: The Uttar Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Parishad (UPMSP) will be declared the Class 10 (SSC) and Class 12 (HSSC) exam results on Sunday, April 29, 2018.

 All candidates who have appeared for the Inter and High School examinations can register below to get result directly on their mobile phone or email ID. The candidates need to enter their details like name, roll number, mobile number (for result SMS), email ID and submit it. The result will be sent through SMS/ email ID once it is declared by the UPMSP Board. 
 
 
  
  
 
 The students can also check their results through the official websites of UPMSP Board - upmsp.edu.in, upresults.nic.in, upmspresults.up.nic.in and results.nic.in. 
 
 As per UP Board officials, the results will be announced at 12:30 pm. The UPMSP will publish Inter results on April 29 at 12.30 pm while High School exams 2018 will be out at 1:30 pm. 
 
 This year, very strict norms were introduced by the UP Board exam authorities. 
 
 A total of 29 lakh students registered to appear for the Class 12 high school board examinations, however, around 10 lakh withdrew consequent due to the crackdown on the cheaters. Thus, the number of students appeared was reduced to around 24 lakhs.
 
 For Class 10, around 36 lakh candidates had registered but after the withdrawal process was over, the numbers came down to below 30 lakh. 
 
 The board exams for UP Class 10 (intermediate) and Class 12 (high school) were concluded on February 22 and March 12, respectively.
