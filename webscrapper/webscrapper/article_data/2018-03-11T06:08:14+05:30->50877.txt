JALANDHAR: A total   of Rs 200 crore would be given to around 40,000 farmers by Punjab chief minister Captain   in the mega function being held on March 14 at Nakodar. 

 Revealing the figure of the debt waiver to be distributed among farmers in the function, Jalandhar deputy commissioner   said district-wise seating arrangements for the farmers of Jalandhar, Ludhiana, Kapurthala, Moga, Ferozepur and Fazilka districts would be made.
 
 Likewise, the DC said necessary arrangements for food would also be made for the farmers arriving in this event. He said that a foolproof plan would also be evolved for smooth movement of traffic in Nakodar during the event.
 
 In the first instalment of debt waiver made on January 7, debt relief certificates worth Rs 167.39 crore given to 47,000 marginal and small farmers. However, the state government could allocate only Rs 1,500 crore in the budget for 2017-18.
