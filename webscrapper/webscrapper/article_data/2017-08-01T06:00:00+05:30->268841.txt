Bharatanatyam dancer and painter   showcased her art works at a city art gallery recently. Titled ‘Imprint’, the artist exhibited a series of mix media paintings drenched in a surrealistic approach to communicate with the viewers. 
The artist used a rare method wherein she first made digital prints of images and juxtaposes them with other pictures creating visual collages. Art aficionados of the city poured in to catch a glimpse of the artist’s aesthetic imagery and were in all praise of 
 her work.
