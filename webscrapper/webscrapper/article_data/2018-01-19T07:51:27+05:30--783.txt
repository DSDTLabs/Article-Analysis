GANDHINAGAR: Vishwa Hindu Parishad (VHP) general secretary   who had filed the missing person complaint about VHP international president Dr   on January 18 has again written to   J K Bhatt. Bharwad has requested Bhat to cancel his complaint immediately and stop further action.

 Bharwad also alleged that he had submitted a complaint to take the help of police in finding Pravinbhai. “But common people and Pravinbhai have been harassed by the police as if they were criminals,” he said.
 
 Bharwad has said in his letter that “hereby I request to cancel my application regarding Pravinbhai and, if further harassment of any kind is attempted, then I will have to take legal action."
