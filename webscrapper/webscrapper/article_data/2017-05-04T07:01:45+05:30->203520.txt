MUMBAI: A 28-year-old dental laboratory assistant was arrested on Wednesday for allegedly sexually molesting an   relative in Chimatpada in Andheri. The Sahar Airport police booked him under provisi- ons of the Protection of Childr- en from Sexual Offences Act and for unnatural sex. He has been remanded in custody till May 9. 

 The police claimed the sexual exploitation came to light last week, when the boy complained of stomach pain. "After his mother took him to a doctor, the boy broke down and narrated the ordeal," a police officer said. 
 
 The accused is married and has two children.
