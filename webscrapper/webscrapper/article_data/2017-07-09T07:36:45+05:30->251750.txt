RAJKOT: Three persons were killed and three others got injured when a state transport bus rammed into a parked truck near Vastavdi village on Sayla-Limbdi highway in   early morning on Saturday.

 The deceased were identified as   (50) from Jamnagar, Mehul Purohit (38) of Junagadh and   (44) of Mehsana. The injured have been shifted to the civil hospital in Surendranagar town.
 
 According to police, the accident took place when the Gujarat State Road Transport Corporation (GSRTC) bus of   route rammed into a parked truck on the Sayla-Limbdi highway. Police said that the bus driver overlooked the truck parked on the highway. All three deceased died on the spot, police added.
