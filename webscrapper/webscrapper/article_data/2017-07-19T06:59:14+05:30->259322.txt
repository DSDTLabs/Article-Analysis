NEW DELHI: Ambedkar   released its fourth cutoff list on Tuesday. While most other courses saw a dip of up to 3% in the cutoff, BA (H) in mathematics for students residing in Delhi has been closed for admissions.

 The cutoff for economics for   and non-NCR candidates fell by just 0.5%, but the English cutoff fell by 2% for NCR candidates while there was a 2.25% difference for non-NCR candidates. The maximum drop of 3.25% was seen in social science and humanities courses for non-NCR candidates.
