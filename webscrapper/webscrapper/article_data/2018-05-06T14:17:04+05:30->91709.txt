Anakapalli:   Town police arrested four persons on charges of organising betting on the ongoing   cricket matches. 

 The police, led by circle inspector Murali, seized cell phones, a TV, calculator and cash worth Rs 12,000 from their possession. According to sources, the four accused were from Anakapalli outskirt area. The circle inspector   said that they had raided a house on a tip-off and caught the four organising the betting by downloading an app ‘Cricket Mazza’ in mobile phones. They would resort to all kinds of betting, be it ball to ball, victory or prediction of rain. 
 
 Earlier, the   task force had busted two gangs involved with IPL cricket betting.
