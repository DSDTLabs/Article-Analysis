LUCKNOW: The BJP on Saturday claimed that it would set up three tasks force in the state to weed out corruption and malpractices.

 Speaking to reporters after the release of the (election manifesto), BJP president   said: “An anti-land mafia task force will be established in every district. The task force will free the grabbed land and punish those found guilty."
 
 He further stated that a 'special task force' would be constituted for immediate disposal of and impartial investigation into cases pertaining to corruption.
 
 Apart from this, a strong mining policy would be drafted to put an end to illegal mining. “A special task force would be formed and all those found guilty of indulging in illegal mining would be punished,“ Shah said.
