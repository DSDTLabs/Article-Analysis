Panaji: The third edition of the international disputes resolution competition, ‘Lex Infinitum’, was inaugurated by Cyril Shroff of Cyril Amarchand Mangaldas Advocates and Solicitors, at the Panjim Convention Centre, Mala, on Wednesday. The event is being hosted by V M Salgaocar College of Law in association with World   Organisation. Competition president John James Samuel, said, “Lex Infinitum is the culmination of our mission to bring international standards in mediation and negotiation to the state of Goa.” tnn
