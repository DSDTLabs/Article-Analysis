
 PUDUCHERRY: Joint managing director and chief executive officer of Lebracs Rubber Linings Pvt Ltd M Nandakumar and managing director of Manatec Electronics Pvt. Ltd. M Kalaiichelvan, have been elected as the chairman and vice-chairman respectively of the Confederation of Indian Industries (CII), Puducherry state council, for the year 2018-19.
 
 Nandakumar, who has been closely associated with the CII, was the vice-chairman of the council in 2017-18. He was also the convenor of the membership and the micro small and medium enterprises’ (MSME) panels in 2016-17 and 2014-15 respectively. He was bestowed with the national award of ‘Outstanding entrepreneur 2018’ by the Council Of State Industrial Development and Investment Corporations Of India (Cosidici).
 
 Kalaiichelvan served as the co-convenor of manufacturing and MSME panels in 2017-18 and member of CII southern regional council in 2013-14.
