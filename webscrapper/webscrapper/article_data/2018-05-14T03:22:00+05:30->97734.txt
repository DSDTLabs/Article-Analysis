Margao: The Rachol gram sabha on Sunday discussed the erection of new speed breakers and construction of gutters.  Panchayat secretary Nilesh Shirodkar told TOI that “the administrative report as well as the annual accounts were approved during this meeting that was attended by three of the five-member panchayat.” Sarpanch Eni Oliveira said, “The jobs of construction of the Sulabh toilets and the dry garbage collection drive have already commenced in the village.” Curtorim Congress MLA Alexio Reginaldo Lourenco was absent for the meeting.
