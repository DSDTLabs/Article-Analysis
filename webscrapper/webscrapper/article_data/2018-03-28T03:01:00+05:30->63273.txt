Panaji: An oncology department will be set up under Dr Anupama Borkar at Goa Medical College (GMC) and hospital, Bambolim. “The file has been processed for setting up the department,” said health minister Vishwajit Rane on Tuesday. The state government has signed a memorandum of understanding with the Centre to set up a regional cancer center at GMC. The Rs 100-crore project, though approved over a year ago, wasn’t pursued by the state government until a few months ago. The Centre has sanctioned Rs 45 crore for the project.
