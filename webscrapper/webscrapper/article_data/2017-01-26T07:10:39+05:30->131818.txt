HYDERABAD: The Bhakta Ramadasu Lift  , completed two months ahead of schedule, will be inaugurated by chief minister   on January 31. 

 The project is designed to provide irrigation facilities to Khammam and Palair assembly constituencies. The project will cover an ayacut of 58, 958 acres in  ,  ,  ,   and Khammam rural mandals. Officials said it was completed a head of the deadline as CM, irrigation minister T Harish Rao and minister Tummala Nageshwara Rao monitored the its progress regularly.
