Noida: A 22-year old man was shot at by two   in Sarfabad   on Friday night.

 The incident took place when the victim, Mohit was returning home in Sector 22 from a hotel after buying food for the family.
 
 “After getting the food, he was returning on his bike, but on the way two persons on a   started following him. The criminals asked him to stop the bike, but he refused. They then opened fire and injured his leg,” said Abhinandan, Circle Officer 1, Noida.
 
 Mohit lost balance and fell off the bike. Thereafter, the criminals fled.
 
 Some locals gathered at the spot and rushed him to Kailash Hospital. 
 
 They then informed his family members and police using his phone. The doctor said, Mohit is out of danger and he has undergone surgery for his wounds. 
 
 Police said that prima facie it seems a case of personal enmity. 
 
 “We have registered a case against unknown persons. We are scanning footage of CCTV cameras of nearby areas to identify and arrest the criminals,” said Abhinandan.
