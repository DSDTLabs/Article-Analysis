Former One Direction   Harry Styles shared a 16-second teaser video for his latest song 'Sign of the Times'. 

 The 23-year-old star took to Twitter to post the first teaser for his upcoming video of his latest single. 
  
 
 The clip offers views of waterfalls, forests and mountains, but there are not any shots of Harry. 
  
  
 The music video teaser is shared after the tickets for Style's upcoming solo tour were sold out in just seconds on May 5. 
 
 The singer will begin his 'Harry Styles Live On Tour' in 13 North American cities in September and October before heading to Europe, Australia and Japan through December.
