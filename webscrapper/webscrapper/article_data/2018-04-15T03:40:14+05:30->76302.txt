BHOPAL/JABALPUR: Six people were injured after a passenger train they were traveling in derailed near   district of   on Saturday night. 

 Those injured were in the general coaches of Beena-Katni-Chopan train that went off the track after a jerk at around 10.30 pm, said sources. 
 
 Rescue teams were dispatched from Jabalpur and Itarsi stations. 
 
 “We were sleeping when the train derailed. Train was at a slow speed, not many are injured” said Surendra Sahu, who was in the train. 
 
 Rescue operations are under way.
