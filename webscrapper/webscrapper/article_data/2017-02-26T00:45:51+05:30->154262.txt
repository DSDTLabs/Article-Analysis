Mumbai: After winning 82 seats in the BMC election, CM   visited   on Saturday “to seek Chh-atrapati Shivaji’s blessings”. 

 He tweeted: “Visited Raigad Fort to seek blessings of   with my cabinet colleagues this morning. We reiterate our commitment to follow the path shown by our Great King Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj and assure development of every citizen.  In December, PM Narendra Modi had laid the foundation stone for a Shivaji memorial in Arabian Sea. State finance minister Sudhir Mungtiwar, revenue minister Chandrakant Patil, education minister Vinod Tawde, BJP state president Raosaheb Danve, BJP city president   and Uran BJP leader Mahesh Baldi accompanied the CM to the fort.
