 The deceased had gone to participate in Navratra festivities in a nearby pandal on Wednesday night, but did not return home later, Anantpura police station circle in-charge Anil Joshi said. Her halfnaked body was found on Thursday morning in Udiya Basti by a passerby who informed the police, he said.
 
 The youths were produced before the Juvenile Justice Board late Thursday night, which ordered to send them to a children's home.
