NEW DELHI: Joint Entrance Examination (JEE Main) 2018 provisional answer keys and response sheets have been released on the official website of  : jeemain.nic.in.

 Here is the link of the official website of JEE Main Examination - jeemain.nic.in. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
  
  
 
 
 As per the schedule available on the official website, the answer keys was to be released today. The students who have appeared in the pen and paper, as well as the candidates who have appeared in the online examination conducted by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) can login to check the answer keys and the response sheets online on the official website. 
 
 The students can raise an objection on the answer keys or raise requests for a wrong capture of the responses against the OMR sheets. A non-refundable fee of Rs. 1000 per question is applicable, therefore, the students are advised to go through the answer keys and OMR sheets carefully before raising objections. However, if the challenge is accepted by the CBSE Board, the amount would be refunded.
 
 The 5th Joint Engineering Entrance Examination [JEE (Main) was conducted in pen & paper mode on April 8, 2018. In computer mode, this examination was conducted on April 15-16, 2018. 
 
 The   2018 result is expected to be released on April 30, 2018.
