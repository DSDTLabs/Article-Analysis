The arrested were identified as S Vinod Kumar (25) and V Dass (18) from Bahour on the outskirts of  . Police launched a hunt for Sharma Vanan, another accused believed to be involved in the crime.

 Police said the murdered Suvedhan, a school drop-out, was in contact with many antisocial elements including a known trouble-maker, Ravisankar, who was murdered by a rival gang on April 25. Two of Ravisankar's friends too were arrested recently in connection with a chain snatching case.
 
 On Thursday , Kumar took Suvedhan to have alcohol to a vacant plot near a lake at Bahour. There, along with his accomplices Dass and Vanan, Kumar killed Suvedhan, severed his head, and left the torso behind. Kumar and Dass then carried the head in a motorcycle and threw it onto the Reddichavadi police station premises in Cuddalore.
 
 The chilling murder triggered tension in the area. SSP (law & order) Rajiv Ranjan said police managed to trace and nab the two of the three accused in coordination with the Cuddalore police within 12 hours.
 
 “The two accused threw the severed head of the juvenile inside Reddichavadi police station in Cuddalore district thinking that Tamil Nadu police will register a case against them and they will be lodged in Cuddalore prison where they could avail all facilities. The gang leader (Kumar) believed that tossing the severed head into the police station will earn him notoriety ,“ said Ranjan.
