PUDUCHERRY: The opposition   on Friday urged the Puducherry government to declare January 17 a public holiday, to observe the birth centenary of  , M G  . 

 The AIADMK legislature wing leader A   told reporters here that he had made a representation to Chief Minister   to declare January 17, the birth anniversary of AIADMK founder and former Tamil Nadu Chief Minister MG Ramachandran, a public holiday in the Union Territory.
 
 The party would hold several programmes including distribution of welfare measures to the poor and have-nots on the occasion of the late leader's birth centenary, that falls on January 17, he said.
