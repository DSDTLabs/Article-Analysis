CHENNAI: The   government on Tuesday announced that a special   gift hamper will be given to each of the 1.8 crore family cardholders in the state.

 In a statement, chief minister   said the gift hamper would be extended “in the midst of prevailing drought condition in the state” to all rice cardholders, police cardholders and Sri Lankan refugees staying in state-run camps. “I am happy to announce special Pongal gift hamper of one kg of raw rice, sugar, cashew nuts, dry grapes and two feet sugarcane to each cardholder,” he said. Panneerselvam said this special gift would be distributed through PDS outlets ahead of Pongal (January 14).
