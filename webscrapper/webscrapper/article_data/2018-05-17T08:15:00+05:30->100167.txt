LUCKNOW: A day after the Varanasi flyover collapse, the   ( ) and Varanasi traffic police accused each other of being responsible for the tragedy. 

 While UPSBCL claimed on Wednesday that it had written many letters to traffic police seeking assistance, traffic cops said they provided all necessary help whenever asked and had lodged an FIR against the UPSBCL project manager. 
 
 The traffic police said UPSBCL did not follow safety norms throughout the ongoing construction but the latter accused the police of not supporting them by managing traffic.
