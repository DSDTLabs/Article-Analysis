INDORE: The election petition of   leader   that was scheduled to be heard by high court on Tuesday was adjourned for three days in absence of election judge  . Justice Jarat Kumar Jain, who had been presiding over the election petition till the last hearing retired last week following which the case was proposed to be listed before Justice  . 

 On Friday, the last witness scheduled to appear before the court on behalf of Kailash Vijayvargiya, is expected to be present before the court for his testimony.
