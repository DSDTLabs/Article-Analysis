AHMEDABAD: As the summer has set in, civic authorities have intensified their efforts towards curbing the spread of waterborne infections. According to the testing conducted by the   of   ( ), some 567   collected by officials were found to be unfit this year.

 A common cause identified by civic authorities is the supply of contaminated water in several parts of the city, due to which AMC health department has increased the number of water samples tested. Civic body data suggests that contaminated water was supplied in areas including Dariapur, Khadia, Danilimda, Behrampura, Chandkheda, Gota, Stadium, Navrangpura and Sarkhej, among others.
