Pune: Villagers in Ralegan Siddhi on Wednesday expressed anger over the Union government’s alleged disregard for social crusader Anna Hazare’s indefinite fast in Delhi and vowed to prevent government officials from entering the village from March 29. 

 The villagers also sent back police personnel deputed for Hazare’s security.
 
 A bandh will be observed in Parner Taluka on March 29 and rasta roko will be organised at 9 am at Supa on Pune Ahmednagar Road, Takali Dhokeshwar on Ahmednagar-Kalyan Road and Javale and Devibhoir on Shirur Alkuti Road.
