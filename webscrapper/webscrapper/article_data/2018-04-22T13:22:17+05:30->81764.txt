 Building plans of commercial/industrial structures are submitted on controlled sheets prepared and issued by the department of urban planning. After the original control sheets were prepared, a number of amendments in building byelaws have taken place but these have not yet been incorporated and old revised control sheets are being issued to the architects.

 For the ease of preparation of building plans of commercial/industrial plots and scrutiny by the approving authority, the architects have demanded that revised control sheets be prepared by the department and issued to the architects concerned.
 
 For benefit of city residents, the department has uploaded digitized zoning plans of residential, commercial and institutional buildings on the official website of the Chandigarh administration.
 
 The residents can download the plans from website --http://chandigarh.gov.in/citizen_zoneplan.htm --free of cost. The department has digitized zoning plans of a total of 41 sectors. There are 56 sectors in Chandigarh that have been further divided into four parts (A, B, C and D) by V4 road.
 
 All plans of   have also been digitised whereas plans of Industrial Area are yet to be uploaded.
 
 The administration had set up phases I and II of Industrial Area during the 1970s on 147 acres. There are 1,884 plots in both phases, of which 700 are one kanal and above, while there are 443 and 180 plots measuring 10 and 15 marlas, respectively. There are as many as 381 plots of five marlas as well. The initiative will spare the residents from visiting the building branch and architect department for getting copies of zoning plans before preparing.
