RAJKOT: A 40-year-old man has lodged a police complaint against his wife, who is 10 years younger to him, accusing her of fleeing with cash and jewellery worth Rs 2.22 lakh from his house.

 Manoj Trivedi on Friday lodged a complaint with   police station against his wife Jagruti, who he had married only 20 days ago and her aide Bhargav Joshi. They belong to the same cast and their marriage was arranged with the consent of both the families. 
 
 Interestingly, Jagruti has also filed a complaint of domestic violence against Manoj with Mahila police station. Both Jagruti and her aide are absconding. According to the police, the couple was living in a housing society on   road. Manoj used to do glass fitting job work. 
 
 On Friday evening, Manoj lodged a complaint with Bhaktinagar police station, alleging that Jagruti breached his trust and in connivance with Bhargav fled with Rs 1.30 lakh cash and jewellery worth Rs 93,000.
 
 Investigation officer of Bhaktinagar police station R C   said, “The duo had married just 20 days ago. Both the accused are absconding and search for them is on.” 
 
 Jagruti and Bhargav have been booked under the Indian Penal Code Sections 406 (criminal breach of trust), 420 (cheating and dishonesty) and 120B (to be a party to criminal conspiracy.)
