Udaipur: In a heart wrenching incident that took place in Sabbalpura village under Kushalgarh police station in Banswara on Tuesday evening, a woman and her two kids were burnt alive when their hut was caught in flames. The flames were so powerful that it gutted the entire hut and the three victims were charred to death. People tried to put off the flames and by the time fire fighting team arrived and controlled the fire, the victims had turned to ashes.
