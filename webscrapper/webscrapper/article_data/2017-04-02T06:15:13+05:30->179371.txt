 The SIT after finding NOC of two CRPF personal and shooters as fake decided to question inspector Jasmer again, as both of them were suspected to be very close to the suspended inspector. Sources said every aspect of the case is being closely examined by SIT, which suspects that Jasmer was involved in smuggling pistols and guns that were used in sporting events. The CRPF troopers Nale Amol Bhagwan and MN Rahman, posted as constables at CRPF’s shooting range in Kadarpur, have been part of many tournaments and won several medals.
 
 An investigator of the SIT said inspector Jasmer is fond of shooting and the CRPF troopers knew him well. They had purporedly re-registered their arms licences when Jasmer was working in the arms licence branch in Gurgaon. “To inquire actual role of the CRPF shooters, we have taken inspector Jasmer Singh on a day’s remand,” said ACP Anil Yadav, head of SIT.
