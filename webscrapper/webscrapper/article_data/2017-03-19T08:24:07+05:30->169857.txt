RANCHI: The school education and literacy department dissolved the   (SMC) of  ,  , on Saturday, after the complaint of   midday meal being served to the students was found true. 

 The department received a complaint on Thursday about the school situated in Garu block of Latehar district, providing poor quality of pulses to the students in their midday meal.
 
 MDM in charge, Jagjit Singh said, “As soon as we got the orders, we directed the district superintendent of education to look into the matter and conduct a proper investigation. The report which he submitted, confirmed the irregularities in MDM.”
 
 Apart from dissolving the SMC, the Saraswati Vahini which prepares MDM has also been dissolved. “We have also issued a direction to suspend the block education officer,” Singh said.
