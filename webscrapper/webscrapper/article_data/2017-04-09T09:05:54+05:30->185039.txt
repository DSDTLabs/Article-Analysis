MARGAO: Three shops were gutted in a fire at Santemol, Raia, late Friday night. 

 Sources in the fire and emergency services (F&ES) informed that of the three shops, one was a bar, another a general store and the third, a hardware shop. While property worth Rs 11 lakhs was gutted n in the fire, firefighters managed to save goods and property worth over Rs 3 lakhs, it was informed. 
 
 Three fire tenders were engaged in the firefighting exercise. 
 
 The incident occurred at around 1 a.m. An electric short circuit is believed to have caused the fire.
