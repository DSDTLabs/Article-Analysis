CHENNAI:   C   has summoned   to meet on January 23. 

 The Assembly Secretariat issued the notification about the meet scheduled to take place at 10am.
 
 "In exercise of the powers conferred upon me by clause (1) of Article 174 of the Constitution of India, I, Ch Vidyasagar Rao, Governor of Tamil Nadu, hereby summon the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly to meet at 10am on Monday, the 23rd January, 2017 in the Assembly Chamber, Secretariat," the notification said.
 
 The first meeting of the Assembly will begin with address by Governor.
