Rajkot: A 27-year-old woman, working as a lecturer at a college in RK University of Thramba village hanged herself at the college on Monday evening. The deceased was identified as Pinal Desai, a native of Amreli. Police said that Desai had got married two years ago with a man from Morbi, but was living separately following domestic dispute with her husband. She had started to work at RK University six months ago. tnn
