BHUBANESWAR: The   gardens will be opened to public from January 8 to January 20. According to an official statement issued by the Raj Bhavan, visitors can come between 2pm and 4:30pm during the period, including Sundays and public holidays. 

 Visitors will have access to lawns, fountains and deer park on the premises of the governors secretariat-cum-residence. The main attractions of the   are rose bushes, cactus house, orchids and deer park. 
 
 The Raj Bhavan has requested visitors not to bring camera, food and other sensitive items.
 
 As entry to the Raj Bhavan is prohibited during the rest of the year, school and college students visit the governor's house in large numbers when it is opened for public every year in January.
