MUMBAI: Two city teenagers who allegedly bunked college for a monsoon   were feared drowned in the waters under the Tungareshwar   in Vasai on Friday evening. 

 The fire brigade said it had launched a search for the two, Chandrakant Gupta (17) and Sunny Kanojia (18). The victims, along with seven friends, all from Vile Parle, had arrived at the spot in the morning. The friends had liquor bottles with them.
