Aslam Khan, who has featured in films like 'Nayi Padosan', 'Kaante' and 'Welcome 2', is all set to enter the TV show ' '. He will play Sunny, a shrewd and sharp troublemaker. Apart from that, there will also be a change in the time slot of the show. 'Dil Deke Dekho', which currently airs at 10.30 pm will take up 'Khatmal-E-Ishq’s' slot at 10 pm from April 3.
