RAJKOT: A jeweller from the temple town Dwarka was robbed of 246 gm   worth Rs 5 lakh by four persons posing as crime branch sleuths in the city on Saturday night. 

 The victim  , 28, had come to the city with gold to make ornaments. He hired an auto at the bus stand and was on his way to   when the incident took place. When he reached   police chowky, four persons stopped the auto and told him they were from the crime branch. They opened his bag on the pretext of checking and fled with the gold.
