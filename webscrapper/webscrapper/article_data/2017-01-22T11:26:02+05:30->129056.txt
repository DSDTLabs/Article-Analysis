 Meanwhile, Southern Railway late on Saturday evening announced a special train from Chennai Egmore to Madurai. According to a statement, train no. 06017 from Chennai Egmore to Madurai left Chennai Egmore at 11.45pm on Saturday. 
 
 This train, with a composition of 12 Sleeper Class Coaches, four general second class coaches and two luggage-cum-brake vans, will run to open timings.
