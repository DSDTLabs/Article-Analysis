 Located in the western part of Hyderabad city, Golconda Fort was originally known as Mankal, and built on a hilltop in the year 1143. Golconda Fort is undoubtedly one of the most magnificent fortress complexes in India and an evergreen tourist destination. 
 
 
 
 
 Constructed in 1591, Charminar has become a global icon of Hyderabad, listed among the most recognised structures of India.
 
 
 
 City's heart-shaped water body, famous as Hussain Sagar, was built by Ibrahim Quli Qutub Shah in 1563. The lake separates Hyderabad from Secunderabad.
