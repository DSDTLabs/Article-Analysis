CHANDIGARH: Haryana   Heritage Development Board and the Oil and Natural Gas Corporation (ONGC) have signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) in New Delhi on Thursday for restoration and research work on the Saraswati river. As per the MoU, the ONGC would dig 100 wells on the flow path of Saraswati river.

 WAPCOS, a public sector enterprise under the aegis of union ministry of water resources, will conduct a survey for the project and initially the ONGC will bore 10 wells on flow path of Saraswati river. The MoU was signed in the presence of Haryana chief minister   and the union minister of state for petroleum and natural gas  .
