 According to police, the complainant, Sunil Yadav, a resident of Khuskhera in Rajasthan, who also owned the cab, was on his way to Bhiwadi after picking up a passenger from the IGI Airport around 9.30pm. 
 
 On reaching the MCD toll plaza, he parked the   on one side of the road and went to the toll booth to pay the tax, leaving the passenger in the car and keeping the car’s ignition on.
 
 “When I returned a few minutes later, the passenger was standing on the road and the car was missing. The passenger told me that two men threatened him at gunpoint and pulled him out of the car and fled with the car. Then I called police,” Yadav stated in the police report.
 
 Acting on the complaint, an FIR has been registered against two unidentified men under section 379-B (snatching & use of force) of the IPC and sections 25-54-59 of Arms Act at DLF 2 police station. 
 
 “We are investigating the matter and trying to identify the accused with the help of CCTV footage of the area. However, there are no clues yet,” said ASI Jitender Kumar, the investigating officer.
