Rajkot:  , a liquor haul worth Rs 26.54 lakh was seized from the outskirts of Navagam and Mohabatpara villages of Vanthali taluka in Junagadh district in the wee hours of Thursday. 

 However, three bootleggers fled, abandoning their truck in which the liquor supply had been loaded. The raid was conducted jointly by the officials of Junagadh LCB and Vanthali police station following a tip-off. 
 
 “We have seized the truck from which we recovered 8,580 bottles of Indian made foreign liquor worth Rs 26.54 lakh. We have also launched a manhunt for the accused,” an official from the Junagadh LCB said.
