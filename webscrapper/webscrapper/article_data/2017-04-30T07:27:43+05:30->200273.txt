Pune: The   (ACB) of CBI, Pune has booked a serving Colonel and his wife for allegedly possessing assets to the tune of Rs 1 crore disproportionate to their known sources of income.

 The   (CBI) has in its FIR stated that the officer earned the assets between January 1, 2008 and March 27, 2017 when he was posted in Pune, Jammu and Rajasthan.
 
 The officer retired on December 31, 2015, but was re-employed again as a Colonel on March 12, 2016. He is currently posted as a Colonel with the 516 Army   ( ), Battalion 16, attached with the Southern Western Command of Indian Army.
 
 CBI sources the officer in 2009 served as the Catering Officer of the National Defence Academy (NDA) and was later transferred to Jammu. After serving it for two years, he was again posted out to Sriganganagar. The officer retired from service there.
 
 The FIR filed by the CBI said the officer and his wife allegedly amassed moveable and immovable assets disproportionate to their known sources of income. The agency has gathered information about the expenditures, savings and assets owned by them. It has found multiple bank accounts of the duo opened in various banks, including one in Kothrud.
 
 The CBI sources said searches would be conducted at various places, including Haryana, by its teams.
 
 Confirming the development, superintendent of police MR Kadole of CBI said, “We have registered an FIR. Our teams will conduct searches at various places and ours investigation is under way. At this juncture, I cannot comment further on the case.”
