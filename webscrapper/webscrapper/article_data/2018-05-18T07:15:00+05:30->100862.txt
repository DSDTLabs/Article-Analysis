GREATER NOIDA: A 25-year-old man was killed and five others   when a speeding truck rammed an auto in Dankaur on Wednesday evening. The victim has been identified as  . He was from Dankaur. Farmood Ali Pundir, SHO of Dankaur, said the auto driver and five passengers were on their way from Bilaspur to Kasna in an autorickshaw. “When they reached near Kherli Canal, a speeding truck coming from the same direction, rammed the auto,” he said.

 
 The auto overturned on the road leaving the occupants injured. Locals who gathered at the spot informed police. A team from Dankaur police station reached the spot and rushed the victims to Sharda Hospital in Greater Noida. However, Salim was declared brought dead due to critical injuries. 
 
 His father Subbu, who was also in the autorickshaw, suffered head injuries and is undergoing treatment. The other two victims were identified as Raju and Furqan. The driver and one passenger, whose identities have not been established, suffered minor injuries and were discharged from the hospital after treatment.
 
 Pundir said a police team reached the spot but the accused driver fled with the truck. A case of rash driving and death due to negligence has been registered against the accused.
 
 The victim’s body was handed over to family members after a postmortem was conducted, police said.
