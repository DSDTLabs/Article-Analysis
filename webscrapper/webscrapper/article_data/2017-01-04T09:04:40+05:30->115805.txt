Police sources said Faisal, married to the daughter of Congressman and state planning board deputy chairman CM  , was angry that his wife had undergone an abortion at the hospital, which had not sought his consent.
A Vikram Hospital release said a woman, whose identity was not revealed, came to the hospital on Monday night with excessive vaginal bleeding caused by incomplete abortion. She was posted for an emergency evacuation procedure under anaesthesia to safeguard her life, after the hospital sought consent from the woman, her mother and sister. The hospital said the situation did not require them to wait for the husband's consent.
