NEW DELHI: The Directorate of Government Examinations (DGE),   announced the TN HSC First Year (Class 11) result on Wednesday, May 30, 2018. The overall pass percentage of TN +1 2018 is 91.3 per cent.

 
 The first year results were declared on Wednesday at 9 am on the official website: dge.tn.gov.in and tnresults.nic.in. 
 
 The TNDGE HSC +1 exams were conducted from March 1 to April 16, 2018. 
 
 The DGE had declared the Tamil Nadu Class 12 (HSC second year) results on May 16, 2018. The overall pass percentage for Class 12 was 91.1 per cent. This year also, the girls had outperformed boys by registering 94.1 per cent while boys scored 87.7 per cent.
 
 The Tamil Nadu SSLC Class 10 results were declared on May 23, 2018
 
  
  
 
 
 
 
 The Tamil Nadu Board of Secondary Education was established in 1910, and later after Independence was carved out as and separate Directorate of Government Examinations under the Department of Education, Government of Tamil Nadu.
