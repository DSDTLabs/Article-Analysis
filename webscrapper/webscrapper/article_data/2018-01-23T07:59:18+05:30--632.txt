BENGALURU: Senior citizens travelling in   buses can now avail concession by producing any government-authorized identity card. KSRTC on Monday decided to stop issuing senior citizen identity cards. However, those already issued will be valid for availing concession. 

 Of ficials said senior citizens staying in Karnataka can produce any one of the ID cards -  ,  ,  ,  , ID issued by central ministries and corporations, state government departments and corporations, ID issued by physically challenged and senior citizens' welfare directorate. The decision was taken after several senior citizens complained they were denied concession by conductors who insisted on identity cards issued by KSRTC.
