PANCHKULA:   ( ) will e-auction 26 residential and institutional sites on Thursday.

 This will be the sixth e-auction since October 2016. Huda this time will be focusing on sectors across the Ghaggar river.
 
 The residential sites include two 10 marla sites in Sector 21, two six marla sites in Sector 21, three 10 marla sites in Sector 25, three 14 marla sites in Sector 25, three six marla sites in Sector 25, three 10 marla sites in Sector 26, two 6 marla site in Sector 26 and two 8 marla sites in Sector 26. Institutional sites include a nursery school in Sector 4, and one in Mansa Devi Complex, Sector 4, a school site in Sector 17 and a clinic site in Sector 4 and two sites in Sector 17
 
 In the last residential e-auction in June, Huda managed to sell 24 sites out of 38 and claimed to have earned a profit of 25% out of the reserve price of Rs 29.73 crore for all 38 sites.
 
 Huda estate officer   said the e-auction will start at 9 am and end by 2 pm and the result will be declared the same day.
 
 The first residential e-auction was held in October last year. Huda had managed to sell just 13 out of 43 plots. In December 2016, Huda managed to sell 12 out of 24 plots after the base prices were reduced as compared to the first auction. On June 1, Huda failed to sell all 35 commercial sites put on e-auction and only three sites were sold. In February 16, Huda had managed to sell 25 out of 26 residential sites.
