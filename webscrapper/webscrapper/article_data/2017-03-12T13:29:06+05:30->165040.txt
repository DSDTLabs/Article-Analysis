Power Star Pawan Kalyan’s soon to be released action entertainer ‘Katamarayudu’ has got prodigious response and crossed one crore views on its official YouTube channel. It is the first Telugu film teaser to reach 10 million views.

 Katamarayudu’s teaser is an amazing action-packed teaser which clocked in 2 lakh 50 thousand likes to become Tollywood’s most liked teaser on YouTube. Currently, ‘Katamarayudu’ team is busy shooting a romantic duet in Italy featuring the lead actors Pawan Kalyan and Shruti Haasan.
 
 Once the team wraps up shoot and heads to Hyderabad, the promotional activities for the film will commence. ‘Katamarayudu’ will hit the screens on March 28th and Sharrat Marar is producing the film.
