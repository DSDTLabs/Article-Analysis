Ahmedabad: The Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board will publish the results of its class XII general stream examination on Thursday.  The results will be available on the board’s website from 8am. According to board officials, it is likely that the HSC general stream passing percentage will be higher than in 2017, when it was 56.82%.  Around 4.76 lakh students took the class XII general stream exam this March. Board officials said 29,000 fewer students took the examination this year, compared to 2017. In 2017, 5.05 lakh students took the exam. Officials said students’ marksheets will be distributed by their schools on Thursday as well.  Board officials said this year’s result was one day late, compared to 2017. Board officials said the special examination for repeaters of the HSC science stream (semester system), will also be declared on Thursday.
