Young actor   has been giving continuous hits and is in no time to take a break.

 
 His upcoming project, 'Andhagadu' with his lucky charm   under the direction of Veligonda Srinivas is ready for release. In the movie, Raj Tarun plays a visually challenged radio jockey with extra sensory perception. 
 
 Andhagadu's trailer was released recently and the response was overwhelming. The trailer hinted at a high dose of comedy with Satya and Sudharshan evoking a few laughs and has Rajendra Prasad in a prominent role.
 
 
 The movie is produced by AK Entertainments and was earlier scheduled to hit the screens on May 26 but now was pushed to June 2.
