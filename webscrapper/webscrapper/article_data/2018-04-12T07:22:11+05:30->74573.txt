BENGALURU: I PU classes will begin on May 14 and II PU on May 2. The department of preuniversity   announced the 2018-19 academic calendar on Wednesday.

 “In view of assembly polls, I and II PU exams were held early. I PU exams were over by February 21and students enjoyed a 69-day vacation,” said board director C Shikha.
 
 
 For SSLC students seeking admission to I PU, the provision for a bridge course will be made in the academic calendar this time, Shikha said. “With colleges opening early this year, we are expecting a rise in enrolment of students in government PU colleges.
 
 The II PU results for the 2017-18 batch are likely to announced in April last week.
