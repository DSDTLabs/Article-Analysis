Chennai: Sun TV Network, one of the largest television broadcasters in the country, has reported a 12% year-on-year (y-o-y) growth in profit after taxes (PAT) at Rs.1030.66 crores for 2016-17. Consolidated revenues were up 7% y-o-y at Rs 2,645.72 crore, as against Rs 2, 473.64 crore for the corresponding year ended 31st March, 2016. 

 Subscription revenues grew by a healthy 18% y-o-y to Rs 961.41 crore for the year. EBITDA (earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortisation) moved up 2.1% y-o-y to Rs 1,769.85 crore for 2016-17.
 
 PAT for the fourth quarter (Q4) of 2016-17 however remained flat at Rs 235.9 crore. Revenues for Q4 inched up 3% y-o-y to Rs 582.5 crore. Subscription revenues increased 12% y-o-y to Rs 241.88 crore during the quarter.
