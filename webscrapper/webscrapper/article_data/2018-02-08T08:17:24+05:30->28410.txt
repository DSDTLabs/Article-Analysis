CHANDIGARH: A central team has reached the city for   (cleanliness  ). It will measure Chandigarh against all the sanitation parameters and rate it for a report.

 Sources in the civic administration said that the team had scanned records and MC documents on Wednesday know about the ongoing and planned works. The team will next go into the field to prepare a ground report. Besides the things mentioned on the parameter form, it will also test the condition of public toilets, slums, and daily sweeping of the streets and residential colonies.
 
 It will submit its report to the central government. This   Survekshan is quite vital for the planned city, as Chandigarh had received a huge criticism last time when it had slipped to the 11th position.
