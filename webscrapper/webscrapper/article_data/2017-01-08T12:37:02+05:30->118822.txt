KALYAN:   chief Uddhav Thackeray on Saturday inaugurated late Bal Thackeray's   in Kalyan.

 It is the first memorial of Thackeray constructed near   where the leader had spent some of the days of his childhood. 
 
 The memorial has a 22-feet bronze statue of Bal Thackeray. The memorial has been constructed at Kala Talab in Kalyan (West) by Kalyan-Dombivli municipal corporation run by Shiv Sena-BJP. 
 
 Over Rs 13 crore have been spent on this. There's also a museum having information on the life of Bal Thackaray.
 
 During inauguration, other leaders were also present including Adity Thackaray, Thane Guardian Minister Eknath Shinde, KDMC Mayor Rajendra Devlekar and Vijay Salvi.
