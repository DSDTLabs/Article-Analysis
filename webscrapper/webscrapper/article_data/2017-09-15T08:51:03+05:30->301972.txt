BENGALURU: Legislators from seven states were in the city on Thursday to discuss the best practices on how to protect children's rights.They were participating at the legislators' regional conference 2017 on child rights at Vikasa Soudha. The legislators submitted a 16-point declaration, which includes developing a state policy for child protection, promoting cooperation within states and central departments, identifying vulnerable areas for children, encouraging civic agencies to monitor childcentric issues locally and escalating to senior authorities, initiating special missions to tackle child and mortality in an integrated manner.
