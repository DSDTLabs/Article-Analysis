CHENNAI: The Enforcement Directorate ( ) on Thursday conducted searches at 18 premises across 10 states including Tamil Nadu, mostly at houses of state and   government employees. 

 In Tamil Nadu, the searches were carried out in Chennai and Madurai. In the state capital, the residence of former regional commissioner of Employees Provident Fund Organisation ( ) Durga Prasad in Keelkattalai was searched, a senior ED official said. "However we did not find any incriminating document," he said. Prasad was arrested by Central Bureau of Investigation ( ) on January 18 last year. He later came out on bail.
 
 A press release from the directorate said searches in TN were conducted against bank officials who are suspected to have sanctioned loans and credit facilities to people without proper documents.
