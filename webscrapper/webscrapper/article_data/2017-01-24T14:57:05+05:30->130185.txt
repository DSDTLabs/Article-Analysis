COLVA: South Goa sessions court sentenced accused   to undergo imprisonment for murdering his colleague,  , on May 5, 2015, at Sirvoi in Quepem. Pathyekhanvar and Sharif, a native of Karnataka, were working as tile fitters at a construction site.

 “The deposition of witness Raju Alikatti, a cousin of the accused, proves that he had seen the accused with the victim, Sharif, at the house of the accused in the afternoon on the day of the murder. The accused had assaulted the victim with a stick on the grounds that the deceased had taken away Rs 10,000 from one plastic container that was in his house,” sessions court judge Desmond D’Costa observed.
 
 Public prosecutor Sarojini Sardinha argued the case.
