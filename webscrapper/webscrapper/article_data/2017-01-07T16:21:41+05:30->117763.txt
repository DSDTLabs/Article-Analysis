 Cops seized 2,700 notes of Rs 2,000 denomination from Manish Chaudhary (35), Nitin Agrawal (21) and Sachin Agrawal (22), all residents of Uttar Pradesh. The accused were in a car with   registration number.
 
 Sub-inspector K Y Vyas of Amirgadh police station, about 40 km from Palanpur, said, “The money was kept in a bag in the rear seat of the car.” The accused told police that they were going to buy some food processing machinery from an exhibition being held in Ahmedabad.
 
 “The income tax authorities have been informed about the seizure,” said Neeraj Badgujar, superintendent of police, Banaskantha.
