PALANPUR: A couple ended their lives by jumping into   pond near   town of   on Thursday. Their bodies were fished out at about 4 pm on Thursday near Radhanpur in Patan district. 

 The two bodies were found floating with their hands tied with a cloth. The woman was identified as Daksha Parmar (18) of nearby Sinad village and Nikul Parmar (21) of Nanpura village. Police said the couple were in love with each other since last three years. But could not succeed in convincing their family members. 
 
 According to statement of parents of the deceased, Daksha had left her house at about 4 am, while Nikul had also left house during wee hours on Thursday, the police said.
