BENGALURU: An ambulance ferrying a patient was stopped for three whole minutes on JC Road on Tuesday evening as chief minister  's convoy was to enter   for   celebrations.

 Senior police officers who were present at the spot refused to comment. Police commissioner   said emergency vehicles should be given priority and he would order an inquiry into the incident.
 
 It happened around 6.45pm when Siddaramaiah arrived at Ravindra Kalakeshtra on JC Road to participate in the programme. The ambulance, which was heading towards Corporation Circle, was stopped for three minutes. Police officials, both from traffic and law and order, were standing near the convoy and were well aware of the ambulance's presence due to its blaring siren.
