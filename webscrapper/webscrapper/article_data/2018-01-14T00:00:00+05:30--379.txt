Originally designed to lower blood pressure, DASH diet is apparently very effective for weight loss, lowering cholesterol, and managing or preventing diabetes. DASH stands for Dietary Approaches to Stop  . In research studies, people who were on DASH lowered   within 2 weeks. DASH was created by research sponsored by the US National Institute of Health. The research found that blood pressure could be lowered without the use of medication. It lowers your blood pressure by limiting salt, saturated fat and cholesterol and increasing foods high in finer protein and nutrients. These nutrients include potassium, calcium and magnesium. Researchers found that by cutting back on “empty carbs” you can not only lose weight, but also control BP. Those on DASH, need to do the following:

 - Eat more fruits, vegetables, and low-fat dairy foods 
 - Cut back on foods that are high in saturated fat, cholesterol, and trans fats 
 - Eat more whole-grain foods, fish, poultry, and nuts 
 - Limit sodium, sweets, sugary drinks, and red meats.
