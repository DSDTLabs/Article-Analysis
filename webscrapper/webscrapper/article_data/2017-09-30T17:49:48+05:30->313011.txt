BENGALURU: The railways have decided to continue the service of Yesvantpur-Visakhapatnam weekly tatkal express special for eight more trips in each direction to clear  .

 Accordingly, the special train will depart from Yesvantpur at 06:35pm on every Friday and will reach Visakhapatnam at 02:35pm the next day. This service will be available from October 6, 2017 to November 24, 2017.
 
 In return direction, the train will depart from Visakhapatnam at 01:45 hours on every Sunday and will reach Yesvantpur at 09:05 am the next day. This service is available with effect from October 8, 2017 to November 26, 2017 .
 
 En route, the train has stoppages at Banasavadi, Krishnarajapuram, Whitefield, Bangarpet, Kuppam, Jolarpettai, Katpadi, Renigunta, Gudur, Nellore, Ongole, Chirala, Tenali, Vijayawada, Eluru, Rajamundry, Samalkot, Anakapalle and Duvvada in both directions).
 
 The train will have 13 coaches, comprising of a single  , two AC-3 tier coaches, eight second-class sleeper coaches and two second-class luggage-cum-brake van with disabled-friendly compartment.
