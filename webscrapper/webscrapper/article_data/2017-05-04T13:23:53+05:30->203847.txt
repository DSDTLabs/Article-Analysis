BHIWANDI: Fire broke out at a slipper factory godown in Aryan compound area in   on Thursday morning. 

 Four fire tenders and three water tankers have been rushed to the spot. 
 
 Officials said that the fire took place at 9 am and it is under control. No injuries and casualties are reported as of now.
 
 The fire brigade officials suspect short circuit to be the reason behind the fire but are yet to come to the conclusion.
