Rocking star  's upcoming flick 'Gunturodu' with a tag line of 'Love lo Paddadu' is all set for the theatrical release. The movie, which is an action packed entertainer is helmed by SK Satya and actress Pragya Jaiswal plays the love interest of Manoj in it. The movie is out from the censor board with a clean U/A certificate. Now the movie makers announced that the film will hit the screens on March 3.

  Actors Kota Srinivasa Rao, Rajendra Prasad and Sampath were a few among other actors who played prominent roles in it.
