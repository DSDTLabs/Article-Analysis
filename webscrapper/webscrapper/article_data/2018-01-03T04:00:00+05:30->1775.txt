Rajkot: A 14-year-old class IX student hanged himself to death allegedly after his father scolded him over playing games on mobile phone in Rajkot on Tuesday. Deceased Mohit Jayatibhai Rathod, a student of Prateek School, hanged himself in his room on Tuesday morning. According to police, Mohit got a scolding from his father Jayatnibhai for spending too much time on playing games on mobile phone. Primarily this seems to the reason behind his suicide, the police added.tnn
