Madurai: Southern Railways will be operating special fare special trains Suvidha specials in the first and second week of October in order to clear the extra rush. 

 According to statement from railways, Train Number 06001 Chennai Egmore – Tirunelveli special fare special train will leave Chennai Egmore at 9.05 pm on October 6 and reach Tirunelveli at 10.45 am the next day. 
 
 Train Number 06002 Tirunelveli – Chennai Egmore special fare special train will leave Tirunelveli at 2.45 pm on October 8 and reach Chennai Egmore at 3.45 am the next day. These trains will have 1 AC 2-tier, 2 AC 3-tier, 12 Sleeper Class & 2 Luggage-cum-brake van coaches. They will stop at Tambaram, Chengalpattu, Villupuram, Vriddhachalam, Tiruchchirappalli, Dindigul, Kodaikkanal Road, Madurai, Virudhunagar, Kovilpatti, and Vanchchimaniyachchi. Train Number 06002 Tirunelveli – Chennai Egmore special fare special train will stop at Mambalam also.
 
 Suvidha special train from Chennai Egmore to Tirunelveli, the Train Number 82601 Chennai Egmore – Tirunelveli Suvidha special train will leave Chennai Egmore at 9.05 pm on October 13 and reach Tirunelveli at 10.45 am the next day. The suvidha special will have 1 AC 2-tier, 2 AC 3-tier, 12 Sleeper Class & 2 Luggage-cum-brake van coaches. It will stop at Tambaram, Chengalpattu, Villupuram, Vriddhachalam, Tiruchchirappalli, Dindigul, Madurai, Virudhunagar, Satur and Kovilpatti. Advance reservations for the above trains commenced at 8 am from October 1, the statement from railways observed.
