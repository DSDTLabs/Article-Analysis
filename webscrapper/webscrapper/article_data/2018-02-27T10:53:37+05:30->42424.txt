CHANDIGARH: A SAD-BJP delegation on Monday met   governor  , seeking directions to the state government to hold fresh elections for the Ludhiana municipal corporation by scrapping the February 24 polls, which witnessed widespread violence and alleged booth capturing.

 
 The delegation urged the governor to direct the Ludhiana administration to register cases against   MP   and MLA Bharat Bhushan Ashu, Congress leaders Surinder   and Rakesh Pandey.
