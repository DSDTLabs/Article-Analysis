Mega Power Star Ram Charan’s ‘Dhruva’ is all set to be remade in Bollywood. 

 ‘Dhruva,' which was an official remake of the Tamil blockbuster ‘Thani Oruvan’, was remade in Telugu in 2016. Filmmaker Sabbir Khan, who is gearing up for the release of his film ‘Munna Michael,’ is also in talks for an official remake of 'Dhruva' as per reports. 
 
 The story of the film revolves around an IPS officer and a rogue scientist, and it was a hit both in Tamil and Telugu. As per rumours,   and Siddharth Malhotra would play the lead characters in the Hindi version of the movie.
