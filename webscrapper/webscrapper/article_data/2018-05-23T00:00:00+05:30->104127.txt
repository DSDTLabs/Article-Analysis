When it comes to fashion, it’s not just women, but even men in the city are up to date with the latest fad in the industry. Recently, the who’s who from Chennai’s fashion world came together to attend the launch of a menswear outlet. We spotted Dushyanth looking dapper in a crisp, self design white shirt, which he paired with a pair of black jeans and a checked belt. While Venket Ram rocked a checked shirt, Chaitanya Rao and Vaseem Thaberz looked stylish and be‘suited’. Vivek Karunakaran carried off his trademark look with elan, and Aditya Patel’s understated sense of style won him many an appreciative glance. We also spotted Sameer Bharat Ram and Rashmi at the do.
