NEW DELHI: The National Board of Examinations (NBE) has announced the NEET PG Exam Result 2018 today on the official website of the Board - nbu.edu.in.

 All the students who have appeared for NEET PG Exam Examination can check their result online on the official website - nbu.edu.in.
 
 The NBE has clarified that the merit list of the candidates will be released after applying the tie-breaker criteria as mentioned in the information bulletin for NEET-PG 2018. 
 
 
 
 
 
 The Board also advised the candidates not to get misled by any false or bogus claims regarding obtaining a rank in NEET PG 2018.
 
 The NEET PG Exam exam result is available on the website - nbu.edu.in. Students who have appeared for the examination and wish to check their NEET PG Exam Result can follow the steps given below:
 
 Step-by-step guide to check NEET PG Exam Result 2018 online through official portal
 
 1) Open the official website - nbu.edu.in
 
 2) On the homepage of the website, you will find the Result link. Click on it
 
 3) Once you click on the link, it will redirect you to a new webpage where you will have to enter your details such as NEET PG Exam 2018 application ID, your name and date of birth etc. Next, click on submit
 
 4) Your NEET PG Exam 2018 result will be displayed onscreen. Cross check all the details given in your NEET PG Exam 2018 Result.
 
 5) You can download and print your NEET PG Exam 2018 Result for further reference.
