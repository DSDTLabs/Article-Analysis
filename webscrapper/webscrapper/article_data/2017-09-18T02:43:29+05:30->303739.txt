 The   wrote to UPSC, asking for a relaxation in age limit and method of recruitment for Kashmiri teachers. The letter was written after a high court order asked the government to take an immediate decision on regularising Kashmiri migrant teachers. 
 
 The cabinet had taken a decision on this matter on July 12 and had subsequently asked for the LG’s approval to push for their regularisation. In its letter to UPSC, the government sought UPSC’s concurrence for one-time relaxation in recruitment rules for the posts of postgraduate teachers, trained graduate teacher, librarian and music teacher working on a contract basis.
