After two impressive tracks in a matter of three weeks, the makers of 'Mubarakan; released their third number, 'Goggle Pake', yesterday. The video features the top members of the cast —  ,  ,   and Ileana D’Cruz — flaunting goggles in keeping with the title of the racy Punjabi song. Anil, who plays Kartar Singh, will be seen matching steps with his nephew Arjun, who has a double role in the film.
