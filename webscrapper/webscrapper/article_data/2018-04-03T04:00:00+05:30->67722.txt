Ahmedabad: Rahul Gupta, a 2004 batch IAS officer, was appointed as the new collector of Rajkot on Monday. Gupta, who was the collector of Junagadh, replaces Dr Vikrant Pandey who has been made Ahmedabad collector. The state government also transferred RJ Makadia, commissioner of tribal development as Morbi collector in place of IK Patel who was shifted to Kheda. tnn
