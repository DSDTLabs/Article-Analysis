GURUGRAM: Representatives of   will be part of a committee that is being set up to keep a check on school buses. The move comes in the wake of rising number of violations of Surakshit School Vahan  . 

 At present, there is a district committee on the Surakshit School Vahan Policy that includes deputy commissioner, secretary  , deputy commissioner of police (traffic) among others. However, despite regular inspections, schools were often found flouting norms. 
 
 Therefore, the district administration has decided that the committee would be expanded and 10 members from different schools will be included in it.
