MARGAO: Things seem to be looking up for the mountaintop village of   in   taluka. Following flak for lack of basic amenities in Vavurla, the state government has directed the tribal welfare department to examine the feasibility of providing basic infrastructural facilities so that adequate funds can allocated in the next financial budget.

 Director of tribal welfare   told TOI that a team of government officials will visit Vavurla on Wednesday to examine the infrastructural needs of the village.
 
 “Officials from the PWD, water supply department, electricity department, health services and education department will comprise the team that will be visiting Vavurla, following which they will submit a report to me at a meeting to be held on January 19,” he said.
