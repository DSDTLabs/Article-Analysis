Ahmedabad: Gujarat University has decided that the procedure for admission to its Bachelor of Arts, Bachelor of Commerce, Bachelor of Business Administration, Bachelor of Computer Applications, Master of Information Technology and other courses will be completely online. Gujarat University has begun giving out PIN numbers for candidates to apply online. Candidates can procure PINs from various GU-affiliated colleges and then register online. 

 Officials said there are 8,880 BA seats at colleges in Ahmedabad city and 5,640 BA seats at 14 colleges in rural areas of Ahmedabad. Registration for BA admissions will begin on June 7 and be open till June 11.
