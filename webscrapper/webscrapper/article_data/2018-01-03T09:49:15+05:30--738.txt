BHOPAL: Two minor girls were found hanging at their homes in separate incidents on Tuesday. 

 In the first incident, a 16-year-old girl, a student of Class 11, committed suicide on Tuesday. The girl was alone at her home when the incident occurred. 
 
 According to police, the girl's father works in   and her mother is a domestic worker. She has two brothers - the elder brother is in Chennai for   training and his younger brother studies in Class 4.
 
 On Tuesday, her younger brother returned home from school, and knocked on the door, but there was no answer. After knocking repeatedly when no one opened the door, he asked his neighbours for help who climbed a wall and entered the house to find the girl hanging from the ceiling. 
 
 Police recovered a suicide note from her which state that it was her decision to end her life and her family members or outsiders were not responsible for the same. A case was registered at   police station and further investigations are on. In the second incident, a 17-year-old girl was found hanging at her home in Kamla Nagar. 
 
 The girl's father works in   on a contractual basis. On Tuesday, the girl's mother went to pick up her brother from school in the afternoon. When she returned, she found her daughter hanging, and rushed her to the hospital, where she was declared brought dead. No suicide note was recovered from the spot and police is yet to ascertain the reason behind the suicide. The statement of her kin are yet to be recorded, said the police.
