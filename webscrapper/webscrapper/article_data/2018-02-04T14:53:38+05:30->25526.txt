CUTTACK: A massive fire broke out at a shopping mall in Cuttack city on Saturday. 

 The fire occurred at a garment showroom in Badambadi and engulfed five more adjacent shops at the City Style shopping mall. About 100 firefighters have been pressed into service to douse the blaze. Fire officials have denied presence of any person inside the mall 
 
 The fire services personnel confirmed that the shopping mall didn't obtain fire safety certificate.
 
 "Inquiry is going on to find out the exact cause of fire. No report of injury or loss of life so far," DGP (fire services) BK Sharma said.
