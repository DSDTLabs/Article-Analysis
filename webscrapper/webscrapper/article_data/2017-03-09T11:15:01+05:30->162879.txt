GHAZIPUR: In a crackdown on Tuesday night, 71 people were arrested for breach of peace in election-bound  .   Subhash Chandra Dubey said police got information that outsiders had come to cast bogus votes in polling stations of Zamania. Police got the name of an educational institute in Seorai village, native place of sitting SP MLA and tourism minister Om Prakash Singh who is in the fray for the seat. 

 Police found that 49 BHU students and 20 residents of Bihar were holed up. 
 
 "We came to know from them that SP candidate Om Prakash Singh had summoned them to influence the electoral process on Wednesday. We have booked them," said Dubey, adding an FIR against Singh will be registered for attempt to influence elections.
