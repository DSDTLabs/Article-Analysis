Nagpur: The fate of almost 24,000 students who applied for free admissions under the Right to Education (RTE) Act quota in Nagpur district will be decided on Wednesday. The online lottery process will begin that day but results won’t be out till March 9.  Premchand Raut, coordinator at Sarva Shiksha Abhiyaan (SSA) said, “There are 7,099 seats up for grabs under RTE and in Nagpur we have received 23,462 applications. Parents will receive admission lottery results on their registered mobile numbers by March 9, or at the most by March 10.”  The RTE applications to seats available ratio in Nagpur district was the highest in the entire state.
