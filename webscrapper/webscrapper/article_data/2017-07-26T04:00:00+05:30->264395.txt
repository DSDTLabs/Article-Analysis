Vadodara: The detection of crime branch (DCB) arrested a person who was wanted in a burglary case in the city. The accused identified as Sahil Malek, a resident of Fatehpura was on the run since 2015 after committing a burglary on Dabhoi Road. According to the police, Malek had entered a house in Vadodara Urban Development Authority (VUDA) apartments on Dabhoi Road and fled with valuables. tnn
