AHMEDABAD: In a new notification, the   has enabled those students who have failed in their final semester exams to apply for reassessment for upto two subjects. Earlier, only those students who failed in not more than two subjects could apply for reassessment. However, the varsity officials felt that this was unjust to other students who could not get the benefit of reassessment.

 MN Patel, vice chancellor of GU said: “Undergraduate and post-graduate students will benefit from this new reassessment rule.”
