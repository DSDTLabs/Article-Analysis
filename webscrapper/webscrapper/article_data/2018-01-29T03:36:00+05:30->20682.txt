Saligao: At the first gram sabha in the new panchayat ghar in Betim on Sunday,   Magos villagers deliberated on the issue of a sports complex in the village. 

 Sports enthusiasts demanded that the panchayat acquire land and provide sports facilities to villagers.
 
 The gram sabha also deliberated on other issues such as garbage disposal, shifting of the primary school to new premises, removal of advertising boards and strewn garbage.
