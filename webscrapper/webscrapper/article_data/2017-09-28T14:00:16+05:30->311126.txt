AHMEDABAD: Selling or wearing any attire that looks similar to  . 

 Citing   wearing   uniforms, collector   has prohibited unauthorized sale or use of any cloth material that resembles the Army uniform. Mohan issued a notification in this regard that will be in effect till November 8. The notification has been issued as a precautionary measure.
 
 “Uniforms resembling those of armed forces are being sold in many towns and villages in the district. Anti-national elements can potentially use these to foment trouble, which poses a potentially grave threat to national security,” the notification stated.
 
 Violation of the notification will attract the punishment under section 188 (disobedience to order duly promulgated by public servant) of IPC.
