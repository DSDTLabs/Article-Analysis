A 50-year-old man and his wife were killed after a state bus knocked them down off a two-wheeler they were riding, near Karambakudi, Pudukottai district, in the early hours on Sunday.

 The deceased were identified as Subramanian and his wife Padma, 42, from Kallakottai village. Police arrested bus driver Durairaj and conductor Ramachandran for culpable homicide not amounting to murder. Thye were remanded in custody.
 
 According to police, the couple were on their way to nearby village Narangiyampatty to visit their relatives. 
 
 As they were travelling, a government bus knocked them down off the bike from behind, leaving both seriously injured. Subramanian died at the scene while his wife breathed her last on the way to hospital. 
 
 On being informed, the police reached the spot and carried out an investigation. The bus was headed for to   from Karambakudi when the   happened, the police said.
 
 The bodies of Subramanian and Padma were sent to the GH for postmortem examination.
