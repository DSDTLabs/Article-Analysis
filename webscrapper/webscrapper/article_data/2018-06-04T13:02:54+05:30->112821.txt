The film's release was reportedly delayed due to not getting the censor certificate on time, an issue that a number of Gujarati films have faced in the past. Chhutti Jashe Chhakka is a film about two friends who get entangled in cricket betting racket and how they help each other out of the situation. Bharat Chawda told us, "The main theme of the film is friendship and how two friends stand by each other during difficult times."
