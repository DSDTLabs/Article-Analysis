 Aurangabad police chief Amitesh Kumar has referred the the matter to the economic offences wing of the police for investigation.

 The couple, who has been staying in Tanzania for over 25 years, had in July 2014 appointed Kantrao Balwantrao Kulkarni (79) as caretaker of the property. The elderly man hired the watchman.
