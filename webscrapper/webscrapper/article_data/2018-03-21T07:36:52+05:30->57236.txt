 He said the car hit the divider, after which the driver lost control on it. The driver then took a sharp left and hit the victim. The impact was such that the victim was thrown to the other side of the carriageway. 
 
 Ajay said he immediately raised an alarm. “It happened at 4am so there weren’t many people around. I called the police immediately and took him to hospital with help from some people, but it was too late,” he said. Police said that they have registered a case under sections 279 (rash and negligent driving) and 304 (A) (causing death due to negligence) of the IPC at the Mandir Marg police station. 
 
 Ajay has given the i20’s registration number that he had noted to the police. Police are scanning the CCTV footage from the area to ascertain the sequence of events. The victim has not been identified till now.
