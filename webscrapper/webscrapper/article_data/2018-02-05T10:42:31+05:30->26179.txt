IMPHAL: Art and culture minister   on Sunday felicitated a state cultural delegation that represented India in   at the 2nd Surin International Cultural Exchange, 2018. At the event, the delegation received the 'Outstanding Performance  ' and the 'Entertaining Performance Award'.

 The minister also said his department, in consultation with chief minister  , would do more to give a boost to art and culture in the state. He also distributed trophies and citations to the cultural delegates.
