 She was on her way to the examination hall when the suspect, who had been following her, intercepted her near the canteen and caught her hand. When the girl refused to entertain his advances, the suspect got abusive and even threatened the girl.
 
 The girl immediately lodged a complaint at the   police station, under sections 354 (D) (stalking), 509 (word, gesture or act intended to insult modesty of a woman), 504 (intentional insult with intent to provoke breach of peace) and 506 (punishment for criminal intimidation) of Indian Penal Code, and 8 (punishment for sexual assault) and 12 (punishment for sexual harassment) of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act.
 
 "The accused is a repeat offender," said senior inspector  . "We are in the process of collecting information of the crime registered against him with the Vishrantwadi police station in 2016. The youth will be arrested at the earliest." 
 
 The suspect was arrested by the police in 2016 for allegedly stalking the girl, but was released on bail.
