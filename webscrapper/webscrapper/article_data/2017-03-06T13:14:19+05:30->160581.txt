Commemorating actor   on his first death anniversary, Mazhavil Manorama’s comedy show ‘Komady Circus’ will pay tribute to him through a special episode today.

 The show will feature actor   and mimicry artiste-turned-actor  , who will share memories about their friend known for his comedy skills on screen. 
 
 Going by the promo of the episode shared on channel’s social networking site the programme will showcase mimicry artistes, who will perform folk songs and eminent movie roles delivered by the late artiste.
 
 The special episode will be aired on the channel at 9 pm on March 6.
