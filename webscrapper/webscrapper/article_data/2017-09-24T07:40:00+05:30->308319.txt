Ludhiana: A man who allegedly raped his minor daughter for seven years has been sent to judicial custody till October 6. Cops on Friday produced the accused in the court. 

 Police officials said that on Tuesday, the girl (14) along and her mother went to the principal of the school she studies in and narrated the harrowing experience of her alleged sexual abuse by her own father.
 
 Cops said that the principal advised her to contact cops following which the girl gathered courage and lodged a complaint with the Bus Stand Police Post. On being informed, cops reached the complainant and started investigation the matter.
 
 Police later booked the accused under sections Sections 376 (rape) and 506 (criminal intimidation) of the Indian Penal Code (IPC) and Sections 3 and 4 of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act, 2012.
 
 In-charge of Bus Stand Police post, assistant sub-inspector (ASI) Tarseem Lal, who is investigating Officer (IO) for the case said that the accused was arrested on Thursday and produced before a local court. He has been sent to judicial custody till October 6.
 
 The IO added that the medical examination of the girl has confirmed rape. The complainant alleged that the accused, who is a drunkard, has been raping her since she was seven and threatened to kill her family members if she disclosed about the incident to anyone.
