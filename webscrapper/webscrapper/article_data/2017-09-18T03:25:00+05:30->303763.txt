Panaji: Chief minister Manohar   on Sunday said that he will not use   and urged the people of the state to devote 100 days a year for cleanliness activities. “I have stopped using plastic since the last 25 days,” he said. 

 Speaking at a function organized on Seva Diwas, to mark PM Narendra Modi’s birthday, Parrikar said while we take a pledge to devote 100 days a year for cleanliness activities we have to start with cleaning our food plate, by not wasting food.
 
 Parrikar also urged people not to use plastic cups, plates ad bottles. He also urged the people to refrain from unnecessary wastage.
