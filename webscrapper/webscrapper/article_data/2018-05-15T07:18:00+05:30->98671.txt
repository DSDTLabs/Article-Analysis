Noida: As many as four   were reported from Noida and Greater Noida in the past two days, including one from the house of a senior GNIDA official.

 The house of Vibha Chahal, OSD, GNIDA, was ransacked by three thieves and the robbery was captured in the CCTV camera installed in the house.The thieves allegedly fled with jewellery and cutlery worth Rs 7 lakh.
 
 SSP, Gautam Budh Nagar, Ajay Pal Sharma said the thieves are yet to be identified.
 
 Similarly, two thefts were reported in Greater Noida on Sunday. In the first robbery, jewellery worth lakhs was stolen from the house of an NRI in AWHO Society in Kasna. While the house belongs to Dr Neeraj, his sister Shweta had been staying in the house. She has alleged that rings, necklaces, bangles, etc were stolen.
 
 The house of an engineer in Delta 1 was also robbed of cash and jewellery worth Rs 9 lakh when he was on duty. The house of an another engineer in Sector 46 was robbed at 10pm while the family was asleep.
