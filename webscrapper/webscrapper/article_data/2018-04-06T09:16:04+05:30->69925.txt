AHMEDABAD: After scrapping semester system in its 1st year engineering courses,   ( ) has decided to introduce reassessment for the students in diploma courses. The decision may benefit the diploma engineering students in gaining additional marks through reassessment of their marks. 

 The decision was taken in the meeting of academic council held on Wednesday in which it was also decided to scrap the semester system in first year diploma engineering courses.
 
 The GTU has also taken another important decision under which a student cannot move to third semester after one year if he or she fails in some subject.
