MARGAO:   town police on Sunday arrested Mohangiri Mahendra  , 52, a native of Jalgaon,  , after he was caught in possession of ganja at station road in the commercial capital.

 Police said they have recovered 86g of ganja worth Rs 25,000 from the accused.
 
 Police sources said that Sagargiri was caught redhanded when he had come to station road in Margao with the intention to deliver the drug to a customer.
 
 He was booked under various sections of the Narcotics Drugs and Psychotropic Substance (NDPS) Act and the investigation is on.
