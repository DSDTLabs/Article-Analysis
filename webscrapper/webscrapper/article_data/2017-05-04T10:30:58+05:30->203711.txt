GUWAHATI: Film enthusiasts in the city were in for a treat as the Cotton University  , in collaboration with the state cultural affairs department, screened acclaimed contemporary films from the state. 

 The ongoing celebration of Assamese films, Kotha Sobi, facilitated interactions between film buffs and filmmakers. 
 
 "The objective has been to develop a critical appreciation of films and filmmaking. Most importantly, Kotha Sobi is an initiative to spread awareness of films from   among the communities in our educational institutions," said   vice-chancellor Dhruba Jyoti Saikia.
