Gandhinagar: Bidya Devi Bhandari, president of Nepal, will be on a state visit to India from April 17 to April 21, 2017, at the invitation of President Pranab Mukherjee. After arriving at Delhi she will come on a two-day tour to Gujarat, on April 19 and 20. During her visit, she will meet chief minister Vijay Rupani and other top dignitaries of Gujarat, as also the Nepalese community in the state. Among places in the state, she will visit Somnath and Dwarka, and Gandhi Ashram in Ahmedabad.

 This is the first state visit of the president of Nepal after assuming office in October 2015. tnn
