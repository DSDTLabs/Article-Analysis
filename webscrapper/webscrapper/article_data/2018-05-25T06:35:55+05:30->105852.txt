CHANDIGARH: A 46-year-old   was killed in a hit-and-run near poultry farm roundabout, Phase I, Industrial Area, early on Thursday. 

 Sector 31 police station SHO   said, “Passersby found Ganga Ram, a resident of Phase I, Ram Darbar, lying in a pool of blood and called up the police around 1.30am. A PCR van took Ram to GMCH, Sector 32, where doctors declared him brought dead.”
 
 
  
 
 Ram, a factory worker, was on way home when an unidentified vehicle hit him. 
 
 Police are scrutinizing the footage of CCTV cameras installed in nearby areas to identify the vehicle. A case under sections 279 (rash driving) and 304-A (causing death by negligence) of the IPC has been registered.
