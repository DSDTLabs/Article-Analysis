PUNE: The civic body will probe the alleged corruption cases in its security department. 

 A decision was taken after a video clip of an officer went viral. In the video, he blames senior officials of corruption. A department official is already facing an inquiry for the same.
 
 “The civic administration has taken serious note and ordered a departmental inquiry. Some contractors have got repetitive extensions. The tender process was not carried out,”   sources said.
 
 Additional commissioner   said it was a departmental inquiry for the delay in floating of new tenders. “It is in process," she added .
 
 The anti-corruption bureau (ACB) had arrested then chief security officer   for allegedly demanding bribe of Rs10 lakh from a   and accepting an 'instalment’ of Rs 4 lakh in June.
