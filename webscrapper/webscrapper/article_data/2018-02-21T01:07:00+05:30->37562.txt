Coimbatore: A Coimbatore-based youth, who aspires to serve in the Indian Forest Service, has bagged the 43rd rank in the Union Public Service Commission exam, result of which was announced on Monday.

 Thejhas Sekhar, currently working as the assistant director in the ministry of defence in New Delhi had studied in the Stanes Higher Secondary School and then pursued BTech in the Amrita College of Engineering here. He is son of BJP state treasurer SR Sekhar.
 
 UPSC had conducted the IFS exams in December last. Those who cleared the main exams were called for personal interviews in February first week. Out of the 330-odd called for the interviews, 110 emerged successful of which 14 were from Tamil Nadu.
