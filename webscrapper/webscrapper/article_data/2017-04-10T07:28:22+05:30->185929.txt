  Jaipurites woke up to a pleasant morning as a minimum temperature of 22 degrees Celsius was recorded in the city. It was followed by a pleasant afternoon as a maximum temperature of 34.2 degrees Celsius was recorded. 
 
 It may be recalled that in this season so far Jaipur's maximum temperature had reached upto 41 degrees Celsius a week ago. The coolers and air-conditioners which were on even in the last week of March were kept on standby mode for the time being. Jaipur has witnessed a dip of almost 8 degrees Celsius in one week.
