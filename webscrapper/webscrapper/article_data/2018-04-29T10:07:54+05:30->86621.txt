 The first one to perform was Shreyan Bhattacharya. The 12-year-old boy sang songs 'Maai Teri Chunariya' which garnered a huge round of applause from the audience. The second one to step on stage was Javed Ali, whose name the crowd chanted loudly. Addressing Lucknowites as nawabs, Javed sang some of his popular songs like 'Guzaarish',' Toh Yeh Subah Nah' and 'Maula Maula'.

 The major highlight of the evening was the performance by Sukh-E and Shaan. As soon as their names were announced, the crowd cheered out loud and applauded for their favourite singers. Sukhe-E sang songs like Insane, 'All Black' and 'Jaguar', on which the audience danced with abandon, while Shaan sang 'Chand Sifarish', 'Main Hoon Don', 'Jab Se Tere Naina' and 'Om Shanti Om'. Shaan who was overwhelmed to see the response of his fans said, "I am so happy to receive the kind of love I always get when I come to Lucknow. Abhi itna late ho gaya hai and still you people are enjoying the concert." Later, as soon as the concert got over, people ran to their favourite stars to catch a glimpse of them and click selfies with them.
