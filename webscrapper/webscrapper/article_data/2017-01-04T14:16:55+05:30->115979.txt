IDUKKI: Two men were nabbed on Tuesday for the murder of a migrant labour woman. The accused were identified as Viswanath (54), a native of  , and Bolak Partha (22), a native of Odisha. They were workers of estates nearby. 

  According to the police, the accused repeatedly stabbed the woman after raping her and abandoned her in the bushes after confirming her death. The incident occurred on Sunday. The victim's husband found her missing on returning home after work. Later, her nude body was found during a search. Idukki SP A V George led the investigation of the case under the supervision of Ernakulam Range IG S Sreejith.
