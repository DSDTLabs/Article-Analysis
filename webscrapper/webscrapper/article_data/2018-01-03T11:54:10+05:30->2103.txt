NEW DELHI: Consumers are getting better service at low rates with telecom majors engaging in a price war to protect their market share in mobile data services. Each telecom company is trying to beat other by offering the best value in minimum possible tariff. Here's a comparison between the best offers from top three telecom service providers in the country - Reliance Jio, Vodafone and Airtel. 

 
  Since its launch, Jio has been the first to offer low-cost internet plans with unlimited benefit offer. As a new year offer, Reliance Jio has come up with a 100% cashback as it launches another plan - Happy New Year 2018. 
 
 
  One day after the launch of Jio's Happy New Year 2018 cashback plan, Vodafone came up with Rs 198 plan to counter Jio's Rs 199 plan. 
 
 
  The leading telecom provider Airtel also joined the price war by introducing more benefits for consumers in its existing plans.
