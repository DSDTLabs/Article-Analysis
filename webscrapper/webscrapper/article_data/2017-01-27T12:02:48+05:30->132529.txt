The Punjab legislative assembly election will be held on February 04, 2017 to select the 117 members of the Punjab legislative assembly. The results of the election will be announced on 11 March, 2017. The current ruling coalition is Shiromani Akali Dal - Bharatiya Janata Party alliance led by chief minister Parkash Singh Badal. * Shiromani Akali Dal (SAD) * Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) * Indian National Congress (INC) * Aam Adami Party (AAP)
