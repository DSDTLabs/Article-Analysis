CHENNAI: Madras high court on Tuesday granted anticipatory   to former higher education minister and TTV   supporter P Palaniappan, who apprehended arrest in connection with the suicide of a government contractor in Namakkal.

 Allowing a petition moved by Palaniappan, Justice P N Prakash granted bail in case of arrest by the CB-CID police. 
 
 On May 8, contractor   committed suicide at his house in Namakkal. The investigation which was initially handled by the state police was transferred to CB-CID on May 13. It was alleged that a suicide note written by Subramaniam alleged Palaniappan and others of abetting the suicide by disrupting his business.
 
 Based on the letter, the CB-CID police included Palaniappan as a suspect in the case.
