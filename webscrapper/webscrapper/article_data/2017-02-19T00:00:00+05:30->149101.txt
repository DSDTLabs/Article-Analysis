The play   performed in   on Friday had everything going for it except for the poor turnout. Blame it on the Friday movie fever, the 45-minute play was attended by just 20-odd people, but it really appealed to the audience and was a laugh riot. 
Directed by  , the play narrated the antics and adventures in the lives of three friends Vijay, Champak and Ajay. Champak’s relentless pursuit of Chandni forces Ajay and Vijay to help him win her over. But things take an unexpected turn when Vijay’s father makes a surprise appearance at their bachelor pad. How the three friends try to cover up their misadventures makes for an engaging watch.
