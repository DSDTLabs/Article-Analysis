Manju Warrior starrer ' ,' which is based on the life of writer Madhavikutty aka Kamala Surayya will be released soon, and the movie's second look is just out.

 The second look shows Madhavikutty with her husband Madhavdas, the character played by Murali Gopi. The couple, in their aged look, is shown to be taking a leisurely walk. It also features Kamala Das' famous lines from one of her literary works, about a woman's love for a man - 'How would you measure a woman's love for a man? Has anyone invented a machine to measure it?'
 
 'Aami' also has Tovino Thomas and Anoop Menon in pivotal roles.
