NEW DELHI: The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) released the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) 2018 Answer Key, Images of OMR and Responses to the questions marked by the candidates and Test Booklet Code of the candidates on Friday, May 25, 2018. 

 The CBSE had conducted the NEET 2018 on May 6, 2018. The students can check their  , scanned Images of their OMR Sheet and Responses and the Test Booklet Code on the official website of the board - cbseneet.nic.in. To check the CBSE   candidates will have to log in into their account using their user ID (Registration No.) and password.
 
 
 
 
  
  
 
 
 The candidates who wish to challenge the NEET 2018 Answer Key, OMR grading of their OMR Sheet and Test Booklet Code may raise objections by paying processing charges of Rs 1000/- per Answer Key/ per response/Test Booklet Code. The candidates should note that the processing charges are non refundable. 
 
 The candidates can check the NEET Answer Keys and or make objections till May 27, 2018. 
 
 The CBSE Board is likely to release NEET 2018 results by June 5, 2018. 
 
 How to download NEET 2018 Answer Key:
 
 1) Open the NEET 2018 official website - cbseneet.nic.in
 
 2) First, log into your account using user ID (registration no.) and password
 
 3) Once you are logged in, check for the Answer Key link
 
 4) Click on the NEET Answer Key link
 
 5) You can download it or take a printout for future use
