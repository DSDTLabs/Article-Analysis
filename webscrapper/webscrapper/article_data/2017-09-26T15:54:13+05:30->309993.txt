Ram’s upcoming movie ‘Unnadi Okkate Zindagi’ is nearing completion. Currently the film’s shoot is progressing in Milan and Italy. According to sources the film unit is canning some scenes on Ram in Italy. 

  As per latest updates the film is gearing up for its grand release on October 27. Ram will be seen playing the role of a singer in the film. The makers recently released few pics of Ram from the film which received good response from the audience. It is heard that Ram will be seen in an entirely new avatar in the film.
