KANPUR: The office of `Mulayam ke log', launched with much fanfare by the splinter group of Samajwadi Party, has been closed in Etawah after the third phase of polling on February 19.

 The party office in Civil Lines area of district is possessed by chief minister Akhilesh Yadav-led faction,   and   loyalists had opened the office under the banner `Mulayam ke Log' in Chaugurji area of the district and conducted their political activities from there during Assembly elections.
 
 Akhilesh Yadav faction had then claimed that `Mulayam ke log' was formed at the behest of Shivpal Yadav. The emergence of this group was only to damage the prospects of the official SP (Samajwadi Party) candidates.
 
 The outfit's office was inaugurated by Mulayam Singh Yadav's younger brother Shivpal Yadav on January 29.
 
 On Saturday , when TOI does a reality check, the main gate of `Mulayam ke log' office was found closed. The red metallic plate with `Mulayam ke log' inscription on it, the banners and cut-outs of Mulayam Singh Yadav and socialist lea ders Ram Manohar Lohia and Janeshwar Mishra were also missing. Chaugurji locality also has the residence of Shivpal.
 
 The outfit members extensively campaigned for Shivpal Yadav, who was in fray from Jaswantnagar and also for former SP leader and party rebel Ashish Rajput, who tried his luck on a Lok Dal ticket from Etawah Sadar seat.
