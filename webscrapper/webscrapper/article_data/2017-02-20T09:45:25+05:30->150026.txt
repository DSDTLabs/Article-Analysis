VISAKHAPATNAM: Indian   Ship Ayush, the last in the series of 20   (FPVs), designed and indigenously built by  , was commissioned on Saturday. The 50-metre FPV, ICGS Ayush, is equipped with state-of-the-art weaponry, communication and navigational equipment. It is propelled by three advance diesel engines coupled with three Rolls Royce jets to achieve a maximum speed of 33 knots and has an endurance of 1,500 nautical miles at economical speed of 14 knots and will be based at Krishnapatnam. The ship is an ideal platform for undertaking multifarious close-coast missions such as surveillance, search and rescue, interdiction and maritime law enforcement. tnn
