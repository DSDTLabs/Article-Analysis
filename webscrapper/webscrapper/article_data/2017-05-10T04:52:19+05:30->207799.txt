VADODARA: Vadodara district collector P Bharathi has issued a notification declaring the residential area surrounding Nirma Limited’s detergent complex at   in   taluka of Vadodara district as cholera-affected. 

 The district administration has also been asked to take necessary steps to curb the spread of the water-borne disease in eight km area surrounding Lamdapura, which has been declared as cholera-hit. 
 
 The notification issued under The Epidemic Diseases Act, 1897 will remain in force for three months. Savli mamlatdar has been appointed as   control officer to supervise preventive measures in the region. Health officials have been asked to take measures to check spread of the disease and to promptly attend to patients’ needs.
