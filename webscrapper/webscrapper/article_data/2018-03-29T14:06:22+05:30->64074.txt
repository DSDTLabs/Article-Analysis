RAJKOT: Gandhidham local crime branch in Kutch district seized 330 boxes containing 9,888 bottles of Indian-made foreign liquor (IMFL) worth Rs 15.1 lakh from   village of   taluka on Tuesday night.

 According to police, the liquor boxes had come from Rajasthan and belonged to listed bootlegger  , a resident of Moti Cherai. 
 
 The consignment was hidden about the bushes on the outskirts of the village.
 
 Police said that the IMFL was to be distributed to other bootleggers in the nearby villages. Jadeja had managed to escape before the police raided the spot, police said.
