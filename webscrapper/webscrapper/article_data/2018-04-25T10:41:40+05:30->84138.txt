NEW DELHI: The Board of Secondary Education, Odisha (BSEO Board) is likely to announce the   Board Madhyama Results 2018 by April end on its official website - bseodisha.nic.in. 

 
 However, the exact timing could not be confirmed with the officials of BSEO Board. The students who have appeared for the Odisha Class 10 examination can register using the form below to get their result directly on their mobile phone or through email. Register now 
 
 
  
  
 
 The boad had successfully conducted the Secondary Examination (Madhyama) and Higher Secondary Examination (HSE) for the session year 2017-18 in the month of Feb-March. 
 
 
 If you are one of the students eagerly waiting for the announcement of BSEO Board Madhyama and HSE Result 2018, you can check it on the official website of the BSEO Board - bseodisha.nic.in. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OFFICIAL WEBSITES of BSEO Board: You can log on to the official websites of BSEO Board - www.bseodisha.nic.in to check your results.
 
 
 IVR: Since there will be huge traffic on BSEO Board website on result day, you can try IVR system that provides you with the results via a call.
 
 
 SMS: Students can also check their BSEO Board Madhyama result 2018 via SMS.
 
 
 SCHOOL WEBSITE: Apart from above three ways, you can also check your BSEO Board result on the local portal of your school.
