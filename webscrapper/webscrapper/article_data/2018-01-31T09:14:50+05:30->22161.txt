JAISALMER: If one decides to do something, it is not difficult to achieve. A   deployed in Jaisalmer for last 20 years has achieved his target and set an example. Prior to entering police service,   wanted to become a doctor. But could not study MBBS because of family conditions. Being in police service he fulfilled his dream of adding doctor before his name by completing his PhD.

 He continued his studies at AI IGNOU and did PhD in public administration. He did research on work satisfaction of low grade employees in police service under the guidance of IGNOU professor Alka Dhameja and former HOD of public administration department RU, P S Bhatnagar. He is the first official of district police to do a PhD.
