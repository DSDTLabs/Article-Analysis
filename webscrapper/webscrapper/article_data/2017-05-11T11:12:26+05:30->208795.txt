NEW DELHI: Alert CISF personnel at two   stations on Tuesday helped reunite three children who got lost while boarding and changing metros. In both cases, the parents had gone inside the metro with the children left behind as the door closed.The first incident took place at   station at 9:30 am. 

 CISF personnel noticed a girl crying at the platform and on inquiry, it was found that she was lost and could not find her mother. The girl was found to be mute and was taken to the station control room where she wrote down her contact number. In another case, two children, identified as   (5) and   (11), were spotted by CISF personnel at the   station.
