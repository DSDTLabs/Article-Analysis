BHOPAL: Two coaches of   22416 AC SF Express train caught fire near Birla Nagar railway station in   district of Madhya Pradesh on Monday afternoon.

 
 The train was stopped 2 km away from the Gwalior railway station where rescue operations were underway. No injuries were reported.
 
  
  
 
 Fire was reported from the B6 and B7 coaches. All passengers have been evaquated safely.
