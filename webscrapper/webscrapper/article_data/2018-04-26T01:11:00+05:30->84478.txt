Salem: More than  ,000 banana plants were damaged in heavy rain at Thalaiyur village in Mettur here on Wednesday early morning. Farmers have demanded the district administration to provide them Rs 1 lakh per acre as compensation.

 “Banana plants cultivated at Thalaiyur and PN Patty town panchayats were damaged in the whirl wind and heavy rain,” said P Kala, who lost nearly 800 banana plants.
 
 According to the farmers, the banana plants were ready to be harvested in a week to four weeks.
 
 Meanwhile, revenue officials visited the villages to assess the damages caused to the banana farmers. The officials said they would submit the report to the district administration for further proceedings.
