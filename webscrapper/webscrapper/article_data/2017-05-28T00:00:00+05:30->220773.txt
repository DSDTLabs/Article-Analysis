 The movie’s director   tells us, “After a long hunt, we have zeroed in on Anu to play a village belle in the film, which is a satire.”
 
 The film, scripted by newcomer Sharath Balan, is set in a rural milieu. “In terms of casting, we have roped in actors such as Innocent, Vijayaraghavan, Mammukoya, Suraj Venjaramoodu and Kanaran Harish to play naadan characters,” he says.
 
 Aana Alaralodalaral also has Anandam fame   playing a pivotal role along with Soubin Shahir. The team will begin shooting the movie in Kollankodu by mid August. National Award-winning cinematographer Deepu S Nair will crank the camera while Shaan Rahman will compose the music.
