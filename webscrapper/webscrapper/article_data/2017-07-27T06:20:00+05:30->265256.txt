Nagpur: Energy minister Chandrashekar Bawankule has been appointed as the joint guardian minister of Bhandara district.

 The state general administration department coming under chief minister Devendra Fadnavis issued a notification on Wednesday appointing Bawankule as joint guardian minister of the adjoining district. Kamptee MLA Bawankule is the guardian minister of the district since formation of BJP-Sena government in the state.
 
 Health minister Deepak Sawant is the guardian minister of Bhandara district and paid very minimal visits in last two and a half years. It is said to be one of the reasons behind increasing the responsibilities of Bawankule.
 
 It is third such decision taken by the state government. Similar pattern of joint guardian minister is existing in Osmanabad and Satara districts in the state.
