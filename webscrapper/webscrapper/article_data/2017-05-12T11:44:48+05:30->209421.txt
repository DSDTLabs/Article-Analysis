CHANDIGARH: With the view to providing   under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojna (PMAY) to people residing in urban areas, the   government will soon conduct a survey. Those who were residents of the state as on June 17, 2015, would be eligible to participate in the survey.

 The decision was taken at a meeting of urban local bodies department held under the chairmanship of chief minister   on Thursday. It was also attended by urban local bodies minister  , principal secretary to chief minister   and principal secretary of urban local bodies department  . 
 
 It was decided that those belonging to economically weaker sections, low and medium income groups and living in urban areas would be eligible for credit linked subsidy and affordable houses. To avail of the benefit of the scheme, the government has ruled that the applicant should have an Aadhar card and a bank account. He or his family members should not own any pucca house in any other locality. The applicant should also not have taken the benefit of any other scheme under the centre or state government.
