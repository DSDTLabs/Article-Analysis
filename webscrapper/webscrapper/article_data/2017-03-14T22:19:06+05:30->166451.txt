Actor   decided to celebrate Holi a little different this year and to achieve his goal, he travelled to the holy Trimbakeshwar Shiva Temple.
 
  
 
 
  
  Ssharad is a strong believer of lord Shiva and decided to visit his maker. He says,"On the auspicious occasion of Holi I went to the Trimbakeshwar Shiva Temple for the first time ever and it was a beautiful experience."
  
  "The vibes, the energy the place exhibits is truly remarkable...Being a strong believer of Lord Shiva I truly cherished every moment of the journey and the visit to the shrine, being one of the 12 jyotilingas in the world. Very powerful and magnetic indeed and a must visit for all," he added.
