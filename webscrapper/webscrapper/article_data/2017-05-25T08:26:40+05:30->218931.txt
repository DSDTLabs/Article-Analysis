PUDUCHERRY: Chief Minister   will present the budget for the fiscal 2017-2018 on May 25. 

 The session began on May 16 with the customary address by the Lt Governor   and after holding meetings for two days the House was adjourned to meet tomorrow. 
 
 The House had passed Vote on Account Bill on March 30 this year during brief session earmarking Rs 1,481 crores for the first three months (April to June) of the fiscal 2017-2018.
