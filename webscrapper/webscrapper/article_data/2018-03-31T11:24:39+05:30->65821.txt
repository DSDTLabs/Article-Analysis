 Speaking to reporters, at the Madurai airport, after presiding over a mass wedding, which was conducted for 120 couples in Madurai on Friday, he said that it was important that feelings of the delta farmers and people be reflected through the protests which will be held between 9 am to 5 pm on Monday as announced by the deputy chief minister  . On being asked if the state government will file a contempt petition against the Central government, he said that the possibilities of doing so would be explored after consulting legal experts.
 
 He said that it should be noted that the AIADMK members of the Parliament have been demanding the implementation of the board ever since the parliament session started and that there had even been instances when the proceedings of the house had to be stalled due to the uproar by the MPs.
