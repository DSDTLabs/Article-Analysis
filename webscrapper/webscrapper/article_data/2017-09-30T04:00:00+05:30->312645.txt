Rajkot: A temple priest in Madhada village of Bhavnagar district committed suicide on Thursday night. His body was found on Friday morning. 

 Police identified the deceased as Bhimji Savani (70), who served as a priest at Meladi Mata temple in Madhada. Savani hanged himself to death from the ceiling of his room at the temple. 
 
 “Preliminary investigation revealed that Savani had been leading a lonely life. As no suicide note has been found, we are yet to ascertain the reasons for his death,” an official from Sihor police station, where the case in this regard has been registered, said.tnn
