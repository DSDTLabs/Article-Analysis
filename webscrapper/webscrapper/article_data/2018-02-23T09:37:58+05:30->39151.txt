 Bachchan himself is among the most followed Indian public figures on   with 33.1 million followers. Tewari on Tuesday thanked him on Twitter for following him, recalling that he had grown up watching his movies “first day first show every new release”. Once a close friend of former Prime Minister Rajiv Gandhi, Bachchan had contested and won on a Congress ticket from Allahabad in 1984. However, relations with the party soured after the Bofors scam was uncovered. He is at present the brand ambassador of the NDA government’s Swachh Bharat campaign, and is also the face of Gujarat Tourism.
 
 On Twitter, the list of accounts one follows is organised in a chronological fashion, with the most recent follow right on top, with the later ones below. Judging by the sequence of the 1730 accounts Bachchan started following international sport figures right after he followed the five Congress leaders.
 
 He had been following other Congress members including Sachin Pilot, Jyotiraditya Scindia, Rahul Gandhi and Digvijay Singh. Names like Prime Minister Narendra Modi, Ravi Shankar Prasad, Sushma Swaraj, Amit Shah, Manohar Parrikar, Arun Jaitley and several others from the BJP appear further below suggesting he followed those accounts first. Outside of Congress and BJP, the 75-year-old actor also follows politicians from TRS and and CPM.
