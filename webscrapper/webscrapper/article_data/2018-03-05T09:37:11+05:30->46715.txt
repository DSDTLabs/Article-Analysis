NASHIK:Nandurbar district in Nashik division sizzled on Sunday with the   touching 43 degrees celsius, the highest in the country, according to   agency Skymet.

 
 With Jalgaon at 38.8 degrees celsius, Malegaon at 37.4 degrees celsius and Nashik at 34.8 degrees celsius, the entire north Maharashtra region has been reeling under hot weather conditions since the beginning of March.
 
  Such high temperature is unusual at this time of the year. Three   are among the top ten hottest places in India, says a   by Skymet.
