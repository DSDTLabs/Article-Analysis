PANAJI: With few days left of the campaigning period for the upcoming  , the   has roped in its party leaders from Maharashtra to galvanize supporters. 

 Aditya Thackeray arrived in Goa on Sunday and conducted a road show and corner meeting in Cuncolim. He later travelled to Vasco. The Thackeray scion will be conducting a road show at Mormugao on Monday, followed by a meeting with party workers in Saligao and later a road show there.
 
 Party leader Uddhav Thackeray will be landing in Goa on January 31. He will hold a public meeting at the Shantadurga temple in Verem. On February 1, he will meet with alliance partners and depart after holding a public meeting in Cuncolim.
