KOTA: A 35-year-old wage   committed suicide late on Friday night by hanging himself from the ceiling fan at his home in   area under   police station. The body was handed over to the family members after post-mortem on Saturday. A case under Section 174 of CrPC was lodged.

 The deceased was identified as Amarlal Banjara.
 
 Around 10pm on Friday, Amarlal hanged himself at his home in Kheda Jugpura area, said an officer of Anantpura police station.
 
 By the time the family members broke open the door of his room and brought him down, he had died, he said.
 
 Reason behind the suicide is yet to be ascertained as no suicide note was recovered. The family members also did not cite any reason for the extreme step.
 
 The deceased belonged to Baran district but for past few years he had been living with his family in Kheda Jugpura area of the city, the officer added.
