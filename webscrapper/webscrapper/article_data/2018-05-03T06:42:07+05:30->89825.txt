NAVI MUMBAI: The Nerul police arrested two   men with 768gm of methaqualone drug, also known as MD, whose market value is over Rs 23 lakh, near   station on Wednesday. The duo had concealed the powder in aluminium foil and packed it in two plastic zipper pouches. 

 Senior inspector Ashok Rajput said, "One of the accused is 17 years old and the other is Nadim Salmani (20). They claimed that they had brought the drugs from Uttar Pradesh to CSMT by train. They then boarded a   train to reach Jui Nagar station where they were to deliver the contraband to a man and receive Rs 50,000 each. They claimed that they did not know the person's identity." -George Mendonca
