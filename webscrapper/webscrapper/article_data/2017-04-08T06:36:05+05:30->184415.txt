BENGALURU: Unidentified miscreants broke into an office in Indiranagar, east Bengaluru,   and escaped with  .

 Complainant  , an event management professional, told police that the burglars gained entry by breaking open the window pane.
 
 “The complainant's office is on the second floor of a three-storey building and the burglars gained entry after removing the iron grills of the window. 
 
 The culprits broke open the cupboard and decamped with the valuables, including Australian and American dollars,“ Indiranagar police said.
 
 However, the complainant has not mentioned the exact amount that was stolen, police added. “She told us that the currencies were collected during a recent visit to the two countries and that she hadn't kept an account of how much she had,“ said police.
