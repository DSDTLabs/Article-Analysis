SURAT: A 35-year –old under trial prisoner lodged in   sub-jail died under mysterious circumstances on Thursday evening. The deceased was identified as Alpesh Dodiya Patel. 

 Patel was arrested for being in possession of liquor by   police. He was sent to jail by court on April 10. 
 
 Deputy jail superintendent LV Vasava said, “He became ill on Wednesday evening and was admitted to the jail hospital. His health deteriorated further on Thursday and was administered saline and referred to   ( ), where he was declared dead on arrival.”
 
 However , some people who witnessed Patel being brought to NCH said that there were injury marks visible on of his stomach, left hand and ankle. Patel’s body was sent for postmortem to know the cause of death.
