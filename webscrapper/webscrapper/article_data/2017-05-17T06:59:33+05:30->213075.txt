LUDHIANA: An assistant sub-inspector has been booked for sexual harassment of a woman constable. ASI   is booked for allegedly sending   on WhatsApp. The woman has complained that he also hugged her while he was under the influence of alcohol.

 In her complaint, the constable said the accused would praise her beauty and drop in at her hostel on the pretext of checking on her. When she refused to respond to his overtures and took offence that he hugged her, he threatened to get her dismissed from service.
 
 After the woman made her complaint, an assistant commissioner of police (ACP)-level officer probed the matter. 
 
 It was on the recommendations of the probe officer that a case has now been registered against the accused assistant sub-inspector and action will be taken soon. 
 
 The accused has been booked under Sections 354 A (sexual harassment), 354 D (stalking), 506 (criminal intimidation), 509 (word, gesture or act intended to insult the modesty of a woman) and 510 (misconduct in public by a drunken person) of IPC. 
 
 Station House Officer (SHO) Division Number Eight police station   said that the accused will be served notice before being arrested. If convicted, the   could face upimprisonment upto seven years in jail. 
 
 Police sources confirmed that there are witnesses in the matter and the evidence is solid.
