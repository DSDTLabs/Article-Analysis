RAJKOT: The   ( ) will organize a garden exhibition   from February 18 to February 21 at Race Course Ground. 

 The show will be inaugurated on February 17 evening at the art gallery in Race Course. RMC has invited citizen, institutions, civic bodies, nurserymen, organic food sellers, professional gardeners and forest produce sellers to exhibit and sell their products at the show. 
 
 RMC will also organize competitions to create awareness about gardening in nearby areas. Professionals and traders selling cactus, Bonsai, garden tools, pots, fertilizers, garden furniture among others have also been invited.
