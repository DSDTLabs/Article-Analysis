BENGALURU: A 32-year-old woman filed a complaint with   police in   district, accusing her husband of molesting their 17-year old daughter, a II PU student. She feared he would kill the daughter for a ‘treasure’.

 
 Police registered a case under the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act and summoned the father, a priest, for questioning.
 
 The couple, married in 2000, has three daughters. The husband and wife suspected each other of infidelity, leading to frequent fights, family members told police. The husband is a priest at the temple set up by his ancestors.
 
 “My husband tells me there is hidden treasure inside the temple and human sacrifice is needed to unearth it. I suspect he plans to kill my elder daughter for the treasure,” the wife said in the statement.
