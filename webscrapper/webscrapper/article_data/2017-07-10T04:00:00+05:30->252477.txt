Vadodara: A body of staffers of grant-in-aid schools have decided to stage protests across all the districts of the state to press for their long pending demand including the benefits of seventh pay commission on Monday. 

 The Gujarat State Shaikshanik Sangh Sankalan Samiti has decided that all its office-bearers will first submit memorandum to district collectors in all the districts of the state on Monday. Later, the body will stage silent ‘dharna’ protests outside the offices of district education office across all districts of the state. The body has school managements, principals, teachers, non-teaching staff and administrators as members. TNN
