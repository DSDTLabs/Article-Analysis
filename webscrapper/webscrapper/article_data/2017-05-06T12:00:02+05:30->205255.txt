NOIDA:  , a body comprising 23 to 30-year-old youth of Noida, have undertaken a drive to collect old/used/read books from residents and distribute them among the underprivileged.
 
 

 Books so donated from residents across the city, are being handed over to public libraries, government schools and schools run by   as per their requirement. The drive, which began on April 20, has already collected about 400 books.
 
 
 
  
  
 The group has set up three collection centres in the city - two in sector 12 and one in sector 29 for collecting the books.
