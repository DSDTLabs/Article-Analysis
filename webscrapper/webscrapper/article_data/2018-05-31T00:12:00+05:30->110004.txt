Kota: A 35-year-old labourer died on early Wednesday morning while working in a cement electric pole manufacturing factory in Gobariya Bawadi area under Anantpura police station of Kota city after a portion of crane, he was working under, fell over him. The family members raised protest against the private factory owner alleging poor machine maintenance and inadequate safety measures at workplace and demanded a compensation of Rs 10 lakh.

 The deceased was identified as Rajaram Bairagi, a resident of Gendoli police station area of Bundi district. He was killed on the spot The deceased labourer along with other workers was engaged in shifting cemented electric poles from one place to other by lifting them in a crane machine when a portion of the crane machine fell over his head killing him on the spot.
