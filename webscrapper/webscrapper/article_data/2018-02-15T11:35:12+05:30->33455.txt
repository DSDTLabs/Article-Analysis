LUDHIANA: A fraudster has stolen a bag containing Rs 2.50 lakh in cash from a car, after befooling the occupant of the vehicle on Wednesday near   in  . 

 In his complaint to the police, a trader from BRS Nagar, Prince Batra, said he had gone to a market near Deepak Cinema in his car, where a man waved at him to stop the car, and said oil was leaking from his car. As soon as he came out of the car to check it, the fraudster fled with the cash bag kept in his car. 
 
 Inspector  , SHO at Salem Tabri police station said the police had lodged a complaint and initiated investigation. The police have been scanning CCTVs to identify the accused.
