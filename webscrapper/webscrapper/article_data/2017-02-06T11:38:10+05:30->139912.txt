RANCHI: Investors from the   have come forward to express interests in setting up units in Jharkhand. 

 An investment promotion road show conducted by the state industries, mines and geology department in Kolkata on Saturday, has brought investment proposals in the foundry and forge sector from three companies. 
 
 A delegation of Jharkhand government, consisting officials from the industries department, took part in the Indian Foundry Congress's ( )   convention in Kolkata. Pune based RSB Group, Parker Foundry Limited and one more company showed interest in setting up their units in Jharkhand, members of the delegation team said. 
 
 "Jharkhand was a partner state in the event that was jointly organized by the directorate of heavy industries of the Union government. ," an official of the Ernst and Young, the project management consultant of state industries department said.
