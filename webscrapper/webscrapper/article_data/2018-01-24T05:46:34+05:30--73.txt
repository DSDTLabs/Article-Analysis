MUMBAI: A special Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (Pocso) Act court on Tuesday convicted and sentenced a man to two years' imprisonment for winking and flashing a 12-year-old girl in 2015. 

 The accused was found guilty under Indian Penal Code Section 354(A) and   Section 12 for for sexual harassment. Additionally, the   fined the accused Rs 10,000. 
 
 The incident took place when the girl was studying outside her room in the building's common corridor. She told the court that the accused first winked at her. Scared, she held her book up to shield herself from his gaze, but he then flashed her, she said. The girl then ran inside her house and complained to her mother, but by then the accused had fled the scene. 
 
 The mother-daughter duo looked for him on the premises. When the girl spotted him, she pointed him out to her mother. 
 
  The mother then filed a police complaint against the man and he was arrested.
