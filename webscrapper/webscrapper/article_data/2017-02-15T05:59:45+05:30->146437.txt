CHENNAI: Police on Tuesday seized a drone from four employees of a private hospital on   for flying it near  . 

 Police said the four people, including a woman, had undertaken a project on   called 'Get Active' and were using the drone to observe slum areas. After studying the area, they were planning to educate the women in the slums. A few people in the slums near Chinthadripet alerted the police, who reached the spot and seized the drone.
 
 "They did not take any permission to fly the drone. We have seized the gadget and are investigating," said Perumal, deputy commissioner, Triplicane. Last year, police announced that it is mandatory to obtain permission from the directorate general of civil aviation (DGCA) and the Chennai police for flying drones in the city.
