Bicholim: Bicholim police recovered the body of an unidentified woman, which was found along the banks of the River Vathadev at Naiginim in Bicholim on Monday. According to police, the incident occurred late Sunday evening. Bicholim police were intimated about the incident by the police control room. On receiving the information, they rushed to the spot and recovered the body.  The deceased woman is said to be in the age group of 50-60 years. A case of unnatural death has been registered.  Parab, who is investigating the case, said that the woman may have slipped into the water and drowned. The body has been preserved at the morgue of the Goa Medical College and hospital, Bambolim. PSI Gaurish Parab is investigating the case.
