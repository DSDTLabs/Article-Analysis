MUMBAI: On Day two of the computer-based   (Main) test on Sunday, some aspirants found the mathematics section lengthier but of similar difficulty level as compared to the offline test earlier this week.

 The online test will be taken by 1.83 lakh candidates who registered for it over two days at 330 centres in 113 cities. “A few questions in the Math section were tricky but the rest were similar to the offline  ,” said Habil Bhagat, a candidate.
 
 Some students found the mathematics section to be lengthy. Candidates said that the physics and chemistry sections had questions of moderate difficulty.
