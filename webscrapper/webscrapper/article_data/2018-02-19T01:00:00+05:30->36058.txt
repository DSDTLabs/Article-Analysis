Upcoming comedy ' ', directed by Chakri Toleti, has already garnered a lot of appreciation for its trailer and songs. And now, raising the anticipation further is the buzz that the film will feature the three Khan brothers — Salman, Arbaaz and Sohail — together.

 Interestingly, the trio, along with producer Vashu Bhagnani, will be collaborating 20 years after their last outing, 'Pyaar Kiya To Darna Kya'.
 
 Wiz Films’ Andre Timmins says, “I really wanted to get the three Khan brothers together. Salman, Arbaaz and Sohail are very different as people, but together, they create magic.”
 The film, with music by Pooja Music, revolves around two youngsters from India — Diljit Dosanjh and Sonakshi Sinha — who are in search of a better life for themselves. An unexpected trip to New York City sets them off on a comic adventure that changes their lives forever.
 
 Welcome To New York, produced by Pooja Films and Wiz Films, releases worldwide on February 23.
