RAJKOT: The rapid response cell (RR cell) of   police arrested 34 people for extracting   illegally in   village of   taluka in Surendranagar district on Saturday. 

 “We had received a tip-off about the illegal mining of coal in Khakharadi village, following which we raided the place. We arrested 34 accused from the spot and seized two dumpers, tractor loaders and nine cranes, all of which have a total worth of around Rs 88.42 lakh,” said an official from RR cell. 
 
 However 16 others, who too were involved in the crime, fled before the raid. The RR cell lodged a case against them with Thangadh police station.
