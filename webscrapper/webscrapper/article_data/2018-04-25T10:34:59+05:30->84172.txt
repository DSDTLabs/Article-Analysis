TRICHY: The   of Karaikudi-Chennai Pallavan Superfast Express   derailed as it was nearing Trichy Railway Junction around 6.25am on Wednesday. No one was injured in this incident, officials said.

 A team of technical team rushed to the spot and rerailed the engine around 9.30am. The train moved to the railway junction at 9.45am. It departed from the station at 10.15am, officials said. 
 
 Due to this incident, Madurai-Chennai Vaigai Express and Intercity Express trains and Trichy-Karaikudi, Trichy-Dindigul and Trichy- Rameswaram passenger trains were delayed.
