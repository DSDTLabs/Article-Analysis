NEW DELHI: A 37-year-old woman allegedly committed suicide by hanging from the ceiling fan in front of her three daughters. The incident took place in  's   on Saturday, a few days after a 27-year-old man committed suicide in the same house. 

 Police said that the matter was reported when someone from the locality heard the daughters' cries for help and went to check what was wrong. The neighbour noticed the body hanging from the fan and immediately alerted everyone, after which a call was made to the cops. The woman, Rubi, lived there with her husband and daughters, police said. Her husband was not home when the incident happened.
 
 Cops said no suicide note was found from her possession and they are trying to ascertain why she took this step.
