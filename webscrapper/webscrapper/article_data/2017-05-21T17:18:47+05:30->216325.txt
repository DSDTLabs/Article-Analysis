AMETHI: Congress leaders here on Sunday paid tributes to Bharat Ratna awardee and former Prime Minister   on his 26th death anniversary at the party office in Gauriganj.

 The programme was attended by Chandra Kant Dubey, the representative of Congress vice president and   MP Rahul Gandhi along with MLC Dipak Singh, the party's district unit chief Yogendra Mishra among others.
 
 Congress activists highlighted Gandhi's contribution in transforming the country into modern India by introducing the use of computers.
 
 Some party leaders also donated blood at the Sanjay Gandhi Hospital at Munshiganj to commemorate the occasion. Gandhi has also represented the   constituency in Parliament from 1980 to 1991.
 
 Rajiv Gandhi was assassinated on this day in 1991 in a suicide bomb attack by   (LTTE) during an election rally at Sriperumbudur in Tamil Nadu.
