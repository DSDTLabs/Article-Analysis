Erode: As many as 117 inter-caste and inter-religious couples on Monday tied knot in the DMK zonal meeting near Perundurai in Erode district in the presence of the party’s working president, M K Stalin. 

 Addressing the gathering, Stalin wished the couples to have long life together and advised them not to fight with each other.
 
 He further said DMK president M Karunanidhi had watched the two-day meeting through TV channels. “He (Karunanidhi) was overwhelmed after watching the meeting. It gave him peace,” he added
 
 Earlier, Stalin handed over four-gram gold ‘mangalsutra’ to each couple. After the speech, he also gave household items like beds, pillows and Rs 15,000 to each couple.
