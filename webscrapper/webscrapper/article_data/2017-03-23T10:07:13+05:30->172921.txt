THIRUVANANTHAPURAM: A retired cop was arrested on Wednesday for   an eight-year-old boy. The police identified the accused as   (62) of   near Thirumala here. He was attached to the armed reserve police battalion and retired as a sub-inspector from the   camp. 

 According to the police, the accused sexually abused the boy last Sunday. Krishna Kumar runs an electronics shop at Arayalloor and the boy is the neighbouring shop owner's son. "The boy used to visit his father's shop regularly and was well acquainted with the accused,'' said Poojapura sub-inspector  .
 
 According to police, when the boy visited his father's shop,on Sunday, the accused took him to his shop and subjected him to unnatural sex. The boy told the matter to his mother the same night itself. However, the parents initially chose not to report it to the police, and decided to confront the accused by themselves. But their attempt failed as the accused kept avoiding them. Later they reported it to the police.
 
  The police recorded the boy's statement after visiting him at his residence in civilian dress. The accused was booked under Section 377 of IPC and Sections 3(d) and 4 of Pocso (Protection of Children from Sexual Offences) Act. He was produced before the court and was remanded in judicial custody.
