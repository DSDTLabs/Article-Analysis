 The boy, who is from   near Eluru is   district, severed his hand after he accidentally put it inside a motor. The shocked parents first tried to get the boy treated in Eluru, failing which he was rushed to Life Line Trimurti hospital in  , where the surgery was done on January 6.
 
 Doctors led by doctor Suresh Yerranki worked for more than 12 hours to reconnect the carpel bones (wrist bones) and elbow bones along with the blood vessels. The boy has fully recovered 20 days after the surgery. Dr Trimurthy Rao, doctor of surgery, said that it was a rare microsurgery and the success has given the doctors confidence.
