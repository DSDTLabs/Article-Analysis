In the latest episode of Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai, Naira finds Kartik buried in the snow. She is shocked and helps him come out. They both hug each other. Kartik asks him if anybody else was there. Guruji threatens Manish and others and tells them to follow his orders else there would be consequences. Kartik asks Naira how did she find him. Guruji also has another plan in mind to smuggle the kids which involves Naitik and Manish. Dadi is distraught. Kartik and Naira decide to follow the van which the goon is driving with the kids in it. The other family members also go after them.
