KALYAN: A man who suspected his wife of having an illicit relationship allegedly set her on fire on Sunday. The incident took place at their Kalyan resident when accused Pappu Rathod (26) had an altercation with his wife Rangubai (24) over her alleged extra marital   and in a fit of rage, Pappu set Rangubai on fire.

 She suffered 75% burns and is critical. The accused too suffered 60% burns and is in serious condition.
