 (Krishna district): As many as six women labourers suffered serious injuries after a tractor ferrying them fell sideways at   village of Vatsavai mandal in Krishna district on Tuesday.

 According to Vatsavai police, the incident happened at around 8.30 am. Cops said the tractor was carrying around 30 women   from   village to Makkapet for work. All the women work at a chilli farm. 
 
 Cops said as soon as the tractor reached inside the field, the gravel beneath the wheels caused the tractor to fall side-ways.
