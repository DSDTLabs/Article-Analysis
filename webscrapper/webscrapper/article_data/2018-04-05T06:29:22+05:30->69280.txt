Two   men riding a bike were mowed down by a paramilitary vehicle whose driver had lost control after coming under attack from a stone-pelting mob in Hiller area of south Kashmir's   district on Wednesday, reports Saleem Pandit.

 
 Two other jawans, including the driver, suffered injuries. SSP Altaf Khan confirmed that the vehicle of CRPF 164 Battalion hit the bike after coming under stone-pelting at Kokernag. The bike skidded off the road, killing the two jawans, identified as Riyaz Ahmed Wani of Verinag and Nissar Ahmed Wani of Kokernag.
