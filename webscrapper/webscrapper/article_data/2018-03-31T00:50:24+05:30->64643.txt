New Delhi: Two   aged between 10 and 12 were killed while playing between two trucks. One of the trucks suddenly reversed and the children got   in north Delhi’s Sarai Rohilla on Friday. Two helpers, who worked on each  , have been arrested, police said. The drivers managed to escape, police said.

 Jatin Narwal, DCP (north) said that the accused, Gautam and Dhananjay Prajapati, both are residents of Zakhira. 
 
 The two children, Iftikar Alam (10) and Mohd Aftab (12) were playing when the incident happened. “The two trucks were parked on the Zakhira flyover and the children were playing in the space between the two,” said the DCP. 
 
 Next, the driver of the truck parked ahead reversed the vehicle and collided with the one parked behind crushing both the children.
 
 A PCR call was made and a team reached the spot and rushed the children to a private hospital in Moti Nagar where they were declared brought dead. Meanwhile, the driver of the offending vehicle managed to flee. “We’ve registered a case under relevant sections of the Indian Penal Code and are investigating the matter,” said police.
