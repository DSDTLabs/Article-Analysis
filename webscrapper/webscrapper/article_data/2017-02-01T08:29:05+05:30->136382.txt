LUCKNOW: Samajwadi Party national president   on Tuesday said people who do not fear God, practise black magic. "I strongly believe one has to appear before God after death. But those who don't believe in this resort to such practices," said Akhilesh. The SP chief's comment came in response to a question at a news channel event regarding reports that his uncle Shivpal had resorted to black magic during the recent crisis in the party's first family.
