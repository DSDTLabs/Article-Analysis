MOHALI: Police here have arrested two persons with 150 boxes of country-made liquor from the airport road. One of the accused has been arrested just five days before his marriage. A case has been registered against the two under the Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985.

 
 According to the police, the accused have been identified as   and   of   village in Muktsar Sahib.
 
 The police said that they had set up a naka at the airport road, where they stopped a pick-up truck and seized 150 boxes of country-made liquor.
 
 During the investigation, the accused reveled that they bought the vehicle a few days ago and two persons from Muktsar Sahib hired their vehicle on the pretext that they had a liquor store in Muktsar Sahib, and they needed them to deliver 150 boxes of countrymade liquor.
 
 Sukhwinder, the accused, said that he was about to get married in the next few days.
 
 “We have arrested two persons with 150 boxes of country-made liquor from airport road and a case has been registered against the two accused at   police station,” station house officer said.
 
 Meanwhile, a team of Kharar city police station nabbed an accused near bus stand with 15 boxes of illegal liquor.
