GURGAON: A 28-year-old woman, a former receptionist of a sector 46 based   moved the women’s police station on Sunday against the  , accusing him of molesting and threatening to kill her. Police has registered an FIR against the accused hospital director.

 According to police, the woman claimed that for the last few months the director of the hospital was allegedly harassing her and wasn’t paying her salary on time.
 
 “On March 20 when I visited the hospital for my salary, the hospital director molested me and when I told him that I will lodge a complaint against him, he threatened to kill me,” the victim said in her complaint.
 
 As per the complaint, an FIR has been registered against the hospital director under sections 354 (molestation), 509 (act or gesture intending to insult modesty of a woman), 506 (criminal intimidation) of IPC at Women police station.
 
 “An FIR has been registered but we are verifying all the facts. We are investigating the case from all angles and actions will taken as per law,” said inspector Kailash Devi, SHO, Women police station.
 
 When contacted, the accused denied all charges and claimed that all the allegations by the woman were baseless. He said the women was trying to frame him.
 
 “On March 26 I had lodged a complaint against the receptionist and the radiographer of my hospital at Sadar police station. Both them were working in the hospital from 2014. They had duped the hospital of more than Rs 24 lakh, while working for the billing department. They took cash from a client but made entry in the system that the payment was made using a credit card. Later it was found that the credit card entry was fake. I had fired them on March 22. After that they tried to blackmail me and now they are framing me, ” the hospital director told TOI.
