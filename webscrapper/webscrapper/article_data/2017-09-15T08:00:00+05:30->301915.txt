Ludhiana: Sadar Khanna police on Thursday arrested a drug peddler — Tejinder Singh of Rasoolra village — after recovering one kilogram of opium from him at Isru village. Cops said a police team was going towards Isru police post while checking suspicious persons on Thursday. They added that the team stopped Tejinder, coming from outside Isru village, and saw him holding a black colour polythene bag in his hand. They said during checking, one kilogram of opium was recovered from the polythene bag, after which the accused was arrested. Sadar Khanna police have booked the accused under various sections of the Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances (NDPS) Act and started investigation. TNN
