 Police said 12 Bangladeshi nationals, including eight Griha Rakshi Bahini (home guard) personnel, two government employees and two civilians, illegally crossed the border and tried to rob Indian villagers at midnight. When the villagers hit back, the trespassers fled. 
 
 However, a home guard identified as Nausad Mia (62) died on the spot and another was injured in the attack. The injured was later shifted to hospital by police. The incident occurred at 12. Muslim families comprising 80 people have been residing in the village outside the border fencing. 
 
 Police said Bangladeshis entered Indian territory in two auto-rickshaws and fled when the villagers chased them.
 
 Border Security Force personnel and police officers, along with reinforcements, have been camping in the tension-ridden area. 
 
 The body will be handed over to Border Guards of Bangladesh (BGB) on Saturday.
