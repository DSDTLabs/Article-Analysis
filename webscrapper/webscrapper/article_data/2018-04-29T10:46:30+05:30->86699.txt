PONDA: Fearing manipulation of votes for the   municipality polls, MGP-backed   Rising Ponda has demanded that CCTV footage of the strong room be made available.

  
 
 Panel in-charge Ketan Bhatikar also said that officials should reveal the number of unused ballot papers.
 
 Returning officer   said that the footage can be provided if an application is made for the same. He also said that details, including polled votes and unused ballot papers, will be shown to polling agents while the ballot boxes are being sealed. Five polling staff and 3 police personnel will be deployed at each of the 26 polling stations on Sunday, Kundaikar said.
