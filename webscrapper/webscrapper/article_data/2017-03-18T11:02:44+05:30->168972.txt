SURAT: A few members in the   ( ) have opposed the appointment of a realtor as the board member of Sachin notified area authority by the government of Gujarat. 

 Ramaben Ramoliya, former secretary of SICSL, has written to chief minister   urging him to re-consider the appointment of Dinesh M Patel as the board member of Sachin notified area authority. Ramoliya has alleged that Patel does not own a single foot of land at the   and that the earlier management of the SICSL had never recommended Patel’s name as the board member. 
 
 Talking to TOI, Ramoliya said, “Patel is not an industrialist, he is a realtor. Still, due to the blessings of some senior officers in GIDC and the present body of SICSL, his name was referred to the government for the notified area authority. He does not own a single foot of land at  .”
 
 Ramoliya added, “If the government fails to revoke the appointment order, the member of SICSL will sit on indefinite hunger strike.”
 
 When contacted, Dinesh M Patel refuted the charges and stated that his appointment has been made by the government of Gujarat and he is ready to tender his resignation, if the government finds any flaws. 
 
 “I have never approached the government or the authority to take me on the board. I own 1.17 lakh square meter of land in Sachin GIDC, which was allotted to me in 2004. I have always worked in the interest of the industry. There are some vested interests in the SICSL who do not want me on the board,” said Patel.
