Featuring 34 paintings that reflect dual perspectives, the mother-son duo   and   put up a painting exhibition which they uniquely titled ‘Raso Vaisaha’ on Friday. 
Kanth Risa’s series of 13 works were titled ‘Vibrance Buddha.’ In his signature style called ‘Risaism’ he showcased paintings of Buddha in acrylic on canvas. Whereas Janakamma’s 21 art works titled ‘Circled Circles’ have been rendered on a variety of mediums ranging from mixed media to acrylic on paper.
  “My mother was inspired by me, both of us are interested in paintings. We have our own signature styles and that’s what we tried to depict here. In the next 5 years we are planning to exhibit our paintings in different parts of the world,” says Kanth Risa.
