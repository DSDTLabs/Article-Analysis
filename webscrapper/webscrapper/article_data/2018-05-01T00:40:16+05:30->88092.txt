Bathinda: Body of a nursing student was found on railway tracks near Faridkot on Monday.  The Governmemt Railway Police (GRP) are investigating whether deceased Ripandeep Kaur, who was a final year student of BSc (nursing), committed suicide or someone threw her body on the tracks.  A resident of Green Avenue locality of Faridkot, Ripandeep (21) was studying in a private nursing college. Her body was found in the tracks after the Jammu Tawi Express train passed Faridkot at about 9am. tnn
