Pune: A 53-year-old woman ended her life by jumping from the 11th floor of a building at Shivnerinagar in Kondhwa around 2.30pm on Friday.

 Police identified the deceased as Farzana Samad Nathani (53) from Bhagyodayanagar. 
 
 Senior inspector Milind Gaikwad of the Kondhwa police said, “Nathani, a homemaker, visited Camp for some work. She then went to Bramha Avenue, where her brother-in-law stays on the fifth floor. She used an elevator to reach the 11th floor and ended her life by jumping from the passage.” tnn
