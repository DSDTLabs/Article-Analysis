After 'Janatha Garage', NTR Jr took a long break before returning to the sets. While there were many speculations about the actor's 27th outing, his brother, actor and producer Nandamuri Kalyanram announced that Jr's next will be produced under NTR Arts banner and   will helm the project. While there is no official announcement about what the movie is all about, reliable sources suggest that the actor will be seen playing three roles and will romance three heroines. 'Jai Lava Kusa' was also rumoured to be the movie's title. Amidst all this, the movie's unit revealed that the regular shooting will begin from February 15 after a formal launch on February 10 in Hyderabad.
