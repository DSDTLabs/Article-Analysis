THIRUVANANTHAPURAM: The   (NGT) order denying permit to the   (INO) project at Theni, bordering Idukki district, has been welcomed by CPM leader and   chairman V S Achuthanandan. Based on the NGT order, he has directed the government to stop the construction of the INO project. 

 "The disastrous consequences of the INO project at Theni bordering Kerala and   was brought to the notice of the Centre and state government earlier itself. The INO facility is functioning without proper environmental impact assessment studies and by keeping the Kerala government in the dark," Achuthanandan said on Monday.
 
 The NGT order exposes the fact that the INO project was commissioned without conducting the environment impact assessment studies, he said in a statement here.
