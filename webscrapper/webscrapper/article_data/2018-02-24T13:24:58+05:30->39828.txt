MAPUSA: Mapusa police have taken custody of the alleged accused in the   central jail ‘bhang’ consumption case. Police on Saturday had filed an application before the Mapusa court seeking custody of undertrial Anil Bhui, who allegedly provided the spiked drink to the jail inmates.

 Police took Bhui into custody after seeking the permission from the court on Friday.
 
 Four employees of Colvale central jail were placed under suspension in connection with the incident of jail inmates allegedly consuming ‘bhang’. Two inmates had taken ill after consuming the drink as part of Mahashivratri celebrations.
 
 Assistant jailor Shivprasad Lotlikar, head guard   and jail guards   and Caitan Godinho were suspended.
 
 Police sources said that samples of the drink have been sent to the   (FDA). 
 
 The incident would not have come to light had the two inmates not fallen sick. The identity of the person who had sourced the drink is also being probed.
