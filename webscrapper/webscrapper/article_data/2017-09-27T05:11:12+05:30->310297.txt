MUMBAI: The Bombay   on Tuesday slammed the   government for doing little to implement the   Act 2005 for over 11 years after it came into force.

 “It is the duty of the state to implement all provisions of the said   which till today has not been done though the Act has come on the statute books more than 11 years back,” said a bench of Justice   and Justice  . The issue was raised in a PIL related to the drought situation in state last year. 
 
 They directed the state to file an affidavit on “steps taken for implementation of the Act and the state plan and policy”.
