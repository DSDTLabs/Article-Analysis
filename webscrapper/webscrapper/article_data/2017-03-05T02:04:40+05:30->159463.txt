GREATER NOIDA: A 35-year-old man died and two others are missing after the car they were travelling in fell into a   in   on Saturday evening.

 The deceased has been identified as Sonu Kumar. His uncle Dara Singh (50) and a driver, who is yet to be identified, have gone missing after the incident, the police said.
 
 Ram Sen Singh, SHO Jarcha police station, said the police received information from a passerby that a speeding Maruti Suzuki car had fallen into the canal. “We rushed to the spot and called a team of divers. We found the marks of the car’s tyres near the canal. The divers jumped into the canal to trace the victims,” he said.
 
 The divers found Sonu’s body following a half-hour search. He was taken to a private hospital where doctors declared him dead on arrival.
 
 The police informed Sonu’s family members in Tatarpur village, who reached the spot and identified the body. The family members said Sonu and his uncle Dara Singh had gone to Vrindavan two days ago. “They had informed us on the phone they were returning to the village in a rented car,” a family member said.
 
 The police suspect there was a driver in the car who has also gone missing. The eyewitnesses said that the car was speeding and it overturned while negotiating a turn. “The body has been sent for an autopsy. We are trying to trace the car and the other occupant,” the police said.
