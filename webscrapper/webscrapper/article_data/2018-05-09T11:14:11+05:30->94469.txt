Kottayam-based artist  's paintings make you long to go back to that childhood memory of walking beside a pretty pond or revelling in the afternoon showers. 

 His watercolour paintings, titled Living Room, has kicked off in a prominent gallery in and this is the tenth exhibition of the series. The 50-odd works showcase very many emotions of nature, be it in the sun, a moonlit night, rain or broad day light. The event was inaugurated by lyricist Madhu Vasudevan and the other celebs spotted at the venue includes Ananth Channar and Rajan Paul.
