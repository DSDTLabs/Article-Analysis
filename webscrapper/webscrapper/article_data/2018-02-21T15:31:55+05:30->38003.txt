NEW DELHI: The Jawaharlal Nehru University has release the results and cut off list of Jawaharlal Nehru University Entrance Exam (JNUEE) 2017 on the official website of the JNU - jnu.ac.in

 The JNUEE 2017 results is uploaded on the website of the University - jnu.ac.in. The candidates who had appeared for the JNU Entrance examination 2017 are advised to visit the official website of JNU to check their results or they can also click on the link provided below:
 
 https://jnu.ac.in/content/jnu-entrance-examination-results-2017-18
 
 The University had conducted the JNUEE 2017 entrance exam on December 27, 28, 29 and 30, 2017, in 53 cities across India, for the admission year 2018-19. 
 
 After the JNUEE written test results are declared, the qualified candidates will be called for the interview. After the interview, the merit list of the selected candidates will be published on the official website of the JNU. 
 
 The score announcement, followed by viva-voce is only applicable for B.A (Hons.) 2nd Year, M.Sc in SCMM & SCIS, M.A (Foreign Language), M.Phil / Ph.D., PGDE, M.Tech, MPH, COP, FOP, and ADOP (Mass Media) courses. 
 
 Whereas the result for B.A (1st Year), M.C.A, M.A (Except Foreign Language), M.Sc. (Except M.Sc, SCMM & SCIS courses will only be announced as merit list. 
 
 Along with the merit list, the university will also announce a wait list. The wait list candidates will be called for admission if seats remain vacant even after the first round of admission.
