Mumbai: Yes Bank, which launched an offer to raise up to $750 million (about Rs 4,900 crore) through placement of shares to institutions, is believed to have closed the offer on Friday morning, after the book was subscribed more than three times. 

 This was the second attempt by the bank to raise funds after its failed fund-raising attempt last September.
