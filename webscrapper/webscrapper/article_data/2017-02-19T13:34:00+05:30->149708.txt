JAIPUR: Government ambulance services were bady affected following an incident of violence against four employees of the call centre of ambulance service on Sunday morning. 

 Those injured, numbering four, are undergoing treatment in Sawai Man Singh hospital. The call centre employees are not taking calls and are protesting.
 
 Ambulance employees leader Virendra Singh said that they wanted immediate arrest of the accused. Singh said that late Saturday evening, the call centre employees and their team leader had altercation which led to the incident. 
 
 When the night shift of the call centre ended, its employees engaged in an altercation with their seniors and were attacked later.
