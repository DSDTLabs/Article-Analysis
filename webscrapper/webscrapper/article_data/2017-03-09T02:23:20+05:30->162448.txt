THANE: The Indian army is believed to have initiated the process of   against the three   accused of leaking the   question papers to coaching classes. In a related development, five of 21 arrested accused have been remanded in police custody for two days. 

 Last week, the Thane crime branch had arrested Ravi Kumar, Dharamvir Singh and Nigam Kumar Pandey of the Nagpur army recruitment office. 
 
 “Our police team in Nagpur has seized the original SSC and HSC certificates of 350 candidates that had been confiscated by the accused as they could not pay for the papers. The documents will be verified and returned to the respective owners,” said DCP Parag Manere.
