The change of guard was set. The son was going to rise. No one saw it coming, but there was going to be a war. Spectacular spats always ended in patch-ups and paternal bonding. But disagreements, conflicting orders and step-motherly meddling will someday have their comeuppance. When the old guard decides that retirement is too boring, and the new will have none of it, minions – filmi and otherwise – are going to have to choose. Trenches are going to be dug, and the muck is going to fly. Are you ready for the dirty fight?
