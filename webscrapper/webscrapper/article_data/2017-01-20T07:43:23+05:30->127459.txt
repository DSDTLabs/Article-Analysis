HYDERABAD: A man was found murdered near   gate in Malkajgiri on Thursday morning. 

 The police said that the victim D Manoj Kumar had left his house around 6 pm on Wednesday. "According to his wife's statement, he left home with his business partners Harish, Praveen and Immanuel. His body was found outside Sringar Colony gate on Thursday morning," Malkajgiri inspector Janaki Reddy said. 
 
 The victim's wife D Geeta told police that her husband had been facing some financial issues with his business partners and she suspected that they might have killed him. However, police said that they are investigating the case from all angles. A case under section 302 (murder) of the Indian Penal Code (IPC) has been registered at the Malkajgiri police station.
