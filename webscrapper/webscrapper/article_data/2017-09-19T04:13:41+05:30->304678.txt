NOIDA: Industrial development minister Satish Mahana, principal secretary (industries) Alok Sinha, and the CEOs of the three authorities — Noida, Greater Noida and Yamuna Expressway — met an 18-member   delegation in the national capital on Monday.

 During the meeting, a presentation was made to the delegation about the ease of doing business in the state. The delegation, led by the president of the Eighteenth Bank from Nagasaki, Japan, is scouting for land and has shared its intent of investing in Uttar Pradesh, officials told TOI.
 
 Officials of three authorities informed the delegation, which was conducting a preliminary enquiry, about the availability of land in the three areas and facilities available there. The delegation, which comprised of Japanese entrepreneurs, might set   IT and/or automobile industry in the state.
