 Man booked for attempting to rape daughter
 
 In another incident, Patiala police have booked a man for attempting to rape his daughter. According to the police, the accused spiked a cold drink and gave it to his daughter and tried to sexually assault her. However, some of the neighbours entered the house and caught him before he could harm the young girl. Police have recorded the statement of the girl on the basis of which a case under Section 506 (criminal intimidation) of the IPC and sections 10, 12 of the Prevention of Children from Sexual Offences (POSCO) Act has been registered. Police are yet to arrest the accused.
