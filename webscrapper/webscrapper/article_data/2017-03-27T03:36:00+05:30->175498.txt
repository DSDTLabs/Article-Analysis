Margao: The Margao Municipal Council (MMC) has given two months, from April 1 to May 31, to the Margao residents and owners of properties, within the jurisdiction of the municipality, to pay their requisite taxes and licence fees for the financial year 2017-18.

 In a press release signed by both MMC chairperson Babita Prabhudessai and chief officer Y B Tavde, MMC has stated that payments for house tax, trade and occupation licence fees, hoardings/signboard taxes, rent on municipal properties, and non-motorized vehicles or handcart licence fees, etc will be subject to a levy of interest or fines after May 31, as provided under the municipal bylaws. tnn
