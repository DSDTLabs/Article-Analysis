Agartala/Shillong: Keeping options open for Trinamool Congress, Tripura Pradesh   Committee (TPCC) on Saturday announced the list of party candidates for 56 constituencies in the coming assembly elections.

 TPCC spokesperson Harekrishna Bhowmik said though the AICC has closed doors for any sort of alliance with any tribal party, the party can have an electoral understanding with the Trinamool Congress if the the top leaders of both the parties agree.
 
 "The party had sent observers to select candidates and they conducted an extensive survey in all 60 constituencies of the state after which candidates having chances of winning polls were selected," added Bhowmik.
