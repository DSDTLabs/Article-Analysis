RAJKOT: A   was cheated of Rs 90 lakh by four  . 

 Prashant Sidpara, who owns Shreeji Guru Krupa Silver shop in Ranchhodnagar, told police that the four persons took 225kg silver plates worth Rs 90 lakh from him to make jewellery in December last year. However, five months on, they have not given him the jewellery.
 
 Police have booked the four persons — Anil Bhanvar, Mahesh, Raju and Yakub — for cheating.
