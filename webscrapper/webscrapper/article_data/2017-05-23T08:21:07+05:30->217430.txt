 The incident of Swami Vivekananda statue being uprooted came to light only on Monday morning. The 12 feet high statute was made from fibre plastic and was installed on an island in the centre of Ramsagar pond in Godhra.
 
 Sources said that the statute was installed in 2005 - the year was observed as urban development year in the state. A donor had sponsored the statue, but there was no structure to protect it. 
 
 According to officials, the fibre plastic had deteriorated due to temperature changes and wind.
