Surat: An empty three-floor building of Sachin   at   collapsed on Sunday morning. All residents of about 800 flats in this   had been asked to vacate it by the district administration in 2016. There was no casualty as no one was living in the building, police said.

 At least 30 buildings were built here in 1985-86 and all of them are in a bad shape. They will be redeveloped by the board for which tenders have been issued.
 
 An officer of district collectorate said one building had collapsed here last year. People living there had been evacuated well in time.
