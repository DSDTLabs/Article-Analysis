 The bandh was organised in protest against the dilution of the SC/ST (Prevention of Atrocities) Act following a recent   judgment. On March 20, the Supreme Court banned automatic arrests and registration of criminal cases under the SC/ST Act, triggering widespread criticism and outcry from the dalit community.However, dalit organisations and some political parties fear the dilution of the provisions of the Act might lead to increase in violence against Dalits.
 
 Nearly 45 per cent of states population accounts for scheduled tribes and scheduled castes.
