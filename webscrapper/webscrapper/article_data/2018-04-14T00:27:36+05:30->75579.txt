Tirupur: The Palladam judicial magistrate court has issued a non-bailable warrant (NBW) against a police inspector after he failed to appear before it multiple times. S Deivasigamani, who was the inspector of Kamanaickenpalayam near Palladam in 2016, had registered a case against Arun of Valaiyapalayam allegedly for stopping Putharachal village administrative officer Devaraj from discharging his duty. The criminal case booked under Section 353 of IPC was being tried before Palladam JM court. The inspector is currently working at Race Course police station in Coimbatore. TNN
