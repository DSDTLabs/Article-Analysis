 In the Tuticorin incident, the 52-year-old victim was identified as Anthonysamy, 52, a daily wage labourer from Keelanattarkulam. He had come to Seithunganallur on Sunday morning where rekla race was being conducted after two years. Anthonysamy was watching the bulls after the event when he was gored by one of them which was being detached from a cart. 
 
 Police said that after the race got over, the bull owners were in the process of relieving the bulls tied to the carts. Anthonysamy was standing near one of the carts when a bull which was freed from it rushed towards him. Despite the owners trying to take control of the animal by pulling its rope, it gored him fatally. He was rushed to a nearby government hospital where doctors declared him brought dead.
