NEW DELHI: An Indian national has been arrested from the F-8 area of   for not possessing travel papers, a media report said on Sunday.

 According to police, Ahmed was stopped at a checkpost in Islamabad. Police demanded he produce his visa after he told them he was an Indian. 
 
 However, Ahmed could not produce any document and was held, the daily said. Sources in New Delhi said   has not officially communicated anything to India on the arrest and the Indian mission has on its own sought more details from the ministry of foreign affairs.
