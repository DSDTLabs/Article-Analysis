Visakhapatnam: A City Task Force team seized 206 Fortwin sedative injections from two persons at Rajeev Nagar here on Wednesday. On a tip-off, the team under the supervision of I Chitti Babu, ACP, apprehended Ch S Vara Prasad and P Sarat Chandra and seized the injections from them along with Rs 7,000 cash and two mobile phones. During interrogation, the accused allegedly revealed that they brought the injections from Odisha and West Bengal and were selling them to drug addicts in the city. The two have been handed over to the SHO, Duvvada Police Station, for further action. tnn
