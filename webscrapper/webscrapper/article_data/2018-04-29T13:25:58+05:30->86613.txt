PATNA: A police constable,   (45), was arrested on Saturday on charges of killing his wife,   (40), by giving her electric shock at Nakatpur village under   police station area in   district.

 Nalanda SP Sudhir Kumar Podika said police have started an investigation into the incident. “Statements of the family members of the couple are being recorded. The cause behind the murder is yet to be ascertained,” the SP added.
 
 SHO of Biharsharif police station   said the woman’s body was found lying in front of her house at Nakatpur village. “The constable is claiming that it was a case of electrocution. We are probing the case from all angles,” the SHO said, adding the woman’s body was sent to sadar hospital for autopsy.
