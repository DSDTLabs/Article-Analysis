PATNA: The radiotherapy department of All India Institute of Medical Sciences-Patna on Tuesday launched an awareness campaign on   to mark the Breast Cancer Awareness month. 

 HoD Dr Pritanjali Singh said they would conduct free medical check-up, mammography, MRI (magnetic resonance imaging), ultrasonography, FNAC (fine-needle aspiration cytology) and biopsy till October 31. In association with the Society of Oncology Bihar and the R S Memorial Cancer Society, the AIIMS-Patna will also organise a ‘Pink Walk’ on October 14 to spread awareness about breast cancer.
 
 Edu chaupal: Education minister Krishnanandan Prasad Verma on Tuesday announced that an ‘Education Chaupal’ would be organised in West Champaran district from October 5. The chaupal will be organised to get feedback from people on how to improve higher education standards in the state.
 
 “The chaupal will be attended by teachers, students, parents and education officials,” said Verma.
