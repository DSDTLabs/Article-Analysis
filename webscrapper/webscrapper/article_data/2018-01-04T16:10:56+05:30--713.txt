CHENNAI: M S Subbulakshmi’s   and Carnatic vocalist   died in Bengaluru on Tuesday night. She was 84. She had not been keeping well for the past few months.

 Radha was the eldest daughter of T Sadasivam and his first wife, Apithakuchambal (alias Parvathi). After her mother’s death, she was brought up by MS Subbulakshmi whom her father married.
 
 A trained Bharatanatyam dancer and vocalist, Radha accompanied MS in her concerts. She gave up dancing for the sake of accompanying MS.
 
 Radha is survived by two sons and a daughter.
