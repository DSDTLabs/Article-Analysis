 Chandigarh: A 40-year-old driver allegedly shot himself in the head at his employer’s house in Sector 38 West on Tuesday morning.
 
 Kalyan Singh had been working for Hardeep Singh Tiwana, managing director, Baba Resorts, Kasauli, for last more than 15 years. He took Tiwana’s 9mm licensed pistol to end his life, police said. No suicide note was recovered.
 
 A forensic team collected blood samples and fingerprints. Police have sent the samples to Central Forensic Science Laboratory (CFSL).
 
 DSP (south division) Deepak Kumar ruled out any foul play. Police said Kalyan was under depression due to an illness.
 
 Kalyan was living with his family in Mohali but had been staying at his employer’s house for the last few days. He is survived by his wife and three children.
 
 Police handed over the body to family members after the post-mortem at Government Multi-specialty Hospital, Sector 16.
 
 Police have initiated inquest proceedings under section 174 of CrPc (Code of Criminal Procedure).
