NEW DELHI: Two men on a   were going towards the airport on Tuesday morning when they suddenly took a U-turn on seeing   ( ) deployed on the stretch. Finding their movement suspicious, CISF’s quick response team chased them. The two were caught. It was later found that they had stolen a bike from southeast Delhi. 

 At 7.10am, the duo lost the way and started going towards the airport. However, they suddenly took a U-turn and entered the wrong side of the road. “The CISF men informed the control room that alerted the quick response team,” said Manjit Singh, deputy commandant and PRO, CISF. The intelligence bureau was also informed and the two were handed over to the police. A case has been registered.
