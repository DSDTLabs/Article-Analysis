Jaipur: A 25-second video showing empty liquor bottles in the gallery near two hostels of Jagadguru Ramanandacharya Rajasthan   University campus went viral on Friday. The video only shows the empty bottles lying in the gallery. The matter gained momentum after the video went viral and started receiving sharp criticism from the viewers. The university sources are questioning the students living in the hostels. Taking the matter seriously, the university administration has issued a statement saying, “This is a sheer attempt to malign the image of the university. It is part of a controversy and university condemn it in the harshest words.”

 Sources said that some bottles were very old indicates that some students may be consuming it in the hostel rooms.
