BHUBANESWAR: Chief minister Naveen Patnaik on Monday wrote to Union minister for tribal affairs,  , asking him to release funds towards   for students belonging to the scheduled tribe (ST). 

 "It is a matter of great concern that adequate funds towards the central share of post-matric scholarships is not being released by the ministry of tribal affairs. However, to ensure that the ST students are not put to any financial hardship, the state government has been providing scholarships to the students from its own resources," Naveen wrote in the letter.
