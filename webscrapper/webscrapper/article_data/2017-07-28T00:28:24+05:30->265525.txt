CHANDIGARH: Punjab education department has decided to shift 28 senior lecturers of 11 Government   (GIST) as principals against vacant posts in the Government Senior Training Centers.

 Education minister Aruna Chaudhary said many Government Senior Secondary Schools in the State are functioning sans Principals which explains why the decision has been taken to shift 28 senior lecturers in the vacant schools. Four senior lecturers would be shifted as principals in Amritsar, three each in Bathinda, Jalandhar, Ludhiana and   with two each in Faridkot, Gurdaspur, Hoshiarpur, Kapurthala, Ropar and Sangrur. Also, 28 senior lecturers would be shifted against vacant posts in accordance with their choice so as to ensure the smooth running of the schools.
