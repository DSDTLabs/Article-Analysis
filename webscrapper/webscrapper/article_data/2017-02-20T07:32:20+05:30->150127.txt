 The Bhosari police have booked the woman under section 353 (assault or criminal force to deter public servant from his duty), 332 (voluntarily causing hurt to deter public servant from his duty) and 504 (intentional insult with intent to provoke breach of peace) of the Indian Penal Code.
 
 Senior inspector of Bhosari police station Dilip Kulkarni told TOI, “The woman’s husband is a criminal. Police had detained him and brought him to the police station.”
 
 Kulkarni said that on Saturday at around 10.30am, the woman was driving towards the police station in her car. “When she reached Dapodi chowk, a traffic police found her driving without a seatbelt. He stopped her car and asked her to pay the fine,” Kulkarni said.
 
 The inspector said that all of a sudden, the woman slapped the constable and used abusive language against him. “We have booked her and our probe is on,” Kulkarni added.
