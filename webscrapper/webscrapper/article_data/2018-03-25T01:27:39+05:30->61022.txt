GURUGRAM: Four commercial structures were sealed in the city on Saturday during an   drive against those who have defaulted on  .

 MCG officials said the owners of the four structures owed a total of Rs 1 crore to the corporation and thus faced the crackdown.
 
 The four properties are Krishna Build Well Corporation (with dues of Rs12.5 lakh) in Sector 35, Shaiya Construction Private Limited (Rs 63.59 lakh dues) in InfoCity, a clothing firm (Rs 12.9 lakh dues) in Sector 74 and a commercial property in the names of Bharat Kumar Gupta and Arun Gupta (Rs 11.05 lakh dues) in Sector 35.
