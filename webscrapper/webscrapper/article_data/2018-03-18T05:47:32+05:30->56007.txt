CHANDIGARH: Almost 23 years after assassination of the then CM  , a special court on Saturday sentenced Jagtar   Tara to life imprisonment. The court had convicted him on Friday. 

 The court of additional district and sessions judge,  , in   jailalso imposed a fine of Rs 35,000 on Tara. The court was sitting where Tara is lodged.
 
 Not considering it as a rarest of rare cases where capital punishment should be imposed, the court convicted Tara for murder, criminal conspiracy and under the Explosive Substances Act.
 
 The judgment said that Tara was an active member of the conspiracy to assassinateBeant Singh and he had all the knowledge about the plan.
