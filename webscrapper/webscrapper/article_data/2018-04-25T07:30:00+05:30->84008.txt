NEW DELHI: The Sir  , Tirupati is going to release the AP EdCET Result 2018 on Wednesday, April 25, 2018.

 The candidates can check their AP EdCET 2018 result by providing their registration number and date of birth in DD/MM/YYYY format. 
 
 The qualifying marks in the entrance test for all the students, excluding SC/ST, are 37 out of total marks i.e., 25 per cent.
