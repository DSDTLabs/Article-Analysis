VARANASI: The Ganga continued to rise in the city with an increase of 74 cm in last 24 hours submerging a major portion of ghat steps. 

 Though the river is showing a rising trend, it was still flowing 7.22 mt below the danger mark in the city on Friday. 
 
 According to the daily water level and forecast bulletin of   Middle Ganga Division-III, the river was flowing at 64.04 mt level against the danger mark of 71.26 mt in Varanasi. 
 
 Rising trend was also recorded in Mirzapur, Ghazipur and Ballia. The water level rose to 81 cm in Mirzapur, 68 cm in Ghazipur and 54 cm in Ballia in last 24 hours.
