AHMEDABAD: Over 5,000 IIT aspirants from   took the first-online JEE (Joint Entrance Exam)- Advanced held on Sunday. Most of the students found the mathematics questions complicated and chemistry questions difficult and lengthy.

 
 The exam comprising two papers of the three subjects physics, chemistry and mathematics were held in two parts. The first paper was conducted between 9am to 12 afternoon and the second paper was conducted between 2pm to 5pm. There were total 108 questions including 54 in each paper in the exam.
 
 Shreyansh Nagori, state topper who stood at 24th in All India Ranking (AIR) in  , said that format of the question paper was changed completely, which brought great difficulty to the students.
