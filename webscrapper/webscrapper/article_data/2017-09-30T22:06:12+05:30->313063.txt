LUDHIANA: A 71-year-old financer was found murdered inside his flat in Jamalpur area on Saturday. The man was stabbed with ice pick several times and also attacked with a rod on his head. The police have recovered the murder weapons near the dead body.

 The deceased has been identified as Mohinder Singh of MIG flats, Jamalpur. The flat was locked from outside, the police broke open the gate and found the body in pool of blood on the floor.
 
 The Jamalpur police have registered a case under section 302 (murder) against unidentified accused on the statement of the son of deceased Harpal Singh.
 
 Mohinder Singh was living in flat number 933 alone while the entire family, including wife  , two sons   and  , widow daughter   and her daughter, was living in flat number 907. Besides, another son Harpal Singh was living separate in Ram Nagar area.
 
 The family stated to police that on Friday they had given dinner to Mohinder. Similarly on Saturday morning when they had gone there to give him breakfast the flat was locked from outside. They thought, he must have gone to Gurudwara, later in afternoon when the granddaughter of deceased had gone there to give food, she found the light and fan on inside the flat. She informed her grandmother Satwant Kaur.
 
 When Satwant Kaur peeked in through the window he found his husband in a pool of blood lying on floor. He called his son Harpal who further sounded police.
 
 The Additional Deputy Commissioner of Police (ADCP-IV) Kuldeep Sharam said that, the man was murdered brutally. The accused has stabbed him several times on his forehead, chest and stomach. Besides, he was hit on his head with the road.
 
 Further   said that the police have sent body for postmortem and a case has been registered.
 
 According to sources the man had property dispute with his family following which he was living alone. The police are probing all angles in the case.
