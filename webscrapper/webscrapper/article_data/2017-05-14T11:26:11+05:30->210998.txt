NEW DELHI: Delhi Police is probing allegations of a man who claimed that a group of policemen barged into his house at the LNJP colony in central Delhi and vandalised his room after they had recieved a PCR call about a cow being slaughtered at his house. Police officers say that a probe has been initiated into the allegations.

 The incident was reported on May 4 when the man Abdul   reported that three policemen had barged into his house and ransacked his room looking for beef being hidden under the bed.
 
 However, they left as they could not find anything. Moin later approached the area   to inform him about the matter after which a probe was initiated.
 
 DCP   later asked the area SHO to identify the caller and take legal actions accordingly for providing false information about the incident.
