Ahmedabad: Seven persons of a family sustained burn injuries after a fire that broke out after LPG gas leakage at Vivekanandnagar on Friday evening. The victims have been shifted to the LG Hospital by EMRI 108 ambulances.

 According to Ahmeadabad Fire and Emergency Services (AFES) officials, they received a call at 7.30 pm from Vinoba Bhave Nagar in Vivekanandnagar about a fire. “The fire started when wife of Amarsinh Solanki started cooking meal. The LPG cylinder in the kitchen was leaking and thus, when the gas caught naked flame, it soon spread in kitchen and other parts of the residence. The neighbours started dousing flame,” said an AFES official.
 
 Harshad Patel, a social worker from Amraiwadi, said that the victims include an eight-year old boy and a 45-year old woman. “The house number 19 and 20 have been destroyed due to the fire. The woman and boy have sustained major burn injuries,” he said.
