 Speaking as special guest, senior World Wildlife Fund official Sita Ram Tagore highlighted the significance of dolphins in maintaining the eco-system of Ganga. He urged people to join save dolphin campaign and help all organisations which are working for it. 
 
 Around 200 students from different school participated in the awareness programme. The children made beautiful paintings, rangoli and clay modelling to express the importance of dolphins.
 
 The programme was jointly organised by Centre For Environment Education (CEE),   ( ) and Kanpur Zoo.
 
 The zoo director said that dolphins play an important role in the cleanliness of the holy river. He said that dolphins can be saved through awareness programmes and by telling people about their importance.
