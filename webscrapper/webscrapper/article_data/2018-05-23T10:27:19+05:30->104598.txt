NEW DELHI: The Tamil Nadu Secondary School Leaving Certificate (SSLC) or Class 10th exam results declared on Wednesday, May 23, 2018. The pass percentage of this year is 94.5 %. The results will be made live soon on the official results website of the National Informatics Centre (NIC) - http://tnresults.nic.in

 
 
 The Directorate of Government Examinations (DGE) has made live the Tamil Nadu SSLC/Class 10 results on the official website (http://tnresults.nic.in/) at 9:30 am.
