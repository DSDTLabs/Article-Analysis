VISAKHAPATNAM: In view of the MLC elections, the district collector has issued orders to close all wine shops till the end of the poll on March 9. City taskforce teams conducted drive against illegal liquor sales at   area, Allipuram, Mourya junction, Old ITI junction, Kancharapalem, Pedagantyada and Venpagunta on Wednesday and apprehended 11 people selling liquor illegally.
