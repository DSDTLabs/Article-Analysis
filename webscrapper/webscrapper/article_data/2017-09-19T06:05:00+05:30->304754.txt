Nagpur: District & additional sessions judge GP Shirsat has put the Pench tiger poaching case application against bail to accused for final order on September 22.

 After hearing arguments by special counsel for forest department, Kartik Shukul, the court had put up the matter for final order on Monday.
 
 However, on Monday, counsel for accused Prakash Jaiswal presented his arguments following which the court will now issue order in the matter on Friday.
 
 All the 16 accused were granted bail by the Ramtek JMFC without hearing the investigating officer (IO). The assistant public prosecutor had requested time to file the say of the IO. However, in absolute haste and without taking the say of the IO, the court granted bail to all the accused.
