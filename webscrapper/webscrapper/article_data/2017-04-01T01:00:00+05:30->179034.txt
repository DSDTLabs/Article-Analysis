Anchor  , fondly known as DD, who was hosting Koffee With   for a long time, will be now be hosting a new show. Titled Anbudan DD, this show will be quite different from the previous format. Unlike the earlier chat show, there are no constant rounds or segments, as every episode will have exciting segments based on each celebrity who will appear on the show. Talking about the new show, DD says, “After Koffee with DD, I am very excited to host a celebrity chat show. We are looking forward to more fun and interesting segments with our celebrities. I hope audience love this format as the show will be a bag of surprises.”
This new celebrity chat show will premiere today. Actor Sivakarthikeyan will be the chief guest for the first episode. And the team says that lots of new things are awaiting the television audience.
