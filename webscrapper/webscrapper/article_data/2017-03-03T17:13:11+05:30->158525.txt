With 86 newbies lined up, Lijo Jose Pellissery’s ‘Angamaly Diaries’ has hit screens and the film is a portrait of characters from the satellite town of Angamaly in Kochi. 

 At the interval break, what strikes you is how natural the whole proceedings are, complete with some fresh acting performances, ease narration and music with a regional flavor. The film’s hero, newbie  , impresses alongside the rest of the pack as a youngster who tries to establish a business of selling pork.
 
 The first half of the movie also has what it takes to get the older section of the audience connect with their fun-filled past, and youngsters to strike a chord through relatable imageries. More updates to follow…
