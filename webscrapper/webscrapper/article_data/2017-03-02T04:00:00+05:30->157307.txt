Vadodara: An unidentified man snatched gold chain worth Rs 60,000 of an elderly woman near Karelibaug water tank on Tuesday evening. The police said that Niranjana Soni, a resident of Harni Road, had gone to pick up her granddaughter Presha from her tuition classes on Tuesday evening. When she stopped at signal, an unidentified man came on a motorcycle and snatched her gold chain. Before, Soni could act the thief sped away from the spot. She told the police that the accused was wearing a helmet so she could not see his face. TNN
