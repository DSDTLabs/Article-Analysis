 ICSSR was established in the year 1969 by the government of India to promote research in social sciences in the country. Jasleen said that she is working on the “Performance and usage of data mining techniques for predictive modeling: A study of selected stock markets” under the guidance of Dr Khushdeep Dharni, Associate Professor, PAU. She added that the award is given for two years for carrying out research work towards completion of doctoral programme.
