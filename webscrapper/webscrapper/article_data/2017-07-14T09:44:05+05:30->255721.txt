COIMBATORE: A fire broke out at a coir pith manufacturing unit at Kattampatti on   here on Thursday afternoon. Four water tenders from Pollachi, Palladam and Coimbatore city reached the spot immediately and doused the fire around 4pm.

 Sparks from a high-tension (HT) line near the unit caused the fire around 12.30 pm, police said. 
 
 “Though there were more than 100 employees at the unit at the time of the incident, they managed to escape. Fire spread out to the main hall of the industrial unit, destroying raw materials and several machines,” a police officer said.
 
 The unit has been exporting coir pith to European countries, manager Ramachandran said.
 
 The rural police have registered a case and further investigation is on. 
 
 The cause of fire is yet to be ascertained, the police said.
