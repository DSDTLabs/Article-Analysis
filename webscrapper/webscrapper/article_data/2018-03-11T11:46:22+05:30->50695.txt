AJMER: Last rites of Army soldier   were performed in his native village Gudda of Roopangarh block on Saturday. He was martyred in Kashmir where he was serving in 27 Rajput Rifle Battalion. He was cremated with full state honours.

 Ajmer MP   and parliamentary secretary   were also present and announced help to the martyr’s family.
 
 Meanwhile,  , father of Om Prakash, said that he is honoured by his son’s sacrifice for the nation. 
 
 He said he will send his grandson too in the Army to serve the nation.
