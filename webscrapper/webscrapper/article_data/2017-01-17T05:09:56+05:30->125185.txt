PANAJI: Goa RSS unit has stated that the organization will not actively campaign for BJP candidates for the February 4 assembly polls. "We won't tell RSS cadre to support any particular party," RSS sanghchalak, Goa, Laxman Behre told TOI.

 Earlier, RSS members have actively campaigned for BJP. But Behre stated that the state unit would leave the choice to its members. He claimed RSS was never actively involved in politics. But BJP state chief   said that RSS cadre would back BJP candidates. 
 
 BJP is reeling under the revolt engineered by former   unit chief Subhash Velingkar.
