By-Meghna Gulati

 NEW DELHI: A day after 14 people including two women and two children were injured after a cylinder kept at a laundry shop inside a market exploded, it was found that more than 100 people had a narrow escape. It was found that the cylinder was illegally stored in side the shop for heating clothes before washing. Police have booked the shopowner.
 
 Locals say that the blast occurred at a busy time in the market, injuring not only the people inside the laundry shop, but also the passersby .They added that two women who worked next to the laundry shop were injured.
