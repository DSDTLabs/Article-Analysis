Panaji: The Panjim Hotel and Traders Association has urged all traders, hoteliers and professionals in Panaji to boycott payments of trade licence fees, garbage fees, signboard tax and house tax to the Corporation of the City of Panaji ( ) until the taxation fee structure is reduced and until the licensing policy is simplified for businesspersons.

 Stating that businesspersons are overburdened with CCP’s high taxation fee structure, the association’s convener Barnabe Sapeco has demanded that the fees be rationalized. The association has also alleged that, despite its huge revenue collection, CCP is not rendering proper service to business and city residents .
 
 The association also questioned why food stalls (gaddas) are not made to comply with any licensing rules. tnn
