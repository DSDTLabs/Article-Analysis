The trailer of the upcoming Malayalam film 'Gold Coins,' starring the National Award winner child actor  , is here. 

 The movie has many little actors including Aby fame Vasudev, actors Sunny Wayn, Tessa, Meera Nandan and Sujith Shankar in prominent roles. From the looks of it, the movie is a thriller, in which kids play a major part. It has Kochi as the backdrop and the trailer has many sequences shot in various prominent locations of the city. 
 
 Directed by newbie Pramod Gopal, the Gold Coins has music by Ouseppachan.
