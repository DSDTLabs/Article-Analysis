SALEM:   officials cancelled 14   driver licenses in   district on Thursday.

 According to RTO officials, the state government ordered action against errant drivers in the state. Based on the order, the RTO officials conducted a surprise inspection in Salem district on Thursday.
 
 During the inspection, the officials found that 14 drivers had driven the trucks with more than the stipulated weight.
 
 The officials cancelled the truck driver licences temporarily for six months. “The licence will be cancelled permanently if they did the same crime in future,” the officials said.
