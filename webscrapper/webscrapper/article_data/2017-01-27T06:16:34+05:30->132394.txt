MANIPAL: The Think Tank Manipal, a   (MIT) Club will organize its first-ever ‘TEDx Manipal University’ on Sunday, at the library auditorium of MIT. The theme is ‘Thinking on the EDGE' (Explore Discover Grow Engage). 

 TEDx was formed to promote TED’s new slogan ‘Ideas Worth Sharing’. It supports independent bodies conducting a TED-like event. It provides a platform for people to tell their inspirational stories to a wide audience. 
 
 TEDx hosted by the Think Tank will have 11 speakers sharing their experiences on environment, success, technology, education and self-expression. The speakers are: Tushar Lal, the man behind fusion of Indo-western styles of music, M S Valiathan, a national research professor and former president of Indian National Science Academy, will be talking on medicine; Lohit Sahu, founder of Phyzok, will talk on education; Sanah Rizvi’s will talk on the importance of success mostly from a psychological perspective. 
 
 Dwiref Oza, a third year MIT student and a poet at heart will talk on self-expression.  , a student of MIT, who has experimented with poetry, filmography and photography, will talk on ‘Coexistence of Art and Science’. Ganesh Nayak will talk on adventure. Anand Sudarshan, the founder of Sylvant Advisors Private Limited, will talk on entrepreneurship. Avani Awasthee of School of Communications is the youngest Indian to travel to Antarctica. She will talk on environmental impact. 
 
 The tenth speaker is a 16-year-old child prodigy Geeve George, who is also a techie and a programmer. He will talk on technology. Finally, Sabyasachi Sengupta, an economist and an investment banker will talk on the art of networking and story-telling.
