AHMEDABAD: Gujarat high court on Monday extended the protection from arrest to activist   and her husband   till June 13 in connection with the alleged Rs 1.4-crore fund embezzlement case.

 The   last month granted interim protection from arrest to Setalvad and Anand till May 31 after they had got the relief from Bombay high court when they sought transit bail. The   extended the protection after the state government sought time to reply to the application.
 
 The HC was moved by Setalvad and her husband after a lower court declined to grant pre-arrest bail in the case of alleged misappropriation case related to their NGO, Sabrang Trust. The HC to hear the anticipatory bail plea on June 12.
