MUMBAI: The   earlier this month has sent a notice to actor Anushka Sharma following a complaint received by the civic body regarding   done by the actor at the common passage of the 20th floor of her apartment in Versova, Andheri. 

 While the notice sent by the civic body does not mention the name of the actor's name in it, it has stated that an unauthorized installation of electric junction box has been done on the common passage in the 20th floor of Badrinath tower in Versova. It is learned that Sharma owns the entire 20th floor of Badrinath tower where she resides. 
 
 The notice has been sent by assistant engineer (building and factory) of K west ward directing to remove the junction box or else has warned that necessary action would be taken under the Mumbai Municipal Corporation Act.
