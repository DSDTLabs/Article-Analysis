Koraput: A Border Security Force (BSF) jawan was found dead in Orissa on a Rourkela-Jeypore bus when it reached Jeypore bus stand in the district on Monday morning.

 Police said the deceased was identified as Naresh Kumar (50), a resident of Haryana. Kumar was posted at the BSF camp in Malkangiri district as a wireless operator.
 
 “Kumar was returning to work after leave. He boarded the bus from Jharsuguda and is suspected to have died of a cardiac arrest,” said P R Chottray, inspector in charge of Jeypore police station.
 
 Chottray said the body was handed over to officials at the BSF headquarters in Koraput. “It will be sent to his village,” he added.
