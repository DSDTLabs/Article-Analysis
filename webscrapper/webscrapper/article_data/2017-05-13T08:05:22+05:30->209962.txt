GANDHINAGAR: The members of the Hardik Patel-led   (PAAS) met   president   and put forth four demands, which Solanki agreed to fulfil if the Congress is voted to power in the 2017 assembly elections.

 Dinesh Bhambhaniya, Varun Patel and a few others from PAAS met Solanki. Solanki, said, “PAAS made four demands, which are an inquiry against police officials, Rs 30 lakh compensation,   and reservation. We promised to meet all their demands.”
