PANAJI: Urging citizens to safeguard Goa for the future generations, Goa ForGiving released a theme song on the occasion of the 50th anniversary of Opinion Poll Day on Monday. With the music being produced by Ronnie Monserrate of Nachom-ia Kumpasar fame, the song titled ‘Goenkarponn’ highlights the subtle aspects of Goan identity.
