GURUGRAM: A   was out in the open when a mother-daughter duo was allegedly assaulted by their relatives on Saturday morning. The women have claimed the perpetrators also thrashed them and attempted to rape them on Thursday night.

 According to police, a brawl broke out between the families of two brothers in Rathiwas village on Thursday night after which the elder brother and some of his family members forcibly entered the house of the younger brother and allegedly thrashed his wife and daughter and also tried raping them. 
 
 On Friday, the women decided to go to the police station and register a complaint, but they were first asked to undergo a medical examination. 
 
 The women allegedly faced another attack on Saturday morning when the mother and daughter were on their way for the medical test. “After stopping our car they tried to push me and my daughter out of the car. When we protested, they started abusing us. My husband somehow managed to bring us to the police station. Earlier the accused had tried to rape me and my daughter,” said the complaint. 
 
 An FIR has been registered against four people under sections 323 (causing hurt), 341 (wrongful restraint), 452 (house trespassing), 354 (molestation), 506 (criminal intimidation), 376-511 (rape attempt) and 34 (common intention) of the IPC at Bilaspur police station.
