GHAZIABAD: The Ghaziabad Municipal Corporation’s initiative to convert all streetlights of the city into power-saving   was inaugurated on Monday. The conversion work is set to begin from April 4.

 Vasundhara, Sector 23 of   and Raj Nagar will be among the first to have LED-lit streets. Areas like Vijay Nagar will follow suit.
 
 “The lights will turn on automatically in the evening and will turn off in the morning,” an official said.
