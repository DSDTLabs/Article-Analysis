A married woman allegedly committed suicide at her husband’s house in Ajnala of Amritsar on Monday late night. Police said the woman identified as Sarabjit Kaur was being allegedly harassed by her in-laws due to which she consumed poison and died. Victim’s father Mahinder Singh, a resident of Kotli Dosandi village, alleged his son-in-law Sahib Singh and his parents had assaulted her a number of times. A case of abetement to suicide has been filed at Bhindi Sehda police station against Sarabjit’s husband and his parents. tnn
