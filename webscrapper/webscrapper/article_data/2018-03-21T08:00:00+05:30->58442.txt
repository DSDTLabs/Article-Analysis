The 6th edition of the biennial automotive show Auto Expo-2018 recently kicked off at Government Science College Ground in Raipur. About 55 dealers of two wheeler, car, tractor and commercial vehicle showcased their products at the expo. In addition to this, ten used cars and four construction equipment and 45 companies associated with automobile trade such as finance, insurance, tyre-tube, battery, lubricants etc. were also seen participating in the expo.  

	 



  Various models from across the country also walked for the ramp at the event. The main attraction was a display of fire show at the Auto Expo by various artists. Many businessmen from the automobile fraternity were seen at the event. Public Works Minister Shri Rajesh Murat and Forest Minister Shri Mahesh Gagra were the special guests for the evening.
