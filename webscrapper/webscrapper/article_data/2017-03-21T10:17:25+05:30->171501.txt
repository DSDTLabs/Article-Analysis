ERODE: Heavy   and rain on Monday destroyed more than 3,000 banana plants on three acres at   near   in   district. 

 K Subban, the owner of the farm who was expecting a good harvest in a couple of weeks, said he lost crops worth 2 lakh. "Even plants that were given support using bamboo poles could not withstand the wind and heavy rain," he said. 
 
 Other farmers in the area have lost nearly 1,000 banana plants. They had cultivated varieties including red banana, nenthran and poovan.
 
 Farmers, who lost the crops, demanded the district administration to issue compensation for the crops.
