MUMBAI: RERA interim chairman   will soon be replaced with a regular chairman, said housing secretary  .

 A three-member search committee headed by justice S S Kemkar has been set up to recommend the chairman and members of the authority. The final decision will be taken by the government.
 
 As per the  , an officer of the rank of principal secretary and additional chief secretary can be appointed as chairman. 
 
 The RERA Act came into force on May 1. Developers are expected to register the ongoing projects within three months with RERA.
