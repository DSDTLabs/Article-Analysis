 Operations at Hyderabad Airport were affected between 10:28pm on Wednesday and 2:22am on Thursday.

 Sources say operations at Bengaluru airport were affected due to additional traffic.

 The Indigo 7117 carrying 77 passengers took off from   around 8.55 pm and landed at Hyderabad airport at around 10:25 pm on Wednesday. One of the tyres burst during landing, however, airline officials, clarified that all 73 passengers including an infant were evacuated safely.
