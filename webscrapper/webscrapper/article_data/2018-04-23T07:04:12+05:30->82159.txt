INDORE: More than 500 passengers at Mhow railway   had to face problems due to change in timing of   on Sunday morning. They were seen wandering on platform no 2 for more than an hour.

 The train was scheduled to depart Mhow at 7:45am. But, after reaching the station, passengers learnt that the train timing has been changed and it will depart at 8:45 am. The passengers had no option but to wait. Passengers faced difficulties due to hot weather.
 
 Railway officials said that there was a train for Indore at 9am and thereafter there was no train till 2pm and the locals were constantly demanding for a train to Indore between 9am and 2 pm. So, the timing of Mhow-Ratlam train was changed.
