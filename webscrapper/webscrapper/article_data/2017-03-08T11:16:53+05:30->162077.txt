Vinayakan, who is often rated as one of the most unconventional yet talented actors of Mollywood, is rarely seen speaking to the media.

 However, he couldn't escape the cameras and microphones on the day on which he won the State Award for the Best Actor, for the first time, on March 7. When the media asked him how sweet he feels the achievement is, the actor said that in his 20 years of acting career, the day of the award has been the sweetest.
 
 Vinayakan, who played the role of Ganga in the movie ' ,' was one of the most speculated winners of the State Awards this year. He had also won quite a few recognitions which were given away as the pre-runner to State Awards.
