Rajkot:  ,   

 In Rajkot, Girish Hadiyal (35) hanged himself from the ceiling fan of his room in slum quarters on Jamnagar Road on Friday night. He was declared dead by doctors of Rajkot Civil Hospital on Saturday morning. “Girish had been tired of tuberculosis ailment which prompted him to take the drastic step,” an official from Pradyumannagar police station said. 
 
 In the second incident reported from Jambudi village of Visavadar taluka, an unidentified youth was found hanging to a tree. “Preliminary investigation revealed that the youth had committed suicide five days ago. His identity is yet to be ascertained as his body is in decomposed state,” a police official said.
