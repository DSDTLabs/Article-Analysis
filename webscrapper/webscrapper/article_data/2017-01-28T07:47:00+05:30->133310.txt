GURUGRAM: A 30-year-old man was killed and his friend injured after a truck rammed into the motorcycle they were riding on the   road near Sector 88 on Thursday night. The truck driver fled the spot.

 According to police, the incident took place near Rampura village around 9.30pm when Anoop Kumar from Uttar Pradesh and his friend Santosh Kumar were on their way from Dhankot village to Rampura to consult a doctor since Santosh was suffering from fever. The duo was working as labourers in Dhankot village. As they approached Rampura village, a speeding truck hit them from behind.
 
 A PCR van arrived at the spot and rushed the victims to a nearby private hospital. Anoop, who was riding the bike, was declared brought dead by the hospital authorities while Santosh is being treated at the hospital.
