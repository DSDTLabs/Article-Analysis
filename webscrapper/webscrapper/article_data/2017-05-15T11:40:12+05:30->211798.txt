Priyanka Chopra’s upcoming Hollywood debut Baywatch had its world premiere on Saturday in Miami. The International sensation, PeeCee joined Dwayne Johnson, Zac Efron and the rest of cast members for the movie premiere.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 There’s no denying that she’s red carpet queen, slaying the Oscars, Golden Globes and recently at Met Gala with her impeccable fashion style. And now the actress stunned us yet again with her latest red carpet appearance for Baywatch’s world premiere.
  
 Taking some fashion notes from the beach-inspired movie Baywatch, Priyanka worked a blue wrap dress by Halston’s vintage collection. She shimmered in this blue dress which mimicked the appearance of the dark Blue Ocean. The dress featured intricate sequin work, plunging neckline and a hot slit which Priyanka worked effortlessly.
  
 She worked this blue dress with coordinating metallic blue eye make-up and dark blue nails and accessorized the look with a statement ring by Nirav Modi.
