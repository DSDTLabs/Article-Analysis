JAIPUR:   bagged two awards at the Lonely Planet Travel Awards on Thursday held in Mumbai for Best Destination for Culture and Best Destination for Indulgence in the Indian category. 

 Joint director, marketing,   who received the awards on behalf of   tourism, said receiving these awards is a proud moment for us. "Rajasthan tourism is working towards creating the best experience for its tourists and creating memories. With destinations that offer a variety of experiences and activities. Rajasthan Tourism is looking forward to welcome more tourists this year."
 
 As Lonely Planet Magazine India celebrated its 100th issue in May, they awarded both Indian and International destinations among different categories.
 
 Some of the other categories for the awards included best destination for shopping,wildlife, adventure, romance, among others.
