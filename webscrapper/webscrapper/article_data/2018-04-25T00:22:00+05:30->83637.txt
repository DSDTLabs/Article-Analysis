Jaisalmer: A Bolero carrying devotees from Jalore district to Ramderoa collided with a truck near Dedia village on Pokaran Jodhpur road in Jaisalmer district. Two persons of a family died, while six were seriously injured. The injured were taken to a government hospital In Pokaran where they are undergoing treatment.

 According to police, family of Hadwant Singh of Sirana village in Jalore district was going to Ramdeora for Darshan of Baba Ramdev when the   took place. Hanuman Kanwar and Lakshman Singh died on the spot, while six people were injured. On getting information police reached the government hospital. All injured are members of the same family. Pokaran police have started the investigation. The bodies were handed over to the family after post mortem.
