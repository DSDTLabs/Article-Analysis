GURUGRAM: As a part of ongoing campaign "Road Safety Week", the district police along with   organization (RSO) organised an awareness campaign at Cyber Hub on Saturday.
 
  
  
 A Nukkad Natak was performed with the aim on raising awareness and educating the community about the rules of the road safety. Students from Amity University and Cartarpuri Government School performed a street play titled ‘Ek Awaaz’.
  
 Also, a   contest was also conducted on traffic awareness by DCP (traffic) Balbir Singh, DCP (east) Deepak Saharan and ACP Manish Sehgal. Following this, Traffic Tau requested all to follow traffic rules and take pledge on Road Safety.
