YAVATMAL: A police   attached to Vasant Nagar   in Pusad city of   shot himself dead on Sunday afternoon while on duty. SP Meganathan Rajkumar, who rushed to Pusad immediately on hearing the tragedy, said head constable Nagorao Tukaram Shinde (52) with Buckle No. 482 shot himself with a SLR rifle in the police station itself.

 The deceased was rushed to Government Rural Hospital in Pusad, where doctors declared him brought dead. An accidental death case has been registered at the police station on a complaint filed by constable Rahul Mahure of the same police station.
 
 The deceased had joined the constabulary in 1989 and is survived by wife, two sons and one daughter.
