Udhagamandalam: The Inter Command Sport Climbing Championship concluded at Thangaraj Stadium in the Madras Regimental Centre (MRC) in Wellington near Coonoor on Thursday. Six teams from various commands of Indian Army participated in the three-day event, held under the aegis of Army Adventure Wing, New Delhi.

 The event, which began on September 12, comprised of six climbers each competing in speed climbing, lead climbing and bouldering events respectively at the Army Adventure Nodal Centre in Wellington and demonstrating exceptional spirit of sportsmanship and courage.
 
 On the concluding day, Maj gen Kulpreet Singh, additional director general, military training gave away the prizes to the winners.
 
 Sport climbing was a demonstration sport in 2016 Olympics and a participating sport in 2020 Olympics. TNN
