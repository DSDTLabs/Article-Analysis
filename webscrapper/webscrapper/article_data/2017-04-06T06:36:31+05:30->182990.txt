Hyderabad: The state government has constituted the Mahatma Jyothiba   Jayanti celebrations committee with BC welfare minister   as its chairman and nearly 50 others as its vice-chairpersons and convenors. 

 The government on Monday issued orders to this effect with a rider that Phule's birth anniversary will be observed at a state function on April 11 but without any political speeches.
 
 This will be the 191st Jayanti celebrations of Jyothiba Phule. The government also made it clear that the celebrations shall not be used for any political campaign and no address shall be made by any political functionary and individuals on political lines. It may be mentioned that the government had given Phule anniversary a state function status in March 2015 itself.
