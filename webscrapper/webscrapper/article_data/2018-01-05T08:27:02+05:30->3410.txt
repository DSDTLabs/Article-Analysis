GANDHINAGAR: After two days of   with   and senior leaders,   Ashok Gehlot and state Congress president Bharatsinh Solanki failed to announce any name for the leader of opposition(LOP). 

 They said that the name will be announced from New Delhi by party president Rahul Gandhi.
 
 “We talked to elected MLAs for two days and now analysis will be done and a report will be sent to the party high command. The high command will take the final call. No pressure will be tolerated from anybody. Rahulji had asked us to get feedback from MLAs on selection of new LOP. It will be only on merit basis,” said Gehlot.
