HYDERABAD: The   on Tuesday stayed the recruitment process for the posts of Sub Engineers of Telangana   upto June 4. It also directed constitution of a committee of heads of departments of IIT, Osmania University and JNTU to identify correct answers to two questions as objections were raised by petitioners on them, and told them to submit a report. 

 Justice A Rajashekar Reddy was hearing a petition filed by H A Venkatesh and another person from Karimnagar, both seeking awarding of one mark each, in view of contradictory answers provided to two questions in the written exam on Feb 25. Petitioners' counsel told the judge that key for the exam re leased by authorities had wrong answers to two questions. The judge directed constitution of HoDs committee and adjourned the case to June 4.
