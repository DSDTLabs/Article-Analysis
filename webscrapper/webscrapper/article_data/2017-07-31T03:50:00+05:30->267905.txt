Vasco: About 90 loaders working for a private agency at the airport decided to go on a sudden strike on Sunday following a dispute over payment and other issues. The strike was called off after the Dabolim airport police intervened into the matter. The company caters to Oman Airways, Qatar Airways and Vistara Airlines.  After the dispute was sorted out, PSI Jatin Potdar attached to Dabolim police station warned the loaders against organizing protests in the airport.
