Hyderabad: Greek aluminium extrusion products manufacturer,  , on Thursday announced its foray into the Indian market in partnership with Hyderabad-based Value Line, which runs a chain of luxury sanitary ware products outlets.

 Value Line will import raw material from the Euro 500 million turnover Alumil and after the necessary fabrication, market it for residential and commercial buildings. Alumil SA chairman and CEO George Alex Milonas said, “Alumil SA is proud to offer its world renowned product range to the Indian market and take part in the architectural development of the sub-continent. We are aiming to offer the most suitable solutions to fulfill the current needs of the Indian consumer. We are looking forward to a long-term relationship with Value Line to cater to the demands of the Indian architecture industry.” tnn
