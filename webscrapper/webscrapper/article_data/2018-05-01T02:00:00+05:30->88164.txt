PATNA: Swati Singh took oath as the head girl of Bishop   Girls’ School at an investiture ceremony organized on Monday. 

 The school’s managing director handed over sashes and badges to the newly elected office-bearers. A plantation ceremony was also conducted on the occasion to encourage afforestation.
 
 CUSB: The academic committee of Central University of South Bihar (CUSB) organized a special talk on Veer Kunwar Singh on Monday. 
 
 According to CUSB’s public relations officer (PRO) Md Mudassir Alam, S N Singh, dean of School of Political Studies, talked about the contributions of Veer Kunwar Singh on the occasion. 
 
 “Army men today should understand and adopt the strategies adopted by Veer Kunwar Singh,” Singh said.
