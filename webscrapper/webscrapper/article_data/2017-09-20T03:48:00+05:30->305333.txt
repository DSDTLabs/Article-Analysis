Calangute/Poinguinnim: Continuing its crackdown on drugs, police nabbed a native of Odisha in Calangute and a Mumbai resident at Canacona, both with ganja.

 Calangute PI Jivba Dalvi said they received a tip-off that Anil Kumar Swain would be coming to the parking lot at Calangute beach at 11pm on Monday to deliver ganja to a customer. A trap was laid and Swain was caught red-handed with ganja worth Rs9,300.
 
 Canacona police registered a case under the Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act against Irfan Shaha, for allegedly possessing 7.3g of ganja, worth Rs1,000.
