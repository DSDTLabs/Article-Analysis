The popular   ‘Annie's Kitchen’ will have veteran actress KPAC as the next guest. 

 The show hosted by yesteryear actress   will feature prominent actress Lalitha, who will talk about her memories working in the industry.
 As per the update from the channel, the National Award winner actress will talk about her culinary skills and other interests in the episode. She will also divulge about her good old days being a theatre artiste also. Going by the promo of the episode, shared on channel’s social networking page, Annie will welcome her guest with a special dish also.
 
 The show will be aired on July 15 at 7 pm.
