DHARAMSHALA: A   letter has been issued to  ,   at the block development office in Kangra, after they were found absent during a surprise raid.

 The letter has been issued on the instructions of sub-divisional magistrate Dharmesh Ramotra, who on his visit to the office spotted 80% of the employees missing. Those found guilty were initially issued show cause notices. 
 
 Ramnotra spoke to TOI on Monday, saying he had asked the block development officer to issue warning to all employees. “There were only three employees present in the office when I visited it in the last week of June. There were two computer operators and one peon in the office,” he said.
 
 The office is in charge of about 55 villages with a population of about 60,000.
