CHANDIGARH: All India Congress Committee ( ) has on Tuesday constituted monitoring and coordination committees, besides appointing 16 spokespersons for the state unit. Apart from party in-charge of Punjab affairs Asha Kumari, chief minister Amarinder Singh and state president  , the two committees include seven cabinet ministers.

 
 Adviser to chief minister Capt Sandeep Singh Sandhu, who is also the general secretary of state unit, and Mandi Board chairman  , are also members of the two committees. Party’s Dalit face and Amritsar (West)   Raj Kumar Verka has been included in the coordination committee while   president Amanpreet Singh Lally and party’s women wing head   have been made members of the monitoring committee.
 
 Ex-MLA Karan Kaur Brar too makes a comeback with inclusion in the monitoring committee. Former chief minister Rajinder Kaur Bhattal is a member of the coordination committee. Newly sworn in minister Vijay Inder Singla and AICC secretary Kuljit Singh Nagra, who could not get a cabinet berth, are a part of the monitoring committee.
 
 The list of spokespersons has thrown up many surprises and includes Hardeep Singh Kingra, former aide of former AAP leader Sucha Singh Chhotepur, besides Jasleen Sethi, a councillor from Jalandhar.
 
 The lists were announced by AICC general secretary Ashok Gehlot on Tuesday.
