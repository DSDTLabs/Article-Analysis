'Avengers: Infinity War' has been one of the most awaited films of the year. It extended its reign over the Indian box-office apart from the worldwide market. The third instalment is breaking record ever since its release. The film has become the highest grossing   film in India as its collection went past 'The Jungle Book' which had a total collection of Rs 187.65 crore.

 According to a recent report on BoxOfficeIndia.com, the multi-starrer superhero film has collected Rs 5 crore on its eleventh day at the ticket counter which has taken its total collection to a staggering figure of Rs 192.02 crore. If experts are to be believed then the Marvel film which is helmed by Joe and Anthony Russo would surpass the Rs 200 crore mark by the end of this week as per its collections until now.
