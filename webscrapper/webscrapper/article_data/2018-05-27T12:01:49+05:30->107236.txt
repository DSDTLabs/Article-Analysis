BICHOLIM: Around 60 workers of the   have commenced a chain hunger strike from Friday near the old   bus stand premises, Bicholim.

 Association president   and secretary   told reporters that the employees have decided to observe a hunger strike as the government has not taken any initiative to restart mining activities.
 
 The association officials said that the agitation will continue till June 30 and if mining is not restarted by then, the employees will undertake an indefinite hunger strike from July 1.
