KOCHI: Different resident's associations at   over a liquor outlet getting shifted to Chettichira here. 

 The   outlet in Vyttila was transferred to Kadvanthara on Tuesday. "They were doing the interior work with the shutters closed and we came to know about the outlet being opened only after they started functioning at around 6pm on Tuesday," said Mahesh, a local resident. Residents are opposing the shift saying that there are two residential apartments close to the outlet along with an anganwadi for children.
 
 The residents started the protest on Wednesday at around 9.30 am and the dharna lasted for about an hour. The protesters managed to block the sales happening on Wednesday. Local MLA   later visited the spot. According to sources, the   outlet is set to start functioning with police protection on Wednesday.
