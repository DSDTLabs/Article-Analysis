MARGAO: Police investigations into the Dramapur   case of 26-year-old Listen   have revealed that the accused,   was provoked by the former’s raucous behaviour, and that the crime was not pre-meditated.

 Colaco was stabbed to death by the accused on Tuesday. 
 
 Sources said that the accused’s daughter was preparing for her SSC examinations, and the noise made by Colaco’s group was disturbing her studies. He, therefore, headed towards them on his bike, some 500mts away, and confronted the deceased. An altercation that ensued turned ugly, and in a fit of rage,   whipped out the knife he was carrying with him and stabbed Colaco in the stomach. 
 
 Police have already attached the weapon and the bike used by the accused.
