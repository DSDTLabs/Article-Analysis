Pimpri Chinchwad: The civic body has appointed seven agencies to take action against unauthorized constructions in the city.

 Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation will provide the agencies machinery to raze such constructions identified in the jurisdictions of A, B, C, D and F zonal offices.
 
 The state government has already notified municipal corporations to regularize illegal constructions built in residential, commercial and industrial zones before December 31, 2015.
 
 This amnesty does not, however, extend to constructions in the buffer zone, red zone, blue flood lines and on hill slopes. To this end, owners of such buildings would be required to submit all the relevant documentation by April 30 this year.
