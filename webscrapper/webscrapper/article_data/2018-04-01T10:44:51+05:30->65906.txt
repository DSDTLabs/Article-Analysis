BHUBANESWAR: Pipili police on Friday arrested two persons in connection with the brutal murders of a man, his wife and daughter on February 10. The accused,   and his son Ilaz, are relatives of the deceased, police said. 

 Mohammad Abdul, 65, his wife  , 55, and their 12-year-old girl   were set ablaze while they were sleeping in a room. During investigation, we found that the accused nurtured a grudge against the deceased's family. Imtiaz's daughter was married in the family of the deceased. He alleged that his daughter was ill-treated by her in-laws (Abdul and Rahima), said a police officer.
