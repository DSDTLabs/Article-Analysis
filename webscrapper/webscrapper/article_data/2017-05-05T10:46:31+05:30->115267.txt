HYDERABAD:   government on Monday issued order allotting 1,000 square yards of housesite to Olympic silver medallist  . Chief minister   promised housesite to Sindhu after she won a medal at Rio games.

 Hyderabad collector identified a plot measuring 1,000 Sq yards in Bharani Layout in Shaikpet village, Hyderabad. Special chief secretary of   M G Gopal issue GO. MS No. 1 on Monday allotting the land to Sindhu.
