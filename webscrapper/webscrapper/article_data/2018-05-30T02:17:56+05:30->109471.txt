GURUGRAM: A leading Indian mobile manufacturing company based in   was allegedly cheated of Rs 2 crore by its accountant  .

  The accused formed his own company to execute the financial irregularities. 
 
 The company in its complaint alleged that the accused diverted funds coming from vendors into the account of his own company and would also take products for his own company. 
 
 Following the complaint, an FIR was registered at Sector 18 police station against the accused under cheating, forgery and other sections of the IT Act. The economic offence wing of Gurugram police is investigating the case.
 
  No arrests have been made so far.
