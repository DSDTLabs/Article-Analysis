Ali Abbas Zafar’s   and   starrer ‘Tiger Zinda Hai’ has been going strong with each passing week. According to Boxofficeindia.com, the spy action thriller grossed around Rs 5.50-5.75 crore nett on its third Saturday. By hitting the Rs 300 crore nett mark, ‘Tiger Zinda Hai’ now has its numbers similar to ‘Sultan’. 

 
 
 The on-screen pairing of Salman and Katrina is back and the film has emerged as a hit. The action flick also has Katrina performing some breathtaking stunts besides the 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' actor and the film has some really picturesque locations as it has been extensively been shot in Abu Dhabi, Austria, Morocco, and Greece.
 
 The film is inspired by the real-life incident of the rescue of Indian nurses held hostage in Iraq by terrorists and at the helm of affairs of the mission is Indian RAW agent Tiger (Salman Khan). This   film is a sequel to the blockbuster film 'Ek Tha Tiger' which also had the same actors in the leading roles and released in 2012.
