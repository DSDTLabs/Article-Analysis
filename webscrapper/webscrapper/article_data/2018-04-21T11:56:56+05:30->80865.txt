PANAJI: The excise department seized   worth Rs 8.2 lakh from a Karnataka-bound truck at  , North Goa, on Friday. 

 An excise official said the vehicle was detained at the Keri post for a routine check, but the driver ran away when apprehended, leaving the truck behind.
 
 “On examination, different brands of alcohol were found in the truck. All the bottles were immediately seized,” an excise official said. It is possible that the alcohol was being smuggled for distribution to voters in  , he added.
 
 Karnataka is going to polls on May 12. During elections, border checks are more stringent in view of the possibility of smuggling alcohol across the border.
 
 The excise department received specific directions from the election commission to be more vigilant.
