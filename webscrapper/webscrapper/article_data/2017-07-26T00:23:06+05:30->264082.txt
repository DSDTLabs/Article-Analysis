NEW DELHI: The   on Tuesday constituted the much delayed  , a separate body which has the mandate to name government   and institutions under the state purview.

 Lieutenant governor Anil Baijal has given his approval for the formation of the authority to be headed by the deputy CM Manish Sisodia, said urban development minister Satyendar Jain. 
 
 The proposal for the authority had been pending with the LG’s office for a long time. The authority has been reconstituted for the first time in the AAP government regime. 
 
 “The authority will have a total of 34 members,” Jain said. The Union home secretary, the Union HRD secretary, the DDA vice-chairman, the Delhi PWD secretary and the Delhi Principal secretary (Home) will also be among its members.
