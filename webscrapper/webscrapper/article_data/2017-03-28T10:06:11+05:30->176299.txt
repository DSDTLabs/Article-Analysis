Vadodara: Rumours on social media have forced the   ( ) to clarify that neither the company nor any of its   will accept old currency notes of Rs 500 and Rs 1,000 at its offices. 

 Messages that any person can deposit the old currency notes of Rs 500 and Rs 1,000 at any office of electricity board on March 20 and March 30 without being questioned went viral since Sunday evening.
 
 GUVNL officials on Monday termed this messages as totally baseless and untrue. Appealing its customers, not to get misled by such rumours, GUVNL said that its offices will continue accepting only the prevailing currency notes. 
 
 “We don’t know the source of this rumours. But we have already started getting inquiries about it. Hence, we have clarified that neither GUVNL, nor any of our subsidiary companies including the Madhya   Vij Company Limited, the Paschim Gujarat Vij Company Limited, the Dakshin Gujarat Vij Company Limited and the   will accept the demonetised currency notes,” said a top official from GUVNL’s finance department.
