VASCO: Locals gathered at the   panchayat office on Friday afternoon to protest against a hotel project in the village. 

 They submitted a complaint to the block development officer (BDO) against sarpanch   and the panchayat secretary.
 
 Earlier in the day, the villagers undertook a protest rally from   to   panchayat. Anticipating a law and order situation,   police personnel arrived on the site. 
 
 With the sarpanch and secretary not available, agitated locals demanded the presence of the BOD, and later submitted a complaint.
