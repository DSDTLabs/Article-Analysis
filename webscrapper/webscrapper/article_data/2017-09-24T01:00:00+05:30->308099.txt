AURANGABAD: An electric wire fell on a DAV Public School bus, carrying more than 50 students, on   under the Town police station area of   on Saturday afternoon. The children had a narrow escape as none of them was injured.

 “As the wire touched the bus, there was a spark which frightened the kids. Fortunately, it caused a short circuit and the power flow in the wire stopped,” said a police source.
 
 Inspector   reached the spot immediately and pacified the people who had gathered there in large number. “All the students are safe and proper security instructions have been issued to the school management for transportation of children,” he said.
 
 Police sources said the electricity department officials had also been asked to check overhead wires so that such accidents did not take place in future.
