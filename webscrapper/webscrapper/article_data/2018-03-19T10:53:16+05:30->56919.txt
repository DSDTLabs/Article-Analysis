NEW DELHI: Sandhar Technologies is opening its Initial Public Offering ( ) on Monday for subscription. The issue will close on March 21. The shares are proposed to be listed on both NSE and BSE.

 Sandhar, an automotive component supplier, will sell 1.5 crore shares at Rs 327-332 apiece in the IPO. The company aims to raise Rs 512 crore from the sale offer. Bids can be made for a minimum lot of 45 equity shares and in multiples of 45 equity shares thereafter.
