Amritha, who plays one of the female leads in the  , was last seen in Padaiveeran. The actress, who had a tryst with short films before making her debut in filmdom, feels that her passion for trying new avenues landed her   offers.

 She says, “It’s important to come out of your shell and try out new things, because you never know what life holds for you. Before doing films, I attended workshops because I wanted to overcome my nervousness. For instance, I would feel conscious while screaming. I learnt how to shut myself to the outside world while performing. I think you can never fully learn any form of art; you evolve as you practise and push the boundaries.”
