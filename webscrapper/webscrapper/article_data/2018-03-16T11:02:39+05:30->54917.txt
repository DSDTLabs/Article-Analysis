TRICHY: Heavy showers lashed the city on Thursday bringing much relief to the city from the soaring temperatures in the last few days. The district on Thursday received above average rainfall of 1.7mm.

 IMD officials have said that moderate rainfall would persist in central districts including Trichy till Friday. Trichy city, parts of Manapparai, Marungapuri and  , also received heavy rainfall that continued for about 15 minutes.
 
 The period of rainfall was short but intense. The temperature in the city that was around 34-35 degree Celsius came down to 31 degree Celsius.
