LUCKNOW: Chief minister Yogi Adityanath will release a book ‘Sansad Mein Do Took—Rashtrawad Aur Chandra Shekhar’ at the central hall in the State Assembly on April 17 in Lucknow .

  MLC and   Memorial Trust chairman Yashwant Singh said author of the book is senior journalist Dhirendra Nath Srivastava. Singh added that Speaker of the Assembly, Hridaya Narain Dixit will be special guest at the book release function.
 
 Singh has been organising programmes and lectures dedicated to former prime minister Chandra Shekhar which have been attended by dignitaries. He said the book is a compilation of speeches of the former prime minister in Parliament and his views on nationalism.
