CHENNAI: The Central Bureau of Investigation ( ) in Chennai arrested E Anandan, principal of Kendriya Vidyalaya,  ,   on Monday night for demanding and accepting a bribe of Rs 1 lakh from the parents of a dalit student for admission into Class 1 under RTE  . 

 The arrest came after a complaint from the parents.   (RTE) Act quota mandates that schools allocate 25% of its seats to students from economically and socially disadvantaged backgrounds. 
 
 He was questioned at his residence which is on the the school campus, where searches led to recovery of certain documents and three locker keys, CBI said in a statement. He was later remanded up to April 24 by the principal special judge for CBI cases.
