GUWAHATI: The   on Thursday asked the state government to reply to an appeal by seven petitioners who complained against the validity of a government advertisement for filling up various posts in the state's forest department. 

 Petitioners' counsel advocate   said, "The petitioners were aggrieved because of the exclusion of basic requirements of law such as reservations for SC and ST candidates, those who are women or physically handicapped, and those based on population pattern, local domicile and various other norms in the said advertisement."
 
 "Dissatisfied with the state government's move, the HC has asked the government to reply on the legality of the said advertisement within four weeks' time. The HC allowed the department to hold the test, but directed it not to announce the selection results till further notice," the counsel added.
