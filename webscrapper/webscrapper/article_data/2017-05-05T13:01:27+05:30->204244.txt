PANAJI:   along with the tourist police arrested six tourists on Thursday for consuming alcohol in   place and creating nuisance. All six tourists are in 25-35 age group and were arrested after medical examination, police said. 

 Police said while four tourists were from Karnataka, one was from Delhi and the other from Maharashtra. All six were produced before the judicial magistrate first class in Mapusa, who released them on bail. 
 
 The arrest comes a day after SP (North)   police to take strict action against those found drinking on beaches and other public places.
