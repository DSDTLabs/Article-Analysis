AHMEDABAD: Twenty-eight pilgrims from Surat who had travelled to Amarnath were on the edge when their bus broke down at Kankal toll booth around 5.30pm on Friday. 

 These passengers were returning to Jammu after their pilgrimage. The luxury bus broke down owing to a technical snag. They were being escorted by a military patrol. 
 
 The local police was also called for help as the passengers were stranded on the highway.
