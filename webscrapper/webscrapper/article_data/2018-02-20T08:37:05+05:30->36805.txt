JAIPUR: A family travelling to Delhi from Jaipur was shocked when they found themselves surrounded by   on the   during early hours on Monday. The robbers managed to decamp with Rs 4,000 in cash, jewellery and their car before leaving the family on the highway. The family has lodged an FIR at the Chandwaji police station stating that it is a robbery of almost Rs 10 lakh.

 According to the police, the victim, identified as Kalim Ahamad, approached the police in the morning and stated that four persons travelling in a jeep stopped their vehicle near Chandwaji. “He said that he thought these persons were in some trouble and stopped the vehicle. Thereafter, one of the assailant pointed a country-made pistol at the family and asked them to give whatever they have,” said a senior police officer. 
 
 Police said that the robbers after snatching cash of nearly Rs 4,000, jewellery worth in lakhs and the vehicle of the family managed to flee from the scene. “The family was left on the highway. Finally, they managed to take lift from some people and reached us. We have initiated barricading and initiated highway patrolling. Nothing concrete so far has been found in this case,” said an officer.
