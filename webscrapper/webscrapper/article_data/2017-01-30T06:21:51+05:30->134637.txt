 Attributing the continued dip in the mercury to the active northerly winds, the Jaipur Met office said that similar conditions will prevail in the state for the next couple of days. Mercury is likely to take a further dip in the state.
 
 People of Pink City woke up to another chilly morning as a minimum temperature of 10.7 degrees Celsius was measured.
 
 Dense fog was witnessed on the city outskirts which continued to affect air, road and rail traffic. At least halfa-dozen early morning flights were affected while more than a dozen trains were running late. Traffic on the highways connecting Jaipur to Agra, Jaipur to Delhi and Jaipur to Ajmer was chaotic due to dense fog in the morning. Visibility on these highways and other highways including SikarChuru was quite poor.
