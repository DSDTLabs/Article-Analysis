 Senior police officers said that some people were demanding the arrest of the madrassa’s maulvi as they suspected his involvement. Cops, however, maintained that the man was being questioned to ascertain his involvement in the case. They said that the case was transferred to Delhi Police’s crime branch due to the inter-state ramifications.
 
 During investigation, it was found that the girl’s family earlier lived in Ghaziabad and the accused was their neighbour. The family had recently moved to Ghazipur. On Saturday, the accused met the minor and took her back to Ghaziabad on the pretext of meeting her friends. In Ghaziabad, he sexually assaulted the girl in a madrassa.
 
 The maulvi has been called in for questioning to check if he was aware of the girl being kept there. Cops are also going through the phone details of the minor to ascertain the involvement of more people.
 
 “The family members of the boy are also being questioned to find if they were in contact with him during this period. The accused has been sent to a   justice home,” said a senior police officer.
