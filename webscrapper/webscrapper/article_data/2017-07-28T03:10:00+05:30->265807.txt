Margao: The South Goa Planning and Development Authority (SGPDA) on Thursday issued a show-cause notice to the organizers of the trade exhibition currently underway at the SGPDA ground, Margao, following raids by the department of legal metrology and the food and drugs administration (FDA) at two of its stalls on Wednesday. The teams had seized various items such as safety devices used for LPG cylinders, seed packets sold as cure for all diseases and currency counting machines. In its show-cause notice, the SGPDA, referring to a news item of the raid carried in the TOI, asked the event organizers to show cause why “the NOC granted to it should not be withdrawn forthwith and action be taken against you for permitting the stalls to conduct business which is in violation of consumer rights.” The organisers have been giv en 24-hours to reply.
