IMPHAL: Forest and environment minister Th Shyamkumar has said the Centre has released Rs 9 crore for setting up dairy farms in the scheduled caste-inhabited areas of Andro and Sekmai in   district. 

 The minister, who represents the Andro constituency, said he has also sought funds from the Centre under the Scheduled Caste Sub-Plan for piggery farming in Andro. The Centre has also released Rs 4.75 crore for the development of Andro High School and another Rs 25 crore has also been sanctioned for the construction of a model residential school in the constituency, he added.
 
 Shyamkumar also added that he has plans to undertake a tree plantation drive in Andro during the 'Go to the Village' mission scheduled to kick off next month.
