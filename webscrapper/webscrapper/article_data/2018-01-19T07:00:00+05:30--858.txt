 The 28-member committee includes representives of Industries Association of Chandigarh (IAC), Chamber of   ( ), Converted Plot Owners Association, Confederation of Indian Industry ( ) and Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce and Industry (FICCI).
 
 With an aim to deliberate and resolve long-pending issues of the industry, the committee was constituted in September 2015 for two years. The term of the committee expired in September earlier this year. Industrialists had lodged a complaint with the Prime Minister over delay on re-constituting the committee.
 
 The committee will make suggestions for the promotion of industry for balanced growth of Industrial Area and for the encouragement of entrepreneurship amongst new and existing entrepreneurs.
