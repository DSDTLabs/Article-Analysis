AHMEDABAD: A state   (ACB) team trapped a   (LRD) constable with the   (East), for demanding and accepting a Rs 5,000  . 

 ACB officials said a man was accused in a case registered under IPC Section 498(A) (cruelty by husband and/or in-laws) by his wife. He was called to the police station to record his statement. Payal Rajput, a constable, had then asked for money.
 
 “A trap was set on Tuesday, where the complainant was called to a counselling room for discussion. Rajput had told him to put the money in the drawer of a computer table in the room. After the money was put these, Rajput took it and was caught at the spot,” said an ACB official. tnn
