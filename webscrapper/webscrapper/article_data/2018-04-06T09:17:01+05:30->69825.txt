PANAJI: The Anti-Corruption Branch ( ) of Goa Police on Thursday caught Goa   executive engineer Amit Shrivastava red-handed while accepting a bribe of Rs 50,000 at Dona Paula. Shrivastava had demanded the bribe from the owner of Panorama Enterprises, who had bagged the housekeeping contract for Goa University complex buildings in July last year.

 Gopi Peket had lodged the complaint with the ACB stating that Hema Sundar Reddy of Panorama Enterprises had bagged the tender for housekeeping work for Goa University campus buildings, for an amount of over Rs 50 lakh per annum, Priyanka  , superintendent of police for ACB, said.
