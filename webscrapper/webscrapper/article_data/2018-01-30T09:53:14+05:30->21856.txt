NEW DELHI: Staff Selection Commission (SSC) announced the results of the tests taken for recruitment of Delhi Police SI, CAPF and CISF Assistant SI Paper II 2017 on January 29, 2018. 

 The results were released on the official website ssc.nic.in. 
 
 Here is the direct link to check the results from the official page - SSC Delhi Police SI, CAPF and CISF ASI Exam 2017 Paper II Results 
 
 
 
 
 
 As per the information available on the official SSC website, a total of 6,660 candidates have been selected for the medical examination process. Of the total, 5,708 are male candidates and 952 are female candidates. 
 
 The candidates can check the cutoff, breakup and other details of the selected candidates is also available on the website. 
 
 These computer based examinations were conducted by the SSC for recruitment of Sub-Inspectors in Delhi Police, CAPFs and Assistant Sub-Inspectors in CISF. The examination for the Paper-I was held from July 1 2017 to July 7, 2017 and the results were announced in September. 
 
 The examination for Paper II was conducted on December 15, 2017 for the candidates who qualified the Paper-I. 
 
 As the Paer-II results are declared now, the shortlisted candidates would now have to undergo medical examination before SSC prepares a provisional recruitment list.
