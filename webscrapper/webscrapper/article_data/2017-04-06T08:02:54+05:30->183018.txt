 Lakhs of devotees started coming to the temple town since early morning for a holy dip in the Saryu and to offer prayers at the local temples on the occasion celebrated as birthday of Lord Ram. The incident took place at Tulsi Udyan area near Ram Janmabhumi shrine, where Dulari Devi (65) died.
 
 Her husband Sadhu Ram, a resident of Naugarh area of SiddharthNagar district told TOI, “We were coming back after puja at Tulsi Udyan when we got trapped in the middle of a huge crowd and as my wife fell down the crowd ran all over her, crushing her to death. We kept crying for help, but nobody came to our rescue. By the time administration officials reached us and took her to hospital, she was already dead.”
 
 According to Faizabad SSP Anant Dev, the woman died of heart attack and an investigation was in progress to ascertain the real cause of death. Another injured woman Lakhpati Devi (70) of Sultanpur district was admitted to district hospital in critical condition.
 
 In another melee at Bandha Tiraha area near the banks of the Saryu, several people were injured. However, local administration couldn't confirm the actual number of people injured.
