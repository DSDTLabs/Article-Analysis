GHAZIABAD: Three people suffered burn injuries when some houses in   caught fire after coming in contact with a 220-kV overhead power line. The fire broke out on Thursday morning and three fire tenders, which were rushed to the spot, brought the blaze under control after around half an hour. The electricity department officials said the houses were illegally built and came in contact with a high-tension wire.

 Yatender Kumar, superintendent engineer, PVVNL (transmission), said, “The buildings were illegally constructed and the height of the 220-kV line was such that the top floor of a building came in contact with the wire, resulting in the fire.”
