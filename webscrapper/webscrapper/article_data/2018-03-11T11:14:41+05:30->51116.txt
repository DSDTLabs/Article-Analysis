LUCKNOW: Polling for the by-elections at the high stake   and   parliamentary seats started amid tight security on Sunday. 

 According to reports, Gorakhpur and Phulpur recorded around 7 per cent and 5 per cent polling till 9 am. UP chief Minister   and deputy CM   cast their votes early in the day. 
 
 Soon after casting his vote, Adityanath said that he expect people to turn out in large number and vote for development. Maurya said that the BJP would emerge victorious in the by-election despite the SP and BSP coming together. 
 
 SP chief Akhilesh Yadav said that the by elections will change the history. “Ye itihas badalne ka Din hai,” he tweeted.
