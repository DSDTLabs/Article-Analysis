MANGALURU: Mumbai police arrested Bajrang Dal activist   on charges of kidnapping a girl, who had allegedly eloped with a man from another community. 

 According to Mangaluru police, Pumpwell, an autorickshaw driver, was picked by Mumbai police in the city on Thursday. He had brought the girl and her parents from Mumbai to Mangaluru in a four-wheeler in the second week of December. The victim’s husband   alleged she had been abducted from Mumbai. He had filed a habeas corpus petition in the  , a few days after she was abducted seeking directions to the Mumbai commissioner of police and Mangaluru police to produce his wife in court and decide if she wanted to stay with him or her parents.
 
 Meanwhile, saffron outfit members alleged this was a case of ‘Love Jihad’ and also approached defence minister   to intervene in the issue during her December 29 visit to the coastal city and submitted a memorandum to her. 
 
 Mohammed Iqbal Choudary, husband of the city-based law student, had met her on social media three years ago and became friends with her. Over the years, they fell in love, eloped and married last July. She had submitted an affidavit at the jurisdictional police station here stating that she had eloped of her own willingness and had not been forced by anyone, police sources said.
 
 Earlier this month, Mumbai police had arrived in Mangaluru in search of the girl but they did not find her and had gone to  , her home town. She had been staying here in a rented house under Mangaluru East police station limits. She is the daughter of a prominent leader of a Hindutva group.
