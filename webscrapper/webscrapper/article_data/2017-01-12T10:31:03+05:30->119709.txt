Krish Jagarlamudi the director of the epic historic film Gautamiputra Satakari, starring   is all praise for his hero. He says, "The day I started writing the script, the only person who came to mind was Balayya Babu. If he would have rejected the film, I would not have made it this soon." As he rattles on about him he also affirms, "I don't think anyone else could have played the role."
