Ahmedabad: Some 30 young artists from across the city are set to be part of an art and culture festival – Abhivyakti – which will be organized in Ahmedabad from January 18 to February 17. 

 Artists in the field of visual and performing arts will get together at Sanskar Kendra in Paldi during these days for dance, music, painting, theatre and photography competitions in the city.
 
 Apart from participants, various theatre artists, puppeteers, dancers and writers will also bring in performances. The inaugural performance of the event will be by internationally acclaimed dancer, Vaibhav Arekar, of Nalanda troupe. That apart, a special play performance is also scheduled on January 26, by renowned documentary filmmaker, Sunil Shanbag.
