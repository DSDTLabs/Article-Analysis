MUMBAI: A 50-year-old man died and a teenager suffered severe head injuries after being run over by trains in separate incidents while crossing the tracks at   on Sunday.

 Vasai GRP said, around 11am, Bhiwandi resident Vinodkumar Gagad got off an outstation train when it halted at a signal near Vasai and began walking on the tracks to the station to board a local train. But he was hit by another long-distance train and died on the spot. In the second incident, Nalasopara resident Pramod Vaishya (19) got off at Vasai and was crossing the tracks to reach his work place, when he was hit by a train. Vaishya, who was listening to music on his earphones, is being treated at Cardinal Gracias Memorial Hospital. TNN
