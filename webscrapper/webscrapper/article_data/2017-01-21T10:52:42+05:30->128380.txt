BIJNOR: In a big breakthrough, police on Friday claimed to have busted   factories and arrested nine people in this regard. The factories were unearthed in Noorpur,  ,  .
 
  Superintendent of police, Ajay Sahani, said, “On Thursday night, we got a tip off from an informer that some people are manufacturing illegal arms in Chandpur, Noorpur, Nagina and Kiratpur. After this, police raided the places where illegal arms factories were being run. We have arrested nine people in this regard.
  
  “We have recovered 176 illegal arms from the spot including four revolvers, two double barrel guns, two rifles, one pistol etc,” he added.
