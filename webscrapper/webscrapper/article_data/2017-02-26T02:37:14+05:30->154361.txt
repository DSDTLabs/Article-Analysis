Vadodara: The students of M S University’s   will organize second edition of Sayaji Literature Festival on March 10, 11 and 12 in the faculty campus. 

 The organizers have lined up several discussions on topics like folk literature in Marathi, Sindhi and Gujarati language, contribution and influence of Maharaja   in literature, origin and journey of Sayajirao’s library movement,   literature, connection of films and literature among others. 
 
 The festival will witness workshops on intellectual property rights and copyrights and writing of columns for newspapers and magazines. The students will also present dance, musical and drama performances.
