VISAKHAPATNAM: Nepal government officials visited   (VPT) on Wednesday to learn about the facilities and operational logistics available here for movement of their cargo as compared to the facilities at the port in Kolkata. 

 VPT deputy chairman L Haranadh explained the facilities and infrastructure at the port through a power point presentation and invited the team to trade with India through the port. The team found the operations at VPT transparent and requested Haranadh to extend concessions and offers so as to attract more business from Nepalese traders. 
 
 The deputy chairman further said the VPT is a secured, transparent and e-port and the traders will be provided support for meeting their logistics.
