PUNE: The   police on Tuesday registered an offence against a man from   for allegedly outraging the modesty of a 30-year-old woman and her minor daughter at their home, late on Monday night. 

 The suspect has been booked under Section 354 (A) [sexual harassment] of the Indian Penal Code and also under the relevant sections of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act.
 
 An officer of the Khadak police station said the complainant and the suspect knew each other. He went to the woman's house around 10.30pm.
 
  "Finding the woman's daughter in the house, the suspect had started touching the girl inappropriately," he added. 
 
 The officer said when the woman noticed it, she questioned the suspect about his act. "The suspect then abused her and outraged her modesty too," the officer said, adding that the man was yet to be arrested.
 
 In another incident, the   police have booked two men for allegedly beating up a 48-year-old woman. They also used obscene language against her for not withdrawing a complaint she lodged against one of them in 2015. 
 
 The police said the duo went to the woman's house and started abusing her on Monday night. "They used abusive language and beat her," said an officer of the Vishrantwadi police station. The woman had lodged a stalking complaint against one of the suspects, he added.
