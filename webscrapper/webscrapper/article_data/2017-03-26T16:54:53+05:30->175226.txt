'Speedunnodu' is a 2016 action and romantic entertainer starring   and Sonarika Bhadoria in the lead roles. Apple beauty  's special song is a highlight in the movie and it has earned a luke-warm response at the box office. 

 The movie, which is a typical Tollywood style flick is directed by Bhimineni Srinivasa Rao under the Good Will Cinema banners. The movie speaks about how a happy-go-lucky village guy (Sobhan) played by Sreenivas falls in love with Sonarika's character (Vasanthi), whose father opposes their alliance. Trouble brews when a murder is charged against Vasanthi's father.
