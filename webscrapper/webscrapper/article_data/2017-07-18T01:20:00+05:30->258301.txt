Chandigarh: UT assistant estate officer (AEO) Varinder Singh Chaudhary has been repatriated to his parent state of Haryana. The orders came on Monday. According to the sources in UT, the Haryana government issued the orders for the abrupt repatriation of Chaudhary with immediate effect to his parent state Haryana.

 Haryana in its orders suggested the names of Kudhir Singh, Yogesh Kumar Mehta, Manisha Sharma and Virat as replacement for Varinder Chaudhary and Ranjit Kaur. The letter reads that any two officers from the list can be appointed in place of the two repatriated HCS officers.
