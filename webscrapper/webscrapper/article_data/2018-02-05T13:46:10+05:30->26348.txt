MUMBAI: A man was killed while his two brothers were injured after an unidentified person opened fired at them following a group clash at Vasai on Monday morning.

 The Valiv police said that the incident took place at Shilottar village at around 8.30 am.
 
 Police said one Baban Mali (42) died of bullet injuries, while his brothers Jagdish and Ravindra sustained injuries and are being treated at the Platinum hospital in Vasai. Their condition is said to be stable.
