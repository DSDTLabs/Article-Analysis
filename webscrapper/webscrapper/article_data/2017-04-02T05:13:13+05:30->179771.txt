NEW DELHI: Two armed assailants shot at a businessman and took away a bag that contained Rs 15   in cash in   on Friday. 

 Police said that the victim, identified as Raman Batla, owns a mobile showroom in Pitampura. 
 
 When Batla reached his showroom and stepped out of his vehicle, the accused shot at his arm and took away his bag of cash.
 
 A case has been registered and cops are scanning   footage to identify the men.
