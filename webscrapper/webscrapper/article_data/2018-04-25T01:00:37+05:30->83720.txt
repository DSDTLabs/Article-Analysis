GURUGRAM: A 30-year-old resident of Pataudi, Jauddin, was arrested on Tuesday for trying to rape a seven-year-old girl in his neighborhood. The accused was produced in a city court on Tuesday and sent to judicial custody.

 According to police, the alleged   took place late on Sunday in Pataudi area where the accused, a  , was working at the girl’s house. He found the girl alone in a room, and dragged her to a corner in order to rape her. 
 
 When she raised an alarm, her family and neighbours gathered in the house. The girl told her family that the carpenter had dragged her to a corner and tried to force himself on her. 
 
 The accused, however, had managed to escape by that time. 
 
 Based on the complaint of the girl’s mother, an FIR was registered against the accused under Section 4 of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act, at women’s police station, Manesar, following which he was arrested from Pataudi area.
 
 “The accused confessed to his crime and we have sent him to judicial custody,” said inspector  , station house officer, women’s police station, Manesar.
