Margao: The Goa Police has sanctioned 32 constables for the new police station at  , which is scheduled to be inaugurated on Saturday, on the occasion of Dussehra.

 PI Navalesh Dessai, who is currently posted at Dabolim airport, will head the new police station with immediate effect, in addition to discharging his own duties, an order issued on Wednesday by superintendent of police (HQ), S M Prabhudessai, stated. 
 
 The station, which will be housed in the police quarters at Arlem, is likely to be inaugurated by chief minister Manohar Parrikar. 
 
  It will later be expanded to accommodate lock-ups and an independent traffic cell for areas within Fatorda, police sources said.
