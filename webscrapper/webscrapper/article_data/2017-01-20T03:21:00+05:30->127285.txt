Keri: Goa government officials will inspect the work undetaken by Karnataka, pertaining to Kalsa canal in Kankumbi, since last one week, in front of a plugged vent .

 Chief engineer of water resources department of Goa, Sandeep Nadkarni, said, “Our team of officials will visit Kankumbi to ascertain the ongoing work of Karnataka in Kankumbi and, there-after, needful steps will be taken.”
 
 The Mhadei Water Disputes Tribunal had directed Karnataka government in 2014 to plug both vents of Kalsa canal to ensure that the water in the Mhadei basin does not automatically flow into the Malaprabha basin.
