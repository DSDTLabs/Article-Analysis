CHANDIGARH: The   ( ) will now be called the Haryana Shahri Vikas Pradhikaran (HSVP). The state cabinet, in a meeting chaired by chief minister Manohar Lal Khattar here on Tuesday, approved the renaming of the urban development agency of the state, according to an official release.

 Health minister Anil Vij had last year said the acronym HUDA was often “misunderstood” as it sounded similar to the title of former CM   of the Congress. The HSVP is the Hindi translation of the Haryana Urban Development Authority. The state cabinet also decided to rename the University of Health Sciences in Karnal after   ideologue Deen Dayal Upadhyaya. It will now be known as Deen Dayal University of Health Sciences.
