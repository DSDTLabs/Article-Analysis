The 75th State Police   and seventh   competition was inaugurated at OSAP sixth battalion ground in a grand ceremony yesterday. Additional   of Special Armed Forces   was the chief guest at the function and he inaugurated the sports events. A special parade by the players was held and the Addl DGP advised the participants to compete with a sporting spirit. As many as 21 police teams took part in Kabaddi while nine teams participated in Handball.
