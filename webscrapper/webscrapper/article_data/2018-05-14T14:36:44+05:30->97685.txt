PATNA: Congress chief   and his sister   did not attend Tej’s wedding even though his sisters Raj Lakshmi Yadav and Ragini Yadav had personally invited   for the event. Rahul, however, had extended his good wishes over the phone to Lalu and his family on Saturday afternoon. “Rahul had called   and talked to Lalu ji and Rabri ji as well.
