Ahmedabad: Gujarat high court on Wednesday sought explanation from   ( ) in response to a public interest litigation (PIL) opposing eviction notices to people from the land acquired for sewage farm in  .

 The PIL was filed by one Ramjibhai Waghela, who raised the issue of possible eviction of 250 families in Vasna area from the land that was acquired for the plant more than 25 years ago. The AMC had recently issued ordered to these families to vacate the land.
 
 The petitioner has submitted that before evacuating these families from the acquired land, AMC should make alternate arrangement for their accommodation. This is because they have been living on this place for over two decades and are eligible for alternate shelter before eviction.
