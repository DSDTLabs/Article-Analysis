PANAJI: Only 50% salaries for the month of February have been credited to around 600 teachers and 1,000 support staff from 24 government-aided colleges across Goa, teachers informed STOI. 

 Sources said that some college teachers have received their full salary as their managements have paid the  , while other colleges have not shared the burden. 
 
 Sources said the government has intimated the colleges about the 50% grant-in-aid payment. 
 
 “Some college managements are not paying staffers claiming they have no additional funds present with them for the purpose,”said a teacher.
