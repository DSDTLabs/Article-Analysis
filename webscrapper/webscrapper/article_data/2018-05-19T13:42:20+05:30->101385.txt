RAJKOT: The freight loading and earning of Rajkot division of Western Railway has been increased by 21.9% in the month of April 2018. As many as 556 wagons per day were loaded against 456 wagons per day in the same month last year. Besides freight loading, freight   also showed the growth. Against earning of Rs 158.64 crore in April 2017, in April this year earning was Rs 182.82 crore, 15.24% higher. The earnings were also 8.42% higher than the target of Rs 168.65 crore for April. Fertilizers, coal and POL (petroleum, oil and lubricants) commodities contribute to the higher freight earnings.tnn
