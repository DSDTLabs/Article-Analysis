AMRITSAR: A 55-year-old balloon seller was critically injured today when the gas cylinder he was using to inflate balloons exploded at a village near Amritsar, police said. 

 Police Commissioner S S   said this evening that Satnam Singh has been admitted to a hospital in   village after the accident and his condition is stated to be critical. 
 
 Earlier today, station house officer of Chehertha police station,  , had said Singh died in the blast. 
 
 Eight other people, including six children, were also injured in the accident.
