As   geared up to take the stage on Friday at a   venue, theatre-enthusiasts poured in in large numbers. After catching up over high tea, the guests shuffled into the auditorium where Suchitra was to present  . They watched in rapt attention as she enthralled the audience. After the hour-and-a-half-long show, the audience rushed to Suchitra to congratulate her on her rivetting performance that made their night worthwhile.
