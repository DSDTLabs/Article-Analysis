MUMBAI: The Bombay   is to decide on Tuesday a challenge against a set of draft rules by the state to sell   only in glass bottles. 

 The All India Plastic Manufacturers Association and the state distillers association had rushed to court to defer finalization of the draft rules. A July 10 notification sets July 25 as the deadline to oppose the draft rules.
 
 The state has amended its rules under the Maharashtra Prohibition Act to stop the use of polyethylene terephthalate ( ) bottles for alcohol. Senior counsels Milind Sathe and Janak Dwarkadas, appearing for these associations, said the state ought to defer finalization of the rule till the court decides a challenge pending since last year.
