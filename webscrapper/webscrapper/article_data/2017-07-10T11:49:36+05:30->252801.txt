BIJNOR:   on Sunday seized 50 kg meat from an unlicensed meat shop here. One man was detained for questioning and the matter is being investigated.
 
  According to police, one the basis of a tip-off, a raid was conducted at the shop and one Nisar Ahmed was arrested, while two of his accomplices managed to escape.
  
  Bijnor SHO Premveer Rana said, “Police seized the meat from a shop located at the fish market near Ghantaghar. A case has been registered against the accused for selling the meat illegally. Police are trying to know what type of meat it was. One person has been arrested and another two are still on the run.”
