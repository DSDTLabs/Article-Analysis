 The film has collected a total of around Rs 209 crore approx worldwide in six weeks, as recorded by Boxofficeindia.com. This makes it the third biggest worldwide grosser of the year, the first two record holders being ‘Baahubali 2: The Conclusion’ which collected over an unimaginable Rs 800 crore and Shah Rukh Khan’s ‘Raees’ making Rs 272.52 crore this year respectively.
 
 
 
 ‘Toilet: Ek Prem Katha’ is an interesting love story of a new bride played by Bhumi, who leaves her marital house in the absence of a proper toilet and how her husband played by Akshay, fights all odds to get one in the house amid the staunch conventional setup.
 
 Besides the lead couple Bhumi and Akshay, ‘Toilet: Ek Prem Katha’ also stars Divyendu Sharma,  , Sudhir Pandey and   in prominent roles. The film is based in the Nandgaon village of Mathura in Uttar Pradesh and also has a special song ‘Gori tu latth maar’ on the famous Latthmar Holi of the region.
 
 Directed by Shree Narayan Singh, ‘Toilet: Ek Prem Katha’ promotes Prime Minister’s pet cleanliness campaign ‘Swacch Bharat Abhiyan’.
