Chandigarh: Children with   and their families were felicitated on the World Down Syndrome Day at Chandigarh Judicial Academy, Sector 43, on Tuesday. The psychiatry department of Government Medical College and Hospital, Sector 32, along with Government Rehabilitation Institute for Intellectual Disabilities (GRIID) collaborated with State Legal Service Authority (SLSA), Chandigarh Down Syndrome Society (CDSS) and Dialogue Highway. Justice Surya Kant, Punjab and Haryana high court-cum-SLSA executive chairman, was chief guest. Dr B S Chavan, CDSS president and GRIID joint director, talked about a self-sufficient institute for such children. The event was marked by a song and Bhangra performance.
