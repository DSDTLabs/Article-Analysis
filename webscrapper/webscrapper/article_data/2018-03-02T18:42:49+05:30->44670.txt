KOCHIKODE: District collector   entrusted   to conduct an inspection and submit a report on cause which resulted in the damage of newly installed ventilators at  . Collector has directed the team to submit the report within two days.

 A direction in this regard was taken at a meeting chaired by district collector at the collectorate here on Friday. Medical college hospital authorities informed the collector that a total of 11 ventilators were ordered for the Medical college hospital. Of the 11, installation of nine machines were commenced on February 14. The damage was detected in six of them soon after the installation. 
 
 Medical college principal  , superintendents Dr Sreekumar of IMCH, Dr Rajagopalan of Super Speciality and Dr K J Sajith Kumar of MCH attended the meeting.
