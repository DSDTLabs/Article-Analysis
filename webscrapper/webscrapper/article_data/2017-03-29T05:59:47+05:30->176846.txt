PUNE: Police are searching for the parents of the three-year-old child, whose body was found on March 12.

 The   police suspect that the parents of the baby boy might have abandoned him realizing that he might not recover from his ailments. Assistant inspector   of the Sinhagad Road police said, “We are searching for his parents.”
 
 The boy’s body was recovered from a spot near Navale bridge on   bypass.
