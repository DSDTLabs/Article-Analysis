Ahmedabad: The cycle rally from Gandhinagar to a border outpost of the BSF, Cyclothon, ended at Narabet on Saturday afternoon. The rally, in which around 60 BSF jawans and officers participated, was taken out under the Gujarat Seema Darshan programme.

 The cycle rally was flagged off from Gandhinagar by Gujarat chief minister Vijay Rupani on Thursday. The cyclists made a night halt at Mehsana. The resumed the next morning and reached Ratanpur in Banaskantha. The rally reached Narabet on Saturday evening. It was received by several senior BSF officials and their families.
 
 After reaching Narabet, the cycles were distributed to the troops deployed on the border.
