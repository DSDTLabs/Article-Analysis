GURUGRAM: There is good news for those employed with the Haryana government as it is considering to raise the   of its employees from 58 to 60 years. 

 Chief minister   while interacting with the media said that a three-member committee, under the chairmanship of   Capt Abhimanyu, would be constituted to study different aspects of raising the retirement age. The other two members of the committee are health minister Anil Vij and minister of state for social justice and empowerment  . 
 
 The committee will submit its report within one month. The CM said that his government might also re-employ those who have retired, but are less than 60 years of age.
