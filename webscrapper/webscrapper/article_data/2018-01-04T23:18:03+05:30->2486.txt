Mangaluru: Two youngsters riding on a two-wheeler, died after a   ran over them. The accident occurred at Narikombu, Nehru Nagar on Thursday, police sources said.

 The deceased are identified as Azad, 20, and Mohammed Harshid, 18. The accident took place at 2.40 pm.
 
 The police said the duo was travelling from BC Road to Melkar. The two sustained critical injuries and were rushed to a private hospital here, but did not respond to treatment, and breathed their last at the hospital.
 
 The two victims were daily wage labourers.
