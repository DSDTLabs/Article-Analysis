KANPUR: Governor   on Thursday said that he has written a letter to chief minister  , demanding action against cabinet minister in the Akhilesh government,  , on grounds of complaints received from common people. 

 "I have written a letter to the chief minister on the complaints I received from the common people. The work of a governor is like that of a postmaster, who ensures that the grievances of the public reach to the government," Naik told reporters at the convocation ceremony of a private college in the city.
 
 Naik said that he has mentioned certain points, including alleged misuse of official position and illegally occupying waqf properties, in his letter on which he has sought action against the SP leader.
