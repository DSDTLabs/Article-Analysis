Calangute: Four domestic tourists, from Chhattisgarh and Jharkhand were nabbed for allegedly illegally drinking alcohol in public on Calangute beach on Wednesday.

 The four persons were taken for medical examination to the district hospital in Mapusa and it was confirmed that they were under the influence of alcohol, Calangute police said. They were later released on bail. Last week, some tourists from Maharashtra were arrested for drinking and creating nuisance in public.
