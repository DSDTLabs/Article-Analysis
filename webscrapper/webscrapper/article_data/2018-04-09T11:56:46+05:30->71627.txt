After spending two nights at the Jodhpur jail in connection to the blackbuck case,   returned Mumbai after the court granted him bail on Saturday evening. And not surprisingly the actor was thronged by a host of celebrities arriving at his humble abode to meet him.

 
  
 
 Seen arriving at his Bandra residence to pay visit to the ‘Dabangg’ star were celebs like  ,  ,  , Remo D’Souza, Kabir Khan, Huma Qureshi, Bobby Deol, Sneha Ullal, Mahesh Manjrekar and others. Also, seen were some of the family members and close friends of the actor that included his step mother  , ex sister-in-law Malaika Arora, Amrita Arora Ladak, Atul Agnihotri and others.
 
 After the actor was convicted in the 20-year-old case, celebs and well wishers had taken to their social media account to show their support to the actor. Actress-friend Preity Zinta visited the actor at the Jodhpur jail on Friday.
