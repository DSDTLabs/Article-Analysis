MUMBAI: More than 36 lakh cultivators in the   will get a full loan waiver, said chief minister   on Sunday. There are nearly 42 lakh farmers who are classified as “debt-ridden”, of these 38 lakh owe less than Rs 1.5 lakh and the sum will be written off under the scheme announced by the state.

 He also said that the investment made in the agriculture sector in the last two years and the proposed spends over the next three years are “unprecedented”.
 
 Fadnavis spoke on the issue of loan waivers in his fortnightly TV address ‘Mi Mukhyamantri Boltoy’.
