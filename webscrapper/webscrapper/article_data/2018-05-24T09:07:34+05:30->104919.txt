JAIPUR: A book comprising Prime Minister  's speeches on “good governance” during his tenure as Gujarat's chief minister will be distributed among   officers in Rajasthan, a senior state official said on Wednesday. The copies of the book titled as “Chintan Shivir” have been sent by the   government and will be distributed among IAS officers after a formal approval by the state government, State Personnel Secretary   said.

 “A file has been moved to seek permission to distribute the books among officers,” the secretary, department of personnel said.
 
 She added that the books were sent to the chief secretary of Rajasthan by his Gujarat counterpart.
