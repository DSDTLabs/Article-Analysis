AHMEDABAD:There is good news for NCC aspirants in the state. Major General  , additional DG, NCC said that there will be an additional increase of 25% in the intake of students for the NCC in Gujarat. 

  He met the state education minister and apprised him of the development. The enrolment quota shall gofrom 60,000 to 75,000, beginning from August this year.
 
 The general also informed the minister that an additional   headquarters will be set up in Jamnagar.
