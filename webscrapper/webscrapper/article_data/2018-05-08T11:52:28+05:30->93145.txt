TRICHY : A grocery shop was   in a massive fire in the early hours of Monday near Thanjavur. Though nobody was injured in this incident, goods worth around Rs 5 lakh were reduced to ashes, police said.

 According to police, Muthuvel, 51, of Kuruvikarambai area near Peravurani in Thanjavur district was running the grocery shop at Madathu Vasal in a thatched structure. He was also running a photo studio beside the grocery shop.
 
 In the early hours of Monday, neighbours noticed smoke emanating from the roof of the grocery shop. Muthuvel alerted the fire and rescue services department in Peravurani. Fire tenders rushed to the spot however, it had gutted the entire shop.Peravurani police is conducting an inquiry. TNN
