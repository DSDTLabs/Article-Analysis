CHANDIGARH: The Haryana government has installed 26 portable cabins to provide shelter to the homeless in the winter. These cabins have been installed in Panchkula, Faridabad, Gurugram, Sonipat, Panipat, Yamunanagar, Kurukshetra, Bahadurgarh and Rohtak.
