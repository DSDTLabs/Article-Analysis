Rajkot: As soon as message that the hospital required more units of blood to treat the injured went viral of instant messaging apps, a group of Muslims reached the hospital to donate blood. Aarif Kalva, a local Muslim community leader, said, “Nearly 75 people of different Muslim organizations came to donate blood.” Students and teachers of Muslim educational institutes Darul Ulam Akwada and workers of Meman social group rushed to hospital. They also got fruits and juice for injured and their family. In all, they donated 200 bottles of blood.tnn
