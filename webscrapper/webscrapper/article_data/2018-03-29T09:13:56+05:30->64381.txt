LUCKNOW: A youth allegedly abducted and raped a minor girl and later dumped on roadside in Itaunja on Wednesday. Locals staged protest after the incident came to light and set a van afire owned by the accused. 

 Police said locals in   locality in Itaunja spotted a girl lying unconscious on the road and alerted the village elders. 
 
 The girl's parents took her to a hospital. Where she regained consciousness, she narrated her ordeal and disclosed the name of the accused who lives in the same locality. 
 
 Angry villagers then went bersek. Additional police force was summoned to control the situation.
 
 CO BKT Chakresh   said a case for rape, abduction, holding the victim hostage was registered against the accused who is absconding.
