NEW DELHI: A 55-year-old man was arrested for allegedly flashing and misbehaving with a group of women at a colony in Rajouri Garden. 

 Cops said they received three complaints from residents in which they mentioned that the man abused a woman and her husband while they were taking a walk near their house on April 9. 
 
 In the second complaint, a woman alleged that the man had rung the bell at their house and disrobed himself outside after hurling abuses at her. 
 
 Police officers said that they had received a third complaint in which a woman had alleged harassment by the man.
