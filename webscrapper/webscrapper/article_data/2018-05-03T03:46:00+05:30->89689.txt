Colva: The additional sessions court in South Goa has   Fatima Mukalleu and Tato Borkar, who were charged for trafficking a girl two years ago in Margao.

 Both the persons were allegedly caught red-handed near ESI hospital in Margao with a victim girl who was allegedly procured from Andheri, Mumbai for the purpose of prostitution.
 
 The prosecution examined four witnesses in the case. The victim did not support the prosecution case.
 
 Judge Sayanora Laad observed that there is no evidence to suggest that the victim was brought for sexual exploitation, and that there was no evidence that the accused were living of the earnings of prostitution, she added.
 
 The case was investigated by police inspector Kapil Nayak.
