LUDHIANA: Government railway police crime investigation agency on Sunday claimed to have busted a gang of robbers with the arrest of four of its members from Ludhiana railway station.

 Eight smart-phones, a bike and sharp-edged weapons were recovered from their possession. 
 
 Investigating officer   said, “On a tip-off,   of Nawanshahr, Palwinder Gill, Vicky and Lucky, residents of Nakodar, Jalandhar, were arrested while they were hatching another robbery plan on Saturday night. The gang struck at late night hours at the railway station. They targeted passengers in trains too.”
 
 Police said the miscreants were also smuggling drugs to Ludhiana through trains.
 
 A case under sections 401 (punishment for belonging to gang of thieves), 411 (dishonestly receiving stolen property) of the IPC and the Arms Act has been registered.
