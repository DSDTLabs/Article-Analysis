SURAT: Police arrested Olpad taluka   president   and his son and brought them to   police station on Sunday on the alleged charge of stealing water from a canal. 

 The accused were booked following a complaint filed by an irrigation department official. Police booked Jaswant Patel and his son Chirag at Olpad police station on the complaint filed by irrigation department official Arun Thakore. 
 
 Farmers of Jahangirpura had informed him that Patel had created a hole in Kakrapar canal and was stealing water through a pipe. The irrigation official found the allegations to be true and so lodged a police complaint about the theft.
