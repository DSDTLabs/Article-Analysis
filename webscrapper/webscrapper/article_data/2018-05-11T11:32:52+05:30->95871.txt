 The board of PC Jeweller on Thursday agreed to the proposal of buying back 1.21 crore shares at Rs 350 a piece, which converts into a premium of 67 percent in Thursday’s closing price of Rs 209.

 The stock of PCJ has been in the news since the past few days because of the huge volatility seen and eroding investors’ wealth in the recent past.
 
 The board of directors of the company will have a meeting on May 25, 2018 to consider and approve the audited standalone and consolidated financial results of the company for the quarter or the financial year ended on March 31, 2018.
 
 The board will also consider recommendation of dividend on preference shares for the duration of April 1, 2017 to September 1, 2017.
