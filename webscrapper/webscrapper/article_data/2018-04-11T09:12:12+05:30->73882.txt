MANGALURU: Members of the Panaara aka  , who traditionally perform  , have filed a caste abuse complaint against   Kalladka Prabhakar Bhat for his recent remark against them.

 The complaint was filed by   Samaja Seva Sangha with the Belthangady police on Tuesday. The complainants said Bhat has questioned the very existence of ‘Bhootaradhane’ of Tulunadu during a protest in Kairangala. Bhat remarked that those performing Bhoota kola and related traditions have no sense.
 
 Police said that they have taken a non-cognizable complaint as of now, and are taking legal advise on whether to file a nFIR or not. 
 
 It may be recalled that the Dakshina Kannada District Congress Committee (DCC) had on Saturday filed a complaint with the Election Commission against Kalladka, for allegedly making a communally-provocative speech at Kairangala in Mangaluru constituency on April 4.
