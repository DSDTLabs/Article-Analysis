Popular Telugu filmmaker Raj Kandukuri has been conferred with the prestigious B Nagi Reddy Memorial Award for 2016. 

 The award was instituted by Nagi Reddy’s son   to honour filmmakers who made a mark in Tollywood. Raj will receive the award on April 16 in a gala event.
 
 Raj’s romantic entertainer ‘Pelli Choopulu’, starring   and   has received rave reviews for its simplicity and superb camera work.
