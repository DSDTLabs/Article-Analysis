Rajkot: A 26-year-old man was arrested with two country-made pistols and equal number of live cartridges near the Kothariya crossroad in Rajkot on Wednesday.

 The accused, Divyesh Thummar, is a resident of Ramnagar on Gondal Road. He was arrested following intelligence input by the city crime branch officials.
 
 According to police, Thummar had bought the weapons from one Manoj who belongs to Madhya Pradesh. 
 
 Thummar also reportedly told police that he had already sold six pistols to different people in Rajkot. He further said that he had ordered three more pistols and 200 cartridges.
 
 Police said that Thummar is accused in two arms cases and one case of firing in Kothariya village.
