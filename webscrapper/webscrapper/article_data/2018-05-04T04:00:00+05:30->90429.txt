Rajkot: A 32-year-old scrapyard owner was blown to bits when the gas pipeline nozzle and other iron spare parts exploded. He was cutting them with a gas cutter in Lakhurai crossroads area of Bhuj on Thursday morning. 

 Police identified the deceased as Jagdish Bhanushali, a resident of Jesthanagar in Ganesh Chowk area of Bhuj. 
 
 “It was such a massive blast that Bhanushali’s body was blown to smithereens. We have collected his body parts of which one leg is still missing. We have contacted the forensic science laboratory for conducting a forensic examination to ascertain the cause of the blast,” an official from B-Division police station of Bhuj police said.
