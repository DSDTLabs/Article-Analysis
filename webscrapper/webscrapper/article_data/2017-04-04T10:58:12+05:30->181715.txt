HYDERABAD: Cheated by an agent and stranded in   for three months, 31   were rescued and brought back to the country on Monday. NRI affairs minister   had taken up the problem of the workers with external affairs minister  .

The workers were sent to Iraq but were left stranded as neither the agent nor employer got in touch with them. They were forced to live without even basic necessities.
