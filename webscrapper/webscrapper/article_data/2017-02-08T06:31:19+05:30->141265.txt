AHMEDABAD: The process of final placements for the   (PGP) class of 2017 of Indian Institute of Management- Ahmedabad (IIM-A) will begin on February 13.

 A statement from the premier B-school said that IIM-A follows a ‘cluster cohort placement process’ wherein companies offering similar job profiles are grouped into cohorts. Several cohorts are invited to the campus in a particular cluster.
 
 The first cluster will hold interviews on February 13 while the second and third on February 16 and February 19 respectively, said the  .
