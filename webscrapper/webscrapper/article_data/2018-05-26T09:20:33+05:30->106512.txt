PANAJI: Following a raid at the Goa meat complex ( ) on Wednesday by a Chennai-based animal welfare   named  , meat traders in the state on Friday clarified none of the cattle brought to Goa was illegal. “We have all necessary permissions,” they said, showing the documents.

 Transportation of live animals to GMC had been discontinued since October 2017 after the NGO had objected to the certification process. After several months, six bovines were brought to GMC on Wednesday for a trial-run of the new stunning device. 
 
 TOI had reported on May 25 that the state veterinary doctor at GMC had certified the six animals as fit for transport and slaughter.
