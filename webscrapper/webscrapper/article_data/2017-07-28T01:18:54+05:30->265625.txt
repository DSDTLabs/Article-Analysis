 A memorandum of understanding (MoU) was signed on Thursday between the university and the India’s national helicopter company, Pawan Hans Limited to collaborate for the course. The course will be dual degree, in which the graduation   will be awarded by JMI and a certificate in aircraft maintenance engineering will be issued by Directorate General of Civil Aviation ( ).
 
 The theoretical aspects of the course will be taught to students at Jamia’s faculty of engineering, while the practical training part will be provided at PHL facilities.
 
 The three-year course will be open to applicants who clear their 12th Board exams with science and maths. An entrance test will be conducted for admission to the course.
