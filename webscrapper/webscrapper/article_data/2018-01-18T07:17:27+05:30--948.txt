BENGALURU: A 21-year-old woman has alleged that she was attacked by her minor friend’s family, after she informed the child’s helpline about the girl’s complaint of sexual harassment by relatives.

 According to her complaint, the accused attacked her on the night of January 15. She identified her attackers as Akbar Sharif, Mohammed Sharif, Jabin Sharif and  , residents of KHB Colony, Koramangala.
 
 “The girl and I have been friends for the last 10 months. She told me she is facing sexual harassment from her family members. In a bid to help her, I took up the matter with the child welfare committee ( ) and called the child helpline recently. An inquiry was conducted. The accused threatened me with dire consequences, saying I should stop talking to the minor girl,” the complainant said.
 
 Police have booked the case under   sections 354 (outraging modesty of woman), 504 (intentional insult to breach peace) and 506 (criminal intimidation). 
 
 The girl’s relatives told police that they had objections to her friendship with the adult woman.
