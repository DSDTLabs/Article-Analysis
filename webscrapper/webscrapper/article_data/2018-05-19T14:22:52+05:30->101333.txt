CALANGUTE:   parish priest Fr Cipriano Silva on Friday said flyovers would soon be a necessity in the village with the volume of traffic constantly rising. Speaking an a function in  , he said roads in Calangute, especially those leading to the beach, are choked with traffic during peak tourist season, making life miserable for locals. 

 Local MLA  , who was also present, said the government would consider the suggestion. “If the people are ready, we can build a flyover which will not affect fields or mangroves,” he said.
