CHANDIGARH: The state vigilance bureau on Monday nabbed   inspector Surinder Kumar   red-handed while accepting Rs 80,000 bribe. Sharma is posted at Pungrain purchase centre Ibrahimwal in   district. 

 He was caught on the complaint of Vinod Kumar of TR Agro Industries at Ibrahimwal. The complainant had approached the vigilance bureau stating Sharma was demanding bribe of Rs 1 lakh to clear the pending transportation bills of their firm and the deal was struck at Rs 80,000. The vigilance bureau has registered a case against Sharma under the Prevention of Corruption Act at the vigilance bureau police station in Jalandhar.
