TRICHY: Air intelligence unit of customs at   international airport have seized 780grams of   bars worth Rs 24 lakh from an Air India Express passenger, who arrived here from Dubai during the wee hours on Sunday.

 According to customs officials on duty, they grew suspicious over the activities of a passenger, who was identified as Sahul Hameed, a native of Trichy. When the officials searched his baggage, they found that gold bars hide inside a torch. The yellow metal weighed 780gram which is priced at Rs 24.07 lakh, officials said.
