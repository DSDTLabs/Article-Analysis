 Dharam Tej's career has seen constant progress and recently the actor mentioned that his uncle   has taught him the real definition of victory.

 Pawan Kalyan had mentioned this to Sai after winning an award. He told Sai that when people standby him whenever he faces failures, then is what real victory is. Sai went on to express his love for his fans, and said that fans have been source of support despite his failures.
 
 He also credited his mother and aunt for teaching him the principles of winning and losing.
