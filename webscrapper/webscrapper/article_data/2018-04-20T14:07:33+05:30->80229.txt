RAJKOT: Nanu  , a forest tracker was attacked by a lion in   range of   taluka at   late night on Wednesday. He was admitted to Dhari hospital where his condition is said to be stable.

 Balech was investigating a complaint by mango orchid owner Lalji Savalia that a lion had been camping at his farm since long time.
 
 According to sources, on Wednesday night, lion trackers were trying to trace the lion and shoot it with a tranquillizer gun. While moving in the farm, the lion attacked Balech. Sources said that Balech received injuries on the neck, legs and on the back.
