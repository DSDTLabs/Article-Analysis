 A large cap company, Aditya Birla Capital Limited (ABCL) is the parent company that holds all financial service businesses of the Aditya Birla Group. Life insurance, general insurance broking, asset management, corporate lending, structured finance, private equity, wealth management, equity, currency and commodity broking, online personal finance management, housing finance, pension fund management and health insurance business are the strong fortes of the company. 
 
 The financial service company was incorporated in the year 2007 has a market cap of Rs 40,661.76 Crore.
 
 Aditya Birla Capital limited’s key products include dividend, which contributed Rs 33.48 Crore to its sales value that was 95.69 %. The company’s income from sale of share and securities, which contributed Rs 1.46 Crore to Sales Value as noted at the end of the Financial Year 2016-17.
 
 Formerly known as the Aditya Birla Financial Services Limited (ABFSL), Aditya Birla Capital ranks among the top 5 fund managing companies of India. As of March 2017, the company has assets worth Rs 2,463 billion and a lending book of Rs 388 billion. Over 12,000 employees work with the company and has nearly 1,300 offices all over India.
 
 The Aditya Birla Group was founded by Seth Shiv Narayan Birla and currently Kumar Mangalam Birla is the Chairman of the finance sector major. As on December 31, 2017 the company has a total of 2,200,906,951 shares outstanding.
