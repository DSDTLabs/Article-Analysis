KOZHIKODE: A 54-year-old man was arrested by the police on the charges of   two minor girls. The accused had been sexually abusing the sisters aged 10 and 11 for more than two years, said the police. The police arrested the accused   (54) following the information passed on by the Childline. The incident came to light as the girls unravelled the sexual abuse incident before the Childline during a counselling session. 

 According to police, the accused had been molesting the girls by threatening them. The police have charged the accused under various sections of the protection of children from Sexual Offences Act. The accused was produced before the Kozhikode Principal  . In another incident, an 85 year-old man was arrested by the Vadakara police on the charges of molesting a differently abled minor girl.
