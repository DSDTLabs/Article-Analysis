COIMBATORE: Wholetime director of Bimetal Bearings Ltd S   has been elected as chairman of the Coimbatore   of the Confederation of Indian Industries ( ). 

 Narayanan, who was elected on Saturday, will serve as the chairman for the year 2017-18, according to a statement issued by the CII Coimbatore chapter. 
 
 He was the vice-chairman of CII during 2016-17. 
 
 Past chairman Nethra J S Kumar's tenure ended on Saturday. 
 
 Managing director of Alphacraft Pvt Ltd M Ramesh has been elected as the vice-chairman of the CII Coimbatore zonal council.
