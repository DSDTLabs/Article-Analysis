Vijayawada: To help businesses in goods and services tax (GST) compliance and digital transactions, GST service provider Masters India (MI) has opened 15 ‘suvidha kendras’ across the state, the company said on Wednesday. All the centres have a team of chartered accountants and subject experts to answer the queries, said MI CEO Nishank Goyal. He said all the centres work as business hubs, and offer core B2B services, which were earlier inaccessible to businesses in tier II and tier III cities.
