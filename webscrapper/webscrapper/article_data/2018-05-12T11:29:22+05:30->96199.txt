TIRUPUR: A group of 15 students of a corporation school created awareness on food adulteration at the   collectorate by explaining to the visitors how food is adulterated in various forms. 

 The district   ( ) has engaged them in creating the awareness.
 
 The state FSDA commissionerate has directed the district units to hold such awareness campaigns in popular places. Tirupur district chose the collectorate and the campaign will continue for next 25-30 days, said a food safety officer.
 
 The students were from   Higher Secondary School. The officials provided training to the students on Thursday.
