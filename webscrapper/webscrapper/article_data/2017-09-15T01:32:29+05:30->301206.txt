Ludhiana: In a fraud, Rs 49,000 was withdrawn from an account of a resident of Pawa village in Sahnewal. In his complaint to police, Rakesh Kumar said on August 23 he had gone to withdraw cash from an ATM on GT Road, Sahnewal. There a man helped him withdraw cash, but next day he received a message that someone withdrew Rs 49,000 from his account. The Sahnewal police have registered a case under Section 420 (cheating) of IPC against unidentified accused.
