Actor   married entrepreneur   in a traditional ceremony in Madurai today morning. Soundarraja has acted in a number of films like  , Varuthapadatha Valibar Sangam,Jigarthanda, Dharmadurai, Oru Kanavu Pola and Thiruttupayale 2. Tamanna is the CEO of Green Apple Entertainment.
