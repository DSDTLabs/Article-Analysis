 Two 24-day-old fawn of barking deer, listed under Schedule III of the Wildlife (Protection) Act 1972, were found on December 29, in   in Aheri range, 10km from Allapalli, by a villager Nirmala Aalam. The woman had gone to collect fuelwood.
 
 According to forest officials, the woman waited till evening for the mother to return but as it was getting dark, she lifted the fawns and took them home. Next day forest officials were informed and they too searched for the mother on December 31.
 
 However, as there is no treatment centre at Allapalli or Aheri, on Sunday the team shifted fawns to Gorewada around 1.30pm with a proper request letter from Allapalli deputy conservator (DyCF) Ginni Singh.
