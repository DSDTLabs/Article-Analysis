Actor Tovino Thomas’ impressive screen presence and performances have gradually made him the actor to watch out for among Malayali movie buffs. He has been quite active on social media as well, replying to fans and followers. However, from time-to-time, he has also made sure that their fanfare doesn’t go over the top. 

 Following the warm reception for his film ' ', his social networking page was flooded with compliments. One of them said that the only actor, who will have to bear the brunt of Tovino’s stardom, will be Dulquer Salmaan. Pat came his reply, saying that if by insulting his peers or co-stars, their intention was to praise him to the skies, then he feels no affinity towards such lot.
