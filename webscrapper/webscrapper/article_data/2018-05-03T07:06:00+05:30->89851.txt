Ludhiana: Police have booked a student of Guru Angad Dev Veterinary and Animal Sciences University (GADVASU) and his two accomplices after his classmate accused the trio of thrashing him. 

 The first-year student said in his police complaint, “On April 17, the three thrashed me in Sarabha Nagar market. My classmate had a tiff with me earlier too over friendship with a girl.” 
 
 Sarabha Nagar police station assistant sub-inspector Avtar Singh said, “We are scanning the footage of CCTV cameras installed in the market. It was found during preliminary investigation that a group of some other boys thrashed the complainant. Arrests will be made after a thorough investigation.”
 
 The police have registered a case under sections 323 (punishment for voluntarily causing hurt), 341 (punishment for wrongful restrain), 506 (punishment for criminal intimidation) and 34 (act done by several persons in furtherance of common intention) of the IPC.
