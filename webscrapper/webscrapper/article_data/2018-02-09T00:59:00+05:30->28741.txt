Dharmapuri: Chief minister Edappadi K Palaniswami ordered the Dharmapuri district administration and the public works department (PWD) officials to release water from Varattaru dam for 30 days starting from February 10 for irrigating farmlands in Harur block. 

 About 77.760 cusecs of water would be released for the benefit of about 5,108 acres, said Palaniswami in a press release.
