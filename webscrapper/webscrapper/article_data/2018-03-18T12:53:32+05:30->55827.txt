NAGPUR: Union minister for road transport, highways, shipping and water resources Nitin  , on Saturday, inaugurated the Nagpur Municipal Corporation’s (NMC) first-ever e-library on Saturday. The e-library has been opened by renovating an abandoned school building in Ratan Colony, Chitnavispura, Mahal.

 Mayor Nanda Jichkar, ruling party leader Sandip Joshi, municipal commissioner Ashwin Mudgal, MLA Vikas Kumbhare, deputy mayor  , corporators Sumedha Deshpande, Neha Waghmare, Dharampal Meshram, Pramod Chikhle, Pintu Zelke, Divya Dhurde, Sarika Nandurkar, Rajesh Ghodpage, Bhagwan Mendhe and others were present on the occasion.
 
 Former mayor and corporator Pravin Datke has developed the e-library. Gadkari appreciated the efforts of Datke in facilitating students with modern facilities at the e-library. He suggested the NMC to also develop senior citizens’ recreational centre in the same premises.
 
 The NMC has facilitated computers with Internet services at the e-library. The students can prepare for online examinations and also entrance tests from the e-library. Also, books and reading rooms facilitated at the e-library.
