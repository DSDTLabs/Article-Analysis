The results of   (OU) first and third semester examinations for BA, BCom, Bsc and BBA courses are declared. The examinations were held in November and December 2017. All students who had appeared for BBA, BCom, BA and Bsc Sem 1 and 3 Nov/Dec 2017 examinations can now check the results on the official website. 

 Students must have their hall ticket numbers while accessing their result. Osmania University released the results late evening yesterday. The direct link to check your results is osmania.ac.in. 
 
 
 
 
 1) Visit the official website of Osmania University: osmania.ac.in
 
 2) Under the Examination Section, click on ‘Examination Results’ 
 
 3)On the new window that opens, select the relevant link for the results 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
  
 
 
 
 
 Candidates are requested to note that the marks provided on the website are not equivalent to a mark sheet. Candidates would be required to collect their mark sheet from the University at a later date.
