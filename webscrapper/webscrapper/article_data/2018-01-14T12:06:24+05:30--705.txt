Filmmaker   today took to   to warn people about a fake   account impersonating him. 

 The   director posted a screenshot of the fake Facebook account which used his name and photo.

 Kashyap is known for giving big breaks to young and aspiring actors and has launched careers of celebs such as Nawazuddin Siddiqui,  ,  , Kalki Koechlin and Vineet Kumar Singh.
