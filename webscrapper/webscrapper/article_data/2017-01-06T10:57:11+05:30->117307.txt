PANAJI: With February 4 declared as the polling day for Goa, the ruling   (BJP) said that the party’s election committee will hold its final discussion on selection of candidates on January 8. 

 BJP MP Narendra Sawaikar announced that Prime Minister   would attend   and national party president   will campaign in Goa on January 22 and 23.
 
 Maharashtra CM Devendra Fadnavis, Union ministers and senior leaders such as Rajnath Singh and Venkaiah Naidu, Goa election in charge Nitin Gadkari and Union minister Smriti Irani and former Karnataka CM B S Yedurappa will also be campaigning in Goa, Sawaikar said. 
 
 “Nitin Gadkari will attend around 10 rallies across Goa. Fadnavis will be part of 4 to 5 rallies over two days,” he said.
 
 Sawaikar said that, for the BJP, Congress is the main opposition in the upcoming polls, because “it has the highest number of seats, after the BJP, in the assembly”, dismissing the Aam Admi Party (AAP).
