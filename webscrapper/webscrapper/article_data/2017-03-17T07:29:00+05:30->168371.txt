PATIALA:   (PRTC) chairman   resigned from the post on Thursday, following the change of guard in the state. He was appointed as the   chairman after the death of his father  . 

 Brar was appointed as the PRTC chairman in December 2016, amid strong opposition from political parties who alleged that his appointment after retirement was unconstitutional and amounted to nepotism. It was also alleged that the PRTC authorities had extended benefit to private transport companies. Brar said with the change of government in the state it was best for him to put in his papers.
