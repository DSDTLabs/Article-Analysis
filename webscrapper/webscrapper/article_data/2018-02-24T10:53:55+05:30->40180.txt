The  , Andhra Pradesh has released the hall tickets for first and second year intermediate on the official website. Students who wish to attempt general or vocational course examinations can download it from jnanabhumi.ap.gov.in.

 Examinations for first year will start from February 28 and the second year exams will start from March 2. The practical exams for general intermediate courses got over on February 21.
