Vadodara: Former president Pranab Mukherjee will not be able to physically come to the city to inaugurate ‘Yugaantar’ – a three-day youth conference that he was scheduled to inaugurate on January 11. According to the organisers, Mukherjee will address the national youth conference through a video message.  The organisers said the former president is facing severe health issues because of which his doctors have advised him not to travel. The conference which is being organised by the Institute of Leadership and Governance of MS University will have six sessions on themes of culture, civilization and heritage, spirituality and philosophy, knowledge and education system, leadership, governance and democracy, innovation and environment.  Member of UK’s House of Lords Bhikhu Parekh will attend the inauguration. tnn
