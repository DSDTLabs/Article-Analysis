NEW DELHI:   prices were trading higher by 2.51 per cent to Rs 1,429.50 per kg in futures market today after participants raised bets on rise in demand from consuming industries at the spot market against restricted arrivals from producing regions. 

 At the Multi Commodity Exchange,   oil for delivery in the current month contract was higher by Rs 35, or 2.51 per cent, to Rs 1,429.50 per kg, clocking a business volume of 137 lots. 
 
 The oil for May delivery was trading higher by Rs 17.90, or 1.47 per cent to Rs 1,238 per kg, with a trading volume of 196 lots.
