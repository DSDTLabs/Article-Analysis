LUCKNOW: She is known to change her party ranks frequently.   chief Mayawati has done it again, this time in her list of party’s star campaigners. According to the list of 40 star campaigners submitted by BSP with the  , Mayawati has dropped 35 BSP leaders, including some seniors, as star campaigners.

 However, Mayawati and senior leaders   and SC Mishra will remain party’s star campaigners. The prominent leaders dropped are BSP state president Ram Achal Rajbhar, BSP legislative party leader Gaya Charan Dinkar, party’s Pasi face Indrajeet Saroj, former UP Assembly Speaker Sukhdev Rajbhar, Rajya Sabha MP Munquad Ali, MLA Ramveer Upadhyay, former minister Anant Mishra, former MLC and kin of SC Mishra, Gopal Narain Mishra. All these leaders were party’s star campaigners as per the BSP’s list submitted to the EC in 2014   elections. BSP rebel leaders like Swami Prasad Maurya and former MP Jugal Kishore were also star campaigners in 2014. The new list, not surprisingly, does not have their names.
