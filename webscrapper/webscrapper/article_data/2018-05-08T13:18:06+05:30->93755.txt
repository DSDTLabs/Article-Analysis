NEW DELHI: The Punjab Board Class 12 Vocational stream result 2018 has been released by the board on its official website. The result is available on the official website and India Results website. 

 The students who have appeared for the examination can check their result by using either their examination roll number or their name as registered with the board. 
 
 Follow the step by step guide given below to check your result:
 
 Here's how to check Punjab Board Class 12th Vocational Exam Result 2018:
 
 1) Visit the official website: www.pseb.ac.in
 
 2) Click on the results tab available on the homepage
 
 3) It will redirect you to a new webpage
 
 4) Enter your examination roll number or name and click on submit
 
 5) Your result will be displayed on screen 
 
 6) Download and save it or take a print out of your result for further reference
