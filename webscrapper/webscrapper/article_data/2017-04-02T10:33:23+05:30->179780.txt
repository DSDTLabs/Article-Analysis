 Attributing the continued rise in mercury to the active northerly winds Jaipur Met office said that similar conditions will prevail in the state for the next couple of days. 
 
 Jaipurities woke up to another warmer morning as a minimum temperature of 25.0 degrees Celsius was recorded in the city. It was followed by a sultry afternoon as a maximum temperature of 40.3 degrees Celsius. The roads gave an empty look in the afternoon as people confined themselves in their respective homes and offices. In the evening, people thronged at places selling cold beverages, as many youngsters were seen at the statue circle to have cold coffee. 
 
 The ongoing spell of heat wave has also increased the power consumption which is also responsible for the abrupt power cuts at many localities. Many areas are witnessing power cuts and power failures during late evening hours.
