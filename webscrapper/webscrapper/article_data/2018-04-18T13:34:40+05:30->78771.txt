RAJKOT: Two people looted Rs 40 lakh from a garments showroom at a mall in   in early hours of Tuesday. 

 The duo broke open the showroom shutter in   near the bus stand, went straight to the counter and took away cash. They carried out the loot within 10 minutes between 2:30am and 2:40am. Showroom owner   has lodged a complaint against two unidentified persons.
 
 Police said that two persons are clearly seen in the   footages obtained from the showroom. It is suspected that they were tipped off by some employee of the showroom. There are 17 persons employed at the showroom.
