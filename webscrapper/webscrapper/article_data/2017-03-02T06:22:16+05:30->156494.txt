HYDERABAD: An 18-month-old boy died and his grandmother suffered injuries when a speeding bike rammed into them at   on the city outskirts on Tuesday night.

 The deceased was identified as Santosh from   village. On Tuesday, Santosh’s grandmother Maria, 55, took him to the   government hospital. On their way back home, a speeding bike rammed into them on   road. “The bike rider, Srisailam, a farmer, rammed his vehicle into them while trying to avoid a tractor coming from the opposite direction,” Shadnagar inspector V Ramakrishna said.
 
 With critical head injuries, Santosh died on the spot. Maria is said to be out of danger. Srisailam was arrested and charged under sections 304-A and 337 of the IPC.
