The crowd at Yamuna Sports Complex couldn't keep calm at the Sadbhawna Diwas 2017 celebration, which had rappers   and singers SukhE and   singing popular numbers. The cast of ' ' too turned up to pay tribute to Shaheed   at the event.

 Raftaar's power-packed rapping was followed by SukhE's popular dance numbers, while singer Ashok Mastie gave a Sufi touch to the event. Harmeet Singh, one of the audience members, shared, "Maza aa gya aaj toh. Pehle rap songs then apne Punjabi gaane aur Sufi bhi saath mein. Even the policemen are enjoying a lot. Sama baandh diya aaj toh."
 
 
 
  
  Ashok Mastie
 
 
 
 Anil Kapoor and Arjun Kapoor danced to some songs from the film. On a fun note, Arjun said, "Ji main hu chacha aur yeh hai mera young bhateeja." Anil said, "Kaunsa gana sunna chahoge, hun toh sari raat gaana hai." When he started singing Mera rang de basanti chola holding the tricolour, everyone joined in. The event ended with the National Anthem.
