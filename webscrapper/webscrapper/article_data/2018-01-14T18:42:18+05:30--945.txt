Going a step ahead , audiences will see popular Marathi show   on an international tour. Show was earlier on Maharashtra tour under which the episodes were shot in different cities of the state. The entire team including the director Dr Nilesh Sable,   Bugade, Bhau Kadam,   Ganeshpure,   Karande and   Badrike travelled to London for the same. The team also went to Japan and Paris. 

  CHYD is one of the most watched Marathi television shows. In fact, along with top Marathi artistes, many Bollywood celebrities have appeared on the show to promote their films and causes including Shah Rukh Khan, Aamir Khan, Akshay Kumar etc.
