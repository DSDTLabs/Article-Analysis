SURAT: A 38-year-old man, who was father to three daughters, died on Monday under suspicious circumstance when he fell from the second floor of a building. 

 Pratap Uday Jena, an employee of a textile weaving unit, fell to his death from the second floor of   building located opposite Akhand Anand College. 
 
 Jena was a native of   district of Odisha. Police suspect that he had gone to steal something when he fell. His relatives told police they are not aware why he had gone to that building.
