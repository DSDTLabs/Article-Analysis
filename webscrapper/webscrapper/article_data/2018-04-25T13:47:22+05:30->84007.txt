NEW DELHI: The   ( ) Class 12 Economics board exam retest is being conducted today, April 25, 201815, at various test centres across India. 

 
 The duration of the exam is of three hours. It begins at 10:30 am and ends at 1:30 pm. 
 
 The students get 15 minutes to read the question paper from 10:15 am to 10:30 am.
 
 The retest of the Economics exam is being held due to the alleged leaks of the paper. In the light of arrests made by the   and following investigations, the CBSE had decided to hold the exam again. 
 
 A total number of 11,86,306 students have registered for class 12 exams this year. The exam is being held at 4,138 centres in India and 71 centres abroad.
 
 Most of the students were happy and expecting good results after appearing in the Economic exam that was held on March 26, 2018. 
 
 Now students, teachers as well as parents are bit worried and anxious as they fear that this time the board might set tough paper as compared to the previous one.
