BHOPAL: Two railway staffers died after being hit by a train in  's Sagar district, an official said on Saturday.

 The deceased were working with the railway's signal maintenance wing, West Central Railway's   division public relation officer I A Siddiqui said.
 
 The two men were engaged in the signal repair work last night when the Hazrat Nizamuddin-Chennai Central Rajdhani Express ran over them at Bina, about 150 kms from here, he said.
