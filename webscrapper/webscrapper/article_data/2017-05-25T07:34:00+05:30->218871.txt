AMRITSAR: Amritsar   member   Aujla clarified that the kirpan ban in Italy was only in case of an individual and not for the entire Italian Sikh community as was being propagated. 

 Aujla on Wednesday met deputy head of mission Luigi Estero and first secretary, political affairs   at embassy of Italy in New Delhi to discuss the kirpan ban judgement of  . He said he was informed by the Italian officials that the judgement of Italian court applied only on the individual involved in the case. 
 
 He added that he had submitted a memorandum to the Italian officials in this regard.
