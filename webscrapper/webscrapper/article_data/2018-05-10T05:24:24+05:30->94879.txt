AHMEDABAD:   has declared that admissions for Bachelor of Science in colleges affiliated to it will done online. The admission process will begin from May 16.

 GU authorities said that the PIN for online admissions will be available till May 25. Students will be required to get their documents verified and mark their college choices till May 25.
 
 GU will declare the provisional merit list on May 28 and the final merit list will be declared on May 31.
