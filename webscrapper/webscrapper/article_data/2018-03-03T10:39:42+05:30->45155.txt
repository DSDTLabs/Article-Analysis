ITANAGAR: Amid agitation by students, Union HRD Minister   has assured to issue appointment order of NERIST permanent director,   Minister Honchung   said.

 Ngandam said Javadekar informed him over telephone last evening.
 
 The president of the agitating students of the North Eastern Regional   of Science & Technology (NERIST), Banta Natung said their stir would continue till they were given proof of the appointment order.
 
 The students have launched an agitation from February 27 at the NERIST entrance gate with eight on hunger strike since March one.
 
 Natung said the agitation has been launched as the Institute's academic standard was hit after departure of last director Deepankar Pal in October 2014.
