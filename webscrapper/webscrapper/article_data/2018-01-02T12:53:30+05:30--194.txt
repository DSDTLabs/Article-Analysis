THANJAVUR: Two people, including a veterinary doctor, died on the spot when a   State Transport Corporation   hit a two-wheeler they were travelling on near Papanasam in Thanjavur district on Tuesday morning.

 The deceased have been identified as Parthiban, 38, who was an assistant government veterinary doctor, and Srinivasa Prabhu, 45. The accident happened when they were going to   from Thanjavur.
 
 The Papanasam police arrested the bus driver on charges of causing death due to rash driving. 
 
 Further investigations were on.
