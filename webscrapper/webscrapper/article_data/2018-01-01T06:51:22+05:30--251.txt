BENGALURU: Around 50 residents from Indiranagar and   gathered in front of a pub on 12th Main Road, Indiranagar to pay homage to the victims of last week's pub fire tragedy in Mumbai. 

  The pub chosen for demonstration is among the 12 buildings housing rooftop hubs that have been issued notice for fire safety violations in the wake of the Mumbai fire. 
 
  Pillai, president-Indiranagar  , said they are concerned about the safety of rooftop establishments. "We're not against celebrations. But we don't want the city to witness fire incidents similar to Mumbai, which claimed the lives of at least 14 revellers," he said.
