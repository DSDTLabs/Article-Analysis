AMRITSAR: Police here have arrested a 25-year-old man for allegedly raping a 12-year-old girl over a period of "several days" and threatening her with murder if she spoke about her ordeal to anyone. 

  The girl, an orphan, lives with her grandmother here and the accused, Sikander, is a tenant in the same house. The case first came to light when the girl informed the mother of a friend about pain in her stomach. The woman got suspicious about the girl's wellbeing and informed her husband, who then complained to the police. Amritsar police commissioner SS Srivastava confirmed the arrest.
