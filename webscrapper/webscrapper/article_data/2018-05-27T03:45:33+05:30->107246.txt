AHMEDABAD: Members of Muslim community on Saturday submitted an application of complaint to   police station about a social media post, which contain defamatory material against the community, they have alleged. Senior police officers from the police commissioner’s office said they have asked the cyber cell of the city crime branch to aid local police in locating the accused named in the application of complaint.

 Farhan Ansari, associated with a religious institution, said that the comments were posted by the accused from May 5 to May 11. “The comments have hurt the sentiments of the Muslim community. We want strict action against the accused,” added Ansari.
 
 ACP, M K Rana, said they have instructed the local police to act on the application of complaint and take action against the accused as soon as possible.
