The wait for the much anticipated trailer of ‘Baahubali 2’ is finally over. The power-packed trailer of the film has many emotional, heavy-duty glimpses from the movie to excite hardcore fans of the franchise. 

 
 
 Also, fans might finally get an answer to why Kattappa killed Baahubali, in this film. The film will also have the complete romantic track of Devasena and Bahubali, which was only hinted in the first film. 
 
 Prabhas, Rana Daggubati, Anushka Shetty and Tamannah are in the lead roles of the movie. If social media reactions are any indication, T’town fans have already circled in on the expected release date of ‘Baahubali 2’ on their calendars. 
 
 
 
 
 Directed by S.S. Rajamouli, the movie releases on April 28.
