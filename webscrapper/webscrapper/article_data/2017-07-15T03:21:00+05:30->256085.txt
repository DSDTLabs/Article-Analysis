Margao: Following a complaint pertaining to the suspected pilferage of LPG (cooking gas) cylinders, the state legal   department has undertaken a drive to check the weight of cylinders stocked in the delivery vehicles of distributors. On Friday, a team of personnel from the department checked vehicles of two distributors at its Margao office. 

 A similar drive was conducted in Panaji and Mapusa on Thursday, it was informed. Official sources said action would be taken against the distributors if any pilferage was detected upon checking the weight of the cylinders. tnn
