Rajkot: A 50-year-old man from Jasdan taluka of Rajkot has tested positive for Crimean-Congo haemorrhagic fever (CCHF) virus. 

 Rajkot chief district health officer (CDHO) Dr V H Pathak said that the man had come to the Rajkot civil hospital on July 21 from Shivrajpur village of Jasdan with high fever. 
 
 “His samples were sent for testing for diseases including CCHF virus. Pathak said that the test results, which arrived on Tuesday evening, stated that he was positive for CCHF virus.
 
 Sources in the civil hospital said that the man’s condition is stable and he was under observation in its isolation ward. tnn
