CHANDIGARH: Two minor fire incidents were reported in the city on Wednesday. No one was injured and the   of the MC managed to douse the flames in time. The first incident took place near the  , adjoining Mauli Jagran. Fire broke out in a godown where wooden material was stored.

 The fire wing of the MC received a call at around 11:15am. Two fire tenders were rushed to the spot and the flames were controlled in around half an hour. Electric material, including fans, cooler and others kept here were gutted in the fire. 
 
 The second fire incident occurred at the industrial area lightpoint in Sector 26, where grass caught fire. The fire department managed to douse the flames in 15 minutes. The fire wing also received cases of dry leaves catching fire.
