 The   charges applicants for booking the centres for an occasion but there have been instances when the applicants ‘forget’ to cancel the booking on time in case the event/wedding is cancelled. The Noida Authority has now decided on slabs for the refund of security amount depending on the time period within which the booking is cancelled.
 
 According to officials, 10% of the security will be deducted in case the booking is cancelled between 30-60 days before the reserved date, 25% if it is cancelled within 15-30 days and 75% in case booking is cancelled within 7-15 days. tnn
