 According to the statement of objects and reasons in the bill, in order to augment the revenue to the state, the government has decided to amend the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006, for making a provision to levy additional tax with retrospective effect from April 1, 2016, on the taxable turnover of the intra-state sale of   of all kinds of human consumption.
 
 “The amount from the additional tax will be deducted from the payment to be received from manufacturing units,” the official said. The government has also decided to make a provision in the Act to notify different rates of additional tax for different types of alcoholic liquors, the statement added.
 
 The statement added that provision for levy of additional tax on goods is available in VAT Acts of other states such as Gujarat, Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand and Madhya Pradesh. However, there is no such provision for levy of additional tax on goods in the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006, the statement said.
 
 The government also moved a bill to amend the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006 to increase the rate of tax on petrol with or without additives to 34% (from 30%) and on High Speed Diesel Oil to 25% (from 23.43%).
