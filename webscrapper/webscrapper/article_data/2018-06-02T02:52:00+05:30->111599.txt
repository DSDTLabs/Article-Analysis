Yavatmal: A 60-year-old woman was killed on the spot while her 24-year-old son was grievously injured after a truck (MH 29/T-1689) dashed against the two-wheeler they were riding on near Mohpa village on Friday. Ironically, the duo was going to attend a funeral at Babhulgaon when the mishap took place in the afternoon. The deceased has been identified as Malabai Subhashrao Raut, a resident of local Singaniya Nagar. Her son Santosh suffered critical injuries and has been shifted to Nagpur Government Medical College and Hospital. Yavatmal police has registered a case against the truck-driver and arrested him.
