The new poster of 'Baahubali: The Conclusion' is out and guess what, like we already know, the posters are going viral over social media. The director, S S Rajamouli released a poster image featuring actors Prabhas and Anushka Shetty as- Amarendra Baahubali and Devasena in all four languages- Telugu, Tamil, Malayalam and Hindi, on his Facebook account. The poster showcases the lead actors aiming arrows from bows. Since shooting of the movie is all done, the makers are racing ahead with post-production works to meet the deadline. The movie is set for a theatrical release on April 28.
