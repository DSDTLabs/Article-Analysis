Rajkot: Tatachem Dav Public School in Mithapur town has entered the Limca Book of Records for its ‘save birds’ campaign. An event to write maximum number of slogans for awaerness to save birds was organized on August 28 last year. In two hours, 1,215 participants wrote 8,999 slogans, breaking the previous record of 5,750 slogans in five hours. 

 The event was organized in presence of district forest officer R. Dhanpal, in-charge of fisheries research department N H Joshi and coast guard commandant Harish More. On conclusion of the event, participants and number of slogans were counted in the presence of verification authority. tnn
