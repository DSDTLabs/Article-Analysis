Vadodara: The number of schools in Vadodara district which have secured 100% results have witnessed a significant improvement this year. 

 Last year, there were only three schools in the district which had achieved 100% results. This year, there is almost a four-fold rise in such schools as a total of 12 schools have bagged 100% results. Ironically, there is also an increase in number of schools which have shown dismal performance. 
 
 Last year, there was not a single school in the district which had zero percent result. This year, however, there are seven schools in the district which have got zero percent result. Similarly, the number of schools with less than 30% results have also increased. tnn
