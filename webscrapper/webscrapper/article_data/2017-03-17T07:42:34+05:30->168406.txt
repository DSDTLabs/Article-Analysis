BHOPAL: Madhya Pradesh Human Rights Commission (MPHRC) on Thursday took cognisance of the incident wherein a 3-year-old girl died after a quack administered her an injection in   district. 

 The commission has sought a report from the   and asked the district collector regarding action against the quack. The incident took place in   area of Satna. The minor got injured after being hit by a bicycle in   village. Her family took her to a quack, BD Singh, who administered her injection. However, she died soon after. 
 
 The commission has asked for a detailed report into the incident. The commission stated that a number of people have died due to treatment by quacks in the state. Officials said a letter has been sent to district collectors, superintendents and chief medical health officers so that such incidents could be curbed.
