Vadodara: Angry residents of Valakhedi village near Santrampur torched a truck after it rammed into bike, killing one of the spot. Vijay Patel and his friend Haridas Patel were going on the former’s bike when a truck carrying bricks hit Patel. While Haridas received minor injuries, Vijay died on the spot. When the news of the incident spread in the village, mob of people rushed to the accident site and burnt the truck. The truck driver fled from the spot. A a team of local crime branch (LCB) and Santrampur police rushed to the spot to control the situation. Deputy superintendent of Mahisagar police also rushed to Valakhedi village. After the situation was brought under comtrol, the police took the custody of Vijay’s body and sent it to government hospital for post-mortem.
