UDHAGAMANDALAM: The district administration has declared May 18 — the day when the 122nd annual flower show would be inaugurated at the   ( ) in Ooty — as a local holiday.

 The decision was taken to ease traffic as tourist flow will be high during the three-day show. Government establishments, schools and colleges would be closed. However, government treasury and essential services will be working as usual, said the administration in a press release. May 26 (Saturday) will be a working day to compensate for the special holiday.
 
 The show is likely to be inaugurated by chief minister Edappadi K Palaniswami. tnn
