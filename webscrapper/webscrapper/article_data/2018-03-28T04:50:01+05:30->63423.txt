MUMBAI: Prime Minister  's proposal for simultaneous Lok Sabha and state assembly polls gained momentum on Tuesday following a high-level two member committee set up by chief minister   on Tuesday. 

 The committee headed by finance minister Sudhir Mungantiwar, former chief secretary and ex-chief electoral officer D K Sankaran will be the other member. During Sankaran's tenure, offences for violation of model code of conduct were registered against opposition members and ministers.
 
 The idea of   was mooted by Modi, it was supported by President Ram Nath Kovind. Addressing the joint session of parliament, Kovind said that frequent polls were burden on the human resources and hampered the process of development due to model code of conduct. No time frame has been fixed for submitting the report.
