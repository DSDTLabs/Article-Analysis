HYDERABAD: The   ’18 programme—a unique zero-fee and zero-equity student acceleration   aimed at fostering entrepreneurship kicked off in the city with a cohort of 10  , on Monday.

 
 Through the four-week programme which comes at zero cost, student entrepreneurs can learn various aspects--from building a product to mastering the lean startup methods. This edition of startup school is being conducted by pan-India startup hub   in partnership with 26 organisations including prominent investment. The second cohort will begin in Lucknow from 12th of June.
 
 Radha  , CEO of iB Hubs said, “Students have tremendous energy and enthusiasm to create innovative solutions; But, they require right guidance for their innovations to flourish as a startup. At iB Hubs Startup School, we equip them with essential knowledge to build a successful startup.”
