 The snow pellets that rained during the hailstorm caused damages to standing wheat, sugarcane and maize crops in many north   districts. Litchi and mango blossoms also sustained damages.
 
 An all-India weather bulletin issued by the   on Saturday evening forecast that isolated thunderstorm accompanied with thunder squall might occur in Jharkhand, sub-Himalayan West Bengal, Sikkim, Assam, Meghalaya, Bihar and Odisha during the next 48 hours.
 
 Addressing a press conference in Patna on Saturday, Amrit said CM Nitish Kumar had already ordered payment of Rs 4 lakh as ex gratia to the next kin of every deceased.
