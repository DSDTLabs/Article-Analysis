Karan Johar’s directorial ' ' became one of the most iconic films of Indian cinema and also became one of lead actor Shah Rukh Khan’s defining roles. Now, almost two decades after the film’s release, its impact can still be felt worldwide and this video is proof.

  
  
 Taking to Twitter, Karan shared an incredible video of the Stuttgart Chamber orchestra performing the titular song from the movie. In the short video clip, it is clear that the audience too is familiar with the iconic tune as they start clapping and cheering as soon as it starts playing. The filmmaker was of course overwhelmed by this tribute as he tweeted, “Was so excited and honoured to see this!!!!! #KuchKuchHotaHai.”
