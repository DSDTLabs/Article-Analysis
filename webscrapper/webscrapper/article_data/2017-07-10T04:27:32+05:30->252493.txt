Noida: A 25-year-old labourer fell to death from the ninth floor of an under-construction building in Gaur   City on the   on Sunday morning. 

 According to police, Rajinder Singh was working at the construction site when the incident took place. He accidently slipped and fell from the ninth floor of the building around 11am. 
 
 The police arrived at the spot only around 3pm after Singh’s father informed them about his son’s death.
 
 “We have sent the body for postmortem. No complaint has been registered in the matter,” Rajveer Singh Chauhan, SHO of Rabupura police station, told TOI.
 
 He added, “No senior person from the builders’s side was present at the spot as it was a Sunday.”
