Margao: A delegation of residents of Margao on Thursday submitted a memorandum to the chairperson, Margao municipal council, Babita Angle Prabhudessai, demanding that all footpaths in the city be cleared of all encroachments and kept free for pedestrian movement, more particularly the footpaths and areas around comunidade building. The residents said encroachments on footpaths by the vendors impeded free movement of the public. tnn
