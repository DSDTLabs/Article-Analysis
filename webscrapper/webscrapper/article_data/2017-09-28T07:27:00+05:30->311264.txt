Himachal Pradesh power minister Sujan Singh Pathania was admitted to a hospital in Shimla on Wednesday after he suffered a brain hemorrhage. Indira Gandhi Medical College and Hospital Medical Superintendent Ramesh Chand told IANS that Pathania's condition is stated to be stable but critical. He is undergoing treatment in the intensive care unit. Six-time legislator Pathania was in the office here when he suffered the paralytic attack. IANS
