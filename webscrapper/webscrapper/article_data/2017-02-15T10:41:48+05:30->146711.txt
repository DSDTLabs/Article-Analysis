JALANDHAR:   is all set to honour Bihar CM   for the "wonderful and flawless" functions organized in Patna to mark the 350th birth anniversary of the 10th Sikh master, Guru Gobind Singh. 

 "All were amazed with the functions. Sikhs from across the country and even abroad who could attend these functions were all praise for Bihar chief minister. SGPC has decided to honour him and a few top officials of his government who were part of the team that made the flawless arrangements," said SGPC president Kirpal Singh Badungar at Guru Teg Bahadur Public School at Hazaara village in Jalandhar.
 
 "I stayed there for a week. It was not only I who felt that the Bihar government did its best for organizing the grand function, even devotees were all praise for Nitish Kumar, the official machinery and locals there," he said. 
 
 "We have already informed Nitish about our wish to honour him, and only the scheduled is to be worked out. He will be honoured at Golden Temple during a specially organized function," Badungar told TOI.
