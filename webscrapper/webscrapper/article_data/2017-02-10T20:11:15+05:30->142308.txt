Singam 3

 
 Surya, Anushka Shetty, Shruti Haasan, Radha Ravi, Mehaboob Nassar, Janaki Sabesh, Raadhika Sarathkumar
 
 
  Hari Gopalakrishnan Nadar
 
 
 09 Feb, 2017
 
 
  2 hrs 36 mins
 
 
  Singam 3 is a Tamil action-masala film written and directed by Hari. A sequel to Singam II and the third film in the Singam franchise, it stars Suriya, Anushka Shetty and Shruti Haasan in the lead roles. Principal photography of the film commenced in January 2016 in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. The film released worldwide on 9 February 2017 along with the Telugu dubbed version, Yamudu 3.
