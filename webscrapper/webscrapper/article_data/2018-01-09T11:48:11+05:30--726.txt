MALAPPURAM: Two students were killed and seven others got injured when a truck ran over a   at   in Nilambur in the district on Tuesday morning.

 One of the deceased has been identified as Fida, 14, class IX student of CK Higher Secondary School Manimooli. The other one is yet to be identified by police. 
 
 The injured have been admitted to a private hospital in Edakkara and Nilambur Taluk hospital.
 
 According to police, the accident occurred around 9.30 AM, when the truck carrying coconuts, coming from Karnataka, rammed into a motorbike and autorikshaw before hitting a group of students, who were walking to school on a footpath.
 
 The accident happened around 200 metres away from the school.
