KOLKATA: An   allegedly committed suicide at his house in   around 8.15am on Tuesday. A resident of Usthi in South 24-Parganas, Kanak Kumar Ghosh (43), was found hanging in his room, police said.

 Ghosh’s brother went to check on him on Monday, as he was not answering calls from his relatives, but returned without any response. Next morning, suspicious neighbours called the police. “A suicide note was recovered but Ghosh has not held anybody responsible for his death. No complaint of foul play has been made so far,” said DC (Behala), Meeraz Khalid.
