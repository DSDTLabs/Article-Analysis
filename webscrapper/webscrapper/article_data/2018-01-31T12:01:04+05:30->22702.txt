VARANASI: The Government Railway Police (GRP) caught a youth with 13kg gold worth of crores of rupees at   in   on Tuesday. The GRP took him under custody after he could not produce relevant documents in support of gold. The matter was informed to the Directorate of Revenue  .

 
 According to reports, during a checking drive at platform number-6 the GRP found gold in the bag of one Mahendra Vajpayee, resident of Kanpur. He was travelling from Kolkata to Kanpur with the consignment of gold..
 
 The   GRP inspector RK Singh told TOI over phone that the youth was detained for interrogation. He was taking the gold worth of Rs 4 crore from Kolkata to Mumbai, singh said adding that the DRI and custom personnel arrived after getting information, and they are investigating the matter.
