Namakkal: A 65-year-old man died after he was knocked down by a bike at Kollapatty on Tiruchengode – Kokkarayanpet state highway on Wednesday. The Tiruchengode police have registered case against a teenage boy who rode the bike. 

 The victim has been identified as K Muthusamy, 65, of Andipalayam near Tiruchengode. The 16-year-old boy was riding his father’s bike as it was summer holidays. The boy’s father K Selvam, 48, of Ujjampalayam, may also face charges. 
 
 On Wednesday afternoon, the boy was riding the bike as usual and knocked Muthusamy down at Kollapatty. “The victim succumbed to his head injuries on the spot,” police said.
 
 The victim’s son M Marimuthu lodged a complaint with the Tiruchengode town police. “We have booked the teenage boy under sections 279 (rash driving or riding on a public way) and 304 (A) (causing death by negligence) and also under the Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicle Act of 1980. 
 
 Recently, the state government warned of penal action against parents of minors who are caught riding or driving vehicles on the road.
