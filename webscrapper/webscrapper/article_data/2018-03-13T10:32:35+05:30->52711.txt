NEW DELHI: State-owned defence manufacturer Bharat Dynamics opened its Initial Public Offering (IPO) for subscription on Tuesday. The IPO will close on March 15.

 
 The IPO offers 2,24,51,953 equity shares for sale at a price band of Rs 413-Rs 428 per share. The company has reserved a portion of 4,58,203 equity shares for allotment to eligible employees. Employees and retail investors will also get a discount of Rs 10 per share on offer price.
 
 The IPO is a part of the disinvestment programme of the government and is an offer for sale ( ). Hence, the company will not receive any proceeds form the IPO.
 
 The book running lead managers to the offer are   Capital Markets, IDBI Capital Markets & Securities and YES Securities (India).
