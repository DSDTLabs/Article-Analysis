Rajkot: The Kodinar police reunited a nine-year-boy who had fled from his uncle's house in Bagodar village near Porbandar. During patrolling on Thursday night, 

 the police team noticed a boy loitering around alone at the Kodinar state transport bus depot. 
 
 When they talked with him, the boy identified himself as Paresh Bambhaniya from Madhya Pradesh's Risov village. Police got to know that he was living with his uncle Tulsi ever since his father died and his mother fled with someone. They called Tulsi to Junagadh juvenile house where the nine-year-old was handed over to him after proper verification. tnn
