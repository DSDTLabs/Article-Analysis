MUMBAI: The   (AIU)  , on Sunday, intercepted passengers in two separate incidents at the international airport and seized crude gold that was found hidden with the passengers in portable car cleaning machine and mixer grinder respectively. Collectively, the AIU officials has seized 3,008 grams of crude gold worth Rs 81.4 lakh.

 Acting on a tip off, the AIU officer intercepted one Irfan bhai Rana who was carrying three gold pieces worth Rs 54.1 lakh concealed in central part of portable car washing machine. In the second case, the official intercepted passenger Rashid Manikoth having with two pieces of crude gold worth Rs 27.3 lakhs in a mixture grinder.
