GANDHINAGAR:   BJP has planned a yearlong celebration to mark four years of Prime Minister Narendra Modi-led   government at the Centre. The programmes will start from May 26. 

 State BJP president  , party general secretary   took part in a meeting chaired by the BJP national president   in New Delhi on Monday where they were instructed to chalk out a year-long plan to celebrate the occasion. 
 
 Sources privy to development said that the party will soon announce the schedule of the events.
