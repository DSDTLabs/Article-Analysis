MUMBAI: Police busted an online   in   and arrested six people, including the owner and conductor of the lottery centre on Tuesday. 

 Police said the lottery centre was misusing its licence. Shop owner   and conductor   scribbled the number selected by a customer on a paper. 
 
 If the number matched the one flashed online, the customer was declared winner, given the prize money and the paper destroyed to evade tax.
