DUMKA: Ahead of   Droupadi Murmu's visit to SKM University on the occasion of the   on January 20, the   student group has accused vice-chancellor Qumar Ahsan of corruption. 

 ''Large scale financial irregularities are being carried out by the university administration at the cost of the academic interests of the students,"  , town secretary of ABVP, said.
 
 In a memorandum sent to the governor in Raj Bhawan, the students said, "On one hand, students are being suppressed by unnecessarily implicating them in cases, on the other hand, the university has turned out to be the epicentre of corruption."
 
 The memorandum also referred to the alleged financial irregularities in the purchase of furniture, books, air conditioners and different work of the examination department.
