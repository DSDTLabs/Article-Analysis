A few years ago Sathyan Anthikad had revealed that he is planning to make a film with Fahadh Faasil in the lead again, and the film will be titled 'Malayali.'

 But now, realizing that the industry earlier had a film with the same title, he has decided to go for a change in the name. It's through his social media page that the director shared the decision with the industry. 
  Fahadh plays a youngster named P R Akash, who later changes his name through gazette to Prakash, in this film. The movie will have script by  , but the team has not announced the rest of the cast of the movie yet. It's the director's second movie with Fahadh, after the hit film 'Oru Indian Pranayakadha.'
