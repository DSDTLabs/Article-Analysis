Vadodara: Vadodara Municipal Corporation (VMC) will launch its Anaemia Free Vadodara campaign on International Women's Day on Wednesday. VMC will be conduct a screening of one lakh women for the purpose. Anaemic women will be provided treatment at its urban health centres under National Iron Plus Initiative guidelines. The VMC will also launch an initiative to treat moderately acute malnourished children by providing them supplementary food Balamul.TNN
