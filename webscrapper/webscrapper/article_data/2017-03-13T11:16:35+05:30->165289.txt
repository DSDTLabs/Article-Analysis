CALANGUTE: Around 25 tourists had a miraculous escape after the boat they were on overturned due to strong winds at the   harbour on Saturday afternoon.

 The boat left the   jetty at around 2.30pm on Saturday with around 25 tourists. The mishap occurred at around 3pm.
 
 Sources say some influential persons have been illegally conducting boat trips from Sinquerim jetty to   island off Mormugao harbour. The boat, which overturned, belongs to one of these operators.
