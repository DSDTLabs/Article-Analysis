TRICHY: A 40-day-old baby girl was found abandoned inside the government hospital at   in   on Saturday. The police were on the lookout for the parents of the baby.

 The baby was spotted lying on the ground near a moped in the hospital around 11 am on Saturday by a nurse. The nurse took her to the government hospital outpost police. However, they could not find anyone related to the baby and handed her over to the hospital authorities. 
 
 The doctors checked the health condition of the baby and confirmed that she was in fine health. The sub-inspector of the outpost,   said that the baby must have been brought from outside and left inside the hospital. Probe is on.
