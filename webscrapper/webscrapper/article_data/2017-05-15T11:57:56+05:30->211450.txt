SURAT: Officials of metrology department of Union Territory of   sealed a nozzle of a petrol pump in   of Daman after some customers complained of getting less petrol from it on Sunday.

 The officials conducted a sudden check at   petrol pump. They found 20ml less petrol for every 10 litre sold from a particular nozzle which was sealed. The pump has four nozzles. The officials also conducted checks at 24 other petrol pumps located in Moti Daman, Nani Daman and Kachigam. 
 
 “About 13ml less petrol for 10 litre is acceptable but not this much. We would be conducting investigations into this,” an officer said.
