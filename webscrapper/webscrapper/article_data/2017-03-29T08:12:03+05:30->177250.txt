ALLAHABAD: UP Madhyamik Siksha Parishad ( ) cancelled class X and XII examinations of several subjects held at 32 centres, of which 10 have been debarred for facilitating mass cheating, as the Board's WhatsApp service to report cheating crashed hours after its launch on Tuesday. Re-examination of cancelled papers would be held in the non-debarred centres, the schedule of which would be announced soon. 

 Allahabad had the most centres, 10, cancelled followed by six in sensitive districts of Azamgarh and five in Aligarh. 
 
 In most of the centres, invigilators were found to be dictating answers to students while in others, guardians or relatives were found to be providing cheating material to those taking class X and XII exams.
