ZIRAKPUR: Residents of   held a protest against the municipal council for not providing them proper garbage disposal in the town. Residents of various societies started the protest from   dhabha till   office. 

 They raised slogans against the MC authorities for not having garbage disposal in the area. From past one week, the door to door collection was also stopped by the sanitation staff as they do not have space to dispose.
 
 Residents who carry garbage bags dispose it of in MC office. The sanitation association also joined them in the protest.
 
 The president of association   said that if the authorities will not act they will jam the roads on May 1, 2018. Residents demanded that MC should provide them basic services as they are the tax payers.
