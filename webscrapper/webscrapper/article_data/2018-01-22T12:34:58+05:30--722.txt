VADODARA: A   in congested   near   caught fire on Sunday night. The fire also spread to two houses above the shop.

 The fire broke out in the shop around 9.15pm. Fire department sources said that two cops who were passing by the market saw the fire and informed them. Three fire engines from   fire station were rushed to the spot which remains crowded throughout the day.
 
 When the shop was caught in flames, two families residing above it felt the heat emanating from the fire and quickly moved out of the old building. Firemen faced some difficulty as the accident had created panic among the locals. As a result, there was chaos in the narrow lane. However, firefighters managed to bring the fire under control within 45 minutes.
