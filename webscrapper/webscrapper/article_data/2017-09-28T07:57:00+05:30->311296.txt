NOIDA: A man has filed a complaint against his ‘business partner’ saying that the latter had tried to lure his wife into a physical relationship. The complainant, who identified himself as a ‘motivational speaker,’ lives with his wife in Indirapuram, Ghaziabad.

 The complainant said that he had launched a joint business with one Suneel Yadav, a resident of Delhi. The duo started an office named – Vedic Shakti – in Sector 63. However, Yadav later expressed his desire to make physical relations with his wife saying he was her husband in previous life, he alleged.
 
 Akhilesh Pradhan, SHO Phase III police station, said that based on the compliant, an FIR has been registered. “We are investigating the matter,” he said.
