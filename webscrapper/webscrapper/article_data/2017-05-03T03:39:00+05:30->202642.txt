Panaji: Shooting of a film cause a huge traffic jam, near a mansion in Goa Velha, for two consecutive days. Confusion prevailed due to the entry of vehicles and people on the NH 66 (NH 17).

 Vehicles from the venue of the shooting, not far from the Siridao bridge, kept entering the highway, disrupting the flow of traffic.
 
 Motorists alleged that curious tourists and others also added to the confusion, triggering chaos on the highway. The long queue of vehicles, stalled on the highway, stretched far and it took more than 15 minutes for them to crawl out of the congestion. Motorists complained that the police at the spot were not helpful. But an official at the Agasaim police station said the traffic jam lasted a few minutes only.
