Mumbai: State chief information commissioner  , in a suo-motu order, has directed all government departments and local self-government bodies to upload the information regarding various   schemes implemented by them in public interest and names of beneficiaries selected under them on their websites by April 30 “without fail”.

  activist   had filed a complaint with the information commission that beneficiaries of various government and civic welfare schemes were either not aware of them or don’t know the eligibility criteria and the procedure to avail the benefits of the schemes.
