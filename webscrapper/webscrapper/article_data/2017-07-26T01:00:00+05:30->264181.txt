ARA: The Bhojpur police on Tuesday attached the   of former spokesperson of HAM(S) Danish Rizwan, situated at Tari Mohalla under Town PS. 

 Police said Rizwan is a named accused in Krishna Singh murder case and is absconding. Singh, a Ara trader was murdered on July 2, allegedly over a land dispute. 
 
 Sources said out of the 10 named accused, only two have been arrested so far while one has surrendered before the court. Sadar SDPO Sanjay Kumar and a large number of policemen were present during property attachment. TNN
