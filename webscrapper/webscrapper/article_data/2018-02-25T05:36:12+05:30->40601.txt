 The victim, Sai Kiran, a resident of Maruthi Nagar, reportedly passed a comment on the PET while conversing with his friends. 
 
 "However, one of the students went to the teacher and told him about the remarks. Kumar hit the student with a stick," Chaitanyapuri sub-inspector G Eroji said. 
 
 According to police, the victim went home and told his parents about the incident. They approached police on Friday night. "The boy has sustained injuries on his hands and shoulder. He was admitted to hospital," the sub-inspector said.
 
 On Saturday, police, after investigation, arrested the teacher and remanded him to judicial custody. The teacher was booked under Section 323 (Punishment for voluntarily causing hurt.) of the IPC and Section 75 (Punishment for cruelty to child) of the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act.
