KALYAN: Three men were killed when the motorcyle they were riding crashed into a divider in Kalyan on Sunday. While two of the men died on the spot, the third was admitted to hospital and he died during treatment. 

  The deceased,   (25), Sumit Nair (25) and   (45) from Kalyan, were returning home after a party when the   lost control of the bike and rammed into the divider. Kolsewadi police registered an accidental death report. "Preliminary probe shows negligence on part of Bhamre," said senior inspector Nilkanth Patil.
