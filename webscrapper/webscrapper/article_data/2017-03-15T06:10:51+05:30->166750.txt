VADODARA: M S University’s Faculty of   and Swarayan, city-based   will celebrate 131st years of music college by organising ‘Dev Gandharva – Prempriya Sangeet Samaroh’ on March 19 and March 20.

 The festival will be held in remembrance of Pandit Bhasharbau Bakhale and Ustad Faiyaz Khan. 
 
 “Pandit Bakhale was a student when the faculty was taking baby steps as ‘gayan shala’ while Ustad Faiyaz Khan was also a teacher at the faculty,” said Ajay Ashtaputre, faculty’s dean, said. 
 
 Padma Shree recipients Pandit Ulhas Kashalkar and Pandit Suresh Talwalkar perform on the first day. On the second day, Debasmita Bhattacharya will be render a sarod perfomance while Ustad Waseem Ahmed Khan, grandson of legendary Ustad Ata Hussain Khan of Baroda, will accompany her on vocal.
