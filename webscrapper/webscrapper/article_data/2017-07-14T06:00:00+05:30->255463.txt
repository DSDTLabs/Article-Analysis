City artist   led Hyderabadis closer to the realms of music and art by showcasing his creations at a star hotel. 
Titled ‘SoundScapes ‘17’, the exhibition drew art connoisseurs in droves as Aqeel enthusiastically explained the context and nuances of each of his works. 
 President Pranab Mukherjee’s daughter,  , an accomplished Kathak dancer, was spotted browsing through the artworks and admiring them too.
