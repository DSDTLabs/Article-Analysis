MUMBAI: A medical   from the state-run   was found lying in a pool of blood at the ground floor of the hostel building on Thursday morning. Hospital authorities said that Triratna Wakode (27), who hails from Hingoli, was fighting for life and was on life support. The police said that prima facie it looked like Wakode had jumped from the fourth floor of the RM Bhatt hostel. 

 The unconscious Wakode was found by security guards at 11.30 am. “He had a subdural   and the bleeding inside the brain had to be released,” said Dr Sanjay Surase, JJ medical superintendent. Doctors said Wakode was undergoing treatment for depression and had a history of suicide attempts.
