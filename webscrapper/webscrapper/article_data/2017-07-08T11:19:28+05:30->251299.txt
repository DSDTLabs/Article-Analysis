VADODARA: The Vadodara district administration has completed the task of sprinkling anti-mosquito insecticides in 20 villages of the district. The villages are believed to have the highest prevalence of the disease in the district.

 The drive to sprinkle the insecticide was taken up as a part of the malaria-free   initiative of the state government. June was observed as the   control month by the state government.
 
 Door-to-door survey of areas prone to malaria was also conducted by health workers. As many as 59 cases of malaria were identified in the drive and were given treatment.
