VIRUDHUNAGAR : Expelled DMK leader and former Union minister   on Monday took a dig at his nephew   Stalin’s political foray, saying politics was a ‘gutter’ and anybody can enter it.

 He was responding to a question from reporters near here on recent comments of Udhayanidhi, son of DMK working president and Alagiri’s younger brother M K  , that he was already in politics.
 
 “Politics is a gutter and anyone can enter politics,” he said.
 
 Alagiri, Madurai-based elder son of DMK patriarch M Karunanidhi, was expelled from the party in 2014 on charges anti-party activities, capping the sibling rivalry in involving him and M K Stalin.
