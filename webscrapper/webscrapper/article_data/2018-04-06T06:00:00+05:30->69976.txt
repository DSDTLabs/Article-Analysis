India’s first ever gold medalist in women’s wrestling in 2010  ,   was recently in the city as a celebrity guest at a fashion designing institute’s annual sports day celebrations. Her presence boosted the confidence of the students who took part in a number of sporting activities including cricket, football, basketball and badminton among others, at the do. 

  
  
 
 Speaking at the event, Geeta asked the students to give their 100 per cent in whatever they do to achieve their goals. “It’s not easy to juggle between studies and sports but yes, if you are clear about your goals and have passion, you can definitely succeed. This is the first time I am visiting Hyderabad for an event like this and I am happy to be here. I want to tell the students to follow their heart and respect their parents and teachers as they are their ultimate gurus.”
