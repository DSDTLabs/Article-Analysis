SRINAGAR:   became the first foreign airline ever on Sunday to start its operations from   in Jammu and Kashmir. Chief minister   waved the flag to clear the landing for the AirAsia flight at Srinagar airport on Sunday morning.

 The flight operation by AirAsia is expected to give a fillip to tourism in the valley. To begin with, AirAsia, a Malaysian low-cost airline, will operate one flight daily between Delhi and Srinagar.
