LUCKNOW: Children in the state capital will now also be vaccinated for   with the city being included for introduction of pneumococcal conjugate vaccine (PCV) as part of the universal immunization programme. The vaccine will also develop resistance against septicaemia and meningitis. 

  To be launched in Lucknow on May 19, the vaccine is said to be effective against pneumococcal bacteria. Children up to one year of age are administered the vaccine. A workshop of officials for this was held on Thursday with the chief medical officer.
