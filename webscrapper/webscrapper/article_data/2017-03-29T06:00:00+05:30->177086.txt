City’s foodies rang in the festivities early at a pre-Ugadi soiree called ‘Telugotsavam’ held recently. The guests were first welcomed with  , and season’s special beverages — sugarcane juice and butter milk. As everyone greeted each other and caught up over drinks and starters, the band   entertained them with Telugu and Hindi melodies.
Delicacies like Nellore vada, pullattlu, kobbari annam, peethala iguru were a hit with the guests, who ended the meal with desserts, such as apricot delight and putharekulu. Looking more than satisfied with the spread, the gourmands slowly said their goodbyes.
