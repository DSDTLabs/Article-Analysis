Ahmedabad: Deputy section officer of finance department, Hitesh Thakor(32) committed suicide at his residence in Sector 5(a) of Gandhinagar on Tuesday night. Gandhinagar police has registered a case of accidental death and are probing the reason behind Thakor’s extreme step.

 According to police sources Thakor’s father Dashrathbhai, also serves as senior section officer in Gandhinagar. “On Tuesday night Thakor hanged himself from a ceiling fan by a sari. The family is grief stricken. Hence, we will carry out the probe once the rituals are over,” added a senior police official.tnn
