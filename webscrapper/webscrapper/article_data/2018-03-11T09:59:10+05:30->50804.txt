RAJKOT: Four robbers who had robbed a truck driver of cement sacks worth Rs 95,972 at knifepoint in   village of   taluka in   district in November 2016 have been arrested. 

 Police have identified the accused as Ramzanali Juneja, Satubha Jadeja, Kuldipsinh Khimji Sodha and Kuldipsinh Mulji Sodha. The case was being investigated by   local crime branch (LCB) on the complaint of the truck driver, who had mentioned Ramzanali as the suspect in the case.
