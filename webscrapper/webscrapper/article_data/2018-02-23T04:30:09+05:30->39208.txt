MUMBAI: At least a dozen policemen and civic staffers of   were injured in stone pelting allegedly by the land mafia while they were demolishing around 1,000 illegal rooms in Rajivali,   (east), on Thursday.

 
 The chawl mafia is accused of constructing 50 to 100 rooms over the weekends. Construction begins late Friday and is completed by Sunday. By Monday, the rooms are sold and occupied. The illegal structures have come up on forested, government and tribal land plots. 
 
 On Thursday, the civic administration undertook the demolition drive amid heavy security. By 4.45 pm, around 1,000 illegal rooms were razed. Soon after, a mob went on the rampage.
