Fr Trung Pham, an   and educator from Seattle, recently held an exhibition of his soap carvings titled, Vessels of Life, in the city. Currently in Bengaluru as an artist in residence at St Joseph’s  , Fr Trung carvings showcase the fragility and delicacy of everyday objects that give rise to sensibility and feelings of intimacy. Speaking to 
 , he said that teaching students in the city has been a fruitful experience and has taught them perspective, patching, shading, charcoal painting and more. “Students here are more like a collective. In the US, its more individualistic work but here it’s like a collective, they have each other,” he says adding that here, he has to push his students towards the objective.
