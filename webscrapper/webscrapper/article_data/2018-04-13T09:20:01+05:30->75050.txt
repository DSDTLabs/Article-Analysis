RAJKOT: Two burglars thrashed brutally a 62-year-old widow of a renowned industrialist in   and looted cash and ornaments total worth Rs 25 lakh in the early hours of Thursday.

 Sharda   is the widow of late Jayanti Kankhara, a well-known name in Jamnagar industrial circle and the Bhanushali community. She was living alone in a sprawling bungalow ‘Riddhi Siddhi’ located in Digvijay Plot area. 
 
 Sometime between 2am and 3am, two burglars entered the house and woke her up. They tied her, stuffed her mouth with a cloth and started beating her till she fell unconscious. The burglars then took away Rs 10 lakh in cash, 50 tola gold ornaments (worth around Rs 16 lakh), gold biscuits and other jewellery including that of diamonds worth Rs 25 lakh from the safe and cupboards.
 
 In the morning, when she regained consciousness, she started shouting for help. The neighbours then took her to the hospital and informed the police.
 
 Kankhara’s son Mukesh is also no more while her grandson Mitesh lives separately with his mother in a flat near the bungalow. He has lodged a complaint in this case.
 
 Cops said that the CCTV cameras installed in the bungalows were non-functional. It is suspected that someone known to the woman might have committed or masterminded the burglary. Moreover, the area where she lives is a very busy one and vehicular movement continues till around 3am. 
 
 While Jamnagar superintendent of police   also visited the spot of crime, the local crime branch (LCB) has joined the probe with the A-Division police station where the offence has been lodged.
