Panaji: Two days after mining dependents held a massive blockade in Panaji, nearly 40 protesters have been summoned by the Panaji police. 

 Drones were used by the police on the day of the protest as a security measure, and sources said that around 60 CDs of footage of the protest day have been gathered with the help of the drone cameras by the police.
 
 After the footage was examined, around 40 persons have been identified for creating a ruckus in the city on the day of the protest, and the summons have been issued in this regard.
 
 On the protest day, 13 persons were detained. Since Monday, there has been an uproar on social media whereby footage is being circulated by netizens of protesters abusing and hitting bystanders.
