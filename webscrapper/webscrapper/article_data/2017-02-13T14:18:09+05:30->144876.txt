PANAJI: Deputy Chief Minister   is expected to make a quick recovery as the angioplasty he underwent at a Bandra hospital was termed successful by doctors. 

 The cardiologists at the hospital operated D’Souza on Saturday and placed a stent in his heart. During the procedure, doctors found one block in his artery. Doctors have advised D’Souza to take rest.
 
 The deputy chief   is likely to be discharged by Wednesday, doctors told TOI. A close aide of D’Souza said that D’Souza had gone to Mumbai for a trip and when he went for a medical check-up, doctors found his ECG abnormal. Immediately, an angiography was performed. during which a block was found in his artery.
