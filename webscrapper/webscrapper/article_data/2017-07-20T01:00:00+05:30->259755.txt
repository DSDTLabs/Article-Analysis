Following the resounding success in the last three seasons, TIMES NOW hosted the fourth edition of ‘TIMES NOW ICICI Bank NRI of the Year Awards powered by Global Indian International School’ on July 11 in the city. The award salutes the achievement of the Global Indian.

 This year, NRI of the Year Awards was open to all Non Resident Indians (NRI), Overseas Citizen of India (OCI) and Person of Indian origin (PIO), who reside in USA, Canada, UK, Middle East, Singapore, Australia and other countries. The jury was led by author  , lyricist  , lawyer Satish Maneshinde, columnist  , and veteran journalist Kumar Ketkar. Awards were conferred upon 23 individuals under 11 different categories from eight countries.
