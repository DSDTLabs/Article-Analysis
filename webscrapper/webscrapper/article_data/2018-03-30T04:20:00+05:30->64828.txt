Vadodara: Three students of a city-based school stook second at the VIII National Science Film Festival organized by Department of Science and Technology of Government of India held last month.

 Siddhant Agrawal, Siddh Jain and Avi Patel, who study at the Cygnus School, had participated in the film festival in the category of films made by school students. Their movie titled ‘Nine’ which was completely shot, directed and edited by them received the Silver Beaver Award, which was the second highest award in their category.
 
 Many students of class VI to class XII from across the country had submitted their films which were not longer than 10 minutes.
