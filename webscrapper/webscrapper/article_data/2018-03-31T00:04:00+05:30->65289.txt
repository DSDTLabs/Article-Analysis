Pimpri Chinchwad: People living in Pimpri Chinchwad can expect to receive adequate water in summer with the state irrigation department agreeing to provide water from Pavana dam.

 Speaking to TOI, Shravan Hardikar, municipal commissioner, PCMC said, “The civic body lifts around 20 to 25 per cent water more than its allotted quota from the irrigation department. We had requested them at the canal committee meeting in Pune yesterday to provide additional water throughout summer. They have assured us to provide requisite water to meet our additional needs.”
