NEW DELHI: A 24-year-old woman committed suicide at her sister's place in outer Delhi on Wednesday. A suicide note recovered from her blamed an Army soldier for her death. His friend, who was also mentioned in the note, was arrested on Saturday. 

 The victim had met the accused on   who had allegedly made sexual relationship with her promising marriage, police said.
 
 In February, she had gone to   for a family wedding where she also met the accused soldier. They formed sexual relationship at that time, but later he reportedly refused to marry her. Following the refusal, the woman went into depression and stopped talking to her family. On Wednesday, she had gone to her sister's place and locked herself in the room. She was found hanging using a dupatta, police said. The body was handed over to the family after an autopsy.
