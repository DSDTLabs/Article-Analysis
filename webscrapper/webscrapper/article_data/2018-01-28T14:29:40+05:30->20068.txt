Margao: The Railway Protection Force ( ) of Konkan Railway,  , during a raid conducted at the   railway station on Saturday, seized 11 unclaimed gunny bags containing 1,581 liquor bottles of 180 ml each.

 The seized goods were brought to the Margao RPF post for further legal action, RPF sources said. The liquor bottles were to be smuggled to various places in   by train No. 12619 Matshyagandha Express, it was informed.
