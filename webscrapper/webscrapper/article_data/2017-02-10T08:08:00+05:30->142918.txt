NOIDA/GHAZIABAD: The polling booth in   village under Dadri assembly constituency, which had been termed ‘critical’ due to past cases, has been made a model polling booth. The GB Nagar administration has identified eight model   in three assembly constituencies where a number of facilities will be provided to voters.

 In Noida, which is primarily urban, the administration has identified three model polling booths. “We will arrange sofas, chairs for people in waiting. There will be drinking water facility. People can also read newspapers and magazines there. Women voters carrying babies will have creche facilities,” said Keshav Kumar, ADM (finance & revenue), GB Nagar. Similarly, the Ghaziabad district administration has identified 10 model polling booths in the five assembly constituencies.
