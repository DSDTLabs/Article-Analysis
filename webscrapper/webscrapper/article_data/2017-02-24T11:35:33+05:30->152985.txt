 Of the total students awarded degrees, 20,633 were girls. On Thursday, 8,692 graduates collected their degrees from the university in person. 
 
 Total 153 students were awarded gold medal while 56 were awarded PhD degrees and 46  . The graduates were conferred degrees in the presence of Gujarat governor O P Kohli, who is also chancellor of VNSGU.
 
 In his address, Kohli told students that, “When you go out try to help those who did not get opportunity to study. Emotional competence is one of the most importance quality one should have along with others.”
 
 Kohli also hinted to the senate and syndicate members at higher education institutes that they should work in coordination with the vice-chancellor and not as opposition. “There are instances were VCs are actually landing in conflicts with senate and syndicate. Recently, an outgoing VC told media that there should not be senate and syndicate in universities. I wish that the environment should be developed in a manner where these bodies help VCs to develop the institution.”
