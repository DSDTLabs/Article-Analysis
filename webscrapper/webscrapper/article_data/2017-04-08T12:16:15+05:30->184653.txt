AURANGABAD: A 25-year-old   aspirant ended her life by   of her house after a fight with her husband over a petty issue in the Cantonment area on Friday morning. The deceased has been identified as Praneeta Neelkanth Ishware. 

 Police said that the deceased woman, who was from Akola, was a post-graduate in psychology, while her husband Neelkanth had a post-graduate degree in Social Work. 
 
 Police said that the duo got married on April 23, 2016 and were living in a rented accommodation in the Cantonment area. 
 
 The primary investigation has revealed that their respective parents were aware of the clashes between the two. In the recent past, their parents had to intervene to break the ice between the couple.
 
 Cantonment police said that on Friday morning once again a heated argument broke out between the two over some petty issue, following which Neelkanth left home for some work.
 
 Police said that the woman was alone in her house when she took the extreme step and the incident came to light at around 11 am, following which she was rushed to the  , where medical officers declared her dead upon arrival. 
 
 The body was handed over to her family members after conducting an autopsy. A case of accidental death has been registered with the area police station.
