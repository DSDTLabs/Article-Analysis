SHAHJAHANPUR: An  , who had come   last month, allegedly attacked six persons of the same family at Kathia village in  , leaving two children, aged five and 12, dead. The other family members were admitted to the district hospital in serious condition. 

  The incident took place on Wednesday night when accused Avdhesh, along with eight or nine others, entered Rajkumar's house and attacked the family. Avdhesh, also a resident of Kathia village, had killed Rajkumar's father, Ramnath, in October 2015 after suspecting the latter of killing his child and was sent to jail. 
 
  Cops said that the accused men had used sharp objects to attack the family members. An FIR has been registered against Avdhesh, Satyaprakash, one of his accomplices, and other unidentified persons. While Avdhesh and Satyaprakash were arrested on Thursday, the others are still on the run.
