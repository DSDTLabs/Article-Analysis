PANAJI: Konkan Railway has decided to run AC express special (weekly) trains in co-ordination with the South East Central Railway to ease rush of passengers on the Konkan railway route.

 Train No. 08297 Bilaspur-Madgaon AC Express Special (Weekly) train will leave from   at 11.55pm every sunday (from April 2 to June 4). It will reach   at 8am on the third day.
 
 Train No. 08298 Madgaon-Bilaspur AC Express Special (Weekly) train will leave from Madgaon at 10am every Tuesday (from April 4 to June 6) and will reach Bilaspur at 6:30pm the next day.
 
 The 17-coach train will halt at  , Durg, Rajnandgaon, Gondia, Nagpur, Badnera, Akola, Bhusaval, Manmad, Nasik Road, Igatpuri, Kalyan, Panvel, Roha, Khed,Chiplun, Ratnagiri, Sindhudurg, Sawantwadi Road, and Thivim stations.
