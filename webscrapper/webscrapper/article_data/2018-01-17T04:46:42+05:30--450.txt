Actors   and Taapsee Pannu have been revealed as the leading pair of the film 'Dil Juunglee'.
The makers of the romantic-comedy film revealed the lead pair in a fun-filled audition video, titled "Casting for Dil Juunglee", which also featured actor-producer  .
 
 
  
 Actors Nidhi Singh, Abhilash Thapliyal and Sristi Srivastava round off the cast of the film, which is written and directed by Aleya Sen, and produced by Deepshika Deshmukh, Bhagnani, Mudit Jain and Mayank Jain.
 Taapsee also shared a new poster of the film on Twitter, captioning, "Let's make this valentine Junglee ! Here's the first poster of 'Dil Juunglee'."
 
 
  
 Presented by Pooja   and Dinesh Jain, the film will release on February 16, 2018.
