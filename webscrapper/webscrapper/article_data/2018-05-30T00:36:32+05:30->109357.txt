Jaipur: The Anti-Corruption Bureau (ACB) on Tuesday arrested one lower division clerk (LDC) of Amber zone of Jaipur Municipal Corporation (JMC) for allegedly accepting a bribe of Rs 20,000. The LDC was demanding the bribe in lieu of issuing a lease deed of a complainant who approached the ACB headquarters for its intervention.

 According to the ACB officials, the complainant Shyam Sharma approached the ACB office and requested immediate intervention as the LDC identified as Jagdish Sen was pressurising him to pay Rs 80,000 to release his land deed. “After verifying the complaint, a trap was laid in which the accused was caught red-handed for accepting a bribe of Rs 20,000,” said Sachin Mittal, superintendent of police, ACB-I.
