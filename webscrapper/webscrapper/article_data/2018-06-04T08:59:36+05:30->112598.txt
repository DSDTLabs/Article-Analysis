GUWAHATI: Seven people, including a woman, have died in the last 48 hours after consuming spurious country-made liquor in   district's Barpathar area. People reportedly started falling ill after consuming the liquor since Saturday evening. 

 Barpathar police station officer in-charge   on Sunday said two persons who sold the spurious liquor have been arrested. They were peddling liquor illegally from their fast food joint, sources said.
 
 Police swung into action after locals came out in large numbers and tried to dismantle the shop from where the liquor was procured.
