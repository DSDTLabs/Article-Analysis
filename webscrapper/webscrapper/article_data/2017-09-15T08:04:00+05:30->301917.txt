Ludhiana: Punjab sports department has postponed the decision of organizing district-level tournaments for 10 sports events at Guru Nanak Stadium in view of students’ exams. The tournaments were supposed to be organised on September 16 and officials have not released the next date. 

 District sports officer (DSO) Kartar Singh said, “the decision of organizing district level sports competition of 10 games has been postponed in the wake of exams going on in several educational institutes across the district. We got the notification to cancel events and the decision is on hold until new orders are released by the department”. tnn
