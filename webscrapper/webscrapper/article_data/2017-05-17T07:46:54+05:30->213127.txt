GURGAON: Five persons have been booked on Monday for allegedly pressuring a 45-year-old building material supplier, Nihal, to commit suicide. Nihal consumed poison in his Rajendra Park office on Sunday evening. 

 Nihal passed away on Monday morning during treatment at a private hospital, after he was taken there by his colleagues who found him in an unconscious state in his office on Sunday evening. Nihal’s family was informed at around 9pm.
 
 Chaturbhuj, Nihal’s brother, told police on Monday that during Nihal’s treatment at the hospital, he told   that he took the extreme step under pressure from five people. The police have registered a case against Rajesh and Lala of Daulatabad, Janak and Mahavir of   village, and Masti of  , all acquaintances of the deceased. 
 
 A post mortem examination was conducted on Monday, after which the body was handed over to the family for the last rites. Nihal is survived by a wife and two children.
 
 On the complaint of Nihal’s brother, an FIR was registered against the five persons at the Sector 53 police station under sections 306 (abatement to suicide) and 34 (act by several people for common intention) of the IPC.
 
 ASI Krishan Kumar, said they are investigating the matter, no arrests have been made so far. “During preliminary investigation we found out that the deceased may have been under financial duress, and could have borrowed money from the accused. The brother told us that he had borrowed money, but the amount in question is not known currently,” he said, adding that police was unable to interrogate Nihal following the suicide attempt as the doctors had declared him unfit for giving statements.
