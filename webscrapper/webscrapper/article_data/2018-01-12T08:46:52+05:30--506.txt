MARGAO: Four residential units were gutted in a fire that broke out in a house at  , Navelim, Thursday evening.

 Two fire tenders were utilized for dousing the fire, fire brigade sources informed, adding that as per preliminary estimates, goods worth Rs five lakh were destroyed in the fire. 
 
 Some   jewellery that was purchased for a wedding in the family of one of the occupants was also destroyed in the fire, sources said. 
 
 The cause of fire is believed to be short circuit, sources in the fire department said.
