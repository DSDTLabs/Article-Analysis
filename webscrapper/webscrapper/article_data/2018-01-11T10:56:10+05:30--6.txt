NEW DELHI: The detailed examination schedule for the Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) 2018 has been uploaded on the official website of the test exam - gate.iitg.ac.in. As per the available information on the website, the GATE 2018 examination will be conducted for 23 papers across eight sessions starting from February 3, 2018. The GATE Examination 2018 will end on February 11, 2018. GATE is an entrance examination conducted jointly by the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore and the seven Indian Institutes of Technology (at Bombay, Delhi, Guwahati, Kanpur, Kharagpur, Madras and Roorkee) on behalf of the National Coordination Board (NCB)-GATE, Department of Higher Education, Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), Government of India. Various Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) also use the GATE score in their recruitment process. The GATE tests comprehensive understanding of the candidates in various undergraduate subjects in Engineering/Technology/Architecture and post-graduate level subjects in Science. This year, GATE 2018 examination will be conducted by the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati.
