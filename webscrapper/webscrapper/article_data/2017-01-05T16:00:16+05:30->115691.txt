CHANDAPUR: Former workers of M/s   staged a day-long sit-in agitation at Jatpura gate on Wednesday under the banner of  .

 The protesters flayed the alleged deception of the company in making them take VRS and demanded action against the management and union leaders.
 
 They alleged that the company announced through a notice that it is suffering heavy losses and is contemplating on laying off its workers in July 2015. The workers were given the option to take voluntary retirement or face lay-off and transfers.
 
 As two more notices were served and a bunch of employees got transferred, the workers panicked and approached the labour union for support. Workers charged that union leaders allegedly connived with the company management and convinced to them to go for VRS. However, after they took VRS, the company allegedly hired contract workers.
