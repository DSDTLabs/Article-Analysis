Rajkot: The city police officials conducted 22 raids on illegal liquor trade and caught 16 bootleggers, including nine women, on Saturday night. Among those arrested also included one minor from whose possession two bottles of Indian made foreign liquor (IMFL) were confiscated. Those bootleggers who gave police the slip include Lakshman Koli in Nadodanagar, Bijal Devipujak in Kitipara, Dipak Jadav in Lohanagar, Shiva Devipujak and Rajesh Sadmiya in Raiyadhar, and Dhani Jakhania in Raiyagam. tnn
