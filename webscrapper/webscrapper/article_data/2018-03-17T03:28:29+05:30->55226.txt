GURUGRAM: A 21-year-old man has been detained for allegedly raping a minor girl in Gurugram’s   village after she lodged a complaint with the women police station on Thursday.

 The 16-year-old girl, in her complaint, alleged that Sumit, a neighbour, had been exploiting her sexually on the pretext of marriage for the last six months or so. The girl finally approached police after he raped her again on Thursday.
 
 Police said both the survivor and the accused were from   and had been living in the same neighbourhood in the city for the past two years.
 
 Preliminary probe revealed that Sumit allegedly lured the girl promising marriage and raped her first time on September 17 last year. After that he allegedly forced himself on the girl on a number of occasions. “Eventually, when the girl became pregnant, he forced her to abort a three-month-old fetus. He again approached her with a fresh proposal for marriage and raped her again on Thursday. This time, the girl confided to her family,” said a cop.
 
 On the basis of the complaint of the girl, an FIR has been filed against Sumit under section 6 of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act, 313 (miscarriage without woman’s consent), 506 (criminal intimidation) of the IPC at the women police station. Subsequently, Sumit was taken into custody while the girl was sent for medical examinations, which probed rape.
 
 “The girl has recorded her statement in court under Section 164 of the CrPC. We are questioning the accused and action will be taken as per law,” said inspector  , SHO, women police station.
