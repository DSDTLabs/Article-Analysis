Panaji: Priyanka Kashyap has been posted as the new anti-corruption branch SP, while Bosco George, who was holding the post has been told to report to the police headquarters in Panaji until further orders. Under George, ACB had been active and several cases including politically sensitive ones had been booked. The reason for his transfer on Wednesday was not known. By another order on Wednesday, Suman Goyal who was on child care leave like Priyanka Kashyap, has been posted now as SP of the economic offences cell, relieving Karthik Kashyap of the additional charge. tnn
