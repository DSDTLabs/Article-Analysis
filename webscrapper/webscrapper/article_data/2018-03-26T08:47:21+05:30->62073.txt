BHOPAL: A 45-year-old man, who climbed on top of a drumstick tree in   locality on Saturday afternoon, lost balance and fell on an adjoining wall. 

 A protruding iron rod pierced through his abdomen and he suffered serious injuries. 
 
  Cops reached the spot after getting information and brought him down after cutting the rod, but without taking it out from his body. He was rushed to JP hospital from where he was referred to   hospital, where doctors operated upon him and the rod was taken out. 
 
 The man was undergoing treatment till the filing of report. ASI   said that the injured was identified as  , a resident of Baba Nagar.
