In two separate incidents, six people died in Nellore district on Sunday. In the first incident,   people died after their car rammed a parked lorry in NTR Nagar area. According to police, the incident took place when they tried to overtake a vehicle from the left and suddenly lost control and rammed a parked lorry. In the second incident, two persons travelling on a scooty rammed into an oncoming RTC bus at Prashant Nagar area in Nellore town. Cops registered cases. tnn
