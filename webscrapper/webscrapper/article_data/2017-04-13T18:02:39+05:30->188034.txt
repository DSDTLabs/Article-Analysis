RAJKOT: With temperatures soaring in the city, the   (RMC) has formed six special squads to keep tab on illegal water connections and water thefts. 

 Municipal commissioner   said the squad consists of assistant manager,   engineer and ward engineers among others. The teams have been asked to visit various wards in their jurisdiction to detect illegal connections, thefts and direct pumping in various parts of the city.
 
 “Demand for water has increased and local sources for Rajkot are   fast. There is a need to keep tab on illegal drawing of water and ensure equal distribution of water for all,” he said.
