 The function began army band performing on ‘Vande Mataram’ with slow march at first and concluded with ‘Sare Jahan Se Accha’ with fast march.
 
 The police brass band also played Spanish Gipsy Dance from movie Espana Cani, Jhumta Mausam Mst from movie Ujala, Hum Tum Yug Yug Se and other such songs.
 
 Beating Retreat is a military custom dating from the days when troops disengaged from battle at sunset or soon after. As soon as the bugles sounded, troops stopped fighting, sheathed their arms and withdrew from the battlefield.
