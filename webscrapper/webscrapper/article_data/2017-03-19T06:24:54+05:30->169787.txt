NAVI MUMBAI: A woman who resides in   has lodged a complaint against her 14-year-old son for allegedly impregnating his 17-year-old sister. Accordingly, on Friday, the local police registered a rape case against the boy and booked him under section 376 of the Indian Penal Code, along with relevant sections of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act. 

 According to the Kopar Khairane police, the complainant, who works as a housemaid, alleged that her daughter was sexually assaulted by her brother on two occasions in October 2016. The incident came to light after the girl complained of stomach pain and was taken to a private doctor. This is when her mother learnt that she was five months pregnant. Upon questioning the girl revealed that her brother had sexually assaulted her twice when she was alone at home while their parents were away at work. Their father is an autorickshaw driver.
 
 The police detained the boy, a student of Class IX, on Saturday and are inquiring into the matter.
