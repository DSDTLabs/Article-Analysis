MUMBAI: The   police on Tuesday arrested four people for allegedly submitting bogus documents to obtain visas from the   in Mumbai. 

 The German consulate at Marine Drive told the police after their staff identified the forgeries during internal scrutiny. The consulate had last year lodged seven cases in which applicants had submitted fake papers to secure visas. The accused were arrested when they arrived to collect their passport and visa.
 
 The four men are from Ahmedabad and met an agent who offered help in obtaining the visas.
