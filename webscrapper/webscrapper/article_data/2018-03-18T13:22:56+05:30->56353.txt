AMARAVATI:   chief minister Chandrababu   has asked his party MPs to be alert for the next two days.

 Speaking to the MPs via a teleconference on Saturday, Naidu wanted them to keep in touch with the leaders of various regional and national parties that are ‘sympathetic’ towards AP’s concerns.
 
 
 He also directed them to expose the ‘corruption’ politics of the  , particularly MP Vijaysai Reddy, who is making regular visits to the Prime Ministers’ Office. He wanted the MPs to expose the YSRCP-BJP nexus that has developed in the recent past. He expressed concern over courts clearing cases against   chief Jaganmohan Reddy and Vijaysai Reddy and wanted the MPs to convey this message to leaders of other parties.
 
 Naidu wanted the TDP MPs to expose the backdoor deals of YSR Congress and BJP. He asked them to keep the protest alive both inside and outside Parliament on the demand over special category status for Andhra Pradesh.
 
 “There is no compromise on the state issues and there is no scope for the YSR Congress to take advantage of the situation and take political mileage,” he said.
