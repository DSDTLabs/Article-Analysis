AGRA: A 9th class student of AMU's  , who had gone for swimming with his friends at Kachla canal drowned on Sunday evening. At time of filing this report efforts were being made trace the body of a young boy.
 
  According to police, seven boys, between the age of 15-17 years, went for swimming on Sunday at around 5 pm. While they were swimming   drowned in the canal.
  
 Other boys immediately informed the villagers who in turn called the police. Talking to TOI, Superintendent of Police (Rural)   said that rescue operation is still on to trace the body.
