TRICHY: Makkal Adhikaram outfit staged a protest by burning the   flag in front of the   at   in   on Saturday over Cauvery row.

 As many as 50 protestors including women and few children gathered in front of the Sabha around 11 am today. The protestors smeared the name of the sabha engraved on the wall with black paint despite police attempting to prevent them.
 
 Further, they turned towards the road and burnt a flag of BJP. They also raised slogans against the central government for not constituting Cauvery management board ( ). The police managed to arrest around 30 agitators including few women in the protest.
 
 The protest also witnessed a heated argument between the journalists and the police personnel following a minor scuffle between the police and a media reporter.
