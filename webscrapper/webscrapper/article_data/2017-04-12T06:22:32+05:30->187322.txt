CUTTACK:   turned ugly at   in   in   after two   on Tuesday leaving four persons injured. Members of one group allegedly set the thatched houses of the rival group members on fire. 

 Police confirmed the violence was related to recent panchayat polls. A few families of Kumarpur had supported a particular candidate due to which there was tension. 
 
 Police said the clash started on Monday night with the two groups using stones and glass bottles as missiles. There was an altercation on Tuesday morning and in no time the area turned into a battlefield.
