THIRUVANANTHAPURAM: After evading arrest for more than a month,   Anchal area secretary   surrendered before the   officials in connection with the murder of   worker   of Punalur in Kollam. Suman turned himself in after appearing before the investigating officers in the CBI office here around 2 pm. He is facing conspiracy charges in the case.

 Congress leader Ramabhadran was killed on April 10, 2010. Many CPM workers, including senior leaders, are accused in the case. In November 2016, the CBI had arrested five CPM workers in connection with the case.
 
 Though Suman was also interrogated earlier by the CBI, he went absconding after his comrades were arrested by the CBI.
