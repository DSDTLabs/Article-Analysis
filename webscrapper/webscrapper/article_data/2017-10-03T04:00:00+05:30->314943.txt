Gandhinagar: Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) organized a major roadshow with around 1,000 vehicles in East Ahmedabad on Monday, Gandhi Jayanti, to start their campaign for state assembly elections. Gopal Rai, minister in the AAP-led Delhi government, and   AAP in-charge, led the rally.

 The roadshow was on the theme ‘BJP hatavo Parivartan lavo (remove  , bring change). In his speech to   supporters, Rai said, “BJP’s ego and corruption has become higher than Mt   and the people of Gujarat will bring them down.’’ 
 
 AAP is expected to announce its detailed campaign plan in a couple of days, and also release its first list of candidates for the assembly elections shortly.
