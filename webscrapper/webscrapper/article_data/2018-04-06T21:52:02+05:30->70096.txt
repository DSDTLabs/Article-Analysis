Ludhiana: The MC on Friday started dismantling illegal structures in Subhash Nagar where a leisure valley would be developed. 

 MC officials also sealed three illegal buildings in the city. The action was taken after the orders by MC commissioner Jaskiran Singh. 
 
 The officials sealed three buildings in Sunder Nagar, Gill Road and Field Ganj. The officials had earlier stopped the construction of these buildings, but when the owners continued the construction, they sealed the premises. 
 
 Recently, deputy commissioner Pardeep Agrawal, MC commissioner Jaskiran Singh and area MLA Sanjay Talwar had visited the leisure valley site for inspection.
