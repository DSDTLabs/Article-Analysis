MUMBAI:  , son of former Maharashtra minister and senior Congress leader late  , today took oath as a member of the Legislative Assembly.

 Speaker Hari Bhau Bagde administered the oath to Kadam at the central hall in Vidhan Bhavan. Congress leaders and former chief ministers Prithviraj Chavan and Ashok Chavan were present on this occasion.
 
 Vishwajeet Kadam was elected unopposed in the by-election from   assembly constituency in Sangli district.
 
 The bypoll was necessitated by the death of Patangrao Kadam who had represented the constituency for six terms.
 
 While Congress fielded Vishwajeet, Shiv Sena declared support to him.
 
 He was elected unopposed after the BJP withdrew its candidate from the fray.
