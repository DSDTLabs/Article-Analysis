MUMBAI: High-level changes are expected in the bureaucracy following the retirement of chief secretary   on Monday. Additional   ( ) D K Jain, an   officer of the 1983 batch, is likely to succeed Mullick. 

 
 An IAS officer of the 1982 batch, Mullick will be new chief information commissioner, a post that has been vacant for over a year.
 
 In the 1983 batch,   (relief and rehab) Medha Gadgil is the seniormost, followed by ACS (home) Sudhir Shrivastava, Jain, metropolitan commissioner U P S Madan, Sanjeevani Kutty, who is on deputation with the Centre, and ACS (industries) Sunil Porwal.
