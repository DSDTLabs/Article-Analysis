PANAJI: The education department has issued a circular to schools to re-schedule their 1st terminal examinations/summative examinations. The aim of the circular is to enable students to attend the   U-17 World Cup matches that will be held at Nehru stadium, Fatorda. 

 The July 5 circular, signed by director of education G P Bhat has directed heads of schools conclude examinations on or before October 7.
 
 The World Cup commences on October 6, but Group C matches in Goa kick off the next day with   taking on Costa Rica at 5pm. Iran plays   at 8pm.
 
 The circular has been marked out to heads of all government/government-aided and unaided primary, middle, secondary, higher secondary and special schools in the state.
