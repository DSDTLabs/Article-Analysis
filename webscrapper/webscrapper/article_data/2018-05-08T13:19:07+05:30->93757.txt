LUCKNOW: A thunder storm accompanied by gusty winds may lash many parts of the state including Lucknow on Tuesday.

 The state Met office in its warning issued on Monday predicted possibilities of thunder/dust storm with squall measuring up to 50-70 km per hour in the entire state from midnight till Wednesday morning. The impact is expected to be more in some West UP districts including Rampur, Moradabad, Gaziabad, Baghpat, Meerut, Bijnor, Muzzafarnagar, Saharanpur, Bareilly, Badaun, Aligarh, Bulandsahar, Gautam Budh Nagar, Mathura, Etah and Hathras. State Met director   said that a western disturbance over J&K along with a cyclonic circulation over   and a `trough line' passing from over   till   has created a situation favourable for thunder/ dust storm in the state.
