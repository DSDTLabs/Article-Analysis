While most of the city dwelled on the devotional aspect of Shivaratri, that follows the mythological episode of ‘Samudra Manthan’, the literati in the city hosted a ‘manthan’ of a different kind with the former Union Minister of Finance,
  in attendance. While the Congress politico’s presence made it a must-go event for the politicos in the city, the young folk also showed up in large numbers with questions about everything, from demonetisation to the political affairs of Kashmir.
