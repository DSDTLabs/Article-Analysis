The Sushant Lok Durga Puja Pandal recreates the iconic fortress from  's Sonar Kella with jute, and has been designed by renowned artist Kashinath Das. The celebration is eco-friendly, with the idol made completely out of mud and all biodegradable materials used in the pandal decorations. It is also the only puja in Gurgaon that has free food for all visitors during the day time.
