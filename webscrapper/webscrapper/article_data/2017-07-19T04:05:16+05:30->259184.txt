GURUGRAM: Two men have been booked for allegedly robbing a 25-year-old   of her bag at gunpoint near   flyover on Monday night. 

 The victim,   — who works at a mobile company in Cyber City — was returning home in Sector 30 when the crime took place. According to Nandini, she was targeted by the assailants around 8.40pm when she reached the Iffco Chowk flyover in an autorickshaw. Two men riding a motorcycle intercepted the auto and within minutes the accused snatched Nandini’s bag that contained Rs 1,500 in cash, two ATM cards, credit card and mobile phones at gunpoint. 
 
 As the accused fled after robbing the woman, the autorickshaw driver informed the cops who reached the spot and lodged a complaint. An FIR was then registered against the duo under Section 379-B (snatching & use of force) of the IPC at Section 25-54-59 of the Arms Act at DLF-2 police staion. “We are investigating the matter and conducting raids to nab the accused,” said inspector Sudip Kumar, SHO, DLF-2 police station. 
 
 In another incident, a Delhi resident was robbed of his cash and valuables by two bike-borne assailants in Udyog Vihar-5 on Saturday. The accused forcefully stopped Kamlesh Sharma and snatched his bag containing a laptop, Rs 1.5 lakh in cash, mobile phone and wallet. 
 
 As per the complaint, an FIR has been registered against two unidentified youths under Section 379-A (snatching) of the IPC at Udyog Vihar police station. 
 
 “We are trying to identify the accused with the help of CCTV footage acquired from the area,” said inspector Arjun Dundhara, SHO, Udyog Vihar police station.
