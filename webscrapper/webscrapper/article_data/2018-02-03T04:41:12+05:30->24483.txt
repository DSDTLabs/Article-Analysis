 The Government Railway Police ( ) said that the men were waiting to board the  , which was to arrive at siding platform number 3. The two aimed to jump into the general coach to sell their wares as the train slowed down while changing over to the siding platform.

 The bodies were badly mutilated and were sent for an autopsy. A case of accidental death was registered.
