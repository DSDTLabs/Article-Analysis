PORVORIM: The   (amendment) Bill, 2017, which seeks to enhance the retirement age of the teaching staff of   (GMC),   and the   (IPHP), Bambolim, from 62 years to 65 years, was tabled in the Goa legislative assembly on Thursday.

 The bill proposes to amend section 15A of the Goa University Act in a bid to prevent the loss of senior and experienced teaching faculty. 
 
 The GMC itself has numerous posts unfilled as it has been unable to attract the desired experienced faculty.
 
 The bill also seeks to repeal the Goa University (Amendment), 2016 which was issued in December 2016.
