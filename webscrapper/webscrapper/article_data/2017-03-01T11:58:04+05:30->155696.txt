PIMPRI CHINCHWAD: Residents from Chakan, Hinjewadi, Talegaon and Lonavla will soon benefit from a new passport service centre, which is expected to come up at a post office in  . 

 In a letter to Shrirang Barne, Shiv Sena MP from Maval, external affairs minister Sushma Swaraj has said that the centre will start functioning in the next few months. 
 
 During his recent meeting with Swaraj, Barne had apprised her about the hardships the people from these places were facing as they had to go all the way to Pune for passport-related work. Barne received the letter from the minister on Monday.
