Bathinda: An indebted farmer ended life by consuming poisonous substance in a Bathinda village. The deceased has been identified as Naib Singh (46) of village Ramniwas. According to Naib Singh's father Mohinder Singh, The deceased had 5 acres of land and owed Rs 12 lakh to a bank and money lender. “Naib was under depression for some time for not being able to repay the loan and as a result he killed himself on Wednesday night by consuming some poisonous substance,” he said.
