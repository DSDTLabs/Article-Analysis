GHAZIABAD: A woman’s body was found near the   in the early hours of Tuesday. 

 “The body was found wrapped in a large piece of cloth along the water pipeline in Uttaranchal Colony. A passerby noticed the suspicious bundle and informed us,” said O P Chaudhary, SHO of Loni police station. There were injuries on the mouth and neck of the body of the woman, who is estimated to be in her early 30s. Her clothes were torn. Cops suspect that the woman was brought to the spot by a relative and sexually assaulted before being murdered. The body has been sent for post-mortem.
