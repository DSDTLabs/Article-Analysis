New Delhi: Secretary (social welfare and women & child development) Dilraj Kaur, whose appointment as member secretary of   Commission for Women by former lieutenant governor Najeeb Jung was disputed by CM Arvind Kejriwal, has been transferred as additional commissioner in South Delhi Municipal Corporation.

 More recently, Kaur was also at loggerheads with   chairperson Swati Maliwal over payment of salaries to the DCW staff. 
 
 Kaur has been replaced by Garima Gupta as DCW member secretary. Gupta is at present director-cum-special secretary (higher education). Anil Kumar Singh, secretary-cum-commissioner (labour) will hold additional charge as secretary (social welfare and women & child development) till further notice.
