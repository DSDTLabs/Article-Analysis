LUCKNOW: Rajasthan governor   is the first among six former UP CMs to move out of his government  .

 Kalyan started shifting of household articles from his Mall Avenue bungalow on Tuesday. He will vacate the bungalow by May end.
 
 The former CM has also cleared the rent and electricity bills of his government bungalow.
 
 He will now move into the house of his grandson   in Dalibagh. Singh is minister of state in   government. Kalyan was allotted the government bungalow after he resigned as UP CM in November 1999, said his aide.
 
 Last week, state government had served notice to six former UP CMs, including Kayan Singh, to vacate their official bungalows following a Supreme Court ruling.
 
 Union home minister Rajnath Singh will also vacate his 4, Kalidas Marg residence and shift to his home in Gomtinagar. Former CM Akhilesh Yadav has sought two years time to vacte his bungalow on Vikramaditya Marg.
