RAJKOT: A rickshaw driver was arrested for allegedly raping a 14-year-old girl living in slums on Morbi Road.

 The girl’s father lodged a complaint against Pintu Vikani alleging that he abducted his daughter on April 17 and sexually assaulted her several times for 10 days. The girl told police that Vikani took her to forest area near Junagadh where he raped her several times. He then dropped the girl to her house on April 17. After she returned, the girl’s family, who is poor and illiterate, left for their native village Tankara. However, after some relatives advised them to lodge a complaint, the girl and her father approached B-division police station on Wednesday.tnn
