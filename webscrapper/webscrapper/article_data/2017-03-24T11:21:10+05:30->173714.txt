Lal Junior's latest film ' : Celebrations,' a sequel to his 'Honey Bee,' released in 2013, is just out.
 
  In the first movie, actress Archana Kavi played the role of Sara, who is the fiancee of Baburaj's character Ferno. However, she was replaced by TV personality cum actress Arya, in the sequel. 
  
  Arya, who has acted in films like 'Thoppil Joppan,' 'Pretham,' 'Lailaa O Lailaa' and 'Kunjiramayanam,' also has many TV shows to her credit.
