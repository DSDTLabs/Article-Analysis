CALANGUTE: A beach shack owner was arrested at Candolim late Wednesday night for playing loud music in his shack without any permission. 

 Following a complaint lodged with the   (SDPO) at   Dias, Calangute PI   and a team of police personnel raided the shack at Vaddi, Candolim, between 9.50 and 11.50pm and found that loud music was being played open-air without any permission, thereby violating rule 4(1) of the Noise Pollution (Regulation and Control) Rules, 2000. Shack owner  , 40, was arrested and a laptop, amplifier, mixer and speakerstogether estimated to be worth Rs 2 lakhwere attached.
