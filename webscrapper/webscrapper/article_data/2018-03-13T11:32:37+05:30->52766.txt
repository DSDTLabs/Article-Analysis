Govinda’s actual phone number was used in the popular track of the 90s ‘What is mobile number?’ from the 1999 film ‘Haseena Maan Jaayegi’. The film had two lead pairings of Govinda and   and   and   and its title track and another song ‘I Love You Bol Daal’ were equally popular. It had a very interesting cast including Anupam Kher, Kader Khan, Aruna Irani and Paresh Rawal in prominent roles.
