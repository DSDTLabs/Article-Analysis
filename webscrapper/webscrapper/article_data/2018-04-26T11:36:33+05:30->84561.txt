MAPUSA: The   ( ) on Wednesday commenced its drive against encroachments by shopkeepers on the nullah behind the municipal market area.

 Sources say that the lane along the nullah, situated between the municipal market and the sub-yard, houses 46 establishments of which 44 have encroached an area of about 1.5m of the nullah.
 
 Sources said that on the first day, authorities cleared the roofing of the structures in the encroached portion. The shopkeepers were given time to clear their belongings from this portion. The demolition drive will continue on Thursday where the part of the structures in the encroached area will be demolished and cleared.
 
 The water resources department is currently executing work of desilting and reconstruction of this nullah.
